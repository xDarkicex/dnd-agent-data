# Mountain Dweller

**description**: An adventurer and explorer by nature, no unmarked path and trail can withstand your determination. You seek something beyond the material and wish to explore the world around you. Altitude Adaptation. You are acclimated to thin air and harsh weather conditions commonly found in elevated regions. You have advantage on checks and saves against exhaustion caused by cold, altitude and climbing fatigue. Skills: Animal Handling (Wisdom), Survival (Wisdom) Tools: Navigator’s Tools or Cook’s Utensils Equipment: A climber’s kit, a herbalism kit, a set of traveler’s clothes, a belt pouch containing 12 gp, and a small telescope. d8 Personality Traits 1 I take the roughest path to my destination. 2 My nature does not always fit the situation. 3 I do not feel at ease with wealthy people. 4 Wilderness survival is second nature to me. 5 I feel claustrophobic in large cities. 6 My eyes always seem to drift to the sky. 7 I enjoy the company of animals. 8 I do not ask for help easily. d6 Ideal 1 Nature: I respect mother nature. 2 Thorough: I strive to do the best job I can. 3 Punish: Criminals and those who cause harm should be severely punished. 4 Oath: I keep my promises. 5 Respect: The traditions and way of life must be cherished and respected. 6 Grit: To never surrender. d6 Bond 1 I once climbed the tallest peak known to humankind. 2 I learned to respect artisans as a noble trade. 3 I always fulfill my part of the bargain when I shake on a deal. 4 I respect local traditions wherever I travel. 5 I survived a mining accident that left me and others in the darkness for weeks. 6 People have come to respect me as someone trustworthy. d6 Flaw 1 I take violence to those who destroy nature. 2 I care little for people who do not respect their trade tools. 3 I awake in the morning in a dark, foul mood. 4 Sometimes, I fall asleep while in the company of others. 5 At mealtime, I do not leave much food for others. 6 My sense of duty makes me inflexible.
