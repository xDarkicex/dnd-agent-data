# Court Apprentice

**description**: Studious and law-abiding, you believed the Inquisition Courts held the truth above any other. Years of service led you through thousands of documents and books. You witnessed countless trials where most were sentenced as guilty, and only a handful absolved. You have now gathered enough knowledge to make up your mind. Court Etiquette. You are well-versed in the customs and manners of the aristocracy. When dealing with a nobleperson, their attitude towards you is better than average unless you have given them cause to distrust you.
