# Village Healer

**description**: From a young age, you were adept at healing those who were infirm. You have honed your skills on your journey through discovering the world of plants and potions. Heartfelt Recognition. Your reputation as a healer precedes you, making it easier to gain trust. When interacting with non-hostile creatures in a village or similar community setting, their attitude towards you is better than average unless you have given them cause to distrust you.
