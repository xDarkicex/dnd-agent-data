# Psychic Overload

**description**: You attempt to overwhelm the mind of one creature you can see within range, leaving it dumbfounded. If the target has 150 hit points or fewer, it is stunned. Otherwise, the power has no effect. The stunned target must make a Constitution saving throw at the end of each of its turns. On a successful save, the stunning effect ends.
