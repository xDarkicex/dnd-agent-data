# Thunderous Distortion

**description**: Evocation cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self (10-foot cone) Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous You produce a distorted wave of noise in a 10-foot cone, which can be heard up to 100 feet away. Each creature in that area must succeed a Constitution saving throw, or take 1d6 thunder damage. An echo of this noise persists until the end of your next turn. If you cast this spell again before the end of your next turn, its damage becomes d8s, instead of d6s. This spell’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
