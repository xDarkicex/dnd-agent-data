# Watchful Eye

**description**: 9th-level lair magic (conjuration) Casting Time: 4 weeks (28 days) Radius: 100 miles Components: V, S, M (the souls of 1000 creatures with an Intelligence score of 8 or greater, which are sacrificed as a part of casting this spell. The souls are consumed by the spell) Duration: 100 years This spell summons a burning eye over the site of its casting, visible from many miles away. The eye can see everything that happens within the radius of the spell, ignoring cover and concealment, with a passive Perception of 20. It relays all important information to the lair's master, keeping them apprised of all happenings in their domain.
