# Stanch

**description**: The target’s blood coagulates rapidly, so that a dying target stabilizes and any ongoing bleeding or wounding effect on the target ends. The target can’t be the source of blood for any spell or effect that requires even a drop of blood.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 1
- School: transmutation
- Components: V, S
- Casting Time: 1 action
- data-RangeAoe: touch

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Midgard Worldbook
