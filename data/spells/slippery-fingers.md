**description**: 1st-Level Enchantment Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous You set a series of small events in motion that cause the targeted creature to drop one nonmagical item of your choice that it’s currently holding, unless it makes a successful Charisma saving throw.
