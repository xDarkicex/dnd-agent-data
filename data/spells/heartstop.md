# Heartstop

**description**: 2nd-Level Necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes You slow the beating of a willing target’s heart to the rate of one beat per minute. The creature’s breathing almost stops. To a casual or brief observer, the subject appears dead. At the end of the spell, the creature returns to normal with no ill effects.
