# Spatial Sever

**description**: You create tiny folds and ripples in the space of a 5-foot area. These distortions are nearly invisible, requiring a successful Wisdom saving throw to see them. A creature takes 2d8 slashing damage when it enters the area of effect for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 3 or higher the damage increases by 1d8 for each rank above 1.
