# Intuition

**description**: 8th-level divination Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V Duration: 1 hour Until the spell ends, when you make a Wisdom check, you can replace the number you roll with a 15. Additionally, no matter what a creature says to you, you magically learn if the creature is lying to you or hiding a secret related to the topic of conversation.
