# Disarming Glance

**description**: This spell allows to force a creature to discard what he is holding just by looking at them and speaking an intimidating word. If the target can see you and hear you (though it need not understand you), it must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or take 2d8 psychic damage and drop all objects the creature is holding. The objects land at its feet. If you are proficient in the Intimidate skill, you add your proficiency bonus to the psychic damage dealt by this spell. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slotof 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for eachslot level above 1st.
