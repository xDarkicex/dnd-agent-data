# Tale of Courage

**description**: 3rd-level illusion Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self (30-foot radius) Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You begin telling a tale of bravery and heroism, radiating an aura of courage in a 30-foot radius around you. Within the aura, an ephemeral vignette appears of heroes bravely battling monsters. Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. Each nonhostile creature in the aura (including you) is immune to being frightened and gains a +1 bonus to saving throws. If a creature can’t hear you or see the vignette, it gains no benefit from this spell. If you speak other than to tell the tale, the spell ends.
