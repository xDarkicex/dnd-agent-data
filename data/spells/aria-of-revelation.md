# Aria of Revelation

**description**: You sing a song that causes all around you to be seen as it truly is. All creatures and objects in the spell’s area are made visible. Creatures and objects in the area that are on the Ethereal Plane appear in the spell’s area as ghostly and translucent. All illusion spells in the area become translucent and can be identified for what they are. If a creature in the area is a shapechanger that is not in its true form, it glows with a purple light as long as it remains in the area. Additionally, while you are concentrating on this spell, any secret doors or traps in the area glow with purple light.
