# Steadfast

**description**: Your spell makes it harder to knock you down or to unseat you if you are mounted. You gain advantage on saving throws against spells and on Strength (Athletics) or Dexterity (Acrobatics) skill checks against grappling creatures and effects that attempt to push you or force you to become prone. You also gain advantage on saving throws and ability checks against any effect that would force you to fall from the back of your mount.
