# Ironfiber

**description**: You touch fungal material and transform it into ironfiber, a magical substance created by druids from normal plant matter. While remaining naturally fibrous in almost every way, ironfiber is as strong, heavy, and as resistant to fire as steel. Spells that affect metal or iron do not function on ironfiber. Spells that affect plants do affect ironfiber, although ironfiber does not burn. Using this spell with fiber shape or crafting, you can fashion fibrous items that function as metal items. Thus, fibrous plate armor and fibrous swords can be created that are as durable as their normal steel counterparts.
