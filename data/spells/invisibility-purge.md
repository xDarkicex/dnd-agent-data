# Invisibility Purge

**description**: 4th-level abjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self (120-foot radius) Components: V, S, M (powdered silver worth at least 100 gp) Duration: Instantaneous You purge magical invisibility effects within 120 feet of you. Any creature, object, location, or effect within 120 feet of you that is hidden by magical invisibility is revealed and the invisibility is dispelled. This spell applies to innate abilities that are magical in nature, such as an imp’s Invisibility, but it doesn’t affect creatures on other planes of existence, such as the Ethereal Plane. It also doesn’t reveal creatures that are naturally invisible. Items that grant magical invisibility (such as a ring of invisibility ) have their effects suppressed for 1 minute.
