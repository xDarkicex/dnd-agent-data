# Cure Beast

**description**: 1st-Level Evocation Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous A beast of your choice that you can see within range regains a number of Hit Points equal to 1d6 + your spellcasting modifier. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the healing increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.
