# Flowering

**description**: Transmutation cantrip Casting time: 1 action Range: 20 feet Components: v Duration: concentration, up to 1 minute You cause fragrant flowers to grow from a nonmagical, inanimate object. If the object is held by an unwilling creature, that creature can make a dexterity saving throw to negate the effect. If sustained for the full duration, the flowers give forth a pleasing scent and may attract bees, hummingbirds, or other pollinators. If the object can’t normally sustain living flowers, the flowers wilt away after 24 hours.
