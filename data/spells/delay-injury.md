# Delay Injury

**description**: Necromancy cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S, M (a bundle of healing herbs) Duration: Instantaneous One creature within range chooses an Injury they have sustained of any severity. That creature makes a Constitution saving throw with a DC equal to the Medicine check DC to temporarily recover from the Injury. On a successful saving throw, they ignore the effects of that Injury until the end of their next turn, or until they take damage, whichever is longer, up to a maximum of 1 minute.
