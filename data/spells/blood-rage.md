# Blood Rage

**description**: You channel all the magical energy in your blood into one lethal attack, you are left with 1HP at the end of the attack. You look around and unleash a pulse of blood exploding outwards from you like canon fire, then you run up to 400ft in a straight line at incredible speeds with no sign of you but the splashes of blood. Any creatures caught within the explosion must make a Dexterity saving throw vs your spell save DC, taking 12d10 force damage and 12d12 + (your HP spent casting this spell) Necrotic damage on a failed save, or half damage on a successful one. Additionally, all creatures caught in the explosion become blood soaked, and the affected area becomes filled with blood. When running because of the effect of this spell, you move as an invisible and ethereal being that moves through objects. Only magical objects/ barriers or adamantine can stop your movement.
