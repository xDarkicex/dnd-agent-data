# Earth Glide

**description**: 3rd-level transmutation (ritual) Casting time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: v, s, m (a piece of a creature that has the earth glide trait) Duration: 1 hour This spell grants up to ten willing creatures you can see within range the ability burrow through nonmagical, unworked earth and stone for the duration. Each target gains a burrowing speed equal to half its walking speed. While burrowing, an affected target doesn’t disturb the material through which it moves. If the duration ends with an affected target within unworked earth or stone, the spell carries the target to the surface at a rate of 60 feet per round.
