# Superior Recall

**description**: Superior Recall Requirements: Level 6 Casting Time: 1 action Range: self Duration: concentration (up tp 10 minutes) Cost: 2 WP As Perfect Recall, but the artist can witness the scene from any point within 20 feet of his or her actual position at the time. As even a second ago is the past, it is effectively possible to do this practically in real time. You can’t take any action at all while using this feature. And no, the artist can’t stay in the remembered scene past the point he originally witnessed.
