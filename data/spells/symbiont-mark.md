# Symbiont Mark

**description**: You place a psychic marker upon a creature that you can see within range. Until the effect ends, you deal an extra 1d6 psychic damage to the target whenever you hit it with an attack. Also, choose one ability when you activate this power. The target has disadvantage on ability checks made with the chosen ability. If the target drops to 0 hit points before the effect ends, you can use a bonus action on a subsequent turn of yours to mark a new creature. Using a restore pattern on the target ends this power’s effect early.
