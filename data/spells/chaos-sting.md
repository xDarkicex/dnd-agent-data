# Chaos Sting

**description**: As part of the action used to cast this spell, you must make a melee attack with a dagger against one creature within the spell’s range, otherwise the spell fails. On a hit, the target suffers the attack’s normal effects, takes an additional 1d8 psychic damage, and the target drops anything that its holding. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.
