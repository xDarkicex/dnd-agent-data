# Cellular Renewal

**description**: You deliver a regenerative solution to a creature that you can see within range. The treatment causes the creature to regain 70 hit points. This power also ends blindness, deafness, and any diseases affecting the target. This power has no effect on automatons or constructs. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 7 or higher, the healing increases by 10 for each rank above 6.
