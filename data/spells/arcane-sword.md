# Arcane Sword

**description**: 7th level evocation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V S M (A miniature platinum sword with a grip and pommel of copper and zinc, worth 250 gp) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute Classes: Bard , Wizard You create a sword-shaped plane of force that hovers within range . It lasts for the duration . When the sword appears, you make a melee spell attack against a target of your choice within 5 feet of the sword. On a hit. the target takes 3d10 force damage . Until the spell ends, you can use a bonus action on each of your turns to move the sword up to 20 feet to a spot you can see and repeat this attack against the same target or a different one.
