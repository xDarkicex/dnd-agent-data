# Dust Cloud

**description**: You create a 20-foot-radius sphere of swirling dust at a point you can see within range. The cloud spreads around corners, and its area is lightly obscured. It lasts for the duration or until a wind of moderate or greater speed (at least 10 miles per hour) disperses it. A creature that starts its turn in the cloud or enters it for the first time on a turn, including when the cloud first appears, must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or become blinded until the start of its next turn. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the radius of the cloud increases by 20 feet for each slot level above 2nd.
