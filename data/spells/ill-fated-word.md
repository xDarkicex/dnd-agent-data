# Ill-Fated Word

**description**: You call out a distracting epithet to a creature, worsening its chance to succeed at whatever it’s doing. Roll a d4 and subtract the number rolled from an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw that the target has just made; the target uses the lowered result to determine the outcome of its roll.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 1
- School: divination
- Components: V
- Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when an enemy makes an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw
- data-RangeAoe: 30 feet

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Tome of Heroes
