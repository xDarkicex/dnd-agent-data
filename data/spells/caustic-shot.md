# Caustic Shot

**description**: You fling a blob of acid at single creature or object within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d6 acid damage. You deal an additional 1d6 acid damage if the target is within 10 feet of you. If the spell hits an object, the hit is automatically a critical hit. This spell’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
