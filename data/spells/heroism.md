# Heroism

**description**: Level 1 Enchantment (Bard, Paladin) Casting Time: Action Range: Touch Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute A willing creature you touch is imbued with bravery. Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot. You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 1.
