# Tracer Strike

**description**: The next time you hit a creature with a weapon attack before this effect ends, the weapon is imbued with a proton charge. The target takes an extra 2d6 radiant and becomes visible if it’s invisible. The target also sheds dim light in a 5-foot radius and can’t become cloaked, intangible, or invisible until the effect ends.
