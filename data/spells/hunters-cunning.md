# Hunter's Cunning

**description**: 2nd-Level Divination Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S Duration: 1 hour You grant a creature you touch preternatural Senses and insight into its immediate environment. Until the spell ends, the target gains advantage on Wisdom (Perception)and Wisdom (Survival) checks and ignores the effect of natural (nonmagical) difficult terrain.
