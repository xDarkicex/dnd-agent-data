# Soul Scorcher

**description**: 4th-level Conjuration Range: Self (15 ft radius) Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous Your body erupts in a torrent of black flames; all creatures in the area must succeed on a CON SAVE or take 6d10 fire damage (or half as much on a successful save). The damage from this spell ignores fire damage resistance; a creature that is immune to fire damage can’t benefit from that immunity against the effects of this spell, but they take half damage.
