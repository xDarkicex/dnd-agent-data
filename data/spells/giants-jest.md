# Giant's Jest

**description**: 4th-Level Transmutation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 25 feet Components: V, S Duration: 1 minute This spell enlarges a weapon or other object that you can see within range, temporarily making it sized for a Gargantuan creature. The object is twelve times heavier than its original weight and in most circumstances cannot be used effectively by Huge or smaller creatures. The object retains its usual qualities (including magical powers and effects) and returns to normal size when the spell ends.
