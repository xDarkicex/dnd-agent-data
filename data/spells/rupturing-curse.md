# Rupturing Curse

**description**: Rupturing Curse 2nd-level evocation (warlock, wizard) Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You focus your magic to shatter the body of your foe. Choose one creature you can within range; it must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 3d6 piercing damage and be knocked prone by the pain. In addition, if the target takes bludgeoning damage equal to 5 times its Constitution modifier (minimum of 5) or more in a single turn, its skeleton shatters, reducing its speed to 0 for the duration of the spell. If it does not take such damage before the start of its next turn, the spell ends. A creature without bones is immune to this effect. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the piercing damage increases by 2d6 for each slot level above 2nd.
