# Bright Sparks

**description**: Sparks fly from your fingers for a moment, generating bright blue light. This illuminates the region around you to a distance of 60 feet until the start of your next turn. Wisdom (Perception) checks have advantage to spot creatures hidden in shadows.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: Cantrip
- School: Evocation
- Components: V M
- Casting Time: 1 action
- data-RangeAoe: Self

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Book of Ebon Tides Compendium
