# Guiding Malevolence

**description**: 3rd-level necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: 120 feet Components: V, S Duration: 1 round You utter a harsh word, and necrotic energy seeps into your target's flesh, rendering them vulnerable and drawing the focus of lethal attacks. One creature you can see within range must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the next attack made against it before the start of your next turn with the intent to Injure has advantage and deals an additional 4d10 necrotic damage if it hits. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the bonus damage increases by 1d10 per spell level beyond 3rd.
