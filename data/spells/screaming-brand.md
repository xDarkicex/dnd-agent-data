# Screaming Brand

**description**: A weapon you touch becomes charged with disruptive magical energies. The next successful weapon attack you make before the spell ends deals an extra 1d4 psychic damage. If your target is forced to make a Concentration check due to this attack, it does so with disadvantage. This spell’s psychic damage changes to 1d6 when you reach 5th level, 1d8 at 11th level, and 1d10 at 17th level.
