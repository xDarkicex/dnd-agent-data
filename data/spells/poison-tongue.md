# Poison Tongue

**description**: Poison Tongue Requirements: Level 3 Casting Time: reaction Range: 60 feet Duration: instantaneous Cost: 2 WP You insert a seemingly random word, which you imbue with the resonance of the Weave, during a single opponent’s utterance, or when he tries some ability check or attack roll. The target suffers –1d6 to his roll unless he successfully makes a Wisdom saving throw. If casting a spell without an attack roll, the target has to succeed on a DC 10 Constitution saving throw or fail to cast the spell.
