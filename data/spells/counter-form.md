# Counter Form

**description**: You attempt to interrupt a creature in the process of using an esper power. If the talent or technique is Rank 3 or lower, it fails to activate and has no effect. If the talent or technique is Rank 4 or higher, make an ability check using your channeling ability. The DC equals 10 + the rank of the esper power. On a success, the creature’s power fails to activate and has no effect. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 4 or higher, the interrupted power has no effect if its rank is less than or equal to the rank you used for this power.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 3
- School: Alteration

- Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you see a creature within 60 feet of you using a power
