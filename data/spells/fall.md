# Fall

**description**: Fall 1st-level transmutation (sorcerer, warlock, wizard) Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, M (an elastic band) Duration: Instantaneous As an action, you can temporarily change which way gravity pulls you and immediately fall 200 feet in that direction. If you hit a solid surface, you take falling damage based on the distance you have traveled.
