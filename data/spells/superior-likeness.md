# Superior Likeness

**description**: Superior Likeness Requirements: Level 13 Casting Time: 1 action Range: touch (the Likeness) Duration: special Cost: 3 WP (invested) Upgrade a Likeness you made with additional Weave Points to enable it to transfer the effects of spells to the target. A magic-user targets the Likeness as if it were the depicted person or object iteself, and the depicted person or object suffers the spell’s effects. Each use of a spell on the target may destroy the Superior Likeness: if the target makes a successful Constitution saving throw after the spell has been transferred (this is separate from any saves versus the spell’s effects), the Superior Likeness is destroyed.
