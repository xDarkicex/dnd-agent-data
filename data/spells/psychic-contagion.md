# Psychic Contagion

**description**: 7th-level enchantment Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S Duration: 7 days Your touch incites an emotional response in your target, which then spreads to others they come in contact with. One creature you touch must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or be overtaken by a powerful emotion (choose one from the list below). In addition, any afflicted creature has disadvantage on Wisdom (Insight), Charisma (Deception), and Charisma (Persuasion) checks. Whenever an afflicted creature touches another creature, that creature must also succeed on a Charisma saving throw against the original spell’s DC or be afflicted itself. This contagion is considered a curse, and can be removed by any means that could end one. The curse ends for all afflicted when the curse is removed from the original target, or when the curse ends naturally after the 7 day period. Casting the spell on the original target with the same emotion while the curse is in effect extends the duration of the spell to another 7 days. A creature that is immune to being charmed is immune to this spell. Emotion Effect Happiness The target is overcome with manic joy. No matter how dire their situation, the target cannot help but grin, laugh, or otherwise be joyful. They will interact with others as though nothing is wrong. Sadness The target is overcome with overwhelming depression. No matter how joyous the occasion, they cannot help but take the worst or most pessimistic view. Fear The target is overcome with overwhelming dread. The thing they worry about most becomes a constant fear in need of immediate attention, and they cannot be talked out of it. Disgust The target is overcome with overwhelming disgust toward the thing they hated the most. They are driven to go to great lengths to destroy (physically or socially) the focus of their disgust. Anger The target is overcome with overwhelming rage. The next time something happens that they don’t like, they are driven to take complete revenge on its source.
