# Bloodshot

**description**: You launch a jet of boiling blood from your eyes at a target within 40 feet of you. You take 1d6 necrotic damage and make a ranged spell attack against the target. If the attack hits, the target takes 2d10 fire damage plus 2d8 psychic damage. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the fire damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 2nd.
