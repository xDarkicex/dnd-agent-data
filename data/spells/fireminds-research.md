# Firemind's Research

**description**: You gain clarity and precision as you enter a focused state of spellcasting. During this spell’s duration, any time you cast a spell of 1st level or higher you gain charges equal to the spell that you cast’s level. These charges last for the spell’s duration. As a bonus action on any of your turns during the duration, you can remove up to two charges from yourself to cast a single cantrip with a casting time of 1 action. Additionally, you can expend 5 charges as an action to make a ranged spell attack against a single creature within 60 feet of you. On a hit, the creature takes 5d10 fire damage.
