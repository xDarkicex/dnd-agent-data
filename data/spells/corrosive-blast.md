# Corrosive Blast

**description**: A spew of acrid chemicals erupts from your hands. Each creature in a 30-foot cone must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 10d4 acid damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The acid destroys any objects made of organic materials in the spell's area. A creature killed by this spell has its flesh entirely dissolved, leaving behind only a corroded skeleton. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the acid damage increases by 2d4 for each slot level above 3rd.
