# Analyze

**description**: You magically learn a single creature that you can see within range's capability to remain standing in combat and its resistences and weaknesses. The creature must succeed on a Intelligence saving throw. On a failure, you learn the creature's current and maximum hit point totals as well as any immunities, resistances, or weaknesses the creature has.
