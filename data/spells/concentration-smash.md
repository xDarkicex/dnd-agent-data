# Concentration Smash

**description**: 1st-level abjuration Casting time: 1 bonus action Range: self Components: v, s Duration: 1 round The next attack you make before the start of your next turn has advantage if the target of the attack is concentrating on a spell. If the attack hits, the target has disadvantage on the constitution saving throw it makes to maintain concentration after being damaged by your attack. If the attack is a critical hit, the target automatically fails the saving throw to maintain its concentration.
