# Stoneskin

**Level**: 4
**School**: transmutation
**Classes**: druid, ranger, sorcerer, wizard
**Casting Time**: action
**Range**: Touch
**Components**: ['v', 's', 'm']
**Duration**: up to 1 hour
**Concentration**: Yes

## Description

Until the spell ends, one willing creature you touch has Resistance to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage.
