# Light Beam

**description**: You gather a swirl of molecular energy and fire it in the form of an energy beam 30 feet long and 5 feet wide. Any creature within the line of fire must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 3d8 radiant damage or half as much on a successful one. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 3 or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each rank above 2.
