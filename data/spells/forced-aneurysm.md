# Forced Aneurysm

**description**: You focus on a creature you can see within range and take control of a portion of blood within the targets brain, at this point you cause the blood to clot profusely, stemming the flow of blood to the brain and causing an aneurysm. A creature can make a constitution saving throw vs your spell save DC to attempt to throw off your control of their blood. On a failed throw, the target takes 10d8 damage to its HP. This damage cannot be negated by resistances or immunities. Additionally, the target becomes paralysed for 4 turns. On a successful save, the target takes half damage and is not paralysed. This spell has no effect on creatures without blood and/or brains, or undead.
