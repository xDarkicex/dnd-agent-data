# Rift Bolt

**description**: A bolt of lightning descends from a rift on a creature that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take 3d8 lightning damage. The target gains no benefit from cover for this saving throw. You can instead cast this spell as a bonus action, but its effects do not resolve instantaneously. Instead, the spell occurs when you use a bonus action on your next turn. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Save: Dexterity
- Level: 1
- School: evocation
- Components: V, S
- Damage Type: lightning
- Casting Time: 1 action or 1 bonus action (see below)
