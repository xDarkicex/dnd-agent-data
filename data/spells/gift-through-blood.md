# Gift Through Blood

**description**: This spell allows you to transfer skills form one willing creature to another. you slice the hands of both creatures and press them together, and use your power to create a link between the two. Choose an effect A chosen stat is lowered by 1 point from the donor and raised by one point for the recipient. Cost is 4d8 HP for the donor creature. An attribute/feature, may be transferred from the donor to the recipient. Cost is 4d12 HP for the donor creature. The process leaves behind a brand on both the donor and the recipient. The recipient creature takes on an aspect of the donor creature (i.e., if a humanoid takes strength from a bear, they may become more bearlike in mannerisms or if a male takes charisma form a female, the make becomes more androgynous looking) If the spell is interrupted, the two creatures and the caster will take 6d12 Necrotic damage each, the donor creature also takes half the damage cost for their chosen effect. This spell has no effect on creatures without blood.
