# Desiccating Sphere

**description**: 4th-level evocation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S, M (a pound of salt) Duration: Instantaneous You reach out, pulling the life-giving water from each creature in a 20-foot radius sphere centered on a point you can see within range. Each living creature in the sphere must make a Constitution saving throw or take 5d10 damage and gain a special Injury, "Dehydrated". While Injured in this way, a creature gains one level of exhaustion and has its non-magical movement speeds reduced by 5 feet. Dehydrated is an Injury of Setback I severity, and is automatically removed at the end of a long rest. A creature cannot gain this Injury more than once, and it cannot be removed with a Wisdom (Medicine) check unless that check is made in conjunction with healing magic or made at the end of a short rest. On a successful save, a creature takes half damage and is not Injured. Plants have disadvantage on the saving throw, are vulnerable to the damage, and the movement speed penalty of the Dehydrated Injury increases to 10 feet.
