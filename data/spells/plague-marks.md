# Plague Marks

**description**: Necromancy cantrip Casting time: 1 action Range: touch Components: v, s, m (a pair of fly wings) Duration: 1 hour You place the fly wings in the mouth of an intact corpse that has been dead no more than 24 hours. The visible signs of a disease of your choice manifest on the body. These signs are temporary and not contagious, but they appear genuine under the scrutiny of anyone not practiced in diagnosing diseases.
