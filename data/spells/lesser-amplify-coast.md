# Lesser Amplify Coast

**description**: 5th-level lair magic (transmutation) Casting Time: 2 weeks (14 days) Radius: 2 miles Components: V, S, M (A rare material or materials found within the local environment worth at least 5000 gold pieces, which the spell consumes) Duration: 1 year This spell amplifies the naturally dangerous effects of the surrounding coast (or other similar environment), making it difficult for all who travel within it. When this spell is cast, the caster can choose up to eight creatures, including themself, who ignore the effects of the spell. Alternatively, the caster can designate a passphrase that must be known or a symbol that must be worn. Harmful effects of the spell do not affect native creatures unless their Intelligence score is greater than 3. Large, rogue waves break hostile ships on the rocks. A ship bearing a protected creature is protected itself from the waves and can find it's way safely to harbor. The captain of any ship entering the area without such protection must make a Wisdom (Nature) or Wisdom (Perception) check against the DC of this spell or have their ship be destroyed, dumping its passengers and cargo into the sea. Once a ship has been destroyed, the ocean neither helps nor hinders shipwrecked creatures any more than it would naturally.
