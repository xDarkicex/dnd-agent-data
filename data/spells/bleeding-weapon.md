# Bleeding Weapon

**description**: You touch a non-magical weapon and soak it with fresh blood and magical energy. Until the spell ends, that weapon becomes imbued with blood magic causing it to deal an extra 2d6 Necrotic damage and gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls. If the blood comes directly from you instead of a vial, the cost of this spell is 3d4 HP. This spell can be used on Sanguine Weapon. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the bonus increases to +2. When you use a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the bonus increases to +3, and the extra damage becomes 4d6.
