# Dampen Gravity

**description**: Dampen Gravity 3rd-level transmutation (sorcerer, warlock, wizard) Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self (60-foot-radius sphere) Components: V, S, M (a feather) Duration: 1 hour You focus your powers to lessen the call of gravity in a 60-foot-radius sphere that moves with you, centered on you. All creatures of your choice in the sphere have their jump distance doubled and their speed increased by 10 feet. They also have advantage on Acrobatics checks and ignore falling damage if they end their fall within the sphere.
