# Gird for Battle

**description**: You gird up to three creatures of your choice within range for battle. Whenever an attacker that a target can see hits a target with a nonmagical weapon attack before the spell ends, the target can roll a d4 and reduce the damage taken from the attack against it. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.
