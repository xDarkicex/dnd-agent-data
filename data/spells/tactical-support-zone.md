# Tactical Support Zone

**description**: You launch micro drones carrying a transmission signal between you and your allies. They mark the point of impact from an ally’s attack and fire a laser beam at the same spot. Until the effect ends, the signal area moves with you, centered on you. While in the signal area, each nonhostile creature in the area (including you) deals an extra 1d4 radiant damage when it hits with a weapon attack.
