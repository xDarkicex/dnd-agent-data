# Clumsiness

**description**: Necromancy Cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: 1 minute You temporarily make a creature within range less dexterous. If the target makes a successful Constitution saving throw, the spell is ineffective. On a failed save, the next time the creature makes a Dexterity check before the spell ends, roll a d6 and subtract the result from the roll. The spell then ends.
