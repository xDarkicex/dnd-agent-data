# Essence Infusion

**description**: You transfer your essence to another creature to heal it. When you cast this spell and as an action during the spell’s duration, you can spend and roll one of your unspent hit dice. One creature within 30 feet of you regains hit points equal to the roll plus your spellcasting ability modifier.

**properties**:

- Level: 2
- School: transmutation
- Components: V, S, M
