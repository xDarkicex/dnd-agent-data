# Bloodbath

**description**: One creature that you can see within range must make a Constitution saving throw vs your spell save DC. If the saving throw fails, the creature suffers 3d8 necrotic damage and becomes blinded for 1 round as blood spurts from its eyes, ears, mouth, and nose. This results in the creature becoming blood-soaked. If the saving throw succeeds, it takes half damage from the strain and is not blinded or blood soaked.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Save: Constitution
- Level: 2
- School: necromancy
- Components: V, S
- Damage Type: necrotic
- Casting Time: 1 Action
- data-RangeAoe: 20 feet.
