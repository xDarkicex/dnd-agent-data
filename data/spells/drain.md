# Drain

**description**: 1st-level necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S, M (a red ribbon) Duration: Instantaneous Stretching out your hand and pointing your finger at a creature within range, a flash of negative energy briefly connects you to the target. It must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 3d4 necrotic damage, and you gain a number of temporary hit points equal to the damage dealt. You lose any temporary hit points granted by this spell after 1 minute. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 2d4 for each slot level above 1st.
