# Keening Wail

**description**: 4th-Level Necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self (15-foot cone) Components: V, S, M (a ringed lock of hair from an undead creature) Duration: Instantaneous You emit an unholy shriek from beyond the grave. Each creature in a 15-foot cone must make a Constitution saving throw. A creature takes 6d6 necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. If a creature with 50 Hit Points or fewer fails the saving throw by 5 or more, it is instead reduced to 0 hit points. This wail has no effect on constructs and undead. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 4th.
