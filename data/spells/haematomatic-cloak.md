# Haematomatic Cloak

**description**: You can wound yourself taking damage equal to the hit points of your Arcane Blood Mass or use your Arcane Blood Mass itself to create a cloak of swirling blood that provides a base AC of 14 + your Constitution modifier. As a bonus action while protected by the cloak, you can command globules of blood from a recently fallen creature to be absorbed by your cloak; each infusion increases your AC by 1, to a maximum of 18 + Constitution modifier. You can also use a bonus action to dispel the cloak, causing it to wrap around you, solidify, and then explode into a shower of flying blood shards that deals 8d6 slashing damage, +1d6 per point of AC absorbed into the cloak, to all creatures within 30 feet of you. If you used Arcane Blood Mass for this spell and dismiss it, this spell ends with no effects. If you used Arcane Blood Mass and dispelled the cloak, you will need to create a new Arcane Blood Mass.
