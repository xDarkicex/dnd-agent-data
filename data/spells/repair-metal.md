# Repair Metal

**description**: A damaged construct or metal object regains 1d8 + 5 hit points when this spell is cast on it. At Higher Levels. The spell restores 2d8 + 10 hit points at 4th level, 3d8 + 15 at 6th level, and 4d8 + 20 at 8th level.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 2
- School: Transmutation (Clockwork)
- Components: V S
- Casting Time: 1 action

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Midgard Heroes Handbook
