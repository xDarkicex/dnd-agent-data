# Haemorrhage

**description**: You invigorate of a large volume of a target’s blood and force it out through their mouth, nose, and eyes causing them to become blood soaked. Unless they make a successful Constitution saving throw vs your spell save DC, the target takes 3d6 + your spellcasting ability modifier in damage direct to their hit points; if the throw was successful, they only take half damage. Regardless of whether the target succeeds or not, the horrific nature of this spell demoralizes the target's allies’ enemies; causing any within 10 feet of the target to have disadvantage on any attacks or skill checks they make until the end of their next turn. This spell has no effect on constructs, undead, and creatures that do not have blood.
