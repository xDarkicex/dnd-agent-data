# Sanguine Wave

**description**: With a flick of your hand, you transform your Arcane Blood Mass into a thin wave of blood 15 foot wide that slices forwards 15 feet. This deals 1d6 necrotic damage and 1d4 slashing damage to each target hit by this spell. Any target within the path of the wave can attempt to dodge by making a Dexterity saving throw vs your spell save DC. If successful, they take no damage. This cantrip has the additional benefit of being able to cut through objects such as rope and thin wooden beams.
