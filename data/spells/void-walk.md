# Void Walk

**description**: Void Walk 6th-level transmutation (bard, sorcerer, warlock, wizard) Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S Duration: 8 hours You touch a willing creature. For the duration, it gains a flying speed equal to its walking speed and can hover. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 6th.
