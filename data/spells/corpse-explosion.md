# Corpse Explosion

**description**: 3rd-level necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S, M (grave dirt) Duration: Instantaneous You overload a nearby corpse with necrotic energy, causing it to bloat - then explode in a shower of body parts. If you target an undead creature with this spell, it must make a Constitution saving throw or take 5d6 force damage, exploding as described below if this damage destroys it. When the corpse explodes, each creature within 10 feet of it must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take 5d6 bludgeoning damage and be poisoned for 1 minute. Creatures can repeat the saving throw at the end of their turn, ending the effect on themselves on a success. On a successful save, creatures take half damage and are not poisoned. At Higher Levels. when this spell is cast using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the force damage dealt to an undead target increases by 1d6 and the damage dealt by the exploding corpse increases by 1d6 for each spell level above 3rd.
