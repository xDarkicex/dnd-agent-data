# Arcane Portal

**description**: You can join two points on the same plane via an arcane portal. Designate a single unoccupied space within range that you can see. A gate appears adjacent to you and at the designated space. These two spaces are considered adjacent for all purposes, such as movement and drawing line of sight. Any creature can move freely from one space to the other by expending 5 feet of movement to do so. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the range of the spell increases by 120 feet for each slot level above 3rd.
