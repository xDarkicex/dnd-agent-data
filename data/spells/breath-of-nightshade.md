# Breath of Nightshade

**description**: You exhale a 60-foot-cone of lethal poison. Each creature in the area when you cast the spell must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the creature becomes poisoned for 1 hour. While poisoned by this spell, the creature is paralyzed. The spell ends for an affected creature if it takes any damage.

**properties**:

- Save: Constitution
- Level: 8
- School: conjuration
