# Vicarious Shriek

**description**: 3rd-level illusion Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S, M (a bat’s vocal cords) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You assault the minds of your enemies, forcing them to experience a horror they imagine inflicted upon their closest companions. Up to three creatures you can see within 30 feet must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed saving throw, each creature takes 3d6 psychic damage, plus 2d6 additional psychic damage per creature that failed its saving throw, or half as much on a successful save. Additionally, each creature that fails its save becomes frightened of you and each target of the spell other than themselves. At the end of each of their turns, they may attempt a new saving throw to shake off the fear. On a creature’s first failed save, the nearest creature within 60 feet of them that is hostile to you and was not a target of the initial casting of the spell must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save this creature takes 6d6 psychic damage and becomes frightened of you and the targets of the spell. On a successful save the creature takes half damage and is not frightened. This creature may also attempt a new save to end its fear at the end of each of its turns, though the spell does not target additional creatures if it fails this save.
