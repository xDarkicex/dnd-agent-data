# Greater Amplify Mountain

**description**: 9th-level lair magic (transmutation) Casting Time: 2 weeks (14 days) Radius: 4 miles Components: V, S, M (A rare material or materials found within the local environment worth at least 6000 gold pieces, which the spell consumes) Duration: 1 year This spell functions like lesser amplify mountain, except that a thunderstorm also gathers around the lair, and lightning bolts strike at intruders. For each group of intruders, one intruder at random is targeted with a blast of lighting every 10 minutes. That creature must make a Dexterity saving throw or take 8d6 lightning damage.
