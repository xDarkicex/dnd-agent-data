# Blood Buffing

**description**: When extracted via this spell, your blood becomes a dark energy with a variety of uses. When you cast this spell, you take 4d4+4 necrotic damage, and your hit point maximum is reduced by the same amount, this damage cannot be reduced by any means, although the reduction to your hit point maximum is reversed after a long rest. Choose one of the following effects: Haemokenesis. One creature of your choice within range must make a successful Constitution saving throw or it takes 6d10 necrotic damage, and they cannot gain hit points by any means until the end of your next turn. If it succeeds, it takes half damage and can still gain hit points. Resilience. Your base AC becomes 15 + your Dexterity modifier and you gain resistance to non- magical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage. This effect lasts for 1 hour. Vigour. One creature of your choice within range gains 25 hit points, and their hit point maximum increases by 25 for 8 hours. Celerity. One creature of your choice within range immediately takes an action for free. Malediction. A number of creatures up to your spell casting modifier (minimum of 1) of your choice that are within range must make a Constitution saving throw or become blinded, deafened, and frightened of you for 1 minute. Affected creatures can reroll their saving throw at the end of each of their turns. The chosen effect of this spell can be used immediately, or stored within blood if put into a vial. When stored this way, it can last for 7 days before becoming unusable.
