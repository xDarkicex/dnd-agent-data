# Fog Cloud

**description**: Level 1 Conjuration (Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard) Casting Time: Action Range: 120 feet Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour You create a 20-foot-radius Sphere of fog centered on a point within range. The Sphere is Heavily Obscured. It lasts for the duration or until a strong wind (such as one created by Gust of Wind ) disperses it. Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot. The fog’s radius increases by 20 feet for each spell slot level above 1.
