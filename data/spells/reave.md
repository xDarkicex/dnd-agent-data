# Reave

**description**: You unleash a spiral of molecular distortion, which envelops a target within range. The target must make a Constitution saving throw. It takes 7d8 + 30 necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature killed by this power has their molecules completely scattered, including everything it is wearing and carrying except forge enhanced items. The creature can be restored to life only by means of a full restore or a cosmic weave power.
