# Gravitational Distortion

**description**: Gravitational Distortion 4th-level transmutation (sorcerer, warlock, wizard) Casting time: 1 action Range: 60 feet (30-foot-radius sphere) Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous Reality swirls in a 30-foot-radius sphere centered on a point you can see within range. Each creature in the area must make a Strength saving throw, taking 6d6 bludgeoning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Additionally, any creature that fails this saving throw is pushed 30 feet in a straight line in a direction of your choice.
