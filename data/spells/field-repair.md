# Field Repair

**description**: You send micro drones to repair up to three automatons, constructs, or mechanical devices (including planetary vehicles) that you can see within range. Each target regains hit points equal to 1d4 + your forging ability modifier. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 4 or higher, the healing increases by 1d4 for each rank above 3.
