# Powerball

**description**: 3rd-level Evocation Casting Time: 1 Action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S, M (a small crystal ball and a bit of rabbit fur) Duration: 1 round Choose a willing creature that you can see within range. A ball of crackling energy surrounds the target. For the duration of its turn, the target's speed is doubled and its movement does not provoke opportunity attacks. Each creature in a 10 ft radius of the target during its turn must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 18 (6d6) lightning damage, or half as much damage on a successful save. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 3rd.
