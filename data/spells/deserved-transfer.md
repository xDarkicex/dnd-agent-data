# Deserved Transfer

**description**: 2nd-Order Metamorphosis Power Manifestation Time : 1 action Rang e: 30 feet Duration: Instantaneous Creatures: Jutkarr Flintfingers You fire a bolt of draining psychic energy at a creature within range.
