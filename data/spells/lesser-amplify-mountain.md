# Lesser Amplify Mountain

**description**: 5th-level lair magic (transmutation) Casting Time: 2 weeks (14 days) Radius: 2 miles Components: V, S, M (A rare material or materials found within the local environment worth at least 5000 gold pieces, which the spell consumes) Duration: 1 year This spell amplifies the naturally dangerous effects of the surrounding mountain (or other similar environment), making it difficult for all who travel within it. When this spell is cast, the caster may choose up to eight creatures, including themself, who may ignore the effects of the spell. Alternatively, the caster can designate a passphrase that must be known or a symbol that must be worn. Harmful effects of the spell do not affect native creatures unless their Intelligence score is greater than 3. The mountainous terrain is permanently shrouded in a blizzard, and the thin air grows even thinner. Unprotected creatures entering the area must immediately make a Constitution saving throw or gain a level of exhaustion. This level of exhaustion cannot be removed until the creature finishes a long rest outside of the area. Ability checks made within the area to climb surfaces or to notice things at a distance greater than 15 feet have their DCs increased by 10. Ranged spell attack rolls are made with disadvantage at a range of more than 20 feet, and ranged weapons have their short range reduced to 10 feet (or less if their short range is normally shorter than 10 feet) and their long range reduced to a distance equal to their normal short range.
