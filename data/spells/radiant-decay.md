# Radiant Decay

**description**: Evocation cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous Classes: Cleric, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard With an outstretched hand you call forth golden light to your fingertips, as frigid black shadows intertwine with it. Make a melee spell attack against a creature within your reach. On a hit, the target takes 1d6 radiant damage and 1d6 necrotic damage. This spell’s damage increases by 1d6 for each damage type when you reach 5th level (2d6 radiant + 2d6 necrotic), 11th level (3d6 radiant + 3d6 necrotic), and 17th level (4d6 radiant + 4d6 necrotic).
