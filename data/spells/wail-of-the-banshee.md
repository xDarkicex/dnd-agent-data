# Wail of the Banshee

**description**: 6th-level evocation Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, M (silver dagger worth at least 200 gp, that you stab into your forearm during casting) Duration: Concentration, up to 3 minutes This spell will not work if the caster is in sunlight. This spell will not work if the caster cannot feel pain while stabbing themselves. You stab your forearm with a silver dagger and magically enhance and amplify a scream of pain and anguish. You take 4 piercing damage, and every living creature within 30 feet of you must make a Constitution saving throw. Each creature in the area of effect, to include the caster, takes 21 (6d6) sonic damage and 21 (6d6) force damage and is stunned until the end of their next turn on a failed save, or half as much and not stunned on a successful one. Deafened creatures have advantage on this saving throw.
