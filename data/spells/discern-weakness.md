# Discern Weakness

**description**: 2nd-Level Divination Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, S, M (a glass lens) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You divine the weak points in your foes’ defenses, allowing you to strike with deadly effect. If you score a critical hit or a successful sneak attack on an opponent, roll damage twice and take the better result.
