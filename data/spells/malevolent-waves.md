# Malevolent Waves

**description**: 8th-Level Abjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, S, M (a profane object that has been bathed in blood) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You create an invisible miasma that fills the area within 30 feet of you. All your allies have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks they make within 30 feet of you, and all your enemies are poisoned while within that radius.
