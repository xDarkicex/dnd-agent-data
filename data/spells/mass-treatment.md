# Mass Treatment

**description**: You medically treat up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within range. Each creature regains hit points equal to 2d8 + your forging ability modifier. This power has no effect on automatons or constructs. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 3 or higher, the healing increases by 1d8 for each rank above 2.
