# Hatred

**description**: Malefic word, e nchantment Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous, ongoing (see this malefic word’s baleful echo) Effects: You speak a malefic Word of Power that brims with unfathomable anger, enraging one creature within range. The creature must make a Wisdom saving throw. If it fails, the target becomes provoked by one other creature of your choice within 30 feet of you (disadvantage on attack rolls targeting creatures other than the one you chose) until the end of your next turn. If the target succeeds, the malefic word’s baleful echo is silenced (and you do not suffer the feedback). Baleful Echo (1 vitality): The target continues to be provoked by the chosen creature until the end of your next turn. It must use its movement to move closer to the chosen creature, taking the most direct safe route to reach them. Feedback: A vicious spite fills your heart, directed at the creature you targeted. For one minute, you are provoked by that creature.
