# Utterance

**description**: 2nd-level evocation Casting time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: v Duration: instantaneous You speak a short phrase, momentarily allowing your patron to voice itself through you in a terrifying display. Your pupils go white, and you body takes on other minor manifestations befitting your patron, such as demonic wings, writhing tentacles, multicolored fur, and similar. Choose up to three creatures within range that can see and hear you. Each target must make a wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, a target takes 3d8 psychic damage and is frightened until the end of its next turn. On a successful save, a creature takes half the damage and isn’t frightened. At higher levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
