# Power Word Bleed

**description**: 7th-level necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: 90 feet Components: V Duration: Instantaneous With a sharp word, you pierce your foe, rupturing veins and arteries. One living creature you can see within range must make a Constitution saving throw or take 10d10 force damage and gain a special Injury of Deadly IIII severity, "Ruptured". While Injured in this way, a creature takes 2d10 damage at the start of each of its turns, has its Injury thresholds reduced by 10, and regains only half the usual hit points from spells and potions. This Injury lasts for 1 minute, or until the creature receives magical healing from a spell of 5th level or higher.
