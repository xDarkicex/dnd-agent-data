# Pygmalion's Folly

**description**: Pygmalion’s Folly Requirements: Level 20 Casting Time: 1 action Range: touch (the Likeness) Duration: special Cost: 20 WP to create + 2 WP (invested) Imbue a perfect Likeness of a humanoid or animal with actual life. A painted creature will come to life inside the environment depicted in the painting and can exit from there into the real world if the artist possesses the Homecoming feature to facilitate this. A sculpture will simply come to life as a real person indistinguishable from a real human being. If the person is a true depiction of an existing person, it will think itself that person if you invest an extra 1 Weave Point during the process of creation. Otherwise the newly created person will be of a childlike disposition, but mature rapidly.
