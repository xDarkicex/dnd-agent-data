# Serpent Arms

**description**: You transform your hands into three serpents each for the duration. As long as you concentrate on this spell, you cannot perform somatic components for spells. You gain a melee bite attack that uses your spellcasting attack modifier and deals 1 piercing damage and 1d6 poison damage. When you take the Attack action, you can make up to six of these bite attacks.
