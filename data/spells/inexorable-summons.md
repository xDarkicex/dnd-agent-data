# Inexorable Summons

**description**: You reach out your hand toward a creature and call out to it. The target teleports to an unoccupied space that you can see within 5 feet of you. An unwilling creature can make a Wisdom saving throw, and if it succeeds, it isn’t affected by this spell. You can’t teleport a target into dangerous terrain, such as lava, and the target must be teleported to a space on the ground or on a floor.

**properties**:

- Level: 3
- School: transmutation
- Components: V S
