# Power Word Rebound

**description**: 2nd-level evocation Casting time: 1 reaction, which you take when a creature you can see within 60 feet of you is hit by an attack that targets only one creature Range: 60 feet Components: v Duration: instantaneous You utter a word of power as a weapon or spell hits a creature you can see within range. The attack rebounds on the attacker, using all the same attack and damage rolls against the attacker.
