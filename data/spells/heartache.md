# Heartache

**description**: 2nd-Level Enchantment Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S, M (a silver locket) Duration: Instantaneous You force an enemy to experience pangs of unrequited love and emotional distress. These feelings manifest with such intensity that the creature takes 5d6 psychic damage on a failed Charisma saving throw, or half the damage on a successful save. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target an additional enemy for each slot level above 2nd.
