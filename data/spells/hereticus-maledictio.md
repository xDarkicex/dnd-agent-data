# Hereticus Maledictio

**description**: 2nd-level Transmutation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S, M Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You invoke the holy rite of the Inquisition to punish heretics. A number of creatures equal to your proficiency bonus of your choice within range must succeed on an Intelligence saving throw or become branded. The branded creatures have disadvantage on attack rolls made against you and attacks against them inflict an additional 1d4 radiant damage. These creatures can repeat the save at the end of each of their turns to remove the brand. Enemies of the Inquisition or creatures that you have successfully passed Judgment upon make these saves with disadvantage.
