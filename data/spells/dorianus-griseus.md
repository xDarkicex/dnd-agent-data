# Dorianus Griseus

**description**: Dorianus Griseus Requirements: Level 18 Casting Time: 1 day ritual Range: touch (the Likeness) Duration: permanent Cost: 20 WP to create + 4 WP (invested) Create a lifelike self-portrait (drawing, painting or sculpture) which can then draw negative effects into itself from the artist. Half of any damage (no matter what kind, even aging effects) inflicted upon the artist will instead be transferred to the artist’s likeness in the painting, no matter how far artist and painting are separated. That likeness can accumulate age and damage indefinitely. If the artwork is ever destroyed, the artist immediately suffers the following consequences: 1d6 damage for every full 10 years of the artwork’s existence, incapacitated for 1d6 minutes, immediately (in a matter of minutes) age half as many years as the artwork has existed. The artist can only have one such object at any time.
