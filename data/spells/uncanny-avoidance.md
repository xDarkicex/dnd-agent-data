# Uncanny Avoidance

**description**: Divination Cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 round You extend a hand and point a finger at a creature within range. Your magic grants the target enhanced awareness of attacks directed at it. The next attack roll against the creature before the spell ends is made with disadvantage.
