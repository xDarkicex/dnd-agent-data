# Distortion

**description**: You warp the air around you into a small sphere and hurl it toward a creature you can see. Make a ranged channeling attack against the target. If it hits, the sphere bursts into a micro-singularity and the creature takes 3d8 force damage. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 2 or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each rank above 1.
