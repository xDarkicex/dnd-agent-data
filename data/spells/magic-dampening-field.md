# Magic Dampening Field

**description**: 7th-level lair magic (abjuration) Casting Time: 3 weeks (21 days) Radius: 1 mile Components: V, S, M (iron filings worth 1000 gold pieces, which are scattered to the winds throughout the casting of the spell) Duration: 6 months This spell dampens magical energies, making it harder to cast spells. Any time a spell is cast, the caster must make a spellcasting ability check with a DC equal to 10 + the spell's level. On a failed check, the spell fails and has no effect.
