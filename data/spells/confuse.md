# Confuse

**description**: Malefic word, e nchantment Casting Time: 1 action Range: 45 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous, ongoing (see this malefic word’s baleful echo) Effects: Your voice tolls like a leaden bell, echoing in the mind of a single creature within range. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw. If it fails, it becomes disoriented (it can’t make opportunity attacks) and distracted (–2 AC, removed when hit by an attack) until the end of your next turn. If the target succeeds, the malefic word’s baleful echo is silenced (and you do not suffer the feedback). Baleful Echo (1 vitality): The target remains disoriented and distracted until the end of your next turn. Feedback: The echoes of the malefic word reverberate inside your skull, causing a pounding headache. You become disoriented and distracted until the end of your next turn.
