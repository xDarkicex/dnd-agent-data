# Dominion

**description**: Malefic word, necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: 45 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous, ongoing (see this malefic word’s baleful echo) Effects: You speak a Word of Power imbued with irresistible command. One unbound undead creature within range must make a Wisdom saving throw. If it fails, it becomes bound to you. If the target succeeds, the malefic word’s baleful echo is silenced (and you do not suffer the feedback). Baleful Echo (1 vitality): The target either regains 1d12 hit points, or suffers 1d12 thunder damage. The hit points regained and damage dealt by this malefic word increases by 1d12 when you reach 5th level (2d12), 11th level (3d12), and 17th level (4d12). Feedback: With a howl of malicious delight, the malefic word lets slip the reins of command on your target. The target becomes unbound and cannot be bound by you for 24 hours. Until it is destroyed, it pursues you with single-minded ferocity and attempts to attack you.
