# Harmonize

**description**: You synchronize with a willing spellcaster within 10 feet of you and learn to manipulate magical energies temporarily in a new way. You gain the knowledge of a single spell of 1st level (it doesn’t need to be a spell on your class’s spellcasting list) of your choice that a creature within 10 feet of you knows or has prepared. For the spell’s duration, you know the spell and can cast it using your spell slots. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can choose a spell higher than 1st level if its level is less than or equal to the level of the spell slot you used.

**properties**:
