# Heat Conversion

**description**: You cause the temperature in the air around one creature to lose its heat and transfer that heat to burn another creature. Choose one creature within range. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d6 cold damage. You may then target another creature within 5 feet of the original creature and it must make a Dexterity saving throw or take 1d6 fire damage. If the first creature failed its saving throw, the fire damage is increased to 1d10. This spell’s damage increases by 1d6 and 1d10 respectively when you reach 5th level (2d6/2d10), 11th level (3d6/3d10), and 17th level (4d6/4d10).
