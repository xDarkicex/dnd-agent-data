# Graviton Strike

**description**: The next time you hit a creature with a weapon attack before the end of the duration, a powerful gravity field appears at the point of impact, and the target must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be restrained by the gravity field for the duration. A Large or larger creature has advantage on this saving throw. If the target succeeds on the save, the field dissipates. While restrained by this power, the target takes 1d6 force damage at the start of each of its turns. A creature restrained by the field or one that can touch the creature can use its action to make a Strength check against your forging save DC. On a success, the target is freed. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 2 or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each rank above 1.
