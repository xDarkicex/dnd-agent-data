# Blast Point

**description**: You extend your hand toward a creature you can see within range and cause an explosive blast of flame to erupt at the target’s location. The creature must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d12 fire damage. This power’s damage increases by 1d12 when you reach 5th level (2d12), 11th level (3d12), and 17th level (4d12).
