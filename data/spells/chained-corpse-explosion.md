# Chained Corpse Explosion

**description**: 5th-level necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: 40 feet Components: V, S, M (grave dirt) Duration: Instantaneous You overload several nearby corpses with necrotic energy, causing them to bloat - then explode in a shower of body parts. Choose up to three corpses you can see within 20 feet of each other as the targets of this spell, with at least one corpse within the spell’s range. If you target an undead creature with this spell, it must make a Constitution saving throw or take 5d6 force damage, exploding as described below if this damage destroys it. Each creature within 10 feet of one of the corpses targeted by the spell must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save creatures take 5d6 bludgeoning damage and are poisoned for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of its turn ending the effect on a success. On a successful save, creatures take half damage and are not poisoned. At Higher Levels. When cast using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, you may choose an additional corpse for each level greater than 5th.
