# Justice Strike

**description**: You speak a word of command at one target creature. The creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, the creature takes damage as if it had hit itself with its own melee attack. This spell deals additional 1d8 psychic damage to the creature when you reach 5th level. This spell’s damage increases by 1d8 when you reach 11th level (2d8) and 17th level (3d8).
