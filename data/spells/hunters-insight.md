# Hunter's Insight

**description**: 2nd-level divination Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: Self Components: V, S, M (a mummified owl's head) Duration: Instantaneous You enhance your eyes and mind, gaining sudden flashes of insight into your opponents. When you cast this spell, you automatically know which creatures within 60 feet of you have identical statistics, and can choose up to two creatures with different statistics to identify strengths and weaknesses in (see the Identifying strengths and weaknesses to target for Injuries section on page 16). If you do, you gain a +5 bonus to the checks. If you succeed a check by 5 or more, you have advantage on your next attack roll against the target of the check, made with the intent to Injure. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you may choose one additional creature to identify for each spell level beyond 2nd.
