# Data Ghost

**description**: You create a digital mask to hide your presence on any computer system or network. This power can be used to target up to six companions and yourself. For the duration of this power, any Intelligence check made in an effort to locate the presence or activity of these targets within a particular computer system is made at a -10 penalty. In addition, any activity performed by you or your chosen targets during the duration of this power is immediately erased from the system when the effect ends.
