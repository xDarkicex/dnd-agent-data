# Bloody Barrier

**description**: 2nd-level transmutation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous You draw blood from a recent wound, hardening it into a protective barrier. One Injured creature you can see within range loses hit points equal to one quarter the threshold for the highest severity Injury it currently suffers from, rounded down. It then gains three times that many temporary hit points, which disappear after 1 minute. While these hit points last, it gains a +1 bonus to AC, and gains resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage. If the creature you target for hit point loss is hostile, it may make a Constitution saving throw to negate the spell's effect. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you can choose a second creature within 30 feet of both you and the initial target to gain the temporary hit points instead.
