# Memento Mori

**description**: You transform yourself into a horrifying vision of death, rotted and crawling with maggots, exuding the stench of the grave. All creatures that see you must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or be stunned until the end of your next turn. A creature that succeeds on the saving throw is immune to further castings of this spell for 24 hours.

**properties**:

- Level: 1
- School: necromancy
