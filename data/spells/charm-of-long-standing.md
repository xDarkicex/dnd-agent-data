# Charm Of Long Standing

**description**: As charm person but with a longer duration. The target of the spell remains charmed for one day per level you’ve attained in your spellcasting class. In addition, so long as the target remains charmed by you, you are also charmed by the target. While you’re charmed this way, you won’t tolerate any harm, abuse, or mistreatment being directed toward the spell’s target.
