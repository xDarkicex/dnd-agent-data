# Quarry's Mark

**description**: 4th-level divination Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: 90 feet Components: V Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour You choose a creature you can see within range and mark it as prey to be hunted. Until the spell ends, any weapon attack that hits the target deals an extra 1d6 damage to it, and any Wisdom check made to find it has advantage. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, if the target drops to 0 hit points before this spell ends, you can use a bonus action on a subsequent turn of yours to mark a new creature you can see within range.
