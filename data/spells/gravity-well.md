# Gravity Well

**description**: You bring into being a localized region of increased gravity that crushes a creature to the ground. Affected targets weigh twice as much as normal, can move only at half speed, jump only half as far as normal, and can lift and carry only half as much as normal. Any flying target has its fly speed halved and plummets to the ground unless it succeeds at a DC 25 Strength (Athletics) check to remain in the air. Freedom of movement negates the effects of this spell.
