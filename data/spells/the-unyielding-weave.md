# The Unyielding Weave

**description**: The Unyielding Weave Requirements: Level 15 Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Duration: 1 minute Cost: 4 WP / special Makes any spellcasting impossible within a radius of 30 feet around the philosopher (the effect remains centered on him if he moves around) unless the magic–user makes a successful Wisdom saving throw for every attempted spell. Spell effects already established when the power is activated are not affected. Spells originating outside the 30 foot radius sphere are not affected and can affect targets within the area of effect. The feature can be amended to allow selected characters to use magic, with an extra WP expenditure of 2 WP for each excepted character.
