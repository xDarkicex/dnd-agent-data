# Artist's Homunculus

**description**: Artist’s Homunculus Requirements: Level 17 Casting Time: 1 hour ritual Range: touch (the Likeness)Duration: permanent Cost: 10 WP + 1 WP (invested) for compliance (optional) Imbue a Likeness of a person with a semblance of life. If in a painting, the depiction will become animated like an actual person, can move about in the background of the painting (or drawing) and can speak with the artist. By investing one additional Weave Point while creating the image, it can be made more compliant, otherwise it will have free will. It is possible to use this feature on a sculpture but it will not be able to move about; instead, it will mostly retain its pose except for the animation of its facial features and the ability to turn its head or move its arms. A bust is of course also an option. The personality and knowledge of the homunculus depend on the creation process, and also whether it is a depiction of a real person or wholly imaginary.The homunculus receives 12 skill points in total which can be assigned to any skill or skills of the artist’s choosing – one skill with a rating of 12 is possible, as are 12 skills with a rating of 1 each, or anything in between. When the homunculus makes a skill check, it rolls a d20 + skill rating. The point distribution is decided upon creation of the homunculus and cannot be altered later on.The artist can only have one homunculus at any given time. And do remember that the creation of a Likeness requires investment of 1 Weave Point to begin with.
