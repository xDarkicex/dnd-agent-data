# Dark Ritual

**description**: You perform a dark ritual to convert the essence of a living creature into magical power. Upon casting this spell, you gain additional spell slots. The spell slots gained can have a combined level of three (three 1st-level spell slot or one 2nd-level and one 1st-level spell slot or one 3rd-level spell slot). The additional spell slots remain for the spell’s duration.
