# It Has Always Been So

**description**: It Has Always Been So Requirements: Level 5 Casting Time: 1 action Range: touch (the Likeness) Duration: special Cost: 4 WP Imbue a Likeness of a place you have made with additional Weave Points to alter that place. For example, when in a prison cell, the artist could draw that cell with its door open. Upon expenditure of the Weave Points the change will become manifested (in this example, the door will open), and the Likeness will be destroyed. A painting of a river could establish a bridge in reality if the artist adds it in the Likeness. The change in reality will remain until the artist’s next short or long rest.
