# Clean Zone

**description**: You create a 10-foot radius sphere of controlled air particles centered on yourself. Within this sphere, you are able to extinguish any unprotected flames and filter out any gas, vapor, or airborne toxins. In addition, you can remove the poisoned condition from any creature within the sphere.
