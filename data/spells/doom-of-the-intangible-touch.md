# Doom of the Intangible Touch

**description**: A creature of your choice within range must make a Wisdom saving throw the first time it attempts a melee attack against you or one of your allies for the duration of the spell. If the target fails this saving throw, it shifts to the border of the Ethereal Plane until the end of its next turn. The target can see only 60 feet while on the Ethereal Plane and can pass through objects on the Material Plane. If it ends its turn while occupying the same location as a creature or object, the target is immediately shunted to the closest unoccupied space when it returns to the Material Plane and takes force damage equal to twice the number of feet it was forced to move. If the target succeeds on its saving throw, the spell has no effect on it. A target can willingly fail the saving throw.
