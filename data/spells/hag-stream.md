# Hag Stream

**description**: 1st-level (hag) evocation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 90 feet Components: S Duration: 1 minute This spell has no effect if the target is not in moonlight. This spell has no effect on any creature with the fey subtype. A teal, wispy, and sinuous stream of energy fires from the hag’s pinky finger towards her target. The hag makes a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target glows with bright teal flames and takes 1 point of radiant damage per round. These teal flames will persist, for up to a minute, as long as the target remains in any amount of moonlight. Targets, who are not blind, suffering from radiant damage caused by a hag ray will have disadvantage on all attacks that target any hag that is also illuminated by moonlight, as their exact location appears blurry and murky: nearby lights seem to stream towards each hag. Creatures not affected by a hag ray will not see this effect. A hag creates more than one stream at higher spellcasting levels: two streams at 5th level, three streams at 7th level, four streams at 9th level, and five streams at 11th level.
