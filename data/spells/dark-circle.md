# Dark Circle

**description**: You unleash a ring of disruption energy around you, engulfing all creatures within 10 feet of you. Each creature in that area must make a Strength saving throw. On a failed save, a target takes 2d6 necrotic damage and can’t take reactions until its next turn. On a successful save, the creature takes half damage, but suffers no other effect. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 2 or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each rank above 1.
