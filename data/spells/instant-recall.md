# Instant Recall

**description**: You gain a near eidetic memory with the ability to clearly recall everything you seen and experienced. For the duration, you have a +10 bonus to any Intelligence checks to recall information about past events or experiences. In addition, while the power remains in effect, if the information you recall can be applied toward an Intelligence (Investigation) or Wisdom (Insight) check, you gain advantage to that roll.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 5
- School: Clairsentient
- Expansion: 27051
- Casting Time: 1 action
