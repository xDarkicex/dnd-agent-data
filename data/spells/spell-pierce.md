# Spell Pierce

**description**: Choose one magical effect with an area and a duration within range. You suppress the magic of a 1st level or lower’s spell in a 10-foot cube area for the duration. If the area of the spell is larger than this spell’s area, it continues as normal outside of this spell’s area. For the area of a spell of 2nd level or higher, make an ability check using your spellcasting ability. The DC equals 10 + the spell’s level. On a successful check, you suppress the magic of the spell’s area as described above. On your subsequent turns, you must use your action to maintain this spell, or the spell ends. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using aspell slot of 2nd level or higher, you automatically supress the effects of a spell’s area if the spell’s level is equal to or less than the level of the spell slot you used.
