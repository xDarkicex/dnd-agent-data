# Phantom Cage

**description**: You create a minor illusory effect of a barbed cage around a one target creature. The creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, the creature takes 1d6 psychic damage and it becomes surrounded by the cage illusion until the start of your next turn. If the target willingly moves before then, it immediately takes 1d6 psychic damage, and the spell ends. This spell’s damage increases when you reach higher levels. Both damage rolls increase by 1d6 at 5th level (2d6/2d6), 11th level (3d6/3d6), and 17th level (4d6/4d6).
