# Thunderclap

**description**: Evocation Cantrip (Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard) Casting Time: Action Range: Self Components: S Duration: Instantaneous Each creature in a 5-foot Emanation originating from you must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d6 Thunder damage . The spell’s thunderous sound can be heard up to 100 feet away. Cantrip Upgrade. The damage increases by 1d6 when you reach levels 5 (2d6), 11 (3d6), and 17 (4d6).
