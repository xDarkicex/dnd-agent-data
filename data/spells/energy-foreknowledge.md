# Energy Foreknowledge

**description**: 4th-Level Divination Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you are the target of a spell that deals cold, fire, force, lightning, necrotic, psychic, radiant, or thunder damage Range: Self Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous When you cast this spell, you gain resistance to every type of energy listed above that is dealt by the spell hitting you. This resistance lasts until the end of your next turn. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you can include one additional ally in its effect for each slot level above 4th. Affected allies must be within 15 feet of you.
