# Avenging Arrows

**description**: You imbue a single ranged weapon with the ammunition trait that you are holding with a spirit of retribution. For the spell’s duration, when an allied creature is hit with a melee attack while within the weapon’s normal range, you can use your reaction to make a single ranged weapon attack with the imbued weapon. This attack does not consume ammunition and deals force damage instead of the weapon’s normal damage type.

**properties**:

- Level: 2
