# Vile Infection

**description**: 6th-level necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (a piece of rotting flesh harvested from a still-living humanoid) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute (special) You reach out and touch the rotting flesh to your enemy, afflicting them with a virulent disease. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or gain a special Injury of Deadly IIII severity, “Infected”. While Injured in this way, creatures cannot regain hit points, the DCs to remove their Injuries other than this one increase by 5. Creatures immune to disease are not affected by this spell, but effects that remove disease do not remove the Injury (though they can be used in conjunction with a Wisdom (Medicine) check to increase the Medicine check result as normal). The Injury is also removed if the spell ends early. If concentration on the spell is maintained for the full duration, the Injury becomes permanent until removed with a successful Wisdom (Medicine) check. Once permanent, an afflicted creature becomes contagious. Any creature that touches an afflicted creature must succeed on a Constitution save or be permanently afflicted with the disease as though concentration had been maintained on them as a target.
