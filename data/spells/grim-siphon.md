# Grim Siphon

**description**: 1st-level necromancy Casting time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: v, m (the skull of a tiny mammal or bird) Duration: instantaneous You twist the forces of entropy around two creatures, bringing distress to one while staving off the demise of the other. A creature you can see within range must make a successful constitution saving throw or take poison damage equal to 1d4 + your spellcasting ability modifier. Another creature you choose that is within 5 feet of the target regains hit points equal to half the amount of poison damage dealt. At higher levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the poison damage increases by 1d4 for each slot level above 1st.
