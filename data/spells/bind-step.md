# Bind Step

**description**: You increase the gravity field around a creature you can see within range. The target must make a Strength saving throw. On a failed save, the target’s movement is reduced by 10 feet and their jumping distance is halved. In addition, their flying speed (if any) is reduced to 0. An airborne creature affected by this power descends at 60 feet per round until it reaches the ground or the effect ends.
