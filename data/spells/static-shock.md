# Static Shock

**description**: You cause a jolt of electricity to surge from you into a single creature you touch. Make a melee spell attack. On a hit, the creature takes 1d8 lightning damage. The creature takes additional damage equal to the number of creatures adjacent to it. This spell’s damage increases by 1d8 when you reach 5th level (2d8), 11th level (3d8), and 17th level (4d8).

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 0
- School: evocation
- Components: V, S
- Damage Type: lightning
- Casting Time: 1 action
- data-RangeAoe: Touch
