# Misdirection

**description**: You create a brief flash of light, loud sound, or other distraction that interrupts a creature’s attack. When a creature you can see within range makes an attack, you can impose disadvantage on its attack roll.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 1
- School: illusion
- Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you see a creature within 30 feet of you make an attack
- data-RangeAoe: 30 feet

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Tome of Heroes
