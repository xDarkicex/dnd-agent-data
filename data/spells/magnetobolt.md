# Magnetobolt

**description**: Evocation cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: 90 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous Class: Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard A near-invisible pulse of arcanomagnetic energy shoots towards a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d6 force damage and must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be knocked prone . A creature made of ferrous metal or wearing ferrous armour has disadvantage on this saving throw. This spell’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level ( 2d6 ), 11th level ( 3d6 ), and 17th level ( 4d6 ).
