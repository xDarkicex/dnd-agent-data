# Blood Drive

**description**: You draw the blood from every creature in a 30- foot cube centred on a point you choose within range. Each creature in that area must make a constitution saving throw. Creatures without blood are unaffected. A creature takes 10d8 necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. For each enemy killed by this spell, you gain 1d8 hit points.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Save: Constitution
- Level: 8
- School: necromancy
- Components: V, S
- Damage Type: necrotic
- Casting Time: 1 Action
- data-RangeAoe: 40 feet.
