# Last Rays Of The Dying Sun

**description**: A burst of searing heat explodes from you, doing 6d6 fire damage to all enemies within 40 feet of you. Immediately afterward, a wave of frigid cold rolls across the same area, doing 6d6 cold damage to enemies. A successful Dexterity saving throw halves all the damage. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 8th or 9th level, the damage from both waves increase by 1d6 for each slot level above 7th
