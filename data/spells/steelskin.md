# Steelskin

**description**: 6th-level abjuration Casting Time: 1 Action Range/Area: Touch Components: V, S, M (adamantine ingot worth 200 gp) Duration: 1 hour, Concentration This spell turns the flesh of a willing creature you touch as hard as adamantine steel alloy, increasing AC while unarmored by +2. Until the spell ends, the target has immunity to nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage. The target’s unarmed attacks deal an additional 1d10 bludgeoning damage and count as magical weapons.
