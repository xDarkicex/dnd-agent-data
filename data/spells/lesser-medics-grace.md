# Lesser Medic’s Grace

**description**: 1st-level abjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S, M (a stalk of ghost pipe flowers, and any of a pinch of herbs, a drop of a tonic or tincture, or other minor medicine) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute For the duration of the spell, the one creature within range ignores the effects of a single Injury chosen by the target when the spell is cast, although the Injury is not healed. When the spell ends, if the creature ignored an Injury of Deadly IIII or higher severity, the creature suffers a level of exhaustion.
