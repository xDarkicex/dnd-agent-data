# Venkar’s Vile Wind

**description**: You send forth a blast of rotting necrotic mist in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in the spell’s area must make a Constitution saving throw. A creature takes 3d4 necrotic damage and it can’t regain hit points until the start of your next turn on a failed save. Until then, the mist clings to the target. If an undead creature takes damage from this spell, it also has disadvantage on attack rolls against you until the end of your next turn.
