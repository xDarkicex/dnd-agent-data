# Stealth Scan

**description**: For the duration, you can see creatures and objects made invisible by stealth tech or esper powers. They appear to you as if they were visible. Intangible creatures and objects appear ghostly and transparent. You can also see hidden openings such as spatial portals leading to other locations or into an extradimensional space.
