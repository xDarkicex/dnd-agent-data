# Gruul War Chant

**description**: You perform a war chant that emboldens up to three creatures of your choice within range to be more agressive. For the spell’s duration, the target creatures can make a single additional weapon attack when they take the Attack action. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 3rd.
