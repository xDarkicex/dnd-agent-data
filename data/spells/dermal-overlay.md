# Dermal Overlay

**description**: You imbue a willing creature with a semi-translucent protective field that hovers an inch above their skin. For the duration, the target’s AC can’t be less than 16, regardless of what kind of armor it is wearing.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 2
- School: Technique
- Expansion: 27051
- Casting Time: 1 action

**publisher**: Alligator Alley Entertainment

**book**: Esper Genesis Core Manual
