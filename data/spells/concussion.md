# Concussion

**description**: With a thdoom that can be heard 100 feet away, you cause a sudden increase in the air pressure surrounding a creature you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d8 thunder damage and become deafened until the start of your next turn. This spell’s damage increases by 1d8 when you reach 5th level ( 2d8 ), 11th level ( 3d8 ), and 17th level ( 4d8 ).
