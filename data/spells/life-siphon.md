# Life Siphon

**description**: Tendrils of necromantic energy spiral off you and leach the life force from nearby creatures. Choose up to six living creatures within a 120-foot-radius sphere centered on you. Each target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 8d6 necrotic damage. For each creature that takes damage, you regain hit points equal to the amount of necrotic damage dealt to that creature.
