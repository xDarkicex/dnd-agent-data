# Haunting Spirits

**description**: 4th-level lair magic (necromancy) Casting Time: 2 weeks (14 days) Radius: 5 miles Components: V, S, M (grave dirt and bones taken from a burial site within the radius, plus 1000 gold pieces in ground onyx) Duration: 1 year This spell summons the spirits of the dead to haunt the surrounding land, draining and terrifying creatures. Living creatures in the area have disadvantage on saving throws against being frightened. Additionally, this spell reduces all sunlight in the area to dim light, allowing creatures with sunlight sensitivity and other light weaknesses to exist without issue within the radius of the spell.
