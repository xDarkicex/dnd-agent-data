# Jam Weapon

**description**: 2nd-level transmutation (renaissance) Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when a creature you can see within range attacks with a firearm Range: 60 feet Components: V, S, M (a pinch of wet gunpowder) Duration: Instantaneous The firearm you target jams upon firing. The attack fails, and the firearm is jammed. A jammed firearm can’t be used to make an attack until a creature uses its action to clear the weapon malfunction. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can jam one additional firearm within range for each slot level above 2nd.
