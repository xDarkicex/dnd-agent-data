# Heed the Whisper

**description**: 1st-level divination Can be cast by druids and rangers Casting Time : 1 action R ange: Self Compon ents: V, S Duration: Up to 8 hours This spell can only be cast while within 1 mile of the Great Forest of Erethor. If you move outside of this range, the spell ends. While this spell is active, you are aware of the presence of all creatures within 300 feet of you and cannot be surprised. If you are asleep and a hostile creature moves within 300 feet of you, you are immediately awakened by a faint but urgent whisper in the back of your mind. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you may choose a single point within the Great Forest of Erethor that you have visited. While this spell is active, you are aware of the presence of all creatures within 300 feet of that point instead.
