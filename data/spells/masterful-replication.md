# Masterful Replication

**description**: You create a temporary duplicate of a magical item you touch during the casting time of this spell. The magic item duplicated can be of rare or lower rarity. The duplicate magic item functions identically to the original. If the item requires attunement, you can select one creature that you can touch and attune the item to that creature for the spell’s duration. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the magic item you duplicate with this spell can be of very rare rarity. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 9th level or higher, the magic item you duplicate with this spell can be of legendary rarity.
