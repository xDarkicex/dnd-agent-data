# Blood Ray

**description**: A warm stream of dark red blood streaks toward a creature within range from your hand. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, it takes 1d8 Necrotic damage, and it becomes blood- soaked. The caster loses 1d6 HP. The spells damage increases by 1d8 when you reach 5th level (2d8), 11th level (3d8), and 17th level (4d8).
