# Cogniphagy

**description**: 6th-level enchantment Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S, M (a web of silk thread in a circle of platinum worth at least 300 gp) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute Jaws of psychic energy temporarily devour a piece of your target's psyche, inflicting pain and weakening their mind, transferring some of their energy to you. Choose one creature you can see within range, the target must make an Intelligence saving throw, taking 8d6 psychic damage on a failed save or half as much on a success. Additionally, a creature that fails its saving throw gains disadvantage on Concentration checks and Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws until the spell ends. While the spell lasts, you have advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the spell on a success.
