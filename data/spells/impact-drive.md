# Impact Drive

**description**: The first time you hit with a weapon attack during this power’s duration, your weapon unleashes a burst of concussive force, dealing an extra 1d6 force damage to the target. Additionally, if the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
