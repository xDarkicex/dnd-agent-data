# Concussive Force

**description**: You create a burst of outward energy in a 10-foot square centered on a point within range. Each creature in that area must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or be knocked prone. In addition, any targets within 5 feet of a creature who failed their save must also make a Dexterity save or be knocked prone as well. Any creatures further than 5 feet from the area are unaffected regardless of failed saves.
