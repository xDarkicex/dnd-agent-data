# Debilitating Strike

**description**: The next time you hit a creature with a weapon attack during this power’s duration, your weapon unleashes a wave of psychic energy, and the attack deals an extra 4d6 psychic damage to the target. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, it has disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks, and can’t take reactions, until the end of its next turn.
