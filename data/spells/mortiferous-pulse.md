# Mortiferous Pulse

**description**: You summon a bolt of negative energy to stimulate and transmit necrotic pathogens into those that stand before you, decaying their internal viscera. Each creature in a 5-foot-wide , 50-foot-long line extending from you must make a Dexterity saving throw , taking 3d8 necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
