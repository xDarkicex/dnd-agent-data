# Gift of Orzhova

**description**: You touch a single creature and it sprouts angelic wings of hardened multicolored glass. For the spell’s duration, it has a fly speed equal to its base movement speed and it gains temporary hit points equal to your spellcasting modifier whenever it hits with an attack.

- Level: 3

- Casting Time: 1 action
