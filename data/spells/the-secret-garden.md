# The Secret Garden

**description**: The Secret Garden Requirements: Level 15 Casting Time: 1 action Range: touch (the Likeness) Duration: special Cost: 2 WP (invested) Create a Likeness of an imaginary place, usually a room with a shelf, and imbue it with 2 invested Weave Points. You may then transfer objects into that place and retrieve them whenever you wish. Each object stored requires an expenditure of an additional invested 1 Weave Point above the creation cost. This invested Weave Point is regained at the next long rest after the object is retrieved from the Secret Garden.
