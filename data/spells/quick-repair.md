# Quick Repair

**description**: You repair an automaton, construct, or mechanical device, or planetary vehicle that you can touch. It regains hit points equal to 1d8 + your forging ability modifier. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 3 or higher, the healing increases by 1d8 for each rank above 2.
