# Ersch’s Suffocating Symbol-Crypt

**description**: 8th-level abjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S, M (a scroll scribed with arcane code) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You conjure a barrier of pure force covered in swirling arcane symbols around your foe and siphon the oxygen from their lungs, trapping them in an encrypted, phrase-locked tomb. Choose one creature that you can see within range. That creature must make a Dexterity saving throw or be trapped in a box of pure force and immediately begin to suffocate as though it had run out of air (see player’s book page 183). Nothing can pass through the barrier, though it is mostly translucent. As an action, any creature within 10 feet of the prison can attempt an Intelligence check against your spell save DC to learn the passphrase necessary to dispel the barrier. On a success, they can speak the phrase and cause the spell to end. When the spell ends, the barrier disappears and the target creature's breath returns. The spell also ends early if the target dies. A creature that does not need to breathe can still be affected by this spell, though it does not suffocate. Ability checks made to dispel the barrier (but not to counter the spell) have disadvantage.
