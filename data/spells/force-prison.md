# Force Prison

**description**: You create an immobile, invisible, cube-shaped prison composed of repelling force around an area you choose within range. The prison is shaped like a box and can be up to 10 feet on a side, creating a solid barrier that prevents any matter from passing through it and blocking any esper powers being used into or out from the area. When you use this power, any creature that is completely inside the prison’s area is trapped. Creatures only partially within the area, or those too large to fit inside the area, are pushed away from the center of the area until they are completely outside the area. A creature inside the prison can’t leave it by mundane means. If the creature tries to use teleportation or dimensional travel to leave the prison, it must first make a Charisma saving throw. On a success, the creature can use that power to exit the prison. On a failure, the creature can’t exit the prison and wastes the use of the power or effect. This power can’t be unraveled by unravel effect .
