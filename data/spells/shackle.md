# Shackle

**description**: 1st-level conjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: 90 feet Components: V, S, M (a manacle’s link) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute Classes: Bard, Cleric, Paladin, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard You point at a creature you can see within range, conjuring ethereal manacles that attempt to bind its limbs. The target must succeed on a Strength saving throw or, for the duration, its speed is halved, it has disadvantage on attack rolls and Dexterity saving throws, and attack rolls against it have advantage . In addition, standing up from prone costs the creature twice as much movement as usual. A creature immune to being restrained automatically succeeds on its saving throw against this spell. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.
