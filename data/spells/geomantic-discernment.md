# Geomantic Discernment

**description**: 3rd-level divination (ritual) Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self (100-foot radius) Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes You gain the ability to sense valuable minerals in a 100-foot radius around you. A glowing aura that only you can see appears around any valuable metals, gems, ores, native elements, or other resources in that radius for the spell’s duration. You can see this aura through any amount of nonmagical material.
