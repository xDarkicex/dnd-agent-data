# Mushroom Apotheosis

**description**: You speak the words of power, point at a target within range and from your hands, a massive, fungal forest springs forth, coating any floor, wall and similar surface within half a mile, instantly generating a new fungal forest; you have limited control over the development of this forest. The entire area becomes filled with mushrooms of all varieties. You may roll on the Mushroom Varieties table a number of times equal to your level to determine which mushroom dominate the spread of mushrooms created by this spell.
