# Fire Dance

**description**: 4th-level illusion Casting time: 1 action Range: 120 feet Components: s, m (a burning mass of rags, a pair of torches, or similar fire in both hands) Duration: concentration, up to 1 hour You dance and twirl two sources of fire in a rapid pattern that leaves glowing trails. Creatures of your choice in the area that see your fire dance must make a wisdom saving throw. Any creature that can’t be charmed succeeds on this saving throw automatically, and if you or your companions are fighting a creature, it has advantage on the save. On a failed save, a target becomes charmed for the duration. While charmed by this spell, the creature is incapacitated and has a speed of 0. The spell ends for an affected creature if it takes any damage or if someone else uses an action to shake the creature out of its stupor. The spell ends if you are incapacitated.
