# Unluck On That

**description**: By uttering a swift curse (“Unluck on that!”), you bring misfortune to the target’s attempt; the affected creature has disadvantage on the roll. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the range of the spell increases by 5 feet for each slot level above 1st.
