# Returnal

**description**: 8th-level necromancy Casting Time: 7 hours Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (spores from a bioluminescent fungus, the target's blood, and a diamond worth 1000 gp, which the spell consumes) Duration: Instantaneous You plant the spores of a bioluminescent fungus in an area with decaying organic matter, feeding it with the blood of a willing creature, and permanently binding the creature to the fungus, allowing it to return from death when slain. The next time the creature would die, its body melts into a pool of decomposed sludge and reforms at the location where the spell was cast. When it reforms, it regains all its hit points. Additionally all Injuries the creature suffered from at the time of death are healed, with the creature suffering from theLasting Effects as normal. None of the creature’s items are transported with it.
