# Hold Monster

**description**: Level 5 Enchantment (Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard) Casting Time: Action Range: 90 feet Components: V, S, M (a straight piece of iron) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute Choose a creature that you can see within range.
