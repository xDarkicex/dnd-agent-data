# Cornucopia of Gore

**description**: 5th-level necromancy Casting Time: 10 minutes Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (a corpse of small or larger size with an intelligence of 8 or higher) Duration: 1 hour (or until consumed) You ritually prepare a corpse so that its wounded flesh will empower those who partake of it. Upon finishing your incantations, the corpse ruptures into a nauseating yet seemingly delicious feast. Up to eight creatures can consume its flesh, becoming gorged on the suffering which it experienced in its last moments. The corpse must be of a creature that was slain within the last day, or which has had gentle repose cast on it every day since the day it was killed. For each Injury that the corpse suffered from at the time of its death, each creature that consumes a piece of its flesh gains 5 maximum hit points (max 25). Additionally, up to two creatures can consume part of its head to gain a +2 bonus to Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma; up to four creatures can consume its arms and legs to gain a +2 bonus to Strength or Dexterity; and up to two creatures can consume its torso to gain a +2 bonus to Constitution. Each creature has advantage on saving throws made with the enhanced ability score. These effects last for 24 hours, until the creature consumes another cornucopia of gore, or until the recipient finishes a long rest.
