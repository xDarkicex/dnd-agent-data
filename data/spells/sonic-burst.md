# Sonic Burst

**description**: You emit burst of sonic energy centered upon you. Each creature in a 5 foot radius must make a Constitution saving throw or suffer 1d6 thunder damage. You are excluded from this effect. In addition, the technique emits a thunderous boom that can be heard from 150 feet away. This power’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
