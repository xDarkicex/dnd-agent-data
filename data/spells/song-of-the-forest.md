# Song Of The Forest

**description**: You attune your senses to the natural world, so you detect every sound that occurs within 60 feet: wind blowing through branches, falling leaves, grazing deer, trickling streams, and more. You can clearly picture the cause of each sound in your mind. The effect gives you tremorsense with a range of 10 feet. In addition, you have advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sound. Creatures that make no noise or that are magically silent cannot be detected through this spell's effect. Song of the forest only functions in natural environments; it fails if cast underground, in a city, or in a building that isolates the caster from nature (GM's discretion). Ritual Focus. If you expend your ritual focus, the spell's effect is equivalent to blindsight with a range of 30 feet.
