# Sensory Boost

**description**: You provide a sensory upgrade to up to three creatures of your choice within range. Each creature gains a +2 bonus to attack rolls and saving throws before the end of the effect’s duration. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 2 or higher, you can target one additional creature for each rank above 1.
