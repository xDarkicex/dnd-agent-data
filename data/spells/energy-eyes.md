# Energy Eyes

**description**: 3rd-Order Resopathy Power Manifestation Time : 1 action Range : 120 feet Duration: Instantaneous Creature s: Goxomoc You beam elemental energy from your eyes at a creature you can see within range. The target must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 5d10 damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Choose the damage type from the following list: cold, fire, lightning, or radiant. Increased Order. When you manifest this power, you can increase its order by 1 or more. For each increase of 1, the damage increases by 2d10.
