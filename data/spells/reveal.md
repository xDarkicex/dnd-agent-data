# Reveal

**description**: Reveal 2nd-level divination Casting Time: 1 action Range: 20 feet Components: S, M (handful of living moths) Duration: Instantaneous You toss a handful of moths towards one opponent in an attempt to force them to reveal their true form. If they are a shapechanged creature they must make a Charisma saving throw or be forced into their true form (or another form, if they have multiple). As well, if they fail, they suffer 8d6 force damage from the abrupt change. Not all shapechangers are affected: if the creature normally takes more than 10 minutes to change their shape, they merely take the damage but retain their existing form. Beneath the Surface When used against any creature with dual natures (such as the grim) if that creature fails its saving throw it switches to its alternate form. Likewise, a tuss that fails its saving throw activates its true nature feature (if not already activated).
