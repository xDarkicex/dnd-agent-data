# Displacement

**description**: You displace light so that you appear to be several feet from your actual location. For the duration, you project a magical illusion that makes it appear you are standing near your actual location, causing attack rolls against you to have disadvantage. Your melee weapon attacks have advantage. An attacker or defender is immune to this effect if it doesn’t rely on sight, as with blindsight, or can see through illusions, as with truesight. If you are subjected to an effect that allows you to make a saving throw to take only half damage, you instead take no damage if you succeed on the saving throw, and only half damage if you fail.
