# Dead Weight

**description**: You target a single creature within range causing its burdens to be magnified in weight. The creature must make a Strength saving throw. On a failure, the creature’s speed drops by 10 feet. If the creature picks up any objects besides what it had on it when this spell was cast, its speed is reduced to 20 and it has disadvantage on ability checks, attack rolls, and saving throws that use Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution so long as it carries that object.
