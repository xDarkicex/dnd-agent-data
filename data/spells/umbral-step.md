# Umbral Step

**description**: You enter a shadow large enough to contain you and teleport yourself from your current location to any other shadowy spot within range of the spell. It can be a place you can see, one you can visualize, or one you can describe by stating the distance and direction, so long as the destination is also a shadow large enough to contain you. If you would arrive in a place already occupied by an object or a creature, you take 4d6 force damage, and the spell fails to teleport you. Stepping into the shadow is a Disengage, and you may Hide as a bonus action upon arrival. You can cast this spell as an action on a creature you can touch, granting them the benefits of this spell, but casting the spell in this way causes you to lose your shadow until you complete a short rest.
