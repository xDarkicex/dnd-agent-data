# Seethe

**description**: Malefic word, abjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous, ongoing (see this malefic word’s baleful echo) Effects: You speak a word of bitter resentment, and enmity becomes so strong in your soul that those who would strike you prick themselves on its barbs. You gain 1d4 temporary hit points. The number of temporary hit points increases by 1d4 when you reach 5th level (2d4), 11th level (3d4), and 17th level (4d4). Baleful Echo (1 vitality): Until the end of your next turn, whenever you are attacked, your attacker takes psychic damage equal to the amount of temporary hit points you lose because of the attack. Feedback: The resentment you feel towards others is magnified back upon you. You become vulnerable to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage for 1 minute.
