# Nature's Aegis

**description**: 1st-Level Abjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, S, M (an area of natural vegetation) Duration: 1 hour Grass, vines, branches, and other vegetation weave themselves over your body into a temporary suit of armor. You can use an action to dismiss this spell. Nature’s aegis gives you an Armor Class of 14 + your Dexterity modifier. The armor weighs 8 pounds and provides you with advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide in the terrain from which you used the vegetation.
