# Aspect of Lamprey

**description**: The touched creature’s mouth changes into that of a parasitic lamprey. The creature gains a bite attack, which is treated as an unarmed attack that uses either Strength or Dexterity and the creature is considered proficient with it. The attack deals 1d6 piercing damage and 2d6 necrotic damage. The target of the bite attack’s hit point maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the necrotic damage taken, and the touched creature regains hit points equal to that amount. The reduction lasts until the target finishes a long rest. The target of the bite attack dies if its hit point maximum is reduced to 0. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the damage dealt by the touched creature’s bite attack increases by 1d6 necrotic for each slot level above 5th.
