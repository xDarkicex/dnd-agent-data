# Personal Sentry

**description**: You create a small pack of tiny stealth drones to protect you. They hover around you to a distance of 15 feet for the duration. When you use this power, you can designate any number of creatures you can see to be unaffected by the drones. An affected creature’s speed is halved in the area, and when the creature enters the area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there, it must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the creature takes 3d8 radiant damage from the drones’ weapon fire. On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 4 or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each rank above 3.
