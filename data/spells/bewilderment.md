# Bewilderment

**description**: Enchantment Cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: 1 minute You temporarily inhibit the cognitive ability of a creature within range. If the target makes a successful Wisdom saving throw, the spell is ineffective. On a failed save, the next time the creature makes an Intelligence check before the spell ends, roll a d6 and subtract the result from the roll. The spell then ends.
