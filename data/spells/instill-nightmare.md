# Instill Nightmare

**description**: 5th-level illusion Casting Time: 10 minutes (special) Range: Unlimited Components: V, S, M (incense made of Angel’s Trumpet flowers) Duration: Instantaneous Your spell infects the thoughts of a sleeping creature on the same plane as you, suffusing their dreams with nightmares of your choosing. The creature must make a Wisdom saving throw or take 1d10 psychic damage, and have their rest disturbed. The nightmare inflicts the creature with a level of exhaustion (this cannot increase their level of exhaustion more than twice if cast on consecutive nights), and they can only regain half of their missing spell slots, starting with the lowest level, for the next 24 hours. Monsters and NPCs with the ability to cast spells a limited number of times not tied to spell slots can use each ability half as many times (minimum once) in the next 24 hours. The saving throw is made with advantage or disadvantage depending on how well you know the creature. If you know the name of the creature, are familiar with them (you know them well), or have a body part, lock of hair, piece of fingernail, or other piece of flesh from the creature, the creature has disadvantage on the saving throw. If you know nothing about the creature (even if you have a piece of its body), it has advantage on the saving throw. If the creature is under the effect of a protection from evil and good or dispel evil and good while you are casting the spell, or the Break Enchantment effect of dispel evil and good is used on the creature, the nightmare is dispelled, and you are stunned for 1 hour. If the target of the nightmare is awake when the spell begins, you can choose to let the spell fail, or to enter a trance until the target goes to sleep, at which point you become alert and finish casting the spell. If you are interrupted while in this trance, you must succeed on a DC 15 Concentration check or the spell ends. While in the trance, you are not aware of your surroundings, and you are considered defenseless. You automatically fail any Strength or Dexterity saving throw, and any attack roll that hits you becomes a critical hit. Creatures that do not dream or do not sleep are immune to this spell.
