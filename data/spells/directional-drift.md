# Directional Drift

**description**: You create a wave of opposing force and direct it into a creature you can see within range. The target must make a Strength saving throw or be pushed up to 5 feet away from you. This power can also be used to move loose or free-standing objects up to 10 feet away from you. The object must weigh 50 pounds or less and cannot be held nor carried. The force generated is not enough to cause damage to the object or anything it is pushed into.
