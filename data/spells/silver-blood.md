# Silver Blood

**description**: 2nd-level abjuration (ritual) Can be cast by bards, sorcerers, and wizards Casting Time : 1 minute R ange: Self Components: V, S, M (a small silver coin) Durati on: 1 hour In a desperate move, you infuse your own blood with the elemental properties of silver, making it a potent weapon against spirits and some undead. For the duration of this spell, you can choose to draw your own blood, suffering 1d4 damage and creating one of the following effects: Coat up to ten pieces of ammunition or one melee weapon with your blood. This ammunition or weapon counts as silvered for 1 hour. With your blood, draw a warding line up to 2 feet long; it remains active for 1 hour. Any creature that is adversely affected by silver (such as a creature that has vulnerability to silvered weapons or loses its resistance to silvered weapons) must make a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw when they attempt to cross the line. If they fail, they can't cross the warding line while it remains active. With your blood, paint yourself in cloaking symbols that prevent an astirax from detecting you with its Scent Magic ability for 1 hour. You can create as many of these effects as you like, suffering the damage each time.
