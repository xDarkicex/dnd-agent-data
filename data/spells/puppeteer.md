# Puppeteer

**description**: You channel the arcane essence in your blood to temporarily enthral the creatures around you. You release a wave of dark crimson energy from your body that seeps into the body of each creature in a 25 foot radius of you, causing them to twitch as you attempt to control all blood in their bodies. Each creature must make a Constitution saving throw vs your spell save DC. On a failed save, the target moves up to its speed in a direction of your choosing. In addition, you can issue any command you choose, with the exception of an activity that would result in instant death. Should you issue a suicidal command, the spell ends on that creature. All affected creatures are paralysed except when carrying out your commands. For the duration of the spell, you can use your action to command a number of affected creatures up to your spell casting modifier (minimum of 1) to move and take an action. When you do this, the commanded creature can make another Constitution saving throw to resist the command and end the spell. Affected creatures can attempt to free themselves of your control by re-rolling their Constitution saving throw at the end of each of their turns. As a bonus action you can dismiss the spell to end it early. This spell can be removed using remove curse . This spell has no effect on creatures without blood.
