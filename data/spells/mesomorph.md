# Mesomorph

**description**: 8th-level transmutation Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V Duration: 1 hour Until the spell ends, when you make a Strength or Constitution check, you can replace the number you roll with a 15. Additionally, you count as one size larger for the purpose of grappling and shoving, as well as determining the weight you can push, drag, or lift.
