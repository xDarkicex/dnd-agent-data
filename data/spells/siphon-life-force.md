# Siphon Life Force

**description**: 6th-level necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, S, M (a censer of burning incense worth at least 250 gp) Duration: 1 minute Necrotic smoke pours forth from your censer in a 30-foot radius, harming the living and drawing in the life force of the dying, furnishing you with power. At the start of each of your turns, each living creature other than you within the area takes 2d6 necrotic damage. Whenever a creature in the radius dies, you gain 20 temporary hit points, and gain advantage on ability checks, attack rolls, and saving throws until the end of your next turn. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the spell deals an additional 2d6 damage per level beyond 6th.
