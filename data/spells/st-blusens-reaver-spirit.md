# St Blusen's Reaver Spirit

**description**: You inspire allies to fight with the savagery of berserkers. You and allies you can see within range have: advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws; resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical weapons; and a +2 bonus to damage with melee weapons. When the spell ends, all affected creatures must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or gain 1d4 levels of exhaustion. At Higher Levels . When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the melee damage bonus increases by 1 for each slot level above 2nd.
