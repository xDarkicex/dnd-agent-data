# Instant Freeze

**description**: You freeze a 5-foot cube of water that you can see within range. Any creatures in the water must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or become restrained. A restrained creature can use its action to make a Strength check against your spell save DC, breaking the ice around it and freeing itself on a success. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the size of the cube increases by 5 feet for each slot level above 1st.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Save: Dexterity
- Level: 1
- School: evocation
- Components: V, S, M
- Casting Time: 1 action

**publisher**: MCDM Productions
