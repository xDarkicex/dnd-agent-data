# Jump

**description**: Level 1 Transmutation (Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard) Casting Time: Bonus Action Range: Touch Component: V, S, M (a grasshopper’s hind leg) Duration: 1 minute You touch a willing creature.
