# Starfall

**description**: Starfall 5th-level enchantment (druid, sorcerer, wizard) Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: Self (30-foot radius) Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You create a violent star shower. When you cast this spell and as a bonus action on your subsequent turns, you can bring down stars on creatures of your choice within 30 feet of you. Each creature struck by a star takes 1d10+1 radiant damage. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 5th.
