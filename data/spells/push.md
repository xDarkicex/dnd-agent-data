# Push

**description**: A gravity wave lashes out from your hand at a target within range. Make a ranged channeling attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d8 force damage and must make a Strength saving throw. On a failed save, you can push the target up to 10 feet away from you. This power’s damage increases by 1d8 when you reach 5th level (2d8), 11th level (3d8), and 17th level (4d8).
