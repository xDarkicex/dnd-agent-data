# Excoriate

**description**: You cause divine radiance to wash over a single creature you touch. The creature must make a Constitution saving throw and it has disadvantage on this save if it targeted you with an attack since the end of your last turn. On a failure, the target takes 6d12 radiant damage. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d12 for each slot level above 3rd.

**properties**:

- Save: Constitution
