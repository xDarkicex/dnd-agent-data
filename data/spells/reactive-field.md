# Reactive Field

**description**: You surround yourself with an aura of extreme heat or extreme cold, as you choose. The heat shield grants you resistance to cold damage, and the cold shield grants you resistance to fire damage. In addition, whenever a creature within 5 feet of you hits you with a melee attack, the shield erupts with reactive energy. The attacker takes 2d8 fire damage from a heat shield, or 2d8 cold damage from a cold shield.
