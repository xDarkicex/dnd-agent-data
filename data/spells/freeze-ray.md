# Freeze Ray

**description**: A frigid white beam of energy springs from your finger toward a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 2d8 cold damage and is restrained. At the end of each of the target’s turns, it can make a Strength saving throw against the spell. On a success, the spell ends. On a failure, the target takes 1d8 cold damage and is still restrained. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the initial damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
