# Woodland Walk

**description**: 3rd-level transmutation Casting time: 1 action Range: touch Components: v, s, m (a twig from a dryad’s tree) Duration: concentration, up to 1 hour You touch a willing creature. Difficult terrain composed of nonmagical plants doesn’t cost the target extra movement. In addition, the target can pass through nonmagical plants without being slowed by them and without taking damage from them if they have thorns, spines, or similar hazards. At higher levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 3rd.
