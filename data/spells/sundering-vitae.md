# Sundering Vitae

**description**: You cause the enchantment of a single magic item within range to become broken for the duration. If a creature is holding or wearing the magic item, the creature makes a Charisma saving throw. On a failure or if the item is unattended, the item’s enchantment is supressed for the duration. This spell has no effect on items with a Legendary rarity or Artifacts.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Save: Charisma
- Level: 2
- School: necromancy
- Components: V, S, M
- Casting Time: 1 action
- data-RangeAoe: 60 feet
