# Enhance Weapon

**description**: You touch a mundane weapon, charging it with energy from your rig. Until the effect ends, that weapon becomes a forge enhanced weapon with a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 4 or higher, the bonus increases to +2. When you use this power at rank 6 or higher, the bonus increases to +3.
