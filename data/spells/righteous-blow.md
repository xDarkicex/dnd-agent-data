# Righteous Blow

**description**: As part of the action used to cast this spell, you must make a melee attack with a weapon against one creature within the spell’s range, otherwise the spell fails. You gain a bonus to the attack roll equal to the number of hostile creatures within 5 feet of you. On a hit, the target suffers the attack’s normal effects, and takes radiant damage equal to twice the number of hostile creatures within 5 feet of you. This spell’s damage increases by 1d8 radiant when you reach 5th level (1d8), 11th level (2d8), and 17th level (3d8).
