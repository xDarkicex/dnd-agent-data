# Nails of the Believer

**description**: 1st-level Evocation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S, M Duration: Instantaneous You call upon the holy Inquisition to grant you power. Three magical nails appear on your hand, which you can use to strike your enemies. Make a melee (reach 5 ft.) or ranged (up to Range) spell attack for each nail. On a hit, the target takes piercing damage equal to 1d4 + your Intelligence modifier. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you create one additional nail for each slot above 1st level.
