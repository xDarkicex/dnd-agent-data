# Scald

**description**: Malefic word, evocation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 5 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous, ongoing (see this malefic word’s baleful echo) Effects: You spit out a fiery Word of Power that scorches your tongue and sets the blood of all creatures within 5 feet of you aboil. Each creature within range must make a Constitution saving throw. If they fail, they take 1d6 fire damage and start bleeding. If they succeed, they take half damage and do not start bleeding. This malefic word’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6). Baleful Echo (1 vitality): All creatures within range take 1d6 fire damage. Feedback: Your own blood boils and sizzles in your veins. You begin bleeding (1d4 piercing damage at the start of each of your turns) for the next 1d6 rounds. While you are bleeding, you cannot regain hit points.
