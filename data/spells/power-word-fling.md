# Power Word Fling

**description**: 3rd-level conjuration Casting time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: v Duration: concentration, up to 1 minute You mutter a word of power that causes a creature you can see within range to be flung vertically or horizontally. The creature must succeed on a strength saving throw or be thrown up to 15 feet vertically or horizontally. If the target impacts a surface, such as a ceiling or wall, it stops moving and takes 3d6 bludgeoning damage. If the target was thrown vertically, it plummets to the ground, taking falling damage as normal, unless it has a flying speed or other method of preventing a fall. If the target impacts a creature, the target stops moving and takes 3d6 bludgeoning damage, and the creature the target hits must succeed on a strength saving throw or be knocked prone. After the target is thrown horizontally or it falls from being thrown vertically, regardless of whether it impacted a surface, it is knocked prone. As a bonus action on each of your subsequent turns, you can attempt to fling the same creature again. The target must succeed on another strength saving throw or be thrown. At higher levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the distance you can fling the target increases by 5 feet, and the damage from impacting a surface or creature increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 3rd.
