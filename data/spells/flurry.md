# Flurry

**description**: 1st-Level Transmutation Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: Self Components: V, S, M (a fleck of quartz) Duration: 1 round A flurry of snow surrounds you and extends to a 5-foot radius around you. While it lasts, anyone trying to see into, out of, or through the affected area (including you) has disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks and attack rolls.
