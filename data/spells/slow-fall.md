# Slow Fall

**description**: Choose up to five falling creatures within range. A falling creature’s rate of descent slows to 60 feet per round until the talent ends. If the creature lands before the end of the duration, it takes no falling damage and can land on its feet, and the talent’s effect ends for that creature.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 1
- School: Kinesis
- Expansion: 27051
- Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you or a creature within 60 feet of you falls
- data-RangeAoe: 60 feet

**publisher**: Alligator Alley Entertainment

**book**: Esper Genesis Core Manual
