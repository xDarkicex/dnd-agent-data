# Greater Amplify Desert

**description**: 9th-level lair magic (transmutation) Casting Time: 2 weeks (14 days) Radius: 4 miles Components: V, S, M (A rare material or materials found within the local environment worth at least 6000 gold pieces, which the spell consumes) Duration: 1 year This spell functions like lesser amplify desert, except that intruders are also followed by a localized sandstorm, pointing out their location and making travel even harder. The sandstorm reduces visibility to 15 feet, and prevents the use of ranged weapons. Creatures protected from the spell are able to see perfectly despite the storm.
