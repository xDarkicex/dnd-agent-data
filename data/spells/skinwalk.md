# Skinwalk

**description**: 8th-level transmutation Casting Time: 1 hour Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (a set of freshly sharpened carving knives, onyx worth at least 500 gp) Duration: Instantaneous Through a ritual carving process, you separate the skin of a dead creature from its body, then do the same for your own and slip into the other creature’s skin, taking on its form. Any gear you wear or carry is left behind unless you can wear or carry it in your new form. While in this form you can perfectly imitate the creature, though you do not gain any of its memories, abilities, skills, or other traits. Another person that is intimately familiar with the creature (i.e. a family member or long-time friend) can make a Wisdom (Insight) check at disadvantage opposed by your Charisma (Deception) check to determine that you are not that creature. If the creature does not have hands, you cannot complete spells that require somatic components, but you can still speak even if the creature’s form normally would not have the ability to do so. If the new form is larger or smaller than your own, your body grows or shrinks to fit the new form. You can use your voice or the creature’s voice when you speak (or attempt to imitate another voice as normal). Additionally, while you are in this form, you are considered to be that creature for the purposes of any divination magic that targets you, and divination magic that would specifically target you (such as the scry spell) will fail as though you do not exist. You can leave this form using a 10-minute ritual to change back into your own skin as long as you have access to it (your skin does not age or decompose while you are in another form, but it can be damaged or destroyed). Once you have left this form, the skin for the creature rapidly decays, and any divination magic used to determine information about any actions you took while in this form reveal information about the creature rather than you. If your skin is destroyed while in another person’s form, it can be recreated using a regenerate spell cast on you after leaving the skin you currently wear.
