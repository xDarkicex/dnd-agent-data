# Cannibalize

**description**: 3rd-level necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (a sharpened fork, which is stabbed into the target’s flesh) Duration: 1 minute (save ends partial) Your touch saps the life force of your target, draining their strongest aspect and empowering you. One creature you touch must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or gain a special Injury of Handicap III severity, “Cannibalized”. While Injured in this way, the creature’s highest ability score is reduced by 2 (along with all associated bonuses, not including max hp). Additionally, your ability score is increased by 2. At the end of each of its turns, the creature may repeat the saving throw to remove the Injury, but the bonus to your ability score remains until the spell ends. At Higher Levels. For every two levels beyond 3rd, the ability score reduction increases by 2, as does the increase to your ability score.
