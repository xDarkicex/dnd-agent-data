# Good Conversation

**description**: Good Conversation Requirements: Level 1 Casting Time: special Range: special Duration: instantaneous Cost: 1 WP The ancient Greeks knew the benefit of talking for mental health. Their term for conversational therapy was Good Conversation. The philosopher can help others lose Dismay points with this power. It can be used on one target during a long rest (lose 1 Dismay) and requires some private time spent together in actual conversation (this does not impinge on general long rest recovery). To be successful, the philosopher has to make a Wisdom (Insight) check against a DC of 10. The Weave Point spent can’t be recovered during the long rest it is spent in – it counts as being spent after that long rest.
