# Snap Freeze

**description**: 3rd-level evocation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S, M (a chunk of ice or amber) Duration: Instantaneous You gesture and utter your incantation, harnessing the elemental power of cold to temporarily freeze your target solid. One creature you can see within range must make a Constitution saving throw or be frozen solid for 1 round. While frozen, the creature is incapacitated and restrained. While they are incapacitated in this way, they are considered Imperiled. If they do not have enough Injuries to be Imperiled without the effects of this spell, the DC for a Death Blow they suffer is reduced by 5. If they have at least the required number, the DC is increased by 5.
