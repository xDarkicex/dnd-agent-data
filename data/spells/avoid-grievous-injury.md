# Avoid Grievous Injury

**description**: 1st-Level Divination Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you are struck by a critical hit Range: Self Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous You cast this spell when a foe strikes you with a critical hit but before damage dice are rolled. The critical hit against you becomes a normal hit.
