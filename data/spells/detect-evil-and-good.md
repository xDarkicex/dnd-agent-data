# Detect Evil and Good

**description**: Level 1 Divination (Cleric, Paladin) Casting Time: Action Range: Self Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes For the duration, you sense the location of any Aberration, Celestial, Elemental, Fey, Fiend, or Undead within 30 feet of yourself.
