# Bloodsharing

**description**: Bloodsharing 3rd-level necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: 10 feet Components: V, S, M (a knife and the blood of the participants) Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes You slice a knife into your flesh (inflicting 1 slashing damage) and murmur ancient words in a language no longer remembered. Each participant within range who uses their reaction to also cut their own flesh (and whom you accept into the spell) becomes part of the bloodsharing for the remaining duration. Until the spell ends, any magical healing that affects one of the bloodsharers, affects all of them.
