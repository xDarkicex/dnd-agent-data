# Omen of the Sun

**description**: You conjure forward two sun soldiers to protect you after you are attacked. The sun soldiers appear in an unoccupied space adjacent to you. A guard is celestial creatures and disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends. The summoned creature is friendly to you and your companions. Roll initiative for the summoned creatures, which have their own turns. The sun soldiers obey any verbal commands that you issue to them, requiring an action from you if either guard takes an action as a part of following the command. If you don’t issue any commands to them, they defend themselves from hostile creatures, but otherwise take no actions. See the entry for Sun Soldier for this creature’s statistics.
