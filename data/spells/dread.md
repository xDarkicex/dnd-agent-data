# Dread

**description**: Malefic word , illusion Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous, ongoing (see this malefic word’s baleful echo) Effects: You speak a Word of Power that conceptualizes the worst nightmares of one living creature within range. That creature must make a Charisma saving throw. On a failure, the target takes 1d6 psychic damage and becomes paralyzed until the end of your next turn as it is trapped in an illusion in which its greatest fears play out. If the target succeeds, it becomes frightened of you until the end of your next turn and the malefic word’s baleful echo is silenced (and you do not suffer the feedback). Baleful Echo (2 vitality): The target must make a Charisma saving throw. On a failure, it takes 2d6 psychic damage and remains paralyzed until the end of your next turn. If the target succeeds, it becomes frightened of you until the end of your next turn and the malefic word’s baleful echo is silenced (and you do not suffer the feedback). The damage dealt by this malefic word increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (3d6), 11th level (4d6), and 17th level (5d6). Feedback: The dread word sinks into your psyche, plunging you into the worst nightmares your own mind can conjure. You are paralyzed for 1d6 rounds.
