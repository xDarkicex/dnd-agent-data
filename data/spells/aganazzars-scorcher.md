# Aganazzar's Scorcher

**description**: 2nd-Level Evocation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S, M (A red dragon's scale) Duration: Instantaneous A line of roaring flame 30 feet long and 5 feet wide emanates from you in a direction you choose. Each creature in the line must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 3d8 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
