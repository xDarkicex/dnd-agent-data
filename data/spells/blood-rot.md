# Blood Rot

**description**: Necromantic energy washes over a creature of your choice that you can see within range, destroying and rotting the blood within. The target must make a constitution saving throw. The target takes 8d8 necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs or things without blood. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 4th.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Save: Constitution
- Level: 4
- School: necromancy
- Components: V, S
- Damage Type: necrotic
- Casting Time: 1 Action
