# Pitifulness

**description**: Enchantment cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You weave an aura about yourself that evokes pity and sympathy from others. Whenever a creature that you can see and that can see you makes an attack roll against you, you can use your reaction to impose disadvantage on the attack roll. A creature immune to being charmed ignores this spell’s effects.
