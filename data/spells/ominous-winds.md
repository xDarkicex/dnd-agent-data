# Ominous Winds

**description**: 2nd-level enchantment Casting time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: v, s, m (a cracked bone) Duration: concentration, up to 1 minute Up to three creatures of your choice that you can see within range must make charisma saving throws. Whenever a target that fails this saving throw makes an attack roll or a saving throw before the spell ends, the target must roll a d8 and subtract the number rolled from the attack roll or saving throw. At higher levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd.
