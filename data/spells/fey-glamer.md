# Fey Glamer

**description**: 1st-Level Illusion Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (a pinch of powdered mica) Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes You lay a glamor upon a touched creature that makes it more interesting, attractive, and compelling. For the duration of the spell, the target has advantage on all Charisma checks.
