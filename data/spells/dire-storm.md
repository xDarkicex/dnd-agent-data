# Dire Storm

**description**: 7th-level lair magic (conjuration) Casting Time: 1 week (7 days) Radius: 2 miles Components: V, S, M (pieces of fulgurite and tanzanite worth at least 3500 gold pieces, which the spell consumes) Duration: 1 year This spell creates an exceptionally potent magical thunderstorm that recognizes intruders to the area and directs its full fury at them, including lightning bolts, torrential downpours, and severe winds that batter and exhaust them as they travel. Any creature in the area that is hostile to the lair’s master travels at a quarter of its normal speed, and for every 15 minutes that it is exposed to the elements it must make a Dexterity saving throw or take 3d6 lightning damage. For every hour of exposure, a hostile creature must also make a Constitution saving throw or gain a level of exhaustion.
