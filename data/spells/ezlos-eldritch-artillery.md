# Ezlo’s Eldritch Artillery

**description**: You cause tendrils of eldritch energy to barrage an area of your choosing. When you cast this spell, choose either line or zone. If you choose line, the spell’s area is a 5 feet wide and 80 feet long line originating from you. If you choose zone, the spell’s area is a 20-foot square originating from you. All creatures in the spell’s area must make a Dexterity saving throw. A target takes 4d10 force damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 3rd.
