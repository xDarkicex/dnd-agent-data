# Wieldskill

**description**: This spell is a minor magical trick that novice spellcasters use to appear more skilled than they actually are. You create one of the following magical effects: As part of the action used to cast this spell, you can make an attack with a weapon against one creature within the weapon's range, otherwise the spell fails. You can use your spellcasting attack modifier for the attack. You gain proficiency with one tool of your choice. This effect lasts for 10 minutes or until you use this spell again.
