# Bull of Falaris

**description**: 5th-level Evocation School: Evocation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 10 feet Components: V, S, M Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour You summon a mystical representation of the mythological Bull of Falaris in an empty space within range. The statue is a Large construct that otherwise uses the stat block of a Mammoth. It obeys any verbal commands that you issue to them (no action required by you). If you don't issue any commands to them, they defend themselves from hostile creatures, but otherwise take no actions. If your concentration is broken or if you end the spell, the Bull disappears at the end of its next turn.
