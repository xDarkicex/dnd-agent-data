# Greenshield

**description**: 2nd-level abjuration Can be cast by druids Casting Time : 1 minute R ange: Self Components: S, M (a twig and a blade of grass) Duration: Up to 12 hours This spell may only be cast in a natural setting with some plant life. When the spell is cast, the local flora grow and swell up to form a spherical shield with a 20-foot radius centered on the spellcaster. The branches and vines that make up the shield spread apart to allow you and your allies to press through, but they remain a twisted knot of dense undergrowth for anyone else. The shield looks like impassibly dense but otherwise natural undergrowth, although a creature may use an action to inspect it to determine if it is an unnatural creation. To determine this, it must succeed on an Intelligence (Investigation) check against your spell save DC. If you are in a location with abundant natural flora, such as a thick forest, the creature suffers disadvantage on its check. In addition, all magical auras of objects or creatures cannot be detected by anything outside the shield while those objects or creatures remain within the shield. The shield does not stop sounds or smells from entering or leaving.
