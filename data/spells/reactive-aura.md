# Reactive Aura

**description**: Swirling energy ripples out from you, forming a dimly glowing sphere in a 30-foot radius around you. Creatures of your choice in that radius when you use this power shed dim light in a 5-foot radius and have advantage on all saving throws until the effect ends. In addition, whenever one of these creatures is hit for the first time with a weapon or esper power attack, the aura erupts with a backlash of reactive energy. The attacker must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 6d10 radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. Subsequent attacks on the same creature does not provoke the energy backlash.
