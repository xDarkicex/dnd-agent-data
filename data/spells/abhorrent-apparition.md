# Abhorrent Apparition

**description**: 4th-Level Illusion Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: M (a gourd with a face carved on it) Duration: Instantaneous You imbue a terrifying visage onto a gourd and toss it ahead of you to a spot of your choosing within range. Each creature within 15 feet of that spot takes 6d8 psychic damage and becomes frightened of you for 1 minute; a successful Wisdom saving throw halves the damage and negates the fright. A creature frightened in this way repeats the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. At Higher Levels. If you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 4th.
