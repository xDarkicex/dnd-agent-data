# Obfuscate

**description**: You bend light around a creature you touch making them invisible to others. Anything the target is wearing or carrying is also invisible as long as it is on the target’s person. The effect ends for a target that attacks or uses a talent or technique. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 3 or higher, you can target one additional creature for each rank above 2.
