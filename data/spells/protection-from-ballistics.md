# Protection from Ballistics

**description**: 2nd-level abjuration (renaissance) Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute A shimmering shield of energy surrounds one willing creature you touch. For the duration, ranged attacks made by firearms have disadvantage against the target, and the target has resistance to any damage dealt by firearms.
