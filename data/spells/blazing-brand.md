# Blazing Brand

**description**: The blade of the sword you are holding erupts in fire. For the duration, you can use your spellcasting ability instead of Strength for the attack and damage rolls of melee attacks using that weapon. The weapon deals fire damage instead of its normal damage type. A flammable object hit by the sword ignites if it isn’t being worn or carried. The spell ends if you cast it again or if you let go of the weapon.
