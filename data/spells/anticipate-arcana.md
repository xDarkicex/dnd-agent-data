# Anticipate Arcana

**description**: 3rd-Level Divination Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when an enemy you can see casts a spell Range: Self Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous Your foresight gives you an instant to ready your defenses against a magical attack. When you cast anticipate arcana, you have advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects until the start of your next turn.
