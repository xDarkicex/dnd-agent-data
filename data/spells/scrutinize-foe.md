# Scrutinize Foe

**description**: 5th-level divination Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous You discern minute details concerning one creature you can see within range. You learn two of the following pieces of information of your choice about the target: its Armor Class, current hit points, condition immunities, damage immunities, damage resistances, damage vulnerabilities, and enchantments (which reveals any spells which are currently affecting it). The GM must share with you the requested information.
