# Incinerate

**description**: You direct an aerial strike in the form of a large plasma beam which fires down onto a location you specify. Each creature in a 10-foot radius, 40‑foot high cylinder centered on a point within range must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 4d6 fire damage and 4d6 radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 6 or higher, the fire damage or radiant damage (your choice) increases by 1d6 for each rank above 5.
