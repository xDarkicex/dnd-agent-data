# Hangover

**description**: 2nd-level enchantment Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S, M (a tiny vial of strong liquor) Duration: Instantaneous You emit a drunken aura that quickly washes away, leaving the feeling of an intense hangover. A creature you choose within range must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 3d8 psychic damage. The target has disadvantage on Constitution saving throws it makes to maintain concentration on a spell due to this damage. Additionally, on a failed save, the target is poisoned until the start of your next turn. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
