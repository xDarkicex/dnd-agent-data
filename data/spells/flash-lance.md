# Flash Lance

**description**: You create three lances of pure radiance and hurl them at targets within range. You can hurl them at one target or several. Make a ranged spell attack for each lance. On a hit, the target takes 1d8 radiant damage. If you hit an undead or fiend with with a lance, the creature must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or be blinded until the end of your next turn. At Higher Levels . When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you create one additional lance for each slot level above 1st.
