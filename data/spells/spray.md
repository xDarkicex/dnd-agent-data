# Spray

**description**: 1st-level conjuration Casting time: 1 action Range: self (30-foot line) Components: v, s Duration: instantaneous You conjure a powerful torrent of water in a line that is 30 feet long and 5 feet wide. Each creature in the line must make a strength saving throw. On a failed save, the creature suffers 3d6 bludgeoning damage, is pushed up to 15 feet away from you in a direction following the line, and knocked prone. On a successful save, the creature takes half the damage and isn’t pushed or knocked prone. At higher levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.
