# Bastion

**description**: An invisible barrier of force appears and protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack. This ability also grants the benefits of a Portable Shield Device.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 1
- School: Kinesis
- Expansion: 27051
- Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you are hit by an attack

**publisher**: Alligator Alley Entertainment

**book**: Esper Genesis Core Manual
