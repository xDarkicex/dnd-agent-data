# Oor Sap

**description**: You hold a stone in your hand and target a fey or elemental creature or any creature with a spellcasting ability and make a ranged spell attack against that target. On a successful hit, oor sap deals 2d8 necrotic damage and saps 2 Oor from its target, which is stored in the stone for later use. This Oor does not go away after a long rest and will remain in the stone indefinitely until it is used. 

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 4
- School: necromancy
- Components: V, S, M
- Damage Type: necrotic
- Casting Time: 1 action
