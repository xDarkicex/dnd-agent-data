# Doom of the Warped Skull

**description**: You raise the skull of a mutated bird of the wastes as you profess your target’s doom. Make a ranged spell attack against a living creature of your choice within range. On a hit, the target is poisoned and takes a –2 penalty to AC until the end of your next turn. The next saving throw the target makes before the end of your next turn is made with disadvantage.
