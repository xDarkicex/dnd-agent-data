# Peregrination

**description**: Cast this spell at the beginning of traveling with up to 6 target creatures you can see within range. Traveling at a normal or slow pace is considered light and non-strenous activity for the purposes of taking a short or long rest. If the travel is uninterrupted, creatures that would require sleep or downtime (from traits like an elf’s trance racial feature) are considered to have rested.
