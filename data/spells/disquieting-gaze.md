# Disquieting Gaze

**description**: 1st-Level Necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, S Duration: 1 minute Your eyes burn with scintillating motes of unholy crimson light. Until the spell ends, you have advantage on Charisma (Intimidation) checks made against creatures that can see you, and you have advantage on spell attack rolls that deal necrotic damage to creatures that can see your eyes.
