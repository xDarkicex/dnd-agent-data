# Hellfire Breather

**description**: 5th-level Conjuration Range: Self Components: V, M Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You can exhale a large burst of dark fire that is shaped like a 60 ft. long line with a variable width; make a Constitution check, then divide the result by 5 (rounding down); this is the width of the line. All creatures in the area must succeed on a DEX SAVE or take 6d8 fire damage and be knocked prone; a creature that makes its save takes half as much damage and is not prone. The damage from this spell ignores fire damage resistance; a creature that is immune to fire damage can’t benefit from that immunity against the effects of this spell, but they take half damage.
