# Doom of Deceleration

**description**: You stab yourself in the leg while you cast this spell, dealing 1d4 damage to yourself. Each creature within range, including you, must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a successful save, the creature’s speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of your next turn. On a failed save, the creature becomes restrained for 1 minute. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make another Wisdom saving throw. On a success, the target is no longer restrained.
