# Sand Blast

**description**: With the point of a finger, you release a short blast of sand and air in a 15-foot cone. Any creatures in the area of effect take 1d6 bludgeoning damage and must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw, or become blinded until the end of their next turn. The spell’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6) and 17th level (4d6).
