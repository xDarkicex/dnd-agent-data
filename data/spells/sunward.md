# Sunward

**description**: 3rd-level abjuration spell Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, S, M (a drop of aloe secretion and a 500 gp gem, which the spell consumes) Duration: 8 hours You choose up to four creatures you can see within range. Each target gains complete protection against sunlight. They automatically succeed on a saving throw or ability check caused by intense sunlight. The spell also negates the sunlight sensitivity trait.
