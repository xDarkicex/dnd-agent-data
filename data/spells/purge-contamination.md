# Purge Contamination

**description**: You apply alchemical fluids or holy water to a contaminated humanoid creature while reciting an exacting magical chant which expels eldritch contaminants from its body. When you finish casting the spell, all contamination levels and mutations are removed from the creature. It then gains 1 level of exhaustion for each contamination level removed with this spell.
