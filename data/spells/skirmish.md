# Skirmish

**description**: 3rd-level transmutation Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (a branch from a tree) Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes You enhance the mobility of one willing creature you touch, enabling them to move easily through enemy formations. For the duration, the target’s speed is doubled, it is unaffected by nonmagical difficult terrain, and it doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks.
