# Soothsayer's Shield

**description**: 2nd-level divination (bard, cleric, druid, ranger) Casting Time : 1 reaction, when you are hit by an attack Range : Self Components : V, S Duration : Instantaneous This spell can be cast when you are hit by an enemy’s attack. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +4 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack.
