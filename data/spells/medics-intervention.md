# Medic's Intervention

**description**: 1st-level abjuration Casting Time: 1 reaction Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous You weave a barrier of magic around your target, taking the brunt of the hit. As a reaction to a creature within range being hit by an attack, that creature gains 10 temporary hit points which last until the end of the current turn. Damage absorbed by these hit points reduces the total damage when determining threshold for Injuries. At Higher Levels. For every spell level above 1st, the target gains an additional 5 temporary hit points.
