# Blood Bond

**description**: You attempt to create a bond between you and a creature within range. The creature must make a Constitution saving throw vs your spell save DC. If it succeeds, the spell fails. If you have previously taken blood from the target creature through spell or ability, then the creature has disadvantage on its saving throw. If the targeted creature fails its saving throw its blood becomes magically connected to you up to 1 minute, or until either you or the creature dies. If you were already bonded to another creature, then that bond ends. During your turn, you may use your bonus action to deal 1d4 necrotic damage to the bonded creature. This spell has no effect on constructs, undead, and creatures that do not have blood. This damage increases by 1d4 when you reach 5th level (2d4), 11th level (3d4), and 17th level (4d4).
