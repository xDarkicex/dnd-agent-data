# Detect Heartbeat

**description**: 2nd-level divination Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self (30 feet) Components: V, S, M (a small hearing horn) Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Tamer, Wizard You magically enhance your hearing to detect the rhythmic thud-thud of hearts. For the duration, you automatically succeed on Wisdom (Perception) checks to hear heartbeats within 30 feet of you, though your magical hearing is blocked by 1 foot of stone, 1 inch of common metal, a thin sheet of lead, or 3 feet of wood or dirt. You know the direction (though not the distance) towards any heartbeat you hear. In addition, for the duration, you have disadvantage on saving throws against the deafened condition and have vulnerability to thunder damage.
