# Conjure Mantelet

**description**: 2nd-Level Abjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You summon a transparent, faintly luminous barrier with a single arrow slit. The magical mantelet provides you with three-quarters cover for the duration of the spell. If you move on your turn, the mantelet moves with you.
