# Shadow of Doubt

**description**: You assault the mind of a single creature within range, causing it to temporarily forget its techniques and spells. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, the creature is considered not to have any spells prepared and cannot use class features, traits, or abilities that have the recharge feature. At the end of each of its turns, the affected creature can make a Wisdom saving throw. If it succeeds, this effect ends for that target.
