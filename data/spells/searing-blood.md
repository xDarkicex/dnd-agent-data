# Searing Blood

**description**: You cause the blood in a single creature of your choice that you can see within range to boil and heat. The creature must make a Constitution saving throw or take 1d6 fire damage. If the creature is reduced to 0 hit points before the start of your next turn, creatures within 5 feet of it must make a Dexterity saving throw or take 1d6 fire damage. This spell’s initial and secondary damage increase by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
