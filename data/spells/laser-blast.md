# Laser Blast

**description**: You fire a high powered laser beam toward a creature of your choice within range. Make a ranged forging attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 4d6 radiant damage. In addition, the next attack roll made against this target before the end of your next turn has advantage. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 2 or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each rank above 1.
