# Emergency Ration

**description**: You create a pack of 10 emergency ration bars. A creature can use its action to eat one bar. Eating a ration bar restores 1 hit point, and the ration provides enough nourishment to sustain a creature for 24 hours. The bars lose their potency and disintegrate if they have not been consumed within 24 hours of the activation of this power.
