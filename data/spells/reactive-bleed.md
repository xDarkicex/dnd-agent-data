# Reactive Bleed

**description**: When another creature deals damage to you with a melee weapon attack, you can use your reaction to cast this spell. Your blood lunges towards that creature, and deals 2d10 piercing damage, then latches onto the wound the symbiotic blood has just created. For the next three rounds, the creature must make a constitution saving throw at the start of each of its turns. On a success, the spell ends. On a failure, the creature takes 1d6 necrotic damage. Upon the spell reaching completion after 3 turns, the creature takes 7d6 necrotic damage as the blood reaches their brainstem and constricts around it. You can use your own blood for this spell, or your Arcane Blood Mass. If you are not already bleeding, you can wound yourself to draw out blood, dealing 1d8 damage to your hit points. The secondary effect of this spell has no effect on constructs. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th or higher, increase the piercing damage by 1d10 for every spell slot level above 4th.
