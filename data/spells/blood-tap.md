# Blood Tap

**description**: The caster wraps his hands with necrotic energy and touches a target, ripping through its flesh and sucking up part of its life force. The targeted creature must make a constitution saving throw. On a failed save the target takes 1d8 necrotic damage, and you recover half of the damage dealt as temporary hit points which disappear at the end of your next turn. The damage of this spell increases by 1d8 when the caster reaches 5th level (2d8), 11th level (3d8) and 17th level (4d8).

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 0
- School: necromancy
- Components: V, S
- Damage Type: necrotic
- Casting Time: 1 action
