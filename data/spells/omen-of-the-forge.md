# Omen of the Forge

**description**: Damage the triggering weapon attack deals is treated as magical for the purposes of overcoming resistance and it deals an additional 1d10 damage of the same type normally dealt by the weapon.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 1
- School: evocation
- Components: V, S
- Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when a creature within range hits with a weapon attack
- data-RangeAoe: 30 feet

**publisher**: This Mind of Mine

**book**: Gathering Magic - Mystical Archives
