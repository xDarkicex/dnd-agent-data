# Immobilize

**description**: Even if this defies gravity, a creature or unattended item you touch becomes magically fixed in place. A creature must succeed on a Strength saving throw or become restrained for the duration. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make another Strength saving throw. On a success, the spell ends on the target. An item immobilized by the spell can hold up to 8,000 pounds of weight. More weight causes the item to fall. A creature can use an action to make a DC 30 Strength check, moving the fixed item up to 10 feet on a success. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell on an item using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the spell lasts until dispelled, without requiring your concentration.
