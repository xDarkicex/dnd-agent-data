# Open Red Portal

**description**: You must tap the power of a titanic or strong ley line to cast this spell (see the Ley Initiate feat in MHH). You open a new two-way Red Portal on the shadow road, leading to a precise location of your choosing in Midgard or on another plane of existence. If located in Midgard, this destination can be in the present day or up to 1,000 years in the past. The portal lasts for the duration. Deities and other planar rulers can prevent portals from opening in their presence or anywhere within their domains.
