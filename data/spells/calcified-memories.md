# Calcified Memories

**description**: Calcified Memories 2nd-level divination (ritual) (warlock, wizard) Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous You touch the bones of a deceased creature. When you do so, a ghostly grey illusion of the fallen creature appears above its corpse to reenact the last 6 seconds of its life. The illusion only shows the creature’s actions, such as reacting to an unseen monster or choking from poisonous gas, but does not show the monster or effect causing the behavior. This spell has no effect on undead creatures. Once a corpse's death has been revealed in this way, it can’t be shown again for 24 hours.
