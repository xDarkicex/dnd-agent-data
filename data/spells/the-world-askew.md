# The World Askew

**description**: The World Askew Requirements: Level 4 Casting Time: 1 action Range: special Duration: special Cost: 2 WP You notice minute details about your surroundings (or about people) that hint at earlier occurrences. A supernaturally gifted investigator, you can look at a place and rapidly determine what happened if any marks of an activity have been left at all. Minute details on a person such as a stain on their clothing, a bad shave or shoddily tied shoelaces can lead you to accurately guess baffling details about them. Understanding the interactions between Possibility and Permanence, you can often envision the actions that made the marks and you can make eeriely accurate guesses as to who or what executed those actions. Sometimes it is as if an entire scene unfolds before your mind’s eye like a theatre play. You have advantage when you make an Intelligence (Investigation) check using The World Askew, and if you succeed the GM gives you not only the required information, but one further piece of information (which would probably be impossible to conclude by any regular investigator using the evidence at hand). On a critical success, an entire scene plays out in your head – although the identities of people involved in the goings-on are probably still not always obvious. The GM should adjust the DC according to circumstances, like how long ago the investigated actions happened, how small or large the observed area is, or any other factors relevant to the situation. At his discretion, the GM might want to prepare a number of levels of information to discover, depending on which DC has been beaten by the check; or he can ask for further checks (still at advantage) to discover more (though without further WP cost, and only as long as the checks are successful), effectively leading the philosopher through the chain of events.
