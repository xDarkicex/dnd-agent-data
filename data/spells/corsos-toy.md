# Corso’s Toy

**description**: A single nonmagical object that is not being worn or carried within range comes to life at your command. You can’t animate an object larger than Medium. The object animates and becomes a creature under your control until the spell ends or until reduced to 0 hit points. This creature uses the Corso’s Toy stat block . The creature is an ally to you and your companions. In combat, the creature shares your initiative count, but it takes its turn immediately after yours. It obeys your verbal commands (requiring a bonus action from you) as long as it is still within range. If you don’t issue any, it takes the Dodge action and uses its move to avoid danger.
