# Survival Suit

**description**: This power creates an individual environmental force field for you and of up to eight willing creatures you can see within range. Each creature gains the ability to breathe in most atmospheric conditions, including toxic, corrosive, or underwater environments, as well as in areas with no air, such as a vacuum. Affected creatures also retain their normal mode of respiration and their movement remains restricted by their current environment. In addition, affected creatures are immune to the effects of inhaled poisons and diseases and other harmful gases generated through mundane effects or esper powers. Affected creatures also don’t have to make Constitution saving throws due to being in environments with extreme heat, cold, or atmospheric pressure.
