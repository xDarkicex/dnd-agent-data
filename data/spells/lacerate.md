# Lacerate

**description**: 1st-level evocation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S, M (a slashing weapon) Duration: Instantaneous You slash the air, creating a blade of force which slashes at your target. Make a melee spell attack roll against a creature in range. On a hit, that creature takes 2d8 slashing damage, and must make a Constitution saving throw or gain a special Injury of Setback I severity, "Laceration". Each time the Injured creature takes an action, it loses 1d6 hit points. This Injury is automatically removed at the end of a short rest, or when the creature regains hit points via magic. At Higher Levels. When this spell is cast using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the target takes an additional 1d8 points of damage per spell level beyond 1st. Additionally, if the spell slot used is 4th level or higher, the Injury's severity increases to Dangerous II, is only removed at the end of a short rest, and the hit point loss increases to 1d8.
