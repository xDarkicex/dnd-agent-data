# Shadow Shackles

**description**: Your magic-infused shadow confines the target in shackles. The creature must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, your shadow anchors the target to the spot, reducing its speed to 0. While held in place by the shackles, the target takes 3d6 cold damage at the start of each of its turns. Shadow shackles have an AC of 18, a damage threshold of 10 and 20 hit points. If the shackles are destroyed, the spell ends, and you lose your shadow until you complete a short rest.
