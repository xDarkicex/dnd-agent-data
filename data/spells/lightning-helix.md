# Lightning Helix

**description**: You cause a pair of lightning bolts to fly at a target creature within range, creating a feedback loop that causes the target damage that heals you. Choose one creature that you can see within range to make a Dexterity saving throw. The target takes 4d6 lightning damage on a failed saving throw and half as much on a successful one. You regain hit points equal to half the damage dealt to the target. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 2nd.
