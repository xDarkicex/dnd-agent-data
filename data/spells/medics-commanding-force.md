# Medic’s Commanding Force

**description**: 3rd-level abjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, S, M (a pinch of herbs, a drop of a tonic or tincture, or other minor medicine) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute For the duration of the spell, all allies ignore the effects of a single Injury they suffer from while within 30’ of the caster. Allies choose the Injury they wish to ignore when the spell first takes effect on them. At Higher Levels. When cast using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the radius is 60' instead.
