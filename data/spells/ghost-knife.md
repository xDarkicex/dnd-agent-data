# Ghost Knife

**description**: A dagger you are holding is imbued with ghostly power. For the duration, you can use your spellcasting ability instead of Strength or Dexterity for the attack and damage rolls of weapon attacks using the dagger and it deals psychic damage. Additionally, you can make ranged weapon attacks with the dagger for the duration if it has the thrown property without throwing the weapon. The spell ends if you cast it again or if you let go of the weapon.
