# Speak True

**description**: Divination cantrip Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: Self Components: V, S Duration: 1 minute When you cast this spell, your honest intentions become clearer to those around you. When a creature makes a Wisdom (Insight) ability check to determine whether or not you are telling the truth for the duration, it adds a bonus equal to your spellcasting ability modifier to its roll.
