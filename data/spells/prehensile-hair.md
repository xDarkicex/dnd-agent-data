# Prehensile Hair

**description**: 1st-level transmutation (ritual) Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, S, M (a braided rope) Duration: 1 hour You grow unduly long and tough hair (even from your eyebrows) which you can manipulate at will. You can use your hair to perform simple tasks within 10 feet of you, such as manipulating an object, opening an unlocked door or container, stowing or retrieving an item from an open container, or pouring the contents out of a vial. You can cast spells with a range of touch using your hair, out to a range of 10 feet. Your hair returns to its normal length when this spell ends.
