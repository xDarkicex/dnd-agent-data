# Omen of the Hunt

**description**: You grant one creature within range a burst of energy when they expend magical energy and the spell is avoided. If the triggering target creature misses with the triggering spell’s spell attack roll or if the triggering spell is saved against and does not generate an effect on a failed saving throw, the caster of the spell regains the spell slot expended on the spell.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 1
- School: transmutation
- Components: V, S
- Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when a creature within range casts a spell of 2nd level or lower that targets one creature
- data-RangeAoe: 30 feet

**publisher**: This Mind of Mine

**book**: Gathering Magic - Mystical Archives
