# Tongue Tied

**description**: 5th-Level Enchantment Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: Concentration up to 1 minute You make a choking motion while pointing at a target, which must make a successful Wisdom saving throw or become unable to communicate verbally. The target’s speech becomes garbled, and it has disadvantage on Charisma checks that require speech. The creature can cast a spell that has a verbal component only by making a successful Constitution check against your spell save DC. On a failed check, the creature’s action is used but the spell slot isn’t expended.
