# Pulsing Strike

**description**: The next time you hit a creature with a weapon attack during this power’s duration, the weapon attaches a device onto the target that delivers powerful electrical pulses. At the start of each of its turns until the effect ends, the target must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, it takes 1d6 lightning damage. On a successful save, the effect ends. If the target or a creature within 5 feet of it uses an action to remove the device, the effect ends.
