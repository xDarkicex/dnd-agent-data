# Tentacle Lash

**description**: One of your arms elongates into a long, wet, suckered tentacle. When you cast this spell, and as an action on each of your turns, you can make a melee spell attack against a creature within 30 feet of you. On a hit, the target takes 5d8 bludgeoning damage and it must make a Strength saving throw if it is Huge or smaller. On a failure, you can choose one of the following effects: The target is knocked prone . The target is shoved 5 feet in a direction of your choice. You disarm the target; you take one item the target is holding and return it to your person. The tentacle also serves as an appendage that can be used like a grappling hook or rope (GM’s discretion). At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 4th.
