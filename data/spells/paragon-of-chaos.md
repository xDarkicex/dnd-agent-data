# Paragon Of Chaos

**description**: You become a humanoid-shaped swirling mass of color and sound. You gain resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage, and immunity to poison and psychic damage. You are also immune to the following conditions: exhaustion, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, and unconscious. Finally, you gain truesight to 30 feet and can teleport 30 feet as a move. Each round as a bonus action, you can invoke a chaos magic surge, choosing yourself or another creature you can see within 60 feet as the caster for resolving the effect. You must choose the target before rolling for the chaos magic surge. The DC of any required saving throw is determined as if you were the caster.
