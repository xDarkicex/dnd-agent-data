# Executioner's Mark

**description**: 1st-level divination Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour You choose a target you can see within range and single it out, marking it for death. Until the spell ends, the number of Injuries required for the target to become Imperiled is reduced by 1 (minimum 1). Additionally, each time you hit the creature with an attack, the creature's Injury thresholds are reduced by 5 (to a minimum of 5) until the end of the turn. If the target dies before the spell ends, you can use your bonus action to apply the effect to a new target you can see within range.
