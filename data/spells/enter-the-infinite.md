# Enter the Infinite

**description**: You draw upon yourself to create a nexus of ideas beyond yourself. Upon casting this spell, you expend all of your hit dice. You can choose expended spell slots to recover. The spell slots can have a combined level that is equal to or less than the number of hit dice you expended as a cost of this spell, and none of the slots can be 9th level.
