# Bolster

**description**: You inject a boosting stim into your allies, providing a temporary increase in toughness and resilience. Choose up to three creatures within range. Each target’s hit point maximum and current hit points increase by 5 for the duration. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 3 or higher, a target’s hit points increase by an additional 5 for each rank above 2.
