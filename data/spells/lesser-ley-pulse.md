# Lesser Ley Pulse

**description**: You set up ley energy vibrations in a 10-foot cube within range, and name one type of damage. Each creature in the area must make a successful Wisdom saving throw or lose resistance to the chosen damage type for the duration of the spell. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a 7th-level spell slot, choose two damage types instead of one.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Save: Wisdom
- Level: 5
- School: Transmutation (Ley Line)
- Components: V S
- Casting Time: 1 action
