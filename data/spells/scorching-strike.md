# Scorching Strike

**description**: The next time you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack during this power’s duration, the weapon erupts with a fiery explosion, dealing an extra 1d6 fire damage to the target and causes the target to burst into flames. At the start of each of its turns until the effect ends, the target must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, it takes 1d6 fire damage. On a successful save, the effect ends. If the target or a creature within 5 feet of it uses an action to put out the flames, or if some other effect douses the flames (such as the target being submerged in water), the effect ends. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 2 or higher the initial extra damage dealt by the attack increases by 1d6 for each rank above 1.
