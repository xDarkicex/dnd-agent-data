# Screaming Ray

**description**: You create a ray of psychic energy to attack your enemies. Make a ranged spell attack against a creature. On a hit, the target takes 1d4 psychic damage and is deafened until the end of your next turn. If the target succeeds on a Constitution saving throw, it is not deafened. At Higher Levels . When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you create one additional ray for each slot level above 1st. You can direct the rays at one target or several.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 1
- School: evocation
- Components: V, S
- Damage Type: psychic
- Casting Time: 1 action
