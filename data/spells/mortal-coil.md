# Mortal Coil

**description**: You create a lash of necrotic energy that strikes one create within range. The target must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be pushed up to 10 feet in a straight line away from you. If this movement is blocked by another creature or it ends the movement within 5 feet of another creature, both creatures take 1d6 necrotic damage. Otherwise, the target takes 1d6 necrotic damage. This spell’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
