# Psychokinesis

**description**: Conjuration Cantrip Range: 30 ft. Components: V, M Duration: Instantaneous Make a ranged spell attack against a creature you can see within range. On a hit the creature takes 1d6 psychic damage. Then, if the creature is Large or smaller, it is teleported to an empty spot on the ground up to 10 ft. from its original position. This spell’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level ( 2d6 ), 11th level ( 3d6 ), and 17th level ( 4d6 ).
