# Greater Amplify Coast

**description**: 9th-level lair magic (transmutation) Casting Time: 2 weeks (14 days) Radius: 4 miles Components: V, S, M (A rare material or materials found within the local environment worth at least 6000 gold pieces, which the spell consumes) Duration: 1 year This spell functions like lesser amplify coast, except that the ocean also fights to drown unprotected creatures beneath its waves. Whenever a creature enters the water for the first time or starts its turn there, it must make a Strength saving throw. After three failed saves, the creature begins to drown. After three successful saves, the creature becomes immune to the ocean’s pull. These saving throws are made with advantage if the creature has a floatation device (such as a suitable piece of driftwood). Additionally, ability checks made to swim within the area are made at disadvantage.
