# Power Word Reveal

**description**: 4th-level divination Casting time: 1 action Range: 120 feet Components: v Duration: 1 round You utter a word of power and every creature within range perceives things as they actually are. Each creature within 120 feet of you has truesight in the spell’s area until the start of your next turn. At higher levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the duration increases by 1 round for each slot level above 4th.
