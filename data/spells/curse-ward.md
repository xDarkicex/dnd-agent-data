# Curse Ward

**description**: 2nd-level abjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S Duration: 1 hour You reach out your hand and touch a willing creature within your reach, raising a smoke-like barrier around it. For the duration, the target has resistance to necrotic damage and can’t be cursed, possessed, or targeted by a hex. Also, its hit point maximum can’t be reduced. If the target is already affected by one of these effects, the effect is suspended until the spell ends.
