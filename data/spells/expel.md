# Expel

**description**: You cause one creature summoned by a spell within range to disappear. Make an ability check using your spellcasting ability. The DC equals 10 + the summoned creature’s CR (rounded up). On a successful check, the summoned creature disappears. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional creature summoned by a spell for each slot level above 2nd.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 2
- School: conjuration
- Components: V, S
- Casting Time: 1 action
- data-RangeAoe: 60 feet
