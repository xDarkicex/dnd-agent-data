# Guardian's Intervention

**description**: 3rd-level transmutation Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you see an ally become Injured Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous Seeing your ally inflicted with a terrible wound, you call out a prayer of safety, taking their burden for your own. Choose one allied creature you can see within range that has just suffered an Injury inflicted by an enemy creature. That Injury is removed and is transferred to you (the ally suffers no Lasting Effects). If that Injury was a core Injury and you already have a core Injury of that severity, you gain one of the nearest available severity (choosing the lower severity if there is a tie). For example, if it was of Dangerous II severity, and you have core Injuries of Dangerous II and Handicap III severity, you would gain a Setback I Injury. If the Injury was a special Injury and you already have the Injury your ally would suffer, you can’t prevent the Injury unless you can gain that Injury multiple times.
