# Caustic Remark

**description**: Caustic Remark Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Duration: instantaneous Cost: – You make a devastatingly snarky remark the way only you can. If the target can hear and understand you, it must succeed on an Intelligence saving throw or take 1d4 psychic damage and also suffer a –2 to its Armor Class until the end of your next turn. This feature‘s psychic damage increases by 1d4 when you reach 5th level (2d4), 11th level (3d4), and 17th level (4d4).
