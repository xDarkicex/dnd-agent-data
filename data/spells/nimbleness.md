# Nimbleness

**description**: Transmutation Cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You touch a willing subject. Once before the spell ends, the target can roll a d6 and add the result to one Dexterity check of its choice. It can roll the die before or after making the check. The spell then ends.
