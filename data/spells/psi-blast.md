# Psi Blast

**description**: You launch a streaming coil of psychic energy at a target you can see. Make a ranged channeling attack. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 psychic damage. The target also loses any benefit from cover against your next attack roll against it. This effect lasts until either the end of your next turn or whenever you hit another target with this power, whichever comes first. This power’s damage increases by 1d10 when you reach 5th level (2d10), 11th level (3d10), and 17th level (4d10).
