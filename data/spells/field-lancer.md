# Field Lancer

**description**: You place a pack of micro-drones in a 20-foot radius centered on a point within range. The drones each fire a cloud of shrapnel bursts at anything that enters the area. The area becomes difficult terrain for the duration. When a creature moves into or within the area, it takes 2d4 piercing damage for every 5 feet it travels. In addition, the tiny micro-drones camouflage themselves into the terrain. Any creature that can’t see the area at the time the power is activated must make a Wisdom (Perception) check against your esper power save DC to recognize the terrain as hazardous before entering it.
