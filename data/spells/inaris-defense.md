# Inari's Defense

**description**: You create an invisible barrier of magical force that protects your ally. The target creature gains a +5 bonus to AC against the triggering attack.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 1
- School: abjuration
- Components: V, S
- Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when a creature within 5 feet of you is hit by an attack
- data-RangeAoe: 5 feet

**publisher**: This Mind of Mine

**book**: Gathering Magic - Mystical Archives
