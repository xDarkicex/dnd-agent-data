# Gloaming

**description**: 1st-level evocation Casting time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: v, m (a piece of smoked glass) Duration: concentration, up to 1 minute Magical gloom spreads from a point you choose within range to fill a 50-foot-radius sphere for the duration. The fading twilight spreads around corners. A creature with darkvision can see through this darkness, and nonmagical light can illuminate it. Ranged attacks made through the gloaming have disadvantage. If the point you choose is an object being worn or carried, the effect emanates from the object and moves with it. Completely covering the source of the gloaming with an opaque object, such as a bowl or a helm, blocks the darkness. If any of this spell’s area overlaps with an area of magical light created by a spell of 1st level or lower, that effect is dispelled.
