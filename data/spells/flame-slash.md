# Flame Slash

**description**: You slash your hand forward and a blade of fire trails after, striking your foe. Make a melee spell attack against a single creature within range. On a hit, the target takes 1d12 fire damage. This spell’s damage increases by 1d12 when you reach 5th level (2d12), 11th level (3d12), and 17th level (4d12).
