# Corso’s Transposition

**description**: When you or an ally are faced with sudden danger you can reach out and swap places with someone else. When you cast this spell, choose a willing allied creature within range. They magically teleport to swap places with you. The target of the spell then becomes the target of the triggering attack, (if the triggering attack was against you), or you become the target of the attack (if the triggering attack was against an ally).

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 2
- School: abjuration
- Components: V, S
- Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you or an ally you can see within range is targeted by an attack
- data-RangeAoe: 30 feet

**publisher**: This Mind of Mine

**book**: Gathering Magic - Mystical Archives
