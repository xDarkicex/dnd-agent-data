# Withering Grip

**description**: You unleash a bolt of antimatter from your hands at a target within range. Make a ranged channeling attack. On a hit, the target takes 4d8 necrotic damage and must make a successful Wisdom saving throw or become frightened of you until the start of your next turn. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 4 or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each rank above 3.
