# Warp Storm

**description**: Bursts of deep space vacuum explode outward from four different points you can see within range. Each creature in a 40-foot-radius sphere centered on each point you choose must make a Dexterity saving throw. The sphere spreads around corners. A creature takes 20d6 force damage and 20d6 cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature in the area of more than one burst is affected only once. The bursts also cause damage to structures and objects in the area that aren’t being worn or carried.
