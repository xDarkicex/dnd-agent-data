# Chasm of Despair

**description**: Chasm of Despair Requirements: Level 9, Stutters of Confusion Casting Time: reaction Range: 60 feet Duration: instantaneous Cost: 3 WP, invested until the target makes a successful saving throw Over the course of at least a short, possibly casual conversation, the philosopher can sow the seeds of blackest despair in the victim.This feature works like Stutters of Confusion, but instead of a penalty on rolls the victim gains 1 Dismay per day until a successful Wisdom saving throw is made (roll once per day). Dismay accumulated up to this point remains and can be dealt with as normal. Initial saving throw is Wisdom.
