# Sharp Rain

**description**: A deluge of sharp rain falls from the air in a location you specify. Each creature in a 10-foot-radius, 40-foot-high cylinder centered on a point within range must make a Dexterity saving throw. Creatures that are Large or larger make this save with disadvantage if the creature is completely within the spell’s area. A creature takes 3d12 slashing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d12 for each slot level above 2nd.
