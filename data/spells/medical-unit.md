# Medical Unit

**description**: You forge a tiny medical drone that moves with you for the duration. You can use your bonus action to administer treatment to one creature within a 30-foot radius around you (including you). The target creature is healed for 2d6 hit points.

**properties**:

- Level: 3
- School: Technique

- Casting Time: 1 action
