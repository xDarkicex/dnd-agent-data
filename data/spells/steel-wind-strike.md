# Steel Wind Strike

**description**: Level 5 Conjuration (Ranger, Wizard) Casting Time: Action Range: 30 feet Components: S, M (a Melee weapon worth 1+ SP) Duration: Instantaneous You flourish the weapon used in the casting and then vanish to strike like the wind. Choose up to five creatures you can see within range. Make a melee spell attack against each target . On a hit, a target takes 6d10 Force damage . You then teleport to an unoccupied space you can see within 5 feet of one of the targets.
