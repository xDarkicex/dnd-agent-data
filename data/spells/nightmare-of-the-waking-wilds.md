# Nightmare of the Waking Wilds

**description**: 9th-level lair magic (transmutation) Casting Time: 2 weeks Radius: 4 miles Components: V, S, M (A single unprocessed jet stone worth at least 3000 gold pieces, which the spell consumes). Duration: 2 years Wilderness within the radius rapidly overgrows with corruption and a hazy fog rolls in, transforming the landscape into a waking nightmare. Evil plant creatures guard the area, and at night howls and the screams of animals fill the air, making rest impossible. Any creature traveling through the area must make a Wisdom saving throw each hour or lose all its memories of traveling since entering the area. Additionally, any creature that fails its saving throw becomes frightened of the wilderness for 1 hour. While frightened in this way, it can still move in any direction. Creatures attempting to fly over the area (but within the spell’s radius) must make a Dexterity saving throw every minute or be pulled down by long, grasping vines or roots.
