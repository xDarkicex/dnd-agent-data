# Directed Strike

**description**: You create a virtual tactical link you share with a nearby allies in a 30-foot radius area. When you activate this power and as a bonus action on subsequent turns for the duration, you can choose one friendly creature within the area that you can see and that can see you. That creature can use its reaction to make one weapon attack against a target within the same area. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 7 or higher, you can choose one additional creature and the area of effect increases by 10 feet for each rank above 6.
