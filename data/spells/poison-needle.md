# Poison Needle

**description**: You launch a conjured dart coated with lethal poison at a creature you can see within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 poison damage. If the poison damage reduces the target to 0 hit points, the target is stable but poisoned for 1 hour, even after regaining hit points, and is paralyzed while poisoned in this way. This spell's damage increases by 1d10 when you reach 5th level (2d10), 11th level (3d10), and 17th level (4d10).
