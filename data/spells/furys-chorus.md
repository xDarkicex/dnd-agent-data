# Fury's Chorus

**description**: You call a group of people to a heightened emotional state, driving them to anger and mistrust. Each Humanoid in a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on a point you choose within range must make a Charisma saving throw. On a failed save, a creature must use its reaction (if available) to make an opportunity attack whenever another creature (including an ally) moves out of its reach. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success. The calm emotions spell suppresses the effects of this spell.
