# Seek the Stream

**description**: Divination cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, M (an empty waterskin) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour You sense the nearest source of running water (a stream, a brook, or a small river) within 10 miles of you. You also learn the direction and distance of this source of running water.
