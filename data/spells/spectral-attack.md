# Spectral Attack

**description**: 5th-level Necromancy Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: Target, 120ft Components: V, M (a lock of hair from a dead person) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You invoke spectral ghosts from another plane; the ghosts are invisible and intangible and can’t be interacted with. When you cast the spell, you choose a creature you can see within range; the creature is tormented by the ghosts and must make an INT SAVE or take 3d10 psychic damage and become Frightened by you or another creature until the end of its next turn; on a save, they take half damage and are not Frightened. For the duration, you can use a bonus action on your turn to target the same or a different creature. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 5th.
