# Anthem of Rakdos

**description**: Volatile energy radiates from you in an aura with a 30-foot radius. Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. Each nonhostile creature in the aura (including you) has a +2 bonus to melee weapon attack rolls. Additionally, whenever a nonhostile creature in the aura (including you) hits with a melee weapon attack, the creature can roll one of the weapon’s damage dice one additional time and add it to the damage roll.
