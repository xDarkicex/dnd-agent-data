# Delay

**description**: 2nd-level transmutation (chronomancy) Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S, M (an octagonal sign) Duration: Instantaneous You briefly slow time for a creature of your choice that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be moved to last place in the initiative order from the beginning of the next round onwards.
