# Doom of the Dead

**description**: Lines of dark power encircle a creature that you can see within range. The creature must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, up to five humanoid corpses within range are each raised as a hostile skeleton or zombie that cannot be controlled. The undead creatures attack any living creature they perceive, which can include you. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a 6th-level spell slot, the first two undead to animate are ghouls rather than skeletons or zombies. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the first undead creature to animate is a wight , the second is a ghast , and the remaining ones are ghouls .
