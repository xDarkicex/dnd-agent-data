# Hobble

**description**: Evocation Cantrip Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S, M (a broken rabbit’s foot) Duration: Instantaneous You create an ethereal trap in the space of a creature you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or its Speed is halved until the end of its next turn.
