# Hod's Gift

**description**: The target gains blindsight to a range of 60 feet. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the duration is increased by 1 hour for every slot above 5th level.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 5
- School: Transmutation (Rune)
- Components: V S M
- Casting Time: 1 round

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Midgard Heroes Handbook
