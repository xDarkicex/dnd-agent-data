# Regrowth

**description**: A creature of your choice that you can see within range can spend and roll one of its unspent Hit Dice and regain a number of hit points equal to the roll plus your spellcasting ability modifier. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the number of Hit Dice that can be spent and added to the healing roll increases by one for each slot above 1st.

**properties**:

- Level: 1
