# Echoes of Doom

**description**: 9th-level divination Casting Time : 1 action Range : 120 feet Components : V, S, M (a piece of black onyx) Duration : 1 minute Classes : Cleric, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard You tap into the fabric of fate and reveal a harrowing vision of the world's end to the target, delivered by the dreaded Ebon Serpent. The target must make a Charisma saving throw. On a failed save, the target takes 35 (10d6) psychic damage and becomes frightened for the spell’s duration. A target that fails its saving throw by 5 or more takes 70 (20d6) psychic damage and becomes paralyzed with fear for the duration of the spell. It also gains an indefinite madness with the flaw: “I find it hard to care about anything that goes on around me”, which lasts until cured. On a successful save, the target becomes frightened for the spell’s duration.
