# Vision Slip

**description**: 2nd-level enchantment Casting time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: v, s Duration: instantaneous You cause the vision of up to three creatures you can see within range to blur, slide, and churn in a nauseating fashion. Each target must succeed on a constitution saving throw or be blinded until the end of its next turn. A creature that fails the saving throw by 5 or more is also poisoned for 1 minute. At higher levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd.
