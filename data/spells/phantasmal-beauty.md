# Phantasmal Beauty

**description**: 3rd-level illusion Casting Time: 1 action Range: 120 feet Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You tap into the dreams of a creature you can see within range and create an illusory manifestation of its deepest desires, visible only to that creature. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the target becomes incapacitated and unable to move for the duration. In addition, it has disadvantage on all Wisdom (Perception) checks it makes. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make another Wisdom saving throw. On a success, the spell ends on the target.
