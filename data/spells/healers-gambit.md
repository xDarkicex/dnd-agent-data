# Healer's Gambit

**description**: 2nd-level conjuration Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: Self (special) Components: V, S, M (a pearl or spherical gem worth at least 50 gp) Duration: Concentration, up to 3 rounds You conjure an orb of healing energy which threatens to overwhelm your ability to control it, maintained by your need for aid. When you first cast this spell, nothing happens. On every consecutive round while you concentrate and are in combat, the spell charges and gains power. You can choose to release the spell on a subsequent round as an action. When released, up to four allies within 10 feet regain 2d8 + your spellcasting ability modifier hit points. For each round of concentration beyond the first, the spell restores an additional 1d8 hit points, and the radius increases by 5 feet. If you do not release the spell before the end of your turn on the final round, the spell automatically releases healing energy. If the concentration on the spell is broken, the spell explodes, dealing the accrued dice in force damage to you and each creature within 5 feet of you. Damage dealt in this way cannot be reduced. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the base hit points restored by the spell increases by 1d8 per level beyond 2nd.
