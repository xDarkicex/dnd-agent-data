# Lesser Amplify Swamp

**description**: 5th-level lair magic (transmutation) Casting Time: 2 weeks (14 days) Radius: 2 miles Components: V, S, M (A rare material or materials found within the local environment worth at least 5000 gold pieces, which the spell consumes) Duration: 1 year This spell amplifies the naturally dangerous effects of the surrounding swamp (or other similar environment), making it difficult for all who travel within it. When this spell is cast, the caster may choose up to eight creatures, including themself, who may ignore the effects of the spell. Alternatively, the caster may designate a passphrase that must be known or a symbol that must be worn. Harmful effects of the spell do not affect native creatures unless their Intelligence score is greater than 3. The temperature and humidity of the swamp is amplified, creating an oppressive atmosphere for creatures not protected from this spell. The mud sticks to their feet, reducing their speed by half. Additionally, the swamp rearranges itself at random, causing travelers to easily become lost. Constant clouds and fog prevent normal methods of determining direction, and even compasses become confused. Will O’ the Wisps readily inhabit the area. For every 30 minutes of travel, a group’s leader must make a Wisdom (Survival) check against the DC of the spell. On a failed check, the group finds themselves having made no progress.
