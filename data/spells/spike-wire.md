# Spike Wire

**description**: You hurl a magical dart at a single creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, you deal 1d6 lightning damage to the target. Until the start of your next turn, any time the hit target would take lightning damage or damage from a weapon attack, the target takes an additional 1d6 lightning damage. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the initial lightning damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.
