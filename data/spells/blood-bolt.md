# Blood Bolt

**description**: A bolt of blood is fired at a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack roll. On a hit, the target suffers 1d12 Necrotic damage. The blood is taken as a toll from the user of this cantrip, causing 1 damage to the caster. The damage dice is increased by 1d12 and the damage taken is increased by 1 at 5th (2d12, 2 damage taken), 11th (3d12, 3 damage taken) and 17th (4d12, 4 damage taken).
