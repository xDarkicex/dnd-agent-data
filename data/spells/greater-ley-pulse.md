# Greater Ley Pulse

**description**: You set up ley energy vibrations in a 20-foot cube within range, and name one type of damage. Each creature in the area must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or lose immunity to the chosen damage type for the duration. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a 9th-level spell slot, choose two damage types instead of one.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Save: Wisdom
- Level: 7
- School: Transmutation (Ley Line)
- Components: V S
- Casting Time: 1 action

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Midgard Heroes Handbook
