# Greater Ward

**description**: 3rd-level abjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: S, M (the carapace of either a spider or scorpion) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger, Wizard A magical barrier envelops up to 4 willing creatures you can see within range including yourself. Attacks made against creatures protected by this spell have disadvantage for the duration. When the ward is ended in any way, including a failed Concentration check, a wave of force bursts out from each warded character. Any creature that was not warded within 5 feet of a warded creature makes a Dexterity saving throw, taking 2d10 force damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 3rd.
