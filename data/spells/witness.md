# Witness

**description**: Witness Requirements: Level 9 Casting Time: 1 action Range: touch (the Likeness) Duration: special Cost: 1 WP per minute for 1 sense in your mind’s eye, 1 WP for each additional sense for the entire duration, 1 WP to let others witness with you for the entire duration. By concentrating on a Likeness you have created you can observe the depicted place or person, allowing you to spy on it. The cost depends on the number of senses involved and whether you observe the target in your mind’s eye only or make its actions visible for others observing the likeness with you.
