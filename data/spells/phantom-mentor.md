# Phantom Mentor

**description**: Phantom Mentor Requirements: Level 7 Casting Time: 10 minute ritual Range: touch (the Likeness) Duration: special Cost: 2 WP per Ability Contest Influence a person you have faithfully depicted in a Likeness through a diligent process of influence through conversation. Suggestions, threats or observations you make towards the depiction of your target (the avatar) will gradually have an effect on the real person. The target will be unable to explain how the changes in his or her thinking or emotional state came about, but will somehow rationalize the change. The artist and the target engage in one single Charisma (Persuasion) contest per day, and each time the artist wins the target’s mind is changed in a small way determined by the GM.
