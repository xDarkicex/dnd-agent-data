# Recall

**description**: 2nd-level conjuration (chronomancy) Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: Self Components: V, S Duration: 1 round Record where you are when you cast this spell. Until the end of your next turn, you can use your reaction to teleport back to that location, or to the nearest unoccupied space. If you use this reaction in response to an attack, spell, magical effect, or any other damaging effect, resolve the triggering effect before teleporting.
