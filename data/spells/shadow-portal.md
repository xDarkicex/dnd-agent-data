# Shadow Portal

**description**: 3rd-level conjuration Casting time: 1 action Range: 200 feet Components: v, m (a silver door latch worth at least 50 gp, which the spell consumes) Duration: 1 minute You create a door in the nearest wall, cliff, tree, or similar structure. It is immediately bound to a similar door up to 200 feet away. The near door must be a place within 10 feet that you can see while the distant door can be one that you can visualize or that you can describe by stating distance and direction, such as 100 feet straight down or 50 feet to the northwest and upward at a 45-degree angle. Until the spell ends, any creature can take actions normally to open and pass through the near portal, which immediately teleports it to the distant portal. The spell ends after the duration or when a creature uses a bonus action to close the distant portal’s door.
