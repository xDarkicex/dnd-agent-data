# Risen

**description**: Risen 4th-level transmutation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S, M (the figurehead) Duration: 1 minute One ship’s figurehead that you touch becomes alive and gains all of the abilities and statistics of an iron golem (but is unable to move). The risen figurehead is considered friendly to you and your allies and follows your commands on your initiative turn (defending itself if no explicit instructions are provided). If it dies, the figurehead returns to its normal form.
