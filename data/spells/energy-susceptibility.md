# Energy Susceptibility

**description**: Choose one damage type from acid, cold, fire, lightning, and poison. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or be affected by this power for the duration. At the start of each turn, when the affected target first takes damage of the chosen type, the target takes an additional 2d6 damage of that type. In addition, the target also loses resistance (if any) to that damage type until the technique’s effect ends. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 5 or higher, you can target one additional creature for each rank above 4. The creatures must be within 30 feet of each other when you target them.
