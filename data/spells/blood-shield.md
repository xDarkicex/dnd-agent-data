# Blood Shield

**description**: Using your own blood, you create a shield of blood surrounding you or a target. Until the start of your next turn, the target of this spell gains a +5 bonus to AC (including against the triggering attack if done as a reaction), you take no damage from Blood Mage Spells, and only take half Necrotic damage from attacks that deal it. The HP cost of this spell is determined by the size of the target you are shielding. Tiny = 1d4 Small = 1d6 Medium = 1d8 Large = 1d10 Huge = 1d12 Gargantuan = 1d20

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 1
- School: abjuration
- Components: V, S, M
- Casting Time: 1 Action/1 Reaction, which you take when you are targeted by an attack or blood mage spell
- data-RangeAoe: 30 ft
