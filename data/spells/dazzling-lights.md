# Dazzling Lights

**description**: You conjure spinning blue lights around the head of a single creature within range. The creature must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failure, the target sheds bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for an additional 20 feet. For the spell’s duration, the creature has disadvantage on attack rolls.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Save: Dexterity
- Level: 1
- School: illusion
- Components: V, S
- Casting Time: 1 action
- data-RangeAoe: 60 feet
