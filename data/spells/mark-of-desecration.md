# Mark of Desecration

**description**: 1st-level necromancy Casting Time: 1 minute Range: 30 ft. Components: V, S, M (a vial of blood) Duration: Instantaneous You perform a heretical rite upon a number of Small, Medium, or Large corpses up to your Intelligence modifier, placing ominous masks over their faces and whispering dark words into their dead ears. The dead are dragged to their feet by the dark spirits possessing them, moving with the jerking, unnatural gait of ill-made puppets. Each of these corpses rises as an undying warrior. At the GM’s discretion, a creature may also be revived as a version of the creature it was in life with the Zombified template.
