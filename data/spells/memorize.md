# Memorize

**description**: 1st-level enchantment (ritual) Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (a page of written text and a length of silver string worth 10 gp, tied in a knot, which the spell consumes) Duration: Instantaneous While casting this spell, your eyes pass over the words on a page, which are committed to your memory. For the next year, you exactly remember the details of all information on this page. After that time, you have advantage on all Intelligence checks you make to recall this information.
