# Open Channel

**description**: For the duration, you can transmit your voice and image to an electronic communication device within range. The device must be capable of transmitting your voice and/or image, and must be one that you have seen or touched within the last 24 hours. You can choose to broadcast to the device directly or instead make a call or signal to the device for someone to respond. So long as the communication device has power, your transmission signal is high-quality, glitch free, and immune to signal jamming and interference. You can end the transmission at any time during the duration, which also ends the effect.
