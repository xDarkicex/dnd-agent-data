# Bathe in Light

**description**: You bathe in light up to three creatures of your choice within range. Whenever a target takes damage before the spell ends, the target can roll a d4 and reduce the amount of damage taken by the number rolled. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.
