# Enshrouding Miasma

**description**: 6th-level lair magic (conjuration) Casting Time: 1 week (7 days) Radius: 1 mile Components: V, S, M (vials of poison and toxins worth at least 3000 gold pieces, which the spell consumes) Duration: 1 year This spell fills the environment with a virulent miasma, slowly killing everything within it. Plants wither and die quickly, and animals flee the area if able. A creature that breathes while in the miasma must make a Constitution saving throw or be poisoned. Any creature not immune to necrotic damage that finishes a long rest in the miasma has its maximum hit points reduced by 2d10 until it ends a long rest outside the miasma. If this would reduce a creature's maximum hit points to 0, that creature’s flesh disintegrates, leaving only its bones and possessions behind.
