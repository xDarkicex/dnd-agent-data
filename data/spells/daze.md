# Daze

**description**: This power puts its targets into a confused haze. Creatures within 20 feet of a point you choose within range must make a Wisdom saving throw or be placed into a state of confusion and considered to be restrained by the psychic energy until the end of its next turn. If the creature succeeds on its saving throw, it is immune to this power’s effects for the next 24 hours. Automatons, constructs, and creatures immune to being charmed aren’t affected by this power.
