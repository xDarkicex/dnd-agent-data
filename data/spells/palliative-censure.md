# Palliative Censure

**description**: 2nd-level enchantment Casting time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: v, s Duration: concentration, up to 1 minute Choose a creature you can see within range. The target must succeed on a wisdom saving throw or be cursed for the duration. While cursed, each time the target deals damage with an attack, the damage is halved, and a creature you can see within 30 feet of the cursed target regains hit points equal to that amount immediately after the attack’s damage is dealt.
