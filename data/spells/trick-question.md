# Trick Question

**description**: 1st-Level Enchantment Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous You pose a question that can be answered by one word, directed at a creature that can hear you. The target must make a successful Wisdom saving throw or be compelled to answer your question truthfully. When the answer is given, the target knows that you used magic to compel it.
