# Aid

**Level**: 2
**School**: abjuration
**Classes**: bard, cleric, druid, paladin, ranger
**Casting Time**: action
**Range**: 30 feet
**Components**: ['v', 's', 'm']
**Duration**: 8 hours

## Description

Choose up to three creatures within range. Each target's Hit Point maximum and current Hit Points increase by 5 for the duration.
