# Surgeon's Touch

**description**: 2nd-level transmutation Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V Duration: Instantaneous You whisper words of prayer or a mnemonic of medical knowledge, and begin your treatment. When you cast this spell, choose one Injured creature within reach. You can make a Wisdom (Medicine) check to remove an Injury from that creature. You gain a +10 bonus on this check. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the casting time becomes a bonus action. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the Medicine check bonus increases to +15.
