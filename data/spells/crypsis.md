# Crypsis

**description**: The touched creature’s features blend in with surfaces behind them. For the spell’s duration, the target creature can take the Hide action by pressing themselves against a solid surface, such as a tree or wall, that is at least as tall and wide as the target creature. During a turn in which the target does not move or take actions, they are treated as invisible. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.
