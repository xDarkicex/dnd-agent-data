# Blood Crystal

**description**: Focusing on the remains of a creature that died within the past 7 days, a blood mage can use this spell to drain the blood from the corpse from up to 30 feet away. The drained blood flies to the caster forming a crystalline spear that levitates a foot above the caster. This blood crystal spear lasts for 1 day before disintegrating. A blood mage can have a maximum of 5 blood crystal spears above them at any given time. When hurled as a ranged spell attack, the spear splits into 3 smaller fragments that can hit multiple targets, dealing 1d6+3 Necrotic damage and 1d6 Piercing damage per fragment. Different bodies can be drained different numbers of times; however, a tiny body cannot be used for this spell. Small bodies: 1 time. Medium bodies: 2 times. Large bodies: 4 times. Huge bodies: 6 times Gargantuan bodies: 8 times. For the purposes of this spell, a blood crystal spear has the same range as a standard spear. The blood crystal spear cannot be used as a standard spear, if this is attempted, the spear disintegrates. After being used to make a ranged spell attack, the fragments disintegrate a second after impact. This spell has no effects on bodies with no blood.
