# The Rushing Wave

**description**: Tier 2 water au gmentation Casting Time: 1 bonus action Base Favor Cost: 1 Range: Self Components (Required): V, S Resonances: You are standing or swimming in a body of fresh water such as a river or pond (1 bonus favor), you are standing or swimming in the ocean (2 bonus favor) Duration: 1 minute Effects: You call forth a wave of water to carry you forward. For the duration of this invocation, your walking speed is increased by 10 feet, and you gain a swim speed equal to your walking speed. Empowerments You can spend favor for the following empowerments of this i nvocation: 1+ favor: You stride with the swiftness of the river, increasing your walking speed by an additional 5 feet per favor spent this way. 1+ favor: You extend the blessing to others. Choose one other willing creature within 30 feet per favor spent this way. Each of those targets also gains the benefits of this invocation and its empowerments for the same duration. 2 favor: For the duration of this invocation, after a creature misses you with a melee attack, you can spend your reaction to move 10 feet. This movement does not provoke opportunity attacks. 2 favor: After performing this invocation, you can immediately move a number of feet equal to half your walking speed. You make this movement as if you were walking, except that creatures cannot make opportunity attacks against you during this movement. 3+ favor: The first time you make a saving throw against an invocation or spell, you do so with advantage.
