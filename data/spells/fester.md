# Fester

**description**: Malefic word, tra nsmutation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 20 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous, ongoing (see this malefic word’s baleful echo) Effects: You speak a word that clicks across your tongue like a venomous centipede, and a malevolent force attempts to crawl into the body of one creature within range. The target makes a Constitution saving throw. If it fails, the target is poisoned until the end of your next turn. If the target succeeds, it takes 1d8 poison damage and the malefic word’s baleful echo is silenced (and you do not suffer the feedback). Baleful Echo (2 vitality): The target remains poisoned until the start of your next turn. If the target dies while this baleful echo is active, it explodes. Each creature within 10 feet of the target must make a Dexterity saving throw. They take 2d8 poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The damage dealt when the target explodes increases by 1d8 when you reach 5th level (3d8), 11th level (4d8), and 17th level (5d8). Feedback: The writhing necrotic force burrows into your guts, leaving you gagging in agony. You are poisoned. At the end of each of your subsequent turns, you may make a Constitution saving throw. If you succeed, you are no longer poisoned. If you fail, you take 1d4 poison damage.
