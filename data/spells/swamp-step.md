# Swamp Step

**description**: Swamp Step 2nd-level Transmutation Casting Time : 1 action Range : 30 feet Components : V, S, M (a few legs from a water strider beetle) Duration : 10 minutes The target of this spell can stand or walk on water, as long as the water is less than 5 feet deep. The target is also unaffected by mud or difficult terrain related to marshes or swamps.
