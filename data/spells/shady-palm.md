# Shady Palm

**description**: 1st-level conjuration (ritual) Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (a palm frond which the spell consumes) Duration: 8 hours You touch a willing creature. A shadowy palm tree appears just behind it and magical pool of shade, 5-feet in radius. Creatures in this area are not affected by high temperature's effects. The palm moves with the target.
