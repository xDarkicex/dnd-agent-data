# Anti Venenum

**description**: 2nd-level Abjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (a nightshade flower) Duration: Special You touch a Poisoned creature or a creature affected by a poison or a disease. The Poisoned condition ends on them or they are immediately cured of the poison or disease. Until the end of your next turn, your next single target spell or attack deals 2d8 extra poison damage. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
