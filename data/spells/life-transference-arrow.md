# Life Transference Arrow

**description**: 1st-Level Necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: 120 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous You create a glowing arrow of necrotic magic and command it to strike a creature you can see within range. The arrow can have one of two effects; you choose which at the moment of casting. If you make a successful ranged spell attack, you and the target experience the desired effect. If the attack misses, the spell fails. The arrow deals 2d6 necrotic damage to the target, and you heal the same amount of hit points. You take 2d6 necrotic damage, and the target heals the same amount of hit points. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the spell’s damage and Hit Points healed increase by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.
