# Prayer of Shielding

**description**: You extend your holy symbol toward a creature within range and utter a short prayer of protection, surrounding the target with a faint halo of light. The first time that creature is hit by a melee attack during the spell’s duration, it can roll a d4 and add the result to its AC against that attack.

- Level: 0
