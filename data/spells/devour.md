# Devour

**description**: Malefic word, necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous, ongoing (see this malefic word’s baleful echo) Effects: Your mouth expands so that a devouring curse can spill forth onto one creature within range, sapping life essence to empower your own. Make a ranged spell attack against the creature. On a hit, the target takes 2d4 necrotic damage and you regain hit points equal to half that amount. If the attack misses, the malefic word’s baleful echo is silenced (and you do not suffer the feedback). This malefic word’s damage increases by 2d4 when you reach 5th level (4d4), 11th level (6d4), and 17th level (8d4). Baleful Echo (1 vitality): The creature takes 2d4 necrotic damage and you regain hit points equal to half that amount. Feedback: Now that it is free of your lips, the hungry malefic word you spoke begins to feast upon your own life essence. You suffer 1d12 necrotic damage and one level of exhaustion.
