# Command Undead

**description**: 3rd-Level Necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V Duration: Concentration You speak a one-word command to all undead creatures you can see within range. Any target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or follow the command on its next turn. The spell has no effect if the target is living, if it doesn’t understand your language, or if your command is directly harmful to it.
