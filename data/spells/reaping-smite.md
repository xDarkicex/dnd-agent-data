# Reaping Smite

**description**: 3rd-level necromancy Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: Self Components: V Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute The next time you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack during this spell’s duration, your weapon digs deep into the enemy and a sense of impending doom washes over them. The attack deals an extra 3d12 necrotic damage to the target. Additionally, if this damage reduces the target to 0 hit points, it cannot be restored to life or raised as undead by any means other than the wish spell.
