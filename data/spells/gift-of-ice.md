# Gift of Ice

**description**: 2nd-level abjuration Casting time: 1 action Range: self Components: v, s, m (a piece of aquamarine) Duration: 8 hours Your body becomes coated in a layer of hoarfrost. For the duration, your ac increases by 1, and you have resistance to cold damage. This spell ends early if you take fire damage while below half your hit point maximum.
