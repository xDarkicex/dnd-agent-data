# Shadow Shunt

**description**: 2nd-level conjuration Casting time: 1 reaction, which you take when you are targeted by an attack or when you are in the area of a spell that deals damage Range: self Components: v Duration: instantaneous You enter the plane of shadow just as danger approaches. You vanish from your current plane of existence and appear in the plane of shadow until the start of your next turn, avoiding the triggering attack or spell. While on the plane of shadow, you are paralyzed, but you can see and hear the plane you originated from, which appears in shades of gray. At the start of your next turn, you reappear in the space you previously occupied or in the nearest unoccupied space.
