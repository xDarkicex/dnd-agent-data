# Twist of Fate

**description**: A halo of six red, alien eyes encircle your head, enabling you to peer into the realm of possibilities. Whenever a creature within 60 feet of you that you can see hits or misses with an attack roll, or fails or succeeds on a saving throw, you can expend one of the eyes to add or subtract 1d10 from the creature’s roll. Additionally, while this spell is active, you do not have disadvantage on any roll, and no attack roll against you has advantage. The spell ends when all six eyes have been expended or when the duration expires.
