# Psychic Drain

**description**: You cause a direct link with a single creature’s mind and drain the target psychically. The target must make a Wisdom saving throw. The target takes 4d6 psychic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. You gain temporary hit points equal to half the amount of damage dealt. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 2nd.
