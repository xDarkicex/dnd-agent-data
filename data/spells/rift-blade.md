# Rift Blade

**description**: A spinning translucent blade surrounded by a small wisps of light-distorting mists forms and hovers a few feet from you for the duration. When the blade appears, you can launch it at a target you can see within range. Make a ranged channeling attack. The target cannot gain an AC bonus against this attack from half or three-quarters cover. On a hit, the target takes 3d10 force damage. Until the effect ends, you can use a bonus action on each of your turns to repeat this attack against the same target or a different one within range.
