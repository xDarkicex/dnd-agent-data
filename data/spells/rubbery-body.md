# Rubbery Body

**description**: 2nd-level transmutation Casting time: 1 action Range: touch Components: v, s, m (a tentacle from a small or Smaller aberration) Duration: concentration, up to 1 minute Your touch changes the composition of a willing creature’s flesh to an oily rubber. Until the spell ends, the target has resistance to bludgeoning damage. At higher levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the duration is concentration, up to 1 hour. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the target has immunity to bludgeoning damage until the spell ends instead.
