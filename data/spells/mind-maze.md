# Mind Maze

**description**: Choose a creature you can see within range. It must succeed on an Intelligence saving throw or be mentally trapped in an imagined maze of mirrors for the duration. While trapped in this way, the creature is incapacitated, but it imagines itself alone and wandering through the maze. Externally, it appears dazed as it turns in place and reaches out at nonexistent barriers. Each time the target takes damage, it makes another Intelligence saving throw. On a success, the spell ends.
