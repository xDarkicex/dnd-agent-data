# Cloud of Aggression

**description**: 4th-level enchantment Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S, M (a powerful stimulant worth at least 10 gp, which the spell consumes) Duration: Instantaneous You create a tiny explosion, spreading a powerful stimulant into the air in a 10-foot radius cloud centered on a point within range. Each creature within the cloud must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature must use its turn to move up to its speed towards the nearest creature and use its action to make a melee attack against that creature. Creatures with more than one attack as part of their attack action use all available attacks against their target. Affected creatures may not use bonus actions or reactions, and must immediately end their turn after finishing their action. If the creature being attacked is one of its allies, the attacker has advantage on its attack rolls. Creatures that are resistant to poison have advantage on their saving throws against this spell, and creatures that are immune to poison are immune to the spell’s effects.
