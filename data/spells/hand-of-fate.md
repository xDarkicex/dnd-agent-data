# Hand of Fate

**description**: You cause a ghostly apparition to appear to give you basic guidance about a course of action. Roll one d20 and record the number rolled. You can replace any attack roll, saving throw, or ability check made by you or a creature that you can see with the foretelling roll. You must choose to do so before the roll. The foretelling roll granted by this spell can be used only once and the spell ends once it is used.
