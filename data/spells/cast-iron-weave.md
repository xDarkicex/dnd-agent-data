# Cast-Iron Weave

**description**: Cast-Iron Weave Requirements: Level 18 Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Duration: 1 minute Cost: 4 WP / special For the duration, no actions are possible within a radius of 30 foot radius sphere around the philosopher; the effect moves with the philosopher. Affected characters will just stand around impotently, unable even to speak coherently unless they make a successful Wisdom saving throw against the philosopher’s spell save DC. The philosopher can move with difficulty (at 1/4 normal rate, rounded down) if he expends an extra WP, and can move normally if he expends 3 WP. The feature can be amended on the spot to allow selected characters to take actions, with an extra WP expenditure of 3 WP for each excepted character.
