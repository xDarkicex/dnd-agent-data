# Gather Courage

**description**: You touch a single creature and fill them with courage. The touched creature deals 1d6 additional damage of the same type with the next weapon attack it hits with during the duration. This spell’s addtional damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).

**properties**:

- Level: 0
- School: enchantment

- Casting Time: 1 action
