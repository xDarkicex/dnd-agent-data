# Greater Medic’s Grace

**description**: 3rd-level abjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S, M (a stalk of ghost pipe flowers, and any of a pinch of herbs, a drop of a tonic or tincture, or other minor medicine) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute For the duration of the spell, one creature within range ignores the effects of three Injuries chosen by the target when the spell is cast, although the Injuries are not healed. If the target no longer has any Injuries, you can choose a new target within 30 feet as a bonus action. When the spell ends, if the creature had an Injury of Deadly IIII or higher severity, the creature suffers a level of exhaustion. At Higher Levels. When cast using a spell slot of 5th or higher, the duration increases to 1 hour. When cast using a spell slot of 7th or higher, the duration increases to 8 hours.
