# Unfetter

**description**: You touch a willing creature and create a field of sustained gravity around it. For the duration, the target’s movement is unaffected by difficult terrain, and other esper powers can neither reduce the target’s speed nor cause the target to be paralyzed or restrained. The target can also spend 5 feet of movement to automatically escape from mundane restraints, such as handcuffs, reinforced restraints, or a creature that has it grappled. Finally, being underwater imposes no penalties on the target’s movement or attacks.
