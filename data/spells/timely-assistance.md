# Timely Assistance

**description**: Timely Assistance Requirements: Level 3, How Hard Can It Be Casting Time: bonus action Range: 10 feet Duration: special Cost: 1 WP The sharp mind of the philosopher allows him to offer useful advice on just about any undertaking, and often it just takes a word or two. Use of this power grants advantage to an ability check made by another character. If used together with the Weave Mastery feature Overcome Boundaries, it additionally allows for the elimination of one requirement for the action undertaken, such as a lock pick attempt without thieves’ tools.
