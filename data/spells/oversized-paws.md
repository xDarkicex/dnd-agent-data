# Oversized Paws

**description**: Until this spell ends, the hands and feet of one willing creature within range become oversized and more powerful. For the duration, the creature adds 1d4 to damage it deals with its unarmed strike. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each spell slot above 2nd.
