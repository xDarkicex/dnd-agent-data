# Kobold's Fury

**description**: 1st-Level Transmutation Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (a kobold scale) Duration: 1 round Your touch infuses the rage of a threatened kobold into the target. The target has advantage on melee weapon attacks until the end of its next turn. In addition, its next successful melee weapon attack against a creature larger than itself does an additional 2d8 damage.
