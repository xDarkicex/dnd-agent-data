# Potency Of The Pack

**description**: You bestow lupine traits on a group of living creatures that you designate within range. Choose one of the following benefits to be gained by all targets for the duration: Thick Fur. Each target sprouts fur over its entire body, giving it a +2 bonus to Armor Class. Keen Hearing and Smell. Each target has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell. Pack Tactics. Each affected creature has advantage on an attack roll against a target if at least one of the attacker’s allies (also under the effect of this spell) is within 5 feet of the target of the attack and the ally isn’t incapacitated. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the duration increases by 1 minute for each slot level above 3rd.
