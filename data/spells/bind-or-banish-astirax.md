# Bind or Banish Astirax

**description**: 3rd-level divination Can be cast by clerics, druids, and paladins Casting Time: 10 minutes Range : 200 feet Components: V, S, M (an obsidian bead for binding, a bracelet made of silver for banishing) Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes You can detect any astirax within 200 feet of you, its form appearing as a flickering black shadow that only you can perceive. During your turn, if you are within 10 feet of an astirax, you can use your action to attempt to bind or banish it. The astirax must make a Wisdom saving throw. If it fails and you were attempting to bind it, the astirax must obey your commands for one year and a day. At the end of that year, you may perform this ritual again to keep the astirax bound. If you do, the astirax suffers disadvantage on its Wisdom saving throw. If it fails and you were attempting to banish it, the astirax takes 4d12 radiant damage. If it is currently possessing an animal, it has advantage on its Wisdom saving throw. If you fail to bind an astirax three times in a 24-hour period, the astirax can no longer be bound by you for one year and a day.
