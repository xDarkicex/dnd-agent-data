# Crimson Rain

**description**: Using your own blood as a catalyst, you cause an arterial burst to create a burst of blood from your body that heals immediately, leaving a scar in its place. This spell costs 3d8 hit points. The arterial burst transforms into a shower of blood that pounds to the ground in a 20-foot- radius, 40-foot-high cylinder centred on a point within range. Each creature in the cylinder must make a Dexterity saving throw taking 6d8 Necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. The affected area becomes drenched in blood turning into difficult terrain until the end of your next turn, any creature that was caught in the rain becomes blood soaked. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 4th.
