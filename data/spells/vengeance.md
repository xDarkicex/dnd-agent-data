# Vengeance

**description**: Malefic word, c onjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous, ongoing (see this malefic word’s baleful echo) Effects: You speak a word of bitter vengeance to summon a ghost that desires revenge on the living. A wraith from the Realm of the Hungry Dead rises from the earth at any point within range of this spell. The wraith is visible to everyone and has a horrific appearance. Any creature that starts their turn within 5 feet of the wraith must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, they take 3d8 necrotic damage. On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage. Any creature that starts their turn within 5 feet of the wraith must also make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, they become frightened of the wraith while it is present. While frightened of the wraith, a creature must take the Dash action and move away from it by the safest available route on each of its turns, unless there is nowhere to move. When you silence the spell, the wraith vanishes, banished back to the Realm of the Hungry Dead. Baleful Echo (3 vitality): You may have the wraith move up to 60 feet in any direction. It can fly and move through solid material, but it must end its movement in an empty space. Feedback: With a howl of rage, the wraith attacks you. You suffer 4d8 necrotic damage and cannot use this malefic word again for 24 hours. The wraith then vanishes back to the Realm of the Hungry Dead.
