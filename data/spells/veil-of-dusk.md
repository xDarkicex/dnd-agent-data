# Veil of Dusk

**description**: 1st-level abjuration Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S, M (a pinch of soot) Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes You incant towards a creature, cloaking them in a shadowy veil of darkness and silence. The target gains a +1 bonus to their armor class and makes Stealth checks with advantage for the duration of the spell.
