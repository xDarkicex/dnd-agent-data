# Nociceptive Feedback

**description**: 5th-level enchantment Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S, M (a doll of a humanoid with an accurate representation of the nervous system worth 100GP) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute One creature you can see within range must succeed on an Intelligence saving throw or take 8d6 psychic damage and be afflicted with a special Injury of Handicap III severity, “Nociceptive Overload”, as long as the spell lasts. While the creature is Injured in this way, it takes a -1 penalty to ability checks, AC, and saving throws per Injury it suffers from (max -3). Additionally, as an action on each of your turns as long as you are within range of the target, you can automatically deal 6d6 psychic damage to the target. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
