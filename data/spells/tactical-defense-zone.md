# Tactical Defense Zone

**description**: You launch micro drones carrying a transmission signal between you and your allies. They mark the position of allies within 30 feet of you and provides additional protection against the harmful effects of esper powers and abilities. For the duration, each friendly creature in the area (including you) has advantage on saving throws against esper powers and other similar effects. Additionally, when an affected creature succeeds on a saving throw made against a power or supernatural effect that allows it to make a saving throw to take only half damage, it instead takes no damage if it succeeds on the saving throw.
