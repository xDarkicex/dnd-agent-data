# Blood Displacement

**description**: You can take 3d6 damage to teleport up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space that you can see. If you reappear in a space filled with blood, the damage taken becomes 1d6. A space is considered filled with blood if purposely covered using an item such as a blood vial, or when a creature became blood soaked in that space. An area may also be filled with blood at the DM’s discretion.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 2
- School: transmutation
- Components: V, S
- Damage Type: necrotic
- Casting Time: 1 Bonus Action

**publisher**: Haigo Dungeoncrafts

**book**: The Blood Mage
