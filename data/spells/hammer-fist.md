# Hammer Fist

**description**: 2nd level Transmutation Casting Time: 1 Action Range: Touch Components: V, S Duration: 1 minute You touch a willing creature. Until the spell ends, one of the target's hands (their choice which) is transformed into a glowing hammer. Any objects held in the hand are dropped. While your hand is transformed, you gain a +2 bonus to melee attack and damage rolls made with the hammer fist. On a hit, the target takes 2d6+2 bludgeoning damage. Proficiency with a warhammer allows you to add your proficiency bonus to the attack roll for any attack you make with it. You can add your Strength modifier to the damage of the hammer fist attack even if you don’t have the Two-Weapon Fighting style. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 2nd.
