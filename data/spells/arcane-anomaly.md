# Arcane Anomaly

**description**: 1st-level abjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self (30-foot radius) Components: V, S, M (a broken mirror) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You spread cracks in the magical energy that suffuses the multiverse. For the duration, whenever a spell is cast within range, roll a d6. On a 1, the spell casting fails, expending a spell slot as normal, but not consuming expensive material components.
