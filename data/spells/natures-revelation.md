# Nature's Revelation

**description**: 2nd-level transmutation Can be cast by druids and rangers Casting Time : 1 action Range : 120 feet Compon ents: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute This spell causes all plants and animals in the area to pinpoint the location of all hidden or invisible creatures in the area of effect. Tree limbs point, vines pull back bushes, and animals seem to stare intently at the hiding creatures. The effect exists in a 120-foot-radius sphere centered on the spellcaster. While within the affected area, creatures lose any benefits of cover. In addition, you know of the presence and location of any invisible creatures. You can designate up to six creatures who are unaffected by the spell. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can increase the radius of the spell by 10 feet per level above 2nd.
