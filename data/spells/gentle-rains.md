# Gentle Rains

**description**: Tier 2 wat er mending Casting Time: 1 action Base Favor Cost: 1 Range: Self (20-foot sweep) Components (Required): V, S Resonances: You are outside at a cloudy or foggy location (1 bonus favor), you are in the Realm of Animals or Realm of Dreams (2 bonus favor) Duration: Ongoing (1 favor per round) Effects: You call upon the water spirits to shower healing rains upon a twenty-foot sweep around you, invigorating the area. The rains continue to fall in this area for the duration, moving with you. At the start of its turn, each creature in the area of effect that is not a fiend, undead, or Lost creature can spend 1 Hit Die to roll that die to regain hit points equal to the result + its Constitution modifier.
