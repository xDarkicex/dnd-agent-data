# Mental Missive

**description**: Choose one creature within range and whisper a message. The target (and only the target) hears the message and can reply in a whisper that only you can hear. You use this power through solid objects if you are familiar with the target and know it is beyond the barrier. 1 foot of stone or common metal, any sorium barrier, or 3 feet of wood or glass blocks the effect, but does function around corners and through openings.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 0
- School: Psychogenic
- Expansion: 27051
- Casting Time: 1 action
