# Granite Armor

**description**: You touch a willing creature. Until the spell ends, the target’s skin has a rough, stone-like appearance, and the target’s AC can’t be less than 18, regardless of what kind of armor it is wearing. Additionally, any critical hit against the creature while this spell is active becomes a normal hit.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 3
- School: transmutation
- Components: V, S, M
- Casting Time: 1 action
- data-RangeAoe: Touch

**publisher**: This Mind of Mine
