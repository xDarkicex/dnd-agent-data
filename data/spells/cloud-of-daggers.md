# Cloud of Daggers

**description**: Level 2 Conjuration (Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard) Casting Time: Action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S, M (a sliver of glass) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You conjure spinning daggers in a 5-foot Cube centered on a point within range. Each creature in that area takes 4d4 Slashing damage . A creature also takes this damage if it enters the Cube or ends its turn there or if the Cube moves into its space. A creature takes this damage only once per turn. On your later turns, you can take a Magic action to teleport the Cube up to 30 feet. Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot. The damage increases by 2d4 for each spell slot level above 2.
