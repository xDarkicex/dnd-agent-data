# Jovi's Timely Retreat

**description**: 1st-level evocation Casting Time: 1 reaction Range: 60 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous You allow an ally to quickly reposition as harm befalls it. As a reaction to you or an ally within range suffering an Injury, you allow the target to move up to their speed without provoking opportunity attacks If the target of the spell would be reduced to 0 hit points, they move before falling unconscious.
