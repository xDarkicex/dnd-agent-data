# Downgrade Metal

**description**: Target any metal object (or portion thereof) within range that can fit into a 5-foot cube. The metal permanently becomes wood. Any weapon targeted in this way does half the damage it once did. 

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 4
- School: transmutation
- Components: V
- Casting Time: 1 action

**publisher**: Renegade Game Studios

**book**: Wardlings Campaign Guide
