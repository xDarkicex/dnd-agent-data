# Raise Shade

**description**: 4th-level necromancy Casting time: 1 hour Range: touch Components: v, s, m (a diamond worth at least 500 gp, which the spell consumes) Duration: instantaneous This spell is identical to raise ghost in every way, except the ghostly figure can manipulate, hold, or carry objects weighing up to 5 pounds. Note that while 5 pounds of force is enough to pick up many melee weapons, it’s not sufficient to wield one in combat or to draw a bow.
