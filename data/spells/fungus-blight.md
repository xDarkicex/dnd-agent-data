# Fungus Blight

**description**: You touch a dead, restrained, unconscious or incapacitated creature. If the creature fails a Constitution saving throw, it instantly bursts open with 2d4 fungus leshy (and the victim now requires a true resurrection or wish spell to return from the dead). These fungus leshy are willing to do your bidding for the duration of the spell but gain free will when the effect ends.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 9
- School: transmutation
- Components: V, S, M
- Damage Type: fungus leshy
- Casting Time: 1 action
- data-RangeAoe: Touch

**book**: Rise of the Drow
