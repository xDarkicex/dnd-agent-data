# Haemostatic Hands

**description**: You can touch a wounded creature and draw power from their blood to restore a number of hit points to that creature, up to twice their total level. Alternatively, you can expend 1d10 of their hit points to cure the target of one disease or neutralize one poison affecting it. This feature has no effect on undead, constructs or creatures without blood.

- Level: 2
