# Bone Shield

**description**: Bone Shield 1st-level abjuration (druid, ranger, warlock) Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you are hit by an attack Range: Self Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous You generate a barrier of elongated femurs that protects you. You reduce the incoming damage by 1d12, and if the attacking enemy is within 10 feet of you, the osseous barrier explodes, dealing slashing damage to them equal to the amount reduced. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage reduction increases by 1d12 for each slot level above 1st.
