# Salvo Strike

**description**: You launch an assault drone which rains down a hail of gunfire centered on a point within range. Each creature in a 40-foot-radius, 20-foot-high cylinder centered on that point must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 8d8 piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
