# Spur Mount

**description**: 1st-Level Transmutation Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (an apple or a sugar cube) Duration: 1 round You urge your mount to a sudden burst of speed. Until the end of your next turn, you can direct your mount to use the Dash or Disengage action as a bonus action. This spell has no effect on a creature that you are not riding.
