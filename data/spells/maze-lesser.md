# Maze, Lesser

**description**: This spell functions as maze, but the target can resist being sent to the extra-dimensional prison with a successful Intelligence saving throw. In addition, the maze is easier to navigate, requiring only a DC 12 Intelligence check to escape.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Save: Intelligence
- Level: 3
- School: Conjuration (Labyrinth)
- Components: V S
- Casting Time: 1 action

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Midgard Heroes Handbook
