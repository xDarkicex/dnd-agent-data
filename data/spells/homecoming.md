# Homecoming

**description**: Homecoming Requirements: Level 14 Casting Time: special Range: touch (the Likeness)/unlimited Duration: special Cost: 2 WP (invested), 1 WP tp transfer Paint a room you are intimately familiar with, such as your own study, and imbue this Likeness with (invested) Weave Points The process of enabling the Likeness to allow Homecoming takes 1 minute – making it ready for imminent use then requires a short preparation that takes only 1 action. You can then travel instantly to the depicted room whenever you wish with the expenditure of 1 Weave Point in the course of 1 action. The act of doing so destroys the painting a few moments after the transition is complete, and the invested Weave Points can be regained with the next long rest. To accommodate the transfer of additional people with the artist, an additional 1 Weave Point per person other than the artist must be spent as each of them goes through. In this case, the artist goes through last, triggering the end of the effect. Each person going through needs to spend 1 action. The painting has to be large enough to at least reach through with one hand.
