# Verbal Assault

**description**: 4th-level evocation Casting time: 1 action Range: self (30-foot cone) Components: v, s Duration: instantaneous You utter words of power laced with deadly psychic energy. Each creature in a 30-foot cone must make an intelligence saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 8d6 psychic damage and is stunned until the end of its next turn. On a successful save, a creature takes half the damage and isn’t stunned. At higher levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 4th.
