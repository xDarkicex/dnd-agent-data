# Mushroom Mount

**description**: You enchant a benign, unintelligent mushroom of Large size or smaller; the fungus uproots itself and attaches to you. Wrapping rhizomorphs envelop your legs and you move as one. Your fused form moves along in short hops that allow you ignore difficult terrain and grants you a climbing speed equal to your walking speed. While fused to a mushroom mount you are immune to the prone condition. When you are hit with a melee attack, you can use your reaction to cause the mushroom mount to expel a cloud of spores that momentarily muddles your assailant’s senses: any additional attacks against you beyond the first from the same creature are made with disadvantage.
