# Barrage

**description**: A small autocannon appears around your arm and unleashes a wave of gunfire before it disappears. Each creature in a 60-foot cone must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 3d8 piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 4 or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for every two ranks above rank 3.
