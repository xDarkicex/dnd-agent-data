# Shadow Shield

**description**: 2nd-level abjuration Casting time: 1 reaction, which you take when you are hit by an attack or targeted by the magic missile spell Range: self Components: v, s Duration: 1 round A misty black barrier of magical force appears and protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +8 bonus to ac, including against the triggering attack, and you are immune to force and radiant damage.
