# Force Buckler

**description**: Abjuration cantrip Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: Self Components: V, S, M (a specially prepared gauntlet worth at least 5 gp) Duration: 1 round You summon a translucent yet visible field of force, which springs forth from the prepared gauntlet. Until the start of your next turn, this shield grants you a +2 bonus to your Armor Class, as if you were wielding a shield. This spell ends early if you are hit by an attack.
