# Lesser Maze

**description**: 3rd-level conjuration Casting time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: v, s Duration: concentration, up to 1 minute This spell functions as maze, but the target can resist being sent to the extradimensional prison with a successful intelligence saving throw. In addition, the maze is easier to navigate, requiring only a dc 12 intelligence check to escape.
