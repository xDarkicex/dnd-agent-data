# Rhizomorph Road

**description**: This spell creates a magical link between a Large or larger inanimate mushroom within range and another mushroom, at any distance, on the same plane of existence. For the duration, any creature can step into the target plant and exit from the destination plant by using 5 feet of movement.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 4
- School: conjuration (ritual)
- Components: V, S, M
- Casting Time: 1 minute
- data-RangeAoe: 10 feet
