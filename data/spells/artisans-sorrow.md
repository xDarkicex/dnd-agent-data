# Artisan's Sorrow

**description**: You cause a single unattended object you touch to shake and break apart. If the object has an AC, make a melee spell attack. You have advantage on this attack. On a hit or if the object does not have an AC, the object takes 3d12 thunder damage. This damage ignores an object’s Damage Threshold. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d12 for each slot level above 1st.

**properties**:
