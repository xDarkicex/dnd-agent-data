# Bone Growth

**description**: 1st-level transmutation Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: Self Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You enhance the bones in your body, rapidly growing them into weapons or armor. When you cast this spell, choose one of the following options: Offense. You fuse and transform the bones in your dominant forearm, which jut out from your wrist to create a jagged blade of solid bone. You are proficient with this weapon, which deals 1d8 slashing damage and you treat Injury thresholds as 5 lower (to a minimum of 5) for attacks made with this weapon. Defense. You enhance your ribs, strengthening them against impact and causing them to burst from your torso, forming an exoskeletal breastplate. You are proficient with the armor, which provides you with an AC of 14 + Dex modifier (max 2), and grants resistance to slashing damage. If you are already wearing armor, you use the better of the two armor classes granted by this spell or your armor. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the weapon deals an additional 1d8 damage (2d8) for every 2 levels beyond 1st. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you may choose both the offense and defense options.
