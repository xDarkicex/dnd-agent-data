# Searing Meditation

**description**: You enter a meditative state that converts the pain of your wounds into flames that burn your enemies. While in this state, any time you regain hit points, you also deal fire damage equal to the amount of hit points you regain to all creatures within 5 feet of you.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 5
- School: transmutation
- Components: V, S
- Casting Time: 1 action
- data-RangeAoe: Self

**publisher**: This Mind of Mine

**book**: Gathering Magic - Mystical Archives
