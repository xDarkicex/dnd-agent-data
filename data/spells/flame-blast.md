# Flame Blast

**description**: A spiral of fire forming a line 100 feet long and 5 feet wide blasts out from you in a direction you choose. Each creature in the line must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 8d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The fire ignites flammable objects in the area that aren’t being worn or carried. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 4 or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each rank above 3.
