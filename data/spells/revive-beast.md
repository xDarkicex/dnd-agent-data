# Revive Beast

**description**: 2nd-Level Necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (emeralds worth 100 gp, which the spell consumes) Duration: Instantaneous You touch a beast that has died within the last minute. That beast returns to life with 1 hit point. This spell can’t return to life a beast that has died of old age, nor can it restore any missing body parts.
