# Bleeding Frenzy

**description**: You focus on a creature you can see within range and attempt to force the arcane essence in its blood to expand, rupturing minor blood vessels and increase blood pressure. The target must make a successful Constitution saving throw vs your spell save DC otherwise it takes an immediate 8d6 damage to the targets HP, this cannot be negated by resistances or immunities. If the save is successful, the target takes half damage. At the start of each an affected creatures turns for the duration of this spell, it takes 8d6 damage to its hit points as more blood vessels burst. Additionally, the pain and pressure now felt all over within the target causes the target to swing and attack in a wild frenzy around itself, attacking all creatures within melee range for the duration of this spell. At the start of the creatures second turn being affected by this spell, more vessels rupture including those in the target’s eyes, causing the target to become blinded and blood soaked. An effected creature can make a new Constitution saving throw at the end of each of its turns to end this spell. This spell has no effect on creatures without blood.
