# Blood Thinner

**description**: You channel your magic to create an area, 15ft in radius, that disrupts coagulation causing all creatures in the area to bleed abundantly. Whilst in the area, creatures suffer the following effects: Every time they take bludgeoning, piercing or slashing damage, they lose an additional 1d6 hit points. Creatures immune to bludgeoning, piercing and slashing damage, that have blood, are instead resistant. This spell has no effect on creatures without blood. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the loss of hit points increase by 1d6 for each slot level above 2nd.
