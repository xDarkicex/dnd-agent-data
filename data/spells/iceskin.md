# Iceskin

**description**: You touch a willing creature: Until the spell ends, the target’s skin turns to hardened ice and they gain immunity to cold damage as well as resistance to piercing and slashing damage.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 2
- School: transmutation
- Components: V, S, M
- Casting Time: 1 action

**publisher**: Renegade Game Studios

**book**: Wardlings Campaign Guide
