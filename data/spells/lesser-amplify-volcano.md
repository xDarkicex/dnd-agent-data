# Lesser Amplify Volcano

**description**: 5th-level lair magic (transmutation) Casting Time: 2 weeks (14 days) Radius: 2 miles Components: V, S, M (A rare material or materials found within the local environment worth at least 5000 gold pieces, which the spell consumes) Duration: 1 year This spell amplifies the naturally dangerous effects of the surrounding terrain (or other similar environment), making it difficult for all who travel within it. When this spell is cast, the caster may choose up to eight creatures, including themself, who may ignore the effects of the spell. Alternatively, the caster may designate a passphrase that must be known or a symbol that must be worn. Harmful effects of the spell do not affect native creatures unless their Intelligence score is greater than 3. The volcano undergoes a steady, slow eruption for the duration of the spell. The area becomes difficult to pass, with lava flows cutting off roads, and ash in the air reducing visibility to a few dozen feet. Creatures chosen to be protected from this spell can see normally, but are still harmed by the lava. Creatures passing through the area must make a Constitution saving throw after the first half-hour and then again every 6 hours after that in which they are exposed to the air. On each failed saving throw, their lungs begin to clog with ash, reducing their movement speed by 5 feet (to a minimum of 5), and giving them a -1 penalty on initiative checks (max -5) until they finish a long rest outside of the area. Creatures that fail their saving throw by 5 or more are sickened until they succeed on a saving throw or finish a long rest outside of the area.
