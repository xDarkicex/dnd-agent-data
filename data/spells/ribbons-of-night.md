# Ribbons of Night

**description**: You launch a dark tendril of necrotic energy and shadow at a single creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 15d6 necrotic damage and you regain an equal amount of hit points. In addition, if the target has spell slots, you gain a bonus spell slot of 3rd level that must be used within 1 minute of gaining it.
