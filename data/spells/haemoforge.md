# Haemoforge

**description**: When you make a melee spell attack using a piercing melee weapon you can cast this spell. On a hit, you cause the iron in the targets blood to rapidly heat and meld with the weapon still thrust in the targets body. The target must make a constitution saving throw against you spell save DC, on a failed throw, the target takes 8d10 fire damage and becomes stunned until the end of its next turn as shock takes hold of its body. On a successful save the target takes half damage and is not stunned After a successful attack that utilised this spell, you lose the weapon. This spell has no effect on creatures without blood.
