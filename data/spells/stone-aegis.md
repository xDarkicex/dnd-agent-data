# Stone Aegis

**description**: A rocky coating covers your body, protecting you. Until the start of your next turn, you gain 5 temporary hit points and a +2 bonus to Armor Class, including against the triggering attack. At the start of each of your subsequent turns, you can use a bonus action to extend the duration of the AC bonus until the start of your following turn, for up to 1 minute.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 1
- School: transmutation
- Components: V S
- Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you are hit by an attack

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Tome of Heroes
