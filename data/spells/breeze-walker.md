# Breeze Walker

**description**: 2nd-level transmutation Casting time: 1 action Range: self Components: v, s, m (a pheasant feather) Duration: concentration, up to 1 hour Choosing a direction, you summon a strong, steady breeze to swirl beneath your feet and propel you. You levitate to a sustained height of 3 feet for the duration, and travel in the chosen direction at 150% of your movement. Travel in any other direction is at 50% of your movement. While mounted, your mount’s movement is affected as well.
