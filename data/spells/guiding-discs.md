# Guiding Discs

**description**: You create a number of small gravity discs that follow and spin a few feet around a creature of your choice. The target has rudimentary control over the movement patterns of these discs and can use them to quickly climb objects or protect you from certain types of attacks. Until the effect ends, the target has a +2 bonus to all Dexterity and Constitution saving throws. They also gain a climbing speed equal to your walking speed.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 1
- School: Kinesis
- Expansion: 27051
- Casting Time: 1 action
