# Hold at Bay

**description**: You grant a single creature a magical defense against harm. The first time the target creature would take damage during the spell’s duration, roll 1d10 + your spellcasting modifier. The amount of damage taken is reduced by the roll’s result. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, roll an additional 1d10 for the spell’s roll for each slot level above 1st.
