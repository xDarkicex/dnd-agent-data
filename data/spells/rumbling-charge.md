# Rumbling Charge

**description**: 1st-level transmutation Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, S, M (a lump of granite) Duration: Instantaneous You imbue yourself with the elemental power of a landslide. When you cast this spell, you immediately move 30 feet in a straight line without provoking opportunity attacks. If your path is blocked by a creature or object, you stop moving there, and that creature or object must make a Strength saving throw or take 2d12 bludgeoning damage, or half as much on a successful save. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d12 for every two slot levels above 1st.
