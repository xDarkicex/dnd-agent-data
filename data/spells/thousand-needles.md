# Thousand Needles

**description**: 4th-level conjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: 20 feet Components: V, S, M (a handful of needles, which have been used to draw blood from an unwilling creature) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You conjure a multitude of tiny needles which embed themselves deep in your target’s flesh. One creature you can see within range must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 6d6 piercing damage and become sickened for the spell's duration. Each time the creature takes an action or bonus action, it takes 1d6 piercing damage. This damage cannot be reduced or negated in any way. The creature may make a new Constitution saving throw at the end of each of its turns to end the effect. On a successful save the creature takes half damage and suffers none of the other effects of the spell. At Higher Levels. For every spell level beyond 4th, the initial damage increases by 1d6. For every 2 levels beyond 4th, the secondary damage increases by 1d6.
