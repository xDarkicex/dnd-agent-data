# Synaptic Static

**description**: Level 5 Enchantment (Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard) Casting Time: Action Range: 120 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous You cause psychic energy to erupt at a point within range. Each creature in a 20-foot-radius Sphere centered on that point makes an Intelligence saving throw, taking 8d6 Psychic damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one. On a failed save , a target also has muddled thoughts for 1 minute. During that time, it subtracts 1d6 from all its attack rolls and ability checks , as well as any Constitution saving throws to maintain Concentration . The target makes an Intelligence saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
