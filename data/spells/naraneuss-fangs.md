# Naraneus's Fangs

**description**: Glowing javelins of poison streak from your hand to four different points you can see within range, leaving vaporous trails of dark green mist that reek of burning skin in their wake. Each creature in a 40-foot-radius sphere centered on each point you choose must make a Dexterity saving throw. The sphere spreads around corners. A creature takes 20d6 poison damage and 20d6 slashing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature in the area of more than one poisonous deluge is affected only once. The spell leaves objects in the area poisoned and untouchable, including any items being worn or carried. All creatures affected by the spell are poisoned for 1 round per point of poison damage taken. All items remain poisonous for 1 day or until washed clean.
