# Lesser Amplify Desert

**description**: 5th-level lair magic (transmutation) Casting Time: 2 weeks (14 days) Radius: 2 miles Components: V, S, M (A rare material or materials found within the local environment worth at least 5000 gold pieces, which the spell consumes) Duration: 1 year This spell amplifies the naturally dangerous effects of the surrounding desert (or other similar environment), making it difficult for all who travel within it. When this spell is cast, the caster may choose up to eight creatures, including themself, who may ignore the effects of the spell. Alternatively, the caster can designate a passphrase that must be known or a symbol that must be worn. Harmful effects of this spell do not affect native creatures unless their Intelligence score is greater than 3. The terrain within the radius becomes exceptionally hot and dry, and wind swiftly whisks away what little water exists in the area. Creatures passing through the area must make a Constitution saving throw every half-hour against dehydration or gain a level of exhaustion. This saving throw is made at disadvantage unless the creature is drinking twice as much water as it normally requires.
