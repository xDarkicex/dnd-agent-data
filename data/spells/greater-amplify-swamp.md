# Greater Amplify Swamp

**description**: 9th-level lair magic (transmutation) Casting Time: 2 weeks (14 days) Radius: 4 miles Components: V, S, M (A rare material or materials found within the local environment worth at least 6000 gold pieces, which the spell consumes) Duration: 1 year This spell functions like lesser amplify swamp, except that for every half-hour a creature spends in the area, it must make a Constitution saving throw or become poisoned for 1 hour. If a creature was already poisoned, it becomes sickened instead. If the creature was sickened, it gains 1 death saving throw failure and the duration of the sickened condition is extended by an hour. These saving throws are made with advantage if a creature is using a breathing filter that can keep out poisonous gasses.
