# Healing Infusion

**description**: You place a single healing infusion on a willing creature you touch. At any point during the spell’s duration, you can end this spell as a bonus action and the creature regains a number of hit points equal to 1d6 + your spellcasting ability modifier. You must be within 60 feet of the creature to end the spell in this way. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the healing increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.
