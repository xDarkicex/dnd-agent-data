# Dreamwine

**description**: You imbue a bottle of wine with fey magic. While casting this spell, you and up to seven other creatures can drink the imbued wine. At the completion of the casting, each creature that drank the wine can see invisible creatures and objects as if they were visible, can see into the Ethereal plane, and has advantage on Charisma checks and saving throws for the duration. Ethereal creatures and objects appear ghostly and translucent.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 4
- School: divination
- Components: V S M
- Casting Time: 1 minute

**publisher**: Kobold Press
