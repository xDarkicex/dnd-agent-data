# Jungle’s Whisper

**description**: 2nd-level divination Casting Time : 1 action Range : Self Components : V, S, M (a leaf from a tropical plant) Duration : Concentration, up to 1 hour Classes : Bard, Cleric, Druid, Ranger Drawing upon the mystical connections of the jungle, you attune your senses to the environment and the creatures that dwell within. The whispers of the jungle grant you insight and knowledge, aiding you in acquiring information or tracking specific targets. You gain advantage on Wisdom (Nature) checks to recall information about plants, animals, and natural phenomena. Additionally, you can decipher tracks and find resources, gaining advantage on Wisdom (Survival) checks made in jungle environments. You gain an intuitive sense of the presence of creatures within 120 feet of you. You can detect their general location and approximate number, although you cannot discern their exact identity or specific details about them. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the duration of the spell increases by 1 hour for each slot level above 2nd.
