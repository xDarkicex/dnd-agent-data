# Boreas’s Kiss 

**description**: 1st-Level Conjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: 1 minute You encapsulate a creature within range in a pocket of fiercely cold air. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or drop any object in hand. They also have disadvantage on any cold-based saving throws until they make a successful Constitution saving throw. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you may extend the duration by 1 minute. Alternatively, for every two levels above 1st level, you may instead choose to have the target suffer a level of exhaustion on the first failed saving throw, up to a maximum of three levels of exhaustion.
