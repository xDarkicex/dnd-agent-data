# Spy

**description**: Divination cantrip Casting time: 1 action Range: 5 feet Components: v Duration: 1 minute You create an inter-dimensional opening the size of a spyglass lens in a space you can see within range. A similar opening appears in a space within 120 feet of you that you have visited in the last hour. Any creature can look into either opening and see through the opposite opening as if the creature were in the opening’s space.
