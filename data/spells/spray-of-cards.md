# Spray of Cards

**description**: 2nd-Level Conjuration Casting Time : 1 action Range: Self (15-foot cone) Components: V, S, M (a deck of cards) Duration: Instantaneous You spray a 15-foot cone of spectral cards. Each creature in that area must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 2d10 force damage and has the blinded condition until the end of its next turn. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage only. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 2nd.
