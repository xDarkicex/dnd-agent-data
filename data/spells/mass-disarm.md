# Mass Disarm

**description**: 4th-level abjuration Casting time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: v, s Duration: instantaneous You magically disarm creatures, giving you and your allies an upper edge in battle. Choose up to five creatures you can see within range. Each target must succeed on a dexterity saving throw or drop any weapons and shield it is holding. Instead of choosing multiple targets, you can choose to focus this spell’s energy on only one creature you can see within range. If you do so, the target must succeed on a dexterity saving throw or any weapons and shield it is holding teleport to the ground in a space within 5 feet of you.
