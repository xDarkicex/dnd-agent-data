# Silver Storm

**description**: 5th-level transmutation Can be cast by sorcerers and wizards Casting Time : 1 action Range: Self (70-foot cone) Components: V, S, M (a handful of silver shavings worth 2 sp) Duration: Instantaneous You toss a handful of silver shavings into the air, and as you chant the spell, they transform into a hail of glinting, shining needles that whip forward at tremendous speed. Each creature in a 70-foot cone must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 10d4 piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. If the creature is adversely affected by silver (such as a creature that has vulnerability to silvered weapons or loses its resistance to silvered weapons), it instead takes 10d8 piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, you can increase the size of the cone by 10 feet per slot level above 5th.
