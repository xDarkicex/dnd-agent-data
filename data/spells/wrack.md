# Wrack

**description**: 3rd-level enchantment Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S, M (the preserved spinal cord and peripheral nerves of an animal) Duration: Instantaneous You create an instantaneous psychic link with a creature you can see within range, sending waves of physical and emotional pain coursing through its body and mind. That creature must succeed on an Intelligence saving throw or take 4d8 psychic damage and be stunned until the end of your next turn. On a successful saving throw, the creature takes half damage and is sickened until the start of its next turn instead. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage dealt increases by 2d8 per spell level beyond 3rd.
