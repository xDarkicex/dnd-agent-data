# Galvanic Arc

**description**: You shock a creature, altering its sense of combat. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the creature takes 5d6 lightning damage and has its place in the initiative count reduced or increased by 10. For example, Kardiff takes his turn on initiative count 16 and hits a hill giant who has an initiative of 15 with this spell. Kardiff elects to reduce the hill giant’s initiative by 10, so it will instead take its turn on initiative count 5. Alternatively, Kardiff could elect to increase the hill giant’s initiative by 10 so that it would take its turn on initiative count 25, meaning it wouldn’t act until the next turn. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 2nd.
