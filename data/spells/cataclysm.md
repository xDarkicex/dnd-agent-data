# Cataclysm

**description**: The next time you hit a creature with a weapon attack before this spell ends, the attack deals an extra 1d8 psychic damage and the target must make a Wisdom saving throw as crackling energy surrounds it. On a failed save, the target has disadvantage on the next attack they make before the end of your next turn. At Higher Levels . When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can increase the damage by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.
