# Summon Blood Demon

**description**: Sacrificing 1d6 + your Constitution modifier HP, you summon a Lesser Blood Demon under your control in an unoccupied space within 30 feet of you. You can use your bonus action to command its movement and actions. The Demon has an HP equal of 1d10 + your spell casting modifier. If the Summoner is C/any (Chaotic) the Demon has resistance to Necrotic and Poison damage. If the Summoner is L/any (Lawful) the Demon has resistance to Fire and Thunder damage. If the summoner is N/any (Neutral) the Demon has resistance to Force and Cold damage. The Demon's AC is equal to your AC – 1. The Demon's attack is unarmed (+5 to hit) and its damage is 1d6 Piercing. When you reach level 10 add another 1d10 to Demon's HP, another 1d6 to its damage, and another 2d6 to your sacrificed HP. When you reach level 15 add another 2d10 to Demon's HP, another 2d6 to its damage, and another 2d6 to your sacrificed HP. When you reach level 20 add another 2d10 to Demon's HP, another 2d6 to its damage, and another 2d6 to your sacrificed HP.
