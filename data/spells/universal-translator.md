# Universal Translator

**description**: You grant one creature the ability to understand any spoken language it hears. Moreover, when the target speaks, any creature that knows at least one language and can hear the target understands what it says.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 3
- School: Clairsentient
- Expansion: 27051
- Casting Time: 1 action

**publisher**: Alligator Alley Entertainment

**book**: Esper Genesis Core Manual
