# Mindstab

**description**: As part of the action used to cast this spell, you must make a melee attack with a dagger against one creature within the spell's range, otherwise the spell fails. On a hit, the target suffers the attack's normal effects, and the head of the creature becomes wreathed in psychic energy until the start of your next turn. If the target makes an attack that doesn’t include you as a target, it immediately takes 1d6 psychic damage, and the spell ends. This spell’s damage increases when you reach higher levels. At 5th level, the melee attack deals an extra 1d6 psychic damage to the target, and the damage the target takes for attacking another creature increases to 2d6. Both damage rolls increase by 1d6 at 11th level and 17th level.
