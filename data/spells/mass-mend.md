# Mass Mend

**description**: You send a group of medical drones to a point of your choice within range to provide emergency restorative salves. Choose up to six creatures in a 30-foot radius sphere centered on that point. Each creature regains hit points equal to 3d8 + your forging ability modifier. This ability has no effect on automatons or constructs. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 6 or higher, the healing increases by 1d8 for each rank above 5.
