# Lesser Restoration

**Level**: 2
**School**: abjuration
**Classes**: bard, cleric, druid, paladin, ranger
**Casting Time**: bonusAction
**Range**: Touch
**Components**: ['v', 's']
**Duration**: Instantaneous

## Description

You touch a creature and end one condition on it: Blinded, Deafened, Paralyzed, or Poisoned.
