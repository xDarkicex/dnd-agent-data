# Ghoul King's Cloak

**description**: 8th-Level Transmutation Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You touch a creature, giving it some of the power of a ghoul king. The target gains the following benefits for the Duration: Its Armor Class increases by 2, to a maximum of 20. When it uses the Attack action to make a melee weapon attack or a ranged weapon attack, it can make one additional attack of the same kind. It is immune to necrotic damage and radiant damage. It can’t be reduced to less than 1 hit point. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a 9th-level spell slot, the spell lasts for 10 minutes and doesn’t require concentration.
