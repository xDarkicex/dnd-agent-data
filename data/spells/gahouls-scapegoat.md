# Gahoul's Scapegoat

**description**: 4th-level abjuration Casting Time: 1 minute Range: Self Components: V, S, M (black chalk, paint, or oil) Duration: 8 hours You trace a complex arcane symbol into the flesh of a willing creature within 5 feet of you, marking them for sacrifice. During the spell’s duration, when you are hit with an attack and the marked creature is within 60 feet of you, you can use your reaction to teleport, swapping places with the creature and causing it to be hit by the attack instead of you. The spell then ends.
