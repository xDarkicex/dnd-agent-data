# Electric Jolt

**description**: You discharge built up static in a burst around you. Each creature within range, other than you, must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take 1d6 lightning damage. If you roll a 1 or 2 on a damage die for this spell and you moved at least 10 feet during your turn before casting this spell, you can reroll the die and must use the new roll, even if the new roll is a 1 or 2. This spell’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
