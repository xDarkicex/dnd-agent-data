# Phantom Confessor

**description**: Phantom Confessor Requirements: Level 3 Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Duration: 10 minutes Cost: 3 WP In your mind’s eye, converse with or interrogate any one person you can see at the time: you can ask up to 3 questions. You can use Perception or Insight checks as you would in an actual conversation. If you have at hand a Likeness instead of the actual person, you can have this conversation with the depicted Likeness of that person. The avatar will respond as the actual person would, so is able to dissemble or lie. The avatar can’t refuse to interact, however. The target is not aware of the use of this feature.
