# Stutters of Confusion

**description**: Stutters of Confusion Requirements: Level 6, Poison Tongue Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Duration: special Cost: 1 WP per –1 on target’s rolls, up to philosopher’s proficiency bonus Embedded within a curiously halting speech, the philosopher’s poisoned words cause confusion in any one target who listens and fails its Wisdom saving throw. The philosopher has to speak only one or two sentences, just a few seconds really, and then let the words fester in the victim’s mind. At any point in time after that, the philosopher can unleash the effect with just one more word uttered. The target does not have to be present at this point in time. The target suffers a penalty determined by the number of WP spent (see cost) on all rolls except damage rolls until they can have a short rest.
