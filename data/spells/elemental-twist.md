# Elemental Twist

**description**: 2nd-Level Evocation Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, S, M (a thin piece of copper twisted around itself) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute During this spell’s duration, reality shifts around you whenever you cast a spell that deals acid, cold, fire, lightning, poison, or thunder damage. Assign each damage type a number and roll a d6 to determine the type of damage this casting of the spell deals. In addition, the spell’s damage increases by 1d6. All other properties or effects of the spell are unchanged.
