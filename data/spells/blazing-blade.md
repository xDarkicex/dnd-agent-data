# Blazing Blade

**description**: You touch a nonmagical weapon. Until the spell ends, that weapon becomes a magic weapon that deals an additional 1d6 fire damage. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the bonus fire damage increases to 2d6. When you use a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the bonus fire damage increases to 3d6.
