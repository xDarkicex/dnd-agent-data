# Gainsay

**description**: You can change the mouth or vocal chords of a creature within range so that it cannot speak or make similar sounds. Choose one creature that you can see within range to make a Constitution saving throw. If it fails, the target cannot speak, make sounds with its mouth, or perform spells that require a verbal component for the duration. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make a Constitution saving throw. On a success, the spell ends.

**properties**:

- Save: Constitution
- Level: 3
- School: transmutation
