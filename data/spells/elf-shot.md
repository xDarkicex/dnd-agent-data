# Elf Shot

**description**: 1st-level enchantment Casting time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: v, m (a pixie arrow) Duration: concentration, up to 1 minute You pretend to shoot an arrow with a tiny bow. Make a ranged spell attack against a creature within range. On a hit, the target creature stumbles (is pushed) 5 feet in a random direction (roll a d8 and assign a direction to each die face), and its speed is 0 until the end of its next turn. Until the spell ends, you can make the attack again on each of your turns as an action.
