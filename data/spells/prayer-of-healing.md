# Prayer of Healing

**description**: Level 2 Abjuration (Cleric, Paladin) Casting Time: 10 minutes Range: 30 feet Components: V Duration: Instantaneous Up to five creatures of your choice who remain within range for the spell’s entire casting gain the benefits of a Short Rest and also regain 2d8 Hit Points.
