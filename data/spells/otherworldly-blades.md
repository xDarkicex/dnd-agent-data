# Otherworldly Blades

**description**: 2nd-level Conjuration Range: Self (10 ft radius) Components: V, M Duration: Instantaneous You can move one or more of your portals to an empty spot on the ground that you can see within range. Then, choose one of your portals; each enemy within 10 ft. of it is pierced by otherworldly blades of energy, emerging through the portal from the Plane of Darkness. Each enemy in the area must make a DEX SAVE or suffer 3d6 cold damage as the warmth is sapped from their bodies and their speed becomes 0 until the start of your next turn. On a successful save, the creature takes half damage, but suffers no other effect. The range for this spell increases to match your Portal Conjurer feature range.
