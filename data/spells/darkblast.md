# Darkblast

**description**: You launch a blast of necrotic energy at a single creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit, the target takes 1d6 necrotic damage and its hit point maximum decreases by an amount equal to half the damage dealt. This reduction lasts until the creature finishes a short rest. The target dies if this effect reduces its hit point maximum to 0. This spell’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
