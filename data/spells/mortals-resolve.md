# Mortal's Resolve

**description**: You steady the resolve, inspiring them to fight with more determination. For the spell’s duration, bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage that the target creature takes from nonmagical weapons is reduced by 2. Additionally, the creature has advantage on death saving throws and treats any death saving throw roll of an 18 or 19 as a 20. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.
