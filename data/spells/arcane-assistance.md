**description**: Arcane Assistance Requirements: Level 6, Timely Assistance Casting Time: bonus action Range: 10 feet Duration: special Cost: 2 WP As Timely Assistance, but it helps with spellcasting, granting advantage to any required rolls by the caster, such as concentration checks or spell attack rolls. If used together with the Weave Mastery feature Overcome Boundaries this allows a one-step range increase (self becomes touch, touch becomes 5 feet, 5 feet and greater ranges are doubled) or a die step increase in damage as with Martial Assistance.
