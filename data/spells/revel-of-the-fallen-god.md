# Revel of the Fallen God

**description**: You summon four satyrs, which appear in unoccupied spaces that you can see within range. A satyr disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends. The satyrs are friendly to you and your companions for the duration. Roll initiative for the satyrs, which have their own turns. They obey any verbal commands that you issue to them (no action required by you), as long as they don’t violate its alignment. If you don’t issue any commands to the satyrs, they defends themselves from hostile creatures but otherwise takes no actions. See the entry for Satyr for statistics (found in the Monster Manual ).
