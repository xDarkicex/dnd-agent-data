# Draconic Senses

**description**: 4th-Level Divination Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self Components: V, S Duration: 8 hours You enhance your senses, granting yourself the keen perception of a dragon. For the duration of the spell, you gain darkvision out to a range of 60 feet. If you already have darkvision, its range increases by 60 feet. You also gain blindsight out to a range of 60 feet.
