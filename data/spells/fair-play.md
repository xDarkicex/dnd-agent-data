# Fair Play

**description**: Fair Play 2nd-level abjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute A creature of your choice within range gains a benefit when fighting larger creatures. Whenever they make an attack roll or skill check against a creature that is one size or more larger than themselves, they have advantage on the roll. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 2nd.
