# Mortality

**description**: Malefic word, divination Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous, ongoing (see this malefic word’s baleful echo) Effects: You speak a word that portents doom, and inevitability coils itself around one creature within range. The next time the target is hit by a melee or ranged attack before the end of your next turn, the hit is automatically a critical hit. Baleful Echo (2 vitality): You must select one creature within range that has not been targeted by this malefic word in the last 24 hours. The next time this target is hit by a melee or ranged attack before the end of your next turn, the hit is automatically a critical hit. Feedback: The word returns to whisper your own fate in your ears. The next time you are hit by a melee or ranged attack, the hit is automatically a critical hit. If 24 hours pass without you being hit by an attack, the feedback ends.
