# Dimir Machinations

**description**: You ensorcel a creature within range to forget how to cast a spell, use a special ability, or perform a specific legendary action. You declare a single spell, special ability that has the recharge trait, or legendary action that you have seen the creature use. The creature must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, the creature cannot use the selected option for the spell’s duration.
