# Chameleon's Mask

**description**: As a part of this spell and as an action on each subsequent turn in which you maintain concentration on this spell, you can take the Hide action but make an Intelligence (Arcana) check instead of a Dexterity (Stealth) check to hide. You can attempt to hide even when you are only lightly obscured by foliage, heavy rain, falling snow, mist, and other natural phenomena. Additionally, you can hide even if you are not obscured so long as you are adjacent to a solid surface. When you do, Wisdom (Perception) checks made to see you have disadvantage, and you have advantage on your check made to hide, as your form’s color shifts to camouflage you.
