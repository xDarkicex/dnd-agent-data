# Armor of Agathys

**description**: Level 1 Abjuration (Warlock) Casting Time: Bonus Action Range: Self Components: V, S, M (a shard of blue glass) Duration: 1 hour Protective magical frost surrounds you. You gain 5 Temporary Hit Points . If a creature hits you with a melee attack roll before the spell ends, the creature takes 5 Cold damage . The spell ends early if you have no Temporary Hit Points . Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot. The Temporary Hit Points and the Cold damage both increase by 5 for each spell slot level above 1.
