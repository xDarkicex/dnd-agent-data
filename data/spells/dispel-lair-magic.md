# Dispel Lair Magic

**description**: 5th-level lair magic (abjuration) Casting Time: 1 week (7 days) Radius: 10 feet Components: V, S, M (powdered silver worth 500 gold pieces, which the spell consumes) Duration: Instantaneous When you cast this spell, make a spellcasting ability check to dispel magic. If the result of this check is greater than 10 plus the level of any lair magic whose focus is within the radius of this spell, that lair magic immediately ends.
