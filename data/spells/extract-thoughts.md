# Extract Thoughts

**description**: 5th-level divination Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 3 rounds You magically dig your fingers through your target's skull, making contact with their brain and inflicting horrific pain in the process. A creature you can see within range must make an Intelligence saving throw or be paralyzed for the spell's duration. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of their turn, ending the effect on a success. Each round that the spell is active, you must spend your action maintaining the spell, or the spell ends early. The second time you spend an action maintaining the spell, you can glean detailed knowledge from the target's mind. You gain all relevant information for the knowledge you seek, but must specify which knowledge you seek (ie. "give me everything this creature knows about Xafirex and his lair"). Once you have gained the knowledge you sought, the spell ends. When the spell ends, the creature becomes sickened for 1 minute, or until it succeeds a Constitution saving throw. If the spell ends as a result of you gaining knowledge, the creature knows what knowledge you gained, and becomes frightened of you for 1 minute.
