# Show of Confidence

**description**: You brandish the weapon used in the spell’s casting and teleport to an unoccupied space adjacent to a target creature within 30 feet of you. Make a melee attack against the target creature. At 5th level, the melee attack deals an extra 1d8 force damage to the target on a hit. The extra damage increases by 1d8 at 11th level (2d8) and 17th level (3d8).
