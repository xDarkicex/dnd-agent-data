# Mystic Snake

**description**: 1st-level Conjuration Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self (60-foot line) Target: Self (60-foot line) Components: V, S, M (a snake skull) Duration: Instantaneous You shape your mystical energies in the form of a shimmering snake; the snake flies outward along the line, then darts back to you. Make a melee spell attack against every enemy in the line. On a hit, the first enemy takes 3d6 poison damage; then each following enemy takes an extra d6 poison damage, up to 5d6. If you hit at least 3 enemies and you don’t miss any attack, you regain the spell slot spent to cast this spell. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the initial and maximum damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.
