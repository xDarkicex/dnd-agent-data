# Invoke Chaos

**description**: Chaos erupts from four different points you can see within range. Roll on the Invoke Chaos table to determine the effect that arises from each point, as though you had just cast the spell in question, either centered on that point or originating from it. If any of the effects require concentration, you can maintain concentration on more than one at a time. If the duration of an effect is normally longer than 1 minute, the duration is shortened to 1 minute for this version of the spell.
