# Lifetrap

**description**: 1st-level transmutation Can be cast by druids and rangers Casting Time : 1 action Range : 150 feet Compon ents: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute This spell causes the plants in the area of effect to rise up and entangle a single creature within range. At the end of the spell’s duration, the plants release their life force into the creature; if the target is undead, this energy can shatter the bond between spirit and flesh. The target must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be restrained until the spell ends. A creature restrained by the plants can use its action to make a Strength check against your spell save DC. On a success, it frees itself. You can end the spell as a bonus action. When you end the spell, if the target is undead and is still restrained, it suffers 1d12 radiant damage for each turn it remained restrained. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you may affect one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.
