# Biting Steel

**description**: Tier 1 fire au gmentation Casting Time: 1 action Base Favor Cost: 0 Range: Touch Components (Required): V, S Resonances: You are at a famous historical battlefield (1 bonus favor), you are wielding one or more awakened weapons (1 bonus favor), you can see the sun from your current location (1 bonus favor) Duration: 1 minute Effects: You imbue a weapon in range with flame’s passion and force.
