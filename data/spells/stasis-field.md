# Stasis Field

**description**: Choose a humanoid that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or be paralyzed for the duration. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make another Wisdom saving throw. On a success, the effect ends on the target. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 3 or higher, you can target one additional humanoid for each rank above 2. The humanoids must be within 30 feet of each other when you target them.
