# Power Word Pain (Midgard)

**description**: When you utter the power word for pain, one creature within 60 feet takes 22 (4d10) force damage. At the start of each of its turns, the creature must make a successful Constitution saving throw or take another 22 (4d10) force damage. The effect ends when the creature's Constitution saving throw succeeds.
