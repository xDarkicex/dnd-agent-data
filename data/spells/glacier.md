# Glacier

**description**: The moisture in the area coalesces and momentarily solidifies into a chunk of ice at a point you choose within range. Each creature in a 5-foot-radius sphere centered on that point must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking 1d12 cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature who fails the saving throw also has its speed reduced to 0 until the end of your next turn. At Higher Levels . When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d12 for each slot level above 1st.
