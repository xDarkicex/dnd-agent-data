# Tranquilizing Toxin

**description**: One target you choose within range must make a Constitution saving throw or take 8d6 poison damage and become poisoned until the end of your next turn. While poisoned in this way, the target is also stunned. On a successful saving throw, the target takes half damage and is not poisoned. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the poison damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 3rd.
