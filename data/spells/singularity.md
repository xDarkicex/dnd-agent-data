# Singularity

**description**: You point toward an area within range and create an expanding globe of gravimetric force which engulfs everything within its area. Each creature in a 20-foot radius sphere centered on that point must make a Dexterity saving throw. A target takes 8d6 force damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. The sphere also does damage to structures and objects in the area that aren’t being worn or carried. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 4 or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each rank above 3.
