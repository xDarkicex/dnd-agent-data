# Lightning Strike

**description**: You cause a lance of lightning to appear and strike a creature of your choice that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take 1d6 lightning damage. This spell’s damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Save: Dexterity
- Level: 0
- School: evocation
- Components: V, S
- Damage Type: lightning
- Casting Time: 1 action
- data-RangeAoe: 300 feet
