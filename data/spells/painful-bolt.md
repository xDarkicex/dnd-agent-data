# Painful Bolt

**description**: Necromancy cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S, M (a rusted iron fishhook) Duration: Instantaneous You make a ranged spell attack roll against a creature you can see in range. On a hit, the target takes 1d4 psychic damage, and has their Injury threshold reduced by 5 (to a minimum of 5) until they receive an Injury or until the end of your next turn, whichever comes sooner. This spell’s damage increases by 1d4 when you reach 5th level (2d4), 11th level (3d4), and 17th level (4d4). The Injury threshold reduction increases to 10 when you reach 11th level.
