# Debilitate

**description**: Malefic word, necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: 45 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous, ongoing (see this malefic word’s baleful echo) Effects: The malefic Word of Power you speak slips from your lips, sapping the strength of one creature within range. The chosen creature must make a Constitution saving throw. If it fails, it becomes maimed (–10 ft. speed and disadvantage on Dexterity saving throws, removed after it regains HP) and weakened (it loses its damage resistances, and its damage immunities become resistances) until the end of your next turn. If the target succeeds, the malefic word’s baleful echo is silenced (and you do not suffer the feedback). Baleful Echo (1 vitality): The target remains maimed and weakened until the end of your next turn. Feedback: The sickening syllables crawl into the roots of your mind, leaving you weak and sickened. You become maimed and weakened until the end of your next turn.
