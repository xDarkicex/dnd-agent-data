# Erase

**description**: You remove writing from a single scroll, one page of paper, a sheet of parchment, or a similar surface that you can see within range that is no larger than a 2-foot-square area. This spell can also automatically remove a glyph of warding or a glyph created by the symbol spell. If the glyph was created using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you must make an ability check using your spellcasting ability. The DC equals 10 + the spell’s level. On a successful check, the glyph is erased.
