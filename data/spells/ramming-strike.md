# Ramming Strike

**description**: The first time you hit with a melee weapon attack during this power’s duration, your weapon explodes with a crushing force that is audible within 300 feet of you, dealing an extra 2d6 thunder damage to the target. Additionally, if the target is a creature, it must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be pushed 10 feet away from you and knocked prone.
