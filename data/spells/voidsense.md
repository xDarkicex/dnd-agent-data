# Voidsense

**description**: If you travel far enough into the Braythian Void, it’s easy to get lost. This spell allows the caster to find the exact direction for one of several possible locations. You have to be traveling in the Braythian Void for the spell to work, even if it’s within a vehicle. Voidsense allows you to find one of the following: Your own home world, if it is one of the world-islands of Braythe A different world-island of Braythe An Old Beast of Braythe The closest natural portal or conduit leading out of the dimension of Braythe All but the first option requires you to have been at the intended location for at least 8 hours somewhere in the past. You have to choose which location you want to find, and you cannot change the intended location after casting the spell.
