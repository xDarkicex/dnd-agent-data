# Give Life

**description**: Transmutation cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous Placing your hand on another creature, you can transfer your own life force to them. You spend and roll one of your Hit Dice, add your spellcasting modifier, and the creature regains that many hit points.
