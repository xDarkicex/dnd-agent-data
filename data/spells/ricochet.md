# Ricochet

**description**: As part of the action used to cast this spell, you must make a ranged attack with a weapon against one creature within the spell’s range, otherwise the spell fails. On a hit, the target suffers the attack’s normal effects, and the fired projectile bounces from the target to a different creature of your choice that you can see within 15 feet of it. The second creature takes damage equal to the weapon’s damage dice with no modifiers. This spell’s damage increases when you reach higher levels. At 5th level, the ranged attack deals an extra 1d8 damage to the target, and the damage to the second creature increases to the weapon’s damage dice + your spellcasting ability modifier. Both damage rolls increase by 1d8 at 11th and 17th level.
