# Restorative Burst

**description**: A wave of primal healing energies bursts out from you. Each creature within 15 feet of you regains hit points equal to 3d8 + your spellcasting ability modifier. Dying creatures receive the maximum amount of healing possible from this spell. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.
