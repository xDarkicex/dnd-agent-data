# Greater Amplify Volcano

**description**: 9th-level lair magic (transmutation) Casting Time: 2 weeks (14 days) Radius: 4 miles Components: V, S, M (A rare material or materials found within the local environment worth at least 6000 gold pieces, which the spell consumes) Duration: 1 year This spell functions like lesser amplify volcano, except that the volcano also actively hurls fireballs at each intruder every 10 minutes. Each creature in a 10-foot radius sphere centered on the intruder must make a Dexterity saving throw. The sphere spreads around corners. A creature takes 2d6 fire and 2d6 bludgeoning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature in the area of more than one fiery burst is affected only once. The spell damages objects in the area and ignites flammable objects that aren’t being worn or carried.
