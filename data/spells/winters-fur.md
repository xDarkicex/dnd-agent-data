# Winters Fur

**description**: Winter’s Fur 3rd-level transmutation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, M (piece of mammoth fur) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour You and up to seven willing creatures in range are suddenly covered in a thick, warm fur of a color of your choice. The fur protects against wind, water, and snow and grants resistance to non-magical cold damage. When the duration ends all of the fur falls off in floating clumps.
