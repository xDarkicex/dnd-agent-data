# Vile Incantation

**description**: 1st-level transmutation Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (the gall of a tree, or a tumor taken from an living creature) Duration: Instantaneous You chant a spell that twists the flesh of your enemy, causing them great pain and suffering. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 2d6 force damage and be inflicted with a core Injury of your choice. This Injury must be of the lowest severity the creature can gain (Setback I for PCs, Dangerous II for monsters and NPCs). On a successful save the creature suffers half damage and is not Injured. Any creature immune to polymorph effects, such as a shapechanger, or not made of organic matter has advantage on the saving throw. Any creature that is incorporeal is not affected by this spell. This spell cannot inflict an Injury if the creature already has one of the severity that would be inflicted. At Higher Levels. For every spell level above 1st, increase the damage by 2d6, and for every two spell levels, increase the severity of the Injury by one step.
