# Anneal

**description**: 1st-level transmutation Casting time: 1 action Range: touch Components: v, s, m (a pinch of iron filings) Duration: 1 hour You touch a nonmagical melee weapon, temporarily making it stronger, sharper, and more destructive. Until the spell ends, the weapon deals an extra 1d6 damage of its type on a hit. This spell doesn’t make the weapon magical. At higher levels. When you cast this spell using a slot of 3rd level or higher, you can target one additional weapon within your reach for each slot level above 2nd.
