# Blood Rune

**description**: You stab into your own palm, drawing a rune on the ground directly below your feet. Once finished, the rune lights up and fills 15-foot radius centred on the caster with a red mist. Each creature that is within the mist when cast, starts its turn in the mist, or enters the mist for the first time on its turn, must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, they take 4d8 Necrotic damage as their blood begins to feed the mist, however on a successful save they take half damage instead. Any Creature that is killed by the mist, feeds the mists duration for an extra turn. The cost of this spell is 2d6 HP, however, the caster can use an extra 1d6 HP to increase the area of effect by 5ft (this can be done indefinitely, as long as there is space for the mist and the caster has sufficient HP). This spell does not affect the caster At higher levels: The spell does 1d8 more damage per spell slot above 5th.
