# Claws Of Darkness

**description**: You shape shadows into claws that grow from your fingers and drip inky blackness. You can make a melee spell attack with these claws that does 1d10 cold damage, and the claws have a reach of 10 feet.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 0
- School: Evocation (Shadow)
- Components: V S
- Damage Type: Cold
- Casting Time: 1 action

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Midgard Heroes Handbook
