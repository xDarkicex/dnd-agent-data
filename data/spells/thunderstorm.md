# Thunderstorm

**description**: 5th-Level Transmutation Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (a piece of lightning-fused glass) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 hour You touch a willing creature, and it becomes surrounded by a roiling storm cloud 30 feet in diameter, erupting with (harmless) thunder and lightning. The creature gains a flying Speed of 60 feet. The cloud heavily obscures the creature inside it from view, though it is transparent to the creature itself.
