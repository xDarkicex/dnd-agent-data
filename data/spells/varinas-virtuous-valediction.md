# Varina's Virtuous Valediction

**description**: 1st-level evocation Casting time: 1 reaction, which you take when a creature that isn’t a construct or undead drops to 0 hit points within 60 feet of you Range: 60 feet Components: v, s Duration: instantaneous You cause the spirit of a dying creature to briefly appear in a flash of holy light. Each creature of your choice within 10 feet of the target must make a constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 2d6 radiant damage and is blinded until the end of its next turn. On a successful save, the creature takes half the damage and isn’t blinded. At higher levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.
