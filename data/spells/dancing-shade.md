# Dancing Shade

**description**: As you cast this spell, you animate your shadow to shield you from harm. Your dancing shade can absorb damage up to half your current hit points (up to 70) when casting the spell before dissipating. This protection extends to physical attacks that deal bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing damage as well as spells and effects that deal necrotic, radiant, or lightning damage. When the shadow absorbs the maximum amount of hit points possible, it dissipates, and you lose your shadow until you complete a short rest.
