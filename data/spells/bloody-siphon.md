# Bloody Siphon

**description**: 4th-level necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous Making rapid twisting motions with your fingers, pointing out your targets, you cause their flesh to tear and blood from their wounds to gather in front of you. When you cast this spell, choose up to four living creatures within range. Each target must make a Constitution saving throw or lose 3d8 hit points and gain a special Injury, “Siphoned” of Setback I severity. This Injury recovers automatically at the end of a long rest. On a successful save the creatureloses half as many hit points and is not Injured. For each Injury you inflicted as a result of this spell, you may choose yourself or another creature within range (up to once per creature). That creature regains 3d8 hit points and may make a Constitution saving throw against the Temporary Recovery DC for any Injury from which it currently suffers. On a successful saving throw, that Injury is removed and the creature gains the appropriate Lasting Effect. At Higher Levels. Whenever you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you can choose an additional target for the spell for each spell level beyond 4th.
