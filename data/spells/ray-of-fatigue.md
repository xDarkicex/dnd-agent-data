# Ray of Fatigue

**description**: Necromancy cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: 90 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous A beam of red energy springs from your finger toward a creature within range. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or suffers one level of exhaustion until the end of its next turn. On a successful save, the effect ends on it. You can affect more creatures when you reach higher levels: two at 5th level, three at 11th level and four at 17th level. A target can be affected up to twice by this cantrip.
