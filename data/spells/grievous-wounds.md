# Grievous Wounds

**description**: Choose a creature you can see within range. You momentarily strip the target's physiology of all resilience, enough that a mere scratch can become a terrible injury. Until the end of your next turn, the target loses any damage resistances or immunities, becomes vulnerable to all damage, and makes all its saving throws with disadvantage.
