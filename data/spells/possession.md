# Possession

**description**: Malefic word, necromancy Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous, ongoing (see Baleful Echo) Effects: You speak a malefic word of power that forces an evil spirit into the corpse of a medium-sized non-undead humanoid creature within range. The creature rises as a summoned undead (see Summoned Undead) and becomes bound to you. Baleful Echo (1 vitality): You reaffirm your dominion over the creature, causing it to stay bound to you (see Bound and Unbound Undead). When you silence this baleful echo, the creature becomes unbound. Feedback: The undead creature becomes unbound (see Bound and Unbound Undead). For one minute, it can telepathically sense your location and hungers insatiably for your flesh.
