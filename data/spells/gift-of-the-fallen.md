# Gift of the Fallen

**description**: You can touch any creature that has blood, that died within the last 10 minutes and draw out the last vestiges of life force from its blood, doing so gives you advantage on your next spell attack. Additionally, you can roll a 1d4 and use the result to reduce the damage you take on your next use of Subsidiary Exsanguination.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 0
- School: necromancy
- Components: V, S
- Casting Time: 1 Action or 1 Bonus Action

**publisher**: Haigo Dungeoncrafts

**book**: The Blood Mage
