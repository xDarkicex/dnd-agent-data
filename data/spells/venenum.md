# Venenum

**description**: Conjuration Cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous Make a ranged spell attack against a creature you can see within range. On a hit, the creature is struck by noxious energies and takes 1d8 poison damage. A Poisoned creature takes 1d12 poison damage instead. The damage of this spell is increased by one die when you reach 5th level (2d8 or 2d12), 11th level (3d8 or 3d12), and 17th level (4d8 or 4d12).
