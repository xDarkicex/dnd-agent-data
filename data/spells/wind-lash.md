# Wind Lash

**description**: Evocation Cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: 20 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous Your swift gesture creates a solid lash of howling wind. Make a melee spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d8 slashing damage from the shearing wind and is pushed 5 feet away from you. The spell’s damage increases by 1d8 when you reach 5th level (2d8), 11th level (3d8), and 17th level (4d8).
