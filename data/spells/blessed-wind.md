# Blessed Wind

**description**: You send forth a wind of healing energy into one creature you touch. The creature regains all hit points, loses all levels of exhaustion, is cured of all diseases and conditions, and all negative spell effects on the creature end. Additionally, the creature is considered to have completed a short rest.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 9
- School: enchantment
- Components: V, S
- Casting Time: 1 action
- data-RangeAoe: Touch
