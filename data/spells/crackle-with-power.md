# Crackle with Power

**description**: You draw forth magical energies and loose them at up to three creatures within range. You choose acid, cold, fire, lightning, poison, or thunder when you cast this spell. Make a ranged spell attack for each target. On a hit, the target takes 8d10 damage of the type you chose. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, you can target one additional creature and the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 5th.
