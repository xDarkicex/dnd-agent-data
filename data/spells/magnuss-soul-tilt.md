# Magnus's Soul Tilt

**description**: Make a melee spell attack against a creature you can reach. On a hit, the target takes 3d10 necrotic damage. You gain temporary hit points equal to half the damage dealt. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 2nd.
