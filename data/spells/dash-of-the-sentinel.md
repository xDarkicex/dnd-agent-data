# Dash of the Sentinel

**description**: 5th-level evocation Casting time: 1 action Range: self (100-foot line) Components: v s m (a jagged twig) Duration: instantaneous You magically launch yourself forward. You move up to 100 feet in a straight line and can move through the space of any creature. This movement doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks. The first time you enter a creature’s space during this move, that creature must make a dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 10d6 force damage and is knocked prone if it is your size or smaller. On a successful save, a creature takes half the damage and isn’t knocked prone. When you move through a creature’s space, you can choose to not affect it. At higher levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 5th.
