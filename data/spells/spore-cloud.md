# Spore Cloud

**description**: You produce a cloud of virulent spores. Each creature within 5 feet of you, other than you, must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d4 poison damage. A creature that fails the saving throw by 5 or more is poisoned until the end of its next turn. The spell’s damage increases by 1d4 when you reach 5th level ( 2d4 ), 11th level ( 3d4 ), and 17th level ( 4d4 ).
