# Blood Boil

**description**: You extend necrotic power to reach into the blood of creatures within a 15-foot radius of you and channel power into the blood cells directly, causing them to heat up and the creatures’ blood to boil inside. The caster chooses which creatures they target within range. Each creature that the caster targets must make a Constitution saving throw vs your spell save DC. On a failed save, a creature suffers 1d8 necrotic damage & 1d8 Fire damage. On a successful save the target only takes half the damage. At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage (of both types) increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 3rd.
