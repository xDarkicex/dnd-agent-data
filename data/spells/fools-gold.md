# Fool's Gold

**description**: Conjuration cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: M (1 gold coin) Duration: Concentration, up to 24 hours When you cast this spell, you enchant the coin you used as the material component for this spell. At any point before the spell ends, you can summon the coin back to your hand. Doing so ends the spell.
