# Lance

**description**: You create a shimmering lance of force and hurl it toward a creature you can see within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 5d6 force damage, and it is restrained by the lance until the end of its next turn.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 2
- School: conjuration
- Components: V S
- Casting Time: 1 action
- data-RangeAoe: 30 feet

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Tome of Heroes
