# Templar’s Mercy

**description**: 2nd-level evocation Casting time: 1 reaction, which you take when a creature you can see within 30 feet of you is reduced to 0 hit points or makes a death saving throw Range: 30 feet Components: v, s Duration: instantaneous Your quick prayer prevents catastrophe. The target regains 1 hit point. The triggering effect is a death saving throw, the target regains the hit point immediately, even if the saving throw is the target’s third failed death saving throw.
