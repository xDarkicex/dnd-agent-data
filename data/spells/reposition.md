# Reposition

**description**: 4th-Level Conjuration Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: 30 feet Components: V Duration: Instantaneous You designate up to three friendly creatures (one of which can be yourself) within range. Each target teleports to an unoccupied space of its choosing that it can see within 30 feet of itself. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, the spell targets one additional friendly creature for each slot level above 4th.
