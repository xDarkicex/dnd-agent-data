# Flaming Discs

**description**: You create three discs of intense flame that spin rapidly above you. When you activate this power or as a bonus action on each of your turns, you can launch any number of these discs, each one at any target within range. Make a ranged channeling attack for each disc. On a hit, the target takes 2d6 fire damage. Each disc can only be launched once, after which they disappear regardless of whether or not they hit a target. At the end of the duration, all of the discs disappear. At Higher Ranks. When you use this power at rank 3 or higher, you create one additional disc for each rank above 2.
