# Gift Of Resilience

**description**: A willing creature you touch has advantage on the first three death saving throws it attempts before the duration of the spell expires. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th, 6th, or 7th level, the maximum duration increases to 48 hours. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 8th or 9th level, the maximum duration increases to 72 hours.
