# Portal Shift

**description**: 1st-level Conjuration Range: 60 ft. Components: V, S, M Duration: Instantaneous Choose a creature that you can see within range. That creature takes 2d6 psychic damage and must make a CHA SAVE or be teleported to an empty spot on the ground that you can see within range that is within 5 ft. of one of your Portals. You can choose not to inflict the damage; a creature can choose to fail its save voluntarily. The range for this spell increases to match your Portal Conjurer feature range.
