# Chandra's Pyrohelix

**description**: You create a helix of flame that moves to encircle up to two target creatures within range. Each target creature must make a Dexterity saving throw. A target takes 3d6 fire damage, or half as much damage on a successful one. If you selected one target instead of three, the target instead takes 5d6 fire damage on a failure, or half as much on a successful one. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 3rd.
