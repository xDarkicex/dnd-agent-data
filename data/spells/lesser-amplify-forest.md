# Lesser Amplify Forest

**description**: 5th-level lair magic (transmutation) Casting Time: 2 weeks (14 days) Radius: 2 miles Components: V, S, M (A rare material or materials found within the local environment worth at least 5000 gold pieces, which the spell consumes) Duration: 1 year This spell amplifies the naturally dangerous effects of the surrounding forest (or other similar environment), making it difficult for all who travel within it. When this spell is cast, the caster can choose up to eight creatures, including themself, who ignore the effects of the spell. Alternatively, the caster may designate a passphrase that must be known or a symbol that must be worn. Harmful effects of this spell do not affect native creatures unless their Intelligence score is greater than 3. The trees of the forest begin to overproduce pollen, clouding the air. When a creature enters the forest, and again every hour of travel thereafter, it must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the DC for Concentration checks made by the creature while in the forest are increased by 5 and creatures in the area have disadvantage on Stealth checks due to constant coughing and sneezing. This effect lasts until the creature leaves the forest. A strong wind can clear the air of pollen, and if sustained for at least 30 minutes the effect of the pollen subsides as though affected creatures had left the forest until the wind dies down.
