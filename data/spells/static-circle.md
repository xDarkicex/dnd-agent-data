# Static Circle

**description**: You extend your arms outward, emitting short streams of electrostatic energy. Each creature of your choice within 5 feet of you must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or take 1d6 lightning damage. This power’s power increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
