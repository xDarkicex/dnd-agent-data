# Demolish

**description**: You swing your hand toward a creature or object you can see within range and project a solid destructive force from your hand. Make a melee spell attack against the target. You have advantage if the target is an object. On a hit, the target takes 1d8 force damage. When you target an object with this spell and deal damage, you ignore damage threshold and maximize your damage dice against the target. This spell’s damage increases by 1d8 when you reach 5th level (2d8), 11th level (3d8), and 17th level (4d8).
