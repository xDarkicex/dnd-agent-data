# Mud

**description**: The ground in a 20-foot radius centered on a point within range becomes thick, viscous mud. The area becomes difficult terrain for the duration. When a creature enters the affected area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there, the creature must make a Strength saving throw. On a failed save, its movement speed is reduced to 0 until the start of its next turn.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 2
- School: transmutation
- Casting Time: 1 action
- data-RangeAoe: 60 feet
