# Destructive Wave

**description**: Level 5 Evocation (Paladin) Casting Time: Action Range: Self Components: V Duration: Instantaneous Destructive energy ripples outward from you in a 30-foot Emanation . Each creature you choose in the Emanation makes a Constitution saving throw . On a failed save , a target takes 5d6 Thunder damage and 5d6 Radiant or Necrotic damage (your choice) and has the Prone condition. On a successful save , a target takes half as much damage only.
