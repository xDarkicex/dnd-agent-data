# Spiral Defender

**description**: As a reaction, you activate a group of small energy shields that spiral upward around your body to deflect an incoming attack from a target you choose. The target’s attack has disadvantage to its attack roll.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 1
- School: Technique
- Expansion: 27051
- Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you are targeted by an attack but before the roll is made.
- data-RangeAoe: Self

**publisher**: Alligator Alley Entertainment

**book**: Esper Genesis Core Manual
