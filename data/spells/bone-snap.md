# Bone Snap

**description**: 2nd-level enchantment Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when you see a creature that has a skeletal system within 30 feet of you attempt to move Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: 1 round Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard You weave a magical grip around the bones of a creature that tries to move. As a reaction, when you see a creature that has a skeletal system move, you can snap your fingers to attempt to slow them down. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw. On a failure, the target’s speed is reduced by 10 feet, they can’t take reactions, and they take 2d10 force damage as your magic begins to crack their bones from within. On a success, they take half as much damage and are able to move and take reactions normally. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the target's speed is reduced by an additional 5 feet and the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 2nd.
