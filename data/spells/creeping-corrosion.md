# Creeping Corrosion

**description**: A wave of transmutation magic erupts from a point of your choice within range. Each nonmagical metal object that is being held or worn in a 10-foot-radius sphere centered on that point must make a Constitution saving throw using the creature that is holding or wearing the object’s saving throw. On a failure, any weapon made of metal carried by the creature begins to corrode. After dealing damage, the weapon takes a permanent and cumulative -1 penalty to damage rolls. If its penalty drops to -5, the weapon is destroyed. Ammunition made of metal that hits is destroyed after dealing damage. If the object is either metal armor or a metal shield being worn or carried, it takes a permanent and cumulative -1 penalty to the AC it offers. Armor reduced to an AC of 10 or a shield that drops to a +0 bonus is destroyed. All nonmagical metal objects that are not being carried or worn in the area automatically fail their saving throw and begin to corrode. If the object isn’t being worn or carried, the wave destroys a 1-foot cube of it every round for the duration.
