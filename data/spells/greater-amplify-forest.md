# Greater Amplify Forest

**description**: 9th-level lair magic (transmutation) Casting Time: 2 weeks (14 days) Radius: 4 miles Components: V, S, M (A rare material or materials found within the local environment worth at least 6000 gold pieces, which the spell consumes) Duration: 1 year This spell functions like lesser amplify forest, except that an adult green dragon is also summoned to defend the lair's entrance. Additionally, the pollen weakens intruders’ immune systems, giving them disadvantage on Constitution saving throws against poison and disease while in the forest, and the forest is enchanted to have permanently still air (winds strong enough to disperse the pollen cannot occur in the forest).
