# Harfang's Hoarfrost 

**description**: 4th-level transmutation Casting time: 1 bonus action Range: touch Components: v, s, m (a piece of ammunition for a ranged weapon) Duration: 1 hour Crackling ice coats a ranged weapon you touch. When you make a ranged weapon attack with that weapon during the spell’s duration, the ammunition becomes wreathed in frost. Make the attack roll as normal. On a hit, the weapon deals an extra 2d8 cold damage, and the target must succeed on a constitution saving throw or become restrained by ice until the end of its next turn. The spell lasts for the duration or until you make six ranged weapon attacks with the weapon. At higher levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you can make one additional ranged weapon attack with the weapon before the spell ends for each slot level above 4th.
