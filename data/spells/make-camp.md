# Make Camp

**description**: 1st-level abjuration (ritual) Casting Time: 1 minute Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (a tent) Duration: 8 hours When you cast this spell while setting up camp, the area within 20 feet of where you cast this spell gains the following benefits until the spell ends: Nonmagical weather and climate effects will not harm anyone within the area. Beasts will not enter the area unless magically compelled to do so. Creatures outside the area have disadvantage on ability checks to perceive or find the camp, unless you choose otherwise. Creatures that complete a long rest in the area gain 1d6 temporary hit points at the end of the long rest.
