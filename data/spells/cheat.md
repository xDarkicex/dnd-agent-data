# Cheat

**description**: Divination cantrip Casting Time: 1 bonus action Range: Self Components: S, M (a weighted die) Duration: 1 round You subtly twist your fingers, and fate seems to follow suit. For the duration, you can reroll any ability check you make to play nonmagical games of chance. Therefore, this spell could influence a game of poker, but not the result of a deck of many things .
