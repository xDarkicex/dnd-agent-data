# Flip sides

**description**: By casting this spell, you can teleport yourself and up to seven willing creatures of your choice to the opposite side of the world-island you are on. You have to be on a world-island of Braythe for the spell to work. Your target location is the exact same spot on the world’s other side, as if you traveled downwards or upwards in a straight line. Effectively, the distance to the World-Chains and, roughly, to the edges of the world-island stays the same. If the target location does not provide solid ground to stand on, the spell automatically fails. Aside from that, the teleportation magic does not prevent the caster and his companions from ending up in a dangerous situation.
