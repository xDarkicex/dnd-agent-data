# Blood Letting

**description**: You slit the artery of one willing creature, causing 1d4 slashing damage. Using your knowledge of blood magic, you draw out any Curses, Diseases or Poisons that are currently affecting the creature. In doing so, the target creature takes 1d10 necrotic damage for each curse, disease or poison cured in this way. After the spell ends, the wound seals itself leaving a small scar in its place. If a creature was cured of two or more ailments, then it suffers one point of exhaustion.

**properties**:

- Level: 2
