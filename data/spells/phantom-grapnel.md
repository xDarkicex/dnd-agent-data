# Phantom Grapnel

**description**: Evocation cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous You conjure a chain and hook made of magical force, which you propel at a creature or unoccupied space you can see within range. When you target a space or a creature of Huge size or larger, your grapnel pulls you to that target in a straight line. You provoke opportunity attacks for this movement as normal. When you target a creature of Large size or smaller, you pull the target up to 10 feet toward you. A creature can make a Strength saving throw to resist this movement.
