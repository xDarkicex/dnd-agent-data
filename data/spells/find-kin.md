# Find Kin

**description**: Touch one willing creature or make a melee spell attack on an unwilling creature. If the target fails a Wisdom saving throw, you learn the identity, appearance, and general location (such as “5 miles north” or “in the city of Vidim”) of one random living blood relative of the target. A creature may choose to automatically fail this save.
