# Blessing of DexMya

**description**: Choose up to six creatures within range that can hear you and utter the words of DexMya’s blessing. For the duration of the spell, each affected creature gains 8 temporary hit points and has advantage on all saving throws. At Higher Levels . When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the temporary hit points increase by 8 for each slot level above 3rd.
