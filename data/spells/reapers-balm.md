# Reaper's Balm

**description**: 2nd-level necromancy Casting time: 1 action Range: touch Components: v, s, m (a pinch of grave dirt mixed with resin) Duration: 1 hour Your touch wards a creature against death. For the duration, the target has advantage on death saving throws. If a dying creature affected by this spell succeeds on three death saving throws and becomes stable, the spell ends.
