# Radiant Rosette

**description**: 2nd-level conjuration Casting time: 1 action Range: self Components: v, s, m (a dandelion stalk) Duration: concentration, up to 1 minute A rosette of magic resembling a dandelion of energy appears in your hand. The magical flower remains there for the duration. When you cast this spell, you blow gently on the rosette, sending a stream of glowing seeds at a creature you can see within 60 feet of you. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 3d6 radiant damage. Until the spell ends, you can blow on the rosette and make the attack again on each of your turns as an action. At higher levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 2nd.
