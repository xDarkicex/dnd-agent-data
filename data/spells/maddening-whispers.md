# Maddening Whispers

**description**: You whisper a string of Void Speech toward a target that can hear you. The target must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or be incapacitated. While incapacitated by this spell, the target’s speed is 0 and it can’t benefit from increases to its speed. To maintain the effect, you must use your action on subsequent turns to continue whispering; otherwise, the spell ends. The spell also ends if the target takes damage.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 2
- School: void
- Components: V, S
- Casting Time: 1 action
- data-RangeAoe: 30 feet
