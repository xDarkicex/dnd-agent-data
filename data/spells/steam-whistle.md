# Steam Whistle

**description**: 8th-Level Evocation Casting Time: 1 action Range: Self (30-foot radius) Components: V, S, M (a small brass whistle) Duration: Instantaneous You open your mouth and unleash a shattering scream. All other creatures in a 30-foot radius around you take 10d10 thunder damage and are deafened for 1d8 hours. A successful Constitution saving throw halves the damage and reduces the deafness to 1 round.
