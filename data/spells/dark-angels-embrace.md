# Dark Angel's Embrace

**description**: 7th-level transmutation Casting Time: 1 action Range: Touch Components: V, S, M (an angel’s pure white flight feather dipped in tar, which the spell consumes) Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You touch a willing creature, empowering them with the traits of a dark angel. Tattered, black feathered wings sprout from their shoulders, magically leaving holes in their clothing and armor to allow their growth. The creature's eyes take on the glimmer of burning embers, and their fingers grow into claws. A burning halo appears over their head. While the transformation lasts, the creature gains a fly speed of 60 feet, gains a +2 bonus to AC, and has advantage on Strength and Dexterity checks and all saving throws against spells and magical effects. Additionally, they gain 70 temporary hit points. While these hit points last, the creature's attacks deal an additional 1d8 necrotic damage and 1d8 fire damage, and are considered magical. The creature's claws are natural finesse weapons and deal 1d6 slashing damage. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 8th level or higher, the target gains an additional 15 temporary hit points for each level beyond 7th.
