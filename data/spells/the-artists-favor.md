# The Artist's Favor

**description**: The Artist’s Favor Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Duration: 1 round Cost: – You carefully craft small inert Likenesses (no invested WP) of your party members (or any other characters well-known to you) and carry them with you for unlimited use. Such a Favor Likeness can be activated by quickly sketching a small symbol on it while the target is within 30 feet. The symbol vanishes moments after use. Each Favor Likeness can depict only one person, so each round you can only have one single target. Depending on the symbol used, you can impart one of the following effects on the target until the start of your next turn: Grant the target damage resistance to one type of damage of your choice Change of damage type inflicted by the target Make a weapon held by the target briefly magical
