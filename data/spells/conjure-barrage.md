# Conjure Barrage

**description**: Level 3 Conjuration (Ranger) Casting Time: Action Range: Self Components: V, S, M (a Melee or Ranged weapon worth at least 1 CP) Duration: Instantaneous You brandish the weapon used to cast the spell and conjure similar spectral weapons (or ammunition appropriate to the weapon ) that launch forward and then disappear. Each creature of your choice that you can see in a 60-foot Cone makes a Dexterity saving throw , taking 5d8 Force damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one. Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot. The damage increases by 1d8 for each spell slot level above 3.
