# Shadowcraft Evocation

**description**: You unleash bursts of magical energy through potent illusions that are partly material. You can choose to emulate any evocation spell of 3rd-level or lower that deals damage and has a casting time of 1 action by casting this spell. Before the regular effects of the replicated spell are determined, the target must first succeed on a Wisdom saving throw to disbelieve the illusion. On a failed save, the spell deals full damage. On a success, the spell only deals half damage. The damage type of the spell is determined by the spell being replicated.
