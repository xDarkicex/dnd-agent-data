# Curse of Blades

**description**: 3rd-level enchantment Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V, S Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute Unholy bands of black energy tie a creature to its weapon. Choose a creature you can see within range that is holding a weapon to make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the creature is cursed for the duration. A cursed target can’t willingly drop or stow its weapon. Additionally, whenever it takes the Attack action on its turn, it makes one additional attack using its weapon targeting itself, against its own AC, dealing damage as normal on a hit. A remove curse spell ends this curse early.
