# illusion of Health

**description**: Illusion cantrip Casting time: 1 action Range: self Components: v, s Duration: 10 minutes An illusion of yourself in your usual state of health and condition overlays your current form. This illusion doesn’t exaggerate your usual state of health, but it conceals wounds, injuries, and any symptoms of disease or poison, including marks, tears, or stains on your clothing or armor. This illusion changes no other facet of your appearance; it simply presents you and your worn equipment in a healthy, whole state. You aren’t healed of any damage or cured of any disease, and any symptoms or effects from your conditions or ailments continue, such as a cough from a disease or the smell of blood if you are actively bleeding.
