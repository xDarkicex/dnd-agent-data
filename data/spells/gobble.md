# Gobble

**description**: 1st-level Transmutation Casting Time: 1 Action Range/Area: Self Components: V, S Duration: 1 hour Your stomach takes on the properties of a Bag of Holding . Any object that can fit in your mouth is safely stored for the duration of the spell and can be regurgitated at will. Your stomach can hold up to 500 pounds, not exceeding a volume of 64 cubic feet. When the spell ends, any objects remaining inside your stomach are also regurgitated. At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the duration extends for 1 hour for each slot level above 1st.
