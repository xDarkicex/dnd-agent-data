# Things Might Be Different

**description**: Things Might Be Different Requirements: Level 11 Casting Time: 1 minute ritual Range: touch (the Likeness) Duration: special Cost: 3 WP initially, then 2WP per day Imbue a Likeness you have created with Weave Points to incrementally alter reality. For example, a depiction of a lowly person as a person of wealth and power will result in a trickle of good fortune towards that person until he or she has achieved the depicted state. This requires a steady expenditure of 1 Weave Point per day. If the expenditure of Weave Points ist ever halted, the effect ends, the scales of fate right themselves again, and the person is again master of their own luck. Whether they can hold on to their new status or wealth is up to them now. The feature can’t alter a person in any way that would normally require a level increase (so, no Ability increases, for example). It is possible to influence matters of fate surrounding the target, so changes in wealth, luck and public perception are possible. For double the daily Weave Point cost (4 WP), positive changes in mental and physical health are possible. Dismay points can be erased at a rate of 1 per 3 days, or you can completely cure a disease over the course of one week, or heal 2d6 hit points per day (at level 11 3d6, at level 15 4d6). It is not possible to reverse aging with this power.
