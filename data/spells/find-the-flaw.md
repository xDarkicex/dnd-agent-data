# Find The Flaw

**description**: You touch one creature. The next attack roll that creature makes against a clockwork or metal construct, or any machine, is a critical hit.

**properties**:
- Category: Spells
- Level: 1
- School: Divination (Clockwork)
- Components: V S M
- Casting Time: 1 action

**book**: Midgard Heroes Handbook
