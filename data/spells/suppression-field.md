# Suppression Field

**description**: Up to three creatures of your choice that you can see within range have a shimmering white field surround them. Until the spell ends, the creatures must make Charisma saving throws whenever they use an action to use a class feature, special ability, or cast a spell. On a failed save, the creature must choose a new action other than the action selected. If a targeted creature is targeted with an attack or spell that does damage, this effect ends for that creature.
