# Glare

**description**: You cast a disdainful glare at up to two creatures you can see within range. Each target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or take no actions on its next turn as it reassesses its life choices. If a creature fails the saving throw by 5 or more, it can’t take actions on its next two turns.
