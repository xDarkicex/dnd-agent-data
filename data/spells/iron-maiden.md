# Iron Maiden

**description**: 4th-level Evocation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 10 feet Components: V, S, M Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute Your target must make an INT SAVE or be encased in a metal coffin lid with spikes. When a creature starts its round in the Iron Maiden, they suffer 3d10 force damage and are paralyzed, but they have resistance to all other sources of damage. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make another Intelligence saving throw. On a success, the spell ends on the target.
