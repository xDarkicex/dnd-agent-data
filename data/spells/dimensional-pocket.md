# Dimensional Pocket

**description**: You create an invisible entrance up to 60 feet in the air directly above you that opens to an extradimensional space that lasts until the effect ends. The extradimensional space can be reached by standing directly below the entrance, which transports them into the space. The entrance can only transport one creature at a time. Exiting takes an action and returns the creature to the same spot. You can use an action to seal the entrance but cannot prevent those inside from exiting. Any attempt to exit by anyone inside the space reopens the entrance. The space can hold as many as eight Medium or smaller creatures and cannot be seen on the outside by mundane senses. Attacks and powers can’t cross through the entrance into or out of the extradimensional space, but those inside can see out of it as if through a 3-foot-by-5-foot window centered on the entrance. Anything inside the extradimensional space drops out when the effect ends.
