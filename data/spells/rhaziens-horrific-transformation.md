# Rhazien’s Horrific Transformation

**description**: 8th-level transmutation Casting Time: 1 action Range: 60 feet Components: V Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute You speak a word of power so great and terrible that all creatures that hear it block it from their minds in the same instant it is spoken. The word transforms the target into a misshapen mass of limbs, mouths, and tentacles. When you cast this spell, choose one creature within range. That creature must make a Constitution saving throw or transform into a Servant of Rhazien (see page 196) until the spell ends. While transformed in this way, the creature serves you and implicitly understands your desired commands. When it transforms, you can give it its initial set of instructions. You can telepathically issue new instructions as a bonus action. If the creature’s new form is reduced to 0 hit points, the spell ends. When the spell ends, the creature must make a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC or lose its memory of the experience. A creature that succeeds this saving throw is frightened of you and sickened for 1 minute. The creature can make a new Wisdom saving throw to end these conditions at the end of each of its turns.
