# Thunder Bolt

**description**: Evocation Cantrip Casting Time: 1 action Range: 30 feet Components: V, S Duration: Instantaneous You cast a knot of thunder at one enemy. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. If it hits, the target takes 1d8 thunder damage and can’t use reactions until the start of your next turn. The spell’s damage increases by 1d8 when you reach 5th level (2d8), 11th level (3d8), and 17th level (4d8).
