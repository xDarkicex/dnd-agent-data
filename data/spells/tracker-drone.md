# Tracker Drone

**description**: You forge a small surveillance drone with which you have a direct link (the GM has statistics for this creature). You can use your bonus action to command the drone to move in a certain direction or toward a particular area. For the duration, you can use the drone’s sensors in two ways. Data Display. The drone transmits audio and video to a datapad of your choice that you are carrying. The transmission is mundane and susceptible to signal jamming or interference. While receiving information from the drone in this way, you have disadvantage to your own Wisdom (Perception) checks. Direct Display. You can use your action to receive sensory input directly from the drone’s audio and visual sensors. This signal cannot be blocked, jammed, or intercepted in any way. While perceiving through the drone’s sensors, you gain the benefits of any special senses possessed by the drone, though you are blinded and deafened to your own surroundings.
