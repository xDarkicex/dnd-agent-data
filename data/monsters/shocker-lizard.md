# Shocker Lizard

**description**: A social beast that dwells in swamps and forests, the shocker lizard can be a suitable familiar for a wizard who specializes in lightning or storm magic. Taking a shocker lizard as a familiar requires an additional material component of 50 gp worth of fulgurite when the find familiar spell is cast.

**properties**:
- Category: Monsters
- Size: Small
- Type: Beast
- Alignment: unaligned
- Challenge Rating: 1.2

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Deep Magic Vol 1
