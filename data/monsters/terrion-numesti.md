# Terrion Numesti

**description**: When the PCs finally encounter him, Terrion Numesti is a prisoner in need of rescue. These stats work well should you create a rescue mission where the PCs are tasked with locating a relatively high-level NPC and bringing them back alive. Traits Formation Fighter. Terrion has advantage on all attack rolls as long as he is within 5 feet of two or more other allies that aren’t incapacitated. Martial Advantage. Once per turn, Terrion can deal an extra 17 (5d6) damage to a creature he hits with a weapon attack if that creature is within 5 feet of an ally of Terrion that isn’t incapacitated. Actions Multiattack. Terrion makes three longbow attacks or three longsword attacks. Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8+4) slashing damage, or 9 (1d10+4) slashing damage if used with two hands. If the target is a creature and is hit by two or more of Terrion’s attacks, it must make a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened of him. A frightened creature can attempt a new saving throw at the end of each of its turns to end this condition. Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, range 150 ft./600 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8+2) piercing damage.
