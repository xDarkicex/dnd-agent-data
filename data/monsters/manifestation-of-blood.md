# Manifestation of Blood

**description**: Traits Legendary Resistance (2/Day). If the Manifestation of Blood fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead. Siphoning Shroud. Bleeding creatures within 10 feet of the Manifestation of Blood count as rolling the maximum result when determining bleeding damage. Actions Multiattack. The Manifestation of Blood makes two attacks with its weeping blade and one attack with its feasting blade. Weeping Blade (Whip Sword). Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 10 ft. one target. Hit: 10 (1d6 + 6) slashing damage. Creatures that suffer damage from this attack gain the bleeding condition for 1 minute. If this is used to make an attack of opportunity, it deals an additional 1d6 slashing damage. Feasting Blade (Broad Saber). Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (1d6 + 6) slashing damage. If a creature suffers damage from this attack, the Manifestation of Blood regains that many hit points. Legendary Actions The Manifestation of Blood can take up to 2 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time, only at the end of another creature’s turn, and only if the Manifestation of Decay did not take a Legendary Action that turn. The Manifestation of Blood regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn. River of Gore. All creatures within 15 ft. of the Manifestation of Blood that have the bleeding condition make a DC16 Constitution saving throw. If they fail, they take 5 (1d8) piercing damage and ground within 5ft of them becomes coated in slippery blood, becoming difficult terrain. Halting Strike. The Manifestation of Blood makes one attack with its weeping blade. If the target is standing on difficult terrain, it must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw. If it fails, it is knocked prone. Whirl of Death. The Manifestation of Blood makes one attack with its feasting blade against all creatures within 5 ft. of it. Searing Blood. The Manifestation of Blood shimmers with heat and it and all bleeding creatures within 30 feet take 6 (1d10) fire damage. Blood Sacrifice. The Manifestation of Blood gestures at one Bloodspeaker ally within 30 feet. The Bloodspeaker immediately dies, and the Manifestation of Blood regains 4d8 hit points.
