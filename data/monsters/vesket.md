# Vesket

**description**: Thanks to the manipulations of a deceptive will-o’-wisp, the king of the Kamelands lizardfolk, Vesket, grows more cruel with time—drawing treacherously close to fully embracing evil. You can further adjust his alignment as you need depending on the type of lizardfolk king you require for your story. Alternatively, Vesket’s stats can be used to represent a member of a rival adventuring group comprised primarily of lizardfolk or other nontraditional ancestries. Traits Brave. Vesket makes three melee attacks, two with his trident and one with his bite. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d6+5) piercing damage. Trident.
