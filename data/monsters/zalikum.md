# Zalikum

**description**: An enormous vulture forged from sand and malignant power, a zalikum is created by mages who capture souls of the damned and infuse them into the superheated sands of the desert. The souls infusing a zalikum are from the Abyss. A zalikum’s creator can gather these souls from the Abyss but, more often, the creator makes a deal with a demon in exchange for the souls. Unfortunately for the creator, the demon usually hands over souls that aren’t the easiest to control, leading many creators to die at the talons of their zalikums. Such destruction frees the demon from its bonds, releasing it and the zalikum into the world. A zalikum doesn’t require air, food, drink, or sleep.
