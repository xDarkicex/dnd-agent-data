# Sunsprite

**description**: Sunsprite Tumbling and frolicking in one of the innumerable sunbeams of the Plane of Fey’s unending sunset, sunsprites are playful, inquisitive, and capricious creatures. Much like giving a cat a belly rub, playing with a sunsprite is fraught with danger. Though the sunsprite (or cat) may appear to be enjoying themselves, their mood can change in an instant, leaving you with naught but a patch of sunburn (or some cat claw scratches). Swarmy. When the cold, dark unseelie influence encroaches, the impetuous and temperamental sunsprites can band together to form an army - also known as a swarmy. As sparrows might mob a predatory bloodhawk, a squadron sunsprites fires devastating volleys from its sunbows when threatened by a predator, each toothpick-sized arrow an irradiating irritant in the hunter’s flank. As a swarmy, sunsprites lack the coordination to collectively turn invisible, but their speed and size make it almost impossible for an assailant to focus on any one individual. Buoyed by their safety in numbers, a swarm of sunsprites is far more tempestuous than any individual sprite.
