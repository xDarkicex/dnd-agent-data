# Funglet Gardner

**description**: These rank-and-file funglet warriors are equipped to deal with unwanted interlopers at the same time as tend to the fields. Their greatclubs are not only weapons, but tools used to tamp down the ground around disturbed fungi, while their slings function also as bags for transporting particular mushrooms set for replanting. Funglet gardeners think nothing of living out their whole existence in a relatively small area. Gardeners resemble closely the mushrooms they grow, as they impart some of their essence during the tending process. As a result, individual funglets find they feel most comfortable in the fields they tend, going so far as to plant a number of their fungi into their outer surface a reminder of home.
