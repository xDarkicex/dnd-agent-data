# Frenzied Mutant

**description**: The newborn of House Ausstyl deemed to become flesh-ripping mutant berserkers by Inheritors of the Unbegotten are raised like vicious animals. They are forcefully infused with the alien life forms venerated by their druidic masters and taught how to enhance the otherworldly symbionts in their bodies. The frenzied mutants are bred to be furious and mindless war machines that terrorize and rip their enemies apart. These barbarians don’t resemble a humanoid anymore, neither in appearance nor way of life.
