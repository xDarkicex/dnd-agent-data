# Enormous Flame Drake

**description**: A wide range of drakes dwell in the River Kingdoms, but it’s those who live long enough to grow particularly notorious that often attract the most attention. The enormous flame drake presented here has long menaced explorers of the Stolen Lands. All drakes look similar to dragons in basic form, yet they lack a front pair of legs. Many believe wyverns to be more properly categorized as drakes as a result. Flame drakes are bright red in color, with curling horns on their heads. Traits Keen Smell. The flame drake has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Smokesense. The flame drake can see through smoke. Smoke does not provide concealment against the flame drake. Speed Surge (3/Day). The flame drake can use Dash as a bonus action. Actions Multiattack. The flame drake attacks twice: once with its bite and once with its tail. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (2d8+5) piercing damage plus 4 (1d8) fire damage. Tail. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d6+5) bludgeoning damage. Fireball Breath (Recharge 6). The flame drake exhales a fireball to a point within 150 feet. Each creature in a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on that point must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 40 (9d8) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
