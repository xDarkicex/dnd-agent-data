# Swarm of Albino Eels

**description**: In the lightless depths of Underworld lakes, the albino eel makes its home. The parasitic eels attach themselves to the shell-folds of cave lobsters and kra’tah, and generally ignore fleshy creatures, like most humanoids. Wand Crafting 101. Though obtaining one is a challenge, even apprentice magic-users can forge a primitive wand of lightning bolt from an albino eel with relative ease.
