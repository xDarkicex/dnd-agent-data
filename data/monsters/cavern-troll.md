# Cavern Troll

**description**: Troll Trolls are one of the most notorious dangers of the Stolen Lands, be they encountered alone or as members of larger bands. Beyond the typical troll, other more dangerous variants of these creatures can be found in the region, each sharing a penchant for violence, sadism, and murderous cruelty. Cavern Troll Cavern trolls rarely venture to the surface world, for they have a debilitating vulnerability to sunlight. Their skin appears to be flinty, like stone, and is often studded with crystalline growths. Traits Keen Smell. The cavern troll has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Regeneration. The cavern troll regains 10 hit points at the start of his turn. If it takes acid or thunder damage, this trait doesn’t function at the start of the two-headed troll’s next turn. The cavern troll dies only if it starts his turn with 0 hit points and doesn’t regenerate. Sunlight Petrification. A cavern troll exposed to natural sunlight can move or take an action, but not both, nor can it take bonus actions. The troll must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw and the end of each of its rounds, turning to stone on a failed save. A greater restoration spell (or similar effect) restores a petrified cavern troll. Spells like sunburst and other effects that create powerful natural sunlight do not petrify the cavern troll, though it does become stunned until the end of its next turn after being exposed to such an effect. Actions Multiattack. The cavern troll makes three attacks: one with its bite and two with its claws. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d8+5) piercing damage. Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d8+5) slashing damage.
