# Swarm of Crawling Claws

**description**: A swarm of crawling claws is a horrid sight to behold. It acts like a normal swarm of insects but with a cruel, malevolent purpose. The swarm often attacks only a single creature at a time, using sheer numbers to overwhelm and knock a target prone so it can finish it with its flesh rending fingers. Unusual Nature. The ghastly claw and the swarm of crawling claws don't require air, food, drink, or sleep. Traits Swarm. The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Claw. The swarm can't regain hit points or gain temporary hit points. Turn Immunity. The swarm is immune to effects that turn Undead. Actions Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 19 (4d6 + 5) bludgeoning or slashing damage (Claw's choice), or 12 (2d6 + 5) bludgeoning or slashing damage if the swarm has half of its hit points or fewer, and the target must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
