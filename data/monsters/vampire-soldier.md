# Vampire Soldier

**description**: Traits Vampiric Toughness. When reduced to 0 hit points, the vampire does not die and is instead reduced to 1 hit point unless killed by a Death Blow. It has advantage on saving throws against Death Blows unless it is in direct sunlight, submerged in running water, or the Death Blow is made with a silvered weapon. Actions Multiattack. The vampire makes three Spear attacks Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., 1 target. Hit: 8 (2d4+4) piercing damage, plus 7 (2d6) necrotic damage. This attack is made with advantage against a creature grappled by the vampire. If this attack reduces a creature to 0 hit points, it must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or rise as a vampire spawn at midnight of the next day. Spear. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6+3) piercing damage. Vampiric Transformation (1/Rest). The vampire transforms into a bat, retaining its hit points and mental ability scores. This transformation lasts for 1 hour, or until the vampire ends it as a bonus action. The vampire can use its Bite attack in place of the bat’s attack.
