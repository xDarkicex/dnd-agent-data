# Kennie Flopwig

**description**: Pronunciation: “Ken-ee Flop-wig” Kennie Flopwig is Kiri’s twin brother and the Chief Data Executive of the Galactic Bank. He lacks Kiri’s gumption and charisma, but he is a genius with crunching numbers. He is also clumsier, both physically and socially, and Kiri often has to re-word his blunderous statements. Being around Darkstar Killagob makes Kennie even sweatier and more anxious than he usually is; he legitimately fears that if he says something overly annoying or awkward, Darkstar might kill him.
