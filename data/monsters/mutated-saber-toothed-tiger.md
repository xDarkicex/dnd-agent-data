# Mutated Saber-Toothed Tiger

**description**: A result of drow experimentation, the mutated saber-toothed tiger was an attempt to recreate the majesty of a displacer beast in a creature they could control. Blind Senses. The mutated saber-toothed tiger sees only by virtue of the tendrils grafted to its nervous system. It cannot sense creatures that are flying or are incorporeal, unless they come into contact with a tendril, in which case the mutated saber-toothed tiger can sense them for 1 round.
