# Sniper

**description**: Death from afar is the sniper's modus operandi. They are professional sharpshooters, trained to either kill or disable a target quickly and covertly. They often use customized firearms and other ranged weapons built to locate and bypass the defenses of their targets. Though most snipers are employed by military forces, some also work as freelancers or are contracted with private organizations.
