# The Maniae

**description**: In Greek mythology, the Maniae appear as the personifications of madness, frenzy and insanity. This horde of evil spirits are the offspring of Lyssa. Whenever Lyssa is upset or attacked, they will descend upon the group to protect their mother. Lyssa may, however, command them to keep their distance. In this case, the Maniae will hover above the heads of the adventurers or surround them like a swarm of giant insects. The Maniae are only semi-corporeal, but will inflict nasty wounds by biting and tearing into their prey with long claws. Some of them have the power to possess a mortal and turn him or her into a raging maniac. Despite their violent disposition, the Maniae are almost silent and will only utter hissing sounds that sting the ears like a cold wind. Traits Non-Corporeal. The Maniae can’t be hurt by anything but magic. Actions Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 2 (1d4) slashing damage, and the target must make a DC 8 Intelligence saving throw or become possessed by the maniae for one round and must attack, by whatever means possible, the nearest living target.
