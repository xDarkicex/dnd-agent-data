# Goblin Witch Doctor

**description**: Witch doctors are goblin priests who serve as the primary support for goblin war parties, as well as religious advisors to the chieftain. They hate and often clash with the tribal shamans. In combat, witch doctors mostly rely on their magical bolts that can curse those hit by them. Actions Dagger. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft, one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage. Cursed Bolt. Ranged Spell Attack: +3 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d8 + 1) psychic damage, and the target has disadvantage on attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws until the end of the goblin’s next turn. Spellcasting. The goblin casts one of the following spells using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 11, +3 to hit with spell attacks): At will: guidance, mending, thaumaturgy 1/day each: blindness/deafness, ray of sickness, hold person Bonus Actions Healing Word (1/Day). The goblin casts healing word using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability. Spiritual Weapon (1/Day). The goblin casts spiritual weapon using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability. Nimble Escape. The goblin takes the Disengage or Hide action.
