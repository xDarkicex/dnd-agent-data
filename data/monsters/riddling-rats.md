# Riddling Rats

**description**: This creature appears in the Puzzles, Predicaments, and Perplexities II module! Traits Keen Smell. The rat has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Sickly Saliva. If the rat pierces the skin on a bite attack, its saliva seeps into the blood stream of its target that causes confusion on a failed DC 8 Constitution saving throw. Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +1 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 2 (1d4) piercing damage.
