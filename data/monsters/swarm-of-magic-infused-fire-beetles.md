# Swarm of Magic-Infused Fire Beetles

**description**: When fire beetles gather into a swarm, their devastation can bring severe blight to any area they ravage. During the theocratic reign of the Order of the Eternal Light, fire beetles were regarded as sacred beings and bred them in hordes, sometimes resulting in rampages when the beats could not be contained. The Midnight Curse has infused a ginormous fire beetle with magic, and the swarms of fire beetles it spawns are also imbued with magical essences, making their attacks magical.
