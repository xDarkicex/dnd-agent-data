# Fyrkat

**description**: Fyrkat Fyrkat, a red dragonborn, is proud of her role among the völv and is among the strongest of the seerguard. Though she longed to become a völv herself in her youth, she never quite had the gift of seeing the future that she needed. Still, she is satisfied with her place. Roleplaying Fyrkat Experienced. Though Fyrkat is not a völv, she has stood guard so many times, overseeing diplomatic meetings between the völv and raid leaders that she has learned much. She knows most details about every other clan and is always willing to share those details if she feels it helps keeps the raiders united. Dual Loyalties. Many like herself return to their home communities, strengthening the binds between individual clans and the völv. Fyrkat has instead remained as a guard to the völv, though prior to recent events she had been intending to return to her homeland when her sixteen years of service come to an end, soon. Sensible. Though just as much a warrior as any other raider, the völv training helps Fyrkat keep reign of her emotions. She seldom succumbs to outbursts or fits of violence and looks down up those who are controlled by their emotions.
