# Hare

**description**: Snowshoe hares are gentle herbivores that live in burrows throughout Icewind Dale. They have shorter ears than other hares and are acclimated to cold weather. Actions Escape. The hare can take the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action as a bonus action on each of its turns.
