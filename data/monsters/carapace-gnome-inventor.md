# Carapace Gnome Inventor

**description**: A large group of gnomish inventors tried to prepare their kind for the apocalypse by inventing the Carapace Armor. When Ashandri shifted to Braythe, these armors exploded and merged with their wearers. This experience had a very different impact on their original creators: Some strive to find a way to undo what has happened, or to help those who were affected by it. Others have gone mad, whether from guilt, shock or the experience of being torn apart and put together again. The Carapace Gnome Inventors have used the time since the apocalypse to improve and optimize the armor that is merged with their body. This gives them superior abilities compared to other Carapace Gnomes.
