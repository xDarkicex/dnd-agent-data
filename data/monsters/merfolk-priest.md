# Merfolk Priest

**description**: Presented in this section are three merfolk nonplayer character examples: the merfolk priest, merfolk scout, and merfolk shaman. Traits Amphibious. The merfolk can breathe air and water. Actions Spear. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d6 + 1) piercing damage, or 5 (1d8 + 1) piercing damage if used with two hands to make a melee attack. Radiant Bolt. Ranged Spell Attack: +5 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (3d8 + 3) radiant damage, and the target is blinded until the start of the merfolk’s next turn. Spellcasting. The merfolk casts one of the following spells using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 13): At will: guidance, light, mending, thaumaturgy 2/day each: cure wounds, hold person 1/day each: control water, dispel magic, spirit guardians Tidal Blast (Recharge 5–6). The merfolk magically unleashes a powerful torrent of water in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw. On a failure, the target takes 22 (5d8) bludgeoning damage and, if it is Large or smaller, is pushed up to 10 feet away from the merfolk and knocked prone. On a success, the target takes half the bludgeoning damage, but is neither pushed nor knocked prone.
