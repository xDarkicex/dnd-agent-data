# Hellbeast

**description**: Actions Multiattack. The hellbeast makes one trample attack and one attack with its scything tusks. Trample. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage. This attack has advantage if the hellbeast moved at least 30 ft. before attacking this turn. If the attack roll exceeds the target’s AC by 4 or more, the target is knocked prone. Scything Tusks. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., any number of creatures within 5 feet of the hellbeast. Hit: 10 (1d12 + 4) slashing damage, and each target is subjected to the bleeding condition (1d4 piercing damage at the start of each of its turns, removed after it regains HP) for 1 minute. If a target already had the bleeding condition, it takes an additional 4 (1d8) slashing damage instead.
