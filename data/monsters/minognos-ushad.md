# Minognos-Ushad

**description**: Not all dragonslaying adventures require true dragons. Minognos-Ushad is a legendary wyvern of the Stolen Lands, but can equally serve in your game as a storied draconic enemy, particularly if you prefer your dragons to function more as feral beasts than intellectual villains. Alternately, these stats could serve as the basis of an entire species of powerful wyvern. Traits Legendary Resistance (3/Day). If Minognos-Ushad fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead. Actions Multiattack. Minognos-Ushad makes one bite attack, two claw attacks, and one stinger attack. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (1d12+10) piercing damage. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 19 (2d8+10) slashing damage plus. Instead of dealing damage, Minognos-Ushad can grapple the target (escape DC 19). Stinger. Melee Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target. Hit: 17 (2d6+10) piercing damage plus 10 (3d6) poison damage and the target must make a DC 19 Constitution saving throw, taking 10 (3d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much poison damage on a successful one. Reactions Reflexive Sting. When a creature fails to escape Minognos-Ushad’s grapple, Minognos-Ushad makes a stinger attack against the creature. Legendary Actions Minognos-Ushad can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature’s turn. Minognos-Ushad regains spent legendary actions at the start of his turn. Detect. Minognos-Ushad makes a Wisdom (Perception) check. Stinger Attack. Minognos-Ushad makes a stinger attack. Wing Attack (Costs 2 Actions). Minognos-Ushad beats his wings. Each creature within 10 feet of the dragon must succeed on a DC 19 Dexterity saving throw or take 17 (2d6+10) bludgeoning damage and be knocked prone. Minognos-Ushad can then fly up to half his flying speed.
