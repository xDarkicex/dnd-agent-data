# Svirfneblin Spy

**description**: Svirfneblin, more commonly known amongst other humanoid races as undergnomes, are an offshoot of the gnomish race. They embrace their fey heritage even more than their surface-dwelling cousins, combining it with their love of invention and engineering. Gemmancy. Many svirfneblin pursue the arcane mysteries inherent in precious stones. Svirf legend says that the world is but a gem with innumerable facets, and it is the refracting light that gives form to the world’s magic. It certainly true that secrets of potent magic lie within the many facets of a gem, and few outside the svirfneblin know of its existence. Technology. Though the devices themselves have a practical, decidedly svirf look, the magic that powers them is a natural one, drawn from the natural energies of gems imbued with the svirfneblin’s own heritage. Rare is the deep gnome who does not use some bit of technology to accomplish even mundane tasks.
