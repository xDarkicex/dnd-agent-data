# Shadow Cult Officer

**description**: Traits Shadowbond. While the shadow cult officer is in dim or dark lighting conditions, it gains a +2 bonus to AC and treats Injury thresholds as 5 lower. Shadow Command. While the shadow cult officer is in dim or dark lighting conditions, each of its allies in dim or dark lighting conditions within 120 ft. gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and a +2 bonus to damage rolls as shadows swirl outward and infuse their weapons with dark energies. Actions Shadowblade. Melee Spell Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (2d6+3) slashing damage, plus 7 (2d6) necrotic damage. This attack ignores cover and affects incorporeal creatures and creatures on the ethereal plane as though they were corporeal and on the material plane. Shadowbind. The shadow cult officer chooses two creatures in bright or dim light, binding their shadows and preventing them from moving. The targets must make a DC 13 Charisma saving throw or be restrained for 1 minute, or until the shadow cult officer uses this ability again. At the end of each of its turns, a target can make a new saving throw to end this effect. Targets in bright light have disadvantage on this saving throw.
