# Twisted Treant

**description**: A treant will awaken when its forest requires protection. A twisted treant is one that has been too long awake, its forest besieged or unbalanced. A twisted treant grows gaunt and gnarled as it wanders, searching for threats to its woods. Long years spent combating countless enemies leave a twisted treant bitter and paranoid. Even when the forest is quiet, a twisted treant is malcontent to sit idle, instead seeking evils both real and imagined. Paranoid Protectors. Folk tales that warn to “never leave the path” when crossing the forest speak of trees that move and shadows that watch. These tales are born of a twisted treant stalking travelers, measuring their intent and testing their nerve, all-too-often finding fault with both. Perhaps the only time a twisted treant remains still for any amount of time is when determining friend from foe. Zealous Guardians. All treants hold dear that which caused them to awaken in the first place: the cycle of the forest and a love of growing things. But even loggers who claim only fallen timbers and the most respectful of hunters fear a forest guarded by a twisted treant. To such a zealot, even campfires and maple syrup are a blight to be uprooted.
