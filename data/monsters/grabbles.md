# Grabbles

**description**: Grabbles is the leader of the mitflits who dwell under the Old Sycamore in the Stolen Lands, but works just as well as the leader of a band of any sort of group of gremlins if you wish. Alternately, he could be an unusual loner the PCs might encounter while exploring the wilderness; that rarest of mitflits who is powerful enough to make something of a life on their own. Though Grabbles is lawful evil, that doesn’t mean he has to be used as a foe the PCs must fight. He could be given another alignment, or he could be used simply as a pathetic bully the PCs might attempt to redeem or forgive. Traits Hatred. Grabbles doubles his proficiency bonus on attack rolls against humanoid creatures of the dwarf or gnome subtype due to special training against these hated foes. Innate Spellcasting. Grabbles’ innate spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 11). The mite can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: prestidigitation 1/day: bane Keen Smell. Grabbles has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Self-Loathing. Grabbles’s self-loathing makes him easy to influence. He has disadvantage on saving throws and ability checks to resist any Charisma-based skills or being charmed. Vermin Keeper. Through sounds and gestures, Grabbles can communicate simple ideas with arthropods. The knowledge and awareness of these arthropods is limited by their intelligence, but at minimum, they can provide information about nearby locations and monsters, including whatever they can perceive or have perceived within the past day. Grabbles can ask for small favors from the arthropods and has advantage on Wisdom (Animal Handling) checks when communicating with them. Actions Multiattack. Grabbles makes three trident attacks. Trident. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d6+1) piercing damage, or 5 (1d8+1) piercing damage if used with two hands to make a melee attack. Reactions Vengeful. When Grabbles is hit by an attack or fails a saving throw, he makes an attack against the creature that harmed him.
