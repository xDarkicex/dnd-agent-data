# Quipper

**description**: A quipper is a carnivorous fish with sharp teeth. Quippers can adapt to any aquatic environment, including cold subterranean lakes. They frequently gather in swarms; the statistics for a swarm of quippers appear here . Traits Blood Frenzy : The quipper has advantage on melee attack rolls against any creature that doesn't have all its hit points. Water Breathing : The quipper can breathe only underwater. Actions Bite : Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1 piercing damage.
