# Monstrous Seastar

**description**: When a monstrous seastar emerges from repose, muscular tentacles explode from the sand and fold inward toward its toothless central maw. These aggressive starfish average 7 to 10 feet in diameter and weigh 600 to 1000 pounds. Creeping Doom. Starfish on some alien ocean worlds contend with a greater number of more dangerous marine threats than on other worlds. Their resulting evolution produced faster, stronger specimens that recover from injury very quickly. Most unique among the species is that some giant seastars have no agenda or reproductive mechanism other than to split and regenerate after attacked by predators. Ironically, the number of natural enemies that find them delicious ensures their survival. One Too Many Mouths.
