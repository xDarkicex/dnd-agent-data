# Biddrina

**description**: Biddrina The biddrina is a predator that inhabits lowland marshes. This massive serpentine creature is armored in thick blue-green scales and with glowing red eyes and a massive head split wide to reveal rows of sharp teeth. It prowls the wetlands and adjacent areas, concealing itself in water or tall grass as it can, hunting for prey. It will often target settled areas near its lair, preferring the ease of herd animals and humanoids found in one place than hunting wild game. Prized Scales. Adventurers sometimes brave a biddrina’s territory to hunt and slay the beast. The scales and hide of the creature are prized as components in construction of armor of invulnerability.
