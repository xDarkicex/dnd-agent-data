# Captain Lysandra

**description**: Pronunciation: “Captain Lies-an-druh” Captain Lysandra is a commander in the Nautilid Flotilla who has come to Port Nomad to listen to Professor Orryn’s presentation about a new gnomish technology that will allow limited use of magic for ships and individuals in Dead Magic Zones. They hold no small amount of hope that this technology could finally be the answer to the nautilids’ endless search for a new homeworld with naturally occurring Poseidon Solution. A year ago, Lysandra's ship was blown up in what was reported as an “accidental drive malfunction”. She believes it was no accident, and suspects the Firm was behind the sabotage. The incident has motivated Lysandra further, and made them a little reckless and desperate, as their goal to find a new homeworld for their people continues to be hampered by outside forces beyond their control.
