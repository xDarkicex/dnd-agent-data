# Quintessa Maray

**description**: Quintessa Maray is a Daggermark spy who has convinced Baron Drelev that she is a poor, exiled noblewoman from Galt in need of shelter. These stats work well for any manipulative aristocrat skilled at getting her way and manipulating society with magic and deceit. They can also be used to represent the leader of a renowned theatrical troupe, a famous traveling entertainer, or even as a provocateur intent on destabilizing an evil regime. Traits Low Profile. The first time in a round anyone would attack Quintessa or target her with a spell or ability that allows a saving throw, they must make a DC 15 Intelligence or Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, they must choose a different target or take a different action not targeting Quintessa. Spellcasting. Quintessa is a 10th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 17, +9 to hit with spell attacks). Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60, one target. Hit: 6 (1d4+4) piercing damage.
