# Minimal Elephant

**description**: Minimals are small versions of normally Medium, Huge or Large sized beasts. Any beast (including, at the GM’s option, non-beast creatures that could be considered magical animals, like a unicorn), can become a minimal. When a creature becomes a minimal, it retains all its statistics except as noted below. Terrarium. Dinosaurs and their ilk are most commonly found as minimals on isolated islands in the Mediterranean where entire miniature pre-historic eco-systems can be found. Challenge. Recalculate the Challenge of the creature based on the guidelines in the 5E Game Master’s Guide to recalculate the rating after you apply the template. A quick way to do this is to reduce its Challenge by 1 for each size category it shrinks and for each die of damage it loses. Size. Reduce the creature to Small size and adjust hit points appropriately. Speed. Reduce the creatures speed by 5 feet for each size category it is reduced by. Ability Scores. The creature gains +2 Dexterity and -2 Strength for each size category the original creature is reduced by. Damage. The number of damage dice are reduced by 1 for each size category the original creature is reduced by. If this reduces the die to less than 1, reduce the die by one step (i.e.., d12 becomes d10, which becomes a d8 and so on).
