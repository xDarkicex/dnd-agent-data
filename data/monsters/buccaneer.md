# Buccaneer

**description**: Buccaneer Sailors who survive several voyages across the Expanse are likely to come across buccaneers. These pirates and privateers prowl busy wind routes, looking for poorly defended ships to pillage. Buccaneers expertly push through the crowded battles that invariably occur when boarding enemy ships.
