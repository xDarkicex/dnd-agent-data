# Goblin Blackblade

**description**: On very rare occasions, a goblin shows an affinity for the dark arts and becomes a warlock, usually making a pact with a fiend or dark fey. A few become hexblades. They are commonly called blackblades by their brethren. Actions Scimitar . Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) slashing damage. Spellcasting. The goblin casts one of the following spells using Charisma as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 12, +4 to hit with spell attacks): At will: eldritch blast, minor illusion, true strike 2/day: (as 2nd-level spells): armor of Agathys, mirror image Bonus Actions Nimble Escape. The goblin takes the Disengage or Hide action. Hexblade's Curse (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest). The goblin targets a creature it can see within 30 feet of it. The target is cursed for 1 minute. The curse ends early if the target dies, the goblin dies, or it is incapacitated. Until the curse ends, the goblin gains the following benefits: It deals an extra 2 damage on damage rolls against the cursed target. Any attack roll it makes against the cursed target is a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20 on the d20. If the cursed target dies, the goblin regains 5 hit points.
