# Dipsa

**description**: The myths and legends of the Mediterranean are replete with strange and mystic serpents of every description. Dipsa. You feel the heat emanating from this dull-colored reptile’s body even before seeing it. Its jaws snap nervously, searching for flesh to bite. Dipsae live in the few humid patches they can find in deserts, sucking all humidity from their prey in order to survive.
