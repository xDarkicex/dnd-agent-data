# Expert Pilot

**description**: Vehicles are invaluable to modern society, and in many cases, they require a professional touch. An expert pilot can operate any type of vehicle from underwater skiffs to capital starships. They work as starship helms, commercial pilots, combat jocks, racers, stunt drivers, getaway drivers, and construction operators.
