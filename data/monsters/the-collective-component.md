# The Collective (component)

**description**: The Collective’s Primary Body The stat block is for the Collective’s components since they are far more likely to be encountered than the Collective itself. However, if you wish to run a combat scenario with the Collective’s primary body present, you could use the component’s stat block but greatly increase its hit points, attack bonuses, and damage, and give it the ability to summon a component form in a space adjacent to it as an action. Traits Intangible Form. The Component is immune to any effect that would alter its form. Magic Resistance. The Component has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. Actions Shadow Grasp. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d8+3) necrotic damage, and the target must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or have its movement speed reduced by 10 and be unable to take reactions until the end of its next turn. If the target fails this saving throw by 5 or more, it is stunned until the end of its next turn instead. Shadow Burst (Recharge 5-6). Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 15 ft., targets each creature that is not a Component within reach. Hit: 18 (4d8) piercing damage, plus 9 (2d8) necrotic damage.
