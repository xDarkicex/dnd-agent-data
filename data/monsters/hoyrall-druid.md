# Hoyrall Druid

**description**: A hoyrall druid is a boon to the area it tends, lending a natural instinct for growth and preservation to the Underworld’s flora and fauna. Their homes frequently serve as a way station, although many of those who use these simple dwelling ever realize a hoyrall is responsible for it. Travelers who visit these hostels—and act respectfully—often find a beneficial potion or potted mushroom waiting for them as they leave.
