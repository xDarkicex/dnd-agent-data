# Void Creature (template)

**description**: Void creatures are beings that have evolved or otherwise adapted to living in the harsh environment of outer space. They may also be the product of magical or technological manipulation. VOID CREATURE TEMPLATE Any creature other than constructs and undead can become a void creature. When a creature becomes a void creature, it retains all its statistics except as noted below. Challenge Rating . With its added defensive abilities, a void creature's challenge rating is typically 1 higher than that of the base creature. Movement . The void creature retains any land or burrow speeds of the base creature but can only use them in zero-gravity or low gravity environments. If the base creature has a swim speed, but no flying speed, it gains a fly speed equal to its swim speed. Base creatures with a fly speed gain the ability to hover. Void creatures caught in higher gravity have all movement rates reduced to 0. Senses . The void creature gains low-light vision. Resistances . The void creature gains resistance to cold, and additional resistances based on its adjusted CR.
