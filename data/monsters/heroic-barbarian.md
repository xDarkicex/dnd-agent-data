# Heroic Barbarian

**description**: A remarkable warrior and a capable leader, this brute is almost as charming as they are mighty. Usually the guiding voice of a group of adventurers, they often raise the morale of their allies while focusing their presence into a powerful force of intimidation for their enemies. Humanoids Humanoids are the most plentiful creatures amongst the creations of the gods. They create vast kingdoms, they make unbelievable technological advances and even achieve breakthroughs in the art of magic. Traits Brute. A melee weapon deals one extra die of its damage when the barbarian hits with it (included in the attack). Heroic Charm. The barbarian has a bonus to all of their Charisma skill checks equal to their Constitution modifier. Magic Weapons. The warrior's weapon attacks are magical. Reckless. At the start of their turn, the barbarian can gain advantage on all melee weapon attack rolls during that turn, but attack rolls against them have advantage until the start of their next turn. Actions Multiattack. The warrior makes three weapon attacks in any combination. While in a rage the barbarian uses its terrifying presence and then makes three weapon attacks. Greatsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 15 (3d6 + 5) slashing damage. Greatsword (Rage). Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 19 (3d6 + 9) slashing damage. Javelin. Ranged Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 30/90ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d6 + 5) slashing damage. Unarmed Strike. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 10 (2d4 + 5) bludgeoning damage. Unarmed Strike (Rage). Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d4 + 9) bludgeoning damage. Terrifying Presence (While in a Rage). Each creature of the barbarian's choice that is within 30 feet of the barbarian and aware of them must succeed on a DC 17 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. If a creature's saving throw is successful or the effect ends for it, the creature is immune to the barbarian's Terrifying Presence for the next 24 hours. Bonus Actions Bolstering Rage (3/day). The barbarian enters a state of rage for 1 minute. During that time they gain resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage. The barbarian also gains a +4 damage bonus to all melee attacks. Additionally, the barbarian and up to 3 allies of their choosing gain the following benefits: They gain 15 temporary hit points. They gain immunity to the charmed and frightened conditions for the next hour.
