# Spirit Carp

**description**: Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (2d6 + 3) piercing damage. If this attack hits and the spirit carp is not restraining a different creature, the target must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw. If the saving throw fails, the spirit carp drags the target into the murky depths and restrains it. Additionally, whenever the carp moves, the restrained creature moves with it. The restrained creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the restraint on a success. This action cannot be taken if a creature is currently being restrained by this effect. Icy Embrace . Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (3d6 + 3) cold damage. Creatures damaged by this attack gain the maimed condition for 1 minute. This attack can only target creatures restrained by the spirit carp.
