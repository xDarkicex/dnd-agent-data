# Vermilion Path Bloodspeaker

**description**: Traits Blood Magic. When the Bloodspeaker scores a critical hit with a melee weapon attack against a creature, the Bloodspeaker immediately regains the use of one of their expended spell slots. Rites of Undeath. The Bloodspeaker’s spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 13). They can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: bestow curse , mark of desecration ( Adventures in Rokugan ), speak with dead 1/day each: inflict wounds , phantasmal killer , ray of enfeeblement , vampiric touch Actions Blacksteel Dagger. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 3) piercing damage. Scores a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20.
