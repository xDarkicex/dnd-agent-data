# Possessed Hunting Eagle

**description**: A trained hunting eagle, possessed by the vile astirax under Jael the Hunter’s control. Traits Keen Sight. The possessed eagle has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight. Magic Hunter. The possessed eagle has advantage on attack rolls targeting any creatures that can cast spells, except rangers and paladins. Scent Magic. The possessed eagle always knows the location of any magic items or any creatures that can cast spells, except for rangers and paladins, within 600 feet. Bonus Actions End Possession. The astirax possessing the possessed eagle can choose to end the possession as a bonus action. When the possession ends, the astirax appears in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the possessed eagle and the possessed eagle becomes a normal eagle. In addition, the eagle is immune to that particular astirax’s possession ability for 24 hours. Actions Talons. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target. Hit: 7 (1d6 + 3) slashing damage. Raking strike. The possessed hunting eagle may make an attack with its talons, then may fly up to 30 feet. It is immune to attacks of opportunity during this movement.
