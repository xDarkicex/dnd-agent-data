# Animated Menhir

**description**: Each of these squat, stony creatures has rough-hewn humanoid features. They lumber forth with effort on blocky legs and uncaring lifeless eyes. TRAITS False Appearance: While the animated menhir remains motionless it is indistinguishable from a normal menhir. ACTIONS Slam: Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d6 + 5) bludgeoning damage.
