# Bounty Hunter

**description**: The bounty hunter typically operates within a more legal framework than an Assassin, pursuing targets for rewards or bounties placed on their heads. Bounty Hunters often work with a guild or law enforcement organization, retrieving criminals who have tried to escape justice. Traits Hit the Mark. When the bounty hunter is seeking a specific target as a bounty, it gains 1d4 on attack and damage rolls against that target. Tracker. The bounty hunter knows the direction and distance to its bounty as long as the two of them are on the same plane of existence. Actions Multiattack. The bounty hunter makes two Rapier attacks and one Hook or Crossbow attack. Crossbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, range 80/320 ft., 1 target. Hit: 6 (1d8 + 2 piercing damage. Hook. Ranged Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, range 20/50 ft., 1 target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2 piercing damage and the target must make a DC 15 Strength saving throw. If it fails, the target is pulled 10 feet toward the bounty hunter. Rapier. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8 + 4) piercing damage. Bonus Actions Hunter’s Insight. As a bonus action, the bounty hunter can make a Wisdom (Insight) check against a creature it can see, contested by the target’s Charisma (Deception) check. If the bounty hunter succeeds, it has advantage on its next attack roll.
