# Ancient Remorhaz

**description**: The rare ancient remorhaz is a monstrous creature that spends most of its time hibernating, active only for a few weeks to hunt for prey before returning to sleep. However, those few weeks can spell disaster to any established community and natural ecosystem within its territory, as the ancient remorhaz is as voracious as its younger cousins. Younger remorhazes stay clear of ancient ones, as they can be considered as food to their larger relatives. Traits Heated Body. A creature that touches the remorhaz or hits it with a melee attack while within 5 feet of it takes 10 (3d6) fire damage. Actions Multiattack. The remorhaz makes two attacks: one with its Bite and one with its Tail. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +15 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 53 (8d10 + 9) piercing damage plus 10 (3d6) fire damage. If the target is a creature, it is grappled (escape DC 19). Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained, and the remorhaz can't use its Bite attack on another target. Swallow. The remorhaz makes one Bite attack against a Large or smaller creature it is grappling. If the attack hits, that creature takes the Bite's damage and is swallowed, and the grapple ends. While swallowed, the creature is blinded and restrained, it has total cover against attacks and other effects outside the remorhaz, and it takes 21 (6d6) acid damage at the start of each of the remorhaz's turns. If the remorhaz takes 40 damage or more on a single turn from a creature inside it, the remorhaz must succeed on a DC 17 Constitution saving throw at the end of that turn or regurgitate all swallowed creatures, which fall prone in a space within 10 feet of the remorhaz. If the remorhaz dies, a swallowed creature is no longer restrained by it and can escape from the corpse using 20 feet of movement, exiting prone. Tail. Melee Weapon Attack: +15 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (2d8 + 9) bludgeoning damage plus 10 (3d6) fire damage. Bonus Actions Burst Forth (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest). While underground, the remorhaz burrows at least 10 feet straight and breaks through a ground or wall's surface. During this move, it can enter Huge or smaller creatures' spaces. A creature whose space the remorhaz enters must succeed on a DC 23 Dexterity saving throw. On a successful save, the creature is pushed 5 feet to the nearest space out of the remorhaz's path. On a failed save, the creature falls prone and takes 33 (7d6 + 9) bludgeoning damage. If the remorhaz remains in the prone creature's space, the creature is also restrained until it's no longer in the same space as the remorhaz. While restrained in this way, the creature, or another creature within 5 feet of it, can make a DC 19 Strength check. On a success, the creature is shunted to an unoccupied space of its choice within 5 feet of the remorhaz and is no longer restrained.
