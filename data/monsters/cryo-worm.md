# Cryo Worm

**description**: Lurking deep beneath the icy crust of frigid tundras and frozen lakes, cryo worms are huge, monstrosities covered in a chitinous hide that glistens like ice. Whether they originate from a rogue planet traversing the empty cold of interstellar space or an exoplanet with cryovolcanoes that support the worms' natural abilities, these worms are terrifying predators that live for centuries. Patient Predators Evolved to thrive in extremely cold climates, the cryo worm's body is built for burrowing through the ice. The worm is also capable of drastically slowing its metabolism, letting it resist attempts to sway its mind or poison its body. Burrowing deep into the ground, the worm uses this trait to enter a state of hibernation where it can endure long periods without sustenance – lying dormant deep beneath the ice until its tremorsense alerts it of prey. Perhaps the worm's most terrifying trait is a gland-like organ that lets it produce various components – which when mixed create supercooled liquid it can spew upon unwitting prey.
