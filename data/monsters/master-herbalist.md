# Master Herbalist

**description**: A true master herbalist has spent their entire life learning from another master, but some are failed wizards or those who can just barely manipulate the weave of magic. A master herbalist knows a little bit about many different subjects and often acts as the school teacher in villages and towns. Every herbalist can make a few potions, but usually only the neutral, evil, or desperate are willing to take the risk of being caught selling poisons. A master herbalist will usually have between 2 and 5 apprentices ( commoners ) helping them gather materials or performing day to day tasks. Depending on the culture surrounding a master herbalist, they may have more power and influence than an elected official or other leader. If a hag has reason to set up a temporary lair in a town or village she will usually pretend to be a master herbalist and will have a complex and far ranging story as to where she grew up and what happened to the previous herbalist.
