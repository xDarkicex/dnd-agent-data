# Vampiric Chimera

**description**: Before the fall of the Founder, vampires mutilated many creatures to understand the extent of their supernatural powers. They preyed on everything and fed on their flesh and blood to see the effects different diets had on them. Some used their unnatural abilities on those creatures to see their limits, and some fed their own blood to them. It was a more chaotic era than the one we’re in now, and vampires were trying to understand their undead nature. The first vampiric chimera came to be when a mortal, who was fed potent vampire blood, lost themselves to hunger for blood after the procedure and swallowed a handful of leeches that were used in blood therapy. The subject died after two painful months. During those months, the subject’s skin became transparent and slimy, the subject developed a taste for blood, and they regularly vomited a mixture of blood and an acidic substance. After that incident, some vampires worked on the subject more methodically and created more chimeras by feeding vampire blood to mortals and grafting creature parts on them. At some point, they started to remove limbs and organs from mortals and grafted them on other mortals. Over time, the process became more and more disturbing and inhumane. There are still vampiric chimeras found in forgotten corners of Kanrath and secret facilities led by cruel vampires as relics of the past. They are in pain and hungry for blood. Even though blood does not sate their hunger, they are unable to comprehend that.
