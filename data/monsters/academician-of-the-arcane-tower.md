# Academician of the Arcane Tower

**description**: Academicians are masters of magic and spell casting techniques unique to the Arcane Tower. They are professors and specialist wizards who continue to optimize spellcasting techniques as set by Master Arlen and the first academicians hundreds of years ago. Rarely, when there is an opening in academic seats, some of the brightest graduating apprentices, distinguished by their mastery and progress in their studies, are offered a position to become an academician of the Arcane Tower.
