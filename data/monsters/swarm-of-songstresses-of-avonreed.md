# Swarm Of Songstresses Of Avonreed

**description**: The Songstresses of Avonreed are a collection of singers Lady Adelhorn inspired as she traveled the world. Since Lady Adelhorn’s fall from grace, these singers have banded together for mutual support and to locate their missing muse. TRAITS Bardic Inspiration (4/Day): The swarm can use a bonus action to choose one creature other than itself. The creature must be able to hear the swarm and be within 60 feet of it. That creature gains a Bardic Inspiration die, a d10. Once within the next 10 minutes, the creature can roll the die and add the number rolled to one ability check, attack roll, or saving throw it makes. A creature can have only one Bardic Inspiration die at a time. Swarm: The swarm can occupy another creature’s space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Medium creature. The swarm can’t regain hit points or gain temporary hit points. ACTIONS Rapiers: Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 19 (4d8 + 1) piercing damage, or 10 (2d8 + 1) piercing damage if it has half of its hit points or fewer. Mass Cure Wounds (1/Day): The swarm selects up to six creatures in a 30-foot-radius sphere centered on a point the swarm can see within 60 feet of it. Each target magically regains 40 (8d8 + 4) hit points, or 22 (4d8 + 4) hit points if the swarm has half of its hit points or fewer. Thunderwave (2/Day): Each creature in a 15-foot cube originating from the swarm must make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 54 (12d8) thunder damage, or 27 (6d8) thunder damage if the swarm has half of its hit points or fewer, and is pushed 10 feet away from the swarm. On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage and isn’t pushed.
