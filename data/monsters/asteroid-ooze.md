# Asteroid Ooze

**description**: Smaller than planet oozes, but no less deadly, the titanic slimes known as asteroid oozes are the survivors of planetoids blown apart during their infancy and populated by insufficiently evolved planet oozes. Trapped on their drifting continent-sized debris, asteroid oozes ultimately seek out more permanent homes in large, cavernous asteroids with little to no gravity. Choking Hazards. Asteroid oozes are predisposed to clog up dark tunnels that are around their size (500 feet in diameter from wall to wall). This makes them very difficult to avoid for unsuspecting ships passing through such tunnels; once stuck and unable to maneuver, the asteroid ooze’s corrosive form can eat quickly through even a corvette-class ship’s hull. Many a greedy crew has shipped out to map the interior of a large, mineral-rich asteroid only to never escape from its rocky depths.
