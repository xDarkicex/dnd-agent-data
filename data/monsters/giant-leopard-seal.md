# Giant Leopard Seal

**description**: Traits Hold Breath. The seal can hold its breath for 15 minutes. Keen Smell. The seal has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Actions Multiattack. The seal makes one Bite attack and one Tail attack. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d8 + 4) piercing damage and if the target is Large or smaller, it is also grappled (escape DC 14). Until this grapple ends, the creature is restrained, and the seal can't use its Bite attack on another target. Tail. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target not grappled by the seal. Hit: 8 (1d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
