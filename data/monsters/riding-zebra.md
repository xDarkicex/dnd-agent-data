# Riding Zebra

**description**: Zebras are used as mounts by the Imbangala, the cavalry casters of the Narumbeki. These animals serve the mages as mounts, but they do not usually let anyone else ride them. Though smaller and a bit slower than horses, zebras have great endurance and agility. They are vicious animals and do not need to be trained to use their hooves or bite in combat. Zebras stand 4 to 5 feet tall at the shoulder and weigh about 700 pounds. Their striped coloration makes it harder to target individuals accurately when they are standing or moving in a group.
