# Tree that Weeps

**description**: In the twisted reaches of the dense woodlands in the easternmost reaches of the Stolen Lands, there grow legendary scythe trees known as the trees that weep. These scythe trees are much more powerful and much more sadistic than their lesser kin. Traits Dead Tree. While the tree that weeps remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from a normal dead tree. Ripping Disarm. If a hostile creature has disadvantage on its melee attacks and misses the tree that weeps, the attacking creature must succeed on a DC 19 Dexterity saving throw or have their weapon disarmed and discarded within 10 feet of them in a square of the tree that weep’s choice. Weeping Aura. When the tree that weeps isn’t using Dead Tree, creatures that start their turn within 30 feet of it must succeed a DC 19 Wisdom saving throw or be paralyzed until the start of its next turn. After it recovers from the paralysis, the creature is frightened of the tree that weeps until the end of its next turn. Woodland Ambush. When the tree that weeps successfully attacks a creature that was unaware of its presence, it deals an additional 11 (2d10) slashing damage on its attack. Actions Multiattack. The tree that weeps makes four scythe branch attacks. Scythe Branch. Melee Weapon Attack: +15 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (1d10+9) slashing damage and the target must make a DC 19 Constitution saving throw, taking 13 (3d8) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success.
