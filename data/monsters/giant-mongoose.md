# Giant Mongoose

**description**: The giant mongoose slinks through the woods, searching out rodents and other small animals to prey upon. Like their smaller cousins, giant mongooses are notoriously resistant to venoms, and their distinctive 'dance' in battle helps them avoid deadly strikes.
