# Dweorg Ambassador

**description**: The dweorg balance traditionalist ideals with commercial needs and cultural diversity. Ambassadors are the arbiters of this balance, their negotiations bringing to new heights the legendary dweorg stubbornness. Diplomatic missions, especially amongst rival clans with opposed ideals on traditionalism, are wont to decay into pitched battle. Even then the ambassador keeps a cool head, using well-timed words to give pause to hot-headed combatants.
