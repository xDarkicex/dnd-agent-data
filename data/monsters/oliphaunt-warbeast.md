# Oliphaunt Warbeast

**description**: An oliphaunt is a massive pachyderm with four scimitar-like tusks and a thick gray-brown hide covered with patches of coarse black hair. Its trunk is short and sits above a large maw full of sharp teeth. Large round ears lie hanging at the sides of its head. Juvenile oliphaunts are often trained for war and are prized among armies for their ability to breach the defenses of enemy settlements and fortifications.
