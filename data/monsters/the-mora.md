# The Mora

**description**: Some say the Mora is a swarm awoken by the Margreve; others that its moths are possessed by the spirits of witches and shadow fey, who gather to drink the dreams and souls of travelers. Actions Lullaby. One creature that the Mora can see and is within 60 feet is targeted by its lullaby. The target make a successful DC 14 Constitution save or gain 1 level of exhaustion.

**properties**:
- Category: Monsters
- Size: Small
- Type: swarm of tiny beasts
- Alignment: unaligned
- Expansion: 16
