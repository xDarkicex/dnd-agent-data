# Swarm of Red Mozzies

**description**: A red mozzy is a Tiny carnivorous mosquito that lives in and around Kanrath. It has bigger wings than regular mosquitoes and a large proboscis which helps them to feed on the blood of the other creatures. They are insatiable creatures that prefer to gather in swarms and attack any creature that possesses blood. These swarms carry bowel fever Pollution and Diseases in Kanrath , a deadly disease of which there were various outbreaks in Kanrath. Even though the swarm of red mozzies can adapt to any damp condition, their breeding ground is mostly in poor neighborhoods of the city. They can rarely be seen in some of the wealthier parts of the city and they will be exterminated on sight by cleaners 24 - Cleaners District . Many researchers have supported that red mozzies were first seen in the Bowels 20 - Dirtwater Belt . According to them, they were once regular mosquitos who have through time adapted to the extremely polluted environment of the Bowels.
