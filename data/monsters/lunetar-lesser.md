# Lunetar, Lesser

**description**: Lunetars are celestial champions that embody the moon and appear as graceful humanoids with purple skin that radiates a silvery light. The intensity of this glow shifts with the lunar cycle, reflecting the ever-changing phases of the moon. Chaotic & Mysterious Despite their inherent goodness, they are whimsical and capricious, often more erratic and unpredictable than celestials devoted to straightforward ideals like law, order, and judgment. A lunetar might aid those lost in darkness or, conversely, deliver harsh retribution to well-intentioned mortals whose actions threaten the greater cosmic balance. Otherworldly Allure Renowned for their mesmerizing beauty, lunetars possess a captivating charisma that lets them sway the hearts and minds of even the most resolute souls – which they may use to influence, persuade, or even command mortals, guiding or forcefully directing their actions. Phases of Power The power of a lunetar ebbs and flows with their patron moon. During the full moon, they transform into Greater Lunetars, beings of immense strength that rival the mightiest celestials. As the moon wanes, so too does a lunetar's divine grace, rendering them weaker and more vulnerable.
