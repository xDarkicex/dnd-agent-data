# Corrilario

**description**: Corrilário Appearing in the form of a large dog, its muzzle crusty with dried blood and its eyes glowing red in dim light, a corrilário is not a beast but rather an incarnation of a lost soul. Corrilários are formed when an exceptionally willful humanoid is killed by a werewolf, or occasionally when such a humanoid dies after losing those dearest to them to a werewolf attack. The urge for retribution returns them to corporeal form, that of a great, wolfish dog. In this form, the corrilário prowls the woodlands, searching for werewolves to destroy. Hunters and Protectors . While fearsome in appearance, the corrilário seeks to destroy werewolves and protect others from the depredations of such creatures. Tales are told of lost children or travelers being led out of the woods to safety by large dogs, often after the hound interrupts a werewolf attack, killing or driving the lycanthrope away. When a corrilário’s unlife ends, its body withers and crumbles to dust. The duration of a corrilário’s time in the world is an uncertain thing. Some linger only long enough to end the life of the werewolf that killed them or their loved one. Others might remain for months, years, or even decades, protecting a particular person, family, settlement, or area until those it protects are no more or it is defeated.
