# Lerritan

**description**: Lerritans are unstoppable humanoid behemoths with skin of volcanic glass and blood of roiling lava. These malicious arsonists would see the world burned to a cinder. They pay homage to Ymeri, the evil elemental demigod of fire, and they bully other fiery creatures. When the urge to immolate grows too great to ignore, lerritans burn forests, destroy villages, and boil rivers, but they avoid large bodies of water. A typical lerritan wields a molten warhammer, and their hunched-over stance only makes the churning, smoking, volcano-like ridge between their shoulders stand out more. Traits Innate Spellcasting. The lerritan’s spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 22, +14 to hit with spell attacks). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: produce flame (17th level) 3/day each: fireball, fire storm 1/day each: earthquake, plane shift (only to the Elemental Planes of Earth and Fire, as well as the Material Plane) Magical Weapons. The lerritan’s weapon attacks are magical. Actions Multiattack. The lerritan makes three attacks, only one of which can be a bite. Warhammer. Melee Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 22 (3d8+9) bludgeoning damage plus 10 (3d6) fire damage. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 25 (3d10+9) piercing damage plus 10 (3d6) fire damage. If the target is a creature or a flammable object, it ignites. Until a creature takes an action to douse the fire, the target takes 10 (3d6) fire damage at the start of each of its turns. Thrown Rock. Ranged Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, range 120/360 ft., one target. Hit: 27 (3d8+9) bludgeoning damage plus 10 (3d6) fire damage. Volcanic Eruption (Recharge 5–6). The volcano on the lerritan’s back erupts and sends lava bombs raining down in a 30-foot radius. Creatures in this area must make a DC 20 Dexterity saving throw, taking 91 (14d12) fire damage on a failure, or half as much on a success. The lava globules quickly cool into heavy stones, transforming the area into difficult terrain creatures that aren’t elementals. A creature that fails their saving throw by 5 or more against this effect are restrained by the globules until the end of their next turn.
