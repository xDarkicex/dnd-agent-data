# Radlee Thugram

**description**: Radlee Thugram was one of the countless human subjects who King Garrow conscripted and sent to war against the forces of Prince Ellan. Radlee’s unit perished when her ruthless commander set them up as bait in an enemy ambush. Thanks to Radlee’s quick thinking, she was the only survivor—and she celebrated her escape by returning to camp and murdering her commander. After that, Radlee deserted and became a sellsword, quickly earning a frightening reputation. The few survivors of her brutality often simply called her “the Grim.” The bloody road of mercenary work led Radlee into partnership with the Scarlet Shadow, a mysterious assassin for hire. Their infamy grew, and one day, the very prince Radlee had been forced to fight came inquiring about a job exploring a remote ruin. Radlee is cold and matter-of-fact, and despises social platitudes—traits which sometimes put her at odds with the prince. In combat, she is ferocious and unrelenting, protecting her fellow Soulbinders while showing no mercy to her enemies. She is bound to the visionar y’s cloak . Traits Exploit Opening ( 3 /Day). When Radlee makes an attack, she has advantage on the attack roll. Sure-Footed. Radlee has advantage on saving throws against being moved against her will or knocked prone. Tactical Vigilance. Radlee has advantage on initiative checks. While she isn’t incapacitated, Radlee and each ally within 30 feet of her who can see or hear her can’t be surprised. Actions Multiattack. Radlee makes three Halberd or three Longbow attacks. Alternatively, she makes one Halberd attack and uses Backswipe. Halberd. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (1d10 + 7) slashing damage. Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage. Backswipe. Radlee swings her halberd’s shaft at a creature within 5 feet of her. The target must succeed on a DC 18 Strength saving throw or take 16 (3d10) bludgeoning damage and be knocked prone. Reactions Protector. When a creature Radlee can see within 10 feet of her attacks one of her allies, Radlee can make a Halberd attack against the attacker.
