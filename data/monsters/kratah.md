# Kra’Tah

**description**: Kra’tah are enormous creatures that skitter on long crab-like legs lined with barbs that resemble a hundred deadly icicles. Their gaping maws filled with razor sharp black teeth devour prey most gruesomely, but an alien cunning belies their terrible forms. The kra’tah came into existence with a full range of the available appendages, defenses, senses, and brain power available for the time. Sharp spines, flexible tentacles, hard shell, teeth, feelers, and more eyes than legs: these beasts have created their own niche somewhere between alpha predators and consummate scavengers. Old before even aboleths, the kra’tah grew mentally as well as physically, becoming colossal within a few generations as nothing stood in their path. But while other creatures couldn’t defeat them either physically, mentally, or both, they did learn to keep out the way, and the greatest of the kra’tah, slow moving in comparison to their prey, quickly fell on each other until very few of them were left greater than large sized. However, to this day, in the greatest depths of the darkest Underworld oceans, it is said specimens of a size beyond imagining still roam, taking on and destroying the cities of other foul, dark underwater races. Notorious Hunters. Kra’tah inhabit the coldest waters, stealthily catching their prey with claw and tentacle. Alien Intelligence. Sages know little of the kra’tah, save that they are deadly and possessed of an incredible cunning for such a seemingly low creature.
