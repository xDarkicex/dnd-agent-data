# Villamor Koth

**description**: Villamor Koth is something of a local hero in Pitax, despite the fact that he’s done little during his violent life that could be considered courageous. He’d work well as a notorious warrior—be he one the PCs face in combat or one whose respect they must earn to recruit his aid against a deadlier foe. Traits Flaming Brute. A melee weapon deals one extra die of its damage plus 1d8 fire damage when Villamor hits with it (included in the attack). Actions Multiattack. Villamor makes three greatsword attacks. Greataxe. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (2d12+5) slashing damage plus 4 (1d8) fire damage. Reactions Counterattack. When struck by an enemy with a melee weapon, Villamor makes one greataxe attack against the creature that hit him.
