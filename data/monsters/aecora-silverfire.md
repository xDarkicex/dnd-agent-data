# Aecora Silverfire

**description**: Aecora Silverfire is the leader of the Nomen tribe of centaurs who dwell in the easternmost reaches of the Stolen Lands. You can use Aecora’s stats for any Desna-worshipping centaur, such as a potential ally wandering PCs might encounter in the wild. If you do so, though, consider changing her alignment from chaotic neutral to chaotic good, since most worshippers of the goddess of luck and travel are, like her, more kind and forgiving in nature than Aecora is. Traits Charge. If Aecora moves at least 30 feet straight toward a target and then hits it with a melee spear attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 10 (3d6) piercing damage. Special Equipment. Aecora wields a spear +1 . Spellcasting. Aecora is a 7th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 16, +8 to hit with spell attacks). Aecora has the following cleric spells prepared: Cantrips (at will): light, resistance, sacred flame, thaumaturgy 1st level (4 slots): command, cure wounds, guiding bolt, sanctuary 2nd level (3 slots): hold person, lesser restoration, silence, spiritual weapon 3rd level (2 slots): dispel magic, sending, spirit guardians 4th level (1 slot): death ward Actions Multiattack. Aecora makes two attacks: one with her spear and one with her hooves. Spear. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 10 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one creature. Hit: 8 (1d6+5) piercing damage, or 9 (1d8 +5) piercing damage if used with two hands to make a melee attack. Hooves. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 11 (2d6+4) bludgeoning damage.
