# Ancient Galeb Duhr

**description**: The ancient galeb duhr towers at nearly 9 feet tall. Its stony body is somewhat shaped like a curvaceous female humanoid, adorned with scattered patches of vibrant mosses. TRAITS False Appearance: While the ancient galeb duhr remains motionless it is indistinguishable from a normal menhir. ACTIONS Multiattack: The ancient galeb duhr makes two slam attacks. Slam: Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (3d6 + 6) bludgeoning damage. Animate Boulders (1/Day): The ancient galeb duhr magically animates up to four menhirs it can see within 60 feet of it. The menhirs remain animated for as long as the ancient galeb duhr maintains concentration, up to 1 minute (as if concentrating on a spell).
