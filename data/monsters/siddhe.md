# Siddhe

**description**: Siddhe Siddhe is a young girl with long dark hair and a pale complexion. She was raised by an elderly couple that found her abandoned as a babe. They recently passed and she has had to take on the responsibilities of tending to their small homestead. Even though neighbors from the nearby village sometimes help her, she finds the work tiring and dull. She does not understand how she ended up in Grimnir, only that she appeared in a flash of rainbow-colored light while exploring caves near her village. She is curious about this new world and feels somehow drawn to explore its many locations. Roleplaying Siddhe Curious. Siddhe already considered herself an explorer in her original world. Now she wants to see everything Grimnir has to offer. And she often sees the best in what she discovers, even when it is dangerous or frightening. Helpful. Though just a child, Siddhe applies herself to helping the heroes when needed. She wants to pull her own weight and be treated like an equal and she does not want to be treated like a child.
