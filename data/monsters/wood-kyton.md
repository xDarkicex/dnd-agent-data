# Wood Kyton

**description**: The kytons of Hel are evil fiends who feed on the fear and suffering of mortals through painful supernatural means. Their monstrous appearances vary, but their stoic and amoral dispositions are universal, for no kyton cares anything for the plights typical of the creatures they prey upon. They are beings wholly dedicated to metamorphosis achieved through horrific trials of self-mutilation and the mystical power provided them by their sapping of mortal anguish. Wood kytons bare strong resemblance in temperament to their extra-planar counterparts. But where a Hel-ish kyton is often decorated in chains and razorwire, a wood kyton rends the flesh of its victims with thorned vines and bladed leaves. The Dark Wood. On the Rybalkan peninsula, the Dark Wood gains its reputation from the wood kyton who has made this place her home. The forest has long been corrupted by her presence, inviting countless fiendish creatures to wander its paths.
