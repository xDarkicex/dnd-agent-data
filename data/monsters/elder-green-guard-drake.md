# Elder Green Guard Drake

**description**: Traits Amphibious. The guard drake can breathe air and water. Actions Multiattack. The guard drake makes three attacks: one with its Bite, one with its Claws, and one with its Tail. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d8 + 4) piercing damage plus 2 (1d4) poison damage. Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d4 + 4) slashing damage. Tail. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6 + 4) bludgeoning damage. Poison Breath (Recharge 6). The guard drake exhales poisonous gas in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw, taking 21 (6d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
