# Jaggedbriar Hag

**description**: The hags of the Jaggedbriar coven are named Eltrid, Irova, and Vikara. These annis hags seek to enhance their ability to use lesser planar ally with their coven magic. Used singularly, though, this stat block can represent a single annis hag with a slightly different set of spell-like abilities. The Jaggedbriar hag’s ability to charm monsters makes a viable foe for use as a diabolical mastermind in your game. Traits Covens. A Jaggedbriar hag that is part of a coven has a challenge rating of 9 (5,000 XP). Innate Spellcasting. The Jaggedbriar hag’s innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 14). She can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: 3/day each: alter self (including the form of a Medium humanoid), fog cloud Actions Multiattack. The Jaggedbriar hag makes three attacks: one with her bite and two with her claws. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (3d6+5) piercing damage. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (3d6+5) slashing damage. If the target is a Medium or smaller creature, it is grappled (escape DC 15). Brutal Rend. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 36 (9d6+5) slashing damage and if the target is a Large or smaller creature, it is grappled (escape DC 15). Until the grapple ends, the target takes 36 (9d6+5) slashing damage at the start of each of the Jaggedbriar hag’s turns. The Jaggedbriar hag can’t make attacks while grappling a creature in this way.
