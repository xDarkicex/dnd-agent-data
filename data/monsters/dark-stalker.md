# Dark Stalker

**description**: Most who commit evil are petty cowards, hiding their true selves in lies and shadows. But some take such a life to heart, leading the weak into greater wrong. From such malice comes the fusion of darkness and depravity known as the dark stalker. These cunning humanoids attract creepers to them, the lesser beings holding a grudging respect for their superiors at the same time as wishing they could replace them. But a stalker knows how to direct a creeper’s hatred and self-loathing that drives dark society. Stalkers go to great lengths to protect their little creeps when times are quiet, yet are willing to sacrifice them over and over to achieve their ends. Creepers quickly fall into mental thrall of a stalker, and work hard to make themselves visible to their chosen leader while renouncing their peers. Of course, no stalker cares for any creeper really. The darkness is theirs to rule, and underlings are nothing more than forgettable fodder.
