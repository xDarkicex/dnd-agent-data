# Eclipse Angel

**description**: Very rarely, a priestess who devotes her entire life to her deity finds her spirit becomes indistinguishable entwined with the being, so much so that she is able, just once, to transform from a mortal creature to into a unique servant, a fiend only because of the foul nature of her new abilities and trenchant beliefs. However, sages and others who watch and study such manifestations fear an eclipse angel is something much worse than a servant fiend. There is a strong belief amongst a select band of intellectuals that is ready to proclaim these individuals to be legendary examples of fallen celestials, a corrupted solar that has had the soul of the priestess and some small part of the mind of her higher power implanted in it over many years, kept ready for the moment when a grand plan comes to fruition, or the chance to take advantage of a truly momentous occasion comes along. At this time, the three full forces of mind, soul and body come together in as wicked a triumvirate as can be imagined by any sane person. As a result, an eclipse angel is an horrific representation of what ought to be an exemplar of good. All that is worthy of respect in a solar is now turned against the hopeful and the trusting; everything about the transformed priestess is to be afraid of, nothing can be taken at face value, and her every act is designed to abuse, contaminate, and ruin the lives of all who cross her path. What happens to the eclipse angel once it has achieved its aims, no one knows. Either it is defeated and disappears in a foul miasma, or no-one survives her onslaught and she is never seen again.
