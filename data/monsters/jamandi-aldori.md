# Jamandi Aldori

**description**: Jamandi serves as one of the first of the PCs’ allies: the dueling aristocrat who gathers and recruits the PCs (among others) for the drive to explore and settle the wilderness of the Stolen Lands. Her stats work well for any well-known or even infamous duelist—perhaps a scoundrel who’s eager to test her mettle against a PC in a public battle. Traits Aldori Duelist. Jamandi has advantage on opportunity attack rolls. Fey Ancestry. Jamandi has advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can’t put her to sleep. Actions Multiattack. Jamandi makes three melee attacks with her Aldori dueling sword. Aldori Dueling Sword. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d8+5) damage. On a critical hit against a Large or smaller creature who is holding a weapon, the creature also must make a DC 18 Strength saving throw or the weapon they’re holding is knocked from their hand and drops to their feet. Reactions Unnerving Prowess (3/day). If Jamandi is critically hit, she can choose to instead take damage as if the critical hit was a regular hit.
