# Githyanki Gish

**description**: Their keen minds and psionic gifts allow the githyanki to master magic. Gish blend their magical abilities with swordplay to become dangerous foes in battle. Their specialized capabilities make them well suited for assassination, raiding, and espionage. Traits Innate Spellcasting (Psionics).
