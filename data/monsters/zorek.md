# Zorek

**description**: Zorek is an ageless guardian who watches over a tomb. As a devotee of Gorum, he works best in other games as a guardian of any site sacred to a chaotic god of war—such as a tomb of honored fallen warlords. However, his stats could be used for a guardian of an active shrine or a famous landmark said to have been touched by the god of war in ancient times. Trait Brute. A weapon deals one extra damage die when Zorek hits with it (included in attack). Spellcasting. Zorek is a 12th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 17, +9 to hit with spell attacks). He has the following cleric spells prepared: Cantrips (at will): guidance, light, mending, resistance, thaumaturgy 1st level (4 slots): command, cure wounds, divine favor, shield of faith 2nd level (3 slots): hold person, magic weapon, spiritual weapon 3rd level (3 slots): bestow curse, dispel magic, spirit guardians 4th level (3 slots): fire shield, freedom of movement, stoneskin 5th level (2 slots): contagion, flame strike 6th level (1 slots): blade barrier, heal Stalwart. Zorek gains advantage on all saving throws against spells and magical effects that have an effect other than damage (even if the spell also deals damage). If such an effect gives additional saving throws each turn, Zorek gains advantage on those saving throws. Actions Multiattack. Zorek makes two attacks. Warspear. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 17 (2d10+6) piercing damage.
