# Kobold Scout

**description**: Kobold Kobolds are small, reptilian humanoids who carry physical resemblances to dragons. They have blunt snouts and horns, and their scale coloration varies through all the colors associated with the various types of true dragon. Kobold Scout Kobold scouts serve as trapsetters, explorers, and guardians for their warrens. Quick and agile, they tend to avoid combat if possible. When presented with no other choice, a kobold scout fights dirty, ducking in and out of combat to catch a foe unaware rather than preferring to stand and fight. Traits Hurried Retreat. The kobold scout can Disengage as a bonus action. Sneak Attack. The kobold deals an extra 7 (2d6) damage when it hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of the kobold that isn’t incapacitated and the kobold doesn’t have disadvantage on the attack roll. Actions Shortsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d6+4) piercing damage. Light Crossbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8+2) piercing damage.
