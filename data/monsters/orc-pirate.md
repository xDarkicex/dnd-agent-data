# Orc Pirate

**description**: Slenderer and less bulky than their tribal warrior counterparts, the orc pirates of the Blood Vengeance are no less vicious. Their standing with one another is based in how well they evoke fear among their victims, and each is determined to win the acclaim of their fellows, their first mate, and above all, their justly-feared captain.
