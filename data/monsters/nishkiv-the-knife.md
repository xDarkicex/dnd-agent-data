# Nishkiv the Knife

**description**: Nishkiv is the second-in-command of the Black Tears—a group of assassins and thieves that launches an attack at the very start of the Kingmaker Adventure Path . As with Kressle, you can use these statistics to flesh out members of a larger band of thieves or bandits. Traits Sneak Attack. Nishkiv deals an extra 3 (1d6) damage when he hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of Nishkiv that isn’t incapacitated and Nishkiv doesn’t have disadvantage on the attack roll. Actions Multiattack. Nishkiv makes two dagger attacks. If his first attack hits, he has advantage on the second attack. Dagger. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d4+4) piercing damage.
