# Corpse Swallower

**description**: Corpse Swallower These creatures appear to be massive bird/bat hybrids but are a strain of Yoten, capable of flight. In many ways they emulate the behavior of natural birds, tending to form flocks, and “nesting” upon sheer cliff faces rising far above oceans and seas. But unlike birds, they delight in attacking and consuming humanoids. Cruel Attackers. A common tactic used by corpse swallowers is to divide an enemy force and then concentrate their attacks on isolated victims. They generally use their Talons to grasp an opponent and fly away with them, dropping them far from help. If the fall does not kill the victim, the corpse swallowers swarm their target, seeking to slay them swiftly.
