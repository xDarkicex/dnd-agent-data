# Cruel Bounty Hunter

**description**: Bounty hunters are paid to find and capture or kill their targets, but some go out of their way to cause their captives just as much harm as technically allowed in their contracts. Traits Sneak Attack. Once per turn, when the bounty hunter hits with an attack against an enemy that hasn't acted yet in combat, who is within 5 ft. of one of their allies, or who they have advantage against, they deal an additional 7 (2d6) damage with that attack. Actions Multiattack. The cruel bounty hunter makes two Cruel Knife attacks. Cruel Knife. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 10/20 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d4+4) piercing damage, and the target's Injury thresholds are reduced by 5 until the end of the turn (this effect can’t apply to the same creature more than once at a time). Bonus Actions Sapping Wound. After hitting a creature with a melee attack that dealt extra damage from Sneak Attack, the bounty hunter can attempt to inflict a Sapping Wound. The target of the triggering attack must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or gain a special Injury of Dangerous ] severity, “Bleeding”. While Injured in this way, a creature loses 2 (1d4) hit points at the start of each of its turns. This Injury lasts for 1 minute, or until the creature receives magical healing.
