# Soul Shard

**description**: These ethereal undead are utterly loyal the vidre that brought about their existence. Their appearance when first created closely mirrors that of their original corporeal form, but over time their features become more crystalline, eventually becoming a ghostly apparition of a vidre. Physical Longing. A soul shard longs to return to a physical form. When in the presence of their former body—most common at the moment of their creation—their desire to repossess it is the only instinct that can override its creator’s command.
