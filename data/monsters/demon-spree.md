# Demon, Spree

**description**: This wild gnome's shaggy hair is matted on one side with blood. It has razorlike claws, and its teeth are filed to points. Gnomes who die while succumbing to vile, homicidal desires sometimes return as murderous, demonic creatures. Instigators of Madness. Spree demons have a similar appearance to gnomes, but they look even more deranged. They seek to infect others with their murderous desires and inflict curses on those they harm. A single spree demon can incite violence that cripples a city for weeks, but they prefer to group up to spread mayhem all the quicker.
