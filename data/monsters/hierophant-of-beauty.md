# Hierophant of Beauty

**description**: Hierophants of beauty are devoted followers and chosen ones of Aphrodite, also colloquially referred to as priests or priestesses. They are remarkably beautiful in very different ways from each other. Outer beauty is the most common similarity, but talents and personality are often an important facet of their being. They are tasked with the duty of preserving beauty in the world, which manifests in several forms. Such a vague task translates surprisingly well to individual ambitions, driving these clerics to protect what they deem beautiful in this world, be it nature, the people in it or something completely different. Humanoids Humanoids are the most plentiful creatures amongst the creations of the gods. They create vast kingdoms, they make unbelievable technological advances and even achieve breakthroughs in the art of magic. Traits Compelling Presence. The hierophant can choose to use their Wisdom modifier in all Charisma-based checks. Enthralling Beauty. While the hierophant wears no armor, their AC equals 13 + their Wisdom modifier. However, they still benefit from shields. Magic Weapons. The hierophant's weapon attacks are magical. Superior Charm. Creatures that break out of the charmed condition caused by the hierophant's spells or abilities take 15 psychic damage if the hierophant wishes so. Spellcasting. The hierophant's Spellcasting Ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 17, +9 to hit with spell attacks). They can cast the following spells: At Will: guidance, thaumaturgy, toll the dead 3/day each: bless, charm person, dissonant whispers (as a 2nd-level spell), guiding bolt (as a 2nd-level spell), suggestion 2/day each: bane, charm monster, crown of madness, enemies abound, fast friends, inflict wounds (as a 3rd-level spell) 1/day each: confusion, dominate person, synaptic static Actions Multiattack. The hierophant makes two shortsword attacks. Shortsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage plus 4 (1d8) radiant or psychic (hierophant's choice). Otherworldly Infatuation (Recharges after a short or long rest). The hierophant chooses any number of creatures within 30 feet that can see them. Each target must succeed on a DC 17 Charisma saving throw or be charmed for 1 minute, or until the target takes damage. While charmed in this way, a creature can do nothing but use its movement to approach the hierophant in a safe manner. While an affected creature is within 5 feet of the hierophant, it cannot move, but will use its action only to observe them.
