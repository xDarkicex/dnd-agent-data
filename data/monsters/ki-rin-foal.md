# Ki-rin Foal

**description**: A ki-rin foal is an adolescent ki-rin the size of a horse. While it lacks the experience and maturity of its adult kin, it more than makes up for it with its youthful inquisitiveness and boundless energy. A ki-rin foal is often in the company of its parents, who carefully watch over it. However, due to its adventurous spirit, the ki-rin foal occasionally slips away from the care of its guardians to escape into the Outer Planes and the Material Plane. Because of its impressionable nature, the ki-rin tends to find itself in the company of strangers it finds interesting, such as good-natured adventurers on a quest, and inadvertently exposes it to unnecessary danger. Traits Magic Resistance. The ki-rin has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. Magic Weapons. The ki-rin’s weapon attacks are magical. Actions Multiattack. The ki-rin makes two Hoof attacks and one Horn attack. Hoof. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d4 + 3) bludgeoning damage. Horn. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage. Spellcasting. The ki-rin casts one of the following spells using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 14, +6 to hit with spell attacks): At will: gaseous form, light, sacred flame (2d8), major image, spare the dying, thaumaturgy, wind walk 1/day each: banishment, cure wounds, create food and water, dispel magic, freedom of movement, guardian of faith
