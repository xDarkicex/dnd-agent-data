# Rezatha

**description**: Among the many triumphs King Irovetti boasts, the submission of the deadly remorhaz Rezatha is one of his favorites. You can use Rezatha’s stats to represent an entire species of particularly powerful remorhazes to challenge high-level PCs. Or—by simply changing the type of energy damage Rezatha inflicts—you can create an entirely new type of creature native to other realms or planes alike. Traits Heated Body. A creature that touches Rezatha or hits it with a melee attack while within 5 feet of it takes 21 (6d6) fire damage. Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 41(6d10+8) piercing damage plus 21 (6d6) fire damage. If the target is a creature, it is grappled (escape DC 19). Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained, and Rezatha can’t bite another target. Swallow. Rezatha makes one bite attack against a Medium or smaller creature it is grappling. If the attack hits, that creature takes the bite’s damage and is swallowed, and the grapple ends. While swallowed, the creature is blinded and restrained, it has total cover against attacks and other effects outside Rezatha, and it takes 10 (3d6) fire damage and 10 (3d6) acid damage at the start of each of the remorhaz’s turns. If Rezatha takes 35 damage or more on a single turn from a creature inside it, Rezatha must succeed on a DC 16 Constitution saving throw at the end of that turn or regurgitate all swallowed creatures, which fall prone in a space within 10 feet of the remorhaz. If Rezatha dies, a swallowed creature is no longer restrained by it and can escape from the corpse using 15 feet of movement, exiting prone.
