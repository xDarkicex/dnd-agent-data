# Twigjack

**description**: Maladjusted forest denizens, twigjacks form from the cruel and prankish combination of fey and the very woods in which they reside. A twigjack’s body is made up of prickly brambles woven with vines. Shaggy, mossy growth, not unlike hair, tops a twigjack’s head. Its mouth is just a canyon of splintered and broken sticks bisecting its face. Leaves and sprigs of new growth randomly sprout from the creature’s body. Traits Bramble Jump. A twigjack does not treat spaces filled with undergrowth as difficult terrain. On its turn, a twigjack can use 10 feet of its movement to step magically into undergrowth within its reach and emerge from a second area of undergrowth within 60 feet of the first undergrowth, appearing in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the second undergrowth. Actions Multiattack. The twigjack makes two claw attacks. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 7 (2d4+2) slashing damage. Splinter. Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, range 30/120 ft., one creature. Hit: 14 (3d6+4) piercing damage. Splinter Spray (Recharge 5–6) . The twigjack sends a volley of splinters in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw or take 14 (3d6 + 4) piercing damage.
