# Swarm of Sparks

**description**: Elemental Motes Masterful users of the Weave can use their magic to rip apart the barriers between worlds, summoning primordial avatars of air, earth, fire, and water from the chaotic depths of the Elemental Planes. When this fails miserably, the magic can produce an elemental mote instead, a tiny proto-elemental nearly indistinguishable from an inanimate manifestation of its element. Like their more powerful brethren, elemental motes have little desire but to return to their native plane and reunite with its natural energy. A Swarm Reshaped. As a swarm, elemental motes combine their individual specks of consciousness into a raging, primaeval mind. What they lack in physical ability, they make up for in combined desperation to abandon the Material Plane. Using their numbers to their advantage, they envelop any who dare stand in their way in a miniature elemental storm. Sparks Sparks are fickle and carefree, darting through the air with wild, crackling energy. Even a glancing touch of a spark is enough to send creatures into a brief, gripping paralysis. A swarm of sparks is a cluster of luminescent ball lightning, flickering in and out of existence while bolts of electricity flash between them in a hypnotising dance.
