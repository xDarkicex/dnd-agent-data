# Drakainia

**description**: A drakainia is a true mother of monsters, an otherworldly abomination of legend that seeds the universe with her foul and monstrous brood. Her upper body is humanoid, shapely and beautiful, but her lower body is bloated, with pulsating tumors and writhing tentacles. She is always encountered with at least a handful of her favored children, as well as other progeny she can sacrifice to protect herself. With their help, she seeds worlds she visits with twisted, monstrous life. These spawn, usually gestated within the drakainia herself but also inflicted on others by means of the horrific plague she imparts with her infectious phage, and any creature born of such horrific means is inevitably a mutated abomination bearing only a terrifying remnant of its other parent’s species. LAIR ACTIONS On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), a drakainia takes a lair action to cause one of the following effects; a drakainia can’t use the same effect two rounds in a row: The drakainia causes four CR 1 spawn-of-drakainia (or less) to appear in an unoccupied space that she chooses within the lair. They vanish after 1 hour and do not count against her CR limit. Up to four creatures infested with black-green phage that she can see must succeed on a DC 22 Wisdom saving throw or use their reactions to move up to their speed and make one weapon attack against any hostile-to-drakainia creature within range. The drakainia uses her malignant aura centred on any single creature within her lair infested with black-green phage.
