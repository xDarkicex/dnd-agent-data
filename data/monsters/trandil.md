# Trandil

**description**: Trandil the Ironthrall Trandil is a Lutan clan magician who has spent most of their life searching for ever greater power. To other magicians they are known as a dreamer and their fascination with the primal world, and with Mirgal, alienated them from many of their peers. Recently their dreams became even more vivid. They wandered away, convinced they might find a powerful artifact. Instead, they were ambushed by the Ironwood Witches and transformed into an ironthrall. They returned to their village with a tale of great treasure, enough to sway the raiders there to take to the sea towards Fallegur, the home village of the Örn. They are fully a pawn of the Witchking and do not care if they die in service to their grim master. Roleplaying Trandil Asleep. Trandil speaks in murmurs, most of their consciousness has already faded. They are a hollowed vessel, only a reflection of the person they used to be.
