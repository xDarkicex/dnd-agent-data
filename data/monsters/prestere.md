# Prestere

**description**: The myths and legends of the Mediterranean are replete with strange and mystic serpents of every description. Prestere. Smoke comes off this little grayish snake’s skin, and the sand sizzles below it as if all of its body was burning with a strange fever. Some say that presteri are the embodiment of the desert’s heat. The disease they bear kills you by burning your body from the inside, even while swelling it with liquid.
