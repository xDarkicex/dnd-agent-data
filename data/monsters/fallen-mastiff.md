# Fallen Mastiff

**description**: Traits Keen Hearing and Smell. The mastiff has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell. Join the Fallen. Whenever the mastiff reduces a creature to 0 hit points, inflicts an Injury, or scores a critical hit, all creatures within 120 feet that can hear or see the target of its attack must succeed on a DC 8 Wisdom saving throw or gain a Mindscar. A creature who gains three Mindscars begins to lose its mind. During its next long rest, it will begin to scar its flesh with whatever tools are available to it. Once its rest is completed, it becomes one of the Fallen. Mindscars can be removed by any process that can remove a curse, although mental trauma will still remain. If the mental trauma is overcome, the Mindscars are removed as well. Relish the Pain. Whenever the mastiff suffers an Injury, they gain a +1 bonus to damage rolls until the Injury is removed (up to their proficiency bonus), and gain temporary hit points equal to ½ the threshold of the Injury gained. The mastiff never has damage loss regardless of its hit point tier for Injury thresholds, and always suffers an Injury when it takes enough damage to become Injured (the Injury it gains is chosen by the damage dealer). Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d6+1) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 11 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
