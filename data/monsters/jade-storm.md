# Jade Storm

**description**: Jade Storm was raised as an orphan on a pirate ship, which put him to work the hardest tasks requiring raw strength. The silent half-giant was never given a weapon and grew up practicing and creating his own unarmed fighting style that he advanced to a level where he could harden his body to become tough like stone. In the meantime, he revered the winds of the open sea and awakened his storm giant heritage to become a devoted druid of the storms. He eventually escaped his captivity and met the halfling Gixx Shortpebble when the two were gangpressed into a pirate ship, the Black Barracuda. The pair fomented a well-planned mutiny and managed to capture the ship. He had many adventures as the first mate of many ships under Captain Gixx until the two retired to live a relatively safer life of smuggling, exploring, and adventuring aboard the Sated Contessa.
