# Simic Merfolk

**description**: Ravnica’s merfolk are unique to the Simic . They emerged from the depths of the plane’s long-buried oceans less than a century ago and immediately took on leadership positions in the guild. They claim a deep connection to Ravnica’s primal oceans, which are almost untouched by civilization. They have the statistics of the merfolk in the Monster Manual, except that they speak Common and Merfolk, and their legs give them a walking speed of 30 feet. Traits Amphibious : The merfolk can breathe air and water. Actions Spear (Melee; One-Handed) : Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d6) piercing damage. Spear (Melee; Two-Handed) : Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d8) piercing damage. Spear (Ranged; One-Handed) : Ranged Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d6) piercing damage.
