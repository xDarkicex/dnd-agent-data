# Serpent Spawn

**description**: Sea Monsters The Serpent Sea, as well as the many rivers and lakes of Grimnir, are host to a wide variety of fish and aquatic mammals, such as seals and whales. The creatures detailed here are, for the most part, the most dangerous of sea borne predators, save for the great serpent herself. Serpent Spawn These smallest serpents are commonly known as sea snakes by the raiders that encounter them. More than one longship has returned to port missing a few crew members after battling these dangerous beasts.
