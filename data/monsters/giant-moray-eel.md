# Giant Moray Eel

**description**: Eels are long, serpentine fish that typically lurk in the shallows, some in freshwater ponds and backwaters and others in warm tropical seas and reefs. Electric Eel. The electric eel is a curious fish that breathes air instead of water, yet certainly its most unusual characteristic is its ability to generate powerful jolts of electricity. An electric eel is 6 feet long and weighs 45 pounds, emitting strange popping and snapping sounds as it moves. Moray Eel. The moray eel is a ferocious predator, capable of holding prey in its primary jaws while a smaller set of jaws inside its throat chews away bite-sized portions.
