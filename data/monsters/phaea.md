# Phaea

**description**: These enormous swine are savage man-eaters, with bloodshot eyes under beetling brows and tough, hairy brownish-black hide stretched taut over rippling muscles, though their fur may be tawny or even golden. Their tusks are deadly sharp and they are extremely aggressive, prone to charge at any creature that crosses their path big enough to seem like a worthy meal. They can subsist on plant matter but prefer the taste of flesh. These beasts may be known by the lands where hey are found, such as the Calydonian Boar, Erymanthian Boar, or Krommyonian Sow and their first progenitors were unleashed upon mortal kingdoms in vengeance by the gods for slights against them.
