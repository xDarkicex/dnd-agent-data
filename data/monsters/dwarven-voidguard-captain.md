# Dwarven Voidguard Captain

**description**: During the apocalypse, all the great dwarven metropolises were destroyed. While millions of dwarves died, there were still thousands upon thousands that made it out. A significant number of experienced dwarven warriors later joined the Voidguard, finding a new home in the military organization. While the Voidguard is a very diverse group, many dwarves quickly rose in ranks simply because of their competency. It's not unusual to meet a Dwarven Voidguard Captain with several dozen soldiers under their command. Occasionally, Voidguard captains take over important missions. Depending on the nature of such a mission, they might be travelling with a smaller group of soldiers.
