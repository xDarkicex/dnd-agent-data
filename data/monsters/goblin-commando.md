# Goblin Commando

**description**: Goblin Goblins are most common in the southeastern reaches of the Stolen Lands, closer to the border of Mivon. Goblin Commando The theoretical leaders of goblin raids are commandos, but in practice these goblins rarely continue to lead their comrades once a battle begins. Most shirk their responsibilities in favor of wading into the fray in an attempt to claim more glory for themselves. Actions Horsechopper. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6+2) slashing damage, or 6 (1d8+2) slashing damage if used with two hands. Shortbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, range 30/60 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6+2) piercing damage. Reactions Goblin Scuttle. When a nonhostile creature the goblin can see ends its movement within 5 feet of the goblin, the goblin moves up to 5 feet. The goblin doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks with this movement.
