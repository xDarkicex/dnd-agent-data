# Swarm Of Boneman's Brothel

**description**: The Boneman’s Brothel is a secret society of warlocks who gather on the third Wednesday of every second month to discuss necromancy while dining on mushrooms and caviar. The Brothel’s lair is in the laboratory of the university where one of its members teaches. Mummified body parts, bottles filled with strange concoctions, and other arcane paraphernalia pack the shelves. TRAITS Swarm: The swarm can occupy another creature’s space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Medium creature. The swarm can’t regain hit points or gain temporary hit points. Staffs: Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 19 (4d8 + 1) bludgeoning damage plus 14 (4d6) necrotic damage, or 10 (2d8 + 1) bludgeoning damage plus 7 (2d6) necrotic damage if the swarm has half of its hit points or fewer. Eldritch Blasts: Ranged Spell Attack: +8 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 66 (12d10) force damage, or 33 (6d10) force damage if the swarm has half of its hit points or fewer. Blight (1/Day): The swarm magically drains the vitality from a creature it can see within 30 feet of it. The creature must make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the target takes 108 (24d8) necrotic damage, or 54 (12d8) necrotic damage if the swarm has half of its hit points or fewer. On a successful save, the target takes half as much damage. Fly (1/Day): Up to four creatures the swarm touches each gain a flying speed of 60 feet. The effect lasts for 10 minutes or until the swarm loses concentration (as if concentrating on a spell).
