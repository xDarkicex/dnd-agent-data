# Corax

**description**: The gruff leader of a group of loggers who have found trouble with local fey, Corax is meant to play less of a combat role and instead provide a social encounter with a disgruntled expert. Should a fight break out, however, he’s more than capable of protecting himself. You can use these stats for any similar character in your game; simply swap out “logger” from Corax’s profession for the occupation of your choice. If you do so, you should also consider replacing or removing his weapons, especially if his new job takes him far away from one that requires him to chop wood! He can also serve as the leader of a group of bandits as well, although if you lean into that role, you should also consider changing his alignment to chaotic evil. Traits Archery. Corax gains a +2 bonus to attack rolls he makes with ranged weapons (included in attack). Spellcasting. Corax is a 7th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 11, +3 to hit with spell attacks). Corax has the following ranger spells known: 1st level (4 slots): alarm, cure wounds, hunter’s mark 2nd level (3 slots): find traps, protection from poison Actions Multiattack. Corax makes two attacks. Greataxe. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d12+2) slashing damage. Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8+3) piercing damage.
