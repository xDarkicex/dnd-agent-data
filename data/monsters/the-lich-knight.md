# The Lich-Knight

**description**: The Lich-Knight was created as part of the Midnight Curse by the Matrons of Malice as a last resort to inflict suffering upon the Kingdom of Aglarion. The soul of Prince Krasnar Azennar was bound to a phylactery with a ritual similar to the one that wizards use to turn themselves into liches. The Lich-Knight seeks to rule over its promised kingdom that it wants to fashion to its own image, a land ravaged by its undead minions. Equipment The Lich-Knight wields a +2 nine lives stealer glaive that has four charges remaining, carries a +1 heavy crossbow , and wears a +1 plate armor
