# Princess-Elector Persephone Joyce

**description**: Persephone Joyce is a middle-aged Noble woman who wears white robes similar in fashion to those seen within the clergy of the Sacred Flame, but greatly embellished with gaudy Caspian baubles. She often wears gold jewelry to contrast her white attire and mimic the colors worn by the Divine Matriarch. Persephone fancies herself very in tune with the teachings of the religion, and she attends the cathedral in Joyce Harbor every day.
