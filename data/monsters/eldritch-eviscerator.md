# Eldritch Eviscerator

**description**: The eldritch eviscerator is an amphibious monstrosity that is a larger and more ferocious cousin of the grick. Legends say that the first eviscerators arrived to the Material Plane from the extraplanar realm of magic and display this connection to magical forces through their abilities. Eldritch eviscerators seek out and nest near magic items, where their innate regenerative abilities can manifest. They siphon off the spell energies of their grappled targets.
