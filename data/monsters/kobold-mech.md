# Kobold Mech

**description**: Barrels, pots, pans, a temperamental arcanomechanical core, and your grandma’s retainer are all key ingredients of a kobold mech. Some say they’ve even seen one that used a kitchen sink. With legs capable of burrowing, javelin-dynamite-missiles for, presumably, mining, and fiery boosters, few things can escape this hunk of lubricated junk.
