# Baba Yaga’s Hut

**description**: Baba Yaga’s hut is a large hut, roughly thirty feet in diameter, that rests upon three massive chicken legs that keep the hut almost twenty feet off the ground. When Baba Yaga is not moving her hut, the legs will usually fold under the hut and stay out of sight. The inside of the hut contains a massive stove, large enough to cook a hill giant whole, and an alchemists bench that wants for nothing. Baba Yaga’s hut does exactly what Baba Yaga tells it to do without question. The hut will often not engage in battle unless attacked or told to do so by Baba Yaga. The hut will always know where Baba Yaga is unless they are on different planes of existence.
