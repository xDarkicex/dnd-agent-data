# Saw-Tongued Feymoth Larva

**description**: Actions Sucking Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, one target, reach 5 feet. Hit: 4 (1d6+1) piercing damage, and the feymoth larva regains 2 hit points. Additionally, whenever the target moves the feymoth larva moves with it. As an action the target can make a DC 11 Strength (Athletics) check to peel the larva off.
