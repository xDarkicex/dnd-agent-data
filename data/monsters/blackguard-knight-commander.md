# Blackguard Knight Commander

**description**: Blackguard knight commanders serve as the leaders of an order of corrupt paladins, dressing in terrifying armor and capable of urging their followers on to inflict great harm on their foes. Traits Weakening Blows. When the blackguard hits a creature with two Greatsword attacks in the same turn, that creature must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be afflicted with a special Injury, "Weakened", until the end of their next turn. While Injured in this way, all damage dealt by the creature is halved, and the blackguard deals an additional 3 (1d6) necrotic damage on attacks made against them. Once the effect has ended, a creature is immune to this ability until midnight of the following day. Actions Multiattack. The blackguard makes two Greatsword attacks. Greatsword. Melee Weapon attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d6+5) slashing damage. Bonus Actions Death Sentence (1/Rest). The commander points their sword at their target, marking them for death. The blackguard chooses an enemy within reach. The blackguard and their allies have advantage on attacks against the target until the end of the blackguard's next turn. The next time the blackguard or one of its allies hits the target with a melee attack, it deals an additional 14 (4d6) necrotic damage, and the damage is considered 10 higher for the purposes of inflicting an Injury.
