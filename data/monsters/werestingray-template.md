# Werestingray Template

**description**: The following information applies to a creature, including player characters, who contract the curse of werestingray lycanthropy. The creature gains the following features only in stingray or hybrid form. Werestingray Template Werestingray Lycanthropy. On a full moon, which happens once every week, an afflicted creature turns into a crafty stingray-humanoid hybrid or into a stingray (use the giant stingray monster entry) based on the DM’s discretion. While in stingray or hybrid form, the character has a Dexterity score of 15 if their score isn’t already higher, a +1 bonus to AC (from natural armor), and a swim speed of 40ft. It gains damage immunity against bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical attacks that aren’t silvered. Its other statistics are the same in each form. Any equipment it is wearing or carrying isn’t transformed. It reverts to its true form if it dies. Darkvision. The werestingray gains darkvision to 60 feet. Amphibious. The werestingray can breathe air and water in hybrid and stingray form. Keen Hearing and Smell . The werestingray has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell. Sting (Stingray or Hybrid Form Only). As a bonus action, the werestingray can make a sting attack that deals 3 (1d6) piercing damage plus 14 (4d6) poison damage and the target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw (DC 8 + the werestingray's proficiency bonus + the werestingray's Constitution Modifier) or be poisoned for 1 minute. Attack and damage rolls for the natural weapons are based on Strength or Dexterity, whichever is greater. If the target is a humanoid, it must succeed on a must succeed on a Constitution saving throw (DC 8 + the werestingray's proficiency bonus + the werestingray's Constitution Modifier) or be cursed with werestingray lycanthropy.
