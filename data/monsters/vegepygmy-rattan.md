# Vegepygmy Rattan

**description**: In a true display of vegepygmy adaptation, the rattan is a tribe’s trap tactician. While all vegepygmys are capable trapsmiths, a rattan creates and arms traps in the midst of combat, utilizing innate vegepygmy magic and cunning engineering. Experienced rattans have an extensive repertoire of traps, and deploy their stratagems according to the tribe’s current foe.
