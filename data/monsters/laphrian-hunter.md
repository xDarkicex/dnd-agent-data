# Laphrian Hunter

**description**: Deadly, but respectful of the order of nature, these hunters are chosen by Artemis to keep the balance of the Hunt and protect her divine domain. The blessings of the goddess of the hunt carry great versatility and power, which makes the Laphrian Hunters remarkable warriors. Humanoids Humanoids are the most plentiful creatures amongst the creations of the gods. They create vast kingdoms, they make unbelievable technological advances and even achieve breakthroughs in the art of magic. Traits Defensive Hunter. The hunter has a +1 bonus to their AC as long as they are wielding a weapon. Magic Weapons. The hunter's weapon attacks are magical. Pack Tactics. The hunter has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the hunter's allies is within 5 ft. of the creature and the ally isn't Incapacitated. Spellcasting. The hunter's spellcasting Ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks). They can cast the following spells: At will: hunter's mark, message 3/day each: absorb elements, entangle, moonbeam 2/day each: cure wounds, haste, pass without trace 1/day each: conjure animals, guardian of nature, holy weapon, polymorph Actions Multiattack. The hunter makes three weapon attacks. Hunting Blade. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d8 + 5) piercing damage plus 3 (1d6) radiant damage. Hunting Bow. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 10 (1d10 + 5) slashing damage plus 3 (1d6) radiant damage. Hunter's Shot. The hunter invokes the divine power of Artemis to conjure magical arrows with powerful effects. When the hunter successfully hits a target with their hunting bow during the multiattack action they can choose to activate one of the following effects. Only one option can be chosen per turn: Revealing Shot (2/day). The creature hit by the arrow takes 9 (2d8) radiant damage and glows with dim light in a 10 ft. radius. They cannot become invisible or take the hide action for 1 minute. Blinding Shot (2/day). The creature hit by the arrow takes 7 (2d6) radiant damage and is hit by an explosion of bright light that covers a 10 ft. radius. Creatures within the radius must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or become blinded. A blinded creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of their next turn. Strangle Shot (2/day). The creature hit by the arrow takes 9 (2d8) bludgeoning damage and must make a DC 15 Strength saving throw or become restrained by vines. While restrained in this way the creature is also suffocating. A restrained creature can use an action to make a DC 15 Strength check to break the vines.
