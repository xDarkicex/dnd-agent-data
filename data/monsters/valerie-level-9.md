# Valerie (Level 9)

**description**: Born into a noble Brevic family, Valerie soon became a shining star of high society. Her father had chosen a great destiny for her: she was to become a champion of Shelyn. However, the teachings of obedience and humbleness before the goddess of beauty and love appealed to Valerie no more than the adoring pursuit of unwanted courters. For many years, she struggled with this against her desire to respect her father, and so she leaned into the martial aspect of her training. Yet one cannot force devotion, and it was only a matter of time before Valerie’s patience expired and, in a fit of frustration, she railed against the traditions of Shelyn’s teaching, denouncing the concept that beauty and art could ever serve as anything more than hollow praise and delusions of fulfillment. Traits Equipment. +1 chain mail , a set of fine clothes, a signet ring, a scroll of pedigree, maul, pike, light crossbow and 20 bolts, explorer’s pack, 650 gp. Great Weapon Fighting. When Valerie rolls a 1 or 2 on a damage die for an attack she makes with a melee weapon that she is wielding with two hands, she can reroll the die and must use the new roll, even if the new roll is a 1 or a 2. The weapon must have the two-handed or versatile property for her to gain this benefit. Improved Critical. Valerie’s weapons score a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20. Indomitable. Valerie can reroll saving throw that she fails. If she does so, she must use the new roll, and she can’t use this feature again until she finishes a long rest. Remarkable Athlete. Valerie adds half her proficiency bonus (round up) to any Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution check she makes that doesn’t already use her proficiency bonus. In addition, when making a running long jump, the distance she can cover increases by 5 feet. Actions Multiattack. Valerie makes two attacks. Greatsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d6+5) slashing damage. Maul. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d6+5) bludgeoning damage. Pike. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (1d10+5) piercing damage. Shortbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d6+1) piercing damage. Second Wind. Valerie has a limited well of stamina that she can draw on to protect herself from harm. She can regain hit points equal to 1d10+9. Once she uses this feature she must finish a short or long rest before she can use it again.
