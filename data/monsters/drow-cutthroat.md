# Drow Cutthroat

**description**: In a society where morality is lax, cutthroats are the most pitiless members. Biding their time, a cutthroat follows orders, earning a foul reputation, until eventually they feel ready to turn on their patron and assume command. Calling a barbarous minder a lackey is a sure way to find yourself in a dying heap down some remote tunnel in the not-too-distant future, for even more than most drow, a cutthroat holds a grudge.
