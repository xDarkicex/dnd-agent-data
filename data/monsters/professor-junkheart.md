# Professor Junkheart

**description**: “Welcome to my laboratory, my home, the heart of invention. I’ll make sure you have a good time here.” Professor Junkheart is a genius living in the deepest corner of Kanrath’s junkyards. He is intelligence incarnate, the embodiment of technological advancement, and the unsung hero of Tolian science; if you ask him, that is. If one manages to distinguish between the Professor and the piles of junk that surround him, he can tell them amazing stories about his glorious inventions and his dream of one day becoming a machine god. Professor Junkheart’s Story Once one of the greatest academics in 10 - CADRAC , Professor Junkheart was kicked out because he neglected his duty to put Cyherested and its advancement before all else, prioritizing his own research instead. After he was kicked out, he searched for a new laboratory with three things: silence, subjects to experiment on (the less people there were to miss the subjects the better), and enormous piles of junk. This led him to the ghetto in Kanrath. There, he paid off some authorities, killed others, and managed to find a place for himself. The first thing he did after he was settled was to learn wizardry, just to insult the sorcerers of Cyherested who had done him wrong. Now, Professor Junkheart works on his research, creates bloodwork automatons from the waste around him, and keeps moving towards his ultimate goal of becoming a machine god in his great laboratory concealed in the junk; almost seven times as big as the rest of the buildings in the ghetto. Professor Junkheart’s Goals Professor Junkheart aims to be a god. He thinks that bloodwork automatons deserve a god, and that he’s tailor-made for such a position. To achieve this goal, he has been working tirelessly to turn himself into a cross between man and machine; a human and an automaton at the same time. Roleplaying Professor Junkheart Professor Junkheart’s laboratory is a machine hell that is almost impossible to sneak into. There are many traps, explosive runes, and bloodwork automatons embedded into its metal walls. In the depths of the laboratory, Professor Junkheart’s towering figure talks to himself, sometimes to the people he has kidnapped, and sometimes to the automatons he has created. Otherwise, he can be seen dragging unconscious people to his laboratory in the dead of night. Junkheart in Combat Projessor Junkheart tries to eliminate spellcasters first, especially clerics.
