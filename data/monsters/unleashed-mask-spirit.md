# Unleashed Mask Spirit

**description**: Traits Incorporeal Movement. The unbound mask spirit can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain. It takes 6 (1d10) force damage if it ends its turn inside an object. Actions Multiattack. The unbound mask spirit makes three attacks with its ghostly talons. Ghostly Talons. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d4 + 4) slashing damage. After being hit by this attack, the target is distracted until the end of the unbound mask spirit’s next turn. Breath Stealer (Recharge 5-6). All living humanoids within 5 feet of the unbound mask spirit must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw. If they fail, they suffer 11 (3d6) necrotic damage and one level of exhaustion. If they succeed, they suffer half damage and no levels of exhaustion. Reactions Vanish. After being hit by an attack, the unbound mask spirit can use its reaction to become invisible until the end of its next turn.
