# Ghostling

**description**: Ghostlings. The animated shards that break off of the ghostmother when damaged are thought to be infantile ghostmothers, and do in fact resemble a tiny version of these monstrous worms. Ghostlings differ from their mothers in that they are much longer than they are wide, and their mouths are entirely circular, rather than having a cross jaw of armor plates. Ghostlings are also not well armored and are much easier to destroy. When a ghostmother appears, she is often accompanied by several ghostlings. Traits Tiny Missile. The Ghostling can enter the space of any other creature while moving, but cannot end its turn there. Additionally, as long as some part of its movement is spent underground, the Ghostling can treat its burrow speed as a fly speed. The Ghostling falls if it does not end its turn on or under the ground. Actions Piercing Ram. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 0 ft., one target. Hit: (1d4+4) piercing damage. and the Ghostling does not provoke opportunity attacks from the target until the end of its turn. Additionally, the target has its Injury thresholds reduced by an amount equal to damage taken until it suffers an Injury.
