# Borda

**description**: Borda Bordas resemble tall, wizened crones that seem to materialize out of the fog in a swamp. Her emaciated frame is covered in a ragged robe and coils of rope, with a ragged scrap of cloth bound over her eyes. The embodiment of humanoid fears of swamps, the dangers that lurk within them, and drowning, bordas are solitary hags that dwell in swamps, marshes, or other wetland areas. Bordas were once common green hags, but a vile corruption elevates her to a new form and status by a supernatural connection to native fears focused on her and her homeland. There can be a only one borda in any given swamp, but only one, and each of them tends to refer to herself as the Borda, a creature unique to its local area. Some say that a new borda will arise in the same locale if the current Borda dies, though others whisper that these hags are truly immortal and survive as long as her swamp remains. True to the legends that surround her, the Borda is inimical to any trespassers in her domain, especially those that lose themselves in it by accident. She will stalk and kill those she encounters, leaving their bound, drowned bodies to mummify in the peat beneath the swamp’s dark waters. The Borda’s body is covered in coiled rope, which she can magically form into nooses with which to catch and strangle her victims, or drag them under the water with her to kill at her leisure. She also has a hag’s claws and her bite is venomous like many of the inhabitants of her home. Ruler of Her Domain. A borda has a magical connection to her swamp home. Even without use of her eyes, she is aware of everything in her immediate area. Further abroad, the borda’s sense of her domain is vaguer. If an intruder enters the swamp, she will eventually become aware of it, knowing the general direction in which the intruder lies. This means the Borda, if so inclined, will eventually find someone if they are within her domain. The closer they are, the more quickly she will find them. Solitary Evil . Unlike other hags, a borda is content with a solitary life in her domain and does not seek to form a coven with other hags. Only when forced by the greatest necessity, typically the threat of powerful heroes invading her swamp, will the borda reach out to her sisters to gain power by forming a triad with two other hags (typically green hags). Leave One Alive . Occasionally, a borda will terrorize a victim, only to allow them to escape, or choose a single victim in a group to leave alive. She knows they will return home to tell their harrowing tale of their encounter with her, instilling fear in most, and enticing the brave or foolhardy into her domain as her next victims.
