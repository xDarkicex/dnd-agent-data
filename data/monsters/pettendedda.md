# Pettendedda

**description**: A pettenedda is an emaciated crone whose features are nearly obscured by a matted tangle of hair that cascades down to her knees. Pettenedda are set apart from their sister hags by this wild mess of hair, which may be longer than the pettenedda is tall, and for their wicked claws the size of daggers, which are forever running through their hair, attempting in vain to remove all the knots and snarls. Legend says that pettenedda were once human, cursed with their current state for the sin of vanity. Well Dweller. Petteneddas lair in wells, often snatching people into the water to enslave and later devour. They may pretend benevolence, encouraging coins or other valuables to be dropped down the well in exchange for wisdom or prophecies, typically lies she dreams up to sow discord. They guard the purity of their wells, wreaking deadly vengeance on any who would foul their waters.
