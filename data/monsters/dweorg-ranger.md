# Dweorg Ranger

**description**: Dweorg are not commonly thought of as wanders and woodsman, but in the Underworld and in the foothills surrounding their mountain kingdoms, dweorg rangers are the front line of any dweorg settlement. Whether it’s finding safer trade routes, hunting grounds, or keeping an eye out for new mineral deposits, a ranger may be an atypical dweorg, but they’re respected for their trade nonetheless.
