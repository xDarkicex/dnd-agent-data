# CBI Sorcerer

**description**: As sorcerers hold the reins in Cyherested, it’s not surprising that the city is ruled, managed, overseen, and protected by sorcerers as well. The police force of Cyherested is made up of the Cyherested Bureau of Investigation sorcerers. So long as they have a hematologic predisposition for arcane arts in their blood, these sorcerers can be of any race and are chosen from a variety of great spellcasters who are willing to devote their lives to the betterment of their home. If they are chosen, they are granted a badge with an arcane mark on it, signifying their devotion to the CBI as well as their position within.
