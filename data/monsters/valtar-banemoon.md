# Valtar Banemoon

**description**: Floating gemstones orbit about the head of this ancient and wispy elf Archmage ; their magic has sustained him for thousands of years. Valtar Banemoon is the hermetic lighthouse keeper, and has not left the lighthouse in millennia. He knows little of the world beyond, and even less about how he got here.
