# Field Engineer

**description**: In dangerous situations, a working vehicle, construct, or control unit could mean life or death. Field engineers blend esper techniques with mechanical know-how to keep machines and devices running that might otherwise become scrap in the ensuing conflict. They can be found working as military personnel, starship technicians, or expeditionary mechanics.
