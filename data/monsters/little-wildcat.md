# Little Wildcat

**description**: Traits Agile Landing. The wildcat takes no damage from falling 40 feet or less, provided they aren’t incapacitated. Hunter’s Pounce. If the wildcat hits a Small or smaller target and had advantage on the attack roll, the target is grappled (escape DC 12). While grappling the target in this way, the wildcat can’t attack another creature. Natural Camouflage. The wildcat has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made in their native terrain. Sprint (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest). When the wildcat moves on their turn in combat, they can triple their speed until the end of their turn. Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage, plus an extra 2 (1d4) piercing damage if the target is grappled or restrained. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) slashing damage, plus an extra 2 (1d4) slashing damage if the wildcat had advantage on the attack roll. Bonus Actions Sneak. The wildcat takes the Hide action.
