# Despair Giant

**description**: These emotionless and sluggish giants are descendants of a primitive hill giant tribe that ventured into the plane of Shadows many thousands of years ago. The despair giants have adapted to the dark and empty Environment of the Shadowrealm over the millennia. Their haggardness and dreariness are so apparent that they engulf the spirit of anyone in their close vicinity.
