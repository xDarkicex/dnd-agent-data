# Amiri (Level 1)

**description**: Amiri often claims to wield the power of giants and indicates her oversized sword as proof. Despite her bravado and fearlessness, she has an equally large need to prove her strength and find a place to belong. If the PCs treat her with respect, she might come to see them as her new family and eagerly protect them and their kingdom with all her considerable ferocity. Amiri relies mostly on her incredible strength and bravery. She has little patience for academics, intrigue, or indirect approaches—and abhors cowards. Often crude, always hot-tempered, and continually full of impulsive bravado, she is perfectly aware of how strong and tough she is. As a result, Amiri stands ready to prove her bravery and might. No one would be faulted for thinking the boastful, brawny, and bull-headed barbarian fears nothing—she hides her insecurities about her past and her fear of not being allowed to belong deftly. Her confidence in her own capabilities can be, at times, misplaced or overestimated, but Amiri is not foolhardy. Faced with a situation where she has to admit that brute force won’t prevail, her typical response is to seek other methods of success while continuing to hone her skills and increase her power. If such an alternative plan doesn’t work, at least she has a good excuse to rely on what she knows best: brute force. Amiri values her oversized sword, and isn’t easily persuaded to wield another weapon, although she will do so begrudgingly if tactics demand it. Traits Equipment. Amiri wields an oversized sword which functions in her hands as a greatsword. She wears hide armor and carries an explorer’s pack, javelins (4), chalk (10 pieces), a grappling hook, antitoxin, a potion of healing , a dagger +1 (which she doesn’t use herself but could potentially gift to the PCs), and a belt pouch containing 80 sp. Rage (2/Day). While raging, Amiri gains +2 damage to melee attacks using Strength, has resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage, and advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws. She can’t cast spells or concentrate on them while raging. Her rage lasts for 1 minute. It ends early if she is knocked unconscious or if her turn ends and she hasn’t attacked a hostile creature since her last turn or taken damage since then. She can also end her rage on her turn as a bonus action. Unarmored Defense. When not wearing armor, Amiri’s AC equals 10 + her Dex modifier + her Con modifier. Actions Greatsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (2d6+3) slashing damage. Javelin. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6+3) piercing damage.
