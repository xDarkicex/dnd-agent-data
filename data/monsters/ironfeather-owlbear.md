# Ironfeather Owlbear

**description**: The ironfeather owlbear has razor-sharp, iron-like feathers that protect the creature like thick scale armor. The beast can spread its wings and use them like cleaving blades, slicing through anything nearby. The owlbear can also suddenly unfurl its wings, unleashing loose feathers like flying knives. Traits Keen Sight and Smell. The owlbear has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight or smell. Actions Multiattack. The owlbear makes two attacks: one with its Beak and one with its Claws. When available, it can use Wing Cleave in place of its Claws. Beak . Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d6 + 6) piercing damage. Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 17 (2d10 + 6) slashing damage. Wing Cleave (Recharge 4–6). The owlbear unfurls its wings in the shape of a fan and violently swings them around. Each creature within 10 feet of it must succeed on a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw, taking 17 (2d10 + 6) slashing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Ironfeather Spray (1/Day). The owlbear flings razor-sharp feathers in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw, taking 28 (4d10 + 6) piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
