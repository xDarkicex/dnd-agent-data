# Fire-Infused Water Elemental

**description**: Actions Multiattack. The elemental makes two slam attacks. Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: (2d10+5) bludgeoning damage plus (2d6)fire damage. On a hit, does 7 (2d6) fire damage from the scalding heat. Scald (Recharge 6). A fire-infused water elemental can project a cone of superheated water. Each creature in a 30-foot cone must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 21 (6d6) fire damage and is knocked prone on a failed save, or takes half as much damage on a successful save and is not knocked prone.
