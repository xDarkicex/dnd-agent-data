# Gitwerc Underterror

**description**: Underterrors adapt in the moment to attempts to damage them and defenses put in place against their attacks. Granted fiendish boons, an underterror no longer fights or defends itself as one might expect a humanoid to do, but instead displays both a physical and mental warping that places them beyond the comprehension of most opponents.
