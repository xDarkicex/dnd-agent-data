# Spellbound Chimera

**description**: After the foundation of Cyherested, the Prime Sorcerers sought ways of empowering their armed forces with all-powerful creatures that were unknown to the rest of Tolia. They experimented on various monstrosities; mutilated them, bound them together through surgical and magical operations, exsanguinated and re-sanguinated them with different sources of blood. They wanted to create a creature that would exhibit the supremacy of sorcery and claim the superiority of Cyherested in Tolia through their magical creations. The first spellbound chimera was created through a year of painful blood experiments that were designed to alter the creature’s magical properties. It is hard to talk about the physical properties of spellbound chimeras as they are shapechangers. Their true form varies as they are created according to the needs of their creator. In their most common true form, a spellbound chimera has the heads of a fiend, a naga and a gorgon, and giant dragon-like wings. Years have passed and the process of creating more spellbound chimeras has been abandoned by some of their creators, as the experiments did more harm than they did good; depleting their resources and resulting in few benefits. However, there are still spellbound chimeras found in forgotten corners of Cyherested as relics of the past, or in secret facilities led by mad sorcerers who want to prove that their art is superior to all others.
