# Ogre Mage

**description**: Almost all ogres are constantly angry, something others take full advantage of when goading these “small giants” (an irksome jape to an ogre) into some violent action or other. And even when an individual manages to control their temper, they quickly finds themselves tested by a bevy of other prejudices that constantly try their patience. But once in a generation the ogre’s ancient heritage manifests in a single, charismatic, wizened, and above all patient leader. The rise of a high ogre inspires legions to follow, and those who once goaded now tremble in fear.
