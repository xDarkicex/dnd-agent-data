# Melder Initiate

**description**: Melder initiates are novice energy channelers who have only recently experienced their awakening as espers. They often find other, more powerful espers to help them master their abilities. In regions where espers are uncommon or space travel is rare, they have little choice but to follow the path of discovery on their own.
