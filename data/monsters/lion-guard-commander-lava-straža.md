# Lion Guard Commander Lava Straža

**description**: This catfolk warrior has led the Lion Guard for the past decade. Still deemed a newcomer to the elite warriors of Orleone, she proved herself in a battle she led against the black dragon Bitterheart, who ventured across the borders from the Eastern Vales looking for farmland to desolate. Lava carries several scars to show for it. A catfolk in her mid-thirties, she has lush yellow and orange fur that matches her yellow cat-eyes and a stern look on her face. She wears many piercings through both her ears and is often dressed in the red and brown leather strappings of the Lion Guard.
