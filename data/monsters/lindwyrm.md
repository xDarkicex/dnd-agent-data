# Lindwyrm

**description**: Lindwyrm With few larger dragons in Grimnir, lindwyrms have claimed many of the forests, caverns, and rivers of the world as their domain. Ambush Predators. These are swift moving creatures, capable of both flight and swimming. They are patient hunters, willing to wait hours for prey to come to them. Nomadic. Unlike many other dragons, lindwyrms do not tend to have lairs or acquire treasure. Instead, they live for the hunt and move often. When possible, they sleep deep underwater in a crevice or cavern, to keep themselves protected.
