# Swarm of Crabs

**description**: A skittering mass of drab green crabs boils forth from the stone edifice. Cerulean edged claws snap in anticipation. Traits Amphibious : A swarm of crabs can breathe air and water. Swarm : The swarm can occupy another creature’s space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Tiny creature. The swarm can’t regain hit points or gain temporary hit points. Actions Claws: Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 0 ft., one target in swarm’s space. Hit : 10 (4d4) bludgeoning damage, or 5 (2d4) bludgeoning damage if the swarm is at half its hit points or fewer.
