# Lamia Harridan

**description**: The lamia matriarch is a powerful and cunning creature resembling a beautiful humanoid woman from the waist up, with a lower body and tail like an immense serpent, an evolutionary throwback to the echidnic mother of monsters that birthed all of lamia-kind. Though judged hideous by their lamia kindred, harridans are nevertheless given honor and reverence among their kind and uses her seductive powers to control and manipulate those around her. With her innate magical abilities and sharp claws, the harridan is a force to be reckoned with. Feral Seductress. The queens of a race consumed by bitterness and predatory instinct, lamia harridans mastermind all manner of foul plots in hopes of breaking the bestial curse that afflicts their kind. They move with shocking ease from silken-tongued temptresses to dervishes, striking with all the deadly precision of vipers. Quick to covet, enslave, and overindulge, lamia harridans luxuriate in gory feasts, violent trysts, and bloody entertainments, reveling until their playthings are broken or until they tire and move on.
