# Stag Lord Bandit

**description**: Criminal Wildlands and cities alike have long been infested by all manner of bandit, brigand, and other forms of criminals. The Stolen Lands themselves are notorious for harboring bandits. Stag Lord Bandit The lowest-ranking members of the Stag Lord’s bandits are a mix between soldiers who abandoned their duties and prisoners who escaped into the wild. While the protection the Stag Lord offers is welcome, they still fear their temperamental ruler and tread lightly in his presence. Actions Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8+2) slashing damage or 7 (1d10+2) slashing damage if used with two hands. Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8+3) piercing.
