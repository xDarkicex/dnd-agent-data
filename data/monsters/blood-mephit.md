# Blood Mephit

**description**: Blood mephits are natives of the elemental realms of blood, and are small in size though sinister in nature. These elementals look like puddles of blood when motionless and like humanoids made entirely of blood when they move. Their fleshless form makes them able to enter even the smallest spaces, thanks to their wings made of blood, they can reach the highest of places like the peaks of mountains. They usually wander the wilderness in small packs. They roam the lands of Tolia as they are summoned and set free by various sources such as sorcerers, vampires who have mastered the arcane arts, bleeding specters and many others. They crave blood and “live” to spill it as they believe blood must flow and be free of mortal veins.
