# Ankheg Broodling

**description**: Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d6) slashing damage plus 2 (1d4) acid damage. Acid Spray (Recharge 6). The broodling spits acid in a line that is 15 feet long and 5 feet wide. Each creature in that line must succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity saving throw, taking 5 (2d4) acid damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
