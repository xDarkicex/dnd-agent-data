# Hermean Legerdemain

**description**: Skilled, capable and backed by the divine grace of Hermes himself! These dexterous individuals have caught the eye of the god of thieves and travelers, a fairly rare event. They wield remarkable magic, as well as several tricks to aid them in their mischievous ways. Humanoids Humanoids are the most plentiful creatures amongst the creations of the gods. They create vast kingdoms, they make unbelievable technological advances and even achieve breakthroughs in the art of magic. Traits Evasion. If the legerdemain is subjected to an effect that allows them to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, the legerdemain instead takes no damage if they succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if they fails. Magic Weapons. The legerdemain's weapon attacks are magical. Sneak Attack (1/Turn). The legerdemain deals an extra 17 (5d6) damage when they hit a target with a weapon attack and have advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 ft. of an ally of the legerdemain that isn't incapacitated and the legerdemain doesn't have disadvantage on the attack roll. Spellcasting. The legerdemain's Spellcasting Ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 15, +6 to hit with spell attacks). They can cast the following spells: 3/day each: bless, searing smite, thunderous smite 2/day each: blinding smite, invisibility, haste, pass without trace 1/day each: banishing smite, freedom of movement Speedy Target. The movements of the legerdemain are so swift that it is almost invisible when in motion. If the legerdemain moves at least 10 feet on their turn, attack rolls against them have disadvantage until the start of their next turn unless the legerdemain is incapacitated or restrained. Actions Multiattack. The legerdemain makes two swift blade attacks. Swift Blade. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 18 (1d6 + 5) piercing damage. Shortbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 30/60ft., one target. Hit: 18 (1d6 + 5) piercing damage. Swift Blade (Celestial Armament). Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 18 (1d6 + 5) piercing damage. Shortbow (Celestial Armament). Ranged Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 30/60ft., one target. Hit: 18 (1d6 + 5) piercing damage. Bonus Actions Celestial Armament (Recharges after a short or long rest). The legerdemain imbues their weapons with the divine power of Hermes, guiding their strikes. For the next minute the legerdemain gets a bonus to their attack rolls that equals their Wisdom modifier (included in the attack). Cunning Action. On each of their turns, the legerdemain can use a bonus action to take the dash, disengage, or hide action. Reactions Uncanny Dodge. When an attacker that the legerdemain can see hits them with an attack, they can use their reaction to halve the attack's damage against them.
