# Descendant of Atlas, the Titan of Endurance

**description**: The power to endure, survive and keep fighting is the core of this bloodline. Live through what'd kill even a powerful warrior by virtue of the divine protection granted by your ancestry. Titan-Blooded Sorcerer A far-off descendant of one of the immortals of the past, carrying divine power in their veins, as well as the ability to manipulate their ancestor’s domain to a certain degree. Their power often sprouts from the divine blood that minor deities and nature spirits who were fathered by the Titans spread in the mortal world through amorous affairs. The blood of four immortals has been identified among these magical warriors: Atlas, Helios, Hyperion and Oceanus. Humanoids Humanoids are the most plentiful creatures amongst the creations of the gods. They create vast kingdoms, they make unbelievable technological advances and even achieve breakthroughs in the art of magic. Traits Divine Empowerment (3/day). Whenever the sorcerer casts a spell, they can choose to empower it. When empowered a spell is treated as if it had been upcast at one level higher. Stone Shell. While the sorcerer is wearing no armor and wielding no shield, their AC is calculated by combining their Constitution and Charisma modifiers. Spellcasting. The sorcerer's Spellcasting Ability is Charisma (spell save DC 17, +9 to hit with spell attacks). They can cast the following spells: At Will: mage armor, mage hand, prestidigitation 3/day each: armor of agathys (cast as a 4th-level spell), bless, enlarge/reduce, mind spike 2/day each: bestow curse, guardian of faith, sickening radiance, spirit guardians 1/day each: flamestrike, summon celestial Actions Celestial Strike. Melee Spell Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 27 (4d10 + 5) radiant damage. Celestial Blast. Ranged Spell Attack: +9 to hit, reach 120ft., one target. Hit: 27 (4d10 + 5) radiant damage. Bonus Actions Quick Spell (3/day). The sorcerer can cast a spell with a casting time of 1 action. Reactions Fortitude of Atlas (Recharge 6). When the sorcerer takes damage from an area of effect, such as a breath weapon or a fireball spell, they can halve the damage.
