# Runewrought Dragonfly

**description**: Built to resemble a dragonfly, this mechanical mount is a large Construct powered by magical runes and capable of flying at full speed while carrying a single Medium or smaller creature. Programmed Loyalty The runewrought dragonfly is encoded to follow a single creature and can understand up to ten simple verbal commands which it follows to the best of its ability. A creature proficient in tinker's tools can spend 1 hour to change any of these command words. If it doesn't receive any commands, it takes the Disengage or Dodge action.
