# Ant, Giant Queen

**description**: Several pony-sized ants swarm around an ant the size of a draft horse, clacking their serrated mandibles and threatening with their stingers. Traits Keen Smell: The giant ant queen has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Queen’s Scent: Giant ants defending a queen gain advantage on all attack rolls. Actions Multiattack: The giant ant queen makes two bite attacks and one sting attack. Bite: Melee Weapon Attack : +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit : 7 (1d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage. The target is grappled, escape DC 13. Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained, and the giant ant can’t bite a different target. Sting: Melee Weapon Attack : +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit : 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage plus 22 (4d10) poison damage. On hit, the target takes 22, 4d10, poison damage, or half as much poison damage with a successful DC 14 Constitution saving throw.
