# Void Wraith, Huge

**description**: Denizens of the Braythian Void, the Void Wraiths are constantly seeking for prey. They lurk mostly in the vicinity of world-islands and other inhabited areas, and survive only for short times outside of the Purple Void (see their "Voidbound" ability). Void Wraiths that feed enough on their targets evolve into larger versions, healing completely in the process. Sometimes, a Void Wraith that is close to evolving might prey on other Void Wraiths in its vicinity. Their targets will not sacrifice willingly, but they also won't turn against their attackers - unless they are close to evolving themselves. Void Wraiths are known to appear in four different sizes: Small, medium, large and huge. There are rumors about even bigger and more powerful versions, but these might be nothing more than myths. What exactly the Void Wraiths are, how they are linked to the Purple Void and what drives them to attack travelers are unsolved mysteries.
