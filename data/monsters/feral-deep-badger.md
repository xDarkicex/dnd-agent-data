# Feral Deep Badger

**description**: Companions, guards, allies, a source of warmth in the cold, and an efficient waste disposal system: deep badgers are constant allies to dweorg of the Underworld. Deep badgers are still found in the wild—ferocious beats not to be crossed—but many thousands of years ago they were tempted to the campsites of dwarven miners and explorers, showing little fear and more than a spark of wise curiosity. For their part, early dweorg trained deep badgers to burrow a well or guide away from pitfalls and unstable caverns. Those stout delvers also made good use of the tunnels deep badgers dug for mushroom farming and defensive structures. The fledgling symbiotic relationship blossomed, and today’s domesticated badgers are the result, as common in dwarf settlements as dogs are in human ones.
