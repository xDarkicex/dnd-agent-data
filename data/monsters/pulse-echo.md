# Pulse Echo

**description**: Pulse echoes are lightning elementals with a humanoid torso atop a trail of lightning that hovers above the ground. Traits Charged Body. A creature that touches the pulse echo or hits it with a melee attack while within 5 feet of it takes 5 (1d10) lightning damage. Actions Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage and 4 (1d8) lightning damage. Shock Pulse. The pulse echo releases a wave of electrical energy in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in the area must make a DC 11 Dexterity saving throw, taking 17 (5d6) lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.
