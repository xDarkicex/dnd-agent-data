# Hagspawn Template

**description**: Hagspawns are either born to hags or women who mated with a hag that took on a male form. These women usually die during birthing the ugly and physically disfigured hagspawn. If they survive the fear and ire of the community, these children are often abandoned, left to survive on their own, or kept hidden from society. They are usually evil individuals who keep their powers secret and find it hard to fit into their despised societies.
