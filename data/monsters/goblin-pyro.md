# Goblin Pyro

**description**: Goblin Goblins are most common in the southeastern reaches of the Stolen Lands, closer to the border of Mivon. Goblin Pyro Some goblins take their people’s affection of fire fully into the realm of deadly obsession. These pyromaniacs can be a great boon to a band of goblin raiders, but more often their presence is a double-edged sword; in the heat of the moment, goblin pyros sometimes lose sight of their tribe’s goals and simply set fire to anything that will burn—including their own allies. Traits Spellcasting. The goblin pyro is a 1st-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with spell attacks). It has the following spells prepared: Cantrips (at will): light, mage hand, produce flame 1st level (3 slots): burning hands, grease Actions Torch. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, one target. Hit: 4 (1d4+2) bludgeoning damage plus 1 fire damage. Reactions Goblin Scuttle. When a nonhostile creature the goblin can see ends its movement within 5 feet of the goblin, the goblin moves up to 5 feet. The goblin doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks with this movement.
