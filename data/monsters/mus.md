# Mus

**description**: Mus Mus are born from the lava that spreads from wherever Muspell emerges when it arrives in a world. Lava itself flows through the veins of their massive bodies. The mus are nearly untouched by any affliction that might harm mortals and live their life accordingly. Lesser beings are mostly ignored by them. Many-Eyed. The mus have numerous eyes, which grant them many advantages. Racers of the Flame. Mus craftspeople can forge massive horses and enchant them to a semblance of life. A mus riding such a steed must appear to others as a storm of flame and crackling energy. Unlike Other Yoten. The mus may be an advanced stage of the Yoten life cycle or an offshoot (or progenitor) of the Yoten. Though related, they do not share the same weaknesses that other Yoten exhibit.
