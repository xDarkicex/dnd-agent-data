# Kargstaad

**description**: Kargstaad is a four-armed frost giant from the First World—one of the nymph queen Nyrissa’s favored minions. You can explain his four arms any way you wish in your games, of course—perhaps he was influenced by Abyssal energies, was granted his extra arms as a boon from his deity, or was exposed to strange alchemical pollution. You could even have him possess only two arms when first encountered by the party, only to grow the extra two during combat at a suitably dramatic moment. Actions Multiattack. Kargstaad makes four attacks—one with each hand. Battleaxe. Melee Weapon Attack: +14 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 21 (3d8+8) slashing damage. Rock. Ranged Weapon Attack: +14 to hit, range 60/240 ft., one target. Hit: 22 (4d6+8) bludgeoning damage. Chill Breath (Recharge 5–6). Kargstaad exhales a 15-foot cone of moisture that freezes. Each creature in the area must make a DC 20 Constitution saving throw, taking 49 (14d6) cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature that fails the save is also grappled in ice (escape DC 16), and while grappled in this way, the creature is restrained and takes 49 (14d6) cold damage at the end of each of its turns.
