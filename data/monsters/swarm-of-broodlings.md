# Swarm of Broodlings

**description**: Broodling Subservient to their aboleth broodmothers, these ray-like amphibians scour the swamps they call home in search of creatures with high biomass or interesting features. Once a broodling has found an adequate target, it releases a pheromone, gathering its brethren and enhancing their hive-mind intellect. With their stings, the broodlings inject a toxin that makes their victim more suggestible, allowing them to use their augmented intelligence to submit the creature to their will. Once entranced, the creature follows the broodlings to the broodmother’s lair, where the matriarch decides how best to extract its assets.
