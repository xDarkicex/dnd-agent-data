# Swarm of Nemathy Hatchlings

**description**: Nemathy are aquatic monstrosities that thrive in both salt and freshwater. Solitary predators, nemathy typically conceal themselves near the seabed or within thick aquatic vegetation, only coming together with other of their kin during their breeding season to produce swarms of hatchlings. Nemathy use a modified fin ray that protrude from their foreheads to create bioluminescent light that can attract prey, but their deadliest feature is the neurotoxic and immobilizing ink it produces.
