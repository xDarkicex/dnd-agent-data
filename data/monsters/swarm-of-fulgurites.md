# Swarm of Fulgurites

**description**: Fulgurite When a powerful bolt of lightning strikes an incompletely summoned earth elemental, that energy courses through the rock, breaking it down into sand and flash-smelting it into elemental fulgurite, a translucent, perpetually sparking crystal. Though shattered by the creation process, fulgurites continue to behave as if they were one single entity, forming a swarm of crackling crystals that roll in perfect unison. Air and Earth. A swarm of fulgurites retains characteristics of the elements from which it was forged. It has the strength and resilience of earth, but the power and alacrity of lightning coursing through air. Energy crackles between each fulgurite crystal, steadily charging them up until they can teleport across the battlefield as a streak of lightning.
