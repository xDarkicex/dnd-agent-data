# Iaculo

**description**: Iaculo are snakes that have developed a unique method of hunting prey. They climb trees and await prey passing below. These reddish-brown serpents coil and then straighten in a sudden motion, flinging itself from a tree while ribbed wings spring out from its sides, guiding it with deadly trajectory as it plummets like a spear toward its target. The iaculo’s head is angular and ends in a sharp point above its mouth, which is actually a fang that grows up through the front of its head. This point is hard and sharp enough to penetrate flesh and even armor with the iaculo’s mass and speed behind it. The iaculo folds in its wings in the moment before it strikes, looking much like a spear or ballista bolt as it plunges into its target.
