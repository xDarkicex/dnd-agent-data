# Azure Worm

**description**: Similar to how their kin, purple worms, burrow through deep underground reaches, azure worms wallow and dig through soggy marshlands and deep bogs. Equally at home in any sort of watery environment, azure worms prefer to lie in wait to ambush prey rather than travel to hunt. Both ends of the dreadful azure worm are equally frightening: one features an immense, cavernous maw filled with dozens of sharp fangs, while the other supports a lance-like stinger that secretes a dangerous poison. Traits Breach. If the worm has moved at least 10 feet underwater this turn, it can leap vertically out of the water up to 30 feet and make a bite attack at a creature within range. After the attack, the worm splashes back down into the water. Actions Multiattack. The worm makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its tail stinger. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 22 (3d8+9) piercing damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it must succeed on a DC 19 Dexterity saving throw or be swallowed by the worm. A swallowed creature is blinded and restrained, it has total cover against attacks and other effects outside the worm, and it takes 21 (6d6) acid damage at the start of each of the worm’s turns. If the worm takes 30 damage or more on a single turn from a creature inside it, the worm must succeed on a DC 21 Constitution saving throw at the end of that turn or regurgitate all swallowed creatures, which fall prone in a space within 10 feet of the worm. If the worm dies, a swallowed creature is no longer restrained by it and can escape from the corpse by using 20 feet of movement, exiting prone. Tail Stinger. Melee Weapon Attack: +15 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 23 (4d6+9) piercing damage, and the target must attempt a DC 19 Constitution saving throw, taking 42 (12d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Thrash. Melee Weapon Attack: +15 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 19 (3d6+9) piercing damage. Reactions Reactive Thrash. When the azure worm is hit by a melee attack, the azure worm can use its reaction to make a thrash attack against the attacking creature, flailing out with its entire body to slam and pound against a foe so foolish as to attack them in close combat.
