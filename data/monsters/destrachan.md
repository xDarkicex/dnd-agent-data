# Destrachan

**description**: A destrachan is a hunched, reptilian beast that lopes about on two clawed feet, its eyeless head dominated by a huge circular maw filled with jagged teeth. Despite its bestial appearance, destrachan s are creature s of cunning and cruel intellect that enjoy inflicting pain and viciously toying with their prey. Eyeless. Destrachans have no eyes and are completely blind but possess a pair of complex, tripartite ears it can adjust to different levels of sensitivity to sound, allowing the destrachan to hunt in absolute darkness as if it were able to see. Howling Hunters. Destrachans are carnivores, preferring to stalk and kill live prey, although they also feast on carrion. This habit serves them well, since they often kill more than they can immediately consume. They often hunt in packs, using a complex series of clicks, shrieks, and whistles to communicate with each other.
