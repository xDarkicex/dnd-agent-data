# Fredero Sinnet

**description**: Fredero once served as a paladin of Shelyn, but he fell from grace when faced with the truth that his method of teaching his students was faulty enough to drive them from the fold. Traits Special Equipment. Sir Fredero has three potions of greater healing . Stubborn Conviction. Sir Fredero has advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened. Actions Multiattack. Sir Fredero makes three attacks with his longsword or longbow. Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8+4) slashing damage plus 7 (2d6) thunder damage. Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8+2) piercing damage. Reactions Parry. Sir Fredero adds 2 to his AC against one melee attack that might hit him. To do so, Sir Fredero must be able to see the attacker and be wielding a melee weapon.
