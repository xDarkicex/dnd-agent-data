# Riccio

**description**: A pretty young man with a ponytail who will do the running. He is the son of an artisan who works at San Marco and is even more loyal than Carlo. No torture will make him give up his master. Magic will, however. Traits Favored Enemy (Tieflings). Riccio has advantage on Wisdom (Survival) checks to track Tieflings, as well as on Intelligence checks to recall information about them. As a god-fearing person and the son of an artisan who has created works of art about his beliefs about Hellish creatures, he can recognize the small details which distinguish them from humans. Dueling. Riccio gains a +2 bonus to damage rolls when he is wielding a melee weapon in one hand and no other weapon (already included in stats below). Hunter’s Prey (Horde Breaker). Once on each of Riccio’s turns when he makes a weapon attack, he can make another attack with the same weapon against a different creature that is within 5 feet of the original target and within range of your weapon. Spellcasting. Riccio is a 5th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 11, +3 to hit with spell attacks). Riccio knows the following ranger spells: 1st level (4 slots): detect magic, jump, longstrider 2nd level (2 slots): spike growth Actions Multiattack. Riccio makes two weapon attacks. Rapier. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8 + 4) piercing damage. Pistol. Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, range 30/90 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d10 + 2) piercing damage. Loading. The pistol takes a full turn to load.
