**description**: "Turn the other cheek, brother, and I will turn the other hand" - Fracas from Trivele - These are the lower-ranking Bounty Hunters. They deal with early investigations and less-dangerous cases, and then regroup or call upon the help of a superior when it comes to arresting Knaves of a certain caliber.
