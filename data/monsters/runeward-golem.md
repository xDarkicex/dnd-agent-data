# Runeward Golem

**description**: Runeward golems are durable constructs designed to protect their master at any cost. Clad in heavy armor and infused with various protective runes, a runeward golem typically resembles a large humanoid. Bound to Serve Every runeward golem is bound to a specific runestone, a warden runestone . It must obey the telepathic commands given by anyone holding its stone and continues to do so even if that creature dies. A runeward golem can have only one corresponding warden runestone , and if that runestone is destroyed, the golem is incapacitated until a replacement runestone is created. Designed to Protect Although a runeward golem is a fearsome combatant on its own, it is a protector at its core. Its quick reflexes allow it to fend off attacks made against its master or their allies while the magical runes etched onto its armor infuse both itself and its master with various layers of protection.
