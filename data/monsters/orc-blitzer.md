# Orc Blitzer

**description**: Traits Gnashing Horde. If an enemy the blitzer can see starts their turn within 5 feet of three or more blitzers, the enemy takes 1 piercing damage for each blitzer within 5 feet of them. Minion. If the blitzer takes damage from an attack or as the result of a failed saving throw, their hit points are reduced to 0. If the blitzer takes damage from another effect, they die if the damage equals or exceeds their hit point maximum; otherwise they take no damage. Relentless Minion. When the blitzer is reduced to 0 hit points while they aren’t incapacitated, they can deal 1 piercing damage to an enemy within 5 feet of them. Actions Spear (Group Attack). Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 1 piercing damage.
