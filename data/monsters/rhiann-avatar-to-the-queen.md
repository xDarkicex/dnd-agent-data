# Rhiann, Avatar to the Queen

**description**: Appearance and Personality: Rhiann is light skinned for a wood elf, with short dark hair. If not for her black avatar’s eyes, her face would be pretty, if a bit careworn. Instead, her gaze, even when softened by her ready smile, is hard to meet. As with all avatars, the remnants of Rhiann’s personality are hard to distinguish from the calm, aloof demeanor that comes with such a connection to the Witch Queen. Rhiann exudes a relentlessly positive and encouraging attitude to everyone in the party. She always makes it a point to congratulate everyone on their successes, reminding them that each is a step toward finally overcoming the shadow. When things go poorly, she never assigns blame. Instead, she offers suggestions for how to improve their strategy moving forward. This includes during battles, when she may direct her allies with her cheerfully offered suggestions. Traits Fey Ancestry. Magic can’t put Rhiann to sleep. Bonus Actions Displacement (Recharge 4–6). Rhiann projects an illusion that makes her appear to be standing in a place a few inches from her actual location, causing any creature to have disadvantage on attack rolls against her. The effect lasts for 1 minute, and it ends early if Rhiann takes damage, if she is incapacitated, or if her speed becomes 0. Actions Multiattack. Rhiann makes three Arcane Burst attacks. Arcane burst. Melee or Ranged Spell Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 20 (3d10 + 3) psychic damage. Spellcasting. Rhiann casts one of the following spells using Intelligence as her spellcasting ability (spell save DC 16): At will: dancing lights, mage hand, message, minor illusion 2/day each: disguise self, dispel magic, invisibility, phantasmal force 1/day each: confusion, phantasmal killer . Reactions When Rhiann is hit by an attack, she magically creates an invisible barrier of magical force. Until the start of her next turn, Rhiann has a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack.
