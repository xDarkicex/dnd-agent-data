# Green Hag, Grandmother Merry Covenstead

**description**: Grandmother Merry Covenstead leads a coven of herself and two great-granddaughters, both normal green hags; they masquerade as traveling fortune tellers and herbalists attached to a circus caravan. The coven uses a heavily enchanted wagon pulled by a pair of jet black and blind oxen, rumored to be were-oxen. The oldest and most respected of the circus troupe know what the “weird” sisters are but have come to rely on them for protection and their ability to draw crowds. Nonetheless, the troupe has begun to gain a reputation for dark and forbidden acts, which of course draws even more customers.
