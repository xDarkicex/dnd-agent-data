# Thylacine

**description**: Thylacines are well known dangers of the central Stolen Lands, rivaling wolves in some regions. These creatures are predatory marsupials that look vaguely like lean wolves at first glance—until they stretch open their jaws in almost unnatural displays of aggression. Thylacine The standard thylacine is small and lean, with a set of fang-filled jaws capable of yawning disturbingly wide. It generally has brown fur with tan stripes or spots along its flanks and hind legs. Traits Powerful Jaws. The thylacine’s bite scores a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20. Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 +2) piercing damage.
