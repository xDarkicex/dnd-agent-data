# Thonir of Valholl

**description**: Thonir When Thonir died fighting Boða, he was taken by the valkry to Valholl, like any other valiant raider. However, the primal magic made the Vanir into gods just as Thonir crossed that threshold. He knew, without understanding how he knew, that the Halls of Slain were now his. A place for him to train the worthy dead and, perhaps, to hide from his many losses. Thonir is a good man, but he feels he failed his family and friends when he brought the cursed apples to the Vanir. And most of all, he feels Grimnir itself punished him for that sin by taking Hel from him. Roleplaying Thonir Heartbroken. Thonir misses Hel with all of his being and the grief often consumes him, turning his thoughts to dark places. He spends far too much time wishing and wondering how things might have been different for the two of them. Wise. Despite his own struggles, Thonir is perhaps wiser than even his mother though he would never give himself this much credit. In him there is a balance between valor and vigilance and humanity. He sees the individual, where others are too afraid to do more than lash out.
