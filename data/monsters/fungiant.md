# Fungiant

**description**: The fungiant is connected to all that grows and decays within the forest. Just as a treant is a protector-advocate of its home, so too is a fungiant. But a fungiant is more than a sentient mushroom guardian: it is a manifestation of the mycelial labyrinth that exists throughout its forest home. A fungiant is the lifeforce of the forest given form. Rhizome Labyrinth. When rooted, the fungiant enters a near-sleep-like state and imparts experiences, ideas, and wisdom throughout the forest’s mycelial network; in return, a sliver of sentience is imparted to every other living plant. Thus the Great Gardener can discover all it needs to about the forest as a whole at the same time as providing care and sustenance to all its charges. Even if broken, the labyrinth pathways regrow, for the mushrooms and fungi aren’t many individuals but one multiplicity; there is no grand, single organization, but a vast, timeless map of the forest’s history.
