# Blood Cult Officer

**description**: Traits Spellcaster. The blood cult officer uses Intelligence as its spellcasting ability. The DC for its spells is 14, and the attack bonus is +6. Frightening Leader. Underlings of the blood cult officer gain advantage on saving throws against being frightened or charmed while they can see the blood cult officer. Additionally, they add 1d4 to their damage rolls. Actions Mace. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, one target, reach 5 ft.. Hit: 7 (1d8+3) bludgeoning damage. Cannibalize (1/Rest). The blood cult officer casts cannibalize * . One creature it touches must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or gain a special Injury of Handicap III severity, “Cannibalized”. While Injured in this way, the creature’s highest ability score is reduced by 2 (along with all associated bonuses, not including max hp). Additionally, the blood cult officer’s ability score is increased by 2. At the end of each of its turns, the creature can repeat the saving throw to remove the Injury, but the bonus to the blood cult officer’s ability score remains until the spell ends (duration 1 minute). Guiding Malevolence (1/Rest). The blood cult officer casts guiding malevolence * . One creature it can see within 120 ft. must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the next attack made against the creature before the start of the blood cult officer’s next turn has advantage and deals an additional 16 (3d10) necrotic damage if it hits. Bonus Actions Bloody Smite. The blood cult officer calls upon their dark powers, enhancing their strength at a cost. The next time the blood cult officer hits with a Mace attack, it deals an additional 9 (2d8) necrotic damage, and the blood cult officer's Injury thresholds are reduced by 5 until they suffer an Injury. This ability can’t be used again until the blood cult officer suffers an Injury.
