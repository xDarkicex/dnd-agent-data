# Storm Priest

**description**: Actions Warhammer. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage, or 8 (1d10 + 3) bludgeoning damage if used with two hands to make a melee attack, plus 10 (3d6) thunder damage. Storm Bolt. Ranged Spell Attack: +5 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (3d8 + 3) lightning or thunder (storm priest's choice) damage. Spellcasting. The storm priest casts one of the following spells using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with spell attacks): At will: mending, resistance, thaumaturgy 2/day each: call lightning, gust of wind, thunderwave 1/day each: banishment, control water, control winds, ice storm Reactions Wrath of the Storm (3/Day). When a creature within 5 feet of the storm priest hits the storm priest with an attack, and the storm priest can see the creature, the storm priest can magically force the creature to make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw. The creature takes 9 (2d8) lightning or thunder damage on a failed save, and half as much damage on a successful one.
