# Dire Vvor

**description**: Vvor are a domesticated food source of many cultures. Although still aggressive when protecting their young or a succulent food source, the presence of a vvor herd is a sign of civilization taming the dangerous Underworld. The exception to this is the dire vvor. These great creatures are a strain that refuses to be subdued and remain a challenging hunt. They are fiercely territorial and possess a long memory and deep hatred for those who infringe. It’s surprising such tender meat comes from such an ugly beast, but once you get a taste of properly cured cuts from a vvor’s belly, you’ll do anything for more, even risking your life taking the buggers down in the wild. Much of the allure comes from the hunt itself; at times you’ll feel like you’re the one being hunted! —Kord Steelfoot, wizard-warrior of Stoneholme
