# Komainu Guardian

**description**: Traits Stone Mimicry. The komainu guardian can remain completely still, making no movements at all. While it does so, mortal eyes perceive it as a stone statue of itself, and its true nature can’t be determined by non-magical means. Actions Multiattack. The komainu guardian makes one bite attack and one swipe attack. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (3d4 + 4) piercing damage. A creature hit by this attack is subjected to the maimed condition (−10 ft. of speed and disadvantage on Dexterity saving throws, removed after it regains hit points) for 1 minute. Swipe. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., up to three targets. Hit: 7 (1d6 + 4) slashing damage +3 (1d6) radiant damage. Reactions Intervention (Recharge 6). When an attack is declared against creature that is friendly toward the komainu guardian, the komainu guardian can use its reaction to move up to 30 feet toward that creature’s attacker. If it ends this movement within 5 feet of the attacker, then the attack targets the komainu instead and is made with disadvantage. Retribution (Recharge 5–6). When the komainu guardian takes damage from an enemy within 5 feet of it, it can use its reaction to deal 17 (5d6) radiant damage to its attacker.
