# Chazrac Hunter

**description**: While Chazrac depend mostly on a vegetarian diet, their hunters occasionally bring meat home to their tribe. But the true role of a Chazrac Hunter is to protect the tribe from outside dangers. They track dangerous animals and other creatures in the nearby environment. If anyone attacks the tribe, the hunters not only defend their people, but are also known to find their enemies and destroy their homes or bases, either slaying them all or driving them out of their lands. They might even make alliances with other tribes to attack stronger enemies. The tribes that have been corrupted by hags often hunt something for their mistresses: Unique or supernatural creatures, hidden treasures or forbidden knowledge. They are known to be ruthless and without mercy, usually fighting to the death.
