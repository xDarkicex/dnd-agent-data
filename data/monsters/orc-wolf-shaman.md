# Orc Wolf Shaman

**description**: The orc wolf shaman is a powerful druid that can transform itself into a dire wolf. In combat, it uses its electrically-charged fist weapons that are designed to look like a wolf's paws. The wolf shaman can also summon up to three wolf spirits that can strike and terrify those they attack. Actions Multiattack. The orc makes two Claw attacks. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage plus 3 (1d6) lightning damage. Storm Bolt. Ranged Spell Attack: +5 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (3d8 + 3) lightning or thunder (orc's choice) damage. Spellcasting. The orc casts one of the following spells using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with spell attacks): At will: druidcraft, guidance, mold earth 2/day each: cure wounds, spike growth 1/ day each: conjure animals (wolves), erupting earth, polymorph Spirit Wolves (Recharge 5–6). The orc targets up to 3 creatures it can see within 30 feet of it. A spectral wolf manifests beside each target and bites it. Each target must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 13 (3d8) psychic damage, is knocked prone, and is frightened until the end of orc's next turn. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and isn't knocked prone or frightened. Change Shape (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest). The orc magically transforms into a dire wolf, while retaining its game statistics. This transformation ends if the orc is reduced to 0 hit points or uses a bonus action to end it. Any equipment it is wearing or carrying isn't transformed. Bonus Actions Aggressive. The orc moves up to its speed toward a hostile creature it can see.
