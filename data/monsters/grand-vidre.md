# Grand Vidre

**description**: In order to expedite ascension, several vidre can undergo a ritual wherein they bind themselves to each other, becoming a new amalgamated entity known as a grand vidre. The creation of a grand vidre is a last resort amongst the vidre, for along with greater power a grand vidre has a greater hunger for soul energy.
