# Emorroissa

**description**: The myths and legends of the Mediterranean are replete with strange and mystic serpents of every description. Emorroissa. Not venom, but fresh blood drips from this reddish snake’s fangs while it slithers towards you through the sand. The emorroissa is not an undead, but an animal that had to acquire a taste for blood in order to keep itself hydrated in a dry, hot environment. These snakes live in deep dens under the sands, emerging to attack passing prey. Emorroisse integrate their diet with little desert reptiles and rodents.
