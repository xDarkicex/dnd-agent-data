# Lord Priitus Nerkra

**description**: Priitus Nerkra was once a rogue and master assassin known for his cunning and agility. However, he found himself trapped during a mission gone wrong and facing certain death. That was until he was saved by the kraken wizard Morgawr. In return for his rescue, the kraken demanded Nerkra's absolute loyalty and threatened dire consequences if he ever betrayed it. Nerkra, seeing no other way out, agreed to the terms and became the kraken's agent. Nerkra secured the rank of a minor noble through lies and an arranged marriage to further his standing and increase his access to resources. However, his wife died mysteriously just a few months after the wedding. Nerkra now had access to great wealth to complement his expert skills at lying and manipulation. He continued to work for Morgawr, using his newfound power to carry out its bidding and keep its secrets safe.
