# Drow Demonbinder

**description**: Drow demonbinders are practitioners of the dark arts. They draw and absorb the essence of demons to gain additional powers and abilities, such as temporarily opening a rift into the Abyss. They do not care that doing so condemns them to eternal damnation. Traits Fey Ancestry. The drow has advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can't put the drow to sleep. Fiendish Blessing. The AC of the drow includes its Charisma bonus. Sunlight Sensitivity. While in sunlight, the drow has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight. Dark One's Own Luck (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest). When the drow makes an ability check or saving throw, it can add a d10 to the roll. It can do this after the roll is made but before any of the roll's effects occur. Actions Dagger. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage plus 3 (1d6) poison damage. Claws (Demonbind Form Only). Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d6 + 2) piercing damage. Spellcasting. The drow casts one of the following spells using Charisma as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 16, +8 to hit with spell attacks): At will: dancing lights, eldritch blast (3 beams, 1d10 + 4), friends, minor illusion, poison spray (3d12), prestidigitation 1/day each: circle of death, darkness, faerie fire, finger of death, fireball, levitate (self only), power word stun, wall of fire Abyssal Rift (Recharge 6). The drow opens a momentary Abyssal rift within 60 feet of it. The rift is a 20-foot-radius sphere. Each creature in that area must make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw, taking 13 (3d8) necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. In addition, there is a 50 percent chance that 2d6 dretches or 1d3 rutterkins then appear in unoccupied spaces in the sphere. They act as the drow’s allies and take their turns immediately after the drow. They remain until destroyed or until the drow dismisses them as an action. Demonbind (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest). The drow transform into a demon. Horns grow from her head, her skin turns leathery-red, bat-like wings sprout from her back, and her hands turn into vicious claws. While in this form, the drow's creature type changes to Fiend (Demon) and it gains 30 temporary hit points, resistance to cold, fire, lightning and to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks, and immunity to poison. The effects last for 1 hour, or until the drow dies or uses a bonus action to end it. Reactions Fiendish Rebuke (2/Day). In response to being damaged by a visible creature within 60 feet of it, the drow forces that creature to make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw, taking 22 (4d10) necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
