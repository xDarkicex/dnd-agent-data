# Ember Tectonic

**description**: Tectonic elementals are formidable embodiments of the primal forces of nature. Composed of roiling, ever-shifting elemental materials, these elementals manifest in forms that align with the core aspects of their environment. Cryo Tectonic Cryo tectonics are embodiments of arctic ferocity, their huge forms an amalgamation of jagged ice, snow, and freezing fog that obscures its surroundings while their heavy steps carry the foreboding sound of an iceberg cracking. A chilling sight – literally and figuratively – it can cause geysers of freezing water to erupt from the ground, making it almost trivial by comparison that it has the strength to toss a cow as if it were a pebble. Ember Tectonic Ember tectonics appear as a mass of smoldering rocks and pebbles that continuously melt and reform. Filling the air around them with dense ash, these elementals can cause geysers of scalding water and steam to erupt around them – and release floods of molten debris on anyone who gets in their path. Tempest Tectonic Tempest tectonics are the embodiment of storms and atmospheric chaos and appear as towering masses of swirling storm clouds, lightning, and howling winds. Their form is ever-changing, with flashes of lightning crackling through their bodies and thunder echoing with every movement.
