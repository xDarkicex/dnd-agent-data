# Dryad Witch

**description**: These dryads were the first to abandon King Dasmag due to the corruption of the Matrons of Malice. The dryad witches cultivate the malicious plant life of the Bleak Mire and serve their midnight hag patrons as informants. They communicate with the plants and beasts of the Feyrealm regularly and prey on those they can charm or lure into a trap.
