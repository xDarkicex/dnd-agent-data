# Weathermancer

**description**: Attuned to the atmospheric forces, a weathermancer is able to control and wield weather itself as both weapons and armor. Able to strike with attacks infused with the elements, casting spells that conjure lightning-filled clouds or destructive cyclones, and using the weather to protect themselves, the weathermancer is a dangerous foe – or a staunch ally.
