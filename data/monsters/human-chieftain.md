# Human Chieftain

**description**: As the head of a wilderness tribe or a borderland village, chieftains are proud, capable warriors and reasoned leaders. Known for their tough-but-fair attitude and generally honest nature, the chieftain has to step between argumentative neighbors as often as drive off foul beasts. The wisest of these leaders are canny individuals, drawing the best from a mixed bunch of residents.
