# Centaur Shaman

**description**: Centaur shamans are druids who often lead their tribes or are advisors to the tribe's chieftain. In combat, they prefer to use their spells to heal their brethren but will not hesitate to unleash nature's wrath against their enemies if pressed. Traits Charge. If the centaur moves at least 30 feet straight toward a target and then hits it with a Pike attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 10 (3d6) piercing damage. Actions Multiattack. The centaur makes two attacks: one with its Pike and one with its Hooves or it uses its Storm Bolt twice. Pike. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d10 + 3) piercing damage. Hooves. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (2d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage. Storm Bolt. Ranged Spell Attack: +6 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (3d8 + 3) lightning or thunder (centaur's choice) damage. Spellcasting. The centaur casts one of the following spells using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 14, +6 to hit with spell attacks): At will: druidcraft, gust, thorn whip (3d6) 2/day each: cure wounds, entangle, moonbeam, thunderwave 1/day each: call lightning, freedom of movement, wrath of nature, wind wall Cyclone Blast (Recharges 5–6). The centaur summons powerful, whirling winds in a 30-foot line that is 10 feet wide. Each creature in that line must make a DC 14 Strength saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 31 (7d8) force damage and is pushed up to 10 feet and knocked prone. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and isn't pushed or knocked prone.
