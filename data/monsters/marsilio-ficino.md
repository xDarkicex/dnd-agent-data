# Marsilio Ficino

**description**: Ficino is one of the most revered philosophers of the Renaissance, and teacher, friend and advisor to Lorenzo de’ Medici. His relationsship with the Medici family goes back to Cosimo de’ Medici, who commissioned him to translate Plato’s work into Latin. Marsilio Neoplatonic Academy is an informal gathering of friends at his property in Careggi outside the city walls. Marsilio Ficino is a true polymath with a degree as a medical doctor, and an astrologer-magician who believes in the soul as mediator between the metaphysical and the tangible, material world. He believes that intellect and will enable the soul to transcend mortality and ascend to the divine sphere. According to him, the true philosopher uses natural magic and astronomy as tools to achieve this goal, fueled by the “universal light” and “universal love”. In our campaign, Ficino does have very real magical powers and is the head of the order of white knights (Le Cavalieri di Platone, the Knights of Plato) that fights for Good in the world and can be seen as the active arm of the Platonic Academy. Such activities and ideas have aroused the suspicion of religious hardliners like Girolamo Savonarola, whose incendiary speeches Ficino counters with humanistic benevolence. But despite his outward calm, Ficino is quite concerned by the fervent hate of this mighty adversary. By the time our story unfolds, the master is 59. He is of less than medium height and rather slight build, but with a small round belly. His hair is fair but streaked with gray, the latter matching the color of his eyes. He usually has a pleasant smile on his lips, and is generally of a reassuring disposition. Tips for the GM: Marsilio Ficino is a historical character. You will find him mentioned in the introduction and in the historical background chapter. Marsilio will speak with a low voice to get the attention of his listeners. He will usually not flaunt his vast knowledge, but can fall into lecturing mode if confronted with a problem or asked an interesting question. Don’t hesitate to find out more about him on the internet or the history books listed in the appendix. Traits Powers. Marsilio is an 8th-level philosopher. He has 16 weave points. His feature ability is Intelligence (feature save DC 14). Marsilio knows the following philosopher features: language master, linguistic recall, peer connections, weave points, caustic remark, secrets of the stars, reciprocal speech, codebreaker, good conversation, timely assistance, unveil the motive Actions Fist. Melee Weapon Attack: +1 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1 bludgeoning damage.
