# Wandering Warehouse

**description**: Waguff's Wandering Warehouse of Wonders is a walking shop that roams the countryside looking for potential buyers. The shop is run by the gnome Waguff , a merchant minute in stature even by gnomish standards, who sells a varied inventory of magic items but specializes in sentient, consumable items. Characters can find the Wandering Warehouse virtually anywhere a huge, walking structure could feasibly access. It might saunter up to them as they're traveling the countryside or they may find it resting its legs on a hilltop, in a clearing, or in a small village.
