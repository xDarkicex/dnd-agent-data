# Cannon Golem

**description**: A cannon golem’s internal workings are a mechanical labyrinth that would entertain even the most inquisitive gnome engineer for weeks. Its extradimensional pockets constantly process new black powder and seemingly create cannonballs out of the prime matter of whichever plane it sits. In addition, once built, it seems able to slowly refine its methods of attack—both its slam and the cannon—to either copy or reflect the nature of its creator, which ever the builder chooses. Cannon golems stand about 12 feet tall, but they have no set form in their original manufacture. As a result, most cannon golems reflect the aesthetic of their creator. A drow-made golem might take the appearance of a spider or scorpion, while a dwarven one might depict a smith or mason.
