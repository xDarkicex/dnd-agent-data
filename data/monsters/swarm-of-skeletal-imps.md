# Swarm of Skeletal Imps

**description**: Imp While imps typically act as messengers and spies for their devilish overlords, a desperate lieutenant might pressgang their imp retinue into a fighting force. When called to arms, this unruly mass of red skin, leathery wings, and barbed tails flutters with inefficient cowardice to the periphery of a battle, engaging in the least lethal confrontation it can find. Combat Prowess. A swarm of imps is a tactless fighting force and can never agree on the timing of their individual invisibility abilities, always flickering in and out of vision. When truly pressed, imps’ innate shapechanging ability allows them to meld into a single large creature.
