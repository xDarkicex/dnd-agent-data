# Magma Roc

**description**: These rocs are slightly smaller than their Upperworld cousins, but completely immune to fire, living in nests placed above magma vents for both comfort and safety. Centuries of existence in the Underworld have changed these avians to life in the dark deep. Auditory Predator. The magma rocs eyes have evolved into bizarre auditory receptors. Whenever a magma roc unleashes a sonic scream (often the last thing their prey hears before being carried off for an untimely plunge into magma) these point away from the head, simultaneously protecting its own senses while paralyzing their victims. Ancient Origins. No one is certain when rocs made their way into the Fungi Forest, how long ago that was or how they survived; they only know that they did. For centuries these huge predators of the sky subsisted on whatever they could inside the Rift but over time, like the rift flumphs, their biologies changed to match their new environment. Local lore claims that the lost race that occupied the ruins of the Rift domesticated these fearsome creatures and used them as mounts, but the truth of that legend remains untested. Untamable . Attempting to tame a magma rocs often leads the wayward soul down into the Rift as the bird plunges directly into a lava flow. Magma Dens. Beneath the lava of the deep Underworld, magma rocs dwell in hidden magma caves. These places are said to contain vast mosaics that chronicle the history of the race that once occupied the Ruins of the Rift; there are carvings of humanoids riding dragons through tunnels, a gathering of underground serpents, and the apparent destruction of many of their settlements by their once-steeds, all centered around five orbs that encircle one of these forgotten peoples.
