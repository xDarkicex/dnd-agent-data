# Orthrus

**description**: Chimera, Orthrus Orthruses are two-headed wolf-hounds with shaggy coats, dark sooty gray above and paler ashy gray on their undersides, with a long serpentine tail ending in a third, snake-like head. They were created by the same chthonic mastors that created the all chimeras as the male counterparts to their leonine cousins. However, after generations their monstrous brood grew too unstable and their creators adapted the chimeras to reproduce on their own. Orthruses found themselves no longer needed and even hated by these new chimeras, and so most retreated to the farthest reaches of the world, where the cruel chimeras would not hunt them down. There some still lurk, licking their wounds and seeking the occasional lone chimera to force themselves upon and mate with, spawning more of their kind with great wolves and waiting for the day they can rise against their oppressors. Some orthruses have abandoned their dreams of vengeance and simply seek to live in plenty. These rare orthruses establish their own packs or even take up service with powerful masters like geryoneos, seeing in them a multi-headed kinship. These orthruses are typically neutral in alignment rather than neutral evil.
