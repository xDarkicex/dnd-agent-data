# Sanshu Denki

**description**: Traits Paralyzing Secretions. A creature whose bare skin touches or is touched by a sanshu denki must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw. On a failure, the creature is paralyzed for 1 minute. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. Ambush Predator. The sanshu denki has advantage on its first attack if it began its turn submerged in water. Actions Multiattack. The sanshu denki makes two attacks: one chomp or digestive acids attack and one thrash attack. Chomp. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d6 + 5) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 18 Constitution saving throw. On a failure, the target is paralyzed for 1 minute and is restrained, becoming trapped in the jaws of the sanshu denki. The target can repeat this saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the condition on a success. As an action, a creature restrained by the sanshu denki or one of the restrained creature’s allies can attempt to free the creature from the sanshu denki’s jaws. To do this, it must succeed a DC 18 Dexterity (restrained creature) or Strength (ally) saving throw. While the sanshu denki is restraining a creature, it cannot use this attack on another creature. Digestive Acids. The creature restrained by the sanshu denki takes 17 (5d6) acid damage. Thrash. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 10 ft., up to three targets. Hit: 11 (1d12 + 5) bludgeoning damage.
