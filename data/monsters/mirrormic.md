# Mirrormic

**description**: Mimica mutafigura are patient predators that use disguise as a means of attracting unwary creatures. A mimic will then adhere themselves to their prey, making up for their slow speed and inability to pursue a quarry. Biomancy. Exposure to high levels of biomantic magic has led to subspecies of mimics, Mimica destorquera , that distort light to unbalance their foes. Their elongated pseudopodia let them strike at a greater range before closing the distance and exogenously digesting prey using their hyper-corrosive stomach acid.
