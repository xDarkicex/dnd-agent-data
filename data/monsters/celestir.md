# Celestir

**description**: Background Like most dark elves, Celestir was born far below ground in a realm of rocky tunnels and black lakes, dotted by ancient cities hewn from the foundations of the surface world. After first learning of the sky and the stars above, the elf devoted his whole self to learning all there is to know about the infinite blackness above the ground. He swears it was written in the stars that he so readily found the perfect teacher, an astral wizard named Celestir, from whom the drow eventually inherited his tower and his chosen name. To further honor his teacher and the heavens, Celestir adorned his own body with a network of shining, star-like tattoos which he displays proudly. Roleplaying Celestir While Celestir strives to exude the patience and thoughtfulness of his late teacher, the drow wizard and artificer has an innate sense of wonder that keeps his mind stirring with new ideas. In conversation, Celestir often follows whatever thread he finds the most intriguing, and somehow, his questions always lead to Celestir confidently guessing the star sign of his interlocutor. A true scientist, the elf revels in an incorrect hypothesis – when a visitor doesn't seem to match their sign – and he excitedly follows new lines of inquiry to find the correct answer. In Celestir's case, this usually means murmuring about rare celestial events that could have caused the deviation. When he's actually invited to lecture about the stars, Celestir prefers to show rather than tell, captivating his visitors with astonishing views through his telescope or magically projecting constellations and other cosmic phenomena onto the domed ceiling of his tower. Though he's an astronomer first and foremost, Celestir joyfully follows these demonstrations with offerings of jewelry to match one's sign, books on the stars, or lenses for gazing into other realms.
