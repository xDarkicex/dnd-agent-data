# Gigiat

**description**: These enormous long-haired mountain goats resemble the nimble chamois, with coats of gray, brown, and white and long, back-curving black horns. They are herbivorous creatures and tend to avoid direct contact with humanoids but can become aggressive when spooked and may charge to attack before fleeing to higher ground, clambering on ledges that seem impossibly small for such a large creature. Those pursuing them onto the heights must beware of being knocked off their feet to fall to their doom.
