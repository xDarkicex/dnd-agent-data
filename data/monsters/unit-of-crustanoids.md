# Unit of Crustanoids

**description**: The Crustanoids' military units, known as "Clawguard," are a fearsome sight to behold. Comprised of 15 to 20 warriors, each equipped with formidable razor-sharp claws, they are unmatched in close combat. Working in unison, they form imposing shield walls with their sturdy exoskeletons, adeptly defending against ranged attacks, such as projectiles and ranged spells, while steadily advancing towards their adversaries. Their coordination, unwavering loyalty, and sheer strength make the Clawguard a force to be reckoned with on the battlefield.
