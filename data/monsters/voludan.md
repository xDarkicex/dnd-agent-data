# Voludan

**description**: These white-scaled dragonblooded serve as scouts and tactical leaders. As they are capable of true flight, they can easily reach vantage points and quickly assess situations which would otherwise take far too long for any other dragonblooded. Additionally, their transformation upon death often causes sufficient confusion and terror that, should they be felled while scouting, their enemy may be unable to properly report their findings to their superiors. Gliders. Although dragonblooded have wings, the majority of them are incapable of true flight and are only able to glide. Except for the Voludan, a dragonblooded’s fly speed can’t be used to gain altitude, and they drop 5 feet per round that they remain aloft. Tactics. The tactics used by the dragonblooded vary by their type, but can be generally summarized as single-minded in service to their creator. Each dragonblooded seeks to make the best use of their death as possible, and may even hasten their own demise when escape seems impossible if it means harming a greater number of foes in the process. Traits Mirrored Death. When the voludan dies, its corpse transforms into the body of its slayer, a nearperfect replica that retains any Injuries sustained by the voludan. The corpse can only be identified as false using potent divination magic (or by a creature that saw the fight). Additionally, the effect is horrifying to the voludan's killer. The first time the killer sees the corpse, it must make a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened of all dragonblooded for 1 minute. The creature can repeat the saving throw to end the condition when it ends its turn out of sight of all dragonblooded. Lead By Example. Once per turn, when the voludan attacks a creature, the next attack made against it by one of the voludan’s allies has advantage. The attack must be made before the start of the voludan’s next turn, and the ally must be within 60 feet of the voludan and be able to see it when it makes the attack. Flyby Attacker. Once on its turn, when the voludan hits with an attack, it does not provoke opportunity attacks from the creature it attacked until the end of its turn. Actions Multiattack. The voludan makes two greatsword attacks. Greatsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (2d6+3) slashing damage.
