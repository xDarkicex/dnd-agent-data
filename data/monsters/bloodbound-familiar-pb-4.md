# Bloodbound Familiar (PB 4)

**description**: Traits Blood Focus. All Bloodspeaker class features that are active within a specified range of you are also active within 10 feet of your bloodbound familiar. Inconspicuous. The bloodbound familiar cannot be the target of attacks of opportunity Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: 2 + your proficiency modifier, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 2 (1d4) piercing damage. If the target is affected by one or more malefic words, the attack inflicts an additional 2 (1d4) piercing damage. Blood Bat. Your bloodbound familiar gains a flying speed of 30 feet and telepathically relays everything it can see to you. Additionally, it can see invisible creatures and objects. Mutant Leech. Whenever your bloodbound familiar makes a successful attack with its bite, the target begins bleeding for 1 minute (1d4 piercing damage at the start of each of its turns, removed after it regains hit points). Plague Vermin. When your bloodbound familiar makes a successful attack with its bite, the target becomes poisoned until the end of its next turn.
