# Swarm of Vile Things

**description**: Swarms of vile things are incredibly dangerous natives of the Feywild: sometimes summoned by magic ( conjure vile swarm ). Each swarm has a different assortment of insects and vermin but each swarm glows with a bright teal light. Unlike most swarms, this swarm has the intelligence of a dim goblin and is just smart enough to try and kill prey at night under moonlight. These swarms innately know when an eclipse is about to happen, breaking off from all activities to burrow as deep into the ground as possible until the eclipse is over.
