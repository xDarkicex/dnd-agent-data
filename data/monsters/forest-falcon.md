# Forest Falcon

**description**: A forest falcon is a large, swift raptor adapted to agile flight through dense canopy rather than bursts of speed in open air. It prefers a high perch, watching for movement from prey on the forest floor. The falcon strikes in a diving ambush and can even run down prey on foot. Actions Beak. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: (2d4+3) piercing damage.
