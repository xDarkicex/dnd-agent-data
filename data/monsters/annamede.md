# Annamede

**description**: In the Kingmaker Adventure Path , Annamede is meant to be something of a nonviolent foil against the PCs: a bard in the employ of the nation of Pitax. She can play the role of any well-known public performer in your game, and with adjustments to her alignment can fit any sort of position related to entertainment you desire. Her skills also make her an excellent choice to play the role of a spy in your game, be it someone the PCs must defeat or an agent they must ally with. She works best when encountered with allies she can aid. Traits Special Equipment. Annamede has a dagger +1 . Spellcasting. Annamede is an 8th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 14, +6 to hit with spell attacks). Annamede has the following bard spells prepared: Cantrips (at will): dancing lights, message, minor illusion, prestidigitation 1st level (4 slots): comprehend languages, silent image, thunderwave 2nd level (3 slots): enthrall, invisibility, shatter, suggestion 3rd level (3 slots): hypnotic pattern, sending 4th level (2 slots): dimension door, locate creature Actions Dagger. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one creature. Hit: 6 (1d4+4) piercing damage.
