# Raid Archer

**description**: Raider Raiders are the standard warriors taking part in raids and comprise the bulk of the healthy adult population. They are not soldiers exclusively, such as is the case generally with the Baendur, but instead some are farmers or crafts people. Raid Archer These warriors are trained to fight alongside other raiders and usually behind a shield wall. Raid Cleric Raid clerics generally accompany raiding parties on longer voyages. Raid Druid Though druids are common to all raiding clans, among the Whar they are more prominent, often serving in leadership roles. Raid Leader A raid leader generally leads the raiders manning a single longship or manages an outpost (as opposed to a jarl who oversees an entire clan). A raid leader tends to spend more time training and the greater their skill, the more effective the raiders they lead are. Raid Leader, Jarl A jarl can be built upon these stats. They have 85 hit points and gain the following ability: Indomitable (2/day). When the jarl fails a saving throw, they may choose to reroll it.
