# Grimwalker

**description**: Most esper sentinels follow a path that defines their code and how they use their powers. Grimwalkers are sentinels that follow the path of death, becoming reapers of souls. They often follow a twisted mode of logic for their actions, either through a zealous sense of justice or merely as a crutch for their sociopathic leanings. They lead or serve in groups that share their views, or at least promote them. Those that operate alone often do so in the fringes of the galaxy.
