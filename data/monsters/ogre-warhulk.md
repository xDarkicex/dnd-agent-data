# Ogre Warhulk

**description**: The warhulk is a large ogre that has mastered the giant flail. During combat, the warhulk can move about and swing its massive flail around, hitting multiple foes. Actions Multiattack. The ogre makes two melee attacks. Great Flail. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target . Hit: 17 (2d10 + 6) bludgeoning damage. Javelin. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d6 + 6) piercing damage. Whirling Flail (Recharge 5–6). The ogre warhulk moves up to half its speed and targets each creature within 10 feet of it during its movement. Each target must succeed on a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw or take 17 (2d10 + 6) bludgeoning damage and be knocked prone.
