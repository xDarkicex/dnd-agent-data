# Doprillu

**description**: Doprillus are hulking humanoid-shaped creatures with banded muscles who wear ornate masks at all times that fill their wearers with magical strength and fighting spirit. Despite their human-like frames, these creatures are without doubt alien in body and mind alike, particularly due to their plantlike manes of hair. Doprillus love to battle, especially by grappling, and are eager to start brawls. On neutral ground, a doprillu offers to duel the strongest-looking opponent, but when a doprillu’s home turf is invaded, no rules apply to the confrontation. As befits the superheated blood that fuels them, doprillus make their homes in warm locations: hot jungles, sunny deserts, and underground caverns near sulfur vents. Traits Magic Weapons. The doprillu’s weapon attacks are magical. Mask of Power. If the doprillu’s mask is removed, it loses its regeneration abilities, its immunity to exhaustion and being incapacitated, and it immediately gains one level of exhaustion. If the doprillu puts the mask back on, it regains its abilities and loses all levels of exhaustion. A creature can pull off the doprillu’s mask with a successful DC 20 Strength (Athletics) check. Otherworldly Perception. The doprillu can see invisible creatures and objects as if they were visible. Regeneration. The doprillu regains 20 hit points at the start of its turn if it has at least 1 hit point. If the doprillu takes cold damage, this trait doesn’t function at the start of the doprillu’s next turn. Actions Multiattack. The doprillu makes three fist attacks, or if it has a grappled creature, it makes two body strikes and a fist attack. Alternatively, if the doprillu makes a whirlwind throw, it makes a body strike as a ranged attack immediately afterward. Fist. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 16 (2d8+7) bludgeoning damage plus 9 (2d8) fire damage. If the target is a Medium or smaller creature, it is grappled (escape DC 17). Until this grapple ends, the target takes 10 (3d6) fire damage at the start of each of its turns, and the doprillu can’t grapple another target. Body Strike. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 10 ft. or range 20/50 ft., one creature. Hit: the doprillu uses a Medium or smaller creature it is grappling to strike another creature and deals 14 (2d6+7) bludgeoning damage to the target and half as much damage to the grappled creature. Whirlwind Throw. The doprillu whirls a grappled creature, using it to strike all creatures within 10 feet of the doprillu. All creatures in the area must succeed on a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw or take 14 (2d6+7) bludgeoning damage. The grappled creature takes half the amount of damage taken by each creature struck.
