# Lili, Cherub of Second Chances

**description**: Lili was a young paladin of Life in her mortal existence, dedicated to protecting children and those who could not defend themselves. She was slain by the dark celestials thousands of years ago while protecting an orphanage and given her current form by the Redeemer for her purity of soul and courage in the face of overwhelming adversity. She was tasked with guarding the Font of Heroes Reborn and granting a second chance at life to heroes of legend who deserve her blessing. She takes her responsibility very seriously, despite her childlike naivete. She is honest to the most extreme degree and values life above all else.
