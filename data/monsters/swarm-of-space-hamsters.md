# Swarm of Space Hamsters

**description**: Miniature Giant Space Hamster Only a chosen few know of the secrets of hamsters: their concealed intelligence, telepathy, and extraterrestrial origins. More accurately known as space hamsters, their chaotic ancestries vary wildly depending on the source. Space hamsters form swarms as a response to immediate significant threats, telepathically joining their minds to output increased mental computational power. The mixing of psionic abilities grants the swarm limited bursts of telekinesis and allows them to speak as if they were a single unit, droning telepathic words in perfect synchronicity.
