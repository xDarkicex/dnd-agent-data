# Disciple of Peace

**description**: Peace Cultists Somewhat ironically, cultists devoted to the God of Peace can sometimes be formidable martial artists – though one wouldn’t necessarily guess it from their subdued manner and humble presentation. Trained in martial arts, these cultists are proficient at pacifying and neutralizing threats, which makes them ideal bodyguards and – in the case of the disciple of peace – lethal assassins, when the situation warrants it.
