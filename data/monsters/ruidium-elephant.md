# Ruidium Elephant

**description**: Trampling Charge . If the elephant moves at least 20 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a Gore attack on the same turn, that target must succeed on a DC 16 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. Melee Weapon Attack : +10 to hit (with advantage if the target is a creature missing any hit points), reach 10 ft., one target. Hit : 25 (4d8 + 7) piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) psychic damage. Stomp .
