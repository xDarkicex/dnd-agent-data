# Saw-Tongued Feymoth Adult

**description**: Traits Brittle Wings. The feymoth’s Injury thresholds for its special mobility Aspect are 5 lower than normal. Neon Death. The saw-tongued feymoth adult emits faint red light in a 20-foot radius. The first time a sighted creature enters the light on its turn, or when it starts its turn there, it must make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be sickened until the end of its turn. On a successful saving throw, the creature is immune to this effect for 24 hours. Other feymoths are immune to this effect. Actions Saw-Tongued Slash. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, one target, reach 5 feet. Hit: 21 (4d8+4) slashing damage, and the target must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or gain a special Injury of Handicap III severity, “Deeper Bloodwell”. While Injured in this way, a creature regains half as many hit points whenever it would regain hit points. This Injury automatically recovers at the end of a short or long rest. Siphoning Lick (recharge 5-6). The feymoth adult drinks blood from an adjacent creature with the Bloodwell or Deeper Bloodwell Injury. That creature takes 18 (4d8) damage, and until the end of the feymoth adult’s next turn, it gains a +2 bonus to AC, a +1 bonus to attack rolls, a +10 bonus to damage rolls, its movement speeds increase by 10 feet, and the DC for the saving throw against the Deeper Bloodwell Injury increases by 1.
