# Bog Troll

**description**: Bog trolls are mottled grey humanoids that dwell in peat bogs and prey upon the outskirts of civilization. Their flesh is slick and warted, their bodies covered in dank mud. Many a farmer or traveler has been lured into the bog, following convincing cries for help, only to be set upon by bog trolls. They enjoy openly toying with their lured prey, making pretense at manners and politeness up to, and even during, their planned murder. Brothers. Bog trolls can often be found in small groups, calling each other “brother,” even if they’re unrelated or female. They use this epithet frequently while trifling with their victims. — What’s this, brother? — A tasty morsel, brother! — Yes, brother! Several tasty morsels! Let’s invite them to dinner, brother!
