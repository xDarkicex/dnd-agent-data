# Lu

**description**: “Blood and blood alone is the true leader of this city.” Lu is an infamous crime lord of the Glivenalle Crew (also known as the Crew for almost half a century) a convicted killer, the owner of many brothels, and the manufacturer of a big portion of drugs abused in Kanrath. Today, he is a rich businessperson and he is one of the exceptional mortals that can benefit from noble privileges such as being allowed entry to the 3 - District of Noble Blood , being a member of certain clubs, and being invited to certain parties. He also enjoys making unique weapons, and enjoys using them even more. Lu’s Story Lu was born to a very low-income family that struggled to survive the day and was raised on the streets. After he completed his compulsory education, he refused to work his whole life and get nowhere the way his family had done, and joined a street gang. He committed various petty crimes, and went in and out of prison. After spending decades in Kanrath, he believed that he could market different drugs in poor neighborhoods, and he was right. In just a few years, he had accumulated many loyal customers and hired a gang of people to do his bidding, willing to spill anyone’s blood. As Lu’s fortune grew in size, along with his gang of miscreants, he decided to train assassins who would earn a great deal of money from one job. Since Lu has never accepted jobs to murder any noble, royal, or anyone who might be important for the royalty or the nobility, he has not gotten into trouble with Kanrath enforcers thus far. Even though many members of the Crew were arrested and sentenced to death, Lu has never stood trial. Nobles have always seen him as a tool for their crimes, protection to their tributes, and a source of submission for the general public. Today, Lu has quite the fortune. One of the most interesting rumors about him is his so-called romantic relationship with the Fourth Child . There is no proof of this, no witnesses, no admissions or explanations, but the rumor remains a source of great intrigue for citizens. Lu’s Goals The Glivenalle Crew is the biggest crime organization in Kanrath, and Lu’s main goal is to secure its position to the best of his ability. Other than that, he aims to expand his business to Cyherested and is currently trying to establish good relationships with those who could help him in this endeavor. Roleplaying Lu Living in Kanrath and ruling a criminal organization grants Lu an unexpressive face; he rarely expresses emotion. He generally wears dark suits, expensive accessories, and woody cologne. He likes to smoke cigars and uses his own drugs recreationally. Lu in Combat Lu isn’t afraid of conflict. To the contrary, he enjoys a good fight where he gets to fire a couple of shots. In combat, his inexpressive face changes and beams with joy, so much that his vicious joy scares many. He shouts to encourage and give orders to his crew.
