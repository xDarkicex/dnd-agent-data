# Euryale

**description**: Euryales are powerful medusa matriarchs, with the upper body of a woman, the lower body of a snake, and a mass of undulating cobras for hair. They are related to the similarly powerful stheno but tend to focus on spellcasting. A euryale is 7 feet tall and weighs 460 pounds. Medusa Queens . These rare heretical euryales may have alignments other than evil as well as additional powers gained through their struggle to control their own destinies. It is not unusual for euryales to have various class abilities, usually with divine abilities, such as a cleric, druid, ranger, or evil paladin).
