# Fetid Wyrmbeast

**description**: The Ixxtar Wilds on the Isles of Xantharos are home to many dinosaurs and giant beasts. Still, one of the oldest threats to the territories of Clan Fralkyn was a gargantuan black half-dragon tyrannosaurus rex. Over the past century, the clan organized several hunting parties were to get rid of the gigantic beast, but they were unable to defeat it. The creature always retreated when severely wounded and emerged again to ravage the jungles when fully healed. Clan Fralkyn eventually decided to train a group of young humans as dragon hunters, the Wyrmslayers of Fralkyn, who would also be of use after they defeated the prized black half-dragon beast. The group became highly skilled over a decade of training and was well-equipped with weapons and magic. The Wyrmslayers of Fralkyn were sent on a few training missions and eventually faced the mighty tyrannosaurus rex.
