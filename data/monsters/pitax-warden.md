# Pitax Warden

**description**: Guard Be they the personal protectors of a traveling celebrity or the highly-trained soldiers of a city watch, guards range widely in their level of skill. Pitax Warden While the official guards of the city and kingdom of Pitax are all required to train in various bardic pursuits, their primary role is to ensure that their King maintains control of the land. Clad in their distinctive suits of full plate, they generally avoid using their bardic magic in combat except in acts of desperation. Traits Spellcasting. A Pitax warden is a 5th-level spellcaster. Their spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks). They have the following bard spells known: Cantrips (at will): light, mage hand, mending, message, prestidigitation, true strike 1st level (4 slots): charm person, cure wounds, longstrider 2nd level (3 slots): calm emotions, hold person, locate object 3rd level (2 slots): dispel magic, fear Actions Multiattack. The Pitax warden makes three greatsword attacks. Greatsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d6+5) slashing damage.
