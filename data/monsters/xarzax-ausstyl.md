# Xarzax Ausstyl

**description**: Xarzax Ausstyl was born a noble drow but Welvynn Ausstyl has not seen hope for his progression as an initiate of the Inheritors of the Unbegotten. The archdruid expelled the noble child and threw him in the midsts of the savage frenzied mutants. Over the past century, the noble child became the mightiest of the savages, trying to prove his worth to his patron and the druids of the comic horrors.
