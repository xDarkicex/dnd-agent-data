# Wickerthing

**description**: Wickerthing The wickerthing vaguely resembles the more common wicker. Wickerthings want nothing more than to be left alone—they often attack humanoids that invade their forests or lairs on sight. Others may be retained by powerful raiders or Baendur to serve as guards. A Multitude of Forms. Each wickerthing is unique and they can be of various sizes. Use the wicker archer or wicker sentry stat block for smaller wicker and the wickerthing stat block for larger creatures.
