# Witchbeetle Swarm

**description**: Witchbeetle Swarm These tiny insects are not actual beetles, but instead wooden knots pried from ironheart trees and enchanted by the Ironwood Witches. When slain they look like bits of iron colored wood, dry enough that they easily crack and crumble. Nefarious Spies. Their true danger lies in their ability to observe others. Any witch may see and hear what a witchbeetle sees and hears, as long as they are on the same plane of existence. The Swarm. An individual witchbeetle is not a threat, but they can be dangerous when encountered in a swarm.
