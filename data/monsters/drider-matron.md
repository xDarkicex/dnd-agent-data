# Drider Matron

**description**: The process for becoming a drider is a torturous one. Not only is the physical change painful—ritual grafting and mutative elixirs—but driders carry a societal burden they will never escape and can never fulfill. Paragons of the Spider Goddess. To the drow, the ideal drider is a soldier, both servant and leader, the embodiment of power, grace, and ambition. At creation it would seem a new-made drider has all the traits needed to achieve this lofty ideal. As they age, they evolve. Their innate poison becomes more potent, their jaws distend and open to become mandibles. Their spider carapace grows layered and sharp. But as their form adapts to suit the drow ideal of what a drider should be, their minds begin to fracture.
