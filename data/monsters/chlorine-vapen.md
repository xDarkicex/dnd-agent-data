# Chlorine Vapen

**description**: Often found in swamps, toxic fens, or areas plagued with noxious fumes, vapens are elementals composed of a volatile mixture of gases. A vapen appears as swirling clouds, although its abilities, smell, color – or lack thereof – are determined by which type of vapen it is. Ammonia Vapens Ammonia vapens are dense, white vapors that churn and roil like mist and exude a sharp, choking aroma that immediately burn the eyes and skin of their prey. Chlorine Vapens Chlorine vapens manifest as swirling, yellow-green clouds with a pungent odor and emit a hissing sound as their form reacts with moisture in the air, searing organic tissue and corroding metal on contact. Monoxide Vapens Monoxide vapens are odorless, colorless, and nearly imperceptible, appearing only as a slight distortion in the air as they silently suffocate unwitting victims. Suggested Weaknesses A vapen may have a weakness related to its unique type that a character can discover with a successful DC 13 Intelligence (Nature) check: Aqueous Aversion (Ammonia Only). If a gallon or more water is poured over the vapen, it can't take any actions until the end of its next turn. Weak Form (Monoxide Only). If the vapen is exposed to a strong wind such as a gust of wind spell, it takes 2d6 Force damage. Sunlight Hypersensitivity (Chlorine Only). If the vapen starts its turn in sunlight or enters it on its turn, it takes 2d6 Radiant damage.
