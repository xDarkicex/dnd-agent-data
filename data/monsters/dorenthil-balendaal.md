# Dorenthil Balendaal

**description**: Dorenthil Balendaal, a courageous deep-sea elven knight, commands the seas astride his mighty steed, Tide-splitter, a giant seahorse. Renowned for his prowess in mounted combat, Dorenthil wields a trident with unparalleled skill, devastating enemies with precise strikes. When the City of A'rvanshy fell under the wrath of Morgawr, the nefarious kraken necromancer, Dorenthil recognized the futility of sacrificing himself and his surviving knights. Determined to aid, protect, and ultimately liberate the city, he leads his loyal companions on a daring quest, seeking alliances, uncovering ancient secrets, and strategizing in his colossal starfish fort, Starhaven, with a single goal in mind—to reclaim A'rvanshy from its sinister captor.
