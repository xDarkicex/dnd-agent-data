# Apaxrusl

**description**: Apaxrusl, or sand drudges, are created through dark rituals that merge a corpse with desert sand. The damned souls filling the apaxrusl give it intelligence, but its constructed form keeps it loyal, making it a valuable asset to its creator. Necromancers often create apaxrusl to lead small groups of undead on specific tasks, confident in the construct’s ability to execute orders and lead the undead while away from the direct control of the necromancer. The apaxrusl doesn’t require air, food, drink, or sleep.
