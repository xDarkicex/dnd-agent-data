# Drow Warmage

**description**: The drow warmage is the esteemed result of a well-rounded drow education. Most of the drow nobility that do not enter the clergy train in the variety of arts required to earn the title warmage. Just as comfortable slinging spells from the rear as they are trading blows on the front line, a drow warmage is the source of their race’s reputation on the battlefield.
