# Xae

**description**: Xae lost her life in the pursuit of a legendary competition held by one of the Eldest, and lingers after death as a ghost. She works best when the story supports the themes intrinsic to her backstory. However, you can also use these statistics to represent a brand new type of undead in your game, such as an incorporeal spirit who has been untethered from time itself. In this case, consider having Xae come from some strange dimension or other reality from the far corner of the multiverse. Traits Ethereal Sight. Xae can see 60 feet into the Ethereal Plane when she is on the Material Plane, and vice versa. Incorporeal Movement. Xae can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain. She takes 5 (1d10) force damage if she ends her turn inside an object. Actions Multiattack. Xae makes two withering touch attacks. Withering Touch. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 24 (7d6) necrotic damage. Etherealness. Xae enters the Ethereal Plane from the Material Plane, or vice versa. She is visible on the Material Plane while she is in the Ethereal Plane, and vice versa, yet can’t affect or be affected by anything on the other plane.
