# Grove Bear

**description**: Grove bears resemble black bears with blond snouts, but they are slightly smaller and noticeably quicker. When grove bears clash to defend territory or compete for mates, they engage in brutal wrestling matches, each attempting to pin the other until one bear loses its nerve and flees. Actions Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: (2d4+3) slashing damage. On a hit, the target is grappled (escape DC 13). Until this grapple ends, the bear can’t use its claws on another target.
