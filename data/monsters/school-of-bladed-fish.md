# School of Bladed Fish

**description**: Sea Monsters The Serpent Sea, as well as the many rivers and lakes of Grimnir, are host to a wide variety of fish and aquatic mammals, such as seals and whales. The creatures detailed here are, for the most part, the most dangerous of sea borne predators, save for the great serpent herself. Bladed Fish (School) These fish are covered with sharp, metal-like scales that inflict considerable damage to prey and anyone foolish enough to get too close to them. From a distance the sound of an especially large school resembles that of the din of battle—blade striking blade.
