# The Fourth Child

**description**: “Weapons are weapons, and words are words. The fate of a fool who mistakes one for the other is to perish in the hands of a swordless monarch or by the steel of a deafened warrior.” The Fourth Child is an old vampire with the looks of a youthful halfling girl. She was turned into a vampire almost 200 years ago by the Founder and she has retained her looks since the transformation. As she wasn’t given a name, neither by her parents nor the Founder; most people address her as “Princess”, “Lady”, or “Highness”. She has bright blue eyes, perfectly smooth and pale skin, platinum blonde hair, and sharp facial features. The Fourth Child’s Story The Founder took a halfling infant from a mortal family to raise as his daughter. He was not able to make a child of his own, so he chose this way instead. He was surrounded by people who trusted him and obeyed him, but they did not love him; not even his wife. He wanted to get that love that he lacked and longed for from a daughter. He raised the Fourth Child in his Royal Castle, only leaving her side for urgent matters. He truly cared for her and eventually, started to love her as a daughter and she in turn loved him as a father. The Founder tended to her education and upbringing personally and attended to her needs. When she turned 19, he turned her into a vampire to immortalize their relationship as father and daughter. For over a century, their respect and love for each other grew. She was well-versed in politics and crowd manipulation. Her ideas, supported by the Founder’s raw power, helped the vampire society establish almost absolute control over the citizens of Kanrath. People followed her aimlessly and offered her their blood without hesitation. Then one day, the First Child betrayed the Founder and struck him down with treacherous tricks. The Fourth Child stood with her father, but to no avail. The First Child did not kill the Fourth Child because she had too many followers and it would probably cause a riot. He thus imprisoned her in the Royal Castle instead, forbidding her to leave. The Fourth Child’s Goals She wants to reunite with her father by resurrecting him and she wants to take revenge on the First Child, ensuring eternal suffering for him. Roleplaying the Fourth Child She is extremely cunning, careful, and seemingly friendly. Her offers are always tempting, her words are always persuasive, and she always seems honest. She is a master of manipulation. Everyone thinks they are her friend, until it’s far too late. She can persuade anyone who she can find to do her bidding without them noticing. The Fourth Child in Combat She doesn’t hesitate in combat. She analyzes the situation and takes the most efficient action without any delay. She uses the environment, the creatures around her, and any resources she can access to her advantage. If she becomes aware that the danger is too great for her to handle, she escapes.
