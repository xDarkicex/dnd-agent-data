# Well Trickster

**description**: Traits Regeneration. The well trickster regains up to 10 hit points at the start of its turns. Actions Multiattack. The well trickster makes one sludge spray attack against one creature, then makes a dilemma attack against another creature. Sludge Spray. Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target. Hit: The target must make a DC 20 Constitution saving throw, taking 26 (4d12) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Dilemma. Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target. Hit: The target must make a DC 17 Wisdom saving throw. On a success, the target is subjected to one of the following conditions of its choice until the well trickster is defeated. On a failure, the target is subjected to three of these conditions of its choice instead. A condition that has been chosen can’t be chosen again in the same combat encounter, even by different targets. If no conditions remain unchosen, then the target takes (17) 5d6 psychic damage per condition that would have been inflicted instead. The conditions are blinded, deafened, disoriented, frightened, poisoned, and maimed, as described in the SRD, and bleeding, disoriented, distracted, maimed, and weakened, as described in New Conditions in Adventures in Rokugan .
