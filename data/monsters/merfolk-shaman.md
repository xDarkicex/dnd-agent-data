# Merfolk Shaman

**description**: Presented in this section are three merfolk nonplayer character examples: the merfolk priest, merfolk scout, and merfolk shaman. Traits Amphibious. The merfolk can breathe air and water. Actions Spear. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d6 + 1) piercing damage, or 5 (1d8 + 1) piercing damage if used with two hands to make a melee attack. Frost Bolt. Ranged Spell Attack: +5 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d8 + 3) cold damage, and the target can't use reactions until the start of the merfolk’s next turn. Spellcasting. The merfolk casts one of the following spells using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 13): At will: druidcraft, light, shape water 2/day each: cure wounds, hold person 1/day each: control water, tidal wave, wall of water, watery sphere Change Shape (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest). The merfolk magically transforms into a giant sea horse or giant octopus, while retaining its game statistics. This transformation ends if the merfolk is reduced to 0 hit points or uses a bonus action to end it. Any equipment it is wearing or carrying isn't transformed.
