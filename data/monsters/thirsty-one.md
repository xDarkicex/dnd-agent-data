# Thirsty One

**description**: Thirsty ones are the disturbed remnants of creatures slain by the catastrophic infusion of negative energy even as their bodies were nearly on death’s door from thirst or from the anhydrous leaching of their body moisture by an alien environment. Thirsty ones typically haunt the places where their undeath began, seeking to leach from the living in hopes of somehow drawing forth enough moisture to literally pull their lives back together.
