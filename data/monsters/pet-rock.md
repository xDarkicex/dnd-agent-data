# Pet Rock

**description**: Widely considered to be the perfect familiar, requiring no food or water and possessing extremely high natural defenses, a pet rock can be conjured by most spellcasters. Though it doesn’t act with any agility (indeed, it doesn’t move at all), it can make a formidable weapon when thrown.
