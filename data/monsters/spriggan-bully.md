# Spriggan Bully

**description**: Spriggan Love, happiness, and beauty have no meaning for nearly all spriggans, for their minds have long since abandoned the capacity to understand and enjoy such positivity in the world. Violence, malice, and distortion have expanded in spriggan society to fill the aching void left behind by their ancient expunging of hope from their culture. Spriggans resemble muted, hairless gnomes with oversized, pointed ears and a feral appearance. Many of them are gaunt and haggard, with cruel eyes. Their skin tones tend to be varied among many different shades of blue, ranging from a pale sky color to a deep midnight. Spriggan Bully The typical spriggan is a bully who prefers to take live prisoners in fights, if only to ensure that they have a large stock of living victims to torment, tease, and abuse. They train to use weapons in nonlethal ways that inflict the maximum amount of pain even as they guard against accidental death. When not bullying prisoners or hunting for new victims, spriggan bullies enjoy drinking, watching others fight, and fighting among themselves. Traits Innate Spellcasting. The spriggan bully’s innate spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 11). The spriggan can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: faerie fire, fear, shatter Actions Multiattack. The spriggan bully makes two attacks with its morningstar. Morningstar. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8+3) bludgeoning damage. Light Crossbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8+2) piercing damage. Reactions Enraged Growth. When it takes damage, the spriggan bully magically increases in size, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying. While enlarged, the spriggan is Large and it gains the following benefits: +5 feet to the reach of its melee attacks. It regains 5 hit points. +2 to its Armor Class and attack rolls. Doubles its damage dice on Strength-based weapon attacks. If the spriggan lacks the room to become Large, it attains the maximum size space possible in the space available. Any creature adjacent to the spriggan must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or is pushed back to allow for the spriggan’s growth. The spriggan bully returns to its normal size at the end of its next round and gains one level of exhaustion that lasts for 1 hour.
