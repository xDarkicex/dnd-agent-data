# Wererat Captain

**description**: A wererat captain is the boss of a local gang of wererats. It reports to and follows the whim of the pack lord. The captain is a crafty veteran that guides its less experienced underlings to fight more efficiently. Traits Keen Smell. The wererat has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Pack Tactics. The wererat has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the wererat's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated. Distracting Strike (1/Turn). When the wererat hits a creature with a weapon attack, the next attack roll against the target by an attacker other than the wererat has advantage if the attack is made before the start of the wererat's next turn. Actions Multiattack. The wererat makes three attacks, only one of which can be a Bite. It can use Maneuver Allies in place of one attack. Bite (Rat or Hybrid Form Only). Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage. lf the target is a humanoid, it must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or be cursed with wererat lycanthropy. Rapier (Humanoid or Hybrid Form Only). Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8 + 2) piercing damage. Hand Crossbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage. Maneuver Allies (Recharge 5–6). Up to four allies within 60 feet of the wererat that can hear it can each use their reaction to move up to half their speed without provoking opportunity attacks. Change Shape. The wererat magically transforms into a rat-humanoid hybrid or into a giant rat or back into its true form (humanoid), while retaining its game statistics (other than its size). This transformation ends if the wererat is reduced to 0 hit points or uses a bonus action to end it.
