# Priest of the Blood God

**description**: The priests of the Blood God, or blood priests, worship blood. Although they can be of any alignment, can chase highly different goals in life, work under different organizations, or even battle each other on the opposite sides of a war, they all respect the value of blood. The Blood God is worshiped in many different cultures, from the great cities of Tolia to the region’s mountain tribes. As a result, different priests of the Blood God can fill different roles in their own environment; they can be great warband leaders, tacticians, healers, cult leaders, wandering scholars, messengers, and many other things. You can add to or subtract from the skills as you see fit. However, whatever role a priest plays in their own societies, they all know how to spill blood or how to stop it. These priests always carry a symbol of the Blood God on their person: a single drop of blood.
