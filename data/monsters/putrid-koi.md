# Putrid Koi

**description**: Traits Weeping Sores. A creature that begins its turn within 5 feet of one or more putrid koi must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw. On failure, the creature is subjected to the poisoned condition until the end of its next turn. If it is already poisoned, it instead takes 4 (1d8) poison damage. Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage +2 (1d4) poison damage.
