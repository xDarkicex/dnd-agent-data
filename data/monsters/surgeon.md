# Surgeon

**description**: A thick apron caked in blood and large scalpels and bone saws in hand, the mad surgeon is obsessed with the dissection and dismemberment of their foes. Formerly trained in the art of medical practice, they are expertly trained at finding a target's weakest points, and exploiting them.
