# Huge Water Elemental

**description**: Huge water elementals appear as gigantic waves 25 feet tall and 15 feet in diameter. These monstrous beings can unleash powerful jets of crushing water. Traits Water Form. The elemental can enter a hostile creature's space and stop there. It can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing. Freeze. If the elemental takes cold damage, it partially freezes; its speed is reduced by 20 feet until the end of its next turn. Actions Multiattack. The elemental makes two Slam attacks. Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d10 + 5) bludgeoning damage. Surging Wave (Recharge 6). The elemental moves up to its speed in a straight line. During this move, it can enter creatures' spaces. A creature whose space the elemental enters must succeed on a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw. On a successful save, the creature is pushed 5 feet to the nearest space out of the elemental's path. On a failed save, the creature falls prone and takes 16 (2d10 + 5) bludgeoning damage. If the elemental remains in the prone creature's space, the creature is also restrained until it's no longer in the same space as the elemental. While restrained in this way, the creature, or another creature within 5 feet of it, can make a DC 15 Strength check. On a success, the creature is shunted to an unoccupied space of its choice within 5 feet of the elemental and is no longer restrained. Whelm (Recharge 4–6). Each creature in the elemental's space must succeed on a DC 17 Strength saving throw. On a failure, the target takes 16 (2d10 + 5) bludgeoning damage. If it is Huge or smaller, it is also grappled (escape DC 15). Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained and unable to breathe unless it can breathe water. If the saving throw is successful, the target is pushed out of the elemental's space. The elemental can grapple one Huge creature or up to two Large or smaller creatures at one time. At the start of each of the elemental's turns, each target grappled by it takes 19 (3d8 + 6) bludgeoning damage. A creature within 5 feet of the elemental can pull a creature or object out of it by taking an action to make a DC 18 Strength and succeeding.
