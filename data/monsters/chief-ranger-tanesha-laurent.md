# Chief Ranger Tanesha Laurent

**description**: Tanesha, one of Prince Abdalla Laurent's sisters, is a fierce woman in her mid-thirties. The Clawstrider Ranger is athletic with flowing wavy black hair, a round face, and stern brown eyes. She is adorned with many piercings in her nose and ears. Tanesha is the current Chief Ranger of the Clawstriders, chosen to speak for all the warrior bands and lead them when they all fight together. She rides into battle atop a great red Tyrannosaurus Rex named Konak.
