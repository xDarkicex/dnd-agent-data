# Mana Ray

**description**: A mana ray bears the flat body of a ray with a tail ending in slender tentacles and specialized fin-wings that let it hover effortlessly in the air. Using the tentacles on its tail, a mana ray can latch onto other creatures and either enhance or weaken that creature's ability to wield magic. This ability makes the ray a coveted subject of study for scholars – and a valuable companion for spellcasters and their like. Crystalline Core At the heart of a mana ray is a crystalline core. When a mana ray dies, this magic core remains, untouched by time and decay, and by infusing the core with arcane energy, the mana ray can be brought back to life. Mana rays procreate by infusing precious crystals with a sliver of their life force – requiring the effort of numerous rays to give life to another. Clever Companions Mana rays are much more intelligent than their seaborne counterparts. Most mana rays live in large herds and inhabit areas that are dense with magical activity. Drawn by magic, it isn't unheard of for mana rays to form lasting bonds with other creatures strongly connected to the arcane, such as sorcerers or wizards. Simultaneously a wizard's best friend and most hated enemy. It all depends on whether you're the one feeding it or not – another good reason to always bring snacks! – the Wanderer
