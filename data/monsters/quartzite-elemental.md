# Quartzite Elemental

**description**: Quartzite Elementals are large elementals composed of rock and quartz and held together by magnetic fields and with crystalline clusters colored in shades of light pink, purple, or yellow, depending on the iron content and other impurities within. Typically found in regions abundant with silicate minerals, quartzite elementals blend seamlessly with their surroundings and could easily be mistaken for ordinary rock formations, as they can remain inert and motionless for long periods unless disturbed. Fearsome Protectors Despite quartzite elementals' apparently peaceful integration with nature, they are fiercely territorial and spring to life with startling aggression if their domain is threatened. A living embodiment of quartz' qualities, quartzite elementals use vibrations to sense their surroundings and can disrupt foes with powerful resonant frequencies or by refracting light into blinding beams. Leveraging their piezoelectric nature, quartzite elementals can also convert the kinetic energy from physical impacts into electrical energy within their crystalline structure and unleash it as deadly electrical bursts against their adversaries.
