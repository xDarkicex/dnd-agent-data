# Cuegle

**description**: Cuegle Though vaguely humanoid in form, the cuegle is not a thing of the natural world, rather being native to the Dimension of Dream. These alien creatures stand about four and a half feet tall, with a broad, bipedal body. The resemblance to a humanoid ends there, as its body is covered in coarse, black hair, and three tentacle-like appendages sprout from its torso—two from the shoulders, and the third from the center of its torso. These arms are more like tails or extensions of its spine, composed of vertebrae and powerful muscles, able to write and constrict like a snake. A curving horn rises from its forehead, beneath which are three large, round eyes: one blue, one red, and one green. The eyes can move independently of one another. Its mouth has five rows of teeth, and its jaws extend outward from the skull when it bites, allowing it to use all its teeth to rend and tear its prey. Cuegle are cruel predators, preferring to hunt sentient prey. They prey upon the solitary, the weak, and the undefended. If its favored prey are inaccessible, they will sustain themselves on pets and livestock, if only to cause grief to those on which it would prefer to feed. Cuegle attacks are typically stealthy, with the creature attempting to kill or subdue its prey quickly before carrying it off to its lair: typically, a cave or ruin nearby. Cuegle lair within a few miles of local humanoid settlements if possible. See Through Time. The eyes of a cuegle have sight beyond the norm, piercing the temporal veil. Its central red eye sees the present and the blue eye sees into the past, while the green eye sees into the future. Focusing these eyes on a creature allows the cuegle to see through the expanse of the creature’s timeline, giving it insight into the creature and its future and past deeds. Temporal Tastes. A cuegle’s palate is influenced by what it sees in the timeline of its victims. The choicest morsels are innocents it views, with its green eye, that will commit great evils in the future. Only slightly less delectable are goodly individuals who have redeemed themselves of evil acts in the past, seen through its blue eye. Child Snatcher . This fiendish beast creature has a predilection for stealing newborns at the beginning of their timeline, and any child in a cradle is in danger. What happens to the babies it steals is not known; The only thing that is certain is that they are never seen again. Because of its aversion to oak and holly, wood from these trees is often placed in babies' cradles to protect them.
