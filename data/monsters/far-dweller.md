# Far Dweller

**description**: The tall gangly humanoid creature looms over its prey. Floating inches above the ground, its hands out to either side as shifting motes of otherworldly energy float amongst its long, gnarled fingers. A bloom of thick writhing tentacles sprout from its neck where a head should be, caressing and grasping towards its foes. Chittering whispers and slurping chorused mumbles of maddening truths and unfathomable realities echo in the minds of those who bear witness to this mastermind of the space between worlds.
