# Alasen

**description**: The weretiger Alasen can be encountered in King Irovetti’s palace in Pitax, where she’s been staying as a guest. In Kingmaker, she’s the leader of the Catspaw Mercenaries, but she can work just as well as the leader of any group of bandits or, even better, a tiger-themed thieves’ guild. For a higher level party, you can use Alasen’s statistics instead to represent an entire cult or secret society of high-level, deadly weretigers. Traits Brute. A melee weapon deals one extra die of its damage when Alasen hits with it (included in the attack). Keen Hearing and Smell. Alasen has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing and smell. Pounce (Tiger or Hybrid Form Only). If Alasen moves at least 15 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a claw attack on the same turn, that target must succeed on a DC 18 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. If the target is prone, Alasen can make one bite against it as a bonus action. Shapechanger. Alasen can use her action to polymorph into a tiger-humanoid hybrid, into a tiger, or back into her true form—which is that of a human. Her statistics, other than her size and AC, are the same in each form. Any equipment she is wearing or carrying isn’t transformed. Alasen reverts to her true form if she dies. Sneak Attack (1/Turn). Alasen deals an extra 17 (5d6) damage when she hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of Alasen that isn’t incapacitated and Alasen doesn’t have disadvantage on the attack roll. Actions Multiattack (Humanoid or Hybrid Form Only). In humanoid form, Alasen makes two scimitar attacks or two longbow attacks. In hybrid form, she can attack like a humanoid but with a bite attack as well, or make two claw attacks and one bite attack. Bite (Tiger or Hybrid Form Only). Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d10+5) piercing damage. If the target is a humanoid, it must succeed on a DC 16 Constitution saving throw or be cursed with weretiger lycanthropy. Claw (Tiger or Hybrid Form Only). Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d8+5) slashing damage. Scimitar (Humanoid or Hybrid Form Only). Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d6+5) slashing damage. Longbow (Humanoid or Hybrid Form Only). Ranged Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8+4) piercing damage.
