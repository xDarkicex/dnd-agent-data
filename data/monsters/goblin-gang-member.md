# Goblin Gang Member

**description**: All members of Krenko’s gang are goblin gang members. Each goblin carries a whistle (worn on a rope around its neck), which it can blow as an action to sound the alarm. A goblin might carry another item it obtained elsewhere, employing it in combat if an opportunity presents itself. You can roll or choose from the items on the Goblin Gang Possessions table. More information on these items can be found in chapter 5 of the Player’s Handbook .
