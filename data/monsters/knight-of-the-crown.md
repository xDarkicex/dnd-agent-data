# Knight of the Crown

**description**: One of a few dozen, these pious paladin champions of the realm swear a lifelong Oath of Protection to the crown and the kingdom. Knights of the Crown are held in the highest esteem throughout the realm, serve in the front lines in times of war, and act as personal guards to the royal family and the royal palace.
