# Warlock

**description**: Bound by a pact with a higher power, whether it be a fiend, fey lord, dragon, or another formidable entity, the warlock seeks to rise to greater heights by harnessing the granted power in exchange for loyal service. Driven by a relentless hunger for power and influence, warlocks delve into forbidden knowledge, weaving eldritch spells with unearthly prowess. Their path is one of cunning and manipulation, utilizing the dark arts bestowed upon them to manipulate allies and adversaries alike, all in pursuit of personal ascension and the fulfillment of their sinister desires.
