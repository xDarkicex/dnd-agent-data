# Othovir

**description**: Othovir is a gifted harness-maker who doesn't talk about his family or where he came from. He cares about his business, his clients, and his good name. Ideal. "Find what you do well, and do it to the best of your ability." Bond. "I won't allow my name to be tarnished." Flaw. "I get angry when others pry into my private life." Traits Spellcasting. Othovir is a 2nd-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 13; +5 to hit with spell attacks). He has the following sorcerer spells prepared: Cantrips (at will): blade ward, fire bolt, mending, prestidigitation 1st level (3 slots): mage armor, thunderwave, witch bolt Actions Rapier. Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d8) piercing damage. Reactions Parry. Othovir adds 2 to his AC against one melee attack that would hit him. To do so, Othovir must see the attacker and be wielding a melee weapon.
