# Gromog

**description**: Gromog isn’t at his best when he’s encountered, as threatening a party of adventures with an ogre during one of their first combats is far too dangerous! These statistics work well for a potential ogre ally the PCs encounter in a dungeon, perhaps a lumbering creature who’s been left imprisoned in a cell for many months, or a hapless ogre who’s been trapped in a cave by a collapse. In any event, he might have a change of heart and learn to be less awful after being rescued from his plight. Traits Blinded. Gromog is blind. Itchy. Gromog’s lice cause him to scratch incessantly, distracting him from what is going on. He stops mid-conversation to focus on ridding himself of the pests, and even in the throws of combat, he must succeed on a DC 10 Wisdom saving throw or only be able to use his action to scratch his irritated skin. Actions Ogre Hook. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d8+4) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
