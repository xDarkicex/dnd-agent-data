# Drow Warrior

**description**: The soldiery of any given house comprises the bulk of the house’s “display”—that is, the standing army the house can muster at any one time. Little is expected of a house’s display forces beyond marching, standing at attention, and wearing the house’s insignia. Even so, nearly all drow undergo training in the basics of sword and crossbow, as well as learning tactics surrounding their innate spellcasting. Most dark elves will serve fifty to one-hundred years amongst the common soldiery before embarking on a path, either to bolster their family’s status or seek influence on their own.
