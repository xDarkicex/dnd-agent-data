# Avatar of Cegilune

**description**: If Cegilune is seen during a new moon then she appears as a haunted and naked pale humanoid woman of a young age. If the avatar is seen on the full moon then she appears as an ancient crone that can barely stand up. On all other nights she assumes her nightmare form, which resembles a night hag with a black smear for a head and the captured souls she contains pushing against her flesh in an attempt to escape. Cegilune only rarely sends her avatar to a Material Plane during the daylight or a night without moonlight. Cegilune’s avatar will immediately plane shift if any form of eclipse begins.
