# Dweorg Paladin

**description**: Dweorg paladins are holy warriors that hold faith and honor above all else. While a clan may claim a dweorg paladin as a favored daughter or son—as the paladin’s name would indicate—all know that those who enter the faith militant do so to honor their clan, but not to serve it. Instead the paladin serves as a beacon of those dwarven ideals to which their faith is dedicated. For a paladin, concerns of family and clan are held secondary to those of the church. In Service to Balir. Dweorg paladins who’ve chosen Balir as their god are a common sight in the dweorg cities of the Underworld. While they adhere to the faith’s tenets of perseverance at industry and dedication to clan and industry, they do so apart from the Church of Balir as a whole. Following in the footsteps of the god himself, dweorg paladins seek to forge their own path and find their own cause in service to Balir.
