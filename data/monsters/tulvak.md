# Tulvak

**description**: Tulvak might strike the PCs as an unusual green hag thanks to her variant spell-like abilities and her disdain of covens. Of course, you can abandon the latter personality quirk to use these statistics to represent the three members of a unique coven of green hags. By swapping out her weapon of choice for another, you can further give a unique spin to this group. Traits Amphibious. Tulvak can breathe air and water. Innate Spellcasting. Tulvak’s innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 17, +9 spell attack). She can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: acid splash, dancing lights, disguise self, invisibility, message, minor illusion, pass without trace, tongues 3/day (each): dominate person, major image, sending Mimicry. Tulvak can mimic animal sounds and human voices. A creature that hears the sounds can tell they are imitations with a successful DC 17 Wisdom (Insight) check. Actions Multiattack. Tulvak makes two melee attacks. Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d8+5) slashing damage and the target must succeed at a DC 17 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned for 1 minute. The poisoned target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns ending the effect on itself on a success. Scythe. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, one target. Hit: 14 (2d8+5) slashing damage. Exhale Miasma (Recharge 5–6). Tulvak exhales a miasma of green vapors in a 15-foot cone. All creatures in the area must succeed on a DC 17 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned for 1 minute.
