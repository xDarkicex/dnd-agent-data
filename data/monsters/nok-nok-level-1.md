# Nok-Nok (Level 1)

**description**: Nok-Nok sees himself as a goblin legend in the making, although he admits that he hasn’t figured out how to get what he wants: to become the fifth of the goblin hero gods and be worshipped by a legion of fearful devotees. Traits Equipment. A crowbar, common clothes, 15 gp, shortsword, shortbow and quiver with 20 arrows, burglar’s pack, leather armor, two daggers, thieves’ tools Nimble Escape. Nok-Nok can take the Disengage or Hide action as a bonus action on each of his turns. Sneak Attack. Once per turn, Nok-Nok can deal an extra 3 (1d6) damage to one creature he hits with an attack if he has advantage on the attack roll; or another enemy of the target is within 5 feet of it, that enemy isn’t incapacitated, and Nok-Nok doesn’t have disadvantage on the attack roll. The attack must use a finesse or a ranged weapon. Actions Dagger. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d4+3) piercing damage. Shortsword.
