# Pavetta Stroon-Drelev

**description**: Baroness of Drelev and imperious socialite, Pavetta Stroon-Drelev concerns herself more with excess and comfort than battle. You can use these statistics for a pampered princess, queen, or other upper-class aristocrat, or—after making the appropriate adjustment to alignment and attitude—they can represent a beloved leader or ally. Her dog Jewel might be a different story, though... Traits Release Jewel. As a bonus action, Pavetta can give her dog Jewel a command, then release the little terror to harry anyone who threatens her. Actions Multiattack. If Pavetta is affected by Vengeful Rage, she makes 3 attacks with her dagger. Dagger. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 2 (1d4) piercing damage. Intimidating Scream (Recharge 6). Baroness Pavetta shrieks in rage, and all foes within 30 feet of her must make a DC 15 Wisdom save or become demoralized by her wail and gain disadvantage on all checks until the end of her next turn. The scream also automatically alerts any allies within 100 feet that can hear her to her need for assistance. Reactions Vengeful Rage. If her beloved dog Jewel is either killed or knocked unconscious, Pavetta flies into a murderous rage. She gains advantage on all attack rolls, deals an additional 11 (3d6) bludgeoning damage with weapons, and can use her Multiattack action. This rage persists for 1 minute, after which she gains two levels of exhaustion.
