# Consul Isabella Ezio

**description**: Isabella is a bold, middle-aged Noble woman with long brown hair usually tied up in a ponytail. She wears a modest long skirt and blouse and has served three terms as Consul of the Republic of Liberio. Isabella is a well-spoken woman and a powerful leader. She is a staunch supporter of the people and has fought hard for the city and its freedoms.
