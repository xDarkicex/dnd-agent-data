# Spellslave Sorcerer

**description**: Spellslave sorcerers are practitioners of magic who have willingly or unwillingly undergone vile transformations through which their bodies and minds have been infused with the essence of aboleths. This empowerment grants them access to powerful psychic abilities they can channel into their magic. These sorcerers typically serve an aboleth master, whom they are bound to obey. They cast spells at their master's orders, acting as pools of spells for their master's use.
