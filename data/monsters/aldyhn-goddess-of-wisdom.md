# Aldyhn, Goddess of Wisdom

**description**: Aldyhn Aldyhn joined the Vanir as a young woman, back when Mirgal led the band of adventurers. Over time her careful deliberation and ability to handle any situation led to the others trusting her more and more with making the most important of decisions. However, she always had a fondness for Mirgal and did not believe him capable of the betrayal he ultimately undertook. She never forgave herself for her blindness. Roleplaying Aldyhn Far Sighted. As she led the Vanir into Grimnir and led her peoples, she began to see only the whole of society, the larger picture, far into the future. This made her come across as distant, even aloof to those around her. In truth she had begun the process of becoming a goddess long before a divine spark found her. Vigilant. Even trapped as she is, she sends her ravens into the world, watchful for signs of the Yoten or, worse, the return of Mirgal. This vigilance borders on obsessiveness. Saga Actions Fate Foreseen. When reduced to zero hit points, Aldyhn performs one last sacrifice to the betterment of Grimnir. Every ally within 1 mile of Aldyhn is fully healed and any magic that would otherwise prevent such healing does not function unless the creature invoking the magic has a higher CR than Aldyhn. When Aldyhn dies, Thonir adds all of the abilities, bonus actions, and reactions that Aldyhn had to his own capabilities.
