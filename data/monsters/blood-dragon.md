# Blood Dragon

**description**: Blood dragons are among the most dangerous creatures known. Their origin is a mystery, yet their existence is proof that blood is a powerful part of the universe. They are extremely clever and love to get involved in the politics of mortals. Their greater aim is to show the might of blood to everyone and make everyone respect it. They generally excel in blood magic. They mostly have mortal servants that execute their plans in the mortal world. More powerful blood dragons even make pacts with warlocks, and some of them are worshiped as gods of blood. They can manipulate all kinds of mortals that live near their lairs in the course of their thousand-year plans. As most other dragons, blood dragons love to hoard treasure. However, since there is nothing more important than blood in their understanding of life, they collect blood and items related to blood. A vial of blood of someone belonging to a long-lost family, the blood of a sorcerer belonging to a famous bloodline, the blood of a powerful vampire or a rare creature; they could all be part of a blood dragon’s hoard. Blood dragons are very hard to kill. As long as there is a creature near them, they can escape death. Also, when too much blood is spilled in one place, like a battlefield, a new blood dragon may rise from the blood drained. Blood dragons do not reproduce like other dragons; they have no eggs. However, just like a vampire turning a mortal into a vampire, blood dragons can turn other dragons into blood dragons. As beings of blood, blood dragons do not breathe, and they do not require food, water, or sleep.
