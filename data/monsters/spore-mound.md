# Spore Mound

**description**: A spore mounds is a grotesque yet awe-inspiring amalgamation of fungal life and mycelial intelligence. Its almost humanoid body appears as a towering mass of various mushrooms and tendrils that stretch and contort with a life of its own, interconnected by a web of mycelium that binds the spore mound into a singular sentience. Bonding Spores A spore mound's primary mode of interaction with the world is through its spores, which are much more than simple, reproductive particles. Laden with microscopic fragments of its mycelium, these spores allow it to extend its neural network to other creatures near it. While it lacks vocal cords, creatures affected by its spores experience the world in part as it does – seeing vivid images and feeling intense emotions that convey the spore mound's thoughts and desires. When it is injured, it can transmit its sensation of pain, effectively sharing its suffering with all creatures connected to its network. While it can use its spores to debilitate and harm its foes, it can also cure ailments and heal wounds using its spores, even though many find the mound's method of doing so quite unsettling.
