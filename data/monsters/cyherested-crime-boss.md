# Cyherested Crime Boss

**description**: Living in Cyherested can be a rough and grueling experience. As a solution, some people decided to live off others. Cyherested Thugs harass people and local businesses to collect “protection tax”. With extortion and bullying, they have become quite the problem for the city. However, nobody seems to meddle in their affairs, since they are in league with the CBI sorcerers.
