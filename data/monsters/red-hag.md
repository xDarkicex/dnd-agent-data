# Red Hag

**description**: Red hags are witches that specifically live in the Tolia region. They vary in appearance but they have their greasy unkempt hair, accessories made of humanoid body parts, and crimson red cloaks in common. They have reddish skin and an upright posture, contrary to common belief. It is said that they were inhabitants of the region long before Kanrath and Cyherested were founded. Red hags are now widespread in the wilderness of Tolia; they live in the mountains, woods, or the tundra of the north. They are one of the primary reasons why the wilderness of Tolia is a dangerous place where even vampires and sorcerers hesitate to tread, and they are the main characters of children’s bedtime horror stories. They are deceitful and bloodthirsty fey, who desperately need blood in order to survive. Thus, they seek ways of deceiving mortals to lure them into their nests.
