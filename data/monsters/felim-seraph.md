# Felim Seraph

**description**: Felim Seraph Likes: acts of kindness, sunlight, choir singing Dislikes: unfairness, greed, being wet Felim seraphs are celestial custodians responsible for the safety of Felidise and all who reside within. As Elurus’ most trusted agents, they are sometimes tasked with aiding select mortals in benevolent quests away from Felidise. Though they generally strive to avoid violence, felim seraphs detest unfairness and won’t hesitate to get into dangerous situations to protect victims of injustice. Pact of the Chain Familiar. At the gamemaster’s discretion, a warlock with a pact of the chain may choose the form of a felim seraph for their familiar.
