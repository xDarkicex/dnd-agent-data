# Windchaser

**description**: Windchaser once served an elderly druid who, on his death bed, granted the horse sapience. You can use Windchaser’s statistics for any magically intelligent horse—adjusting his alignment, perhaps, to match that of his rider. You can even use his statistics as the baseline for an entire new species of supernatural steed in your campaign! Traits Trampling Charge. If Windchaser moves at least 20 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a hoof attack on the same turn, that target must succeed on a DC 16 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. If the target is prone, Windchaser can make another attack with its hoof against it as a bonus action. Actions Multiattack. Windchaser makes two attacks with its hooves. Hoof. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d8+6) bludgeoning damage.
