# Lizardfolk Witch Doctor

**description**: The witch doctors are the priests of the lizardfolk who serve as both tribe healers and spiritual advisors to its chieftains. They commonly worship the goddess Semuanya. Traits Hold Breath. The lizardfolk can hold its breath for 15 minutes. Actions Multiattack. The lizardfolk makes two melee attacks, each one with a different weapon. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: + 4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage. Heavy Club. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) bludgeoning damage. Cursed Bolt. Ranged Spell Attack: +4 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d8 + 2) psychic damage, and the target has disadvantage on attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws until the end of the lizardfolk’s next turn. Spellcasting. The lizardfolk casts one of the following spells using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 12, +4 to hit with spell attacks): At will: mending, resistance, thaumaturgy 2/day each: cure wounds, hold person 1/day each: banishment, spirit guardians, revivify
