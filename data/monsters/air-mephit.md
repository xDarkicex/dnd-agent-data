# Air Mephit

**description**: A curse toward the wind—perhaps for being too strong to raise a tent, or too light to dry laundry—an air mephit comes into being. Of all the mephits, air mephits are most likely to wander away from their summoner in pursuit of some distraction, only to return on a whimsical breeze.
