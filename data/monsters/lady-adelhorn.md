# Lady Adelhorn

**description**: The youngest daughter of the Adelhorn family, Lady Leda Adelhorn, lost her parents at an early age and left Mount Ontarr to pursue her passion as a singer. Often compared to a siren, Lady Adelhorn traveled the world blessing others with her angelic voice. Cut off as she was from the protective influence of both her deity and extended family, however, Lady Adelhorn slowly succumbed to arrogance and greed. When Lady Adelhorn’s debauchery resulted in the death of an innocent, the Father of Dragons was forced to intervene. He stripped Lady Adelhorn of her angelic powers and bound her in golden shackles in her family’s estate atop Mount Ontarr. TRAITS Angelic Weapons: Lady Adelhorn’s weapon attacks are magical. When Lady Adelhorn hits with any weapon, the weapon deals an extra 10 (3d6) radiant damage (included in the attack). Divine Awareness: Lady Adelhorn knows if she hears a lie. Innate Spellcasting: Lady Adelhorn’s spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 17). She can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: detect evil and good 1/day each: commune, programmed illusion, sending Magic Resistance: Lady Adelhorn has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. ATTACKS Multiattack: Lady Adelhorn makes three attacks with her +2 greatsword. +2 Greatsword: Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d6 + 8) slashing damage plus 10 (3d6) radiant damage.
