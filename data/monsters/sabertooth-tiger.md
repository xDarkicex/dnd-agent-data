# Sabertooth Tiger

**description**: The saber-toothed tiger, also known as a smilodon, is a powerful feline predator that savors the flesh of large creatures such as mastodons, mammoths, elephants, bison, buffalo, deer, and other enormous mammals. Smilodons are noted scavengers and have no problem devouring another animal’s kill. Animals that resist the smilodon usually become part of the meal as well.
