# Kundal

**description**: Kundal is a hunter with a secret: he’s a werewolf. Kundal makes for a great foe, especially if you take time in your game to establish him as a member of society before the truth comes out. You can also use his stats to represent the average member of a band of nomadic warriors who have become lycanthropes. Traits Reckless. At the start of his turn, Kundal can gain advantage on all melee weapon attack rolls during that turn, but attack rolls against him have advantage until the start of his next turn. Shapechanger. Kundal can use his action to polymorph into a wolf-humanoid hybrid or into a wolf, or back into his true form, which is humanoid. His statistics, other than his AC, are the same in each form. Any equipment he is wearing or carrying isn’t transformed. Kundal reverts to his true form if he dies. Keen Hearing and Smell. Kundal has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell. Actions Multiattack (Humanoid or Hybrid Form Only). Kundal makes three attacks: one with his bite and two with his claws or greataxe in hybrid form, or two with his greataxe in humanoid form. Bite (Wolf or Hybrid Form Only). Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8 +3) piercing damage. If the target is a humanoid, it must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw or be cursed with werewolf lycanthropy. Claws (Hybrid Form Only). Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (2d4 +3) slashing damage. Greataxe (Humanoid or Hybrid Form Only). Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d12 +3) slashing damage.
