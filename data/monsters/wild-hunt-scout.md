# Wild Hunt Scout

**description**: Wild Hunt The wild hunt is an awe-inspiring and enigmatic group of fey who stalk and pursue their prey between worlds. Those few who have caught glimpses of these elusive beings and lived to tell the tale speak of clouds of green mist filled with spectral hounds, archers who fire bolts of magic with inhuman accuracy, and the sound of a melodious horn echoing for miles across the landscape. Wild Hunt Scout These compact yet agile humanoids have pointed ears, glowing green eyes, and ram’s horns. Wild hunt scouts relish the chance to seek revenge upon those who have wronged or insulted the wild hunt, but they tend to grow bored when the stakes are not lethal. Despite this, they rarely let their bloodlust cloud their judgment when making tactical choices. Traits Crystal Attack. A wild hunt scout can manifest a green crystal in their free hand as a bonus action; this crystal vanishes as soon as it leaves their possession. The crystal is necessary for the wild hunt scout to use its Emerald Beam attack. Greensight. The wild hunt scout ignores leaves and other foliage when determining cover for a target. Innate Spellcasting. The wild hunt scout’s spell casting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 20, +12 to hit with spell attacks). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: pass without trace, speak with animals, speak with plants 3/day: misty step 1/day: dimension door, true seeing Instinctive Cooperation. The wild hunt scout has advantage on saving throws against effects caused by other members of the wild hunt. Planar Acclimation. A wild hunt scout always treats the plane it is currently on as its native plane. Surprise Attack. If the wild hunt scout surprises a creature and hits it with an attack during the first round of combat, the target takes an extra 10 (3d6) damage from the attack. Wild Gaze. Those who meet the gaze of a wild hunt scout come face-to-face with the hunt’s fearful power and find it difficult to act in the face of such a legendary legacy. When a creature ends its turn within 30 feet of the wild hunt scout, it must succeed on a DC 20 Wisdom saving throw or be slowed as if it were affected by the slow spell until the end of its next turn. This aura can be disabled or turned on as a bonus action. Wild Hunt Link. All members of the wild hunt within 500 feet of the wild hunt scout, including the scout itself, can’t be surprised. Actions Multiattack. The wild hunt scout makes three attacks with its crystal scimitar, or twice with its crystal scimitar and one with its emerald beam. Crystal Scimitar. Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 19 (3d8+7) slashing damage and 13 (3d8) force damage. Emerald Beam. Ranged Spell Attack: +13 to hit, range 40/100 ft., one target. Hit: 17 (5d6) force damage and the target must succeed on a DC 20 Wisdom saving throw or become blinded for 1 minute.
