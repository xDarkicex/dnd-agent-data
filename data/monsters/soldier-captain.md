# Soldier, Captain

**description**: Armies are organized into detachments of soldiers that need commanders. A captain must have the respect of his unit, even if this is only because he is far tougher than his followers. He must also have the combat skill and willingness to lead from the front. A wise captain also keeps his men alive by not committing them to battles without a solid chance of victory.
