# Hooded Lantern Ranger

**description**: Although the term “ranger” has often been used to describe lone hunters on the fringes of society, or great groups of warriors from the jungles and forests, the Hooded Lanterns are renowned for their mastery of urban environments, military strategy, and skilled marksmanship. The talents and accuracy of the Hooded Lanterns has allowed them to survive and maintain a foothold in the ruins of Drakkenheim, arguably the most dangerous environment in the entire continent. “I have learned that when traveling around the continent, just say you are on the side of whoever it is who is asking; it avoids a lot of complications”
