# Zenurim

**description**: A notorious succubus spy even amongst the demons of the Abyss, Zenurim served many masters through her unimaginably long existence. One of its most notable achievements was luring Lord Vantraqx Ironclaw the Fiend Slayer to a trap set by Marla-Thra, the Conjoined Suppressors. She now serves Lord Rhogar Zlatozar the Dauntless whose binding compels her to do his bidding.
