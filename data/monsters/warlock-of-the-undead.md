# Warlock of the Undead

**description**: Actions Quarterstaff. Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d6) bludgeoning damage, or 4 (1d8) bludgeoning damage if wielded with two hands, plus 9 (2d8) necrotic damage. Entropic Rift (Recharge 5–6). The warlock opens a momentary Abyssal rift within 60 feet of it. The rift is a 20-foot-radius sphere. Each creature in that area must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 13 (3d8) necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. In addition, there is a 50 percent chance that 1d4 shadows then appear in unoccupied spaces in the sphere. They act as the warlock’s allies, take their turns immediately after the warlock. They remain until destroyed or until the warlock dismisses them as an action. Spellcasting. The warlock casts one of the following spells using Charisma as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks): At will: eldritch blast (3 beams, 1d10 + 4), disguise self, infestation (3d6), levitate (self only), mage hand, poison spray (3d12), silent image 3/day (as 5th-level spells): animate dead, antilife shell, blight, blindness/deafness, cloudkill, greater invisibility 1/day each: circle of death, finger of death Bonus Actions Form of Dread (3/Day). The warlock transforms into its Form of Dread which lasts for 1 minute. Until the Form of Dread ends, the warlock gains the following benefits: It gains 20 temporary hit points. Once during each of its turns, when the warlock hits a creature with an attack, the warlock can force it to make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw, and if the saving throw fails, the target is frightened of the warlock until the end of the warlock's next turn. It is immune to the frightened condition and to necrotic damage.
