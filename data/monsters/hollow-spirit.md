# Hollow Spirit

**description**: The Purple Void of Braythe is a dangerous environment. Its dangers don't end with death: Those who die here dissolve into purple mist, and turn into a disembodied spirit. Very often, these spirits lose the memories of their former lives, but they retain their personality. Young Hollow Spirits vaguely resemble the person or creature they were in life. But the longer they exist in the Braythian Void, the more they turn into a spherical shape. A fully developed Hollow Spirit gains control over the purple clouds of the void and draws new powers from it. Hollow Spirits are stuck in the Purple Void, unable to enter the world-islands of Braythe as if there was an invisible border they can't cross. The world-chains are a deadly environment for these spirits: If they come into contact with the chains, they get sucked in and instantly die.
