# Yrsa

**description**: Yrsa the Wisest Yrsa is an older half-orc, who has dedicated her life to the völv. Though her hair is growing white, she still has the energy and passion of a much younger soul. Yet she is haunted. All völv have watched for Ragnarök, that final moment when the forces of good and evil must collide. Yet none expected it in their lifetime. However, to Yrsa, this burden of responsibility has fallen. She must ensure the raiders survive. Roleplaying Yrsa Dedicated. No matter what needs to be done, Yrsa will do it, even if that includes sacrificing the few to save the many.
