# Feeder

**description**: Feeder Feeders are massive maggot-like creatures spawned by Muspell when it arrives in a world. Anything they consume directly—or indirectly—helps replenish Muspell; and these monstrosities are voracious eaters, capable of consuming anything. Siege Monsters. In addition to their core duty to consume for Muspell, feeders are effective at destroying enemy fortifications. Variable Payload. Other Yoten can equip feeders in various ways, customizing their combat capabilities. Sleepers. When not given orders, feeders often fall into long slumbers—waking only when needed or they are threatened. Variant: Engine of Carnage Gargantuan-sized feeders are known to exist. These have 400 (24d20+144) hit points, an AC of 18 and are capable of being equipped with the War, Destruction, and Inferno payloads all at the same time. All damage inflicted by a gargantuan feeder is considered magical and their Slash attack does 14 (3d4 + 7) magical slashing damage, and their Chomp attack does 26 (3d12 + 7) magical piercing damage plus 13 (3d8) acid damage..
