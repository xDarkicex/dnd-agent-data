# Warlock of the Rat God

**description**: “Once we have all the shiny, the Rat God will come and help us! We’ll get food! We will eat! Lots of food! All the food! We will eat the whole world! All the earth! Yes yes yes!” Many ratlings worship a fiendish entity they call the Rat God. They construct crude effigies to the Rat God, who whispers to the most cunning and intelligent ratlings, granting them magic through eldritch pacts. Ratlings understand surprisingly little about the Rat God, and make up stories and fables about the Rat God’s exploits.
