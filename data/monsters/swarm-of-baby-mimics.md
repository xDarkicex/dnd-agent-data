# Swarm of Baby Mimics

**description**: Baby Mimic Mimica mutafigura are patient predators that use disguise to surprise unwary creatures. A mimic will then adhere themselves to their prey, making up for their slow speed and inability to pursue a quarry. They are popular choices for warding dungeons and tombs from treasure hunters; black dragons even breed mimics to populate their lairs as false hoards, stalagtites, and dragon eggs. Most mimics reproduce through a budding process, producing hundreds of children. Baby mimics disguise themselves as objects related to the parent’s, such as coins to a chest or fruits to a basket.
