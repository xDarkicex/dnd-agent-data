# Screaming Skulls

**description**: Sounding their anguish with mournful cries and pained screams, severed skulls possess only fragments of their former lives. Whether a necromancer collects skulls from a mass grave or a series of executions causes tortured and fragmented souls to coalesce their energies, the skull exist now only to suffer. Screaming skulls seem to only recall the final moments of their former lives. Their screams recount their horrific deaths in a constant cacophony.
