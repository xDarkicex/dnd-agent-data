# Dødelig Bandit

**description**: Born of the skeletal remains of long-interred halflings and animated by a dying lich’s final spell, dødelig are a race that was never meant to be. In spite of this, or perhaps because of it, they have a cheery and amicable disposition. Nomadic. Dødelig who have banded together to form a settlement do so where ever they can find safe harbor. Their tent villages are notable for their brightly adorned colors. Those dødelig who settle amongst other races likewise tend toward not staying in one place for too long. The exception, of course, being Dødeligtown near Embla, where the undead race has found a tentative home. Undead Nature. Dødelig do not breathe and don’t require sustenance or sleep.
