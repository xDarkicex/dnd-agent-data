# Sepse

**description**: The myths and legends of the Mediterranean are replete with strange and mystic serpents of every description. Sepse . This brownish snake gives off an acrid odor. Vapor rises from the ground where its fangs drip globules of acid on the sand. A sepse’s bite injects acid into the victim rather than venom, melting it from the inside. This snake lives into holes among the desert’s rocks.
