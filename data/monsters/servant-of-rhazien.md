# Servant of Rhazien

**description**: Traits Mutable Form. The servant of Rhazien is immune to any effects that would forcibly alter its shape, and it can squeeze through gaps of 1 foot in diameter or larger without reducing its speed. Actions Multiattack. The servant of Rhazien makes two melee attacks, one of which can be its Gnashing Bite. Gnashing Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d10+4) piercing damage. This attack has advantage against a creature grappled by the servant of Rhazien’s Sticky Slam. Sticky Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d8+4) bludgeoning damage, and the target must make a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be grappled (escape DC 15). The servant of Rhazien can have up to four creatures grappled in this way at one time, after which it must let go of one to use this attack again.
