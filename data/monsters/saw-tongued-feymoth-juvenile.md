# Saw-Tongued Feymoth Juvenile

**description**: Traits Brittle Wings. The feymoth’s Injury thresholds for its special mobility Aspect are 5 lower than normal. Actions Saw-Tongued Slash. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, one target, reach 5 feet. Hit: 10 (2d6+3) slashing damage, and the target must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or gain a special Injury of Dangerous II severity, “Bloodwell”. While Injured in this way, a creature regains half as many hit points whenever it would regain hit points. This Injury automatically recovers at the end of a short or long rest, or after the creature next receives magical healing. Siphoning Lick (recharge 6). The feymoth juvenile drinks blood from an adjacent creature with the Bloodwell or Deeper Bloodwell Injury. That creature takes 7 (2d6) damage, and until the end of the feymoth juvenile’s next turn, it gains a +2 bonus to AC, a +1 bonus to attack rolls, a +5 bonus to damage rolls, its movement speeds increase by 10 feet, and the DC for the saving throw against the Bloodwell Injury increases by 1.
