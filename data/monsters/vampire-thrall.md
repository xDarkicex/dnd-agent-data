# Vampire, Thrall

**description**: Servants and lackeys of vampires, vampire thralls receive blood from their masters' veins periodically, granting them a number of benefits so long as they continue to receive their masters' blood. Vampire thralls are not themselves undead, though many are hopeful they soon will be. Ingesting humanoid blood is in no way beneficial to a thrall, but this fact has not prevented many from trying.
