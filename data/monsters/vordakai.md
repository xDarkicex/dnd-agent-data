# Vordakai

**description**: Vordakai is one of the primary villains of the Kingmaker Adventure Path : a cyclops lich whose power has atrophied to a shadow of his former glory. As with Nyrissa, he’s such a memorable foe that transplanting him into other games runs the risk of breaking verisimilitude for any players who have previously played Kingmaker. That said, you can use his statistics to represent a new type of undead creature based on any Large magic-using humanoid, such as an undead ogre magi. Traits Legendary Resistance (3/Day). If Vordakai fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead. Spellcasting. Vordakai is a 9th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 18, +10 to hit with spell attacks). Vordakai has the following wizard spells prepared. Cantrips (at will): chill touch, mage hand, prestidigitation, ray of frost 1st level (4 slots): charm person, grease 2nd level (3 slots): blindness/deafness, blur, detect thoughts, suggestion 3rd level (3 slots): bestow curse, dispel magic, protection from energy, tongues, vampiric touch 4th level (3 slots): dimension door, phantasmal killer 5th level (1 slot): dominate person Turn Resistance. Vordakai has advantage on saving throws against any effect that turns undead. Actions Paralyzing Touch. Melee Spell Attack: +10 to hit, reach 10 ft., one creature. Hit: 10 (3d6) cold damage. The target must succeed on a DC 16 Constitution saving throw or be paralyzed for 1 minute. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a successful save. Legendary Actions Vordakai can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature’s turn. Vordakai regains spent legendary actions at the start of his turn. Cantrip. Vordakai casts a cantrip. Paralyzing Touch (Costs 2 Actions). Vordakai uses his paralyzing touch. Frightening Gaze (Costs 2 Actions). Vordakai fixes his gaze on one creature he can see within 10 feet of him. The target must succeed on a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw against this magic or become frightened for 1 minute. The frightened target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. If the target’s saving throw is successful or the effect ends for it, the target is immune to Vordakai’s gaze for the next 24 hours. Disrupt Life (Costs 3 Actions). Each living creature within 20 feet of Vordakai must make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw against this magic, taking 14 (4d6) necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
