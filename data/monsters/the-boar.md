# The Boar

**description**: As the characters know, Satan’s true body is trapped in the eternal ice of the Inferno. He can, however, project his mind into any beast on Earth and take any form that will serve his purpose. Taking the boar’s form is a joke based on the legend of the bridge. If defeated, the boar will disperse into black smoke. Only his enormous tusks and a tuft of black hair remain. Satan’s motivation to engage the adventurers is not only hurt pride, he also knows that they are carrying a powerful gift from Heaven, which he will want to destroy. Sure, he is still confident that Savonarola will prevail, but it does not hurt to help his victory along by destroying the artifact. He will instantly sense the celestial aura of the rose and attack its guardian. Should the characters have stolen a hair directly from his body, he will be furious and not only try to get it back, but also to destroy the group. Traits Plane of existence. Satan can be considered as native to most planes of existence, except maybe Paradiso. The spell banishment won’t work at all. Noxious Stench. Any creature that is within a 10-feet radius of Satan will have disadvantage on melee attacks (sorry rogues). Target. Satan will mainly target the bearer of the crystal rose, to destroy this gift from Heaven (but please let the carrier have a lot of rolls to evade attacks, like acrobatics or athletics, the rose shouldn’t be destroyed in this fight, and you can create interesting action sequences). Charge. If Satan moves at least 20 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a gore attack on the same turn, that target must succeed on a DC 10 Ac robatics roll or be knocked prone. Actions Gore. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (3d8 + 5) piercing damage.
