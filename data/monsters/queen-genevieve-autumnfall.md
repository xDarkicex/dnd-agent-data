# Queen Genevieve Autumnfall

**description**: The current Queen of Eladria, Genevieve Autumnfall, is an elven Mage with platinum hair, wide-set bright blue eyes, a long neck, and long, thin arms and legs. She wears faded brown robes interwoven with spun gold, and crown set with jewels to resemble red, yellow, and orange leaves upon her head.
