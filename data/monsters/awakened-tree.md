# Awakened Tree

**description**: An awakened tree is an ordinary tree given sentience and mobility by the awaken spell or similar magic. Traits False Appearance : While the tree remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from a normal tree. Actions Slam : Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (3d6 + 4) bludgeoning damage.
