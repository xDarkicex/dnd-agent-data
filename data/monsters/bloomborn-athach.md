# Bloomborn Athach

**description**: Athach The towering and brutish giants known as athaches are reviled for both their monstrosity and cruelty. In addition to having slovenly habits and poisonous fangs, this giant sports a gangly third arm that protrudes from its torso and ends in a twitching, long-fingered claw. These giants despise civilization almost as much as they despise their own kind, and they delight in acts of sadism and terror. They generally don’t keep long-lasting territories or lairs, and are fond of a more nomadic lifestyle—traveling from region to region and often leaving little more than ruins behind. Bloomborn Athach Athaches who have become infused with the raw power of twisted life (such as that which suffuses Nyrissa’s realm of Thousandbreaths) grow vastly in power, transforming into creatures known as Bloomborn athaches. While these athaches are devastating in combat, many powerful fey force these giants to serve them as gardeners, farmers, or laborers—oft believing that visitors who see these powerful creatures working on such minor tasks will come to understand and respect the power of their leader.
