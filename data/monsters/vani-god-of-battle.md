# Vani, God of Battle

**description**: Vani Though Vani the halfling is not the giant-like warrior that mortals believe him to be, he is an accomplished warrior and as loyal as all the tales tell. But fond of drink and frolicking and good-natured banter, Vani was also perhaps the heart of the Vanir. Roleplaying Vani Charming. Vani always has a kind word for those around him and it takes little for him to endear himself to others. Even his enemies can be taken in by his warm smile or the offer of playing just one more game of chance. Loyal. Vani does not break his word and in his heart the Vanir are his true family. Saga Actions Fate Foreseen. When reduced to zero hit points, Vani performs one last sacrifice to the betterment of Grimnir. Every ally within one mile of Vani is fully healed and any magic that would otherwise prevent such healing does not function unless the creature invoking the magic has a higher CR than Vani.
