# Hag Tree

**description**: Hag trees are vengeful treants that have succumbed completely to a hag’s evil manipulations and distrust of civilization. They resemble old twisted, barkless, and leafless trees oozing black tar. These trees have numerous yellow and red eyes on the trunk that constantly look in all directions for anything not invited by the hag that created it. Hag trees are sad and evil, often moving only when the hag that made them is threatened. These trees prefer to fling rocks over melee combat because they don’t want to be bothered by walking. Hag trees will fight to the death, subconsciously wanting a release from their twisted existence.
