# Swarm of Pebbles

**description**: Elemental Motes Masterful users of the Weave can use their magic to rip apart the barriers between worlds, summoning primordial avatars of air, earth, fire, and water from the chaotic depths of the Elemental Planes. When this fails miserably, the magic can produce an elemental mote instead, a tiny proto-elemental nearly indistinguishable from an inanimate manifestation of its element. Like their more powerful brethren, elemental motes have little desire but to return to their native plane and reunite with its natural energy. A Swarm Reshaped. As a swarm, elemental motes combine their individual specks of consciousness into a raging, primaeval mind. What they lack in physical ability, they make up for in combined desperation to abandon the Material Plane. Using their numbers to their advantage, they envelop any who dare stand in their way in a miniature elemental storm. Pebbles A pebble is weak on its own, but mighty in a swarm. A swarm of pebbles clatters as it tumbles and rolls like a miniature landslide, sweeping creatures off their feet and burying them in stone. They can’t glide through earth as easily as an earth elemental, but are equally effective ambushers because, well, they’re rocks.
