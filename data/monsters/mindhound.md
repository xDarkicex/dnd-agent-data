# Mindhound

**description**: The mindhound is an enigmatic creature that thrives on the fringes of reality. It possesses a lithe frame covered in tawny fur, marked with streaks of darker shades that seem to shift and change in the corner of one's eye. The mindhound's appearance is accentuated by its distinctive facial features; a pronounced snout that ends in a peculiar, sensitive tongue, capable of tasting the mental energies in the air. Mind Tracker Though it may not be the most intelligent beast, the mindhound is highly perceptive and has great mental acumen. It can communicate telepathically, through images and emotions, and can even detect the mental activity of other creatures nearby. It can use this ability to track other creatures over long distances, making it a favored companion for adventurers and bounty hunters alike. Exactly the friend you need if you have a wandering mind – get it? It's because, well... you get it, right? – the Wanderer
