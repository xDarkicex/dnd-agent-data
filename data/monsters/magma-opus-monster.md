# Magma Opus (monster)

**description**: A Magma Opus is a fiery elemental with a fluid form that is brought forth by the spell Magma Opus . It wouldn't be unheard of to find an Opus wandering the depths of some forgotten crypt or vault, a forgotten accident of a last cescendo. Use a +6 for the proficiency bonus and a +5 for the spellcasting modifier in this case.
