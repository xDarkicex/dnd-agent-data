# Tarkanan Assassin

**description**: Tarkanan assassins are the elite killers, spies, and thieves who work for House Tarkanan, a criminal organization specializing in theft and assassination. In addition to their deadly skill, a Tarkanan assassin possesses an aberrant dragonmark—a twisted sigil that provides them with magical power. House Tarkanan actively seeks and recruits people with aberrant dragonmarks. Tarkanan Assassin: Alternative Spells The power granted by an aberrant dragonmark is unpredictable.
