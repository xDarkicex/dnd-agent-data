# Mandragora Swarm

**description**: Mandragora Normally small, humanoid-looking masses of blood-drinking plant life, the influence of the First World can transform mandragoras into lumbering behemoths or organize them into ravenous swarms. An individual mandragora can easily be mistaken for a root, burl, or tuber that grew into a vaguely humanoid shape. Mandragora Swarm When mandragoras gather in vast numbers, they can bond their memories and thoughts through a shared network of rootlike filaments, allowing them to organize into a deadly and overwhelming swarm of blood-thirsty plant monsters. Traits Regeneration. The swarm regains 10 hit points at the start of its turn. If the swarm takes fire or thunder damage, this trait doesn’t function at the start of the swarm’s next turn. The swarm dies only if it starts its turn with 0 hit points and doesn’t regenerate. Swarm. The swarm can occupy another creature’s space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Tiny plant. The swarm can’t regain hit points other than by its own regeneration, nor can it gain temporary hit points. Vulnerable to Supernatural Darkness. In areas of magical darkness (such as those created by the darkness spell), the swarm is slowed, as the slow spell. Actions Multiattack. The swarm makes three attacks: one with its bites and two with its vine whips. Bites. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 0 ft., one creature in the swarm’s space. Hit: 22 (5d6+5) piercing damage plus 21 (6d6) poison damage. Vine Whips. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d6+5) slashing damage plus 7 (2d6) poison damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 18 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned. The creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success. Shriek (Recharge after Long Rest). The swarm can give voice to an unsettling shriek. All creatures within a 30-foot of the swarm must succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom save or become stunned for 1d4 rounds.
