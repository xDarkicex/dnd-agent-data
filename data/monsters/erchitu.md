# Erchitu

**description**: Erchitu This giant humanoid is covered in shaggy, white hair, with an ox-like head crowned by gleaming, metal horns. A massive yoke of black iron is fastened about its burly neck. An erchitu is formed from a humanoid that has committed heinous crimes in their lifetime. Fearful of the punishments that await them, they bargain with hellish powers, seeking to stave off their final judgement. Hell grants a reprieve, sending the humanoid back as a monstrous agent to wreak more havoc in the mortal world, knowing its soul will eventually make its way back to the hells in time. The erchitu’s new form is a massive ox wearing an iron yoke, symbolic of its hellish bargain. The horns of an erchitu are gleaming steel. Hell’s Servant. An erchitu is free to go about the world sowing misery as it sees fit, but whatever lord of Hell forged the bargain will want to direct the erchitu to specific acts of evil when it chooses. Often when an erchitu is encountered, it is in the company of one or more lesser devils, sent to deliver orders to the erchitu and see that they are carried out, occasionally assisting in the task if necessary. Reluctantly Penitent. On rare occasions, an erchitu is a soul that genuinely seeks redemption, but was too late. The humanoid takes the deal to become an erchitu as a last attempt at salvation. Such an erchitu will go to outrageous lengths, trying any ritual or taking any quest in order to find a way to cast off its hellish yoke and become its normal self again. Such repentant erchitu nearly always have devils nearby, attempting to sabotage its attempts at salvation and goad it into doing the bidding of its diabolical master.
