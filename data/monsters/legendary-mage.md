# Legendary Mage

**description**: Traits Legendary Resistance (2/Day). If the mage fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead. Spell Penetration. When the mage casts a spell and is interrupted by an enemy's counterspell, treat the spell's level as 3 levels higher. Superior Focus. The mage has advantage on saving throws it makes to maintain its concentration. Actions Dagger. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage. Spellcasting. The mage casts one of the following spells using Intelligence as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 16, +8 to hit with spell attacks): At will: fire bolt (2d10), light, mage hand, minor illusion, prestidigitation 2/day each: scorching ray, shield 1/day each: cone of cold, counterspell, fireball (as a 4th-level spell), lightning bolt, mage armor,* stoneskin,* thunderwave (as a 2nd-level spell) *The mage casts these spells on itself before combat. Legendary Actions The mage can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The mage regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn. Move. The mage moves up to half its speed without provoking opportunity attacks. Fire Bolt. The mage casts fire bolt . Dagger. The mage attacks with its Dagger. Evocation (Costs 2 Actions). The mage casts magic missile, scorching ray , or thunderwave , if available.
