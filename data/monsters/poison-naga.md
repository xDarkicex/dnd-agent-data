# Poison Naga

**description**: When an eon of spite and vengeance consumes a spirit naga, it becomes a paragon of its kind: the poison naga. No longer possessed of the patience required to develop new magics and subvert an enemy’s guard, poison nagas relish tormenting communities of otherwise peaceful humanoids, eliciting heroes to confront the naga in its lair, where it can toy with its prey. The worst of the torments are the lies; a poison naga will disguise itself and engage in charming, disarming lies simply for the sake of treachery. Sowing dissent before devouring its prey is the only true pleasure a poison naga experiences.
