# Atrociraptor

**description**: These savage lizards roam the wild in small groups, either seizing prey killed by other predators and stealing it from them or rushing in from all directions as a pack and ripping great hunks from a carcass. These traits can be developed by skillful trainers, as can a variety of related actions, making atrociraptor-riding cavalry a truly fearsome opponent. Adept at moving stealthily across most terrain, little escapes their keen senses when hunting.
