# Geryoneo

**description**: Geryoneo These hideous giants have three malformed upper bodies joined at the belly, each one well muscled and strong, with its own arms and head, all joined at the belly and supported by a single pair of thick legs below its massive trunk. The three heads of each geryoneo share a common mind, though each may have a somewhat different personality. A fully-growned geryoneo is about 13 feet tall and weighs over 5,000 pounds. Herdmaster. Geryoneos are pastoral creatures, loving nothing more than tending their flocks of sheep or oxen, husbanding them to prodigious size and fine fettle. They tend to keep to themselves but are taciturn and territorial, quick to anger against those that intrude on their solitude. They often train great hounds to defend their herds and flocks, and they have a particular affinity for orthrus chimeras (see page xx) and death dogs, sometimes infusing ordinary canines with their own tainted blood to cause them to sprout multiple heads like their masters. Oddling Offspring . Geryoneos can interbreed with other giants, though their progeny are often malformed, with additional heads (though rarely the fully separated tripartite torsos of a true geryoneo). These half-breed geryoneos have statistics identical to ettins, though they often have 3 heads rather than 2 and usually lack the vicious temperament and chaotic evil alignment of a typical ettin.
