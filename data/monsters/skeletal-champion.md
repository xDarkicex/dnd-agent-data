# Skeletal Champion

**description**: Skeleton Undead skeletons are a well-known but relatively minor threat to those who would explore ancient tombs, but in the Stolen Lands, more powerful variants of these monsters lie in wait. Some of them even retain shreds of their personalities in life, and are capable of much more complicated tasks than the typical necromantic monster most assume they face when a frame of clattering bones rises up to oppose them. Skeletal Champion These skeletons retain the cunning they possessed in life and fight with great skill. Actions Multiattack. The skeletal champion makes two melee attacks. Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8+2) slashing damage or 7 (1d10+2) slashing damage if used with two hands.
