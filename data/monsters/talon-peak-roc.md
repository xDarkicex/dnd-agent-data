# Talon Peak Roc

**description**: Rocs are legendary beasts, immense birds well known for their ability to carry off sizable prey. However, the roc said to dwell atop Talon Peak in the eastern mountains of the Stolen Lands is reputed to be even larger than normal—and more voracious when threatened by intruders. Actions Multiattack. The roc makes three attacks: one with its beak and two with its talons. Beak. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 23 (3d8+8) piercing damage. Talon. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 26 (3d10+8) slashing damage. If the target is a Huge or smaller creature, it must succeed on a DC 17 Strength saving throw or be grappled (escape DC 17). Until this grapple ends, the creature is restrained. The roc can grapple up to two creatures at a time. Wing Strike. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d6+8) bludgeoning damage and the target must succeed on a DC 17 Strength saving throw or be pushed back 10 feet. Flying Strafe. The roc flies up to 60 feet and makes two talon attacks, each against a separate creature along its path. The roc doesn’t provoke attacks of opportunity when it flies out of an enemy’s reach. Reactions Wing Rebuff. When a creature moves within 15 feet of the roc, the roc can make a wing strike attack against it.
