# Copyblob Artisan

**description**: Copyblob Artisan Likes: crafting and talking about its craft, being recognized by other artisans, rock candy Dislikes: tight spaces, being called a monster, running out of raw materials Staying in one shape for too long can be boring, so copyblobs explore many different shapes and activities throughout their lives. When a copyblob finds a craft it is passionate about, it strives to learn all it can about it and becomes a copyblob artisan, a true master of its craft. Copyblob artisans can take the shape of and function as the artisan’s tools they are experts in, allowing them to practice their craft anytime and anywhere. Their social nature makes most copyblob artisans eager to meet and work with other experts in their field, and they love nothing more than talking about their craft and collaborating with other artisans on wonderful creations. Pact of the Chain Familiar . At the gamemaster’s discretion, a warlock with a pact of the chain may choose the form of a copyblob artisan for their familiar.
