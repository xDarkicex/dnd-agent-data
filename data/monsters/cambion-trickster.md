# Cambion Trickster

**description**: Some cambions prefer the more subtle tactics of manipulation to toy with and torment their enemies. These are the cambions who take the path of the arcane trickster. Traits Evasion. If the cambion is subjected to an effect that allows it to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, the cambion instead takes no damage if it succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if it fails. Fiendish Blessing. The AC of the cambion includes its Charisma bonus. Sneak Attack (1/Turn). The cambion deals an extra 24 (7d6) damage when it hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of the cambion that isn't incapacitated and the cambion doesn't have disadvantage on the attack roll. Actions Multiattack. The cambion makes two melee attacks or uses its Befuddling Bolt twice. Rapier. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8 + 4) piercing damage plus 3 (1d6) fire damage. Befuddling Bolt. Ranged Spell Attack: +5 to hit, range 60 ft., one creature. Hit: 10 (3d6 + 3) psychic damage and the target has disadvantage to Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws and can't use reactions until the end of the cambion's next turn. Fiendish Charm. One humanoid the cambion can see within 30 feet of it must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be magically charmed for 1 day. The charmed target obeys the cambion's spoken commands. If the target suffers any harm from the cambion or another creature or receives a suicidal command from the cambion, the target can repeat the saving throw, ending the effect on itself on a success. If a target's saving throw is successful, or if the effect ends for it, the creature is immune to the cambion's Fiendish Charm for the next 24 hours. Spellcasting. The cambion casts one of the following spells, requiring no material components and using Charisma as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks): At will: mage hand, minor illusion, mind sliver (2d6), poison spray (2d12) 3/day each: alter self, command, detect magic 1/day each: fear, fireball, hold person, invisibility, mirror image, plane shift (self only), silent image, shadow blade, Tasha's hideous laughter Bonus Actions Distracting Hand . While the cambion has the mage hand spell active, it designates a creature within 5 feet of the spectral hand. The cambion has advantage on its attack rolls against that creature until the end of the turn.
