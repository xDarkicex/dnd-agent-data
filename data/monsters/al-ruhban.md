# Al-Ruhban

**description**: Al-Ruhban The al-ruhban are the offspring of a djinni and a humanoid. An al-ruhban typically appears as the same race as their humanoid parent, though they tend to be taller than average and often have some physical characteristic that hints at their extraplanar bloodline, such as an odd hair or eye color. The commanding aura of their sky-blue eyes and noble stance hint at something else. Some al-ruhban remain in the courts of their djinni kin, though most djinn view them as low in station due to what they see as a diluted bloodline. Many more seek a living among their humanoid kin. Most keep a low profile, hiding their lineage and passing themselves off as nothing more than another member of whatever race they are descended from. Some few flaunt their differences, considering themselves exceptional, and thus due admiration, respect, and whatever riches they can acquire. This wealth can be consequential, as the al-ruhban can live for centuries, with an average lifespan of 500 years. Secretive or Selective . The main reason for the secrecy of the al-ruhban is that certain ambitious wizards and other powerful folk believe they can use the genie-kin for their own ends. Some seek to harvest the powers of the elements from them through experimentation. Others wish to use them as bargaining chips, holding the al-ruhban hostage to leverage their djinn parent into acquiescing to the kidnapper’s demands. Those that flaunt their lineage seek to leverage their power, putting themselves into a position of fame and/or power that leaves them untouchable by those that would seek to use the al-rhuban in their machinations and power-grabs.
