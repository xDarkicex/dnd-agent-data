**Skills** Arcana +6, History +6, Perception +4<br>
**Gear** Wand<br>
**Senses** Passive Perception 14<br>
**Languages** Common plus three other languages<br>
**CR** 6 (XP 2,300; PB +3)

#### Actions

<hr>

**_Multiattack._** The mage makes three Arcane Burst attacks.

**_Arcane Burst._** _Melee or Ranged Attack Roll:_ +6, reach 5 ft. or range 120 ft. _Hit:_ 16 (3d8 + 3) Force damage.

**_Spellcasting._** The mage casts one of the following spells, using Intelligence as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 14): <br>
&emsp;**At Will:** _Detect Magic_, _Light_, _Mage Armor_ (included in AC), _Mage Hand_, _Prestidigitation_ <br>
&emsp;**2/Day Each:** _Fireball_ (level 4 version), _Invisibility_ <br>
&emsp;**1/Day Each:** _Cone of Cold_, _Fly_

#### Bonus Actions

<hr>

**_Misty Step (3/Day)._** The mage casts _Misty Step_, using the same spellcasting ability as Spellcasting.

#### Reactions

<hr>

**_Protective Magic (3/Day)._** The mage casts _Counterspell_ or _Shield_ in response to the spell's trigger, using the same spellcasting ability as Spellcasting.
