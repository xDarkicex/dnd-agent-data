# Bleeding Specter

**description**: Tolia is no stranger to supernatural forces, especially those associated with the underworld. Whatever the reason for this may be, it is certain that the dead are patiently waiting for an opportunity to take form on these lands. Murders are common in Tolia, both in the cities and in the wilderness. When a murder takes place and leaves a blood-curdling scene behind, there is a chance that a bleeding specter might be born out of it. If it does, it starts to wander slowly and patiently, trying to find an opportunity to recreate its own murder. The bleeding specter is invisible. Its presence can be discerned from traces of blood and bloody footprints without any visible source. Some spellcasters claim that they have seen the specter through the use of magic. They stated that it looked like a humanoid figure, mostly transparent, bleeding out of all orifices, and staring intently ahead with bright, gloomy eyes. The bleeding specter prefers to move through its prey to bind them in a bloodbond; then it harms itself, thereby harming its victim. Yet, the bleeding specter doesn’t care for sloppy murders and will not chase every creature it sees to kill them. The specter will try to create horrific murder scenes, painted with blood and decorated with flesh.
