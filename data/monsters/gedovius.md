# Gedovius

**description**: Gedovius plays the role of torturer and jailer in the dungeons of the king’s palace in Pitax. He can also be used as a relentless killer who hides in plain sight amid the structures of a beleaguered city, or even as the mysterious leader of a particularly sadistic thieves’ guild. To provide a fun change of pace, Gedovius can work well in an adventure where all signs point to a human criminal preying upon a society ill-equipped to deal with them, only to have the PCs discover that, in this case, the predator that has been stalking the city streets is a gargoyle or elemental. Traits False Appearance. While Gedovius remains motionless, he is indistinguishable from an inanimate statue. Magic Weapons. Gedovius’ weapon attacks are magical. Sadistic Brute. A melee weapon deals one extra die of its damage plus 1d6 psychic damage when Gedovius hits with it (included in the attack). Sneak Attack (1/Turn). Gedovius deals an extra 17 (5d6) damage when he hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of Gedovius that isn’t incapacitated and Gedovius doesn’t have disadvantage on the attack roll. Actions Multiattack. Gedovius makes four attacks: one with his bite, one with gore, and two with his claws. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit:1211 (2d6+5) piercing damage plus 3 (1d6) psychic damage. Gore. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d8+5) piercing damage plus 3 (1d6) psychic damage. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d8+5) slashing damage plus 3 (1d6) psychic damage.
