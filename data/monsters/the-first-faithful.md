# The First Faithful

**description**: The First Faithful is the leader of the sinister Kingdom of the Cleansed, but these stats will also serve quite well for a lone priest of an obscure demon lord, perhaps even for someone filling a role like a notorious killer or other lone menace threatening society from its fringes. Remove his Release the Inmost Worms ability if you use these stats outside of his role in the Kingmaker Companion Guide . Traits Spellcaster. The First Faithful is a 14th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 16, +8 to hit with spell attacks). He has the following cleric spells prepared: Cantrips (at will): guidance, light, resistance, thaumaturgy 1st level (4 slots): command, detect magic, inflict wounds, shield of faith 2nd level (3 slots): blindness/deafness, hold person, spiritual weapon 3rd level (3 slots): bestow curse, dispel magic, spirit guardians 4th level (3 slots): freedom of movement, guardian of faith 5th level (2 slots): flame strike, insect plague 6th level (1 slot): harm, heal 7th level (1 slot): firestorm, regenerate Touch of Corruption. The First Faithful’s melee attacks are considered magical and deal an additional 14 (4d6) necrotic damage (included in attack). Actions Multiattack. The first faithful makes three attacks with his staff. Staff. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6+3) bludgeoning damage plus 14 (4d6) necrotic damage. Release the Inmost Worms (Recharge 5–6). The First Faithful spends an action to expel a bloody worm from the scar on his forehead, targeting a creature within 30 feet that it can see. The creature must succeed at a DC 16 Constitution saving throw or the worm burrows into the creature’s head, dealing 42 (12d6) psychic damage and incapacitating the target until the beginning of the First Faithful’s next turn. On a successful save, the worm deals half damage and falls to the ground, vanishing, and the target is not incapacitated.
