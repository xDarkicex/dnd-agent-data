# Explorer

**description**: Forester Be they workers seeking to harvest the forest’s resources or explorers taking pains to map the wilds, foresters harbor a wide range of skills, motives, and agendas. While most foresters have little interest in the workings of urban life, scoff at “city folk,” and mock travelers who find themselves needing aid when they inevitably get in over their heads while traveling through the woods, the majority of these creatures aren’t evil. While foresters might not have patience for those who haven’t chosen the woods as their home, they’ll usually offer aid to the lost or the needy they encounter in the wilds—if only to ensure that the intruders can make their way back out of the woodlands and return to wherever they came from as soon as possible. Explorer The gnomes who accompany Jubilost on his journey into the Stolen Lands are not accomplished explorers, and they get in over their heads quickly. That doesn’t diminish the passion they have for their jobs, however. Traits Gnome Cunning. The gnome has advantage on all Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws against magic.
