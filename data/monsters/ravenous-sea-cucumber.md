# Ravenous Sea Cucumber

**description**: The gargantuan ravenous sea cucumber is an eerie aquatic animal that lies in wait at the ocean's depths. With its colossal, gelatinous body stretching over 50 feet, it possesses retractable branched tentacles that serve as its primary feeding appendages. This terrifying creature ensnares its prey with its sinuous tentacles, swiftly immobilising and drawing them into its grasp, ensuring a swift and horrifying demise.
