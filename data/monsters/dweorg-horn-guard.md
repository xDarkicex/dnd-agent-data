# Dweorg Horn Guard

**description**: More commonly called by their nickname, the Parched Throats, these guards who patrol the entertainment district keep an eye out for good humor gone too far, and drunken revelers too lost in their mugs. They use a leaded beanbag known as a sapsop to quiet public partygoers who’ve gotten out of hand.
