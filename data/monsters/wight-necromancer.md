# Wight Necromancer

**description**: Command over life and death is no easy feat. Those spellcasters who undertake to master necromancy often do so with the intent of overcoming the fate of all things. The process to becoming a lich is both difficult and profoundly arcane, thus many an impatient necromancer seeks out knowledge of becoming a wight. Though far less powerful than even a demilich or other sentient undead such as a vampire, the wight is free-willed, does not require sustenance, and given time they could wield magic to rival any.
