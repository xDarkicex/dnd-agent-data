# Akidan

**description**: The black-scaled akidan serve primarily as elite soldiers and heavy hitters to smash through tougher opponents with their large weapons. On death, they burst into acid, causing severe damage to enemies around them, and rendering them vulnerable to follow up attacks. Created by corrupting humanoids through the ritualistic injection of dragon's blood, these creatures resemble dragonborn and kobolds to a certain extent. However, they are distinct in that every dragonblooded creature has wings, and a distinctly unique response to death. Some turn to stone, others explode in a variety of nasty elements, and some even transform into their killer, threatening their killer's sanity. Gliders. Although dragonblooded have wings, the majority of them are incapable of true flight and are only able to glide. Except for the Voludan, a dragonblooded’s fly speed can’t be used to gain altitude, and they drop 5 feet per round that they remain aloft. Tactics. The tactics used by the dragonblooded vary by their type, but can be generally summarized as single-minded in service to their creator. Each dragonblooded seeks to make the best use of their death as possible, and may even hasten their own demise when escape seems impossible if it means harming a greater number of foes in the process. Traits Acid Bomb. When the akidan dies, it bursts into highly corrosive acid, melting everything around it. Each creature in a 10-foot radius must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw or take 14 (4d6) acid damage and gain a special injury of Handicap III severity, "Acid Burns". While injured in this way, a creature is blinded and has disadvantage on Constitution saving throws. This Injury recovers automatically after washing the creature’s eyes with water for at least 1 minute. If this acid damage kills a creature, it melts into a puddle of goo, leaving no corpse. Powerful Strikes. When the Akidan attacks with its greatsword, it can choose to take a -2 penalty to its attack roll to deal an additional 5 damage on a hit. Actions Multiattack. The Akidan makes two melee attacks, one of which can be with its bite. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6+3) piercing damage, plus 3 (1d6) poison damage, and the target must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns ending the poisoned condition on a success. Greatsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 feet, one target. Hit: 10 (2d6+3) slashing damage.
