# Bluecap

**description**: Sometimes weary adventurers and even experienced miners find themselves in a deserted part of the Dark Below. Some lose all hope and wander aimlessly in the dark, and others trust their Experience with underground locations. Still, some who are lucky enough to encounter a bluecap can hopefully survive and find a way in the endless caverns. These generally elusive fey help miners find hidden ore deposits, and in exchange for an honest wage, they do what they can to assist.
