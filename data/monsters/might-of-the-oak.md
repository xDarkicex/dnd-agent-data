While in this form, you are a Large plant, and you have the following statistics: AC 15 Hit Points 10d10 + 50 Speed 30 ft. Strength 22 (+6); Dexterity 9 (–1); Constitution 20 (+5) Damage Resistances bludgeoning, piercing Damage Vulnerabilities fire False Appearance .While you remain motionless, you are indistinguishable from a tree.
