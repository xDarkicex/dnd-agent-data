# The Lonely Warrior

**description**: The Lonely Warrior is a solo encounter in the Kingmaker Adventure Path , but you can use these stats as the basis for an entire category of variant wights to populate a larger, more complex tomb. Traits Sunlight Sensitivity. While in sunlight, the Lonely Warrior has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom (Perception) check that rely on sight. Actions Multiattack. The Lonely Warrior makes two greatsword attacks. Greatsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (2d6+3) slashing damage and 7 (2d6) necrotic damage. The target must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or its hit point maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the necrotic damage taken. This reduction lasts until the target finishes a long rest. The target dies if this effect reduces its hit point maximum to 0. A humanoid slain by this attack rises 24 hours later as a zombie under the Lonely Warrior’s control, unless the humanoid is restored to life or its body is destroyed. The Lonely Warrior can have no more than 12 zombies under its control at one time.
