# Wood Ward

**description**: The edges of the village are thick with strange figures staring outward, scarecrows of sack and stick. Each has been carefully fashioned to appear angry and wakeful. Actions Multiattack. The wood ward makes two slam attacks or uses its Horror Gaze. Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: (1d4) bludgeoning damage. Horror Gaze (1/day). All creatures within a 30-foot cone of hte wood ward that it can see make a DC10 Charisma saving throw. On a failed save, the target is frightened of the wood ward for 1 minute. The target may repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success. If the saving throw succeeds, the target is immune to this wood ward’s gaze for 24 hours.
