# Tomten

**description**: Tomte The tomte are small fey that live among mortals (though they always keep themselves hidden). During the evenings they wake and tend to the chores their mortal companions have not completed. They are generally kind, if treated well—homesteaders know to leave out thimbles of mead or baked goods for them. Willing to Leave. If mistreated or abused, they will pack up, knock over a few chairs, and depart for a more appreciative family. They generally avoid combat but have been said to give the occasional thrashing to unruly or impolite house guests.
