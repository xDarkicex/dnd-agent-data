# Vegepygmy Sprout

**description**: Sprouts are newborn vegepygmys. Within each sprout is a conundrum of continuity: they possess no thoughts or memories prior to their birth, yet they are drawn to the artifacts of the corpse from which they were born. From harvested tooth, bone, claw, and jewelry a vegepygmy creates a personality that mimics that of its former self—or so it thinks—using that to inform their choice on how best to serve the tribe and the cycle.
