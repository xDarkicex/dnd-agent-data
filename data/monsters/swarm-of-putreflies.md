# Swarm of Putreflies

**description**: A single putrefly is an annoyance that can ruin an evening. A swarm of putreflies can ruin a village. They resemble common fireflies but for a long barb protruding from the abdomen. Ravenous. Putrefly swarms are common in marshlands or fungal caverns teeming with life; in most other habitats a swarm eventually consumes itself after exhausting its food supply. Innocuous. When a putrefly swarm makes its way into lands where fireflies are otherwise common, even skilled woodsman don’t recognize the deadly swarm for what it is.
