# Unicorn Foal

**description**: Unicorns are wise and powerful celestial guardians, but they aren’t born that way. The forces of good encourage unicorn foals to travel with worthy adventurers to gain experience. While a foal isn’t as mobile or dangerous as its elders, their gifts of telepathy and ability to heal make them excellent companions for the pure of heart. Traits Magic Resistance . The foal has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. Actions Hooves . Melee Weapon Attack : +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit : 4 (1d4 + 2) bludgeoning damage. Healing Touch (1/Day) . The foal touches another creature with its horn. The target magically regains 11 (2d8 + 2) hit points. In addition, the touch removes all diseases and neutralizes all poisons afflicting the target.
