# Elder Thing

**description**: Elder things are strange beings with star-shaped heads and numerous writhing appendages arranged radially around its barrel-shaped body. Known by various names on various worlds, often translating to "elder things" or "old ones," these alien creatures are interested in creating lasting works of art, architecture, and even life (such as the dreaded shoggoths). They possess a boundless capacity for war and egotism. Devices.
