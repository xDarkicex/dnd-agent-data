# Skeletal Hulk

**description**: Skeleton Undead skeletons are a well-known but relatively minor threat to those who would explore ancient tombs, but in the Stolen Lands, more powerful variants of these monsters lie in wait. Some of them even retain shreds of their personalities in life, and are capable of much more complicated tasks than the typical necromantic monster most assume they face when a frame of clattering bones rises up to oppose them. Skeletal Hulk These oversized skeletons are animated from the bones of giants. They originate traditionally from cloud giants, but you can use them to represent the animated bodies of any Huge humanoid as well, such as an oversized cyclops. Actions Multiattack. The skeletal hulk makes two melee attacks. Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6+4) slashing damage. Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d10+4) bludgeoning damage.
