# Soul Eater

**description**: Only the most desperate or foolhardy conjurers call upon the aid of these strange fiends from the inky swamps of Abaddon. They appear as inky clouds of darkness from which reach two all-too-solid taloned arms. Traits Caster Link. A conjured soul eater forms a mental link with its conjurer. While both are on the same plane, the soul eater knows the location of its conjurer. If the soul eater’s target dies before the soul eater can drain its soul, or if the soul eater is defeated (but not killed), the soul eater returns to its conjurer and tries to kill them. Find Target. When summoned, the soul eater is given the name of its prey. It knows the direction and distance of the creature, even if the creature and the soul eater are on different planes of existence. Actions Numbing Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d6+5) slashing damage plus 10 (3d6) necrotic damage and the creature must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw. On a failure, the creature is stunned until the end of its next turn. Drain Soul. If the soul eater is adjacent to a dying creature, the soul eater can attempt to devour the dying creature’s soul. The target must attempt a DC 17 Wisdom saving throw, dying on a failed save. Only a wish spell can restore the creature back to life, unless the soul eater is slain within 100 feet of the creature’s corpse within 1 minute of death. If this occurs, the creature’s soul returns to its body and it is dying with one failed death saving throw.
