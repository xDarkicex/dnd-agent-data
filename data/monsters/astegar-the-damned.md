# Astegar the Damned

**description**: Appearance and notes: Astegar’s skin has become desiccated and clings tautly over the bones of his face and body. His eyes have turned a milky white, and his voice rasps with every word. As he speaks, flecks of skin fall off from his cheeks and emerge from his mummified mouth in small clouds of dust. Prior to his death, Astegar was a powerful legate who commanded the temple at the town of Redwinde. He has still not completely accepted his death. Instead, he is confused by what has happened, and he remains uncertain how to proceed in unlife. He does, however, know enough about the fell process to recognize that he must consume the flesh of the living. As such, this remains his priority, so that he can keep his memories and skills intact for as long as possible. In order to make this easier, Astegar has set up various strategies among the fell townsfolk to try to draw outsiders into the village to feed his needs. If the PCs have an opportunity to interact with Astegar, he is direct about his needs. He may offer to barter with them, promising freedom for some of them in exchange for others. However, this is merely a ploy. He fully intends on consuming as many of the party as he can. Traits Fell Curse. If Astegar spends at least one action consuming flesh from a recently living creature with an Intelligence score of 6 or greater, he regains 4d8 hit points. For every week he goes without doing this, he must make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw. If he fails, he devolves into a Fell Ungral. Legendary Resistance (3/Day). If Astegar fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead. This cannot be used to automatically succeed Wisdom saving throws as a result of the Fell Curse, but it may be used to succeed on Constitution saving throws as a result of Undead Fortitude. Undead Fortitude. If damage reduces the Astegar to 0 hit points, he must make a Constitution saving throw with a DC of 5 + the damage taken, unless the damage is radiant or from a critical hit. On a success, Astegar drops to 1 hit point instead. Actions Multiattack. Astegar makes two attacks, one with his staff and one with his teeth. Staff. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 2) bludgeoning damage. Teeth. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5ft, one target. Hit: 7 (2d4 + 2) piercing damage. Spellcasting. Astegar casts one of the following spells using Wisdom as his spellcasting ability (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks): At will: resistance, thaumaturgy, toll the dead 2/day each: bane, inflict wounds, hold person, dispel magic, blight 1/day: harm Reactions Fell Obedience. When a creature Astegar can see targets him with an attack, Astegar chooses another Fell within 5 feet of him. The two undead swap places, and the chosen Fell becomes the target instead.
