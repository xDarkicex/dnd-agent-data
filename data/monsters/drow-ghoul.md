# Drow Ghoul

**description**: All drow males face a singular problem: they are male. Born as second class citizens, drow males have few options if they are to strive for their culture’s ideals of gathering power and influence and wielding it ruthlessly. Most males who aspire to more than mere servitude enter the military, training with sword, bow and poison. A few pursue the arcane arts, there to wield power outside the established hierarchy. But too often the innate drow lust for status leads a male to attempt to move beyond their station. When such attempts fail, there is but one option if the drow would avoid becoming a slave or sacrifice: to become a ghoul. A drow ghoul is a dishonored drow, given one last chance for redemption. These former dark elves retain their innate lust for power, but as undead they are thrall to any priestess. With nothing left to lose, they undertake impossible missions in an attempt to regain what small measure of freedom they once had.
