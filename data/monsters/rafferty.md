# Rafferty

**description**: TRAITS Special Equipment: Rafferty carries a wand of binding and wears a ring of protection and a pendant set with a stone of Celestia . His fine velvet smoking jacket is worth 800 gp. Limited Magic Immunity: Rafferty can’t be affected or detected by spells of 6th level or lower unless he wishes to be. He has advantage on saving throws against all other spells and magical effects. Innate Spellcasting: Rafferty’s innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 18, +10 to hit with spell attacks). He can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: detect thoughts, disguise self, mage hand, minor illusion 3/day each: charm person, detect magic, invisibility, major image, misty step, suggestion 1/day each: dominate person, fly, plane shift, true seeing ACTIONS Multiattack: Rafferty makes two claw attacks. Claw: Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d6 + 2) slashing damage, and the target is cursed if it is a creature. The magical curse takes effect whenever the target takes a short or long rest, filling the target’s thoughts with horrible images and dreams. The cursed target gains no benefit from finishing a short or long rest. The curse lasts until it is lifted by a remove curse spell or similar magic. LEGENDARY ACTIONS Rafferty can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature’s turn. Rafferty regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn. Cantrip: Rafferty casts mage hand or minor illusion. Claw: The rakshasa makes a claw attack. Distract: Rafferty locks his penetrating gaze on a creature he can see within 20 feet of him. If that creature can see Rafferty, it must make a successful DC 18 Wisdom saving throw or become stunned until the end of its next turn. Move (Costs 2 Actions): Rafferty moves up to half his speed without provoking opportunity attacks. LAIR ACTIONS While in his lair, Rafferty can invoke the ambient magic to take lair actions. On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), Rafferty can take a lair action to cause one of the following effects: Play Pretend: Rafferty casts suggestion , if he is able to do so. Rest for A While: A creature Rafferty can see within 30 feet of him must make a successful DC 18 Wisdom saving throw or fall unconscious for 1 hour. The creature awakens if it takes damage or if another creature uses an action to rouse it. Vicious Mockery: If Rafferty is under the effects of a disguise self spell, he casts vicious mockery .
