# Wild Hunt Hound

**description**: Wild Hunt The wild hunt is an awe-inspiring and enigmatic group of fey who stalk and pursue their prey between worlds. Those few who have caught glimpses of these elusive beings and lived to tell the tale speak of clouds of green mist filled with spectral hounds, archers who fire bolts of magic with inhuman accuracy, and the sound of a melodious horn echoing for miles across the landscape. Wild Hunt Hound Wild hunt hounds are intelligent hunters and expert trackers that take the lead against elusive prey. Left to their own devices, they seek out creatures that are notoriously difficult to track. They are always surrounded with green mist composed of fragments of the spirits of the wild hunt’s victims, a mystic fog that forms itself into a spectral pack to fight at its side. Traits Greensight. The wild hunt hound ignores leaves and other foliage when determining cover for a target. Innate Spellcasting. The wild hunt hound’s spell casting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 17). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: speak with animals, speak with plants 3/day each: dispel magic 1/day each: locate creature Instinctive Cooperation. The wild hunt hound has advantage on saving throws against effects caused by other members of the wild hunt. Planar Acclimation. A wild hunt hound always treats the plane it is currently on as its native plane. Wild Gaze. Those who meet the gaze of a wild hunt hound come face-to-face with the hunt’s fearful power and find it difficult to act in the face of such a legendary legacy. When a creature ends its turn within 30 feet of the wild hunt hound, it must succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw or be slowed as if it were affected by the slow spell until the end of its next turn. This aura can be disabled or turned on as a bonus action. Wild Hunt Link. All members of the wild hunt within 500 feet of the wild hunt hound, including the hound itself, are treated as if they have see invisibility cast on them. Actions Multiattack. The wild hunt hound can use Focus Gaze. It then makes three bite attacks. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d8+7) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed at a DC 18 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. Focus Gaze. The wild hunt hound fixes its glare at a creature it can see within 30 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw or be affected by the wild hunt hound’s Wild Gaze. If the target is already slowed by the Wild Gaze, it instead becomes paralyzed until the end of its next turn. Summon Pack. With a haunting howl, the wild hunt hound summons a fog that manifests into several misty hounds. These foggy hounds shift and race and howl in a 20-foot radius around the wild hunt hound as long as it maintains the effect as though it were concentrating on a spell.
