# Miah

**description**: Miah’s history, he likes to say, is a familiar one. He was an adventurer until an angry wizard cursed him with a flesh to stone spell. His companions tried everything they could to remove the curse, but nothing worked until they happened upon a ring of three wishes in a treasure hoard. After wishing themselves to riches and better fortunes, they used the last wish to free Miah, stating "We wish for a way to turn our friend Miah from stone to flesh." The ring changed into a magical amulet that grants the benefit of a stone to flesh spell, but only while worn by Miah. He has long since left the adventuring lifestyle, and now spends his days hammering out treaties in the dwarven city of Embla. He serves as steward to Maylorin Obsidianaxe, meeting to discuss ways to improve the city’s economy and initiate trade routes with other Underworld species. The dwarves have had great success negotiating peace and gold in trade for their goods and services; deals with the mercane, the krodlyns and even the drow were struck up and peace maintained for many years. That is, up until recently. Despite hailing from a low-ranking family, Miah has established himself as a stalwart supporter and emissary of the dwarven city of Embla. Respected by many yet not as well-known as some of the merchant house leaders, business owners, or politicians within the city, one could say that Miah is part of the pulse of Embla, the one who would be first in line to give his life for the city and the people he loves. Miah is a dweorg arbalist when the heroes first meet him.
