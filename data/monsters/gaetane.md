# Gaetane

**description**: Gaetane is an agent of the nation of Pitax—a wererat ranger who prefers to spend most of his time in hybrid form. He also works as a specialized bounty hunter; you can adjust his favored enemy list to more closely match a party’s makeup if you want to use these stats as a hunter hired specifically to track the PCs down. Traits Archery. Gaetane gains a +2 bonus to attack rolls he makes with ranged weapons (bonus included below). Keen Smell. Gaetane has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Shapechanger. Gaetane can use his action to polymorph into a rat-humanoid hybrid or into a giant rat, or back into his true form, which is humanoid. His statistics, other than his AC, are the same in each form. Any equipment he is wearing or carrying isn’t transformed. Gaetane reverts to its true form if he dies. Spellcasting. Gaetane is a 10th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 14, +6 to hit with spell attacks). Gaetane has the following ranger spells known: 1st level (4 slots): cure wounds, jump, longstrider 2nd level (3 slots): animal messenger, lesser restoration 3rd level (3 slots): protection from energy Actions Multiattack (Humanoid or Hybrid Form Only). Gaetane makes two melee attacks with his bite, or three ranged attacks with his longbow. Bite (Rat or Hybrid Form Only). Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8+4) piercing damage. If the target is a humanoid, it must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be cursed with wererat lycanthropy. Longbow (Humanoid or Hybrid Form Only). Ranged Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8+4) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 17 (5d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much poison damage on a successful one.
