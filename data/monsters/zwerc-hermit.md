# Zwerc Hermit

**description**: Shunned by other dwarves, the zwerc are seen as aloof intellectuals, detached from the dwarven traditions of ancestry and crafting. Long ago the zwerc sought isolation in the Underworld, steeping themselves in magic. Amber Road. The zwerc are the original travelers of the mysterious veins of orange rock known as the Amber Road. Not only is the Amber Road a potentially faster and safer route through the Underworld—to those who know its secrets—but a method of traveling the planes themselves. The zwerc still use the Amber Road, despite its degrading structure, in their unending quest for new knowledge.
