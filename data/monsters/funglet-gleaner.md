# Funglet Gleaner

**description**: Gleaners are spellcasters that draw their magical energy direct from the fungi around them, converting infinitesimally small amounts of natural life force from every single plant they are in part responsible for and converting it into the force required to employ druidic spells. Gleaners and garlands work closely together when their community comes under attack, particularly if they need to allow gardeners and grangers to retreat. A garland protected by its shield wall is a force to be reckoned with when a nearby gleaner enhances the shield with spike growth.
