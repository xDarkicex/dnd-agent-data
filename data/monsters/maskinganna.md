# Maskinganna

**description**: Similar to fauns or satyrs, but much more feral in appearance, the maskinganna is a forest-dwelling fey. A maskinganna’s humanoid form has shaggy, goat-like legs ending in hooves. Its torso is also hairy, topped with a head that appears half-human, half goat, yellow eyes with rectangular pupils, and a pair of horns beneath a shaggy mop of hair. They consider themselves defenders of their woodland home and use their magical abilities to bedevil, mislead, and frighten any who would harm or despoil their sylvan homeland. Tricksters. Maskinganna are mischievous pranksters, using their ability to change shape and deceptive nature to play tricks on others for their own amusement. They are not overtly cruel, however, and typically stop short of causing serious harm to another creature unless that creature has proven itself an enemy of the maskinganna or a threat to the forest in which the maskinanna dwells. Viewers of Dreams. Maskinganna are unable to dream themselves, and are fascinated with the dreams of other creatures. They will take every opportunity to view the dreams of a sleeping creature, reveling in the sights and experiences of the dreamer.
