# Cager

**description**: Lurking in the darkness of dead ends, remote train stations, sewage tunnels, and the wilderness of Tolia, the cager is a dangerous creature with an endless bloodlust. They are silent creatures that prefer to live alone. Despite their size, they are very stealthy creatures, and they can easily escape and hide from the guards of the cities. Despite the ongoing decades-long research about their true nature, no one knows the exact reason or source of their existence. Many have suggested that they are undead who have come back from the dead through the sheer power of their formidable nature. Once torturers, serial killers, and criminals, they now continue their crimes as cagers. A cager is a highly wild and dangerous hunter who enjoys feeding on warm organs and spending time in the gory scene of their crimes. A cager’s favorite method of killing is using its sharpened and elongated ribs to “cage” and squeeze its victim to death, as it crushes the victims’ internal organs, which explode all over their cage.
