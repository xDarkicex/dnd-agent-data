# Ameon Trask

**description**: Ameon Trask is one of Baron Drelev’s right-hand thugs. He is an unpleasant “pig of a man” who—despite being a poor leader—has been put in charge of one of the first military operations made against the PCs’ kingdom. Ameon Trask can also play the role of a bounty hunter or even a specialist in a thieves’ guild in other campaigns, as the balance between frontline combatant and stealthy stalker allows him to fit a wide range of villainous roles. Traits Brave. Ameon has advantage on saving throws against being frightened. Special Equipment. Ameon has two elemental gems (red corundum) and a potion of greater healing . Actions Multiattack. Ameon makes three shortsword attacks (two main hand and one offhand). Shortsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d6+4) piercing damage and the target must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw, taking 7 (2d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Shortbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d6 +4) piercing damage.
