# Tolian Siren

**description**: Tolia is home to many intriguing yet deadly creatures. One of them is the Tolian siren, also known as the “bloody siren” by the common folk. These sirens lure sailors and sea workers into their trap using their beautiful, mind-controlling songs, only to devour them by drinking their blood and eating their flesh while their victims are still alive. Tolian sirens are half-bird and half-humanoid creatures that have a fish’s tail below the waist. Contrary to common belief, Tolian sirens are not beautiful creatures who have a female human’s body. There are sirens of every sex and some of them have the torsos of different creatures than that of a human. Tolian sirens are inhabitants of the shores of Tolia, mostly residing in the [Isles of Llanberg] and near Kanrath where they can lure people in their traps.
