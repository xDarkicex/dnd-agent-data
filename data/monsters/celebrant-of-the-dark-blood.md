# Celebrant of the Dark Blood

**description**: Celebrants of the Dark Blood are sahuagin chosen for their savage wisdom from the ranks of the tribe and elevated to the position of tribal leader. These chosen few are initiated into the faith of the Darkness Within, as their forebears have been for millennia. The tribe of Rakhix Gharthok Kol’jah has also been coopted by Faeryl Myrryn, who has granted warlock powers to these clerics as their patron. The celebrants have received the vampire queen’s blood gift and have been turned into vampire spawns by the tribe’s leader, Mor'jh Koah. They now tend to the head priest, the Temple of Bleeding Darkness, and the creation of dark blood sahuagin armies.
