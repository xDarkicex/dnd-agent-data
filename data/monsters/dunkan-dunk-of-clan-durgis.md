# Dunkan Dunk of Clan Durgis

**description**: Appearance and Personality: Dunk is pale and a bit pudgy, but hardy of both body and spirit. Light-­hearted for a dwarf, others find him coarse-­mannered and abrupt even for his kind. He is emotional and easily distracted, but loyal to his friends and a good ally in a fight. Dunk considers any of the PCs from Durgis Rock to be his students, regardless of their background. He is a warrior from the town, and he sees it as his duty to make certain that the next generation is ready to come to its defense at any time. Because of this, he constantly nags and reminds everyone about routine duties. This can include properly disposing of a corpse, knowing how to prepare shelter for the night, or just basic hunting tips for common game animals. He always makes it a point to speak of tradition and the honor of the clan when he makes these pronouncements, as a way to make sure that everyone recognizes the importance of what he is saying. Traits Dwarven Resilience. Dunkan has advantage on saving throws against poison. Battle Fury (1/Day). Dunkan can push himself into a momentary flurry of battle rage. On his turn Dunkan can take one extra action, but it must be used to take the multiattack action. Bonus Actions Second Wind (1/Day). Dunkan can use a bonus action to regain 1d10 + 5 hit points. Actions Multiattack. Dunkan makes two attacks with his urutuks. Urutuk. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft, or range 20/60ft, one target. Hit: 7 (1d6 + 3) slashing damage.
