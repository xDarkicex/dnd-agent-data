# Angry Mob

**description**: Angry mobs are notoriously hard to control once they form and can easily turn against an agitator, but often an angry mob forms after a long period of growing anger or in reaction to something that truly violates cultural norms. An angry mob behaves as a single press of bodies bereft of normal morality and behavior associated with otherwise rational humanoids. An angry mob could tear a pregnant creature to pieces if it got worked up enough or burn down a village, even if some in the mob end up burning their own houses. Many monsters and monster slayers have met their end at the hands of an angry mob. Angry mobs usually have lit torches or other means to light things on fire and any structure adjacent to an angry mob that is not currently attacking something can begin to take fire damage up to the discretion of the DM.
