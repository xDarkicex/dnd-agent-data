# Ghost of A’rvanshy

**description**: The ghost of A'rvanshy, a renowned deep-sea elven archmage and hierophant cleric devoted to the Wondermaker, the god of Magic, lingers as a spectral guardian of his people's legacy. Once transformed into a lich by the collective sacrifice of his community, he became an immortal leader, guiding his kind for ages to come. A'rvanshy valiantly battled the Dark Star and triumphantly founded the hidden city bearing his name, a testament to his power and wisdom. However, the city of A'rvanshy's peace was disturbed when the malevolent Morgawr, the kraken necromancer, overtook the city and dealt a fatal blow to the lich archmage. Now, A'rvanshy's spectral form serves as a haunting reminder of the once-glorious leader and his tragic fate, forever bound to his tomb to protect his memory and seek retribution against those who threaten his people's heritage.
