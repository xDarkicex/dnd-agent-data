# Grandmaster Delilah Willowart

**description**: This wizened old Archmage of the Amethyst Academy resides at Starspire Observatory. She has held her position for nearly two centuries, having used magic to extend her natural lifespan. Delilah diligently manages the observatory's students and faculty, but is intensely devoted to the collection and study of astrology, the cosmos, the planes, and theories about time and space. Perhaps the greatest living expert on astronomy, she is a studied academic who has a tendency to talk for hours on end about any topic she is excited about.
