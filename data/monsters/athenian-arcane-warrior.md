# Athenian Arcane Warrior

**description**: The arcane warrior is the prime example of a front line fighter representative of Athens. Trained as both warriors and spellcasters, these soldiers have more options on the battlefield than your average soldier. However, they are usually focused on preparing magic that will supplement their martial prowess. Despite being superior to the average soldier in combat, these fighters are rather commonplace within Athens’ very focused military. For every five mundane soldiers, an arcane warrior is present. Athenian Army Athens is the biggest magocracy in the entirety of the Greek world, represented by a plethora of powerful spellcasters. It is rare for an experienced warrior in the city to not know at least one spell and there are orders of mages specialized in battle magic. However, the city honors the patron goddess of the city, Athena, the Goddess of Wisdom. She is specially appreciated by warriors and scholars, which makes the amount of faithful within the city quite extensive. As such, the ranks of the army are not just powerful, but also versatile. Both martial prowess and varying degrees of arcane and divine expertise are the building blocks for one of the three superpowers in the Greek world. Naval Supremacy. Aside from their hold on magic and its institutionalized learning, the Athenian state holds the most powerful navy in the continent, with a fleet of 500 ships, which requires 100.000 sailors to operate. Humanoids Humanoids are the most plentiful creatures amongst the creations of the gods. They create vast kingdoms, they make unbelievable technological advances and even achieve breakthroughs in the art of magic. Traits Magic Weapons. The warrior's weapon attacks are magical. Spellcasting. The warrior's Spellcasting Ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with spell attacks). They can cast the following spells: At Will: ray of frost 3/day each: expeditious retreat, shield 2/day each: blur, magic missile, silvery barbs, sleep 1/day each: cloud of daggers, invisibility Actions Multiattack. The warrior makes two weapon attacks. They can replace one weapon attack with ray of frost. Longsword (One-Handed). Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) slashing damage. Longsword (Two-Handed). Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d10 + 3) slashing damage. Shield Bash. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage. If the target is large sized or smaller, it must make a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone or pushed back 5 ft. (warrior's choice) Ray of Frost (Cantrip). Ranged Spell Attack: +5 to hit, reach 60ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d8) cold damage. On a hit, the target's speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of the warrior's next turn. Bonus Actions Swift Casting (2/day). The warrior can cast a spell with a casting time of 1 action.
