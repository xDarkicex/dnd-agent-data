# Bloom of Lamashtu

**description**: A thick, green stalk with spiraling rows of thorns stands at the center of this enormous flowering plant. Each flower has a growing seed pod at its center, surrounded by vibrant petals in a wide variety of colors. Crafted after consecutive seasons of experimentation, prayer, and ritual, the bloom of Lamashtu is the triumph of the cultist Darivan and his Cult of the Bloom. The fiendish plant aids the cult’s effort to infect the humanoid population of the Stolen Lands and replace them with monstrous predators. Cultivated to spread misery and violence, the bloom is quietly malevolent, growing its cursed seeds as rapidly as it can. It stands 12 feet tall and weighs 600 pounds. While only one bloom of Lamashtu exists in the Kingmaker Adventure Path , you can use these statistics for an entire invasive species of abyssal foliage that might spring up in a forgotten corner of your campaign setting. In such a case, the spread of the bloom curse is not so much the result of a cult, but a simple side effect of this alien incursion of inimical plant life into the world. Trait Bloom Curse. A living creature is exposed to the bloom curse if it eats any portion of a bloom of Lamashtu, fails a save against the bloom’s seed pod, or is critically hit by the bloom’s thorny vine. The creature can resist the bloom curse with a successful DC 15 Constitution saving throw, otherwise it becomes poisoned for as long as the curse persists. Every 24 hours, the cursed victim must attempt a new DC 15 Constitution saving throw; on a failure, its hit point maximum decreases by 14 (4d6). If the curse reduces the target’s hit point maximum to 0, the target dies and its body bursts open in a blast of strange plant life that then releases a monster into the world. This creature is a chaotic evil low-intelligence monster chosen by the GM; the creature should be approximately the same challenge level as the original victim was in life. The curse itself lasts until removed by the remove curse spell or other magic. Actions Multiattack. The bloom of Lamashtu makes 2 thorny vine attacks. Thorny Vine. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, range 60/100 ft., one creature. Hit: 12 (2d6+5) piercing damage plus 4 (1d6) acid. Spew Seed Pod (Recharge 5–6). The bloom of Lamashtu’s central stalk disgorges a pulsing seed pod from its flower, spitting it at a range of up to 60 feet. The seed pod explodes into a spray of supernatural acidic poison, and all creatures within 20 feet must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 21 (6d6) acid damage and 21 (6d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
