# Wild Hunt Archer

**description**: Wild Hunt The wild hunt is an awe-inspiring and enigmatic group of fey who stalk and pursue their prey between worlds. Those few who have caught glimpses of these elusive beings and lived to tell the tale speak of clouds of green mist filled with spectral hounds, archers who fire bolts of magic with inhuman accuracy, and the sound of a melodious horn echoing for miles across the landscape. Wild Hunt Archer These lithe, androgynous humanoids have pointed ears, glowing green eyes, and six fingers on each slender hand. They are methodical hunters who fire their bows with uncanny accuracy, and often rely on wild hunt horses to provide increased mobility. Rather than carrying normal arrows, they can produce arrows as needed from magical quivers—these quivers do not function in this manner for anyone other than wild hunt archers. Traits Greensight. The wild hunt archer ignores leaves and other foliage when determining cover for a target. Innate Spellcasting. The wild hunt archer’s spell casting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 19, +11 to hit with spell attacks). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: speak with animals, speak with plants 3/day: black tentacles, haste, sleet storm 1/day: cloudlkill, disintegrate Instinctive Cooperation. The wild hunt archer has advantage on saving throws against effects caused by other members of the wild hunt. Planar Acclimation. The wild hunt archer always treats the plane it is currently on as its native plane. Wild Gaze. Those who meet the gaze of a wild hunt archer come face-to-face with the hunt’s fearful power and find it difficult to act in the face of such a legendary legacy. When a creature ends its turn within 30 feet of the wild hunt archer, it must succeed on a DC 19 Wisdom saving throw or be slowed as if it were affected by the slow spell until the end of its next turn. This aura can be disabled or turned on as a bonus action. Wild Hunt Link. All members of the wild hunt within 500 feet of the wild hunt archer, including the archer itself, ignore half cover and three-quarters cover with their ranged weapon attacks. Actions Multiattack. The wild hunt archer makes two attacks with its antler gore or three attacks with its living bow, or one antler gore attack and two attacks with its longbow. Antler Gore. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (2d12+5) piercing damage. Living Bow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d8+7) piercing damage and 10 (3d6) cold damage. The wild hunt archer spontaneously generates arrows as needed from its quiver when using its living bow. If the bow is taken from the archer or lost, the living bow rots away in 1 hour. A wild hunt archer can manifest a replacement living bow by concentrating for one minute.
