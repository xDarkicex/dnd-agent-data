# Nilak

**description**: Nilak serves the Six Bears as something of a nontraditional leader, but she’s exactly what her followers need to recover from their recent troubles. As a druid, her stats work well for any druid, of course, particularly one who focuses more on combat than passive forms of nature worship. Rather than presenting her as an ally to the PCs, you can also adjust her alignment and flavor text to transform her into a vengeful force of nature who seeks to destroy those who dare encroach upon her domain. Traits Rapid Change Shape (2/Short or Long Rest). As a bonus action, Nilak magically polymorphs into a humanoid or beast that has a CR of 5 or less, or back into her true form. She reverts to her true form if she dies. Any equipment she is wearing or carrying is absorbed or borne by the new form (Nilak’s choice). While transformed, all of Nilak’s attacks are magical. In a new form, Nilak retains her game statistics, but her AC, movement modes, Strength, Dexterity, and other actions are replaced by those of the new form, and she gains any statistics and capabilities (except class features, legendary actions, and lair actions) that the new form has but that she lacks. If the new form has a bite attack, she can use its bite in that form. Spellcasting. Nilak is a 9th-level spellcaster. Her casting stat is Wisdom (save DC 16). She has the following druid spells prepared: Cantrips: guidance, produce flame, shillelagh 1st-level (4 slots): cure wounds, thunderwave 2nd-level (3 slots): darkvision , flaming sphere 3rd-level (3 slots): call lightning, dispel magic, sleet storm 4th-level (3 slots): ice storm, polymorph, stoneskin 5th-level (1 slot): wall of stone Actions Multiattack. Nilak makes two attacks. Club. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6+3) bludgeoning damage. Shortbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6+3) piercing damage.
