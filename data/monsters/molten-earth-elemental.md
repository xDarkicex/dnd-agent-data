# Molten Earth Elemental

**description**: Volcanic Elementals Portals to the Elemental Plane of Fire occasionally open within the larger islands of the Expanse. When these portals remain stable for long periods, such as on Mount Spitfire, at the heart of Calderé Island, volcanic activity often follows. Various elementals make their home on the other side of these portals and are frequently spewed forth from them. Molten Earth Elemental Combining the earth's strength and fire's blazing power, molten earth elementals pulverize their enemies with burning fists.
