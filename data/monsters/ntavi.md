# Ntavi

**description**: Ntavi’s role is to play the part of the bad influence, but with a change to her alignment and personality, she can just as easily play an opposite role as an intimidating but helpful source of advice. Traits Brave. Ntavi has advantage on saving throws against being frightened. Great Weapon Fighting. When Ntavi rolls a 1 or 2 on a damage die for an attack she makes with a melee weapon that she is wielding with two hands, she can reroll the die and must use the new roll. Actions Multiattack. Ntavi makes three attacks. Maul. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d6+5) bludgeoning damage and the target must succeed at a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8+4) piercing damage.
