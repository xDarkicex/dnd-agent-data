# Enormous Dragonfly

**description**: The dragonflies of Hooktongue Slough grow twice as large as the typical giant dragonfly. Brightly colored, these enormous dragonflies are often regarded with awe by local boggards, who often marvel at these dragonflies’ beauty while plotting to devour them at the same time. Traits Flyby. The dragonfly doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks when it flies out of an enemy’s reach. Skimmersense. The dragonfly can sense other creatures touching the same water, functioning as tremorsense with a range of 30 feet. Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (2d12+5) piercing damage and the target is grappled (escape DC 15). Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained, and the dragonfly can’t grapple another target. The dragonfly can continue to bite by transferring the grappled target to its limbs.
