# Deathdreamer

**description**: Deathdreamer This is a spellcaster that has devoted their life to unraveling the secrets behind Grimnir’s creation. This pursuit often renders deathdreamers insane. Waterwalkers Creatures slain by the deathdreamer’s Primal Breath can be awakened by the deathdreamer via a cryptic ritual. When this happens, they animate as waterwalkers.
