# Amethyst Academy Wizard

**description**: The wizards of the Amethyst Academy perform varied arcane tasks throughout the nations. The purple-robed arcanists study ancient ruins, maintain their magical strongholds, and accompany nobles, kings, and dukes on errands or participate as members of their council. The Academy's mages are known well throughout the continent and are always identifiable.
