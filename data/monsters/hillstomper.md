# Hillstomper

**description**: Hillstomper once served an elderly druid who granted the mammoth sapience. You can use Hillstomper’s statistics for any magically intelligent mammoth—adjusting his alignment, perhaps, to match that of his rider, or even using the stats as the baseline for an entire new species of supernatural steed in your campaign. Traits Trampling Charge. If Hillstomper moves at least 20 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a gore attack on the same turn, that target must succeed on a DC 19 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. If the target is prone, Hillstomper can make a stomp attack against it as a bonus action. Actions Multiattack. Hillstomper makes two attacks: one with its gore and one with its stomp. Gore. Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 22 (3d8+9) piercing damage. Stomp. Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 25 (3d10+9) bludgeoning damage.
