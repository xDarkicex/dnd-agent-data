# Storm Elemental

**description**: A storm elemental is a chaotically shifting mass of dark clouds, only illuminated by a core of blue, white or purple lightning that lets out loud thunderclaps whenever it flies at high speed. It is said that these elementals are born every time a powerful storm evoked by the Lord of the Sky takes place. They are spread out through high places where lightning strikes often strike the earth, or in the vast expanse of the sky above the ocean, where maritime storms manifest. However, they tend to be inactive unless rain or the clap of thunder are present. Temperamental Wanderer. Though not inherently antagonistic, the storm elemental is easily riled up. It might see something as harmless as an attempt at communication without the auran language as a threat and prepare to fight. Elementals These beings are intrinsically tied with the domains of various gods, even said to be divine servants at times. They are the object of study of many wizards and theologists for this reason. About the Greek Elementals These elementals very much resemble those present in the Monster Manual and work on similar mechanical structures. Elemental Nature: An elemental doesn’t require air, food, drink, or sleep. Subject to Conjuration: An spellcaster can summon these elementals through the conjure elementals spell, by using an appropriate spell slot to the Challenge Rating of each elemental. Traits Cloud Form. The elemental can enter a hostile creature's space and stop there. It can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing. Lightning Aura. Each creature that starts its turn within 10 feet of the elemental or moves there for the first time on its turn takes 5 (1d10) lightning damage. Actions Multiattack. The elemental makes two storm slam attacks. Storm Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d8 + 5) bludgeoning damage plus 10 (3d6) lightning damage. The target can't take reactions until the start of its next turn. Storm Bolt. Ranged Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 100/200ft., one target. Hit: 19 (4d6 + 5) lightning damage. Thunderous Nova (Recharge 5-6). The elemental creates a burst of ear-splitting thunder and lightning. Each creature within 30 feet of the elemental must make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw. On a failure, a target takes 7 (2d6) lightning damage plus 14 (4d6) thunder damage and is deafened. On a success, a target takes half the damage but isn't deafened. The deafness lasts until it is lifted by the lesser restoration spell or similar magic.
