# Woolly Rhinoceros

**description**: Traits Trampling Charge. If the woolly rhinoceros moves at least 20 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a Gore attack on the same turn, that target must succeed on a DC 16 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. If the target is prone, the woolly rhinoceros can make one Stomp attack against it as a bonus action. Actions Gore. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 19 (3d8 + 6) piercing damage. Stomp. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one prone creature. Hit: 15 (2d8 + 6) bludgeoning damage.
