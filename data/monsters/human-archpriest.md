# Human Archpriest

**description**: Though few priests of any faith use the title formally, an archpriest is the leader of district or congregation centered around one or several churches or shrines. The archpriest leads the faithful, both the clergy and members of the community, in practicing the tenets of their faith. In times of trouble, many humans seek out an archpriest for their wisdom and guidance. For this reason, many archpriests sit in counsel to monarchs.
