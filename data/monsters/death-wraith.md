# Death Wraith

**description**: A death wraith is the terrifying result of a soul being trapped on the plane where its physical body perished. Capable of commanding shadows and siphoning the life out of living creatures, these powerful Undead retain few, if any, memories of their mortal life – appearing and acting as resentful, spectral slivers of their former selves. Cursed Creatures Unlike other wraiths who are typically spirits of creatures that were evil in life, the fate of becoming a death wraith can befall even goodly creatures as their souls are trapped and twisted through dark magic. In most cases, slaying the death wraith allows the creature’s soul to pass on to the afterlife and find peace. When delving into an archpriest’s crypt, it’s my experience that there’s a 50/50 chance you’ll find a death wraith down there. They’re not too bad, really – just make sure you bring a bunch of healing potions and a “sunny” disposition! – the Heretic
