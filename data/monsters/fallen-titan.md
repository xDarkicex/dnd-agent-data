# Fallen Titan

**description**: Unlike the mortal races, a celestial is not truly free to choose their own path. By their very nature they are bound to certain ideals, and though they are free to act as they choose in all pursuits, when a celestial falls victim to mortal desires, they become known as fallen titans. These titans have not truly betrayed their celestial cause, merely abandoned the strict, unwritten laws of their immortal kind in favor of mortal whim. Fallen titans often have a skewed perspective—that borders on obsession—toward mortal affairs. They may recite poetry that is doggerel, practice medicine that borders on torture, inspire folly instead of bravery, or take far too serious the mannerisms of a noble court. They are oblivious of their exaggerated behaviors, thinking themselves exemplars of mortal behavior.
