# Treant Keeper of the Grove

**description**: The keeper of the grove is an advanced treant that has druidic abilities. It lives its existence as the natural guardian of its woodland domain. Traits False Appearance. If the treant is motionless at the start of combat, it has advantage on its initiative roll. Moreover, if a creature hasn’t observed the treant move or act, that creature must succeed on a DC 18 Intelligence (Investigation) check to discern that the treant is animate. Siege Monster. The treant deals double damage to objects and structures. Actions Multiattack. The treant makes two Slam attacks. Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (3d6 + 6) bludgeoning damage. Rock. Ranged Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, range 60/180 ft., one target. Hit: 28 (4d10 + 6) bludgeoning damage. Spellcasting. The treant casts one of the following spells using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 16, +8 to hit with spell attacks): At will: druidcraft, poison spray (3d12), thorn whip (3d6) 2/day each: conjure animals, cure wounds, moonbeam 1/day each: call woodland beings, hallucinatory terrain, wrath of nature Entangling Plants (Recharge 5–6). The treant targets one or more creatures it can see within 60 feet of it. Grasping roots, vines, and vegetation sprout underneath each target. Each target must make a DC 16 Strength saving throw or be restrained by the entangling plants for 1 minute. As an action, the restrained creature can make a DC 16 Strength check, escaping from the briars on a success. The briars can also be destroyed to end the effect. The entangling plants has AC 10, 15 hit points, vulnerability to fire damage and immunity to bludgeoning, piercing, poison, and psychic damage. Animate Trees (1/Day). The treant magically animates one or two trees it can see within 60 feet of it. These trees have the same statistics as a treant, except they have Intelligence and Charisma scores of 1, they can't speak, and they have only the Slam action option. An animated tree acts as an ally of the treant. The tree remains animate for 1 day or until it dies; until the treant dies or is more than 120 feet from the tree; or until the treant takes a bonus action to turn it back into an inanimate tree. The tree then takes root if possible.
