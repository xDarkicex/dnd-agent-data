# Terminus Warden

**description**: These robotic prison masters stand nine and a half feet tall and weigh 1,500 pounds, clad in golden armor and wielding staves with crackling energy at their ends. Wardens are the singular presence of order within the prison planets where they are stationed, carrying out the will of the unseen overseers that rule the prison. They are unlikely to intervene in any disputes among the inmates unless specifically ordered to do so by their masters (how they receive these orders is unknown). All wardens have standing orders to collect any resources mined or otherwise gathered or built by the inmates, distributing food and water in exchange. Those who do not produce their quota do not eat.
