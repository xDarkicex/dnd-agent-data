# Eclipse of Darkmantles

**description**: Darkmantle Any seasoned adventurer who’s visited The Low swears by three tenets: “bring more water than you need”, “keep a clean change of clothes”, and “always look up”. Nicknamed the “black death from above”, a darkmantle’s favourite hunting strategy involves masquerading as a stalactite and dropping on its prey’s head. If its natural camouflage proves insufficient, it conjures a cloud of magical darkness to conceal itself. Just one darkmantle can incapacitate the ill-prepared, quietly suffocating an entire party of adventurers one by one. They’re All Darkmantles! Travellers fear the danger that a single darkmantle represents. Imagine, then, the terror caused by an entire swarm of these creatures rapidly descending from a cavern ceiling, where an inconspicuous cluster of stalactites previously seemed to be. Luckily, swarms of darkmantles are rarely found in the wild, but creatures of great power have been known to control these dreaded predators, teaching them to work in large groups to be used in the defence of their underground lairs.
