# Nicoaleta

**description**: The child-like guardian of the Demiplane of Venom is a drow scion. Marked at birth by the hand of a drow deity, a scion is a portent of greatness. Unfortunately for the ruling house, the birth of a scion also heralds their downfall. While no matron is foolish enough to strike down a god-touched child, allowing them to grow in power among a burgeoning house is also not an option. Often a drow scion remains as the last member of a forgotten house, robbed of their destiny and secluded away from the halls of influence.
