# Petrous

**description**: The deep earth harnesses strange energies that reveal themselves in astonishing ways. The petrous is one such manifestation, a creature of stone and bone and hunger. Whether the petrous is a naturally evolved beast, a conjured outsider, or a created monster, its body appears to be entirely petrified bone. Little is known of the life cycle of this wandering predator, except that it seems to appear from the rock itself, and meeting a petrous’s eye is tantamount to staring death in the face. Blending into the terrain of jagged crevices and stark caverns, a petrous preys upon any and all things it catches unawares in the Underworld. Aklo Language. Though the petrous is capable of speech, it seldom makes sense to mortal ears. Its soft voice, almost a whisper, is punctuated by only the echo of a deep rumble as it waxes on about time’s river, the source, the temple of fog, and other nonsensical ramblings. Void Within. Those who survive its gullet speak of clinging to a stony perch at the edge of blackness, a single yellow orb beckoning them toward it, and a maddening cacophony of distant sounds, recognizable but elusive.
