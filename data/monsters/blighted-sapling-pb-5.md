# Blighted Sapling (PB 5)

**description**: Traits Hit Points. The sapling's hit points are equal to twice your druid level (currently set to the lowest level). Toxic Demise. Starting at level 10, when the creature is reduced to 0 hit points, it explodes in a burst of toxic mulch or fetid viscera. Each creature within 5 feet of the exploding creature must succeed on a Constitution saving throw against your spell save DC or take 5d8 necrotic damage. As an action, you can also cause a summoned creature to explode, immediately killing it. Actions Multiattack. Starting at level 14, the blighted sapling makes two claw attacks. Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +0 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 2d4+5 piercing damage. The attack uses your spell attack modifier to hit (Currently set to 0)
