# Regulus

**description**: Regulus Regulus is one of the Baendur, not so distantly related to King Cenric. Long ago he vowed his service to the Ironwood Witches and helped them infiltrate several Baendur kingdoms. His ultimate reward, or so he believed at the time, was when he was granted the mantle of Witchking. However, after being sewn into the armor, he was changed. His need for power and control intensified. As he served the witches, he began to challenge the Matron (Boða) whenever she suggested the correct course of action to take. He wanted to make all of the decisions—he was after all, the Witchking! Boða eventually tired of his bluster and poisoned him. As he died she gave him a new mission. She sent him into the Underworld to ensure that the unworthy dead would serve the witches. Though angry at his unfair death, he obeys her still, though imagines himself as the next ruler of the Underworld. Now restored to his former handsomeness, Regulus has become vain once more, more eager to use his charm to win his way, then the brute strength being the Witchking once offered him. Roleplaying Regulus Arrogant. In any room, Regulus feels he is the smartest and most skilled. He is an accomplished warrior and incredibly intelligent, but these are too often his own undoing for he struggles to see his own varied weaknesses. Scheming. Especially after being poisoned, Regulus holds no loyalty to anyone. He is willing to depose Hel and take over the Underworld, but in his name, not for the Ironwood Witches. But he has learned to keep his mouth shut and his secrets to himself. Palter’s Mace. Regulus carries Palter’s mace, a powerful magical item that once belonged to Palter himself.
