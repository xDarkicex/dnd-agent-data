# Red Urchin

**description**: The small, thin child pulls back its ragged hood revealing not a face but a rasping oval maw of jagged fangs. Red urchins are fey creations set loose in cities or other densely populated communities to help stem the spread of humanoids and their civilizations into wild areas. Created to look like common street waifs, red urchins easily slide into the lower rungs of a population, where they can hunt undetected. Periodic Hunters. Red urchins lurk in abandoned buildings, old sewer systems, and other seldom-used places within cities. They leave these lairs every few weeks to feast on an unsuspecting citizen before retreating to their lair for another month. To avoid raising suspicion, they typically prey on the often-overlooked members of society, though some red urchins develop a taste for specific types of people, such as drunkards or well-fed nobles, and hunt them exclusively. Chattering Charm. Imbued with fey magic, red urchins can emit a quiet chattering that subtly affects the minds of humanoids, making their victims more pliable. They use this to lure prey closer to them and to assault the minds of those who resist.
