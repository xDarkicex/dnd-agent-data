# Musca Macedda

**description**: This insectoid creature resembles a massive mosquito blended with other insects in a horrific hybrid. A furious buzzing rises from it as a hovering mass of black dots surrounds its rapidly fluttering wings like miniature bloodsucking companions. Its eyes are faceted but its mouth is crooked in a sinister smile below its long proboscis.
