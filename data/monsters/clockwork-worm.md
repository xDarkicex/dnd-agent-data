# Clockwork Worm

**description**: While Ernart is no push-over, he isn’t nearly powerful enough to dissuade all aggressors on his own. Luckily, you don’t need to be, when you can create magically-powered machines to guard you. Most prominent of these creations is his sentient and gargantuan clockwork worm which always lingers close to the tinker, either towering far above the customers or burrowed under the ground nearby. At the slightest hint of violent conflict, the worm makes its presence known, acting swiftly to protect Dippletopp and his wares.
