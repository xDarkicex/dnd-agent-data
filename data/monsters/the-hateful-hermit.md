# The Hateful Hermit

**description**: The hateful hermit is a bitter old man who’s lived in the Stolen Lands for longer than he cares to remember. By transplanting him from the wilds to the big city, though, he can play the role of a cruel “beggar prince” or other leader of a small-time gang of criminals. In Kingmaker, the hateful hermit is accompanied by a pet puma he simply calls “Cat.” Use the statistics for a panther to represent this ally. Traits Cunning Action. On each of his turns, the Hateful Hermit can use his bonus action to take the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action. Focus Hatred. At the start of his turn as a bonus action, the Hateful Hermit unleashes a vexing stream of profanities and threats at a single foe he can see within 30 feet. The Hermit gains advantage on the first attack roll he makes against that target on this turn. Sneak Attack (1/Turn). The Hateful Hermit deals an extra 17 (5d6) damage when it hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of the Hermit that isn’t incapacitated and the Hermit doesn’t have disadvantage on the attack roll. Actions Multiattack. The Hateful Hermit makes two melee attacks. Sickle. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4+2) slashing damage. Whip. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4+2) slashing damage. Blowgun. Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 1 piercing damage plus 3 (1d6) poison damage.
