# Unit of Zombies

**description**: Spawn from the petitioners of the Abyss, these zombies bear the traits of the demonic plane they spawn from. The mass of dozens of mindless, loudly moaning zombies crawl and bite at any creature that comes within their reach. The horde of abyssal undead climb over their targets, engulfing all creatures in their way before tearing them apart.
