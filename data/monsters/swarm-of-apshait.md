# Swarm of Apshait

**description**: These deadly beetles move in an undulating swarm of red and black. These beetles exclusively live off the corpses of the dead. They are harmless individually and only pose a threat as a swarm, erupting from the remains of the ancient embalmed dead when those mortal remains are disturbed.
