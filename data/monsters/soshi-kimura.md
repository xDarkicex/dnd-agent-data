# Soshi Kimura

**description**: Traits Cryptic Goals. When another character attempts to learn Kimura’s motivation, Kimura can make a Charisma saving throw with DC equal to the other character’s passive Wisdom (Perception) score. On a success, the other character learns this motivation instead: Test those who seek the Yogo Mask to see if they a re worthy. Beacon in the Dark. Kimura has 8 favor and can perform the following invocations: biting steel, bind the shadow, call of cinders, jade strike, Lord Moon’s gaze, threshold barrier, token of memory Focus Points. At the end of each of his turns, Kimura gains 1 focus point for each attack he made and 1 focus point for each time he was hit by an attack since the end of his last turn. Martial Techniques. Kimura can perform the following martial techniques so long as he has sufficient focus points to spend: 1+ focus points: snapping branch strike , soul-­sundering strike 2+ focus points: flashing steel cut , flowing water strike , iaijutsu cut: rising blade 3+ focus points: iaijutsu cut: crossing blade (Note: the damage is times five) Sniff You Out. Creatures within 5 feet of Kimura have disadvantage on Charisma (Deception) checks. Actions Multiattack. Soshi Kimura makes two attacks with his katana and one attack with his swordbreaker. One or more of these attacks may use his martial techniques. Katana. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (1d8 + 7) or 12 (1d10 + 7) slashing damage. This attack scores a critical hit on a roll of 18, 19, or 20. Swordbreaker. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d4 + 7) bludgeoning damage. Once per turn, instead of dealing damage, may force target to make a DC 14 Strength or Dexterity saving throw. On a failure, the target is restrained. Reactions Disarming Parry. Kimura adds +6 to his AC against one melee attack that would hit him. The attacker makes a DC 17 Strength saving throw. On a failure, the attacker is disarmed and the weapon lands 10 ft. away in a random direction. Legendary Actions Kimura can take two legendary action per round, choosing from the options below, but only at the end of another creature’s turn. Kimura regains spent legendary actions at the start of his turn. Spirit of Saibankan. Kimura may perform one of his known invocations. Steely Gaze. One creature within 15 feet of Kimura must make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, that creature becomes frightened of Kimura unless it chooses to take 11 (2d8 + 2) psychic damage. A creature that fails the saving throw can repeat the attempt at the start of each of its turns.
