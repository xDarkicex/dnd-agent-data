# Svirf Siege Slug

**description**: Siege slugs are notoriously ill-tempered, even for their trainers. They are also an indispensable defense for svirf enclaves; the threat of a siege retaliation looms heavy when considering raiding svirf caravans. Mobile Siege Platform. Siege slugs are bred to feel no pain upon their backs. Whole regiments of svirf soldiers, siege towers, ballistae, and more are arrayed on a siege slug equipped for war.
