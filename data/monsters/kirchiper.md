# Kirchiper

**description**: In Kanrath, some houses in mortal neighborhoods hang bundles of garlic on their front door. Some other houses place lavender on the window and door sills. Both of these precautions are taken against kirchipers; garlic to satisfy them to invoke their mercy and lavender to keep them away. Kirchipers are fey that prey on the blood of anxious mortals. They sneak into mortal households in the night and change the place of small everyday items. They do this until the people living in the house are in a constant state of agitation, and then drain the blood of these mortals in their sleep, without waking their prey. The mortal realizes they’ve been fed upon when they wake, as they become sick and weak. These fey like the taste of garlic and hate the smell of lavender. A kirchiper that finds garlic in the entrance of a household might move on, or it may come back for more. The same goes for the lavender; the fey might steer clear of a house that smells of lavender, or it might be provoked. They have a slim physique, long fingers and toes, wide mouths full of fangs, and dark purple skin. They have no eyes. A kirchiper uses its long, tube-like, fanged tongue to drain blood. They can also twist and warp their flesh and bones to travel through small cracks and openings.
