# Bloodwork Automaton

**description**: In the great city of Cyherested, where all kinds of people prosper, the streets are kept safe not only by mere guards but with the astonishing invention of bloodwork automatons. Generally, these constructs are crafted as humanoid bodies that carry a blood engine on their chest. A patrol bloodwork automaton is built to be 7 feet tall and weighs around five hundred pounds; quite heavy since its body is made out of various metals. A patrol bloodwork automaton’s duty is to accompany guards in areas that are considered to be less “friendly”. While patrolling, a patrol bloodwork automaton’s main objective is to protect the guards and eradicate any threat that exists in its perimeter. To that end, they are designed with a right arm that can extend into a blade instantly and a left arm that can fire projectiles to targets from afar. Worker bloodwork automatons mostly work in mines and construction, but they are sometimes given errands like cleaning the streets or cutting grass. Soldier bloodwork automatons work with the military and become perfect weapons to fight alongside each other. They prioritize keeping citizens safe and protecting military personnel. Most of them stay in and protect the Dragon’s Nest district and patrol the perimeters of the Spellspires. Blood Engine. Bloodwork automatons are blood engines that are enhanced by limbs, combat capabilities, and a decent amount of magic. The automatons still run with processed blood and contain a small amount of angst, located in the middle of their chest. Controlled Construct. Bloodwork automatons are controlled by those who activate them by sparing an amount of their own blood. Such an automaton’s mission or objective is imbued in its existence by magic and blood. Automatons obey any verbal commands that the activating party issues (no action required). If they don’t issue any commands to the automatons, they defend them from hostile creatures but otherwise take no actions.
