# Drow Cleric

**description**: To outward appearances, all drow worship but one deity. But behind this facade, myriad deities are called on to support specific aims and objectives. Religion and politics are closely intertwined, and faiths rise and fall, just as houses do, even if the tides are borne on the centuries, rather than a single lifetime. Few drow of regressed faiths rise to any real power, but all acknowledge the necessity of healthy competition.
