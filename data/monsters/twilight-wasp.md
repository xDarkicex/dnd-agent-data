# Twilight Wasp

**description**: Twilight Wasp When the sun is low over the horizon, the crystalline carapaces of these venomous predators transform the sky into a panorama of twinkling lights, a thousand shimmering stars weaving an elaborate and hypnotic dance. This enchanting display lures prey into the open, where the swarm can make short work of its bedazzled quarry. Rangers make a practice of catching twilight wasps with baited glass jars, keeping them as an offensive booby trap or crushing them into healing salves.
