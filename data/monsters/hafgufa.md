# Hafgufa

**description**: Sea Monsters The Serpent Sea, as well as the many rivers and lakes of Grimnir, are host to a wide variety of fish and aquatic mammals, such as seals and whales. The creatures detailed here are, for the most part, the most dangerous of sea borne predators, save for the great serpent herself. Hafgufa This is the largest of the sentient islands that can be found in the Serpent Sea (and in the seas of Valholl). It has many mouths running along the entire perimeter of its body. These look just like rocks or reefs when closed. It also has numerous eyes that resemble boulders when shut. Sea Ambush. Like a lyngbakr, the hafgufa is an ambush predator, trying to trick terrestrial creatures into walking on its surface. Instead of diving immediately, the hafgufa can use its tentacles and mouths to restrain and eat surface dwellers. Some even allow entire communities to inhabit them, selectively feeding only as needed. Ancestry. Whether a hafgufa is a longer-lived version of a lyngbakr, or a completely separate creature, is the subject of debate among magicians.
