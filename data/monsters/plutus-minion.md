# Plutus Minion

**description**: These infernal beings help Plutus whip the sinners into submission. They somewhat resemble Plutus with their white, flabby skin, ugly faces and misshapen limbs. They do not wear armor, but are stark naked and obviously of both sexes. They fight with rusty swords. The disposition of these Hell monsters is particularly mean and somewhat cowardly. Not only do they love torturing the souls of the dead, they will also attack and bite anyone who is wounded or looks weaker than they are. If the story requires some more action, why not have them ambush one group member from behind a big stone. They could also lure someone away from their comrades with a heap of shining gold and then bind them and use them for their own amusement. These greedy fiends are also known to hoard Hell’s Heavy Gold in small holes in the ground or beneath stones. Not that they could ever use it for any purpose, they just like its shininess. Plutus’ minions are mortal, they do not carry Lucifer’s hair. They do however carry a hair of Plutus in various ways to show their loyalty. Actions Rusty Shortsword: Melee Weapon Attack: +0 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 2 (1d4) slashing damage.
