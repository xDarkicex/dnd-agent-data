# Evil Mage

**description**: Evil mages hunger for arcane power and dwell in isolated places, where they can perform terrible magical experiments without interference. Traits Spellcasting. The mage is a 4th-level spellcaster that uses Intelligence as its spellcasting ability (spell save DC 13; +5 to hit with spell attacks). The mage knows the following spells from the wizard's spell list: • Cantrips (at will): light, mage hand, shocking grasp • 1st level (4 slots): charm person, magic missile • 2nd level (3 slots): hold person, misty step Actions Quarterstaff. Melee Weapon Attack: +1 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: (1d8-1) bludgeoning damage.
