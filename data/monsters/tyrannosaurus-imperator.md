# Tyrannosaurus Imperator

**description**: Amongst the tyrannosaurs, rex isn’t the largest, or heaviest, or most powerfully built. But when taken as a whole, it is the balance of all three that makes tyrannosaurus rex the most well-known apex predator. Tyrannosaurs defy traditional domestication, but those trainers and riders who treat the dinosaur as an equal find a ready partner in carnage. The promise of fear-filled food is a powerful lure for a tyrannosaur, and a trainer that provides it becomes a welcome hunt-friend. A rex that has grown large and ancient is known as tyrannosaurus imperator. Tyrannosaurs are solitary hunters, but imperator is known to gather a pack of its own kind. Their pack hunts are seldom more successful than before. However, weaker members of the pack often become prey themselves, and eventually, the lead imperator is the last of the pack to remain, having driven off or eaten all its competition.
