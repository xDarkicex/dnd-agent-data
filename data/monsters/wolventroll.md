# Wolventroll

**description**: Wolventroll This is not a distinct creature, though raiders believe it to be so. Instead, the wolventroll is a form that Ironwood witches wear, slightly larger than a dire wolf and capable of much greater carnage. At a distance, the large shape is often mistaken for a bear (for the raiders begin the campaign with no concept of wolves). Fast Travel. The witches use their wolventroll form to travel overland, its speed and endurance traversing great swathes of forest and plain effortlessly. Its fierceness also discourages any potential predators. The Wild Hunt. Other witches may use wolventrolls as mounts when attacking a settlement. Though they assemble in such a way rarely, when they do there might be dozens of witches and wolventrolls. The hunt begins with the growls and shrieks of the beasts, and the eerie droning of chanting witches, emanating from the forests. Entire communities have vanished in the night, torn apart by the fangs and deadly magic of such a fast moving and powerful war party.
