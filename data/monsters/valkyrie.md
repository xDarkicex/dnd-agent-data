# Valkyrie

**description**: Valkyries are proud celestial warriors embodying the pinnacle of martial prowess, divine grace, and valor. Often appearing as striking female humanoids with winged helmets, their beauty is matched only by their skill in battle. Valkyries are renowned for their mastery of mounted combat, their presence a beacon of hope on the battlefield as they ride atop horses or pegasi, fearlessly charging their fiendish adversaries. Soul Shepherds Many valkyries serve as spiritual guides to the souls of fallen warriors, ensuring their safe passage to the hallowed halls of their deity’s eternal domain. In times of great strife, these celestial emissaries can be glimpsed on the battlefields in the Mortal World, their ethereal forms a comforting presence as they shepherd the spirits of the fallen to the afterlife.
