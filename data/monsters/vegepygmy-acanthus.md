# Vegepygmy Acanthus

**description**: The vegepygmy acanthus feels a natural connection to the magic that created their kind. Whether this is true wisdom inherited from its “ancestors” or some intuition over having seen countless creatures succumb to russet mold is not known, but it matters little to the acanthus. Many acanthus revere the russet mold to which their race is inextricably tied, speaking the mold’s will their tribe and ensuring the cycle continues.
