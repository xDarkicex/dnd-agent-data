# Nomad Guard

**description**: Appearance and Personality: The guards are rugged, but well-­worn soldiers. All take clear pride in the patterns with which their hair is braided and the condition of their spears. They recognize that the survival of their tribe is entirely dependent upon their skill at arms. To insure this, they drill regularly and are unrelentingly dour and invariably focused upon security measures. The guards are routinely suspicious of any friendly gestures the PCs might make, suspecting that these could have sinister motives. Traits Halfling Nimbleness. The nomad guard can move through the space of any enemy that is size Medium or larger. Lucky. Whenever the nomad guard rolls a 1 on an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, they may reroll the die and must use the new roll. Pack Tactics. The nomad guard has advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one of the nomad guard’s allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn’t incapacitated. Actions Javelin. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.
