# Angler Wyrm

**description**: The angler wyrm, a huge sea serpent melding draconic might and angler fish traits, prowls the ocean's abyss. Gleaming scales cover its immense form, and its head features a massive maw filled with razor-sharp teeth. Through its mastery of illusions, it deceives and disorients, rendering its adversaries vulnerable to its devastating attacks. Legends recount its ability to conjure phantom images, luring unsuspecting prey into its deadly trap. The angler wyrm moves gracefully through the water, its muscular form propelled by powerful fins and a serpentine tail.
