# Yshara, the Soft-spoken

**description**: Yshara the Soft-spoken, a young elven woman born into slavery under Clan Fyrr, emerged as a symbol of hope two hundred years ago. Speaking softly and calmly with an air of confidence, she fearlessly led the revolt against her cruel oppressors, her words infused with powerful magic capable of halting armies in their tracks. Through her collection of poems, the Calls of Unbinding, she inspired and rallied countless enslaved individuals to rise against their chains. Though her demise came at the hands of the Scaled Council, Yshara's legacy endured through the unwavering efforts of the secret society known as the Children of the Coffle, who continue to carry her torch, fighting to eradicate hereditary slavery in Xantharos.
