# Ceres Kyton

**description**: Ceres kytons are the hunters of the Choir, tracking prey for days before emerging from the shadows to rend flesh—or worse, to drag mortals away to unthinkable fates. Each ceres is made from an okanta, tortured and disfigured beyond the reach of sanity. Ceres are in constant pain, snarling wetly through wired jaws and with flesh restrained by taut chains and metal plates. Their agony wanes only when they can inflict it on others, especially with the wicked blades that replace their severed forearms.
