# Graeae

**description**: Graeae A graeae is a haglike creature, though more human in appearance. Some describe them as emissaries of the gods, while others believe graeaes to be physical manifestations of fate. While other hags gather sometimes in covens, graeae are almost never found alone, as the strands of their fate are woven together with other hags, especially with others of their own kind. While each individual graeae can craft her own mystic eye, a coven of graeae often shares a single mystic eye between them as they feel it magnifies the power of their visions of past, present, and future.
