# Ironthrall Commoner

**description**: Ironthralls Ironthralls are humanoids that have been taken control of by an Ironwood Witch via the Treethrall ability. They still have some free will but can be manipulated (subtly via dreams or more directly by telepathic command). Ironthralls are detectable by the detect evil and good spell or similar effects. Ironthrall Variants An ironthrall raid leader is a raid leader with 82 hit points and the additional properties: Aversion to Light. If the ironthrall leader takes radiant damage, they have disadvantage on saving throws and ability checks until the end of their next turn. Call: Ironroots. Tendrils emerge to entangle a target within 120 feet. The ironheart tree must be within 15 feet of the target and the ironthrall leader must be able to see the target. The target must succeed a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or be grappled (escape DC 15). While grappled, the target is restrained. Only 1 target can be grappled by this ability at a time. An ironthrall raider is a raider with 22 hit points and the Aversion to Light property.
