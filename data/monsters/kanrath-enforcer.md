# Kanrath Enforcer

**description**: Kanrath enforcers are the backbone of Kanrath’s security. Composed of mortals, Kanrath enforcers can easily blend into mortal criminal societies, and can thus end the conspiracies against their vampire masters before things get worse. Also, Kanrath enforcers can be more reluctant to give asymmetric responses, display brutality, and oppress their fellow folk, just to impress their vampire masters and to gain a chance to become a vampire. Kanrath enforcer squads are led by captains. Usually, the most ruthless enforcers are chosen to be captains. They do not hesitate to harm any mortal who “breaks the law”, which actually means if the mortals act against the vampires’ will. If an enforcer on their squad begins to show signs of mercy, they too suffer the captain’s wrath. Their merciless nature makes them perfect candidates to be the tools of vampires. The blood-red breastplate they don has resulted in some people calling them the “Red”. There are also some vampires among the enforcers, though it’s extremely shameful for a vampire to be an enforcer.
