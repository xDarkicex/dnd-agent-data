# Gravity Golem

**description**: Gravity golems are fierce arcane constructs crafted from various alloys of metal etched with magical runes. A fascinating sight that seems to defy common sense, a gravity golem's disjointed body parts are held together by gravitational energy from numerous glowing, rune-enchanted orbs. A Forceful Guardian One of the most magnificent constructs invented by the ambitious runecarvers of the Arkrune Enclave, a gravity golem is a fearsome guardian that acts upon its master's commands with unyielding and single-minded determination, using gravitational magic and pure energy to destroy whoever tries to stand against it.
