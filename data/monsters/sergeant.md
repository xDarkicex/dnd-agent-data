# Sergeant

**description**: Actions Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, range 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) slashing damage, or 8 (1d10 + 3) slashing damage if used with two hands. Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one creature. Hit: 4 (1d8) piercing damage. Command. The sergeant targets one ally it can see within 30 feet of it. If the target can see and hear the sergeant, the target can use its reaction to make one weapon attack. Maneuver Allies (Recharge 5–6). Up to four allies within 60 feet of the sergeant that can hear it can each use their reaction to move up to half their speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
