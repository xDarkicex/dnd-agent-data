# Voidguard Soldier

**description**: The Voidguard has become the most important military organization in Ashandri. Even though their headquarter is in Delvaroth, Voidguard soldiers can be found anywhere in the world - including the subterranean Hollows. They can appear in groups, under the command of a leader like a Dwarven Voidguard Captain, or alone on some kind of mission. There are also small Voidguard outposts all over Ashandri, usually manned with 3-12 soldiers.
