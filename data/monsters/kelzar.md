# Kelzar

**description**: Kelzar the troll has molded a pair of one of its family members’ arms to its torso, the eyes of some beasts to the back of its head, and the gills of a merrow on its neck to enhance an already menacing body. The mutated troll considers the Ulden Swamps its kingdom and loathes all who enter its domain.
