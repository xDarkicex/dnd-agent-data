# Lyngbakr

**description**: Sea Monsters The Serpent Sea, as well as the many rivers and lakes of Grimnir, are host to a wide variety of fish and aquatic mammals, such as seals and whales. The creatures detailed here are, for the most part, the most dangerous of sea borne predators, save for the great serpent herself. Lyngbakr The surface of this large whale resembles a mossy island covered in shrubs. A voracious carnivore, it consumes vast schools of fish and even the occasional riverwhale. False Sanctuary. If birds or other animals, including raiders, happen to explore its surface, it will dive, hoping to drown the interlopers and consume them at its leisure. Driven by Hunger. If a lyngbakr is particularly hungry they might charge a longship fleet, or even risk attacking a pod of riverwhales.
