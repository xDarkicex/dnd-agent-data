# Deinos

**description**: Deinos These sinister stallions and magnificent mares are related to ordinary horses and at first glance they are simply strong and sleek horses, well-muscled and with shining coats but otherwise unremarkable. At times, they even behave like ordinary horses, if spirited and headstrong. However, they carry a hidden and accursed nature, an uncanny taste for flesh and blood, especially for humanoids. While no more intelligent than other animals, they cunning enough to be capable of subterfuge, feigning docility in order to draw close to their prey. When inflamed with bloodlust, however, they are tireless in pursuit of their prey, inspiring terror with their screaming whinnies. Royal Lineage. Legend tells that the first deinos were bred from ordinary horses by a cruel king, who fed his royal herd on the flesh of unsuspecting guests or strangers to his island. He kept them lashed with iron chains to their feasting troughs, releasing them from time to time to terrorize the countryside until a mighty hero overcame them and lashed them to his own chariot, feeding the cruel king to his own mares before binding their mouths and calming their spirits for a time. Many horses sired or foaled from that lineage grow up as ordinary horses, though of excellent stock, but in others the madness of their sires and dams breeds true and they grow into savage hunters, fleeing into the wild
