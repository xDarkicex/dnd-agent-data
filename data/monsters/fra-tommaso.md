# Fra' Tommaso

**description**: This clerical magician is a true believer in God and follows Savonarola out of the conviction that his superior is doing the right thing. The prior has persuaded him that these people have come to help the Medici in their evil and degenerate ways and that they must be stopped. He will fight valiantly, but is, however, liable to recognize the truth. Should he find that the adventurers fight nobly, do not use any doubtful magic or even address him personally, he might waver or even change sides. If not, he will die in ignorance, fighting for the wrong cause. A tragic ending for a true man of God. Fra’ Tommaso is a monk of middle age with the round face of a peasant, a friendly smile and little hair left from his original brown mane. He is clad in a white robe with a black cloak, the typical Dominican outfit. In this fight, he will look determined and committed, at least initially. Depending on how it goes, he might become visibly worried and his actions might become hesitant. Traits Spellcasting. Fra’ Tommaso is a 7th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with spell attacks). Fra’ Tommaso knows the following wizard spells: Cantrips: light, mage hand, message, ray of frost, shocking grasp 1st level (4 slots): alarm, burning hands, comprehend languages, detect magic, expeditious retreat, grease, identify, shield, unseen servant 2nd level (3 slots): alter self, arcane lock, darkvision, enlarge/reduce, gentle repose, gust of wind, locate object, nystul’s magic aura, shatter 3rd level (3 slots): dispel magic, gaseous form, glyph of warding, magic circle, nondetection, protection from energy 4th level (1 slot): control water, fabricate, leomund’s secret chest, mordenkainen’s private sanctum Minor Alchemy. Fra’ Tommaso can alter objects when using the wizard’s transmutation class ability. Transmuter’s Stone. Fra’ Tommaso used the wizard transmutation class ability to speed himself up (already included in the stats). Actions Shortsword. Melee Weapon Attack: -1 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d6 − 1) piercing damage.
