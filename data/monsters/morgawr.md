# Morgawr

**description**: Morgawr is an ancient kraken that has dedicated its existence to mastering the school of necromancy and becoming an archmage. Wielding almost unrivalled power through the study of the arcane and dominating the seas it has called its home, Morgawr eventually found the lost city of A’rvanshy in the Abysmal Rift and sought to claim it. Its attack was halted by the lich necromancer of the deep sea-elves, A’rvanshy, when the lich ascended to an aspect of necromancy and imprisoned the kraken in a minimus containment spell. For a thousand years, Morgawr slumbered in its gemstone prison, until a green dragon oracle named Ossiazzxontyr finally broke it free. Morgawr emerged in a fury and razed the city of A’rvanshy, murdering and then raising most of the population as undead servitors. Hundreds who were left alive are mere fodder for the kraken and live in terror under Morgawr’s rule. Morgawr has a singular goal - finding the lost secrets of A’rvanshy’s magical powers and claiming them as its own. The kraken’s minions and agents scour the seas, and even dry lands, to obtain any trace of the deep-sea elves and A’rvanshy’s legacy.
