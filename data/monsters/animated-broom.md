# Animated Broom

**description**: Traits False Object . If the broom is motionless at the start of combat, it has advantage on its initiative roll. Moreover, if a creature hasn’t observed the broom move or act, that creature must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom (Perception) check to discern that the broom is animate. Flyby . The broom doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks when it flies out of an enemy’s reach. Actions Multiattack . The broom makes two melee attacks. Broomstick . Melee Weapon Attack : +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit : 5 (1d4 + 3) bludgeoning damage.
