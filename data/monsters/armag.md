# Armag

**description**: Armag is one of the primary villains encountered during Kingmaker, but his villainy is in large part the result of influence from the deadly weapon he wields, Ovinrbaane . During your campaign, you can maintain this relationship between Armag and his sword, or you can replace Ovinrbaane and simply use Armag as a leader of ferocious warriors. Likewise, you can establish Ovinrbaane as the goal of a quest; perhaps the PCs race against a rival adventuring group to recover the dangerous sword from a crypt in order to prevent it from giving rise to a new warlord. Traits Cleaving Swing. If Armag reduces a target to 0 hit points with a melee attack, he can make a melee attack as a bonus action against another target within reach. Special Equipment. Armag wields Ovinrbaane , a powerful (but cursed) sword. Actions Multiattack. Armag makes three Ovinrbaane attacks. Ovinrbaane. Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d6+8) slashing damage.
