# Trollcat

**description**: Cat, Trollcat This creature appears to be a normal raider cat, but upon closer inspection the bulk of their body is formed from discarded scraps of yarn, leather, and even sticks. Servants of the Arcane. They are usually created by spellcasters to steal objects or to serve as sentry. Artificial Constructs. Once slain, the trollcat falls apart into its component parts, though they are soaked in foul smelling milk.
