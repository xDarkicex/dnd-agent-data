# Nalfeshnee Captain

**description**: The vicious nalfeshnee captain, unlike its lower-ranked brethren, can wield spells and force demon underlings to follow its command. It is so brutal in battle, it can incapacitate a creature momentarily with its vicious iron-like claws. Among the known nalfeshnee captains are Nalfeshnee himself and Zerevimeel. Traits Legendary Resistance (1/Day). If the nalfeshnee fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead. Magic Resistance. The nalfeshnee has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. Actions Multiattack. The nalfeshnee uses Horror Nimbus if it can. It then makes one Bite attack and two Claw attacks. If both Claw attacks hit the same creature, the target takes an extra 10 (3d6) slashing damage and must succeed on a DC 19 Constitution saving throw or be stunned until the end of the nalfeshnee's next turn. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 33 (5d10 + 6) piercing damage. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (3d6 + 5) slashing damage. Spellcasting. The nalfeshnee casts one of the following spells, requiring no material components and using Intelligence as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 18): At will: detect magic 3/day each: call lightning, dispel magic, polymorph (self only), slow 1/day: feeblemind Horror Nimbus (Recharge 5–6). The nalfeshnee magically emits scintillating, multicolored light. Each creature within 20 feet of the nalfeshnee that can see the light must succeed on a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened for 1 minute. A creature that fails the save by 5 or more is also paralyzed for the same duration. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. If a creature's saving throw is successful or the effect ends for it, the creature is immune to the nalfeshnee's Horror Nimbus for the next 24 hours. Teleport. The nalfeshnee magically teleports, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying, up to 120 feet to an unoccupied space it can see. Legendary Actions The nalfeshnee can take three legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The nalfeshnee regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn. Claw. The nalfeshnee makes a Claw attack. Ruthless Command. The nalfeshnee targets one ally it can see within 30 feet of it. If the target can see and hear the nalfeshnee, the target can use its reaction to make one weapon attack with advantage on the attack roll, but attack rolls against it have advantage until the start of the nalfeshnee's next turn. Teleport (Costs 2 Actions). The nalfeshnee magically teleports, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying, up to 120 feet to an unoccupied space it can see. Cast a Spell (Costs 2 Actions). The nalfeshnee uses Spellcasting.
