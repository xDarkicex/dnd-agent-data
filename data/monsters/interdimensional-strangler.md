# Interdimensional Strangler

**description**: These abominations are the hunters of the aberrations from the Farrealm. No creature can escape their interdimensional receptacles and tentacles that both know no boundaries. Once in the paralyzing grasp of the interdimensional strangler, the victim is prevented from escaping. Interdimensional stranglers are known to hold such victims for eternity on the Farrealm where time and space have no meaning.
