# Ravager Elf Soldier

**description**: While most of the armies of Va'Laeth retreated to their home world after the first two invasions, there are still a number of Laethian agents in Ashandri. Some of them have specific missions, others are just here to keep the fear of their kind alive. There are even rumors of hidden Ravager Elf settlements, built as military locations for future invasions. Whether this is true or not, Ravager Elf soldiers are still a menace to the people of Ashandri.
