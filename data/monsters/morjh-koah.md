# Mor'jh Koah

**description**: Mor’jh Koah is the leader of the Rakhix Gharthok Kol’jah, the Blood Feeders from the Dark—the tribe of sahuagin inhabiting the Calhay Sea. He is the tribe’s spiritual and military leader, dedicated to preserving the tribe’s two-thousand-year devotion to the Darkness Within in the Temple of Bleeding Darkness. Mor’jh Koah was recently approached and turned into a vampire by Faeryl Myrryn, and tasked to find the Dark Star’s blood in the region. Mor’jh Koah called for divine intervention, and miraculously, an agent of the Dark Star arrived at the temple in the form of a dark couatl and offered aid in return for the tribe’s alliance. The divine serpent soon found the source of the blood—the Fang of Xantharos. Learning the blood’s location, Mor’jh Koah relocated to the fang and is harvesting the dark celestial blood to use in transforming the tribe’s brood into sahuagin tainted by the power of darkness.
