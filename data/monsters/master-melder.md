# Master Melder

**description**: Master melders are extraordinarily powerful espers that have realized full control over the primordial and elemental energies that shape the forces of reality. Many serve in high-ranking positions, often as counselors, academy instructors, judges, historians, or state leaders. Those who are self-centered or reclusive can easily become tyrants or galactic overlords, often operating from a secluded base of operations with an army of henchmen and esper disciples.
