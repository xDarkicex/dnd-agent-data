# Thornbush (PB 3)

**description**: Traits Plant Camouflage. The thornbush has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide in areas with abundant plant life. Power of Nature. The thornbush’s weapon attacks are magical. Stickler. When the thornbush hits a creature with an opportunity attack, that creature’s speed becomes 0 for the rest of the turn. Creatures provoke opportunity attacks from the thornbush even if they take the Disengage action before leaving its reach. Actions Multiattack. The thornbush makes a number of thorny strike attacks equal to half your proficiency bonus, rounded down. Thorny Strike. Melee Weapon Attack: +3 + PB to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 2d4 + 3 + PB piercing damage.
