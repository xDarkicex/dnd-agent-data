# Ogre

**description**: Ogres are massive, brutish creatures of prodigious strength, dim wits, and sadistic tempers. All of these qualities make them perfect shock troops in the armies of the Shadow. Actions Greatclub. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 10 ft, one target. Hit: 13 (2d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage. Javelin. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., or range 30/120 ft, one target. Hit: 11 (2d6 + 4) piercing damage. Swat (recharge 4-6). One creature within 5 feet of the ogre must make a DC 14 Strength saving throw. If it fails, it is pushed up to 5 feet away from the ogre and is knocked prone. The ogre may then make a greatclub attack.
