# Wood Elemental

**description**: Wood elementals clamber slowly and methodically across and through the impossibly vast towering trees that make up the Elemental Plane of Wood, seeking peaceful vistas to take in for a few hours—or a few millennia—and rotted-out wood to absorb and remake as new wood. These creatures are famous for destroying and absorbing wooden weapons and items carried by adventurers, most often in areas of Kir-Sharaat where dense thickets open into wood elemental vortexes. Wood Shapes. Many wood elementals have a roughly humanoid shape like several tree trunks joined together with a tangle of branches, vines, and brambles, with two arm-like masses protruding from its sides. Dark holes like eyes and a mouth are formed in the upper half of the central mass.
