# Hobgoblin War Priest

**description**: The war priest is a worshipper of Maglubiyet, the goblinoid god of war and domination. They are usually found leading goblinoid armies to battle or planning military strategies with other goblinoid leaders. It prefers to use its spells to wreak havoc on its foes rather than heal its wounded allies. Traits Martial Advantage (1/Turn). The hobgoblin can deal an extra 10 (3d6) damage to a creature it hits with a weapon attack if that creature is within 5 feet of an ally of the hobgoblin and that ally isn't incapacitated. Actions Multiattack. The hobgoblin makes one Heavy Mace attack and uses Holy Fire. Heavy Mace. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage plus 4 (1d8) necrotic damage. Holy Fire. The hobgoblin targets one creature it can see within 60 feet of it. The target makes a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the target takes 12 (2d8 + 3) radiant damage and is blinded until the start of the hobgoblin's next turn. On a successful save, the target takes half as much damage and isn't blinded. Spellcasting. The hobgoblin casts one of the following spells using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with spell attacks): At will: guidance, light, thaumaturgy 1/day each: bane, cure wounds, crusader's mantle, dispel magic, hold person, revivify, spirit guardians Reactions Maglubiyet's Guidance (3/Day). The hobgoblin grants a + 5 bonus to an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw made by itself or another goblinoid within 30 feet of it. The hobgoblin can make this choice after the roll is made but before the result is determined.
