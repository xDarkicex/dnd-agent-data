# Caddos Birdes

**description**: These miniature horses dwell in forests and scrub brush hills, with their unusual coats of dappled shades of green making them hard to spot by predators. A caddos birdes is around 2-3 feet high at the shoulder, with a long frond-like tail of mossy green hair. Some claim that caddos birdes are magical fey creatures, but despite their appearance they are entirely natural. Some tiny fey have been known to train and ride them, though on their own they are relatively docile and skittish.
