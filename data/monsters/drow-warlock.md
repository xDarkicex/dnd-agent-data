# Drow Warlock

**description**: Warlocks facilitate the summoning of outsiders to strike a deal for power and influence amongst the drow elite. Few nobles undertake to become warlocks themselves. Instead, warlocks often exist outside the normal drow hierarchy, their services for sale to the highest bidder. The nature of a warlock’s business lends itself to such mercenary practices; once the demon or devil is summoned, the warlock is under no obligation to ensure their customer enters into a favorable pact. Unless, of course, the client has retained the warlock for that additional service.
