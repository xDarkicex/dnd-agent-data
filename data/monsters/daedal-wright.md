# Daedal Wright

**description**: The daedal wright is a multifunction construction and production golem invented by Master Arlen. It is equipped with multiple smaller and larger mechanical arms with different appendages for digging, drilling, fine and bulk manipulation, and many more tasks. The archmage’s routines imprinted in the daedal wrights allow it to find the most optimal solution to any given problem.
