# Sabina Montecraxi

**description**: This attractive woman in her mid-thirties is the leader of the gang. She is wearing bicolor trousers with high boots, a red blouse and a leather jerkin. Her dark hair is tied in a ponytail and partially covered by a leather cap. Her radiant smile reveals the lack of a few teeth, and the remaining ones don’t look very healthy either. Yes, this makes her a little less attractive, but don’t forget that dental hygiene was not at its best in 15th century Italy. Only a few people will have all their teeth after the age of thirty. In case of a conflict, Sabina will fight two-handedly with a short sword and a dagger. She is very agile and can dodge most blows effortlessly. Sabina is not married, as she prefers to remain independent and free to choose her current lover from amongst her troops. But as her relationships tend to break up sooner or later, she will not become too attached. Sabina is wearing a silver bracelet that can heal its owner (equivalent to a level 2 cure wounds spell.) If questioned, she will describe it as a family heirloom, which it is indeed. Traits Bracelet of Healing. The bracelet can be used to cast cure wounds (1x/day) as a 2nd level spell (Heals 2D8 hit points). Spellcasting. Sabina is a 3t h-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 9, +1 to hit with spell attacks). Sabina knows the following ranger spells: 1st level (3 slots): alarm, hunter’s mark, longstrider Actions Two-Weapon Fighting. Sabina makes two weapon attacks: one with her shortsword and one with her dagger. Shortsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage. Dagger. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage.
