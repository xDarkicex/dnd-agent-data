# Maw Vermin

**description**: The Maw Vermin may have been a rat that fed on too much delerium, or perhaps the sickly scavengers of another dimension altogether. Its pink flesh is caked with thick patchwork tufts of fur, with three gangly long arms that end in long bony fingers with talon like claws. Rather than a head, the top of its massive body has two beady rodent-like eyes and rat-like features. Upon its belly is a large gaping maw filled with jagged teeth and a massive purple tongue slurping and slathering gobs of vile saliva out of it as it drags itself towards its next meal.
