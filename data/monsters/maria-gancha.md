# Maria Gancha

**description**: Maria Gancha, or Hooked Mary, is an exceptionally cruel, cunning, and ancient pettenedda (see page 138), always dripping wet and with arms ending in rusty, barbed hooks rather than hands. She has survived several encounters with mighty heroes seeking to vanquish her, though one such conflict cost her both of her arms at the elbows. She had her hands and forearms replaced with the pair of wicked hooks to which her name refers, using them to wreak her revenge on the hero that crippled her. Though her hooks prevent her from casting spells like her pettenedda sisters, she has channeled her magics into more devious manifestations.
