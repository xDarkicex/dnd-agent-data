# Grimtroll

**description**: Grimtroll When an Ironwood witch in giant form consorts with an actual giant, there is a chance the offspring are tallfolk or grimtrolls instead of normal giants. While much is known about tallfolk, much less information exists about these more ferocious cousins of theirs. Several feet taller than tallfolk, grimtrolls are stooped, with very broad heads and overly large torsos. They are incredibly strong and certainly even other giants are wise to avoid them. Recently grimtrolls have been spotted near the edges of the northernmost Ironwood Forest. Watchful Foes. While grimtrolls are not the cleverest of foes, they are not simple brutes either. They tend to stalk their prey, taking advantage of their natural camouflage to study an opponent for hours if required. If they attack, it is either because they have been ordered to by someone they fear, because they are hungry, or because they believe they might find interesting trinkets to loot from the corpses of their victims. Solitary. Unlike their tallfolk cousins, grimtroll seldom form attachments to others and hence, no real community of them has ever arisen. Decorative. They often adorn themselves with tattered red cloth, colored the same as that which the Ironwood witches wear. Whether that’s because they are servants of the witches, or simply ate one or two of them, is not entirely clear. But these giants do seem fond of brightly colored cloth and are known to wear bands of bone tied together with such material.
