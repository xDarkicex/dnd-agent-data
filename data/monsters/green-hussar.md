# Green Hussar

**description**: The forest awakens animals to speak and to help travelers, spawns green hussars as guardians, and creates permanent protectors like treants and leshy to ward certain springs, runestones, groves, and ley line sites. All serve the forest, and many act as extensions of the forest’s defenses. Actions Shortbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: (1d6+2) piercing damage. Flowering Lance. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: (1d12+3) piercing damage.
