# Talos

**description**: This towering automaton of verdigrised bronze moves with an implacable tread, shaking the earth with every step. He resembles a mighty armored warrior with breastplate, greaves, and crested helm, wielding a mighty blade welded into its closed fist. Its cast visage and hollow eyeholews give it a pitiless aspect when it rises to move, its metallic thews creaking and groaning with impossible strength. Each talos possesses a spark of divinity, said to have been imbued by the gods themselves, and their inmost workings roar with heat like a furnace as molten metal surges within it.
