# Cavalo Do Sonho

**description**: Cavalo do Sonho This bat-winged horse can ride through the skies and leap among the clouds. Its body is nearly hairless, with purple-gray hide that resembles cracked stone. It does sport a lush, flowing mane of black hair, and its tail is a long cascade of gray-black hair. Its eyes and nostrils grow with a livid internal fire, while its wings creak ominously when flapped. The beast lives in dark caves in remote mountains and feasts on animals, humanoids, and plants with equal abandon. Horse of Thought. Some sages aver there is only a single Horse of Thought, sometimes called pensamento, though that may simply be an advanced variant of the typical cavalo do sonho. These hell-horses may materialize when a connection to the human mind is established and that its nature is defined by the thoughts of its summoner. The pensamento is thought to be benevolent, called to a dreamer by the purity and generosity of their thoughts. In a broken and dangerous world, however, negative thoughts and dreams seem far more common, and thus the evil-aligned cavalo are far better known. This intellectual link requires a perfect synchronization of both minds involved, and those casting their consciousness into the void seeking contact with a cavalo do sonho may find terrible consequences, with madness and senility being two of the better possible scenarios). On the other hand, success bestows immeasurable power upon the rider through the willing alliance of the cavalo, as it is may be called to materialize when the right thought is formulated and its mighty power allows it to traverse time, space, and even reach unimaginable planes of existence.
