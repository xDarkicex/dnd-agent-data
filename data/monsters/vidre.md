# Vidre

**description**: Emotionless warriors, the vidre crave ascension through gathering souls. The vidre work on a grand scale to harvest soul energy, preferring subtle methods for obtaining their sustenance. A common tactic the vidre use is to gift a Vidrefacte to a community of humanoids, in this way passively collecting souls without their knowledge. The vidre are a cultural collective. Individuals think independently and possess their own, albeit limited, personality, but as a group their motivations are solely focused on the survival of the collective. Many humanoids mistake this selfless predilection as subservience to a hive mind.
