# Egua Branca

**description**: Velha da Égua Branca, "the Old Woman on the White Mare," is a mysterious and terrifying figure. She is a wrinkled, aged woman wearing a white headdress adorned with red ribbons and clutches a dagger. It is said that at night, she rides her white mare, startling animals and folk alike. Despite this, she does not usually harm others. Woe to those that offend her sense of justice, however. Some say she belongs to the night sky, while others say she is the remnant of a long-forgotten titan or deity. She rarely speaks, what she has to say, is likely of great import, perhaps a warning or exhortation. If found in her lair, she may be persuaded to speak, but finding her home is a quest for the truly determined and daring. Her mare is her formidable companion.
