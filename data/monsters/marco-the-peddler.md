# Marco the Peddler

**description**: In Memory of Marco Krämer Background Marco always had a keen mind, an aptitude for crafting stories, and a curious soul that longed to explore the many wonders of the world – and other worlds beyond it. In short, Marco is a born adventurer. For many years, Marco followed this calling and spent his days delving into dangerous dungeons, slaying monsters and pilfering treasures from dragon hoards alongside his troupe of trusted comrades. A trained bard with a creative mind, Marco's many adventures are outdone only by his recounting of them. Eventually, the years caught up with Marco, who found himself more excited by tales of adventure than the actual act of adventuring (and the perils that entailed). In the end, he settled down and opened up Marco's Mysteries , a shop where kindred spirits can purchase goods to aid them on their adventures, sell their valuable spoils, or just swap tales with the good-humored bard. As for adventuring, Marco regularly meets up with his old party to undertake new quests, but now using enchanted figurines, illusory displays, glyph-inscribed dice, and their own imaginations, rather than putting their lives at risk. Roleplaying Marco Marco is a good-natured and jovial man with a sharp mind and a big heart. His laugh is genuine and contagious, and his appetite for adventure – in any shape or form – permeates everything he does. There are few things that Marco treasures more than storytelling. Whether it's sharing his boundless wisdom with fledgling adventurers ("If you've got questions, now's the time to ask them, young friend, and not when you've already disturbed the dragon – trust me, I speak from bitter experience here!”) or swapping stories of adventure with his customers. Marco cannot abide cheaters and liars ("A bit of exaggeration isn't a sin, but if you tell me you've slain a giant, I better not find a halfling when I dig up the corpse!”). He also has a deep sense of justice and hates when things are unfair or someone thinks the rules don't apply to them. Marco's quirky and creative mind is mirrored in his surroundings – including his shop, which appears to visitors as a disorganized mess. Marco does not see it that way, however, insisting that everything is exactly where he wants it to be – and backs it up by always knowing where an item can be found ("The Damnation sword? Right over there, just under the Spell Scroll of Teleport and the Hat of Disguise , you can't miss it!”) Though I've only spoken with Marco a few times, it's clear that he is as great a friend as one can have; full of excitement, fiercely loyal, and always good for a tale or two. The world would be a better – and more adventurous – place with more people like Marco in it! – the Wanderer
