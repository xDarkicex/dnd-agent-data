# The Misbegotten Troll

**description**: The Misbegotten Troll is a violent minion of Nyrissa. She often leaves him to his own devices, which run the gamut from cruel to sadistic. He can just as easily play a role where he is no mere minion. He works well in a game that seeks to tell a Beowulf -style story, where a group of high-level heroes are called upon to save a remote kingdom from attacks by an unbelievably powerful giant. Traits Brave. The Misbegotten Troll has advantage on saving throws against being frightened. Brute. A melee weapon deals one extra die of its damage when the Misbegotten Troll hits with it (included in the attack). Keen Smell. The Misbegotten Troll has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Regeneration. The Misbegotten Troll regains 10 hit points at the start of his turn. If he takes acid or fire damage, this trait doesn’t function at the start of the Misbegotten Troll’s next turn. The Misbegotten Troll dies only if he starts his turn with 0 hit points and doesn’t regenerate. Actions Multiattack. The Misbegotten Troll makes three melee attacks, two with his glaive and one with his bite, or two with his claws and one with his bite. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +15 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d6+9) piercing damage. Glaive. Melee Weapon Attack: +15 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 25 (3d10+9) slashing damage. Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +15 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d6+9) slashing damage. Reactions Counterattack. When struck by an enemy with a melee weapon, the Misbegotten Troll makes one glaive or claws attack against the creature that hit him. Legendary Actions The Misbegotten Troll can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature’s turn. The Misbegotten Troll regains spent legendary actions at the start of his turn. Detect. The Misbegotten Troll makes a Wisdom (Perception) check. Glaive Attack. The Misbegotten Troll makes a glaive attack. Jangle the Chain (Costs 2 Actions). The Misbegotten Troll shakes his chain of caged sprites, causing the supernatural cages to amplify their despair. All creatures other than the Misbegotten Troll or his smilodon pets within 30 feet must attempt a DC 18 Wisdom save or become overwhelmed with sadness, causing them to be stunned until the end of their next turn.
