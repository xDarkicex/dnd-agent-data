# Monstrous Water Weird

**description**: The monstrous water weird is a much larger species of the water weird, able to reach lengths of up to 40 feet. Powerful clerics and shamans summon these from the Elemental Plane of Water and bind them to very sacred places to act as guardians. Even if the original summoners have all died out and the area being protected has fallen into ruin, the monstrous water weird will remain as an eternal sentinel. Traits Invisible in Water. The water weird is invisible while fully immersed in water. Water Bound. The water weird dies if it leaves the water to which it is bound or if that water is destroyed. Actions Multiattack. The water weird makes two attacks: one to Constrict and one with its Tail. Constrict. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 15 ft., one creature. Hit: 19 (4d6 + 5) bludgeoning damage. If the target is Large or smaller, it is grappled (escape DC 15) and pulled 5 feet toward the water weird. Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained, the water weird tries to drown it, and the water weird can't use Constrict on another target. Tail. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 15 ft., one creature. Hit: 12 (2d6 + 5) bludgeoning damage.
