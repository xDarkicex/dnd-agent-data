# Bayal Dethirinn

**description**: Appearance and Personality: Bayal looks the part of the quintessential snow elf—long white hair, pale skin and hard, fierce eyes. He is a harsh person with no patience for delay, indecision, or mercy. He is a deliberate and cunning wildlander, but not very imaginative. He is fiercely loyal to Aradil, and in turn her Avatar Rhiann. When interacting with PCs, Bayal is initially reluctant to even speak to them. This is especially true for anyone who is not an elf. However, if they express an interest in the natural world or demonstrate some skill with it, he reluctantly opens up, sharing interests and expertise with them. Over the course of conversations, he confesses to having lost many close friends in past battles. He is hesitant to make new ones, in part to protect himself from potential losses. Traits Fey Ancestry. Bayal has advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can’t put him to sleep. Wraith of the North. Bayal can attempt to hide even when he is only lightly obscured by foliage, heavy rain, falling snow, mist, or other natural phenomena. Bonus Actions Bonus Action. Bayal may take the Disengage Action as a bonus action. Actions Tireless (3/Day). Bayal gives himself 1d8 + 3 temporary hit points. Multiattack. Bayal makes three attacks, two with his erunsil fighting knife and one with his staghorn. Erunsil Fighting Knife. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d4 + 4) piercing damage. Staghorn. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d4 + 4) piercing damage. Shortbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d6 + 4) piercing damage. Reactions Staghorn Parry. When a creature damages Bayal with a melee attack, he can use his reaction to reduce the damage by 1d8 + 4.
