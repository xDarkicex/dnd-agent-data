# Briar Army

**description**: Hundreds of children of the briar crouch here, swaying and clicking as one. Occasionally, one of them stretches up to catch a falling droplet in its mouth Actions Multiattack. A briar army makes two claw attacks. If both attacks hit the same target, the target is engulfed and the briar army uses its Thorny Embrace on it. Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: (6d6+3) piercing damage plus (3d6+3)piercing damage. Or 13 (3d6 + 3) piercing damage if the swarm has half its hit points or fewer. Entangle (1/day). The briar army can cast a version of the entangle spell, requiring no components. The affected area must be within 15 feet of the swarm. Creatures in the area must make a successful DC 13 Strength saving throw or be restrained. All briar creatures are immune to the effect. Thorny Embrace. An engulfed creature takes 24 (6d6 + 3) piercing damage or 13 (3d6 + 3) piercing damage if the swarm has half its hit points or fewer at the end of the creature’s turn for as long as it remains engulfed.
