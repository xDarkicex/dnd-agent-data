# Storm of Brain Devourers

**description**: Brain Devourer Brain devourers are among the most gruesome denizens of The Low—wrinkled, grey brains supported by legs formed from twisted nerves. Seeing one instantly strikes fear into seasoned veterans, a delicious sentiment which is promptly drained by the hungry abomination. A single brain devourer can syphon the emotions of an entire city until its inhabitants are mindless husks. Astral Transcendance. Brain devourers were once humanoids on a quest for cognitive transcendance. Gradual biomantic alterations ultimately caused them to shed all semblance of physical form, leaving only what they consider an intermediate step in humanoid evolution. Brain devourers believe the final step requires the consumption of a massive amount of brains, after which they will finally leave their bodies behind and ascend to the Astral Sea. Ravenous Hordes. When a city’s guards prove too tough to crack, brain devourers are driven to form ferocious swarms by their voracious hunger. In a group, they trade subtlety for sheer numbers, and the resulting condensation of psychic energy manifests as a massive brain storm. To avoid this, mind flayers carefully manage brain devourer populations by feeding them periodically.
