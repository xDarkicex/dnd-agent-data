# Menagerie Caretaker

**description**: Menagerie Caretakers are rangers who specialize in handling and tending for beasts and monstrosities, specializing as monster tamers. Their knowledge and everyday practices sometimes bring them into wilderness areas when beasts or monsters need to be subdued by skill or spells. Menagerie Caretakers are usually not the most social of people, preferring to spend time in the presence of beats or monsters, with whom they share a closer affinity. Equipment The menagerie caretaker has 4 copper pieces, 5 silver pieces, 19 gold pieces and a leash of the beast tamer on his person.
