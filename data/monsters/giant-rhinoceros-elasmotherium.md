# Giant Rhinoceros (Elasmotherium)

**description**: Traits Trampling Charge. If the giant rhinoceros moves at least 20 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a Gore attack on the same turn, that target must succeed on a DC 17 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. If the target is prone, the giant rhinoceros can make one Stomp attack against it as a bonus action. Actions Gore. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 20 (3d8 + 7) piercing damage. Stomp. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one prone creature. Hit: 16 (2d8 + 7) bludgeoning damage.
