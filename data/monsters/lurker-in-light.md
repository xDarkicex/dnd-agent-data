# Lurker in Light

**description**: These malicious and strange fey appear as two-foot-tall fairies with insectile wings, large eyes, and bulbous heads. They slaughter and cause mischief in a pattern only their alien intellect understands, but they’ve been seen to take particular umbrage against dwarves, gnomes, and creatures who live in darkness. With their grudges and pursuit of their own wicked and sadistic whims, lurkers in light deftly overturn the conventional wisdom that creatures associated with light are benign and friendly. Traits Blend with Light. When the lurker is in an area of bright light, it can turn invisible as a bonus action. Any equipment the lurker wears or carries is invisible with it. Innate Spellcasting. The lurker’s innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 14). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: dancing lights, light, mage hand, minor illusion 3/day each: blindness/deafness, conjure woodland beings, sacred flame (3d8) Ritual Gate (1/Day). If the lurker has reduced a creature to 0 hit points since the beginning of its previous turn, it can use conjure woodland beings as a bonus action. In addition, if the creature(s) it summons have the same alignment as the lurker, it does not need to concentrate to maintain the spell. If it casts conjure woodland beings again, the creatures summoned with the ritual gate disappear. Sneak Attack (1/Turn). The lurker deals an extra 10 (3d6) damage when it hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of the lurker that isn’t incapacitated and the lurker doesn’t have disadvantage on the attack roll. Actions Multiattack. The lurker makes two attacks with its claws or its motes of light in any combination. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 6 (1d4+4) slashing damage. Mote of Light. Ranged Spell Attack: +7 to hit, range 10/30 ft., one creature. Hit: 3 (1d4+1) force damage and 3 (1d4+1) radiant damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 14 Dexterity saving throw or be outlined in golden radiance for 1 minute (as faerie fire). If a creature fails another saving throw against this effect while already outlined, the duration is reset to 1 minute and the creature is blinded for 1 round. Daylight Door (Recharges after a Short Rest). The lurker magically teleports itself from an area of bright light, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying, to a location up to 120 feet away to an unoccupied space it can see that is also in bright light.
