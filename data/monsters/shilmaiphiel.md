# Shilmaiphiel

**description**: Shilmaiphiels are celestial guardians tasked with protecting sacred rivers, blessed streams, and even parts of the ocean. Shilmaiphiels appear as large, humanoid-like creatures with massive feathered wings, scaly armor, and snake-like hair cascading from their heads, giving them an aura of untamed power. Rather than engaging in combat with malicious intent, shilmaiphiels typically challenge those who venture near the holy waters they protect with tests of strength, wit, and courage, as well as riddles that probe the minds and hearts of those seeking passage.
