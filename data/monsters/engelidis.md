# Engelidis

**description**: The spirit naga Engelidis serves as a consort and advisor to King Irovetti, but is a dangerous foe on her own. Spirit nagas always make excellent behind-the-scenes antagonists due to their skill at mind control and magic; as such, these stats can serve perfectly for a monster that plays a “power-behind-the-throne” role. Traits Rejuvenation. If Engelidis dies, she returns to life in 1d6 days and regains all her hit points. Only a wish spell can prevent this trait from functioning. Spellcasting. Engelidis is an 15th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 18, +10 to hit with spell attacks), and she needs only verbal components to cast her spells. Engelidis has the following wizard spells prepared: Cantrips (at will): dancing lights, mage hand, message, minor illusion, ray of frost 1st level (4 slots): charm person, detect magic, sleep 2nd level (3 slots): alter self, invisibility 3rd level (3 slots): clairvoyance, dispel magic 4th level (3 slots): dimension door, ice storm, stoneskin 5th level (2 slots): dominate person 6th level (1 slot): chain lightning, mass suggestion 7th level (1 slot): delayed blast fireball 8th level (1 slot): power word stun Actions Multiattack. Engelidis makes two attacks with her bite. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d6+4) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 36 (8d8) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.
