# Kharonoi

**description**: The legendary Charon is famed as ferryman of the dead, carrying spirits across the Styx if they were buried or cremated with a coin in their mouth, leaving them to wander the banks forever if they don’t. However, Charon himself does not deign to pick up the dead from every world at all times, instead dispatching an armada of minions to cross the planar divide as psychopomps, leading the spirits of the dead to their final rest. These servants are the kharonoi, servants of the cult of Charon gifted with a kind of twisted immortality, though bound to serve as deathless ferrymen. They appear as ugly older men with a grim faces and crooked noses, often with a beard and conical hat. They await lost spirits to take payment for their crossing into the afterlife, and if confronted with violence they may try to knock their passengers into the eerie waters of the Styx or may simply abandon their skiffs and fly away leaving their companions adrift until the eddies of the mystic river bring them to the bank hours later. Those thus forsaken must find their own way back to the land of the living.
