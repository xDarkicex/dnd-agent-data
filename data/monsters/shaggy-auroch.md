# Shaggy Auroch

**description**: Shaggy Auroch These massive herbivores form large herds that roam almost all regions of Grimnir. Wise raiders know to steer clear of these mercurial beasts for they are easily triggered to stampede. When this happens, they become as chaotic and unstoppable as a landslide. Natural Weapons. The horns of an auroch bull can be several feet in length and the beast is capable of throwing a human over a dozen feet. Even individually the auroch is not to be trifled with as many a raider rancher has discovered. Vast Herds. Though they are hunted for their meat and warm hides, the auroch are plentiful in Grimnir. In some regions on the Inner Continent and in the High North, great herds, comprising hundreds of aurochs, travel together. When these creatures run, their footfalls fall such that it feels as if an earthquake shakes the world. Cooperative The shaggy auroch, along with several other new monsters introduced for Raiders of the Serpent Sea, has a special ability called Cooperative. Creatures with this feature can combine specific attacks to increase their severity. In the case of the aurochs, if the heroes encounter a herd and the herd Stampedes, heroes suffer additional damage when the auroch cooperate. This feature has been introduced, in part, to simplify running larger groups of creatures. Instead of making the GM roll separate saving throws, the rolls are combined into one, with increasing effects on failure. As an optional rule, if the GM is finding that the heroes are easily defeating cooperating enemies, they may increase the DC of any saving throws by +1 for each creature participating (up to a maximum of +5). Likewise, if the cooperative feature is well received, the GM may consider expanding it to other groups of enemies!
