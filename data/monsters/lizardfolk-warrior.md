# Lizardfolk Warrior

**description**: Lizardfolk The lizardfolk of the Stolen Lands tend to keep to themselves, but when a violent overlord seizes control they can be worked into a dangerous frenzy. Humanoid in shape, but with green or bluish scaled skin and frill-like adornments on their heads, lizardfolk are known as iruxi among their own people. Lizardfolk Warrior A lizardfolk enclave’s warriors are its defenders and protectors. They tend to react quickly to threats with violence of their own, and are more commonly encountered in lizardfolk villages where the leadership itself is more violent and cruel-minded. Traits Hold Breath. The lizardfolk warrior can hold its breath for 15 minutes. Terrain Adept. The warrior has advantage on attacks and contested ability checks against non-lizardfolk in difficult terrain, or against targets in water that lack a swim speed. Actions Multiattack. The lizardfolk warrior makes two melee attacks, each one with a different weapon. Jaws. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6+2) piercing damage. Longspear. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 10 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8+2) piercing damage. Javelin. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6+2) piercing damage. Spiked Shield. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6+2) piercing damage. Sudden Charge. The lizardfolk warrior dashes up to 60 feet toward an enemy, then makes a single melee attack.
