# Moura Encantada

**description**: Mouras are fey women as kindly as they are beautiful. Everyone who gazes upon a moura encantada tends to see them slightly differently according to their own inner image of beauty, though all find her to possess immense loveliness. They appear curiously ageless, with a youthful suppleness of skin and limb and yet eyes that speak of wisdom and experience and moving with a practiced grace and ease. Their hair is uniformly long, and they often disentangle it with princely combs of precious metal embellished with gemstones. Site Bound. They have the ability to transform into serpents and can be found near streams, springs, caves, and megalithic monuments, such as dolmens and burial mounds. Their souls are bound by a timeless enchantment to a specific location, and though no visible walls exist, they cannot stray far from this vicinity. For some, this binding also ties them to a specific creature, binding them as guardians of a creature or treasure. Bound mouras spend their days in a semi-lethargic state, with only one of two things on their minds: protecting the territory or escaping from their 'prison.' If the enchantment is broken, they return to the spirit-realm they call Mourama, and those who have paid the price will be rewarded. Breaking such an enchantment involves not battles, but rather demonstrations of love.
