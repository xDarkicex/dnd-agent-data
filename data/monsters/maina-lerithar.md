# Maina Lerithar

**description**: Since its rise to power, the Gullion family has taken many subservient families beneath it, some publicly, some in the shadows. The Lerithar family is one of the latter, providing their expertise in the subtle arts of poisons and assassination. Maina is a talented member of her family, but young. She’s a particularly capable trapsmith, and she isn’t happy with her mother assigning her to a group on the surface. Of course, she’s more than smart enough not to risk her own neck by complaining about it.
