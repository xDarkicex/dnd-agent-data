# Witchking

**description**: Witchking Mere mention of the Witchking can make a hardy raider hesitate. An immortal warrior known to shrug off the deadliest of attacks, the Witchking is believed unstoppable. The truth of the creature is worse. The Witchking is not immortal but the armor, Bloodbane, is. Crafted in the long ago by the Matron from the wood and leaves of the eldest ironheart tree, the armor (and the shield grafted onto the right gauntlet) is a living, hungering, beast. Any honor the man chosen to wear the armor might initially feel vanishes the moment he is pinned down, his flesh sewn with barbed needles into the inner lining of the armor itself. The last Witchking, Regulus, was murdered. His ego outmatched his usefulness to the Matron and she poisoned him, though his service to her did not end with his death (see Regulus). Roleplaying the Witchking Controlled by His Armor. Bloodbane is a sentient suit of +2 splint armor and a +2 shield, fused together (Intelligence 11, Wisdom 12, Charisma 22). It has hearing and normal vision out to a range of 120 feet. It communicates empathetically by transmitting emotions to its wearer. It can only be worn by someone that the Matron selects. Hungry. Bloodbane forces the Witchking to desire nothing more than to bathe in the warm blood of mortals. Dull. The Witchking says little, beyond simple commands to witches and ironthralls. It is more or less a vessel undertaking Boða's wishes. Saga Actions Warding of Roots. When reduced to zero hit points, the Witchking becomes covered in thick roots erupting from Bloodbane. These completely cover the Witchking, and grant him immunity to all damage types. At the start of their turn, while warded, the Witchking regains 100 hit points. The warding may be attacked and destroyed (AC 15, 125 hit points). While warded in this way, the Witchking can take no action other than using a bonus action to dismiss this warding. This ability can only be used once per day. If the Witchking is reduced to 0 hit points again, before this ability is refreshed, they die.
