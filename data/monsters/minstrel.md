# Minstrel

**description**: A minstrel is often dressed in vibrant fashions, carrying their preferred instrument of choice everywhere they go. Many great artists and musicians have found the powers to weave their creative minds to channel arcane energies. The minstrel uses their cunning, guile, and charm to outwit and enthrall their enemies, or bolster their allies. “A minstrel! You want me to fight a person tootling on a flute? That seems highly unfair for them.”
