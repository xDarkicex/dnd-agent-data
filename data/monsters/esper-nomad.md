# Esper Nomad

**description**: Esper nomads live and dwell on the fringes of society. Many of them are reclusive by choice, either reluctant to play a part in modern day society or unwilling to come to terms with their role or place in the galaxy. Some can also be on the run from authories or are too far displaced from modern society to relate to others of their kind.
