# Tarantasio

**description**: Related to linnorms according to some scholars, or to sea serpents according to others, the tarantasio haunts peaceful lakes in the woods. People deem it responsible for persistent fog banks, epidemics, and rotting plants in the whole region around its den. They spend most of their time underwater, but when prey approaches their huge, horned head emerges from the lake’s troubled waters on top of a long, snakelike body, with flames and noxious fumes erupting from its mouth and flared nostrils.
