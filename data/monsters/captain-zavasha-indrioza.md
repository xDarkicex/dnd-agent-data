# Captain Zavasha Indrioza

**description**: Captain Zavasha Indrioza, a cunning werestingray pirate captain, was born on a pirate ship as the daughter of her werestingray pirate captain father, whose life was brutally ended by molten silver. Seeking vengeance, she carefully assembled her crew, offering them lycanthropy in return for undying loyalty. With her wits and merciless marauder approach, Captain Zavasha and her crew, known as the Dread Stingers, became the most feared pirates of the Calhay Sea, striking fear into the hearts of sailors and coastal settlements as they pillaged and plundered, leaving chaos and destruction in their wake. Equipment Captain Zavasha Indrioza’s shortsword is a +2 dancing sword . She also wears a ring of protection and an amulet of health . She has a bottle of hurricane in a holster on her belt.
