# Nemean

**description**: The nemean is a massive lion of tremendous strength and ferocity. Though their tawny fur and flowing mane looks the same as any lion’s, it is incredibly tough and almost impossible to pierce with mortal blades. It is said the first nemean created (or cursed) by the gods had skin so impervious to blades that nothing but its own claws could cut it. The nemeans still existing in the world are not quite so invulnerable, but even so only weapons forged from truly legendary material and blessed with enchantment have any hope of shedding even a drop of its blood. Unlike ordinary lions, they tend to be solitary, only rarely seeking the company of other felines. If they encounter an existing pride, they swiftly dispatch all rivals and dominate the survivors until they tire of their company and move on. They are loathe to let others hunt for them, preferring the thrill of the chase and the glory of the kill with their own jaws.
