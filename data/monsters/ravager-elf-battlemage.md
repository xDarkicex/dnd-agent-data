# Ravager Elf Battlemage

**description**: Feared throughout Ashandri, the Ravager Elf Battlemages are powerful spellcasters that also received battle training. Groups of these spellcasters laid waste to entire settlements during the first invasions. Nowadays, they usually appear alone or accompanied by a group of Ravager Elf soldiers under their command. The Ravager Elf Battlemage carries a Wand of the War Mage +1. While holding this wand, they gain a +1 bonus to spell attack rolls (already included in calculations) . In addition, they ignore half cover when making a spell attack.
