# Master of the Ebon Serpent

**description**: A trio of half-black dragon halflings—Lords Culfix, Morgix, and Brolix Astrillax—the sons of Lady Sentrix “the Coil” Astrillax bear the title of Master of the Ebon Serpent. Embracing their draconic heritage, they combine the disciplines of monkhood with the dark power of warlocks, following the teachings of the Ebon Serpent, the heralded bringer of the end of times. Wielding a deadly blend of spells, martial arts, and their own draconic prowess, these enigmatic individuals wreak havoc and bring desctruction wherever they tread, their actions moving the world closer to its prophesied end.
