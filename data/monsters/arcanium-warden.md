# Arcanium Warden

**description**: A remnant of a long-lost civilization reaching far across the multiverse, wardens are humanoid-like constructs that serve a singular, unchanging command or goal. Ancient Guardians Several types of wardens exist, though each share the fate of being bound by a singular, unchanging command. Wardens are often stationed as guardians at sealed crypts, forgotten temples, or other sites where powerful artifacts or forbidden knowledge is kept. Some wardens, however, follow less tangible directives and roam relatively freely, guided only by their goal; whether that involves upholding a specific code or ideal – or preventing a calamity through any necessary means. Arcanium Warden Arcanium wardens are constructed from arcanium, an alloy famed for its magnetic properties. Aside from being physically imposing, they're capable of manipulating magnetic fields – using it both as a protective measure and a deadly weapon. They are typically found in places of science and invention. Ember Warden Forged from heat-resistant materials, ember wardens excel in controlling thermal energy and are capable of superheating their surroundings. They typically serve as guardians of volcanic regions or ancient forges where their resistance to heat is most crucial. Unlike other wardens with flexible directives, almost all ember wardens are strictly bound to protect their designated sites – and do so with unyielding fervor. Weave Warden Weave wardens are specially crafted to interact with the fabric of magic. While often tasked with protecting magical artifacts or sensitive knowledge, some weave wardens are appointed as custodians of the weave of magic itself, embarking on quests to thwart malevolent rituals and preserve the balance of magical forces. These wardens are particularly well-equipped to deal with those who wield magic – while they can't cast spells on their own, they can see magical auras and absorb the residual energy from spells cast by others, turning them into formidable foes against any who wield magic with ill intent.
