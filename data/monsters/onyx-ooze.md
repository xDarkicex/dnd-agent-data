# Onyx Ooze

**description**: Black puddings are the scavengers of the Underworld, constantly on the lookout for a meal. They can sense organic or metallic objects within 60 feet and mindlessly attack such items or beings until they are dissolved or the ooze is killed. A black pudding reproduces by breaking off a piece of its body and forming a new, smaller black pudding, which grows to full size in 1 month’s time. Some of the more intelligent creatures in the underworld use black puddings as natural garbage disposals, creating stone pits to house the pudding and throwing organic refuse or foes in as needed. When a black pudding fails to split as it should, it grows in size. Before long, it evolves into something else entirely. As ravenous and corrosive as they ever were, an onyx ooze can grow to fill entire dungeon complexes. Catabolist Form (Optional). When an onyx ooze takes damage that reduces it to half its maximum hit points, and again when reduced to one-quarter its maximum hit points, it spawns a black pudding into a nearby space.
