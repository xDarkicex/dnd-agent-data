# Ooze, Suppurating

**description**: A sentient pool of blood and protoplasm seeps forward, the thin membrane encasing its form rippling with undulating pustules. Unnatural Antibodies. Suppurating oozes lurk in horrible, disease-ridden places. These semi-transparent accretions of pus and coagulant are borne from the liquefied remains of victims of a disease called the Seeping Death. Morbid Liquefaction. The contagion spread by the suppurating ooze is a radically advanced form of liquefactive necrosis and helps the ooze slowly digest its fleshy prey.
