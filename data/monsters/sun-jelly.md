# Sun Jelly

**description**: Sun Jelly Sun jellies resemble their marine cousins in many ways, except they glow and swim through air instead of water. Their long tendrils of light trail lazily across the sky, void of a jellyfish’s typical sting. Benign and mindless, sun jellies will not harm other creatures unless truly agitated, and they are drawn to acts of kindness, self-sacrifice, and expressions of genuine vulnerability. A swarm gathered around a creature implies goodness and purity of heart, and the ability to attract such a bloom is a prerequisite for a Shai’tan squire to take their paladin oath.
