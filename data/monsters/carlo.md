# Carlo

**description**: A small guy with a nondescript face who will follow the group. He has formerly embarked on a career as a pickpocket and is indebted to Savonarola who spared him the loss of a hand when he was caught stealing a purse. Traits Second-Story Work. Climbing no longer costs extra movement. Cunning Action. On each of his turns, Carlo can use a bonus action to take the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action. Sneak Attack. Once per turn, Carlo deals an extra 7 (2d6) damage when he hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of Carlo’s that isn’t incapacitated and Carlo doesn’t have disadvantage on the attack roll. Actions Lucky Dagger of Stealing. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage. The dagger adds +2 to Sleight of Hand (already included in the stats above).
