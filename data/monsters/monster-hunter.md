# Monster Hunter

**description**: Nomad The Tiger Lords are the most notorious roving warriors to reside in the Stolen Lands, but other gatherings of nomadic people dwell throughout the region, particularly those hunters who prefer to make the most dangerous monsters of the wilds their prey. Monster Hunter Be they hunters who have traveled far from their home to chase down legendary prey, or members of a small gathering like the Six Bears, monster-hunting warriors find no shortage of targets to track and hunt in the Stolen Lands. Monster hunters might also resent wandering adventurers, who might kill a targeted monster before the hunters have a chance. Traits Ferocious Charge. If the monster hunter moves at least 10 feet straight toward a target and then hits it with a melee attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 9 (2d8) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be pushed up to 10 feet away and knocked prone. Rage. The monster hunter can enter rage as a bonus action. While in rage, the hunter has advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws, they deal an additional 2 damage with each attack, and they gain resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage. The rage lasts for 1 minute or until the hunter falls unconscious, or if their turn ends and they haven’t either attacked a hostile creature or taken damage since its last turn. Relentless Rage. The monster hunter’s rage can keep them fighting despite grievous wounds. If the hunter drops to 0 hit points while they are raging and doesn’t die outright, they can make a DC 10 Constitution saving throw. If they succeed, they drop to 1 hit point instead. Each time the monster hunter uses this feature after the first, the DC increases by 5. When they finish a short or long rest, the DC resets to 10. Actions Multiattack. The monster hunter makes two attacks. Greataxe. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (1d12+4) slashing damage. Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8+3) piercing damage.
