# Swarm Of Knights Of Solemn Vows

**description**: The Knights are headquartered in the metropolis of Wateredge, in the basement of the Church of Trois Ocularis. Their meetings are notably silent. One elected speaker raises a motion, and then each Knight votes for or against the matter by touching a black or white stone. In the event of a tie, the speaker casts their vote by touching one side of a third, hybrid stone of both black and white. TRAITS Swarm: The swarm can occupy another creature’s space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Medium creature. The swarm can regain hit points only if it uses its Lay on Hands action on itself. ACTIONS Multiattack: The swarm makes two greatswords attacks, or one attack if it has half of its hit points or fewer. Greatswords. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 33 (8d6 + 5) slashing damage, or 19 (4d6 + 5) slashing damage if the swarm has half of its hit points or fewer. Lay on Hands (1/Day): The swarm regains 60 hit points, or 30 hit points if it has half of its hit points or fewer.
