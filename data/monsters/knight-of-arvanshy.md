# Knight of A’rvanshy

**description**: The valiant knights of A'rvanshy, loyal followers of Dorenthil Balendaal, are a dedicated brotherhood devoted to their people's cause. Despite the despair that lingers over their captive ancestral city, these knights rise each day with unwavering determination, honing their skills through rigorous training. Their steadfast commitment to service drives them to work tirelessly, riding giant seahorses as the defenders and beacons of hope for their fellow deep-sea elves held hostage by Morgawr's vile grip.
