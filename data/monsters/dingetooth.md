# Dingetooth

**description**: Dingetooth and Uurch These two mitflits are more accomplished than most of their kind, although their choice to focus their efforts on a marble launcher might not be the most efficient use of their training. You can use these statistics for any mitflit gremlin that’s managed the luck and skill to live long enough to hone their combat prowess—whether or not you let them keep their marble launcher. Of course, switching the type of damage the marble launcher deals when it hits a target also lets you change the launcher into a glass slinger (slashing damage), a coal flinger (fire damage), or a poison toad–launcher (poison damage). Traits Hatred. Dingetooth and Uurch double their proficiency bonus on attack rolls against humanoid creatures of the dwarf or gnome subtype due to special training against these hated foes. Innate Spellcasting. The gremlin’s innate spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 11). The gremlin can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: prestidigitation 1/day: bane Keen Smell. The mitflit has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Self-Loathing. The mitflit’s self-loathing makes them easy to influence. They have disadvantage on saving throws and ability checks to resist any Charisma-based skills or being charmed. Vermin Keeper. Through sounds and gestures, the mitflit can communicate simple ideas with arthropods. The knowledge and awareness of arthropods is limited by their intelligence, but at minimum, they can give information about nearby locations and monsters, including whatever they can perceive or have perceived within the past day. The mitflit can ask for small favors from arthropods and has advantage on Wisdom (Animal Handling) checks when communicating with them. Actions Shortsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6+3) piercing damage. Marble Launcher (1/Day). Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, range 30 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d6) bludgeoning damage, and the target must succeed at a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw or become prone. Reactions Vengeful. When the mitflit is hit by an attack or fails a saving throw, the mitflit makes an attack against the creature that harmed it.
