# Swarm of Embers

**description**: Elemental Motes Masterful users of the Weave can use their magic to rip apart the barriers between worlds, summoning primordial avatars of air, earth, fire, and water from the chaotic depths of the Elemental Planes. When this fails miserably, the magic can produce an elemental mote instead, a tiny proto-elemental nearly indistinguishable from an inanimate manifestation of its element. Like their more powerful brethren, elemental motes have little desire but to return to their native plane and reunite with its natural energy. A Swarm Reshaped. As a swarm, elemental motes combine their individual specks of consciousness into a raging, primaeval mind. What they lack in physical ability, they make up for in combined desperation to abandon the Material Plane. Using their numbers to their advantage, they envelop any who dare stand in their way in a miniature elemental storm. Embers A swarm of embers is the flecks of light dancing around an invisible fire. Embers lack the searing heat of fire elementals, but are nevertheless equally capricious, blinding creatures with well-placed bursts of tiny cinders. They are the only elemental motes that regularly choose to follow mortals, particularly those with strong connections to fire. Embers are also sworn enemies of droplets and sparks, as both those elements can briefly extinguish their flames.
