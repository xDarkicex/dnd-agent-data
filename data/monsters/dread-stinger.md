# Dread Stinger

**description**: The Dread Stinger is a veteran in the pirate organization by the same name, serving on one of the group's ships. Captives who show loyalty and are deemed worthy of becoming members of the Dread Stingers are bestowed with werestingray lycanthropy rather than being sold as slaves or eaten. All Dread Stingers show admiration for their leader, Zavasha Indrioza, who leads the fleet of ships from the Rusty Nail with her silver parrot named Neb. Dread Stingers are cutthroats and backstabbers toward those not in their crew, ruthless and slippery by nature.
