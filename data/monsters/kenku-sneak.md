# Kenku Sneak

**description**: The kenku sneaks form the majority of kenku gangs. They are expert con artists, lockpicks, pickpockets, and thieves. They know all the streets and structures where they operate like the back of their hand, and can almost always break into any building without getting detected. Traits Ambusher. The kenku has advantage on attack rolls against any creature it has surprised. Mimicry. The kenku can mimic any sounds it has heard, including voices. A creature that hears the sounds can tell they are imitations with a successful DC 14 Wisdom (Insight) check. Sneak Attack (1/Turn). The kenku deals an extra 3 (1d6) damage when it hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of the kenku that isn't incapacitated and the kenku doesn't have disadvantage on the attack roll. Actions Shortsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage. Shortbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage. Bonus Actions Cunning Action. The kenku performs the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action.
