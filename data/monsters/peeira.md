# Peeira

**description**: A peeira is a fey who is bound to a particular pack of wolves, serving as its guardian and healer. As such, she is the bane of hunters who trap or kill wolves for their furs. With an otherworldly beauty, she stands with an imperious attitude surrounded by her pack of wolves. While fierce and wild, however, the peeira is generally good-natured, steering her pack clear of lost travelers or children and eager to keep them from being man-eaters. While a peeira is not actively hostile towards outsiders, it is very protective of its pack and the territory in which they hunt. Those who hunt her pack or despoil their hunting grounds gain the fey’s ire, and she will not rest until such interlopers are dead or driven from the area. Werewolf Redeemers. The presence of a peeira can have a calming effect on werewolves, suppressing their bloodlust and wicked nature. Over time, if a werewolf chooses to run with theh peeira’s pack, she can teach the werewolf to control its bestial urges and turn from its murderous ways.
