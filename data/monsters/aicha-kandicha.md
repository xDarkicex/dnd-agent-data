# Aicha Kandicha

**description**: Aicha Kandicha This strange, beautiful woman wears a long skirt, and the sound of clacking accompanies her otherwise graceful steps. An Aicha kandicha is a mysterious woman who lurks by night. She covers her legs with broad skirts because she has hooves like a cow rather than human feet. She otherwise looks beautiful and flirts with men and seduces them but then turns into a demon-cow and devours them claiming their souls for one of the lords of the lower planes.
