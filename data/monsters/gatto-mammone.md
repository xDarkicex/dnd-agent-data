# Gatto Mammone

**description**: Gatto Mammone These mysterious and magical beings resemble ordinary housecats, except for their titanic proportions. They are quite intelligent, with a deep, purring voice echoing softly through the shadows even while the beast advances soundlessly despite its great bulk on its powerful paws. They are quite rare, often believed to be unique by those who have encountered them and who venerate them as the “king of cats.” Their fur pattern can vary, but the most common is a tabby pattern or an all-black coat with a white M-shaped marking on its muzzle or brow. While a gatto mammone ordinarily moves about on all fours, it is able to stand erect in a bipedal stance when it wishes and sometimes do so to unnerve humanoid prey while conversing with them. Gatto mammones are lovers of pleasure and finery and some consider them possessed of a spirit of greed, or able to inspire avarice in others. While they owe no allegiance to the lower planes, they are sometimes known to consort with demons and devils in pursuit of some angle or scheme.
