# Swarm of Mind Maggots

**description**: Mind maggots can sometimes be found in fetid swamps, but a swarm of them is never found in the wild. Swarms of these nasty little peach-colored worms have been bred and trained by a hags or other creatures capable of great evil. Once trained, these swarms require a Medium sized victim or larger every week or the swarm begins to eat itself.
