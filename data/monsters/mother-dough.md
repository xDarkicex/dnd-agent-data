# Mother Dough

**description**: Fermenting for over a century, the mother dough is a sticky, pungent ooze that is used as a component of most of the Bimbleford Bakery 's breads, buns, and baked goods. Typically this bubbling beige mass is kept contained in the bakery's cellar and is fed daily to ensure its compliance. That said, the Mother Dough isn't very picky about what – or who – it consumes, and will happily devour (and then digest) would-be brigands who try to harm Dalfin or Caradoc.
