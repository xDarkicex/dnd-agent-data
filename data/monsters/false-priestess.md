# False Priestess

**description**: Cultist Just as bandits are drawn to the Stolen Lands, all manner of cultists find this region ripe for hideouts and hidden shrines to deities and demigods whose worship has been outlawed in most cities and towns. Some of these cultists are solitary members of groups with influence throughout the world, while others, such as the Cultists of the Bloom, are entirely regional threats. Note that these cultists, along with the more powerful specialized cultists presented later in this book under the entry for Thresholders, can be used together as different agents of a sprawling sect of sinister worshippers in your game simply by changing their descriptions to match whatever nefarious deity or evil faith you’ve decided they might serve. False Priestess False priestesses are charlatans who claim to be devout members of a benign faith, but harbor sinister plans of their own. These in particular claim to be worshippers of Shelyn, but in truth serve a cruel hag mistress. Trait Distant Spell (2/day). When the false priestess casts a spell with a range of 5 feet or more, she can double the range of the spell. When the false priestess casts a spell with a range of touch, she can increase the range to 30 feet. Primal Weapon. The false priestess’s melee weapon attacks deal an extra 5 (1d10) damage on a successful hit. This extra damage is already factored into the false priestess’s attacks. Spellcasting. The false priestess is an 8th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks). She knows the following sorcerer spells: Cantrips (at will): fire bolt, minor illusion, poison spray, prestidigitation, shocking grasp 1st level (4 slots): charm person, false life, mage armor 2nd level (3 slots): blindness/deafness, enhance ability, misty step 3rd level (3 slots): lightning bolt, slow 4th level (2 slots): blight Actions Multiattack. The false priestess makes two attacks with her glaive. Glaive. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d10+2) slashing damage.
