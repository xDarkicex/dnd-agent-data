# Automaton Angel

**description**: There is a rumor about an automaton being built somewhere near Cyherestead. These rumors say that this gigantic robot is shaped like the emissaries of the gods, is as tall as a mountain, has cutting-edge angst reactors built inside its reinforced metal frame, and can purify freshly drawn blood to use as fuel. Most people believe that the rumors were started by the sorcerers as a deterrent against Kanrath. At least one such automaton exists, hidden in a big hangar located in the Garrote Mountains. This knowledge is highly classified, even among Cyherestead sorcerers, and no one is sure if there is more than one of these giant machines.
