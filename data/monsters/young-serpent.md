# Young Serpent

**description**: Sea Monsters The Serpent Sea, as well as the many rivers and lakes of Grimnir, are host to a wide variety of fish and aquatic mammals, such as seals and whales. The creatures detailed here are, for the most part, the most dangerous of sea borne predators, save for the great serpent herself. Young Serpent These more fully grown serpents are not as commonly encountered as serpent spawn but far more dangerous to longships. They are more than capable of destroying smaller vessels on their own but often hunt in small groups, making such encounters all the more fatal.
