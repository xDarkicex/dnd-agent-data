# Rakaryx

**description**: Rakaryx are sinister predators that thrive in barren wastelands and rocky mountains. They have a lean, muscular build and appear almost like a large humanoid – were it not for their eerie lack of eyes and the tendrils that flow back from their head. Nocturnal Bloodseekers Rakaryx hunt primarily at night, cloaked by darkness and using their uncanny ability to detect the scent of blood to guide them toward prey. Unleashing a bloodcurdling scream as they engage in combat, rakaryx' grasp their prey with their hair-like tendrils while cutting it with their razor-sharp claws and using their bite to inflict a vicious disease that prevents wounds from healing. Omens of Misfortune Perhaps not unsurprisingly, rakaryx are considered ill omens in local folklore – their ghostly appearances in the twilight, their lack of eyes, and their ability to silently appear wherever blood is spilled contributing equally to their mythos. A stable in the cautionary tales parents tell their children, rakaryx are portrayed as phantoms of the wasteland; once divine beings that were twisted for their transgressions and turned into mindless predators.
