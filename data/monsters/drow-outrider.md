# Drow Outrider

**description**: Outriders—or outrunners if they go unmounted—are fleet spies that lurk on the outskirts of settlements in order gauge their strengths and weaknesses. Part spy, part wilderness survivalist, an outrider is the vanguard of drow raiding and expansion. If a well-known hunter suddenly leaves town, or a guard goes missing, it may well be that dark elf outriders are in the vicinity.
