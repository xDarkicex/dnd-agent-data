# Eirinn Alluon

**description**: Appearance and Personality: Eirinn is gaunt, even for an elf, and his mood is as light as his hair and skin are dark. Though not talkative and rarely showing a smile, he seems to always be at peace. His voice is low and kind, and his movements are graceful and deliberate. Eirinn is observant and quiet, but a demon in battle, where his highest priority is protecting Rhiann. Eirinn has undertaken this mission solely for the purpose of protecting Rhiann. He considers her family, even though she remains distant from him. If anyone brings up that point in conversation, he reluctantly admits that they were close before she became an avatar. Since that time, his interest in her remains unrequited. In spite of this, he continues to protect her, in the hope that someday Rhiann might discharge her responsibilities to the queen, after the war has ended. Until that time, he wishes to protect her from all possible harm. Traits Fey Ancestry. Eirinn has advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can’t put him to sleep. Bonus Actions Stalwart Defender. When Eirinn is within 5 feet of Rhiann on his turn, he can use his bonus action to switch places with Rhiann, provided he spends at least 5 feet of movement and Rhiann is willing and isn’t incapacitated. This movement doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks. Until the start of Eirinn’s next turn, all melee weapon attack rolls against Rhiann have disadvantage. Actions Multiattack. Erinn makes two attacks with his longsword. Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target. Hit: 8 (1d8 + 3) slashing damage. Innate Spellcasting. Eirinn’s casts the following spell using Charisma as his spellcasting ability (spell save DC 11): At will: blade ward (self only) Reactions Loyal Bodyguard. When Rhiann is hit by an attack roll while she is within 5 feet of Eirinn, he causes himself to be hit instead.
