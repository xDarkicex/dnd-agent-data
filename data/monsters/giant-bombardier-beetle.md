# Giant Bombardier Beetle

**description**: The giant bombardier beetle is among the most surprising creatures lurking on the forest floor. A placid herbivore content to go about its business, the beetle has a powerful defense mechanism in the form of a boiling liquid it can spray to scald would-be predators as it makes its escape. Action Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: (1d6+2) slashing damage. Spray. Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 15/30 ft., one target. Hit: (2d4+2) fire damage.
