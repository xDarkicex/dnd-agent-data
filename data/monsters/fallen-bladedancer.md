# Fallen Bladedancer

**description**: Traits Precise Strike. Once during its turn, when the bladedancer hits with an attack against a creature that is Injured, is within 5 feet of one of the bladedancer's allies, or against which it has advantage, that attack deals an additional 14 (4d6) damage. Evasion. Whenever the bladedancer succeeds on a Dexterity saving throw and would take half damage from an effect, it instead takes no damage. Join the Fallen. Whenever the blademaster reduces a creature to 0 hit points, inflicts an Injury, or scores a critical hit, all creatures within 120 feet that can hear or see the target of its attack must succeed on a DC 8 Wisdom saving throw or gain a Mindscar. A creature who gains three Mindscars begins to lose its mind. During its next long rest, it will begin to scar its flesh with whatever tools are available to it. Once its rest is completed, it becomes one of the Fallen. Mindscars can be removed by any process that can remove a curse, although mental trauma will still remain. If the mental trauma is overcome, the Mindscars are removed as well. Relish the Pain. Whenever the blademaster suffers an Injury, they gain a +1 bonus to damage rolls until the Injury is removed (up to their proficiency bonus), and gain temporary hit points equal to ½ the threshold of the Injury gained. The blademaster never has damage loss regardless of its hit point tier for Injury thresholds, and always suffers an Injury when it takes enough damage to become Injured (the Injury it gains is chosen by the damage dealer). Actions Multiattack. The bladedancer makes two melee or ranged attacks with its Serrated Knives. Serrated Knives. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, one target, reach 5 feet or range 10/20. Hit: 7 (1d6+4) slashing damage, and the target must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or have its Injury thresholds reduced by 5 until the start of its next turn or until it gains an Injury, whichever comes first. Reactions Devastating Counter. When an attack misses the bladedancer, they can make a Serrated Knives attack against its attacker. If that attack hits, it deals an additional 14 (4d6) damage.
