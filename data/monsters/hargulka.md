# Hargulka

**description**: The troll warlord Hargulka poses an early threat to the PCs’ kingdom and might be the first opposing ruler they’ll face during the Kingmaker Campaign. These stats can be used to flesh out the ranks of any larger society of trolls in your game, or perhaps to create a specialized gladiator in a city’s largest arena. Traits Brave. Hargulka has advantage on saving throws against being frightened. Keen Smell. Hargulka has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Regeneration. Hargulka regains 10 hit points at the start of its turn. If he takes acid or fire damage, this trait doesn’t function at the start of Hargulka’s next turn. Hargulka dies only if he starts his turn with 0 hit points and doesn’t regenerate. Actions Multiattack. Hargulka makes three melee attacks, two with his morningstar and one with his bite, or two with his claws and one with his bite. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d6+6) piercing damage. Morningstar. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d8+6) piercing damage. Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d6+6) slashing damage.
