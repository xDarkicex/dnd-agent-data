# Scythe Tree

**description**: Malevolent, vicious, and cruel, scythe trees pose as normal trees near deep forest paths, where they wait to slaughter passersby. Instead of drawing sustenance from light and soil, scythe trees gorge themselves on the flesh, blood, and bone of sapient creatures. Scythe trees are solitary creatures by nature, and their size and power mean that they don’t need to compete for food with other carnivorous plants. They appear as dead or dying deciduous trees with long, twisted branches that end in scythe-like blades. Their disturbing mouths remain closed when not eating, resembling little more than jagged gashes on their trunks. Traits Dead Tree. While the scythe tree remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from a normal dead tree. Lunging Slice (1/Round). As a bonus action, the scythe tree can extend the reach of its next scythe branch attack to 20 feet. This attack scores a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20. Actions Multiattack. The scythe tree makes three scythe branch attacks. Scythe Branch. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d6+5) slashing damage.
