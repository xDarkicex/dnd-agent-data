# Krestauros

**description**: These magnificent bulls are paragons of the bovine form, sleek and perfect in fetlock and flank, with hides that glisten in perfect silver-gray, snow-white, or burnished gold. These sacred animals were created by the gods as pleasing and perfect sacrifices, but their majestic appearance was so fine that the long ago kings for whom they were created kept back the krestauros for their own and gave back inferior beasts in sacrifice to the gods. In vengeance, the gods laid curses upon these feckless royals, bringing terror upon their houses with strange passions, monstrous births, and bouts of destruction across the land. Some of the krestauros were captured or slain by mighty heroes, but some still roam the land and tend to bring doom in their wake all unknowing. The krestauros is not malicious and can sometimes be lured away from an area to remove its doom-bringing presence elsewhere, but striking down these blessed beasts may bring its own divine wrath upon their slayers.
