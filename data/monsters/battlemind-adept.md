# Battlemind Adept

**description**: Battleminds are psions trained to use their abilities to defeat their opponents in direct conflict. They can be as formidable to other psions as they can toward mundane beings. Their versatility allows them to adapt to various roles such as commonwealth ambassadors, tactical aides, and corporate enforcers.
