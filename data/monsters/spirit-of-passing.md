# Spirit of Passing

**description**: Shamanic ghosts are the assimilated remains of ancient wisdom kept alive by persistent ancestor worship among primitive cultures. Their devotion keeps alive these ancient spirits, which linger in the sacred spaces of their tribe to offer prophecy and counsel to those they deem worthy, or wrath and judgment to those who profane their shrines. Each shamanic ghost typically represents an ethos or a tribal principle, and while each may embody the appearance of a specific tribal elder of ancientry, their strength represents the pooled mystic energies of untold generations of priestly power. Shamanic ghosts appear as translucent spirit-forms hovering decorated in mysterious body markings and primitive tribal garb, with a mien suggesting great age and wisdom. Withered Wisdom. A shamanic ghost’s spectral form often manifests as an older man, slender and bald, and wearing only a loincloth, or an older woman dressed in hides or furs. Hundreds of scars and brands of cryptic, mystic symbols cover every inch of their ghostly, incorporeal flesh. With their petitioners, shamanic ghosts share holy whispers, spirit sight, and the ability to commune with the dead—provided those that petition them prove themselves worthy and do not fear passing over into the ghost realms of the ancestors. Blood Offerings. To grant their blessings and knowledge, a shamanic ghost often requests blood offerings in the form of ritualized scarification in which the petitioner inflicts at least 10 hp of damage upon himself and spills that blood into the scrying pool. Performing the ritual placates the spirit, with those failing to make the proper offerings quickly condemned to suffer the spirit’s violent wrath. Lair Actions On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), a shamanic ghost takes a lair action to cause one of the following effects; the shamanic ghost can’t use the same effect two rounds in a row. The cave where it resides trembles violently for a moment. Each creature touching the ground must succeed on a DC 19 Dexterity saving throw or be knocked prone. The shamanic ghost targets one creature it can see within 60 feet of it. A silence spell fills the space of the target, moving with it until initiative count 20 on the next round. The shamanic ghost is immune to this effect. The shamanic ghost targets any number of creatures it can see within 30 feet of it. The target ages to the next age category for its species (for example, a young human becomes an adult, an adult becomes middle aged and so on).
