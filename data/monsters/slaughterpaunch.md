# Slaughterpaunch

**description**: Slaughterpaunch, the corpulent nalfeshnee demon, relishes in tormenting mortals with a sadistic pleasure, manipulating its victims and toying with them. With a bloated, rotund frame and wicked tusks, this vile creature is no ordinary demon: it is a vampire, turned by Faeryl Myrryn, a drow vampire arch-conjurer who discovered a dark ritual to afflict even outerplanar beings with vampirism. Slaughterpaunch’s mastery of dark magic amplifies pain and suffering, while its disfigured wings grant it swift aerial assaults.
