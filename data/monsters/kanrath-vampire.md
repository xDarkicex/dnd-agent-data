# Kanrath Vampire

**description**: Kanrath vampires are some of the most proud citizens of Tolia. Although there is a huge power difference between the Founder and these vampires, being a descendant of the Founder is enough for them to feel pride. Living in the haven the Founder built, now under the rule of the Crimson King, the Kanrath vampire has many options to serve Kanrath. They can work in ministries as officers, can do trade, run personal projects, or find a life of comfort, recreation, and nutrition. Although the Kanrath vampire can taste and have reflections in the mirrors unlike regular vampires, they cannot give birth to children like living humanoids. They also don’t have designated resting places. They lead free lives thanks to their powers and unique undead nature. Afterlife Gifts. When a new Kanrath vampire is created by another Kanrath vampire, the latter gives the former a weapon, an armor, or a shield that is special to the vampires of Kanrath. Called the Kanrath armory, it is a tradition for a Kanrath vampire to carry the weapons and armors in this armory as a sign of power and pride. Noble Vampires of Kanrath. When a Kanrath vampire serves the city well and its services are noticed by the Royal Family, the Crimson King elevates the position of the vampire in society by offering them a chance to drink a blood of power. A vampire who drinks the blood of power is declared a “noble vampire” and gains an additional vampiric power that is also passed down to future generations in its family. Although each noble family has unique powers granted to them, you can also determine these powers randomly by rolling on the table below. d10 Vampiric Power 1 There is a birthmark around the vampire’s wrists. Its melee attacks deal an extra 1d6 blood damage on a hit. 2 The vampire has a crimson, crown-like mark around its head. The DC of the Charm feature increases by 2. 3 The vampire can cast the false life spell without expending a spell slot, thrice per day. 4 The vampire has a birthmark on its hands. It gains the shocking grasp cantrip, but it deals blood damage instead. Charisma is the ability to hit with this spell. 5 The vampire’s fangs are longer and wider than they should be. Its bite attacks deal 1d10 piercing damage instead of 1d6. 6 The vampire can use the Legendary Resistance trait one more time per day. 7 Ashes and shadows are the eternal friends of the vampire and follow the vampire wherever it goes. The vampire has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks, and automatically becomes invisible in a magical way while in dim light. 8 The vampire regains 10 hit points instead of 5 with the Regeneration trait. 9 One of the ability scores of its choice becomes 20. 10 The vampire gains immunity to poison damage and being poisoned.
