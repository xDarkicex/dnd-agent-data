# Fiendish Handler

**description**: Handlers Handlers are a case study in misdirection. Due to their imposing presence, musculature, and distinctly fiendish glare, they are often mistaken for a pygmy raksha’s shapeshifting disguise. This is entirely intentional; the handler’s sole job is to protect its fiendish master.
