# Rift Crawler

**description**: Rift crawlers in the Fungi Forest do not to eat flesh, instead consuming vast amounts of mushrooms (and other plant material). Over time this strange new diet has enacted considerable changes in their demeanor and disposition, granting the rift crawler a vastly improved intelligence and charisma while empowering them with innate arcane abilities. They have become permanent denizens of the region and maintain small cloisters (not settlements by any account) throughout the Fungi Forest, generally trying to stay entertained and well fed—they particularly enjoy lunarae caps and will trade whatever they can for them. Some rift crawlers have taken to riding giant toads that live in the region.
