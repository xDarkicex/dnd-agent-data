**description**: Traits Keen Sight and Smell . The wererat has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight or smell. Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack. +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 +2) piercing damage. If the target is a humanoid, it must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw or be cursed with wererat lycanthropy.
