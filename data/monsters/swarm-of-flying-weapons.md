# Swarm of Flying Weapons

**description**: Animated Objects An animated, levitating object is deadly on its own, but its effectiveness compounds when united by a swarming hivemind. Powerful mages can summon such whirling dervishes from the forgotten debris of battlefields. Flying Weapons An ancient orcish war idiom summarises the swarm of flying weapons: “Even the toughest warrior falls to the untrained chaos of an army.” With over fifty weapons in its retinue, such a swarm is an unrestrained cyclone of wood and metal, blades banging against spear points, mace heads clashing into hafts. This tornado of death is a striking menace on any battlefield, more of an obstacle than a combatant. When not commanded by its creator, the swarm searches eagerly for the next battle.
