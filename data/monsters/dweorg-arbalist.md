# Dweorg Arbalist

**description**: Arbalists are as much engineer and smith as they are renowned crossbowmen, though it the latter for which they are named. A heavy crossbow is notoriously difficult to load, but an arbalist—through training and clever invention—is able to achieve what other dweorg have accepted as impossible. This is the true strength of an arbalist’s outlook: the ability too out think and move beyond that which is rote and inflexible. If ingenuity in crafting is the right of all dweorg, it is the arbalist who best exemplifies this trait.
