# Barlat Lakbour

**description**: Barlat Lakbour These mystical women prowl the byways of cities and towns, making their habitation in graveyards and catacombs, the source of their nickname “mule of the graves.” They are shapechangers, able to take the form of a mule or adopt a humanoid form at need, and by night they assume a monstrous hybrid of the two. They bury themselves in shallow graves when taking their rest, usually during the daytime. Dream Hunter . Changing their appearance to adopt the guise of common women of the marketplace to observe the thoughts and dreams of people around them with their cunning mind. Sometimes they may put on a regal beauty bedecked in fine robes and jewels when the need suits them and they find a particularly choice target of wealth and taste. Once they have selected a victim, they assail their dreams by night, seeking to drive them to madness and despair as they feast on the psychic essence released by their torment. Child Stealer .
