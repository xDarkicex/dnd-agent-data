# Tristian (Level 1)

**description**: Tristian has no last name, nor does he have any real memories of his parents. He grew up in a Qadiran orphanage run by a small group of Sarenite priests, and they instilled within him a love of the Dawnflower that helped fill the void left by his missing parents. As he came of age, Tristian left the orphanage to travel the world, eager to spread Sarenrae’s blessings and to aid those who, like himself, came into this world seeking a better place in it. Traits Equipment. 2 holy symbols, a prayer book, 5 sticks of incense, vestments, a set of common clothes, and a pouch containing 15 gp, splint armor, mace, light crossbow and 20 bolts, priests’ pack, scale mail, shield. Disciple of Life. Whenever Tristian uses a spell of 1st level or higher to restore hit points to a creature, the creature regains additional hit points equal to 2 + the spell’s level. Spellcasting. Tristian is a 1st-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with spell attacks). He has the following cleric spells prepared: Cantrips (at will): resistance, sacred flame, spare the dying 1st level (4 slots): bless, cure wounds, detect evil and good, guiding bolt, healing word, protection from evil and good Actions Mace. Melee Weapon Attack: +1 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 2 (1d6–1) bludgeoning damage. Light Crossbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d6) piercing damage.
