# Pod-Spawned (template)

**description**: Creatures of any type other than construct, elemental, plant, or undead can become a pod-spawned creature. It keeps its statistics, except as follows. Challenge. There is no need to recalculate the creature’s challenge rating. Type. The pod-spawned creature’s type changes to plant. Senses. The pod-spawned creature gains low-light vision. New Ability: Mimic. A pod-spawned creature retains all the knowledge of its original. It can impersonate the original perfectly except for its lack of emotion. This emotionlessness can be detected with a successful Wisdom (Insight) check (DC 20). A creature familiar with the original has advantage on this check. If he pod-spawned creature feigns sincere emotion, the Wisdom (Insight) check is opposed by its Charisma (Deception) check, instead. Pod-spawned creatures resemble the original down to hair and blood. Noticing small physical imperfections requires a successful Wisdom (Perception) check with a DC determined by the spawning bodythief’s Charisma (Deception) check, made when the pod hatches. Ability Scores.
