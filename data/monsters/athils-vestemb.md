# Athils Vestemb

**description**: Athils Vestemb The jarl of the Whar, Athils Vestemb is in many ways the model of a successful raider. She is brave and daring in battle and now as jarl, treats her clan with respect and reverence. Yet in other ways she is so very different. For to her came one of the largest and most powerful wolves and their bond is stronger than any seen elsewhere among the Whar. Stronger even perhaps than the bond between Athils and her brother, Gulli. Roleplaying Athils Fated. Athils believes a day will come when the Whar are as respected as the Hallharn or Lutan clans. She wants her people to have a vote on the inner council. She also believes that this is why the Grimnir frost wolf was sent to her. Wolfdreamer. Athils wolf is connected to the primal world of Grimnir and Athils shares her wolf’s dreams. In them she sees visions of places and events and this dreaming has guided her during ascension to jarl of the Whar. Disciple of Hel. Athils wears a charm of Hel, with 3 uses remaining. Each use grants her 10 temporary hit points and whenever she makes an attack roll or a saving throw, she can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to the attack or saving throw roll. This effect lasts for 1 hour.
