# Empusa

**description**: Empusa These shapeshifting fiends appear outwardly beautiful, with an enchanting majesty about them that belies their murderous intentions. They go about most often in the guise of lovely women, though they may adopt animal forms as well to move about unnoticed. When the empusa attacks, its true appearance is revealed, as its head is swathed in a corona of flames, while one leg is made of burnished brass and the other is hooved and shaggy like a donkey. Empusas are crafty and clever but eschew direct confrontations. Intimidation and fear may cause them to flee an encounter entirely, plotting revenge if possible with the aid of charmed underlings, or if not simply moving on to easier prey.
