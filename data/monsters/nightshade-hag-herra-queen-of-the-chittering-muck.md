# Nightshade Hag, Herra, Queen of the Chittering Muck

**description**: Herra was declared Queen of the Chittering Muck when she slew the two grandmother hags in a large swamp known for its evil druid groves, hags, poisonous bogs, trolls and an out of place hillock filled with treants. Herra has left the treants alone, hoping to slowly turn most of them into hag trees over time. Herra’s continued survival against nearby powers is her usefulness. She has guaranteed the safety of merchants as long as they don’t wander from a prescribed road, built and patrolled by her minions and mercenaries. Merchants were reluctant at first, but some have started calling “The Chittering Way,” the safest road around. Her elite hobgoblin mercenaries charge a small toll, brutally enforce draconian laws, and her road has now become one of the safest paths to transport goods. At the center of this new road is a trading town, called Midden, where anything is for sale and all crimes are punished with maiming or death. In the words of one Merchant Consortium, “Herra has made herself indispensable.”
