# Descendant of Oceanus, the Titan of the Ocean

**description**: The power of the ocean, the flow of water and the calm of the deep. This bloodline excels at manipulating their ancestor's domain in a multitude of ways. Titan-Blooded Sorcerer A far-off descendant of one of the immortals of the past, carrying divine power in their veins, as well as the ability to manipulate their ancestor’s domain to a certain degree. Their power often sprouts from the divine blood that minor deities and nature spirits who were fathered by the Titans spread in the mortal world through amorous affairs. The blood of four immortals has been identified among these magical warriors: Atlas, Helios, Hyperion and Oceanus. Humanoids Humanoids are the most plentiful creatures amongst the creations of the gods. They create vast kingdoms, they make unbelievable technological advances and even achieve breakthroughs in the art of magic. Traits Amphibious. The sorcerer can breathe air and water. Divine Empowerment (3/day). Whenever the sorcerer casts a spell, they can choose to empower it. When empowered a spell is treated as if it had been upcast at one level higher. Power of the Depths (3/day). The sorcerer can change the damage type of their spells to cold or bludgeoning damage. Spellcasting. The sorcerer's Spellcasting Ability is Charisma (spell save DC 17, +9 to hit with spell attacks). They can cast the following spells: At Will: mage armor, mage hand, prestidigitation, shape water 3/day each: armor of agathys (cast as a 4th-level spell), bless, enlarge/reduce, mind spike, create or destroy water, fog cloud, tidal wave 2/day each: bestow curse, guardian of faith, sickening radiance, spirit guardians, control water, maelstrom 1/day each: flamestrike, summon celestial Actions Celestial Strike. Melee Spell Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 27 (4d10 + 5) radiant damage. Celestial Blast. Ranged Spell Attack: +9 to hit, reach 120 ft., one target. Hit: 27 (4d10 + 5) radiant damage. Bonus Actions Quick Spell (3/day). The sorcerer can cast a spell with a casting time of 1 action.
