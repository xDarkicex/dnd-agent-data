# Grorrarr

**description**: Upon his death at old age, Kalymorr the Gorgon Rider, the solitary druid of the King’s Forest, has reincarnated as an owlbear. The Eternal Mother gifted the venerable druid with a young body of a monstrosity and allowed him to continue his work by retaining his memories and knowledge. The owlbear knows who it was in its past life, re-named itself Grorrarr, and continued with its duties.
