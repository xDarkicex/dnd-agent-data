# Sprite Warden of the Grove

**description**: The warden of the grove is the primary defender of its community, and leads other sprites to fight if needed. Traits Slay Foe (1/Turn). The sprite deals an extra 9 (2d8) damage with its weapon attack if it hits a creature that doesn't have all its hit points. Actions Multiattack. The sprite makes two melee attacks or two ranged attacks. Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1 slashing damage. Shortbow. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 1 piercing damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 10 Constitution saving throw or become poisoned for l minute. If its saving throw result is 5 or lower, the poisoned target falls unconscious for the same duration, or until it takes damage or another creature takes an action to shake it awake. Heart Sight. The sprite touches a creature and magically knows the creature's current emotional state. If the target fails a DC l0 Charisma saving throw, the sprite also knows the creature's alignment. Celestials, fiends, and Undead automatically fail the saving throw. Invisibility. The sprite magically turns invisible until it attacks or casts a spell, or until its concentration ends (as if concentrating on a spell). Any equipment the sprite wears or carries is invisible with it. Spellcasting. The sprite casts one of the following spells using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 12): 1/day each: animal messenger, spike growth, conjure barrage
