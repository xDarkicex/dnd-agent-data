# Dragon Disciple

**description**: Whether a dragon takes a humanoid disciple as an ally or an indentured servant, the sorcerer learns to harness their magic in ways beyond that of their arcane peers. It takes a certain type of person to even want to become a dragon disciple: an overbearing sense of self-worth, the will to wield ancient power, and the presence to command in the dragon’s name. Of course, the chosen aim of all disciples to learn as much as possible from their dragon aegis. As a result, most disciple are sorcerers able to innately cast spells that mirror the magical ability of their dragon. However, disciples rarely, if ever, reach the height of power that they crave. It would be a foolish dragon that let one of its underlings potentially learn and develop to the point where it might rival the dragon itself.
