# Aberrant Template

**description**: The drow of House Ausstyl grafts aberrant flesh into the bodies of their children to infuse them with the essence of their unfathomable masters. These creatures become aberrations in part as the unnatural implant spreads and takes over their bodies. They gain new abilities, which they inherit from the aberrations they serve. Their mindset changes to that of an otherworldly creature, and they live out their lives almost as if they were in a dreamlike state controlled by the unspeakable horrors that are now part of them.
