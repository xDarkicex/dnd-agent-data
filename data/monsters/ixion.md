# Ixion

**description**: Centaur, Ixion Legendary hunters and skilled warriors, centaurs are part man and part horse. Typically found on the fringes of civilization, these stoic people vary widely in appearance, their skin tones typically appearing deeply tanned but similar to the humans who occupy nearby regions, while their lower bodies borrow the colorations of local equines. Centaur hair and eyes trend toward darker colors and their features tend to be broad, while the overall bulk of their bodies is influenced by the size of the horses their lower quarters resemble. Thus, while an average centaur stands over 7 feet tall and weights upward of 2,000 pounds, there are vast regional variations—from lean plains-runners to burly mountain hunters. Centaurs typically live to be about 60 years old. Isolationist. Aloof with other races and at odds even with their own kind, ixions are ancient lines of the centaur race only slowly coming to accept the modern world. While many centaurs have become more cosmopolitan in their outlook and engaged with other races, ixions inhabit the depths of vast prairies or the fringes of eldritch forests, holding firm to the isolationist ways of their ancestors. They are sometimes considered outdated relics and shunned by other centaurs, but keep their own counsel and drive away outsiders who trespass into lands they claim. Their sometimes-violent resistance to engagement with other races leads many other races to remain wary of centaurs, keeping alive legends of territorial beastmen and the regular, violent encounters the centaurs have with stubborn settlers and expansionist countries.
