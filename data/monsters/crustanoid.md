# Crustanoid

**description**: These large humanoid creatures, evolved from a rare combination of crustaceans and humans, find solace in the bountiful depths of their coral sanctuaries. They embrace a way of life harmonious with nature, embodying an unwavering commitment to their surroundings. Each member of a crustanoid tribe, regardless of age or gender, contributes to the community's well-being, fostering a sense of collective responsibility and unity. They select their leaders through a tug-of-war competition held each year.
