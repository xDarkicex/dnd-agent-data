# Huldra

**description**: Huldra At times mistaken as being the witches of the Ironwood, the huldra are actually among the primal beings that emerged as Grimnir formed. They are almost supernaturally attractive humanoids, but they also have beast-like features, such as long tails and cat-like ears. They easily blend into the woods and their skin is covered with ironleaves, protecting their otherwise exposed flesh. Though most glimpses of huldra are of females, there are also male huldra, but little is known about what communities, if any, they form. But huldra are seldom encountered on their own. Carnivorous. Intruders into their forests seldom know they are being observed by the huldra and the creatures never hesitate to ambush weaker foes. They have a fondness for flesh of any kind and take most any opportunity to consume other creatures. Forest Dwellers. They see the woods in which they live as theirs and protect them ferociously. They tend to form alliances with sentient trees, when possible, especially ironheart trees. They never start a fight they are unlikely to win, unless they are truly desperate and feel their forest sanctuary is threatened. The Witches. The huldra live near to, and occasionally work with, the Ironwood Witches, but they are not true allies. They tolerate one another because generally their purposes align.
