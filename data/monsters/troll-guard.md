# Troll Guard

**description**: Troll Trolls are one of the most notorious dangers of the Stolen Lands, be they encountered alone or as members of larger bands. Beyond the typical troll, other more dangerous variants of these creatures can be found in the region, each sharing a penchant for violence, sadism, and murderous cruelty. Troll Guard The ruler of Pitax has recruited a number of brutal trolls to his employ. Arming them with fine armor and weapons and helping to train them further in the art of war has afforded King Irovetti with a number of these particularly dangerous palace guards. Traits Brave. The troll guard has advantage on saving throws against being frightened. Brute. A melee weapon deals one extra die of its damage when the troll guard hits with it (included in the attack). Keen Smell. The troll guard has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Regeneration. The troll guard regains 10 hit points at the start of his turn. If it takes acid or fire damage, this trait doesn’t function at the start of the troll guard’s next turn. The troll guard dies only if it starts its turn with 0 hit points and doesn’t regenerate. Actions Multiattack. The troll guard makes three melee attacks, two with its greataxe and one with its bite, or two with its claws and one with its bite. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (1d6+8) piercing damage. Greataxe. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 27 (3d12+8) slashing damage. Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d6+8) slashing damage.
