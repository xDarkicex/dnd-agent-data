# Feyrealm-born Template

**description**: The ancestry of a feyrealm-born creature can be traced back to some kind of fey roots. They are either born on the Feyrealm or have powerful ties to it. A feyrealm-born creature has larger than usual eyes of strikingly light color and long pointy ears. It is an emotional creature that expresses its feelings and is prone to sudden mood changes. Feyrealm-born creatures love nature and animals and feel at home in the wilderness. A humanoid or giant can become a feyrealm-born creature . It keeps its statistics, except as follows.
