# Melianse

**description**: Melianse is a put-upon nixie who tries her best not to react too violently against the loggers who invaded her home. In your game, she can serve as a much more sinister force. By shifting her alignment to evil she can become an unexpected foe for a lower level series of adventures focused on an entire “cult” of charmed victims acting strangely in a remote riverside village. Traits Amphibious. Melianse can breathe air and water. Innate Spellcasting. Melianse’s innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 15). She can innately cast the following spells, requiring on material components: 3/day each: charm person 1/day each: water breathing Magic Resistance. Melianse has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. Spellcasting. Melianse is an 5th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks). Melianse knows the following sorcerer spells: Cantrips (at will): dancing lights, mage hand, message, minor illusion, ray of frost 1st level (4 slots): burning hands*, fog cloud 2nd level (3 slots): blur, scorching ray * 3rd level (2 slots): hypnotic pattern, sleet storm *These spells deal cold damage instead of fire damage. Actions Shortsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d6+4) piercing damage. Light Crossbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8+4) piercing damage.
