# Mindclaw

**description**: A mindclaw is a red-brown crab the size of a wagon scuttles forward with an unusual amount of aggression. An adult mindclaw is 15 feet tall and weighs 4,000 pounds. Echoes of a Dead World. Mindclaws retain the psychic impressions of countless dead of the ocean depths, including those lost at sea, consumed for survival, or the victims of diluvian cataclysms that drowned entire continents.
