# Shadow Wing

**description**: Actions Multiattack. The shadow wing makes two attacks: one with its flaying claws and one with its infected bite. Alternatively, it makes two spine spray attacks. It can’t make both spine spray attacks against the same target. Flaying Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d4 + 3) slashing damage, and the target is subjected to the bleeding condition (1d4 piercing damage at the start of each of its turns, removed after it regains hit points) for 1 minute. Infected Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw; on a failure, it takes an additional 9 (2d8) poison damage. Spine Spray. Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage, and the target is subjected to the distracted condition (−2 AC, removed after it is hit by an attack) until the end of the shadow wing’s next turn.
