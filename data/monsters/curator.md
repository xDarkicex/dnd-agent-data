# Curator

**description**: Cities often hold collections of rare and valuable things. Art galleries, historical archives, and museums collect, catalog, and preserve cultural artifacts, and curators oversee and run such places. Curators are educated individuals who are knowledgeable about and protective of the items under their purview. Those in search of knowledge about such items, seeking to sell them, or wanting to authenticate their veracity often turn to a curator for aid. Magical Protectors. Curators learn minor magic to help them maintain and protect the items in their care, most often from wear, tear, and time. They tend to hire guards to protect particularly vulnerable or valuable items, but they aren’t above getting their hands dirty if the items in their care are in danger.
