# Giant Animated Statue

**description**: Animated Object This animated object is a strange sight indeed—an apparently abstract statue made of more than a dozen stone orbs, several of which are hollow and feature openings that reveal the fires burning within them. When this statue animates, the orbs grind and roll against each other without separating but remain devoid of any obvious underlying support (almost as if the statue were an animated mass of spherical magnets). This statue is a depiction of the Outer God Yog-Sothoth, and can be found guarding any eldritch temple or location of interest to those who worship the so-called Lurker at the Threshold. Traits Burn Alive. As a bonus action, the statue roasts a creature it has grappled in the fires that burn inside its hollow spheres. The target takes 16 (3d10) fire damage. If the target is a creature or a flammable object, it ignites. Until a creature takes an action to douse the fire, the target takes 5 (1d10) fire damage at the start of each of its turns. Actions Multiattack. The statue makes two attacks. Sphere. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 15 ft. Hit: 19 (2d12+6) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Medium creature, it must succeed on a DC 14 Dexterity save or be grappled in one of the statues hollow spheres (escape DC 14). The statue can hold up to 12 creatures in this way. Flaming Coal. Range Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, range 80/160 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d6+6) bludgeoning damage plus 9 (2d8) fire damage.
