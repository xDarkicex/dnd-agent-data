# Giant Aether Horse

**description**: Aether Horse Resembling the common sea horse, aether horses roam the shallower depths of the Aetherial Expanse. Their colorations vary greatly from herd to herd, though invariably, colors are vibrant, and their elongated tails glow with a pale shimmering light. Astral merfolk frequently tame giant aether horses, using them to patrol the aethereal depths of their territory.
