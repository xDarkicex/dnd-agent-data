# Auchs

**description**: Lumbering and slow-witted, Auchs is one of the more dangerous bandits in the Stag Lord’s employ. With an adjustment to his alignment, though, he can serve as an imposing bouncer at a tavern or even as a lovable rural character who tends to get in over his head. Traits Brute. Auchs deals one extra die of its damage when he hits with his greatclub (included in the attack). Actions Greatclub. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d8+3) bludgeoning damage.
