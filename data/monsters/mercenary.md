# Mercenary

**description**: Hired guns, soldiers of fortune, legionnaires, or just plain mercs — these are just some other names used to describe mercenaries, who take up often dangerous tasks and missions in exchange for goods and services. The galaxy is infinite, and there's always someone looking for people willing to get their hands dirty to get a job done. Working either alone or in groups, each merc carefully weighs the dangers of a job with its potential payout.
