# Stymphalian Bird

**description**: These brutal creatures are native to the swamps of Stymphalia, known to be voracious and bloody. They are recognized for the danger they represent, destroying crops, fruit trees and unfortunate travelers as they swarm the countryside. These man-eating birds are widely known because of the sixth Labor of Heracles, who was tasked with getting rid of them. With the aid of Athena and Hephaestus the demigod was able to quickly dispatch the birds, using a rattle built by the smith god, called a krotala. The sound produced by the artifact stunned the avians, giving Heracles a chance to shoot them with arrows tipped with the poisonous blood from the Hydra. Beaks and Feathers of Bronze. With their strange beaks and feathers these birds were able to rip through armor, making them a menace even to marching armies. Sacred Beasts of the Land Throughout the ages the world has existed, unique animals have made themselves known in legends. Stronger, faster, more majestic and, perhaps, even a little magical. The most well known of these creatures are the Sacred Beasts of the Land. This title refers to all the unique animals associated with the Twelve Labors of Heracles. Polymorphing into a Sacred Beast Players attempting to use Polymorph to become any of the Sacred Beasts of the Land will find that they cannot replicate the magic properties of the creature in question. For example, they will not be able to use the innate spellcasting feature, legendary actions or legendary resistances. Additionally, only players who have substantial experience with the creature in question should be able to attempt such a transformation. An example of such experience could be the slaying of the beast, as well as the creation of a friendly bond, but it must involve personal experience from the character. Traits Weak to Sound. If the Stymphalian Bird takes thunder damage it becomes stunned until the end of its next turn. Vicious Man-Eater. The Stymphalian Bird has advantage on melee Attack Rolls against any creature that doesn't have all its Hit Points. Actions Bronze Beak. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5ft., one target.
