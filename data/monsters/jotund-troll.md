# Jotund Troll

**description**: Troll Trolls are one of the most notorious dangers of the Stolen Lands, be they encountered alone or as members of larger bands. Beyond the typical troll, other more dangerous variants of these creatures can be found in the region, each sharing a penchant for violence, sadism, and murderous cruelty. Jotund Troll Jotund trolls are gigantic, nine-headed horrors who prowl frigid moors, marshes, and wastelands. While a jotund troll’s nine heads possess their own brains and senses, they work much more in tandem than the dual-minded nature of the two-headed troll. Despite this, the heads often argue and bicker, particularly over which head gets to eat the carcass of a victim. The fact that all nine maws lead to the same shared stomach seldom makes much difference in these culinary disagreements. Traits Head Regrowth. If the jotund troll has one of its nine heads severed, such as by a vorpal sword , it is able to regrow the head during the troll’s regeneration. After the troll regains hit points, it makes a Constitution check; on a result of 25 or lower, one missing head is fully restored, while two missing heads are restored on a result of 26 or higher. If a jotund troll’s last head is severed, the troll dies immediately. Regeneration. The jotund troll regains 40 hit points at the start of its turn. If the troll takes acid or fire damage, this trait doesn’t function at the start of the troll’s next turn. The jotund troll dies only if it starts its turn with 0 hit points and doesn’t regenerate. Legendary Resistance (3/Day). If the jotund troll fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead. Actions Multiattack. The jotund troll makes one bite attack and two claw attacks. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft. Hit: 17 (3d6+7) piercing damage. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 10 ft. Hit: 14 (2d6+7) slashing damage. Cacophonous Roar (Recharge 5—6). The jotund troll emits a cacophonous roar from all its heads, exerting a mystical power that distorts the listener’s mind. Each creature within 100 feet that is not a giant and can hear it must make a DC 17 Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save the creature is stunned until the end of their next turn. Ravenous Jaws (Recharge 6). The jotund troll makes a bite attack with each of its heads. Each attack must be directed at a different target within 10 feet of the troll.
