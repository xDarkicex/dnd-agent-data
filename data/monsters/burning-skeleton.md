# Burning Skeleton

**description**: Burning skeletons are animated skeletons that radiate supernatural fire. When destroyed, burning skeletons explode in a fiery burst that can take their slayers with them. Unusual Nature. A skeleton doesn't require air, food, drink, or sleep. Traits Death Burst. The skeleton explodes when it drops to 0 hit points. Each creature within 5 feet of it must succeed on a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw or take 7 (2d6) fire damage. Heated Body. A creature that touches the skeleton or hits it with a melee attack while within 5 feet of it takes 3 (1d6) fire damage. Heated Weapons. Any metal melee weapon the skeleton wields deals an extra 3 (1d6) fire damage on a hit (included in the attack). Actions Shortsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage plus 3 (1d6) fire damage. Hurl Flame. Ranged Spell Attack: +4 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d8) fire damage.
