# Awakened Zurkhwood

**description**: Awakened Zurkhwood This creature is an ordinary zurkhwood mushroom given sentience and mobility. An awakened zurkhwood can be created by casting the awaken spell on a normal zurkhwood mushroom. A myconid sovereign can create one by performing a lengthy ritual.
