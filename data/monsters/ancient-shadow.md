# Ancient Shadow

**description**: Ancient shadows are undead that have lingered on the Material Pane for generations, slowly absorbing necrotic energy as it seeps through their lairs within lost catacombs and corpse-strewn oubliettes. Their darkness is heavy and cloying, their evil tangible, and their very essence a terror-inducing madness.
