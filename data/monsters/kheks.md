# Kheks

**description**: Background Kheks is highly skilled and self-sufficient, proving at an early age to be a good hunter and an even better tracker. She gained the title "Swiftseeker” after winning a tournament that required participants to track down a specific bird that had been released into the forest before nightfall. Kheks had often spent time practicing bird calls and was able to capture her feathered treasure before midday. After winning the game Kheks joined the ranks of other tribe members who work on secret assignments to locate artifacts and items seen in visions by the tribe's shaman. Kheks has not been home in nearly three years, as her current mission keeps turning up dead ends, and she cannot return without the artifact she had been assigned to find without shaming herself and her family Roleplaying Kheks Kheks is direct to the point of seeming gruff, but it is a difference in culture more than an indicator of attitude or how much she likes someone. She does not understand or enjoy pleasantries or small talk and prefers getting straight to the heart of a matter. She appreciates when she meets non-lizardfolk who are capable of communicating directly as well ("I want to trade. You want to trade? Good. Let us trade.”) Kheks trades as a means of survival while she is away from her tribe, and as such she is not preoccupied with growing her wealth. She accepts gold but will just as readily accept useful gear or credible information on what she's searching for as payment. Maps are especially useful to her and she might discount an item in an attempt to procure a map of an area she is unfamiliar with. Kheks' bird companion, Pineapple, is a colorful budgie – a small songbird that can grow very attached to caring companions. She is most often out and about with Kheks as she travels, but occasionally Kheks will put Pineapple safely into her bag when traveling through rougher areas to make sure she stays safe.
