# Giant Walrus

**description**: Walruses are bulky marine mammals that favor arctic climates and communicate by grunting and roaring. A typical adult specimen weighs at least 2,000 pounds. Humans hunt them for their meat, fat, skin, bones, and tusks. Giant Walrus. Giant walruses are enormous, ill-tempered creatures as big as elephants.
