# The Traitor (Ice Zombie)

**description**: Some of the the dead may break free from the ice and stumble towards the group. Unlike classical zombies they are well preserved, but naked and do not wield any weapons. These monsters are, of course, not carrying Lucifer’s hairs. If killed, their bodies will revert to their former frozen state in the ice. Some of the dead will be former elves, dwarves or other species. Actions Ice Shard. Melee Weapon Attack: Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 2 (1d4) slashing damage.
