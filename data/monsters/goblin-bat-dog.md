# Goblin Bat-Dog

**description**: Goblin Dog Goblins’ eponymous pets aren’t true canines at all, but rather large, blunt-nosed rodents with thin bodies and long legs. Those who point out the legendary fear of dogs most goblins have might wonder why these creatures carry such a name, but as any goblin can tell you, goblin dogs represent what a real dog should be. All the rest are little more than monsters that are worthy of being killed on sight–or more often, a panicked retreat. As cowardly as they are ugly, a goblin dog prefers to lurk in bushes or deep shadows, pouncing on lone or wounded prey, although hunger or encouragement at the hand of goblin trainers can make them much more bold. Their patchy bald hides are constantly infested with rashes and dander. Goblin Bat-Dog These mutant goblin dogs have batlike wings, and as they flop around in the air, their dander tends to dust out in clouds. The methods by which these particular goblin dogs grew their wings involved months of feeding on eldritch concoctions brewed from barghest blood. Traits Goblin Pox. A creature exposed to goblin pox must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or become afflicted with goblin pox. On a successful saving throw, it is immune to goblin pox for 24 hours. A creature with goblin pox is poisoned and must repeat the saving throw at the end of its next turn. On a success, the effect ends. On a failed save, the creature can use either an action or a bonus action on its turns, but not both. The creature can then repeat the saving throw after each long rest, ending the effects on itself on a success. Goblins and goblin dogs are immune to goblin pox. Irritating Dander. A creature that hits a goblin bat-dog with an unarmed attack, tries to grapple it, or otherwise touches it is exposed to goblin pox. Keen Hearing and Smell. The goblin bat-dog has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing and smell. Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (2d4+2) piercing damage. Dander Dusting (Recharge after a Short or Long Rest). The goblin bat-dog flaps its wings furiously, causing a noxious cloud of dander to fill the air in a 5-foot radius around it. All creatures in the area of effect are exposed to goblin pox, and any creature other than a goblin, goblin dog, or goblin bat-dog must succeed at a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw or be blinded until the beginning of the goblin bat-dog’s next turn.
