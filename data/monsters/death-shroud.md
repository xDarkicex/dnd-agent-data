# Death Shroud

**description**: Death Shroud A death shroud can be created by combining cloth imbued with necromantic energy with flesh free of a soul. One blast of biomantic energy later and you have an animate, cloth-wrapped limb. A death shroud actively recruits fresh corpses into its own form, temporarily increasing its size and strength. Over time, some of these additions stick, and the death shroud can grow to a mass of writhing limbs, heads, and torsos. Fluttering Swarm. From a distance, the unliving limbs and rotten cloth that makes up a death shroud swarm can appear like a single, enormous specimen. However, unlike their solitary cousins, which incorporate recently-unliving matter into their own form, a swarm of death shrouds manifests when individual shrouds pick upon a single, enormous corpse. The concentration of necromantic energy can cause small parts of the corpse to wrench themselves free from the unanimated tissue and join the clambering, tumbling mass that is driven by a singular curiosity for unliving flesh.
