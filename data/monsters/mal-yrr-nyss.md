# Mal Yrr Nyss

**description**: Lieutenant and Confidant While she might be a servant and perceived concubine of Gal Gullion, Mal Yrr Niss knows the value of having the ear of a drow noble in Holoth’s First House. True to the ways of her order, the Priestess of Naraneus has every intention of utilizing her position with the youngest son of the Matron Mother to plot Maelora’s downfall. However, this means she must ensure his survival; the two are practically inseparable and short of pointlessly laying down her life for Gal, Mal Yrr Niss will go to the ends of the Underworld to protect the noble dark elf, and thus save herself.
