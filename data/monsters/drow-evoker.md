# Drow Evoker

**description**: While all magic-users enjoy the thrill of drawing forth energy and bending it to their will, evokers deal in raw, unmitigated power. To be a worthy practitioner is to disregard safety. Unlike the clergy, who hide behind armor-clad thralls, an evoker takes combat by the shortest route: burn them where they stand. As a result, most wizards employ spells that have a decent range and at least secondary if not tertiary effects. Fire, for example, is popular as it damages, blinds many light-sensitive creatures, and then continues to burn flammable material. Such efficiency can earn even a lowly male mage some grudging respect.
