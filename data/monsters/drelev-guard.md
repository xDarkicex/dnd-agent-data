# Drelev Guard

**description**: Guard Be they the personal protectors of a traveling celebrity or the highly-trained soldiers of a city watch, guards range widely in their level of skill. Drelev Guard The soldiers who guard Fort Drelev were hand-picked not only for their skill with the longsword and longbow, but for their personalities. Hannis Drelev wanted guards that would inspire fear as much as loyalty from his citizens. Traits Devoted to Drelev. A Drelev guard has advantage on saving throws against effects that would force them to abandon their duty to Drelev. Actions Multiattack. The guard makes three attacks with his longsword or longbow. Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d8+4) slashing damage, or 9 (1d10+4) slashing damage if used with two hands. Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8+2) piercing damage.
