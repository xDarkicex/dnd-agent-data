# Urda

**description**: Darg, Fesl, and Urda These three hill giants seek to take advantage of the growing instability in the Stolen Lands as the threat of war escalates. Of course, these statistics for hill giant fighters also work quite well for hill-giant leaders, or if the party is much higher level, as the rank and file guards in a hill-giant fort. Traits Knockback. When the giant hits a foe with a melee attack, the giant can use a bonus action to make the target roll a DC 16 Strength saving throw. On a failed save, the target is pushed back 10 feet. Brutality. One per round, when the giant hits a target with a weapon attack, it can deal 4 (1d8) additional damage of the same type as the weapon. Special Equipment. Each giant carries a Huge greatclub +1 . Actions Multiattack. The giant makes two greatclub attacks. Greatclub +1. Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 20 (3d8+7) bludgeoning damage. Thrown Rock. Ranged Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, range 60/240 ft., one target. Hit: 22 (3d10+6) bludgeoning damage.
