# Fire Mephit

**description**: A fire raging out of control is already a hotbed of anxious energy that it takes a mere thought to give form to such a blaze. Thankfully fire mephits are also short-lived their forms bound to the same rules of existence as their substance. Fire mephits delight in spreading the flames of their birth and in tormenting those who extinguish them. Controlled Flame. A fire mephit properly acclimated to a forge or oven provides a constant heat as it consumes the fuel provided it, taking delight in watching work form from its flames, then lying dormant in the glowing coals only to rise again with a new day. Establishing such control over a fire mephit can be an arduous affair, as spark and flame tend to exit the hearth at the worst opportunity.
