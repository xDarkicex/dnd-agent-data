# Ice Ripper

**description**: Ice Ripper Few raiders, outside of the Knattle of the High North, believe ice rippers exist. Even in those frozen northern lands, these massive monstrosities are seldom encountered, preferring to prey on herds of mammoth that roam the tundra far from the eyes of raiders. There are stories of these great beasts being slain, but no proof... the Knattle say the meat from rippers spoils the moment the beast dies, so they have never had reason to harvest a carcass. Extreme Predator. An ice ripper’s body is covered in hundreds of vaguely claw-like appendages. These work in unison to push the ripper through snow and solid ice. They are ambush predators... striking from below and dragging their foe beneath the ice to be eaten. If caught on the surface, they attempt to burrow as soon as possible.
