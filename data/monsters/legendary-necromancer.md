# Legendary Necromancer

**description**: Traits Legendary Resistance (2/Day). If the necromancer fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead. Spell Penetration. When the necromancer casts a spell and is interrupted by an enemy's counterspell, treat the spell's level as 3 levels higher. Superior Focus. The necromancer has advantage on saving throws it makes to maintain its concentration. Actions Withering Touch. Melee Spell Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 12 (5d4) necrotic damage. Entropic Rift (Recharge 5–6). The necromancer opens a momentary Abyssal rift within 60 feet of it. The rift is a 20-foot-radius sphere. Each creature in that area must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 22 (5d8) necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. In addition, there is a 50 percent chance that 1d4 wights then appear in unoccupied spaces in the sphere. They act as the necromancer’s allies, take their turns immediately after the necromancer. They remain until destroyed or until the necromancer dismisses them as an action. Spellcasting. The necromancer casts one of the following spells using Intelligence as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks): At will: chill touch (3d8), dancing lights, mage hand, mending 2/day each: blight, mage armor,* blindness/deafness, ray of sickness, vampiric touch 1/day each: animate dead,* circle of death, cloudkill, dimension door, stoneskin * *The necromancer casts these spells on itself before combat. Legendary Actions The necromancer can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The necromancer regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn. Move. The necromancer moves up to half its speed without provoking opportunity attacks. A Taste of Undeath. The necromancer casts chill touch . Withering Touch. The necromancer attacks with its Withering Touch. Necromancy (Costs 2 Actions). The necromancer casts blight, blindness/deafness, ray of sickness , or vampiric touch , if available.
