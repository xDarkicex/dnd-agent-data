# Wharfling

**description**: Tiny, doglike creatures with slippery, hairless skin and webbed claws, wharflings are occasionally chosen as familiars by spellcasters who are unconcerned about their personal appearance, who frequently engage in acts of larceny, or who dwell in watery conditions.

**properties**:
- Category: Monsters
- Size: Tiny
- Type: Beast
- Alignment: unaligned
- Challenge Rating: 1.8

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Deep Magic Vol 1
