# Hamadryad

**description**: TRAITS Innate Spellcasting. A hamadryad can innately cast the following spells using Wisdom as her spellcasting ability (spell save DC 14; +5 to hit with spell attacks) and requiring no material components: At will: charm person, sleep 1/day: suggestion Tree Dependent . A hamadryad is mystically bound to her tree, and can never stray more than 300 yards away from it. If she does, she gains the poisoned condition and will die in 4d6 hours. Wild Empathy . A hamadryad can speak with animals and plants, and has advantage on all Wisdom (Animal Handling) checks. ACTIONS Dagger . Melee Weapon Attack : +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit : 6 (1d4 + 4) piercing damage.
