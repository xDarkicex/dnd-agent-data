# Cosmic Turtle

**description**: Cosmic Turtle Few creatures in the Aetherial Expanse predate cosmic turtles. These ancient turtles are born of star stuff fragments and share an unbreakable bond with cosmic life and creation. Their bodies appear crystal-like, infused with swirling stars, exploding supernovas, and reflections of the deepest voids in space. Cosmic turtles are immortal; instead of “death” as mortal creatures experience, cosmic turtles enter a slumber that can last from hundreds to thousands of years, depending on the circumstance. Disciplined cosmic turtles can temporarily break out of their slumber if they sense imminent danger. A cosmic turtle can only be killed using a wish spell while hibernating using its Slumber trait. A fully grown cosmic turtle can be as large as five miles long; when it enters hibernation, entire islands can form on its shell and body. Guardians of the Aetherial Expanse. Cosmic turtles act as stewards of the Aetherial Expanse, awakening from their slumbers in times of grave turmoil to rebalance the shifting tides. Many civilizations upset the natural order of the Aetherial Expanse, only to be wiped out by an attack from a cosmic turtle. Woe be to those who summon the ire of the cosmic turtles.
