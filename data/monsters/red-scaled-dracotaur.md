# Red-Scaled Dracotaur

**description**: The creature stood as tall as three strong warriors, armored from horned head to clawed foot in azure scales, and aimed a bow that hurled thunderbolts. Countless stories surround the cloudy Origins of the dracotaur, though most of them involve centaurs. Dragonborn believe that a dragon captured centaurs during a skirmish on the plains and drowned them in its blood centuries ago. When their bodies were fished out, they had been transformed into perfect, Draconic warriors and a new, distinct species. Dracotaurs in Midgard Dracotaurs are so rare and fearsome that most Mharoti consider them legend. Many believe that the wandering dracotaur are centaurs blessed by the dragon god Azuran. In decades past, some ambitious Centaur raiders were intercepted and overpowered by a band of four dracotaurs. Now, these four dracotaurs are the so-called divine warlords of their own Centaur herds, galloping freely along the distant border reaches of the Dragon Empire.
