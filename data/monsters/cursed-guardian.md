# Cursed Guardian

**description**: This unusual bear guards a long-forgotten shrine of Erastil. You can present the guardian as cursed by other faiths by simply changing the nature of its Accursed weakness so that worshippers of other faiths gain advantage against it. The cursed guardian can be used as a particularly powerful species of grizzly bear in your game by simply removing the Accursed weakness, removing its languages, and reducing its Intelligence score to 2. Traits Accursed. The cursed guardian has vulnerability to all damage inflicted upon it by a worshipper of Erastil, and it has disadvantage on all saving throws to resist effects created by worshippers of Erastil. Keen Smell. The cursed guardian has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Pounce. If the cursed guardian moves at least 20 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a claw attack on the same turn, that target must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. If the target is prone, the cursed guardian can make one bite attack against it as a bonus action. Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 12 (2d6+5) piercing damage, or 15 (3d6+5) piercing damage if the target is grappled by the cursed guardian. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 10 (2d4+5) slashing damage, and the target is grappled (escape DC 15). Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained, and the cursed guardian can’t grapple another target.
