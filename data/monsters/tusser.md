# Tusser

**description**: Tusser The tussers are a race of medium sized humanoids, more gangly than stocky. They often walk stooped over, underplaying their height. Their skin is usually gray or paler, and their features are not as defined as other humanoids—small noses and ears and little detail to their features, as if each were an unfinished sculpture. In truth their ancestors were the offspring of Yoten and humanoids. The creatures ended up accumulating sufficient numbers in Grimnir’s past to found their own peoples. Facetaker About one in ten tussers are capable of limited shapechanging. As a bonus action they can change their face into a monstrous caricature of any humanoid within 10 feet of them. That humanoid rolls their next attack against the tusser with disadvantage.
