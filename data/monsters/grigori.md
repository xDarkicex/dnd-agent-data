# Grigori

**description**: Grigori’s role as a provocateur and spreader of propaganda makes him an enemy your players might love to hate, but his statistics can work just as well as the leader of a troupe of actors or a well-traveled entertainer. Adjust his alignment as needed. Traits Sneak Attack (1/Turn). Grigori deals an extra 14 (4d6) damage when he hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of Grigori that isn’t incapacitated and Grigori doesn’t have disadvantage on the attack roll. Spellcasting. Grigori is an 8th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks). Grigori knows the following bard spells: Cantrips (at will): message, minor illusion, vicious mockery (2d4) 1st level (4 slots): charm person, hideous laughter, longstrider 2nd level (3 slots): calm emotions, enthrall, invisibility 3rd level (3 slots): clairvoyance, nondetection, sending 4th level (2 slots): confusion, dimension door Actions Rapier. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8+3) piercing damage.
