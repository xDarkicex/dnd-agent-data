# Shadow Blade

**description**: Actions Multiattack. The shadow blade makes one attack with its shuriken, and one attack with its wakizashi. Shuriken. Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, Reach 15/60 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d4 + 4) piercing damage. Creatures damaged by this attack gain the distracted condition for 1 minute. Wakizashi. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, Reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d6 + 4) slashing damage. This attack deals an additional 7 (2d6) damage for each negative condition the creature it hits has. Smoke Screen (Recharge 5-6). The shadow blade throws a small bomb that explodes into a cloud of dense smoke that lingers in the air for 3 rounds in a 5 ft. radius around the shadow blade. The area is heavily obscured and creatures other than the shadow blade that aren’t proficient in Ninja Tools are blinded while in the smoke screen.
