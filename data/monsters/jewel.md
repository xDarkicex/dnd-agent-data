# Jewel

**description**: Traits Distracting Yips. As long as Jewel is fighting, she unleashes a torrent of grating, high-pitched yips. At the start of its turn, any creature other than Pavetta within 20 feet of Jewel must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or gain disadvantage on Perception checks and Constitution saving throws made to concentrate. Return to Pavetta. If Jewel ends her turn in a square adjacent to Pavetta, and Pavetta has a hand free, Jewel can leap up into Pavetta’s clutches as a bonus action. Actions Ankle Bite. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 piercing damage, plus the target must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw to avoid becoming distracted by the furious ankle biting. A distracted foe gains disadvantage on all checks until the end of their next turn.
