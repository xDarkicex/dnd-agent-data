# Akiros Ismort

**description**: Akiros Ismort is a fallen paladin who joined the Stag Lord’s bandits, but has come to realize that particular lifestyle is even more hollow than he anticipated. His unusual class combination makes him a great choice for any sort of “fallen knight” in your game—someone who has abandoned civilization to live in the wild, perhaps. Whether or not he can be redeemed and returned to his prior faith is left to your party to decide. Traits Reckless. At the start of his turn, Akiros can gain advantage on all melee weapon attack rolls during that turn, but attack rolls against him have advantage until the start of his next turn. Shameful Memories. The first time in a day during which Akiros sees another creature openly wearing Erastil’s holy symbol, he must attempt a DC 12 Wisdom save. On a failure, he has disadvantage on all rolls for 1 minute. Actions Multiattack. Akiros makes two melee attacks. Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8+3) slashing damage or 8 (1d10+4) slashing damage if used with two hands to make a melee attack. Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, range 150 ft./600 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d8+1) piercing damage.
