# Fra' Matteo

**description**: (Savonarola’s secretary, Kalliya’s spy) Fra’ Matteo has remarkable gray eyes and a fair complexion. He is a very tall and slim elf, whose appearance was changed surgically to make his ears seem human. This intelligent and perceptive young monk has been chosen by Kalliya as her spy in San Marco. Matteo is to remain in Florence indefinitely and inform the queen as soon as anything relevant happens. He is ideal for this assignment: As a true believer in God, he decided as a boy to enter the clergy – a very unusual decision for an elf. Matteo is convinced that he will somehow contribute to reuniting the races that are now separated by fear and ignorance. There is nevertheless a tragic aspect: Although Matteo is absolutely loyal to the queen, he suffers from the possibly permanent loss of his characteristic pointed ears which makes him an outcast, not belonging to either world. Traits Cunning Action (1/turn). Matteo can use a bonus Action to take the Dash, Disengage or Hide Action. Sneak Attack (1/turn). Matteo deals an extra 14 (4d6) damage when he hits a target with a weapon Attack and has advantage on the Attack roll, or when the target is within 5 ft. of an ally of Matteo‘s that isn’t Incapacitated and Matteo doesn’t have disadvantage on the roll. Spellcasting. Matteo is a 8th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 14, +6 to hit with spell attacks). Matteo knows the following wizard spells: Cantrips (at will): blade ward, mage hand, message 1st level (4 slots): alarm, disguise self, illusory script, silent image 2nd level (2 slots): blur, misty step Actions Magical Fae Dagger +2. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d4 + 5) piercing damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 14 Wisdom sa ving throw or suffer the effects of the Confusion spell for 1 minute. The Confused target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
