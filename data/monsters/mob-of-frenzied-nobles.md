# Mob of Frenzied Nobles

**description**: This lynch mob of crazed nobles has been whipped into a frenzy to attack anyone it has picked as a target without any logic or reason. They are armed with small weapons, tools, or improvised weapons and attack as a group, stabbing and slashing from every possible angle at their target, usually a single foe that they overrun.
