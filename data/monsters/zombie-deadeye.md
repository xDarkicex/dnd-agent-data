# Zombie Deadeye

**description**: Actions Multiattack. The zombie deadeye makes three attacks with its repeating crossbow against a single creature. Repeating Crossbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d8) piercing damage. A creature damaged by this attack is subjected to the distracted condition (−2 AC, removed after it is hit by an attack) for 1 minute. Bash. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) bludgeoning damage. A creature damaged by this attack is subjected to the disoriented condition (it can’t make opportunity attacks) until the end of its next turn.
