# Thug

**description**: Thugs are ruthless enforcers skilled at intimidation and violence. They work for money and have few scruples. Traits Pack Tactics : The thug has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the thug's allies is within 5 ft. of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated. Actions Multiattack : The thug makes two melee attacks. Mace : Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) bludgeoning damage. Heavy Crossbow : Ranged Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, range 100/400 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d10) piercing damage.

**properties**:
