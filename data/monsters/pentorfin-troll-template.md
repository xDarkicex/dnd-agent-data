# Pentorfin Troll (template)

**description**: Creatures transformed by a pentorfin overlord take on this template, becoming pentorfin trolls, subservient to the pentorfin overlords and acting according to their whims. They are typically used as shock troops, and to convert more of the enemy into pentorfin trolls to serve the overlords. When a humanoid or giant becomes a pentorfin troll, it retains all its statistics except as noted below. Challenge. With its increase to AC, hit points, and ability scores, a creature that becomes a pentorfin troll typically has a Challenge Rating 1 higher than the base creature. Servile. A pentorfin troll obeys any order from a pentorfin overlord without question and is treated as if under the effects of dominate person when interacting with a pentorfin overlord. Armor Class. A pentorfin troll's skin toughens and thickens, granting it a +2 bonus to Armor Class. Ability Scores.
