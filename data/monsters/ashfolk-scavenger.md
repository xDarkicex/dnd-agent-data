# Ashfolk Scavenger

**description**: Many Ashfolk have gathered in small communities in the Ocean of Ash, or left their former home lands forever. But some of them have chosen a lifestyle of scavenging and hunting. They seek buried treasures and either sell them or use them for their own advantage. Most Ashfolk Scavengers are peaceful (unless being attacked themselves). But there are those who aren't beyond taking treasures that still belong to a living person. The greatest dangers these individuals pose are their traps. Some Scavengers wait until they caught a victim, and go in for the easy kill. Others check their traps every few hours or days, and only approach if their prey seems to be dead.
