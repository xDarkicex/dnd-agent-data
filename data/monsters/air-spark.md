# Air Spark

**description**: Traits Minion. If the spark takes damage from an attack or as the result of a failed saving throw, their hit points are reduced to 0. If the spark takes damage from another effect, they die if the damage equals or exceeds their hit point maximum; otherwise they take no damage. Actions Wind Sweep (Group Attack). Melee Spell Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 slashing damage, and if this attack was made by more than one air spark against a Large or smaller creature, the sparks can move that creature up to 5 feet horizontally for each spark that joined the attack.
