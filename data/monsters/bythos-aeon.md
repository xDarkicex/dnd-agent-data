# Bythos Aeon

**description**: Aeon Aeons are paragons of law above all else. They embody opposing concepts or forces and are tasked with maintaining balance between those two things. A wide number of different types of aeons exist, from relatively harmless scouts and diplomats to incredibly powerful protectors of creation and oblivion. These extraplanar creatures are native to the lawful neutral outer plane, where they can be encountered within that reality’s vast cities and urban sprawls. In the Kingmaker Adventure Path, though, only the bythos aeon plays a role. Bythos Aeon The bythos is a guardian of space and time that seeks out those who misuse planar and temporal magic. A bythos is a roughly humanoid creature with four arms and a body made of swirling clouds and mist. Despite its appearance, its body feels like dry stone. A bythos seeks out paradoxes caused by irresponsible planar or dimensional travelers and repairs breaches where the barriers between the planes have become thin or damaged. If the mortals responsible for the damage remain in the area and can’t be convinced to cease their activities, the bythos has no qualms about removing the perpetrators from the situation. Traits Confusing Gaze. A creature that ends its turn within 30 feet of the bythos must make a DC 19 Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, it is confused until the end of its next turn, as per the spell of the same name. A creature that successfully saves is immune to this bythos's confusing gaze for the next 24 hours. This is a magical effect, and immunity or protection against being charmed apply. Innate Spellcasting. The bythos's spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 18). The bythos can innately cast the following spells, requiring no components: At will: dimension door, plane shift, slow 3/day each: counterspell, haste 1/day: imprisonment Magic Weapons. The bythos's weapon attacks are magical. Regeneration. The bythos regains 15 hit points at the start of its turn. Temporal Reversion (1/day). If the bythos fails a saving throw, it can choose to reroll the saving throw. If it does, it must use the new roll. Actions Multiattack. The bythos makes two slam attacks. Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 21 (3d8+8) bludgeoning damage plus 9 (2d8) cold damage. If a creature is hit by two or more of the bythos’s slam attacks in one turn, that creature must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw. On a failure, it ages 1d10 years and gains one level of exhaustion. On a successful save, the target is immune to the aging and exhaustion effects of the bythos’s slam attack for the next 24 hours.
