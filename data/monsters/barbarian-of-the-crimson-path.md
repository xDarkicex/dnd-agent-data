# Barbarian of the Crimson Path

**description**: Terrorizing the wilderness of Tolia, the barbarians of the Crimson Path are fearsome opponents to those unlucky enough to cross their path. They can hunt unlucky travelers to steal from them, to sacrifice them to their tribal blood gods, or just for fun. They are adapted to the harsh conditions of Tolia’s wilderness. On top of that, the fact that they can transform into a blood-seeking monster called the “crimson monster”, an even more dangerous and more endurant foe than the barbarian when harmed, makes them some of the most dangerous predators of the region.
