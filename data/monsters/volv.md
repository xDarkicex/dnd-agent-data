# Volv

**description**: Völv The völv guide the rival raiding clans without preventing their natural tendencies. They encourage raiders to test their skills as warriors, to raid one another, and to explore new territories but prevent all out conflict from erupting among them. By doing so, the völv ensure the raiders will be ready for Ragnarök.
