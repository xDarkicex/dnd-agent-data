# Honor Guard

**description**: Traits Guardian Stance. Each creature within 10 ft. of the honor guard that it perceives as an ally has +1 to its AC. Actions Multiattack. The honor guard makes two attacks with its Katana. Katana. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, Reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8 + 4) slashing damage. Creatures that take damage from this attack make a wisdom saving throw DC 16 and become provoked by the honor guard for 1 minute if they fail. Reactions Crescent Moon Defense (Recharge 4-6). When a creature no more than 5 ft. away misses an attack against the honor guard, it can use its reaction to make an attack on that creature with its Katana. If that creature is already provoked by the honor guard the attack has advantage and deals an additional 9 (2d8) damage.
