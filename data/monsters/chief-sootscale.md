# Chief Sootscale

**description**: Chief Sootscale’s role in Kingmaker is variable—he could simply be another kobold that the PCs must defeat in combat, or he could become an unexpected ally. In your games, he can certainly play similar roles, but as a kobold rogue you can also use his stats to represent an entire category of specialized and unusual robbers employed by a larger thieves’ guild. Traits Pack Tactics. Chief Sootscale has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of his allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn’t incapacitated. Sneak Attack (1/Turn). Chief Sootscale deals an extra 7 (2d6) damage when he hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of Chief Sootscale that isn’t incapacitated and he doesn’t have disadvantage on the attack roll. Sunlight Sensitivity. While in sunlight, Chief Sootscale has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight. Actions Multiattack. Sootscale makes two club attacks. Club. Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 2 (1d4) bludgeoning damage.
