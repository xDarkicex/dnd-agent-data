# Primal Wolf

**description**: Wolves A common creature in most lands, wolves did not pass into Grimnir during the Exodus, nor were they manifested when the world was created, as many other animals were. They were hidden away in the Underworld, nurtured by Hel. In recent years, they have been released, primarily by way of being given to the Whar clan.
