# Swarm Of Votary Cultists

**description**: Several dozen cultists attend Zamyra at all times. TRAITS Swarm: The swarm can occupy another creature’s space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Medium creature. The swarm can’t regain hit points or gain temporary hit points. ACTIONS Scimitars: Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 44 (12d6 + 2) slashing damage, or 23 (6d6 + 2) slashing damage if the swarm has half of hit points or fewer.
