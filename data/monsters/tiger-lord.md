# Tiger Lord

**description**: Nomad The Tiger Lords are the most notorious roving warriors to reside in the Stolen Lands, but other gatherings of nomadic people dwell throughout the region, particularly those hunters who prefer to make the most dangerous monsters of the wilds their prey. Tiger Lord The most powerful of the Tiger Lords lead larger groups of warriors that dwell in the uplands of Glenebon. These roving warlords have gathered together recently after being deluded by the cult of Gyronna, who tricked them into thinking one of their ancestors, Armag, has returned from death to lead them in a war. Traits Rage. The Tiger Lord can enter rage as a bonus action. While raging, the Tiger Lord has advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws, they deal an additional 2 damage with each attack, and they gain resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage. The rage lasts for 1 minute., or until the Tiger Lord falls unconscious, or if their turn ends and they haven’t either attacked a hostile creature or taken damage since their last turn. Reckless. At the start of their turn, the Tiger Lord can gain advantage on all melee weapon attack rolls during that turn, but attack rolls against them have advantage until the start of their next turn. Actions Multiattack. The barbarian makes two attacks. Battleaxe. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d8+5) slashing damage or 10 (1d10+5) slashing damage if held with two hands. Handaxe. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d6+5) slashing damage.
