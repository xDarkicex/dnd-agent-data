# Dire Corby

**description**: Dire corbies are denizens of the Underdark that resemble humanoid avians with brutish features. Although they have a measure of intelligence, these savage creatures barely have any societal structure and do not often use tools. They nest in underground cliff-side caves and normally hunt their prey by leaping down from ceilings or overhangs to rend targets with their claws. When attacking as a pack, they turn into a savage frenzy and become nearly suicidal. Traits Pack Frenzy. While it can see two allied creatures within 30 feet of it, the dire corby gains advantage on saving throws against being frightened and charmed, and all melee weapon attack rolls, but attacks rolls against it have advantage. Actions Multiattack. The dire corby makes one Beak attack and one Claws attack. Beak . Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage. Claws.
