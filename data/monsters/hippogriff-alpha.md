# Hippogriff Alpha

**description**: A hippogriff alpha is the leader of a hippogriff pack and is often the largest male in the group. Traits Keen Sight. The hippogriff has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight. Actions Multiattack. The hippogriff makes two attacks: one with its Beak and one with its Claws. Beak.
