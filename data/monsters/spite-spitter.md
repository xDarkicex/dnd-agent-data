# Spite Spitter

**description**: These foul creatures, former humanoids consumed by petty malice towards others, continue to cause havoc wherever they dwell much as they did in life. However, they are cowards at their core, taking foes by surprise and targeting only those perceived to be weaker than they. Occasionally two or three such undead work together, but rarely for long, as the inner hatred they feel for any other being, alive or dead, overrides the benefits of being in a team, with narrow-minded rivalries leading to nugatory name-calling that split the partners. If questioned, the fault of any disagreement is always laid at the door of the other.
