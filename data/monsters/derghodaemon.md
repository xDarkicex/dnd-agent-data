# Derghodaemon

**description**: Daemon Daemons are sinister neutral evil fiends who form from the most despicable of souls and personify the most horrible ways one can die. Natives to the neutral evil outer planes, daemons lack the strict order and regiment associated with devils, yet also lack the almost gleeful addiction to spreading chaos and mayhem common to demons. They are, many scholars posit, one of the purest forms of evil. Daemons as a whole cover a wide range of power, and many are associated with the legendary demigods known collectively as the Horsemen of the Apocalypse. Derghodaemon Derghodaemons represent death through unfathomable violence and being eaten alive. Derghodaemons look like walking masses of razor-sharp insect claws connected to equally menacing chitinous limbs. They don’t particularly represent a specific Horseman of the Apocalypse, and are often conjured by evil spellcasters to serve as guardians for important sites. Traits Chittering Screech. A derghodaemon can grind its mandibles and chitin plates together to form a distended and cacophonous scraping noise that can drive a mortal being insane. Anyone within 30 feet must succeed on a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw or be affected by a symbol spell ( insanity ). A creature that makes this save is immune to this derghodaemon’s screech for 24 hours. Cloud of Misery. A derghodaemon is surrounded at all times by an insect swarm (as the spell insect plague ) extending 5 feet from its space and moving with it without requiring concentration. The area surrounding the daemon is lightly obscured and acts as difficult terrain, as well as damaging all creatures adjacent to the daemon when it ends its movement. As a bonus action, it can concentrate to move the swarm away from itself up to 60 feet away, forming it into a 10-foot-radius sphere. It can move the swarm up to 60 feet each round thereafter as a bonus action to any point within 60 feet of itself. If it stops concentrating, the swarm disperses and reforms around the daemon at the beginning of its next turn. If the daemon takes 20 or more damage from an area effect while surrounded by the swarm, it is dispersed until the daemon uses a bonus action to reform it. Magic Resistance. A derghodaemon has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. See Invisible. A derghodaemon can see invisible creatures and objects as if they were invisible. It can see Ethereal creatures or objects, though they appear ghostly and translucent. Tearing Rend. If a derghodaemon hits a creature with 2 or more claws in the same round, it deals an additional 6 (1d8+2) slashing damage. As a bonus action, it can increase this bonus damage to 13 (2d8+4) slashing damage and cause the target to gain one level of exhaustion (as long as the target has no other levels of exhaustion). Actions Multiattack. The derghodaemon makes five claw attacks. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack. +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d8+4) slashing damage.
