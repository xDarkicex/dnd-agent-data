# Iara

**description**: These creatures are the cursed offspring of hags that mated with intelligent aquatic creatures. The iara hunt small bodies of water and feed on intelligent lifeforms that fall prey to their luring scent or spells. These creatures naturally feel inferior to hags and act subserviently towards them, offering their slaves and even their Treasure to them.
