# Skildpadder

**description**: The dwarves of the Underworld have a saying, “Rock or dwarf it doesn’t matter, avoid the path of a skildpadder.” Ravenous eaters, skildpadders consume or crush all in their path. The gargantuan beasts of burden not only transport enormous amounts of ore, but also hold processing facilities and even dwellings upon their backs. Bulette Ancestry. Though a skildpadder resembles a land tortoise, the dwarves that breed them claim the beasts are descended from bulettes. If true, little evidence of their ancestry remains in the skildpadder, except perhaps for their ability to consume inorganic rock.
