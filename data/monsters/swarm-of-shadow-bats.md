# Swarm of Shadow Bats

**description**: Normal bats rarely, if ever, form a true swarm. But when a great evil infects normal bats, they undergo a change in which they become twisted by that influence, a shadowy reflection of their former selves. Vampires, wraith lords, necromancers, even the ambient evil of the Dark Wood are all enough to corrupt bats into shadow bats. Influenced by Evil. A shadow bat swarm will respond to the commands of the entity that created it, or to any nearby source of dark energy sufficient to exert its will over the swarm.
