# Jade Kirin

**description**: Traits Reassembly Protocols. An hour after the jade kirin is destroyed, its pieces slowly begin reassembling themselves. The jade kirin is restored to life with 5 hit points. Regeneration. The jade kirin regains 5 hit points at the start of each of its turns. Actions Multiattack. The jade kirin makes one slam attack and one trample attack. Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d8 + 5) bludgeoning damage. Before making this attack, the jade kirin can move up to 15 feet if it ends this movement within five feet of a hostile creature. If the attack hits, the target must make a DC 18 Strength saving throw. On a failure, it is pushed 10 feet away from the jade kirin and is knocked prone. Trample. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, range 5 ft., one target. Hit: 19 (4d6 + 5) bludgeoning damage. If this attack hits a prone creature, the creature takes an additional 10 (3d6) bludgeoning damage.
