# Squamp

**description**: Squamps are lemures that have started on the process to become imps, yet aren’t fully grown. They do not have a fully grown imp’s ability to assume alternate forms or commune questions. They are about 12 inches high and weigh about 2 pounds. A squamp’s natural weapons, as well as any weapons it wields, are treated as evil-aligned and lawful-aligned for the purpose of overcoming damage reduction.
