# Earth Mephit

**description**: When a wagon driver curses muddy roadways or a farmer complains on the unyielding soil, an earth mephit is conjured into being. Plodding and humorless, the mephit makes great effort to aid the mortal who summoned it, but often the elemental is too dim to know weal from woe. The farmer finds rows plowed through his barn and home; the wagon driver finds rocky, uneven roads more detrimental than muddy ones.
