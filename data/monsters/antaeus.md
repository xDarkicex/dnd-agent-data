# Antaeus

**description**: Antaeus An antaeus is a hulking humanoid, typically with dark skin and long, shaggy hair and beard, that love wrestling. They tend to avoid open battles but look for opportunities to challenge enemies to a one-on-one wrestling match, sometimes for stakes of gold but more often with life and death on the line. They claim a divine lineage to Mother Earth herself, Gaea, and use the skulls of enemies they crush with their mighty thews to lay the foundation of shrines or temples in the name of their divine parent or parents. They often waylay travelers, challenging them to feats of strength in exchange for free passage or even a reward. In battle, they tend to focus on a single opponent, locking them up with their grappling prowess until they submit, fall unconscious, or die. An antaeus is the size of a tall, broad man, usually close to or over 7 feet in height and covered in muscles, often oiling their skin to emphasize their physique.
