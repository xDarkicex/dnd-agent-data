# War Dog

**description**: Traits Keen Hearing and Smell. The war dog has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell. Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack. +3 to hit, 5 ft. reach, one target. Hit: 5 (1d8+1) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 11 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
