# Reju Ume

**description**: She/Her Reju Ume, the daughter of Lord Jikai, was framed in a shrine desecration scandal when she was only eleven years old. At the insistence of Toshio, the court ritualist, she was banned for life from not only Closed Shell Castle, but all of Twin Blessings Village. Her first few years were spent in isolation as she learned how to survive in the forest. After suffering a grave injury to her right leg, Ume learned to adapt and thrive by relying on her instincts and ingenuity. With practiced balance, she walks with her left leg and a cane strapped to her right arm. At the age of fifteen, she formed a band of forest hermits that live in peace and look out for one another. She is an adult now and is confident in her abilities. Ume knows Closed Shell Castle belongs to her family and plans to reclaim her birthright once her father dies, or sooner, if necessary to keep Reju Toshio, the man who framed her, out of power. Traits Tree Movement. When surrounded by trees, Reju Ume can use her action to climb up to 10 ft in a tree and move another 30 ft through them. Moving through trees doesn't count as difficult terrain for her as she uses hook tools and spends much of her time in the trees. Reju Ume has advantage on Stealth rolls when moving through trees. Cunning Action. Can use a bonus action to Dash, Disengage, or Hide. Actions Katana. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) slashing damage. Crutch Sweep. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage. The creature hit must succeed on a DC 12 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. If they are knocked prone, Ume can immediately make a katana attack against the creature as a bonus action. Dart. Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, range 20/60 ft., 1 target. Hit: 5 (1d4 + 3) piercing damage.
