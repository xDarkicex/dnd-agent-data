# Funglet Granger

**description**: Grangers, as can be surmised from their name, are the rangers of the funglet world. They roam far and wide as the eyes and ears of the garlands and gleaners (and even, it is said, some fungiants) assessing the health and vitality of their fungi forests and reporting back to the leaders of the colony. Grangers are often the first contact outsiders have with funglets. While, like all funglets, they tend mirror the social queues of outsiders, grangers are more cautious, observing a group for hours or even days before approaching them. Poison Specialist. When a funglet settlement is under prolonged assault by a specific foe, grangers develop potent poisons to target that enemy and no other. The change from standard funglet poison to something more targeted takes a full week, but once in place, the change lasts until the last foe is defeated, at which time the poison type reverts to the generic funglet variant.
