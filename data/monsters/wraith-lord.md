# Wraith Lord

**description**: A wraith lord is a wraith that has endured existence for an unimaginable length of time. Their evil is so great they’ve regained some ability to manifest physically when they so desire. Strong of will in life, in undeath the wraith lord clings to that which it once commanded, be it noble cause or religious belief, though with twisted logic that justifies its actions as only a corrupt monarch can devise.
