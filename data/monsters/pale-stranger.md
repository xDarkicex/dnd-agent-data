# Pale Stranger

**description**: A pale stranger appears much as it did in life, though desiccated and obviously undead with a pistol in each hand, its garb dusty and weathered and smelling of decay. A pale stranger stands 6-1/2 feet tall and weighs 130 pounds. Undying Revenge. Sometimes death itself cannot come between a fallen soldier, security guard, or sniper and its final revenge. When a sniper or marksman is slain by a hated enemy or murdered before it can achieve vengeance against a hated foe, the anger and wrath can animate its remains as a vengeful undead monstrosity. When a pale stranger first rises, it seeks out the source of its anger to finish the job—thereafter, it wanders the desolate parts of the world looking for new victims to vent its unending rage upon. Mockery of Life. Rare pale strangers can hide their undead nature and appear as they did in life using veil as a constant spell-like ability.
