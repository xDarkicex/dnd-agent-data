# Seeker

**description**: Seeker Seekers are the antithesis of deathdreamers. These magicians seek out those that have strayed too far into the dreaming realm of the primal. Fearing that these dreamers might unleash horrors similar to what befell the Lost Lands, the seekers strike those rival magicians down.
