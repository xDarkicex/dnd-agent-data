# Lord Beremo Fizzmos

**description**: Lord Beremo Fizzmos is a red half-dragon gnome from a young noble clan in Xantharos. He is an aboleth bloodline sorcerer and an accomplished enchanter, known for his great power and feared even by his own clan members. A few decades ago, he made a pact with a cabal of aboleths in the Silent Pearls, allowing his masters to infuse him with their essence and bodywarp him for more power. As one of their most powerful agents, he serves the aboleths without question and executes their bidding ruthlessly. Lord Beremo Fizzmos exudes a sense of superiority and entitlement, looking down on those he considers beneath him, like the spellslave sorcerer apprentices he trains in the ways of his aberrant masters. He is often cold and calculating, using his powers of manipulation and enchantment to bend others to his will.
