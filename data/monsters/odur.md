# Odur

**description**: Odur Odur is a humble man, used to stooping to hide his height. He is grateful to the other Risa for taking him in as an infant and serves his community faithfully. He is also proficient with woodworking tools and loves to carve small animals from the wood he collects on his treks into the woods. His skin is a striking blue, but he often wears a long-sleeved tunic and a cloak to hide his features. Roleplaying Odur Brave. Despite his preference to avoid competing in what he considers a wasteful arena battle, Odur is brave and resilient. He has often served as a scout, encroaching into the deep wilds to ensure nothing threatens Farlanding. Helpful. Odur helps out whenever he can, even if he does not know the folks he is helping. If he sees a job to be done, he does it.
