# Coral Guardian

**description**: Coral Guardian A sign of merfolk ingenuity is the coral guardian. Granted sentience through an elemental aether spirit, the coral guardian is made from the reefs and flora surrounding the astral merfolk’s Constellation Isles home. They are peaceful guardians unless faced with an outsider to their community.
