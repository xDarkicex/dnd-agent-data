# Swarm of Spidercrows

**description**: Of average lethality, the spidercrow is a monstrous bird native to Penumbria, where the Malavita clique have adopted it for their coat-of-arms. Its appearance combines crow-like and arachnid features. Its rostrum beak is its deadliest weapon, as it hides strong jaws and chelicerae bristling with small saw-toothed fangs. While the larger specimens can reach the size of hawks or vultures, most broods are made up of organized swarms of tiny creatures that hunt together. Traits Spider Climb . The swarm can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check. Swarm . The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a tiny creature. The swarm can't regain hit points or gain temporary hit points. Web Sense . While in contact with a web, the swarm knows the exact location of any other creature in contact with the same web. Web Walker . The swarm ignores movement restrictions caused by webbing. actions Bites . Melee Weapon Attack : +5 to hit, reach 0 ft., one target in the swarm's space. Hit : 7 (2d6) piercing damage, or 3 (1d6) piercing damage if the swarm has half of its hit points or fewer. The target must make a DC 10 Constitution saving throw, taking 7 (2d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
