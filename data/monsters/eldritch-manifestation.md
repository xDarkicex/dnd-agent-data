# Eldritch Manifestation

**description**: Forged from the primal essence of a distinct school of magic, the eldritch manifestation is a monstrous embodiment of terror for all wielders of magic. This relentless aberration is impervious to its own school of magic and siphons the arcane power of any spellcaster it strikes with its colossal arm-like appendages. Additionally, it is a boundless wellspring of spells, but its capabilities are limited to the school of magic from which it originated.
