# Swarm of Owls

**description**: Actions Talons. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: (4d6) slashing damage plus (2d6)slashing damage. 4d6 slashing damage, or 7 2d6) slashing damage if the swarm has half its hp or fewer. Screech (Recharge 5-6). The swarm of owls releases a screech in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw. On a failure, a creature takes 18 (4d8) thunder damage and is deafened until the end of its next turn. On a success, a creature takes half the damage and isn’t deafened.
