# Shadowblood Priestess of the Old

**description**: The Old gods, the pantheon of deities ruling over the Shadowblood Elves, have many clerics. Most of them serve a single god, but there are also servants of the entire pantheon. These priests and priestesses of the Old are often called upon in times of conflict within Shadowblood society. This makes them both well-respected, and particularly hated by those unhappy with how they resolve a conflict. While the gods do not favor any gender, more female Shadowblood Elves are attracted to serving the entire pantheon. About three-fourths of these clerics are female, but their male counterparts (and those in the spectrum in between) are just as respected as the female clerics. Priestesses of the Old are often sent into war zones to bring terror and fear to the enemies of the Shadowblood Elves. Just as often, they are sent on individual missions, often accompanied by monstrous aberrations willingly serving the gods. The deities whisper horrible secrets into the minds of these clerics as they pray or meditate. The more powerful among them have unique abilities allowing them to bend reality itself.
