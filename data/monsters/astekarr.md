# Astekarr

**description**: The chieftain of the minotaurs of Kirakku Island fights like a ferocious beast when it comes to protecting his tribe. He is both feared and held in awe by his kin, who blindly follow his commands. Astekarr is aggressive, fearless, and unpredictable in his actions and considers other members of his tribe, even his younger brother, as his personal servants. He is one of the greatest warriors that has ever lived in the history of the Rockhorn minotaurs, with a legendary gore attack that can crack even the hardest of stones.
