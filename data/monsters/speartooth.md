# Speartooth

**description**: The smilodon Speartooth is the type of legendary creature that many trophy hunters spend their whole lives searching for—and die trying to kill. These stats could be used to represent a creature the PCs are sent to hunt, or alternatively, asked to protect from dangerous poachers. You can also use these stats for a species of hearty saber-toothed-tigers, perhaps pets bred for their ferocity by a clan of even more deadly giants. Traits Keen Smell. Speartooth has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Pounce. If Speartooth moves at least 20 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a claw attack on the same turn, that target must succeed on a DC 16 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. If the target is prone, Speartooth can make one bite attack against it as a bonus action. Actions Multiattack. Speartooth makes three melee attacks, one with its bite and two with its claws. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 17 (2d10+6) piercing damage. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 20 (4d6+6) slashing damage.
