# Tardo, Lobisomem

**description**: The tardo is a troublesome being with the appearance of a strange urchin. It delights in changing shape, misleading travelers, and causing nightmares. Although usually more of a pest than an immediate danger, like many otherworldly creatures, they can be perilous in anger. It is said tardos sometimes abduct young children, turning them into tardos themselves. They will target a child younger than seven years old. The tardo will make contact with the child, sometimes in a different shape or in dreams. Ideally, no adult is aware of this initial contact. When the child is ready, the tardo enchants them and takes them away. If the child is not recovered in time, usually seven years relative to the Material Plane, they may become a tardo themselves. Wolfling. The mischievous and cruel tardo can in time become something more fearsome. The lobisomem is a baron among their kind, capable of taking on the form of a dire wolf.
