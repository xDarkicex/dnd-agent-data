# Nabasu

**description**: Demon Demons are cruel and chaotic fiends who form from the souls of sinful mortals who have been consigned to the nightmare realm of the chaotic evil outer planes. As fecund as they are destructive, demons remain a favorite type of minion of many a cult. However, those who mistake demons’ predilection for chaos as evidence that these creatures are mindless forces of entropy often doom themselves by underestimating these creatures’ capacity for subtle manipulation, trickery, and patience when it comes to seeing long-term goals of devastation play out over the passage of time. Nabasu A nabasu is a cruel monster formed from the sinful souls of evil gluttons, particularly from cannibals, blood-drinkers, and those who prefer to savor the tang of undead flesh. This demon appears somewhat like an emaciated gargoyle in appearance, complete with large batlike wings and jaws full of sharp fangs. Its tiny eyes glow with an unsettling yellow hue. Traits Death-Stealing Gaze. The nabasu exudes an aura of death in a 30-foot radius. Creatures that end their turns in this aura must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw. On a failure, they gain a level of exhaustion, and the nabasu gains 10 temporary hit points. A creature that dies while subject to the effects of this exhaustion level rises as a ghoul at the next midnight. Innate Spellcasting. The nabasu’s spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: 3/day each: black tentacles, hold monster, vampiric touch Magic Resistance. The nabasu has advantage on saving throws against spells and magical effects. Actions Multiattack. The nabasu makes two melee attacks: one with its bite, and one with its claws. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d8+7) piercing damage and 7 (2d6) necrotic damage. Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d8+7) slashing damage and 7 (2d6) necrotic damage. Teleport. The nabasu magically teleports, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying, up to 100 feet to an unoccupied space it can see. Summon Demon (1/Day). The nabasu chooses a demon to summon and attempts a magical summoning. The nabasu has a 50 percent chance of summoning 1d4 shadows, or 1 barlgura. The summoned demon appears in an unoccupied space within 60 feet of its summoner, acts as an ally of its summoner, and can’t summon other demons. It remains for 1 minute, until it or its summoner dies, or until its summoner dismisses it as an action.
