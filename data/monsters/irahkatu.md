# Irahkatu

**description**: Most of the ahuizotls (badger-like monsters with grasping hand-like talons at the tip of their tails) that lurk in the waters of Hooktongue Slough are typical members of their kind, but Irahkatu is a particularly powerful (and cruel) monster. You can use these stats as a baseline for an entire species of “greater ahuizotls” in your game. Traits Keen Senses. Irahkatu has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell or sight. Voice Imitation. Irahkatu can mimic the sounds of a person in distress by making a Charisma (Deception) check to lie. Irahkatu has advantage on this check. Actions Multiattack. Irahkatu makes three attacks: one with its bite, one with its claws, and one with its tail claw. If Irahkatu has a Medium or smaller creature grappled with its tail claw, it replaces that attack with Disfigure. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (1d8+6) piercing damage plus 5 (1d10) poison damage. Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (3d6+6) slashing damage. Tail Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (3d6+6) slashing damage. If the target is Medium sized or smaller it is grappled (escape DC 19) and it must succeed on a DC 19 Strength saving throw or be pulled 5 feet toward Irahkatu, or knocked prone if already adjacent to Irahkatu. Disfigure. Irahkatu mauls a Medium or smaller creature it has grappled with its tail claw. The target must make a DC 19 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the grappled creature takes 24 (7d6) slashing damage and has disadvantage on Dexterity-based ability checks, saving throws, and attacks for the next 24 hours. If the creature failed its saving throw by 5 or more, it is blinded for the next 24 hours, or until it has fully healed. On a successful saving throw, the grappled creature takes half the damage and is subjected to no other effect. Whirlwind of Claws (Recharge 5–6). Irahkatu makes a claw attack against each creature of its choice within 5 feet of it.
