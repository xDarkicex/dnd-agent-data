# Zi Liang

**description**: Zi Liang is a devout worshiper of Chauntea, the Earth Mother. She has considerably less faith in Goldenfields' defenders, so she patrols the temple-farm during her off-duty hours. Ideal. "If we faithfully tend to our gardens and our fields, Chauntea will smile upon us." Bond. "Goldenfields is the breadbasket of the North. People depend on its safety and prosperity, and I'll do what must be done to protect it." Flaw. "I don't trust authority. I do what my heart says is right." Traits Unarmored Defense. While Zi is wearing no armor and wielding no shield, her AC includes her Wisdom modifier. Actions Multiattack. Zi makes two melee attacks. Quarterstaff. Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d6 + 1) bludgeoning damage, or 5 (1d8 + 1) bludgeoning damage if used with two hands. Sling. Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) bludgeoning damage. Zi carries twenty sling stones.
