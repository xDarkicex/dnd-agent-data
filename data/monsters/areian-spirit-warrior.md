# Areian Spirit Warrior

**description**: All Areian Elves feel a strong bond towards nature, but for some of them, the connection is deeply spiritual. They hear the whispers of the spirit world, and become defenders of their home lands. These Areian Spirit Warriors are strongly allied with the fairy world and similar supernatural entities. They value freedom, life, happiness and compassion, and are fierce opponents of anyone threatening these ideals. And they do not fight alone: Areian Spirit Warriors can call upon the powers of their spirit allies to defeat their enemies.
