# Golfu

**description**: Golfu Rare and fearsome creatures, golfus are enormous, physically powerful birds that are feared for their ferocity as much as they are for their deadly screech. Four-legged, with a wingspan of approximately ten feet, their appearance is somewhat similar to an enormous owl. They have large, piercing eyes that are always alert, and their body is covered in dark, coarse feathers. Their sharp, curved beaks are designed for tearing flesh and cracking bones, and their powerful talons are capable of inflicting deadly wounds. Stony Screech. Perhaps the most feared aspect of the golfu is their ability to petrify their enemies with their screech. When attacked or threatened, they make a loud screeching sound that can turn their foes into crude granite statues. These statues appear mostly as they were in life, with a crude, rough-hewn texture, and a frozen, lifeless expression. This ability is especially potent against other flying creatures, causing their petrified form to crash to the ground, often shattering on impact. Territorial Nesting. Golfus are territorial creatures that fiercely defend their nests and their young. They build their nests in high, rocky crevices or in the tall grasses of mountainous regions. golfu eggs are large and speckled, and it takes four months for them to hatch. They have been known to attack humans and other intelligent creatures who venture too close to their nests or their young.
