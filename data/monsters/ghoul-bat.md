# Ghoul Bat

**description**: This bat has an emaciated, three-foot-long torso and a head that looks like skin stretched over bone. Its jaws are unnaturally distended, and its mouth is full of needle-like teeth. Ghoul bats are popular messengers and pets amongst darakhul and can be found both in colonies and alone throughout the Ghoul Imperium.
