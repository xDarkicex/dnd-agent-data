# Captain Scarbelly

**description**: A terror of the seas, more than one vessel has increased their speed by hurling cargo overboard upon seeing the tusked skull on red background of the Bloody Vengeance. Scarbelly is hungry and vicious, and slakes his taste for blood on those ships he takes on the high seas. Possessions. Captain Scarbelly has a +1 battleaxe, a chain shirt, five gold rings (worth a total of 150 gp), and a money pouch with 50 gp.
