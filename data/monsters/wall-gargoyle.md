# Wall Gargoyle

**description**: These gargoyles defend the walls of Drakkenheim and have the following additional traits: Senses truesight 120 ft. Faultless Tracker. The creature that caused the gargoyle to animate becomes its quarry. The gargoyle knows the direction and distance to its quarry as long the quarry remains within Drakkenheim or until the gargoyle is destroyed. Keen Sight. The gargoyle has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight. Magic Weapons. The gargoyle’s weapon attacks are magical. Rejuvenation. If the wall gargoyle is destroyed, it regains all its hit points in 1 hour unless a disintegrate spell is cast on its remains.
