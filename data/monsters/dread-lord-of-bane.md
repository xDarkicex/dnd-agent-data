# Dread Lord of Bane

**description**: Traits Aura of Dread. A creature hostile to the dread lord that starts its turn within 20 feet of the dread lord must succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw, unless the dread lord is incapacitated. On a failed save, the target is frightened until the start of its next turn. While frightened in this way, the target can't perform any actions except move up to half its speed. If a creature's saving throw is successful, the creature is immune to the dread lord's Aura of Dread for the next 24 hours. Legendary Resistance (3/Day). If the dread lord fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead. Actions Multiattack. The dread lord makes two Morningstar attacks or uses Dire Bolt twice. Morningstar. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage plus 13 (3d8) psychic damage and the target must succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened until the end of its next turn. Dire Bolt. Ranged Spell Attack: +10 to hit, range 60 ft., one creature. Hit: 18 (3d6 + 5) psychic damage and the target must succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened until the end of the dread lord's next turn. Dreadful Word (Recharge 5–6). The dread lord utters a terrifying word of power. Each creature of the dread lord's choice within 30 feet of it must succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 28 (8d6) psychic damage and is frightened for 1 minute. If the target is already frightened, it becomes paralyzed while frightened. On a successful save, the target takes half as much damage and isn't frightened. The target can make a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself with a success. Spellcasting. The dread lord casts one of the following spells using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 18, +10 to hit with spell attacks): At will: guidance, mending, thaumaturgy, toll the dead (4d8 or 4d12) 2/day each: command, hold person, spirit guardians 1/day each: antimagic field, banishment, flame strike, gate, harm Legendary Actions The dread lord can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The dread lord regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn. Attack. The dread lord makes a Morningstar attack or uses Dire Bolt. Move. The dread lord moves up to half its speed. Cast a Spell (Costs 2 Actions). The dread lord uses Spellcasting.
