# Blood Cultist

**description**: Traits Spellcaster. The blood cultist uses Intelligence as its spellcasting ability. The DC for its spells is 12, and the attack bonus is +4. Actions Sacrificial Dagger. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, one target, reach 5 ft.. Hit: 5 (1d6+2) slashing damage. This damage counts as 3 higher for determining if it inflicts an Injury. Painful Bolt. The blood cultist casts painful bolt * . Ranged Spell Attack: +4 to hit, range 30 ft., one creature. Hit: 2 (1d4) psychic damage, and the target’s Injury threshold is reduced by 5 (to a minimum of 5) until they receive an Injury or until the end of the blood cultist’s next turn, whichever comes sooner. Vile Incantation (2/Day). The blood cultist casts vile incantation *. One creature the blood cultist touches must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or take 7 (2d6) force damage and be inflicted with a core Injury of the blood cultist’s choice. This Injury must be of the lowest severity the creature can gain (Setback I for PCs, Dangerous II for monsters and NPCs). A successful save negates the Injury and reduces the damage by half. Any creature immune to polymorph effects, such as a shapechanger, or not made of organic matter has advantage on the saving throw. Any creature that is incorporeal is not affected by this spell. This spell cannot inflict an Injury if the creature already has one of the severity that would be inflicted. Bonus Actions Blood Infusion. The blood cultist infuses their next spell with power from their own blood. The blood cultist gains an Injury of their choice, and that spell's DC or attack bonus increases by 2, and it deals an additional 7 (2d6) damage.
