# Xana

**description**: Xanas are water spirits that dwell near natural pools, springs, and lakes. They are said to represent the lifegiving nature of water and can be prideful, living for thousands of years. They are nocturnal, often shapeshifting into the form of blackbirds to explore the surrounding territory unwatched, though their eyes are always keen and bright with intelligence. While xanas can be kind, some are more malicious, stealing from travelers or exchanging treasures for worthless trinkets, and their sultry voice is eerily alluring. They are even known to sneak into homes and exchange fey changelings for the children of mortals, though they can be capricious and may be as likely to bring healing or boons unlooked for in times of trouble. Aloja. Some xanas are as benevolent as they are beautiful, bringing prosperity to the lands and waters where they dwell. They may give generous gifts to those they observe doing good for their fellows or finding lost items and bestowing them on travelers through their domains.
