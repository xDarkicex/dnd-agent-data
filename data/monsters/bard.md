# Bard

**description**: Bards are versatile spellcasters and performers who use their charismatic abilities to weave magic through music and verse. They possess a wide array of spells, ranging from charming and healing magic to divination and illusions. Bards are not just spellcasters but also masters of inspiration who possess a captivating blend of musical talent, storytelling, and magical abilities. They often travel far and wide, using their artistry to entertain and inspire audiences, weaving tales of heroism and wonder.
