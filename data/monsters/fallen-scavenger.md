# Fallen Scavenger

**description**: Traits Join the Fallen. Whenever the scavenger reduces a creature to 0 hit points, inflicts an Injury, or scores a critical hit, all creatures within 120 feet that can hear or see the target of its attack must succeed on a DC 8 Wisdom saving throw or gain a Mindscar. A creature who gains three Mindscars begins to lose its mind. During its next long rest, it will begin to scar its flesh with whatever tools are available to it. Once its rest is completed, it becomes one of the Fallen. Mindscars can be removed by any process that can remove a curse, although mental trauma will still remain. If the mental trauma is overcome, the Mindscars are removed as well. Relish the Pain. Whenever the scavenger suffers an Injury, they gain a +1 bonus to damage rolls until the Injury is removed (up to their proficiency bonus), and gain temporary hit points equal to ½ the threshold of the Injury gained. The scavenger never has damage loss regardless of its hit point tier for Injury thresholds, and always suffers an Injury when it takes enough damage to become Injured (the Injury it gains is chosen by the damage dealer). Actions Rusty Dagger. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, one target, reach 5 feet. Hit: 4 (1d4+2) slashing damage, and the target must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or gain a special Injury of Setback I severity, "Infected", until it finishes a short or long rest. While Injured in this way, its Injury thresholds are reduced by 5, and it has disadvantage on saving throws against disease and poison. Scavenge the Dead (recharge 6). The scavenger looks over a corpse for the ripest, most necrotic tissue and consumes it. The scavenger gains 10 temporary hit points and a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls for 1 minute. The scavenger gains these bonuses regardless of freshness as long as there is flesh left to consume. A scavenger can not use this ability on the same corpse twice.
