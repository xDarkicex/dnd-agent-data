# Piero Capponi

**description**: Piero Capponi (born 1447) and his wife Nicola Capponi da Neri This middle-aged couple of noble heritage are among the inner circle of the Medici allies. The Capponi are one of the traditional wool merchant clans and are also involved in banking. Piero Capponi has traveled extensively as ambassador for Lorenzo de’ Medici and is one of the most respected men of his class. He is not a member of the Cavalieri di Platone but very sympathetic to their cause. There is just one small problem for Piero Capponi: He is well aware of Piero de’ Medici’s inability to defend the position of the Medici family should Lorenzo die, and is therefore carefully monitoring the situation – and prepared to change sides if necessary. The couple will nevertheless openly speak out against the schemes of Savonarola who demands that more power be given to the middle classes – a group that is much too undefined for the established elite. Piero and Nicola are the most courteous hosts, but might come over as quite arrogant in conversation with someone who is not of their status. Nicola is a patron of the arts and immensely well versed in philosophy and poetry. She is aiming for an invite to Ficino’s Neoplatonic academy, but so far, this group has only been open for men. This she resents and tries to change by supporting Ficino in his endeavors. Piero is tall, with a fine roman nose and prominent chin. His fair hair crowns a balding head held high and proud. His wife Nicola is almost as tall as he. A cascade of red hair arranged in the latest fashion, a voluptuous figure and of course the most exquisite jewellery and dress make her an impressive sight. Traits Spellcasting. Piero is an 8th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 12, +4 to hit with spell attacks). Piero knows the following bard spells: Cantrips (at will): mage hand, mending, true strike 1st level (4 slots): comprehend languages, disguise self, identify, unseen servant 2nd level (3 slots): calm emotions, detect thoughts, locate animals or plants 3rd level (3 slots): clairvoyance, sending 4th level (2 slots): dimension door, phantasmal killer Actions Multiattack. Piero makes two weapon attacks. Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8 + 2) slashing damage.
