# Heir of Entropy

**description**: As inheritors of the bloodline of chaos, these sorcerers are true manifestations of entropy, bending the natural order of reality around them and destroying matter with their touch. These entropists are true believers in the power of their heritage and fanatically strive to bring about the eventual ruin of everything into chaos. In Aglarion, the heirs of entropy share the bloodline of Lord Ziademos Gorso, which grants them the sorcerous powers of the Elemental Chaos.
