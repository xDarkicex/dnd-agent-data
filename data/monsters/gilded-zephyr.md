# Gilded Zephyr

**description**: Gilded zephyrs are tiny Celestials spawned on the higher planes. Said to be the heartwarming result of a love affair between the God of the Oceans and the God of the Moon, a gilded zephyr looks similar to a goldfish, except it has feathered, fin-like wings that give it the ability to fly. Beacon of Truth Gilded zephyrs have an inherent ability to slightly alter the fate of creatures and events around it. Gilded zephyrs can also sense lies, which has made their companionship sought by court officials, merchants, and bureaucrats as a way to ensure honesty and transparency in diplomatic affairs. Cyclic Memory A testament to their divine nature, gilded zephyrs' minds are cleansed during each full moon. Their memories fade away – some scholars believe they're transferred to a divine realm to be collected by some god or another – while the traits and opinions they helped form still remain. Playful and Curious Gilded zephyrs possess a playful spirit that sometimes draw them to the material world where they eagerly explore new environments and seek exciting encounters. Gilded zephyrs are careful creatures and they often seek the companionship of stronger allies that share their lust for adventure. It can be a bit hard to read the gilded zephyr, as it is about as expressive as a goldfish. Unless, of course, you start lying to it – you'll know straight away how it feels about that! – the Wanderer
