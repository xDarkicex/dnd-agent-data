# Unit of Dark Blood Sahuagin

**description**: Dark blood sahuagin were created by the infusion of the Dark Star’s blood into the unhatched fish eggs of the sahuagin tribe of Rakhix Gharthok Kol’jah. Sahuagin born from these eggs bear the traits of the solar of darkness - they can see through even magical darkness and their bodies are infused with necrotic energies. The sahuagin swarm from their spawning grounds, raiding the coastal cities of the Isles of Xantharos to feed on flesh and blood.
