# Foras

**description**: Foras is a powerful sorcerer devoted to the pursuit of bringing the Outer God Yog-Sothoth into this world. You can use these stats for any sort of mysterious leader of a cult or secret society. Remember that as a sorcerer, his powers aren’t intrinsically tied to a specific deity or even to a single faith. If you switch the object of his devotion or obsession, you should also consider removing or replacing his Time Siphon and Accelerated abilities with those that are more appropriate thematically. Traits Outside of Time. Foras exists outside of time; he doesn’t age, doesn’t need food or drink to survive, and can’t be affected by the haste or slow spells unless he chooses to be. Spellcasting. Foras is a 19th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 19, +11 to hit with spell attacks), and he needs only verbal components to cast its spells. He has the following cleric spells prepared: Cantrips (at will): guidance, light, sacred flame 1st level (4 slots): bless, command, sanctuary 2nd level (3 slots): blindness/deafness, silence, spiritual weapon 3rd level (3 slots): dispel magic, mass healing word, sending 4th level (3 slots): banishment, guardian of faith, locate creature 5th level (3 slots): flame strike, greater restoration 6th level (2 slots): blade barrier 7th level (1 slot): fire storm 8th level (1 slot): antimagic field 9th level (1 slot): mass heal Special Equipment. Foras has a quarterstaff +2 and magical traveler’s clothes (as bracers of defense ). Unseen Sight. Foras has the ability to see the truth of the world, granting them darkvision and truesight to a distance of 60 feet, as long as they remain in a region under Yog-Sothoth’s influence. Actions Staff. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d6) bludgeoning damage, or 4 (1d8) bludgeoning damage if used in two hands to make a melee attack. Legendary Actions Foras can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature’s turn. Foras regains spent legendary actions at the start of his turn. Detect. Foras makes a Wisdom (Perception) check. Accelerated. Foras moves up to his speed . This movement does not provoke opportunity attacks. Time Siphon (Costs 2 Actions). Foras warps time around him in a 60-foot radius. Any creature that is not a construct or undead that moves into the area for the first time or ends its turn there must succeed on a DC 19 Wisdom saving throw or become slowed (as the slow spell) until the end of its next round. Worshippers of Yog-Sothoth are immune to this effect.
