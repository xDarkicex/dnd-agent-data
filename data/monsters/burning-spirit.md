# Burning Spirit

**description**: Unique to the world-chain between Ashandri and Va'Laeth, Burning Spirits are rumored to be creations of Marisha Veilbreaker herself. They are undead entities that have been soldiers of Va'Laeth before they died on the world-chain. Now, they hunt any living denizen of their former home world. They are known to occasionally attack other travelers, as well. Any living being originating from Va'Laeth that gets killed by one of these creatures turns into a Burning Spirit themselves. Everybody else seems to be immune to this effect. For some reason, the Burning Spirits stay clear of Marisha Veilbreaker and anyone in her company.
