# Methane Crab

**description**: Methane crabs are colossal crustaceans with shells composed of ice. Almost indistinguishable from the icebergs and glaciers that surround them, they are vicious predators that employ various highly specialized techniques to catch their unlucky prey. Explosive Tactics Using bacteria in their shells and skin, methane crabs can convert methane into nutrients. They also exude dense clouds of methane, which they use to suffocate and disorient prey, though at the risk of self-combustion if the gas is exposed to open flames.
