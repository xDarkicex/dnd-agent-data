# Great Gray Owl

**description**: Great gray owls are stealthy predators, and the largest of the non-giant varieties of owls. Unlike other owls, great grays aren't territorial — with the exception of females raising young — and don't flush or spook when other creatures approach. Rather, they remain still on their low perches, often going overlooked.
