# Sacred Fist

**description**: Traits Unarmored Defense. While the sacred fist is wearing no armor and wielding no shield, its AC includes its Wisdom modifier. Actions Multiattack. The sacred fist makes three Unarmed Strike or three Dart attacks. Or it can make one Unarmed Strike and cast one spell that takes 1 action to cast. Unarmed Strike. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) bludgeoning damage + 4 (1d8) radiant, fire, or necrotic damage (if Sacred Flames is active). If the target is a creature, the sacred fist can choose one of the following additional effects: The target must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or drop one item it is holding (sacred fist’s choice). The target must succeed on a DC 14 Dexterity saving throw or be knocked prone. The target must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or be stunned until the end of the sacred fist’s next turn. Dart. Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d4 + 4) piercing damage. Spellcasting. The sacred fist casts one of the following spells using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 14, +6 to hit with spell attacks): At will: guidance, light, sacred flame (2d8), thaumaturgy 2/day each: bless, cure wounds, spirit guardians 1/day each: banishment, dispel magic, spirit shroud Bonus Actions Inner Armor (1/Day). The sacred fist invokes its inner tranquility, gaining advantage on all Saving Throws. The effect lasts for 1 minute. Sacred Flames (3/Day). The sacred fist causes its hands and feet to be wreathed in divine energy. Each hit of its unarmed strikes deal an extra 4 (1d8) radiant (good), fire (any), or necrotic (evil) damage. The effect lasts for 1 minute. Reactions Deflect Missile. In response to being hit by a ranged weapon attack, the sacred fist deflects the missile. The damage it takes from the attack is reduced by 16 (1d10 + 11). If the damage is reduced to 0, the sacred fist catches the missile if it’s small enough to hold in one hand and the sacred fist has a hand free.
