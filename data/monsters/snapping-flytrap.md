# Snapping Flytrap

**description**: Flytrap Flytraps are enormous versions of their more common, smaller kin—carnivorous plants that retain their name more out of tradition than anything, as they are more than capable of feeding on prey much larger than flies. Snapping Flytrap The snapping flytrap is a ravenous, carnivorous plant that is quick to bite at any creature that passes by. A much smaller variant than the more deadly giant flytrap, a snapping flytrap typically has a long stalk that ends with two sets of 3-foot-wide leaves, each edged with several sharp teeth. Actions Multiattack. The snapping flytrap makes two melee attacks. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6+2) slashing damage.
