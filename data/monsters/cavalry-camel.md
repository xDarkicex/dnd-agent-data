# Cavalry Camel

**description**: In deserts, a cavalry camel is worth its weight in exotic spices. Aggressive to others but loyal to their masters, these combat-trained beasts are as much a weapon as a mount and fight to the death to protect their riders if the riders are dismounted. Cavalry camels are usually single-humped dromedaries, though double-humped camels are not unknown on desert battlefields.
