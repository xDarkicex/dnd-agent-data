# Warsworn

**description**: A warsworn is an animate mass of corpses composed of dozens, or sometimes hundreds, of victims of battle. They are formed by deities of undeath or war or, rarely, spontaneously manifest from the devastation of an especially horrendous battle. When the War of the River Kings begins, these immense undead become a dangerous threat throughout the Stolen Lands to soldiers from all sides of the conflict. Traits Absorb the Dead. A warsworn can consume any dying creature by moving into its space. The creature immediately dies and is absorbed into the warsworn, healing the warsworn by an amount equal to the creature’s Constitution score. Absorbed creatures can’t be returned to life except by a wish as long as the warsworn exists. If the warsworn is destroyed, absorbed creatures can be brought back to life with the appropriate magic. Trampling Charge. If the warsworn moves at least 20 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a slam attack on the same target, that target must succeed on a DC 18 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. If the target is prone, the warsworn can make one slam attack against it as a bonus action. Innate Spellcasting. The warsworn’s innate spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 19). The warsworn can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: 3/day each: animate object (weapons only), telekinesis (weapons only) Actions Multiattack. The warsworn makes three attacks: two with its slam and one attack with its scrap ball. Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 27 (6d6+6) bludgeoning damage. Scrap Ball. Ranged Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, range 20 ft., one target. Hit: 31 (8d6+3) slashing damage.
