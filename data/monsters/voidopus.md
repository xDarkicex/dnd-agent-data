# Voidopus

**description**: Voidopus Likes: sleeping during long trips across the void, the ceaseless passage of time, friendly telepathic chats Dislikes : edgy villains with plans to destroy the world, strong magical light, jokes about fried octopus Mysterious and literally otherworldly, voidopuses (also known as voidopi) are tiny creatures of eldritch magic and dark cuteness. Native to the unimaginable emptiness between the stars, these unusual critters can live comfortably in almost any environment. Their unusual nature allows voidopuses to take millennia-long naps and live in their dreams while floating from star to star. However, the void can become dull and monotonous after a few millennia, and many voidopuses will leave it to discover and visit new worlds full of color and wondrous creatures. While their bodies do not age like a mortal creature’s and they don’t require air, food, or drink, voidopuses are physically weak and diminutive, so they have to rely on their eldritch powers for protection. All voidopuses possess powerful telepathy and the ability to produce magical ink of unimaginable darkness. When threatened, they will not hesitate to use their mental powers to weaken and confuse the minds of aggressors and expel magical darkness to escape to safety. Pact of the Chain Familiar. At the gamemaster’s discretion, a warlock with a pact of the chain may choose the form of a voidopus for their familiar. More Stuff!
