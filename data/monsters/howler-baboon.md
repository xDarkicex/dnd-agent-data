# Howler Baboon

**description**: Howler baboons are territorial primates that claim stretches of forest and hills in large troops. Their presence is usually heard before it’s seen, thanks to the whooping calls they use to communicate danger and call for their troop mates. When angered, they attack in ferocious packs, hurling rocks and pummeling threats en masse. Actions Fist. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: (1d6+2) bludgeoning damage. Rock. Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 20/60 ft., one target.
