# Aarakocra Brave

**description**: Aarakocra braves often represent the fiercest, largest, and most skilled warriors of an aarakokra tribe. They are masters of aerial combat and employ hit-and-run tactics by taking full advantage of their flyby feature. In battle, they always engage the most dangerous foes in order to draw attention from their more vulnerable allies. Traits Dive Attack . If the aarakocra is flying and dives at least 30 feet straight toward a target and then hits it with a melee weapon attack, the attack deals an extra 10 (3d6) damage to the target. Flyby . The aarakocra doesn't provoke an opportunity attack when it flies out of an enemy's reach. Actions Multiattack . The aarakocra makes three melee attacks: one Javelin attack and two Talon attacks. Alternatively, it can make two ranged attacks. Talon . Melee Weapon Attack : +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit : 7 (1d6 + 4) slashing damage. Javelin . Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack : +6 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit : 7 (1d6 + 4) piercing damage.
