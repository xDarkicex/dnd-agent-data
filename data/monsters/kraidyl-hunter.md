# Kraidyl Hunter

**description**: Most kraidyl live solitary lives, though small enclaves do arise in regions with ample and abundant with prey. These tribal societies are incredibly militant and primitive, treating each other with open disdain and most outsiders with extreme hostility. A chieftain maintains control of a clan through force of will and physical domination, defending its position against challengers on a regular basis.
