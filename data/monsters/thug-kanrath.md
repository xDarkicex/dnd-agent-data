# Thug, Kanrath

**description**: The city of Kanrath is filled with thieves and miscreants. These people are Kanrath thugs. Hiding in alley corners or next to the entrance of various establishments to attack passers-by, Kanrath thugs are a problem for the mortals in the city. They usually don’t mess with nobles and royals so as not to bite off more than they can chew.
