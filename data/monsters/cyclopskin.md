# Cyclopskin

**description**: Cyclopskin are the smaller cousins of the cyclops, standing about seven to eight feet tall. Although weaker, they appear to be slightly more intelligent, as they can carry a conversation better than their more brutish cousins. Traits Poor Depth Perception. The cyclopskin has disadvantage on any attack roll against a target more than 30 feet away. Actions Multiattack. The cyclopskin makes two melee attacks. Greatclub.
