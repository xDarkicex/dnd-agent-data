# Voidbender

**description**: Voidbenders are chaotic Fiends that manipulate time and space, shaping reality to their will. They thrive in the unstable voids between realms, where the fabric of existence is thin and malleable. As agents of chaos, voidbenders view structured realms and their inhabitants as offensive. They actively seek to disrupt the natural order and sow chaos wherever they can. In combat, they use reality-bending abilities to unmake the laws of physics and turn gravity, time, and space against their foes.
