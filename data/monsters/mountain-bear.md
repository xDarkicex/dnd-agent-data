# Mountain Bear

**description**: Mountain Bear While bears are common throughout Grimnir the mountain bear is seldom seen. When on its hind legs it stands almost 20 feet tall and is often mistaken for a giant. Loners throughout most of their life cycle, these bears fear very little, and few raiders have survived encounters with these beasts. Fortunately, they tend to keep to the higher elevations upon Turnfjall, surviving on a diet of elk, mammoth, and possibly even giants.
