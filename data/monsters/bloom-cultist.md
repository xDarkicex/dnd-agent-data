# Bloom Cultist

**description**: Cultist Just as bandits are drawn to the Stolen Lands, all manner of cultists find this region ripe for hideouts and hidden shrines to deities and demigods whose worship has been outlawed in most cities and towns. Some of these cultists are solitary members of groups with influence throughout the world, while others, such as the Cultists of the Bloom, are entirely regional threats. Note that these cultists, along with the more powerful specialized cultists presented later in this book under the entry for Thresholders, can be used together as different agents of a sprawling sect of sinister worshippers in your game simply by changing their descriptions to match whatever nefarious deity or evil faith you’ve decided they might serve. Bloom Cultist The Cult of the Bloom is a relatively recent addition to the dangers of the Stolen Lands, and it’s not until a kingdom begins to grow within that realm that these cultists rise to a level of prominence that marks them as a significant regional threat. They worship Lamashtu as a source of fecund life and see nonworshippers as nothing more than fertilizer within which they hope to cultivate their own new uprising of monsters infused with energies siphoned from the First World. Traits Sneak Attack. Once per turn, the bloom cultist deals an extra 3 (1d6) damage when it hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of the cleansed cultist that isn’t incapacitated and the cleansed cultist doesn’t have disadvantage on the attack roll. Spellcasting. The bloom cultist is a 5th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 14, +6 to hit with spell attacks). The bloom cultist has the following druid spells prepared: Cantrips (at will): druidcraft, poison spray, shillelagh 1st level (3 slots): animal friendship, create or destroy water, cure wounds, entangle 2nd level (4 slots): animal messenger, enhance ability, pass without a trace 3rd level (3 slots): conjure animals, plant growth Actions Kukri. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 5 (1d4+3) slashing damage. Acid Dart (6/day). Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, range 30/60 ft., one creature. Hit: 20 (5d6+3) acid damage.
