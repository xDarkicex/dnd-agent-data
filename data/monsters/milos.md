# Milos

**description**: Subtle and deadly because he is so unassuming, Milos has served the Brotherhood of the Yellow Sign for many years. Cunning and very patient, Milos knows that his service to the dark powers will go rewarded, in time. And as one of the serpent people, he knows he has almost nothing but time. Milos does occasionally underestimate other folk, however, seeing them as messy and juvenile.
