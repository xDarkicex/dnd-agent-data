# Viper Eel

**description**: These voracious beasts are at home on land but are particularly swift in water. They are known to gather in large groups—called cells—to breed and hunt. They trawl their bodies along the sediment-filled trenches of oceans and underground lakes, absorbing sand and silt to recharge their venom sacs.
