# Rag Doll

**description**: Crudely constructed but oddly endearing, rag dolls are constructs formed from children’s toys, stuffed with soft material and adorned with smiling faces. Besides being generally comforting to have around, spellcasters favor rag dolls as familiars for their soft construction and inconspicuous nature; nobody questions a discarded doll, after all.
