# Night Scavver

**description**: Night scavvers are 15 feet long. Their coloration resembles that of Wildspace itself: white spots like stars sprinkled amid dark patches and patterns of color. Cooked night scavver meat is a popular offering in taverns across Wildspace. Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit (with advantage if the target is a creature that is missing any hit points), reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 27 (4d10 + 5) piercing damage.
