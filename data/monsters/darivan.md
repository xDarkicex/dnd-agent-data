# Darivan

**description**: Not all cult leaders are clerics invested with the power of a god, and one does not need divine spellcasting to devote their life to a higher power. Darivan is a perfect example as he is a powerful sorcerer who received visions from Lamashtu that compelled him to found the Cult of the Bloom. You can use Darivan’s stats for any cult leader associated with some sort of life-giving source of vitality; simply rename his Bloom Regeneration ability as needed and swap out his languages and alignment as appropriate to fit the new role. Traits Profane Leather. Leather armor Darivan wears gains a +6 bonus, which ceases when he removes the armor. Spellcasting. Darivan is a 11th-level cleric spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 17, +9 to hit with spell attacks). Darivan has the following cleric spells prepared: Cantrips (at will): light, mending, resistance, sacred flame, thaumaturgy 1st level (5 slots): create or destroy water, cure wounds, inflict wounds, shield of faith 2nd level (4 slots): aid, lesser restoration, spiritual weapon 3rd level (3 slots): bestow curse, mass healing word, protection from energy 4th level (3 slots): banishment, divination, stone shape 5th level (2 slots): contagion, flame strike 6th level (1 slots): harm Actions Multiattack. Darivan makes two falchion attacks. Falchion +2. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 10 (2d4+5) slashing damage. Reactions Bloom Regeneration. When Darivan takes damage while located within the Cradle of Lamashtu, he can cry out a quick prayer to the Mother of Monsters, and filaments of fungus slither up into his wounded body. He regains 15 hit points as the filaments heal his wounds.
