# Zorra Berradeira

**description**: The zorra berradeira, or "yelling fox," appears at first to be an ordinary fox of majestic size. Some say it is a wandering soul, given a new form, which would explain its cunning. The zorra calls loudly throughout the day, intensifying its cries at noon and midnight. If it is interrupted in its keening, it responds aggressively. According to tales, if offended, it will pursue its enemy until the end of their days.
