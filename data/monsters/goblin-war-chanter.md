# Goblin War Chanter

**description**: Goblin Goblins are most common in the southeastern reaches of the Stolen Lands, closer to the border of Mivon. Goblin War Chanter While many goblins enjoy singing, goblin war chanters pride themselves on mastering the art of vocal performance. Their ballads and jingles tend to strike others as undeniably catchy, though whether they are actually enjoyable depends on the listener—and whether the goblin follows the performance up with an attack. Traits Spellcasting. The war chanter is a 1st-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 14, +6 to hit with spell attacks). It has the following spells prepared: Cantrips (at will): guidance, mage hand, magic stone, message, resistance 1st level (3 slots): bless, healing word Actions Dogslicer. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, one target. Hit: 5 (1d6+2) slashing damage. Shortbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, range 30/60 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6+2) piercing damage. Goblin Song. The war chanter sings annoying goblin songs. Each creature within 60 feet of the war chanter that can hear it must succeed on a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw or become distracted for 1 minute. Distracted creatures have disadvantage on Wisdom saving throws and Wisdom (perception) checks that rely on hearing. Reactions Goblin Scuttle. When a nonhostile creature the goblin can see ends its movement within 5 feet of the goblin, the goblin moves up to 5 feet. The goblin doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks with this movement.
