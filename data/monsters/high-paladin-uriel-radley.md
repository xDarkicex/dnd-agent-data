# High Paladin Uriel Radley

**description**: Uriel Radley is a lanky but incredibly fit man. He has wispy, unkempt, dirty-blonde hair that falls down just past his shoulders, and he keeps a blonde trimmed beard and mustache. The man has a prominent chin and dark sunken eyes of piercing sky blue. His angular jaw and thick brow give him a stern and brooding appearance. He is often found wearing his shimmering full plate armor with a large waving blue and white cape, and a matching large tower shield both emblazoned with the symbol of the Sacred Flame. His left pauldron is in the shape of a large griffon head; highlights of gold and white decorate the armor. His warhammer, aptly named The Judge, is renowned for its many battles, and passed down amongst the High Paladins. It is a massive silver warhammer with a white hilt filigreed with gold with a head shaped like a griffon.
