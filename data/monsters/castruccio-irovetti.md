# Castruccio Irovetti

**description**: King of Pitax, Castruccio Irovetti plays the role of one of Kingmaker’s primary villains, a foe whom the PCs learn of and encounter long before their destined fight. He works best in a similar role in other games, perhaps as the leader of a nation of your own design. Consider swapping out his Numerian technology for equivalent magic items if you do so, or alternately lean into this flavor and set him up as an up-and-coming challenger to the rule of a Numerian city. Traits Brave. Irovetti has advantage on saving throws against being frightened. Inspired. King Irovetti has been inspired by Nyrissa, granting him advantage on Performance checks. Legendary Resistance (3/Day). If Irovetti fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead. Magic Armor. Any armor Irovetti wears gains a +3 bonus, which ceases when he removes the armor. Spellcasting. Irovetti is an 11th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 18, +10 to hit with spell attacks). Irovetti has the following bard spells known: Cantrips (at will): dancing lights, light, mage hand, message 1st level (4 slots): alarm, charm person, cure wounds, longstrider 2nd level (3 slots): detect thoughts, suggestion 3rd level (3 slots): clairvoyance, sending, tongues 4th level (3 slots): dimension door, greater invisibility, stoneskin 5th level (2 slots): dominate person, mass cure wounds 6th level (1 slot): contingency Thunder Brute. A melee weapon deals one extra die of its damage plus 1d10 thunder damage when Irovetti hits with it (included in the attack). Actions Multiattack. Irovetti makes two melee attacks. Halberd. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d10+4) slashing damage plus 5 (1d10) thunder damage. Legendary Actions Irovetti can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature’s turn. Irovetti regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn. Flechettes (Costs 2 Actions). Irovetti fires multiple darts from his high-tech halberd. Ranged Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 30 (6d8+3) piercing damage plus 9 (2d8) thunder damage. Halberd Attack. Irovetti makes a halberd attack. See Invisibility. Irovetti casts see invisibility on himself. This requires no spell slots or material components, and lasts for 1 minute.
