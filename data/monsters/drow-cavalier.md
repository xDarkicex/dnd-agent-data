# Drow Cavalier

**description**: Above all else, drow cavaliers prize mounts that can move them efficiently about the unpredictable terrain of the Underworld. Whether atrociraptors with their prodigious ability to jump, spiders that can climb, or creatures with even a passing ability to fly, cavaliers seek mounts they can rely on to quickly find sure footing. Tactical Terrain. Cavaliers use the Underworld terrain to their advantage. If their mount is a skilled climber, they seek out locations with sheer cliffs and tall chimneys from which to fight. A mount with great speed does well in open passages. Arrogant Soldier. Cavaliers loath postings and patrols that include those who cannot ride. Even if the patrol is fully mounted, the other members of the patrol seldom have trained in a cavalier’s mounted tactics, and almost none know how best to care for their beasts of burden, thus the cavalier becomes as much a stable hand as a mounted warrior. A cavalier loses their mount, or whose mount becomes injured and can no longer bear a rider, quickly finds themselves left behind by their peers.
