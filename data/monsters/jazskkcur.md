# Jazskkcur

**description**: Jazskkcur is a cunning creature born from Elemental Chaos. Its body is composed of the cosmic dust that swirls between the different planes, allowing it to effortlessly travel through the multiverse. Jazskkcur actively pursues alliances with other beings, adeptly ensuring that these arrangements often demand a profound and tragic compromise, leading to a drastic transformation in the moral fabric of the creature involved in the agreement. Jazskkcur regularly visits the creatures that made a deal with it and reminds them to fulfil their tasks lest they wish their souls severed from their bodies or sent to a horrible plane of eternal terror.
