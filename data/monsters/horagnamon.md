# Horagnamon

**description**: Horagnamon plays the role of Vordakai’s familiar—who acts as a makeshift spy for the lich while the PCs explore the empty village of Varnhold. You can use these statistics for any unusually intelligent raven, of course; he doesn’t have to be someone’s familiar. Perhaps he’s a reincarnated soul, or maybe a unique bird given intelligence by random chance or the influence of the First World. Traits Cunning. Horagnamon can use simple items as tools, such as poking a stick into nooks and crannies to tease out a piece of food. He’s also quite adept at stealing objects. He can use Sleight of Hand to accomplish simple tasks, or to steal objects that weigh 1 pound or less, which he can carry in his beak or talons. Death Throes. If Horagnamon dies, he explodes into a burst of feathers and seething black tendrils of smoke and energy. Each creature within 15 feet of him must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw, taking 33 (6d10) necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. A creature that fails its save by 5 or more gains one level of exhaustion. Keen Sight. Horagnamon has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight. Actions Multiattack. Horagnamon makes three beak attacks. Beak. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6+2) piercing damage.
