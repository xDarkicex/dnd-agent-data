# Bristle of Spiketooths

**description**: Spiketooth These gentle, leathery creatures possess four stumpy legs, tiny blue eyes, and an oversized dermal horn that juts above a wide, overly-toothed maw. Despite what their shark-like rows of serrated teeth might imply, spiketooths aren’t carnivores. Their diet consists almost entirely of lichoozes; jelly-like symbioses between plants and oozes that grow on rock and bark in their native Shai’taryn. Anthropology. While some families adopt spiketooths as pets, most councils throughout Shai’taryn outlaw the rearing of spiketooth herds, or ‘bristles’, as they are collectively known. Though spiketooths are not aggressive, fear can propagate amongst a bristle with alarming alacrity, resulting in a devastating stampede. The Shai’tan hold these creatures in high regard and, in academic circles, scholars have long debated the plural of the creature; most prefer the term “spiketooths” for simplicity, but a fierce few cling to “spiketeeth.”
