# Gurija

**description**: Gurija is the advisor and consort to Hargulka. She’s not particularly more powerful than a typical troll, but her magical abilities can keep the PCs on their toes if you add her to an encounter where they would not expect to combat sorcery. Traits Spellcasting. Gurija is a 5th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks). She can cast the following sorcerer spells: Cantrips (at will): electric arc (as firebolt but damage is changed to 2d10 lightning), prestidigitation, ray of frost, shocking grasp 1st level (4 slots): cause fear, detect magic, shield, thunderwave 2nd level (3 slots): magic weapon, misty step 3rd level (2 slots): dispel magic, lightning bolt, slow Keen Smell. Gurija has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Regeneration. Gurija regains 10 hit points at the start of her turn. If she takes acid or fire damage, this trait doesn’t function at the start of her next turn. Gurija dies only if she starts his turn with 0 hit points and doesn’t regenerate. Actions Multiattack. Gurija makes three attacks: one with her bite and two with her shocking claws. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d6+4) piercing damage. Shocking Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6+4) slashing damage and 4 (1d8) lightning damage. Reactions Resist Energy (3/Day). When Gurija is dealt damage from a type of energy, she redirects a portion of that energy away from herself. Gurija gains resistance to the energy type that triggered her reaction, and on her next successful melee weapon attack, she deals 3 (1d6) damage of the triggering type. Sudden Stormburst. When an ally within 60 feet takes acid or fire damage, Gurija summons a torrential but localized rainstorm around the ally. This rainstorm washes away acid and douses fire, immediately ending any ongoing acid or fire damage.
