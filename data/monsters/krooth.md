# Krooth

**description**: Krooths, sometimes called crocodile eaters, are fast and vicious bogland hunters. While they are known to hunt and eat large predators, they tend to also prey upon lizardfolk, boggards, and dinosaurs. Indeed, a krooth looks vaguely like a dinosaur, with its ridged head frill, long spiky tail, and mouth full of sharp teeth. Traits Amphibious. The krooth can breathe air and water. Augment Critical. If the krooth scores a critical hit, it rolls damage dice three times instead of twice. Fearless. The krooth has advantage on saving throws against being frightened. Keen Smell. The krooth has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Actions Multiattack. The krooth makes one bite attack and one tail spike attack. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d8+6) piercing damage. If a krooth hits a target creature with its bite attack, it can snap off one of its teeth in the wound. The creature must succeed on a DC 16 Constitution saving throw or become poisoned; the poisoned creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns ending the effect on itself on a success. Tail Spike. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (1d8+6) piercing damage.
