# Bisso Galeto

**description**: Bisso Galeto These creatures appear to be a hybrid of bird and lizard or snake, with feathered upper bodies, a bird-like head with a wattle and comb, and scaly lower quarters and a long, serpentine tail. At first glance, one might easily mistake a bisso galeto for a cockatrice, especially since the two share both diet and habitat. However, where a cockatrice has bat-like wings, the bisso galeto’s wings are feathered, like a birds, save for the long, curving spines they bear, which are the creature’s primary weapon. Bisso galetos are territorial, attempting to run off anything they see as a threat or competition. They use their magical ability to increase their size to frighten off opponents, or to increase their ability to fight, should the enemy not retreat when faced with a larger threat. Mortal Enemies . As they compete with cockatrices for both territory and food, bisso galetos are hostile to cockatrices, often attacking on sight. A bisso galeto will typically win such encounters, being immune to the cockatrice’s petrification ability. Some creatures, particularly humanoids, will raise and train a bisso galeto, keeping it as a guard against cockatrices if they live in territory where such monsters are common.
