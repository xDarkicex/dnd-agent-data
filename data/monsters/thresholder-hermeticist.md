# Thresholder Hermeticist

**description**: Thresholder In the dungeons hidden below Candlemere Island, a cabal of cultists has managed to defeat the passage of time itself. These cultists are known collectively as Thresholders. While they have managed to achieve immortality in their worship of the Outer God Yog-Sothoth, they are also prisoners, trapped within their own hidden temple by an ancient sect of Pharasmins who gave up their life and death to ensure that, while the Thresholders will live forever, they will never escape their own lair. All Thresholders have gained supernatural vision that allows them to see things left unseen, even though this ability required the sacrifice of their eyes to achieve. In addition, their devotion to Yog-Sothoth has pulled them outside of time itself, so that they do not age or need to drink and eat to survive—as long as they do not stray too far from the region where their cult’s power is focused. Thresholder Hermeticist The hermeticists of the cult are master alchemists whose research into forbidden formulas was a key to pushing their cult to adapt beyond humanity into something far more strange and sinister. Traits Cruel Anatomist (1/Turn). The Thresholder hermeticist deals an extra 31 (9d6) damage when it hits with a melee weapon attack if the target is within 5 feet of an ally of the Thresholder hermeticist’s that isn’t incapacitated. Juggernaut. The Thresholder hermeticist has advantage on all Constitution saving throws. Outside of Time. The Thresholder hermeticist exists outside of time; it doesn’t age, doesn’t need food or drink to survive, and can’t be affected by the haste or slow spells unless it chooses to be. Unseen Sight. The Thresholder has the ability to see the truth of the world, granting them darkvision and truesight to a distance of 60 feet, as long as they remain in a region under Yog-Sothoth’s influence. Actions Multiattack. The Thresholder hermeticist makes four dagger attacks. Dagger. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d4+5) piercing damage and the target must make a DC 18 Constitution saving throw, taking 10 (3d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. Enhanced Alchemist Fire (3/Day). Ranged Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, range 20 ft./60 ft., one target. Hit: 36 (8d8) fire damage. Creatures within 5 feet of the target must make a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw, taking equal damage as the target on a failed saving throw, or half as much on a successful one. Enhanced Healing Potion (3/Day). The Thresholder hermeticist drinks a potion and regains 18 (4d8) hit points, up to its hit point maximum.
