# Satinder Morne

**description**: Satinder Morne runs The Velvet Corner in Fort Drelev: a gambling hall that can provide the PCs with a (somewhat) safe place to rest during their mission to the hostile town. Her statistics work well for any innkeeper or similar professional who has hidden talents, such as someone who is a retired adventurer or a cleric of a faith whose interests align closely with industry, society, or intrigue. Traits Spellcasting. Satinder is a 6th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 14, +6 to hit with spell attacks). She has the following cleric spells prepared: 1st level (4 slots): bane, charm person, command, cure wounds 2nd level (3 slots): aid, hold person, invisibility 3rd level (3 slots): bestow curse, suggestion Actions Dagger. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60, one target. Hit: 3 (1d4+1) piercing damage.
