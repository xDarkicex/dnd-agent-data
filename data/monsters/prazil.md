# Prazil

**description**: The kobold spy Prazil works and his ally Kereek serve as agents of Hargulka. Prazil can serve as the leader of a kobold warren in your game, or even as a member of an adventuring party. Traits Pack Tactics. Prazil has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of his allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn’t incapacitated. Sunlight Sensitivity. While in sunlight, Prazil has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight. Sneak Attack. Once per turn, Prazil deals an extra 7 (2d6) damage when he hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of the kobold that isn’t incapacitated and Prazil doesn’t have disadvantage on the attack roll. Actions Multiattack. Prazil makes two melee attacks. +1 Silver Warhammer. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8+2) bludgeoning damage, or 7 (1d10+2) if used with two hands. Spear. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d6+1) piercing damage, or 5 (1d8+1) piercing damage if used with two hands to make a melee attack. Smash Kneecaps. Prazil makes a warhammer attack with a +2 bonus against a creature that is Medium or larger and that has legs. He deals an extra 4 (1d8) damage on a hit, and the target must succeed at a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or have its speed reduced by 10 feet for 1 minute.
