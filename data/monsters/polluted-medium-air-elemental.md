# Polluted Medium Air Elemental

**description**: A funneling cloud, about 8 feet high, whirls about the chamber, and picks up black acrid dust. Two faint glowing eyes peer through the smoggy whirlwind. Traits Air Form : The air elemental can enter a hostile creature’s space and stop there. It can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing. Actions Multiattack : The elemental makes two slam attacks. Alchemically-Enhanced Slam. Melee Weapon Attack : +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit : 11 (2d6 + 4) bludgeoning damage plus 5 (1d6 + 2) acid damage from alchemical dust. Whirlwind: (Recharge 4-6). Each creature in the elemental’s space must make a DC 12 Strength saving throw. On a failure, a target takes 8 (2d6 + 1) bludgeoning damage plus 5 (1d6 + 2) acid damage and is flung up to 10 feet away from the elemental in a random direction and is knocked prone. If a thrown target strikes an object such as a wall or floor, the target takes 3 (1d6) bludgeoning damage per 10 feet thrown. If the target is thrown at another creature, that creature must succeed on a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw or take the same amount of damage and be knocked prone. If the saving throw is successful, the target takes half the bludgeoning and acid damage and isn’t flung or knocked prone.
