# Vik

**description**: Vik “Taking matters into your own hands is often the only way to make sure that things get done.” An ambitious male halfling residing in Calderé City, and the leader of its militia, Vik wants nothing but to see his home drive off the elementals that constantly attack it. Though this desire is genuine, his frustrations with the city’s mayor and belief he could do better means that his wants can be easily distorted into something more malicious. “Vik? Yeah, he’s a good guy. Trying his best to change the world, and isn’t that…just grand. Sure, I admire the guy. I just wouldn’t shout it from the rooftops. I ain’t looking for trouble.” —Old Waldorf, local drunk
