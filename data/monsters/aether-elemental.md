# Aether Elemental

**description**: Aether Elemental Aether elementals are pure aether given form, often through a spell or other arcane ritual. They are often found in service to powerful spellcasters, either as assistants or bodyguards. Sometimes, mages conjure aether elementals to defend specific areas or items. This is not uncommon, as the elemental requires no nourishment or rest and can watch over their charge for centuries.
