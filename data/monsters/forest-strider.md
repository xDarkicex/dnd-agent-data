# Forest Strider

**description**: Forest Strider The forest strider is a large, spider-like Yoten. Though originally bred to patrol forests and defend other Yoten, there are many strider clusters throughout Grimnir that act much like any other wild animal pack. They hunt, they feed, they survive. Effective Ambushers. Striders tend to remain hidden in the branches before flinging a web at a foe to restrain them and then leaping down, using their poisonous stinger to further render their target helpless. Merciless. These foul creatures swiftly hunt down any prey that attempts to flee from them, so there are few survivors to tell any tales of them. The striders do not do this because they are intelligent; they are simply hungry and do not want food to go to waste. Webbed Denizens. The strider can vomit forth spider-like webbing. They do not generally construct large webs, but instead use the webbing to create overhead trails to travel high above unsuspecting victims or to fling at foes to capture them.
