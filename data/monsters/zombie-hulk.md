# Zombie Hulk

**description**: Zombie Zombies might move slowly, but one shouldn’t underestimate the danger posed by these ever-hungry undead. Zombie Hulk Only slightly less dangerous than cyclops zombies, these immense hulking undead foes are nonetheless dangerous creatures that possess terrifying strength. They are typically animated from the remains of huge giants. Traits Undead Fortitude. If damage reduces the zombie to 0 hit points, it must make a Constitution saving throw with a DC of 5 + the damage taken, unless the damage is radiant or from a critical hit. On a success, the zombie drops to 1 hit point instead. Actions Multiattack. The zombie hulk makes two slam attacks. Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 15 ft., one creature. Hit: 17 (3d6 +7) bludgeoning damage. Throw Body. Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, range 10/60 ft., one creature. Hit: 14 (2d6 +7) bludgeoning damage. While the zombie hulk can throw any dead body in this way, it sometimes throws zombies of Medium size or smaller. In this case, the thrown zombie also takes the damage from the attack, and lands prone, but if it is not destroyed, it can rise and act normally on its turn.
