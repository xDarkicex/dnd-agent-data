# Fate-Sworn Paladin

**description**: The oath to serve fate guides these powerful warriors, who are given glimpses of how the future must be by the three Sisters of Fate. Their divine magic is remarkable and their ability to fight even more so, as their oath powers their self-imposed duty of protecting everything. Humanoids Humanoids are the most plentiful creatures amongst the creations of the gods. They create vast kingdoms, they make unbelievable technological advances and even achieve breakthroughs in the art of magic. Traits Fate's Blessing. The paladin has a bonus to their saving throws equal to their Charisma modifier. Guided Blade. Once on each of their turns, when the paladin misses with a weapon attack they can make another weapon attack as part of the same action. Magic Weapons. The paladin's weapon attacks are magical. Spellcasting. The paladin's spellcasting Ability is Charisma (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks). They can cast the following spells: At will: compelled duel, sacred flame, shield of faith 3/day each: bless, searing smite, thunderous smite 2\day each: branding smite (as a 3rd level spell), command (as a 2nd level spell), dispel magic, wrathful smite (as a 2nd level spell) 1/day each: banishment, blinding smite, find greater steed, spirit shroud, staggering smite Actions Multiattack. The paladin makes three sacred blade attacks or shoots two fated flames. Sacred Blade (One-Handed). Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d8 + 5) slashing damage plus 9 (2d8) radiant damage. Sacred Blade (Two-Handed). Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 10 (1d10 + 5) slashing damage plus 9 (2d8) radiant damage. Fated Flame. Flame-like radiance descends on a creature that the paladin can see within 60 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take 9 (2d8) radiant damage. The target gains no benefit from cover for this saving throw. Pledge of Triumph (Recharges after a short or long rest). The paladin invokes the power of its oath to the three sisters of fate, touching one willing creature and imbuing it with divine power. The creature gains the following benefits for 1 minute: The creature gains a +1 bonus to their AC. When they make an attack roll or saving throw and fail, they can add a d4 to the total. The creature's weapon attacks and cantrips deal an additional 2 (1d4) radiant damage on a hit.
