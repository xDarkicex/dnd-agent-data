# Basidirond

**description**: The strange fungal basidirond is a deadly plant monster that feeds on mineral-rich moisture, be it runoff from cave walls or fresh blood. By ensuring a constant flow of nutritious moisture, canny cave dwellers can use basidironds as guardians for their lairs, although they must take care to avoid the plant’s hunting routes lest they become its latest victims. Migratory. If both moisture and warmth ebb away, colonies of basidironds are known to set off on a “root march” as they search for a living cave or more frequently used tunnel in which to re-establish a home. The sight of dozens, even hundreds of these plants moving steadily away from an inhospitable cavern is one of the wonders of the Underworld, although few who see it actually believe their eyes thanks to the reputation these organisms have for befuddling humanoids.
