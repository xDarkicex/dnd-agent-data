# Degmadu

**description**: A massive, fleshy polyp emerges from the ground, ringed with hundreds of wobbly eyes and filled with a whirlpool of stony teeth. Tentacles burst from within this gaping maw, ready to strike. The Millennium Polyp. Degmadu the Shuddering Maw, or the Millennium Polyp, is said to grow active on the surface for three days every thousand years when it bursts from its earthly confines.
