# Dweorg Beastmaster

**description**: Beastmasters among the dweorg specialize in the rearing and training of skildpadders, deep badgers, and—for travel on the surface—the common mule. While insular dweorg cities make little effort to accommodate the animals of the rare traveler, those rare settlements that welcome outsiders rely on beastmasters to stable all manner of Underworld mounts and beasts of burden.
