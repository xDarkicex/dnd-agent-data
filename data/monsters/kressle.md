# Kressle

**description**: Kressle leads a group known as the Thorn River bandits, and is in the process of becoming a part of the Stag Lord’s greater plan. You can use Kressle’s stats as the leader in a group of low-level bandits, or for the bandits themselves if you need a slightly more dangerous group of brigands. Traits Lucky Shot. Before Kressle makes a ranged attack, she can choose to have disadvantage on the attack roll, but if her attack hits it deals one extra die of the weapon’s damage. Actions Multiattack. Kressle makes two melee or ranged attacks. Handaxe. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d6) slashing damage. Dagger.
