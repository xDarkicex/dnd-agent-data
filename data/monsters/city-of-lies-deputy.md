# City of Lies Deputy

**description**: Traits Corrupt. Depending on the severity of their offense, players may pay the City of Lies deputy to look the other way and not punish them for perceived wrongdoing. This costs 4 sp (2 bu) per caught player, but can cost several times more for serious crimes. A deputy can’t be bribed this way if they are in the presence of a higher ranked magistrate that does not have the corrupt trait. Excessive Force. If the City of Lies deputy’s attack roll exceeds their target’s AC by 5 or more, the City of Lies deputy can subject the target to one of the following conditions: bleeding , distracted , or maimed . Illuminating Truth. Creatures within 5 feet of the City of Lies deputy have disadvantage on Charisma (Deception) checks. Actions Swordbreaker. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d4 + 3) bludgeoning damage. Criminal-Catcher. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: The target must make a DC 16 Strength or Dexterity saving throw. On a failure, the target is restrained. As an action, a creature may make a DC 16 Strength or Dexterity check. Success means the creature removes itself or another creature within reach from the criminal-catcher. While a creature is retrained this way, the City of Lies deputy cannot make attacks.
