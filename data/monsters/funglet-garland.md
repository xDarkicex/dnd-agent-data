# Funglet Garland

**description**: To an untrained eye, garlands don’t look all that different from gardeners or grangers, but a closer understanding of funglet culture reveals a different picture. The decoration worn by a garland is, to another funglet, a clear and true description of all it has achieved, as well as the rewards it has received from its peers for its actions. These adornments are no mere baubles, however; they are in part what enhances the garland and give it its powers. A garland’s cap or helmet allow it to amplify the guiding words that come from its leadership skills so that they are heard by all those in range. Clusters of rubbery mushrooms across its shoulders grow rapidly on command to form its shieldwall.
