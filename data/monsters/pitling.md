# Pitling

**description**: Traits Horrid Stench. If an enemy starts their turn within 10 feet of three or more pitlings, the enemy is poisoned until the start of their next turn. Minion. If the pitling takes damage from an attack or as the result of a failed saving throw, their hit points are reduced to 0. If the pitling takes damage from another effect, they die if the damage equals or exceeds their hit point maximum; otherwise they take no damage. Soul Feast. When the pitling reduces a creature who isn’t a Construct or an Undead to 0 hit points or deals damage to a dying creature, the creature must make a DC 11 Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the pitling consumes the creature’s soul, and the pitling becomes a Category 2 demon of the GM’s choice with 1 soul. A creature whose soul is consumed in this way immediately dies, and they can’t be restored to life by any means short of a wish spell. If the creature was reduced to 0 hit points by a group attack, the GM picks one pitling who joined the attack to consume the soul. Actions Spit (Group Attack). Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 15/30 ft., one target. Hit: 4 poison damage.
