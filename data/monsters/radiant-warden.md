# Radiant Warden

**description**: The enigmatic and strange radiant wardens were constructed thousands of years ago to protect observatories and scholars against the incursion of alien aggressors from the darkest corners of the unknown universe. Over time, their roles as guardians expanded to include watching over any region where the laws of time and space have worn thin, particularly near portals and permanent gates between planets, planes, or dimensions. Named for both the radial nature of the concentric rings that make up their bodies as well as the glowing radiance of their attacks, radiant wardens carry on their orders, defending sites from invasion with single-minded purpose. Traits Gatekeeper Aura. A creature that uses a teleportation ability within 30 feet of the warden or enters a space within 30 feet of the warden must succeed a DC 18 Charisma save or have its destination changed to a point of the radiant warden’s choosing within 30 feet of the warden. Whether or not the save is successful, the creature gets disadvantage on all attack rolls and ability checks until the end of its next turn. Innate Spellcasting. The warden’s innate spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 18). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components. At will: misty step, dimension door 1/day each: prismatic spray, teleport, disintegrate (as an 8th-level spell) Magic Weapons. The warden’s weapon attacks are magical. Orrery. Until it acts, the radiant warden appears to be a large mechanical orrery. A successful DC 21 Perception check is required to detect it as anything other than an orrery. Actions Multiattack. The warden makes two attacks. Radiant Hammer. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 25 (3d12+6) bludgeoning damage plus 6 (1d12) radiant damage. Radiant Beam. Ranged Spell Attack: +10 to hit, range 30/60 ft., one target. Hit: 33 (6d10) radiant damage. Forced Teleport (Recharge 5–6). The warden targets up to five creatures that it can see within 30 feet of itself. Each target must succeed on a DC 18 Charisma save or be teleported to an unoccupied space that the warden can see within 60 feet of the warden. Radiant Blast (Recharge 5–6). The radiant warden generates a 50-foot cone of bright energy. Every creature in the cone must attempt a DC 18 Dexterity save, taking 65 (10d12) radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
