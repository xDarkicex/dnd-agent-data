# Ferdinando Strozzi

**description**: Ferdinando Strozzi (who is not a historical character) is a member of a powerful patrician family. Although the Strozzi have been political opponents of the Medici in the past, Filippo will form ties with everyone who can further his ambitions. He has already spent two election periods in the people’s council and aspires to the job of gonfaloniere di giustizia. Ferdinando has caught the eye of Girolama Savonarola who exploits the young man’s lust for fame. This is why he has sent a noble middleman to enrol Ferdinando in his scheme. Ferdinando has not been informed about his true employer, but he has been ordered to speak out against the ascetic prior of San Marco in order to make friends with the humanistic elite around Ficino. Savonarola’s agent has payed Ferdinando richly and promised an influential position, which is why he has committed to killing the young and unattached prostitute Paola (see chapter 1) . Ferdinando is an attractive gentleman in his late thirties with black hair and rather cold blue eyes. He will often smile and give compliments, but anyone with some knowledge of human behaviour will be able to see through the pleasant facade and discern his cold and cruel nature. Tips for the GM: In the case that someone suspects him to be Paola‘s murderer, there is no reason to keep him on for the remainder of the campaign. He can well be executed as a felon with dark plans to discredit either the Medici party or Savonarola. Traits Cunning Action. Ferdinando can use his bonus action once per round to take the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action. Uncanny Dodge. Ferdinando can use his reaction to halve an attack’s damage against him, provided he can see the attack coming. Evasion. If Ferdinando is subjected to an effect that allows him to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, he instead takes no damage if he succeeds at the saving throw, and only half damage if he fails. Sneak Attack. Once per turn, Ferdinando deals an extra 14 (4d6) damage when he hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of Ferdinando’s that isn’t incapacitated and Ferdinando doesn’t have disadvantage on the attack roll. Actions Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 2) slashing damage. Dagger. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d4 + 3) piercing damage.
