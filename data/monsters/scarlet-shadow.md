# Scarlet Shadow

**description**: Scarlet Shadow doesn’t share his name or background. Only the dragonborn’s closest friend, Radlee Thugram, knows he once belonged to an assassin’s guild that bore his name. After a bloody guild war left Scarlet as his organization’s only survivor, he took on the guild’s name as his own and became an independent contract killer. Eventually, Scarlet joined forces with Radlee and then the rest of the Soulbinders. Long before Scarlet’s curse, he’d already lost most of his humanity. Killing is his only purpose and sadistic pleasure, and he doesn’t want to be just any god—he wants to be the god of death. The assassin is bound to the ring of scarlet shadows , the last symbol of his old guild. Traits Assassinate. During his first turn, Scarlet has advantage on attack rolls against any creature who hasn’t taken a turn. The first hit Scarlet scores against a surprised creature is a critical hit. Sneak Attack ( 1 /Turn). Scarlet deals an extra 14 (4d6) damage when he hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of Scarlet who isn’t incapacitated and Scarlet doesn’t have disadvantage on the attack roll. Actions Multiattack. Scarlet makes three Shadow Blade or three Shortbow attacks. Shadow Blade. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d6 + 7) necrotic damage. If a target is hit by two or more Shadow Blade attacks on a turn, they must succeed on a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw or be blinded until the end of their next turn. Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (1d8 + 7) piercing damage. Razor Garrote. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (1d8 + 7) slashing damage. If the target is a Medium or smaller creature, they are grappled (escape DC 17). Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained, they take 22 (5d8) slashing damage at the start of each of their turns, and Scarlet can’t attack another target. Umbral Breath (Recharge 6 ). Scarlet exhales dark flame in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 28 (8d6) necrotic damage and is frightened of Scarlet for 1 minute (save ends at end of turn). On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and isn’t frightened. Bonus Actions Cunning Action. Scarlet takes the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action.
