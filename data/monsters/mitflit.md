# Mitflit

**description**: Gremlin, Mitflit Gremlins are cruel fey tricksters and saboteurs who have fully acclimated to life on the Material Plane. Mitflits, also known as mites, are self-loathing and pitiful cowards with oversized heads, blue skin, and a penchant for befriending the ugliest of vermin as allies. Of all the types of gremlin, mitflits have the least affinity for magic and have largely lost most of their ancestral gremlin abilities. They find companionship in the other base creatures of the world, and forge bonds of friendship with vermin like spiders, centipedes, and cave-dwelling insects, the only other beings that seem willing to accept them. A social structure, even one in which they are bullied or demeaned, partially fills their need for some type of companionship, and they rarely rebel or against their fellows unless their rage hits a breaking point from which there can be no return. Traits Hatred. Mitflits double their proficiency bonus on attack rolls against humanoid creatures of the dwarf or gnome subtype due to special training against these hated foes. Innate Spellcasting. The mitflit’s innate spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 11). The mite can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: prestidigitation 1/day: bane Keen Smell. The mitflit has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Self-Loathing. A mitflit’s self-loathing makes them easy to influence. They have disadvantage on saving throws and ability checks to resist any Charisma-based skills or being charmed. Vermin Keeper. Through sounds and gestures, the mitflit can communicate simple ideas with arthropods. The knowledge and awareness of arthropods is limited by their intelligence, but at minimum, they can give information about nearby locations and monsters, including whatever they can perceive or have perceived within the past day. The mitflit can ask for small favors from arthropods, and has advantage on Wisdom (Animal Handling) checks when communicating with them. Actions Dagger. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d4+1) piercing damage. Dart. Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d4+1) piercing damage. Reactions Vengeful. When the mitflit is hit by an attack or fails a saving throw, the mitflit makes an attack against the creature that harmed it.
