# Otis the Historian

**description**: Otis the Historian Otis is a long-lived elf and is one of the few to still remember the old world. He even dresses different than others, wearing the well cared for clothing he brought with him during the Exodus. He remembers the Lost Lands fondly, though seldom speaks of them. He is beloved by all the clans—belonging to none and all of them. He is welcome anywhere, even into the Baendur lands. He alone knows the truth of which Vanir were trapped at the Stone Court but sees no point in mentioning it or squashing the beliefs of current raiders. Roleplaying Otis Artistic. Otis loves to paint—people, places, and objects. Much of the most prized artwork that the raiders possess are his creations. Mischievous. He also loves being amused and is not above pulling a prank, even on strangers, if it might make everyone smile. Reticent. Though he is generally quite talkative, when it comes to the Lost Lands, he says little. He feels it is better to forget what was lost there, for there is no restoring it.
