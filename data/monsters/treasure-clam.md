# Treasure Clam

**description**: Treasure clams are rare mollusks that grow to giant size in shallower coaster waters. They lure their prey by growing semi-precious gemstones of various kinds that glint in the light. They catch whoever swims near them in a vice-like clamp while gnawing on their flesh. It takes a treasure clam six months to grow a gemstone worth 500 gold pieces.
