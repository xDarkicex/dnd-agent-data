# Cloudshrike

**description**: Elemental Beasts Occasionally, aetherial sailors find an elemental portal their craft can travel through, bringing them into the mysterious worlds of the Elemental Planes. These planar gates are typically guarded by bestial elemental spirits that jealously guard the entrance to their worlds. Sailors know to keep a distance from these portals unless they’re prepared to fight past these elemental beasts. Cloudshrike Air elemental beasts are invisible, for they’re made of swirling gusts of wind. When their movements kick up sand or debris, or they’re perceived by creatures with truesight, it’s revealed they possess a bestial form in the vague shape of a large, carnivorous bird.
