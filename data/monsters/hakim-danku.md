# Hakim Danku

**description**: Appearance and Personality: Hakim is an accomplished Sarcosan soldier, but his time possessed by a Haunting Angel and his battles against the wrathstalker have left him considerably the worse for wear. Hakim and his gear are bloodied and worn. His armor and weapons show signs of heavy use and neglect for maintenance. Under the influence of the Haunting Angel he is very determined to contain or eliminate the wrathstalker. He has no concern for the limitations of the human host. If the PCs manage to free Hakim from his imprisonment, he appreciates their assistance. He believes that they have saved his life, and he is willing to dedicate his life to aiding them in whatever way he can. He insists on accompanying the party as an ally for the remainder of their quest. Bonus Actions Bonus Action. Hakim can use his bonus action to make a single Haft attack. Actions Multiattack. Hakim makes three attacks with his glaive. Glaive. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 10 ft, one target. Hit: 9 (1d10 + 3) slashing damage Haft. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target. Hit: 6 (1d4 + 3) bludgeoning damage Reactions Swift Counterstrike. When a creature misses Hakim with a melee or ranged weapon attack, he can use his reaction to move 10 feet. This movement does not provoke attacks of opportunity. After completing this movement, if the creature is within 10 feet of him, Hakim can make a glaive attack against the creature.
