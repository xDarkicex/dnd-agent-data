# The Serpent

**description**: This large green snake with emerald eyes is one of the many manifestations of Lucifer. In this form he reminds not only mortals, but also the heavenly legions that he keeps laying in wait. Each evening, it appears in the Valley of the Princes and every time is driven out by the two angels dressed in green robes with their blunt swords. Actions Multiattack. The Serpent makes two bite attacks. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d4 + 3) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 12 Const itution saving throw, taking 10 (3d6) poison damage for two rounds, or half as much damage on a successful one.
