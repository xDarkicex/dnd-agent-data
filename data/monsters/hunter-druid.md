# Hunter Druid

**description**: A druid from a circle focused on changing the shape of others, while maintaining their mental faculties and abilities intact. Their abilities and way of thought evoke a particularly powerful ability to create a bond between nature and other creatures. Humanoids Humanoids are the most plentiful creatures amongst the creations of the gods. They create vast kingdoms, they make unbelievable technological advances and even achieve breakthroughs in the art of magic. Traits Aegis of Nature. When the druid uses their Gift of the Wilds action, the ally they transformed gains 15 temporary hit points Magic Weapons. The druid's weapon attacks are magical. Speak with Beasts and Plants. The druid can communicate with beasts and plants as if they shared a language. Spellcasting. The druid's Spellcasting Ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 17, +9 to hit with spell attacks). They can cast the following spells: At Will: druidcraft, produce flame, resistance, shillelagh, thorn whip 3/day each: cure wounds (as a 2nd-level spell), faerie fire, thunderwave, beast sense 2/day each: call lightning (as a 4th-level spell), dispel magic, dominate beast, pass without trace, plant growth, wall of fire 1/day each: commune with nature, conjure elemental, freedom of movement, wall of thorns Actions Produce Flame. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 30ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d8) fire damage. Shillelagh Quarterstaff. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d8 + 5) bludgeoning damage. Thorn Whip. Melee Spell Attack: +9 to hit, reach 30ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d8 + 5) bludgeoning damage. If the attack hits and if the creature is large or smaller, the druid pulls the creature up to 10 feet closer to themselves. Gift of the Wilds (2/day). The druid targets a willing ally and can change their shape into a beast of their choosing, with a Challenge Rating as high as 3. The target's statistics are replaced by the statistics of the beast, but the target retains its alignment, personality, and Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma scores. When the target transforms, it assumes the beast's hit points and hit dice. When the target reverts to its normal form, it returns to the number of hit points it had before it transformed. However, if the target reverts as a result of dropping to 0 hit points, any excess damage carries over to the normal form. In beast form the target can't cast spells, but this doesn't break concentration on a spell already cast. However, the target retains all racial and class features while transformed.
