# Pankration Champion

**description**: An incredible athlete and warrior that participates in the Olympic Games held in the name of, and dedicated to, the gods every four years. They specifically practice the martial art known as Pankrátion, which is focused on physical combat without weapons, where very few limits exist during a match. However, these limits do not exist outside of the sacred Olympic Games. At their strongest and most successful these champions have been known to be graced with the favor of the gods. Humanoids Humanoids are the most plentiful creatures amongst the creations of the gods. They create vast kingdoms, they make unbelievable technological advances and even achieve breakthroughs in the art of magic. Traits Unarmored Defense. While the champion is wearing no armor and wielding no shield, their AC includes their Wisdom modifier. Stunning Strike (3/day). On a successful unarmed strike the champion can force the target to make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be stunned until the end of the champion's next turn. Magic Weapons. The champion's weapon attacks are magical. Actions Multiattack. The champion makes four unarmed strikes or three javelin throws. Unarmed Strike. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d8 + 5) bludgeoning damage plus 3 (1d6) radiant damage. The champion can choose to grapple a target on a successful hit. While grappled in this way all unarmed strikes have advantage against the grappled creature. Javelin. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 30/120ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d6 + 5) piercing damage. Bonus Actions Swift Footing (3/day). The champion can use a bonus action to take the dash, disengage or dodge action. Reactions Deflect Missile. In response to being hit by a ranged weapon attack, the champion deflects the missile. The damage they take from the attack is reduced by 1d10 + 14. If the damage is reduced to 0, the champion catches the missile if it's small enough to hold in one hand and the champion has a hand free.
