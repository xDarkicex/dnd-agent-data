# The Utter Blindness

**description**: The Utter Blindness is a gargantuan gelatinous cube that has been amalgamated from smaller specimens through a vile ritual that requires the self-immolation by corrosion of those who undertake it. The gargantuan ooze becomes infused by the divine energies of the deity known, only to a few, as the Blind God and can cause blindness through its pseudopod.
