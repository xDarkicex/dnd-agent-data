# Luigi

**description**: Luigi is a cappelletto, a small humanoid creature of apparently jolly and even silly disposition. Just over a foot (30 cm) in height, he is a pudgy little manlike fellow with a pug nose and clad in what seems to be a dirty old handkerchief around his loins. He is wearing a tiny cap, lovingly stitched together from the sparkliest fabrics that Luigi could steal. It is decorated with a few buttons and a silver bell that will tinkle gently when he shakes his head. He is very proud of his cap, which at times makes him a less effective spy than he could be – the soft tinkle is the only means by which the PCs might spot him in the forest. When encountered, he will often be seen hastening from cover to cover, with his bell tinkling, breathlessly exhorting himself to be careful so he won’t be spotted, all the while blissfully unaware of the gawping humans observing his every move. Then he will suddenly realize he has been spotted, and he will either descend into panicked babble and other – frankly adorable – antics, or he will dart behind the next bush and turn his cap inside-out so the bell is on the inside. This not only silences the bell, but also triggers an Invisibility effect (equivalent to the 2nd level spell) woven into the cap, which may allow him to slip away. It is hard to say how much of this is an act, some kind of private joke, or calculated distraction. Luigi can indeed be quite cunning and stealthy, so if he is seen it usually means he wants to be seen. When the characters meet Luigi, he will be acting as a spy for the queen, making contact with them to gain a better understanding of who they are. His behaviour is often a strange mix of worry (wanting to run away and hide) and bravery (being within reach of the giant humans), with what amounts to a running commentary on this in his screechy voice as he talks to himself. Traits Invisibility. If he turns his cap inside out, it triggers an Invisibility spell (2. Level). Innate Spellcasting. Luigi’s spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 11). He can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: Druidcraft 3x/day each: animal friendship, faerie fire Actions Sling. Ranged Weapon Attack: +0 to hit, reach 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 1 (1d4 − 2) bludgeoning damage. Feign Death. Luigi can collapse theatrically only a DC 16 Me dicine check can tell that he is alive.
