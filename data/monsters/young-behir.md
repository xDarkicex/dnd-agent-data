# Young Behir

**description**: Young behirs are rarely found outside of their lairs, and often in the company of their mother. Ones that are found alone or wandering have probably been abandoned or orphaned. While they may be young, these specimens are still very dangerous, reaching lengths of 20 feet. Actions Multiattack . The behir makes two attacks: one with its Bite and one to Constrict. Bite . Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d10 + 4) piercing damage. Constrict. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one Medium or smaller creature. Hit: 9 (1d10 + 4) bludgeoning damage plus 9 (1d10 + 4) slashing damage. The target is grappled (escape DC 14) if the behir isn't already constricting a creature, and the target is restrained until this grapple ends. Lightning Breath (Recharge 5–6). The behir exhales a line of lightning that is 15 feet long and 5 feet wide. Each creature in that line must succeed on a DC 14 Dexterity saving throw, taking 49 (9d10) lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
