# Jurgrindor

**description**: When the PCs encounter Jurgrindor, the frost giant has been wounded severely. These stats can serve for any frost giant prisoner the PCs might encounter, or if (as in the Kingmaker Adventure Path ) you narratively need to have a low-level party encounter what’s normally a much more powerful foe. Alternatively, you can use a mechanic like Wounded Arm to make any other powerful foe more usable at lower levels, as long as you work to adjust the creature’s statistics so that they aren’t unfair in a fight at your target challenge rating. Traits Wounded Arm. Jurgrindor’s left arm has been wounded severely during a previous fight. At the end of each of his turns, Jurgrindor must succeed at a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or the wound begins to bleed again, dealing 3 (1d6) necrotic damage to him. His wound prevents him from throwing rocks. Actions Fist. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d4+4) bludgeoning damage. Chill Breath (Recharge 5–6). Jurgrindor exhales a 15-foot cone of moisture that freezes. Each creature in the area must make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw, taking 9 (2d8) cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature that fails the save is also grappled in ice (escape DC 13), and while grappled in this way, the creature is restrained and takes 9 (2d8) cold damage at the end of each of its turns.
