# Cerberus Spawn

**description**: Descendants of the Underworld’s guardian, they carry the blood of one of the most prolific creatures in all of the Greek world. Born from the union of Cerberus and a common canine or descended from one of Cerberus’ hellhound litters, these are exceptionally rare creatures that somewhat resemble a mortal beast more than their supernatural ancestor, at least in attitude and intelligence. Their appearance will be completely dependent on the mix of canine breed they hail from, but they will always have three heads. Moreover, their blood carries powerful potential for fire and shadow magic. Descendants of the Watchdog. Due to their innate connection with the Underworld these dogs are able to peer into the ethereal plane, where spirits dwell. Additionally, they are very perceptive when it comes to the presence of undead creatures. Variant: Cerberus Spawn Familiar Cerberus spawn can often be found in the service of hunters of undeath, followers of Hades and other heroes who dedicate themselves to maintaining the order of the cycle of life and death. Familiar. The Cerberus spawn can enter into an agreement to serve another creature as a familiar, forming a telepathic bond with its willing master. While the two are bonded, the master can sense what the cerberus spawn senses as long as they are within 1 mile of each other. While the cerberus spawn is within 10 feet of its master, the master shares the Cerberus spawn’s Sight beyond Worlds and Detector of Undeath traits. At any time and for any reason, the cerberus spawn can end its service as a familiar, ending the telepathic bond. Fiends Dark creatures that inhabit the expanse of Hades and Tartarus. They are often in the service of chthonic gods as minions or simply roam through the cursed expanse of Tartarus until an opportunity to reach the material plan presents itself. Traits Keen Hearing and Smell. The cerberus spawn has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell. Detector of Undeath. The cerberus spawn can detect any undead creature within 60 feet of it. Sight beyond Worlds. The cerberus spawn can look into the ethereal plane. Three Heads. The cerberus spawn has advantage on saving throws against being blinded, deafened, stunned, or knocked unconscious. Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage plus 3 (1d6) fire damage. Flame Surge (Recharge 6). A 5 foot-radius explosion of flames is unleashed, centered on the cerberus spawn. Each creature in that area other than the cerberus spawn must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw, taking 10 (3d6) fire damage on failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
