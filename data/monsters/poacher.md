# Poacher

**description**: Forester Be they workers seeking to harvest the forest’s resources or explorers taking pains to map the wilds, foresters harbor a wide range of skills, motives, and agendas. While most foresters have little interest in the workings of urban life, scoff at “city folk,” and mock travelers who find themselves needing aid when they inevitably get in over their heads while traveling through the woods, the majority of these creatures aren’t evil. While foresters might not have patience for those who haven’t chosen the woods as their home, they’ll usually offer aid to the lost or the needy they encounter in the wilds—if only to ensure that the intruders can make their way back out of the woodlands and return to wherever they came from as soon as possible. Poacher Those who seek illegal prey or hope to capitalize on black markets must be capable hunters and have the proper connections within organized society to make a profit from their ill-gotten gains. Their cruel natures and despicable practices make them hated by all who protect the creatures of the forest. Traits Favored Enemy: Beasts. The poacher gains advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks to search for their favored enemies and on Wisdom (Survival) checks to track them. Precision Edge. The first time the poacher hits a beast in a round, they deal an additional 9 (2d8) piercing damage. Actions Multiattack. The poacher makes two attacks. Dagger. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d4+4) piercing damage. Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8+4) piercing damage.
