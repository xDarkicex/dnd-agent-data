# Swarm of Steel Darts

**description**: Steel Dart A byproduct of CRE’s misguided mechanical meddlings (see Forge of the Divine Machine in Motes of the Divine ), steel darts are clockwork songbirds formed of hinged brass wings and steel that hides minuscule organs: a heart, tongue, stomach, lungs, and twitching vocal cords. A long, tapered beak allows steel darts to sup a meal of nectar and oil, keeping their hearts beating and their gears turning. Songbirds. The frantic beating of one steel dart’s wings produces a steady drone over which it layers complex harmonies. When steel darts form swarms, however, they synchronise that gentle buzz into a single thunderous pitch that harmonically links their clockwork minds. One agitation of a steel dart quickly arouses the rest of the swarm and, with joined voices, the swarm’s song becomes a terrifying dirge of doom.
