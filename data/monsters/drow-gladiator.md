# Drow Gladiator

**description**: Gladiatorial combat exists in a somewhat different form amongst the drow than it does elsewhere. Each house will train a favored son or daughter in bloody, ostentatious combat. When open warfare is too costly and sabotage or assassination too subtle a message, a house can assert themselves through their gladiators. But such combat is more than a mere fight to the death. A prolonged fight is the most desirable, with the victor claiming the honor of toying with an inferior foe, even if the truth is much more drawn.
