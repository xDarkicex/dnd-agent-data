# Sanguine Elemental

**description**: Looking like ghosts completely covered in blood, sanguine elementals are some of the strangest creatures of this world. As elementals, they are a fundamental force of the universe, of nature. According to legend, they are created with the blood of dead creatures, as well as those who have not yet been born. Sanguine elementals understand all languages, as it is theorized that they are composed of the blood of all types of creatures. The fact that they can only speak draconic is a topic of another theory, which claims that sanguine elementals are products of experiments of blood dragons, or experiments made on blood dragons. There are stories of sanguine elementals that walk the world, learning the arts of mortals such as wizardry or the art of battle. Although they have their own bizarre motives to do so, such sanguine elementals can accompany adventurers as long as they share a common goal. If their opinions clash, a sanguine elemental can also be an adventurer’s worst nightmare. As elementals, sanguine elementals do not require breath, food, water, or sleep.
