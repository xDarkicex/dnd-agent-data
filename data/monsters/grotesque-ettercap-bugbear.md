# Grotesque Ettercap Bugbear

**description**: Grotesques are merged creatures made by the spell, aberrate, or they are sometimes found in areas of strong wild magic. This grotesque is an example of an ettercap and bugbear base creatures turned into a grotesque for 24 hours by a hag. This grotesque is mostly ettercap in shape but it has bulging muscles and its skin is a mottled purple and dark yellow. A hag will sometimes send a grotesque into a village to create mayhem. She will even offer to stop the mayhem or come to the village’s rescue: for a price.
