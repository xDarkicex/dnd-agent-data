# Mosaic Golem

**description**: Composed of countless tiles of varying color and composition, a mosaic golem folds itself into decorated chambers, its form only limited by the imagination of its creator. Once an interloper treads upon a mosaic golem, it reveals its true form and lays waste to intruders. Clever golem makers also install traps into their mosaic chambers, luring would-be trap-disarmers into a mosaic golem’s reach. Infinite Forms. Most mosaic golems are designed to blend in to a surrounding work of art, thus their forms are determined by the decor.
