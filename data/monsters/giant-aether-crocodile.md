# Giant Aether Crocodile

**description**: Aether Crocodile These fearsome beasts infest the still bog-like aether pools found on many unpopulated islands across the Aetherial Expanse. Their tough hides carry a muted prismatic sheen, allowing them to hide effectively in their native habitat. Often appearing in lethargic floats, groups of these creatures can burst into a frenzy when prey comes near.
