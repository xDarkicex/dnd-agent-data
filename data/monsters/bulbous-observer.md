# Bulbous Observer

**description**: Spawned in the Abyss by Orcus, these colossal bloated undead are made from the flayed skin of gigantic gibbering mouthers and those of giants. These floating undead spheres fly slowly over the domain of the Demon Lord of the Undead, looking for enemies and alerting lesser minions to any threat they encounter. Their favorite tactic involves lifting their opponents from the ground and dropping them from a great height, or breaking off parts of buildings or natural formations to drop them onto their enemies.
