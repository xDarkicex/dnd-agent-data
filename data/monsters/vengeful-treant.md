# Vengeful Treant

**description**: Vengeful treants have agreed to be transformed by a hag living near the treant’s home. Vengeful treants will smell like rotting fruit and they stay in a constant state of agitation and movement so they are never confused for normal trees. Rangers and druids will usually go out of their way to avoid a vengeful treant, because even they are not guaranteed safety near one.
