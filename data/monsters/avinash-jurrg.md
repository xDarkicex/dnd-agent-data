# Avinash Jurrg

**description**: Avinash Jurrg is the general of Pitax’s armies, and in many ways can be considered to be the second-in-command of the nation of Pitax. His unusual heritage makes him a great choice for the final boss of a long-running campaign of your own design, perhaps as the leader of an army of monsters that has been menacing the region for years. Traits Innate Spellcasting. Jurrg’s innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 18). Jurrg can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: darkness, invisibility 1/day each: charm person, cone of cold, gaseous form, sleep Magic Resistance. Jurrg has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. Magic Weapons. Jurrg’s weapon attacks are magical. Regeneration. Jurrg regains 10 hit points at the start of its turn if it has at least 1 hit point. Spellcasting. Jurrg is a 10th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 18, +10 to hit with spell attacks). Jurrg has the following bard spells known: Cantrips (at will): mage hand, message, minor illusion, vicious mockery 1st level (4 slots): charm person, cure wounds, longstrider 2nd level (3 slots): blur, mirror image, suggestion 3rd level (3 slots): dispel magic, slow, tongues 4th level (3 slots): confusion, dimension door, stoneskin 5th level (2 slots): cone of cold, hold monster Actions Multiattack. Jurrg makes three attacks, either with his claws or his greatsword. Claw (Oni Form Only). Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8+4) slashing damage. Greatsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (4d6+4) slashing damage, or 11 (2d6+4) slashing damage in Small or Medium form. Change Shape. Jurrg magically polymorphs into a Small or Medium humanoid, into a Large giant, or back into his true form. Other than his size, his statistics are the same in each form. The only equipment that is transformed is his greatsword, which shrinks so that it can be wielded in humanoid form. If Jurrg dies, he reverts to his true form, and his greatsword reverts to its normal size. Reactions Counterattack. When damaged by an enemy within reach, Jurrg makes a greatsword attack against the creature that hit him.
