# Amazon Warrior

**description**: A member of the tribe of proud war-like people, which typically is only formed by women. They are expert riders, sharpshooters and front-line fighters. They are all considered daughters of the God of War and many of them have been blessed by him, granted a war spirit that takes the form of a powerful steed. The steeds are only as powerful as the warriors themselves, so the most remarkable of Amazons are known to soar the skies atop Pegasi. The Amazon's Steed The mount of the Amazon Warrior should be stronger than the average specimen of its kind. Enhancements. The steed should possess 45 additional hit points, the Evasion trait and 18 AC. Humanoids Humanoids are the most plentiful creatures amongst the creations of the gods. They create vast kingdoms, they make unbelievable technological advances and even achieve breakthroughs in the art of magic. Traits Blessed Rider. The warrior cannot be dismounted against her will. Charging Assault. If the warrior moves at least 15 feet straight toward a target and then hits it with a melee weapon attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 10 (3d6) damage of the weapon's type. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 16 Strength saving throw or be pushed up to 15 feet away and knocked prone. Deadeye. The warrior adds +2 bonus to all attack rolls made with ranged weapons. Magic Weapons. The warrior's weapon attacks are magical. War-Blessed Marksman. A ranged weapon deals one extra die of its damage when the warrior hits with it (included in the attack). War God's Child. The warrior deals an additional 9 (2d8) radiant damage with all attacks. Actions Multiattack. The warrior makes three weapon attacks with her longsword or longbow. The warrior can replace a longsword attack with a shield bash. Longsword (One-Handed). Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) slashing damage plus 9 (2d8) radiant damage. Longsword (Two-Handed). Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d10 + 3) slashing damage plus 9 (2d8) radiant damage. Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d8 + 4) piercing damage plus 9 (2d8) radiant damage. Shield Bash. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage. If the target is large sized or smaller, it must make a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone or pushed back 5 ft. (warrior's choice) Bonus Actions Second Wind (Recharges after a short or long rest). The warrior regains 20 hit points.
