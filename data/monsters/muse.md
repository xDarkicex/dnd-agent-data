# Muse

**description**: Muses are the fey incarnation of inspiration, rousing poets, musicians, and artists to greater works of beauty. Each appears as a beautiful woman moving with impossible grace, a knowing smile on her lips. Muses are great scholars of all forms of artistic endeavor from poetry and music to theatre and dance. Each muse also specializes in one particular form of art or an academic field of study. Muses may spend centuries in their own private artistic creation and devotion to evoking beauty in the world. However, they often roam the world in mortal guise looking for particularly gifted mortal artists and scholars, whom they may observe for a time or offer their inspiration and encouragement to achieve great masterworks and illuminate the world with a touch of kindled joy.
