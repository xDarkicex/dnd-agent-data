# Sister of the Bloodshot Eye

**description**: Cultist Just as bandits are drawn to the Stolen Lands, all manner of cultists find this region ripe for hideouts and hidden shrines to deities and demigods whose worship has been outlawed in most cities and towns. Some of these cultists are solitary members of groups with influence throughout the world, while others, such as the Cultists of the Bloom, are entirely regional threats. Note that these cultists, along with the more powerful specialized cultists presented later in this book under the entry for Thresholders, can be used together as different agents of a sprawling sect of sinister worshippers in your game simply by changing their descriptions to match whatever nefarious deity or evil faith you’ve decided they might serve. Sister of the Bloodshot Eye The worship of the hag goddess Gyronna is more common in the River Kingdoms than elsewhere in the world. The Sisters of the Bloodshot Eye comprise an even smaller subset of Gyronna’s faithful—worshippers who seek to spread hate through the world by manipulating the ancestral beliefs of the unsuspecting. Those found in the Stolen Lands have specialized in corrupting the marauders known as the Tiger Lords. Trait Aura of Madness. Any enemy within 30 feet of the Sister of the Bloodshot Eye must succeed on a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw or become confused, as per the spell confusion. If the creature moves more than 30 feet from the Sister of the Bloodshot Eye, the effect ends immediately Spellcasting. The Sister of the Bloodshot Eye is a 10th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 16, +8 to hit with spell attacks). She has the following cleric spells prepared: Cantrips (at will): guidance, light, mending, resistance, thaumaturgy 1st level (4 slots): command, cure wounds, shield of faith 2nd level (3 slots): blindness/deafness, hold person, spiritual weapon 3rd level (3 slots): bestow curse, dispel magic, protection from energy 4th level (3 slots): blight, confusion, conjure minor elementals, death ward 5th level (2 slots): contagion, insect plague Actions Dagger. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60, one target. Hit: 2 (1d4) piercing damage.
