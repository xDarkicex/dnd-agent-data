# Dweorg Smith

**description**: Known for their superior quality in metallurgy, stonework, and crystalsculpt, dweorg revere “smiths” as both practical craftsman and savvy artisans. Specialized Skillset. A smith amongst the dweorg is known as a master of their trade, be it blacksmithing, sculpting, masonry, intaglio, engineering, baking, brewing, farming, or any other trade dwarves appreciate. A dweorg smith might be able to translate a subset of their skills to another task, but only at their chosen profession does their skillset truly shine.
