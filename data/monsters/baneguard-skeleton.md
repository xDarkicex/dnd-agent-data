# Baneguard Skeleton

**description**: Baneguards are skeletons animated by clerics of Bane to serve as guards for Bane's temples and churches. Some continue to haunt the ruins of such places. They are able to cast magic missiles and blink effortlessly. Unusual Nature. A skeleton doesn't require air, food, drink, or sleep. Actions Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8 + 2) slashing damage. Spellcasting. The skeleton casts one of the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: magic missile (2 missiles) 3/day: blink
