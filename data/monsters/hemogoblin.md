# Hemogoblin

**description**: Hemogoblins are bloodthirsty maniacal goblins infused with the magical powers of blood. They are blessed with a bloody frenzy and the ability to track their bleeding enemies. They mostly do not favor one weapon over another. They believe the best weapon is the one that bleeds the enemy the most. Most of them carry two weapons, thinking that more weapons will harm their enemies more. However, since they do not favor craftsmanship in their culture, most hemogoblins’ weapons are battle-worn. The hemogoblins love to wound their enemies first, let them escape with their bleeding wounds, and then track them down, ripping their flesh to pieces and drinking their blood in a wicked revelry of pure, malicious ecstasy. They are very easy to differentiate from normal goblins. They are always covered in blood, giving the appearance that their very skin is made of blood. The blood that constantly oozes out of their bodies makes them relatively easy to track. However, this is not a bad thing for them. If they can be tracked, then hunters can find them. And if hunters find them, they have more people to kill. Some hemogoblins evolve into a more powerful type of goblin after they spill and drink an immense amount of enemy blood. These hemogoblins are called “Bloodchiefs” by the other hemogoblins, and they generally lead the big raids and planned attacks. Some scholars call hemogoblins “rampage goblins”, while others believe them to be an entirely different species.
