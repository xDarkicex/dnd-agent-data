# Bandit

**description**: The bandits are a ragtag mob, some wearing leather vests, some slightly rusty steel hauberks. They will either use short bows or slings at a distance and wield a diverse array of weapons in close combat: swords, maces or clubs. One huge fellow actually looks similar to the troll-like man on the inn’s signboard. One never knows with these mountain people! The group of bandits will have at least 2–3 combatants more than the number of PCs. They should seem threatening enough to make the characters think about the proposed deal, but not too intimidating to prevent a fight, if the group feels up to it. The bandits’ exact strength should be calculated according to the PCs combat prowess. Actions Club. Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 2 (1d4) bludgeoning damage. Shortbow.
