# Threndavi Warrior

**description**: Threndavi warriors are the protectors of their Phaenec tribes. They are usually found near their kin. Some of them have lost their entire tribes during the apocalypse, and travel the world alone or in small groups. Others have left their tribes to embark on important quests.
