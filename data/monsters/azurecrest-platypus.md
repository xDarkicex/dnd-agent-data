# Azurecrest Platypus

**description**: The azurecrest platypus is known for its adorable appearance and bewildering abilities. The platypus has tawny fur, radiant blue webbed feet, and a golden and azure colored plume. Chaotic Shrieker Azurecrests are tempestuous and can emit shrieks that disorient other creatures. More notably, its presence warps magic, making it both beloved and feared by spellcasters. Scholars believe that this trait evolved as a defense mechanism against magical predators. Azurecrests tend to be cautious around magic and those who wield it. Bond of Curiosity Although most azurecrests are solitary, they occasionally bond with intelligent creatures, if they believe they may benefit from the partnership. Their needs are rather simple; they enjoy excitement and exotic snacks and are curious at their core, even if they sometimes act erratic, especially around magic. Annoying? Sometimes. Strange-looking? Sure. But also kinda cute, in its own, very weird way. Be warned, though; it has a habit of using its spellwarping abilities when it's most inconvenient! – the Wanderer
