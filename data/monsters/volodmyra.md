# Volodmyra

**description**: Volodmyra is a mid-ranking member of the Black Tears, and the commander of the attack force sent to invade Jamandi Aldori’s manor at the start of the Kingmaker Adventure Path . Her stats work well for any grizzled mercenary, or perhaps even an executioner or torturer in the employ of a cruel overlord. Traits Opportunist. Volodmyra has advantage on all opportunity attacks. Spinning Sweep. Every time Volodmyra hits a creature with a greataxe attack, she increases her speed by 10 feet until the end of her turn. Actions Multiattack. Volodmyra makes two greataxe attacks. Greataxe. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (1d12+4) slashing damage. Overhead Smash. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 17 (2d12+4) slashing damage plus the target must succeed a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
