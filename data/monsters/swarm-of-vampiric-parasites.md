# Swarm of Vampiric Parasites

**description**: A vampiric parasite is an insect that primarily feeds on blood. These gruesome Tiny beasts resemble centipedes with pincers on their body that pierce into the flesh on contact as they move, which they do in a swift manner considering their size. Vampiric parasites have countless teeth-like structures inside their mouths, which can tear into one’s body and help them suck blood. They target creatures with blood to be able to feed. Vampiric parasites are originally creatures of the Tolian sea depths; however, they can live both in and out of water, and they can easily lay their eggs inside a creature. Their eggs hatch a day after they have been laid, the larvae feed on the body of the host, and this causes the creature to suffer from blood diseases. The disease cannot be cured completely without getting all of the larvae out of the body. If it is not cured, the creature dies after a week of painful stomach aches. When the host dies, a new swarm of vampiric parasites spawns out of the corpse.
