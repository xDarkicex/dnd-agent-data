# Blighted Beast

**description**: Blighted Beasts are Dangerous II creatures, both in their violent and aggressive nature and as a result of their ability to spread the Shadeblight disease. As a group, they are primarily made up of predatory mammals, though there are rare occurrences of much less conspicuously Dangerous II creatures becoming carriers of Shadeblight as well. Due to the nature of their disease, blighted beasts often appear ragged and malnourished, or even gangrenous. Their fur (if they have it) is greasy and matted, and their eyes and bodily fluids glow a sickly green. Unlike other afflicted creatures, blighted beasts never fully succumb to the disease, instead surviving to pass it on to any who survive their attacks and even use it in their hunts. Beasts of the Waste. They are driven to congregate in packs, and where they gather in sufficient numbers the disease they carry can even spread to plants; causing a horrible wilting and blackening of flora in their environment and leaving behind an inhospitable wasteland. It is for this reason that they are occasionally referred to as "beasts of the waste". Tactics. As pack hunters, blighted beasts tend to avoid conflict unless they outnumber their prey or can reasonably assume their prey is able to be singled out from its group. They focus on one opponent at a time when they can, but also have a sense that their bites can weaken their foes, and will try to bite any hostile creature that has not yet been bitten. The beasts will flee a losing fight, but may track their bitten prey, watching for disease progression that may weaken them further for a future hunt. Traits Injury Threshold. II: 15, III: 20, IIII: 30, Damage Loss: 0. Pack Tactics. The blighted beast has advantage on attack rolls as long as the target is within 5 feet of one of its allies. Actions Multiattack. The blighted beast makes a Corrupted Bite and two Claw attacks. Corrupted Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: (1d8+3) piercing damage plus (5d6) poison damage. The target must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned until the end of its next turn. A creature that fails its saving throw by 5 or more contracts the Shadeblight disease. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: (1d6+4) slashing damage.
