# Pixie

**description**: Sprite Elusive, flighty, and ebullient, sprites are what many first imagine when they hear the terms “fey” and “fairy.” While there are a wide range of sprites, the largest of them rarely match the size of even a small child—and the vast majority of these wee fey are much smaller. Pixie Insatiably curious, overly excitable, and just a bit puckish, the pixies of the Stolen Lands are more powerful than others of their kind. They are wanderers and tricksters who use their pixie dust to create all sorts of whimsical situations, as well as to defend themselves. Other creatures often have trouble understanding a pixie’s rapid, rambling way of speaking. A pixie of the Stolen Lands looks like a tiny little elf, typically with solid white eyes and clad in clothing or armor made from bits of tree bark, leaves, and flower petals. They flit about on a set of brightly-colored butterfly wings. Traits Innate Spellcasting. The pixie’s innate spellcasting ability which is Charisma (spell save DC 13). The pixie can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: invisibility (self only) 1/day: entangle, faerie fire 3/day: dancing lights, detect magic, minor illusion, shield Sprinkle Pixie Dust. As a bonus action, the pixie sprinkles pixie dust onto one of its arrows. If the pixie hits a creature with that arrow before the start of the pixie’s next turn, the deals no damage. Instead, the target must make a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the arrow causes one of the following effects, based on the target’s saving throw result: Charm (11–13). The creature is charmed by another creature of the pixie’s choice. Memory Loss (7–10). The target makes a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw, losing the last 5 minutes of its memory on a failed save. Sleep (2–6). The pixie rolls 9d8. If the target has less hit points than the pixie’s result, it falls unconscious. Subdual (1). The target makes a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw, taking 14 (4d6) psychic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. If the pixie’s ranged attack scores a critical hit, treat the results of the target’s original saving throw as one tier lower. Actions Multiattack. The pixie makes two attacks. Rapier. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d8+5) piercing damage. Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d8+5) piercing damage.
