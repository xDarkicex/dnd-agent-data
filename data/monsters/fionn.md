# Fionn

**description**: Fionn is a very unusual ghost of a very unusual frost giant from the First World. These statistics work best when used for a unique foe in a long-term campaign, where can you tie Fionn’s tragic story to one of the PCs’ allies, or even to one of the PCs. You can adjust his Bound to the Mortal Night weakness as well to be keyed to other times of the day, month, or even a year. Traits Bound to the Mortal Night. Fionn’s restless spirit is bound to the night itself. He can travel anywhere he wishes, so long as it is night. During the day, he cannot travel further than thirty feet from the location he occupies as the sun rises. If he gains an effect that would normally transport him to another plane, he instead remains on the Material Plane and is stunned until the end of his next turn. Ethereal Sight. Fionn can see 60 feet into the Ethereal Plane. Incorporeal Movement. Fionn can move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain. He takes 5 (1d10) force damage if he ends his turn inside an object. Innate Spellcasting. Fionn’s innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 17, +9 to hit with spell attacks). He can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: ray of frost 3/day each: dispel magic, slow, speak with dead 1/day each: cone of cold, wall of ice Actions Multiattack. Fionn makes two attacks with his ghostly battleaxe or his withering touch. Alternately, he can make one attack with each. Ghostly Battleaxe. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d8+5) necrotic damage plus 11 (2d10) cold damage. Withering Touch. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 19 (4d6+5) necrotic damage. Legendary Actions Fionn can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature’s turn. Fionn regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn. Detect. Fionn makes a Wisdom (Perception) check. Command Undead (Costs 2 Actions). Fionn chooses two undead minions within 30 feet. A chosen undead uses its reaction to perform either an Attack on an enemy, or a Dash action to move to an enemy. Fearsome Blizzard (Costs 3 Actions). Fionn exhales a swirling blizzard of snow and ice that chills both flesh and soul. All creatures other than those with the undead type within 30 feet must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 21 (6d6) cold damage and is frightened of Fionn for 1 round. On a successful save, the creature takes half damage and is not frightened.
