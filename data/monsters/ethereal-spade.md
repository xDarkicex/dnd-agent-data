# Ethereal Spade

**description**: “If you dig enough graves, you learn all you need to about life.” It is thought that this common proverb amongst the dødelig has its origins with this macabre construct. What sliver of intelligence or soul gives life to an ethereal spade is a mystery. Perhaps it is a confluence of latent necromancy and the ambient energy of grief that accompanies death, for these former grave-digging tools are commonly found in cemeteries and ossuaries. Regardless of their origins, an ethereal spade watches over their territory with silent aplomb. Trespassers, graverobbers, and even scavengers learn to avoid any place that has been adopted by an ethereal spade. Mortal Bond. Certain mindful visitors, such as those who come to honor their dead, are not only tolerated but could find an ally in an ethereal spade. A character who practices such reverence regularly, such as a priest of death, could find themselves the new focus of a spade. Though never truly wielded as a weapon in the traditional sense, the spade does allow its bonded character to travel the Border Ethereal as it does.
