# Halfling Templar

**description**: A halfling templar is a crusader and missionary, intent on bringing light and truth into dark places, whatever cost. To fellow travelers their cheerful demeanor can become overbearing at times. In many places, despite being a gentle, helpful sort, a halfling templar soon finds themselves on the road again as their welcome runs out as quickly as does the variety of subjects within their proselytizing.
