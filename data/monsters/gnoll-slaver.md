# Gnoll Slaver

**description**: Even in the Underworld, with all its sinister and distinctly unwholesome elements, gnolls are rarely welcomed anywhere; barely tolerated is perhaps the best reaction they get. They are, however, valued as mercenaries: ruthless, brutal, and better as allies than as enemies. It’s not uncommon for a tribe to appear at the outskirts of a battle and accept payment in advance to turn the tide in one direction or another.
