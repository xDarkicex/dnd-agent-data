# Spartan Soldier (Archer)

**description**: The life of a Spartan is oftentimes quite grim. Discipline, combat, death and duty are the driving forces in their society. This brutal way of life has resulted in a kingdom where even the weakest of soldiers still stands heads and shoulders above your average warrior and bandit. Furthermore, Spartans are at their strongest when they work together. A fact they’re well aware of, which has led to their strong collective approach to combat in groups. Facing a Spartan unit is dangerous at best, foolish at worst. If you do so, you better hope you thoroughly outclass or outnumber them, because anything else would be a recipe for disaster. Spartan Army Sparta is the largest military power in the entirety of the continent, with almost half of their population being dedicated to the army in some capacity, and the rest dedicated to the constant development of their city. With this overwhelming might, neighboring kingdoms and city-states cannot help but look for approval from the Spartans when it comes to commerce, trade and alliances, lest they incur their wrath. Although the presence of arcane expertise is much smaller in comparison to Athens, an incredibly dedicated clergy is present in Sparta. The Faith in Warfare, following the teachings and will of Ares, the God of War, is the dominating religion of the few allowed within the territory of Sparta. As a result, powerful clerics and paladins serving the brutal ideals of combat and conflict fill out the upper echelon of the army’s best. Masters of the Land. Despite having a rather weak naval presence in comparison to Athens, Sparta is unmatched when it comes to direct encounters within solid ground. There have been tales over the years of the Spartan Army sweeping through the land and never stopping. An unstoppable force of the battlefield, as many kingdoms can attest to. Even powerful and ancient beings like dragons avoid antagonizing the Spartans without good reason. Humanoids Humanoids are the most plentiful creatures amongst the creations of the gods. They create vast kingdoms, they make unbelievable technological advances and even achieve breakthroughs in the art of magic. Traits Archery Expert.
