# The Beast

**description**: The enormous owlbear called “the Beast” should in fact carry the name of the PC’s kingdom in its name. In your games, the Beast could well be a regional threat to the realm you’ve chosen as the focus of your campaign, changing its name to “The Beast of Sandpoint” or “The Beast of Falcon’s Hollow” or whatever sounds appropriate. Alternately, the Beast’s stats could simply serve as those for a particularly enormous breed of owlbears native to a region in your game. Traits Keen Sight and Smell. The Beast has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight and smell. Actions Multiattack. The Beast makes three melee attacks: one with its bite and two with its claws. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d6+7) piercing damage. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 17 (3d6+7) slashing damage.
