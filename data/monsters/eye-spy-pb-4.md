# Eye Spy (PB 4)

**description**: Actions (Requires your Bonus Action) Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: your Intelligence modifier + 4 (PB) to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: your hemocraft die + 4 (PB) piercing damage. Eye Ray. The eye spy shoots one of its magical eye rays at random, choosing one target it can see within 60 feet of it. If it has already used the eye ray this turn, reroll until you roll one it hasn’t. Each eye ray’s save DC equals your hemocraft save DC.
