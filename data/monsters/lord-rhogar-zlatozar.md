# Lord Rhogar Zlatozar

**description**: Lord Rhogar Zlatozar is a zealous paladin of Magic and the sole descendent of the Xantharosian half-gold dragon clan of his name, tracing back his heritage and sacred oath to over two thousand years. Ever since discovering and harnessing the powers of the orb of Conjuration under his Clan Fort, Lord Rhogar Zlatozar has considered himself the Spellhoarder —the divine emissary one of the Wondermaker chosen to reign over the Isles of Xantharos.
