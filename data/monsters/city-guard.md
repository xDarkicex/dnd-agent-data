# City Guard

**description**: All of Florence’s city gates are manned by small units of guards. Two men will usually be on active duty, and two others stand by in case backup is needed. All gates are sealed after nightfall. The guards report to the gonfaloniere di giustizia. Savonarola can therefore not give them direct orders. But most are fearful of God’s (and Savonarola’s) damnation and will therefore go along with Savonarola’s suggestions. Here are 4 guards for you to use: Arturo: A rather stocky fellow with brown hair and moderate intelligence. Marco: A tall young guard, naive but dutiful. Massimo di Carlo: An older fellow with a round belly. He is the sergeant of this unit. (If the characters inquired about the death of Paola in Act 1, they might have already met him.) Francesco: A bald guy of average height who is quite quick. All guards are well equipped with pikes, shortswords and metal plated leather armor. Actions Pike. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d10 + 2) piercing damage. Short Sword. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.
