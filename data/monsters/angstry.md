# Angstry

**description**: There can be no doubt that the marvelous and magical material, angst, is the key to the current technological advancement in the region, and that it has made life much easier in many ways. However, there are some who use angst crystals in inappropriate ways for inappropriate reasons. They either eat angst dust, inhale it, or inject themselves with foul alchemical mixtures made from it. Such careless behavior results in unexpected allergic reactions, internal bleeding, or multiple-organ failure. However, if one takes too much angst into their system at once, they enter an unending frenzy, losing their identity and understanding of the environment, and searching for more angst to consume in a state of neverending delirium from that point on. These people, called angstries, are no longer what they used to be, and have become hunters of the unlucky masses for the smallest pinch of angst. An angstry is a highly tough creature. It does not require food or water to sustain itself. Angst feeds it, yet it is also not mandatory for it to stay alive. Consuming angst just makes the angstry more powerful, and more addicted. There is some ongoing research about angstries, especially on how to turn them back to their original state. But none of them have yet bore fruit.
