# Goldbug

**description**: The goldbug is a strange sort of insect, a creature whose round, flat body resembles a gold coin. This “disguise” helps goldbugs get close to their prized food source: gold. When placed in a pouch or chest along with the gold coins it resembles, a goldbug will happily devour the coins at the rate of one per day. Goldbugs live underground near veins of gold and are common in areas where gold is used as coinage and in art objects.
