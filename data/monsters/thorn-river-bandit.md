# Thorn River Bandit

**description**: Criminal Wildlands and cities alike have long been infested by all manner of bandit, brigand, and other forms of criminals. The Stolen Lands themselves are notorious for harboring bandits. Thorn River Bandit The least of the bandits in the central Stolen Lands are known collectively as Thorn River bandits—named for the body of water whose banks they prefer to camp along. The local wildlife along the Thorn River isn’t as dangerous as in other parts of the Stolen Lands, offering these bandits a safe place to gather and share news. Actions Shortsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6+2) piercing damage. Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, range 150 ft./600 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d8+1) piercing damage.
