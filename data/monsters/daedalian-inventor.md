# Daedalian Inventor

**description**: The grand inventor Daedalus is a massive source of inspiration for these tinkerers. They have taken the designs of the ancient engineer and made it their own. With grand mechanical wings, their movement is remarkable, loaded up with several weapons and devices within the bronze feathers at their back. Humanoids Humanoids are the most plentiful creatures amongst the creations of the gods. They create vast kingdoms, they make unbelievable technological advances and even achieve breakthroughs in the art of magic. Traits Spellcasting. The inventor's Spellcasting Ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 17, +9 to hit with spell attacks). They can cast the following spells: 3/day each: divine favor, shield 2/day each: branding smite, locate creature, slow, tasha's mind whip 1/day each: steel wind strike, summon construct Daedalian Craft. The inventor adds their Intelligence modifier to all weapon attack and damage rolls, instead of Strength or Dexterity (included in the attack). Magic Weapons. The inventor's weapon attacks are magical. Actions Multiattack. The inventor makes three weapon attacks in any combination. Daedalian Blade. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 10 (1d10 + 5) slashing damage plus 9 (2d8) force damage. Daedalian Crossbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 100/400ft., one target. Hit: 10 (1d10 + 5) slashing damage plus 9 (2d8) force damage. Daedalian Feather Barrage (Recharge 5-6). The inventor unleashes a razor-sharp storm of bronze feathers in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw, taking 33 (6d10) piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Additionally, if a creature fails the save their walking speed is reduced by 10 feet until the creatures regain at least 1 hit point. Bonus Actions Sharing Wings (Recharges after a short or long rest). The inventor grants a flying speed of 50 ft. to a willing creature they touch for 1 minute.
