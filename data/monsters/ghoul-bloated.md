# Ghoul, Bloated

**description**: Bloated ghouls are ghouls who have engaged in ritual gorging on the flesh of hundreds of humanoids. Followers of the Bloated Path, the ghouls’ bodies swell with undigested meat and bone. They eventually become pot-bellied undead maddened with hunger. A bloated ghoul’s lair is often slick with spilled blood and guts, which it happily licks off the floor and walls. Hungry Dead Nature. The ghoul requires no air or sleep.
