# Lurker on the Threshold

**description**: The Lurker on the Threshold is a vile and maniacal being, drifting into our realm through thin places and screaming in the minds of those it witnesses. It laughs in hysterical dissonant tones and peers out with its three unblinking dark pools of eyes, with a mass of fleshy tendrils dangling from its slimy form. It ripples with black voidlike hues and splashes of deep purples and blues as if its flesh was a moving image of the cosmos.
