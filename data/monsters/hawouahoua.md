# H'awouahoua

**description**: This fiend is a chimeric collection of animal parts fused together in a vaguely humanoid form, mostly hidden under a draping patchwork coat. Its torso is like a turtle, complete with a hard shell. It is bipedal, standing on one cloven-hoofed goat leg, and the leg of a panther. One arm ends in a monkey’s paw, the other a crab-like claw. Its head is apish, with flaming eyes and curling, ram’s horns. Instead of hair, asps drape down over its head and shoulders. A segmented tail bearing a scorpion’s sting extends out of the bottom of its shell, arcing up to hover over the h’awouahoua’s head. The h’awouahoua is deceptively swift, despite the awkward, hodgepodge makeup of its body. It quickly rushes prey to kill them, flaying the body afterwards and adding the skin to its grotesque coat, then feasting upon the flesh and bones. Soul Collector. The souls of those whose skin is sewn into the h’awouahoua’s coat are bound to those mortal remains. The h’awouahoua uses them to power its magical abilities, and also trades them to other fiends for goods and services. They regularly traffick souls to night hags. When a soul leaves the h’awouahoua’s possession, the skin to which it was bound falls away from the coat and crumbles to dust.
