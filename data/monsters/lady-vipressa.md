# Lady Vipressa

**description**: Background Vipressa Veilshade was born into wealth and power. As the oldest of six sisters, Vipressa has commanded others as long as she's been able to speak. Since her mother died – the medusa matriarch's neck severed by an overzealous adventurer's blade – Vipressa has been responsible for both her siblings and the family business: a lucrative emerald mine. Chafing under Vipressa's domineering rule, her sisters struck out on their own one after another, until Vipressa eventually found herself alone in her subterranean mine, save for a dozen minotaur workers. Envious of the freedom her sisters enjoyed, Vipressa decided to sell the mine and become a merchant. For a few years now, Lady Vipressa, assisted by her minotaur guard Arôn, has run Vipressa's Wares. From her maze-like shop, she sells magic items and adventuring equipment – most of which was left behind by thieves and adventurers who have trespassed into the medusa family's lair over the decades. Roleplaying Lady Vipressa As beautiful as she is arrogant, Vipressa is accustomed to obedience. She can be prideful and domineering and anything – or anyone – that fails to meet her exacting standards is met with derisive remarks ("This dagger is not as I was promised – clearly the work of a second-rate enchanter”). She is also quick to respond to disrespect with thinly-veiled threats ("That's what I like about statues – they don't talk back!”) Those who can see past her difficult demeanor, however, will discover that Lady Vipressa can also be delightfully charming when she chooses to. While in the company of those she likes or wants to impress – such as her sisters, her favored minotaur servant, Arôn, or handsome, wealthy strangers – Vipressa is quick to offer genuine compliments ("Your dress is absolutely stunning, dear, you must tell me where you've bought it!”) and outright flattery ("I see that there's no need to make a statue out of you, warrior, for you are as if carved out of stone already!”) Aside from tending to her shop, Lady Vipressa is also an enthusiastic bard and performer. She loves playing music, acting (she attends weekly acting classes and performs in amateur theaters), and poetry recitals. Vipressa appreciates nothing more than being able to perform – and, of course, receiving due praise!
