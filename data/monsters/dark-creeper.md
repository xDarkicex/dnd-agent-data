# Dark Creeper

**description**: Barely more than a malicious thought made real, dark creepers seem to be little more than a manifestation of a grudge, lie, or hatred given physical form. These foul little humanoids feed on yet more bile behavior to survive, stalking good-hearted folks and drawing sustenance from their suffering.
