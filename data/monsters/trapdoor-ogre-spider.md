# Trapdoor Ogre Spider

**description**: Spider Giant spiders are feared by many, and those that dwell in the Stolen Lands are certainly adept at giving adventurers new things to fear beyond being merely poisoned or caught in a sticky web. Trapdoor Ogre Spider In the easternmost plains of the Stolen Lands, giant trapdoor spiders grow particularly large to become behemoths capable of snatching mammoths for meals. Traits Eerie Flexibility. A trapdoor ogre spider can move through any space large enough for a Large creature. Trapdoor Lunge. If the spider surprises a creature and hits it with an attack during the first round of combat, the target takes an extra 7 (2d6) damage from the attack. The spider can then move up to its speed. Spider Climb. The spider can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check. Web Walker. The spider ignores movement restrictions caused by webbing. Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d8+4) piercing damage and the target must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 18 (4d8) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. If the poison damage reduces the target to 0 hit points, the target is stable but poisoned for 1 hour, even after regaining hit points, and is paralyzed while poisoned this way.
