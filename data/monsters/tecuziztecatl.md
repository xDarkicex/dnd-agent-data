# Tecuziztecatl

**description**: Clinging to the wall of a water-filled chamber inside the Hidden Shrine of Tamoachan is Tecuziztecatl, also known as the Lord of Snails. This giant slug is not only intelligent, it's capable of speech. If faced with enemies that are too much of a threat for it, the slug might offer to provide assistance, or it might submerge itself to avoid further harm. Traits Amphibious . Tecuziztecatl can breathe air and water. Glowing . Tecuziztecatl sheds dim light within 20 feet of itself. Flexible . Tecuziztecatl can enter a space large enough for a Medium creature without squeezing. Spider Climb . Tecuziztecatl can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check. Actions Multiattack. Tecuziztecatl makes two pseudopod attacks. Pseudopod. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: (2d8+3) bludgeoning damage. Spit Acid (Recharge 4-6). Tecuziztecatl exhales acid in a 30-foot line that is 5 feet wide. Each creature in that line must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 18 (4d8) acid damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
