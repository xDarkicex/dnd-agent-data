# Trollhound

**description**: Trollhounds are squat, slavering beasts akin to trolls in canine form. Requiring vast quantities of meat to fuel their regenerative metabolisms, packs of wild trollhounds prowl the foothills of regions where trolls dwell, their voracious hunger driving them to slaughter and consume prey. In some regions, trolls breed trollhounds as pets, utilizing the beasts’ keen sense of smell to aid in the hunt. A trollhound looks like a squat, broad-shouldered hound, save that its mostly hairless body has green flesh studded with patches of tan warts and pustules. Traits Keen Hearing and Smell. The trollhound has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing and smell. Regeneration. The trollhound regains 5 hit points at the start of his turn. If it takes acid or fire damage, this trait doesn’t function at the start of the trollhound’s next turn. The trollhound dies only if it starts his turn with 0 hit points and doesn’t regenerate. Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (3d6+4) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
