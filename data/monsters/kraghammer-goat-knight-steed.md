# Kraghammer Goat-Knight Steed

**description**: A goat-knight’s steed uses the giant goat stat block with the following modifications: Its Intelligence score is 6 (−2), and it can understand Dwarvish. Its creature type is celestial. Any spell the goat-knight casts with a range of self also targets the goat. When a goat-knight steed drops to 0 hit points, it disappears, leaving behind no physical form. The goat-knight can also dismiss the steed at any time as an action, causing it to disappear. In either case, the same steed is summoned when the goat-knight casts find steed . Traits Charge. If the steed moves at least 20 ft. straight toward a target and then hits it with a ram attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 5 (2d4) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. Sure-Footed . The steed has advantage on Strength and Dexterity saving throws made against effects that would knock it prone. Actions Ram. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (2d4+3) bludgeoning damage.
