# Anka

**description**: First mate of Captain Callous, Anka is a simple ogre pirate who loves life on the High Seas. Although she’s somewhat dim-witted, Anka has a good natural sense of direction and is a dedicated crewmember of the Devil’s Fin. Anka’s most curious feature is her missing arm, which has been replaced with an enormous ship’s anchor. Actions Anchor Hand. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (2d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage.
