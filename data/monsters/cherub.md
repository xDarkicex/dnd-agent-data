# Cherub

**description**: Cherub When gods receive prayers for protection but lack personnel, the miniscule cherubim bravely answer the call. Standing at only six inches tall, these four-winged celestials weave their radiant magic to fulfil their god-given tasks, which range from protecting the devout to delivering divine blessings. Unfortunately, their eagerness to prove their worth often gets them killed. Cherubim Swarms. Even the holiest of celestials are capable of making mistakes. In rare instances, when a solar doesn’t notice that the same repeated prayers belong to a single individual or when the death of a planetar breaks the chain of command, multiple cherubim are assigned to the same mortal. United by their common cause, the cherubim form a swarm, which is capable of channelling divine energy to distract foes with blinding light.
