# Gigantopithecus

**description**: A gigantopithecus is a large ape that stands over 10 feet tall and weighs over 500 kilograms. It resembles an orangutan with a thick, muscular body and a prominent jaw. While somewhat territorial, most species of gigantopithecus aren't aggressive and live on a diet of fruits, nuts, and vegetables. However, there are a few clans that, driven by starvation, have grown accustomed to the taste of meat. Actions Multiattack. The gigantopithecus makes two Fist attacks. Fist.
