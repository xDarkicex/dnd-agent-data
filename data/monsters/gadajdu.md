# Gadajdu

**description**: Gadajdu Gadajdu are predatory humanoids that hunt in the dark network of tunnels which connect the daylit underworlds. It waits, and like a spider, pounces on any animal or human victim that passes by. They speak their own strange chittering language and creep along walls, floors, ceilings, and small crevices with equal alacrity, able to move through seemingly impossibly small spaces without breaking stride. Some call them spiderfolk, but they have no particular affinity for arachnids, simply emulating their hunting tactics. Gadajdus do not like bright sunlight and are entirely nocturnal, fleeing with the first rays of dawn. Torchlight may give them pause but not for long, as hunger can drive them to leave the shadows and strike, hoping to disable their prey and drag it off into the darkness to feast upon at their leisure.
