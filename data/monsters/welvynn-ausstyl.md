# Welvynn Ausstyl

**description**: The archdruid of the Inheritors of the Unbegotten is served even by the circle’s other druids as a godling. The vile drow druid has taken their entire house to the Farrealm thousands of years ago and has returned to avenge their exile and to sacrifice the Feyrealm as a whole to his aberrant masters. Welvynn Ausstyl is motivated only by the service to the entities he and his circle venerates and has no remorse about achieving the goals set forth by them. Equipment Welvynn Ausstyl wears a robe of eyes and wields a whip of tentacles .
