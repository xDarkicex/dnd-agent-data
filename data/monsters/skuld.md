# Skuld

**description**: Skuld the Valkry Skuld has walked the world of Grimnir for several decades. Though she pretends otherwise, she does remember that she once was a valkry. Ages ago she was caught by an outcast magician when he led an ambush party against a raider clan during a funeral. The attack was intentional—the wizard hoped to capture a valkry as she came to bring a hero to the Halls of the Slain. She never removes her armor, but if seen without it there are two parallel scars running down her back from where the magician cut her wings off, to use them in a ritual spell. Due to the lack of her wings she cannot fly. Skuld is very knowledgeable about the true nature of death in Grimnir but avoids talking about it—or anything. Still, she will try to guide the heroes from any wrong conclusions they may draw regarding the afterlife. If the heroes seem to think Hel is evil, for example, she attempts to steer them straight. Roleplaying Skuld Stoic. She was once bitter about being trapped in Grimnir but now accepts her fate and believes that others should as well. She keeps to herself and appears often lost in thought. Reckless. Willing to take risks, Skuld does not think about the lives of those around her. Fully confident in her martial skills she would rather engage an enemy than allow herself to be trapped, taken prisoner, or helpless again.
