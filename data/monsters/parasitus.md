# Parasitus

**description**: The result of cruel experiments, the Parasitus lives in the dark shadows of Tolia. Some say that they escaped from the labs of the universities, while others believe that they were released by a group of students. Either way, they can be seen in sewers, dark streets, caves, basements, and train stations.
