# Hrigg Roundrook (Level 7)

**description**: Hrigg is a gregarious dwarf who laughs heartily and lives to feast on the finest food and fight the vilest evil. He is one of five siblings, each of whom were chosen by Kiri-Jolith, the god of war, to receive his divine power. From their homeland of Kayolin, the five siblings each set out on their own path to bring Kiri-Jolith’s message directly to the Dragon Armies. Hrigg’s path brought him to Kalaman, where he happily finds himself on the front line of an invasion. Traits Bonus Proficiencies. Hrigg is proficient with simple and martial weapons and light and medium armor. Dwarven Resilience. Hrigg has advantage on saving throws made to avoid or end the poisoned condition on himself. Spellcasting. Hrigg’s spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 14, +6 to hit with spell attacks).
