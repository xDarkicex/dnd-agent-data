# Seerfish

**description**: Sea Monsters The Serpent Sea, as well as the many rivers and lakes of Grimnir, are host to a wide variety of fish and aquatic mammals, such as seals and whales. The creatures detailed here are, for the most part, the most dangerous of sea borne predators, save for the great serpent herself. Seerfish The völv nurture these primal fish in a nursery within their temple near the Well of Wisdom, but seerfish are also occasionally found by fishers. Under the care of the völv, several of the largest seerfish can be harnessed to tow the floating town of Drifthall. Through magical means the völv guide and direct these seerfish and steer Drifthall across the sea. This effort, however, puts tremendous strain on the seerfish, and they usually die shortly after returning Drifthall to its resting place. Bottomfeeders. The reason these fish are seldom encountered is that, in the wild, they spend much of their lifecycle at the bottom of the Serpent Sea. Most raiders go their entire lives without encountering one of these fish. Vulnerable. Seerfish found in the wild are never as large as the ones that völv have prepared for the duty of hauling Drifthall, for they have many predators.
