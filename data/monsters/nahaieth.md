# Nahai'eth

**description**: Nahai'eth is a high elf wizard who sells magic wares from her home within the hollow trunk of the Blood Willow . The strange alabaster tree's vein-like roots and branches can absorb blood and convert it into raw magic that Nahai'eth uses to fuel her enchantments. The magical tree can magically teleport itself and everything within it up to a hundred miles, disappearing and appearing in a shroud of heavy mist. Nahai'eth usually wills the tree to do so every few weeks and typically has her shop appear in dense forest areas where the veil between the Material Plane and the Fey Realm is thin. Background Descending from a long lineage of wizards, Nahai'eth was sent away to an academy of arcane studies as a young child. What she lacked in motivation, Nahai'eth compensated for with raw talent. After only a few decades of study, she was already a skilled wizard and chosen to serve in her king's royal army as a warmage. Nahai'eth's service was short-lived, however. In her very first battle, she was gravely wounded by a troll's cruel claws. With no way of healing herself and no help in sight, the young elf bled to death under the sprawling crown of an ancient willow tree. As she died, her blood seeped into the soil and suffused the willow's roots. Through some inexplicable magic, the tree seemed to absorb Nahai'eth's essence and assumed an alabaster, blood-streaked appearance that matched her own. As elf and willow became bonded by blood, Nahai'eth was returned to life – albeit changed forever. Nahai'eth quickly realized that she weakened with every step she took away from the tree, almost as if its magic kept her alive. Thus, she made herself a home within the tree's trunk and began studying the weird blood magic that had brought her back to life. Over the years, Nahai'eth has learned how to use the willow tree's blood-fueled magic to weave enchantments and teleport the tree across vast distances. Always in need of blood to fuel her studies, Nahai'eth invites adventurers and other wanderers to purchase the items she crafts within the Blood Willow. Roleplaying Nahai'eth Nahai'eth is a fair, alabaster-skinned elf with red scars covering her face and gleaming red eyes – eerie side effects of her resurrection and experiments with blood magic. She displays formal etiquette worthy of a high elf noble – using honorifics and being polite to a fault – but possesses none of the superiority and condescension common among her kin. The elf's brush with death has left her somewhat pessimistic and she can seem overly gloomy to strangers ("The average life expectancy of an adventurer is less than two years, so you'll excuse me if I'm not eager to extend any credit”). She also has a disturbing habit of casually guessing at when a customer will perish ("You're a lone warrior tasked with protecting three spellcasters? I'd recommend a good life insurance – or a career change.”)
