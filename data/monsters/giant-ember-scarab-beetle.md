# Giant Ember Scarab Beetle

**description**: These insects are about 3 feet long, with iridescent carapaces and a glowing anterior. Traits Illumination: The giant ember scarab beetle sheds bright light in a 15-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet. This illumination is generated from a pair of anterior glands. If removed, the glands continue to shed light for 1d4 days. Actions Bite: Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d6 + 1) slashing damage.
