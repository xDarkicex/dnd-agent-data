# Warlock of the River Styx

**description**: Warlocks of the River Styx gain their powers by forging a magical pact with the river of the Underworld, submerging themselves in its waters after a secret ritual has taken place. The nature of the pact is mysterious at best, incomprehensible at worst, as communication with their patron ranges from vague whispers in dreams to nonexistent. Some of these Warlocks associate with clerical groups who worship chthonic gods, as they have a close association with the River Styx by virtue of belonging to the realm of Hades. Humanoids Humanoids are the most plentiful creatures amongst the creations of the gods. They create vast kingdoms, they make unbelievable technological advances and even achieve breakthroughs in the art of magic. Traits Power of the Styx. The warlock can add their Charisma modifier to one damage roll of any spell attack. Spellcasting. The warlock's spellcasting Ability is Charisma (spell save DC 17, +9 to hit with spell attacks). They can cast the following spells: At will: control water, mage hand, minor illusion, prestidigitation, speak with dead 3/day each: blight, hex, summon undead 2\day each: enervation, spiritual weapon (cast as a 5th-level spell) 1/day each: antilife shell, circle of death, contact other plane, death ward Stygian Skin. While the warlock is wearing no armor and wielding no shield, their AC is calculated by combining their Constitution and Charisma modifiers. Actions Multiattack. The warlock makes three stygian strikes or blasts in any combination. Stygian Strike. Melee Spell Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d10 + 5) necrotic damage. Stygian Blast. Ranged Spell Attack: +9 to hit, reach 120ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d10 + 5) necrotic damage. Stygian Geyser (2/day). Each creature in a 10-footradius, 30-foot-high Cylinder centered on a point within 120 feet from the warlock must make a DC 17 Constitution saving throw, on a success they take 36 (8d8) necrotic damage, on a failure the creatures take the damage and become vulnerable to either bludgeoning, piercing or slashing damage until the beginning of the warlock's next turn. Reactions Protection of the Waters (2/day). When the warlock takes damage from a spell or magical effect, they gain resistance to that damage type for the next minute.
