# Vampire Sentry

**description**: Vampire sentries are elite guards who protect the important places in Kanrath. They can be seen patrolling places that are off-limits to mortals or that have an immense impact on Kanrath’s economy (such as Kanrath train station). Vampire sentries are known for their speed and alacrity. They can use, and have the ability to use, all kinds of weapons, yet they choose to use the weapons that they can wield with speed and dexterity. Their favored weapons are either bloodwork weapons or a special graceful sword made for the sentries called the “Kanrath falchion”. They do not wear any armor, as they rely on their dexterous nature to escape attacks. Their uniforms are luxury suits. The sight of these suits may bring joy to vampires, while it inspires terror in others Since these suits are their signature, people also call them “suits”. Since vampire sentries can’t enter a residence without an invitation from one of the occupants, and they bring Kanrath enforcers with them if they have to enter mortal residences without being invited in first.
