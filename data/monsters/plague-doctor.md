# Plague Doctor

**description**: The plague doctor speaks through their muffled mask, eyeing up the battlefield. Behind their large trench coat is thick armor and a bandoleer of toxins and chemicals. They have concocted rare and deadly diseases and poisons to torment their enemies with, and are eager to see their performance on the battlefield.
