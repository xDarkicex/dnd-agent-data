# Stellar Bunny

**description**: Stellar Bunny Likes: playing in the night sky, being cuddled and stroked, leafy greens Dislikes : confinement, predators with sharp teeth, jump scares Shy and kind-hearted, stellar bunnies are tiny celestial critters that hover jollily in the sky and glow with soft starlight. Wary of predators and erratic mortals, stellar bunnies will often use their starry camouflage to observe a creature while hiding against the clear night sky. If they decide it’s safe to approach, they will carefully fly closer to the creature and make their presence known with a polite and often timid telepathic whisper. Stellar bunnies love being cuddled and stroked by creatures they trust. When content, they express their gratitude by glowing a little bit brighter and sending their friends small telepathic messages of appreciation. Peaceful by nature, a stellar bunny will never seek combat. If it feels threatened, a stellar bunny will emit concentrated starlight from its body to dazzle its enemies and fly away from danger. Familiar . At the gamemaster’s discretion, a player character who can cast find familiar may choose the form of a stellar bunny for their familiar.
