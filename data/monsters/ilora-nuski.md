# Ilora Nuski

**description**: An exile from Pitax, Ilora Nuski can provide the PCs with valuable information about their neighbors—assuming they can gain her trust. You can use Ilora’s statistics for a legendary hunter that could become either a friend or foe of the PCs, or simply adjust her alignment as needed to let her serve the role of a guild leader or even the ruler of a rural region. Traits Evasion. If Ilora is subjected to an effect that allows her to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, Ilora instead takes no damage if she succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if she fails. Spellcasting. Ilora is a 16th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with spell attacks). Ilora has the following ranger spells known: 1st level (4 slots): alarm, cure wounds, hunter’s mark 2nd level (3 slots): animal messenger, barkskin 3rd level (3 slots): conjure animals, protection from energy 4th level (2 slots): freedom of movement, stoneskin Two-Weapon Fighting. Using a shortsword in each hand, Ilora can make one attack as a bonus action, still adding her ability modifier to the damage. Actions Multiattack. Ilora makes three melee attacks or two ranged attacks. Shortsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6+3) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 17 (5d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Longbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8+3) piercing damage, and the target must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 17 (5d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
