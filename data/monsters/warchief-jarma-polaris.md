# Warchief Jarma Polaris

**description**: Jarma leads the Netherwind Reavers , leading them into battle against giants, dragons, and even the occasional battle with the Ironhelm Dwarves over the rich mountain resources to the south. They are a 9-foot-tall half-giant and wield a colossal greatsword carved with Jordic runes. They have a flowing mane of white hair and bushy white eyebrows that frame their pale gray skin and sunken piercing blue eyes. They wear the pelt of a mammoth across their back.
