# Mohrg Spawn

**description**: Mohrg The weight of murder wears heavy on the soul. With souls marked by a lifetime of dealing death, these killers, whether mass murderers, bloodthirsty soldiers, or sadistic executioners, sometimes do not let judgment and lawful execution stanch their slaying sprees. When such individuals are brought to justice, they might rise after death as mohrgs to continue their work, transforming into skeletal horrors to which strips of decayed meat and unsettlingly writhing entrails cling. The spiny length of gristle and muscle that extends from their jaws like a long tongue is, though, their most disturbing feature. Mohrg Spawn Those who succumb to a mohrg’s attacks are doomed to rise as mohrg spawn within the span of what would have, in life, been but a handful of heartbeats. Traits Mohrg Minion. If the mohrg that created a mohrg spawn is destroyed, the mohrg spawn is destroyed as well, collapsing into a pile of decayed flesh and bones. Actions Multiattack. The mohrg spawn makes one tongue attack and one claw attack. Tongue. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d6) necrotic damage. The target must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or be paralyzed. The creature can make a new saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the paralysis on a success. The DC decreases by 1 each round the creature remains paralyzed. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8+2) slashing damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 16 Strength or Dexterity saving throw or be grappled by the mohrg.
