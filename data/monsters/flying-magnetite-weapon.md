# Flying Magnetite Weapon

**description**: Animated Magnetite Crafted from highly magnetic magnetite, these animated objects share the common property of being immobilised by heat. An animated suit of plate armour is capable of conjuring bolts of arcanomagnetic force that can knock a creature off its feet. A swarm of animated weapons compounds the damage of its constituent parts with a devastating flurry of follow-up strikes. Magnetite Weapons Animated weapons are a common staple in a mage’s lair. In an instant, a well-stocked armoury transforms into a whirling flurry of flying swords, pikes, and hammers. The movement of the magnetic fields generates powerful bursts of lightning in the swarm’s epicentre, emitting forceful pulses that launch ferrous objects at deadly speeds. When whipped into an enormous swarm, their greater number allows them to redistribute the negative effects of heat to avoid complete immobilisation.
