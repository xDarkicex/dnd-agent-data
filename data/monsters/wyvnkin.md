# Wyvnkin

**description**: Wyvnkin A wyvern’s young grows quickly after hatching. By two months of age, a wyvnkin looks much like its parents, bearing similar leathery wings, sharp teeth, razor claws, and a potent poison stinger. A wild wyvnkin ventures out of its nest by six months and is expected to fend for itself. If the hatchling hasn’t been tamed by this age, it remains forever feral. Wyvnkin Flights. Though usually solitary creatures, some wyvnkin gather into collectives known as flights when making their homes in dangerous areas, such as the actively hostile environment of The Low or the territory of a deadlier predator. Unlike their parents, wyvnkin don’t shy away from ground battles thanks to their overwhelming numerical advantage. A multitude of tiny, looming shadows is often a sign of impending death by a swarm of dive-bombing draconic critters.
