# Spawn of Parzelon

**description**: A drape of long hair obscures the countenance of this shapely woman. As she turns, her sharp-toothed leonine visage is revealed. Suddenly, long needlelike claws protrude from her lithe fingertips, and her dark blue feline eyes narrow in hungry anticipation. Daughters of the Dread Prophet. Born from accursed liaisons with mortal bloodlines, these amalgams of lioness and lady are the insidious progeny of the Arch-Devil Parzelon, King of Secrets. The fiendishly graceful spawn of Parzelon live only to serve their infernal father and his hideous whims.
