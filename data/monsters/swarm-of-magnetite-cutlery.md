# Swarm of Magnetite Cutlery

**description**: Animated Magnetite Crafted from highly magnetic magnetite, these animated objects share the common property of being immobilised by heat. An animated suit of plate armour is capable of conjuring bolts of arcanomagnetic force that can knock a creature off its feet. A swarm of animated weapons compounds the damage of its constituent parts with a devastating flurry of follow-up strikes. Magnetite Cutlery From the depths of the monastery dining rooms flies a swarm of steel knives, forks, spoons, and — most lethally — sporks. Such creatures can be animated by protective magics, or be under the direct control of a creature capable of manipulating the arcanomagnetic spectrum, such as a magnetite dragon!
