# Blodeuwedd

**description**: The mysterious blodeuwedds dwell in those parts of the world where the boundaries between the Material Plane and the First World have worn thin, or around portals between the two planes. Although blodeuwedds share certain traits with nymphs, their role as guardians of the pathways between this world and the First World prevents them from forming as powerful a magical bond with the natural world. Many blodeuwedds consider nymphs to be spoiled “princesses” who take the beauties of the world for granted. Blodeuwedds are roughly humanoid in shape, but have bodies made of what appears to be thickly woven blades of grass and wildflowers instead of flesh. Traits Allergen Aura. The blodeuwedd exudes an aura of pollen and irritating allergens that forces living creatures who start their turn within 30 feet to make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the target is poisoned, coughing and sneezing for 1d6 rounds. A blodeuwedd can suppress or initiate this aura as a bonus action. Shapechanger. The blodeuwedd can use its action to polymorph into a single, unique Medium humanoid or an owl. Other than its size, the blodeuwedd’s statistics are the same in each form. Any equipment it is wearing or carrying isn’t transformed. A blodeuwedd reverts to its true form upon death. Innate Spellcasting. The blodeuwedd’s innate spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 13). The monster can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At will: dancing lights, entangle, message, plant growth 3/day each: modify memory, sleep, spike growth 1/day each: geas, goodberry, hallucinatory terrain Actions Multiattack. The blodeuwedd makes two attacks with its claws or sling. Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d6 +6) slashing damage. Sling. Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d4+5) piercing damage.
