# Drow Beastmaster

**description**: In world where much of the fauna revolves around mindless oozes, jellies, mushrooms, and insects, it’s not surprising that drow beast-handlers have a passion for the more exotic species taken from the deep Underworld: spiders, ettercaps, and titanic dinosaurs have all been tamed by a beastmaster’s lash. More monstrous humanoids, too, find themselves broken in service to a drow beastmaster. For example, trolls in the wild are little more than dim-witted predators, but under a beastmaster’s training become daunting combatants. Indeed, the line between pest, pet, and slave is a fine one, as many kobolds and halflings would testify.
