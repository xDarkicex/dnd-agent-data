# Antipaladin

**description**: Cultist Just as bandits are drawn to the Stolen Lands, all manner of cultists find this region ripe for hideouts and hidden shrines to deities and demigods whose worship has been outlawed in most cities and towns. Some of these cultists are solitary members of groups with influence throughout the world, while others, such as the Cultists of the Bloom, are entirely regional threats. Note that these cultists, along with the more powerful specialized cultists presented later in this book under the entry for Thresholders, can be used together as different agents of a sprawling sect of sinister worshippers in your game simply by changing their descriptions to match whatever nefarious deity or evil faith you’ve decided they might serve. Antipaladin Antipaladins often serve as captains or secondary leaders for evil cults, particularly those associated with the worship of demon lords. Traits Divine Smite. When the antipaladin hits a creature with a melee weapon attack, it can expend one spell slot to deal 9 (2d8) radiant damage to the target, in addition to the weapon’s damage. If the antipaladin expends a slot of 2nd level or higher, the extra damage increases by 4 (1d8) for each level above 1st. Menacing Monologue. As a bonus action, the antipaladin can deliver a magically enhanced menacing speech that inspires fear in their enemies. Each creature of the antipaladin’s choice within 60 feet that can hear the antipaladin must make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the target is frightened until the end of the antipaladin’s next turn. On a successful save, that creature is immune to the antipaladin’s Menacing Monologue for the next 24 hours. Spellcasting. The antipaladin is a 10th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks). It has the following paladin spells prepared: 1st-level (4 slots): command, divine favor, protection from evil and good, shield of faith 2nd-level (3 slots): branding smite, aid* *The antipaladin casts this spell on itself before combat if able. Actions Multiattack. The antipaladin makes two attacks. Greataxe. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d12+3) slashing damage. Javelin. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6+3) piercing damage.
