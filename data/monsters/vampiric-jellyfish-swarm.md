# Vampiric Jellyfish Swarm

**description**: The region of Tolia has a long coastline. However, its residents don’t seem to be that familiar with the ocean. Tribes mostly live in the mountains, Cyherested is far from the shore, and only the most unfortunate brave fools of Kanrath choose to sail to make a living. The ocean is known to be treacherous and murderous. There are creatures of unknown nature, living in large groups, called the “vampiric jellyfish”. Although not the most dangerous creature of the sea, it is probably the most terrifying. It mostly stays dormant deep beneath the waves, almost immobile, invisible and hidden. It is nearly impossible for an unfortunate soul to realize they are swimming alongside this jellyfish swarm, spanning maybe miles, until it’s too late. Although they don’t display any vampiric features, the people of Tolia call the jellyfish “vampiric”. Maybe it’s because of their ability to regenerate, their terrifying nature, or their deadly stings. No one knows the reason for their name, but one thing is for certain; sailors who survive an encounter with this swarm will probably never be the same.
