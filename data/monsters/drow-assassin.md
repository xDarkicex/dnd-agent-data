# Drow Assassin

**description**: Amongst the drow, “assassin” is not merely a term used to describe a means to an end. The tactics of infiltration and escape that result in the death of a rival are practiced by drow of all stations. To truly earn the title of Assassin is to earn a reputation for effectiveness in killing, without incurring collateral losses and undue attention. Ambassadors of Death. In drow cities where the perception of power is as important as the actuality, assassins offer more services than simple murder. Hiring an assassin to speak on behalf of your House is a subtle message of ruthless intent and a brazen flaunting of wealth. Loyal Mercenary. Unlike other killers-for-hire, drow assassins are motivated by more than coin. Often an assassin will tie their fate to that of an individual house, or a group of allied houses. An assassin declares their loyalty through their kills, and as long as the house to which they’re allied remains ascendant, the assassin takes no job that will conflict with their house’s interests. Their loyalty is unquestioned, and attempting to bribe away an assassin is one of the lowest forms of dishonor amongst the drow. However, convincing the assassin that their best interests are served by betraying their matron is an honor that reflects well on both the assassin and their new alliance.
