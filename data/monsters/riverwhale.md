# Riverwhale

**description**: Sea Monsters The Serpent Sea, as well as the many rivers and lakes of Grimnir, are host to a wide variety of fish and aquatic mammals, such as seals and whales. The creatures detailed here are, for the most part, the most dangerous of sea borne predators, save for the great serpent herself. Riverwhale These sleek predatory whales hunt larger whales and other sea life, including serpents and raiders. Though they generally do not target longships, they have been known to bump into them, knocking some of the crew into the water so they might be snatched and dragged underwater.
