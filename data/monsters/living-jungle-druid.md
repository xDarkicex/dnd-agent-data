# Living Jungle Druid

**description**: After an absence of 400 years, druids have returned to the Living Jungle. The druids of the Circle of Orchids seek to restore the jungle to its original natural splendor by combating the vine lords and tendril puppets and preventing Yawchaka from being overrun by abnormal plants and aberrant beasts. But among their number are some that have fallen under the Green Walker’s influence and are unwittingly sharing the druids’ plans with their new master.
