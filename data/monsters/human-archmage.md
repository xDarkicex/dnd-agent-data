# Human Archmage

**description**: The archmage has spent decades gathering knowledge and arcanum, carefully watching the settlements of friend and foe alike. Always looking outward for signs of impending war or and incursion, the archmage keeps an ear to the ground, for being prepared is the essence of an archmage’s power. Quest Giver. An archmage receives countless bits of news and rumors from informants and auguries, but most are cast aside as fearmongering or superstition. A handful the archmage will pass on—again through clandestine means—to a newly arrived group of adventurers to investigate their veracity.
