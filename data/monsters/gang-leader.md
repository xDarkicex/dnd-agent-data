# Gang Leader

**description**: It takes a strong personality, ruthless cunning, and a silver tongue to keep a gang of outlaws in line. The gang leader has these qualities in spades. Operating outside the arm of the law, they need to stay one step ahead of the authorities while managing a group of malcontents. They can take charge of roving bands of thieves and raiders, a league of vigilantes, and various groups in regions with no law-enforcement. They can also be space pirate captains, with a ship of their own and a crew at their command.
