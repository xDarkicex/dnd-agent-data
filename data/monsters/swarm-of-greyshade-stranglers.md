# Swarm of Greyshade Stranglers

**description**: Greyshade Strangler Any adventurer worth their salt knows the rhyme “Grey and blue? Walk on through. Red and grey? Run away!” These bloodthirsty plants grow in dense patches, their thorny vines wrapped in coils ready to spring towards any creature with a heartbeat. There is never a safe path through a forest containing greyshade stranglers; with their diet of blood, they’ve no need for roots and can relocate to new pastures whenever creatures learn to avoid their current hunting ground.
