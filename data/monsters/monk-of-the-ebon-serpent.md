# Monk of the Ebon Serpent

**description**: The Monks of the Ebon Serpent, diverse in species yet united in their dedication, train under the infamous Masters of the Ebon Serpent. These disciples have honed their bodies and minds to become vessels of martial prowess and arcane might. Channelling the potent energy of the Ebon Serpent, they master an array of combat techniques and eldritch spells, weaving them seamlessly together in their relentless pursuit of power. As they delve deeper into the forbidden knowledge of their dark arts, their loyalty to the Ebon Serpent grows, driving them to seek further enlightenment and become agents of impending doom.
