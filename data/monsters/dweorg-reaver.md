# Dweorg Reaver

**description**: Reavers may not be the most highly regarded soldiers in a dwarven army, but they are proven combatants capable of surprising resilience on the battlefield. Reavers throw caution to the wind, entering combat without regard for their own safety. The thrill of battle, the camaraderie of the vanguard, the promise of glory: these are what defines a reaver, and what the normally stoic dweorg politely shake their heads at, while rushing to keep up with the charge.
