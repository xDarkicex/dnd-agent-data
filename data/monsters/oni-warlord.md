# Oni Warlord

**description**: Actions Multiattack. The oni warlord makes two attacks with its greatsword and uses its Endless Whispers action. +3 Greatsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d6 + 6) slashing damage. Once per turn, if the attack roll exceeds the AC of the target by at least 6, the oni warrior can make an additional attack with this weapon. Legendary Actions The oni warlord can take up to 2 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time, and only at the end of another creature’s turn. The oni warlord regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn. Corrupting Mutation . Roll a d12, the Oni Warlord gains the corresponding Shadowland Power (see Table A-11: Shadowland Powers, Adventures in Rokugan on pages 410-411). Use this legendary action only if the oni warlord has half of its starting hit points remaining or less. Torment . The Oni Warlord targets a non-adjacent creature with its Maddening Whispers ability. If that creature fails its saving throw, the oni warlord teleports to a location 5 ft. away from that creature. Taste their Fear . The oni warlord makes a Corrupting Claw attack against an adjacent creature. If that creature is frightened of the oni warlord, the attack has advantage and the oni warlord regains hit points equal to the amount of damage dealt by that attack. Lair Actions When fighting inside its lair (the Chasm of Souls), the oni warlord can control the wretched souls it imprisons. On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), the oni warlord takes the following lair actions: Summon the Damned. The oni warlord summons a hungry ghost (see the Hungry Ghost (Gaki) profile on page 137). It appears somewhere within the Chasm of Souls. Haunting War Cry . The oni warlord bellows a fearsome challenge to a creature within 50 ft. of it. That creature makes a wisdom saving throw DC 16, if it fails it becomes provoked by the oni warlord until the end of their next turn. Regardless of whether it failed or succeeded, until the end of the round, all hungry ghosts will try to move to that creature and attack it, ignoring all other creatures.
