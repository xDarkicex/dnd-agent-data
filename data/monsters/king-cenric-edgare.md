# King Cenric Edgare

**description**: King Cenric Edgare Cenric Edgare’s family had ruled one of the largest and most bountiful Baendur kingdoms for many generations. Each generation seemed to build upon the successes of the previous, expanding land and creating an ever larger alliance between the kingdoms. Cenric’s ambitions were fueled by his respect for the history of his lineage. As a young man he secretly constructed a navy and was on the verge of overwhelming the clan of the Tvar, before the combined might of the raiders stopped him. A large portion of his kingdom was taken by the raiders and all the Baendur were pushed to the fringes deeper into the volcanic lands of the west and south, further into the frigid wilds of the north and east. Frustrated, Cenric spent decades recovering and building a new army before waging war again, but the other Baendur kings refused to cooperate. Again, Cenric was forced to withdraw and retreat. The years, and his defeats, have weighed heavily on him and he knows this latest attempt to write a place for himself in history shall be his last. Cenric has numerous children, many of them married into other Baendur kingdoms. His current wife and their young daughter live with him at Rockpike. Roleplaying Cenric Edgare Legacy. Cenric deeply cares about his family, both his ancestors and his legacy, as well as how his own deeds influence how his family will be remembered. He is willing to do what needs to be done to be remembered forever but can also be brought to worry about how he will be perceived by history. Self-Centered. Cenric is a genuinely friendly man, though driven. His desire to be revered often overrides other matters and he has a tendency to dominate conversations, pulling them back to what matters to him most—destruction of the Whar initially, and later, to open ports for the Baendur to build a naval presence. Persistent. If Cenric dies while wearing his ring of mind shielding, he tries to telepathically guide any hero deciding to wear it.
