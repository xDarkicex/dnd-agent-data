# Rhox

**description**: The rhox is a rare spider that appears unremarkable at first glance with a smoky black top side, but its underside is a vivid grape-like purple-red color. When closely examined, the underside of each rhox has a weirdly human-like mouth studded with bony teeth at the junction of its thorax and abdomen. Its underside also has a multitude of short, stubby legs that move in succession, more like a millipede than a true spider. The venom of the rhox is extremely deadly, causing the victim’s fluids to congeal into a sticky, web-like fluid, particularly in the urine, before solidifying and bringing an agonizing death
