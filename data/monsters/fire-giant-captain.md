# Fire Giant Captain

**description**: The fire giant captain is a veteran warrior that knows how to maneuver its troops in battle and expose enemy weaknesses. It is typically encountered leading a patrol squad or raiding party. Traits Distracting Strike (1/Turn). When the giant hits a creature with a weapon attack, the next attack roll against the target by an attacker other than the giant has advantage if the attack is made before the start of the giant's next turn. Actions Multiattack. The giant makes two Longsword attacks. It can use Maneuver Allies in place of one Longsword attack. Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 21 (3d8 + 8) slashing damage, or 24 (3d10 + 8) slashing damage if used with two hands. Rock. Ranged Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 60/240 ft., one target. Hit: 30 (4d10 + 8) bludgeoning damage. Maneuver Allies (Recharge 5–6). Up to four allies within 60 feet of the giant that can hear it can each use their reaction to move up to half their speed without provoking opportunity attacks. Reactions Directed Strike (2/Day). When a creature the giant can see within 30 feet of it makes an attack roll, and the creature can hear the giant, the giant grants advantage to that roll.
