# Giant Slug

**description**: These massive, primeval relatives of ordinary slugs squirm slowly through swamps and grasslands. Giant slugs are always voracious and prefer to eat flesh or plants high in water content. The mucus secreted by a giant slug is powerfully acidic and viscous. Wherever it travels, the creature leaves a trailing brown strip that kills most vegetation. Giant slugs are the size of elephants, and while they move slowly, their predatory natures result in them moving more quickly than their much less dangerous normal-sized kin. Traits Malleable. The giant slug’s body is very malleable, allowing it to fit into narrow areas with ease. The slug can move through any space large enough for a Large creature without squeezing, and it can squeeze through any space large enough for a Medium creature. Susceptible to Salt. A handful of salt burns the giant slug as if it were a flask of acid, causing 2d6 damage per use. Larger quantities of salt can cause increasing damage, as determined by the GM. Actions Tongue. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 28 (6d6+7) piercing damage plus 24 (7d6) acid damage. Spit Acid. Ranged Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. Hit: 35 (10d6) acid damage.
