# Aseadweed

**description**: Naraneus, covetous of the sargassum-filled seas of a distant planet, stole samples of the seaweed and then bred a need for acid and malicious sentience into them. Now great forests of vicious kelp exist to hunt down those who infiltrate the goddess’ oceans, adopting the traits of their most frequent victims.
