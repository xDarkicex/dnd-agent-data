# Incubus Pirate

**description**: Incubus pirates pillage the coasts of the Styx, the multiverse, or even the Material Plane for loot and slaves. They often serve as crews on planar vessels captained by greater fiends. They are hardened sailors who employ roguish tactics in addition to the guiles ebbing from their fiendish natures. They are masters of infiltration and often break down their targets’ defenses before storming them in full force. Equipment An incubus pirate has thieves’ tools , a potion of healing , and 75 gold pieces on its person.
