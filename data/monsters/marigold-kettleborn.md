# Marigold Kettleborn

**description**: Marigold is an Academy Director , and possibly the most mysterious of them all. This two-and-a-half foot gnomish woman is about as wide as she is tall. She has curled yellow hair that falls about her round rosy face. She is always beaming with a large smile, red dimpled cheeks, and an abundance of chins. She has a round nose and big bright eyes; one is green, the other is a strange crimson red. She carries a short staff made of iron laced with golden swirls. Marigold wears a wide-brimmed, pointed, purple hat and a well-fitted purple Academy tunic. She is always a delight to be around and many Caspians admire her optimistic attitude and overt friendliness. “I thought some of my professors were bad, but my brief encounters with the Directorate have been at their best, unpleasant”
