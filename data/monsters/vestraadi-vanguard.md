# Vestraadi Vanguard

**description**: These reclusive and alien humanoids are a law unto themselves in the Underworld. The few cultures who are aware of them say they do not follow any guidelines, rules, regulations or conventions at all. However, there is method in the seeming madness, in that the vestraadi are extremely wary of the drow and their love of poison, not least because of these chaotic humanoids’ susceptibility to it. Many of their skills and abilities have been fine-tuned over the generations specifically to combat the dark elves, who the vestraadi take upon themselves to eliminate at every opportunity, especially if it’s from distance or a carefully chosen hiding place.
