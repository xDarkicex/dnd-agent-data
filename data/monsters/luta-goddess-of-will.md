# Luta, Goddess of Will

**description**: Luta Luta is the goddess of will and is possibly the greatest warrior the Vanir ever knew. But she was too young to fight during the final battles in the Lost Lands, so never had the opportunity to truly show her abilities. And throughout much of her life, she was overshadowed by the more outgoing twins. She became very fond of Thonir, but never told him how she felt. She knew he had fallen in love with Hel and after discovering that, she spent more and more time away from the rest of the Vanir. She was there when Thonir handed out the poisoned apples, but she took hers and left on a long pilgrimage. She only ate it much later. This is why she is not among the Stone Court. Roleplaying Luta Humble. Though skilled, Luta has little interest in the opinions of others, so she does not brag. Her abilities are hers to develop as she sees fit. Nomadic. She has always wanted to wander, and her travels have taken her across all of Grimnir and beyond. She will never feel comfortable staying in one place for long. Serious. There was a saying among the surviving Vanir that it was easier to catch the moon than it was to make Luta smile (though they never said this in front of her). She enjoys life on her own terms but is much more likely to smile when she finds a new valley laden with lush greenery or listens to the songs of newly discovered birds.
