# Nugrah

**description**: Nugrah is a bitter old man. You can use his statistics to play the part of the sinister hermit who isolates himself in the woods. Perhaps he is a cruel-hearted druid, disenchanted or frustrated that the end of his life might soon come before he completes a specific goal. Or maybe he harbors lifelong regrets about a missed opportunity during his youth that now poisons his mind. Traits Spellcasting. Nugrah is a 7th-level spell caster. His spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 16, +8 to hit with spell attack.) Nugrah knows the following druid spells: Cantrips (at will): guidance, poison spray, produce flame, shillelagh 1st level (4 slots): cure wounds, entangle, fog cloud, summon monster, thunderwave 2nd level (3 slots): barkskin, hold person, spider climb, spike growth 3rd level (1 slots): meld into stone Actions Club. Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d4+1) bludgeoning damage. Wildshape (1/Day). Nugrah magically polymorphs into a giant badger, or back into his true form. He reverts to his true form if he dies. Any equipment he is wearing or carrying is absorbed or borne by the new form (Nugrah’s choice). In this new form, Nugrah retains his game statistics, but his AC, movement modes, Strength, Dexterity, and other actions are replaced by those of the new form, and he gains any statistics and capabilities (except class features, legendary actions, and lair actions) that the new form has but that he lacks.
