# Ironheart Tree

**description**: Ironheart Tree An ironheart is a thick tree with silver-hued star shaped leaves. The leaves are prized because they can be worked to make iron-like weapons (known as ironwood weapons). However, there is something disturbing about being in the presence of an ironheart tree. Sentient. The trees have their own motives and are intelligent. Bearer of Ironleaf. Their leaves cannot be harvested—if taken, they crumble. However, an ironheart can voluntarily shed leaves, often as a bribe or reward.
