# Captain Gixx Shortpebble

**description**: Gixx Shortpebble was always drawn to the open sea and fled his home to join a Draylish merchant ship when he turned fourteen. His aptitude for the sailor’s life and keeping his cool in the most dire situations earned him acclaim on the vessel, and he quickly climbed through the ranks to become first mate. However, his thirst for glory and gold could not be sated on this ship, and he turned on his crew, making a deal with a pirate gang to raid the vessel in exchange for placing him in the captain’s chair. Unfortunately, his life of piracy was interrupted by a rival pirate group that boarded his new ship, massacred the crew, and gang-pressed him into service on the Black Barracuda. This is where he met Jade Storm, who would become his lifelong companion and confidant. Gixx used his wit and the towering physique of Jade Storm to organize a mutiny and capture the Black Barracuda, becoming captain again. He has commanded many ships since then and sailed across many seas until the time came when the pair gave up the life of privacy for less risky endeavors at the helm of the Sated Contessa, which he won in a game of Spell Wars from a wealthy merchant. Perhaps Gixx’s most notable accomplishment is his voyage to the Isles of Xantharos over a decade ago. Equipment Captain Gixx has +1 rapier and a potion of greater healing on his person. He also wears slippers of spider climbing .
