# Mangianime

**description**: A goat-like creature with the wings of a bat. Its body seems to be covered by, or consist of, the distorted faces of the dead. The Mangianime are Hell monsters, hybrid creatures that were once animals. They strayed into Hell by accident, were warped by this evil dimension, and now suck the souls of the dead for sustenance, leaving only an empty shell that crumbles to dust. They can take whatever small to medium-sized form the GM decides. Usually they don’t come bigger than a dog or hyena because the more dangerous animals are usually transformed into true war-machines for the armies of Hell. Feel free to invent a few interesting specimens and have the characters encounter them on their journey through Hell. The Mangianime do not carry Satan’s hairs. They are mortal. Dismay check: Anyone watching a Mangianime feeding should roll against a difficulty of 8, at least when experienced for the first time. Actions Feces. Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit. A creature hit by the feces of the mangianime must succeed on a DC 11 Dexterity saving throw or suffer 1 damag e and be blinded for 3 rounds, having been hit in the eyes. The mangianime enjoy the challenge of hitting moving targets. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +1 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 2 (1d6 − 1) piercing damage.
