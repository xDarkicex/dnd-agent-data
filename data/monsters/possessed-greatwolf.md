# Possessed Greatwolf

**description**: A massive wolf, possessed by the vile astirax under Jael the Hunter’s control. Traits Keen Hearing and Smell. The possessed wolf has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell. Magic Hunter. The possessed wolf has advantage on attack rolls targeting any creatures that can cast spells, except rangers and paladins. Pack Tactics. The possessed wolf has advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one of the possessed wolf’s allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn’t incapacitated. Scent Magic. The possessed wolf always knows the location of any magic items or any creatures that can cast spells, except for rangers and paladins, within 600 feet. Bonus Actions End Possession. The astirax possessing the possessed wolf can choose to end the possession as a bonus action. When the possession ends, the astirax appears in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the possessed wolf and the possessed wolf becomes a normal wolf. In addition, the wolf is immune to that particular astirax’s possession ability for 24 hours. Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (2d4 + 2) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
