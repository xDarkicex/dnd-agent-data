# Ironwood Witch

**description**: Ironwood Witch Whether they were once human, elf, dwarf, or other, the magic available to the ironwood witches gradually makes their limbs lengthen and narrow, their skin pale, and their hair thin and gray. At times, they even appear spider-like. To compensate for their growing hideousness, they cover themselves with vibrant red robes and silver jewelry… as well as magical disguises. Witch Shape. They use their Witch Shape ability to adopt humanoid forms when necessary to infiltrate humanoid settlements—either to permanently take over and masquerade as important NPCs, or to trick victims into trusting them before transforming them into ironthralls. Ironheart Allies. Witches can use an ironheart tree to teleport to another ironheart tree anywhere in the world. Treethrall. Three Ironwood witches are required for this ability. When working together, they are able to cast an eight-hour ritual spell on a target that has been captured by an ironheart tree’s Roots ability. The Ironwood witches cannot be interrupted during this ritual or the spell fails. At the completion, if the target fails a DC 20 Charisma check, they become permanent ironthralls that must obey the witches involved. Ironthralls. If the ironthrall ever drops to 0 hit points, the witches must sever their link to it. A protection from evil and good spell cast on the body also severs the link. Finally, by using an action, any of the witches can voluntarily sever the connection to the ironthrall. In any of these cases, the ironthrall dies.
