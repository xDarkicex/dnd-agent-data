# Hannis Drelev

**description**: The baron of Fort Drelev, Hannis is a proud and cruel man destined to become one of the primary antagonists moving against the PCs nation. These statistics work particularly well when used for the role of any petty tyrant or awful king you might wish to unleash upon the party. Traits Defender Weapon. Drelev’s longsword is a magical weapon +3 . The first time he attacks with the sword on each of his turns, Drelev can transfer some or all of the sword’s bonus to his Armor Class, instead of using the bonus on any attacks that turn. The adjusted bonuses remain in effect until the start of his next turn, although Drelev must hold the sword to gain a bonus to AC from it. The bonus is not included in Drelev’s Armor Class or Defender Longsword entries. Sneak Attack (1/Turn). Drelev deals an extra 6d6 (21) damage when he hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of Drelev that isn’t incapacitated and Drelev doesn’t have disadvantage on the attack roll. Actions Multiattack. Hannis Drelev makes three attacks. Defender Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d8+5) slashing damage, or 10 (1d10+5) slashing damage if used with two hands. Disarming Strike. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: the target must make a DC 17 Strength check or drop one held item of Drelev’s choice. Handaxe. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d6+5) slashing damage.
