# Malgorzata Niska

**description**: Malgorzata is the leader of a small band of Gyronna clerics. You can use these statistics instead for the rank-and-file priests of a larger cult of Gyronna, perhaps using Sisters of the Bloodshot Eye or other Gyronna-linked characters in this book to build an entire campaign focused on opposing this violent religion. Gyronna is a relatively obscure deity outside of the River Kingdoms and the Stolen lands, but within their reaches many fear her cult for she is a cruel deity whose worshippers hold sacred her teachings of extortion, hatred, and spite. Traits Divine Eminence. As a bonus action, Malgorzata can expend a spell slot to cause her melee weapon attacks to magically deal an extra 14 (4d6) necrotic damage to a target on a hit. This benefit lasts until the end of the turn. If Malgorzata expends a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the extra damage increases by 1d6 for each level above 1st. Spellcasting. Malgorzata is a 7th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with spell attacks). Malgorzata has the following cleric spells prepared: Cantrips (at will): guidance, resistance, sacred flame, thaumaturgy 1st level (4 slots): bless, cure wounds 2nd level (3 slots): aid, silence, spiritual weapon 3rd level (3 slots): bestow curse, dispel magic, spirit guardians 4th level (1 slots): guardian of faith, locate creature Actions Dagger. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d4+1) piercing damage.
