# Reaver

**description**: Many of the northern warriors have adopted the title “reaver”. Donned in animal furs, wielding axes, and painting themselves with blood, these brutal warriors rage in to battle with an unrelenting ferocity. Many reavers believe they are closely tied to nature, the elements, and the wild animals of the places they inhabit. Whether it be the Netherwind Reavers, or the Northfolk from Skye, these terrifying warriors are infamous for their brutality. “Big burly warriors with axes seem cool, but they still are no match for the world's greatest sorcerer.”
