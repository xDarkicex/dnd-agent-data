# Unit of Knights of A’rvanshy

**description**: The valiant knights of A'rvanshy, astride their majestic giant seahorses, employ cunning tactics in their tireless quest to defend their people. Masters of hit-and-fade maneuvers, they strike swiftly and decisively against their foes, exploiting their seahorses' agility and speed to confound enemies with surprise attacks. In battle, their tight formation and coordinated assaults exhibit the seamless cohesion of a well-trained brotherhood, overwhelming adversaries with combined strength and precision. Ever vigilant, they keep their ancestral city's safety at heart, ensuring it remains a beacon of hope amidst the darkness, as they stand unwaveringly against the sinister forces of Morgawr.
