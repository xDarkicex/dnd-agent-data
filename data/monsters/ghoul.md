# Ghoul

**description**: Though they don’t require food to exist, ghouls endlessly hunger for mortal flesh. When a ghoul comes into being, their limbs and tongue grow longer, each ending in jagged claws and barbs. Negative energy courses through these weapons, allowing the ghoul to slow their prey and tear flesh from bones. Actions Multiattack. The ghoul makes one Barbed Tongue attack and one Claws attack. If both attacks hit the same creature, the target is dazed until the end of the target’s next turn. Barbed Tongue. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) slashing damage, and the target’s speed is reduced by 10 feet until the end of their next turn. Claws. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) slashing damage, and the target can’t take reactions until the end of their next turn. Bonus Actions Hungry Charge. The ghoul moves up to half their speed in a straight line. If the ghoul moves at least 15 feet and ends this movement within 5 feet of a creature, they have advantage on the next attack roll they make against that creature until the end of the ghoul’s next turn.
