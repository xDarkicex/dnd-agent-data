# Kholkikos

**description**: This large greenish-gold reptilian dwells in rocky caves and clefts, hoarding its shiniest treasures. Their jaws can extend wide enough to swallow an entire human or sheep, and while digesting such a meal they may take on the appearance of torpor, but they are ever-vigilant, never needing to sleep, and are perfectly willing to lash out if their relaxation is disturbed. Hunting kholkikos, in fact, sometimes pretend to be sleeping in order to lure prey into coming too close, using their keen senses to track those coming near before leaping to the attack. Lonely Guardian. The kholkikos is solitary and prefers to be left alone; however, they are not immediately hostile to creatures they encounter. If bribed with additional treasures, especially items that glitter and shine, a kholkikos can be a diligent and faithful guardian, displaying its booty for others to see but protecting it with every ounce of their immortal life.
