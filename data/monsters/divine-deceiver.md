# Divine Deceiver

**description**: A divine deceiver is a cunning Celestial that sustains itself on holy energy and the prayers of mortals. A deceiver might, for example, kill a local cleric and adopt their identity so it can slowly corrupt the congregation, until they are no longer praying to their god but to the deceiver itself. Charged by Divinity While the deceiver typically has no interest in combat – preferring subterfuge that allows it to trick mortals into surrendering their prayers and spiritual essence willingly – it is still a fearsome combatant. It steals its foes essences and prayers and uses the divine energy to fuel its attacks against them.
