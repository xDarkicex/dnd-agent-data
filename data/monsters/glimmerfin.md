# Glimmerfin

**description**: A glimmerfin is an amphibious aberration with a smooth, teal body, and vibrant pink frills. Its most noticeable features, however, are an eye-stalk tail and a third, central eye that glows with a soft golden hue. Glimmerfins are inherently magical creatures that dwell in lagoons, freshwater lakes, and parts of the ocean wrought with ancient magic as if drawn by arcane energy. Magical Beings The toothless appearance of a glimmerfin may trick people into thinking the adorable herbivore is harmless, but its additional eyes grant it more than a keen sight. With its central eye, a glimmerfin can disrupt other creatures' connection to the weave of magic and it can use its eye-tip tail to shoot beams of arcane force. Moreover, the glimmerfin's frills shift color whenever they're close to a source of magic. The nuances of these shifts in color allow others to determine the type of magic the glimmerfin is reacting to. Drawn to Adventure Although most glimmerfins live in larger flocks in secluded underwater gardens or reefs, glimmerfins are curious beings, and sometimes a glimmerfin bonds deeply with other creatures that fascinate it. Glimmerfins make fantastic adventuring companions, not only for their unique abilities, but also because they are notoriously hard to kill – as long as just a bit of them remains, the glimmerfin can regenerate an entirely new form within a day. So cute you can't take your eyes off it – and, as it happens, it won't take its eyes off you either. At least, not all of them. A bit unnerving at first, but you'll get used to it. – the Wanderer
