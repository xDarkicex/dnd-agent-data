# Chiron

**description**: Centaurs were said to be children of king Ixion of Thessaly and the nymph Nephele. They are basically horses with the upper body of a human attached to where the head would normally be. In Greek mythology, only the bearded, male kind is shown, but the Romans also depicted female versions. Centaurs were also seen in India and Russia. Although many accounts describe them as untamed, barbaric beings, they can be heroic and are some of the more agreeable monsters in Hell. All centaurs carry Lucifer’s hair in golden amulets around their necks. Although they fulfill the rather menial task of punishing sinners, they are proud warriors who adhere to a strict hierarchy with Chiron as their leader. The centaur Chiron is the offspring of titans (just like the dwarves are) and was raised and taught by the god Apollo. He is skilled in many arts like medicine, music, hunting and prophecy and is the most civilized of the centaurs. Numerous heroes like Achilles, Jason and Odysseus have been under his tutelage. Due to his prophetic abilities, Chiron will be able to spot any character whose Dark Secret is based on Violence against others. He will politely ask the person to come with him and tell them that they are about to have a personal experience. If the group has antagonized the centaurs, the person will be taken by force. Traits Charge. If Chiron moves at least 30 feet straight forward toward a target and then hits it with his trample attack, the target must make a DC 10 Constitution saving throw or be knocked prone. Spellcasting. Chiron is a 5t h-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with spell attacks). Chiron knows the following druid spells: Cantrips (at will): druid craft, guidance, produce flame 1st level (4 slots): animal friendship, detect magic, longstrider 2nd level (3 slots): animal messenger, locate object, moonbeam 3rd level (2 slots): conjure animals, speak with plants Actions Trample. Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 2 (1d4) blugeoning damage. Short Sword. Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d6 + 1) piercing damage.
