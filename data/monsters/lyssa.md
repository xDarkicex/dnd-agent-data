# Lyssa

**description**: Euripides identifies Lyssa as the daughter of the night goddess Nyx, who sprung from the blood of Uranus’s wound from his castration by Cronus. In fact, Lyssa was an Earthbound spirit created by the catastrophe that tore the mortal and magical planes apart. She is the impersonation of the rage and frenzy the world felt when being ripped to pieces. For centuries, this demoness infected mortals with her madness until she was drawn into the Inferno and made corporeal.
