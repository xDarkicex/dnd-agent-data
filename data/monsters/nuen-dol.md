# Nuen Dol

**description**: Traits Paranoid. Nuen Dol cannot be surprised when she is sleeping, and when she rolls initiative, she immediately takes the Dodge action (as a free action, even though it is not her turn). Staghorns. When Nuen Dol makes an attack with her staghorns, she no longer counts as being armed with a shield until the beginning of her next turn, causing her AC to drop to 16. Actions Multiattack. Nuen Dol makes one attack with her longsword and one attack with her staghorns. Longsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8+3) slashing damage. Staghorns. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d4+3) piercing damage.
