# Half-Drow Chamberlain

**description**: More than many whose heritage comes from two races, a half-drow has a difficult time finding a place in either society from which their parents come. Their way through this problem, as is often the case with any half-elf, is to demonstrate their ability to use multiple skills, such as martial and spellcasting, and then forge a role as a go-between who understands both points of view. This flexibility can be a blessing and a curse.
