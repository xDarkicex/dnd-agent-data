# Barbtongued Wyvern

**description**: Wyvern Wyverns are the most commonly encountered drakes within the Stolen Lands, although they tend to prefer the westernmost or easternmost reaches of the region as their hunting grounds. Barbtongued Wyvern With their long, thorny tongues, these wyverns serve as the favored pets of the nymph queen Nyrissa. As natives of the First World, they are only rarely encountered on the Material Plane. They appear similar to the typical wyvern, save that they have extraordinarily long, barbed tongues that they can use to lash out at victims. Furthermore, their long stay within the supernatural House at the Edge of Time has granted them the ability to hurl barbs from their tongues by snapping the length like a whip; these hurled barbs can pierce through the fabric of reality itself and affix those they strike in place, as if pinning them to the sky. Actions Multiattack. The wyvern makes three attacks: one with its bite, one with its claw, and one with its stinger. While flying or against a grappled target, it can make an additional claw attack in place of its bite. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 15 ft., one creature. Hit: 19 (3d8+6) piercing damage, and the target is grappled (escape DC 18). Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained, and the wyvern can’t bite another target. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 19 (3d8+6) slashing damage. Sting. Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 15 ft., one creature. Hit: 19 (3d8+6) piercing damage and 14 (4d6) poison damage. Barbed Tongue. Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 30 ft., one creature. Hit: 20 (4d6+6) slashing damage, and the target is grappled (escape DC 18). Until this grapple ends, the grappled creature takes an additional 13 (2d6+6) slashing damage at the start of the creature’s turn. Pin to the Sky. Ranged Spell Attack: +11 to hit, range 100/200 ft.; two targets. Hit: 13 (2d6+6) piercing damage, and the target becomes restrained (escape DC 18).
