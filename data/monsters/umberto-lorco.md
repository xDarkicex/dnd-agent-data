# Umberto L'Orco

**description**: Umberto is an enormous hirsute brute. He is actually a half-ogre, and as a child was abandoned by his ogre family because of his insufficient size. He stands only 7 feet (2.10 meters) tall, which is not a lot by ogre standards. Umberto was found and raised by the Montecraxi family who see him as a perfect addition to their troops. Umberto does not speak a lot, but rather wields his mighty axe or – if he loses it in the melee – anything he can lay his hands on. Umberto is absolutely loyal to Sabina and will immediately come to her help if she should be in serious danger. He might be a bit in love with her. Umberto is carrying a surprisingly shiny battleaxe with magical properties (+1) that he was given by his family as a send-off gift. Traits Magic Weapon. Umberto’s axe attacks are magical. Actions Battleaxe +1. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d8 + 4) slashing damage.
