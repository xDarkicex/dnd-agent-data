# Agai

**description**: Agai is the leader of the Culcheck spriggans, the band who invades the village of Varnhold after Vordakai steals away its people. While Agai can work well in your game as the leader of other spriggan enclaves, he also works well as an unusual member of a rival adventuring party, particularly one based on a composition of more monstrous members than a group composed mostly of humans and other core options. Traits Improved Critical. Agai’s weapon attacks score a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20. Innate Spellcasting. Agai’s innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks). Agai can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components: At Will: dancing lights, poison spray 2/day: blur, dispel magic, thunderwave 1/day: shatter Martial Advantage (1/Turn). Agai deals an extra 14 (4d6) damage to a creature he hits with a weapon attack if that creature is within 5 feet of an ally of Agai that isn’t incapacitated. Actions Multiattack. Agai makes three greatclub or javelin attacks and can use Enlarge if it is available. Greatclub. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, one target. Hit: 9 (1d8+5) bludgeoning damage. Javelin. Ranged Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d6+5) piercing damage. Reactions Enraged Growth. When Agai takes damage, he magically increases in size, along with any equipment he is wearing or carrying. While enlarged, Agai is Large and he gains the following benefits: +5 feet to the reach of his melee attacks He regains 15 hit points +2 to his Armor Class and attack rolls Doubles his damage dice on Strength-based weapon attacks If Agai lacks the room to become Large, he attains the maximum-size space possible in the space available. Any creature adjacent to Agai must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or be pushed back to allow for Agai’s growth. Agai returns to his normal size at the end of the next round that he does not deal damage to a target, and Agai gains one level of exhaustion that lasts for 1 hour.
