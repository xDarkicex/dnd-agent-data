# Lady Sentrix “the Coil” Astrillax

**description**: Lady Sentrix “the Coil” Astrillax governs the clan of half-black dragon halflings, descendants of an ancient lineage bound to unraveling mysteries. As a skilled diviner and seeker of the world's end, she possesses unparalleled insight into the cosmic tapestry. Lady Sentrix's authority emanates from her piercing gaze, which sees through the veils of time and fate. Her clan thrives under her tutelage, honing their abilities in divination and deciphering the secrets that hold the key to the inevitable apocalypse.
