# Dajna

**description**: Dajna This is a camel of titanic proportions. It doesn’t seem hostile, but its sheer size is a danger to anything smaller than itself. Also called a thunder camel, a dajna has the head and neck of a camel but is ten times the size of an elephant. They were once more numerous, but in the wake of a primordial deluge or similar cataclysm it was left behind where other beasts found rescue and went extinct in most lands as a result, but a population of these colossal camels survived in the netherworld. Although a dajna is highly dangerous to human beings, the yellowish milk produced by the females is a remedy for baldness and many other afflictions. Only heroes dare retrieve it.
