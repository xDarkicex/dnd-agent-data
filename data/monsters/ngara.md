# Ngara

**description**: Ngara dwells in Hooktongue Slough and has been manipulating the region’s boggards and marsh giants for some time. As with Engelidis, Ngara would work well as the main villain behind the scenes who manipulates a society for her benefit. Indeed, you can use Ngara’s stats to represent less powerful serpent agents of Engelidis if she serves as the primary villain of your plot. Traits Spellcasting. Ngara is a 10th-level spellcaster. Her spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 15, +7 to hit with spell attacks), and she needs only verbal components to cast its spells. She has the following wizard spells prepared: Cantrips (at will): mage hand, minor illusion, poison spray, ray of frost, shocking grasp 1st level (4 slots): charm person, detect magic, sleep 2nd level (3 slots): detect thoughts, hold person, invisibility 3rd level (3 slots): fireball, lightning bolt, water breathing 4th level (3 slots): blight, dimension door 5th level (2 slots): cone of cold Actions Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one creature. Hit: 7 (1d6+4) piercing damage and the target must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw, taking 30 (7d8) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
