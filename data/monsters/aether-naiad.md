# Aether Naiad

**description**: Aether Naiad Aether naiads are reclusive fey creatures who live within the aether, along shorelines, in shallow coves, or the deepest depths of the aetherial seas. Individuals often grow attached to a specific location, viewing their chosen locale as something precious and deserving of their protection and care, regardless of how unsettling or mundane the area may appear to mortal eyes. Keeper of Secrets. Their affinity for unusual locations often provides aether naiads with an uncanny understanding of the area, its history, and the treasures hidden within. Most selfishly guard these secrets, becoming angered if ignorant outsiders disturb sites they’ve chosen to safeguard. Expert Wayfinders. Because of their ties to the magical seas, aether naiads are experts at interpreting the undulation of the aetherial waters. Many sailors believe that aether naiads know of secretive wind routes not found on any map. Most crews would be lucky to count an aether naiad among their crew, but coaxing these fey from their watery homes is no easy task.
