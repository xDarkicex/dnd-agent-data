# Bicho Cidrao

**description**: The bicho cidrão is the tormented soul of a shepherd. The specifics behind the shepherd’s death determine the habits of the bicho cidrão it spawns. Some die in defense of their flock, and in death are unable or unwilling to divest themselves of their duty. Others die in acts of cowardice, attempting to flee danger only to die anyway, and the resulting bicho cidrão lingers in an attempt to find redemption for its craven actions. Unlike most undead, the bicho cidrão is not overtly hostile to the living. They will not knowingly take any action likely to result in harm or death to any sheep or goats. The presence of a bicho cidrão can be a blessing to herders, as its presence protects their flocks. However, the obsessive attention of the creature can cause frustration and confusion for them as well, as it has no regard for any humanoids tending the flocks, only the animals it is obsessed with protecting. A humanoid attempting to halt or subvert a bicho cidrão’s plans to protect a herd are likely to be considered an enemy and provoke a hostile response, even if they their actions are meant to be in the flock’s best interests. Bringer of Gloom . The bicho cidrão’s presence causes clouds to gather and rain to fall within 2 miles of its location. Unseasonal or prolonged wet weather in an area is a strong sign that a bicho cidrão is haunting the region.
