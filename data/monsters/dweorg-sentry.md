# Dweorg Sentry

**description**: All dweorg dedicate themselves to their chosen profession, and career soldiers are no exception. Dweorg sentries learn the basics of warfare and guardianship, perfecting their methods as rigorously as a blacksmith or a brewsmith. Bypassing or fooling a dweorg sentry is a practice in futility to most outsiders, and even skilled dweorg thieves, familiar with the sentries’ tactics must spend more time and effort than the job is usually worth.
