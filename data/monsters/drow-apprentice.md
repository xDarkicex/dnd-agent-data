# Drow Apprentice

**description**: Amongst the drow, a sure avenue to power is magic. Though the arcane may come easy, navigating the halls of politics and subservience becomes a magic all its own to males who seek to enrich their own station. Few male drow who would become known as mage undertake to do so without an instructor. The master-apprentice relationship affords both the protection of a partnership, and either can dispose of the other should doing so become necessary to avoid a matron’s wrath.
