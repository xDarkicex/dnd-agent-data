# Elder Magma Elemental

**description**: In the border areas between the Plane of Earth and Plane of Fire, volcanoes and continent-sized lava flows are commonplace. Elementals in this area tend to have aspects of both planes, and the typical sort is the magma elemental, an earth elemental with a core of liquid fire. Magma elementals generally have a somewhat feral or bestial appearance. Magma elementals standing still may appear like rocky monsters glowing with an internal heat. Red light spills from its eyes and mouth, as well as fractures in its outer surface, and when it surges into motion its rocky exterior cracks apart and its body becomes a turgid mass of red-hot liquid stone raging like a furnace.
