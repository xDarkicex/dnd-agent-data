# Guard of San Marco

**description**: The abbey of San Marco employs heavily armed guards who are led by the paladins. They are no monks, but laymen in the service of the abbey. The guards wear full plate and fight with short spears, but also carry swords and shields on this occasion. If the adventurers use long range weapons or spells, a small number of guards may also carry bows and arrows or a crossbow. The number of fighters is up to you as the GM and should depend on the strength of the group. One single guard is no match for an adventurer of a higher level. Actions Mace. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6+3) bludgeoning damage. Halberd. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d10 + 3) slashing damage. Crossbow, heavy. Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, range 100/400 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d10 + 3) piercing damage.
