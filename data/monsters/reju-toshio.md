# Reju Toshio

**description**: He/Him Reju Toshio has been the spiritual advisor to Reju Jikai for nearly fifteen years, and although he has always acted loyal, he secretly covets the title of daimyō. Toshio cared deeply about Twin Blessings Village, the land, and its people. However, Jikai’s lack of initiative in addressing the problems faced by Twin Blessings Village caused Toshio to lose faith in him. Toshio confided in the castle’s steward, Otomo Nobu, who convinced Toshio that together, they could prove Jikai was unfit. Specifically, if the spirits were angered and the harvest failed, the villagers would look to Toshio to appease the spirits and save them. Nobu would convince the Otomo family and the Lion Clan that there was no choice but to replace Jikai with Toshio. Toshio knew he could not upset the spirits himself if he was to later recall them to their shrines, so he summoned a yōkai that had been haunting the nearby forest. This yōkai was Nocturne, a Dream-Devouring Demon, which was more than happy to drive off the elemental spirits and make Twin Blessings Village its new home. Toshio believes he can defeat or banish the yōkai when he becomes daimyō. Otomo Nobu agreed to disturb the castle’s earth shrine, and Nocturne drove off the weakened earth spirit. Toshio blamed the resulting destruction on Jikai’s daughter, Ume, who frequented the garden where the damage was worst. Toshio and Nobu fabricated proof to frame the then eleven-year-old heir. Lord Jikai doubted his daughter was responsible, but neither did he suspect his steward and spiritual advisor were both plotting against him. In the end, Jikai chose to banish Ume so the destruction could not be blamed on him instead. Toshio has been performatively meditating at shrines throughout the area and inviting villagers to join him in soothing the spirits distressed by the yōkai’s presence. He uses these gatherings to spread rumors that Jikai’s incompetence is partly the cause of the spirits’ anger. He also suggests the banished heir, Ume, has never strayed far from the village, and that she comes to desecrate shrines and steal from the villagers with her group of so-called “bandits” at night. Once Jikai is dealt with, Toshio plans to kill Ume to end her claim to the castle. Then, he will step in to fill the power void and restore peace and harmony to the land and its people. Traits Ritualist. Reju Toshio’s invocation ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with invocation attacks). Reju Toshio has 4 favor available to empower his invocations, and knows the following invocations: Armor of stone, Blade of the elements, Earth becomes sky, Guardian of the sacred gate, Jade strike, Sinkhole's hunger Actions Wakizashi. Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d6 + 0) slashing damage. Wakizashi (Thrown). Ranged Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, range 10/30 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d6 + 0) slashing damage.
