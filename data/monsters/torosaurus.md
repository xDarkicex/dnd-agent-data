# Torosaurus

**description**: The triceratops is a stubborn and ill-tempered herbivore. They travel in grazing herds to better protect themselves and their young from predators—both other dinosaurs and the humanoids who hunt them. Though many a culture has tried to domesticate the triceratops, none were known to succeed. The beast is simply too unruly. The torosaurus is a larger and more aggressive variety of triceratops. Other variations of these creatures are said to exist deep in the Underworld: a five-horned monster known as a pentaceratops, a gargantuan beast known in legend as the titanoceratops, and the god-like creature known as the certopos eterna.
