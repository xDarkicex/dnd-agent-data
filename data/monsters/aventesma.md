# Aventesma

**description**: Aventesma These enormous apparitions resemble enormously tall but eerily thin humans, stretching around 12 feet tall but willowy slender and able to contort itself with disconcerting ease. Mortal eyes strain to take in their appearance, as they seem to grow the longer one looks at them, eventually seeming to almost merge with the sky or to arch and bend over like a bridge. If this bridge examines, it appears stable and safe to all appearance, as stout as a span of wood or stone. However, the aventesma’s face and twisted limbs remain clearly visible, bent and warped through the substance of the span. The aventesma may allow free passage or it may block those wishing to cross, or set them a task or a riddle that must be completed before it allows them through. Even if their terms are met, wise travelers sometimes toss a stone through the arch of the aventesma’s bridge, for if the stone disappears or cannot pass through the archway, they know that eerie fey magic is afoot and they had best find a different path. Aventesma are not inherently malevolent, but meeting one is considered an ill omen and an echoe of stray spirits left behind.
