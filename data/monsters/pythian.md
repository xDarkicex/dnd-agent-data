# Pythian

**description**: Pythians are large serpents blessed with a divine gift of sight beyond time. An ancient encounter with the immanent glory of a god or prophecy blinded the first pythianns to the mortal world yet opened their minds to the possibilities of worlds beyond and things to come. They typically lair in mountain caves, often amassing small cultic followings who may lead those in need of their wisdom to the mount to receive the gifts the pythian can offer. A pythiann resembles a dark-scaled serpent around 15 feet long, usually shrouded in mist with a cloying scent. Virtual Voice. The pythian is aware of the strangeness of its appearance and the prejudice that some have against snakes. They often recruit young maidens, often blind or simply blindfolded, to serve as their voice. The pythian can cause their sibilant, inhuman speech to emerge from the mouth of their chosen oracle, knowing that those who need their advice may find it more believable uttered with a human tongue.
