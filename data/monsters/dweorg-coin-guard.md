# Dweorg Coin Guard

**description**: Known colloquially as the Tanglebeards, coin guards of the trade district travel in pairs. Each coin guard carries a whistle that they use to summon more help if needed; usually four additional guards will come to their aid. Legend tells that a particular note will summon more celestial aid, although even the coin guard themselves believe this is just a folktale.
