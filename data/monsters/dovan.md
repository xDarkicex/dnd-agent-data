# Dovan

**description**: Dovan served as the Stag Lord’s second-in-command until recently, but now that this role has been usurped, he’s been plotting betrayal. You can use Dovan’s stats to serve the role of a typical mid-tier burglar or bandit. Traits Evasion. If Dovan is subjected to an effect that allows him to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage from the effect, Dovan instead takes no damage if he succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if he fails. Sneak Attack (1/Turn) . Dovan deals an extra 10 (3d6) damage when he hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 feet of an ally of Dovan that isn’t incapacitated and he doesn’t have disadvantage on the attack roll. Actions Multiattack. Dovan makes two ranged attacks with his daggers, or one melee attack with his rapier and one with his dagger. Dagger. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20 ft./60 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 +2) piercing damage. Rapier. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8+2) piercing damage.
