# Tactician

**description**: Actions Multiattack. The tactician can use its Expose Weakness and Rallying Cry abilities. Expose Weakness. Choose a creature up to 60 ft. away. Roll a d20 and add 7 to the result. If the result is higher than the creature's passive perception, attacks against that creature have advantage until the start of the tactician's next turn. If the result exceeded the creature’s passive perception by 6 or more then the next attack that hits that creature critically strikes it instead. Rallying Cry. Choose up to two other creatures no more than 30 ft. away. Those creatures gain 2d8 + 5 temporary hit points. If they have any negative conditions, one of them of your choice is removed. Dagger. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, Reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d4 + 1) piercing damage. If this attack would critically strike it deals 20 (3d12 + 1) damage instead.
