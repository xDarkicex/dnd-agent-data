# Manifestation of Decay

**description**: Traits Shroud of Decay. Creatures that start their turn within 5 ft. of the Manifestation of Decay take 10 (3d6) poison damage. Undead and fiend creatures are healed that amount instead. Actions Multiattack. The Manifestation of Decay makes an attack with its Bow of Famine and uses its Viral Contagion ability. Bow of Famine. Ranged Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 150/600 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d8 + 4) necrotic damage. Creatures dealt damage by this attack becomes weakened (loses damage resistances, and damage immunities become resistances) until the end of the Manifestation of Decay’s next turn. Viral Contagion. One creature within 30 ft. of the Manifestation of Decay must make a DC 20 Constitution saving throw. If it fails it becomes poisoned until the end of the Manifestation of Decay’s next turn and takes 13 (2d12) poison damage, and one other creature within 5 feet of the original target must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw. If it fails, it becomes poisoned until the end of the Manifestation of Decay’s next turn and takes 7 (1d12) poison damage. Legendary Actions The Manifestation of Decay can take up to 2 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time, only at the end of another creature’s turn, and only if the Manifestation of Blood did not take a Legendary action that turn. The Manifestation of Decay regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn. Shared Anguish. One creature within 30 ft. of the Manifestation of Decay gains all conditions that the Manifestation of Decay currently has until the end of the Manifestation of Decay’s next turn. That creature and the Manifestation of Decay then take 4 (1d6) psychic damage for each condition that the Manifestation of Decay has. Corrupt Flesh. The Manifestation of Decay commands a corpse of the fallen to rise up and serve it. The body of a non-undead creature within 60 ft. of the Manifestation of Decay transforms into a skeletal marauder (page 128) armed with weapons made of bone. Foul Reformation. The Manifestation of Decay’s body shudders before violently erupting in puss and bile. Creatures within 10 ft. of the Manifestation of Decay must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw. If they fail, the suffer 7 (2d6) acid damage. The Manifestation of Decay then teleports to a location within 20 feet of its original location.
