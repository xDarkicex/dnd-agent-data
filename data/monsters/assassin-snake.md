# Assassin Snake

**description**: Despite its name, clockwork crafters market this device solely as a pest control option. The assassin snake can eliminate nuisance pests by crawling through a house’s walls or deep into rodent burrows in a garden. The snake has the Chemical Reservoir modification, and it is sold with one dose of basic poison or one use of alchemist’s fire (purchaser’s choice) preloaded in the reservoir.
