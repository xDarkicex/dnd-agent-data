# Frost Giant Priest of Auril

**description**: A devout worshipper of Auril the Frostmaiden, the frost giant priest can unleash powerful cold-based spells and abilities. In addition, the frost priest radiates an unnatural cold that can freeze the blood of anyone foolish to approach it. Traits Aura of Chilling Frost. A creature that starts its turn within 10 feet of the giant takes 7 (2d6) cold damage and can't use reactions until the end of the giant's next turn. Actions Multiattack. The giant makes two War Pick attacks. War Pick. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (3d8 + 5) piercing damage plus 10 (3d6) cold damage. Frostburst (Recharge 4-6). Freezing, icy shards explode in a 10-foot-radius sphere centered on a point within 120 feet of the giant. Each creature in that area must make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 22 (4d10) cold damage and 11 (2d10) piercing damage, has its speed is reduced by 10 feet and it can't use reactions until the end of its next turn. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage with no additional effects. Auril's Embrace (1/Day). Each creature within 40 feet of the giant must make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 45 (10d8) cold damage and is hindered by ice formations for 1 minute, or until it or another creature within reach of it uses an action to break away the ice. A creature hindered by ice has its speed reduced to 0. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and isn’t hindered by ice. Spellcasting. The giant casts one of the following spells using Wisdom as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 16): At will: guidance, mending, resistance, thaumaturgy 3/day each: armor of Agathys, Snilloc's snowball swarm, ice storm 1/day each: cone of cold, harm, wall of ice
