# Woden, Dor of Clan Durgis

**description**: Appearance and Personality: Woden is as broad as he is wide. White curly hair pours forth from under his helmet and blends into his massive, completely uncontrolled beard. His largely unkempt hair emerges in all directions and seems to constantly pick up dirt, and any possible stray objects, including detritus from combat. In spite of this, his weapons and armor remain immaculate, the mithral gleamingly clean in any light. Woden is a pragmatist. He has agreed to turn over his precious resources to Aradil, because he fears that Clan Durgis may not be able to maintain its holdings for much longer. While he is tremendously proud of his clan and its traditions, he recognizes that it is much more important for some remnants of dwarven society to survive, even if he and his heirs are not a part of that. In spite of this, he is also an enthusiastic and nigh unstoppable combatant. He charges into battle with little regard for his own safety, trusting in his abilities and those around him, even if that trust is misplaced. Traits Dwarven Resilience. Woden has advantage on saving throws against poison. Bonus Actions Rallying Cry (Recharge 5-­6). Woden can use his bonus action to inspire his allies. Each creature of Woden’s choice that can hear him within 30 feet of him gains 4 temporary hit points. In addition, each affected creature can immediately make a saving throw to overcome either the charmed or frightened conditions (or both). Actions Multiattack. Woden makes three attacks with his mithral great hammer. Mithral great hammer. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6 + 4) bludgeoning damage Headbutt. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage and the target must make a DC15 Constitution saving throw or be stunned until the end of Woden’s next turn. Reactions Retributive Headbutt. When Woden suffers damage from a melee weapon attack made by a creature within 5 feet of him, he may use his reaction to make a Headbutt attack targeting the creature that dealt the damage.
