# Prince Abdalla Laurent

**description**: Prince Abdalla is the current head of House Laurent. He is a human Noble man of some forty years. He has short, tightly curled black hair, large brown eyes, brown skin, and a full face, with piercings through his nose and several along one ear. He wears a golden chain with the symbol of Arwyn the Moon Hunter as a pendant, but is an avid follower of the Old Gods and holds worship to Danu, Lugh, and Nodens as well. The prince wears a crown fashioned out of golden reeds upon his head and wears a yellow robe with red lining.
