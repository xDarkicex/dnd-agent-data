# Gogiteth

**description**: A gogiteth is a slavering nightmare of teeth, eyes, and hairy spiderlike legs, and its appearance is invariably seared into the minds of any who witness it. Native to the Darklands, gogiteths are more intelligent than their appearance would suggest, yet they rarely put this intellect toward anything other than awfulness. The gogiteth’s alien anatomy has inspired no shortage of speculative origin stories. Some believe they are a warp of a fleshwarping experiment gone horribly wrong, while others maintain that they could only have come to this world from some distant and fundamentally hostile planet. Traits Carry Off Prey. The gogiteth can move at its full speed while grappling a creature, moving the creature with it. Relentless Hunter. The gogiteth has advantage on Wisdom (Survival) checks made to track creatures. Actions Multiattack. The gogiteth makes three attacks: one with its bite and two with its legs. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 24 (4d8+6) piercing damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it is grappled (escape DC 18). Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained and takes 24 (4d8+6) piercing damage at the start of each of the gogiteth’s turns, and the gogiteth can’t bite another target. Leg. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 20 (4d6+6) piercing damage. Skittering Assault. The gogiteth moves up to its speed three times, making a leg attack against a different creature within reach each time it moves. Reactions Skittering Reposition. When a creature the gogiteth can see moves within 10 feet of its space, the gogiteth can move up to 10 feet. The gogiteth doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks with this movement.
