# Puffin

**description**: Puffin Puffins have incredibly sharp talons, their bodies are generally black in color, or black and white, and they have large brightly colored beaks. They are capable fliers and proficient swimmers. The puffins of Grimnir are much like similar birds in other lands, but stand taller and, like most of this world’s beasts, are more capable of defending themselves. Though no one has ever claimed to be attacked by an angry puffin, a few raiders carry a scar from reaching for one of these cute birds, assuming them to be more docile than they are.
