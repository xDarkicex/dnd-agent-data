# Kereek

**description**: The kobold sorcerer Kereek works alongside his friend and ally Prazil as a spy for the troll warlord Hargulka. Kereek is strong for a kobold—he can serve as the leader of a kobold warren in your game, or as a member of a monstrously themed rival adventuring party. Traits Spellcasting. Kereek is a 5th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 16, +8 to hit with spell attacks). He has the following sorcerer spells prepared: Cantrips (at will): fire bolt, mage hand, mending, minor illusion, poison spray 1st level (4 slots): chromatic orb, expeditious retreat, mage armor 2nd level (3 slots): scorching ray, blur 3rd level (2 slots): fireball Pack Tactics. Kereek has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the kobold’s allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn’t incapacitated. Sunlight Sensitivity. While in sunlight, Kereek has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight. Special Equipment. Kereek carries a wand of burning hands . The wand has 7 charges. Using an action to expend 1 charge, the wielder casts the burning hands (save DC 15) spell at 1st level. Kereek can increase the spell slot level by one for each additional charge expended. The wand regains 1d6+1 charges daily at dawn. If the last charge is expended, roll a d20. On a 1, the wand crumbles into ashes and is destroyed. Actions Multiattack. Kereek makes two dagger attacks. Dagger. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4+2) piercing damage. Wand of Burning Hands. Kereek casts burning hands using his wand.
