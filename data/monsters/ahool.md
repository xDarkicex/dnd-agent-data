# Ahool

**description**: Ahool soar through the endless caverns of the Underworld of Aventyr, seeking blood prey upon which to feed. They are primal hunters that strike swiftly and without warning, carrying their captives away to a moss cavern for later draining. Fierce and primal, few ahool aspire to more than sated bloodlust and fostering a brood of ahooling. Those that do embrace forming a pack and building a settlement also take blood servants, lesser humanoids upon which to feed at will. Bloodthirst. Ahool must consume the blood of the living in addition to normal sustenance. Their bloodthirst not only drives the need to hunt and feed, but creates an ahool’s instinct toward transformation, a primordial call to return to the demonic form that was theirs at the dawn of their creation. Known as true ahool, Underworld scholars speculate if such creatures truly exist, or if they’re merely the hopeful desires of a race of predators.
