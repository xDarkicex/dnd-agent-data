# Harpy Matron

**description**: Save for the tattered wings and taloned feet, a harpy matron resembles a feral woman with a wild look about her. Often viewed as vicious and corrupted creatures, harpies know how creatures think and act. This understanding gives them an advantage when it comes to finding their favorite meals. While creatures of the wild easily fall victim to their captivating songs, these vile bird-women prefer their meals spiced with complex sentient thoughts. Easy prey makes for a boring meal. While ultimately savage and without remorse for their actions, a number of harpies live close to humanoid societies and enjoy parlaying with creatures that they see as potential meals. A harpy matron is a rarity. Usually, a harridan leads a very large clan of harpies and each noble swears fealty to a queen. The noble is often underestimated as she does not look much different to a normal harpy, which she uses to her advantage. Creatures closely examining the matron, however, notice that rather than ratty, matted hair her head is wreathed in snakes similar to a medusa’s.
