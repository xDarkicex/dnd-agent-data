# Troll King

**description**: Troll Trolls are one of the most notorious dangers of the Stolen Lands, be they encountered alone or as members of larger bands. Beyond the typical troll, other more dangerous variants of these creatures can be found in the region, each sharing a penchant for violence, sadism, and murderous cruelty. Troll King Some trolls have learned the skills necessary to survive well beyond the typical life expectancy of their kind and rally others to follow their lead. Troll kings demonstrate a cunning far beyond their younger kin, and can be dangerous foes even when encountered alone. While they look no different than the typical troll, the trophies, armor, and weapons they wear leave no doubt as to their roles in troll society. Trait Keen Smell. A troll king has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Regeneration. The troll regains 15 hit points at the start of its turn. If the troll takes acid or fire damage, this trait doesn’t function at the start of the troll’s next turn. The troll dies only if it starts its turn with 0 hit points and doesn’t regenerate. Actions Multiattack. The troll king uses its primordial roar if available, then makes three attacks, one with its bite and two with its claws. If the troll king successfully hits the same creature with both claw attacks, it rends the target, dealing an additional 14 (4d6) slashing damage. Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 10 ft., one creature. Hit: 15 (2d8+6) piercing damage. Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 10 ft., one creature. Hit: 16 (3d6+6) slashing damage. Primordial Roar (Recharge 5–6). The troll king unleashes a bestial roar. Each creature within 100 feet that is not a troll and can hear it must succeed on a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened for 1 minute.
