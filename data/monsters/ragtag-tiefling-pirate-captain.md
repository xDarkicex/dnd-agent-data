# Ragtag Tiefling Pirate Captain

**description**: After the end of the old world, many ragtag tieflings found their way to the Ocean Heart, the great sea in the center of Ashandri. Some of them became successful pirate captains, fighting for freedom, treasure or simply for adventure and thrill. Occasionally, their ships set anchor for a while, and these pirate captains can be found at land - either for a short vacation, or to pursue a personal mission. Most of the time, they are accompanied by at least a few trusted members of their crew.
