# Blood Cult Acolyte

**description**: Blood Cultists Evil to their core, blood cultists typically pay homage to evil gods of chaos, darkness, death, evil, murder, tyranny, or war. They are what most would call classic cultists: simple people with simple goals, such as destroying the world, mass murder, demon summoning, and other similar activities. Usually amassed in small cabals with several acolytes, a few fanatics, and a single hierarch, Blood cultists can serve as dangerous and reckless foes in any adventure. What is it with cultists and blood and sacrifice? If you’re so enamored with gore and death, why not apprentice as a butcher or become a mortician? Running around and conjuring demons just seems so.. unnecessary. – the Heretic
