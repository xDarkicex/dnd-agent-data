# Goblin Archer

**description**: The goblin archer is a skilled hunter and a sharpshooter with the shortbow. A target's cover does little to deter the aim of a goblin archer. Traits Sharpshooter. The goblin's ranged weapon attacks ignore half cover and three-quarters cover and attacking at long range doesn't impose disadvantage on the goblin's ranged weapon attack rolls. Actions Scimitar. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) slashing damage. Shortbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) piercing damage. Bonus Actions Nimble Escape. The goblin takes the Disengage or Hide action.
