# Drow Mystic

**description**: Priestess is a formal title amongst the drow clergy, used to denote one with authority to speak with the deity’s voice—to drow of lesser rank. Mystic is a more generic term that describes any clergy member in good standing, male or female. Mystics can be found serving many roles with a house, from advisor to matron.
