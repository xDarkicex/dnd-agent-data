# Robe of Eyes

#### Robe of Eyes

_Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement)_

This robe is adorned with eyelike patterns. While you wear the robe, you gain the following benefits:

**All-Around Vision.** The robe gives you Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight.

**Special Senses.** You have Darkvision and Truesight, both with a range of 120 feet.

_Drawbacks._ A _Light_ spell cast on the robe or a _Daylight_ spell cast within 5 feet of the robe gives you the Blinded condition for 1 minute. At the end of each of your turns, you make a Constitution saving throw (DC 11 for _Light_ or DC 15 for _Daylight_), ending the condition on yourself on a success.
