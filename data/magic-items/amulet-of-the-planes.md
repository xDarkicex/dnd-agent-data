# Amulet of the Planes

#### Amulet of the Planes

_Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement)_

While wearing this amulet, you can take a Magic action to name a location that you are familiar with on another plane of existence. Then make a DC 15 Intelligence (Arcana) check. On a successful check, you cast _Plane Shift_. On a failed check, you and each creature and object within 15 feet of you travel to a random destination determined by rolling 1d100 and consulting the following table.
