# Garden Born

**description**: Effectively living dirt, the Garden Born are sentient Creatures created from a bond between the earth and life itself within the Feywild . Gardenlings typically are host to some type of plant organism or insect, and must keep that organism alive in order to stay sentient. Gardenling aren’t born with an outer shell, instead transferring themselves from casing to casing as they mature. Gardenling often wear handcrafted masks so that they can blend in more comfortably with intelligent Humanoids , but beneath the mask have no face. Garden Born are without Family , as they are created by natural circumstance. They speak, but not with lungs. They drink, but not with a mouth. Because they are self-created Gardenlings either have no names or adopt a name for themselves. Garden Born Traits Ability Score Increase. Your Constitution score increases by 2, your Wisdom score increases by 1. Age. No one knows how Gardenlings are created or how long they live, and finding one is very rare. They spring from the ground seemingly at random and perish only when their bond with their garden lifeform has been severed. Given enough care, one might live for eons. You are immune to magical aging effects. Alignment. Any. Size. Your size is Medium. Constructed Humanoid. You have two creature types: humanoid and construct. You can be affected by a game effect if it works on either of your creature types. Speed. Your base walking speed is 30 feet. Turn the Earth. From death we grow new life. You gain the following benefits: You have resistance to Necrotic damage. You don’t need to eat, but you must drink water once a day. Pollinator Ally. You know the Infestation Cantrip. Wisdom is your spellcasting modifier for it.(XGE p.158) Fruit of the Earth. Once per day you can cast Goodberry, Wisdom is your spellcasting modifier for it. Languages. You can speak, read, and write Common and can cast Speak With Plants at will.
