# Tserka

**description**: The tserka are descended from insectoids that rapidly evolved within a Crucible. They reside in subterranean cities fashioned from the scavenged technologies of other worlds. A full description of their history and culture can be found in the Threats Database, where the tserka were originally introduced. Ability Score Increase. Your Constitution score increases by 2 and your Strength score increases by 1. Age. Tserka reach adulthood at 10 years old and can live up to 60-70 years. Size and Speed. Your size is Medium. You have a walking speed of 30 feet. Subterranean Sight. You have blindsight with a range of 30 feet and darkvision with a range of 60 feet. Keen Hearing. You have advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing. Mental Resilience. You have advantage on on saving throws against being charmed or frightened. Natural Armor. When you aren’t wearing armor, your AC is 12 + your Dexterity modifier. You can use your natural armor to determine your AC if the armor you wear would leave you with a lower AC. A shield’s benefits apply as normal while you use your natural armor. Languages. You speak Common and Tserka.
