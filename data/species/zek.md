# Zek

**description**: The following example is the zek, a species presented as Threats in the Threats Database. They are a rodent-like species covered in colorful scales. You can use this sample as is or you can alter it as you choose using any of the options presented earlier. The zek are an ancient species whose history dates back almost as far as that of the Dendus or Eldori. As a society, they’ve proclaimed themselves as the unofficial defenders of the galaxy’s “fledgeling” societies. Their curiosity, zeal, and optimism can often be overwhelming and similarly troublesome. See the Zek in the Threats Database for a full description of their history and society. Ability Score Increase. Your Dexterity score increases by 2. and your Charisma score increases by 1. Age. Zek reach adulthood at 50 years old and can live as long as 400-500 years. Size and Speed. Your size is Small. You have a walking speed of 25 feet. Fortune’s Favor. Whenever you make an ability check, Attack roll, or saving throw, you can roll a d6 and add the result to the initial die roll. You can use this feature once and regain the ability to do so after when you finish a short or long rest. Graceful Agility. You can move through the space of any creature that is of a size larger than yours. Strong Will. You have advantage on Saving Throws against being charmed or frightened, and you can’t be put to sleep by esper powers or effects. Languages. You speak Common and Zekish.
