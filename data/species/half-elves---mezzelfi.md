# Half-Elves - Mezzelfi

**description**: In our setting, half-elves are often integrated into human society and may well be members of important families due to their special traits and graceful appearance. It is said that the humanist Pope Pius II (Enea Silvio Piccolomini) was a half-elf and instructed his family in the ancient history of this species. Pius founded the city of Pienza as a secret refuge for all elves and half-elves who felt rejected or threatened by human society. Please read the introduction (chapter 1) and the text about elves to understand the dilemma of half-elves: Contrary to mere humans, they know that the other species are very real and have a strong emotional connection to them, but they also feel the need to be part of the human society that has distanced itself from all non-humans. Torn between the wish to live according to both ancestries, they actually fit perfectly into our story. By the way: The singular masculine and feminine forms of mezzelfi are mezzelfo and mezzelfa. Half-Elves – Mezzelfi Traits Languages. You speak the language of the country you live in as well as the elvish tongue, which is a bastardisation of the ancient fae. Religion. Depending on their loyalties, mezzelfi will either follow one of the human faiths or an elven belief system. All other traits as described in the basic rules may be used.
