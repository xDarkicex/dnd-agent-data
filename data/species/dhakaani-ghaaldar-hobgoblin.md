# Dhakaani Ghaal'dar (Hobgoblin)

**description**: Dhakaani hobgoblins are known as ghaal’dar , “mighty folk”—not to be confused with the Ghaal’dar tribes that dominate Darguun. The ghaal’dar are soldiers, scholars, and leaders, valued for their versatility and their vision. Your ghaal’dar character has the following traits. Ability Score Increase. Your Constitution score increases by 2. Age. Ghaal’dar mature at the same rate as humans and have lifespans similar to theirs. Size. Ghaal’dar are between 5 and 6 feet tall and weigh between 150 and 200 pounds. Your size is Medium. Speed. Your base walking speed is 30 feet. Darkvision. You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can’t discern color in darkness, only shades of gray. Discipline. You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed. Languages. You can speak, read, and write Common and Goblin. Subrace. Within the Dhakaani caste system, young ghaal’dar are trained to fulfill one of two roles. Guides are diplomats and scholars, while soldiers are devoted to war. Choose a subrace for your ghaal’dar character. Guide Soldier
