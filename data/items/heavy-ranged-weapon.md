# Heavy Ranged Weapon

**description**: Heavy Ranged Weapon Ranged weapon Price: 75 gp A large weapon used to attack from a distance. (See rules for weapons in the Combat section) Ranged. Ranged weapons are used to attack enemies at a distance. These weapons can be either shot like a bow and arrow or crossbow, or thrown like a spear or javelin. All ranged weapons use Dexterity for both the Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus. Heavy. Heavy weapons require both hands to wield and often require significant Strength to use effectively. Heavy melee weapons must use Strength for the Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus. Damage: 1d8
