# Bezoar of the Behir

**description**: Wondrous item, rare While this bezoar sits in your stomach, you have resistance to lightning damage. In addition, you can use an action to exhale lightning in a line that is 30 feet long and 5 feet wide. Each creature in that line must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d10 lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. You must finish a long rest before you can exhale lightning again.
