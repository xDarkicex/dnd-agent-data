# Wok of Frying

**description**: Wok of Frying Weapon (club), rare (requires attunement) Damage: 1d6+1 bludgeoning Weight: 3 lbs. Properties: Light This heavy steel wok is used by the dwarven monks of Kendu for cooking and self defense. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic cooking pan. Flaming Wok. You can use a bonus action to speak the wok’s command word, causing flames to erupt from the bottom of the pan. These flames shed bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for an additional 30 feet. While the wok is ablaze, it deals an extra 1d6 fire damage to any target it hits. The flames last for 1 minute or until you use a bonus action to speak the command word again. This feature can be used a number of times per day equal to your proficiency bonus. Hearty Meal. Once per day, you can spend 5 minutes cooking a nutritious meal. Eating this meal restores 2d8 hit points and removes 1 level of exhaustion.
