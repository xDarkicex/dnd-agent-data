# Vintner's Chalice

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) Any fluid substance poured into this magical chalice can be purified and rendered free of poison and disease, as long as you slowly wave the chalice, carefully smell the substance, and then taste it with a classy and concentrated look.
