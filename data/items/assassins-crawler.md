# Assassin's Crawler

**description**: Weapon (dagger), rare This dagger’s blade is made of thick metal plates, giving it an appearance similar to that of a trilobite. It functions as a +1 dagger , and can be activated as an action, causing it to grow insectoid legs and animate for 1 minute. While animated, the creature who activated it can move it up to 30 feet including up, down, and across vertical surfaces and upside down on ceilings, and make an attack with it as a bonus action. They can also give it a simple command, such as “enter this room and kill the person inside”. The weapon will attempt to accomplish the task within the time period, returning once it succeeds. While animated, the weapon has an AC of 18 and 10 hit points. Any creature that attempts to grapple it takes 2 points of slashing damage. Its movement speed cannot be reduced and it is immune to the restrained and grappled conditions. When reduced to 0 hit points, or at the end of the minute, it folds up into a dagger and cannot be animated again until midnight of the next day.
