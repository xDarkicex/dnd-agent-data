# Gnomesalt Taffy

**description**: Potion, uncommon This sweet and sticky candy comes in a variety of flavors. When you consume this food, your reach increases by 5 feet for 1 hour. During this time, you also gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls made with melee weapons that have the Reach property.
