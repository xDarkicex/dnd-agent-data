# Shadecarver

**description**: Weapon (Any Sword), Rare (Requires Attunement) Shadows seem to ooze from this magic sword. While holding the sword, you have Blindsight out to a distance of 10 feet. The sword has 3 charges for the following properties and regains all expended charges daily at dawn: Murksight. When you hit a creature with the sword, you can expend 1 charge to force the creature to make a DC 13 Charisma saving throw. On a failure, the creature's sight is limited to a distance of 5 feet until the start of your next turn; it is Blinded beyond this radius unless it has Blindsight, Tremorsense, or Truesight. Sheen of Darkness. When you make a melee weapon attack with the sword, you can expend 1 charge to have it leave behind a 1-inch-thick, 5-foot-wide, and 5-foot-tall sheen of opaque, magical darkness in its wake. The sheen dissipates after 1 minute.
