# Klavek Moonblade

**description**: Longsword, legendary (requires attunement) This Klavek sword has the phases of the moon etched into the blade. You gain a +1 to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. Light. If the sword is on your person, you can use to it cast the light cantrip at will. Moonbeam. Once each day, if the sword is on your person, you can use it to cast moonbeam.
