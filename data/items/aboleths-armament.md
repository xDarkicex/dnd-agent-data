# Aboleth's Armament

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic mask is carved from jade and depicts an aboleth's visage. Aberrational Mind. While wearing the mask, you can speak and understand Deep Speech and you can communicate simple ideas, emotions, and images telepathically with any creature within 60 feet that can understand a language. Probe. As a bonus action, you probe the mind of a creature you can see within 60 feet. The creature must make a DC 15 Charisma saving throw. On a failure, you learn your choice of one of the following pieces of information about the creature: Base Statistics. The creature's type, speeds, and current hit points Damage. The creature's damage immunities, resistances, and vulnerabilities Conditions & Proficiencies. The creature's condition immunities and saving throw proficiencies Ability. One significant action or trait the creature has (GM's choice) On a success, the creature is immune to this property for 24 hours.
