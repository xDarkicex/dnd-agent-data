# Fire-Blossom Arrows

**description**: Fire-Blossom Arrows Ammunition Fire-blossom arrows were originally designed by the Agasha as an alternative to the traditional fireworks shows, displaying dazzling, varied colors in smaller quantities to save on resources. A fire-blossom arrow illuminates an area of 50 feet around every point along its path, and can be seen at a much greater distance. If it strikes a creature, it deals 1 fire damage.
