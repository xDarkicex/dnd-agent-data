# Quiver of Ammunition

**description**: Quiver of Ammunition Wondrous Item, very rare (requires attunement) Weight: 2 lbs. Each time you make a ranged attack, this quiver magically replaces the piece of ammunition you used with a similar piece of nonmagical ammunition. The quiver can also produce up to 5 magic arrows or bolts per day and recharges at dawn. You choose which magic property the ammunition will take before you roll the attack. Fire. +2 to hit and damage. Hit: Additional 2d6 fire damage. Any creatures within 10 ft that fail a DC 13 Dexterity save take 1d6 fire damage. Lightning. +2 to hit and damage. Hit: Additional 2d6 lightning damage and the creature must make a DC 13 Constitution save or become stunned until the end of its next turn. Ice. +2 to hit and damage. Hit: Additional 2d6 cold damage and the creature must make a DC 13 Constitution save or become petrified (frozen) until the end of its next turn. Gas. +2 to hit. Hit: Normal damage but the target is also hit by a Stinking Cloud spell. (DC 13 Constitution save.) Web. +2 to hit. Hit: Normal damage but the target is also hit by a Web spell. (DC 13 Dexterity save or be restrained.) Darkness. +2 to hit and damage. Hit: Additional 1d6 necrotic damage and the target is also hit by a Darkness spell.
