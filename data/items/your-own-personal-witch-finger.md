# Your Own Personal Witch Finger

**description**: Wondrous item, Unique This witch finger is unique and if you are the victim of this particular witch finger then you get +1 to your spell DCs or +1 to your spell attack rolls. This decision is made when the finger is created. It cannot be changed. This unique bonus is in addition to the bonuses associated with a living witch finger.
