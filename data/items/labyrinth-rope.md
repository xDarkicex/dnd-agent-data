# Labyrinth Rope

**description**: Wondrous Item, Common When you speak a command word, this rope slithers like a snake toward the nearest exit out of any maze, dungeon, or labyrinth for 1 hour at a speed of 20 feet per round. When it reaches the exit, it coils itself up and waits for you to retrieve it.
