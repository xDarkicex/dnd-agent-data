# Bootleather

**description**: Bootleather Wondrous item, very rare This boot, sized appropriately for a giant, appears to be poorly made, from many scraps of shoe leather. The boot sizes itself to whomever wears it, but it is always slightly bigger than any footwear on the other foot. The boot is indestructible, and the wearer gains a +1 bonus to their Armor Class and resistance to all piercing damage, no matter its source. The wearer also becomes immune to being rendered prone or being shoved or knocked backwards without their consent.
