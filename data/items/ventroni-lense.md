# Ventroni Lense

**description**: Ventroni Lense Wondrous item Price: 1,500 gp A complex-looking monocle with several different lenses that can be lowered. The main lens has a red hue. The outer ring has an engraving reading “Dr. Ventroni” on it. A character may look through the lens as a Bonus Action. They are able to discern any magical illusion or shapeshifting that a creature may be using, revealing its true form.
