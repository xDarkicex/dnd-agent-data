# Hatred Bracelets

**description**: Wondrous item, Weird, Very Rare Hatred bracelets allow a hag to cast and store any spell, with a range of touch, in the bracelet for up to an hour. The hag can choose to cast the spell on a target that she successfully hits with her claws or hands. If the spell is not used after an hour then the spell dissipates. This weird can only be used to store a spell once per moonrise. This weird also increases the hag’s claw attack damage by 3 (1d6) necrotic damage if the target is in moonlight. Any creature, other than a hag, foolish enough to put these on will find themselves stuck fast to the nearest surface. They will be unable to move until they take the hatred bracelets off or succeed on a DC 15 Strength (Athletics) ability check. This check must be made each turn if the wearer wishes to move. If the wearer fails the check then they gain the restrained condition and can attempt another check at the beginning of their next turn.
