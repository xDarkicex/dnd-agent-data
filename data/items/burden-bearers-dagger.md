# Burden Bearer's Dagger

**description**: Weapon (dagger), rare The dagger is a magic item, initially intended to be used for live sacrifices by a necromancer cult, the Scholars of the Awakened Mind. Anyone who is injured by this dagger must succeed on a DC 12 Wisdom saving throw, or be charmed for one round, feeling a deep sense of calm and a lack of willingness to fight back. On a successful save, the dagger will not affect the target again.

**properties**:

- Item Type: Weapon (dagger)
- Properties: Finesse, Light, Range, Thrown
