# Sealskin Cloak

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) While wearing this cloak, as an action, you can assume the form of a seal, gaining its bite attack (1d8 piercing damage), AC 12, and 40 ft swim speed. You retain your own ability scores, hit points, and saving throws.
