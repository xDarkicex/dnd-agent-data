# Gloves of the Grappler

**description**: Gloves of the Grappler Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) These fingerless gloves are made of black, hardened leather. While wearing them, you have advantage on your grapple check when you attempt to grapple a creature. In addition, when you move a grappled creature your speed is not halved.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
