# Ring of Blood Seal

**description**: Ring, rare This ring is made from Kanrath silver with gothic carvings. The ring has 3 charges and regains 1d3 expended charges daily at midnight. While wearing it, you can use your action to expend 1 charge by touching a door or a gateway and seal it with a blood seal for 10 minutes. Any creature who passes through the sealed door or the sealed gateway for the first time within the duration takes 1d8 blood damage multiplied by your Charisma modifier (minimum of one damage).
