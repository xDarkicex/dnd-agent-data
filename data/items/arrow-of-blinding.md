# Arrow of Blinding

**description**: Weapon (arrow), uncommon When a target takes damage from this magic arrow, the arrow bursts into a flash of bright light. Any creature in a 30-foot-radius sphere of the target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, or become blinded for 1 minute. At the end of each of its turns, an affected creature can repeat the saving throw, ending the effect on a success.
