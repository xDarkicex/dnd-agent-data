# Shattered Staff

**description**: Shattered Staff Staff, Rare (zero fragments), Very Rare (1-3 Fragments) (requires attunement by a sorcerer, warlock, or wizard) At a first glance, this staff looks like a broken relic, but arcane spellcasters can still use its remaining magic. This staff can be wielded as a magic quarterstaff that grants a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with it. While holding it, you gain a +1 bonus to Armor Class, saving throws, and spell attack rolls. Spells. The staff has 10 charges. While holding it, you can use an action or reaction (as determined by each spell’s casting time and reaction trigger) to expend 1 or more of its charges to cast one of the following spells from it, using your spell save DC and spell attack bonus: absorb elements (1 charge), chromatic sphere (1 charge), color spray (1 charge), levitate (2 charges), or shield (1 charge). The staff regains 1d8 + 2 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20. On a 20, the staff regains 1d4 + 1 charges. Mysterious Sockets. The staff looks incomplete. A legend lore spell or divination magic of equal power can reveal the staff’s true nature and offer hints and adventure hooks towards discovering the four missing elemental fragments. Elemental Fragments. Four elemental fragments have splintered off the staff. When reunited with the staff, a fragment becomes awakened, merging with the staff and granting it its magic, as well as additional benefits. Awakened elemental fragments use the same attunement slot as the Shattered Staff, becoming a single item. Each awakened elemental fragment grants the Shattered Staff the following benefits: The staff’s bonus to attack and damage rolls, as well as the bonus to Armor Class, saving throws, and spell attack rolls increases to +2, unless it’s +2 already from another awakened fragment. The staff gains 10 additional charges for every awakened elemental fragment, and extra spells are added to its spell list as specified in each fragment’s description. The staff regains an additional 1d8+2 expended charges per awakened fragment daily at dawn. When you roll a d20 after having expended the last charge and get a 20 on the roll, the staff regains an additional 1d4+1 charges per awakened fragment. When all four elemental fragments have been recovered, the Shattered Staff is transformed into the Staff of Elemental Mastery.
