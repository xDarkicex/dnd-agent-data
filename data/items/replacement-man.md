# Replacement Man

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) If damage would reduce you to 0 hit points while you are attuned to this human-shaped figurine, you drop to 1 hit point instead and the figurine fades away with a sad smile on his face.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: uncommon
- Requires Attunement: requires attunement

**publisher**: Mage Hand Press

**book**: Valda's Spire of Secrets
