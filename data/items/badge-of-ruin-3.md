# Badge of Ruin +3

**description**: Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement by a Beastheart or a Companion) This gold badge glows whenever your companion gains ferocity in combat. If your companion is attuned to the badge and uses a ferocity action that requires another creature to make an ability check or saving throw, the DC of the check or save is increased by an amount determined by this item’s rarity. While you wear the badge, your exploit save DC increases by an amount determined by the item’s rarity.
