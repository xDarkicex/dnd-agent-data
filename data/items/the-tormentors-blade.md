# The Tormentors Blade

**description**: The Tormentor’s Blade Weapon (longsword), rare (requires attunement) Damage: 1d8+1 slashing Weight: 3 lbs. Properties: Versatile (1d10+1) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic longsword. Menacing Presence: While holding the Tormentor’s Blade, you gain advantage on Intimidation checks. Torturous Strikes: On a successful hit with the Tormentor's Blade, the target must make a Wisdom saving throw (DC 15), taking an additional 2d6 psychic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. This ability can be used once per turn. Shadow Veil: Once per day, you can command the Tormentor's Blade to shroud you in an eerie, protective shadowy aura. You gain advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks and resistance to all damage except radiant damage for 1 minute.
