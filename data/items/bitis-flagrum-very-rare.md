# Bitis Flagrum (Very Rare)

**description**: Bitis Flagrum Weapon (Whip), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you hit a creature with this whip, the target must succeed on a DC 16 Constitution saving throw or take an additional 2d6 poison damage. Snake Form. As a bonus action or an action, you can grab the Bitis Flagrum’s tail and transform it into a whip if it is a snake, or a snake if it is a whip. The whip’s snake form is a poisonous snake or a giant poisonous snake . The snake is friendly to you and your allies. In combat, the creature shares your initiative count, but it takes its turn immediately after yours. It obeys your verbal commands (no action required by you). If you don’t issue any, it takes the Dodge action and uses its move to avoid danger. Progression The Bitis Flagrum progresses once it has killed 150 creatures. When a new creature attunes to the whip, that counter resets to 0 and the whip reverts to its common rarity.
