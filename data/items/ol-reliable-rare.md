# Ol' Reliable (Rare)

**description**: Ol' Reliable Weapon (Light Crossbow), Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. The weapon’s damage die increases to a d10. This magic crossbow lacks the loading property. Curse. The weapon is cursed and becoming attuned to it extends the curse to you. As long as you remain cursed, you are unwilling to part with the weapon. Whenever you attempt to take a long rest while attuned to this weapon, you must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or spend the rest tinkering with Ol’ Reliable to the exclusion of all other activities (including sleep). As such, you don’t gain the benefits of a long rest. At the end of the 8 hours, roll on the Memory Forgotten table and lose the resulting memory. You are unaware that the memory is gone, and it can only be restored with the wish spell or similar magic. Memory Forgotten Table d100 Memory Forgotten 1–10 The name of a person unimportant to you. 11–20 The face of a business acquaintance. 21–30 A taste or smell that brings you joy. 31–40 How to navigate a place you visit often. 41–50 The steps to a task you perform daily. 51–60 A treasured moment from your childhood. 61–70 A fact important to your profession. 71–80 Where you stored something important. 81–90 Everything about a departed loved one. 91–93 Proficiency in a random skill. 94–96 Proficiency in a random weapon. 97–99 Proficiency in a random saving throw. 100 You forget how to breathe and begin suffocating. If you die, you remember how to breathe if you are later restored to life. Progression Ol’ Reliable progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature gains an ability score improvement, the weapon’s rarity increases.
