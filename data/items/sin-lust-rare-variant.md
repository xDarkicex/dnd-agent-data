# Sin: Lust (rare variant)

**description**: Item modification, uncommon (optional or enhanced attunement) The Task. The fiend requests a minor task, such as delivering a sultry message, flirting with a nobleperson, or gifting someone a bottle of cursed perfume. OA or EA: Lust. While the item is on your person, you can cast the charm person spell at will (save DC 13). If your target succeeds on the saving throw, you feel the sting of rejection; you are afflicted by the bane spell for the next hour and this property can’t be used again until the next dawn. Rare variant: Increase the DC to 15. The fiend asks for a medium task, such as ensuring someone drinks a love potion or courting a nobleperson to gain their favour.
