# Wyvern poison (Injury)

**description**: Wyvern Poison Adventuring gear (poison, injury) This poison must be harvested from a dead or incapacitated wyvern. A creature subjected to this poison must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 24 (7d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Damage: 7d6 Damage Type: Poison
