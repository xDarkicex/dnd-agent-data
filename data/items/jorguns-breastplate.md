# Jorgun's Breastplate

**description**: Armor (breastplate), artifact (requires attunement) When Jorgan Halfbeard died on the mushroom temple grounds, a curious mix of myceloid mysticism and dwarven magic merged to create Jorgan's breastplate. As he fell face-first to the ground, collapsed from the purple pox, his bandolier of divine potions shattered beneath his weight. When his corpse subsequently bloated and exploded, the saturated gore didn't spawn a funglet as it should have, but instead bound itself to his enchanted armor. You have resistance to poison and radiant damage and a +2 bonus to AC while wearing this breastplate of corroded steel and phosphorescent fungus.
