# Shield of Blinding

**description**: Armor (Shield), Uncommon While holding this shield, you can speak its command word and choose one creature that you can see within range to make a Constitution saving throw. If it fails, the target is blinded for 1 minute. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make a new saving throw to regain their sight.
