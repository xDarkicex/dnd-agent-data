# Cyber Optics

**description**: Cyber Optics Augment (head), varies (requires affinity) The standard version of this augment replaces or modifies your eyes with cybernetic components. It is a rare augment. With this augment, you have darkvision out to a range of 120 feet. The augment has no effect if you already have darkvision at this range or greater. Spectral Optics Spectral Analyzer
