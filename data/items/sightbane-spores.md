# Sightbane Spores

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) These spores appear as dense, fibrous clumps of mycelium with an organic texture. Symbiotic Attunement To attune to this symbiont, you must place them onto your eyes whereupon they integrate with your optic nerves and neural pathways. You gain the following effects while attuned to the spores: Blind. You are Blinded. Blindsight. You have Blindsight with a range of 60 feet, perceiving your surroundings through bio-electric fields. Poison Resistant. You have Advantage against being Poisoned and resistance to Poison damage To end your attunement to the spores, a creature must spend 1 minute and make a DC 20 Dexterity (Medicine) check to remove the spores. On a success, the spores are safely removed, ending your attunement to them. On a failure, the spores are destroyed and you remain Blinded for 1d4 days.
