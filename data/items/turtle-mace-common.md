# Turtle Mace (Common)

**description**: Turtle Mace Weapon (Mace), Common (Requires Attunement) Push. Once per turn, when you hit a creature with this magic weapon, the target must succeed on a DC 10 Strength saving throw or be pushed back 5 feet. Progression The Turtle Mace progresses whenever time is taken to restore the full model. By spending 8 hours and 500 gp of bronze, a creature proficient with smith’s tools or tinker’s tools can make a DC 13 Dexterity check to attempt to increase the weapon’s rarity. On a success, the weapon’s rarity increases by one step. On a failure, you lose half the value of the materials and must try again. The weapon’s rarity can’t be increased by more than one step in the same week.
