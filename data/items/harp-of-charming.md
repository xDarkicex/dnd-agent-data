# Harp of Charming

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) While you're playing the harp, you have advantage on Persuasion checks, and creatures that can hear you have disadvantage on checks or saving throws they make against being Charmed by you. Enchanting Performance. As an action, you can speak the harp's command word and cause it to hover in the air while playing an enchanting tune for up to 1 hour. On each of your turns, you can mentally command the harp to move up to 30 feet (no action required) in any direction. Any creature of your choice that starts its turn within 30 feet of the harp and can hear it, must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or it becomes Charmed by the harp until it can't hear the harp's tune or it takes damage. While Charmed in this way, the creature spends its movement on its turn moving to within 5 feet of the harp; it can't take other actions or reactions. The creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. The harp's performance stops if it is ever more than 120 feet away from you or if you use a bonus action to end it. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
