# Bag of Shatter Gems

**description**: Bag of Shatter Gems Wondrous Item, rare Weight: 1 lb. This ordinary looking bag contains up to four uncut gemstones. You can use an action to pull one gem from the bag and throw it up to 30 feet. When the gem lands, it breaks and activates a spell centered on the gem. Roll 1d8 to determine which gem is pulled. d8 Gem Spell Effect 1 Amethyst Shatter 2 Sapphire Entangle 3 Ruby Fireball 4 Topaz Cloudkill 5 Moonstone Fog Cloud 6 Emerald Spike Growth 7 Onyx Darkness 8 Diamond Ice Storm Once four gems have been pulled from the bag, the bag can’t be used again until the next dawn, when 1d4 new gems appear in the bag.
