# Feasting Blade

**description**: Feasting Blade Melee weapon (martial, broad saber), legendary (requires a ttunement) 1d6 slashing damage, Finesse, light, paired. Attacks made with this weapon have +1 to hit and deal +1 damage. When this weapon damages a creature with the bleeding condition it deals an additional 1d6 damage, and the wielder regains hit points equal to half the total damage dealt by the attack (rounded down) against the bleeding creature. Finesse. When making an attack with a finesse weapon, you use your choice of your Strength or Dexterity modifier for the attack and damage rolls. You must use the same modifier for both rolls. Light. A light weapon is small and easy to handle, making it ideal for use when fighting with two weapons. Paired. When you engage in two-weapon fighting using two weapons that have the same name and the paired property, you can add your ability modifier to the damage of the second attack.
