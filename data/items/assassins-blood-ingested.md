# Assassin's blood (Ingested)

**description**: Assassin's Blood Adventuring gear, poison ( ingested ) A creature subjected to this poison must make a DC 10 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, it takes 6 (1d12) poison damage and is poisoned for 24 hours. On a successful save, the creature takes half damage and isn’t poisoned.
