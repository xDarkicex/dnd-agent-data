# Scribe's Surprise

**description**: Scribe’s Surprise Weapon (Whip), Uncommon A simple-looking, ink-stained writing quill. Until it twists into a whip, dripping with loose ink and moving through the air with an unpleasant slushing. When you dip this long, white feather into an inkwell and use a bonus action to speak the command word, “surprise,” the ink congeals into a semi-solid chain and the quill hardens into a functional, if uncomfortable, handle. This transformation lasts until you use a bonus action to speak the command word again. While in quill form, this item is indistinguishable from a normal quill. A creature hit with an attack using this magic weapon is coated in magical ink until they or another creature within 5 feet of them uses an action to wipe the ink off. While coated, the target can’t benefit from being invisible, and other creatures have advantage on Wisdom (Survival) checks to follow the target’s tracks.
