# Spellgaze Amulet

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) This magical amulet is inset with colorful gems and has an eye in its center. You have advantage on Arcana checks while wearing the amulet. Magic Detection. As an action, you can cast the Detect Magic spell from the amulet. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn. Spell School Resistance. As an action, you can calibrate the amulet to protect against a specific type of magic. Choose a school of magic from abjuration, conjuration, divination, enchantment, evocation, illusion, necromancy, and transmutation. Until you use this feature again, you have advantage on saving throws you make against spells of that school of magic, as well as ability checks you make to discern, identify, or recognize spells of that school.
