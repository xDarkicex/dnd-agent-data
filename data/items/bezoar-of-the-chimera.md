# Bezoar of the Chimera

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon While this bezoar sits in your stomach, red scales cover your body, two small horns curl from your temples, and your nose widens and flattens. When you aren’t wearing armor, your AC equals 13 + your Dexterity modifier, and with a 10-foot running start, you can long jump up to 25 feet. In addition, you have advantage on Strength and Dexterity saving throws made against effects that would knock you prone, and you have advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.
