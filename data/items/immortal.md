# Immortal

**description**: Legendary armor property Components: Masterwork suit of exotic medium or heavy armor When you are reduced to 0 hit points but not killed outright, you can make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw. If you succeed, you drop to 1 hit point instead. You can use this ability three times, and you regain all expended uses of it when you finish a long rest. Additionally, you have advantage on death saving throws.
