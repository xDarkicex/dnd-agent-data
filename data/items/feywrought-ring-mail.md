# Feywrought Ring Mail

**description**: Feywrought Ring Mail Armor (Heavy), Rare (Requires Attunement) This colorful, flowery armor was forged in the Feywild and is infused with that plane’s captivating magic. While wearing this armor, you have advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the charmed condition on yourself. This armor has 3 charges. You can use an action to expend a charge to cast the Compulsion spell (save DC 15) from this armor. The armor regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. Ring Mail. This armor is leather armor with heavy rings sewn into it. The rings help reinforce the armor against blows from swords and axes. Ring mail is inferior to chain mail, and it's usually worn only by those who can't afford better armor. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks.
