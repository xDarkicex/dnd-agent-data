# Evil Eye Charm

**description**: Evil Eye Charm Wondrous Item, Rare (requires attunement) Orc shamans have been using eye-shaped glass amulets to protect against charms, curses, hexes, and misfortunes for millennia. The Evil Eye Charm has been imbued with strong defensive magic. It provides two benefits while it is on your person: You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and against enchantment and necromancy spells. The charm has 5 charges. If you fail a saving throw against a spell or an effect that would inflict a curse on you, you can use your reaction to expend 1 charge and turn the failed save into a successful one. Additionally, you can expend 1 charge to cast remove curse from it. The charm crumbles into powder and is destroyed when its last charge is expended.
