# Winter's End Longbow (legendary variant)

**description**: Weapon (any bow), rare (requires attunement) Component: bundle of plant roots Woven from the first branches to bloom at the end of winter, this living bow is strung with a length of snowspider’s silk. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While holding this bow, you can cast the druidcraft cantrip. In addition, when an arrow is nocked on this bow, the arrow immediately lights up, emanating blue mist and shedding bright light in a 15-foot radius and dim light for an additional 15 feet. This light lasts for 1 minute after the arrow is shot from the bow. Winter Hunt. This weapon deals an extra 1d6 cold damage to each creature it hits that is in freezing temperatures or is standing on snow or ice. Ice Shower. As an action, you can choose a point you can see within the normal range of this weapon and fire a volley of up to 6 arrows. These arrows levitate in the air when they enter a 15-foot radius centred on that point. For 1 minute, or until all the arrows are used, whenever a creature other than you enters the area for the first time on its turn or starts its turn there, one arrow shoots at it. The creature must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take 1d6 piercing damage and 1d6 cold damage. Once this property of the bow has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn. Uncommon variant: Remove the bonus to attack and damage rolls. Decrease the cold damage of all properties to 1d4. Reduce the Ice Shower property’s radius to 10 feet and the DC to 13. Very rare variant: Increase the bonus to attack and damage rolls to +2 and the cold damage of all properties to 1d8. Increase the Ice Shower property’s radius to 20 feet, the DC to 16, and the maximum number of arrows you can fire with it to 7. Legendary variant: Increase the bonus to attack and damage rolls to +3 and the cold damage of all properties to 1d10. Increase the Ice Shower property’s radius to 30 feet, the DC to 17, and the maximum number of arrows you can fire with it to 8.
