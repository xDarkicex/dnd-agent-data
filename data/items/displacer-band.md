# Displacer Band

**description**: Displacer Band Utility, rare (requires affinity) This nanoweave strip contains thousands of micro-emitters and is worn around the waist. While you wear this band, it projects an illusion that makes you appear to be standing in a place near your actual location, causing any creature to have disadvantage on attack rolls against you. If you take damage, the property ceases to function until the start of your next turn. This property is suppressed while you are incapacitated, restrained, or otherwise unable to move.
