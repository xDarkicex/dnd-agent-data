# Belt of Halflingkind

**description**: Belt of Halflingkind Wondrous item, Rare (requires attunement) Created by a determined halfling wizard after she encountered belts that correspond to other races and found them lacking, this belt allows everyone to experience life from the perspective of a halfling. While wearing this belt you gain the following benefits: Your Dexterity score increases by 2, to a maximum of 20. You have advantage on Charisma (Persuasion) checks made to interact with halflings. In addition, while attuned to the belt, you have a 50 percent chance each day at dawn of waking up feeling optimistic and happy-go-lucky about your day. If you aren’t a halfling, you gain the following additional benefits while wearing the belt: When you roll a 1 on an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can reroll the die and must use the new roll. You have advantage on saving throws against being frightened. You can speak, read, and write Halfling.
