# Enhanced Machinist Goggles

**description**: Wondrous item, rare Enhanced machinist goggles are bronze goggles, initially made to protect the eyes of machinists and some machine operators from dust and strong winds. Yet with some magical additions and detailed carvings, they can turn into incredibly lavish and useful accessories. While you are wearing the goggles, you gain the following benefits: You have darkvision out to a range of 60 feet. If you already have darkvision, wearing the goggles increases the range by 60 feet. You have advantage on Wisdom (Perception) and Intelligence (Investigation) checks that rely on sight. You have immunity to being blinded.
