# Labyrs of the Raging Bull

**description**: Weapon (battleaxe or greataxe), rare You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While you wield the axe, you have advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks to shove a creature, and you can shove a creature up to two sizes larger than you.
