# Belt of Gnomekind

**description**: Belt of Gnomekind Wondrous item, Rare (requires attunement) The legendary technognome demigod of innovation and discovery Sporkus the Inventor tinkered and improved upon the formula for crafting belts of dwarvenkind to create this new and improved belt. While wearing this belt you gain the following benefits: Your Intelligence score increases by 2, to a maximum of 20. You have advantage on Charisma (Persuasion) checks made to interact with gnomes. In addition, while attuned to the belt, you have a 50 percent chance each day at dawn of waking up feeling curious and inventive. If you aren’t a gnome, you gain the following additional benefits while wearing the belt: You have advantage on all Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws against magic. You have darkvision out to a range of 60 feet. You can speak, read, and write Gnomish. Adventure Hook: Sporkus’ Spectacular Survey! The technognome demigod Sporkus might occasionally contact player characters who wear a Belt of Gnomekind to inquire about their experience with the belt or to offer them quests related to the betterment of gnomekind. These quests often involve participating in unusual experiments while wearing the belt, such as asking player characters to get swallowed by a huge monster, defeat an enemy while wearing the Belt of Gnomekind and nothing else, or give an experimental answer to a sphinx’s riddle. If the task seems dangerous, Sporkus offers guarantees for the wearer’s safety, which boil down to a free resurrection spell in case the experiment results in the wearer’s untimely death.
