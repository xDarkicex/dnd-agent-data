# Moustache (Common)

**description**: Moustache Weapon (Shortbow), Common (Requires Attunement) As a bonus action, you place the bow against your upper lip, shrinking it to the size and form of a handlebar moustache, which magically attaches to your face. While in moustache form, it can’t be used as a weapon. It can be removed and returned to shortbow form as a bonus action. Progression Moustache progresses each time the creature who is attuned to it completes an audacious plan, heist, or scheme (what satisfies this definition is at the GM’s discretion).
