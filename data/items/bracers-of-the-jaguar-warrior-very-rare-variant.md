# Bracers of the Jaguar Warrior (very rare variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) Component: beast (panther) pelt These golden bracers, adorned with layers of spotted fur and three glowing green gems, are worn by the fearsome Jaguar Warriors. Tasked with defending the Sun God’s temple against intruders and wild animals, these bracers imbue these warriors with the ability to summon jaguar spirits from the fey realms. While wearing these bracers, you have a +1 bonus to your AC. Jaguar Spirit Claw. While wearing these bracers, you can use a bonus action to speak their first command word, causing three radiant claws to appear from the gems. For the next minute, you gain the following benefits: Your unarmed strikes deal radiant damage instead of bludgeoning damage. You can add your Wisdom modifier to the damage rolls of your unarmed strikes. Jaguardian. While you wear these bracers, you can speak their second command word to cast the spirit guardians spell (save DC 15), calling forth jaguar spirits to protect you. Once the Jaguar Spirit Claw or Jaguardian property has been used, that property can’t be used again until the next dawn. Uncommon variant: Reduce the DC to 13. Remove the bonus to AC. Very rare variant: Increase the DC to 16. The Jaguar Spirit Claw property can be used twice, regaining all expended uses daily at dawn.
