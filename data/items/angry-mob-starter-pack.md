# Angry Mob Starter Pack

**description**: Wondrous item, rare Component: fey egg With safety pitchforks and protest signs strapped to the back, this plain leather satchel’s unassuming exterior hides a secret within: it houses an illusory mob. Assemble the Mob! When you open this satchel as an action, you can designate up to four willing creatures you can see within 30 feet of you to be ‘multiplied’ for 1 hour. Each affected creature sprouts nine illusory duplicates that all have minor alterations: different clothes, hair, and heights. The duplicates surround and follow the original and seem completely real, including sounds, smells, and temperature appropriate to the creature depicted. So long as you and any multiplied creatures remain within 100 feet of the satchel, these duplicates muddle about, shouting vaguely angry phrases such as “Get them!”, “Begone!”, and “Why is existence vacuous and happiness illusory!?” If a duplicated creature is ever more than 100 feet away from a satchel, its duplicates vanish. A creature that uses its action to examine the mob can determine that it is an illusion with a successful DC 15 Intelligence (Investigation) check, ascertaining which members of the mob are real creatures. If a creature targets a member of the mob with an attack, roll a d10. On a 10, it hits a real creature within the mob (GM’s choice). On a 1-9, it hits one of the duplicates, who doesn’t react, causing the creature to gain advantage on all Intelligence (Investigation) checks to see through the illusion, and it can immediately make one as part of its attack. Once this property of the item has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
