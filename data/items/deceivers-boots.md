# Deceiver's Boots

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) While wearing these boots, you can cast the gaseous form spell and the invisibility spell. Once cast, you can't cast that spell again until the next dawn.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: Uncommon
- Requires Attunement: Yes

**publisher**: Cruor Games

**book**: Vault of Magic
