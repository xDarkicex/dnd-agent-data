# Manticore's Tail

**description**: Weapon (morningstar), uncommon (requires attunement) Ten spikes stick out of the head of this magic weapon. While holding the morningstar, you can fire one of the spikes as a ranged attack, using your Strength modifier for the attack and damage rolls. This attack has a normal range of 100 feet and a long range of 200 feet. On a hit, the spike deals 1d8 piercing damage. Once all of the weapon’s spikes have been fired, the morningstar deals bludgeoning damage instead of the piercing damage normal for a morningstar until the next dawn, at which time the spikes regrow.
