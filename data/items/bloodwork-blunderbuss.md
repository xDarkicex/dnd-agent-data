# Bloodwork Blunderbuss

**description**: Blunderbusses look like longer and wider-bore versions of pistols but they work quite differently and without a revolver. The ammunition is dropped into the barrel rather than loaded, and then it is briefly compressed with a loading stick. Once loaded and triggered, the angst touches the barrel and the reaction shoots the projectile(s) within the barrel.
