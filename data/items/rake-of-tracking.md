# Rake of Tracking

**description**: Rake of Tracking Wondrous item, uncommon You can use this item to rake over a 30-foot-square area, which takes 10 minutes. This has the effect of highlighting the tracks of any creatures that have recently passed through. All ability checks made to follow tracks in the area have advantage for the next 24 hours. Once you have used this property of the rake, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
