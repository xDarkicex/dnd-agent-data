# Glaive of Gravity

**description**: Glaive of Gravity Weapon (glaive), very rare (requires attunement) Damage: 1d10+2 slashing Weight: 6 lbs. Properties: Heavy, Reach, Two-Handed You have a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Gravity Pulse . The glaive has 1d4 + 1 charges. When you hit a creature with the glaive, you can expend a charge to create a gravity pulse. The creature must make a DC15 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes an additional 2d8 force damage, and its speed is halved until the end of its next turn. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and suffers no reduction to its speed. The glaive regains 1d4 charges at dawn.
