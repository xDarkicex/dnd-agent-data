# Frozen Bolt (Common)

**description**: Frozen Bolt Weapon (Spear), Common (Requires Attunement) When you hit with an attack using this magic weapon, the target’s speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of your next turn. Progression The Frozen Bolt progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. As the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, so does the weapon’s rarity. However, in addition to the required increase in proficiency bonus, to progress from very rare to legendary, the attuned creature or their allies must kill an ancient red dragon during a combat encounter in which the dragon has been wounded by the Frozen Bolt in its very rare form.
