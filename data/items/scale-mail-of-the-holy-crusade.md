# Scale Mail of the Holy Crusade

**description**: Scale Mail of the Holy Crusade Armor (medium), rare (requires attunement) While you wear this golden armor, it sheds dim light in a 5-foot radius around you. If you are within 60 feet of a fiend or undead, the armor instead sheds bright light in a 30-foot radius, and dim light for an additional 30 feet, and you gain a +2 bonus to your AC. The bright light shed by this armor is sunlight. Scale Mail. This armor consists of a coat and leggings (and perhaps a separate skirt) of leather covered with overlapping pieces of metal, much like the scales of a fish. The suit includes gauntlets. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC : 14 + Dex modifier (max 2) Stealth : Disadvantage Weight : 45
