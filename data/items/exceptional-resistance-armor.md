# Exceptional Resistance Armor

**description**: Exceptional Resistance Armor Armor (any), varies (requires affinity) This armor provides the wearer with protection against a specific type of damage. The damage type is chosen by the GM or determined randomly. You can use an action to gain resistance to the chosen damage type for 10 minutes. You can’t use this feature again until you finish a short or long rest. d10 Damage Type d10 Damage Type 1 Acid 6 Necrotic 2 Cold 7 Poison 3 Fire 8 Psychic 4 Force 9 Radiant 5 Lightning 10 Thunder Exceptional. You have resistance to the chosen damage type. This feature replaces the one provided at standard grade.
