# The Key

**description**: The Key This silver coin depicts a crowned female on one side and a horned creature on the other. It is Maria’s personal key that was given to her by Kalliya. The key opens the door to the transition chamber that connects the Inferno and Purgatorio with the mortal world and with the Great Loom. It also allows travel directly between the Inferno and Purgatorio. Entering the chamber with this key is relatively easy as there are only a few symbols on the outer side of the door and the coin will fit exactly into a specific cavity in the rock. To open the door from the inside will require some careful examination, as the chamber walls are covered in intricate patterns, many with a three-dimensional effect. Ask for the appropriate Wisdom checks to succeed in finding the way in and out. Please note that this key does not open other transition chambers, e.g. those into the fae realm.
