# Interface Cloner

**description**: Interface Cloner Utility, uncommon This handheld device has 7 charges. While holding it, you can use an action and expend 1 of its charges to activate the remote connection power with it. The device regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges when you finish a long rest. If you expend the device’s last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the device burns out and becomes useless.
