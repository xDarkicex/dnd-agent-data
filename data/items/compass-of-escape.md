# Compass of Escape

**description**: Wondrous item, rare Emerald’s Price : 21,000 gp The needle on this small brass compass doesn’t move while outdoors. While the compass is in an enclosed space such as a building or cave, the needle always points toward the nearest exit to open air, without accounting for barriers, obstacles, or danger along the way.
