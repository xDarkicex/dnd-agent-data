# Belt of Championship

**description**: Belt of Championship Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement by a pugilist) While wearing this belt, you gain the following benefits: You have a +3 bonus to any Strength or Charisma check you make, as well as to the attack rolls of your pugilist weapons and unarmed strikes. Whenever you roll your fisticuffs die, you gain a +3 bonus to the result. Whenever you start your turn in combat with fewer than half your moxie points remaining, you regain 1 moxie point. When you would gain a level of exhaustion, you can choose not to gain it. Once you use this ability, you can’t use it again until you finish a short or long rest.
