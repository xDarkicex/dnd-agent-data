# Ahool Moss Rope

**description**: Unlike standard ropes, a moss rope is capable of stretching considerably before breaking. A moss rope normally has 6 hit points and can be torn with a DC 25 Strength check. Most moss ropes are designed for ahooling, and thus support up to 250 pounds of weight at a length of 50 feet. For every additional 50 pounds of weight the moss rope bears, it extends by 10 feet, up to a maximum length of 100 feet. For every 10 feet the moss rope stretches, its effective hit points reduce by 1 (to a minimum of 1) and should it bear more than 500 pounds, it immediately snaps. Any exposure to fire (1 point of fire damage) entirely destroys a moss rope as it immediately goes up in flames.
