# Sash of Anonymity

**description**: Sash of Anonymity Wondrous item, legendary (requires a ttunement) An inconspicuous dark sash worn around the waist. While you wear this item, the gaze of others unconsciously wanders away from you. Looking directly at you requires conscious effort and leaves the viewer with a feeling of slight discomfort. While wearing the sash you can’t be detected by magical spells or effects, and you make Dexterity (Stealth) and Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) checks with advantage. Additionally, you can conceal a one-handed weapon by tucking it into the sash. The weapon cannot be seen until you draw it.
