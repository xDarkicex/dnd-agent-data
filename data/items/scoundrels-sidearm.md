# Scoundrel's Sidearm

**description**: Weapon (Blowgun), Uncommon This magical weapon functions as both a blowgun and a dagger, and can be used as either. The blowgun's barrel can hold up to three pieces of ammunition at a time, allowing you to forgo the Loading property of the blowgun until its barrel is emptied. Magical Needles. When you use the blowgun to make a ranged weapon attack, you can cause one of the following effects: Hobble. A creature hit by the attack must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or its speed is reduced by 10 feet until the end of its next turn. Magic Missile. Instead of making an attack roll, the needle becomes a missile of arcane energy that deals 1d4 + 1 Force damage and unerringly hits any target of your choice you can see within 60 feet. Push. A creature hit by the attack must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be pushed back 10 feet.
