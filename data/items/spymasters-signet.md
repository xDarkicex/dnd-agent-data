# Spymaster’s Signet

**description**: Spymaster’s Signet Ring, legendary (requires attunement) While wearing this ring, you can cast disguise self at-will. In addition, you can turn invisible as an action. Anything you are wearing or carrying is invisible with you. You remain invisible for one minute, or until you use a bonus action to become visible again. Once you use this power, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
