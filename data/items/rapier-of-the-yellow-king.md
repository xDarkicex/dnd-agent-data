# Rapier of the Yellow King

**description**: Weapon (rapier), legendary (requires attunement) Component: aberration main eye The guard of this slender, sickly, yellow blade houses a snake’s eye that seems to follow its quarry around the room. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon, which deals an extra 1d4 psychic damage to any target it hits. You can speak and understand Deep Speech. Imposing Presence. While holding this rapier, you can cast the dominate person spell (save DC 17). Once the spell has been cast in this way, it can’t be cast again until the next dawn. Shackles of Pain. As a bonus action, you can choose one creature you can see within 60 feet. That creature must succeed on a DC 17 Charisma saving throw or become cursed and bound to you for 1 minute. So long as it is on the same plane of existence as you, whenever you take damage from any source, the cursed creature takes half as much damage of the same type. This effect can be removed by any spell or effect that removes curses. Once this property of the rapier has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
