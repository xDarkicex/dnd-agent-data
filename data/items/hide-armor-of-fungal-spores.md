# Hide Armor of Fungal Spores

**description**: Hide Armor of Fungal Spores Armor (Medium), Uncommon While wearing this armor, you can take a bonus action to make the armor emit poisonous spores, which fill a 10-foot-radius sphere centered on yourself. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or have the poisoned condition until the end of your next turn. Once this property is used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn. Hide Armor. This crude armor consists of thick furs and pelts. It is commonly worn by barbarian tribes, evil humanoids, and other folk who lack access to the tools and materials needed to create better armor. AC : 12 +Dex modifier (max 2) Weight : 12
