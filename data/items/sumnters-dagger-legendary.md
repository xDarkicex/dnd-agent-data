# Sumnter's Dagger (Legendary)

**description**: Sumnter's Dagger Weapon (Dagger), Legendary (Requires Attunement) You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon and the weapon deals an additional 3d4 radiant damage. You can speak a command word to cause the weapon’s iron blade to emit moonlight, shedding bright light in a 10-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet. The light lasts for 1 hour or until you end it (no action required). Power of the Moon. When you deal damage to a creature cursed with lycanthropy with this weapon, the creature must succeed on a DC 17 Constitution saving throw or instantly revert to its humanoid form for a duration of 1 minute. Show Yourself. While holding this weapon, you may use an action to cause the weapon’s moonlight to expand into a 50-foot-radius sphere that lasts until the end of your turn. Each creature within this radius cursed with lycanthropy must succeed on a DC 17 Wisdom saving throw or instantly change into its hybrid form. You can use this property twice, regaining all expended uses when you finish a short or long rest. Cure the Cursed. You may utter a command word when you hit a creature cursed with lycanthropy with this weapon. When you do, in addition to dealing its normal damage, the weapon removes the lycanthropy curse from the creature. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest.
