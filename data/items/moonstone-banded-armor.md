# Moonstone Banded Armor

**description**: Armor (Heavy), Rare (Requires Attunement) This armor is made of woven moonlight by the shadow goblins. It weighs half as much as comparable armor and always glows with a gentle radiance. While wearing this armor, you have advantage on saving throws against spells and effects involving radiant, cold, or necrotic damage.
