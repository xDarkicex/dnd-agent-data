# Ring of Enlightening

**description**: Ring of Enlightening Wondrous item Price: 25,000 gp This ring has an inlaid set of gems in the shape of a feather. Despite its delicate and detailed appearance, it feels rather heavy for a ring. The wearer of this ring can touch an object to reduce its weight to one third its normal weight. The object must be non-living, non-magical, and must be able to fit into a 6 foot diameter sphere. The object cannot be worn or carried by someone else. The object can remain at its reduced weight for as long as the wearer wishes, though as soon as they stop touching the object it immediately returns to its normal weight. This ring allows the wearer to use two-handed weapons as one-handed weapons, using Dexterity instead of Strength for the Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus of those weapons.
