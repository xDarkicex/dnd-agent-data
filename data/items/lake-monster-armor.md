# Lake Monster Armor

**description**: Lake Monster Armor Medium armor, rare AC 14 + Dex (max 2) You have a swim speed equal to your walking speed while wearing this armor and can use an action to cast dominate beast ( save DC 15 ) from it on a beast that has an innate swimming speed. Once you have cast this spell in this way, you cannot do so again until the next dawn. This armor consists of a coat and leggings made from the scales of a lake monster. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks.
