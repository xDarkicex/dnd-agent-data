# Calming Cantor

**description**: Poison, Rare This viscous, black, odorless liquid is contained in one of two vials worn around the neck of all sworn Arbiters. Normally used to elicit a confession, the poison causes the eyes to become bloodshot and a dark purple bruising forms below the eye that fades after 12 hours. Duration: 1 hour per dose. Any creature ingesting this liquid must succeed on a DC 20 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature can’t speak a deliberate lie while under the poison’s effect but they are aware of this effect.
