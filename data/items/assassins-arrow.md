# Assassin's Arrow

**description**: Assassin’s Arrow Weapon (arrow), uncommon Damage: 1d10 piercing + 2d6 force Weight: ¼ lb. Properties: Ammunition, (Range 150/600) Before firing this arrow, you speak the name of a target you know that is within 600 feet of you. You do not have to see the target. When you fire the arrow, you do not make an attack roll. Instead, the arrow seeks its target, flying around corners and through openings as narrow as 1 inch wide. When the arrow reaches its target, the target must succeed on a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw or take 1d10 piercing damage and 2d6 force damage. Once the arrow hits its target, it becomes an ordinary arrow.
