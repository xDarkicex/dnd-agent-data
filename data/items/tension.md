# Tension

**description**: Journeyman weapon property Components: Masterwork exotic ranged weapon that doesn’t have the Firearm or Thrown properties This weapon gains the Tension property. Tension. When making a ranged weapon attack with a tension weapon, you use your choice of your Strength or Dexterity modifier for the attack and damage rolls. You must use the same modifier for both rolls.
