# Elixir of Swimming

**description**: Elixir Of Swimming Potion, uncommon This vial holds an oily blue fluid. The mixture is slippery on the tongue, and has a slight fishy odor. When you drink this elixir, you gain a swimming speed of 30 and swimming costs no extra movement for 1 hour.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Potion
- Item Rarity: uncommon

**publisher**: Goodman Games
