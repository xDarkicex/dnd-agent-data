# Wild Stench

**description**: Wild-boar hair's mud, undergrowth moss, mouflon scrotum degreasing, and forest's puddle water. Effect : Spreading this prodigious tincture on your skin gives you advantage on all Wisdom (Animal Handling) checks. However, due to the disgusting components of which the mixture is made, you also have disadvantage on all Charisma checks. The effects of the concoction end after 1 hour.
