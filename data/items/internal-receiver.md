# Internal Receiver

**description**: Internal Receiver Augment (head), uncommon (requires affinity) This sensorineural implant functions as both a comm system and an auditory booster. This implant grants the following benefits. The implant can be used as a two-way communicator (see chapter 5 of the Core Manual ). You can link or change frequencies as a bonus action. You have advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing. You have advantage on saving throws made to avoid being deafened.
