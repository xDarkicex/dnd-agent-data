# Shadow Dragon Scale Mail

**description**: Shadow Dragon Scale Mail Armor (Scale Mail), Very Rare (requires attunement) Crafted from the scales of a dragon that embraced the shadows, this magic scale mail protects the wearer while imparting some of the dark power of shadow dragons to them. Mighty heroes might claim these scales from a shadow dragon they defeated. Rarely, shadow dragons might give their scales away willingly to mortals they deem worthy. While wearing this armor, you gain a +1 bonus to AC, you have advantage on saving throws against the Frightful Presence and breath weapons of dragons, and you have resistance to necrotic damage. Shadow Stealth. While you are in dim light or darkness, you can take the Hide action as a bonus action. Dragonsense. You can focus your senses as an action to magically discern the distance and direction to the closest shadow dragon within 30 miles of you. This special action can’t be used again until the next dawn. Hemera on Shadow Dragon Scale Mail “I hope no dragons were harmed in making this suit of armor…. Not all shadow dragons are evil, you know…”
