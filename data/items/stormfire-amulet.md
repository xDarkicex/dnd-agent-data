# Stormfire Amulet

**description**: Stormfire Amulet Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement) This item consists of a flame-shaped electrum pendant set with a large fire opal. While wearing this amulet, you gain immunity to fire damage. The amulet has 3 charges. You can use an action to expend one charge and cast the fire storm spell. The amulet regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. Another very powerful item, with much potential to disrupt the Balance. Perhaps we are better off destroying such as this—I am torn on the matter.
