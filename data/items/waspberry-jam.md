# Waspberry Jam

**description**: Wondrous item, common Component: phial of plant sap This terracotta jar, 4 inches in diameter, contains 1d4 + 1 doses of a sweet, sticky, pink jam. The jar and its contents weigh ½ pound. Insect Trap. As an action, you can spread a single dose of jam to cover a 5-foot-square surface. Tiny, venomous waspberries immediately teleport from the Plane of Fey, forming a swarm of insects that stays in the same spot as the jam and takes its turn on initiative count 0 of each round. The swarm attacks a creature it shares a space with, and otherwise takes the Dodge action. A creature that starts its turn in the swarm’s area or enters the area for the first time on its turn must make a DC 10 Dexterity saving throw, taking 1d4 piercing damage and 1d4 poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. If the swarm has half its hit points or fewer, creatures take no damage in this way. It takes the swarm 1 hour to completely consume the jam, after which it returns to the Plane of Fey. The swarm doesn’t like smoke; if the area where the jam is applied is ever heavily obscured by smoke, the swarm teleports back to the Plane of Fey. If the smoke disappears, the swarm immediately returns to continue eating the jam. Uncommon variant: Increase both damages to 2d4. If the swarm has half its hit points or fewer, creatures take 1d4 piercing damage and 1d4 poison damage instead. Additionally, the swarm is more active in protecting the jam; on each of its turns, it can move up to 15 feet away from the jam’s space, and attacks the creature closest to it.
