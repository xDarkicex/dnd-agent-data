# Pyroclastic Saber (Common)

**description**: Weapon (Scimitar), Common (Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster) Common This magic weapon has 1 charge. If you lack proficiency with this weapon, you can expend 1 charge to gain proficiency with it for 8 hours. This weapon regains 1 expended charge when you finish a long rest. You can expend a spell slot to regain a number of charges equal to the level of the spell slot expended. Once you have regained charges this way, you can’t do so again until you finish a long rest.
