# Reliable Repository

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This circular box has a diameter of 1 foot and is 8 inches high, made of a light but tough metal. On its top, it has a rotating disc labeled from one to ten that must be turned five times to input a numeric code in order for it to open. It opens only to someone who has already attuned to it. Once opened, the chest will relock itself after 2 rounds if unattended. If any creature tries to unlock the chest without using the correct code or being attuned to it, it targets the creature with a fiery magical ray that makes a ranged attack with a +8 bonus against the target within 10 feet of it (vision is irrelevant to this attack roll). On a hit, the target takes 28 (8d6) fire damage. The reliable repository has AC 20, 42 hit points, a damage threshold of 10, damage resistance against slashing and piercing damage, and damage immunity against poison and psychic damage, it is immune to the effects of the knock spell. It can be picked with thieves’ tools on a successful DC 25 Dexterity ability check , or can be forced open with a successful DC 25 Strength (Athletics) skill check.
