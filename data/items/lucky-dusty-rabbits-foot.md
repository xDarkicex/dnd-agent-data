# Lucky Dusty Rabbit's Foot

**description**: Wonderous item, uncommon This worn rabbit’s foot is fluffy with several decade’s worth of rock dust. The foot has two charges and regains all expended charges at dawn. While holding this item, you can use an action to expend one of its charges to cast the jump spell.

**properties**:

- Item Type: Wondrous Item
- Item Rarity: Uncommon
