# Wheel of Burning

**description**: Wheel of Burning Wondrous item, rare This wheel is 2 inches in diameter and constructed from black iron. If you place it on the ground and use an action to speak its command word, a ringed wall of fire appears, 20 feet in diameter, 20 feet high, 1 foot thick, with the wheel at the center. The wall is opaque and lasts for one minute. Any creature that enters the wall or ends its turn there must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, the creature takes 5d8 fire damage, or half as much damage on a successful save. Once used, the wheel can’t be used again until the next dawn.
