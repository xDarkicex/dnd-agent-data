# Iron Ring

**description**: Iron Ring Ring, rare (requires attunement) While wearing this ring, you can use an action to speak a passphrase known only to the High Command of the Black Iron Pact—“Blood of Rioja”—and choose a target destination. If you are not in the demiplane known as the Cyst, you must choose a destination within the Cyst. If you are already in the Cyst, you must choose a destination you have visited before. Once you choose a destination in this way, the ring teleports you there. The ring can be used to teleport twice and regains both uses daily at dusk. Curse. Once you attune to this ring, you can’t take it off or end your attunement to it unless you are targeted by a cure ailment power of 4th order or higher or the greater restorat ion spell.
