# Ceremonial Macuahuitl Longsword (legendary variant)

**description**: Weapon (greatsword or longsword), rare (requires attunement by a creature with a Strength of 16 or higher) Component: phial of humanoid blood Unlike the traditional, functional macuahuitls, this exceptionally heavy specimen replaces wood and bone with gold and jade. Used for ritual sacrifice, this weapon retains the magic bestowed by years of appeasing the Sun God. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The sword has 3 charges and regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. Eclipse! While holding this sword in sunlight, you can expend 1 charge to ask the Sun God to hide its presence and cast the darkness spell. Might of the Sun God. While holding this sword under the night sky or in darkness, you can expend 1 charge to call upon the help of the Sun God and cast the daylight spell. Very rare variant: Increase the attack and damage bonus to +2. When you use Might of the Sun God, you can instead expend 3 charges to cast the sunbeam spell (save DC 16). Legendary variant: Increase the attack and damage bonus to +2, the charges to 5, and the recharge to 1d4 + 1. When you use Might of the Sun God, you can instead expend 3 charges to cast the sunbeam spell (save DC 17).
