# Cantrip Darts

**description**: Weapon (Dart), Uncommon These silver darts are fletched with phoenix feathers. The sharp tips hum softly, the vibrations drawing in potential energy. When you make an attack using this magic weapon, you can infuse it with the magic of a cantrip you know that has a range of touch. The target suffers the weapon’s damage and the effects of the cantrip. The dart then loses all magical properties and becomes an ordinary dart. These darts tend to be found in bundles of 10 darts. Encounter Ideas Explorer’s Gear. A legendary explorer named Horaash Simm (high elf war priest) wore a bandolier of these darts on every expedition, often using them to bestow guidance or spare the dying from a distance. She never returned from her final journey into the Shadow Caverns. Metamagic Market. A renegade halfling mage named Bix Bixby has gone into business for himself, selling illegal magical weapons which alter the properties of spells. Pub Games. The Broken Staff is the watering hole for professors at the local mage’s college. Tossing these darts is a famous pastime of inebriated arcanists, often to explosive effect.
