# Origamicon

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare This is a collection of instructions on how to fold particular origami forms from mundane paper. Folding a form requires a character to spend one minute and succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity check. Unlike mundane origami, folding a form following the tome’s instructions creates a useful item. The book details the creation of the following items that function similar to a feather token: an anchor, a bird, a fan, a swan boat, a tree, and a whip. In addition, the book details the creation of the following items that function similar to a figurine of wondrous power: dog, elephant, fly, griffon, horse, owl, and raven. Lastly, the book details the folding of a paper mace. The paper mace requires a large single sheet of paper that takes an hour to fold, but once created functions as a mace of smiting. The Origomicon also mentions that it is but an introductory tome to a larger field of magic, but no additional volumes are known to exist.
