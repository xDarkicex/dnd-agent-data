# Cornucopia

**description**: Cornucopia Wondrous Item, Very Rare The first instance of this incredible item was created when Heracles wrestled the river god Achelous with so much ferocity that he ripped off one of the deity’s horns. This horn, in kind, was blessed with the divine power of nourishment and incredible vitality. As an action you can cast the following spells from the cornucopia: At Will: create food and water, purify food and drink 1/day: aid (as a 5th-level spell), heroes’ feast
