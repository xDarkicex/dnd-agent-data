# Emblazon Erratica

**description**: Armor (Shield), Very Rare (Requires Atunement) When a spell targets only you while you're carrying this shield, you can use your reaction to roll a d8. If the result is higher than the spell's level, the spell has no effect on you and instead targets another random creature within 30 feet of you. If no other creatures are within the area, the spell has no effect.
