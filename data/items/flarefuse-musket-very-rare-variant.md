# Flarefuse Musket (very rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (any firearm), rare Component: aberration heart Flarefuses are firearms powered by tiny stars. These balls of fusion consume stardust, the remains of exploded stars, that can be conveniently harvested from drifting sunfrond plants. Stardust Ammo. Instead of normal ammunition, flarefuses consume stardust to fire. A sufficient amount of stardust for a single shot is generally worth 1 gp. You load stardust into the firearm as you would normal ammunition. Firing Modes. Refractive prisms allow a flarefuse to fire in one of two modes; focused or flared. As a bonus action, you can switch between these firing modes. Regardless of the firing mode, each shot uses up the same amount of stardust. Focused. The weapon deals an extra 1d4 radiant damage on a hit. Flared. Instead of making an attack roll, you shoot a wave of radiant shrapnel in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in the area must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take 2d8 radiant damage. Very rare variant: Increase the DC to 16, the damage of the Focused firing mode to 2d4, and the damage of the Flared firing mode to 3d6.
