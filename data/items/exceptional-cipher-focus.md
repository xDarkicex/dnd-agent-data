# Exceptional Cipher Focus

**description**: Cipher Focus Focus, varies (requires affinity by a cybermancer) While holding this focus, you have a +1 bonus to channeling attack rolls and to the saving throw DCs of your cybermancer talents. In addition, you can regain 1 talent slot as an action while holding the focus. You can’t use this feature again until you finish a long rest. Exceptional. The bonus to attack rolls and saving throw DCs increases to +2 Superior. The bonus to attack rolls and saving throw DCs increases to +3 Optimum. The number of talent slots you regain increases to 2.
