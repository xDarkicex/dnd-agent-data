# Sword of Balance

**description**: Weapon (Longsword), Uncommon This magic longsword is perfectly balanced and has the Finesse property. While holding the sword, you have advantage on ability checks and saving throws you make to avoid being knocked Prone or moved against your will. Balanced Blow. The sword has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. When you make an attack roll using the sword, you can expend 1 charge to gain advantage on the attack roll. If the attack hits, and the target is a Large or smaller creature, it must succeed on a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw or be knocked Prone.
