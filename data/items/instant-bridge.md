# Instant Bridge

**description**: Wondrous item, rare You can use an action to place this 1-inch miniature of a metallic bridge on the ground and speak its command word. The bridge rapidly grows into a large metal bridge that remains until you use an action to speak the command word that dismisses it, which works only if the bridge is empty. The bridge is 50 feet long and 10 feet wide, with a 4-foot-tall battlement on both sides. Each creature in the area where the bridge appears must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw , taking 8d10 bludgeoning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. In either case, the creature is pushed to an unoccupied space outside but next to the bridge. Objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried take this damage and are pushed automatically. The bridge is made of adamantine, and its magic prevents it from being tipped over. It needs at least a 5-foot footing on both ends to stand firmly. The bridge has AC 21, 100 hit points, immunity to damage from nonmagical weapons excluding siege weapons, and resistance to all other damage. Only a wish spell can repair the bridge (this use of the spell counts as replicating a spell of 8th level or lower). Each casting of wish causes the bridge to regain 50 hit points.
