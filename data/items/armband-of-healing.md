# Armband of Healing

**description**: Wondrous item, rare This copper armband bears the insignia of two stags prancing around a staff. While wearing this armband, any healing spell you cast cures the maximum number of hit points. Once used, this property of the armband can’t be used again until the next dawn.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: rare
