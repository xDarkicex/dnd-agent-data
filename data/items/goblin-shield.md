# Goblin Shield

**description**: Shield, rare (requires attunement) This shield is wrought to resemble a goblin’s head. Three times per day as a bonus action, the goblin’s head can be commanded to attack, biting with the owner’s attack bonus and dealing 2d4 piercing damage. The object regains all uses at the next dawn.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Shield
- Item Rarity: rare

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Margreve Player's Guide
