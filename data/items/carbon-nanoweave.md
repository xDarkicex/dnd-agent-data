# Carbon Nanoweave

**description**: Armor (Any), Very Rare This armor is made of microscopic, black fibers. Nanoweave Contraction You have resistance to Fire and Lightning damage. If you take Fire or Lightning damage, the armor's fibers harden and contract. For 1 minute, you lose resistance to Fire and Lightning damage and instead have a +2 bonus to your AC while you wear the armor.
