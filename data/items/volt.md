# Volt

**description**: Volt Weapon (Dart), Rare (Requires Attunement) This dart has a bright silver sheen and is fashioned in the shape of a lightning bolt. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you make an attack against a creature using this weapon, a bolt of lightning crashes down where the dart impacts or lands and every creature within 5 feet of that space must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 3d10 lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. If you hit your target with the dart, it has disadvantage on the saving throw. If you miss your target by 5 or less, the dart lands on the ground at their feet and they do not have disadvantage. If you miss your target by more than 5, it lands in a random space; the GM rolls 1d8 to determine the direction the dart falls away from your target (1 indicating north and moving clockwise from there) and 1d4 to determine how many spaces away it falls.
