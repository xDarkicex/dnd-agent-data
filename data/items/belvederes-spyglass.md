# Belvedere's Spyglass

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) When looking at a target through the spyglass, you can cast the enlarge/reduce spell, but you cannot choose which of the two effects to apply. Instead, the effect cast is randomly generated by rolling 1d6: on a result of 1, 2, or 3, your target is reduced by the spell, while on a result of 4, 5, or 6, it is enlarged by the spell. After using this magic item to cast the spell, it no longer works until you finish a long rest.
