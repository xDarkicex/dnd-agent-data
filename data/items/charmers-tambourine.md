# Charmer's Tambourine

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) You have advantage on Charisma (Performance) checks while playing this magic tambourine. In addition, you can cast the minor illusion cantrip at will. Enthralling Performance. This magic tambourine has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. You can expend its charges to cast the spells charm person (1 charge) or enthrall (2 charges) from it. The spell save DC is 13 or your spell save DC, whichever is higher.
