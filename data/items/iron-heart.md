# Iron Heart

**description**: Iron Heart Wondrous item, very rare This iron charm is the size and shape of a regular heart. When placed on the chest of a humanoid, it is absorbed into the creature’s body and replaces its real heart. The recipient immediately gains a +2 bonus to its Constitution ability score.
