# Belt of Humankind

**description**: Belt of Humankind Wondrous item, Rare (requires attunement) Crafted by ancient giant sages shortly after the rise of humankind, this belt was designed to help members of the elder races get closer to humans and understand their unusual ways. While wearing this belt you gain the following benefits: Two different ability scores of your choice increase by 1, to a maximum of 20. Once chosen, you gain the same two ability score increases whenever you attune to this item again. You have advantage on Charisma (Persuasion) checks made to interact with humans. In addition, while attuned to the belt, you have a 50 percent chance each day at dawn of waking up with mildly uncomfortable existential anxiety about the meaning of life and the ceaseless passage of time. If you aren’t a human, you gain the following additional benefits while wearing the belt: You gain proficiency in one skill of your choice. Once chosen, you gain the same skill whenever you attune to this item again. You gain one feat of your choice that does not have a racial prerequisite. Once chosen, you gain the same feat whenever you attune to this item again. You can speak, read, and write Common. Optional Rule: Fixed Features and Previous Owners At the gamemaster’s discretion, player characters may find the belt with its two ability scores, its skill, and its feat already chosen and fixed. This could happen if the belt retains some fragments from the personalities of previous owners, or if the belt’s abilities were originally modeled after a specific human.
