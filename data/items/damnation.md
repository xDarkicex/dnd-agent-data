# Damnation

**description**: Weapon (Any Sword), Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic sword was forged in the depths of the Abyss. The weapon deals an extra 1d8 Necrotic damage on a hit against Celestial and Fey creatures. The sword has 3 charges for the following properties and regains all expended charges daily at dawn: Amplify. As a reaction when a creature within 30 feet of you takes damage, you can expend 1 charge to have it take an extra 2d8 Necrotic damage. Bane. When you hit a creature with the sword, you can expend 1 charge to force it to make a DC 15 Charisma saving throw. On a failure, the creature is cursed until the end of its next turn. While cursed in this way, it can't regain hit points and whenever it makes an attack roll or saving throw, it must roll a d4 and subtract it from the result.
