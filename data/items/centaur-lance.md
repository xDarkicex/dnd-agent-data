# Centaur Lance

**description**: The massive spears used by some of the centaur tribes of the Plain are made from heavy wood and iron bands. Nearing 12 feet in length, creatures of Medium size and smaller may not effectively throw these weapons. The spears do twice the normal damage, require two hands, and provide advantage on attacks made against mounted opponents. Clever Winter Folk have devised combat maneuvers that involve two halflings set to one spear.
