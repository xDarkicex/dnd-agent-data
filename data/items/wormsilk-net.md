# Wormsilk net

**description**: A wormsilk net is a lighter, more durable variant of a standard net. A creature restrained by a wormsilk net must succeed on a DC 14 Strength check to free itself. In addition, a wormsilk net has AC 12 and 10 hit points.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Weapon
- Properties: Special, thrown

**publisher**: Kobold Press
