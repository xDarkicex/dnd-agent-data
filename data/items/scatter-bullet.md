# Scatter Bullet

**description**: Scatter Bullet (requires a weapon with the spread fire property) This bullet ignores the spread fire property and is fired like a standard bullet. On a hit, the target takes your normal weapon damage and the bullet explodes, sending shrapnel flying in a 15-foot cone behind the target. Each creature in the area must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw against your Firearm save DC or take damage equal to your weapon damage.
