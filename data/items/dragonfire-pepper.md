# Dragonfire Pepper

**description**: Potion, uncommon The intense spice of this stuffed pepper makes your mouth feel as if it is on fire. When you consume this food, you have resistance to fire damage for 1 hour. In addition, once before the duration ends, you can use a bonus action to exhale fire in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in the cone must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d4 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
