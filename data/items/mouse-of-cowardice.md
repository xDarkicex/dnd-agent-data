# Mouse of Cowardice

**description**: Wondrous item, rare This wind-up clockwork mouse terrifies even the largest beasts. When you speak its command word and set it rolling toward a Large or larger creature within 15 feet of you as an action, you cast the spell phantasmal killer (save DC 15) using the mouse. The mouse can’t be used this way again until the next dawn.
