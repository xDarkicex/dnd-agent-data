# Last Laugh (Rare)

**description**: Last Laugh Weapon (Mace), Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. You can utter a command word to cause the weapon to emit soft giggling sounds like that of a small child, which lasts until the end of your next turn. Last Laugh. When you attack a creature with an Intelligence score of 5 or more with this weapon and roll a 20 on the attack roll, the target begins laughing uncontrollably. They fall prone, are incapacitated, and are unable to stand up for 1 minute. The target can make a Wisdom saving throw at the end of each of their turns to end the effect. The DC is equal to 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Charisma modifier. Progression The Last Laugh progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
