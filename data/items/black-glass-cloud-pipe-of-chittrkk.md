# Black Glass Cloud Pipe of Chittr'k'k

**description**: A pipe of black glass etched with blasphemous demonic triumphs. This pipe is associated with a particular gaseous magical effect: cloud of daggers . A creature smoking the pipe can cast the associated spell as a bonus action 1/day.
