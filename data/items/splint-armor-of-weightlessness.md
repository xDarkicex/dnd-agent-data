# Splint Armor of Weightlessness

**description**: Splint Armor of Weightlessness Armor (Heavy), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This armor has 5 charges. While you wear it, you can use a bonus action to expend 1 or more charges to cast one of the following spells from the armor, targeting yourself: Jump (1 charge) or Levitate (2 charges). This armor regains 1d4 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. Splint Armor. This armor is made of narrow vertical strips of metal riveted to a backing of leather that is worn over cloth padding. Flexible chain mail protects the joints. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC : 17 Stealth : Disadvantage Weight : 60
