# Quilled Cuirass

**description**: Quilled Cuirass Armor (breastplate), rare Weight: 12 lbs. Armor Class: 14 + Dex modifier While wearing this armor, you gain a +1 bonus to AC. You can also use a bonus action to speak the armor’s command word and cause the armor to sprout spiky quills. While the armor is quilled, any creature within 5 feet of you that hits you with a melee attack must succeed on a DC 15 dexterity save or take 1d4 piercing damage. Additionally, any creature that attempts to grapple you takes 2d4 piercing damage. If the creature successfully grapples you, it takes an additional 2d4 piercing damage at the start of its turn for every turn that you are grappled. This damage also applies to any creature that you grapple. The armor’s quills last 1 hour and can be activated 3 times per day.
