# Flail of Tangling Vines

**description**: Flail of Tangling Vines Weapon (Flail), Rare (Requires Attunement) Green thorny vines sprout from the wooden handle of this flail. Each vine ends in a heavily spiked seed the size of a fist. Druids are proficient with the flail of tangling vines . You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you attack a creature with this weapon and roll a 20 on the attack roll, the target must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or be restrained by one of the flail’s thorny vines. A creature restrained by a vine can use its action to make a DC 15 Strength or Dexterity check to free itself.
