# Pulpit Shard

**description**: Pulpit Shard Spell catalyst, uncommon A piece of wood from a pulpit imbued with divine magic from years of priests using it to perform sermons. When you cast the healing word or mass healing word spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, the spell’s range increases to 150 feet.
