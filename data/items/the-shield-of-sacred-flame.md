# The Shield of Sacred Flame

**description**: The Shield of Sacred Flame Shield, rare (requires attunement) This shield has 3 charges, which you regain each day at dawn. When a spellcaster within 60 feet of you targets you with a spell or includes you in the area of a spell, you may expend a charge from the shield to cast counterspell as a reaction.
