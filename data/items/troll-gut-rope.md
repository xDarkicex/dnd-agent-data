# Troll Gut Rope

**description**: Troll Gut Rope Wondrous item, uncommon This 50-foot-long rope is woven from wet, rubbery troll intestines and is very pungent. You can command this rope to grow to any length up to 350 feet, and it reverts to its original length after 12 hours.
