# Chemist's Kit

**description**: Chemist's Kit Adventuring gear (artisan's tools) A chemist’s kit allows a character to test the properties of substances they encounter, create medicines, and even craft combustible materials. It often contains rare materials and substances, along with vials, a small stove, and stands to hold these. A chemist's kit is required to create poisons, antitoxins, fireworks, and flares.
