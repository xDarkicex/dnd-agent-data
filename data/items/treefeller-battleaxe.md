# Treefeller Battleaxe

**description**: Treefeller Battleaxe Melee weapon (martial, battleaxe), rare You gain a +1 bonus to the attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you deal damage to a plant creature with this weapon, you deal an additional 1d6 necrotic damage. While you are wielding this magic item, you have advantage on all Charisma (Intimidation) checks you make against plant creatures. When used to fell a mundane tree, it takes no more than three chops with this weapon, regardless of the size of the tree. Versatile. This weapon can be used with one or two hands. A damage value in parentheses appears with the property—the damage when the weapon is used with two hands to make a melee attack.
