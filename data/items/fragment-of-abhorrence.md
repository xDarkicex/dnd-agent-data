# Fragment of Abhorrence

**description**: Fragment of Abhorrence Benefit . Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with a weapon attack roll, you can force the target to move up to 10 feet away from you in a direction of your choice. A creature that can’t be frightened is immune to this effect. Drawback . If you start your turn frightened, you take 2d6 psychic damage.
