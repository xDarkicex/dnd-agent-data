# Necklace of Strangulation

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare A necklace of strangulation appears to be magical jewelry. When worn on the neck of a humanoid, the necklace immediately tightens, dealing 6 bludgeoning damage at the start of each of the creature's turns. It cannot be removed by any means short of a wish, and remains clasped around the victim’s throat even after their death. Only when the corpse has decayed to a dry skeleton (after approximately 1 month) does the necklace loosen, ready for another victim.
