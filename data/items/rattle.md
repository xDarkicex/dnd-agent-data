# Rattle

**description**: Weapon (Mace), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This silvered mace resembles a finely crafted, giant-sized baby rattle. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. In addition, as a bonus action, you can shake the weapon, creating a rattling sound which grants you a +5 bonus to saving throws against fear effects until the start of your next turn. Once used, you can’t use this property again until you finish a short rest. Encounter Ideas What’s in a Name? A storm giant’s name is elaborately engraved on the shaft of Rattle. The storm giant’s parents may be happy to see their baby’s keepsake returned. You Shoulda Put a Ring on It. In a great pit, a giant poisonous snake (a rattlesnake, to be exact) has wrapped itself around Rattle, seemingly finding its new love interest. Anyone who interrupts the lovebirds is attacked by multiple swarms of poisonous snakes in the area. Shake Your Booty. An alluring bard is traveling the region, and their favorite instrument is Rattle. They shake it with such immodest and passionate techniques, that they have become the talk of the realm!
