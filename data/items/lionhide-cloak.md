# Lionhide Cloak

**description**: Lionhide Cloak Wondrous item, Legendary (requires attunement) Myth has it that an ancient hero once faced a legendary lion that was impervious to the weapons of mortals. The hero wrestled the lion with his bare hands and emerged victorious, using the beast’s own claws to skin it and claim its hide. Now, the Lionhide Cloak is an enduring symbol of strength and bravery. Lion’s Endurance. You have resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage while you wear this cloak. Lion’s Strength. Your Strength score becomes 23 while you wear this cloak. If your Strength is already 23 or higher, the cloak increases your Strength score by 1 instead, up to a maximum of 30. Lion’s Bravery. You can’t be frightened while you wear this cloak. Hemera and Nyctos on Lionhide Cloak “But can you hide lions in it?” Nyctos asks, flapping his wings. “… No,” Hemera replies flatly. “It’s made of a legendary lion’s hide. The Lionhide Cloak does not ‘hide’ lions.” Nyctos nods his head excitedly. “Right! But at least you can cloak a lion with it! A lion cloaked in a Lionhide Cloak. Awesome!”
