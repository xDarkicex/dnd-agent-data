# Alces' Gift

**description**: Alces’ Gift Wondrous item, artifact (requires attunement) While wearing this circlet, you gain the following benefits: You can use an action to cast the awaken and conjure woodland beings spells once per day. You are immune to any spell or effect that would alter your form. All normal plants within 5 feet of you become thick and overgrown as if under the effects of the plant growth spell.
