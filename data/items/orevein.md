# Orevein

**description**: Orevein This mushroom grows underground in iron rich environments. When ground into a paste, it can serve as a minor healing agent. One cup of mushrooms yields one vial of paste. As an action a vial of the paste can be applied to a creature, which heals 1 hit point after 1 minute.
