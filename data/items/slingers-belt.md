# Slinger's Belt

**description**: Slinger’s Belt Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) The ostentatious gold buckle of this leather belt portrays a trio of hummingbirds in flight. While you are wearing this belt, you can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to any Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) checks you make. Furthermore, on your turn you can draw a weapon or object, or a combination of the two, into each of your hands without using an action.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
