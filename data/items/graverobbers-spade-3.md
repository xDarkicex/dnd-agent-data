# Graverobber's Spade +3

**description**: Weapon (shovel), very rare (+3) (requires attunement by a necromancer) You can use this magic weapon as a spellcasting focus for your necromancer spells. While holding this magic weapon, you gain a bonus to attack and damage rolls made with it, and you gain a bonus to spell attack rolls and the saving throw DCs of your necromancer spells. The bonus is determined by the weapon’s rarity. Additionally, you can cast the exhume spell at will without using a spell slot or material components.
