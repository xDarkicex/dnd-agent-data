# Sin: Wrath

**description**: Item modification, uncommon (optional or enhanced attunement) The Task. The fiend requests a minor task, such as delivering an aggressive message, intimidating someone, or starting a bar fight. OA or EA: Wrath. While the item is on your person, you can use a bonus action to enter a fiendish rage for 1 minute. When you activate this property and as a bonus action on subsequent turns for the duration, you gain additional movement equal to half your speed until the start of your next turn. For the duration, if you move at least 15 feet straight towards a creature and then immediately hit it with a melee attack, it must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. While this property is active, you lose all sense of self-preservation and are filled with bloodlust; any movement you make must be towards a hostile creature, and the only actions you can take on your turn are the Attack and Dash actions, or an action to break free of restraints or a grapple. Once this property of the item has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
