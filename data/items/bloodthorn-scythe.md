# Bloodthorn Scythe

**description**: Weapon (scythe), rare This scythe is made entirely of modified vines from the Bloodthorn Grove and its blade appears to be an enormous and sharpened red thorn. This weapon deals 2d4 piercing or slashing damage, has the heavy and two-handed properties, and deals an extra 2d4 damage on a critical hit (in addition to any bonus damage from the critical hit). Additionally, this weapon ignores damage resistances to piercing and slashing damage dealt by non-magical weapons, and creatures that are immune to such damage are treated as resistant instead.
