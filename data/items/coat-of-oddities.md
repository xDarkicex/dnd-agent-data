# Coat of Oddities

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) Coats of oddities are created with top-shelf Cyherestead aesthetics; these magical suit tops are mostly black with majestic, silver or golden vertical strips and decorations. One can wear the coat of oddity over armor. When you are attuned to it, you gain two of the following benefits of your choice while wearing the coat: You have a +1 bonus to AC. Your speed increases by 10 feet. You learn a cantrip of your choice from a spell list of your choice, until the attunement is broken. You gain a flying speed of 30 feet. You have advantage on Dexterity saving throws. You have advantage on Charisma saving throws. Your spells deal an extra 1d4 damage of its damage type. If a spell deals damage to multiple targets, you choose which creature takes this extra damage. Your spell save DC increases by 1. If your attunement ends, you lose the benefits granted by this coat. If your attunement ends and you attune to it again, you can change the benefits you had previously chosen.
