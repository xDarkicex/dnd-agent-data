# Staff of Illusion

**description**: Staff, very rare (requires attunement by a cleric, druid, sorcerer, warlock, or wizard) You have advantage on Intelligence checks to see through illusions while you hold this staff. The staff has 10 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more of its charges to cast one of the following spells from it, using your spell save DC: silent image (1 charge), blur (2 charges), fear (3 charges), phantasmal killer (4 charges), or seeming (5 charges). The staff regains 1d6 + 4 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the staff turns intangible and disappears from existence.
