# Orb of Storms

**description**: Orb of Storms Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) This small crystal sphere contains a boiling blue cloud. While holding the orb, you gain the following benefits: You have resistance to lightning damage. You can use an action to cast the control weather spell. Once used, this property of the orb can’t be used again until 15 days have passed. You can use an action to cast the storm of vengeance spell (save DC 15). Once used, this property of the orb can’t be used again until 15 days have passed. It should not surprise you to learn that this orb was originally designed by Otiluke, who was a connoisseur of such magical devices.
