# Phantom Limb

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement by a creature missing a limb) Component: undead (ghost) ethereal ichor This nebulous, floating cluster of green-blue light can take the form of a hand, arm, foot, leg, or similar absent appendage. Despite its spectral appearance, the phantom limb is quite solid—a fact that becomes painfully evident to unfortunate creatures on the receiving end of its strikes. You must have a missing limb to attune to this item. Once attuned to it, it takes the place of your missing limb, functions as a normal limb, and can’t be removed against your will. As a bonus action, you can cause the phantom limb to disappear, or manifest again. If you have multiple phantom limbs, they count as a single magic item with regard to the number of magic items to which you can attune. Mage Limb. As an action, you can cause the limb to detach from your body and manipulate it like the hand produced by the mage hand spell. The limb vanishes when you dismiss it (no action required) and reappears attached to you once more. Limber. The reach of unarmed strikes made with the phantom limb and the reach of attacks made with weapons that have light property held by the phantom limb increases by 5 feet.
