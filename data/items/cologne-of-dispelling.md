# Cologne of Dispelling

**description**: This intricate glass vial has an atomizer pump connected to its sprayer at its top. The vial contains 10 doses of cologne with a refreshing smell of the blue oceans. When a dose is sprayed on an object or creature within 5 feet, it functions as a dispel magic spell. The cologne of dispelling ends spells of 3rd level or lower on the target if it succeeds on the check against the spell’s DC using a modifier of +3. When all 10 doses have been expended, the vial loses its magic and functions as a simple spray bottle.
