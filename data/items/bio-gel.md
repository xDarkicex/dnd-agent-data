# Bio-Gel

**description**: Bio-Gel Utility (limited), uncommon This small container, 3 inches in diameter, contains 1d4 + 1 doses of a thick blue mixture that glows faintly. The container and its contents weigh 1/2 pound. As an action, one dose of the gel can be ingested or applied to the skin. The creature that receives it regains 2d8 + 2 hit points, ceases to be poisoned, and is cured of any disease.
