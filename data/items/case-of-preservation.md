# Case of Preservation

**description**: Wondrous item, common This item appears to be a standard map or scroll case fashioned of well-oiled leather. You can store up to ten rolled-up sheets of paper or five rolled-up sheets of parchment in this container. While ensconced in the case, the contents are protected from damage caused by fire, exposure to water, age, or vermin.
