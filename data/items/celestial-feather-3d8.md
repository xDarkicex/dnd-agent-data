# Celestial Feather (3d8)

**description**: Celestial Feather (3d8) Spell catalyst, rare (+3d8) These feathers only function as spell catalysts when gifted by certain winged celestials. When you cast the cure wounds spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, the healing provided by the spell increases by an amount determined by the rarity of the spell catalyst. In addition, the target has advantage on all ability checks, attack rolls, and saving throws it makes until the end of its next turn.
