# Shinjo Horsebow

**description**: Shinjo Horsebow Ranged weapon (martial, bow) Among the Unicorn Clan’s many adopted weapons is a small, sturdy bow used by the nomadic Ujik people who inhabit the Plains of Wind and Stone to the west. Called the Shinjo horsebow in Rokugan, this weapon is a compact bow designed to be fired from the back of a moving horse. Special: When you attack from horseback with this weapon, its damage profile is 1d8 piercing. Two-­Handed: This weapon requires two hands when you attack with it. Damage: 1d6 Damage Type: Piercing Properties: Ammunition, Special, Two-Handed Range: 100/400 Weight: 2
