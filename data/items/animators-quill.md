# Animator's Quill

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon As an action, you can animate a tiny creature that you have drawn with this quill. The creature manifests as a three-dimensional representation of the drawing made out of a solid, purple substance. The creature uses a rat ’s statistics but has 6 Intelligence, +4 to Dexterity (Stealth) checks, and can cast invisibility (targeting only itself) at will. It can’t speak but shares a telepathic bond with you and understands simple commands, which it follows as best as it can. As an action, you can see through the creature’s eyes. There is no limit to how far away from you the creature can move, but it can’t enter another plane of existence. After 1 hour it dissipates into thin air. Once used, this property can’t be used again until the next dawn.
