# Ring of Chains

**description**: Ring, very rare (requires attunement) Used by the authority figures in the cities of Tolia, this ring has the power to subdue or detain anyone. This ring has 3 charges, and it regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the ring, you can use an action to expend one of its charges to summon a chain in your hands. The summoned chain is 15 feet long and it has little spikes on each of its ends. You can use this summoned chain as a whip. Instead of using the chain as a whip, you can also choose to animate it. An animated chain can float in the air on its own. As a bonus action, you can issue a command to the chain (one of the four explained below), and it obeys your commands to the best of its abilities. If you don’t issue any commands to it, it doesn’t do anything. You can have up to three summoned chains at a time and issue a different command to each with the same bonus action. A chain can take one command per turn. The summoned chains remain for 1 minute, until they are dismissed (no action required by you), or until they are destroyed. A summoned chain has AC 12, 20 hit points, immunity to poison and psychic damage, and immunity to being poisoned. The commands you can issue to a chain are as follows: Attach. You command the chain to attach its ends to certain points. At least one of the points must be on a surface. If one of the points is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or become grappled by the chain. At the start of each of its turns, the target can repeat the saving throw to free itself, and can’t move more than 15 feet away from the chain’s other end on a fail. Attack. You command the chain to attack a creature of your choice within 60 feet of either of its ends. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take 1d8 piercing damage. Blaze. You command the chain to set itself on fire. The flaming chain deals 1d6 fire damage to anything it touches. You can also command the chain to extinguish the flame using this command. Otherwise, the chain stays aflame until it is dismissed. Detain. You can command the chain to wrap itself around a creature within 60 feet of you that you can see. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or be grappled by the chain. At the start of each of its turns, the target can make a DC 15 Strength saving throw, ending the grappled condition on itself on a success. If it is grappled by the chain for 3 consecutive turns, it becomes restrained.
