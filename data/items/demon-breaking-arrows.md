# Demon-Breaking Arrows

**description**: Demon-Breaking Arrows Ammunition These consecrated arrows were designed by the Kaito family of the Phoenix Clan, and can deal terrible harm to supernatural creatures. When a demon-breaking arrow hits an undead or infernal creature, it must make a DC 15 Charisma saving throw. On failure, it suffers 3d6 radiant damage.
