# Supplicant’s Jaw

**description**: Wondrous item, rare Supplicant’s Jaw is found with 1d10 + 1d6 teeth socketed into the jawbone. The number of teeth determines the total number of charges that can be expended. Once you have used all the teeth, this item loses its magical properties and becomes a mundane jaw You can pull a tooth as an action to expend a charge to cast one of the following spells: silence , speak with dead , and wall of bone* .
