# Winter Coat

**description**: Potion, uncommon This syrupy mead is deep brown in color and smells of honeyed ham. When you drink this mead, you sprout a thick, shaggy coat of fur. The fur lasts for 8 hours, after which time it falls off harmlessly. This fur mimics the effects of cold weather clothing, allowing you to tolerate temperatures as low as -50 degrees Fahrenheit without any additional protection.
