# Potion of Psychic Hardening

**description**: Potion, uncommon This potion swirls glittering silver and purple. When you consume this potion, you gain resistance to psychic damage, and have advantage on saving throws against magically-induced pain (such as the thousand needles *, agonizing eternity *, wrack *, or vile incantation * spells) for one minute.
