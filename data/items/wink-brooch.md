# Wink Brooch

**description**: Wink Brooch Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) A ruby engraved with a winking face is set at the head of this silver pin. While wearing the pin, you gain proficiency in the Persuasion skill, and you double your proficiency bonus on checks made with that skill.
