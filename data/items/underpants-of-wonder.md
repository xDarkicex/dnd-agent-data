# Underpants of Wonder

**description**: Underpants of Wonder Wondrous Item, rare (requires attunement) Weight: ¼ lb. The bard / spy Jamis Bundino wore magic underwear to aid their top secret missions. While wearing the underpants, you gain the following benefits: You have advantage on Charisma (Persuasion) checks. You can cast Alter Self once per day without expending a spell slot. You can cast Enlarge / Reduce once per day without expending a spell slot. Gender Swap. As an action, you can transform to another gender, though none of your statistics change. This transformation lasts until you decide to reverse it.
