# Bag of Shared Holding

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon This bag has an interior space considerably larger than its outside dimensions, roughly 1 foot in diameter at the mouth and 2 feet deep. The bag can hold up to 250 pounds, not exceeding a volume of 32 cubic feet. The bag weighs 5 pounds, regardless of its contents. It functions as a bag of holding , except as noted below. Shared Holding. Two creatures each holding one end of the drawstring at the bag’s neck can use an action to tie it shut. As they do so, their names (the names most commonly used to refer to them) appear on the side of the bag, etched in gold thread and the bag can be reopened only by these two creatures pulling on its string together. Destroying the bag causes its contents to be scattered in the Astral Plane. Once reopened, the creatures’ names disappear from the bag and it can be used freely until its string is tied again this way.
