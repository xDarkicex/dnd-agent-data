# Plate Armor

**description**: Plate Armor Armor (heavy) Plate consists of shaped, interlocking metal plates to cover the entire body. A suit of plate includes gauntlets, heavy leather boots, a visored helmet, and thick layers of padding underneath the armor. Buckles and straps distribute the weight over the body. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC: 18 Stealth: Disadvantage Weight: 65
