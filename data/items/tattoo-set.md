# Tattoo Set

**description**: Tattoo Set Adventuring gear (artisan's tools) Tattoo needles are ordinarily part of a kit that includes needles, various pigments, and other tools used by tattoo artists in their work. Widespread not only in the Dragon Clan, tattoos are also prevalent among the Empire’s numerous criminal cartels, which use them both to identify members of specific cartels and to record individuals’ criminal exploits.
