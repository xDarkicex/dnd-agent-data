# Nullifying Net

**description**: Wondrous Item (Rare) This net is made from a mesh of thread-like wires and embedded with crystal nodes that disrupt energy. The net can't be destroyed by dealing damage to it. Disrupting Net At the start of each of its turns, a creature Restrained by the net takes 1d4 Force damage and must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution save. On a failure, it can't cast spells and all spells and magical effects on the creature, as well as the magic properties of all non-Artifact magic items it is wearing or carrying, are suppressed until the start of its next turn.
