# Ring of Alarm

**description**: Ring, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) While wearing this magic ring, you can use an action to speak its command word and specify a target creature (e.g., “Captain Yannis”) and a condition or set of conditions that involve you (e.g., “If I take damage or shout ‘Help’”). If at least one of the conditions is met, the ring sends a mental alarm to the target creature if it is on the same plane of existence, awakening it if it is sleeping. Once used, this property can’t be used again until the next dawn.
