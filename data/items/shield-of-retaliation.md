# Shield of Retaliation

**description**: Armour (shield), uncommon Component: fiend heart Legend says that when the war twixt fiends and celestials ravaged the Material Plane, a great infernal general had these shields forged for his captains. Through them he observed the battlefield, and with them he smote the angels that dared challenge his armies. Whether or not the fiendish general still watches through these shields, or even still lives, is a question yet to be answered. Searing Vengeance. As a reaction when a creature within 5 feet of you hits you with an attack, you can pull a trigger on the shield’s handle, causing it to breathe a plume of flame at the creature. The creature must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 2d6 fire damage on a failure, or half as much damage on a success. After you use this property, roll a d6. On a 1-2, the shield runs out of fuel and this property can’t be used again until you feed the shield 1 gp of coal or other fuel and spend 1 hour tending to the flame (which can be done during a short or long rest), as the shield burns and chews its way through your offering.
