# Oil of Timelessness

**description**: Oil of Timelessness Potion, very rare This silver-colored oil has the consistency of mercury, and is often held in small metal vials shaped like an hourglass. Any organic object (wood, a body, leather) coated with this oil resists the passage of time. For every year that passes, the object only ages one day. There is enough oil in a vial to coat the equivalent of a Medium-sized humanoid.
