# Lich's Die

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare This magic, eight-sided die is crafted from a lich's knuckle-bone and imbued with arcane glyphs. As a bonus action, you can roll a d8 and regain one expended spell slot of a level equal to or lower than the result. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
