# Bloodvayne (Common)

**description**: Bloodvayne Weapon (Longsword), Common (Requires Attunement) Blood Sacrifice. When you attune to Bloodvayne , you sacrifice one of your Hit Dice to the sword. This sacrificed Hit Die cannot be regained and is lost permanently. You can utter a command word to cause Bloodvayne to emit the metallic scent of freshly spilled blood for 1 minute. This scent is noticeable to all creatures within 30 feet of you who can smell. Progression Bloodvayne progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
