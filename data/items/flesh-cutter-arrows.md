# Flesh-Cutter Arrows

**description**: Flesh-Cutter Arrows Ammunition Flesh-cutter arrows are designed with curved, slicing heads that split open flesh and muscle, causing wounds that heal much more slowly. Flesh-cutter arrows deal slashing damage instead of piercing damage, and when you inflict a critical strike with a flesh-cutter arrow, the creature suffers the bleeding condition (1d4 piercing damage at the start of each of its turns, removed after it regains HP) for 1 minute.
