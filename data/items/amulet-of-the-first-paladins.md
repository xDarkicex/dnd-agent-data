# Amulet of the First Paladins

**description**: Amulet of the First Paladins Wondrous Item, Legendary (requires attunement) Worn by the ancient heroes who first rose up against the warring gods of Naam, these amulets were crafted to protect against divine influence. Blessed by Dysangelist Lethimus himself and handed down from hero to hero across the ages, nine of these amulets are believed to exist. Protection from Gods. While attuned to this amulet, you are always under the effects of the nondetection and the protection from evil and good spells. Freethinking. You are immune to magic that allows other creatures to read your thoughts, determine whether you are lying, know your alignment, or know your creature type. Creatures can telepathically communicate with you only if you allow it.
