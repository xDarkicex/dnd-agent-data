# Corsair's Compass

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) The needle of this compass always points to the most valuable object that is further than 100 feet from it and closer than 10 miles. Oceanic Magic. The compass has 7 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. As an action, you can cast one of the following spells from the compass, expending 1 charge per level of the spell and requiring no material components (spell save DC 15): Call Lightning , Fog Cloud , Gust of Wind , or Sleet Storm . Camouflage. As an action, you can cause any vessel (such as a ship or wagon) no larger than 100 feet on any side that you are traveling on or in to become Invisible for 1 hour or until you end the effect as a bonus action. You and any other objects or creatures traveling on or in the vessel become Invisible with it. The effect ends for all affected creatures and objects if any creature or object affected by it makes an attack, casts a spell, or causes harm to another creature or object. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
