# Mounted

**description**: Journeyman weapon property Components: Masterwork martial or exotic ranged weapon with the Heavy property This weapon gains the Mounted property and its damage die increases by two steps. Mounted. This weapon is normally used while attached to a tripod, vehicle, or other bracing mount. You can mount or unmount this weapon as an action. While it is mounted, it can’t be moved. It can only be used to make an attack while unmounted if held by a Medium or larger creature with a Strength score of at least 15.
