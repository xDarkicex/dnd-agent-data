# Saint Palio's Bridles

**description**: Relic, rare Any mount equipped with these bridles loses its possible shoddiness and its base speed increases by 10 feet. Any creature riding the mount has advantage on any check made to stay in the saddle. If you use these bridles during a Poorman's Carousel game, you have advantage on all ability checks.
