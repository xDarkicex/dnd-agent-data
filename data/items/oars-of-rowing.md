# Oars of Rowing

**description**: Oars of Rowing Wondrous item, uncommon These items look like regular boat oars and are usually found in pairs. When you attach these oars to the oarlocks of a boat, you can speak a command word and cause the oars to row the boat on their own at a speed of 2 miles per hour for up to 12 hours. Speaking the command word again causes the oars to cease rowing. Once used, this property of the oars can’t be used again until the next dawn.
