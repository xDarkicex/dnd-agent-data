# Mawling Maul Warhammer (rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (any hammer), uncommon Burning with ravenous hunger and unquenchable hanger, this mimic-infused weapon struggles relentlessly against the sturdy iron chains that bind it. Loosening the chains stimulates the weapon’s digestive juices which, if not quenched, can have caustic consequences for its wielder. Mimic Unleashed. As a bonus action while holding the hammer, you can loosen the chains restraining the mimic, leaving it free to mercilessly maul your foes. While the chains are loose, the hammer bites targets that it hits, dealing an extra 1d4 piercing damage. If you end your turn holding the hammer while the chains are loose, and you didn’t hit a creature with an attack using the hammer that turn, the hammer’s excess digestive juices pour down the handle and you take 1d4 acid damage. The chains tighten on their own if the hammer isn’t held for 1 minute or if you use a bonus action to tighten the chains while holding the weapon. Rare variant: Increase the extra piercing damage to 1d6 and the acid damage you take to 1d6 . The hammer has the Bilious Blow property. Bilious Blow. When you roll a 20 on an attack roll made with this hammer, the mimic spews bile all over the target, covering it completely. A creature covered in bile takes 1d4 acid damage at the start of each of its turns until it or another creature uses its action to scrape or wash the bile off. Increase this acid damage to 2d4 if the chains were loose when the hammer hit.
