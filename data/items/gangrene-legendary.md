# Gangrene (Legendary)

**description**: Gangrene Weapon (Mace), Legendary (Requires Attunement) You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. As an action, you can touch this weapon to a portion of food no more than 1 cubic foot in volume, instantaneously rotting it. Once used, you can’t use this property again until you finish a long rest. Rot. When you hit a creature with an attack using this weapon, its runes pulse a sickly green and the target takes an extra 2d8 necrotic damage. Fester. When you hit a creature with an attack using this weapon, the target must succeed on a DC 18 Constitution saving throw or be unable to regain hit points until lesser restoration or similar magic is cast on them. In addition, if the target is a flesh-and-blood creature and Fester’s damage reduces them to 0 hit points, the remainder of their flesh turns black, falls off their bones, and they die immediately.
