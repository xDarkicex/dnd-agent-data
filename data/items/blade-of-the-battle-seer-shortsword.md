# Blade of the Battle Seer Shortsword

**description**: Weapon (Shortsword), Rare (Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster) This sword shifts and phases in and out in the hands of its wielder. These rare blades offer diviners a powerful weapon on the front lines of combat. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magical weapon. Fate Slice. If you know at least one spell from the divination school, whenever you hit with an attack using this weapon, you may use your reaction to expend a spell slot. If you do, that creature has disadvantage on its attack rolls until the end of its next turn.
