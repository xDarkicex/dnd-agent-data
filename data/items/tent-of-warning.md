# Tent of Warning

**description**: Tent of Warning Wondrous item, uncommon This appears to be an ordinary canvas two-person tent. When you pitch the tent and speak the command word, the tent produces the sound of a hand bell for 10 seconds whenever a small or larger creature comes within 100 feet of the tent. When you speak the command word, you can designate creatures that won't set off the alarm. Packing up the tent cancels the effect.
