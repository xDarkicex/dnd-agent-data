# Helmet of the Harbinger

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic helmet was forged from dull steel and is worn from centuries of use. While wearing the helmet, you have resistance to Poison damage and advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the Poisoned condition on yourself. The helmet has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. As a reaction, you can expend 1 charge to cause one of the following effects: Decay. When a creature you can see within 30 feet regains hit points, it regains only half as many hit points. Despair. When a creature you can see within 30 feet rolls an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can have it make the roll with disadvantage. Doom. When you or another creature you can see within 30 feet makes an attack, you can have it make the attack roll with advantage. If both rolls of the d20 would be enough to hit the target, the attack is a critical hit.
