# Pupula Prism

**description**: Pupula Prism Spell catalyst, common This spherical translucent prism contains an opaque black cone within. When turned in the light, a rainbow of colors pour out from around the wide end of the cone. When you cast the color spray spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, choose any number of creatures within the spell’s cone. The chosen creatures are immune to the blinding effect of the spell and their hit points are ignored when determining which creatures are affected by the spell.
