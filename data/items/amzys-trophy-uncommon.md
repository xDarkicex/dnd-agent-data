# Amzy's Trophy (Uncommon)

**description**: Amzy's Trophy Weapon (Dagger), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) Your hit point maximum increases by 5 while you are attuned to this weapon. When you score a critical hit with this weapon, the attack deals an extra 1d8 piercing damage. Additionally, on a critical hit, you can activate a Celestial Shield option. Celestial Shield (Self). You gain a +2 bonus to AC until the end of your next turn. Progression Amzy’s Trophy progresses any time it reduces an evil-aligned creature with a CR equal to or greater than the attuned character’s level to 0 hit points.
