# The Golden Hart's Crown (Very Rare)

**description**: The Golden Hart's Crown Weapon (Longbow), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When interacting with Beasts native to forests, you make any associated Wisdom (Animal Handling) checks with advantage. You can speak a command word to create the illusion of a glorious set of golden antlers growing from your head. The illusion lasts 1 minute or until you end it (no action required). Call the Hart. As a bonus action, you summon the spirit of the Golden Hart, which uses the Golden Hart Spirit stat block. This stat block uses your proficiency bonus (PB) in several places, and the Golden Hart Spirit’s Ram attack deals an extra 1d6 radiant damage. The creature disappears after 1 minute or when it drops to 0 hit points. The creature is an ally to you and your companions. In combat, the creature shares your initiative count, but it takes its turn immediately after yours. It obeys your verbal commands (no action required by you). If you don’t issue any, it takes the Dodge action and uses its move to avoid danger. Once used, this property can be used three times between long rests. Progression The Golden Hart’s Crown progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, so does the weapon’s rarity.
