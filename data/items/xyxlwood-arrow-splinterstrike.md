# Xyxlwood Arrow Splinterstrike

**description**: Ammunition (arrow), rarity varies Component: bundle of plant roots The incredible durability of xyxlwood means that a single arrow can see many battlefields and be wielded by multiple generations of a sylvan family. Xyxlwood arrows take nature-based enchantments exceedingly well and have been crafted with a plethora of effects. After an effect triggers, the arrow is destroyed. The following types of xyxlwood arrows can be found: Splinterstrike; Uncommon. On a hit, this magic arrow deals the weapon’s normal damage and then erupts in a storm of splinters and thorns. The target and each creature within 5 feet of it must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 1d10 piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Bramblestrike; Rare. On a hit, this magic arrow deals the weapon’s normal damage and the target must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or become restrained by magical vines until the end of its next turn. A Large or larger creature has advantage on this saving throw. While restrained by this arrow, the target takes 1d6 piercing damage at the start of its turn. The restrained creature, or a creature within 5 feet of it, can use its action to make a DC 15 Strength check, freeing it on a success. Tanglestrike; Very rare. If the arrow hits an object or solid surface, grasping weeds and thorny vines sprout on the surface in a 5-foot-radius sphere centred on the arrow. The area becomes difficult terrain for 1 minute and each creature within the area must succeed on a DC 16 Strength saving throw or become restrained by the entangling plants for 1 minute. The restrained creature, or a creature within 5 feet of it, can use its action to make a DC 16 Strength check, freeing it on a success.
