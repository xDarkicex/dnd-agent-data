# Pearl of Diving

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This white pearl shines iridescently in almost any light. While underwater and grasping the pearl, you have resistance to cold damage and to bludgeoning damage from nonmagical attacks.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: rare
- Requires Attunement: requires attunement

**publisher**: Kobold Press
