# Resistance Armor

**description**: Resistance Armor Armor (any), varies (requires affinity) This armor provides the wearer with protection against a specific type of damage. The damage type is chosen by the GM or determined randomly. You can use an action to gain resistance to the chosen damage type for 10 minutes. You can’t use this feature again until you finish a short or long rest. d10 Damage Type d10 Damage Type 1 Acid 6 Necrotic 2 Cold 7 Poison 3 Fire 8 Psychic 4 Force 9 Radiant 5 Lightning 10 Thunder Exceptional. You have resistance to the chosen damage type. This feature replaces the one provided at standard grade. Superior. When the armor reaches this grade, it gains the following properties. You have a +1 bonus to AC while wearing this armor. If an attack that deals the chosen damage type reduces you to 0 hit points, you are instead reduced to 1 hit point. Optimum. When the armor reaches its optimum grade, it gains the following properties. The bonus to AC increases to +2. The armor gains an additional chosen damage type, determined using the table above. If determined randomly, you may reroll if the result is the damage type the armor possesses already.
