# Phoenix Feather

**description**: Phoenix Feather Wondrous Item, rare Weight: none Once per day, you can use an action and this red hot feather to take on a fire form for 1 minute. You and any object you are wearing or carrying are immune to the flames. Fire Form. Add 4 (1d8) fire damage to any successful melee attack you make. In addition, any creature that touches you or hits you with a melee attack while within 5 feet of you takes 4 (1d8) fire damage. With a touch, you can also ignite flammable objects that aren’t being worn or carried (no action required). The feather can be used five times. After the fifth time, it crumbles into dust.
