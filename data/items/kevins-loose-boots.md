# Kevin's Loose Boots

**description**: Kevin's Loose Boots Wondrous item Price: 500 gp These boots have stripes down the sides to help them go faster! A character who puts on these boots cannot remove them for one week. After that time they can be removed. These boots grant the wearer double their normal Movement Speed and Advantage on any Dexterity checks that involve dodging out of the way. However, the boots also require the wearer to dance whenever they hear music. The wearer of these boots cannot resist the dancing, and the boots may require the person to dance even while deafened or unconscious. While dancing, the wearer of the boots has Disadvantage on attacks, but Advantage on Charisma checks.
