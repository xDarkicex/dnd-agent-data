# Demonbane Armour Half Plate

**description**: Armour (breastplate, half plate, or plate), rare Component: fiend (demon) bone Hewn from a deformed demon’s skull, this armour allows its bearer to rival the resistances of fiends. Energy is drawn in through its mouth and channelled to the eyes, where it is stored until unleashed in an elemental rebuke. This armour has 4 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. Consume Energy. When you take cold, fire, lightning, or poison damage, you can use your reaction to speak the armour’s first command word and expend 1 charge. The mouth opens, consuming some of the incoming energy. You gain resistance to the triggering damage and one of the armour’s eyes lights up with the stored energy. The armour has two eyes, and an eye can’t store more energy if it already is storing some. Unleash Energy. When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack, you can utter the armour’s second command word, unleashing the energy stored in all of the armour’s eyes. For each eye that was storing energy, the creature takes 1d8 damage of the stored type, after which the stored energy disappears.
