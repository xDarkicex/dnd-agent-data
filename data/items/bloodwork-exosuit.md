# Bloodwork Exosuit

**description**: Armor (plate), rarity varies (requires attunement by a sorcerer) A bloodwork exosuit is an exosuit armor that is designed exclusively for the use of the most respected commanders in Cyherested. The suit operates with a big blood tank filled with your own blood. When you first attune to the bloodwork exosuit, your hit point maximum decreases by an amount equal to your level. This reduction lasts until you are no longer attuned to the item. While attuned to the suit, you gain the following benefits, depending on the item’s rarity: Very Rare. You have +1 bonus to AC while wearing this armor, and your Strength score becomes 19 if it is not higher than that already. While wearing the exosuit, you have darkvision out to a range of 60 feet, you only need to breathe once per 10 minutes, and you have advantage on saving throws against inhaled poisons. Roll a d6 (1-2 bludgeoning, 3-4 piercing, 5-6 slashing). The suit was designed to prevent the rolled damage type. Whenever you are subjected to the rolled damage type, reduce the damage you take by 3. You can use this armor as an arcane focus for your sorcerer spells. When you cast a sorcerer spell, you can use your reaction to cast the magic missile spell. Legendary. You have a +2 bonus to AC while wearing this armor and your Strength score becomes 21 if it is not higher than that already. You have darkvision out to a range of 90 feet, you only need to breathe once per 10 minutes, and you have advantage on saving throws against inhaled poisons. Whenever you take bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing damage, reduce the damage by 3. You can use this armor as an arcane focus for your sorcerer spells. When you cast a sorcerer spell, you can use your reaction to cast the magic missile spell, but you can choose to deal radiant damage instead of force damage. Artifact . You have a +3 bonus to AC while wearing this armor and your Strength score becomes 23 if it is not higher than that already. You have truesight out to a range of 60 feet, you only need to breathe once per 10 minutes, and you have advantage on saving throws against inhaled poisons. You have resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage. You can use this armor as an arcane focus for your sorcerer spells. As a bonus action, you can turn on an antimagic spotlight, creating a 15-foot cone of antimagic and bright light originating from you for 1 minute. Once you use this feature, you must finish a long rest to use it again.
