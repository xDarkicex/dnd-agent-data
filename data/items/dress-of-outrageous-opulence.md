# Dress of Outrageous Opulence

**description**: Dress of Outrageous Opulence Wondrous Item, Very Rare (requires attunement) Sewn from the finest materials and imbued with powerful enchantments for a haughty princess who absolutely wanted to impress, this dress has since found its way in the possession of various extravagant heroes across the centuries who don’t shy away from high fashion. Made to Last. This dress never gets dirty and magically mends itself to counteract daily wear and tear. Light as a Feather. The Dress of Outrageous Opulence is light as a feather and magically airy. When you fall while wearing this dress, you descend 60 feet per round and take no damage from falling. Additionally, you can use a bonus action to make it billow dramatically. Alluring Attractiveness. Your Charisma score is 21 while you wear this dress. If your Charisma is already 21 or higher, the dress increases your Charisma score by 1 instead, up to a maximum of 30.
