# Gruesome Weapon

**description**: Weapon (Any), Common This rusted magic weapon bears the scars of countless battles. When you roll a 20 on your attack roll against a creature with the weapon, the target becomes cursed until the end of your next turn. While cursed in this way, the creature takes an extra 1d8 damage the first time each turn it takes damage. Cursed. When you roll a 1 on your attack roll using this weapon, you become inflicted with the weapon's curse until the end of your next turn.
