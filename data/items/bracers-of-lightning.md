# Bracers of Lightning

**description**: Bracers of Lightning Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) These bracers are made of rectangular quartz crystals held together by copper wire. While wearing them, any time you hit a creature with a melee attack, you inflict an extra 1d8 lightning damage to the target.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: rare
- Requires Attunement: requires attunement
