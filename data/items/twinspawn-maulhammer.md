# Twinspawn Maulhammer

**description**: Weapon (maul), rare (requires attunement) Component: aberration bone Made of the fossilised remains of a Deep One’s spawn, the holes within this double-headed maul sparkle with illusory motes of light. As a bonus action while holding this weapon, you can separate this maul into two warhammers, or convert them back to a maul. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with either form of this magic weapon. Duplicate. When you separate the warhammers, or as a bonus action when they’re already separated, you can cause one of the warhammers to float in the grasp of an illusory double of yourself for 1 minute. When you create the illusion, and as bonus action on each of your turns, you can move the duplicate and hammer up to 20 feet to a point you can see and make a melee weapon attack against a target within 5 feet of the hammer. Use your Charisma modifier for attack and damage rolls made in this way. The duplicate can’t be harmed, though it can be dispelled by a dispel magic spell or similar magic. A creature that succeeds on a DC 15 Intelligence (Investigation) check or that physically interacts with the duplicate perceives that it is illusory and that the weapon is floating. Once this property of the weapon has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
