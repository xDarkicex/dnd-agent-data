# Navigator's Eye

**description**: Wondrous Item, Common (Requires Attunement) This magic eyepatch has a compass etched on its surface. While wearing it, you have advantage on ability checks you make to determine your location or avoid getting lost. Moreover, you can see normally in areas that are lightly obscured, ignoring the effects of dim light, patchy fog, moderate foliage, and similar visual impediments.
