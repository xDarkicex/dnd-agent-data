# Thorium Impaler

**description**: Weapon (Spear or Trident), Rare (Requires Attunement) This weapon's tip is reinforced with thorium encased in a lead sheath that retracts when used to make an attack. It deals an extra 1d6 Radiant damage on a hit. Radiation Ward You have resistance to Radiant damage and Advantage on saves against Radiation Sickness and Acute Radiation Effects . Radioactive Burst The weapon has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. When you hit a creature with the weapon, you can expend 1 charge to release a burst of radiation. The target and each creature within 10 feet of it (including yourself) must make a DC 15 Constitution save. On a failure, the creature suffers an Acute Radiation Effect until the start of your next turn. On a failure by 5 or more, it also suffers Radiation Sickness .
