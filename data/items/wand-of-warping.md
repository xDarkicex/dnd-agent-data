# Wand of Warping

**description**: Wand of Warping Wand (arcane focus) , very rare (requires attunement) The wand has 7 charges you can expend to cast spells from it (listed in the Wand of Warping table). A spell cast from the wand has a spell attack bonus of +7 and a spell save DC of 15. The wand regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the wand’s last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, unless you are attuned to a ring of weaving , the wand crumbles into ashes and is destroyed.
