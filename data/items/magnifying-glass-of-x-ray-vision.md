# Magnifying Glass of X-Ray Vision

**description**: Magnifying Glass of X-ray Vision Wondrous item, very rare While holding this magnifying glass, you can use an action to speak its command word. When you do so, you can see into and through solid matter for 1 minute. This vision has a radius of 30 feet. To you, solid Objects within that radius appear transparent and don’t prevent light from passing through them. The vision is blocked by more than 1 foot of stone, 1 inch of common metal, 3 feet of wood or dirt, or any thin sheet of lead. You must complete a long rest before using this item again.
