# Hidden Armament

**description**: Weapon (any melee weapon that isn’t two-handed), uncommon (requires attunement) While holding this magic weapon, you can use an action to transform it into a tattoo on the palm of your hand. You can use a bonus action to transform the tattoo back into a weapon.
