# The Cervelliere

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon This simple steel skullcap is adorned with plain steel rivets. While wearing this helm, your physical form changes to that of a gnome or svirfneblin. You can take a bonus action to revert to your normal form for a number of minutes equal to your Constitution score. You can use this ability again after a short or long rest.
