# Snarl

**description**: Snarl Weapon (club), very rare This magic ironwood club has a dragon’s face carved upon it. It is a +1 weapon and on a successful hit, delivers an additional 2 (1d4) necrotic damage. However, if a natural 1 is rolled when making an attack with this weapon, it bites its wielder for 3 necrotic damage instead.
