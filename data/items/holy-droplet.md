# Holy Droplet

**description**: Holy Droplet Spell catalyst, common This small drop of holy water can be used as a seed by spellcasters to create more. When you cast the create or destroy water spell and choose its Create Water effect, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, all of the created water is holy water .
