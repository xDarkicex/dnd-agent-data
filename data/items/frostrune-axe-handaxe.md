# Frostrune Axe Handaxe

**description**: Weapon (any axe), uncommon (enhanced attunement) Component: pouch of dragon (white or silver) scales The Frozen Forge is the site of frost giants’ cold forging secrets, a manufactory in the heart of a frigid volcano. As they hammer these ingots, they sing the Song of Steel, an ancient melody that prevents the cold, brittle metal from shearing beneath their mighty blows. By pressing the rune-engraved metal into one’s skin, one creates a nigh-unbreakable bond with the Frozen Forge. This axe has 4 charges and regains 1d4 expended charges after it spends 8 continuous hours in snow, ice, or cold water. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge to cause cold mist to emanate from the axe for 10 minutes. It glows a soft blue, shedding bright light in a 10-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet, and deals an extra 1d4 cold damage on a hit. Enhanced Attunement: Brand. Over the course of an hour, you can undergo a branding ritual to become bonded to the axe, gaining access to the Razorice property. In addition, when the weapon regains charges, it regains all expended charges. Once you have undergone this branding ritual, you can’t willingly end your attunement to the axe until another creature willingly undertakes the branding ritual, at which point it becomes attuned to the axe instead. EA: Razorice. As an action, you can expend 2 charges to cast the spike growth spell (save DC 13), creating spikes of razor-sharp icicles. Rare variant: Increase the charges to 5, the recharge to 1d4 + 1, and the cold damage to 1d6. When you use the axe to cast spike growth , increase the DC to 15 and the damage to 3d4 for every 5 feet travelled.
