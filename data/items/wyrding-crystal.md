# Wyrding Crystal

**description**: Wyrding Crystal Wondrous Item, rare (requires attunement by a spellcaster) Weight: ¼ lb. This glowing crystal holds the power of ancient wyrding magic. The crystal starts with 10 charges, and it regains 1d6 expended charges daily at dawn. You can use an action to speak a powerful magic wyrd, expending a number of charges based on the chosen wyrd. Binding Wyrd (1 charge). One creature you can see within 90 ft. must succeed a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be affected by the Hold Monster spell. Confusing Wyrd (1 charge). One creature you can see within 90 ft. must succeed a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be affected by the Confusion spell. Ice Wyrd (3 charges). You cast the Cone of Cold spell at 6th level, dealing 9d8 cold damage. Lightning Wyrd (3 charges). You cast the Lightning Bolt spell at 6th level, dealing 11d6 lightning damage. Ethereal Wyrd (5 charges). You cast the Etherealness spell on yourself.
