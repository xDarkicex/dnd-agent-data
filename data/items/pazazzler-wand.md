# Pazazzler Wand

**description**: Pazazzler Wand Wondrous item Price: 4,000 gp This wand has made its way around the fashion industry in Toverus for years before it was finally banned. Created by the fabled wizard Razzmatazz, this curious object consists of a stuffed toad which has been mounted on a short wand and encrusted with glitter, non-precious gemstones, and jingle bells. Anything that this wand touches becomes instantly pazazzled, meaning that it is suddenly more sparkly and better looking than it was before. Pazazzled items seem like they have a higher value, and might sell for more, or can make someone seem more persuasive and fancy if used on an item that they are wearing. Any Charisma checks made while wearing a pazazzled item are made with Advantage, and any checks made to negotiate the sale of a pazazzled item are made with Advantage. The effect lasts for up to 1 hour.
