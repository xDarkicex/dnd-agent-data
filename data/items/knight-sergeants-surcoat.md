# Knight-Sergeant's Surcoat

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) Fashioned from durable canvas and fine silk, this long, padded knight’s tabard bears the colors and heraldry of a long-forgotten order of knights. While wearing this surcoat, you have advantage on Wisdom (Animal Handling) checks when interacting with horses, and you have advantage on Charisma (Persuasion) checks made to interact with humanoids of a higher social rank than you, such as an army’s general or a region’s monarch. Knight’s Livery. While attuned to and wearing both the knight-sergeant’s surcoat and the argent mantle , your Charisma score is 20. This has no effect on you if your Charisma is already 20 or higher.
