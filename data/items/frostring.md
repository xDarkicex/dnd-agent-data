# Frostring

**description**: Frostring Ring, rare (requires attunement) This is a crystalline piece from the Frostheart that has been formed into a crude giant-sized ring. On Large or smaller creatures, the ring must be worn as a bracelet (but is still otherwise considered a ring). This ring has a maximum of 4 charges, and it regains 1d4 expended charges daily, at nightfall. While wearing the ring, the wearer can use an action to expend 2 charges to cast cone of cold or 4 charges to cast wall of ice. When wall of ice is cast this way, it does not require concentration and the ice wall remains until the temperature is warm enough to melt it. Additionally, anyone, who starts their turn within 5 feet of the wearer (including the wearer) takes 1 point of cold damage.
