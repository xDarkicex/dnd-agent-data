# Kell

**description**: Kell Weapon (scythe), legendary (requires attunement) This magical weapon was once wielded by Thonir himself and was used by him to slay the last Yoten, Boða. The origins of this scythe are not fully known, though legends suggest Mirgal crafted it and gifted it to Aldyhn, who in turn gave it to her son. The scythe weighs 2 pounds and is a two-handed weapon. It has a range of 10 feet and does 1d10 slashing damage when it hits. Additionally, it has magical properties. Attacks made with this scythe have a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls. When a critical hit is made while using this scythe, the critical hit damage is the maximum possible and if the target fails a DC 17 Charisma saving throw, any items held in the target’s hands are destroyed (rendered permanently unusable). If a legendary magical item or artifact is destroyed in this manner, it is broken into 2d20 parts. If those parts are pressed against one another, a mending cantrip can be used to fuse each part to another part. The destroyed items cannot be fully restored unless all parts are recovered.
