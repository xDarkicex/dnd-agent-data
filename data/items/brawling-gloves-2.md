# Brawling Gloves +2

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (+2) (requires attunement by a barbarian, captain, craftsman, fighter, rogue, or warden) While attuned to these fingerless magical gloves, you have proficiency with all improvised weapons and gain a bonus to attack and damage rolls made using them. The bonus is determined by the gloves’ rarity.
