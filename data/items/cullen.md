# Cullen

**description**: Cullen Weapon (sax), legendary (requires attunement) Inscribed on the blade on one side is the word, Cullen, and on the other, Vani’s Fang. This magical weapon has a +1 bonus to hit and a +1 bonus to damage. When used against a creature one size or more larger than the wielder, the bonuses change: becoming +3 to hit and +3 to damage.
