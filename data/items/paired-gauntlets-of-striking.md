# Paired Gauntlets of Striking

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) These leather gloves are reinforced with steel plates and decorated with images of two humanoids fighting side-by-side. While you and another creature are each wearing one of the gauntlets and are each within 5 feet of the same creature, you both have advantage on the first attack roll you make against that creature on each of your turns.
