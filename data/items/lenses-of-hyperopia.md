# Lenses of Hyperopia

**description**: Lenses of Hyperopia Wondrous item, common (requires attunement) While wearing these glass lenses, you can see up to 300 feet away with no difficulty, able to discern fine details as though looking at something no more than 100 feet from you. Curse. The lenses are cursed. Attuning to them curses you until you are targeted by the remove curse spell or similar magic. Removing the lenses fails to end the curse on you. As long as you remain cursed, you have trouble focusing your eyes on things that are close to you. You have disadvantage on Intelligence (Investigation) and Wisdom (Perception) checks you make using sight involving an object or creature within 10 feet of you.
