# Ricochet

**description**: Weapon (Sling), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This sling is fashioned from a leopard’s hide. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While attuned, being within 5 feet of a hostile creature doesn’t impose disadvantage on attack rolls made with this weapon. Richochet has 8 charges, and regains 1d6 + 2 expended charges when you finish a long rest. Prior to making an attack roll with this weapon, you can expend up to 3 charges to empower it. If the attack hits, the ammunition ricochets. Choose additional targets up to the number of charges expended to be struck by the ricocheting ammunition. Each additional target must be within 10 feet of the last, and each target hit in this manner takes half as much damage as the original amount. If the attack misses, the ammunition does not ricochet, and the charges are expended. Encounter Ideas Trick Shot. Lugo Lurch, an underhanded street entertainer (bard), is making hundreds of gold betting that he can knock over bottles on three people’s heads with one sling stone. A disgruntled loser wants the party to either retrieve his money or expose the entertainer’s cheating methods. Unlikely Hero. Teethnash, a very ordinary-looking kobold with a wicked sling arm, helps the characters from time to time, leaving only after saluting the party. Grateful Halfling. A halfling adventurer (a Small-sized gladiator welding Ricochet instead of a spear) named Biglilly Littletoe is found nearly dead. If she is helped, she offers Ricochet as a reward for the party’s good deed.
