# Iridium Edge

**description**: Weapon (Dagger), Uncommon This white-silvery dagger is forged from iridium and has tiny channels to hold a reactive powder. You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with it. Catalytic Strike When you hit a creature wearing nonmagical metal armor with the dagger, you can release the dagger's powder to deal an extra 2d6 Fire damage and cause the AC of the target's armor to be reduced by 2 (armor reduced to an AC of 10 or a shield that drops to a +0 bonus is destroyed). Once used, this property can't be used again until you spend 1 hour refilling the dagger with 5 gp worth of reactive material.
