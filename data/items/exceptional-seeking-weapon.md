# Exceptional Seeking Weapon

**description**: Exceptional Seeking Weapon Weapon (ranged: uses ammunition), varies (requires affinity) This weapon enables the wielder to find their mark in difficult environments. While you hold this weapon, you have darkvision to a range of 60 feet. If you already have darkvision, its range increases by 60 feet when it is held. Exceptional. When the weapon reaches this grade, it gains the following properties. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. The weapon’s normal range increases by 50 feet.
