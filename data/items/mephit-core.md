# Mephit Core

**description**: Mephit Core Spell catalyst, uncommon Mephits are tiny fragments of the paraelemental planes come to life. When cracked open they have no internal organs, but they do have a core that acts as a doorway between the material plane and their home. When you cast the find familiar or find greater familiar UAH spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, your familiar takes the form of a mephit of your choice, rather than the spell’s normal options.
