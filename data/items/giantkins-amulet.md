# Giantkins Amulet

**description**: Giantkin's Amulet Wondrous Item, rare (requires attunement) Weight: 5 lbs. These amulets were created by Stone Giants and bestowed upon smaller humanoids who helped the giants in times of need. Giant Power. While wearing the amulet, your carrying capacity is doubled, and you can wield oversized weapons without disadvantage. The amulet also provides advantage on checks to resist being grappled or shoved. Giant Resilience. Once per day, you can speak a command word. You gain resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from non-magical weapons for 1 minute.
