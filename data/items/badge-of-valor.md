# Badge of Valor

**description**: Badge of Valor Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This gold badge is engraved with a lion’s head surrounded by a circle of aquamarines. While wearing it, each friendly creature within 20 feet of you (including you) has advantage on saving throws against being frightened.
