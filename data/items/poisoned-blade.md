# Poisoned Blade

**description**: Poisoned Blade Melee weapon Price: 1,000 gp The blade of this dagger is always coated with a quick-acting toxin. This blade is enchanted to continually coat the blade in a poisonous substance. This dagger does an extra 1d4 poison damage on attacks. Additionally, the blade can be physically dipped or whipped across food and drink to poison them. Anyone eating those substances takes 2d4 poison damage after a short time. The blade cannot poison more than 1 square foot of food or liquid each hour. Melee. Melee Weapons are used to attack enemies up close in hand-to-hand combat. Small. Small weapons are used one-handed and are usually small enough to be hidden away in a boot, up a sleeve, or secretly tucked into a small bag. Small and light melee weapons can use either Strength (Str) or Dexterity (Dex) for the Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus, whichever is higher. Damage: 1d4 Secondary Damage: 1d4
