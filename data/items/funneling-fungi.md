# Funneling Fungi

**description**: Audire Boletus, uncommon These exotic mushrooms are the size of a child’s finger in the quieter chambers of the Underworld and several stories tall in the most cacophonous caves. Audire boletus subsist on sound as much as they do the nutrients and when introduced to an environment with sufficient aural stimuli they prove to be a vital resource to subterranean humanoids. Underworld rangers plant these mushrooms in hidden areas of caves they frequent, using their growth as an indicator of how much traffic the chambers see in their absence, and legends tell of a team of devoted dwarves that utilized funneling fungi to successfully avoid a siege of their city. Ambient Amplifier. A character near a funneling fungi larger than themselves makes Wisdom (Perception) checks with advantage. Spell Component. When these unique mushrooms are successfully harvested they may be used in conjunction with spell components for thunder blasts and other thunder-based energy damage spells either to increase the damage die of the spell by one step or increase the DC to resist its effects by 1.
