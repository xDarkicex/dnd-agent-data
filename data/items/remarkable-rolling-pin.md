# Remarkable Rolling Pin

**description**: Remarkable Rolling Pin Weapon (Club), Rare (requires attunement) Crafted for a legendary halfling pastry chef who took up adventuring, this rolling pin has been used countless times to innovate the culinary arts and create delicious baked goods, but also to defend halfling caravans against raiders and roaming monsters. The Remarkable Rolling Pin is a magic club that looks like and weighs as much as a regular wooden rolling pin. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. Dominion over Dough. While attuned to the Remarkable Rolling Pin, you have advantage on any ability checks and saving throws related to preparing baked goods with it. Freshly Baked Goodness. Any creature that eats a portion of freshly baked goods that have been prepared using this pin gains 1d4 + 4 temporary hit points. A creature can gain this benefit only once per long rest. Euphoric Flattening. When you hit an ooze or any other creature with the amorphous trait with the Remarkable Rolling Pin, instead of dealing damage you can use the pin to magically flatten it. A flattened creature is pleasantly stunned for 1 minute and must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw at the end of each of its turns. On a successful save, this stunning effect ends.
