# Cocoon of Possibility

**description**: Cocoon of Possibility Spell catalyst, rare A cocoon touched by fey magic that contains an insect yet to be reborn. When you cast the reincarnate spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, the target can choose for its new body to be any humanoid race that it has seen (including its original race), instead of the DM rolling on the spell’s table.

- Item Type: Spell catalyst
