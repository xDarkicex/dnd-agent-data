# Thorn the Caustic Torment (Legendary)

**description**: Thorn, the Caustic Torment Weapon (Halberd), Legendary (Requires Attunement) You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. When you make an attack using this weapon, the target takes an extra 1d6 acid damage on a hit. You can cast acid splash from this magic weapon (save DC 11 + your proficiency bonus). Once cast, the spell can’t be cast this way again for 10 minutes. Corrosion. When you hit a creature wearing armor with an attack using Thorn , the armor is partially dissolved and takes a permanent and cumulative −1 penalty to the AC it offers. The armor is destroyed if it is reduced to an AC of 10. Acid Lash. When you hit a creature with an attack using this weapon, each creature within 5 feet of the target must succeed on a DC 19 Dexterity saving throw or take 1d10 acid damage as a whip of acid lashes out. You can use this property twice, regaining all expended uses when you finish a long rest. Acid Storm. As an action, you summon a 20-foot-radius storm cloud centered on the weapon, which pours acidic rain. Each creature in the area must make a DC 19 Dexterity saving throw, taking 12d8 acid damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. You are immune to damage from this effect. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. Progression Thorn progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, so does the weapon’s rarity.
