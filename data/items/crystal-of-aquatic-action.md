# Crystal of Aquatic Action

**description**: Crystal of Aquatic Action Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) While holding this small, sea-green crystal, you can breathe underwater and gain a swimming speed equal to your walking speed. In addition, being underwater imposes no penalties on your attacks.
