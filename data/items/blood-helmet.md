# Blood Helmet

**description**: Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic helmet is fashioned from a bone-like material and red, vein-like strands flow from it. When you attune to the helmet, its veins flow into your skin. Dormant. In its dormant form, the helmet has the following properties: While you have fewer than half your hit points, you have Blindsight out to a radius of 30 feet. Whenever you take non-Psychic damage, the helmet gains 1 charge. The helmet can have up to 3 charges and loses unspent charges 1 hour after gaining them. As a reaction to succeeding an Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma saving throw against another creature's spell or effect, you can deal 1d10 Psychic damage to the creature per charge you expend. Infused. In its infused form, the helmet has the following additional properties: While you have fewer than half your hit points, you have advantage on ability checks and saving throws using Wisdom, Intelligence, and Charisma. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge to focus your mind on the vitals of another creature you can see within 30 feet. You learn the creature's current and maximum hit points and any damage or condition immunities and resistances it has, and you have advantage on the next attack roll you make against the creature before the end of your next turn. Blood Infusion It is up to the GM when the Blood Armor and Blood Helmet become infused. It might just require that a character attunes to both at the same time, completing the set, but it may also only happen when the character dies while wearing the armor or the helmet. If they're brought back to life, the armor and/or helmet is now infused for that character.
