# Eye of the Planesrunner (rare variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement by a creature missing an eye) Component: humanoid (spellcaster) eye This crystal sphere of shifting scenes depicts the landscape of the plane it last visited. An iridescent lattice borders the eye, developing in complexity the more it sees of the planes of existence. You must have a missing eye to attune to this item. Once attuned to it, it takes the place of your missing eye, you can see through it as if it were a normal eye, and it can’t be removed against your will. Planar Magic. As an action, you can channel the eye’s planar energy to cast one of the following spells (save DC 16): banishment , blink , contact other plane , planar ally , or plane shift . Once this item has been used to cast a spell, roll a d12. If the result is equal to or lower than the level of the spell, the eye’s energy floods into your brain; you take psychic damage equal to twice the number rolled and this property of the eye can’t be used again until the next dawn. Uncommon variant: Reduce the DC to 13 and the d12 to a d6. The only spell that the eye can be used to cast is blink. Rare variant: Reduce the DC to 15 and the d12 to a d8. Remove planar ally and plane shift from the list of spells you can cast using the eye.
