# Incense of Understanding

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon When lit, this magical incense produces a sweet aroma of honeysuckle and peppermint, and burns for 1 hour before it is consumed. A creature who inhales the incense during a short rest gains advantage on Insight and Investigation checks for 8 hours. Moreover, it can understand any spoken language it hears, and any creature who knows at least one language and can hear the creature also understands what it says.
