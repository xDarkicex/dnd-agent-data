# Ring of the Thunderspeaker

**description**: Ring, rare This stone ring is shot through with lightning patterns made of a vibrant blue mineral, and vibrates with a deep, subaudible rumble. Once per day as an action, you may cause the volume to increase to a sickening thunder that rattles the very bones of creatures around you. Each creature other than yourself within 60 feet must make a Constitution saving throw or take 4d8 thunder damage and be sickened for 1 minute. Affected creatures may make a new saving throw at the end of each of their turns to end the sickened condition. On a successful save creatures take half damage and are not sickened. A creature that fails its initial saving throw by 5 or more falls prone. Whenever you attune to the ring or finish a short or long rest, you may designate any number of creatures within 60 feet to be immune to the ring.
