# Blue Dragon Scale Mail

**description**: Armor (Scale Mail), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) Blue Dragon Scale Mail is made of the scales of a blue dragon. Sometimes dragons collect their cast-off scales and gift them. Other times, hunters carefully preserve the hide of a dead dragon. In either case, Blue Dragon Scale Mail is highly valued. While wearing this armor, you gain a +1 bonus to Armor Class, you have Advantage on saving throws against the breath weapons of Dragons, and you have Resistance to Lightning damage. Additionally, you can focus your senses as a Magic action to discern the distance and direction to the closest blue dragon within 30 miles of yourself. This action can’t be used again until the next dawn.
