# Whirlwind

**description**: Legendary weapon property Components: Masterwork exotic melee weapon with the Finesse property and the Double property or Light property When you use your bonus action to make an additional melee attack with this weapon—such as when you engage with two-weapon fighting or use a double weapon—you can make an attack with it against any number of creatures within 5 feet of you, with a separate attack roll for each target. You can't move between these bonus attacks.
