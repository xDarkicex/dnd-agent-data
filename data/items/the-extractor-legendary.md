# The Extractor (Legendary)

**description**: The Extractor Weapon (Maul), Legendary (Requires Attunement) You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon and it deals an extra 1 piercing damage on a hit. Shuck the Oyster. When you score a critical hit with this magic weapon against a creature wearing magical or nonmagical ar­mor, the armor is partially damaged and takes a permanent and cumulative −2 penalty to the AC it offers. Armor reduced to an AC of 10 offers no protection until it is repaired. Dig Deep. When you hit a creature that is wearing armor with an attack using this weapon, it deals an additional 2d6 piercing damage to the target. In addition, on a critical hit, the next attack against the target has advantage.
