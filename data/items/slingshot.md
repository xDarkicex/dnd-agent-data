# Slingshot

**description**: Slingshots Slingshots are simple ranged weapons comprising an elasticated material strung between a Y-frame brace. They deal 1d6 bludgeoning damage on a hit and have the Ammunition (range 20/60) and Two-handed properties. They use the same ammunition as slings.
