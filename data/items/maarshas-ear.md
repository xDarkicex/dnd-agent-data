# Maarsha’s Ear

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare Maarsha's Ear is a withered ear of the sea hag mummy lord, Maarsha. It allows its wielder to use an action to send a message to Maarsha as if using the sending spell. Once used, the magic item can’t be used again until the next dawn. When you hold the withered ear to your ear, you gain a +5 bonus to Wisdom (Perception) checks made to hear undead creatures.
