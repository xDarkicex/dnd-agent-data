# Figurine of Wondrous Power, Basalt Cockatrice

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Basalt Cockatrice (Uncommon). This basalt statuette is carved in the likeness of a cockatrice. It can become a cockatrice for up to 1 hour. Once it has been used, it can’t be used again until 2 days have passed. While it is in cockatrice form, you and your allies within 30 feet of it have advantage on saving throws against being petrified.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: uncommon
