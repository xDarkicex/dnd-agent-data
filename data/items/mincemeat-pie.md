# Mincemeat Pie

**description**: Potion, rare This savory pie has a hint of sweetness. For 8 hours after you consume this food, you gain 5 temporary hit points each time you reduce a creature to 0 hit points. Once you have gained a total of 20 temporary hit points, you can’t gain more temporary hit points from this consumption of mincemeat pie .

- Item Type: Potion
- Item Rarity: rare
