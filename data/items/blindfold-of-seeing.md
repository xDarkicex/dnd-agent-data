# Blindfold of Seeing

**description**: Blindfold of Seeing Wondrous item, rare While you have this blindfold covering your eyes, you suffer the blinded condition. The blindfold can’t be removed unless you are attuned to it or dead. Transcend Sight (Requires Attunement). While you are attuned to and wearing this blindfold, you gain blindsight to a range of 60 feet. This blindsight doesn’t require you to be able to hear or smell.
