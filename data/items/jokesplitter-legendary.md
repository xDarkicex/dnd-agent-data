# Jokesplitter (Legendary)

**description**: Jokesplitter Weapon (Handaxe), Legendary (Requires Attunement) Jokesplitter normally takes the form of a harmless wooden hatchet. As a bonus action, you can speak a command word, causing a metal blade to emerge for 10 minutes, transforming it into a magical handaxe. You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. In addition, you gain proficiency in the Deception and Persuasion skills. While holding this weapon, you can give yourself a bonus to Charisma (Deception) and Charisma (Persuasion) checks equal to your Strength modifier. Made You Look. When you are targeted by a spell or magical effect, you can use your reaction to teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see, leaving a wooden replica of your body in your original space, which the spell targets. You can use this property twice and regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. What an Unworthy Effort. You can use an action to mock someone’s use of magic, casting dispel magic through this weapon. If you must make an ability check to see if this spell succeeds, you make a Charisma (Deception) check against the DC instead. You can use this property twice and regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.
