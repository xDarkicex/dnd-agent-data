# Suppressed Firearm

**description**: Suppressed Firearm Weapon (any weapon with the black powder property), rare The weapon has a strange metal tube on its barrel that muffles the thunderous sound of its shots. When you make a ranged weapon attack with this weapon, its thunderous boom is audible only to a range of 10 feet, and you aren’t deafened when you roll a 1 on the d20.
