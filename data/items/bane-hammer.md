# Bane Hammer

**description**: Weapon (Light Hammer), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This simple hammer has a leather-wrapped handle with an impish face carved in relief on its head. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you make an attack with this weapon and speak the command word, it begins to emit necrotic energy. On a hit, the creature takes an extra 1d4 necrotic damage and must subtract the number rolled on the necrotic damage die from any attack rolls or saving throws it makes until the start of your next turn. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a short or long rest. Encounter Ideas Crypt Keepers. An ancient crypt with untold treasures is crawling with ghosts, ghasts, and all manner of zombies. A particularly oafish ogre zombie wields a bane hammer. What You Don’t Know... Sabina, a small-town carpenter, is constructing a new building, unknowingly using a bane hammer to secure nails into the structure’s wooden beams. Disciplinary Pact-tion. A mysterious fiend offers a bane hammer as a reward for taking care of a bearded devil that hasn’t been delivering results.
