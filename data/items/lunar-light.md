# Lunar Light

**description**: Wondrous item, common This miniature moon is the size of a large marble, able to be held comfortably in one hand. Light of the Moon. The lunar light sheds bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for 20 feet beyond that. This light is shed forever but can be blocked by something opaque like heavy cloth or a leather pouch. Lunar Cycle. The lunar light always shows the phase of the nearest moon. When on the Material Plane, you can always determine the date by looking at it. The light of the moon can comfort even in the darkest places.
