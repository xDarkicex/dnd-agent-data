# Wyrmheart Amulet

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) This golden amulet features a polished ruby, clasped within the talons of a golden dragon. You have resistance to Cold damage while wearing it. The amulet has 3 charges for the following properties and regains all expended charges daily at dawn: Aura of Warmth. As an action, you can expend 1 charge to cause the amulet to emit an aura of warmth in a 10-foot radius for 1 minute. Each creature within the aura gains resistance to Cold damage. Fiery Purge. When you fail a Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma saving throw, you can expend 1 charge to cause searing heat to spread from the amulet, cleansing your body. You take 1d10 Fire damage and can reroll the saving throw. You must use the new result.
