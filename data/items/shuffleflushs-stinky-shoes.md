# Shuffleflush’s Stinky Shoes

**description**: Shuffleflush’s Stinky Shoes Wondrous Item, Legendary (requires attunement) Once worn by the legendary halfling gambler Shiny Shuffleflush, these fancy shoes are always magically clean and spotless. Stories say that Shuffleflush used the magic of these shoes to get other gamblers to leave the table whenever fortune did not favor him. While wearing these shoes, your walking speed increases by 10 feet. Summon Stupefying Stink. While wearing these shoes, you are enveloped in a pleasant, musky aroma. However, you can use your action to cause them to emit a terrible odor, as if you had cast stinking cloud (DC 25) centered on yourself and not requiring concentration. You can trigger this effect at will and you automatically succeed on its saving throw. Shuffleflush’s Stinky Salvation. Any creature that swallows you while you are wearing these shoes must succeed on a DC 25 Constitution saving throw against poison or be forced to regurgitate you immediately as the shoes’ magical stink automatically seeps out and overwhelms it. The creature repeats the saving throw at the beginning of each of its turns for as long as you remain swallowed. Creatures that don’t need to breathe or are immune to poison automatically succeed on this saving throw.
