# Refined Liquid Courage

**description**: Potion, rare This translucent liquid is extracted from the blood drained from tortured creatures. It is stored in glass syringes. Unlike other potions, it needs to be injected directly into a muscle, preferably the leg. When you are injected with this potion, you gain the effects of the haste spell for 1 minute (no concentration required) and you regain all your hit points. At the end of each of your turns for 1 minute, you must make a DC 18 Constitution saving throw. The potion wears off and its effects end on a failed save or after 1 minute, whichever is sooner. When the potion wears off, you drop to 0 hit points. This potion has no effect on creatures that have no blood and no beating heart.
