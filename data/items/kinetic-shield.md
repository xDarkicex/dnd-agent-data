# Kinetic Shield

**description**: Kinetic Shield Armor (shield), very rare When a creature misses you with a melee weapon attack while you wield this shield, you can use your reaction to catch the attack with your shield, forcing the creature to roll the damage for the attack. The creature takes half the amount of damage it rolled, as though it had hit itself with its own attack. AC : 2 Weight : 6
