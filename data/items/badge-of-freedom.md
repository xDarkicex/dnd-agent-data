# Badge of Freedom

**description**: Badge of Freedom Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This silver pin has gilt edges and is shaped like a tiny pair of wings. While wearing it, magic can neither reduce your speed nor cause you to be paralyzed or restrained.
