# Blood Shard

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic ruby is infused with a vampire's blood. Blood's Boon. As an action, you can transform the ruby into a magical weapon of any type that deals an extra 1d10 Necrotic damage on a hit. When you do, you can also choose that the weapon has one or more of the following properties: Bloodthirsty. You have advantage on attacks with the weapon against any creature that has fewer than half its hit points. Essence Thief. When you hit a creature with the weapon, you gain temporary hit points equal to the Necrotic damage dealt by the attack. Life's Bane. When you hit a creature with the weapon, the creature can't regain hit points until the start of your next turn. When you transform the ruby, you take 1d10 Necrotic damage plus an extra 1d10 Necrotic damage per property you chose for the weapon, and your hit point maximum is reduced by the same amount until you finish a long rest (this damage and effect can't be reduced or negated in any way). The ruby returns to its original form after 1 hour.
