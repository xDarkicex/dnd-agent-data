# Shroud Strips

**description**: Shroud strips are alchemically treated strips of cloth or leather used to wrap a corpse. When the body is completely wrapped in shroud strips, it ceases to decay and can’t become undead. The first month a body is wrapped in these strips doesn’t count against the time limit of spells such as raise dead. Some clerics of life or sun deities create enchanted shroud strips that prevent bodies from being turned into undead creatures.
