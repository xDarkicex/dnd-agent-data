# Devil's Luck

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This magic amulet has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. When you fail an ability check or saving throw while wearing it, you can expend 1 charge to reroll it. You must use the new roll. After using this property, you have disadvantage on ability checks and saving throws that use the same ability until the next dawn.
