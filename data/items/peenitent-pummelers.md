# Peenitent Pummelers

**description**: Peenitent Pummelers Melee weapon (simple, brass knuckles), rare (requires attunement) These brass knuckles are made of gold and etched with a holy symbol over each knuckle. You gain a +1 to attack and damage rolls with this magic weapon. When you deal damage to a fiend or undead with this weapon, you deal an additional 1d6 radiant damage. Light. A light weapon is small and easy to handle, making it ideal for use when fighting with two weapons. Unarmed. When you make an unarmed strike, you can choose to deal the damage of this weapon instead of the bludgeoning damage normal for an unarmed strike. Damage : 1d4+1 Damage Type: Bludgeoning Properties: Light, Unarmed Weight: 1
