# Bracelet of Animal Speech

**description**: Bracelet of Animal Speech Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) When you place this item on your wrist, you gain the ability to comprehend and verbally communicate with one beast of your choice. A side effect is that you can no longer speak any other language, but can only make the noise, such as a bark or meow, of the last beast you spoke to. This effect lasts while you have the bracelet on your wrist. The knowledge and awareness of many beasts is limited by their intelligence, but at minimum, beasts can give you information about nearby locations and monsters, including whatever they can perceive or have perceived within the past day. You might be able to persuade a beast to perform a small favor for you, at the DM's discretion.
