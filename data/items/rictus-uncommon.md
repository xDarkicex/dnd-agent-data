# Rictus (Uncommon)

**description**: Weapon (Mace), Uncommon (Requires Attunement by a Good Aligned Creature) Common When Undead are within 120 feet of this magic weapon, its silver skull splits, revealing golden spikes. Uncommon Rictus deals an extra weapon die of damage to Undead creatures on a hit. Decaying Countenance. As an action, you can assume an illusory shroud of undeath for 1 hour or until you dismiss it (no action required). A successful DC 13 Wisdom (Insight) check allows a creature to see through your illusion. Undead with an Intelligence of 6 or less automatically fail this check. A creature who fails this check perceives you as Undead. Spells and magical effects that detect creature types, such as a paladin’s Divine Sense or the trigger of a symbol spell treat you as Undead unless you fail a DC 19 Charisma saving throw, in which case they discern your true nature.
