# Illusionist's Flute

**description**: Wondrous Item, Common (Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster) This magic flute looks quite ordinary. You can use the flute as a spellcasting focus. Deceptive Note. As a reaction, when a creature you can see within 30 feet succeeds on an ability check or saving throw made to discern, disbelieve, or see through the effects of an illusion spell you've cast, you can play an inaudible note on the flute and the creature must reroll the d20 and use the new result. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
