# Spear of Virtue

**description**: Weapon (spear), rare (requires attunement) Damage: 1d6 piercing damage, Versatile (1d8) Virtuous. On a successful hit, this spear deals an additional 1d6 radiant damage. While attuned to this spear, you gain advantage on saving throws against being frightened and charmed. Righteous Strike. As an action, you can command the spear to unleash a burst of radiant energy in a 10-foot radius around you. Each hostile creature in that area must make a CON SAVE with a DC equal to (12 + your proficiency bonus), taking 13 (3d8) radiant damage on a failed save or half as much on a successful one; evil Fey and Fiends make this save with disadvantage. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a short or long rest. Radiant Surge. When you take the attack action, you can replace one of your attacks with this feature. Choose an Aberration, Fey, Fiend, Monstrosity or Undead that you can see within 60ft. They must succeed on a WIS SAVE with a DC equal to (13 + your proficiency bonus) or become Blinded until the end of your next turn. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a short or long rest.
