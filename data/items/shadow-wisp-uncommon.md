# Shadow Wisp (Uncommon)

**description**: Shadow Wisp Weapon (Longsword), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This magic weapon deals an extra 1d6 necrotic damage on a hit. You can speak a command word, causing shadowy wisps to leak from the blade for 1 hour or until you speak the command word again to end the effect. Shadow Walk. When you hit with an attack using this weapon, you can teleport to an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the target. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. Progression Shadow Wisp progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, so does the weapon’s rarity.
