# Bell Tower of the Peaceful Path

**description**: Chimes of the Peaceful Path Wondrous Item, Uncommon (requires attunement) Created as a gift to a young pacifist cleric, these chimes are infused with a desire for peace and the redemption of struggling souls. While attuned to these chimes, you can jangle them to create a light chiming noise that can be heard only by creatures of an alignment of your choice up to 300 feet away. The chiming noise penetrates magic barriers and grants those who hear it advantage on Wisdom (Survival) checks made to find you. The chimes have 3 charges. You can use an action to expend 1 charge and cast calm emotions (DC 10) with a radius of 30 feet centered on yourself. The chimes regain all charges daily at dawn. When casting calm emotions using a spell slot while attuned to these chimes, you can expend 1 charge to add a +2 bonus to your spell save DC for that singular casting of calm emotions . Item Variant: Bell Tower of the Peaceful Path At the gamemaster’s discretion, a huge-sized, legendary version of the item can be found. The Bell Tower of the Peaceful Path can be heard up to 10 miles away. When calm emotions cast through this item, the DC is 20 and the radius is 1 mile, centered on the bell.
