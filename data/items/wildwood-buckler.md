# Wildwood Buckler

**description**: Wildwood Buckler Often used by dual-wielding swordsmen and stealth fighters, the Wildwood buckler is a small shield made from Wildwood trees that is strapped to the wearer’s forearm and allows the wearer to wield a weapon in the same hand. The magical properties of the Wildwood grants the wearer +1 to attacks with a weapon using the same arm as the buckler, but the bearer loses the shield benefit until the start of their next turn if they do so. AC: 1 Weight: 2
