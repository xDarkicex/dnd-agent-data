# Stargazer's Spyglass

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare This magic spyglass is inlaid with silver and small diamonds. The spyglass has 3 charges for the following properties and regains all expended charges daily at dawn: Celestial Guidance. After peering at the open sky through the spyglass for 1 minute, you can expend 1 charge to cast the Commune spell from the spyglass. The spell works as usual, except you aren't told the answers but divine them from the positions of the stars. Extraplanar Scrying. After peering into the spyglass for 10 minutes and calibrating it correctly, you can expend 1 charge to cast the Scrying spell (save DC 15) from the spyglass. The spell works as usual, except that it can only target a creature or location on another plane of existence. Otherworldly Sight. As an action, you can expend 1 charge to peer through the spyglass and cast the See Invisibility spell from it.
