# Talisman of Skill

**description**: Talisman of Skill Wondrous item Price: 1,000 gp These small talismans can be attached to any weapon and guides your attacks to hit the most vulnerable spots. The talisman may be attached to any weapon (ranged or melee) to give it a +1 bonus on Attack Rolls and Damage Rolls. Only one talisman can be in effect at a time on a weapon. The talisman may be removed and added to another weapon, but takes 1 minute to take effect.
