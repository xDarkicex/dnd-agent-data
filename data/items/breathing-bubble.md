# Breathing Bubble

**description**: Breathing Bubble Wondrous item, common This translucent, bubble-like sphere has a slightly tacky outer surface, and you gain the item’s benefits only while wearing it over your head like a helmet. The bubble contains 1 hour of breathable air. The bubble regains all its expended air daily at dawn.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
