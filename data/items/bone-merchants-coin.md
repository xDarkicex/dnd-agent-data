# Bone Merchant's Coin

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon You have advantage on death saving throws while carrying this magic coin. In addition, you can flip the coin as an action to send a magical ping to an undead creature with which you are familiar. The creature recognizes you as the sender if it knows you and becomes aware of your precise position.
