# Steelsting Pauldron

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This magic shoulder pauldron is adorned with adamantine spikes. Any spikes that are removed from the pauldron vanish after 1 minute and magically regrow at the next dawn. The pauldron has 3 charges for the following properties and regains all expended charges daily at dawn: Spike Cloud. As an action, you can expend charges to shoot spikes in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in the area must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 1d6 Piercing damage per charge you expend on a failure, or half as much on a success. Spike Growth. As an action, you can expend charges to cause spikes to magically grow forth in a 5-foot square within 30 feet per charge you expend. For 1 minute, the area is difficult terrain and a creature that moves into a spike-filled square on its turn takes 1d8 Piercing damage for each spike-filled square it enters. Thorny Rebuke. As a reaction when a creature within 5 feet of you misses you with an attack, you can expend charges to deal 1d10 Piercing damage to the target per charge you expend.
