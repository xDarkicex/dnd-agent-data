# Armor of Junk

**description**: Armor (any armor), uncommon An armor of junk is quite literally an armor made of various layers of junk. Metal, leather, ragged clothes, and meltable objects are used in the making. Most customize these armors with carvings, buttons, colors, and pins. While wearing this armor, you gain the following benefits: You have advantage on your Dexterity (Stealth) checks made on the streets, junkyards, and railroads. Also, you have a +1 bonus to AC while wearing this armor. However, its limited magic has a chance to fail. Each time you roll for initiative, you have to roll a d4. If the result is 1, 2, or 3, you cannot gain this +1 bonus to AC until you roll for initiative again.
