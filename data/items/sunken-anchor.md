# Sunken Anchor

**description**: Weapon (war pick), uncommon (requires attunement) Component: monstrosity antler Covered in flakes of blue-green bronze and barnacles, this sturdy implement looks like it’s seen better days. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Anchor. As an action, you can point this weapon at a point on a surface within 60 feet of you, causing an ethereal chain to tether you to that point for the next 10 minutes, or until you dismiss it as a bonus action. For the duration, you can’t be willingly moved away from that point, except by a weight of 8,000 pounds or more, or if a creature trying to move you succeeds on a DC 25 Strength check, which causes the chain to snap and the effect to end. The effect also ends early if you ever willingly move more than 60 feet from the point or if you fall unconscious. Hydrodynamic. Attacking while underwater doesn’t impose disadvantage on attack rolls you make with this weapon.
