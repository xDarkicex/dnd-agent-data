# Breastplate of Ease

**description**: The illithid around here tell me they prefer their meals with a crunchy coating. — Xanathar Breastplate of Ease Armor (medium), uncommon While the magic of this armor doesn't provide any additional protection, breastplate of ease can be slept in with no detrimental effects. Breastplate. This armor consists of a fitted metal chest piece worn with supple leather. Although it leaves the legs and arms relatively unprotected, this armor provides good protection for the wearer's vital organs while leaving the wearer relatively unencumbered.
