# Bracers of Gunnery

**description**: Bracers of Gunnery Wondrous item, Uncommon (requires attunement) Technognome artificers give these bracers to their apprentices to help them learn how to use firearms, and seasoned artillerists and gunslingers use them to perfect their craft and gain an edge over their enemies. While wearing these bracers, you have proficiency with firearms, and you gain a +2 bonus to damage rolls on ranged attacks made with such weapons.
