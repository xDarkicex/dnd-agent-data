# Plaguescarred Weapon

**description**: Weapon (any melee), rare This pockmarked weapon contains colonies of virulent bacteria, which infect the wounds it inflicts. Any Injury inflicted by this weapon has its Medicine DC increased by 5, and the creature suffers from a Lasting Effect regardless of the method in which it is healed. If the Injury is healed through resting, those rests count toward the number of rests required for the Lasting Effect to be removed.
