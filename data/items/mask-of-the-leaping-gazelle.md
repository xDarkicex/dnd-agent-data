# Mask of the Leaping Gazelle

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Favored by warriors and hunters on the grasslands of Narumbeki, this painted wooden animal mask is adorned with a pair of gazelle horns. When you wear this mask, your walking speed increases by 10 feet, and you can make a long jump of as much as 25 feet with a 10-foot running start.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: uncommon
