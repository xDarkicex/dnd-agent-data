# Ensorcelled Weapon

**description**: Ensorcelled Weapon Weapon (any), uncommon When you make an attack with this magic weapon, you can choose to use your spellcasting ability modifier, instead of your Strength or Dexterity modifier, for the attack and damage rolls. You must use the same modifier for both rolls. Additionally, you can use this weapon as a spellcasting focus.
