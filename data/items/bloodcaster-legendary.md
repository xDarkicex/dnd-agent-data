# Bloodcaster (Legendary)

**description**: Bloodcaster Weapon (Light Crossbow), Le gendary (Requires Attunement) You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Blood Bolt. You can fire this magic weapon without ammunition by losing 1 hit point as part of the action used to fire it. If you do so, your blood coalesces into a liquid bolt, which deals necrotic damage instead of piercing. Septic Shot. When you fire a blood bolt, you can lose an additional 2 hit points to deal an additional 3d4 poison damage. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw (the DC is equal to 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Dexterity modifier) or be poisoned for 1 minute. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the condition on a success. Blood Aim. When hit with a blood bolt, you can choose to lose an additional 10 hit points to make the attack a critical hit. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a short or long rest.
