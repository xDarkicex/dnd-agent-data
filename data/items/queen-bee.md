# Queen Bee

**description**: Potion, rare This spicy mead is brewed from royal hives and shimmers with a rose-gold hue. When you drink this mead, your Charisma score increases to 20 for 8 hours. The mead has no effect on you if your Charisma score is already equal to or greater than 20.
