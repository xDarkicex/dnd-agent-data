# Giantsmight Greatclub (Legendary)

**description**: Giantsmight Weapon (Greatclub), Legendary (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls with this magic weapon. When you hit a creature larger than you with this weapon, the creature takes an extra 1d6 bludgeoning damage. While wielding this weapon, your Strength score becomes 20 (+5). As a bonus action, the club turns into a greatclub, or from a greatclub into a club. In addition, you can read, write, and speak Giant. Roll20 Note: Add both the Giantsmight Club and Giantsmight Greatclub for the appropriate rarity to your character sheet for attack automation for both the club and greatclub. Knock ‘Em Down! When you score a critical hit against a creature larger than you with this weapon, the creature must succeed on a DC 17 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. Giant-Size! As a bonus action, you become Huge for 2 hours. For the duration, you gain temporary hit points equal to three times your character level and the reach of your melee attacks increases by 5 feet. Additionally, while transformed, properties of this weapon which only affected creatures larger than you now affect creatures of any size. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest.
