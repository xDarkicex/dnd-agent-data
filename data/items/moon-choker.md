# Moon Choker

**description**: Wondrous item, Weird, Very Rare These black leather and gold chokers are very elaborate and difficult for a hag to make. This hag weird will not function in sunlight. Once made, they allow a hag to generate bright moonlight out to 30 feet and dim moonlight out to 60 feet once per day. The light lasts up to an hour or if dispelled by the hag. This moonlight allows a hag to use spells requiring moonlight. While producing moonlight, the moon choker also grants the hag resistance to one source of damage chosen when the moonlight is turned on. This choker will not function if worn by any creature other than a hag.
