# Saddle of the Cavalier

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon This saddle confers the following benefits while you are seated in it and astride a mount. Protected Mount. Attack rolls against the mount have Disadvantage. Secure Rider. You can’t be dismounted against your will. This property is suppressed while you have the Incapacitated condition.
