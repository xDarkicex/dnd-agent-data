# Countess's Consort (Rare)

**description**: Countess's Consort Weapon (Rapier), Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Your Constitution score permanently decreases by an additional 2 (4 total). When you attune to this magic weapon, your Constitution score permanently decreases by 2. When you finish a long rest while attuned to it, you gain temporary hit points equal to the number of hit points you lost from all of this weapon’s decreases to your Constitution score. Whenever this weapon kills a creature with a CR equal to at least half your level (rounded up), you gain temporary hit points equal to your level. Shapechange. As an action, you can transform into a bat. While in bat form, you can’t speak, your walking speed is 5 feet, and you have a flying speed of 30 feet. Your statistics, other than your size and speed, are unchanged. The rapier, and anything you are wearing, transforms with you, but nothing else you are carrying does. You revert to your true form if you die. You can use this property a number of times equal to half your proficiency bonus and regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. Progression Whenever you kill a creature with a CR equal to your character level or higher, you can choose to have Countess’s Consort progress, immediately gaining its new effects and reducing your Constitution score by the indicated amount.
