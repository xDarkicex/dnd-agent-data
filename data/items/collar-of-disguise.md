# Collar of Disguise

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement by a Companion) While your companion wears this leather collar, you can use an action to speak a command word and polymorph the companion into one of the following creatures: cat, frog, goat, mastiff, mule, owl, rat, or raven. Any equipment the companion is wearing or carrying is absorbed or borne by the new form (your choice). While in this form, your companion retains all their statistics except for size, and they can’t use ferocity actions. You can speak the command word again as an action and return your companion to their original form.

**properties**:

- Item Rarity: Rare
