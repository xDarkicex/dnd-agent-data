# Coin of Luck

**description**: Coin of Luck Wondrous item Price: 2,500 gp This unassuming silvered coin seems stamped with the language from an ancient civilization. It is said to control your fate. Once per day a player who has this item can activate the coin to change a roll to a 50/50 success chance. Roll the d20 as normal but all even rolls are considered a Critical Success and all odd rolls are considered a Critical Failure.
