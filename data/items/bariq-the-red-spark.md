# Bariq, the Red Spark

**description**: Bariq, the Red Spark Weapon (Scimitar), Artifact (requires attunement) Forged in the heart of an entropic maelstrom by a deranged aberrant demigod, Bariq took the shape of the weapon its insane creator thought would bring the chaotic storms it so loved in the world of mortals. Now lost in the kaleidoscopic dunes of Prisma Desert, Bariq is all but forgotten, its chaotic magic waiting to be unleashed again. You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. It deals an extra 3d6 lightning damage to any target it hits, and the target can’t take reactions until the start of its next turn. The weapon gains an additional +1 to attack and damage rolls if you are outdoors in stormy conditions. Forged in Chaos. Bariq crackles with red lightning that immediately betrays its profoundly magical nature. It’s lightweight yet indestructible by ordinary means. The Red Spark. While holding Bariq, you can use your action to cast lightning bolt (spell save DC 20) from it at will. When cast this way, lightning bolt produces red lightning that ignores lightning resistance. Eye of the Maelstrom. While attuned to this weapon, you are immune to lightning damage. Additionally, whenever a spell or effect that deals lightning damage is created or passes within 30 feet of you, you can bend its path and condense its power so that it passes through your space and you become the single target of this spell or effect within that radius (no action required). Entropic Link. While holding Bariq, you can use a bonus action to hide it into an extradimensional space, the entropic maelstrom where it draws its powers from. While Bariq is hidden, fractal lightning scars appear all over the hand and arm that held it. You can use another bonus action to retrieve Bariq from the entropic maelstrom.
