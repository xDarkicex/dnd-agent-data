# Lockshiv

**description**: Weapon (dagger), uncommon Component: ooze vesicle This bladed syringe holds a lead-heavy concoction that inhibits a creature’s ability to interact with the Weave. With intricate mechanics, the injection mechanism can be pre-loaded, allowing the weapon to release its magic-inhibiting effects when thrown. Suppression Serum. When you hit a creature with this weapon, you can inject some of the dagger’s fluid into the creature. The creature must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or be unable to concentrate on spells until the end of its next turn. In addition, if it tries to cast a spell before the end of its next turn, it must make a DC 13 ability check using its spellcasting ability. On a failure, it wastes its action but doesn’t expend a spell slot or use of the spell. This property can be used twice, regaining all expended uses daily at dawn.
