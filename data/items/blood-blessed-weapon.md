# Blood-Blessed Weapon

**description**: Weapon (any), uncommon These jagged weapons are blessed by the Blood God’s clerics. Once they taste the blood and flesh of the enemy, they get more hungry for them with each strike. Each third weapon attack you make with this weapon deals an extra 1d6 damage of the weapon’s damage type on a hit.
