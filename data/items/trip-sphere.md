# Trip sphere

**description**: This apple-sized iron sphere contains gunpowder in an alchemical solution. As an action, you can throw this sphere up to 30 feet. It detonates with concussive force on impact and is destroyed. Each creature within 5 feet of where the sphere landed must succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity saving throw or be knocked prone.
