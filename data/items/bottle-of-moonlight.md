# Bottle of Moonlight

**description**: Bottle of Moonlight Wondrous item, uncommon This crescent-shaped bottle is made of pale stone and contains a thick, silvery liquid. When you use an action to pour this liquid on a lycanthrope, that creature immediately assumes its bestial form for 1 hour.
