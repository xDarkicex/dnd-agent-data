# Vampiric Syringe

**description**: Weapon (crossbow ammunition, syringe pistol/rifle ammunition), rare You can load your crossbow or syringe pistol/rifle with these syringes. The syringe has two sharp tips instead of a needle, resembling the fangs of a vampire. On a hit, the target takes 2d6 blood damage, and its hit point maximum is reduced by an amount equal to half of the blood damage dealt. This reduction lasts until the target finishes a long rest. The syringes come in packs of five. Once it hits a target, the syringe is no longer magical.
