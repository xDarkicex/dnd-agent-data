# The Soul of Dal Hali

**description**: Wondrous Item, Covenant Item (requires attunement) The Sarcosan invasion came swift and hard to Eredane, although it slowed significantly when the horsemen attempted to invade the forest kingdom of Erethor. As the Sarcosans were contemplating a retreat from the elven wood, several scouting missions were sent north to gather information about the land and its defenders. A Sarcosan knight named Bejhi Awanhu led one such mission, which followed the Eren River all the way to the Ardune before heading back to the main Sarcosan line. On their way back, they were ambushed by a force of Danisil elves, who slaughtered the entire force of Sarcosans and sent their riderless horses back to the Sarcosan lines to serve as a warning. Bejhi Awanhu survived the attack, only barely, and found himself alone on the central plains with no food, too injured to make his way back to his army. It is said that a magic amulet is all that saved his life. It is here that legend has overshadowed reality. However, those who have claimed to possess his amulet in later years have seemed unusually hearty and hale.
