# Mask of Comedy and Mask of Tragedy

**description**: Mask of Comedy and Mask of Tragedy Wondrous item, Very Rare (requires attunement) Myth has it that the earliest bards in recorded history used the original Mask of Comedy and Mask of Tragedy to perform in ancient plays. Since then, many magical performers have sought to create their own pair and several exist across the world. While wearing the Mask of Comedy, you have advantage on Charisma checks related to performing in comedies and telling funny stories. While wearing the Mask of Tragedy, you have advantage on Charisma checks related to performing in tragedies and telling sad stories. Masks of comedy and tragedy come in pairs. If one of the masks in a pair is destroyed, the other one becomes nonmagical. Each mask can be attuned to one creature at a time. A creature attuning to both masks uses a single attunement slot to do so. Thousand Faces. While wearing one of the masks, you can use an action to cast the disguise self spell from it at will. When cast from the Mask of Tragedy, disguise self can create only tragic disguises that look perpetually sad and dramatic. When cast from the Mask of Comedy, disguise self can create only comedic disguises that look perpetually cheerful and funny. The spell ends if the mask is removed. Theatrical Telepathy. While attuned to one of the masks, you can cast the sending spell at will, targeting the creature who is attuned to the other mask. To do so, both you and the creature must wear the masks.
