# Healer's Mace

**description**: Mace, uncommon (requires attunement) This mace functions as a holy symbol for a servant of a deity associated with life or healing. You gain +1 to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. Font of Life. If the mace is on your person, when you cast a healing spell (such as cure wounds), the mace gains a charge. The mace holds up to 3 charges. While holding the mace you can expend 3 charges to cast mass healing word, 2 charges to cast prayer of healing, or 1 charge to cast cure wounds.
