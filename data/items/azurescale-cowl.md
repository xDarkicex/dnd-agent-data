# Azurescale Cowl

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This cowl is made of shimmering fish scales and can be unfolded into a hood resembling a large fish. While wearing the cowl, you have a swimming speed equal to your walking speed. Branchia. As a bonus action, you can speak the cowl's command word to surround your head in a translucent sphere of energy, or dissipate the sphere. The sphere allows you to breathe air and water through the cowl's gills. In addition, you are immune to any effect that requires you to breathe in order to be affected, such as the Stinking Cloud spell. Aquatic Transformation. As an action, you can use the cowl to cast the Polymorph spell on yourself, except you can only transform into a Beast that has a swimming speed. Once used, this property can't be used against until the next dawn.
