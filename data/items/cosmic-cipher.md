# Cosmic Cipher

**description**: Cosmic Cipher Utility, epic This pair of sorium rings has a silvery surface with a tinge of shifting colors. They are worn as one on each hand or side-by-side on two fingers. While wearing these rings, you can use an action to expend 1 of their 3 charges to activate the cosmic weave power. The rings disintegrate when you use the last charge.
