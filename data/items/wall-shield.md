# Wall Shield

**description**: Wall Shield Armor (shield) Members of the Crab Clan occasionally use these massive shields alongside crossbows or spears. While their weight makes it difficult to move while wielding one, they are excellent for entrenched holding actions. Wearer has half-cover against ranged attacks, but -10 movement speed and disadvantage on Acrobatics, Athletics, and Stealth. Requires Attunement: Str 15 Acrobatics: Disadvantage Athletics: Disadvantage Stealth: Disadvantage
