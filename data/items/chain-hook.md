# Chain hook

**description**: This weapon consists of a 20-foot, hefty chain that terminates in an iron weight set with a bladed hook. After you throw the chain hook, you can use a bonus action to bring the bladed hook back to your hand.

**properties**:

- Item Type: Weapon
- Properties: Reach, special, thrown
