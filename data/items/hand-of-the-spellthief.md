# Hand of the Spellthief

**description**: Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This magical glove is imbued with arcane glyphs. Glyphcarved. When another creature casts a spell of 7th level or lower that targets only you or makes a spell attack against you, you can use your reaction to attempt to absorb the spell. You gain advantage on your first saving throw against the spell or the spell attack against you is made with disadvantage. If you succeed on your saving throw or the spell attack misses you, the spell has no effect on you and becomes stored in the glove, erasing any other spell stored within it. While a spell is stored in the glove, you gain the following benefits: You gain a +1 bonus to AC and saving throws. When you hit with an unarmed strike using the hand wearing the glove or a weapon held in that hand, you can cast the spell stored in the glove on the target as part of the attack. The spell uses the slot level, spell save DC, and spellcasting ability of the original caster, but is otherwise treated as if you cast the spell. If the spell requires a spell attack, it automatically hits the target. The spell is no longer stored in the glove.
