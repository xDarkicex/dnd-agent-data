# Masterful Makeup Kit

**description**: Masterful Makeup Kit Wondrous Item, Rare Created by a legendary drag queen who mastered the art of transformation, this magic makeup kit allows anyone to put on any type of makeup and face painting imaginable, create tattoos, change their skin color, and live the fantasy. The kit comes with a magically-refillable array of cosmetics and dyes, facial props including noses and horns, and a fierce makeup removal towel. Makeup Mastery. You can spend 10 minutes to put on makeup that visually alters your facial features, and the color and texture of your skin. Your face can appear as the face of a member of any other race with which you share the same basic arrangement of eyes, ears, nose, and mouth. Your skin can appear as the skin of a member of any other race, unless that race has fur, scales, or feathers. Otherwise, the extent of this transformation is up to you and may also include conventional makeup and tattoos. This transformation can only be applied to one creature at a time. Demakeup Resistance. Once applied, makeup from a Masterful Makeup Kit can only be removed with this or another Masterful Makeup Kit, the application of Universal Solvent or Oil of Etherealness , or with a wish spell. Makeup from a Masterful Makeup Kit can also be removed if you die. Using a Masterful Makeup Kit, you can spend 1 minute to remove any makeup from a face, or 10 minutes to remove any makeup from a body. Universal Solvent , Oil of Etherealness , and the wish spell remove any makeup from a Masterful Makeup Kit immediately. Realness. Unless you purposefully wear it so its nature is obvious, makeup from the Masterful Makeup Kit is indistinguishable from real skin. Only a wish spell can reveal your true features and skin color as long as you wear the makeup. The makeup envelops whoever wears it in a faint aura of transmutation magic if targeted by detect magic . Stylish Sunblock. While wearing makeup from the Masterful Makeup Kit, you are immune to sunburn.
