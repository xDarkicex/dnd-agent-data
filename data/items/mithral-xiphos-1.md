# Mithral Xiphos +1

**description**: Weapon, dagger, uncommon This mithral-forged dagger has a straight, leaf-shaped blade, which is excellent for thrusting and piercing between shields in phalanx formations. Attacks made with this weapon have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls. When you make an attack with this weapon, you ignore any AC that the enemy gains from wielding a shield.
