# Slime Bomb

**description**: A slime bomb deals no damage, but coats each creature within the blast radius in a viscous, green slime. A creature coated in slime can’t take the Dash or Disengage actions. Slime can be removed by dousing a creature with a gallon of water or by taking an action to remove most of it.
