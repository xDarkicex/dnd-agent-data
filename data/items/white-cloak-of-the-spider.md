# White Cloak of the Spider

**description**: White Cloak of the Spider Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This white velvet cloak is embroidered with the image of a large black spider sitting in a web. While wearing it, you gain the following benefits: You have resistance to poison damage. You can use an action to cast the spider climb spell. Once used, this property of the cloak can’t be used again until the next dawn. You can use an action to cast the hold person spell (save DC 13). Once used, this property of the cloak can’t be used again until the next dawn.
