# Purifier Tablet

**description**: When this tablet is dropped into up to a pint of liquid, it dissolves and transforms the liquid into clean drinking water. The tablet has no effect on liquids made of synthetic chemicals (such as antitoxins and medical ampoules) or harmful substances such as poison.
