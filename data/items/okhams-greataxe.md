# Okham's Greataxe

**description**: Okham's Greataxe Melee weapon Price: 60,000 gp At first glance this appears to be a simple stick, or the handle of an axe without the blade. It is rumored to have been wielded by Toverus’ acclaimed but notoriously bad-tempered philosopher-warrior. There are no unique features, except the word ‘reason’ is carved into the handle. This weapon can, as a Free Action, manifest a large axe head made entirely of some ethereal substance. You can barely see the axe head when it is moving, but it is as hard as steel despite appearing to be made of air. Okhham’s Greataxe acts as a +2 weapon, providing an additional +2 bonus to Attack Rolls and Damage Rolls, and also adds an additional 1d8 slashing damage on a hit. Once per Long Rest, the axe can be thrown at a target with tremendous accuracy, as the wind itself guides the throw. When thrown in this way the weapon grants an additional +10 bonus to the Attack Roll. Melee. Melee Weapons are used to attack enemies up close in hand-to-hand combat. Heavy. Heavy weapons require both hands to wield and often require significant Strength to use effectively. Heavy melee weapons must use Strength for the Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus. Damage: 1d12+2 Secondary Damage: 1d8
