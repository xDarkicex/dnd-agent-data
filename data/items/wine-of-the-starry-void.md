# Wine of the Starry Void

**description**: Potion, Rare Found largely in the realms of the Court of a Million Stars and in the hands of disciples of the Void, this wine enables you to see the future—for a time. Roll 10d20 and apply each resulting die in any order when ability checks, attack rolls, saving throws, or even death saving throws are required. However, all ten results must be applied before any normal rolls can resume.
