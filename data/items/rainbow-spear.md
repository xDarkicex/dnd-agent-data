# Rainbow Spear

**description**: Rainbow Spear Wondrous item, artifact This artifact once belonged to Mirgal the Betrayer and was among his most prized (and powerful) possessions. Whether he created it himself, or obtained it from his pact with the Yoten, Boða, no one is certain and Mirgal is unlikely to tell the truth. Once in Grimnir, the Vanir defended the spear against any use, yet several times it flared to life on its own and transported unwilling creatures from other worlds to random locations across Grimnir. Even after it was destroyed by Thonir, the spear still brings the unsuspecting to Grimnir. And now, as the witches have mostly reassembled it, they have gained some semblance of control over it. Controlling Portals. A socket on the spear allows a gem to be inserted. Different gems will allow portals to different worlds but most of these secrets have long been lost. After a gem is placed, it takes a bonus action to open a portal. Source & Destination. If the spear is undamaged, the user of the spear simply imagines a location on another plane of existence. A portal opens there and creatures that move through the portal emerge near the Rainbow Spear or a location of the user’s choosing within two miles. The Damaged Spear. Because the spear is broken, any gem placed is consumed within a few minutes. Boða has learned that garnets create portals to the Lost Lands. Emeralds apparently connect to Siddhe's homeland. Chaos Portal. The magicians of Grimnir theorize that the act of lightning striking near the spear can also trigger a portal to open. But without gems to control the source, the portal opens in a completely random location; likewise, the destination in Grimnir is random.
