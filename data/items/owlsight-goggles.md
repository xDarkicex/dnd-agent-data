# Owlsight Goggles

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Component: beast (giant owl) eye “Don’t go out at night or the giant owls will get ya,” has been said by more than one exhausted rakin parent in a mostly-unsuccessful attempt to curb their offspring’s exploratory and mischievous tendencies. Unfortunately for rakin, this isn’t an exaggeration: giant owls are the number one predators of forest-dwelling tanukin. The naming of this acuity-enhancing eyewear is undoubtedly a nod at this cruel twist of nature by the infamous and ungracious artificer, L’Arsène Upin. Owlsight. While wearing these goggles, you have darkvision out to a range of 60 feet and attacking at long range doesn’t impose disadvantage on your ranged weapon attack rolls. In addition, you have advantage on Perception checks made to spot Small or smaller creatures.
