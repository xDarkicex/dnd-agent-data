# Astronomer's Spyglass

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon This spyglass is inlaid with mithral and tiny crystals. Astronomical Insight When you look at the sky through this spyglass, you can identify and chart celestial bodies regardless of the time of day or weather conditions. If you spend 1 hour observing the sky, you also gain these benefits until the next dawn. Time. You know the number of hours to the next sunrise and sunset. Direction. You know the way of each cardinal direction. You have Advantage on Wisdom (Survival) checks made to track and navigate. If you spend 1 hour observing the sky with the spyglass for 5 consecutive days on the same world, you can also determine the length of the world's year, your approximate location on the world, and the length of the world's seasons, including the number of days before the current season changes.
