# Martial Pitchfork

**description**: Melee weapon (martial) The pitchfork is a tool used for harvesting hay, straw, manure, and turning over topsoil. Due to the effort often required when using it, it is also colloquially called "pitchpork". The "Martial" version of it is more expensive, and expressly created to alternate the work in the fields with the defense of the granaries against bandits, wolves, and marauders.
