# Golden Dawn

**description**: Potion, uncommon This amber mead is flecked with edible gold and has a sour taste. When you drink this mead, you immediately reduce your exhaustion level by one. If you don’t have any levels of exhaustion, this mead has no effect. Once consumed, you can’t benefit from golden dawn again until 24 hours have passed.
