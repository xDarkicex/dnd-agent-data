# Arrow of Teleportation

**description**: Weapon (arrow), uncommon An arrow of teleportation is one of a few arrows known as trick arrows. When this arrow is fired from a bow at an unoccupied space within 100 feet of the archer, the archer immediately teleports to its location. The arrow is destroyed on impact.
