# Axespear

**description**: This weapon features an iron-banded wooden haft with an axe blade on one end and a spear point on the other. This weapon is balanced so that the axe head is the primary head. If you utilize this weapon’s double-headed property, the spear deals piercing damage equal to the amount indicated in parenthesis.

- Category: Items
- Item Type: Weapon
- Properties: Double-headed (1d4), two-handed
