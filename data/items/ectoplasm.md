# Ectoplasm

**description**: Ectoplasm Spell catalyst, rare When a ghost or other incorporeal undead is destroyed, there is a chance that it leaves behind a glob of ectoplasm. When you cast the blink spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, for the spell’s duration, you choose whether or not you vanish into the Ethereal Plane at the end of each of your turns, instead of rolling.

- Item Type: Spell catalyst
