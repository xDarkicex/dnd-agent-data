# Storm Lord's Charm

**description**: Storm Lord’s Charm Wondrous item, uncommon This small pin is shaped like a lighting bolt. While wearing this item, whenever you deal lightning damage to a target, you deal an additional 1d8 lighting damage to a different creature of your choice within 5 feet of that target.
