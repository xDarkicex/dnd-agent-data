# Nightcap of Vision

**description**: Nightcap of Vision Wondrous item, uncommon Wearing this nightcap grants you darkvision. You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can’t discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.
