# Candied Spider

**description**: Potion, uncommon As you eat it, the candied spider seems to wriggle on your tongue for the briefest moment before you swallow it. When you consume this food, you gain a climbing speed equal to your walking speed, and you can move up, down, and across vertical surfaces and upside down along ceilings, while leaving your hands free for 1 hour. In addition, you have advantage on saving throws against poison for the duration.
