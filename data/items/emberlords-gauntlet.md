# Emberlord's Gauntlet

**description**: Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This gauntlet is forged from dark steel and set with rubies that hold a fire elemental's essence. While wearing the gauntlet, you have resistance to Fire damage and you gain a +1 bonus to AC if you're not using a shield. Fiery Blows. Unarmed strikes you make using the hand wearing the gauntlet and attacks you make with a weapon held in that hand, deal an extra 1d6 Fire damage on a hit, or an extra 2d6 Fire damage if you have taken Fire damage since the start of your last turn. Fiery Focus. Once per turn, when you make an ability check, attack roll, or saving throw, you can choose to make the roll with advantage. If you do, you take 2d6 Fire damage. You can't use this property if you are immune to Fire damage.
