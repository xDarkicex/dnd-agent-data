# Pestilent Spear

**description**: Weapon (spear), uncommon The head of this spear is deadly sharp, despite the rust and slimy residue on it that always accumulate no matter how well it is cleaned. When you hit a creature with this magic weapon, it must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or contract the sewer plague disease.
