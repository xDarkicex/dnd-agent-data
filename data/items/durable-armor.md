# Durable Armor

**description**: Durable Armor Armor (medium or heavy), varies (requires affinity) This armor is overlaid with a tough, protective coating that is resilient to being damaged. It has the fortified property (see Armor Upgrade Properties). Exceptional. You have a +1 bonus to AC while wearing this armor. Superior. When the armor reaches this grade, it gains the following properties. The bonus to AC increases to +2. The armor gains the hardened property if it did not have it already. Optimum. When the armor reaches its optimum grade, it gains the following properties. The bonus to AC increases to +3. You have resistance to mundane damage while you wear this armor.
