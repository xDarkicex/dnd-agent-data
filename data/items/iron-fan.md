# Iron Fan

**description**: Iron Fan Melee weapon (simple, iron fan) Carried by courtiers and military leaders alike, iron fans or tessen, are specially built for self-defense. However, they are not commonly viewed as weapons and can be openly carried even into formal court chambers or negotiations. Usually made of wood with steel tines, they generally serve as defensive weapons, but wielders can also use them to deliver clubbing blows or to reinforce a punch. Defensive. When you are wielding one or more defensive weapons, increase your AC by 1. You cannot benefit from both a defensive weapon and a shield. Light. A light weapon is small and easy to handle, making it ideal for use when fighting with two weapons. AC: 1 Damage: 1d4 Damage Type: Bludgeoning Properties: Defensive, Light Weight: 1
