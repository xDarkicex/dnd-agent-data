# Elegant Pillow Book

**description**: Elegant Pillow Book Wondrous item, very rare A book detailing the wanderings and philosophical musing of a traveling artist. The names of the locations and people don’t match any in historical record. A creature attuned to this item gains advantage on a Persuasion or Performance check once per long rest in which they read from this pillow book.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: very rare

**publisher**: Edge Studio
