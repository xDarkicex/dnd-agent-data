# Enspelled Leather Armor (Level 6)

**description**: Armor (Leather Armor), Legendary (Requires Attunement) Bound into this armor is a spell of level 6. The spell is determined when the armor is created and must belong to the Abjuration or Illusion school of magic. The armor has 6 charges and regains 1d6 expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the armor, you can expend 1 charge to cast its spell. The spell’s saving throw DC is 17 and its attack bonus is +9.
