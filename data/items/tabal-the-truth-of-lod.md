# Tabal, the Truth of Lod

**description**: Tabal, the Truth of Lod Glaive, Artifact (requires attunement) Crafted by Lod the Entropian Emperor, an elder one who ruled over mortalkind ages ago, this weapon of mythical power was a symbol of trust between Lod and the mortals who served them most fervently. After Lod’s fall and the rise of a new pantheon, one of Lod’s surviving progeny hid this weapon away until such time as a mortal would rise and challenge the impudent gods who dared to strike the Entropian Emperor down. You gain a +4 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. It functions as a Weapon of Warning , deals an extra 1d10 force damage to any target it hits, and it scores a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20. Rift Slash. This weapon has 7 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to spend one charge and create a momentary rift in the fabric of time and space in a 240-foot line that is 10 feet wide in front of you. Each creature in that line must make a DC 18 Strength saving throw, taking 8d8 force damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Creatures that fail the save are also knocked prone. Objects and structures in that line take double damage. The weapon regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. Lod’s Whispers. While attuned to this weapon, you can hear Lod’s whispers in your mind from across time and space. You can speak Deep Speech, you have advantage on all Intelligence (Arcana) and Wisdom (Insight) checks, and you magically know when someone speaks a willing lie in your presence. Hostile aberrations are indifferent toward you and indifferent aberrations are friendly toward you unless you threaten or harm them first. Random Properties. Tabal, the Truth of Lod has the following randomly determined properties: 1 minor beneficial property 1 major beneficial property 1 minor detrimental property 1 major detrimental property
