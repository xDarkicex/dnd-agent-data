# Vent Tape

**description**: This durable adhesive tape is useful for any application that involves sticking two things together. While more creative minds might use large quantities of vent tape for other uses, most people use it for quick repairs and to affix flashlights to blasters in a pinch. A single roll of vent tape contains 60 feet of 2-inch wide tape.
