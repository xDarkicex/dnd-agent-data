# Direct Impact Limiter

**description**: Direct Impact Limiter Mod (armor: any shield except PSD), rare (requires affinity) You gain a +2 bonus to AC against ranged attacks while you wield a shield with this mod. This bonus is in addition to the shield’s normal bonus to AC. In addition, whenever an attacker makes a ranged attack against a target within 5 feet of you, you can use your reaction to become the target of the attack instead.
