# Shovel of Digging

**description**: Shovel of Digging Wondrous item, uncommon This item looks like an ordinary shovel with a 4-footlong wooden shaft and a blade of iron. When you speak its command word, it begins digging by itself at the rate of 10 cubic feet per minute. The shovel can only dig soil or sand and digs for a maximum of 4 hours per day.
