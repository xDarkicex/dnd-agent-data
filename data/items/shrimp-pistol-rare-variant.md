# Shrimp Pistol (rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (pistol), uncommon Component: pouch of aberration claws A biomantic fusion of crustacean and firearm, this magic weapon thrums with stored energy, ready to release a devastating strike like its pistol shrimp namesake. This pistol fires its ammunition at such tremendous velocities that cavitation bubbles form, dealing an extra 1d6 thunder damage to the first target it hits on each of your turns. Cavitation Blast. As an action while you are underwater and holding this weapon, you can release a series of pressure waves from the pistol, causing pockets of cavitation. Each creature in a 15-foot cone must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw, taking 4d8 thunder damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Once this property of the pistol has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn. Misfire. If you roll a 1 on your attack roll with this weapon, the ammunition itself disintegrates, clogging the barrel. The attack automatically misses and you must spend an action to unclog this weapon before it can be used again. Rare variant: Increase the DC to 15, the damage of the Cavitation Blast property to 5d8, and its range to a 20-foot cone. The pistol deals an extra 1d6 thunder damage to each target it hits, instead of the first target on each of your turns.
