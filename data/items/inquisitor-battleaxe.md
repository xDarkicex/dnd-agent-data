# Inquisitor Battleaxe

**description**: Weapon (battleaxe), rare (requires attunement) Holy Fighter. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. Attacks against Fiends, Undeads, Monstrosities or Spellcasters inflict 3 (1d6) extra radiant damage. For the purpose of this, a Spellcaster is defined as any creature that can cast Spells or that has at least one melee or ranged spell attack on its stat block. Aura of Justice. Once per day, as an action, you can invoke a magical bright light in a 30 ft radius. For the next minute, you and every ally within 30 ft that can see the Axe gain a +1 bonus to AC. Sentenced. When you score a critical hit with the Inquisitor Battle Axe, the target is frightened of you until the end of its next turn.
