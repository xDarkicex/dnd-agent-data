# Twilighter (Rare)

**description**: Twilighter Weapon (Halberd), Rare (Requires Attunement by a Creature That Can Cast at Least One Spell) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Life for Death. When you cast a necromancy spell of 1st-level or higher, you gain a number of temporary hit points equal to twice the spell’s level. Progression The Twilighter progresses after the creature attuned to it casts 120 necromancy spells. Once the weapon progresses, it can’t do so again until the attuned creature’s proficiency bonus increases.
