# The Golden Hart's Crown (Uncommon)

**description**: The Golden Hart's Crown Weapon (Longbow), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When interacting with Beasts native to forests, you make any associated Wisdom (Animal Handling) checks with advantage. You can speak a command word to create the illusion of a glorious set of golden antlers growing from your head. The illusion lasts 1 minute or until you end it (no action required). Progression The Golden Hart’s Crown progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, so does the weapon’s rarity.
