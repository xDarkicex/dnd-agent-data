# Telepathic Band

**description**: Telepathic Band Utility, uncommon (requires affinity) This circlet is worn around the head. While wearing it, you can use it to activate the detect thoughts power as an action (save DC 13). As long as you maintain concentration on the power, you can use a bonus action to send a telepathic message to a creature you are focused on. The creature can reply--using a bonus action to do so--while your focus on it continues. While focusing on a creature with detect thoughts , you can spend an action to use the suggestion power (save DC 13) on that creature. Once used, the suggestion property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest.
