# Tome of the Deep

**description**: Tome Of The Deep Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement by a spellcaster) You have resistance to cold damage while attuned to this tome, you can breathe underwater, and you have a swimming speed of 30 feet. The tome has 10 charges, While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more of its charges to cast one of the following spells from it, using your spell save DC: control water (4 charges), create or destroy water (1 charge), maelstrom (5 charges), tidal wave (3 charges), wall of water (3 charges), water breathing (3 charges), water walk (3 charges), watery sphere (4 charges), tsunami (8 charges). The tome regains 1d6 + 4 charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the tome turns to water, pours through your fingers, and is destroyed.
