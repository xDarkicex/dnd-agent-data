# Gulgrim the Sword

**description**: Gulgrim the Sword Weapon (greatsword), legendary (requires attunement) This is a magical greatsword with a +2 to attack rolls and a +2 to damage. It also delivers an additional 13 (2d12) necrotic damage to any Yoten it hits. The soul of a Vanir hero also happens to be trapped within its blade, making it an intelligent item with an Intelligence of 10, a Wisdom of 12, and a Charisma of 13. Gulgrim’s alignment is Neutral Good, it speaks (and understands) Common and can hear and see up to a range of 30 feet. Gulgrim was a valiant warrior, fighting alongside Aldyhn and the others against the Yoten. However, they were mortally wounded on the battlefield as Exodus occurred and dragged elsewhere by other survivors. Prior to their death a ritual was used, ensconcing their soul into the greatsword they once wielded. If Gulgrim sees a Yoten, they begin to shout loudly at it and goads their wielder to attack.
