# Gangrene (Very Rare)

**description**: Gangrene Weapon (Mace), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. As an action, you can touch this weapon to a portion of food no more than 1 cubic foot in volume, instantaneously rotting it. Once used, you can’t use this property again until you finish a long rest. Rot. When you hit a creature with an attack using this weapon, its runes pulse a sickly green and the target takes an extra 1d8 necrotic damage. Fester. When you hit a creature with an attack using this weapon, the target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be unable to regain hit points until the start of your next turn. Progression Gangrene progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, so does the weapon’s rarity. However, in addition to the required increase in proficiency bonus, to progress from very rare to legendary, three creatures must be reduced to 0 hit points by an attack using Gangrene in a single combat.
