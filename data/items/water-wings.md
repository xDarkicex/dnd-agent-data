# Water Wings

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon While a creature with the Mounted trait wears these adorable water wingies, it gains the following properties: Its fastest speed becomes a swimming speed if it does not already have one. It has an additional 10 foot swimming speed while rising toward the surface of the liquid in a straight line. It and one creature riding it can breathe in water.
