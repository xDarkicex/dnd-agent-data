# Dugmore's Wrath

**description**: Weapon (warhammer), very rare (requires attunement by a dwarf) This warhammer belonged to a famed dwarven ranger by the name of Dugmore, known for exploring ever deeper as an advance scout for his people, investigating the deepest caverns, always willing to go just a little farther to make sure he knew what was out there. He has been missing for many months now, and his people fear the worst. You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. It has the thrown property with a normal range of 20 feet and a long range of 60 feet. When you hit with a ranged attack using this weapon, it deals an extra 1d8 damage or, if the target is an aberration, 2d8 damage. Immediately after the attack, the weapon flies back to your hand.
