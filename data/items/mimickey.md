# Mimickey

**description**: Wondrous item, common (socketable) This bone and flesh keyring can be socketed on any weapon or held item. As an action, you can hold the item by the keyring and spin it in a circle, causing the socketed item to shrink to the size of a key. Using an action to spin it while key-sized returns it to its normal size.
