# Splint Chaos Armor

**description**: Splint Chaos Armor Armor (heavy), legendary (requires attunement) This armor swirls with a multiverse of intoxicating colors. While wearing it, any critical hit against you awakens the armor, you roll a d20 to create a random effect. Each time you roll, the new effect replaces any previous effect. Unless otherwise stated, the armor returns to its normal state when you finish a short or long rest. Splint Armor. This armor is made of narrow vertical strips of metal riveted to a backing of leather that is worn over cloth padding. Flexible chain mail protects the joints. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks.
