# Book of the Fallen

**description**: Wondrous Item, legendary (requires attunement by a spellcaster capable of casting 5th-level or higher spells) This ancient tome appears at first glance to be crafted of a pale leather, stitched together at odd angles. Upon closer inspection, however, it becomes clear that both the cover and the vellum pages are made from the skin of humanoids, and the rusty brown ink that lines its pages is their blood. Any creature that reads the book must make a DC 17 Wisdom saving throw or gain a Mindscar (see Join the Fallen). A creature that succeeds on its saving throw becomes immune to this effect. A creature with the Join the Fallen trait that acquires this book other than one that is attuned to it is compelled to preserve it at all cost, but cannot read it or open it willingly. Attuning to this item requires reading from the book each day at midnight for three consecutive nights. While attuned to the Book of the Fallen, the you are considered an ally of any creature with the Join the Fallen trait, and such a creature will obey your every command (which can be delivered telepathically across any distance so long as you have met the creature). Additionally, while attuned to the book you gain the following benefits: You always have the vile incantation *, lacerate *, bloodthorn bonds *, wrack *, thousand needles *, and nociceptive feedback * spells prepared, and can cast them up to a total of 3 times at 5th level without expending a spell slot each day. Your spell save DC increases by 2 for creatures that have been Injured. You gain the Join the Fallen trait and are immune to its effects. Join the Fallen. Whenever you reduce a creature to 0 hit points, inflict an Injury, or score a critical hit, all creatures within 120 feet that can hear or see the target of its attack must succeed on a DC 8 Wisdom saving throw or gain a Mindscar. A creature who gains three Mindscars begins to lose its mind. When it finishes a long rest, it becomes one of the Fallen. Mindscars may be removed by any process that can remove a curse, although mental trauma will still remain. If the mental trauma is overcome, the Mindscars are removed as well. You gain the Scar the Mind action: As an action, you may force one creature you can see within 60 feet to make a DC 10 Wisdom saving throw or gain a Mindscar as per the Join the Fallen trait.
