# Venomous Draught of Healing

**description**: Potion, very rare You shrug off the effects of short-term madness and regain hit points as a potion of supreme healing (10d4 + 20), but this potion’s sickly green liquid entices you to drink more than you need. With each drink, a character must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw. On a failed roll, the character can only take actions that further its goal of finding and drinking another draught. This effect persists until the character makes a successful saving throw, or until 10 minutes have passed. The DC to resist drinking another draught increases by +1 for each failed saving throw, to a maximum of DC 20.
