# Atharak

**description**: Halflings use their incredible agility to overcome their lack of strength, and this strategy extends to their weapon choices. An atharak is one such weapon; it appears to be nothing more than a length of braided leather and rope with two hollowed-out stones tied to the ends, but in the hands of a trained halfling warrior, it can become a whirlwind of stinging attacks. Atharaks use the weapon profile of a whip. However, they deal bludgeoning damage instead of slashing damage.
