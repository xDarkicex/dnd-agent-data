# Iron-Hardened Maul

**description**: Iron-Hardened Maul Weapon (Maul), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This cracked and battle-worn maul appears mundane until the moment of impact, bathing its target in pale blue energy. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. In addition, the weapon deals an extra 1d6 force damage on a hit. When you attack a creature with this weapon and roll a 20 on the attack roll, the target must succeed on a DC 17 Strength saving throw or suffer a random effect (roll on the table below) until the end of their next turn. d4 Effect 1 The target can take an action or bonus action on their turn, but not both, and can’t take reactions. 2 The target is knocked prone. 3 The target has disadvantage on attack rolls. 4 The target’s speed is halved.
