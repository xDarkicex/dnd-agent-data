# Ring of Hidden Light

**description**: Ring, Legendary (requires attunement) The Ring of Hidden Light is a unique magical artifact, crafted by the same dwarves who created the Crowns of Shadow. When they made those devices for Izrador, they secretly crafted the ring from the leftover scraps of material as a way to fight back against the power of the Crowns. The Ring of Hidden Light is unaffected by the dispelling and magic item draining powers of a black mirror. It is treated as a nonmagical item for the purposes of any and all abilities that detect, identify, or scry upon magic items. In addition, while wearing this ring, you have advantage on saving throws against magic and to reduce the damage taken by the destruction of a black mirror. Further, while attuned to a wielder, the Ring of Hidden Light has the ability to disrupt a black mirror. While a character attuned to the Ring of Hidden Light is within a black mirror’s radius of effect the black mirror reduces the level of spells it can automatically dispel or counterspell by one (i.e. a blood mirror between 11-­30 years old can only automatically dispel or counterspell spells of level 3 or lower). As well, any ability checks made for the black mirror to dispel or counterspell spells of a higher level are made at disadvantage. Finally, when a character attuned to both the Ring of Hidden Light and the Crown of Shadow successfully hits a black mirror with an attack, the black mirror is automatically destroyed.
