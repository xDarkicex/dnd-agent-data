# Staff of the Observer

**description**: Staff of the Observer Staff, Very Rare (requires attunement by a sorcerer, warlock, or wizard) The secretive wizard order of the Fateweavers seek to predict, alter, and ultimately control the future. In their bid for magical supremacy, the Fateweavers use divination and powerful rituals of prognostication to find sources of ancient power and uncover hidden truths about the fabric of the cosmos and the warring gods that seek to dominate it. A mage reaching the rank of Observer of Fate within the Fateweavers may be entrusted with a Staff of the Observer. This staff can be wielded as a magic quarterstaff that grants a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with it. While holding it, you gain a +1 bonus to Armor Class, saving throws, and spell attack rolls. The staff has 10 charges for the following properties. It regains 1d8 + 2 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the staff retains its +1 bonus to attack and damage roll but loses all other properties. On a 20, the staff regains 1d4 + 1 charges. Fateweaving. Whenever you make an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can expend 1 or more charges, up to your proficiency bonus, and add that number to the roll (no action required). Spells. While holding this staff, you can use an action to expend 1 or more of its charges to cast one of the following spells from it, using your spell save DC and spell attack bonus: arcane eye (4 charges), augury (2 charges), clairvoyance (3 charges), detect magic (1 charge), detect thoughts (2 charges), divination (4 charges), identify (1 charge), see invisibility (2 charges), or scrying (5 charges).
