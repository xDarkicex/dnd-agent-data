# Sunshard Cortex

**description**: Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This helmet is crafted from an alloy of silver and meteoric iron and has a visor made of a rare crystal. Radiant Visor You gain the following benefits while wearing the helmet: Blindsight. You have Blindsight with a range of 30 feet. Radiation Resistance. You have resistance to Radiant damage and you have Advantage on saves against Radiation Sickness and Acute Radiation Effects . Enhanced Vision As a Bonus Action, you can gain your choice of one of these benefits. Truesight. You gain Truesight with a range of 30 feet for 1 minute. Heatsense. You can use the heat signature of a non-Construct, non-Undead creature within 30 feet to locate its vitals. You learn its current and maximum Hit Points and know whether it has any Immunities, Resistances, or Vulnerabilities, and if the creature has any, you know what they are. Moreover, you have Advantage on the next attack you make against it before the end of your next turn. Once used, this property can't be used again until the helmet has been worn in direct sunlight for 1 hour.
