# Cloak of the Tallfolk

**description**: Cloak of the Tallfolk Wondrous item, rare While wearing this item the user may squeeze through spaces one size smaller than it without all the standard penalties. They do not have to spend extra movement and they do not have disadvantage on attack rolls and Dexterity saving throws. Enemy creatures still get advantage when attacking them in the smaller space, however.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: rare
