# Arrow of Darkness

**description**: Weapon (arrow), uncommon When a target takes damage from this magic arrow, the arrow creates a 15-foot-radius sphere of magical darkness centered on the target that lasts for 1 minute. The darkness spreads around corners. A creature with darkvision can't see through this darkness, and nonmagical light can't illuminate it.
