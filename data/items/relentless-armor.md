# Relentless Armor

**description**: Relentless Armor Armor (any), varies (requires affinity) This armor protects the wearer against being forcibly moved or obstructed. While you wear it, the first time each turn that a creature makes an opportunity attack against you, you can use your reaction to impose disadvantage on the attack roll. Exceptional. When the armor reaches this grade, it gains the following properties. You have a +1 bonus to AC while wearing this armor. If an effect moves you against your will along the ground, you can use your reaction to reduce the distance you are moved by up to 10 feet. Superior. When the armor reaches this grade, t he bonus to AC increases to +2. Optimum. When the armor reaches its optimum grade, it gains the following properties. The bonus to AC increases to +3. While wearing this armor, you gain the effects of the unfetter power.
