# Boots of Trekking

**description**: Boots of Trekking Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) While you wear these boots, your base walking speed increases by 10 feet, it costs you no extra movement to move through difficult terrain, and difficult terrain can’t slow your group’s travel.
