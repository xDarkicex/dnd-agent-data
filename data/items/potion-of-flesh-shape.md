# Potion of Flesh Shape

**description**: Potion of Flesh Shape Potion, uncommon When you pour this pinkish liquid over flesh no more than 5 feet in any dimension, for 1 minute you may shape the flesh into any shape that suits your purpose. After the time expires, the flesh retains whatever shape it was formed into. The flesh can return to its original shape through the lesser restoration spell or other magic.
