# Paradigm Shard

**description**: Paradigm Shard Focus, very rare (requires affinity by a channeler or forger) This 2-inch oblong crystal is clear with a few tiny golden flecks along its surface. While wearing it, whenever you use a prime power, you can use a bonus action on the same turn to use that power a second time.
