# Blood Cartridge

**description**: All bloodwork weapons require a blood cartridge to activate the angst and work. Any weapon with the reloading property needs their blood cartridge changed in each reload. For the loading weapons, the energy required for the projectile to be shot is so large that they need a new blood cartridge in each loading. A blood cartridge holds 2 hit points worth of blood, changing the blood cartridge is done as a part of the reloading or loading, depending on the weapon.
