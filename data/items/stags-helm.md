# Stag's Helm

**description**: Stag's Helm Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) This striking helmet is crafted to resemble the skull of a mighty stag; although made from bone, the antlers and helm are as strong as metal. When worn in a forest or similar terrain, the helm greatly enhances your eyesight and hearing, granting you advantage to Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight or sound. Insightful Shot. Once per day, you can activate the helm as a bonus action to gain advantage on your next ranged attack. A worshipper of Erastil can use this ability up to three times per day instead.
