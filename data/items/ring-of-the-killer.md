# Ring of the Killer

**description**: Ring, rare (requires attunement) This ring appears to have been forged from tiny daggers, which threaten to pierce the skin of the wearer when attuned. It can hold up to two charges, and gains one charge whenever you kill a creature using a Death Blow. As long as the ring has at least one charge, the DC to resist your Death Blow increases by 2. As an action, you may spend a charge to have the ring do one of the following: Cast invisibility on you at 2nd level Cast dimension door Make an attack as though you had cast vampiric touch at 5th level
