# Seraphim's Crossbow

**description**: Weapon (Any Crossbow), Rare This magic crossbow fires bolts of radiant energy. It requires no ammunition, its damage is Radiant instead of Piercing, and it doesn't have the loading property. The crossbow has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. When you attack with the crossbow, you can expend 1 charge to gain advantage on the attack roll and cause one of the following effects on a hit: Guiding. The attack deals an extra 1d8 Radiant damage and the first attack made against the target before the end of your next turn has advantage. Healing. If the target is a creature, it doesn't take damage from the attack and instead magically regains hit points equal to 1d8 plus the damage the attack would have dealt.
