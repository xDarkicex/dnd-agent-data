# Collapsible Frame

**description**: Collapsible Frame Mod (weapon), uncommon When you holster or sheathe a weapon with this mod, it collapses down to a third of its normal size. The weapon regains its normal size when drawn. You can’t use the weapon while it's in its collapsed state. While holstered or sheathed, a one-handed weapon has the concealed property if it does not already possess it.
