# Black Shield of Grisal

**description**: Shield, rare The black shield bears the silver shield symbol of the Black Canton of Grisal. While holding this shield, you gain a +1 bonus to AC, and you have advantage on saving throws against effects that reduce your hit point maximum, such as a specter’s Life Drain or the harm spell.
