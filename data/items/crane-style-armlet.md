# Crane Style Armlet

**description**: Crane Style Armlet Wondrous item, rare This rose-gold armlet depicts a crane with its wings outstretched. While wearing this wondrous item, you gain a +1 bonus to the attack and damage rolls of your unarmed strikes. In addition, you move with effortless grace. You can use a bonus action on each of your turns to take the Dash or Disengage action.
