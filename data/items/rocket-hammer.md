# Rocket Hammer

**description**: Only the dwarves would conceive of a weapon so unwieldy as the rocket hammer. As its name implies, it is very much a conventional warhammer with a rocket thruster built into the head to magnify its bludgeoning potential. Once per turn, when you make an Attack with this weapon, you can engage its thrusters to deal an additional 1d4 bludgeoning damage on a hit.
