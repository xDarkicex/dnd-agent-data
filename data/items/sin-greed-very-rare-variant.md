# Sin: Greed (very rare variant)

**description**: Item modifier, uncommon (optional or enhanced attunement) The Task. The fiend requests a minor task, such as stealing a small item, lying to your party about how much treasure you found, or acquiring a certain lost (and potentially cursed) treasure. OA or EA: Greed. This item has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. While it is on your person, you can expend 1 charge to cast the locate object spell. When you cast the spell using this property, you can target only the nearest precious metals or gems not currently worn or carried by a creature. If there are no such treasures within range of the spell, you become demotivated, gaining disadvantage on all Charisma and Wisdom checks for the next hour, and this property can’t be used again until the next dawn. Rare variant: The Greed property has no charges: you can cast the locate object spell at will with the same limitations. The fiend asks for a medium task, such as purloining a family’s sacred heirloom or tricking an honest salesman into buying a cursed item. Very rare variant: The Greed property has no charges: you can cast the locate object spell at will, and it’s no longer limited in what it can target, but you can still become demotivated if you name a target not within range of the spell. The fiend asks for a major task, such as stealing a piece of royal jewellery or committing arson to benefit from insurance fraud.
