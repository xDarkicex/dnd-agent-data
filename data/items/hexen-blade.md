# Hexen Blade

**description**: Weapon (shortsword), rare (requires attunement) The colorful surface of this sleek adamantine shortsword exhibits a perpetually shifting, iridescent sheen. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The sword has 5 charges and regains 1d4 + 1 charges daily at dawn. While holding it, you can use an action and expend 1 or more of its charges to cast one of the following spells from it, using spell save DC 15: disguise self (1 charge), hypnotic pattern (3 charges), or mirror image (2 charges).
