# Ring of the Water Dancer

**description**: Ring, rare (requires attunement) This thin braided purple ring is fashioned from a single piece of coral. While wearing this ring, you can stand on and move across any liquid surface as if it were solid ground. In addition, while walking atop any liquid, your movement speed increases by 10 feet and you gain a +1 bonus to your AC.

- Item Type: Ring
