# Advanced Microcomputer

**description**: Microcomputer Augment (head), varies (requires affinity) This uncommon augment is a tiny digital device that functions as a personal computer and portable media device. You can view and input data through an augmented reality display that only you can see. As an action, you can connect to an unsecured display either wirelessly or through a built-in chip port. Advanced Microcomputer. The rare version of this augment starts as a Rating 1 device and is customizable up to Rating 4 (see “ Computers and Virtualities ”).
