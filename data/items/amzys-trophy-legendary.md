# Amzy's Trophy (Legendary)

**description**: Amzy's Trophy Weapon (Dagger), Legendary (Requires Attunement) Your hit point maximum increases by 5 while you are attuned to this weapon. The weapon deals an extra 2d8 piercing damage on a hit. This weapon ignores all resistance and immunity to piercing damage. When you score a critical hit with this weapon, the attack deals an extra 2d8 piercing damage. Additionally, on a critical hit, you can activate a Celestial Shield option. You can also activate a Celestial Shield option as a bonus action, without needing to score a critical hit. Once you have activated a Celestial Shield option this way, you can’t do so again until you finish a short or long rest. Celestial Shield (Self). You gain a +2 bonus to AC until the end of your next turn. Celestial Shield (Them). You form a protective shield around another creature you can see within 15 feet, granting them a +2 bonus to AC until the end of your next turn. Celestial Shield (Us). You form a protective shield around yourself and one other creature you can see within 30 feet. You each gain a +2 bonus to AC until the end of your next turn. Celestial Shield (We). You may form a protective shield around yourself and up to 2 other creatures you can see within 60 feet. You each gain a +3 bonus to AC until the end of your next turn.
