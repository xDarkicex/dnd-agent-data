# Dagger of Corrupting Shadows

**description**: Weapon (dagger), rare (requires attunement) This brass-hilted ritual dagger is fitted with a wavy blade of razor-sharp steel. Stories tell of nigh unimaginable pain inflicted by its wielders, as its victims find their very soul rent by the wounds it inflicts. Injuries inflicted by this dagger have their recovery times increased by half their normal time, and the DC to treat them increases by 2. Corrupting Shadows. Once per day as a bonus action, you may cause the blade to become a hazy shadow, gaining a +2 bonus to attack rolls with the dagger for 1 minute. While this effect lasts, the blade deals an additional 1d8 points of necrotic damage, and whenever a creature is hit by an attack made with the weapon, it must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or be unable to regain hit points until the end of your next turn. Additionally, each time you reduce a creature to 0 hit points while this effect is active, the dagger gains a +10 bonus to its next damage roll. If the creature was killed by a Death Blow, it turns to dust, leaving behind any items it wore or carried.
