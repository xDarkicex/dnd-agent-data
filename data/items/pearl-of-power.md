# Pearl of Power

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster) While this pearl is on your person, you can take a Magic action to regain one expended spell slot of level 3 or lower. Once you use the pearl, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
