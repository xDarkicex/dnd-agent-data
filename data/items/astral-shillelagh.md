# Astral Shillelagh

**description**: Weapon (Club), Rare (Requires Attunement) A two-foot length of black ironwood formed of two equal-length, connecting pieces. When separated, the two sections remain attached by an unbreakable gossamer thread of astral energy. This magic weapon has two forms: a club and a nunchaku. The nunchaku is a simple melee weapon with the two-handed property, and it deals 2d4 bludgeoning damage + your Strength modifier on a hit. As a bonus action, you can transform the weapon from one form to another. This weapon has 3 charges, and regains all expended charges when you finish a long rest. On a hit, you can expend 1 charge to deal an additional 3d6 force damage. Encounter Ideas Lunar Trinket. A stranded master thief from the Astral Plane stole this weapon from a vengeful silver dragon and is now desperate to shift the blame. Mummy’s Gift. In a cave on a windswept mountain sits the body of a self-mummified monk, still holding this weapon. He will grant it to any warrior who can best him in single combat. Separated at Death. This weapon was crafted by twin druids who died on the same day but in different lands. Each was buried with one half of the whole.
