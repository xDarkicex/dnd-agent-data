# Birch's Beak

**description**: Weapon (Warpick), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) A small wooden bird perches on the tip of this magic warpick. Nature's Call. When you attack a creature with this warpick, you can use a bonus action to cause the warpick to produce a distracting bird's whistle. The target must succeed on a DC 13 Intelligence saving throw or you have advantage on your attack roll. Bird's Eye. As an action, you can cause the warpick's wooden bird to come alive. The bird functions as the Arcane Eye spell, except it is visible, can move up to 60 feet when you move it, and lasts for only 10 minutes (no concentration required), after which the bird vanishes. The wooden bird regrows on the warpick each day at dawn.
