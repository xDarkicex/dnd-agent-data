# Cloak of the Grove Guardian

**description**: Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement by a druid) This magnificent cloak is fashioned of living leaves in a dazzling array of green hues. While wearing this cloak, you are always under the effects of the speak with animals spell, and you gain a +3 bonus to spell attack rolls and to the spell save DC of your druid spells. In addition, if you aren’t wearing armor, your base Armor Class is 11 + your Dexterity modifier + your Wisdom modifier. As an action, you can magically animate up to two trees you can see within 60 feet of you. An animated tree has the same statistics as a treant, except it has Intelligence and Charisma scores of 1, it can’t speak, and it only has the Slam action option. An animated tree acts as your ally. The tree remains animate for 1 day or until it dies; until you fall unconscious or are more than 120 feet from the tree; or until you take a bonus action to turn it back into an inanimate tree. The tree then takes root if possible. Once you use this property, you can’t use it again until the next dawn.
