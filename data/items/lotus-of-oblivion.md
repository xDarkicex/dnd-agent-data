# Lotus of Oblivion

**description**: Lotus of Oblivion Wondrous item, Legendary Myth has it that a magic fruit exists that can cure any disease and heal any wound, but whose unimaginable taste can make anyone who eats it forget who they are. Many have sought out the Lotus of Oblivion only to lose themselves in its otherworldly sweetness. Aspiring heroes who cannot resist the magic of the lotus soon abandon everything to spend their lives under a lotus tree and indulge in pleasure. The Sweetest Fruit. You can spend one minute to eat a Lotus of Oblivion. Doing so grants you several benefits. You are cured of all diseases, poison, and any condition that lowers your hit point maximum. You also regain 1 hit point at the start of each of your turns (10 hit points each minute) and your severed body members, if any, are restored after 2 minutes. If you have the severed part and hold it to the stump, the magic of the lotus instantaneously causes the limb to knit to the stump. Additionally, you gain inspiration and become immune to disease, poison, and being frightened. These benefits last for 24 hours. Curse of the Lotophagus. Each time you eat a Lotus of Oblivion, there is a 10 percent cumulative chance that you will become cursed and magically addicted to its sweetness. While cursed, you forget your ideals and bonds, and you must consume at least one lotus every 24 hours or have disadvantage on all attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws. A greater restoration, remove curse , or similar effect ends the curse on you but does not reset the chance to become addicted again.
