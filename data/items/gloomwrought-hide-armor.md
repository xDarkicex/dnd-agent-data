# Gloomwrought Hide Armor

**description**: Gloomwrought Hide Armor Armor (Medium), Rare (Requires Attunement) This intricate grayscale armor was forged in the Shadowfell and is infused with that plane’s gloom. While you’re wearing this armor, you have advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the frightened condition on yourself. This armor has 3 charges. You can expend a charge to cast the Calm Emotions spell (save DC 15)from the armor. This armor regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. Hide Armor. This crude armor consists of thick furs and pelts. It is commonly worn by barbarian tribes, evil humanoids, and other folk who lack access to the tools and materials needed to create better armor.
