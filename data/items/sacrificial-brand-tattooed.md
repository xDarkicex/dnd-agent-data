# Sacrificial Brand (Tattooed)

**description**: Sacrificial Brand Eldritch Carving, uncommon, requires special attunement The carving, notorious among hunters and commonly dubbed the "Brand of Death," is a harbinger of an early demise for those burdened with its curse. Nevertheless, Steinhardt and Martyr Tulio—valiant souls undaunted by fear—etched the eldritch sigil into their own skin willingly. By bearing the ominous symbol, they sought to lure the beasts closer to them during the Hunt, granting their beleaguered brethren moments of respite amidst the chaos. During the three days of the Blood Moon, all evil-aligned creatures within Luyarnha gain advantage on Perception or Survival checks to locate creatures with this carving. If they are within 120 feet of a creature bearing the carving, they know their exact location and receive a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls against them.
