# Noble Taster’s Spork

**description**: Noble Taster’s Spork Wondrous Item, Uncommon Crafted by court wizards to protect nobles from assassination, this magic utensil is an indispensable tool for any self-respecting food taster, as well as for adventurers who dare sample unusual and exotic delicacies. This spork has 3 charges. While holding it, you can expend 1 charge as an action to cast the detect poison and disease spell from it. The spork regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. You have advantage on all saving throws related to foodborne poisons and diseases from any food you eat using this spork. Additionally, you have resistance to poison damage from eating poisoned food with this spork. Item Variant: Royal Taster’s Spork At the gamemaster’s discretion, a version of this item that is fit to taste the food of kings and queens may be found. The Royal Taster’s Spork is very rare, allows the wielder to cast detect poison and disease will, and makes the wielder immune to disease, poison, and the poisoned condition as long as the spork is on their person. I can’t really tell!” Her smile widens as she turns to face you. “Well, that’s fascinating, don’t you think?” she asks, eyeing you curiously through the spork’s tines.
