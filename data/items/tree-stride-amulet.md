# Tree Stride Amulet

**description**: Tree Stride Amulet Requires attunement by an undead While wearing it, you can use an action to expend one charge and cast the tree stride spell from it. You can also bring along one creature of your size or smaller when you move between trees using that spell. You must be grappling the creature to do so. This amulet has 3 charges, and regains 1 expended charge daily at dawn.
