# Boutonniere of Bewildering

**description**: Boutonniere of Bewildering Wondrous item, uncommon This cluster of tiny blue roses is ever-blooming and needs no sunlight or water. While you wear this accessory fastened to your clothing, tied around your wrist, or pinned in your hair, the world takes on a slightly hazy, sparkling quality. Everyone around you seems charming and beautiful, and you find yourself laughing and talking more than normal. You have advantage on Charisma checks, and you have disadvantage on saving throws against charm effects and attempts to influence your mind.
