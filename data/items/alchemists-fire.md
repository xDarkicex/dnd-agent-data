# Alchemist's Fire

**description**: Alchemist's Fire (flask) Adventuring gear This sticky, adhesive fluid ignites when exposed to air. As an action , you can throw this flask up to 20 feet, shattering it on impact. Make a ranged Attack against a creature or object, treating the alchemist's fire as an improvised weapon . On a hit, the target takes 1d4 fire damage at the start of each of its turns. A creature can end this damage by using its action to make a DC 10 Dexterity check to extinguish the flames. Damage: 1d4 Damage Type: Fire Properties: Improvised Weapon Range: 20 Weight: 1
