# Saw of Cutting

**description**: Saw of Cutting Wondrous item, uncommon This is a regular hand saw and is about 2 feet long. As an action, you can speak the saw’s command word and for the next 10 minutes you can use the saw to cut through any non-magical wood, metal, or stone, at the rate of 1 foot per minute. Once used, this property of the saw can’t be used again until the next dawn.
