# Clanbreaker (Common)

**description**: Clanbreaker Weapon (Light Hammer), Common (Requires Attunement) While wielding this magic weapon, you have advantage on ability checks to understand and bridge cultural gaps and to convince people to put aside their hatreds. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls when attacking dwarves. Progression Clanbreaker was created to destroy the repressive, hateful leaders of its maker’s society. The weapon isn’t sentient but knows when it faces people of similar disposition. Using Clanbreaker to slay a powerful figure whose hatred destroys the joy of the people around them increases the weapon’s rarity. If Clanbreaker’s wielder uses nonviolent methods to cause such a figure to see the error of their ways and change their course, this also increases the weapon’s rarity.
