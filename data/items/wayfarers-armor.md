# Wayfarer's Armor

**description**: Armor (Any), Common (Requires Attunement) This magical armor is enchanted to be as comfortable and supple as possible. While wearing it, you have advantage on ability checks and saving throws you make to avoid or end the Exhaustion condition on yourself. Wayfarer's Boon. You can cast one of the following cantrips: Druidcraft , Guidance , or Light . Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
