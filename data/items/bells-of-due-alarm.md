# Bells of Due Alarm

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon This trio of bells warns you of intruders. You can arrange the bells up to 500 feet apart from each other, and can use your action to speak a command word to cast the alarm spell, warding the area between the bells.

**properties**:

- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: uncommon
