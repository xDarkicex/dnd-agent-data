# Flute of Faerie

**description**: Flute of Faerie Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) You must be proficient with wind instruments to use this flute. If you play the flute as an action, you summon fey creatures that appear in unoccupied spaces that you can see within range. Roll on the following table to determine what appears: d10 Fey 1 8 blink dogs 2 8 boggles * 3 8 pixies 4 8 sprites 5 4 satyrs 6 4 darklings * 7 2 dryads 8 2 quicklings * 9 1 darkling elder * 10 1 sea hag * These creatures are from Volo’s Guide to Monsters. A summoned creature disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or after an hour. The summoned creatures are friendly to you and your companions. They obey any verbal commands that you issue to them (no action required by you). Once used, this property of the flute can’t be used again until the next dawn.
