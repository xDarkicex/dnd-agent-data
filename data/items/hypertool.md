# Hypertool

**description**: Hypertool Utility, rare (requires affinity by a channeler or forger) This thick glove has an interwoven sorium mesh. It can be worn on any hand. As a bonus action, you can activate the glove, which emits an adaptable energy construct that functions as a versatile multi-tool. While the glove is activated, you can use it as if you were holding a set of basic tools that may be required when making a Wisdom (Mechanics) skill check. It can also perform the functions of cybersmith’s tools, digital technician’s tools, gunsmith’s tools, and shipwright’s tools. The multi-tool emits bright light in a 5-foot radius and dim light for an additional 5 feet. While using the multi-tool, you can’t use that hand for other purposes. The glove also has 3 charges. As an action, you can expend a charge and use the glove to activate the quick repair power. The glove regains 1d3 expended charges when you finish a long rest.
