# Bottled Fiend

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare This large crystal bottle holds an imp trapped in a fuming yellow liquid. The flask is impervious to damage and while within the flask, the imp is Incapacitated, has a speed of 0, and can't be affected by attacks, spells, or other magical effects. The imp has 10 hit points and regains all hit points daily at dawn but is otherwise unable to regain hit points. If the imp is reduced to 0 hit points, it disappears and the flask shatters. Infernal Inhalation. As an action, you can lift the flask's stopper and inhale the fumes to gain your choice of one of the following benefits for 1 hour: Darkvision. You have Darkvision out to a range of 60 feet that isn't impeded by magical darkness. Invisibility. As an action, you can magically turn Invisible until you attack or until your concentration ends (as if concentrating on a spell). Magic Resistance. You have advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. Whenever you use this property, the imp takes 1d6 Psychic damage that can't be negated or reduced. Bottoms Up. As an action, you can drink the flask's contents to gain all three benefits from inhaling the fumes for the next 8 hours. When you do so, the bottle shatters and the imp is freed and appears in a space within 5 feet of it.
