# Light Ranged Weapon

**description**: Light Ranged Weapon Ranged weapon Price: 50 gp A medium weapon used to attack from a distance. (See rules for weapons in the Combat section) Ranged. Ranged weapons are used to attack enemies at a distance. These weapons can be either shot like a bow and arrow or crossbow, or thrown like a spear or javelin. All ranged weapons use Dexterity for both the Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus. Light. Light weapons can be wielded with finesse in one hand, leaving the other hand free to carry other essentials, like a torch or a shield. Small and light melee weapons can use either Strength (Str) or Dexterity (Dex) for the Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus, whichever is higher. Damage: 1d6
