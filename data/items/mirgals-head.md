# Mirgal’s Head

**description**: Mirgal’s Head Wondrous item, very rare This iron bust is actually Mirgal’s head. It has no inherent magical properties but may be restored to ‘life’ later, after which Mirgal is able to hear and see whatever the person who carries it sees and hears.
