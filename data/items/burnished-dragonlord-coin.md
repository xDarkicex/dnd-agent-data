# Burnished Dragonlord Coin

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare This burnished golden coin carries the face of Xander Huorath on one side, and an engraving of the legendary ship Ultros on the reverse. The coin emits a faint golden light. Anyone who possesses a Dragonlord Coin can understand the Draconic language.
