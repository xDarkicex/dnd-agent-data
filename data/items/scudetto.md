# Scudetto

**description**: Armor (shield) This small parade shield bears the emblems of the city it belongs to, and it's a symbol of pride for those enthusiasts who follow the Draconian Football game. Draconian Football Heraldry : you gain a +1 bonus to all Charisma checks against the inhabitants of the city to which the shield is consecrated.
