# Figurine of Wondrous Power, Lapis Camel

**description**: Wondrous item, rare Lapis Camel (Rare). This lapis camel can become a camel. It has 24 charges, and each hour or portion thereof it spends in camel form costs 1 charge. While it has charges, you can use it as often as you wish. When it runs out of charges, it reverts to a figurine and can’t be used again until 7 days have passed, when it regains all its charges. While in camel form, the lapis camel has a blue tint to its fur, and it can spit globs of acid at a creature that attacks it or its rider. This spit works like the acid splash spell (save DC 9).
