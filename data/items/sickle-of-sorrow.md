# Sickle of Sorrow

**description**: Sickle of Sorrow Weapon (sickle), rare Damage: 1d6+2 piercing Weight: 2 lbs. Properties: Light You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Aura of Sorrow. As a bonus action, you can speak the command word to cause the sickle to emanate dark melancholy for 1 minute. Any creature that hits you with a melee attack or ends its turn within 10 feet of you must make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw, taking 2d8 psychic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. This feature can be used a number of times per day equal to your proficiency bonus.
