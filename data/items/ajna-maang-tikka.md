# Ajna Maang Tikka

**description**: Ajna Maang Tikka Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) This beaded headpiece attaches to your hair and hangs down in the center of your forehead. When attuned to this item, you gain a +1 increase to your Intelligence and Wisdom scores, and you have advantage on Intelligence (Investigation), Wisdom (Insight), and Wisdom (Perception) checks.
