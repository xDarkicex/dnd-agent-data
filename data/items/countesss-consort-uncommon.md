# Countess's Consort (Uncommon)

**description**: Countess's Consort Weapon (Rapier), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) When you attune to this magic weapon, your Constitution score permanently decreases by 2. When you finish a long rest while attuned to it, you gain temporary hit points equal to the number of hit points you lost from all of this weapon’s decreases to your Constitution score. Whenever this weapon kills a creature with a CR equal to at least half your level (rounded up), you gain temporary hit points equal to your level. Progression Whenever you kill a creature with a CR equal to your character level or higher, you can choose to have Countess’s Consort progress, immediately gaining its new effects and reducing your Constitution score by the indicated amount.
