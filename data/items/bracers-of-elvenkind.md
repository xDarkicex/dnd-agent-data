# Bracers of Elvenkind

**description**: Bracers of Elvenkind Wondrous item, uncommon These leather bracers are inscribed with Elvish runes of primal magic. While you wear them, you ignore difficult terrain created by dense foliage. In addition, you have advantage on Wisdom (Animal Handling) checks and Charisma (Persuasion) checks made when dealing with woodland creatures.
