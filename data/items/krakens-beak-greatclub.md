# Kraken's Beak Greatclub

**description**: Weapon (club or greatclub), very rare Component: pouch of dragon teeth Carved from the tooth of a giant sea snake, this huge club sports a thriving ecosystem of halophilic moss, barnacles, and seaweed. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Sea Serpent’s Might. As an action, you can slam the tooth into the ground or thrust it into a body of water, creating a shockwave in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in the area must make a DC 16 Strength saving throw. On a failure, a creature takes 5d10 thunder damage and is knocked prone. On a success, it takes half as much damage and isn’t knocked prone. Once this property of the club has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn. Legendary variant, Kraken’s Beak: Change the component to monstrosity (kraken) beak. Increase the attack and damage bonus to +3. Replace the Sea Serpent’s Might property with the Kraken’s Storm property. Kraken’s Storm. As an action, you can swing the beak forwards, creating a crackling lightning storm in a 40-foot cone. Each creature in the area must make a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw, taking 6d10 lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Once this property of the club has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
