# Suneater Axe Greataxe (very rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (any axe), rare (requires attunement) Component: pouch of celestial teeth When the mighty wolf Fenrir threatened the world, the dwarves fashioned an impossibly strong thread with which to keep him bound. This thread, known as Gleipnir, was crafted from the sound of a cat’s footfall, the roots of a mountain, and the breath of a fish, amongst other things. With the leftover material, the Weapons of Binding were made. The suneater axe is one such weapon, a depiction of the wolf Sköll, who the dwarves believe will one day devour the sun. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The axe has 6 charges and regains 1d6 expended charges daily at dawn. Suneater. As an action, you can expend 2 charges to cast the darkness spell. When you cast the spell in this way, you can choose to expend 1 additional charge. If you do, you can see through the magical darkness of the spell while holding the suneater axe. Gleipnir. When you make an attack with this weapon, you can expend 1 charge to launch the axe’s blade from the wolf’s mouth, attached to the weapon by a long thread. Instead of a melee weapon attack, make a ranged weapon attack against a target within 30 feet of you, using your Strength modifier for the attack and damage rolls. On a hit, the target suffers the attack’s normal effects and it must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or become grappled (escape DC 15) as the thread wraps itself around it. A creature that fails this saving throw by 5 or more is restrained while it is grappled in this way. Huge or larger creatures have disadvantage on the saving throw. The grappled creature or a creature within reach of it can attempt a DC 15 Strength check, ending the grapple on a success. The grapple also ends if you pull the axe’s blade back towards the weapon (no action required) or if you let go of the axe. While you have a creature grappled in this way, you can’t attack with the suneater axe. As a bonus action, you can try to reel the creature in, making a Strength (Athletics) check, contested by one from the grappled creature. On a success, you pull the target up to 15 feet towards you. Very rare variant: Increase the DCs to 16. While holding this weapon, you have resistance to radiant damage.
