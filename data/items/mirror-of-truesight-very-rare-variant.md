# Mirror of Truesight (very rare variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Component: fiend eye Nothing so delicate as glass or so pure as silver survives long on the Lower Planes. That in itself makes this magic item a rarity. Held by a handle of claw-like fingers, this magic mirror grants its user the ability to see who is truly the fairest of them all. Real Reflections. As an action while holding the mirror, you can speak its command word to cause its reflective surface to disappear for 1 minute. During that time, a creature that looks through the mirror while holding it has truesight out to 30 feet. Once this property of the mirror has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn. Rare variant: Increase the range of truesight to 60 feet. The mirror can be used three times and regains all expended uses daily at dawn. Very rare variant: Increase the range of truesight to 120 feet. The mirror can be used five times and regains all expended uses daily at dawn.
