# Mask of the Outsider

**description**: Mask of the Outsider Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) Each of these stylized masks exhibit features commonly associated with one of the following creature types: celestial, fey, or fiend. While you are wearing the mask, you can speak with any creature of that type as long as you can both speak at least one language. Additionally, creatures of that type will perceive you as being one of them rather than a humanoid. As an action, you can cast the polymorph spell on yourself. When you do, instead of choosing a beast, you must choose a creature of the mask’s type whose CR is no more than half your level. After you cast this spell using the mask, you cannot cast it in this way again until the next sunrise.
