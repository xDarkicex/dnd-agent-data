# Headband of Magical Arts

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) While you wear this headband, you know Arcane Echo, Reflect Magic, and Whirling Magic maneuvers, even if you don’t meet the usual prerequisites. When a maneuver calls for your spellcasting ability modifier, you use your Constitution modifier if you don’t have one.
