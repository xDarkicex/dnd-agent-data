# Mining Bomb

**description**: Miners often make bombs to help clear out large amounts of stone and ore; however, the bombs also can be used against structures or to destabilize foundations. These heavy bombs must be placed and ignited rather than thrown. After you light a mining bomb’s fuse, it explodes at the end of your next turn. Each creature within 10 feet of the mining bomb when it explodes must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw, taking 2d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A nonmagical object or structure in the area that isn’t being worn or carried takes 4d6 fire damage instead.
