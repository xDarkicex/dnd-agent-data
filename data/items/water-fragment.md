# Water Fragment

**description**: Water Fragment Wondrous item, Rare (requires attunement) While attuned to this fragment, you have resistance to cold damage, you can breathe air and water, and you have a swimming speed of 30 feet. Additionally, you can speak, read, and write Aquan, and you can cast the water shape cantrip at will. Awakened Water Fragment. Attuning to the Water Fragment while also attuned to the Shattered Staff awakens the fragment and it magically merges with the staff, becoming one item that takes up a single attunement slot. After the Water Fragment has been awakened, the following spells are added to the staff’s spell list: cone of cold (5 charges), control water (4 charges), fog cloud (1 charge), wall of ice (6 charges), and water walk (3 charges).
