# Talisman of Precarious Beauty

**description**: Talisman of Precarious Beauty Wondrous item, legendary A hauntingly beautiful trinket of fine silver shaped in a crescent moon. It has a loop that allows it to be worn on a chain around the user’s neck or wrist. A creature wearing this item gains a +4 bonus to their persuasion, deception, and performance checks, but failing one of those checks causes anyone the creature is trying to persuade, deceive, or impress, to instead regard them with intense suspicion and an irrational sense of dislike for the next 24 hours.
