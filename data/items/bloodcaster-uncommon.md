# Bloodcaster (Uncommon)

**description**: Bloodcaster Weapon (Light Crossbow), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Blood Bolt. You can fire this magic weapon without ammunition by losing 1 hit point as part of the action used to fire it. If you do so, your blood coalesces into a liquid bolt, which deals necrotic damage instead of piercing. Septic Shot. When you fire a blood bolt, you can lose an additional 2 hit points to deal an additional 1d4 poison damage. Progression Bloodcaster progresses when its wielder uses it to commit a sufficiently spectacular assassination, as determined by the GM.
