# Snake Oil

**description**: Snake Oil Potion, rare Weight: ½ lb. When you drink this potion, you Polymorph into a Constrictor Snake or a Giant Constrictor Snake for 10 minutes or until you drop to 0 hit points. You also gain the effect of a Freedom of Movement spell for the duration. The dark liquid appears to have a small living snake swimming in it. This tiny snake slides down the drinker’s throat to activate the transformation.
