# Penetrating

**description**: Legendary weapon property Components: Masterwork exotic weapon with the Ammunition or Thrown property that deals piercing damage This weapon pierces through its targets. When you make a ranged attack with this weapon and don’t have disadvantage, you can target all creatures in a straight line within this weapon’s normal range. You don’t make an attack roll for this attack. Instead, each creature in the line must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take damage as if it were hit by the weapon.
