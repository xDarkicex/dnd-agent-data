# Arcanist's Conduit

**description**: Staff, Rare (Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster) This magic staff is made from adamantine and tingles with unbridled arcane force. The staff has 7 charges for the following properties and regains all expended charges daily at dawn: Ward. As a reaction when you are the target of a spell, you can expend charges equal to the spell's level (minimum of 1) to gain your choice of advantage on the first saving throw you make against that spell or resistance to any damage dealt by that spell this turn. Unstable Recovery. As a bonus action, you can expend charges to regain a spell slot of a level equal to the number of charges you expend this way. When you do, you must succeed on a saving throw using your spellcasting ability with a DC of 10 plus the level of the spell slot regained or take 1d6 Psychic damage for each level of the spell slot regained.
