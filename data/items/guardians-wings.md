# Guardian's Wings

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) This delicate cloak is fashioned from the prismatic feathers of an angelic being. Angelic Flight. As a bonus action, you can speak the cloak’s command word to transform it into a pair of angelic wings that provide you with a fly speed 60 feet for 1 minute. Once used, this property can’t be used again until the next dawn. Angelic Protection. When an attacker you can see hits you or another creature within 5 feet of you with an attack, you can use your reaction to reduce the damage taken by an amount equal to your proficiency bonus.
