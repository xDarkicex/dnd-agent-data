# Mirror of Divination

**description**: Mirror of Divination Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) While holding this silver hand mirror, you can ask a single question concerning a specific goal, event, or activity to occur within a tenday. A ghostly face appears in the mirror and offers a truthful reply, which might be a short phrase, a cryptic rhyme, or an omen. Once used, this property of the mirror can’t be used again until the next dawn.
