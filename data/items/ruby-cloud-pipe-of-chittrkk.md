# Ruby Cloud Pipe of Chittr'k'k

**description**: A ruby pipe with mithral inlay in various disturbing patterns, a work of pure chaos artistry. This pipe is associated with a particular gaseous magical effect: incendiary cloud . A creature smoking the pipe can cast the associated spell as a bonus action 1/day.
