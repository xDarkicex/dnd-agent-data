# Mask of Tragedy

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This gray mask depicts a face with a frowning mouth and sad eyes. When you use an action to don this mask, a creature of your choice that can see you and is within 30 feet of you must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, the creature is overcome by grief and falls prone in a fit of wailing for 1 minute, becoming incapacitated and unable to stand up for the duration. A creature with an Intelligence score of 4 or less isn't affected. At the end of each of its turns, and each time it takes damage, the target can make another Wisdom saving throw. The target has advantage on the saving throw if it's triggered by damage. On a success, the effect ends. Once used, this property of the mask can’t be used again until the next dawn.
