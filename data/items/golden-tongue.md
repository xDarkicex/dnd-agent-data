# Golden Tongue

**description**: Golden Tongue Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This charm looks like a small golden tongue. While wearing it around your neck, it grants you advantage on Charisma (Persuasion) checks—however, you are required to make your argument in rhyming couplets. If you fail to speak in rhyme when making the check, you take 1d8 psychic damage. I cannot claim to be a poet. Perhaps others will find this item of more use.
