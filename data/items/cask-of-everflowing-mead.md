# Cask of Everflowing Mead

**description**: Cask of Everflowing Mead Wondrous item, rare This is a finely made mead cask. When emptied, if filled with water, the next morning, the water will have transformed into a random beverage. Roll on the following table. Random Mead Table d20 mead 1-3 Sweet tasting water 4-6 Raider Wine 7-10 Honey-Water Mead 11-13 Whalemead 14-15 Stertka 16-17 Mother’s Brew 18-19 Mead of the Twins 20 Ten drinking horns can be filled with this unusual mead. Drinking a horn confers the benefits of a potion of healing (greater). When the cask is filled with water, an inspiration point may be expended to select a specific mead, instead of rolling. Finally, any mead from the cask vanishes at dusk, even if transferred to another container.
