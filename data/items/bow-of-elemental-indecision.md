# Bow of Elemental Indecision

**description**: Bow of Elemental Indecision Ranged weapon Price: 17,000 gp This longbow is ancient in appearance, and is covered in carved runes and designs. Around the bow handle is a small dial with small symbols representing different elements that the holder can move with a flick of the thumb. Etched on the side of the bow, in an ancient form of Elven, it says, “Just pick one already.” This bow acts as a normal longbow, except that the user can add 1d4 damage to any arrow shot from the bow. This extra damage can be of any damage type, selected by the holder of the bow each time they make an attack. Examples include: Fire, Ice, Water, Electricity, Sonic, Earth, Banana, Rainbow, Irony, Surprise, etc. The game master may decide that the arrows have additional effects depending on the type of element used and the situation. Ranged. Ranged weapons are used to attack enemies at a distance. These weapons can be either shot like a bow and arrow or crossbow, or thrown like a spear or javelin. All ranged weapons use Dexterity for both the Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus. Damage: 1d8 Secondary Damage: 1d4 Damage Type: Piercing Properties: Two-Handed
