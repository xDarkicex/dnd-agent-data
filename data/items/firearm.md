# Firearm

**description**: Apprentice weapon property Components: Masterwork ranged weapon with the Loading or Reload property that doesn’t have the Tension property This weapon becomes a firearm and gains the Firearm property. Its damage die increases by four steps and moves to two damage dice, if possible. Firearm. You don’t add your ability modifier to this weapon’s damage rolls.
