# Officer Wand

**description**: Wand, uncommon This wand is carried and used by law enforcement officers. While holding this wand, you can cast the ray of frost spell using your spellcasting ability. If you don’t have the ability to cast spells, your spell attack modifier for this spell is equal to your proficiency bonus plus your Dexterity modifier.
