# Bog Mimic Greatclub (Uncommon)

**description**: Weapon (Greatclub), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) When you attune to this magic weapon, and during every long rest while attuned, you must feed the bog mimic one ration’s worth of meat. Otherwise, it attacks you at the end of the rest (use the mimic stat block). Common While attuned to this magic weapon, you can use a bonus action to verbally command it to change shape between a club, greatclub, or quarterstaff. Uncommon You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. Stick-y. When you hit a creature with an attack using this weapon, you can choose for it to adhere to the target. You drop the weapon and the target becomes grappled by it (the escape DC equals 11 + your proficiency bonus). A creature that starts their turn grappled by the bog mimic takes 1d8 piercing damage and 1d8 acid damage. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest.
