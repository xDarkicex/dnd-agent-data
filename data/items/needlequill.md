# Needlequill

**description**: Needlequill Weapon (Javelin), Rare (Requires Attunement) A javelin made from the hollow, black-and-white banded quill of a colossal beast. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. In addition, while wielding it, you have advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks to spot nearby creatures, if those creatures have hostile intent. While the javelin is on your person but not being wielded, you gain a +1 bonus to AC.
