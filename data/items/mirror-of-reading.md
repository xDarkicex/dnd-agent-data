# Mirror of Reading

**description**: Mirror of Reading Wondrous item, uncommon While holding this hand mirror, you can use an action to speak its command word and activate it. It remains activated for 1 hour, during which time you understand any written language that you see reflected in the mirror’s surface. Once used, this property of the mirror can’t be used again until the next dawn.
