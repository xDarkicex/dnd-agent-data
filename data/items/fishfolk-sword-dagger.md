# Fishfolk Sword Dagger

**description**: Weapon (any sword or dagger), uncommon Component: elemental volatile mote of water Coastal fishfolk typically repurpose scavenged steel from less fortunate travellers into wicked blades, adorning the hilts with coral and seashells. It is common practice to bless such a blade with water before battle; sometimes this can even attract the Deep One’s blessing. This weapon has 2 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. Blessing of the Deep. As a bonus action, you can coat this blade with water from a water source within your reach and make a quick prayer to the Deep One, expending 1 charge and turning the coating to flawless ice for 1 minute. While coated in ice, the blade deals an extra 1d6 cold damage to any target it hits.
