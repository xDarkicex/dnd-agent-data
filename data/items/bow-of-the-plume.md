# Bow of the Plume

**description**: Bow of the Plume Weapon (Longbow), Uncommon This longbow is covered in brush strokes of various colors. The tips of the bow are dipped in wax and have a small wick affixed to each end which constantly release a faint whisp of smoke. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. This bow has 3 charges. When you make an attack with this longbow, you can expend 1 charge and speak the command word to have the arrow release a plume of colored smoke (your choice of color) that traces out the path of the arrow. The plume creates a 5-foot-wide line that extends out from you to the target. The plume’s area is heavily obscured. The plume lasts for 1 minute, but a strong wind (at least 20 miles per hour) disperses it. The bow regains all expended charges when you finish a long rest.
