# Shadeblight Extract

**description**: Poison, rare This tar-like substance is made by extracting blighted bodily fluids from creatures carrying the Shadeblight disease (see page 12). A creature that gains an Injury as a result of being hit by an attack made with a weapon coated in Shadeblight Extract must succeed on a DC 16 Constitution save or contract midstage Shadeblight disease, taking the usual penalties for gaining an Injury while afflicted by the disease.
