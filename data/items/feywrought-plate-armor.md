# Feywrought Plate Armor

**description**: Feywrought Plate Armor Armor (Heavy), Rare (Requires Attunement) This colorful, flowery armor was forged in the Feywild and is infused with that plane’s captivating magic. While wearing this armor, you have advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the charmed condition on yourself. This armor has 3 charges. You can use an action to expend a charge to cast the Compulsion spell (save DC 15) from this armor. The armor regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. Plate Armor. Plate consists of shaped, interlocking metal plates to cover the entire body. A suit of plate includes gauntlets, heavy leather boots, a visored helmet, and thick layers of padding underneath the armor. Buckles and straps distribute the weight over the body. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC: 18 Stealth: Disadvantage Weight: 65
