# Dark Stars Blood

**description**: The blood inside the container is thick and blackish. Any creature that smears a dose of the blood on itself gains resistance to necrotic damage. Any weapon or a natural weapon treated with a dose of the blood deals an extra 1d6 necrotic damage . Any creature that drinks a dose of the blood can see through darkness, even magical darkness, and gains immunity to the frightened condition. All these effects last for 1 minute.
