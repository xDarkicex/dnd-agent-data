# Tome of Mystix

**description**: Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster) This magical tome tingles with eldritch energy and is wrapped firmly in a silver chain. You gain a +1 bonus to your spell save DC and spell attacks while carrying it. Archmage's Favor. As an action, you can touch the tome's chain to either lock or unlock the tome. While unlocked and opened, you can use one of the following properties as a bonus action: Knowledge. You can address a question to the tome by speaking it or writing it on one of its blank pages. If the tome knows the answer (it has a +10 bonus to Arcana, History, Nature, and Religion checks using Intelligence), the tome provides the answer in cramped handwriting on its pages. Otherwise, the words "I do not know” appear, and the tome refuses to answer any more questions until the next dawn. Power. You can ask the tome for a spell. If the spell is 8th-level or lower and on the Wizard spell list, it appears on the tome's pages. You can cast the spell from the book as if from a spell scroll, using the spell's casting time and your own spellcasting ability, and you must provide any material component that has a cost. The spell vanishes from the book's pages when cast; otherwise, it vanishes after 1 hour. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn. A creature that attempts to use this property without being attuned to the Tome of Mystix is affected as by the Feeblemind spell (save DC 17). Sentience. The Tome of Mystix is a sentient, lawful evil object with an Intelligence of 20, Wisdom of 14, and Charisma of 14. It has hearing and Darkvision out to a range of 120 feet. The tome can read and understand Common, Elvish, and Undercommon, and communicates by forming words on its pages. The tome is arrogant and prideful. Malignant Spirit. The Tome of Mystix holds a sliver of the drow archmage Mystix' soul. Whenever the tome is used to create a spell, roll a d100. If the result is equal to or lower than the spell's level, the spirit breaks free and appears within 5 feet of the book. Mystix' spirit has the statistics of a ghost , except it has an Intelligence of 20, Wisdom of 14, and Charisma of 14, a proficiency bonus of +6, it is proficient with Intelligence and Wisdom saving throws, and it can cast any spell on the Wizard spell list of 8th level or lower once per day each, requiring no material components that don't have a cost (spell save DC 19, +11 to hit with spell attacks). The spirit's goal is to possess a body and escape with the Tome of Mystix . If brought to 0 hit points or separated from the tome by more than 120 feet, the spirit disappears back into the tome.
