# Big Brain Time

**description**: Wondrous item, rarity varies (requires attunement) Multiple Neurosurgical Mind Amplifier in scientific terms, or Big Brain Time in soldier slang, this item is technically a backpack that contains spellcaster brains preserved in angst crystals. By connecting these brains to yourself through silver veins containing humanoid blood mixed with special alchemical mixtures, you can enhance your spellcasting abilities. The sorcerers of Cyherested have brought praiseworthy destruction in many battles thanks to these items. The power this item gives changes depending on the amount of brains it contains. It increases your spell save DC, and the number of spells you can cast per day from a spell level of your choice (maximum 4th spell level) a number of times equal to the number of creatures whose brains it contains. One of these items can contain up to 4 creatures’ brains at a time. The rarity of this item depends on how many creatures’ brains are inside of it: uncommon (1 brain), rare (2 brains), very rare (3 brains), legendary (4 brains).
