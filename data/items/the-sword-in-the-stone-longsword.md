# The Sword in the Stone Longsword

**description**: Weapon (any sword), uncommon (requires attunement by a creature with a Strength score of 16 or higher) Component: astral brain (unique - bonemonger) The Lady of the Lake, her arm clad in the purest shimmering samite, once bestowed a blade just like this one unto the rightful ruler of the land, that he might wield it in defence of the downtrodden and oppressed. This replica is just as good as the real thing… but its craftsman seems to have forgotten a tiny little detail. This sword, which deals bludgeoning damage instead of piercing or slashing damage, deals an extra 1d6 bludgeoning damage to the first target it hits on each of your turns. Moderate Executive Power. As the owner of this mighty sword, you command respect and obedience… but the comically large boulder does detract from it a little. While carrying the sword, you have advantage on Charisma (Persuasion) checks made to convince others to perform tasks or favours for you.
