# Loaded Dice

**description**: Loaded Dice Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This set of six-sided dice is indistinguishable from mundane dice but blessed with good fortune. After you make an ability check, attack roll, or saving throw, but before the DM declares success or failure, you can expend 1 charge to reroll the d20. Loaded dice can have up to 7 charges and regain 1d6+1 charges each week. Additionally, when you use this magic item to play a dice game, you have advantage on ability checks made to determine the winner of the game.
