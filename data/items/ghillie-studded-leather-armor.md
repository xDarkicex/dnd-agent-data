# Ghillie Studded Leather Armor

**description**: Ghillie Studded Leather Armor Armor (studded leather armor), rare This magical armor is made from natural materials, even if the armor type is traditionally created using metal. When you are wearing this armor and in the wilderness, you have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks, ignoring any disadvantage the armor would normally impose on the roll. AC: 12 + Dex modifier Weight: 13
