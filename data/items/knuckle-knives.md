# Knuckle Knives

**description**: Knuckle Knives Melee weapon (simple, knuckle knives) Light. A light weapon is small and easy to handle, making it ideal for use when fighting with two weapons. Unarmed. When you make an unarmed strike, you can choose to deal the damage of this weapon instead of the bludgeoning damage normal for an unarmed strike.
