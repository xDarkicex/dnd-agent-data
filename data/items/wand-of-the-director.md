# Wand of the Director

**description**: Wand, Uncommon This magic wand has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. As an action, you can expend 1 charge and point the wand at a creature you can see within 30 feet. That creature can immediately use its reaction to move up to its speed or make one weapon attack.
