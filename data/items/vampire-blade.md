# Vampire Blade

**description**: Weapon (longsword, shortsword, or dagger), very rare (requires attunement) This serrated weapon has an enchanted channel, which causes blood it collects to flow into a vial in the hilt. This weapon functions as a +1 weapon . Whenever you inflict an Injury with this weapon, the weapon gains 1 charge, up to a maximum of 5 charges, which are lost when you finish a long rest. While the weapon has at least 3 charges, it functions as a +2 weapon , and whenever you hit a creature with an attack made using the weapon you may expend one charge to deal an additional 2d8 damage and gain temporary hit points equal to the damage dealt by the weapon’s damage dice (ie. 3d8+2 for a one-handed longsword). While the weapon has 5 charges, it functions as a +3 weapon , deals an additional 1d8 damage, and whenever you inflict an Injury with the weapon you ignore all damage loss. If you kill a creature with this weapon while it has 5 charges, it loses 3 charges and you gain temporary hit points equal to half the creature’s maximum hit points. These temporary hit points last until you finish a long rest.
