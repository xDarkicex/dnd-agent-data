# Gavyn's Knuckle (Common)

**description**: Gavyn's Knuckle Weapon (Greatclub), Common (Requires Attunement) When you score a critical hit against a Large or smaller creature with this magical weapon, you can choose to grapple the target (escape DC 11). You may only grapple one creature at a time with this weapon and while it grapples a creature, you can’t attack with it. Progression Gavyn’s Knuckle progresses when it accomplishes a public feat of strength, such as successfully pinning or winning in one-on-one combat, against a larger or brawnier foe, though what makes such a feat worthy of progression is at the GM’s discretion. However, in the case of defeating a larger foe in single combat, to progress to very rare rarity, the foe must be at least one size category larger than you, and to progress to legendary, they must be two size categories larger.
