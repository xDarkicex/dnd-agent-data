# Circlet of the Cosmos

**description**: Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic circlet is a band of adamantine inset with crystal from a fallen meteorite. While wearing the circlet, you have Blindsight out to a range of 30 feet and advantage on Arcana and Insight checks. The circlet has 7 charges for the following properties and regains all expended charges daily at dawn: Cosmic Veil. As a reaction when you take non-Psychic damage, you can expend charges to reduce the damage you take by 1d10 per charge you expend. Starfall. As an action, you can expend charges to conjure meteorites to hit one creature you can see within 60 feet per charge you expend. Each creature must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 1d10 Radiant damage and 1d10 Force damage on a failure, or half as much on a success. On a failure, the creature is also dazed. Until the end of its next turn, it must choose whether it gets an action, a bonus action, or a move on its turn; it gets only one of the three.
