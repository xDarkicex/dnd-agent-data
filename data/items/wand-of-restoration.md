# Wand of Restoration

**description**: Wand of Restoration Wand, rare (requires attunement) This wand has 6 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 2 of its charges to cast the lesser restoration spell from it, or 4 charges to cast the greater restoration spell from it. The wand regains 1d4 + 2 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the wand’s last charge, roll a d20. On a roll of 1, the wand crumbles into dust and is destroyed.
