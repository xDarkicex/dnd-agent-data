# Powder of True Form

**description**: A brass decanter holds a fine powder with a golden sheen. There is enough for 6 doses. When you use an action to throw a dose of powder into the air, the true forms of all creatures that are within 10 feet from you are revealed for 1d4 minutes. The duration is the same for all subjects, and the powder is consumed when its magic takes effect. The powder shows the original form of a shapechanger or a creature that is transformed by magic into another form. However, it does not force the subjects to revert to their original forms.
