# Adder's Embrace (Common)

**description**: Adder's Embrace Weapon (Pike), Common (Requires Attunement) The pike has 3 charges. The pike regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. Snakebite. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge to make a melee attack with the pike’s serpent head. On a hit, the pike deals 1d4 piercing damage plus 2 poison damage. Progression Adder’s Embrace progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
