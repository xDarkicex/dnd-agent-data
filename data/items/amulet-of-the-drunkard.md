# Amulet of the Drunkard

**description**: Amulet of the Drunkard Wondrous item, uncommon This amulet smells of old, ale-stained wood. While wearing it, you can regain 4d4 + 4 hit points when you drink a pint of beer, ale, mead, or wine. Once the amulet has restored hit points, it can’t do so again until the next dawn.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: uncommon
