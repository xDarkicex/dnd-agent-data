# Button of Blasting

**description**: Button of Blasting Wondrous item, rare This small, bone button may be thrown unerringly to a point you choose within 150 feet of you. When it reaches the target, it explodes in a ball of flame. Each creature in a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on that point must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw. A target takes 10d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The button is consumed in the blast.
