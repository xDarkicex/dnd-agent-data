# Shashka

**description**: A cavalry saber employed to great effect by mounted warriors, the shashka can be swung while on horseback in a repeated, looping downward arc. When riding through enemy ranks, a mounted warrior employs a one-handed, whirling swing, crisscrossing the rider’s pommel to strike at enemies on foot on both sides in a downward double arc. A shashka can’t be wielded with two hands while you are mounted.
