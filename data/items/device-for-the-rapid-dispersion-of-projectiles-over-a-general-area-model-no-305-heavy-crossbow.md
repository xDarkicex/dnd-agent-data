# Device for the Rapid Dispersion of Projectiles Over a General Area (Model No. 305, heavy crossbow)

**description**: Device for the Rapid Dispersion of Projectiles Over a General Area (Model No. 305) Weapon (Light or Heavy Crossbow), Uncommon A silver-trimmed wooden box with a long lever jutting from the side, mounted to the top of a seemingly standard recurve crossbow. Pulling the lever fires a bolt and simultaneously loads a new one. The added weight from the magazine makes it cumbersome to wield. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The weapon has the two-handed property regardless of its size, doesn’t have the loading property, and its normal and long ranges are halved. It can fire nine shots before the magazine is empty. Refilling the magazine takes an action. Rapid Fire. As an action, make three attacks using this weapon against a single target within its normal range. Each attack has disadvantage.
