# Adder's Embrace (Uncommon)

**description**: Adder's Embrace Weapon (Pike), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The pike has 5 charges. The pike regains 1d4 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. Snakebite. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge to make a melee attack with the pike’s serpent head. On a hit, the pike deals 1d4 piercing damage plus 2 poison damage. Lunging Coils. As an action, you can expend 2 charges to cause the serpent to lock around a Large or smaller creature within the weapon’s reach, grappling the target (escape DC 10). Progression Adder’s Embrace progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
