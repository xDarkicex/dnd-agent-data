# Summer's Dance

**description**: Summer's Dance Weapon (scimitar), rare (requires attunement) This beautiful golden blade appears to bend and flex like a reed with your movements. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Additionally, the scimitar has 3 charges and regains 1 expended charge daily at dawn. While wielding this scimitar, you can expend 1 charge as a bonus action to cast the misty s tep spell. Damage: 1d6+1 Damage Type: Slashing Properties: Finesse, Light Weight: 3
