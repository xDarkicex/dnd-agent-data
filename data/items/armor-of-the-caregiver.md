# Armor of the Caregiver

**description**: Armor (Light, Medium, or Heavy), Rare (Requires Attunement) When your companion turns their rage against you, this armor converts your pain into power. While wearing this armor, you have a +1 bonus to AC. Additionally, when you take damage from your companion while they are in a rampage, you take only half as much damage, and the next attack you make before the end of your next turn deals an extra 2d6 damage if it hits.
