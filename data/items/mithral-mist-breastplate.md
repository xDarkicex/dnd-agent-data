# Mithral Mist Breastplate

**description**: Mithral Mist Breastplate Armor (medium, breastplate), rare (requires attunement) Mithral is a light, flexible metal. A mithral chain shirt or breastplate can be worn under normal clothes. If the armor normally imposes disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks or has a Strength requirement, the mithral version of the armor doesn’t. While wearing this armor, you can use an action to cause one of the following effects: Billowing Mist. Your space fills with billowing silver mist. You are heavily obscured while in the mist, which lasts until the end of your next turn. Gaseous Form. You cast the gaseous form spell on yourself. Once you have used an effect, you can’t use that effect again until the next dawn. AC: 14 + Dexterity Modifier (Maximum of 2)
