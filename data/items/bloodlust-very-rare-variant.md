# Bloodlust (very rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (dagger), uncommon Component: phial of monstrosity (minotaur) blood When mined, blood crystal appears as a pale, almost colourless pink. However, after touching blood, its colour temporarily shifts to a red deeper than most rubies. For this reason, it’s favoured among vampires as a reminder of when to feed. Blood Bond. When you damage a creature that has blood with this magic weapon, or it touches blood spilled from a creature less than 24 hours ago, the light pink crystals that make up its blade turn a deep, sanguine red as the weapon becomes bonded to the creature, which becomes its quarry. For the next 24 hours you can, as an action, toss the dagger in the air and speak its command word. For 6 seconds after, it hovers in the air, pointing in the direction of its quarry. If its quarry is on a different plane or is warded against divination magic, the dagger spins wistfully in the air. Once the dagger absorbs blood, it can’t absorb another creature’s blood until 24 hours have passed and it returns to its normal pink colour. Rare variant: You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Very rare variant: You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.
