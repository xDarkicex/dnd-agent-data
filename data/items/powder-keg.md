# Powder Keg

**description**: Damage . 10d6 Damage Type . fire Cost . 500 gp Properties . Special* Range . 30-foot-radius sphere. *Powder Kegs . When a barrel takes fire damage, it explodes in a 30-foot-radius sphere. Creatures in the area must succeed on a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 35 (10d6) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.
