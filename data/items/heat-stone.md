# Heat Stone

**description**: Wondrous item, common Prized by reptilian humanoids, this magic stone is warm to the touch. While carrying this stone, you are comfortable in and can tolerate temperatures as low as -20 degrees Fahrenheit without any additional protection. If you wear heavy clothes, you can tolerate temperatures as low as -50 degrees Fahrenheit.
