# Ring of Light

**description**: Ring, common Emerald’s Price : 200 gp While wearing this silver ring with a round amber stone, you can use an action to speak its command word to cause the stone to shed a bright golden light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for an additional 30 feet. Covering the stone blocks the light. The light lasts for 1 hour or until you use an action to speak the command word again. The ring can’t be used again until the next dawn.
