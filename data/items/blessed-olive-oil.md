# Blessed Olive Oil

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon This amphora of fragrant olive oil has been blessed by the Mother Goddess. Characters may bathe in this olive oil to be receive the same effects as a bless spell. Anyone using the oil must remove their armor and spend one minute bathing. The oil is consumed in the process.
