# Skycrystal Shard

**description**: Skycrystal Shard Wondrous Item, Rarity Varies (requires attunement) Crafted by ancient mages, this crystal shard holds a mote of the unending magic of creation. As an action, you can focus your mind onto the Skycrystal Shard and create the effect of one spell that has a casting time of 1 action. The maximum level of the spell you can create depends on the shard’s rarity as shown in the table below. The spell can be from any class’s spell list, and you don’t need to meet the requirements in that spell, including costly components; the spell simply takes effect as part of this action, using your spell save DC, spellcasting ability modifier, and spell attack bonus. Once the Skycrystal Shard is used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
