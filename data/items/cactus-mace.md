# Cactus Mace

**description**: Cactus Mace Weapon (mace), rare (requires attunement) Damage: 1d6+1 bludgeoning Weight: 4 lbs. Properties: Simple The cactus mace is a living weapon commonly used by the Azikani desert elves. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you hit a creature with AC 14 or less, the creature takes an additional 1d4 piercing damage. Plant Speech. While attuned to the mace, you can cast Speak with Plants as an action once per day. Water Source. The head of the mace is a living cactus that can provide 3 gallons of water per day.
