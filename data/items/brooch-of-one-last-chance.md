# Brooch of One Last Chance

**description**: Wondrous item, rarity varies This simple brooch is made of leather and wood, and is marked with a symbol of healing and protection. Once per day when you are hit by an attack or fail a saving throw against a damaging effect, you may have the brooch cast medic’s intervention* on you at the level indicated in the table below (no action required). Additionally, for brooches of rarity Rare and above, if the damage prevented would have brought you to 0 hit points, you may use your reaction to make an attack, cast a spell with a casting time of 1 action, or take the Dash action without provoking opportunity attacks from creatures within 5 feet of you when you spend your reaction.
