# Demon's Edge

**description**: Weapon (greatsword), very rare (requires attunement, cursed) This evil and cursed blade should be far too large and too heavy for easy use, but once attuned, its wielder finds themself possessed with great strength - and an overwhelming desire to inflict carnage. The serrated blade itself is carved from the bones of an elder demon, and glows with an eerie purple miasma. While attuned to this weapon, your Strength score increases to 21 unless it was already higher, and you may not make attacks using other weapons. Any other weapon you are attuned to that is of lower rarity immediately becomes unattuned and you may not attune to them while you are attuned to this weapon. The weapon has a +2 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls. Whenever you take the Attack action and make an attack with the weapon you must attempt to Injure your foe, and whenever you reduce a foe to 0 hit points you must kill it. Foes you Injure have their Imperiled threshold reduced by 1, and the DC to resist your Death Blows increases by 5. Attunement to this weapon may not be ended voluntarily. Instead, it ends when you have a remove curse or similar spell or effect cast on you.
