# Ring of Air

**description**: Ring of Air Wondrous item, legendary A flawlessly cut opal band that is cool to the touch. As the band is turned, the colors within change based on the reflection of the light. When perfectly still, the band appears to be pure white. The wearer of this ring may spend an action to unleash a powerful gust of wind within a 30-foot sphere centered on themself. Each other creature caught in the blast must make a DC 18 Strength saving throw. If they fail, the creature is pushed ten feet away from the ring and takes 2d8 bludgeoning damage. If the creature hits a solid surface like a wall or other obstacle, they take an additional 1d8 bludgeoning damage. If the Ring of Air is worn during a long rest, the wearer becomes attuned. The empowerment word can only be used once per long rest. While attuned, the wearer benefits from the following effects: If the wearer speaks the empowerment word “Transient” as they active the ring, the gust of wind becomes a 15-foot cone, the DC increases to 20, and the amount of initial bludgeoning damage the effect deals is increased to 4d8, and the damage dealt from impacting on a solid surface is increased to 2d8. The wearer no longer needs to breathe and is immune to suffocation. The wearer’s movement speed is increased by 5 feet.
