# Sanguine Horn

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare This iron-bound ram’s horn has been stained a rusty red by the gore of a thousand bloody battles. Some believe these horns to be artifacts of gods of death or war, but others claim they simply came into being over centuries of warfare. When sounded for a full round, the horn becomes audible at a range of 5000 feet, summoning an Angel of Blood to fight for the summoner. The angel appears anywhere the summoner can see within 200 feet. The angel fights for 1 hour, or until there is no imminent threat to the summoner. Once used, the horn’s magic fades for a week, after which it can be used again.
