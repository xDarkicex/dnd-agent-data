# Proximity Javelin

**description**: Proximity Javelin Weapon (Javelin), Rare (Requires Attunement) This sleek javelin is made entirely of metal and is wrapped in a leather grip. The words “up close and personal” are etched in Orcish on the shaft. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. This weapon has 3 charges. When you make a ranged weapon attack with this weapon, you can expend 1 charge to instantly teleport to an unoccupied space within 5 feet of your target. When you teleport to your destination, you appear holding the javelin. Additionally, on a hit, you can immediately attempt to grapple the target. This weapon regains all expended charges when you finish a long rest.
