# Ring of Truth

**description**: Ring, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This magic ring looks like an unremarkable band of iron. You have advantage on Insight checks while wearing it. Interrogator's Question. The ring has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. As an action, you can expend 1 charge and ask a question of a creature within 10 feet of you. If the creature can hear and understand you, it must succeed on a DC 13 Charisma saving throw or be compelled to answer the question truthfully, in the most accurate and concise way it can. You know whether the creature succeeded or failed on its saving throw.
