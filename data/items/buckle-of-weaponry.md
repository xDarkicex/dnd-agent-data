# Buckle of Weaponry

**description**: Buckle of Weaponry Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) This iron buckle is shaped like a small weapons rack and holds several miniature weapons—a mace, spear, warhammer, battleaxe, and greatsword. You can use a bonus action to detach one of these weapons from the buckle, causing the item to enlarge to full size. Once enlarged, they function as ordinary weapons. You can use another bonus action to touch the weapon to the buckle, causing it to shrink back down and reattach itself.
