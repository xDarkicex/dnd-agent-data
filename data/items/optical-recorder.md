# Optical Recorder

**description**: Optical Recorder Augment (head), uncommon (requires affinity) Microcameras are integrated into your visual cortex, allowing you to record what you see and hear. You can start or stop a recording as an action. The digital storage within the implant can hold up to 2 hours of recorded video and audio.
