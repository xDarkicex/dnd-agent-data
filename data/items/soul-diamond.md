# Soul Diamond

**description**: Soul Diamond Wondrous Item, Legendary Ages past, a dark cult of deranged priests created these diamonds to capture the souls of fallen heroes and villains alike in a mad attempt to control the course of history. A creature whose soul is trapped in a Soul Diamond can be resurrected only by a wish spell, unless the creature attempting the resurrection is holding the diamond. While holding the Soul Diamond, you can use your reaction to trap the soul of a creature you can see that dies within 60 feet of you. The soul remains trapped until the creature is resurrected or until you use an action to release it while holding the Soul Diamond. The Soul Diamond can hold only one soul at a time. When you cast raise dead , reincarnate , resurrection , revivify , or true resurrection on a creature whose soul is trapped in the Soul Diamond, you may use the diamond as the material component for the spell. When cast this way, any of the aforementioned spells forcefully resurrects the creature even if it is not willing and empties the Soul Diamond without consuming it in the process. As long as a soul is trapped in the Soul Diamond, it can telepathically communicate with any creature touching it. If you die while the Soul Diamond is on your person, your soul becomes trapped in the Soul Diamond, unless it already holds a soul.
