# Metaspectrum Light Rod

**description**: Metaspectrum Light Rod Utility, uncommon You can expend an action to switch on or turn off this powered glow rod. While on, the rod sheds bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for an additional 30 feet. Invisible creatures and objects are visible as long as they are in the rod’s bright light. As an action you can decrease the intensity, reducing the light to dim light in a 5-foot radius.
