# Crushing

**description**: Legendary weapon property Components: Masterwork exotic weapon that deals bludgeoning damage When you hit a creature that has natural armor or is wearing armor, the target takes a cumulative −1 penalty to the AC its armor offers. Armor reduced to an AC of 10 in this way is destroyed. A creature can repair its armor or heal the damage dealt to its natural armor over the course of a long rest.
