# Wand of Stone Shape

**description**: Wand Of Stone Shape Wand, rare (requires attunement by a spellcaster) This wand has 7 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend one of its charges to cast the stone shape spell from it. The wand regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the wand’s last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the wand crumbles into ashes and is destroyed.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wand
- Item Rarity: rare
- Requires Attunement: requires attunement by a spellcaster
