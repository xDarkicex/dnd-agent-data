# Pegasus Bridle

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This golden bridle may be used to tame a pegasus, so that the mythical creature may be used as a flying mount. Once the pegasus has been bridled, it may only be commanded by the character who is attuned to this item.

- Item Type: Adventuring Gear
- Item Rarity: Rare
