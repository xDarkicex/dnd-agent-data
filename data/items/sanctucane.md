# Sanctucane

**description**: Weapon (quarterstaff), very rare (requires attunement) This stark quarterstaff could easily be mistaken for a cane. The hard edges of the haft seem to flow in and out of existence, like the lapping waves on a shore. You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While wielding the staff, a character gains the benefit of a sanctuary spell versus undead. The character can deactivate or reactivate this ability as a bonus action.
