# Coral Crusher Light Hammer (very rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (any hammer), uncommon Component: beast chitin Through biomantic infusions, the coral that comprises this weapon can grow rapidly, quickly replenishing itself under the right conditions. Coralborn have mixed feelings towards such magic weapons. Some believe that it is sacrilege to damage a reef, while others argue that the foes these weapons shred are prevented from doing any harm to anything, including reefs. The jury is still out. These heavy weapons, with their fragile, spiky exteriors, send splinters of coral flying, hurting all creatures within close proximity. Safety glasses are recommended. Shred. Once on each of your turns when you hit a target with a melee attack using this weapon, you can cause coral shrapnel to burst out. The target and each creature within 5 feet of it must succeed on a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw or take piercing damage equal to one of the weapon’s damage dice. You have advantage on this saving throw. Hydrodynamic. Attacking while underwater doesn’t impose disadvantage on attack rolls you make with this weapon. Rare variant: Increase the DC to 15. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Very rare variant: Increase the DC to 16. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon.
