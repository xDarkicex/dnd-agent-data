# Hangman's Noose

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) Certain hemp ropes used in the execution of final justice can affect those beyond the reach of normal magics. This noose has 3 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 of its charges to cast the hold monster spell from it. Unlike the standard version of this spell, though, the magic of the hangman’s noose affects only undead. It regains 1d3 charges daily at dawn.

**properties**:

- Item Type: Wondrous item
