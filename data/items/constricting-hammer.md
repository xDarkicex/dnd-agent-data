# Constricting Hammer

**description**: Weapon (Any Hammer), Common This magic hammer is decorated with a snake figurine that hisses whenever the hammer is swung. Constricting Strike. When you hit a creature with the hammer, you can cause ephemeral serpents to wrap around the target. The creature must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be Restrained for 1 minute (escape DC 13). Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
