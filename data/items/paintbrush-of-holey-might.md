# Paintbrush of Holey Might

**description**: Paintbrush of Holey Might Wondrous item Price: 4,000 gp This paintbrush has no paint on it, and when you dip it in ink or paint it doesn’t seem to stick to the tip of the brush. This paintbrush can paint holes into any non-magical surface. Over the course of 1 minute, the wielder can paint a hole into the surface of a material. This hole looks exactly as if the material itself had been crafted this way, with clean lines and no jagged edges. The hole can be up to 2 feet deep into or through the material, and up to 3 feet in diameter. The wielder can make multiple holes connected together, but it will require a second use of the item. Once the hole is created, it acts exactly like a hole. Creatures can see, hear, and pass through the area. The paintbrush cannot affect living material, so it has no effect on a living creature. The paintbrush cannot unmake the holes, so make sure you really want a hole there!
