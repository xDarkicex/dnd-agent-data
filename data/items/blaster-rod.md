# Blaster Rod

**description**: This metal cylinder has an L-shaped handle large enough for a human hand. A button on the handle fires a beam of magical energy with a range of 60 feet. It can be used as a light ranged simple weapon. A target hit with an attack from it takes 1d6 force damage (no ability modifier added to damage). The damage is magical.

- Item Type: Ranged Weapon
- Properties: Light
