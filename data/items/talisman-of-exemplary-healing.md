# Talisman of Exemplary Healing

**description**: Talisman of Exemplary Healing Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement by a bard, cleric, druid, paladin, or ranger) This circular talisman is made of silver and inset with small diamonds in the shape of a triangle. While wearing it, you can use a bonus action to expend a charge and maximize the number of hit points regained when casting a healing spell using a spell slot of 6th or lower level. The talisman has 3 charges that are replenished every day at dawn.
