# Dragon Egg

**description**: Dragon Egg Spell catalyst, uncommon Using the arcane potential within a dragon’s egg, spellcasters can learn to take draconic shapes. When you cast the polymorph spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, the target of the spell turns into a dragon rather than a beast. All other rules of the spell still apply.
