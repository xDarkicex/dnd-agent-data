# Helm of the Slashing Fin

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) While wearing this helm, you can use an action to speak its command word to gain the ability to breathe underwater, but you lose the ability to breathe air. You can speak its command word again or remove the helm as an action to end this effect. Fin Slash. The helm has 3 charges, and it regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. You can use an action to expend 1 of its charges to swing your head in the direction of a target you can see within 10 feet of you. The helm’s fin extends and makes an attack roll with a +5 bonus. On a hit, the target takes 2d6 slashing damage. If you move at least 20 feet straight toward the target and the fin hits on the same turn, the hit is a critical hit.
