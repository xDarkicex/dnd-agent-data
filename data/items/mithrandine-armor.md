**description**: Armor (Medium or Heavy, but not Hide), Rare A unique mix of mithral reinforced with adamantine in vital sections, this magic armor doesn’t impose disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks and has no Strength requirement. Additionally, while wearing this armor any critical hit against you becomes a normal hit.
