# Brawn Armor

**description**: Armor (hide), rare (requires attunement) This armor was crafted from the hide of an ancient grizzly bear. While wearing this armor, you gain a +1 bonus to AC, and you have advantage on grapple checks. The armor has 3 charges. You can use a bonus action to expend 1 charge to deal your unarmed strike damage to a creature you are grappling. The armor regains all expended charges daily at dawn.

- Item Type: Medium Armor
- Item Rarity: rare
