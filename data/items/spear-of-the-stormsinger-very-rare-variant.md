# Spear of the Stormsinger (very rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (quarterstaff), uncommon (requires attunement) Component: dragon (blue or bronze) breath sac Stormsingers—disciples of the Allfather—are said to be able to channel the wrath of storms into holy weapons. If one of their sacred sites is threatened, the air is filled with a berserker’s chant as they throw themselves into combat with a zealousness inspired by the prospect of entry into the Allfather’s halls. When inactive, this weapon appears and behaves as an elegantly adorned metal quarterstaff. Crackling Tip. As a bonus action, you can speak this magic weapon’s command word, causing crackling lightning to appear at its tip. The weapon becomes a spear, sheds bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for an additional 20 feet, and deals lightning damage instead of piercing damage. While activated, whenever you make a ranged attack with this spear, a small lightning bolt shoots forth; you need not throw the weapon itself. You can use a bonus action to cause the lightning to disappear and the weapon once again becomes a quarterstaff. Allfather’s Wrath. As an action, you can cast the guiding bolt spell (+5 to hit), dealing your choice of either lightning or radiant damage. Once you use this property, you can’t do so again until you spend 1 hour outside singing a storm song in praise of the Allfather (which can be done as part of a short or long rest). Upon completion, a lightning bolt strikes the weapon and Allfather’s Wrath can be used again. Rare variant: The Allfather’s Wrath property can be used to cast either guiding bolt at 5th level (+7 to hit), or lightning bolt (save DC 15). These spells deal your choice of lightning or radiant damage. Very rare variant: The Allfather’s Wrath property can be used to cast either guiding bolt at 8th level (+8 to hit), or lightning bolt at 5th level (save DC 16). These spells deal your choice of lightning or radiant damage.
