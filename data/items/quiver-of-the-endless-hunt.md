# Quiver of the Endless Hunt

**description**: Quiver of the Endless Hunt Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement by a ranger) This magic quiver produces a limitless amount of thrown weapons and ammunition for ranged weapons as needed. Each weapon and ammunition drawn from the quiver is magical and grants a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls. Immediately after such a weapon or piece of ammunition is used for a ranged attack or leaves your hand for a purpose other than an attack, it disappears. While wearing the quiver, you can’t gain levels of exhaustion. Additionally, when you miss with a weapon attack, you can make one additional weapon attack as part of the same action. You can make a number of additional attacks with this ability equal to half your proficiency bonus, and you regain all of your expended uses at the start of your next turn.
