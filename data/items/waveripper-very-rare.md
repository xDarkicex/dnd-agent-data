# Waveripper (Very Rare)

**description**: Weapon (Scimitar), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) Common Attack rolls made with this magic weapon do not suffer disadvantage as a result of being underwater. Uncommon While holding this weapon, you have a swim speed equal to your walking speed and can breathe both air and water. Rare Surging Wave. This weapon has 3 changes, and regains all expended uses when you finish a long rest. As an action, you can expend 1 charge to summon a wave of water that surges forward in a 60-foot line. Each creature in this line must make a DC 15 Strength saving throw. A creature takes 4d6 bludgeoning damage and is pushed 20 feet on a failed save, or half as much damage and is not pushed on a successful one. Very Rare The DC of Surging Wave increases to 16. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon, which deals an extra 1d6 force damage on a hit. Douse. Elementals with immunity to fire damage are considered vulnerable to the force damage dealt by this weapon’s attacks and properties.
