# Trekker's Helmet

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) While wearing this magic helmet, your proficiency bonus is doubled for any ability check you make that uses the Survival skill. In addition, when you make a Strength or Dexterity ability check, you can choose to gain advantage on the roll. Once used, this property can’t be used again until the next dawn.
