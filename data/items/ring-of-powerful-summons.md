# Ring of Powerful Summons

**description**: Ring, rare (requires attunement by a cleric, druid, warlock, or wizard) When you summon a creature with a conjuration spell while wearing this ring, the creature gains a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls and 1d4 + 4 temporary hit points.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Ring
- Item Rarity: rare
- Requires Attunement: requires attunement by a Cleric, Druid, Warlock, or Wizard

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Vault of Magic
