# Jetpack

**description**: This device is a miniature version of the impulse engines used in starships, which has been fitted with straps and a small handheld controller. While wearing it on your back, you gain a flying speed equal to your walking speed. You can fly with a jetpack for a total of 1 minute each day, after which it can’t be used until it recharges at dawn.

**properties**:

- Item Type: wondrous item
- Item Rarity: uncommon
