# Synth Skin

**description**: Synth Skin Augment (body), varies (requires affinity) A thin web of synthetic mesh is grafted over your skin to provide additional protection. It is a rare augment. While you are not wearing any armor, your base AC equals 12 + your Dexterity modifier. If you are wearing armor, your AC can’t be less than 12, regardless of what kind of armor you’re wearing.
