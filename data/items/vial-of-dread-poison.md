# Vial of Dread Poison

**description**: Potion, Uncommon This vial contains 3 doses of dread poison. As an action, you can apply one dose of this poison to a weapon or a piece of ammunition you're holding. The first time you hit a creature with the poisoned weapon or ammunition within the next minute, the creature takes 2d6 Psychic damage and must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or be Frightened for 1 hour. While Frightened in this way, the creature has disadvantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws.
