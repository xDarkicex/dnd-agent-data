# Angstblazed Grip

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) Made in junkyards by the creative and mad crafters in Tolia, these gauntlets are generally used by street gangs and anarchists since they are usually made of junk metal, a small amount of angst dust, two long blood tubes, and old rags (for a comfortable grip). While wearing this gauntlet, you gain the following benefits: Your Strength score increases by 1, to a maximum of 20. You have advantage on checks you make to grapple a Medium or smaller creature. Your unarmed strikes deal an extra 1d8 fire damage on a hit.
