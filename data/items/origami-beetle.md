# Origami Beetle

**description**: Wondrous item, common Component: pouch of plant leaves Over the course of a minute, you can fold this enchanted paper into the shape of a 1-inch beetle, activating its magical potential. Dropping Eaves. With extremely acute hearing and camouflage capabilities, the origami beetle is an effective tool in covert eavesdropping. When you craft this origami animal, you can give it a short set of instructions including a destination and a wait time. For example, you could tell the beetle to “land underneath that bench, wait five minutes, then return here.” The beetle has a flying speed of 15 feet, AC 10, and 1 hit point. It records all sound within 20 feet of it for up to 10 minutes. While waiting, it blends into its surroundings, and can only be noticed with a successful DC 13 Wisdom (Perception) check. Upon returning to you, the beetle repeats all the sounds it recorded, shifted up two octaves. After 1 hour elapses, the origami beetle becomes a mundane, folded piece of paper.
