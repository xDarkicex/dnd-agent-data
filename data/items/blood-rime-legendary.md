# Blood Rime (Legendary)

**description**: Weapon (Longbow), Legendary (Requires Attunement) Common Frozen Arrow. While holding Blood Rime, you can cast ray of frost from it at will. When you cast the spell this way, you use the attack bonus you have with Blood Rime as the spell attack bonus. Uncommon You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Boreal Blessings. While holding this weapon, you ignore difficult terrain created by ice or snow. Rare Boreal Blessings allows you to see normally in areas that are lightly or heavily obscured by heavy snowfall, fog, or other wintry conditions. Arctic Casting. As long as the temperature is below freezing, you can cast dancing lights at will. In these conditions, you can also cast the faerie fire spell (spell save DC 12 + your proficiency bonus). You can cast the spell this way a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. Very Rare When you hit with an attack using this weapon, the target takes an extra 1d6 cold damage. In addition, Boreal Blessings grants you resistance to cold damage. Legendary The bonus to attack and damage rolls increases to +2. Ice in Your Veins. When you score a critical hit with Blood Rime on a target that has blood, the target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw (DC 12 + your proficiency bonus). On a failed save, it is restrained as its blood thickens and begins to freeze. A creature restrained by this effect must make another Constitution saving throw at the end of its next turn. If it successfully saves against this effect, it ends. If it fails, the creature is frozen solid, becoming petrified. If the creature is physically broken while petrified, it suffers from similar deformities if it reverts to its original state.
