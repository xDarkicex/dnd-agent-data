# Commander's Cuirass

**description**: Armor (Any Medium or Heavy), Rare (Requires Attunement) This golden breastplate is adorned with three wyrms, each biting a ruby. While wearing the armor, you have advantage on Intimidation and Persuasion checks. The armor has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge to cause one of the following effects: Courage. You exude an aura of courage in a 30-foot radius around you. Each allied creature in the area has advantage on saving throws to avoid or end the Charmed and Frightened conditions on itself. Dread. You exude an aura of dread in a 30-foot radius around you. Each hostile creature that enters the area for the first time on its turn or starts its turn there must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or be Frightened of you until the start of its next turn. Each aura effect lasts for 1 minute or until you become Incapacitated.
