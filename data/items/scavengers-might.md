# Scavenger's Might

**description**: Weapon (Flail), Rare (Requires Attunement) The heavy head of this magic flail has been imbued with eldritch runes. The flail deals an extra 1d6 Force damage on a hit. The flail has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. When you hit a creature with the flail, you can expend 1 charge to cause one of the following effects: Mighty Blow. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be thrown up to 20 feet horizontally in a direction of your choice, landing Prone and taking 1d6 Bludgeoning damage for each 10 feet it was thrown. If it hits another creature, that creature must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take the same damage. Siphon Might. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or, until the end of your next turn, it has disadvantage on ability checks, saving throws, and attack rolls using Strength, and you have advantage on ability checks, saving throws, and attack rolls using Strength.
