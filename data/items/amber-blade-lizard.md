# Amber Blade (Lizard)

**description**: Amber Blade (Lizard) melee weapon (martial, sword) This magical longsword deals additional 1d6 slashing damage to Giants and Yoten. The sword’s pommel is decorated with a chunk of amber, inside of which is a trapped object. The sword confers an additional benefit depending on the type of object: Lizard. The bonus damage changes from 1d6 to [[1d8]] (1d8).
