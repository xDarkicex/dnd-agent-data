# Starforged Trinket

**description**: Wondrous Item, Common (Requires Attunement) This magic trinket supposedly holds a sliver of a crystallized star. When you finish a long rest while attuned to the trinket, roll two d4s. If both dice show the same number, the stars have aligned in your favor; until the next dawn, you gain the corresponding benefit while wearing the trinket: Foresight. You have advantage on Initiative rolls and Dexterity saving throws. Leadership. You have advantage on Charisma checks and you and friendly creatures within 10 feet of you have advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the Frightened condition on yourself. Luck. When you roll a 1 on the d20 for an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can reroll the die and must use the new roll. If another feature already allows you to reroll 1s, you can also reroll the d20 on a roll of 2. Resilience. You gain temporary hit points equal to the number of hit dice you have.
