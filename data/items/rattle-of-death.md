# Rattle of Death

**description**: Rattle of Death Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement) This crimson wooden stick has a leering, skull-shaped rattle attached to one end. As an action, you can point and shake the rattle at one creature you can see within 60 feet. If the creature you choose has 100 hit points or fewer, it dies. Otherwise, there is no effect. A humanoid killed by this item rises at the start of your next turn as a zombie that is permanently under your command, following your orders to the best of its ability. Once used, the rattle can’t be used again until the next dawn. An object of remarkable and alarming potency, and few individuals in the multiverse can stand before it. I will not comment on rumors that I have such an object in my vaults.
