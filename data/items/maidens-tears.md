# Maiden's Tears

**description**: Potion, rare This fruity mead is the color of liquid gold and is rumored to be brewed with a tear from the goddess of bearfolk herself. When you drink this mead, you regenerate lost hit points for 1 minute. At the start of your turn, you regain 10 hit points if you have at least 1 hit point.
