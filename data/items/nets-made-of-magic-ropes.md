# Nets Made of Magic Ropes

**description**: Rope of Vanishing Wondrous Item, Uncommon This 50-foot length of rope looks completely mundane and unremarkable. The rope has been infused with subtle illusion magic. Any creature that knows the rope’s nature can touch it and use a bonus action to turn it invisible. The rope remains invisible until it is cut, or until a creature that knows its nature touches it and uses another bonus action to make it visible again. Optional Rule: Nets Made of Magic Ropes At the gamemaster’s discretion, all eight ropes could be woven or found in the form of a net. Characters with proficiency in weaver’s tools may weave a net out of one of those ropes as part of a long rest. A net made of one of the eight magic ropes is a magic weapon with the properties of a mundane net in addition to the properties of the rope that was used to weave it.
