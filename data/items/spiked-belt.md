# Spiked Belt

**description**: Whip, rare Typically used for more violent forms of flagellation, this wide, leather belt is run through all over with metal spikes. The belt functions like a whip except that it has a reach of 5 feet and deals 1d8 piercing damage on a hit. As an action, the wielder may attempt to grapple a large or smaller target with the belt. On a success, the target takes 2d6 piercing damage and loses the ability to use one of its limbs. The target has disadvantage on all attack rolls and ability checks that involve the use of that limb while grappled in this way. On a subsequent turn, the wielder may use their action to release the grapple and force the target to make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or take 6d6 slashing damage and have disadvantage on ability checks and attack rolls that involve the use of that limb. A new save can be made against this effect at the end of each of its turns. This effect counts as an Injury of Handicap severity.
