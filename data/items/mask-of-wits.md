# Mask of Wits

**description**: Mask of Wits Wondrous item, rare This strange wooden mask covers only the left side of the wearer’s face. When asked to make an ability or skill check the wearer is allowed to use a different ability score to determine their modifier. The mask may be used in this way three times. After that a long rest must be completed before the mask may be used again.
