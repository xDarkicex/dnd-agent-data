# Mechanist's Tools

**description**: This kit contains a wide variety of tools used for metalworking and robotics, including a compact saw-grinder-mill tool, a welding torch and mask, an auto-hammer, and a lathe. It also includes enough scrap metal to begin and end most inexpensive projects. Proficiency with this kit lets you add your proficiency bonus to any ability checks you make to build, repair, and modify constructs and complex machines.
