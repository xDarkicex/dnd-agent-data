# Pick Shank

**description**: Weapon (War Pick), Uncommon This weaponized prosthesis is designed to replace the wielder’s lower leg. A curved steel blade with a spiked tip acts as the “foot” with a counterweighted hammerhead for a “heel.” The thick wooden shaft magically attaches itself to the wielder’s residual limb by extending root-like tendrils which bore painlessly into the wielder’s flesh. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon, which allows you to make an attack even if all your hands are full. Attacks made with this weapon are considered unarmed strikes. Affixing or removing the weapon requires an action. Encounter Ideas Killer Contraband. Amalie, a female assassin born without a lower leg, is using this weapon to commit murders in high security locations by sneaking the weapon in under her flowing skirts or long trousers. Performance Anxiety. A one-legged burlesque dancer’s pick shank was stolen by agents from a rival nightclub, and she refuses to dance without it. Those in Need. A retired dwarven craftsman who creates pick shanks free of charge has vanished into an abandoned mine.
