# Button of Rosy Vision

**description**: Button of Rosy Vision Wondrous item, rare These buttons are always found in pairs, one made of a rose-colored gemstone and the other made of brass. When the rose button is sewn onto an item of clothing, if you hold the brass button up to your eye, you can see through the rose button as if you were located where it is. These buttons must both be on the same plane for this effect to work.
