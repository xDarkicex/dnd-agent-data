# Feathering Shortbow (Uncommon)

**description**: Feathering Weapon (Shortbow), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. This bow weighs nothing. Quick. When you roll initiative, roll a d4 and add the result to your initiative roll. You can use this property a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus and regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. Progression Feathering progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s Dexterity modifier increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
