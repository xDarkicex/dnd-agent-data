# Well Seasoned Frying Pan

**description**: Well Seasoned Frying Pan Wondrous item Price: 1,000 gp This frying pan has been owned by the most accomplished chefs from across the now and ever. Each time a chef makes their fame and fortune, it disappears from their cookware shelf, and seeks out a new owner. This frying pan imbues the holder with amazing cooking abilities. The owner of the frying pan gets a +10 bonus to any rolls made to prepare food, so long as the frying pan is used in the creation of the dish. Any dish prepared this way is always edible, cannot be poisonous or carry disease, and seems deliciously flavorful regardless of the ingredients used.
