# Dynamic Clothing

**description**: This material is tailored with responsive nanofibers whose true appearance resembles light gray cloth, but can be programmed to change shape and color. You can use a bonus action to alter the outfit’s style, cut, color, pattern, or apparent quality using up to ten templates programmed into your portable media device. The material always retains the structure of a soft, woven fabric and can’t be used to mimic any other type of material.
