# Parchment of Rolling

**description**: Parchment of Rolling Wondrous item, uncommon This 8-inch-wide sheet of parchment is wrapped around two elaborately carved mahogany rollers. No matter how far you roll down, you never run out of fresh paper to write on and the thickness of the paper wrapped around the rollers never increases. It is an infinitely large piece of writing paper.
