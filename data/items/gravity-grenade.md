# Gravity Grenade

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon This fist-sized grenade shines from within with azure light and weighs several pounds. Gravity Bomb When you take the Attack action, you can replace one of your attacks with throwing the grenade at a point on the ground you can see within 60 feet, causing the grenade to explode and produce a gravitational implosion. Each creature within 20 feet of the grenade must make a DC 15 Strength save. On a failure, the creature takes 2d8 Force damage, is pulled up to 15 feet toward the point of impact, and falls Prone. On a success, the creature takes half as much damage only.
