# Demonic Siege Bow Longbow (rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (any bow), uncommon (requires attunement by a creature with a Strength score of 13 or higher) Component: fiend (demon) horn What this weapon lacks in manoeuvrability, it makes up for in power. Wrought from the horns, bones, and sinew of demons, this weapon holds malice in every fibre. With its very own bracing mechanism, a user can free up a hand to fire using both strings at once. The damage die of this weapon is increased by one size (e.g., a longbow deals 1d10 damage, a shortbow deals 1d8). The bow has 2 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. Siege Brace. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge and speak the bow’s command word, causing a skeletal, demonic hand to burst from the ground or another surface and hold the bow steady until you use another bonus action to dismiss it. The bow can’t be moved while grasped by the skeletal hand. While in this ‘siege mode’, each time you make a ranged weapon attack using this bow, you can make one additional attack against the same target (no action required). When you do so, both attacks are made with disadvantage. Rare variant: Increase the charges to 3. The damage die of this bow is increased by two sizes instead of one, (e.g., a longbow deals 1d12 damage and a shortbow deals 1d10).
