# Slumbering Dragon Vessel

**description**: Slumbering Dragon Vessel Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) This vessel can be a potion bottle, drinking horn, or other container meant to hold a liquid. As a bonus action, if the vessel is empty, you can speak the command word to fill the vessel with one of the following (your choice): ale, olive oil, a potion of healing , or a potion of climbing . Once this property is used, it can't be used until the next dawn. A potion you create in this way loses its magical properties if it isn't imbibed within 24 hours.
