# Poisonpuff

**description**: Suffocatio, uncommon The bones of corpses who stumbled into a poisonpuff litter the ground near these dangerous and volatile fungi. Suffocatio fungi grow in small clusters throughout the Rift, making climbing into or out of the huge canyon a suicidal effort. Spore Cloud. Jostling the bulb of a poisonpuff releases a cloud of caustic vapor in a 30-foot radius. A character who caught in the cloud becomes poisoned, is unable to breathe, and takes 3 (1d6) poison damage on its turn. A character poisoned from caustic vapor can make a successful DC 15 Constitution saving throw at the end of each turn to end the ongoing damage and regain its breath, but the character remains poisoned for the next 24 hours.
