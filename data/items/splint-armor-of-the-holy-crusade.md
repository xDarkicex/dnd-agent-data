# Splint Armor of the Holy Crusade

**description**: Splint Armor of the Holy Crusade Heavy armor (splint armor), rare (requires attunement) While you wear this golden armor, it sheds dim light in a 5-foot radius around you. If you are within 60 feet of a fiend or undead, the armor instead sheds bright light in a 30-foot radius, and dim light for an additional 30 feet, and you gain a +2 bonus to your AC. The bright light shed by this armor is sunlight. AC: 17 Stealth: Disadvantage Weight: 60
