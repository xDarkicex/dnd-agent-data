# Portal Loupe

**description**: Portal Loupe Wondrous item, uncommon This brass item resembles a palm-sized, squat telescope featuring a magnifying lens at one end. A thin circle of rune-marked silver is inset into the side of the lens. As an action, the wielder can place the end of the loupe onto a vertical or horizontal surface and peer into the lens to see through a wall, floor, or door. The portal loupe can see through wood, stone, or metal with a thickness of no more than 5 feet. Using the loupe’s magic to see through a barrier doesn’t allow the wielder to use its own darkvision, truesight, or other method of perception.
