# Banner of the Crimson Dawn

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) This banner is enchanted such that the blood it carries never fades and never oxidizes, and carries the symbol of the Crimson Dawn blood cult. Its crimson cloth is made of silk taken from vernassian bloodmoths, soaked with the blood of this once-powerful cult’s victims. While attuned to this item, as long as it is carried on a polearm, quarterstaff, or spear, it flutters in a supernatural wind and grants the weapon a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls unless it already had a higher bonus. Additionally, whenever you inflict a Death Blow with the weapon, the banner gains one charge. The banner loses all charges whenever you finish a long rest. As long as the banner has at least one charge, it emits an aura of bloodlust granting you and your allies bonuses depending on the number of charges as shown in the table below. The aura has all the effects for each level of charges it has, and uses the largest radius for all of its effects. Number Of Charges Radius Effect 1-2 10 feet Whenever you or an ally within the aura inflicts an Injury, they gain 5 temporary hit points 3-5 20 feet Whenever you or an ally within the aura declares their intent to Injure, you may use your reaction to cause the next attack they make to deal an additional 2d6 damage 6+ 30 feet The DC to resist Death Blows inflicted by you and each ally within the aura is increased by 2
