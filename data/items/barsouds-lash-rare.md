# Barsoud's Lash (Rare)

**description**: Barsoud's Lash Weapon (Greatsword), Rare (Requires Attunement) Barsoud’s Lash’s reach increases to 10 feet. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you hit a Celestial or a Demon with this weapon, the target takes an extra 1d6 slashing damage. Progression Barsoud’s Lash progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
