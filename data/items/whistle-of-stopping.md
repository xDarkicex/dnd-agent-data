# Whistle of Stopping

**description**: Whistle of Stopping Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) When you blow this silver whistle, choose a humanoid that you can see within 60 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be paralyzed for one minute. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make another Wisdom saving throw. On a success, the effect ends on the target. Once used, this property of the whistle can’t be used again until the next dawn.
