# Retractable Melee Weapon

**description**: Retractable Melee Weapon Augment (limb), common (requires affinity) You have a one-handed melee weapon mounted into one of your arms. As a bonus action, you can retract or extend the weapon. While it is extended, you can use the weapon as if you were holding it, and you can’t use that hand for other purposes. It can’t be detached nor thrown. While retracted, the weapon is invisible. Proficiency with the weapon allows you to add your proficiency bonus to the attack roll for any attack you make with it.
