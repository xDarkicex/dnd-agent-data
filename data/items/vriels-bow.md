# Vriel's Bow

**description**: Weapon (longbow, Heavy), rare (requires attunement) Range: 150/600 feet Ammunition (range 150/600 feet), Heavy, Two-Handed Hero’s bow: this longbow is oversized and can only be wielded by a creature with a Strength score of 13 or more. It deals 1d12 piercing damage. Shredding Tip: When you hit a creature with a ranged weapon attack with this bow, you can use your reaction to choose another creature within 5ft of the first one. They suffer piercing damage equal to your Strength or Dexterity modifier (whichever is higher). Infernal Arrow (5/day): When you make a ranged weapon attack with this bow, you can use a bonus action to imbue the arrow with infernal energies. This attack deals an additional 4 (1d8) necrotic damage on a hit on top of your regular damage.
