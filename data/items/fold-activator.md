# Fold Activator

**description**: Fold Activator Utility, very rare (requires affinity) This device has 3 charges. It can be worn around the wrist or on the back of your hand. While wearing it, you can use an action to expend 1 charge and use the device to activate the space fold power. The device regains 1d3 expended charges when you finish a long rest.
