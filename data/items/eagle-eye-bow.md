# Eagle Eye Bow

**description**: Weapon (longbow), legendary (requires attunement by an elf) This fine bow of golden oak is inlaid with a foliage pattern, reminiscent of sharp aspen leaves. You gain a +1 to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. Eagle Eye. If your attack roll hits, but you did not score a critical hit, you may roll the attack again. If the second roll also hits, you may treat the attack as a critical hit.
