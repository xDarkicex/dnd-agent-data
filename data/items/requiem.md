# Requiem

**description**: Weapon (Glaive), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic glaive produces an eerie whisper when swung. The glaive deals an extra 1d8 Necrotic damage on a hit and attacks you make with it score a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20. Dark Step. While you are in dim light or darkness, you can teleport up to 10 feet to an unoccupied space immediately before or after each attack you make with the glaive. Unleash Shadow. When you score a critical hit against a creature with the glaive, you wrest control of the creature's shadow. A shadow appears in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the creature. The shadow takes its turn immediately after yours and obeys your telepathic commands (no action required). The shadow has advantage on attack rolls against the creature it was spawned from. The shadow vanishes after 1 minute or when it is reduced to 0 hit points.
