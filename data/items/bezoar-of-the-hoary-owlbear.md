# Bezoar of the Hoary Owlbear

**description**: Wondrous item, rare While this bezoar sits in your stomach, you have resistance to cold damage, and you can tolerate temperatures as low as -50 degrees Fahrenheit. In addition, your speed increases by 5 feet. Curse. The bezoar is not sitting well with you and triggers intense heartburn and nausea. When you would regain hit points for any reason (magical healing, resting, or similar), you regain only half the amount.
