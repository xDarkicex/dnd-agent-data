# Antimagic Ring Mail

**description**: Antimagic Ring Mail Armor (Heavy), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) While wearing this armor, you can use your reaction to give yourself advantage on a saving throw you make against a spell. Once this property is used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn. In addition, while you wear this armor, you can use it to cast Antimagic Field , requiring no spell components.
