# Starlight Morningstar

**description**: Starlight Morningstar Weapon (Morningstar), Rare The iron head of this morningstar is forged in the shape of a starburst. When you hit with an attack roll using this magic weapon, the target takes an extra 1d6 radiant damage. While under a night sky, you can call down a hail of falling stars in a 30-foot radius centered on the weapon. Each creature of your choice in the area must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw. A target takes 4d6 radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Once used , this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest.
