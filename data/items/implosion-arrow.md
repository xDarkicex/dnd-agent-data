# Implosion Arrow

**description**: Implosion Arrow Ammunition, uncommon When you hit with an attack using this arrow, it creates a sphere of gravity 15 feet in diameter centered on the target. Each creature other than the target within the sphere must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature is pulled 10 feet toward the target, and it takes 3d6 bludgeoning damage and 1d6 force damage. The target and objects automatically fail this saving throw.
