# Edephiar's Mirror Staff (Legendary)

**description**: Weapon (Quarterstaff), Legendary (Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster) Common While holding this magic weapon, you can cast prestidigitation. Uncommon The weapon has 6 charges, and regains 1d4 + 2 expended charges when you finish a long rest. You can expend 2 charges to cast mirror image as a bonus action. Rare You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. Reflective Protection. While holding this weapon, you have advantage on saving throws made against gaze attacks. Very Rare The weapon’s total number of charges increases to 9, and it now regains 1d6 + 3 expended charges when you finish a long rest. You can expend 5 charges to cast mislead. When you make a Charisma (Persuasion) check while holding this weapon, you can treat a d20 roll of 9 or lower as a 10. Legendary The weapon’s total number of charges increases to 12, and it now regains 1d8 + 4 expended charges when you finish a long rest. You can expend 7 charges to cast project image or 8 charges to cast glibness.
