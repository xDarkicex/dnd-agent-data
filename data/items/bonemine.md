# Bonemine

**description**: Explosive, uncommon As an action, a character can throw the bonemine at a point up to 40 feet away or plant and arm the bonemine in the ground. If thrown, the bonemine explodes immediately, sending shards of bone shrapnel into nearby creatures. If planted, the bonemine detonates when a creature enters a space within 5 feet of it. Each creature within 20 feet of an exploding bonemine must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw , taking 3d6 piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
