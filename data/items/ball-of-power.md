# Ball of Power

**description**: Ball of Power Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement) This crystal sphere is 3 inches wide and glows with a soft, purple light. While holding it in your hand, you can use an action to speak the command word and activate it. It remains activated for 1 minute. During this time, you have resistance to all damage and your Strength score changes to 25. The item has no effect on your Strength score if it is already 25 or higher. Once used, the ball can’t be used again until the next dawn. Modifiers: Strength: 25
