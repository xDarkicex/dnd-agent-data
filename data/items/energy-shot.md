# Energy Shot

**description**: Available as an ampoule or a 50ml bottle, consuming this liquid gives you allows you to remain awake during a long rest and still gain its benefits. In addition, you can’t be put to sleep by any substances, items, esper powers, or similar effects. This benefit lasts for 8 hours. After using an energy shot, you become immune to its effects for 24 hours.
