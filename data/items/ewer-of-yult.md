# Ewer of Yult

**description**: Wondrous item, unique The ewer of Yult is an unadorned pewter jug. On the underside is engraved in common, “If you’ d have it cleaner, earn it.” Each day the ewer can create four gallons of dirty, bitter-tasting gutter water. Any creature drinking the untreated water must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution save or become sick for 1d4 days. While sick, the creature will not be able to take a long rest. The water produced is not magical, and can be cleaned by boiling or with the appropriate spells.
