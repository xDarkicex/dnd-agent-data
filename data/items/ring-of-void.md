# Ring of Void

**description**: Ring of Void Wondrous item, legendary A band of black onyx. Looking at the band for more than a moment fills the viewer with a sense of eerie calm. The wearer of this ring may spend an action to create a small vortex that attracts and consumes all free-flowing gas and liquids in a 30-foot radius of the wearer over the course of 6 seconds. This includes toxins, water, and oxygen. Liquids and gas inside of a creature’s body are unaffected. Liquids inside water skins and other containers are affected. The consumed matter is transported to a pocket dimension. If the Ring of Void is worn during a long rest, the wearer becomes attuned. The empowerment word can only be used once per long rest. While attuned, the wearer benefits from the following effects: If the wearer speaks the empowerment word “Eternity” as they active the ring, the vortex instead expels all matter that was previously consumed by the ring. The wearer gains +2 to all saving throws they make. The wearer gains resistance to psychic damage. If they already had resistance, they instead reduce the damage they would receive by 10, after resistance is applied.
