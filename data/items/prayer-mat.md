# Prayer Mat

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement by a cleric or paladin) This small rug is woven with intricate patterns that depict religious iconography. When you attune to it, the iconography and the mat’s colors change to the iconography and colors most appropriate for your deity. If you spend 10 minutes praying to your deity while kneeling on this mat, you regain one expended use of Channel Divinity. The mat can’t be used this way again until the next dawn.

**properties**:
