# Death Petal Rose

**description**: Wondrous Item, Common (Requires Attunement) This magic rose has 3 charges. When you fail on a death saving throw while carrying this rose, you can expend 1 charge to succeed instead. Once all of its charges have been used, the rose withers and is destroyed.
