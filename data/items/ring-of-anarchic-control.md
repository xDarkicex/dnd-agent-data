# Ring of Anarchic Control

**description**: Ring of Anarchic Control Ring, rare (requires attunement) This glass ring has small imperfections in its form, including small cracks and bubbles. When you cast a spell that has the chaos descriptor while you are attuned to this ring, you gain a +2 bonus on your spell attack roll, and your spell save DC increases by 2. In addition, whenever you roll on the Chaos Magic table (see Chapter 6 ) or any similar table while wearing this ring, you can roll twice and use the result you prefer.
