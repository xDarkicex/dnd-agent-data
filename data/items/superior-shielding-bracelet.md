# Superior Shielding Bracelet

**description**: Superior Shielding Bracelet Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) This metal bracelet is fashioned from a strange reddish-gold damascene metal with one or more green stones set into it. When activated as a bonus action, the bracelet generates a barrier of invisible force forming a bubble around you. While you are surrounded by the barrier created by the bracelet, all damage you take is reduced by 3d6 per attack. Each variety of shielding bracelet has a maximum amount of damage it can deflect over the course of a single activation before dissipating. Once the bracelet reaches 50 damage, it can’t absorb more and dissipates. Once you activate the bracelet, you must finish a short or long rest before you can activate it again.
