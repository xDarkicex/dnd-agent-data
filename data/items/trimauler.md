# Trimauler

**description**: The Trimauler shows unrelenting cruelty to its enemies and selfless kindness to its friends. Legend The Trimauler is an ancient trident with an obsidian handle, its three prongs fashioned from the pilfered teeth of an adolescent dragon turtle. It was forged millennia ago by merfolk for their champion Phrygia, whom legend described as a warrior as compassionate as they were fearsome. The orange and red scarf tied to the Trimauler is a remnant from the dress of their lover, Locria, and is said to have healing properties. The material never gets wet, even when submerged in water. Location When Phrygia died, their trident was buried with them, deep inside a cavern leagues beneath the waves. Its location is thought to be lost to time, though the disgruntled dragon turtle whose teeth were used to fashion the weapon knows where her missing teeth are buried. Encounter Ideas Tomb Hunters. The party are hired to accompany a group of deep-sea archaeologists in search of Phrygia’s tomb, where they find the trident hung above the urn containing the merfolk’s ashes. Belly Treasure. The party is swallowed by a gargantuan aquatic beast and finds the trident lodged in the leviathan’s belly. Superstition. While the party is visiting a fishing town, the trident washes up on shore and alarms the superstitious townsfolk. Progression The Trimauler progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. As the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, so does the weapon’s rarity. Statistics Weapon (Trident), Rarity Varies (Requires Attunement) Common . Trimauler’s Touch . The trident has 3 charges. As an action, you can expend a charge to restore 1d4 + 1 hit points to all creatures of your choice within 10 feet of you. The trident regains all expended charges when you finish a long rest. Uncommon . You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The hit points restored by Trimauler’s Touch increase to 2d4 + 2. Rare . The hit points restored by Trimauler’s Touch increase to 3d4 + 3, and it can now affect creatures up to 20 feet away from you. Very Rare . The bonus to attack and damage rolls increases to +2, and the hit points restored by Trimauler’s Touch increase to 4d4 + 4. Legendary . The bonus to attack and damage rolls increases to +3, the hit points restored by Trimauler’s Touch increase to 5d4 + 5, and it can now affect creatures up to 30 feet away from you.
