# Ascension (Uncommon)

**description**: Ascension Weapon (Greatsword), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) While attuned to this magic weapon, you have advantage on Charisma checks made to advance the interests of the territory you rule, such as negotiating a treaty or securing resources for your kingdom. It is not intended for checks that only benefit you personally. Progression Ascension progresses each time its owner significantly increases the size of the territory they rule, as determined by the GM.
