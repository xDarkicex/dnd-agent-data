# Hunter's Charm, common

**description**: Wondrous item, common (+1) (requires attunement by a ranger) This small fetish is made out of bones, feathers, and semi-precious gems. Typically worn around the neck, this charm can also be wrapped around your brow or wrist or affixed to a weapon. While wearing or carrying this charm, you have a bonus to attack and damage rolls made against your favored enemies. The bonus is determined by the charm’s rarity.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: common
