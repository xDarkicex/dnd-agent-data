# Trimauler (Common)

**description**: Trimauler Weapon (Trident), Common (Requires Attunement) Trimauler’s Touch . The trident has 3 charges. As an action, you can expend a charge to restore 1d4 + 1 hit points to all creatures of your choice within 10 feet of you. The trident regains all expended charges when you finish a long rest. Progression The Trimauler progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. As the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, so does the weapon’s rarity.
