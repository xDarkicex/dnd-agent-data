# Smothering Cape

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic cape has 33 hit points and regains all hit points daily at dawn. When an attacker that you can see hits you with an attack, you can use your reaction to halve the attack’s damage against you and the cape takes the other half. If the cape is reduced to 0 hit points, you take any remaining damage. Rug of Smothering. As a bonus action, you can speak the cape’s command word to cause it to animate. The animated cape has the statistics of a rug of smothering , except for its hit points, which remain the same in either form. The cape is under your control and acts on its own initiative count. On your turn, you can mentally command the cape if it is within 60 feet of you and you aren’t incapacitated. You decide what action the cape takes and where it moves during its next turn, or you can issue it a general command, such as to attack your enemies. The cape reverts to its inanimate form after 1 minute or when it is reduced to 0 hit points. Once used, this property can’t be used again until the next dawn.
