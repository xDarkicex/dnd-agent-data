# Sand Skimmer

**description**: This wooden land vehicle slides atop sand, propelled by a single sail. The skimmer sits atop two narrow runners attached to a flat wooden platform. The platform is five feet wide and ten feet long. One driver pilots the skimmer; it can also hold one passenger. Depending on the wind, the sand skimmer reaches speeds up to 8 miles per hour.
