# Flumph Whip (Uncommon)

**description**: Flumph Whip Weapon (Whip), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you hit a Large or smaller target with the whip, you can also attempt to grapple the target using either a Strength (Athletics) or Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) check. You can still use the whip to attack a creature you have grappled. Only one creature can be grappled at a time. You can use this malodorous whip to interact with or grab an unattended object within 10 feet that weighs less than 20 pounds. You can drop an object held by the whip in a space of your choice within 10 feet. An evil creature wielding or touching this weapon takes 1d6 force damage at the end of each their turns. Odor. The whip emits a strong smell of decay, which is noticeable to creatures that can smell that are within 15 feet of the whip. Perception checks based on smell have advantage to locate the whip or notice any creature who might be in possession of it. In addition, while attuned to the whip, you have disadvantage on Charisma checks. Progression The Flumph Whip progresses each time the character performs impactful lawful or good acts, as determined by the GM.
