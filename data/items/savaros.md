# Savaros

**description**: Savaros Elder Rune of Goblinkind Bane Effect: All nonmagical coins and gems on the target’s person vanish. Boon Effect: The rune magically summons a goblin (use the goblin stat block in the Monster Manua l), which appears in an unoccupied space within 20 feet of the target. The goblin is rude to everyone except the target and obeys the target’s commands. The goblin vanishes in a puff of smoke if it drops to 0 hit points.
