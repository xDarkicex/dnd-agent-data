# Anachronism

**description**: Anachronism Weapon (Dagger), Rare (Requires Attunement) This blade is forged from unusually hard steel, shaped with impossible precision, and coated in a strange black glaze that absorbs light and resists corrosion. The hilt is made of stacked, tightly woven leather discs. Four strange runes are stamped into the bottom of the blade. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you take damage from an attack while wielding Anachronism , you can use your reaction to mark the attack. If the attacker dies before the end of your next turn, remove the damage dealt by the marked attack, as if it had never hit you (restoring any hit points lost). If the attack also inflicted a negative effect or condition, that effect or condition ends on you. Once this property has successfully nullified a marked attack, it can’t be used again until you finish a short or long rest.
