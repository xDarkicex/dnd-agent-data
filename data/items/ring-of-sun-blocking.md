# Ring of Sun Blocking

**description**: Ring, uncommon Some residents of the city of Kanrath have a particular apathy towards sunlight but they still want to enjoy daytime. This ring is a common solution to this problem. While wearing this ring, the radiant damage you take from sunlight decreases by 5, if you are affected by it. Also, you are immune to being blinded.
