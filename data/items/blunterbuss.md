# Blunterbuss

**description**: Blunterbuss Weapon (Greatclub), Uncommon This greatclub was once a hand cannon, but its barrel was filled with rocks and sealed. Copious scorch marks, crude welds, and dents indicate that it probably didn’t work well as a firearm. As a bonus action, you can load the blunterbuss with gunpowder. The next time you hit with a melee weapon attack using this magic weapon, it detonates. The target and each creature (including you) within 10 feet of them must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 2d6 fire damage and 2d6 thunder damage and is knocked prone. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and isn’t knocked prone. Once used, this property can’t be used again until the blunterbuss is repaired. This requires 5 gp of materials and takes 1 hour, which can be done during a short or long rest.
