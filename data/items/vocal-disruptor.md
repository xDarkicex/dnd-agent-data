# Vocal Disruptor

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon This metallic box features a sleek handle, a small antenna, and an array of dials on its surface. Distorted Sound As a Bonus Action, you can use this device to create a 120-foot-Cone that disrupts speech for 1 minute or until you end it (no action required). A creature in the area that tries to speak or cast a spell with a verbal component for the first time on a turn must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom save or it can't speak until the start of its next turn. At the start of each of your turns, you decide which way the Cone faces. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
