# Honey of Dead Fields

**description**: Potion, uncommon This spirit honey is made from flowers that bloom in undead-filled graveyards, on battlefields, and in other areas filled with undead or that are ripe grounds for creating undead. When you consume this honey, you have resistance to necrotic damage for the duration. In addition, when you roll a 20 on an attack roll made with a melee weapon against a creature that isn’t a construct or undead, you regain hit points equal to half the damage dealt. Insight. At the end of the spirit honey’s duration, choose a corpse within 10 feet of you. You give the corpse a semblance of life and intelligence, allowing it to answer your questions. This works like the speak with dead spell, except the corpse must answer truthfully, even if it recognizes you as an enemy.
