# Mask

**description**: Mask Adventuring gear Many sets of armor include a mempō, or protective mask, to cover the wearer’s face on the battlefield. This mask is crafted from iron or leather, covered with lacquer or clay, and painted to resemble a face. While some depict humanlike features, many showcase jagged oni teeth, a ferocious mouth, or a frightening expression. Members of the Scorpion Clan also often wear elaborate masks at court, to remind themselves and others that the perils of court are no less deadly than those of the battlefield.
