# Treebleed Bucket

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon This combination sap bucket and tap is used to extract sap from certain trees, after which the sap magically changes into a potion. The potion remains viable for 24 hours, and its type depends on the tree as follows: oak (potion of resistance), rowan (potion of healing), willow (potion of animal friendship), and holly (potion of climbing). A treebleed bucket has 20 charges. When its charges are expended, the bucket and tap become nonmagical.
