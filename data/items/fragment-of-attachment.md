# Fragment of Attachment

**description**: Fragment of Attachment Benefit . You can’t be frightened while within 10 feet of an ally. If you’re already frightened and move within 10 feet of an ally, the frightened condition ends on you. Drawback . You have disadvantage on Wisdom and Charisma saving throws while you aren’t within 10 feet of an ally.
