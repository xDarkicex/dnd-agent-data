# Avalanche Smasher (Common)

**description**: Avalanche Smasher Weapon (Flail), Common (Requires Attunement) Avalanche Smasher has 1 head and 2 charges. It regains 1 charge daily at dawn. While holding this magic weapon, you can expend 2 or more of its charges to activate one of its properties. The saving throw DC for this weapon’s properties is equal to 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Constitution modifier. Infuse (2 Charges). As a bonus action, you infuse biting frost into the weapon. The next time you hit a creature with an attack using Avalanche Smasher , the attack deals 1d6 cold damage and the target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or have their speed halved until the end of their next turn. Progression The tears of a cold-breathing dragon form new heads that can be attached to the flail.
