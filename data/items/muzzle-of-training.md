# Muzzle of Training

**description**: Muzzle of Training Wondrous item, uncommon This leather muzzle can be placed on any beast that has a snout, with the muzzle magically growing or shrinking to fit correctly. While a beast is wearing the muzzle, any Animal Handling checks made against it have advantage.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
