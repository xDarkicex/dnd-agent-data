# Encyclopedia Multiplanaria

**description**: This datapad is enchanted to hold an incredible wealth of information. However, the data is not indexed, so the datapad only provides data that it believes will be useful at any given moment. The datapad has 3 charges, and regains all charges daily at dawn. You can use your action to access the datapad when you make an Intelligence check to gain a +20 bonus to the roll.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: wondrous item
- Item Rarity: legendary

**publisher**: Mage Hand Press

**book**: Dark Matter Starter Kit
