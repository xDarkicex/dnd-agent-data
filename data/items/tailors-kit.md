# Tailor's Kit

**description**: Tailor's Kit Adventuring gear (artisan's tools) Rokugani tailor's kits often contain cloth patches of linen or homespun cotton (and rarely silk), hemp thread, sewing needles, and dye tablets. Some kits include cleaning powder, a small seam ripper, and a collapsing bamboo drying rod; to clean a kimono, the garment must be deconstructed, each cloth panel must be washed and dried separately, and then the panels must be sewn back together.
