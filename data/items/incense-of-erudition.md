# Incense of Erudition

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon This magical incense burns for 1 hour and produces a soothing scent of nutmeg and morning dew. A creature that inhales the incense during a short rest gains advantage on Wisdom, Intelligence, and Charisma ability checks and saving throws for 8 hours.
