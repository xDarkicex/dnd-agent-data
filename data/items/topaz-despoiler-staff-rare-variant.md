# Topaz Despoiler Staff (rare variant)

**description**: Rod, staff, or wand, uncommon (requires attunement) Component: dragon (topaz) heart The articulated mechanical hand at the tip of this item sporadically animates with bursts of negative energy. The fingers fondly caress the flawless topaz orb fixed to the palm, which pulses hypnotically with a gentle, steady glow. Entrap Essence. While holding this item, you can absorb the last breath of a dying creature. When you reduce a Small or larger creature to 0 hit points, you gain 5 temporary hit points. Induce Decay. You can use this item to feed off of the imitation of life you shield yourself with in order to harm and destroy. When you hit a creature with an attack while you are holding this item, you can expend up to 3 temporary hit points to deal extra necrotic damage to the creature equal to the number of temporary hit points expended. Rare variant: This item has the Entropic Shield property.
