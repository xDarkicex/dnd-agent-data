# Pamphlet of Bogo

**description**: Pamphlet of Bogo Spell catalyst, very rare A strange, paper card printed using technology far beyond what is commonly available, featuring images of food and extolling the glory of some unknown entity named Bogo. When you cast the heroes’ feast spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, up to twenty-four creatures can partake of the feast, instead of twelve.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Spell catalyst
- Item Rarity: very rare

**publisher**: Sterling Vermin Adventuring Co.

**book**: The Ultimate Adventurer's Handbook
