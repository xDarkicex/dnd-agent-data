# Technomagical Puzzle Box

**description**: Mechanical Trap This decorative metal crate has dozens of interlocking rods, plates, and pressure points. Casual inspection of the mechanisms reveals the metallic rods secure most of the moveable parts, thereby preventing their manipulation. A successful DC 20 Disable Device check can remove the rods, or the PCs can break them off with a few carefully placed blows (AC 17, hp 10). Either approach, however, triggers an electrical surge that conjures a unique ball lightning effect (treat as a flaming sphere that deals lightning damage). This phenomenon aggressively pursues any living creature within 60 feet of the crate for 3 rounds. The trap resets on the next round, and the PCs must either disable it before further manipulating the crate’s locking mechanisms (DC 25 Dexterity check with thieves’ tools) or avoid triggering it by moving the parts in the correct order. The trap can be detected and disabled with successful Intelligence (Arcana) checks (DC 20 for both), or with a successful dispel magic ability check against DC 16. Solving the puzzle box requires a DC 25 Dexterity check with thieves’ tools. A successful DC 15 Intelligence (Investigation) check before the attempt grants advantage on the Dexterity check. Other PCs can attempt this Intelligence check by using the Help action, even if they are otherwise unable to open the lock on their own. In addition, several clicks, whirs, and pops emanate from the box’s interior as the PCs manipulate the mechanisms.
