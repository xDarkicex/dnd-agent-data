# Rifle, sniper

**description**: Rifle, Sniper Ranged weapon (martial) Using this weapon requires an attack action and a bonus action. The minimum distance for this weapon is 40 ft. If used at a closer distance, the attacker will suffer a disadvantage to the attack roll. Attacks with this weapon score a critical hit on a 19 or 20. If you use this weapon with a weapon stand or while you are prone, the recoil value is reduced to 1. Using this weapon while prone does not give you disadvantage to your attack rolls due to the condition. Damage : 2d8 Damage Type : piercing Properties : Heavy, high-velocity, recoil (3), reload, two handed, special Range : 300/2000 Weight : 8
