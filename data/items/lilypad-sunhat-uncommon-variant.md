# Lilypad Sunhat (uncommon variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, common (requires attunement) Component: pouch of plant leaves The water in the top of this hat never sloshes and can’t be poured out. It can, however, be scooped out in a drinking vessel, and contains up to four pints. Once water is removed, the hat fills back up over the course of 1 minute. While you wear the hat outdoors during the day, it can feed you through photosynthesis. The time you take to gain a day’s worth of nourishment depends on the brightness: 1 hour in bright, direct sunlight or 8 hours in overcast conditions. Uncommon variant: The item has the Thrive in the Sun property. Thrive in the Sun. If you wear this hat in sunlight during a short rest in which you spend at least one Hit Die to regain hit points, you regain an additional 3d6 hit points at the end of the rest. Alternatively, you can choose to end the poisoned condition on yourself or cure a disease that’s currently affecting you.
