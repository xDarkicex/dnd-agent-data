# Fireorb Bomb

**description**: These ornately engraved metal spheres are favored by dwarves. On a hit, this bomb deals 1d4 piercing damage plus 1d6 fire damage to the target, and each creature within 5 feet of the target must succeed on a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw or take 1d6 fire damage.
