# Sunken Anchor (very rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (war pick), uncommon (requires attunement) Component: monstrosity antler Covered in flakes of blue-green bronze and barnacles, this sturdy implement looks like it’s seen better days. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Anchor. As an action, you can point this weapon at a point on a surface within 60 feet of you, causing an ethereal chain to tether you to that point for the next 10 minutes, or until you dismiss it as a bonus action. For the duration, you can’t be willingly moved away from that point, except by a weight of 8,000 pounds or more, or if a creature trying to move you succeeds on a DC 25 Strength check, which causes the chain to snap and the effect to end. The effect also ends early if you ever willingly move more than 60 feet from the point or if you fall unconscious. Hydrodynamic. Attacking while underwater doesn’t impose disadvantage on attack rolls you make with this weapon. Rare variant: While holding this weapon, you have advantage on ability checks and saving throws made to avoid being knocked prone or moved against your will. Very rare variant: Increase the attack and damage bonus to +2. While holding this weapon, you have advantage on ability checks and saving throws made to avoid being knocked prone or moved against your will. Additionally, while holding this weapon, you can cast the riptide * spell (save DC 16) from it. Once the weapon has been used to cast this spell, it can’t be used to cast it again until the next dawn. *See Appendix A
