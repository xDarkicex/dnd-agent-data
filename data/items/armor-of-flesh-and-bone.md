# Armor of Flesh and Bone

**description**: Armor (any), rarity varies This horrifying armor is made of a living amalgamation of flesh and bone. It fits snugly over its wearer’s body, protecting them from grievous harm. The armor grants the wearer an extra Injury slot of any severity up to the maximum allowed by the armor’s type in the table below. When the wearer becomes Injured, they may have the armor become Injured instead, filling that Injury slot. The wearer ignores the effect of that Injury, but it counts as one of their Injuries for any other effect, such as the Masochistic Bloodrage feat. The armor recovers from Injuries much faster than normal, but cannot be healed of them. It recovers from a Setback or Dangerous Injury after 1 long rest, or a Handicap or Deadly Injury after 2 long rests. A Lethal Injury recovers after 3 long rests. Sets of armor with Deadly or Lethal Injury slots also grant a +1 bonus to AC.
