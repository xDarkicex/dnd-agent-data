# Siselmi Crystal

**description**: Siselmi Crystal Spell catalyst, common This pyramid-shaped crystal can be found in magic-enriched mines or, rarely, inside of earth elementals. When you cast the magic missile spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, the spell creates an additional dart.

- Item Type: Spell catalyst
- Item Rarity: common
