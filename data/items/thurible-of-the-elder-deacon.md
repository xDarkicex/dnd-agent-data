# Thurible of the Elder Deacon

**description**: Weapon (flail), uncommon (requires attunement) Component: aberration fat This solid steel incense dispenser dangles at the end of a black metal chain. Heady perfume follows in the wake of the bearer of this weighty weapon. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The flail has 3 charges and regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. Gaseous Spells. While holding it, you can expend 1 or more of its charges to cast one of the following spells from it (save DC 13): fog cloud (1 charge), misty step (2 charges), and stinking cloud (3 charges). You can increase the spell slot level by one for each additional charge you expend.
