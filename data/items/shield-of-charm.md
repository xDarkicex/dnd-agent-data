# Shield of Charm

**description**: Armor (shield), rare (requires attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to AC while holding this shield. When you are hit by a weapon attack, you can use your reaction to cast the charm person spell on your attacker (save DC 13). Once used, this property of the shield can’t be used again until the next dawn.
