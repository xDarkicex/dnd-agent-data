# Acid

**description**: Acid (vial) Adventuring gear As an action , you can splash the contents of this vial onto a creature within 5 feet of you or throw the vial up to 20 feet, shattering it on impact. In either case, make a ranged Attack against a creature or object, treating the acid as an improvised weapon . On a hit, the target takes 2d6 acid damage . Damage: 2d6 Damage Type: Acid Properties: Improvised Weapon Range: 20 Weight: 1
