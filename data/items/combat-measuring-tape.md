# Combat Measuring Tape

**description**: Combat Measuring Tape Weapon (whip, dagger, shortsword, or longsword), Rare (requires attunement) Crafted by combat-ready technognome artificers, this item appears to be a measuring tape with an ergonomic hilt. While grasping the hilt, you can use a bonus action to cause a metal strip with measurement markings to extend out of the hilt up to its maximum length of 15 feet, adjust the strip’s extended length, or make the strip disappear. While the strip is extended, you can use it to measure lengths or distances of up to its extended length or use it as a weapon. You decide the Combat Measuring Tape’s weapon type depending on how much you choose to extend its metal strip. Regardless of weapon type, the Combat Measuring Tape always has the finesse and light properties. The tape can be used as a dagger that lacks the thrown property, as a shortsword, as a longsword that lacks the versatile property, or as a whip that adds 10 feet to your reach when you attack with it, instead of 5. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with the Combat Measuring Tape when used as a weapon. Item Variant: Greater Combat Measuring Tape At the gamemaster’s discretion, a legendary version of this item can be found. The Greater Combat Measuring Tape grants a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls (instead of +2) and scores a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20. Player characters may undertake a quest to upgrade a Combat Measuring Tape into a Greater Combat Measuring Tape, possibly by discovering a secret technognomish schematic.
