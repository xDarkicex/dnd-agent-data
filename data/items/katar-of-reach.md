# Katar of Reach

**description**: Katar of Reach Weapon (longsword), rare Damage: 1d8+2 slashing Weight: 2 lbs. Properties: Finesse, Light, Reach This magic weapon has three blades attached to a cross bar handle. While wielding it you gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls. You have advantage on any roll to avoid being disarmed of this weapon. In addition, you can speak a command word to extend the katar’s reach to 10 feet for any melee attack made with this weapon. Proficiency with a longsword allows you to add your proficiency bonus to the attack roll for any attack you make with the katar.
