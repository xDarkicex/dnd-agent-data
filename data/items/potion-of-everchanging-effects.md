# Potion of Everchanging Effects

**description**: Potion, very rare When you first drink this potion, and again at the start of each of your turns while under its effects, roll on the table below to determine which effect occurs for that turn. Each Potion of Everchanging Effects lasts for 1d4+1 rounds. If you are proficient in alchemist’s supplies you can attempt to create a potion of everchanging effects by using alchemist’s supplies to make a DC 15 Intelligence check and mixing together one of each of the following: potion of flying, potion of growth, potion of healing, potion of invisibility, potion of speed, and any variation of a potion of resistance (acid, cold, fire, force, lightning, necrotic, poison, psychic, radiant, thunder). On a failed check, you accidentally create a flask of alchemist’s fire instead. d6 Result 1 You gain a flying speed equal to your walking speed until the beginning of your next turn. 2 You gain the “enlarge” effect of the enlarge/reduce spell until the beginning of your next turn. 3 You regain 4d4 + 4 hit points. 4 You become invisible until the beginning of your next turn. Anything you wear or carry is invisible with you. The effect ends if you attack or cast a spell. 5 You gain the effect of the haste spell until the beginning of your next turn. 6 You gain resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.
