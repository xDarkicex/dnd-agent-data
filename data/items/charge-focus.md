# Charge Focus

**description**: Charge Focus Focus, uncommon (requires affinity by a channeler or forger) This focus comes in the form of a small piece of jewelry (ring, bracelet, earring, etc.). While you’re wearing it, you can use an action to harness its energy and regain 1d4 + 1 talent points or 1 expended tech slot. If the expended tech slot was rank 4 or higher, the new slot is rank 3. Once you use this focus, it can’t be used again until you finish a long rest.
