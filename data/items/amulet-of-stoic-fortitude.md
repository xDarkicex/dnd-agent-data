# Amulet of Stoic Fortitude

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This simple iron amulet is carved with a triskelion of interconnected lines. While attuned to this amulet, your Injury thresholds increase by an amount equal to half of your level. Additionally, when you would be reduced to 0 hit points, you may spend your reaction to gain twice your level in temporary hit points before taking damage. These temporary hit points last until the end of your next turn, and damage dealt to them does not apply against your Injury thresholds. Once used, the amulet cannot be activated until dawn the following day.
