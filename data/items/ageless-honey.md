# Ageless Honey

**description**: Wondrous item, rare Crafted only by the Honey Queens of the Old Margreve from honeys made from the pollen of the effildawnan ﬂower, a small spoonful of this purple-tinged honey causes you to fall into a deep sleep for 1 month (a successful DC 20 Constitution saving throw reduces this time to 1 day). During this time, you are subject to neither aging nor hunger.
