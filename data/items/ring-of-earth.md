# Ring of Earth

**description**: Ring of Earth Wondrous item, legendary A band of emerald etched with the silhouette of a mountain range. The ring constantly produces the smell of fresh, wet soil. The wearer of this ring may spend a bonus action or reaction to instantaneously form a 3-foot-tall mound of earth directly in front of them. The mound counts as half-cover, giving a creature +2 to their AC and Dexterity saving throws. A mound cannot be created on top of another mound. If the Ring of Earth is worn during a long rest, the wearer becomes attuned. The empowerment word can only be used once per long rest. While attuned, the wearer benefits from the following effects: If the wearer speaks the empowerment word “Patience” as they active the ring, the mound of earth instantly hardens into stone and gives three-quarters cover instead, giving a creature +5 to their AC and Dexterity saving throws. For the next hour, if the wearer is targeted by a melee attack, the attacker must make a DC 15 Strength saving throw. If they fail, the attack automatically misses and the attacker drops any weapons they are holding in their hands and are knocked prone. The wearer ignores difficult terrain caused by unstable ground like sand or rocky surfaces. The wearer is immune to the petrified and stunned conditions.
