# Temporal Totem

**description**: Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This fist-sized totem is carved with strange glyphs. Spirit Sight As an Action, you can see into the Ethereal Plane and see magical auras as with the Detect Magic spell, until the start of your next turn. Quantum Synchronization As a Bonus Action, you can create a quantum link with one willing creature you can see within 60 feet, causing the following effects for you and that creature for the next 10 minutes: You can detect the other creature's thoughts and can communicate telepathically with it. You gain any skill, tool, and weapon proficiencies that the other creature has. When you cast a spell of 1st level or higher, the other creature can expend the spell slot used for the spell instead. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn. Astral Projection As an Action, you can use the totem to cast the Astral Projection spell without using material components. The totem then crumbles and is destroyed.
