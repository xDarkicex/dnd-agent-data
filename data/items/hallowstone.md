# Hallowstone

**description**: Hallowstone Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) While holding this smooth, obsidian stone, you can use an action to cast the spell hallow , with no material components required. The spell lasts for as long as you are within the area of its effect and maintain concentration on it, or until it is dispelled. Once used, this feature can’t be used again until seven days have passed.
