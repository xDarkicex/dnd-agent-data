# Forger's Fury

**description**: Weapon (Warhammer), Rare (Requires Attunement) The head of this magic warhammer seems to always glow red-hot. Superheat. Once per turn, when you hit a creature with the hammer, you can deal an extra 1d8 Fire damage and cause a metal object worn or carried by the creature to glow red-hot. The creature must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or drop the object, if it can. If it fails the saving throw and can't drop the object or picks it up before the start of your next turn, it has disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks until the start of your next turn. Shower of Sparks. As an action, you can cause a burst of sparks to fly from the hammer in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw. On a failure, the creature takes 5d8 Fire damage and is Blinded until the start of your next turn. On a success, the creature takes half as much damage and isn't Blinded. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
