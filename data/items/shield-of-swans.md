# Shield of Swans

**description**: Armor (Shield), Common This shield has 3 charges, and it regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. When you speak its command word and expend one charge, you gain a fly speed of 60 feet for 1d4 rounds (and you know when this ends). In addition, while holding this shield you can speak with swans and other aquatic birds.
