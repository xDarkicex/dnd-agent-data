# Runewrought Grenade, Gravity

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon This fist-sized metal ball is engraved with magical runes. As an action, you can activate the grenade's magic and hurl it at a point on the ground you can see within 60 feet of you. The grenade explodes and produces a specific effect, depending on which type of grenade it is. Gravity. This grenade produces a gravitational blast. When the grenade lands, each creature within 20 feet of it must make a DC 15 Strength saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 2d8 force damage and is pulled 10 feet toward the point of impact or pushed 10 feet away from the point of impact (your choice). On a success, a creature takes half as much damage with no additional effects.
