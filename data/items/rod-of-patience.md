# Rod of Patience

**description**: Rod of Patience Rod, Artifact (requires attunement by a spellcaster) Forged by the legendary deep dwarf demigod Rolim the Technurge, this indestructible rod empowers its wielder to master the forces that underlie the casting of spells and the creation of magic items. While you hold this rod, you gain a +3 bonus to spell attack rolls and to the saving throw DCs of your spells. Additionally, you have advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects, and spell attacks have disadvantage against you. Mind Quickening. The Rod of Patience has 7 charges. When you cast a spell that has a casting time of 1 action while holding this rod, you can expend 1 charge and change the casting time to 1 bonus action for this casting. The rod regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. Technurge’s Patience. While attuned to this rod, you can harness Rolim’s magic to speed up or optimize the creation of magic items. When crafting a magic item, you can choose to use double the materials normally required to magically speed up the process, reducing the crafting time by half. Alternatively, you can choose to use only half the materials normally required and rely on Rolim’s magic to complete the process, which doubles the crafting time. Random Properties. The Rod of Patience has the following randomly determined properties: 2 minor beneficial properties 1 minor detrimental property 1 major detrimental property
