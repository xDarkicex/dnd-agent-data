# Bonze's Bokken

**description**: Weapon (katana), rare (requires attunement) This magic weapon deals bludgeoning damage instead of its normal slashing damage. As an action, you can use the bokken to blow a strong wind, dispersing any gases within a 15-foot cube adjacent to you. When you take the Attack action while holding this weapon, you can replace one or more of your attacks with concussive waves of sound. Make a ranged spell attack roll with a range of 30/60 feet and a +7 bonus to hit. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 + 3 thunder damage, and on a critical hit, the target is deafened. This attack is audible out to 100 feet.
