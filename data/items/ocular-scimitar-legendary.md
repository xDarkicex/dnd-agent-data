# Ocular Scimitar (Legendary)

**description**: Weapon (Scimitar), Legendary (Requires Attunement) Common While holding this magic weapon, you can use an action to cast minor illusion (spell save DC 10 + your proficiency bonus). You can cast the spell this way a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. Uncommon You can now cast minor illusion from the weapon at will. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. Rare Everywhere All at Once. When you hit a creature with this weapon, you can choose up to four additional creatures within 30 feet. The original target and each additional target must make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be stunned until the start of their next turn. Once used, you can’t use this property again until you finish a long rest. Very Rare The DC of Everywhere All at Once increases to 16. Protective Phantasm. If another creature hits you with a melee attack while you are holding this weapon, you can use your reaction to gain a +2 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack, until the start of your next turn. Legendary The DC of Everywhere All at Once increases to 17. While holding this weapon, you can cast blur as a bonus action. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a short or long rest. Unseen Approach. Your attacks with this weapon score a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20. Additionally, when you score a critical hit with this weapon, the target must succeed on a DC 17 Constitution saving throw or be stunned until the end of your next turn.
