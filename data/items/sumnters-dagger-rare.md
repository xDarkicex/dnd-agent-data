# Sumnter's Dagger (Rare)

**description**: Sumnter's Dagger Weapon (Dagger), Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon and the weapon deals an additional 2d4 radiant damage. You can speak a command word to cause the weapon’s iron blade to emit moonlight, shedding bright light in a 10-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet. The light lasts for 1 hour or until you end it (no action required). Power of the Moon. When you deal damage to a creature cursed with lycanthropy with this weapon, the creature must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or instantly revert to its humanoid form for a duration of 1 minute. Progression Sumnter’s Dagger progresses as the creature attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s Wisdom modifier increases, so does the weapon’s rarity.
