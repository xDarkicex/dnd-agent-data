# Armor of Cowardice

**description**: Armor (medium), very rare (requires attunement) This +2 breastplate grants the wearer a +2 bonus to Charisma (Intimidation) and Charisma (Persuasion) checks. These effects cease to function if you hide or conceal the armor in any way. Curse. This armor is cursed. Attuning to it curses you until you are targeted by the remove curse spell or similar magic. Removing the armor fails to end the curse on you. The armor causes the wearer and any allies within 30 feet to suffer disadvantage on saving throws versus fear effects.
