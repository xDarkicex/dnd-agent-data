# Gloomwrought Chain Mail

**description**: Gloomwrought Chain Mail Armor (Heavy), Rare (Requires Attunement) This intricate grayscale armor was forged in the Shadowfell and is infused with that plane’s gloom. While you’re wearing this armor, you have advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the frightened condition on yourself. This armor has 3 charges. You can expend a charge to cast the Calm Emotions spell (save DC 15)from the armor. This armor regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. Chain Mail. Made of interlocking metal rings, chain mail includes a layer of quilted fabric worn underneath the mail to prevent chafing and to cushion the impact of blows. The suit includes gauntlets. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC: 16 Stealth: Disadvantage Weight: 55 Minimum Strength: 13
