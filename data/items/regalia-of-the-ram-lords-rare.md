# Regalia of the Ram Lords (Rare)

**description**: Regalia of the Ram Lords Weapon (Warhammer), Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. You can speak a command word to cause the ram heads’ eyes to glow red for 1 minute. Call the Ram Court. You call forth spectral rams to protect you. They clamor about you within a 10-foot-radius sphere for 1 minute. When you activate this ability, you can designate any number of creatures you can see to be unaffected by it. When a creature enters the area for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there, it must make a Strength saving throw. The DC is equal to 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier. On a failed save, the creature takes 3d8 bludgeoning damage and is knocked prone. On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage and isn’t knocked prone. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. Sure-Footed. While holding this weapon, you have advantage on Strength and Dexterity saving throws made against effects that would knock you prone or move you against your will. Progression The Regalia of the Ram Lords progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
