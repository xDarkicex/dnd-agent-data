# Binoculars (Rangefinding)

**description**: Binoculars (Rangefinding) Miscellaneous gear These are slightly larger and function the same as micro binoculars. These, however, also provide a readout of exact distance between you and a chosen point of focus. You also receive advantage to any Intelligence (Investigation) rolls made when searching for any details regarding your point or object of focus. Weight : 2
