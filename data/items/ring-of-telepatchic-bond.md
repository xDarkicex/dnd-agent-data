# Ring of Telepatchic Bond

**description**: Ring, very rare (requires attunement) This ring has 4 charges and regains 1d4 charges at dawn. If the ring reaches 0 charges it crumbles to dust and is destroyed. While wearing this ring, you can expend one charge to cast the telepathic bond spell as a bonus action.

- Item Type: Ring
- Item Rarity: very rare
