# Cloak of the Lady

**description**: Wondrous Item, very rare (requires attunement) These finely crafted cloaks of elvenkind are made of the softest wool and inlaid with a golden embroidery that marks its wearer as friend to elves. The embroidery is enchanted to only appear when the wearer wills it and may not be called forth by any but the individual the cloak was woven for. In addition, the cloak obscures the aura of all magic items borne by the wearer, such that the Scent Magic ability of an Astirax can only detect them within a range of 100 feet. Further, the wearer receives a +5 bonus to the Wisdom save to resist the effects of a scrying spell unless the caster is Queen Aradil herself. Finally, the cloaks allow Aradil to know the wearer’s location and state of mind.
