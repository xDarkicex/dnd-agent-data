# Slate of Identification

**description**: Slate of Identification Wondrous item, uncommon This is a 12-by-6-inch piece of black slate in a lavish wooden frame. When you touch it to a magic item and say “Identify,” the magical properties of the item are neatly printed out on the slate in chalk, including any required command words. Once used, this property of the slate can’t be used again until the next dawn. Touching a non-magical item has no effect.
