# Shuffleflush’s Cunning Coin

**description**: Shuffleflush’s Cunning Coin Wondrous Item, Common Stories have it that the legendary halfling gambler, Shiny Shuffleflush was the first to use magic to get a coin to land on its edge in order to win an impossible bet. Since then, countless such coins have found themselves in the hands of aspiring grifters and master scammers alike. This coin looks like an ordinary gold piece that feels somewhat lighter than it should. When tossing it, you can use your reaction to mentally command it to land on any side you choose, including landing on its edge.
