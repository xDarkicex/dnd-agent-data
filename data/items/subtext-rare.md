# Subtext (Rare)

**description**: Subtext Weapon (Warhammer), Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon and it deals an extra 1d6 psychic damage on a hit. Tragedy . When you roll a 20 on your attack roll with this magic weapon, the target is frightened of you 1 minute. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make a Wisdom saving throw if it no longer has line of sight to you, ending the condition on a success. The DC is equal to 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Charisma modifier. Progression Subtext progresses each time its attuned wielder completes an adventure worthy of being turned into a play (what satisfies this definition is at the GM’s discretion). While the weapon prefers tragedies and horrors, any compelling story suffices.
