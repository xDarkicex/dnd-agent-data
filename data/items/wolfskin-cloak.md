# Wolfskin Cloak

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) While wearing this cloak, as an action, you can assume the form of a dire wolf , gaining its bite attack, AC, and speed. You also gain its Keen Hearing and Smell trait. You retain your own ability scores, hit points, and saving throws.
