# Saint Eathan's Cutlery

**description**: Relic, rare Whatever a creature eats using this cutlery becomes edible and is treated as one Ration, but does not change its flavor. If you take part in a Brancalonian Buffet game and you use this cutlery, you gain a +5 bonus on any roll.
