# Fruit of Shielding

**description**: Fruit of Shielding Wondrous item, uncommon If a creature makes an attack against you, you can use your reaction to eat this dried apricot after your attacker has rolled, but before you know whether the attack hit you. Until the start of your next turn, you add your proficiency bonus to your Armor Class.
