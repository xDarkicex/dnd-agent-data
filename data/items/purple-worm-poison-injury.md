# Purple worm poison (Injury)

**description**: Purple Worm Poison Adventuring gear (poison, injury) This poison must be harvested from a dead or incapacitated purple worm. A creature subjected to this poison must make a DC 19 Constitution saving throw, taking 42 (12d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Damage: 12d6 Damage Type: Poison
