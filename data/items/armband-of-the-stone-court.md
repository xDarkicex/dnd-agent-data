# Armband of the Stone Court

**description**: Armband of the Stone Court Wondrous item, very rare This item is rewarded to a hero during the Noticed by the Stone Court event. Depending on the god represented on this armband, it confers different bonuses (but only when worn by someone who regularly prays to this god). As soon as the item is worn, the wearer knows the item’s capabilities. Its bonuses are only able to be used once, but if the armband is continued to be worn, the gods may reward the wearer in the future. Aldyhn. Speak, understand, and read all languages for 3 hours. Gnark. You may use this feature during a short rest. You and all companions within 30 feet gain the benefits of a long rest. Idin. Gain resistance to all damage for 3 hours. Knat. Gain +3 to any skill checks for the next 3 hours. Luta. As a reaction to delivering damage to a target you do maximum damage. Palter. As a reaction to taking damage from a creature, you may use command on the creature that damaged you. The creature automatically fails their saving throw. Vani. As a reaction, when you lose Glory, you do not. If any companions would have lost Glory for the same reason as you, at this time, they do not lose any Glory either.
