# Khaliber's Revolver +2, DC 15

**description**: Weapon (any one-handed firearm), rare (+2, DC 15) (requires attunement by a gunslinger) This magic weapon, which is blessed by Khaliber, the goddess of gunpowder, magically produces its own ammunition immediately after it is fired. You have a bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The bonus is determined by the weapon’s rarity. When you score a critical hit with this weapon, the bullet explodes on impact. Each creature you choose other than the target within 5 feet of it must make a Dexterity saving throw, taking fire damage equal to half the damage dealt on a failure. The DC for this saving throw is determined by the weapon’s rarity.
