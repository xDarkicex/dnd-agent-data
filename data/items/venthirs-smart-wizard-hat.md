# Venthir’s Smart Wizard Hat

**description**: Venthir’s Smart Wizard Hat Wondrous Item, Uncommon (requires attunement) Originally a gift to a beloved teacher of the arcane, this semi-sentient and undeniably stylish wizard hat has been imbued with elegant divination magic. While attuned to this hat, you can use an action to mentally ask the hat a yes-no question about the results of a specific course of action that you plan to take within the next 10 minutes. The gamemaster chooses from the following possible predictions: • Affirmative. The hat answers “yes” to your question. • Negative. The hat answers “no” to your question. • Non-committal. The hat refuses to answer, or its answer is uncertain. Instead of a direct “yes” or “no”, the hat may choose to give its answer with varying degrees of confidence. For example, instead of “yes” it may reply “most likely” if it’s somewhat uncertain, or “yes definitely” if it’s confident about its prediction. The hat’s divination magic does not consider any possible circumstances that might change the outcome, such as the casting of spells or the loss or gain of a companion. If you ask the hat four or more times before completing your next long rest, there is a cumulative 25 percent chance for each question after the third that you get a non-committal answer. The gamemaster makes this roll in secret. Orelyn on Venthir’s Smart Wizard Hat “Ah yes, my master’s iconic purple hat,” Orelyn says as she gently pinches the hat’s rim. “You might be interested to know that this is not quite as unique as it might appear. I know for a fact that my colleagues and Master Venthir’s former pupils, the twin trickster mages Ferion and Feranis, have crafted no less than three Smart Wizard Hats that are almost identical, save for a miniscule variation in the shade of purple. Before you ask, no, I cannot say if Master Venthir once wore this particular one…”
