# Staff of the Eel

**description**: Staff Of The Eel Staff, uncommon (requires attunement by a cleric, druid, or warlock) You can use an action to speak this staff’s command word and throw the staff on the ground within 10 feet of you. The staff becomes a giant lightning eel (appendix B) under your control and acts on its own initiative count. By using a bonus action to speak the command word again, you return the staff to its normal form in a space formerly occupied by the eel. On your turn, you can mentally command the eel if it is within 60 feet of you and you aren’t incapacitated. You decide what action the eel takes and where it moves during its next turn, or you can issue it a general command, such as to attack your enemies or guard a location. If the eel is reduced to 0 hit points, it dies and reverts to its staff form. The staff then shatters and is destroyed. If the eel reverts to staff form before losing all its hit points, it regains all of them.
