# Pendant of Grave Regeneration

**description**: Pendant of Grave Regeneration Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement) While you wear this pendant, your skin becomes pale and cold to the touch, and you regain 5 hit points at the start of your turn if you have at least 1 hit point. Curse. Attuning to this pendant extends its curse to you. Until the curse is broken with a remove curse spell or similar magic, you can’t take it off, and your creature type is Undead instead of your normal type.
