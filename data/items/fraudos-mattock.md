# Fraudo's Mattock

**description**: Weapon (martial mattock), legendary (requires attunement) Despite being magical, this +1 martial mattock is a shoddy weapon. Fraudo’s Weapons (require attunement) You must wield Fraudo's mattock and Fraudo’s Brand to attune to both Fraudo’s Weapons. The attunement ends if you lose either of those items. While you are attuned to Fraudo’s Weapons, both Fraudo’s Brand and Fraudo’s Mattock become magic +3 weapons, lose the shoddy property, and are considered light weapons.
