# Drow poison (Injury)

**description**: Drow Poison Adventuring gear , poison (injury) This poison is typically made only by the drow, and only in a place far removed from sunlight. A creature subjected to this pois on must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned for 1 hour. If the saving throw fails by 5 or more, the creature is also unconscious while poisoned in this way. The creature wakes up if it takes damage or if another creature takes an action to shake it awake.
