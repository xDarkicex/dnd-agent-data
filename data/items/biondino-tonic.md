# Biondino Tonic

**description**: The tord-boyeux sherry, that makes damsels merry: this tonic is crystal-clear, its flavor unrivaled amongst magic potions and aperitifs, and in medication lore. Effect : When you drink this concoction, you regain 1d4 + 1 hit points and reduce your exhaustion level by 1. Additionally, for the next 8 hours you can ignore all exhaustion effects and you earn inspiration (the inspiration earned this way vanishes together with the tonic effects).
