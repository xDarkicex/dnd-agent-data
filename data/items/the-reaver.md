# The Reaver

**description**: The Reaver Weapon (Longbow), Very Rare (requires attunement) Crafted by a sinister demon lord for a dark hero who sold his soul for power, the Reaver is a terrifying weapon in the hands of a skilled archer. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Barbed Arrow. The Reaver has 2 charges. While attuned to this longbow, you can expend one of its charges and use the attack action to make a special ranged attack with a barbed arrow that is attached to a demonic chain. This attack has a normal range of 50 feet and a long range of 100 feet. If you’re able to make multiple attacks with the Attack action, this attack replaces one of them. On hit, the barbed arrow is embedded into the target, dealing an extra 1d6 piercing damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, that creature must succeed on a DC 17 Strength saving throw or be pulled to the longbow and impaled on its balance spike for an additional 2d6 piercing damage. The Reaver regains all expended charges daily at dawn.
