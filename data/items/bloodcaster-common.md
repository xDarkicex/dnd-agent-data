# Bloodcaster (Common)

**description**: Bloodcaster Weapon (Light Crossbow), Common (Requires Attunement) Blood Bolt. You can fire this magic weapon without ammunition by losing 1 hit point as part of the action used to fire it. If you do so, your blood coalesces into a liquid bolt, which deals necrotic damage instead of piercing. Progression Bloodcaster progresses when its wielder uses it to commit a sufficiently spectacular assassination, as determined by the GM.
