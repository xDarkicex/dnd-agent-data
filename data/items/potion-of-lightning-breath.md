# Potion of Lightning Breath

**description**: Potion of Lightning Breath Potion, uncommon Weight: ½ lb. After drinking this potion, you can use a bonus action to exhale lightning at a target within 30 feet of you. The target must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d6 lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The effect ends after you exhale the lightning three times or when 1 hour has passed. This potion's blue liquid shimmers and sparks inside its bottle. Lightning beetle carapace and behir blood are the potion’s primary ingredients.
