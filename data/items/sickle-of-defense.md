# Sickle of Defense

**description**: Sickle of Defense Melee weapon (simple, sickle), rare The weapon has the defensive property, seeming to move by itself to aid in its wielder’s defense. Light. A light weapon is small and easy to handle, making it ideal for use when fighting with two weapons. Defensive. While you wield one or more weapons with this property, you gain a +1 bonus to AC if you are proficient in the weapon and not using a shield.
