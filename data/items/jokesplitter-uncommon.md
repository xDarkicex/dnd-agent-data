# Jokesplitter (Uncommon)

**description**: Jokesplitter Weapon (Handaxe), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) Jokesplitter normally takes the form of a harmless wooden hatchet. As a bonus action, you can speak a command word, causing a metal blade to emerge for 10 minutes, transforming it into a magical handaxe. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. In addition, you gain proficiency in the Deception skill. Progression Jokesplitter progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s Strength modifier increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
