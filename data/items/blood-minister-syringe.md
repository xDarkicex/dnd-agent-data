# Blood Minister Syringe

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Component: phial of beast blood Made of silver and glass, this peculiar apparatus magically refills with blood. A small dial around the plunger depicts carved images of beasts. Legend says blood from the sacred animal spirits of each of these species was extracted in the crafting of this macabre, performance-enhancing syringe. Beastblood Serum. The dial around the plunger can be rotated to select one of the following symbols, changing the hue of the blood within: bear (iridescent green); bull (sanguine red); cat (silky purple); eagle (piercing yellow); fox (opulent orange); and owl (midnight blue). As an action, you can select a symbol and inject the blood in the syringe into a willing creature within your reach. The creature gains the benefits of the enhance ability spell that correspond to the chosen animal for 10 minutes. Once this property of the item has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
