# Duster

**description**: Duster Wand, common While holding this wand, you can use an action to instantly clean an area within 10 feet of you that is no larger than 1 cubic foot, removing dirt and grime. There are many variations of the duster ; the two-handed staff of cleansing covers a five-foot cube with a single action, while the bright basin cleans clothing and cloth objects placed within it.
