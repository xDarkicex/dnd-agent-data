# Arcanium Aegis

**description**: Armor (Shield), Uncommon This shield is forged from an alloy of adamantine and aluminum known for its magnetic properties. Magnetic Discharge The shield has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. As a Bonus Action, you can expend 1 charge to cause one of these effects. Push. A Large or smaller creature you can see within 10 feet that is wearing metal armor must succeed on a DC 15 Strength save or be pushed up to 15 feet away from you. Pull. Until the start of your next turn, attacks using metal weapons or ammunition have Disadvantage against you. Moreover, when a weapon attack using a metal weapon or piece of metal ammunition is made against a target within 5 feet of you, the attack targets you instead.
