# Longbow of Unlimited Ammunition

**description**: Longbow of Unlimited Ammunition Ranged weapon Price: 40,000 gp This ornate bow has been meticulously designed with fillegry and carvings all along its length. This bow never requires ammunition to fire. When the wielder pulls the string of the bow back, it immediately conjures an arrow made of magical force. After being fired, these arrows exist for 1 minute before they disappear again. Anyone using this bow may make an additional attack each turn as a Bonus Action. Ranged. Ranged weapons are used to attack enemies at a distance. These weapons can be either shot like a bow and arrow or crossbow, or thrown like a spear or javelin. All ranged weapons use Dexterity for both the Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus. Damage: 1d8
