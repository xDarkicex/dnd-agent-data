# Deferred Energy Emitter

**description**: Deferred Energy Emitter Mod (armor: any except shields), uncommon (requires affinity) This device is integrated into your armor from the sleeves up to the palm of your gloves or gauntlets. They contain rows of nano-emitters that generate a field of opposing force that repels direct, high-velocity motion. When a ranged weapon attack hits you while you’re wearing this mod, you can use your reaction to reduce the damage by 1d10 + your Dexterity modifier, provided that you have a free hand.
