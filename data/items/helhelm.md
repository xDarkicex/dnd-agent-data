# Helhelm

**description**: Helhelm Wondrous item, rare This is an item created in the Lost Lands. It allows the wearer to charm and command undead. It has a maximum of 5 charges and regains 1d4 expended charges once a day, at nightfall. As an action, the wearer may expend a charge and select one undead creature within 30 feet of them. That creature must make a DC 20 Charisma saving throw. If it fails, the undead creature is permanently charmed if it has an Intelligence of 8 or lower. If it fails, but has an intelligence higher than 8, it repeats the check at twilight of each day. If the wearer harms the charmed creature directly, the charm ends. If the saving throw was successful, the creature can never be charmed by the helm again. The helhelm does not require attunement and can be used by any creature wearing it. The moment another creature wears it any undead controlled by the previous wearer are no longer controlled and will have advantage on any checks to resist being controlled again.
