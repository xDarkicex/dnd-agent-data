# Flumph Whip (Rare)

**description**: Flumph Whip Weapon (Whip), Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you hit a Large or smaller target with the whip, you can also attempt to grapple the target using either a Strength (Athletics) or Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) check. You can still use the whip to attack a creature you have grappled. Only one creature can be grappled at a time. You can use this malodorous whip to interact with or grab an unattended object within 10 feet that weighs less than 20 pounds. You can drop an object held by the whip in a space of your choice within 10 feet. An evil creature wielding or touching this weapon takes 1d6 force damage at the end of each their turns. Odor. The whip emits a strong smell of decay, which is noticeable to creatures that can smell that are within 15 feet of the whip. Perception checks based on smell have advantage to locate the whip or notice any creature who might be in possession of it. In addition, while attuned to the whip, you have disadvantage on Charisma checks. The weapon has 3 charges, and it regains all expended charges when you finish a long rest. You can use an action to expend 1 charge to do one of the following to a creature you have grappled with the whip: Flumph Friend. The creature must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be charmed as if by a charm person spell (regardless of creature type). Grasp of the Flumph. The creature is restrained while grappled. Acid Bath. The creature must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 4d6 acid damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Progression The Flumph Whip progresses each time the character performs impactful lawful or good acts, as determined by the GM.
