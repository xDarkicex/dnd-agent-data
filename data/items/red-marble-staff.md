# Red Marble Staff

**description**: Staff, uncommon This staff looks like a marble column. There are thin cuts along its four sides. While holding this staff, each time you take damage, blood fills these cuts, and the staff looks like it has veins. When you take damage, the staff collects an amount of spilled blood equal to the damage you take. The staff can hold a maximum of 40 hit points worth of blood and the collected blood disappears daily at dawn. While holding the staff, you can take a bonus action to deal yourself 20 hit points worth of blood damage and feed the blood to the staff, and can cast one of the following spells of your choice; [ Ameliorate] , [Blood Coating] or [Piercing Drops] . The spellcasting ability for these spells is Charisma.
