# Raven's Whistle

**description**: Raven's Whistle Wondrous item Price: 14,000 gp This wooden penny whistle is black in color with carved leaves running down its length. It has no holes, making it seem to be a poorly designed penny whistle. It was once owned by the famous bard Syren, but was stolen by a murder of crows that found it appealing. It has been missing ever since. When the holder blows air into the Raven’s Whistle it can perfectly recreate any sound that the holder has heard before, including songs with full orchestra, voices speaking, and the sounds of battle. It can create multiple sounds at the same time, and the sounds can come from multiple locations within any locations that the holder can see within 100 feet. The whistle cannot create sounds loud enough to cause damage or deafen a target, but the holder can change the volume of the sound from very loud to extremely quiet. The holder may attempt to create sounds that they haven’t heard before, but the sound is not guaranteed to be accurate, and attempts to trick others with made up sounds may not succeed.
