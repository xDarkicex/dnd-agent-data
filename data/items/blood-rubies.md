# Blood Rubies

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare You can spend a long rest affixing one of these deep red gemstones to a melee weapon of your choice. After, whenever you hit with this melee weapon, you can immediately expend the blood ruby’s charge to deal an extra 11 (3d6) force damage. A single melee weapon can have up to three blood rubies affixed to it. Blood rubies regain their expended charges after a long rest.
