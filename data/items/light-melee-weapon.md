# Light Melee Weapon

**description**: Light Melee Weapon Melee weapon Price: 50 gp A medium weapon used to attack at close range. (See rules for weapons in the Combat section) Melee. Melee Weapons are used to attack enemies up close in hand-to-hand combat. Light. Light weapons can be wielded with finesse in one hand, leaving the other hand free to carry other essentials, like a torch or a shield. Small and light melee weapons can use either Strength (Str) or Dexterity (Dex) for the Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus, whichever is higher. Damage: 1d6
