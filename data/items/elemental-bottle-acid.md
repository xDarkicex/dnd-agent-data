# Elemental Bottle (Acid)

**description**: As an action, you can throw this bottle up to 20 feet, shattering it on impact. Make a ranged attack against a creature or object, treating the elemental bottle as an improvised weapon. On a hit, the target takes damage (and any additional effect), depending on the type of liquid in the bottle. A target hit by this bottle takes 2d6 acid damage.
