# Ointment of Soothing

**description**: Ointment of Soothing Wondrous item, rare This stubby tin jar is 2 inches in diameter and contains 1d4 + 1 doses of a thick pink mixture that smells faintly of lavender. As an action, one dose of the ointment can be applied to the skin and it removes any exhaustion you are suffering and cures any disease or poison affecting you.
