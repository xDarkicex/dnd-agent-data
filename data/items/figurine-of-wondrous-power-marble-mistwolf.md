# Figurine of Wondrous Power, Marble Mistwolf

**description**: Wondrous item, rare Marble Mistwolf (Rare). This white marble statuette is of a wolf. It can become a dire wolf for up to 6 hours. At your command, it can cast the fog cloud spell. Each time it does, its duration is reduced by 1 hour. Once its duration ends, it can’t be used again until 5 days have passed.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: rare
