# Turtle's Shelter

**description**: Armor (Shield), Common (Requires Attunement) This magic shield is crafted from the shell of a giant tortoise. While wearing it, your speed is reduced by 5 feet but spells and other magical effects can't otherwise reduce your speed. Moreover, you have disadvantage on Initiative rolls but treat any roll of 9 or lower as a 10.
