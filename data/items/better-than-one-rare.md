# Better than One (Rare)

**description**: Weapon (Greataxe), Rare (Requires Attunement) Common When you score a critical hit with this magic weapon, it unleashes a cacophonous peal that deals 1d6 thunder damage to each creature within 5 feet of the target (excluding you). Uncommon The thunder damage dealt on a critical hit increases to 2d6. Three Headed. While holding this weapon, you gain two illusory heads. The heads move on their own, behaving as if they are real, but can’t speak and don’t have any senses. Whenever a creature without truesight scores a critical hit against you, roll a d6. The attack becomes a normal hit on a roll of 5 or 6. Rare Sonic Boom. You can use a bonus action to speak this weapon’s command word (“Three heads!” in Giant), causing it to tremble with sonic energy. While activated, it deals an extra 1d6 thunder damage to any target it hits. The effect lasts until you use a bonus action to speak the command word again or until you drop or sheathe the weapon.
