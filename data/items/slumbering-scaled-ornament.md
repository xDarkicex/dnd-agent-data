# Slumbering Scaled Ornament

**description**: Slumbering Scaled Ornament Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) This ornament can be jewelry, a cloak, or another wearable accessory. It appears to be fashioned from a dragon's scale, tooth, or claw, or it incorporates images in those shapes. You have advantage on saving throws you make to avoid being charmed or frightened or to end those conditions on you.
