# Pyroclastic Saber (Uncommon)

**description**: Weapon (Scimitar), Uncommon (Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster) Common This magic weapon has 1 charge. If you lack proficiency with this weapon, you can expend 1 charge to gain proficiency with it for 8 hours. This weapon regains 1 expended charge when you finish a long rest. You can expend a spell slot to regain a number of charges equal to the level of the spell slot expended. Once you have regained charges this way, you can’t do so again until you finish a long rest. Uncommon The weapon now has 3 charges and regains 1d3 charges when you finish a long rest. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. While holding the weapon, you have the ability to move across lava as if it were harmless solid ground. If you expend 2 charges, you do not take damage from the heat of molten lava for 1 hour.
