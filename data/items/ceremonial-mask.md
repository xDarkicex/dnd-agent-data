# Ceremonial Mask

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement by a spellcaster) Component: undead undying flesh This smiling silver mask is lined on its back side with stained, purple velvet. It is typically donned by a cult’s leader, allowing them the ability to communicate with the dark powers they worship. Dark Empowerment. While wearing this mask, you have two additional 1st-level spell slots, which you regain daily at dawn. In addition, you can cast the augury and commune spells from the mask. Once the mask has been used to cast a spell, it can’t be used to cast that spell again until the next dawn. When you cast commune using the mask, your questions are answered by the deity or power associated with this item: an entity of death and decay. Curse. This mask is cursed, and becoming attuned to it extends the curse to you until you are targeted by the remove curse spell or similar magic. When you first don this item after attuning to it, the silver tentacles reshape and tighten around your neck. While cursed, you can’t remove the mask, and each time you cast a spell, you take 1d4 psychic damage.
