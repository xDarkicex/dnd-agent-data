# Clockwork

**description**: Master armor property Components: Masterwork suit of exotic heavy armor This suit of armor has dozens of clockwork mechanisms integrated into it, granting you a number of benefits. While wearing this armor, your jump distance is tripled, and you have advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks (other than grapple checks). Additionally, your armor locks down when you take the Dodge action, causing your AC to become 22, if it was lower.
