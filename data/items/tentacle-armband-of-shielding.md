# Tentacle Armband of Shielding

**description**: Tentacle Armband of Shielding Wondrous item, uncommon This silver band is carved in the shape of a tentacle and encircles your upper arm. While you wear this armband, you can cast the shield spell once per day. The shield takes the form of a translucent barrier of writhing tentacles. After you use this item, you can’t use it again until the next dawn.
