# Amulet, Bulla Talisman

**description**: Wondrous item, neck This acorn-shaped golden amulet is engraved with a winged figure wielding a harp. While wearing this amulet, if your alignment is good, you have advantage on saving throws against spells and magical effects cast by fiends and fey creatures.
