# Crescent Moon Spear

**description**: Crescent Moon Spear Weapon (spear), rare Damage: 1d6+1 slashing Weight: 3 lbs. Properties: Thrown (range 20/60), Versatile (1d8+1) This magic spear has a crescent moon shaped head that glows with moonlight and delivers slashing damage instead of piercing. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic spear. Silver Moonlight. The spearhead sheds dim light in a 30-foot radius and deals an additional 1d6 radiant damage to Lycanthropes and creatures that are vulnerable to silvered weapons. Moon Bolt. While you are holding the spear, you can use an action to cast Guiding Bolt at 1st level. This feature can be used a number of times per day equal to your proficiency bonus.
