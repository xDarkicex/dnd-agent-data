# Hither-Thither Staff

**description**: Hither-Thither Staff Weapon (quarterstaff), very rare You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. This staff has 6 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn . While holding this staff, you may use an action or bonus action to speak its command word, expend a charge, and point it at a spot you can see within 1500 feet. A portal opens at that spot. When the staff is used a second time, another portal opens at the newly targeted spot. The two portals are connected and any object or creature that enters one portal then exits from the connected portal. The portals work in both directions and remain open until a bonus action is used to close them, the staff is used again to create another portal (which then closes the first portal that was opened), or 48 hours pass, at which point both portals close. Versatile. This weapon can be used with one or two hands. A damage value in parentheses appears with the property—the damage when the weapon is used with two hands to make a melee attack. Damage : 1d6+1 (1d8+1) Damage Type : Bludgeoning Properties : Versatile Weight : 4
