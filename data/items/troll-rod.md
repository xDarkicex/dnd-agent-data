# Troll Rod

**description**: Weapon (mace), uncommon (requires attunement) This uncouth pommel has a troll’s arm attached—still living—to one end. As a bonus action, you can issue it commands in the troll dialect of Giant to pick up items, drop them, or make a fist (typical command words include “grabbit,” “leggo,” and “maik’a’fist.”) If closed into a fist, the rod acts as a magic mace, and you have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made using it.
