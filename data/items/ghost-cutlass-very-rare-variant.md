# Ghost Cutlass (very rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (scimitar), uncommon Component: undead (ghost) ethereal ichor A curved blade of ethereal green smoke rises from its barnacle-encrusted hilt, swimming with ghastly, ghostly visages. Legend says these faces belong to those who fell while wielding the blade, a part of their soul trapped forever in its decrepit metal. This item appears to be a round, rusted hilt. While grasping the hilt, you can use a bonus action to cause an ethereal blade to spring into existence, or make the blade disappear. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon, which deals psychic damage instead of slashing damage. Hydrodynamic. Attacking while underwater doesn’t impose disadvantage on attack rolls you make with this weapon. Rare variant: Increase the attack and damage bonus to +2. Very rare variant: Increase the attack and damage bonus to +3.
