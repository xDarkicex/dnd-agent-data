# Aegis of Dread

**description**: Armor (Shield), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) When a creature misses you with a melee weapon attack while you are holding this shield, you can use your reaction to cause the shield to emanate a sense of dread. The attacker must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened of you until the end of its next turn.
