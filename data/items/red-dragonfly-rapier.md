# Red Dragonfly Rapier

**description**: Weapon (Rapier), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) The hilt of this delicate weapon is carved into the shape of a crimson dragonfly, with a protective basket of sculpted glass wings curving around the wielder’s hand. The slender blade is etched with serene prayers in the secret language of the monks who crafted it. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. This weapon can be used as a monk weapon and any monk attuned to it is proficient with it. While attuned to this weapon, you gain advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened. Additionally, you can cast the levitate spell (spell save DC 15) from the weapon. Once you cast the spell this way, you can’t do so again until you finish a long rest. Encounter Ideas Final Test. The last surviving member of an ancient order of monks (use the githzerai monk stat block but change their race to human) offers one of the characters the Red Dragonfly Rapier if they can answer an impossible riddle carved on an obelisk atop a mountain summit. Lost Hero. A brave monk (use the githzerai monk stat block but change their race to human) entered the Temple of the Viper alone but succumbed to the poisonous gaze of the viper’s emerald eyes, leaving this weapon behind. Sad Song of Salong. Every autumn, the town of Salong holds a Dragonfly Festival and offers this weapon as a prize to the competitor who composes the most bittersweet poem.
