# Rustward

**description**: Armor (Shield), Uncommon This magic shield is reinforced with the carapace of a rust monster. The shield has 5 charges for the following properties, and regains all expended charges daily at dawn: Rustward. As a reaction when a melee weapon attack made with a nonmagical metal weapon misses you, you can expend 1 charge to corrode the weapon. The weapon takes a permanent and cumulative −1 penalty to damage rolls. If its penalty drops to −5, the weapon is destroyed. Corrosive Touch. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge and attempt to touch the shield to a nonmagical metal object within 5 feet of you. If the object is being worn or carried by a creature, the creature can make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw to avoid the effect. If the shield touches the object, you can cause it to rust. A nonmagical armor or shield touched in this way suffers a cumulative −1 penalty to the AC it offers. Armor reduced to an AC of 10 or a shield that drops to a +0 bonus is destroyed.
