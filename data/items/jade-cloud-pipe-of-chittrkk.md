# Jade Cloud Pipe of Chittr'k'k

**description**: A pipe of glowing green jade that shines in the night. This pipe is associated with a particular gaseous magical effect: cloudkill . A creature smoking the pipe can cast the associated spell as a bonus action 1/day.
