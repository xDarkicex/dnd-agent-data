# Ring of Perpetual Sanctification

**description**: Ring, Very Rare (Requires Attunement by a Cleric or Paladin) This gold ring is set with a large sapphire surrounded by seven small diamonds. While wearing the ring, you glow with a soft radiance that produces dim light in a 10-foot radius around you. You and friendly creatures within this radiance cannot be Charmed, Frightened, or possessed by Aberrations, Celestials, Elementals, Fey, Fiends, or Undead. If you are already Charmed, Frightened, or possessed by such a creature, you have advantage on any new saving throw against the effect. Moreover, creatures of those types have disadvantage on attack rolls against you.
