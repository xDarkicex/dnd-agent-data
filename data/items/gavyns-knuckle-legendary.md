# Gavyn's Knuckle (Legendary)

**description**: Gavyn's Knuckle Weapon (Greatclub), Legendary (Requires Attunement) You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you score a critical hit against a Huge or smaller creature with this magical weapon, you can choose to grapple the target (escape DC 19). You may only grapple one creature at a time with this weapon and while it grapples a creature, you can’t attack with it. While you wield Gavyn’s Knuckle, it has a reach of 10 feet. Grab. When you hit a creature with this weapon, you can immediately attempt to grapple the creature using the weapon (Athletics +11). Throw. As a bonus action, you can throw a creature grappled by Gavyn’s Knuckle up to 60 feet in a random direction. The creature takes 1d6 damage for every 10 feet thrown and is knocked prone. If thrown directly at another creature and the two collide, that creature must succeed on a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw or take the same damage.
