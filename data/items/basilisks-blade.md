# Basilisk's Blade

**description**: Weapon (Dagger), Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic dagger has 5 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. When you hit a creature with an attack roll using this dagger, you can expend 1 charge to deal an additional 2d6 poison damage to the target or 3 charges to turn the target into stone as if you had cast the flesh to stone spell (save DC 15) from the dagger.
