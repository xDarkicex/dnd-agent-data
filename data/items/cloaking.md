# Cloaking

**description**: Master armor property Components: Masterwork suit of exotic light armor This armor can easily hide the wearer when needed. While wearing this armor, you can cast the invisibility spell without expending a spell slot or material components. Once you use this property to cast the spell, you can’t do so again until you finish a short or long rest.
