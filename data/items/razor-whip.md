# Razor Whip

**description**: Weapon (whip), very rare This weapon functions like a whip, except that a creature grappled by it takes 1d6 slashing damage every time it takes an action. Additionally, as an action, the wielder may jerk the whip, release the grapple, and make a single attack with advantage against the target that it had been grappling. On a hit, the attack deals 8d6 slashing damage and the target must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or lose 1d6 hit points at the start of each of its turns. A successful saving throw ends the effect on the target. This effect counts as an Injury of Handicap severity.
