# Skeletal Hardening

**description**: Skeletal Hardening Augment (body), very rare (requires affinity) Your skeleton is reinforced with impact absorbing compounds. This augment provides the following benefits. Bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from weapon attacks is reduced by 2. Impact damage, such as falling or crashing damage, is reduced by half. The damage you deal with hand-to-hand attacks increases by 1.
