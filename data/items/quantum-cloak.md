# Quantum Cloak

**description**: Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This cloak seems to distort the air around it. You have Advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks and gain a +1 bonus to AC while you wear the cloak. Superposition As a Bonus Action, you can cast the Quantum Copy spell, requiring no components and using Constitution as the spellcasting ability. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
