# Xyxlbark Armour Breastplate (very rare variant)

**description**: Armour (breastplate, half plate, or plate), rare (optional attunement) Component: plant bark Armour made from the cured bark of the xyxl tree is stronger and lighter than steel and possesses the ability to nourish its wearer. The source of the xyxl tree’s immense durability is the mithral particles it incorporates into a rigid lattice throughout its wood. The art of curing the slow-growing xyxl bark is known to but a few denizens of the Plane of Fey to which it is native. The armour weighs half as much as normal and, if it normally imposes disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks or has a Strength requirement, the xyxlbark version of the armour doesn’t. Optional Attunement. If you attune to xyxlbark armour, it nourishes you, causing you to gain 3 temporary hit points at the start of each of your turns while wearing it. Very rare variant: You gain a +1 bonus to AC while wearing this armour. Increase the temporary hit points gained to 4.
