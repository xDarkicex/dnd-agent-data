# Divination Kit

**description**: Divination Kit Adventuring gear (mystic implement) Popular among both priests and superstitious layfolk, divination kits are used to tell the future or otherwise commune with the Spirit Realms. A divination kit may contain special coins, sticks, bones, or other small items with metaphysical import. Diviners use these kits by casting their coins or sticks on the ground and reading the patterns they create for any omens or portents.
