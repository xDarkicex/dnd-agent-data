# Arbiter's Bible

**description**: Book, Rare (requires attunement) A book capable of communication via written script. Used for bounties, marks, descriptions, and instructions. A Lily of the Valley is ironed into the leather cover. When attuned to this book, you can send a message to any other Arbiter’s bible, by writing a message and signing the recipient’s name. The message then appears in the recipient’s book.
