# The Brothers (Very Rare)

**description**: Weapon (Greataxe), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) Common While attuned to this magic weapon, you can read, speak, and write Dwarvish. Uncommon The damage this weapon deals on a hit changes based on your alignment. Lawful. 1d6 acid damage and 1d6 slashing damage. Neutral. 1d4 acid damage, 1d4 lightning damage, and 1d4 slashing damage. Chaotic. 1d6 lightning damage and 1d6 slashing damage. Rare You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. This bonus increases against certain creatures, based on your alignment. Lawful. +3 against chaotic creatures. Neutral. +3 against lawful and chaotic creatures. Chaotic. +3 against lawful creatures. Very Rare Once per turn, when you hit a creature with an attack using this weapon, you can choose to deal additional damage based on your alignment. Lawful. +1d6 acid damage. Neutral. +1d4 acid damage and 1d4 lightning damage. Chaotic. +1d6 lightning damage.
