# Bonecrusher (Very Rare)

**description**: Bonecrusher Weapon (Club), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) You can use Bonecrusher as a holy symbol. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this adamantine magic weapon. When you hit a skeleton or zombie with it, the target takes an extra 4d4 radiant damage, and it deals an extra 2d4 radiant damage to all other Undead creatures on a hit. Corporeal Undead creatures have disadvantage on saving throws against your Channel Divinity effects. Progression Bonecrusher progresses each time the creature who is attuned to it gains a use of the Channel Divinity class feature. The weapon progresses to legendary when the creature reaches 20th level or equivalent.
