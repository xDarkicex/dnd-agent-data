# Maneuvering

**description**: Journeyman armor property Components: Masterwork suit of exotic light or medium armor This armor contains a set of spring-loaded, automatically retracting grappling hooks. As a reaction when you fall, or as a bonus action on your turn, you can project a grappling hook at a target location you can see that is no farther away than walking speed. If the target location can hold your weight, you are pulled there, expending movement as normal. This movement doesn’t provoke opportunity attacks. You must have a Dexterity score of 16 or higher to wear this armor.
