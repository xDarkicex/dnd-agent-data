# Bog Mimic Quarterstaff (Common)

**description**: Weapon (Quarterstaff), Common (Requires Attunement) When you attune to this magic weapon, and during every long rest while attuned, you must feed the bog mimic one ration’s worth of meat. Otherwise, it attacks you at the end of the rest (use the mimic stat block). Common While attuned to this magic weapon, you can use a bonus action to verbally command it to change shape between a club, greatclub, or quarterstaff.
