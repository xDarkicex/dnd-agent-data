# Bellows of Breath

**description**: Bellows of Breath Wondrous item, uncommon These bellows are made from black rubber and dark wood, with a steel nozzle. You may use an action to pump the bellows causing them to exhale a cloud of green fog in a 20-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 3d6 poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Creatures are affected even if they hold their breath or don't need to breathe. Once used, this property of the bellows can’t be used again until the next dawn.
