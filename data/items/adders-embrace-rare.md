# Adder's Embrace (Rare)

**description**: Adder's Embrace Weapon (Pike), Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The pike has 7 charges. The pike regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. Snakebite. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge to make a melee attack with the pike’s serpent head. On a hit, the pike deals 1d4 piercing damage plus 2 poison damage. Lunging Coils. As an action, you can expend 2 charges to cause the serpent to lock around a Large or smaller creature within the weapon’s reach, grappling the target (escape DC 13). Blinding Venom. As an action, you can expend 3 charges to spit venom from the serpent’s mouth in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in the cone must succeed on a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw or take 5 poison damage and be blinded until the end of their next turn. Progression Adder’s Embrace progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
