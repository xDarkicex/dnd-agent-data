# Glimmerbolt Light Crossbow (rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (any crossbow), very rare (requires attunement) Component: celestial hair Another weapon from Sister Victoria’s arsenal, this simple crossbow has been blessed by the gods. At your command, the slack string of the crossbow glows bright gold and snaps into its catch; a glimmering quarrel of brilliant, radiant energy coalesces in place. You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. This weapon has 8 charges and regains 1d4 + 4 expended charges daily at dawn. Glimmerbolt. When you take the Attack action on your turn while holding this crossbow, you can speak its first command word, expending 1 charge and causing a piece of radiant ammunition to load into the crossbow. The next attack you make with the crossbow before the end of your turn deals radiant damage instead of piercing damage, and deals an extra 1d6 radiant damage on a hit. Lumoflare. As an action while holding this crossbow, you can speak its second command word and expend 3 charges to fire an especially bright bolt at a point within 60 feet of you, where it detonates. Each creature in a 20-foot radius must succeed on a DC 16 Constitution saving throw or become blinded until the end of your next turn. After this flash, the light, now dimmer, hangs in the air for 1 minute, shedding bright light for 20 feet, and dim light for an additional 20 feet. Uncommon variant: Reduce the attack and damage bonus to +1, the DC to 13, the charges to 4, and the recharge to 1d4. Rare variant: Reduce the attack and damage bonus to +2, the DC to 15, the charges to 6, and the recharge to 1d4 + 2.
