# Mail of Barbed Chain

**description**: Armor (chainmail), rare (requires attunement) This suit of chainmail is woven through with heavy barbs, which dig painfully into the wearer’s flesh. While attuned, it grants a +2 bonus to AC, but the wearer’s Injury thresholds are reduced by 5. Additionally, whenever the wearer grapples or becomes grappled by another creature, or is swallowed by a creature, that creature takes 1d6 points of piercing damage. It takes that damage again at the start of each of the wearer’s turns while the condition remains.
