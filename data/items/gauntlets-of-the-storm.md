# Gauntlets of the Storm

**description**: Gauntlets of the Storm Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) These metallic gloves, also known as storm gauntlets , crackle with electricity and fill the air with the scent of ozone. While wearing them, you have resistance to lightning damage. The gauntlets have 4 charges. You can use an action to expend 1 or more charges to cast one of the following spells (save DC 15): shocking grasp (1 charge), lightning bolt (3 charges), or storm sphere (4 charges). The gauntlets regain 1d4 expended charges daily at dawn.
