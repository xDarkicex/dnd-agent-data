# Deathwalker's Ward, Dormant

**description**: Deathwalker's Ward, Dormant Armor (studded leather), legendary (requires attunement) This beautiful black leather armor is covered in intricate scrollwork, and has a mantle bearing the black feathers of the Matron of Ravens. Legends say that it has been worn by a number of that deity’s champions over the centuries—most recently, Vax’ildan of Vox Machina. Dormant When the Deathwalker’s Ward is in a dormant state, you gain a +1 bonus to AC and you have advantage on death saving throws while wearing the armor. AC: 13 + Dex modifier Weight: 13
