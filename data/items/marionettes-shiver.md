# Marionette’s Shiver

**description**: Poison, rare Named for the uncontrolled shakes, shivers, and spasms this toxin induces in a target’s muscles, it grants perhaps one of the most horrific deaths imaginable. When ingested by a creature, that creature must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, it gains 3 levels of exhaustion as its muscles begin to spasm uncontrollably. Every hour for the next 24 hours, it must repeat that saving throw, gaining another level of exhaustion on every failed saving throw. A successful saving throw delays the next required saving throw by 2 hours, but does not remove the poison. During this time, the creature cannot rest, and attacks made against the creature have advantage.
