# Chained Battle-Stein

**description**: A chained battle-stein is a distinctly dweorgish weapon: a massive, weighted stein on a heavy chain. A trained wielder of the battle-stein is able to fight and drink at the same time with little spillage. It can be purchased for 6 gp. Special. A battle-mug can be used to quickly chug potions, lend them to your allies, or deliver harmful liquids to your enemies. As a bonus action, you can take a battle-stein in one hand and fill it with a potion or other liquid. With a filled stein you may take a reaction to drink from it at the end of any creature’s turn. You may also spend a bonus action to deliver the contents of the stein to an ally within the weapon’s reach. The ally must spend a reaction to quaff from the stein at the end of your turn. Lastly, when you hit a target with the battle-mug, you may spill its contents over the target. The target suffers any ill effects of the liquid on a failed saving throw (DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your class’s primary ability modifier). Monk Weapon. Dweorg monks treat chained battle-steins as simple weapons that qualify as monk weapons for the purpose of the Martial Arts class feature, in spite of it being two-handed.
