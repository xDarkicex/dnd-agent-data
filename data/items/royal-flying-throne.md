# Royal Flying Throne

**description**: Flying Disk Wondrous item, Very Rare Ancient sun elf nobles used these disks to fly up to the fabled floating palaces that defied gravity at the height of the elven kingdom of Per-Menoth. Today, the secrets of crafting Flying Disks are closely guarded by a few half-elven descendants of Per-Menoth, as well as the secretive wizard order of Fateweavers. You can speak the disk’s command word as an action to make the disk hover and fly. It moves according to your spoken directions, provided that you are within 30 feet of it. Five sizes of Flying Disk exist. The gamemaster chooses the size of a given disk or determines it randomly. Disk Transformation. As bonus action, you can order your Flying Disk to arcanomechanically transform and fold into a coin-sized token. In this form, the disk cannot fly. You can use another bonus action to unfold the Flying Disk again. Item Variant: Royal Flying Throne At the gamemaster’s discretion, a legendary version of this item, the Royal Flying Throne, may be found. The Royal Flying Throne is a 10-feet-radius disk with a luxurious ceremonial chair in the middle. It can carry up to 5000 pounds and has a flying speed of 30 feet. The Royal Flying Throne has 5 charges. A creature sitting on the throne can use an action and expend 1 charge to cast the teleport spell from it. When cast that way, teleport transports the throne and up to eight willing creatures of the caster’s choice that are on the disk. The throne regains 1d4 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn.
