# Kim'unt'kal's Comet

**description**: Weapon (sling ammunition), uncommon This small, icy orb can be held in one hand and serves as effective sling ammunition, or as a thrown weapon in its own right. Stellar Traveller. The comet has the thrown (range 60/120) property on its own, dealing 1d6 cold damage. When loaded into a sling, the sling deals 1d8 cold damage instead of its normal damage and has the range (80/320) property instead of its normal range property. Kim’unt’kal’s comet grants a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with it. Orbital Body. Once thrown, the comet returns to your hand or sling at the beginning of your next turn. Astronomer’s Will. You may choose to use your Intelligence modifier for attack and damage rolls made with Kim’unt’kal’s Comet, steering its path with the strength of your mind. So named for its first master, the vor’tai monk Kim’unt’kal, her comet proves that in battle, size can be deceiving when it comes to an opponent’s weapon.
