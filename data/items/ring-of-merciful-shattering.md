# Ring of Merciful Shattering

**description**: Ring Of Merciful Shattering Ring, Rare (Requires Attunement) The ring of merciful shattering functions as a ring of protection . In addition, when a creature attuned to this ring would fall to 0 hit points while grappled by a lamia, all of the wearer’s bones shatter, killing them instantly. This prevents the constricted creature from being turned into a frenzied lamia thrall.
