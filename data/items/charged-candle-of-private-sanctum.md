# Charged Candle of Private Sanctum

**description**: Charged Candle of Private Sanctum Wondrous item, rare When you light this candle, you activate the spell stored in it, with its effect centered on the candle. For 24 hours, the candle sheds bright light in a 5-foot radius and dim light for an additional 5 feet. If you extinguish the candle, the spell effect ends. A charged candle can be extinguished and relit multiple times, as long as the candle’s burn time does not exceed the duration of the spell. When the burn time equals the maximum duration of the spell, the candle goes out and is destroyed.
