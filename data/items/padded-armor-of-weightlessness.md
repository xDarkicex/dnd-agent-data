# Padded Armor of Weightlessness

**description**: Padded Armor of Weightlessness Armor (Light), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This armor has 5 charges. While you wear it, you can use a bonus action to expend 1 or more charges to cast one of the following spells from the armor, targeting yourself: Jump (1 charge) or Levitate (2 charges). This armor regains 1d4 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. Padded Armor. Padded armor consists of quilted layers of cloth and batting. AC: 11 + Dex Modifier Stealth: Disadvantage Weight: 8
