# Greatsword of Intimidation

**description**: Weapon (Greatsword), Uncommon This mammoth sword is intricately designed, inlaid with patterns and inscriptions that give off an air of power. You have advantage on Charisma (Intimidation) checks while holding this magic weapon. When swung, a magical enchantment placed on the weapon makes the whooshing cut of air even louder. As an action, you can cast the thunderwave spell (spell save DC 13) from the weapon by slamming it into the ground. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. Encounter Ideas Brutish Boss. An ogre or bugbear chief uses this sword to keep their underlings in line. Doomed Last Stand. The sword is found clutched in a chainmail clad skeleton’s boney hands. The thunderwave attracted more ghouls than it destroyed. Sold for Ego. A formidable weapon steeped in history, an elderly knight offers to sell this blade to a young adventurer for a mere 500 gp, but only if the buyer sits and listens to the knight’s stories of days past, which take several days to tell.
