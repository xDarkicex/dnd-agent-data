# Responsive Bracer

**description**: This lightweight armguard has a built-in switch that extends a two-foot-diameter shield. The disc-shaped shield is either energy-based or made from collapsible plates. You can wear this shield while wielding a weapon with two hands or with a weapon in each hand. However, you do not gain the shield’s AC bonus while doing so. It has the reflexive property (see “ Armor and Shields ”).
