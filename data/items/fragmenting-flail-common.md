# Fragmenting Flail (Common)

**description**: Fragmenting Flail Weapon (Flail), Common (Requires Attunement) As an action, you twist a metal ring on the handle, causing 1d10 Tiny or smaller nonmagical metal objects not being worn or carried within a 10-foot radius to be drawn to the flail’s head and attached to it. The attached objects may change the weapon’s damage type from bludgeoning to piercing or slashing (at the GM’s discretion). As an action, you can release all attached objects harmlessly. Progression The Fragmenting Flail progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, so does the weapon’s rarity.
