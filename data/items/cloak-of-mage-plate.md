# Cloak of Mage Plate

**description**: Cloak of Mage Plate Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement by a spellcaster) The cloak seals around the wearer and becomes harder than iron, mimicking the appearance of platemail. While wearing this cloak, your AC can’t be less than 13 + your spellcasting ability modifier. If you have more than one spellcasting ability, you use the one with the highest modifier.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: very rare
