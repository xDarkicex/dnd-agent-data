# Drakenhorn

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) Component: dragon horn Sculpted in the shape of a dragon’s head, this elaborate brass-and-gold horn sparks small flames whenever it is blown. This horn has 5 charges, symbolised by the waxed cloth wrapped five times around the horn. For each charge expended, one loop of the cloth magically burns away. Over the course of 10 minutes, you can wrap a new cloth around the horn while singing a ballad of a legendary dragon. As long as no properties of the horn are used, the horn regains 1d4 + 1 expended charges 8 hours later. Whenever a property calls for a cone, the size of the cone equals 10 times the number of charges expended in feet. Embolden. As an action, you can expend any number of charges as you blow into the horn, creating a loud noise audible from as far away as 300 feet. All creatures in a cone originating from you become immune to the frightened condition and are under the effects of the bless spell for the next minute. Charm Drake. As an action, you can expend any number of charges and blow into the horn, creating a sound that is beguiling to dragons and audible to all creatures within 300 feet. Each dragon in a cone originating from you must succeed on a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw or be charmed by you for 1 hour. A creature that succeeds on the saving throw is immune to the horn’s Charm Drake property for the next 24 hours. Roar. As an action, you can expend any number of charges as you blow into the horn, creating a loud noise audible from as far away as 300 feet. Each creature in a cone originating from you must succeed on a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened of you and be affected by the bane spell for 1 minute. An affected creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effects on itself on a success. A creature that succeeds on the saving throw is immune to the horn’s Roar property for the next 24 hours.
