# Replica Clay

**description**: Wondrous item, rare When you touch this lump of clay to an object that can fit within a 5-foot cube, the clay shifts and molds into an exact replica with the same color and texture. The replica even mimics visual magical elements, such as glowing runes. Touching the replica reveals it to be made of clay, as it yields under any significant force, but it is impossible to distinguish with a visual inspection. Once the clay is shaped into a replica, it becomes nonmagical.
