# Road Warden Shield

**description**: Armor (Shield), Common While holding this shield, you can speak its command word to seal a portion of a shadow road, turning it into a looping path. Once that occurs, creatures walking the path always return to their starting point after 120 feet of movement. To end the looping effect, a creature on the looping path must step off the path. The effect also ends if the shield is destroyed or you speak the command word while holding the shield.
