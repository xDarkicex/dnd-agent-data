# Pentacle of Woe +3

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare (+3) (requires attunement by a witch) While attuned to this medallion, you gain a bonus to spell attack rolls and to the saving throw DCs of your hexes and witch spells. This bonus is determined by the pentacle’s rarity. Additionally, this pentacle has 3 charges. You can expend a charge as a bonus action to cast a hex that usually takes an action. The pentacle regains all expended charges daily at dusk.
