# Utaku Steed

**description**: Utaku Steed Mounts The Blessed Steeds of the Utaku are the finest among Unicorn horses. Powerful, intelligent, and willful, these horses reject all unworthy riders. They are primarily used by the Utaku Battle Maidens, the most skilled and valorous bushi of the Unicorn Clan who comprise their most legendary heavy cavalry unit. The profile for these creatures is provided in the NPC Appendix .
