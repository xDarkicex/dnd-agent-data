# Spiked

**description**: Apprentice armor property Components: Masterwork suit of heavy armor This suit of armor is bristling with spikes. While wearing this armor, creatures who are in contact with you (either by grappling you, being grappled by you, or having swallowed you whole) take piercing damage equal to 1d4 + your Strength modifier at the start of your turn.
