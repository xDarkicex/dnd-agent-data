# The Extractor (Rare)

**description**: The Extractor Weapon (Maul), Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon and it deals an extra 1 piercing damage on a hit. Shuck the Oyster. When you score a critical hit with this magic weapon against a creature wearing magical or nonmagical ar­mor, roll a d6. On a 1 or 2, the armor is partially damaged and takes a permanent and cumulative −1 penalty to the AC it offers. Armor reduced to an AC of 10 offers no protection until it is repaired. Dig Deep. When you hit a creature that is wearing armor with an attack using this weapon, it deals an additional 1d6 piercing damage to the target. In addition, on a critical hit, the next attack against the target has advantage. Progression The Extractor progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s Strength modifier increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
