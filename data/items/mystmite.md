# Mystmite

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) This tiny mite glimmers with a deep amber hue. Symbiotic Attunement To attune to this symbiont, you must press it against an open wound and allow the mystmite to latch onto it. While attuned to it, you are affected in the following ways. Magic Repellent. You can't be considered a willing target of a spell or magical effect, even if it is beneficial. If you are the target of a beneficial spell or magical effect that has no save, roll a d20. On a roll of 10 or less, it has no effect on you. Magic Resistance. You have Advantage on saves against spells and other magical effects. Mindguard. When you fail a Wisdom, Intelligence, or Charisma save against a spell or other magical effect, you can choose to succeed instead. If you do, you take 1d10 Necrotic damage that can't be reduced and this property can't be used again until the next day at dawn. To end your attunement to the mite, a creature must spend 1 minute and make a DC 20 Dexterity (Medicine) check to remove it. On a success, the mite is safely removed, ending your attunement to it. On a failure, the mite is destroyed and you remain affected by Magic Repellent for 1d4 days.
