# Helm of Thunder

**description**: Helm of Thunder Wondrous Item, rare Weight: 2 lbs. While wearing this helm, you have resistance to thunder damage. Thunder Strike. When you hit with a melee weapon attack that deals bludgeoning damage, your weapon booms with thunder that is audible within 300 feet of you, and the attack deals an extra 2d6 thunder damage to the target. Additionally, if the target is a creature that is medium size or smaller, it must succeed on a DC 12 Strength saving throw or be pushed 10 feet away from you and knocked prone. This ability can be used once per round and can be used a number of times per day equal to your proficiency bonus.
