# Ottos Flammer

**description**: Exotic Ranged Weapon - Rare, Range (30/45), Two-handed Damage: 2d8 fire damage Pressurized Fuel Tank: The flamer needs to be pressurized as a Bonus Action before every attack or before using the Fire Spray feature. Fire Spray: As an action, you blast each creature in a 15-foot cone with sticky alchemical fire. They must make a DEX SAVE with a DC equal to (14 + your proficiency bonus) saving throw or take 9 (2d8) fire damage (or half as much on a successful save). Fury Mode: As a Reaction when you damage a creature with Otto’s Flamer, you can activate Fury Mode. You immediately inflict an additional 4 (1d8) fire damage to the target(s) of the original damage. When rolling on the Inventor Warranty table, you have a cumulative -2 modifier for each time you have used this feature, until you get a Malfunction. Inventor Warranty: After each attack or Fire Spray, you must roll on the following table to determine if the unstable nature of the Flamer is causing troubles: Malfunction: The weapon must be fixed to be operable again. A creature with Tinker’s Tools, Smith’s Tools or Alchemist Supplies proficiency can repair this during a Short Rest; the Flamer loses all features and counts as an improvised weapon until then. Overheating: until the end of your next turn, while you hold the weapon you have disadvantage on attack rolls and enemies have advantage on the save against the Fire Spray feature. Overspill: You receive 6 (1d12) fire damage. Leakage: You receive 4 (1d8) fire damage. Nothing occurs. Nothing occurs.
