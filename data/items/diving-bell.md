# Diving Bell

**description**: The diving bell is made of wood that has been covered by gray resin to make it water-resistant. It is 10 feet tall, has a diameter of 15 feet, and weighs 1,300 pounds. It has AC 15, 75 hit points, a damage threshold of 5, and damage immunity to poison, psychic, and damage resistance to piercing and slashing damage. Four medium creatures can fit inside the bell with enough space to stand. A wooden bench runs around the inside of the bell where creatures can sit, and four triple-lined glass windows allow a view from the inside to all sides of the bell. Creatures can enter and exit the bell from its bottom, which is not covered. The steel tube on the top of the bell is its suspension device and also allows the drum operator to pump oxygen into the bell. The tube is 900 feet long, which is also the maximum diving range of the bell. Sacks can be attached to the bottom rim of the bell for descent and can be discarded to rise, both at a rate of 30 feet per round. As a full-round action, a creature must constantly pump air into the bell from the surface using the hand-operated pump on the steel drum that holds the bell’s tube. If fresh air is not supplied to the bell, creatures inside start to suffocate within 10 rounds.
