# Jade Skull of Nogg

**description**: Jade Skull of Nogg Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) Named after an ogre magi warlord who terrorized the wastes for several decades and was infamous for his collection of skulls, these objects look like normal human, elf, or orc skulls constructed out of semiprecious stone. The saving throw DC for any spell cast from a skull is DC 15. Jade Skull (Very Rare). While holding this greenish skull, you have immunity to the paralyzed condition and you can cast hold monster from it once per day.
