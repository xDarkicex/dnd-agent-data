# Double

**description**: Journeyman weapon property Components: Masterwork exotic melee weapon that doesn’t have the Heavy property This weapon gains the Double property and its damage die decreases by one step. Double. This weapon has two damage-dealing ends. When you take the Attack action and make an attack with this weapon, you can use your bonus action to make an additional attack with it; you don’t add your ability modifier to the damage roll of this additional attack.
