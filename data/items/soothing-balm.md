# Soothing Balm

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon This small, cylindrical stoneware vessel contains a long-lasting paste made of various healing herbs, which makes a healer’s touch more effective. As part of a Wisdom (Medicine) check, you may apply this balm to gain a +2 bonus to the check. The vessel contains a maximum of 10 doses, and typically has 2d4+2 doses remaining when found.
