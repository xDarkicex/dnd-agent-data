# Beam Emitter

**description**: Beam Emitter Mod (ranged weapon, ammunition property), uncommon While this module is attached, you can use your attack action to discharge a laser beam from your weapon in a line 120 feet long and 5 feet wide. Each creature in the line must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d6 radiant damage on a failed save, and half as much damage on a successful one. After using this mod, its power supply is depleted and you can’t use it again until you finish a long rest.
