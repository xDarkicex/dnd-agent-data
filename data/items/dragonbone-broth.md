# Dragonbone Broth

**description**: Potion, rare Drinking this rich, translucent, umber liquid fills your body with strength and vigor. When you consume this broth, you can reduce your exhaustion level by up to 2, and if you are not (or no longer) exhausted, you have advantage on Strength checks and Constitution saving throws for 1 hour.

- Item Type: Potion
