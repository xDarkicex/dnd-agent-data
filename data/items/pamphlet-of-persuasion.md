# Pamphlet of Persuasion

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon This slim volume has a number of short phrases which, when learned over the course of 8 hours and recited, help persuade people to come around to your point of view. These phrases mean you have advantage on Charisma (Persuasion) checks. The writing in the pamphlet fades away as it is being read, but reappears at the next spring equinox.
