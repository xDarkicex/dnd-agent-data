# Gloomwrought Half Plate

**description**: Gloomwrought Half Plate Armor (Medium), Rare (Requires Attunement) This intricate grayscale armor was forged in the Shadowfell and is infused with that plane’s gloom. While you’re wearing this armor, you have advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the frightened condition on yourself. This armor has 3 charges. You can expend a charge to cast the Calm Emotions spell (save DC 15)from the armor. This armor regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. Half Plate. Half plate consists of shaped metal plates that cover most of the wearer's body. It does not include leg protection beyond simple greaves that are attached with leather straps. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC : 15 + Dex modifier (max 2) Stealth : Disadvantage Weight : 40
