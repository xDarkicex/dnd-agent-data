# Wand of Cleanliness

**description**: Wand of Cleanliness Wondrous item Price: 1,000 gp A long piece of wood with “Oxen Clean” carved into the side of the wood. This wand can clean at any clothing by simply touching the tip of the wand to the offending outfit. The wielder of the wand says the activation word, “Clean, please,” and the wand will immediately remove any dirt or stains from the clothing. The wand can clean up to 5ft of cloth and leather every 10 seconds. The only question is... where does the dirt go?
