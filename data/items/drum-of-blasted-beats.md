# Drum of Blasted Beats

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This drum has 6 charges. While attuned to it, you can use an action to strike it and expend one charge to cast the thunderwave spell, or two charges to cast the shatter spell (save DC 15). The drum regains 1d6 expended charges daily at dusk. If you expend the drum’s last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the drum ruptures and loses its magic.
