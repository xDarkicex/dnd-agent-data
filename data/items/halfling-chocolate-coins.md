# Halfling Chocolate Coins

**description**: Halfling Chocolate Coins Wondrous Item, Common (Gold Coins), or Uncommon (Platinum Coins) Crafted by master halfling chefs, these beautifully minted coins are prized by adventurers, coin collectors, and gourmands who realize their true value. A halfling chocolate coin looks, weighs, and feels like a regular coin in mint condition with depictions of halfling heroes on one side, and farm animals on the other. However, when placed in a creature’s mouth, it melts into delicious and magically nourishing chocolate. The coin emits a faint aura of transmutation magic if targeted by detect magic . An identify spell reveals its true nature. Culinary Currency. Two varieties of Halfling Chocolate Coins exist. A gold chocolate coin tastes like milk chocolate and provides enough nourishment to sustain a medium-sized creature for one day. A platinum chocolate coin tastes like white chocolate and provides enough nourishment to sustain a medium-sized creature for ten days. Legal Tender Fondue. Halfling Chocolate Coins never go bad. If they are broken, crushed, pierced, or submitted to heat or other conditions that would melt nonmagical metal coins, they turn into delicious chocolate fondue and are destroyed. Orelyn on Hafling Chocolate Coins “Look closely. A domestic Overworld animal (a sheep, if I am not mistaken) on one side, and a presentable merryfolk (also known as halfling in the common tongue) lady on the other side. The inscription over the head reads: ‘Melt-In-Your-Mouth-Money, by N.F.’. It is safe to assume this is a so-called halfling chocolate coin. No, you may not eat it. It is part of the treasure. Here, have a spider-cake instead.”
