# Birchwood Club (Common)

**description**: Birchwood Club Weapon (Club), Common (Requires Attunement by a Creature Who Can Read) Unless stated otherwise, each of the weapon’s properties can only be used once between long rests. Curl of Light. One curl forms on the club with the rune for light. You can use an action to rip the curl off and speak its command rune aloud, choosing a point that you can see within 100 feet as you do so. Bright white light fills a 30-foot-radius area emanating from that point for 1 minute. Progression The Birchwood Club progresses each time the attuned creature learns a new language, but it can be progressed only once between proficiency bonus increases. As the club progresses, additional curls form in the bark of the club’s wood, a rune inscribed on the underside.
