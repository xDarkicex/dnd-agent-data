# Iolite Skull of Nogg

**description**: Iolite Skull of Nogg Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) Named after an ogre magi warlord who terrorized the wastes for several decades and was infamous for his collection of skulls, these objects look like normal human, elf, or orc skulls constructed out of semiprecious stone. The saving throw DC for any spell cast from a skull is DC 15. Iolite Skull (Rare). While holding this dark purple skull, you have resistance to necrotic damage and you can cast death ward from it once per day.
