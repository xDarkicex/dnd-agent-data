# Chalice of Identification

**description**: Chalice of Identification Wondrous item, uncommon This bronze chalice is gilded with silver and engraved with swirling wave patterns. When a potion is poured into it, the name of the potion appears on the surface of the liquid in misty white letters. The chalice may be used an unlimited number of times but must be washed thoroughly with water between uses or the letters do not appear.
