# Bead of Dew

**description**: Bead of Dew Wondrous item, uncommon This green bead is made of chalk and measures an inch in diameter. When dropped in water, it immediately dissolves and purifies up to 1,000 gallons of liquid, rendering it free of poison and disease. It also turns saltwater into fresh water.
