# Firebird Feather

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon This feather sheds bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for an additional 20 feet, but it creates no heat and doesn’t use oxygen. While holding the feather, you can tolerate temperatures as low as -50 degrees Fahrenheit. Druids and clerics and paladins who worship nature deities can use the feather as a spellcasting focus. If you use the feather in place of a holy symbol when using your Turn Undead feature, undead in the area have a -1 penalty on the saving throw.
