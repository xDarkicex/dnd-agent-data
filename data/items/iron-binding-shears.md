# Iron Binding Shears

**description**: Iron Binding Shears Weapon (Shortsword), Rare These large iron shears are stained black with a tarnish that never completely wipes off. The word “fettered” is inscribed upon one blade in Sylvan. You can use these magical iron shears as a tool or as a shortsword. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. As an action, you can drive the shears through the shadow of a creature within 5 feet, causing the creature to be restrained if it fails a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw. At the end of each of its turns, the creature can make another Wisdom saving throw. On a successful save, the creature is no longer restrained. You can free the target prematurely by withdrawing the shears from its shadow as a bonus action. While the shears are being used to restrain a creature, you cannot use them to attack.
