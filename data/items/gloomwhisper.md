# Gloomwhisper

**description**: Weapon (Greataxe), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic axe is carved with abyssal glyphs. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with it and it scores a critical hit on a roll of 18–20. When you score a critical hit against a hostile creature with the axe, the axe gains 1 charge. The axe can hold up to 3 charges for the following properties: Essence Strike. When you hit a creature with the axe, you can expend 1 charge to deal an extra 2d8 Necrotic damage to the target. Doom Bringer. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge to force a creature you can see within 30 feet to make a DC 17 Charisma saving throw or become cursed until the end of your next turn. While cursed in this way, the creature can't regain hit points and attack rolls against it have advantage. Echoes of the Damned. As an action, you can expend 1 charge to unleash a bloodcurdling shriek from the axe. Each other creature within 10 feet of you that can hear the scream must succeed on a DC 17 Wisdom saving throw or take 4d8 Psychic damage and until the end of its next turn, it must choose whether it gets an action, a bonus action, or a move on its turn; it gets only one of the three.
