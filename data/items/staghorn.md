# Staghorn

**description**: This exotic weapon consists of a set of overlapping stag horns mounted to a leather backing that is bound to the forearm, with the horns jutting outward over the hand. Typically each horn is coated with steel to make its tip sharper and the rest of it more durable in battle. The Erunsil elves use these weapons for both offense and defense. A staghorn uses the profile of a shield. At the start of your turn, you can choose to use it as a weapon. If you do, it uses the weapon profile of a dagger instead of a shield until the start of your next turn.
