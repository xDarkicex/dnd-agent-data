# Imaging Visor

**description**: Imaging Visor Utility, uncommon (requires affinity) This visor enhances the range of your normal vision. You have advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight. In conditions of clear visibility, you can make out details of even extremely distant creatures and objects as small as 2 feet across.
