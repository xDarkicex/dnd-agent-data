# Ring of Dimensional Ward Piercing

**description**: Ring, rare (requires attunement) This ring has 1 charge, and it regains the expended charge daily at dawn. While wearing the ring and casting a dimension door spell you gain the unique ability to freely enter areas warded against dimension door, effectively bypassing any magical barrier that would prevent the use of this spell in its area.
