# Dragon’s Whiskers

**description**: This bluish alchemical powder is highly explosive. When a dose of the powder makes contact with fire, it explodes in a thunderous blast of bluish-bright flame. Each creature in a 60-foot-radius sphere centered on that point must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw . Creatures and objects in the area take 27 (5d10) fire damage plus 27 (5d10) radiant damage plus 27 (5d10) thunder damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The blast spreads around corners. It ignites flammable objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried.
