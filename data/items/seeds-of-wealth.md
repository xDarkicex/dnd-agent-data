# Seeds of Wealth

**description**: Seeds of Wealth Wondrous item, rare These resemble the seeds of a beech tree, but when planted they grow into a tree that bears coins instead of fruit. This takes six months from planting. The tree bears one harvest of fruit only, consisting of 20d20 coins. Roll on the following table to determine what type of coin the tree bears: d20 Type 1-5 Copper 6-10 Silver 11-15 Electrum 16-19 Gold 20 Platinum
