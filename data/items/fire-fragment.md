# Fire Fragment

**description**: Fire Fragment Wondrous item, Rare (requires attunement) While attuned to this fragment, you have resistance to fire damage, and darkvision out to a range of 60 feet. If you already have darkvision, attuning to the fragment increases its range by 60 feet. Additionally, you can speak, read, and write Ignan, and you can cast the flame control cantrip at will. Awakened Fire Fragment. Attuning to the Fire Fragment while also attuned to the Shattered Staff awakens the fragment and it magically merges with the staff, becoming one item that takes up a single attunement slot. After the Fire Fragment has been awakened, the following spells are added to the staff’s spell list: burning hands (1 charge), fireball (3 charges), flaming sphere (2 charges), scorching ray (2 charges), and wall of fire (4 charges).
