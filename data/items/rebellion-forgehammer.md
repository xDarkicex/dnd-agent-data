# Rebellion Forgehammer

**description**: Rebellion Forgehammer Weapon (Light Hammer), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This well-worn iron hammer has the symbol of a flaming wheel engraved on the haft. Rebellion forgehammers gained notoriety as the favored weapon of the Oldburrow Union in the Eighth Foundry Uprising. Attacks you make with this magic weapon have advantage if you are within 5 feet of at least one ally and the ally isn’t incapacitated. If the ally is also wielding a rebellion forgehammer , the weapon deals an extra 2d4 fire damage on a hit.
