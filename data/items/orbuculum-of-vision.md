# Orbuculum of Vision

**description**: Orbuculum of Vision Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) As an action, you can touch the orbuculum and cast one of the following spells from it without material components: arcane eye , augury , clairvoyance (seeing only), or scrying (save DC 15). Immediately after you cast a spell from the orbuculum, you must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or become blinded and unable to cast a spell from the orbuculum for 24 hours or until you become targeted by the remove curse spell or similar magic. The starting DC of the Constitution saving throw is 10. Immediately after you make the saving throw, the DC increases by an amount equal to the level of the spell you cast from the orbuculum. The DC resets to 10 each day at dawn.
