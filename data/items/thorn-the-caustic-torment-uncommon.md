# Thorn the Caustic Torment (Uncommon)

**description**: Thorn, the Caustic Torment Weapon (Halberd), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) When you make an attack using this weapon, the target takes an extra 1d6 acid damage on a hit. You can cast acid splash from this magic weapon (save DC 11 + your proficiency bonus). Once cast, the spell can’t be cast this way again for 10 minutes. Progression Thorn progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, so does the weapon’s rarity.
