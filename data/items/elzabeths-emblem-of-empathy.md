# Elzabeth's Emblem of Empathy

**description**: Elzabeth's Emblem of Empathy Wondrous item Price: 5,000 gp Toverus’ acclaimed inventor Elzabeth created these items to help her decipher her partner’s stoic demeanor. They became incredibly popular for a time, however Elzabeth never released the designs, and when she passed away the items became more and more rare. The object itself is a small sheet of delicate vellum paper, upon which is painted a stylized image of an eye with a heart-shaped pupil. Once, and only once, the holder of the item can transfer the emblem to themselves, in the form of a permanent tattoo. Once transferred the vellum will become blank, making the item single use. At any time the bearer of the emblem may read the emotions of any creature that the bearer can see and hear. The bearer has no control over the thoughts or emotions of the target. This has the additional benefit that you can better detect the lies of anyone who’s emotions you’re reading. The bearer of the emblem of empathy has a +5 bonus to any checks made to discern lies, and makes those checks with Advantage, so long as the bearer is reading the target’s emotions.
