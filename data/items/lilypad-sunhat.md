# Lilypad Sunhat

**description**: Wondrous item, common (requires attunement) Component: pouch of plant leaves The water in the top of this hat never sloshes and can’t be poured out. It can, however, be scooped out in a drinking vessel, and contains up to four pints. Once water is removed, the hat fills back up over the course of 1 minute. While you wear the hat outdoors during the day, it can feed you through photosynthesis. The time you take to gain a day’s worth of nourishment depends on the brightness: 1 hour in bright, direct sunlight or 8 hours in overcast conditions.
