# Black Heart (Common)

**description**: Black Heart Weapon (Longsword), Common (Requires Attunement) You gain darkvision out to 120 feet. As an action, you can grant another creature this darkvision for a number of hours equal to your proficiency bonus, losing it yourself when you do so. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a short or long rest. Progression Black Heart progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases. To reach legendary rarity, an amethyst gemstone worth at least 200 gp must be set into the sword next to the stone that imprisons Teindrian.
