# Shield of the Ancient

**description**: Armor (Shield), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic shield appears like a simple disk fashioned from rough oak and seems to have seen many battles. Dormant. In its dormant form, the shield has the following properties: You gain a +1 bonus to AC. You have advantage on Insight and Perception checks. The shield has 5 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. As an action, you can cast one of the following spells from the shield, expending 1 charge per level of the spell, and requiring no material components (spell save DC 15): Dispel Magic , Identify , or Hold Person . Awakened. In its awakened form, the shield has the following properties: You have advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the Charmed or Frightened conditions on yourself. The shield has 7 charges, the save DC of its spells increases to 17, and you can use its charges to cast the spells Circle of Power and Dominate Person from it. The shield is a sentient, neutral object with an Intelligence of 18, Wisdom of 18, and Charisma of 12. It has hearing and Truesight out to a range of 30 feet. The shield can understand any language and communicates telepathically, speaking in a deep, gravelly voice. The shield's sentience belongs to an ancient saurian creature that predates history. If the shield is treated with reverence and respect by its wielder, it may use its Truesight and proficiency in Intelligence (Arcana and History) checks (the shield has a +10 bonus to each) to aid them. Awakening the Ancient One It is up to the GM when the Shield of the Ancient becomes awakened. It might happen automatically as the character reaches 9th level, or it may require that the character performs a truly heroic deed – such as slaying an ancient dragon or cleansing long lost ruins of an ancient curse.
