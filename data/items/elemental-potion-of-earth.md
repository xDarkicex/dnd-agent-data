# Elemental Potion of Earth

**description**: Potion, Very Rare Muddy liquid swirls inside this glass bottle. When you drink the contents of this potion, your skin hardens and gains a rough, stone-like appearance for 1 hour, granting you the following benefits: You have resistance to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage. You have advantage on Strength saving throws. You gain a Climb speed equal to your walking speed and ignore difficult terrain created by earth or stone. As a bonus action, you can end the effect early to create a small earthquake on the ground in a 100- foot radius centered on you. Each creature of your choice in that area must succeed a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw or take 2d10 Bludgeoning damage and be knocked Prone.
