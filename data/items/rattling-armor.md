# Rattling Armor

**description**: Armor (Splint), Rare (Requires Attunement) This armor is made of humanoid bones and enchanted with glamours of terror. While wearing this armor, you have resistance to necrotic damage. In addition, you can use an action to cast the fear spell. This property of the armor can't be used again until the next dawn.
