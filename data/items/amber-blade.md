# Amber Blade

**description**: Amber Blade Weapon (longsword), very rare (requires attunement) This magical longsword has a +1 bonus to hit and a +1 bonus to damage and an additional 1d6 slashing damage to Giants and Yoten. The sword’s pommel is decorated with a chunk of amber, inside of which is a trapped object. The sword confers an additional benefit depending on the type of object: Lizard. The bonus damage changes from 1d6 to 1d8. Ant. The wielder may use a bonus action to gain the benefits of the enlarge reduce spell. This effect does not require concentration and lasts one hour. After being activated, this feature may not be used again until the wielder completes a long rest. Scorpion. The wielder may make two reactions during a combat round but is still limited to one reaction per turn. Plant. If the wielder is standing in direct sunlight, they may use a bonus action to cast the heal spell on themselves. This feature may not be used again until the wielder completes a long rest.
