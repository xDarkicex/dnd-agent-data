# Quickstep Weapon

**description**: Weapon (Rapier or Dagger), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) When you wield this weapon and a creature you can see targets you with an attack, you can use your reaction to move to an unoccupied space within 5 feet of your position without provoking opportunity attacks. If this movement would put you out of reach of the attacker, the attack is at disadvantage.
