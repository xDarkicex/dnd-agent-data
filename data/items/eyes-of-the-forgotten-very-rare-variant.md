# Eyes of the Forgotten (very rare variant)

**description**: Ring, uncommon Component: aberration eye The barnacle-like yellow eyes that adorn this ring flick to and fro with reptilian suddenness. When you make eye contact with one of them, brief feelings of drowsiness, terror, and nausea wash over you before the eye darts away once more. This ring has 1 charge, represented by a single open eye, and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. Evil Eye. As an action, you can expend 1 charge, thrusting the ring towards a creature you can see within 60 feet of you while speaking the command word. The creature must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or be affected by a randomly determined effect from the eyebite spell (asleep, panicked, or sickened) for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. While a creature is under this effect, the eye on the ring becomes an inky void, imbued with dread power. When the effect ends, the eye closes. Rare variant: Increase the charges (and number of open eyes) to 2 and the DC to 15. Very rare variant: Increase the charges (and number of open eyes) to 3 and the DC to 16.
