# Gunnspier Quarterstaff

**description**: Weapon (any polearm), rare “ Stab, bang, hope. ” If consulted, these are the instructions any kobold will give on using a gunnspier . With a lengthy reload time and complete lack of accuracy, this matchlock-augmented polearm is usable at only one range: point blank. It also comes with a lovely ribbon and a neat basket to hold all your spare shot. Cute. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Point Blank Shot. When you hit a target with an attack using a loaded gunnspier , you can use a bonus action to pull the trigger, firing a plum-sized leaden ball at the target. The attack deals an additional 2d8 bludgeoning damage . As an action, you can reload the gunnspier . Backfire. When you use Point Blank Shot and roll an 8 on both of the additional bludgeoning damage dice, the gunnspier backfires. Each creature within 10 feet of you (including you) must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take 4d6 piercing damage as shrapnel erupts from the weapon.
