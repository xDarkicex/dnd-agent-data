# Bee venom

**description**: Injury This poison must be harvested from living giant bees. A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or take 10 (3d6) poison damage and be poisoned for 1 minute. The poisoned creature is paralyzed. The creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
