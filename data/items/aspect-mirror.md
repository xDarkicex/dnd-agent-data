# Aspect Mirror

**description**: Aspect Mirror Wondrous item, rare An aspect mirror looks like a plain, circular, silvered mirror, usually 1.5 feet in diameter. Aspect mirrors come in pairs, and mirrors in a pair match each other. As an action, you can touch an aspect mirror and establish a link to its paired mirror. For the next minute, you can see, hear, and speak as if you were in the same space as the paired mirror. While using the mirror like this, you and your surroundings are visible to those who see through the paired mirror as if the paired mirrors were a window. Communication through the mirrors ignores distance, but you can only use the mirrors when they are on the same plane of existence. You cannot cast spells through the mirrors. Once an aspect mirror is used in this manner, the mirrors can’t be used again until the next dawn. If one of the mirrors in a pair is destroyed, the other one becomes nonmagical.
