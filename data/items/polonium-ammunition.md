# Polonium Ammunition

**description**: Weapon (Ammunition), Rare The tip of this ammunition is made of highly radioactive polonium encased within a brittle lead crystal designed to shatter upon impact. Polonium Strike When you hit a creature with a ranged attack using the ammunition, it takes an extra 2d6 Necrotic damage and must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution save or suffer Radiation Sickness . The creature must repeat the save at the end of every minute, suffering Radiation Sickness with each failure, until it or another creature spends 1 minute and succeeds on a DC 15 Wisdom (Medicine) check to remove the polonium fragments from its body. Once it hits a target, the ammunition is destroyed.
