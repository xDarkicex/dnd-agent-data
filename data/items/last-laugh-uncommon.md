# Last Laugh (Uncommon)

**description**: Last Laugh Weapon (Mace), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. You can utter a command word to cause the weapon to emit soft giggling sounds like that of a small child, which lasts until the end of your next turn. Last Laugh. When you attack a creature with an Intelligence score of 5 or more with this weapon and roll a 20 on the attack roll, the target begins laughing uncontrollably. They fall prone, are incapacitated, and are unable to stand up until the end of their next turn. Progression The Last Laugh progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
