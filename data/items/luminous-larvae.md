# Luminous Larvae

**description**: Wondrous Item, Common This small glass vial holds bio-luminescent larvae submerged in magical liquid. Guiding Glow. As a bonus action, you can shake the vial, agitating the larvae and causing them to increase their glow. The vial glows with bright light in a 5-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet for 1 minute. When the vial has glowed for a total of 1 hour, it can't glow again in this way until the next dawn. Protective Pests. As an action, you can open the vial and toss the larvae into the air, where they transform into a swarm of glowing butterflies that surround you or another creature of your choice within 5 feet. For 1 minute, that creature has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. Once used in this way, the larvae and the vial lose their magic properties and become nonmagical.
