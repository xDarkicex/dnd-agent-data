# Hammer of Chimes

**description**: Hammer of Chimes Melee weapon Price: 4,000 gp This warhammer is well worn and simple. Each strike of the hammer sounds like a bell is being rung, regardless of the material that the hammer hits. When a character makes an attack with the Hammer of Chimes, they may add 1d8 sonic damage to their attacks. Doing this extra damage can never be done in a quiet way, and anyone within a 300 foot radius can hear the chimes from the attack. Once per Short Rest, when an attack successfully deals damage to a target, the wielder may add one of these two effects to the attack. Dispel Silence - Any magical silence in the area is immediately dispelled. Deafen - All creatures—including the wielder of the hammer and teammates—within 50 feet become deafened for 1d4 rounds. Melee. Melee Weapons are used to attack enemies up close in hand-to-hand combat. Light. Light weapons can be wielded with finesse in one hand, leaving the other hand free to carry other essentials, like a torch or a shield. Small and light melee weapons can use either Strength (Str) or Dexterity (Dex) for the Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus, whichever is higher. Damage: 1d6 Secondary Damage: 1d8 Damage Type: Bludgeoning
