# Warrick's Holy Symbol

**description**: Warrick's Holy Symbol Wondrous item Price: 12,000 gp Warrick, the servant of Malvex, did not always serve evil. He was once a paladin, who was corrupted by Malvex’s magic. Her holy symbol no longer serves her, and her god abandoned her long ago. However, the symbol seeks to do good in the world, now untethered from Warrick. The holy symbol gives some minor paladin powers, but may abandon the holder if they perform an evil act. Once per Long Rest the owner may hold the symbol and use their Main Action to heal a target that they are touching for 2d8+10 hit points. Also once per Long Rest, when the owner of the symbol hits with an attack and does damage, they may choose to add an additional 2d8+5 radiant damage to the attack. This damage often does double damage to undead and evil targets, to the discretion of the game master. If the owner performs an evil act (also determined by the game master), the holy symbol will suddenly disappear from their bag and end up in the hands of one of the owner’s enemies, specifically to help extract revenge for the betrayal.
