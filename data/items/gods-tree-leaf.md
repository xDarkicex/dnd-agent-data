# God's Tree Leaf

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon This leaf provides dim light in a 5-foot radius. It has 3 charges and loses 1 of its charges daily at dawn. As an action, you can expend 1 charge to cast one of the following spells: cure wounds (as a 2nd-level spell), lesser restoration , or zone of truth . Once it has no charges left, the leaf withers and crumbles into dust.
