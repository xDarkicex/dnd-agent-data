# Serpent Hide

**description**: Armor (Hide), Rare (Requires Attunement) While wearing this armor, you can speak its command word to take on the form of a giant constrictor snake . You retain this form as long as you like, but once you return to your normal form, you cannot use it again until after a long rest.
