# Moustache (Very Rare)

**description**: Moustache Weapon (Shortbow), Rarity Varies (Requires Attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. You also gain proficiency with a disguise kit while attuned to this weapon. If you already have proficiency, your proficiency bonus is doubled for any check that uses a disguise kit. As a bonus action, you place the bow against your upper lip, shrinking it to the size and form of a handlebar moustache, which magically attaches to your face. While in moustache form, it can’t be used as a weapon. It can be removed and returned to shortbow form as a bonus action. Forceful Follicles. As an action, you can utter the command word while pretending to fire an arrow at a creature. The target’s facial hair grows excessively long and wraps around its body. If the target has no facial hair, it sprouts some for the duration. Any hair cut or burned away regrows instantly. The target must succeed on a DC 16 Strength saving throw or be restrained by the hair for 1 minute. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success. The excess hair falls away when the effect ends or when the target succeeds on a saving throw. You can use this property three times, and regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. Play Dead. When you are reduced to 0 hit points but not killed outright, you can drop to 1 hit point instead and appear dead to any onlookers until the start of your next turn. A creature succeeding on a DC 20 Wisdom (Medicine) check determines you are not actually dead. While in this state, you are fully aware of your surroundings and can see through your closed eyelids. If you move or speak, the deception is instantly revealed. Once this property has been used, it can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. Progression Moustache progresses each time the creature who is attuned to it completes an audacious plan, heist, or scheme (what satisfies this definition is at the GM’s discretion).
