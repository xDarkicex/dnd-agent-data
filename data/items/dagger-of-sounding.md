# Dagger of Sounding

**description**: Weapon (dagger), rare (requires attunement by a rogue) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While you are attuned to it, you can use a bonus action to gently tap its tip against a surface. If there is a hollow space behind the surface, the daggert gives a resounding ring, quite different from the dull tone emitted if struck against solid stone. After you do this, you have advantage on any Wisdom (Perception) or Intelligence (Investigation) check made to detect a secret door or compartments on the same turn.
