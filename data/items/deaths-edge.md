# Death's Edge

**description**: Weapon (Sickle), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This sickle has a handle made of bone and a blade made of shadow. While attuned to this magic weapon, your head is shrouded in shadow. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. If you attack a creature with this weapon and roll a 20 on the attack roll, the creature takes an extra 2d8 necrotic damage, and you regain a number of hit points equal to the necrotic damage taken. In addition, while you hold this weapon, you have resistance to necrotic damage. Master of Death . You can use an action to cast finger of death (spell save DC 15) from the weapon. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. Encounter Ideas Wizard’s Tower. A prolific necromancer has a glass case display for a death’s edge in their tower. It has recently gone missing and must be recovered. Zombies! A vindictive enemy of the party has created a horde of zombies from the risen victims of their death’s edge and returns to seek revenge. Mysterious Figure. A woman shrouded in shadow stumbles into town after dark. Whispered rumors insist she is up to no good and the death’s edge at her hip supports this claim.
