# Mag-lock

**description**: Mag-Lock Miscellaneous gear This magnetic clamp lock can be placed on any medium to large container, normal door, or latch. It has a digital coded lock and can only be opened with a successful DC 16 Dexterity check with Infiltration tools or a DC 15 Intelligence check with a Hacker’s kit. Weight : 3
