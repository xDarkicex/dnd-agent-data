# Bonecleaver

**description**: Bonecleaver Weapon (greataxe), very rare (requires attunement) Damage: 1d10+2 slashing Weight: 7 lbs. Properties: Heavy, Two-Handed You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic axe. When you attack a creature with this weapon and roll a 20 on the attack roll, that target takes an extra 2d8 slashing damage. Then roll another d20. If you roll a 20, you lop off one of the target’s limbs, with the effect of such loss determined by the GM. If the creature has no limb to sever, you lop off a portion of its body instead.
