# Psion's Skull

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic skull is from a psionic creature and is decorated with golden ornaments. While carrying it, you have resistance to Psychic damage, you are immune to magic that allows other creatures to discern your thoughts, whether you are lying, your alignment, or your creature type. The skull has 3 charges for the following properties and regains all expended charges daily at dawn: Intellect Fortress. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge to create an aura of psychic energy in a 10-foot radius around you for 1 minute. You and each creature of your choice within that area have advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws. Mind Blast. As an action, you can expend 2 charges to emit psychic energy in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 15 Intelligence saving throw or take 18 (4d8) Psychic damage and until the end of its next turn, the creature must choose whether it gets an action, a bonus action, or a move on its turn; it gets only one of the three.
