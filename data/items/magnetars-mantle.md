# Magnetar's Mantle

**description**: Armor (Medium or Heavy Metal Armor), Uncommon This armor is crafted from an alloy of cobalt, iron, and silver. Magnetic Overload This armor has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. As a Bonus Action, you can expend 1 charge to cause one of these effects. Attraction Field. You create a magnetic field in a 10-foot radius around you for 1 minute or until you end the effect as a Bonus Action. When a creature in the area attempts to move away from you while wearing or carrying a metal object weighing 1 lb or more, it must spend 4 feet of movement for every 1 foot it moves. Magnetic Pulse. You release a magnetic pulse in a 10-foot radius around you. Each creature in the area that is wearing or carrying an object made of metal weighing 1 lb or more must succeed on a DC 15 Strength save or be pushed 10 feet away from you and fall Prone.
