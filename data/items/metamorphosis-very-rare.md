# Metamorphosis (Very Rare)

**description**: Weapon (Longbow), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) If insect plague is on your class or subclass’s spell list, you are proficient with Metamorphosis and can use it as a spellcasting focus. While attuned to this magic weapon, your body develops arthropod features based on the weapon’s rarity. If your attunement ends, roll a number of d10s equal to your level. You take necrotic damage equal to your roll, which can’t be reduced in any way, and you lose your arthropod features. Common When you make an attack with this weapon, you may choose to fire a locust instead of an arrow. If you do, the attack ignores half cover and three quarters cover and deals 1d4 piercing damage on a hit instead of the weapon’s normal damage. Uncommon Armor. You gain a chitinous exoskeleton. While you are wearing no armor, your AC equals 13 + your Constitution modifier, and you are resistant to bludgeoning and slashing damage from nonmagical attacks. Rare Antennae. You gain a pair of olfactory antennae. You have advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell. Additionally, you can taste objects by touching them with your antennae, and have advantage on checks to determine if objects you touch this way are poisoned. Very Rare Wings. You gain dragonfly-like wings. While you are wearing no armor, you have a flying speed equal to your walking speed.
