# Ghost Robe

**description**: Ghost Robe Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) This cloak is almost insubstantial to the touch. When you wear it, you become equally insubstantial, able to move through Medium or smaller creatures and walls thinner than five feet as if they were difficult terrain. If you end your turn inside a creature or object, you take 2d8 force damage. You cannot move through magical barriers or lead of any thickness.
