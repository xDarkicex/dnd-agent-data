# Shieldcrusher

**description**: Shieldcrusher Weapon (Lance), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) A stout lance carved from pale ash wood, with a fluttering silver and black pennant. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon, and an additional +2 damage against mounted opponents. When you score a critical hit with this weapon, it creates a thunderclap that radiates 10 feet outwards from you. Creatures in that area must make a DC 17 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. Afterwards, the lance is broken and can’t be used as a weapon, but it repairs itself over the course of a short or long rest. Master of the Tourney. While you are mounted, you can speak the command word as a bonus action to grant your mount the following benefits for 1 minute: their speed increases by 30 feet, they are immune to the frightened and prone conditions, they gain 2d6 temporary hit points, and they can make one additional attack on each of their turns. You can use this property three times, and regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.
