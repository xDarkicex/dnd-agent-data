# Ring of Treachery

**description**: Ring, very rare (requires attunement) This ring has 3 charges. While wearing this ring, when you’re damaged, you may use a reaction to expend a charge and transfer that damage to a random creature (which could include an ally) within 60 feet. All charges are restored when you finish a long rest.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Ring
- Item Rarity: very rare
- Requires Attunement: requires attunement

**publisher**: Wizards of the Coast

**book**: Chains of Asmodeus
