# Ring of the Dolphin

**description**: Ring, uncommon (requires attunement) This gold ring bears a jade carving in the shape of a leaping dolphin. While wearing this ring, you have a swimming speed of 40 feet. In addition, you can hold your breath for twice as long while underwater.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Ring
- Item Rarity: uncommon
- Requires Attunement: requires attunement

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Vault of Magic
