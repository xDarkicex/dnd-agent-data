# Tent of Luxury

**description**: Tent of Luxury Wondrous item, rare This appears to be an ordinary canvas two-person tent. When you speak the command word, it pitches itself, and when you speak the command word again, it packs itself back up. While pitched, the interior of the tent is an extradimensional space that is 40 feet square. It contains fine silken beds, a small bathroom area, and a long table laden with a hearty feast. There is enough accommodation and food for 8 people, and the feast replenishes itself daily. The tent has AC 14 and 40 hit points. If it is destroyed, the contents of the tent are scattered around a 40-foot-radius area.
