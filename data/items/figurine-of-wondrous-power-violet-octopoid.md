# Figurine of Wondrous Power, Violet Octopoid

**description**: Wondrous item, rare Violet Octopoid (Rare). A disturbing statuette carved in purple sugilite, the tentacled, violet octopoid can become an ambulatory, amphibious giant octopus for up to 6 hours. Use the statistics of a giant octopus, except it has 100 hit points and can make two tentacle attacks each turn. Once it has been used, it can’t be used again until 3 days have passed. If you speak the command word in Void Speech, the octopus has an Intelligence score of 9 and can make three tentacle attacks each turn.
