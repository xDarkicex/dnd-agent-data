# Table of Cornucopia

**description**: This simple-looking magical table is made out of wood. Once per day, you can cause the table to become filled with the food and drink you desire the most. If uneaten, the food remains until dawn, at which point it disappears. It takes 1 hour to consume the food and drink on the table. A single creature who eats everything from the table is completely nourished for one day and benefits from the effects of a heroes’ feast spell.
