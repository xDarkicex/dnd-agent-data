# Ram, Portable

**description**: Ram, Portable Adventuring gear Price: 20 gp You can use a portable ram to break down doors, or other stationary objects. When doing so, you gain a +4 bonus on the Strength check. One other character can help you use the ram, giving you Advantage on this check. On a success the Ram does 2d6+Strength damage to the object and, if applicable, pushes the target 10 feet. The Ram has Disadvantage against living creatures.
