# Sling Bullets

**description**: Sling Bullets Ammunition You can use a weapon that has the ammunition property to make a ranged attack only if you have ammunition to fire from the weapon . Each time you attack with the weapon, you expend one piece of ammunition. Drawing the ammunition from a quiver , case , or other container is part of the attack (you need a free hand to load a one-handed weapon). At the end of the battle, you can recover half your expended ammunition by taking a minute to search the battlefield. Weight: 0.075
