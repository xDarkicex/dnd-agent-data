# Gem of Darkness

**description**: Gem of Darkness Wondrous Item, Uncommon This prism has 50 Charges. While you are holding it, you can use an action to speak one of three command words to cause one of the following effects: The first command word causes the gem to extinguish all nonmagical light and light created by uncommon items in a 60-foot radius. If any of this effect’s area overlaps with an area of light created by a spell of 1st level or lower, the spell that created the light is dispelled. This effect doesn’t expend a charge. The second command word expends 1 charge and causes the gem to create magical darkness that spreads from a point you choose within 60 feet of you to fill a 5-foot radius sphere for 1 minute or until you use the gem to create that effect again. The darkness spreads around corners and extinguishes all nonmagical light and light created by uncommon items. A creature with darkvision can’t see through this darkness, and nonmagical light can’t illuminate it. If any of this effect’s area overlaps with an area of light created by a spell of 2nd level or lower, the spell that created the light is dispelled. The third command word expends 5 charges and allows you to cast the darkness spell from the gem. When cast this way, darkness has a range of 120 feet and the sphere of magical darkness it creates has a radius of 20 feet. When all of the gem’s charges are expended, the gem becomes a nonmagical jewel worth 50 gp.
