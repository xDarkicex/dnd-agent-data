# Whipsaw

**description**: A whipsaw is comprised of razor-sharp teeth loosely riveted together and attached to a T-shaped handle at each end. Used as a tool, a whipsaw allows a single creature to cut down a Medium or smaller tree with 30 minutes of work. When not in use, a whipsaw can be coiled and hung from a belt or pack.
