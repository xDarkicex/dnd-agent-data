# Wand of Bubbles

**description**: Wand of Bubbles Wand, rare (requires attunement by a Spellcaster) Weight: 1 lb. This wand has 5 charges, and it regains 1d4 expended charges daily at dawn. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 of its charges and choose 1 of its effects. Air Bubble. You create a magical bubble of air around a willing target's head or similar sized object. The bubble lasts for 1 hour and allows the target to breathe freely underwater or in similar airless environments. The bubble is destroyed if the wearer takes damage. Blinding Bubbles. You create a 20-foot-radius cloud of colorful bubbles centered on a point within 100 feet. Each creature in that area is blinded until they leave the area. The bubbles last for 1 minute or until a wind of moderate or greater speed (at least 10 miles per hour) disperses them. Acid Bubbles. You create a 10-foot-radius cloud of dark bubbles centered on a point within 60 feet. When the bubbles pop, they splash acid. Each creature in the area must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw, taking 3d6 acid damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. If you expend the wand's last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the wand melts into colorful goo.
