# Small Melee Weapon

**description**: Small Melee Weapon Melee weapon Price: 15 gp A small weapon used to attack at close range. (See rules for weapons in the Combat section) Melee. Melee Weapons are used to attack enemies up close in hand-to-hand combat. Small. Small weapons are used one-handed and are usually small enough to be hidden away in a boot, up a sleeve, or secretly tucked into a small bag. Small and light melee weapons can use either Strength (Str) or Dexterity (Dex) for the Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus, whichever is higher. Damage: 1d4
