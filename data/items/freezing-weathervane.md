# Freezing Weathervane

**description**: Wondrous Object, Uncommon This simple tin and wood weathervane is shaped like a snowflake. It can be worn around the neck as a pendant or sewn into the hood of a cloak. Bearing this weathervane, you may reduce any cold damage by an amount equal to your proficiency bonus. In addition, when you hit a creature with a weapon attack, its speed is reduced by 5 feet until the end of its next turn.
