# Hat of the Hermit Hag

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) Component: fey (hag) brain Though this headpiece may easily be mistaken for an average conch, its external dimensions bely its roomy interior; it is much bigger on the inside. Hermit hags love to decorate their hats with trophies and trinkets traded, tricked, or otherwise taken from unfortunate adventurers. Hag’s Magic. While wearing this hat, you can use it to cast the following spells (save DC 13): disguise self and suggestion . When you use the hat to cast a spell, roll a d3. If the result is equal to or lower than the spell’s level, the hat can’t be used to cast any spells until the next dawn. Hermit. As a bonus action while wearing the hat, you can hide inside of it, causing it to expand in order to accommodate you. Until you emerge, you gain total cover from attacks and effects originating from outside the hat, your speed can’t exceed 5 feet, you are blinded, you have disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks, and you automatically fail Dexterity saving throws. You can use your action to emerge from the hat. If you are knocked prone while within the hat, your underbelly is exposed, and you no longer have total cover.
