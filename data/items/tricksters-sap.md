# Trickster’s Sap

**description**: Poison, rare This sap has been treated to simply feel like water. However, a creature that comes into contact with this sap and is exposed to sunlight within 1 hour of contact must succeed on A DC 16 Constitution saving throw or be blinded for 1 hour. A successful saving throw reduces the duration to one minute. The creature can make a new saving throw to end the effect by washing its eyes with water as an action.
