# The Twins

**description**: The Twins Melee weapon Price: 15,000 gp This pair of short swords were originally a single blade, but were magically separated during the Toverus Asunderland cataclysm. One glows red with heat while the other is frosted over and emits a faint blue hue. The Fire and Ice blades are considered light melee weapons. They use either your Dexterity or Strength on their Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus, and do 1d6 slashing damage. Each blade also does an extra 1d6 elemental damage on Damage Rolls (fire and ice damage respectively). Once per Long Rest the swords can be combined together into a single two-handed blade which then emits a blast of energy. Make an Attack Roll against all targets in a 5 ft by 30 ft line. You may use your melee Attack Bonus for this Attack Roll. Any target that is hit takes 4d6 force damage. Targets in range that are missed by the attack take half damage. After the attack the swords break apart again into two weapons. The swords cannot be further than 100 feet from each other. The instant that they are moved further apart than that, they both disappear and reappear in a space halfway between where they were located. Melee. Melee Weapons are used to attack enemies up close in hand-to-hand combat. Light. Light weapons can be wielded with finesse in one hand, leaving the other hand free to carry other essentials, like a torch or a shield. Small and light melee weapons can use either Strength (Str) or Dexterity (Dex) for the Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus, whichever is higher.
