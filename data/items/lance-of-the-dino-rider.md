# Lance of the Dino-Rider

**description**: Weapon (Lance), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) A seven-foot shaft of white shorea heartwood topped by a finely-honed obsidian blade. The shaft is carved with stylized images of fantastical creatures and decorated with raptor feathers. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While holding this weapon, you can cast find steed . When cast this way, the spirit you summon must take the form of a Medium or larger Dinosaur with a CR equal to or less than half your proficiency bonus. The steed vanishes when it drops to 0 hit points, after 1 hour, or until you dismiss it as an action. Once used, you can’t use this property again until you finish a long rest. Encounter Ideas Feral Child. Alison, a noble scion (champion) lost in the jungle and raised by dinosaurs, has returned to claim her inheritance, bearing this lance as a weapon. Lost Island. Hullcracker Island is surrounded by razor sharp reefs, but inhabitants carrying this lance occasionally ride tame plesiosauruses out to trade with passing vessels. Scholastic Sabotage. Arhmedies, a halfling sage, has uncovered a battlefield containing relics of an ancient war between dinosaur-riding knights, but the lance has been stolen by an unscrupulous rival.
