# Proximity Coil

**description**: Proximity Coil Weapon (Whip), Common This whip is comprised of braided unicorn tail hair, which appears silky and iridescent. When you reduce a creature to 0 hit points with an attack using this magic weapon, they are not killed outright. The target becomes stable, is immediately pulled 5 feet closer to you, and remains unconscious for 1 hour or until it regains any number of hit points.
