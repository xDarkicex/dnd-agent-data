# Wisp of the Void

**description**: Potion, uncommon The interior of this bottle is pitch black, and it feels empty. When opened, it releases a black vapor. When you inhale this vapor, your eyes go completely black. For 1 minute, you have darkvision out to a range of 60 feet, and you have resistance to necrotic damage. In addition, you gain a +1 bonus to damage rolls made with a weapon.
