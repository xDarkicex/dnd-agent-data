# Grimlock Ear

**description**: Grimlock Ear Spell catalyst, uncommon When you cast the blindness/deafness spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, if a target fails its saving throw against the spell, it becomes both blinded and deafened for the duration.
