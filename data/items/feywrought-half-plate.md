# Feywrought Half Plate

**description**: Feywrought Half Plate Armor (Medium), Rare (Requires Attunement) This colorful, flowery armor was forged in the Feywild and is infused with that plane’s captivating magic. While wearing this armor, you have advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the charmed condition on yourself. This armor has 3 charges. You can use an action to expend a charge to cast the Compulsion spell (save DC 15) from this armor. The armor regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. Half Plate. Half plate consists of shaped metal plates that cover most of the wearer's body. It does not include leg protection beyond simple greaves that are attached with leather straps. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC : 15 + Dex modifier (max 2) Stealth : Disadvantage Weight : 40
