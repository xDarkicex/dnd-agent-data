# Weighted chain

**description**: Weighted Chain Melee weapon (martial, weighted chain) Light. A light weapon is small and easy to handle, making it ideal for use when fighting with two weapons. Reach. This weapon adds 5 feet to your reach when you attack with it, as well as when determining your reach for opportunity attacks with it.
