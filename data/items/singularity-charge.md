# Singularity Charge

**description**: Singularity Charge Utility (limted), rare This small, spherical device measures 3/4 of an inch in diameter and weighs an ounce. Typically, 1d6 + 3 singularity charges are found attached together to a wearable polymer band or belt. You can use an action to detach a charge and throw it up to 60 feet away. When it reaches the end of its trajectory, the charge detonates as a rank 3 singularity power (save DC 15). You can hurl multiple charges, or even the whole band, as one action. When you do so, increase the rank of the singularity by 1 for each charge beyond the first.
