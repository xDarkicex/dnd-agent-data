# Traitor's Tabard

**description**: Traitor's Tabard Wondrous item, common (requires attunement) When the fog of war is at its thickest, soldiers know to attack anyone wearing the enemy’s colors or emblems. The traitor’s tabard is a finely tailored piece of clothing that can be worn over armor. As an action, the creature wearing the tabard can change the color and crest on the tabard to anything they want.
