# Helmet of Disjunction

**description**: Helmet of Disjunction Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement) While wearing this helmet, you can use an action to speak its power word and activate its ability. When activated, the helm disables all enchantments within 100 feet if you. This instantaneous effect cancels all spells and magic item effects that belong to the Enchantment spell school. For concentration spells, they are automatically dispelled and must be cast again for the effect to be resumed. For magic items with Enchantment spells, the effect is disabled for 2 hours, after which the magic item’s power returns to normal. Once used, the helm can’t be used again until the next dawn.
