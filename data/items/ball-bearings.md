# Ball Bearings

**description**: Adventuring Gear Weight: 2 lb. Cost: 1 GP As a Utilize action, you can spill Ball Bearings from their pouch . They spread to cover a level, 10-foot-square area within 10 feet of yourself. A creature that enters this area for the first time on a turn must succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity saving throw or have the Prone condition. It takes 10 minutes to recover the Ball Bearings.
