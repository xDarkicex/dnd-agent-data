# B.O.N.K. Mk. 2 Light Hammer (very rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (any hammer), rare (requires attunement) Component: construct gears After the failed launch of the B.O.N.K. Mk. 1, which had a history of blowing the user’s hands off or melting their face, genius artificer Anton Spark unveiled its successor. The Mk. 2 is bigger, faster, and, allegedly, “safer”. When you hit a Small or larger creature with an attack using this weapon, it gains 1 charge as the pressure within builds, up to a maximum of 3 charges. If no charges are expended for 1 minute, the weapon loses all charges as it depressurises. B.O.N.K. As a bonus action while holding this weapon, you can expend 1 charge to activate its built in engine and boost the next attack you make with it before the start of your next turn, which deals an extra 1d8 bludgeoning damage on a hit. B.O.O.O.N.K. As an action while holding this weapon, you can expend 3 charges to fire a bolt of lightning in a 30-foot long, 5-foot wide line. Each creature in the line must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 5d8 lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Very rare variant: Increase the DC to 16, the lightning damage to 6d8, and the maximum number of charges the weapon can hold to 4.
