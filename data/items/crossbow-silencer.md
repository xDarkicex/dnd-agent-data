# Crossbow Silencer

**description**: Crossbow Silencer Wondrous Item, Common This small magical band can be clamped to or removed from the stirrup of any crossbow as an action. While the silencer is attached to a weapon, Wisdom (Perception) checks to hear the weapon firing are made at disadvantage. In addition, if you are hidden when you make an attack roll using the silencer’s attached weapon, you can activate the silencer (no action required) to remain hidden, and your attack doesn't give away your location. Once this property is used, it can’t be used again for 10 minutes.
