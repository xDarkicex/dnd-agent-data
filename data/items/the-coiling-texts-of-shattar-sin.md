# The Coiling Texts of Shattar-sin

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) These two long vellum scrolls are inscribed with animated text in a long-dead language, and can be worn around the forearms as handwraps. While attuned to and wearing the Coiling Texts, you gain the following benefits: The Weight of Knowledge. The raw strength of the knowledge contained within the Coiling Texts is enough to enhance your attacks beyond their normal strength. Your unarmed attacks deal an additional 1d4 psychic damage. A Mind of Their Own. The Coiling Texts contain enough knowledge to give the scrolls themselves a limited form of sentience. While worn, the scrolls move gently around your arm like a pair of snakes, and once per round you may make an unarmed opportunity attack without expending a reaction, as the scrolls lash out independently of you. Consult the Texts. Once per day, recharging at dawn, you may focus your mind and consult the knowledge contained within the Coiling Texts to cast the legend lore spell without expending any material components. Wrought from the hide of an ancient leviathan and writ upon in a language none now know how to speak, the Coiling Texts of Shattar-sin hold a wealth of knowledge thought lost to time’s march – but hold it only for those with the required focus.
