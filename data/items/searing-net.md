# Searing Net

**description**: Searing Net Weapon (Net), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) Thin, metal strands sparkle and shine within the net’s normal cordage. You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls made with this magic weapon. A creature restrained by this net can use its action to make a DC 15 Strength check, freeing itself or another creature within reach on a success. Dealing 10 slashing damage to the net (AC 13) also frees the creature. The net magically repairs itself when you finish a long rest. Molten Mesh. As an action, you can speak the command word, causing the net to glow red-hot until the start of your next turn. Any creature in physical contact with the net before the start of your next turn takes 2d8 fire damage and has disadvantage on attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws until the end of their next turn. Once used, you can’t use this property again until you finish a short or long rest.
