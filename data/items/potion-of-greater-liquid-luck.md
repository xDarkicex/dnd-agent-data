# Potion of Greater Liquid Luck

**description**: Potion of Greater Liquid Luck Potion, rare When you drink this potion, you begin to feel very lucky. You gain a number of luck points based on the rarity of the potion imbibed. These luck points last for up to 7 hours. When you make an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can spend 1 luck point to roll an additional d20. You can use this ability after the original roll, but before the outcome is revealed. You choose which of the two results is used. You can also spend one luck point when an attack roll is made against you. Roll a d20, and choose whether the attacker’s roll uses their result or yours. If multiple creatures use a luck point on the same roll, they cancel out, resulting in no additional dice. Immediately after you use the last luck point, your luck runs out and you become unlucky. You must make a Charisma saving throw with a DC determined by the rarity of the potion (Unlucky DC). If you succeed on the saving throw, you are unlucky for 13 minutes. If you fail the saving throw, you are unlucky until you complete a long rest. While you are unlucky as a result of this potion’s effects, whenever you make an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you are at disadvantage. Additionally, whenever an attack roll is made against you, that attack has advantage.
