# Blessed Holy Symbol

**description**: Blessed Holy Symbol Wondrous item, very rare This holy symbol emanates an invisible but palpable aura of divine radiance. You gain a +1 bonus to your spell save DC and spell attack modifier while using this holy symbol as your spellcasting focus. In addition, this magic item holds 7 charges. Whenever you use a feature that would allow you to expend a use of your Channel Divinity, you can expend a charge from this magic item instead. On the last day of each week, the holy symbol regains 1d6 + 1 charges.
