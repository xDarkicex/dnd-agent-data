# Mithral Kopis +1

**description**: Weapon, shortsword, uncommon This mithral-forged, forward-curving shortsword is heavier along the end of the blade. This gives it more cutting power than most shortswords. Attacks made with this weapon have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls. When you make an attack with this sword, you may choose to have it inflict piercing or slashing damage.
