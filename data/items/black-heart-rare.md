# Black Heart (Rare)

**description**: Black Heart Weapon (Longsword), Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. You gain darkvision out to 120 feet. As an action, you can grant another creature this darkvision for a number of hours equal to your proficiency bonus, losing it yourself when you do so. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a short or long rest. Amethyst Wings. As a bonus action, you can speak a command word, causing a pair of purple dragon wings to sprout from your back. You gain a flying speed of 30 feet for 1 minute, or until you speak the command word again to dismiss the wings (no action required). Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. Violet Outburst. As an action, you can exhale a 30-foot cone of purple energy. Each creature in the area must make a DC 15 Strength saving throw, taking 6d8 force damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. Progression Black Heart progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases. To reach legendary rarity, an amethyst gemstone worth at least 200 gp must be set into the sword next to the stone that imprisons Teindrian.
