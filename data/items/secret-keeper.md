# Secret Keeper

**description**: Secret Keeper Weapon (Dagger), Rare (Requires Attunement) This appears to be a completely smooth silver hilt with no blade. A misty moonlit blade appears moments before finding purchase. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you kill a creature with a melee attack with this weapon, the target must make a DC 20 Wisdom saving throw. If they fail, you can immediately pull them close and ask a yes or no question that the creature must answer truthfully just before they die. The creature must be able to communicate with and understand you for this effect to work.
