# Orb of Ill Omen

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This magic orb is carved from some unnamed, alien mineral. Ill Omen. As an action, you can toss the orb into the air and cause it to orbit the head of a creature you can see within 30 feet. The creature suffers one of the following effects of your choice: Amplify. The first time each turn the creature takes damage from a spell, it takes an extra 1d6 damage of that type. Distract. The creature must roll a d4 and subtract the result from each attack roll it makes. Slow. The creature's speed is reduced by 10 feet and it has a –1 penalty to AC and Dexterity saving throws. If a creature hits the orb with an attack (AC 24) or seizes it with a successful DC 24 Dexterity (Acrobatics) check, the orb stops hovering and loses all magical properties until the next dawn. While it hovers, you can use a bonus action to recall it to your hand or cause it to fly up to 30 feet to orbit the head of another creature you can see.
