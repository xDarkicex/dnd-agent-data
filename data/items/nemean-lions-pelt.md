# Nemean Lion's Pelt

**description**: Nemean Lion’s Pelt Wondrous Item, Legendary (Requires attunement) When Heracles defeated the Nemean Lion, he skinned it by using its own claws. The pelt, worn as a cloak protected the demigod in his many adventures, making him almost impervious to simple mortal weapons. While wearing the pelt you gain the following benefits: While wearing the pelt you have resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing and slashing damage. Your Strength score increases by 2, to a maximum of 20. You are immune to the frightened condition.
