# Portal Chalk

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare Each of these two pieces of chalk radiate a different luminescent aura: one orange, the other blue. Each piece of chalk has 10 uses. As an action, you can expend one use of a piece of chalk to draw a circular or rectangular portal on a solid stone surface large enough for a Medium creature. This portal instantly becomes linked to the most recent portal drawn with the other color of chalk, unlinking any portals created by these pieces of chalk other than these two. A linked portal is a glowing ring or rectangle filled with opaque mist; an unlinked portal is simply mundane chalk. A pair of linked portals acts as a magical gate. Any creature or object entering a linked portal exits from the other portal as if the two were adjacent to each other.
