# Nanas Soothing Cigar

**description**: Nana’s Soothing Cigar Potion (cigar), rare Weight: none Nana Kindleaf's hand-rolled cigars contain magic herbs grown by the gnome granny. As an action, you can light and take a few puffs of the cigar. You regain 4d8 hit points and end either one disease or one condition afflicting you. The condition can be blinded, deafened, frightened, or poisoned. The cigar can be used five times before it is expended.
