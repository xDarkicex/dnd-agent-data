# Scale Mail of Weightlessness

**description**: Scale Mail of Weightlessness Armor (Medium), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This armor has 5 charges. While you wear it, you can use a bonus action to expend 1 or more charges to cast one of the following spells from the armor, targeting yourself: Jump (1 charge) or Levitate (2 charges). This armor regains 1d4 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. Scale Mail. This armor consists of a coat and leggings (and perhaps a separate skirt) of leather covered with overlapping pieces of metal, much like the scales of a fish. The suit includes gauntlets. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC : 14 + Dex modifier (max 2) Stealth : Disadvantage Weight : 45
