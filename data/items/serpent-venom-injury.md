# Serpent venom (Injury)

**description**: Serpent Venom Adventuring gear (poison, injury) This poison must be harvested from a dead or incapacitated giant poisonous snake. A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw, taking 10 (3d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Damage: 3d6 Damage Type: Poison
