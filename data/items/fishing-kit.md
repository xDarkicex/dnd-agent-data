# Fishing Kit

**description**: Fishing Kit Adventuring gear (artisan's tools) Kits made for people who catch fish for a living often contain fishing line, glass floaters, small nets, miso paste (for curing), and wrapping paper in a straw basket. Some fishing kits may include a hemp tether, tiny collar, and padded glove which are used for handling a cormorant bird trained to dive for fish.
