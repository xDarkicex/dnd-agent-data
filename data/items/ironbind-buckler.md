# Ironbind Buckler

**description**: Armor (Shield), Uncommon This shield is crafted from meteoric iron. Ironbind As a Reaction, when a creature misses you with a melee attack using a metal weapon, you can bind the target's weapon to the shield. While stuck, the weapon can't be used and the weapon's wielder is Grappled if it can't or won't let go of the weapon (escape DC 13). The target creature can use its Action to make a DC 13 Strength (Athletics) check, freeing its weapon on a success. You can also release the weapon at any time (no action required).
