# Trident of the Sharklord

**description**: Weapon (trident), rare (requires attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. This magic weapon has 6 charges and it regains 1d6 expended charges daily at dawn. While holding it, you can use an action and expend 2 charges to gain the effects of water breathing for 1 hour. You can expend 3 charges to gain the blood frenzy trait for 1 hour. You can expend 4 charges to summon a giant shark, which appears in a space you chose within 30 feet of you and obeys your telepathic commands for 1 minute or until it is reduced to 0 hit points, after which time it disappears.
