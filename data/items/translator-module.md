# Translator Module

**description**: Translator Module Utility, uncommon This device has an adhesive lead that attaches to the head, either at the temple or behind at the base of the neck. Applying or removing it requires an action. When you use this device, you understand the literal meaning of any spoken language that you hear. You also understand any written language that you see, but you must be within 10 feet of the words that you are reading. You can read up to 300 words a minute. This implant doesn’t decode secret messages in a text or identify any symbol that isn’t part of a written language.
