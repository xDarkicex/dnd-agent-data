# Turtle Mace (Very Rare)

**description**: Turtle Mace Weapon (Mace), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Push. Once per turn, when you hit a creature with this magic weapon, the target must succeed on a DC 17 Strength saving throw or be pushed back 15 feet. Steam Cloud. This weapon has 2 charges which is regained when you finish a long rest. As part of an attack you make with the Turtle Mace , you can expend 1 charge to create a concentrated cloud of steam in a 5-foot radius around you. Each creature in the area (other than you) must make a DC 17 Constitution saving throw, taking 3d12 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. Additionally, the area is heavily obscured until the end of your next turn. Progression The Turtle Mace progresses whenever time is taken to restore the full model. By spending 8 hours and 10,000 gp of bronze, a creature proficient with smith’s tools or tinker’s tools can make a DC 20 Dexterity check to attempt to increase the weapon’s rarity. On a success, the weapon’s rarity increases by one step. On a failure, you lose half the value of the materials and must try again. The weapon’s rarity can’t be increased by more than one step in the same week.
