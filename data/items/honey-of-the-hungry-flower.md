# Honey of the Hungry Flower

**description**: Potion, uncommon This spirit honey is made from the nectar of carnivorous plants and plant creatures. When you consume this honey, you have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks as long as you remain motionless. In addition, when you would regain hit points from the rolling of one or more dice, you regain the highest number possible for each die. Insight. At the end of the spirit honey’s duration, you can name or describe a specific kind of beast or plant to learn the distance and direction to the closest creature or plant of that kind within 5 miles. This works like the locate animals or plants spell, except you can locate up to three different kinds of beasts or plants instead of one.
