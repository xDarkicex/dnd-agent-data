# Shadowgrasp (Uncommon)

**description**: Weapon (Mace), Uncommon (Requires Attunement ) Common This magic weapon has 3 charges, and regains all expended charges when you finish a long rest. As an action, you can expend 1 charge to extinguish all nonmagical flames within a 30-foot radius. Uncommon You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. Soul Pilfer. When you attack with this weapon and roll a 19 or 20 on the attack roll, you can choose to deal damage or reduce the target’s Strength score by 1d4. The target dies if this reduces its Strength score to 0. Otherwise, the reduction lasts until the target finishes a short or long rest.
