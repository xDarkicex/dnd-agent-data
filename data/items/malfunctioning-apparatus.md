# Malfunctioning Apparatus

**description**: Mechanical Trap All creatures in the room are attacked by a mechanical apparatus every turn they are inside the room. It attacks with a +8 bonus and deals 13 (3d6+3) bludgeoning damage plus 2 (1d4) lightning damage. A malfunctioning apparatus is usually encountered with a robot or other automaton that is likewise malfunctioning and hostile.
