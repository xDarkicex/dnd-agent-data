# Vardatch

**description**: This crude, brutal weapon, with a name that translates to “cleaver” in the Orcish tongue, is a heavy, broad-bladed sword with a blunt edge and a serrated edge. It is sturdy, heavy, and cheap to produce. Built on the same principle as the vardatch, but even larger, the greater vardatch is favored by oruk shock troopers and by those orcs who disdain shields in favor of killing power. Vardatches use the weapon profile of a battleaxe.
