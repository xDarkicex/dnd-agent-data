# Lion's Roar (Rare)

**description**: Weapon (Warhammer), Rare (Requires Attunement) Common As an action, you can wave this magic weapon in a circle over your head, causing it to emit a lion’s roar that can be heard up to 1 mile away. Uncommon This weapon has 3 charges, and regains 1d3 charges when you finish a long rest. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 charge to cast thunderwave or 2 charges to cast shatter (spell save DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Charisma modifier). Rare Silencing Roar. When you hit a creature with this weapon, you can attempt to curse it. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Charisma modifier) or be cursed for 1 minute. While cursed, the creature is deafened and cannot speak or make sound. Casting a spell that includes a verbal component is impossible. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make another Wisdom saving throw. On a success, the curse ends. Once used, you can’t use this property again until you finish a short or long rest.
