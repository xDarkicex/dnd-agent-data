# Handygnome’s Precision Gloves

**description**: Handygnome’s Precision Gloves Wondrous item, Rare (requires attunement) Created by gnome artificers who value efficiency, these sturdy gloves have been imbued with potent and practical transmutation magic that helps tinkerers fix things under pressure, but also allows locksmiths and shady characters pick locks more efficiently. While wearing these gloves, you gain a +5 bonus to any ability checks related to repairing objects and constructs, disarming traps, and picking locks. Remote Rapid Repairs. While wearing these gloves, you can use an action to cast the mending cantrip from them. When cast that way, mending has a range of 30 feet.
