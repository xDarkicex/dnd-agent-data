# Feather of Detection

**description**: The tail feathers of the most legendary couatls sometimes permanently hold the magical traits of the feathered serpents of wonder. The feather functions as a divining rod, pointing towards effects sought by its wielder. The feather has 3 charges. By extending 1 charge, you can cast any of these spells: detect evil and good , detect magic , detect thoughts . The feather regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn
