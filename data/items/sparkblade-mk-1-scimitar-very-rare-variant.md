# Sparkblade Mk. 1 Scimitar (very rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (any sword), rare (requires attunement) Component: dragon (blue or bronze) breath sac As arcanodynamos have become more common, there has been an explosion in devices making use of this magic-generating technology. From lanterns to rollerskates, and now: sparkblades. This item has 6 charges and regains 1d4 + 2 expended charges daily at dawn. Arcanodynamo. While holding this weapon, you can cause it to regain expended charges by swinging it vigorously for 10 minutes without interruption. At the end of this time, make a DC 10 Strength (Athletics) check. The item regains 1d6 expended charges on a successful check, or half as many charges on a failed one. Once this property of the sword has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn. Lightning Blade. As a bonus action while holding this sword, you can pull the trigger 1, 2, or 3 clicks, energising the blade for 1 minute as described below: 1 Click. You expend 1 charge. For the duration, each time you hit with an attack using this weapon, you deal an extra 1d4 lightning damage. 2 Clicks. You expend 3 charges. For the duration, each time you hit with an attack using this weapon, you deal an extra 1d6 lightning damage. The first time you hit a creature with this weapon each turn, the creature must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be unable to take reactions until the start of its next turn. 3 Clicks. You expend 6 charges. For the duration, each time you hit with an attack using this weapon, you deal an extra 1d8 lightning damage. The first time you hit a creature with this weapon each turn, the creature must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be unable to take reactions until the start of its next turn. If it fails the saving throw by 5 or more, it is stunned until the start of its next turn. Uncommon variant: Reduce the DCs of the Lightning Blade property to 13, the charges to 4, and the recharges at dawn and from the Arcanodynamo property to 1d4. Remove the 3 Clicks option. Very rare variant: Increase the DCs of the Lightning Blade property to 16 and the damage of 1, 2, and 3 Clicks to 1d6, 1d8, and 1d10, respectively.
