# Humming-Bulb Arrows

**description**: Humming-Bulb Arrows Ammunition These arrows have heads carved to produce a loud noise that serves as a warning signal. A humming-bulb can be heard at a distance of 500 feet from every point along its path. If it strikes a creature, it deals 1 bludgeoning damage.
