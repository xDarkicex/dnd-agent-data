# Goggles of Blood

**description**: Wondrous item, rare Goggles of blood are special, magical goggles that are developed by those who hunt people to drain their blood. It has multiple functions that ease the hunt. While wearing the google, you have advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks made to perceive creatures with blood within 120 feet of you. In addition, by concentrating on a creature or a bloodstain (up to 1 day old) of your choice within 120 feet of you, you can see the creature’s blood-flow or the properties of the bloodstain and understand its creature type if the creature has blood. This property pierces magical and nonmagical disguises. This google can’t be used in this way again until the next dawn.
