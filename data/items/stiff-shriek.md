# Stiff Shriek

**description**: Weapon (Light Crossbow), Common This crossbow is carved to resemble a grotesque pixie with a gaunt face frozen in mid-scream. The pixie’s arms are tied back to form the taught string and limbs of the weapon. This magic weapon does not require ammunition. Instead, at your discretion, when the trigger is pulled, a sudden high-pitched scream heard only by the target emanates from the weapon’s mouth. When used this way, the weapon deals thunder damage instead of piercing damage. Encounter Ideas Warning Shot. Gugath, an elderly human woman, offers the weapon as a good luck charm to ward off mischievous fey. Estate Sale. The adventurers find the relative of an eccentric gardener desperate to give away some kind of distasteful pixie statue. Faerie Plea. After finding the weapon in a green hag’s home, the adventurers are approached by a trio of sprites requesting their pixie friend be freed from petrification.
