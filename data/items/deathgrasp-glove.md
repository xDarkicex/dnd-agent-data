# Deathgrasp Glove

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) While you wear this magic glove, a creature grappled by you can’t regain hit points and takes 1d8 necrotic damage at the start of each of its turns. Death’s Grasp. As an action, you can speak the glove’s command word and cause it to animate for 1 minute. The animated glove detaches from your hand, begins to hover, flies up to 30 feet, and attempts to grapple one creature of your choice within 5 feet of it, using your Strength (Athletics) check. A creature grappled this way is considered grappled by you. While the glove hovers, you can use a bonus action to cause it to fly up to 30 feet to another spot within 30 feet of you and grapple a creature within 5 feet of it. Once used, this property can’t be used again until the next dawn.
