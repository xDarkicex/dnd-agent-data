# Massive

**description**: Journeyman weapon property Components: Masterwork exotic melee weapon with the Superheavy property This weapon gains the Massive property and its damage die increases by two steps. Massive. You can only make an attack with this weapon when you take the Attack action, and only as the first attack you make on your turn. Once you make an attack with this weapon, you can’t make any attacks until the start of your next turn. If you would be able to attack more than once when you take the Attack action on your turn, you deal an extra two dice of damage for each attack you forgo when using this weapon.
