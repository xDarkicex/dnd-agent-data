# Sumnter's Dagger (Uncommon)

**description**: Sumnter's Dagger Weapon (Dagger), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon and the weapon deals an additional 1d4 radiant damage. You can speak a command word to cause the weapon’s iron blade to emit moonlight, shedding bright light in a 10-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet. The light lasts for 1 hour or until you end it (no action required). Progression Sumnter’s Dagger progresses as the creature attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s Wisdom modifier increases, so does the weapon’s rarity.
