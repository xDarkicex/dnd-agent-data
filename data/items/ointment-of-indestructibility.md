# Ointment of Indestructibility

**description**: Potion, very rare This container holds a transparent oil with a green metallic hue. The oil can cover a medium or smaller object. Applying the oil takes 1 minute. The affected object becomes permanently immune to all types of magical and non-magical damage and can only be destroyed by the use of a wish spell. The oil’s effect does not work on construct type creatures.
