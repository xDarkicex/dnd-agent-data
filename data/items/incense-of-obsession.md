# Incense of Obsession

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon These cursed sticks of incense appear to be of a type that would normally aid meditation. However, if you meditate while the incense is burning, you become confident that your spell ability is superior due to the magic incense. You are compelled to use your spells at every opportunity. This effect last until you run out of magic or you complete a long rest.
