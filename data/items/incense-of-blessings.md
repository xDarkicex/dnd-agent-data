# Incense of Blessings

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare When lit, this magical incense produces a scent of lavender and myrrh, and burns for 1 hour before it is consumed. A creature who inhales the incense during a short rest gains a number of d6s equal to its proficiency bonus. For the next 8 hours, whenever the creature makes an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, it can expend one of those d6s, roll it, and add the number rolled to the result before the outcome is determined.
