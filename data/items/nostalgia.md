# Nostalgia

**description**: Wondrous Item, Legendary (Requires Attunement) This pendant features an intricate array of gears. While wearing it, your Intelligence score increases by 2, to a maximum of 22. Relive Memory You relive a memory any creature has gained while attuned to Nostalgia . You can specify any condition for the memory (such as an encounter with a specific creature type or when a certain event occurred) and relive the memory that best matches that condition, through the eyes of the creature that experienced it. The memory lasts for up to 1 hour and you are Unconscious while you relive it. When it ends, you must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom save or take 3d6 Psychic damage. Time Anchor You set an anchor in time and space. At the start of each of your subsequent turns, you can choose to revert time back to when you set the anchor (no action required), undoing anything that has happened since then, and causing you and all other creatures and objects to magically appear exactly where you were when the anchor was set. The anchor then disappears. If you choose not to reset time at the start of your turn, roll a d4. On a roll of 1, the anchor disappears. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
