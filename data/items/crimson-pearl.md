# Crimson Pearl

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement by a spellcaster) Also occasionally referred to as a Vampiric Pearl of Power, this pearl is over an inch in diameter and dyed crimson with untold bloodshed. It is said that a pearl of power that is exposed to the blood of 1000 violent killings will transform into a crimson pearl . While this pearl is on your person, you can use an action to speak its command word and regain one expended spell slot. If the expended slot was of 4th level or higher, the new slot is 3rd level. Once you use the pearl, it can’t be used again until you deliver a successful Death Blow.
