# Belt of Wide Earth

**description**: Belt of Wide Earth Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) This thick leather belt is full of cracks and fissures and is fastened by a heavy buckle of obsidian. While wearing this belt, difficult terrain doesn’t cost you extra movement and you count as one size larger when determining your carrying capacity and the weight you can push, drag, or lift. In addition, you can use an action and cast the teleport spell. Once used, this property of the belt can’t be used again until the next dawn.
