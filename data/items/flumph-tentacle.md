# Flumph Tentacle

**description**: Flumph Tentacle Spell catalyst, common Flumphs are drawn to good-aligned creatures, and will freely offer their quickly-regrowing tentacles to such creatures when asked. When you cast the detect thoughts spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, whenever you focus on a creature to read its surface thoughts during the duration, you also learn the creature’s alignment, Ideal, Bond, and Flaw, if the target has any.
