# Gergulsush (Uncommon)

**description**: Gergulsush Weapon (Whip), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The whip’s handle is tacky, allowing you to grip it firmly. You can’t drop this whip unless you choose to and checks made to forcefully remove the whip from your hand have disadvantage. A Mimic Says What? You know how to speak Mimic, which is a series of grunts, slurps, belches, groans, and guttural sounds. What Is It? As a bonus action, you can speak a command word to change the form of the whip into an object of similar size, or back into its whip form. While in object form, it is indistinguishable from an ordinary object. Sticky. When you hit a target with an attack using this weapon, you can choose to have the whip adhere to it. The target is grappled (escape DC 13). Progression Gergulsush progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, so does the weapon’s rarity.
