# Tome of Tongues

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon This book contains grammar and vocabulary for a specific language, and its words are charged with magic. If you spend 8 hours over a period of 2 days or fewer studying the book’s contents and practicing its guidelines, you learn to read, write, and speak whatever language it concerns. The tome then loses its magic forever.
