# Seed of the Hagtree

**description**: The seed of the Hagtree holds a fragment of a hag’s soul and resembles a monstrous eye, covered by a thick seed coat and prickly thorns. The seed has 3 charges. While attuned to it, you can expend 1 charge to cast the tree stride spell. The seed of the Hagtree regains 1d3 expended charges at midnight.
