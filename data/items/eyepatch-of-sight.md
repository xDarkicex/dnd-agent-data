# Eyepatch of Sight

**description**: Eyepatch of Sight Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This item consists of a small piece of black leather attached to a thin headband. When worn over your eye, it gives you truesight out to 60 feet. The effect lasts for 1 hour or until you take off the eyepatch. Once used, this property of the eyepatch can’t be used again until the next dawn.
