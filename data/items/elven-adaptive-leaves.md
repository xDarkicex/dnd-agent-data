# Elven adaptive leaves

**description**: These adhesive leather leaves are dyed in hues of green, brown, and gray and can cover one suit of armor or set of clothing of a Medium or smaller creature or one shield for 24 hours. Applying the leaves takes 10 minutes. When you wear armor or clothing or wield a shield covered in these leaves, you have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide in a forest or other terrain with ample vegetation. When the duration ends, the leaves fall off one by one, and they can’t be reused.
