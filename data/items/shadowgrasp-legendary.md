# Shadowgrasp (Legendary)

**description**: Weapon (Mace), Legendary (Requires Attunement ) Common This magic weapon has 3 charges, and regains all expended charges when you finish a long rest. As an action, you can expend 1 charge to extinguish all nonmagical flames within a 30-foot radius. Uncommon You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. Soul Pilfer. When you attack with this weapon and roll a 19 or 20 on the attack roll, you can choose to deal damage or reduce the target’s Strength score by 1d4. The target dies if this reduces its Strength score to 0. Otherwise, the reduction lasts until the target finishes a short or long rest. Rare The weapon’s total number of charges increases to 5, and it now regains 1d4 + 1 expended charges when you finish a long rest. Darkness. As an action, you can expend 2 charges to cast the darkness spell, centered on the weapon. The area of darkness moves with the weapon and you can see through it. Very Rare The bonus to attack and damage rolls increases to +2, and Soul Pilfer now also deals the weapon’s attack damage when you choose to reduce the target’s Strength score. In addition, the weapon’s total number of charges increases to 7, and it now regains 1d6 + 1 charges when you finish a long rest. Summon Shadows. As an action, you can expend 3 charges to summon 1d4 shadows. These shadows share your initiative count but take their turn immediately after yours and obey your verbal commands. They disappear after dropping to 0 hit points or after 1 hour. These shadows cannot create other shadows. Any Strength score reduced by these shadows counts towards the weapon’s total. Legendary The bonus to attack and damage rolls increases to +3. Shadow of Death. If a creature is killed by Soul Pilfer, a shadow immediately appears in the space of its corpse or the nearest unoccupied space. This shadow is under your control and disappears after dropping to 0 hit points or after 2 hours.
