# Dagger of Silence

**description**: Dagger of Silence Weapon (dagger), very rare (requires attunement) Damage: 1d4+2 piercing Weight: 1 lb. Properties: Finesse, Light, Thrown (range 20/60) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Silent Killer. While you are holding the dagger, you can use an action to cast Silence with the following changes: The 20-foot-radius sphere of silence is centered on the dagger. While attuned to the dagger, you can hear while inside the silence sphere but you can not produce sound, including the verbal component of a spell. This property can be used a number of times per day equal to your proficiency bonus.
