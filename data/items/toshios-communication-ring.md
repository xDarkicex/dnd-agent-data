# Toshio's communication ring

**description**: Toshio's Communication Ring Rare, wondrous item, (requires attunement) These rings are made in pairs and have a direct connection to their twin. When both rings are attuned to by separate creatures, the wearer can use the ring to communicate with its twin and anything said by the wearer is transmitted telepathically to the wearer of its twin. Both rings are able to do this, but one must speak verbally into the ring for the message to be sent. The twin of Toshio’s ring is held by Nocturne. So long as it possesses the other ring, it can also use the connection to harm a creature attuned to the ring with its baleful power, spending an action to cause that creature to suffer the bleeding condition (see New Conditions in Adventures in Rokugan ).
