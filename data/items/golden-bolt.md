# Golden Bolt

**description**: Weapon (heavy crossbow), uncommon (requires attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. This crossbow doesn’t have the loading property, and it doesn’t require ammunition. Immediately after firing a bolt from this weapon, another golden bolt forms to take its place.
