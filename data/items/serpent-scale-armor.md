# Serpent Scale Armor

**description**: Serpent Scale Armor Armor (scale mail), uncommon This suit of magic armor is made from shimmering scales. While wearing it, you can apply your full Dexterity modifier (instead of a maximum of +2) when determining your Armor Class. In addition, this armor does not impose disadvantage on your Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC: 15 + Dex modifier Weight: 45
