# Neutronium Maul

**description**: Weapon (Maul), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) The head of this maul has a tiny core of neutronium and deals an extra 1d8 Force damage on a hit. Neutron Manipulation The maul has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. Gravitational Surge. As a Bonus Action, you can expend 1 charge to force a Large or smaller creature you can see within 30 feet to make a DC 15 Strength save. On a failure, the creature is pulled up to 15 feet in a straight line toward you. If the creature ends this move within your reach, you can make a melee attack with the maul against it. Temporal Distortion. When you make an attack roll against a creature with the maul, you can expend 1 charge to gain Advantage on the attack roll. On a hit, the target takes an extra 2d8 Force damage and must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution save or on its next turn, the target can do only one of the following: move, take an Action, or take a Bonus Action. If the attack misses, you must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution save or suffer the effect on your next turn instead. Neutron Implosion As an Action, you can release the maul's neutronium core and cause the maul to be destroyed in an implosion. Each creature within 30 feet must make a DC 20 Constitution save. On a failure, it takes 10d10 Force damage and is pulled 10 feet toward the maul. On a success, it takes half as much damage only.
