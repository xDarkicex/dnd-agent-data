# Grasshopper Staff (Uncommon)

**description**: Grasshopper Staff Weapon (Quarterstaff), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While attuned to it, your walking speed is increased by 5 feet. Rubbing the grasshopper causes the staff to emit a soft, buzzing sound. Progression The Grasshopper Staff progresses each time the creature who is attuned to it defeats a powerful insectoid Beast or Monstrosity. When the attuned wielder deals damage to such a creature with an attack using the Grasshopper Staff and the creature is reduced to 0 hit points in the same encounter, the staff progresses. To progress to rare, the creature must be CR 8 or greater, CR 13 or greater for very rare, and CR 18 for legendary. The staff cannot progress more than once per week.
