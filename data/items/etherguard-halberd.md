# Etherguard Halberd

**description**: Etherguard Halberd Weapon (Halberd), Legendary (Requires Attunement) An iron halberd covered in crude etchings of ghosts, phantoms, and other malevolent spirits. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Solid Spirit. You have advantage on saving throws against being possessed by Undead and creatures with the Incorporeal Movement trait can’t move through you. Ethereal Senses. You can hear and see (in shades of gray) 60 feet into the Ethereal Plane when you are on a plane that borders it (such as the Material Plane), and vice versa. Plane-Shifting Steel. You can use this weapon to make attacks against targets on the Ethereal Plane.
