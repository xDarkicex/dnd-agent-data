# Dornish Horse Spear

**description**: This heavy lance was a favorite of the Dornish cavalry, who learned to ride and fight from horseback after the Sarcosan invasion during the Second Age. Eschewing the long, lighter lances of the Sarcosans, the Dorns favored a heavy lance that took advantage of their natural size and strength. The Dornish horse spear can be used on foot as well as on horseback and can be set to receive a charge as well as deliver one. Dornish horse spears use the weapon profile of a halberd. However, they deal piercing damage instead of slashing damage.
