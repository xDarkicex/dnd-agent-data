# Lagoon Submersible

**description**: Lagoon Submersible Wondrous item, uncommon This Large capsule-shaped vehicle is adorned with green seaweed and is colored the same as dark oceanic rock. It can snugly fit up to two Medium or smaller creatures inside and can be controlled by either creature via a control console of well-labeled buttons and levers. The submersible has a swimming speed of 60 feet. If it descends below 500 feet, its movement speed is halved, and if it descends past 600 feet, its movement speed is reduced to 0. While in the submersible, you have blindsight out to a range of 120 feet and you can breathe normally.
