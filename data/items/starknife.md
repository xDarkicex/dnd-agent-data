# Starknife

**description**: Starknives Starknives are martial melee weapons; three to five double-edged blades set on a central handle that are most effective as thrown weapons but can also be used for close combat. They deal 1d4 piercing damage on a hit, can count as a monk weapon, and have the Adaptable (slashing), Finesse, Light, Thrown (range 20/60), and Thrown Versatility ( 1d6 ) properties.
