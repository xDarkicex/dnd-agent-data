# Rings of Fire

**description**: Ring, Rare (Requires Attunement) These four magic rings glow with fiery runes. All four rings must be worn together on the same hand. Unarmed strikes you make using the hand wearing the rings and attacks you make with a weapon held in that hand, deal an extra 1d6 Fire damage on a hit. Ring of Fire. The rings have 4 charges and regain all expended charges daily at dawn. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge and throw a ring from your hand at a point you can see within 60 feet. The ring becomes an opaque, ringed wall of fire that is 15 feet in diameter, 10 feet high, and 1 foot thick. A creature that starts its turn within the wall's area or moves through it for the first time on a turn, must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 2d8 Fire damage on a failure or half as much on a success. At the start of your next turn, the wall of fire becomes a ring again and magically returns to your hand.
