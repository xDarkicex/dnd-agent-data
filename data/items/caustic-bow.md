# Caustic Bow

**description**: Weapon (Shortbow), Rare (Requires Attunement) A composite bow of bamboo and oryx horn painted with a glossy green lacquer. The string is made from an ankheg’s enchanted sinews, which ooze viscous acid. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The weapon has 3 charges, and regains all expended charges when you finish a long rest. Once per turn, as part of an Attack action, you can expend 1 charge to conjure and release an arrow dripping with green acid. Instead of its normal damage, the arrow deals 4d4 acid damage on a hit and an additional 2d4 acid damage at the start of the target’s next turn. Encounter Ideas Acid Rain. Every five years, on the Night of the Black Dragon, acidic rain falls on the accursed village of Stonehafen. An ordinary bow left out overnight has a chance of gaining the properties of a caustic bow. Hidden Shrine. An acidic waterfall hides a cavernous shrine dedicated to the lizardfolk’s god of the hunt. The god’s statue holds this bow in its clawed hand. One Man’s Trash. A circle of trash druids who have been driven from their sacred landfill by a trio of otyughs offer the party this bow in exchange for “cleansing” their home.
