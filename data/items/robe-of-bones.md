# Robe of Bones

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This robe is adorned with embroidery that resembles various undead creatures. As an action, you can grasp a bit of embroidery to bring forth one of the following undead within 30 feet of you: 2d4 skeletons 1d8 zombies a flaming skull a ghoul You cannot use the robe again in this way until the next dawn.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: rare
- Requires Attunement: Yes
