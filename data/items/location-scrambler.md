# Location Scrambler

**description**: This 2-inch diameter disk can be placed onto a creature or object. It sends out a signal that confuses digital locators such as GPS devices and sensor tags, making the creature or object appear to be 1d10 x 10 miles from its actual location. This device has no effect on detection-based esper talents or techniques.
