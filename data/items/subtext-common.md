# Subtext (Common)

**description**: Subtext Weapon (Warhammer), Common (Requires Attunement) Tragedy . When you roll a 20 on your attack roll with this magic weapon, the target is frightened of you until the end of its next turn. Progression Subtext progresses each time its attuned wielder completes an adventure worthy of being turned into a play (what satisfies this definition is at the GM’s discretion). While the weapon prefers tragedies and horrors, any compelling story suffices.
