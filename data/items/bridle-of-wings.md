# Bridle of Wings

**description**: Bridle of Wings Wondrous item, rare This bridle is made from red leather stamped with a feather pattern and has a copper bit. When the bit is fitted to a mount, you can use an action to speak its command word. This causes a pair of bird wings to sprout from the mount’s back for 1 hour or until you repeat the command word as an action. The wings give the mount a flying speed of 60 feet. Once used, this property of the bridle can’t be used again until the next dawn.
