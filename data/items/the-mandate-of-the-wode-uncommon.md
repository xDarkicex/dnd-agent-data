# The Mandate of the Wode (Uncommon)

**description**: Weapon (Mace), Uncommon (Requires Attunement by a Druid) This magic weapon has 5 charges. It regains 1d4 expended charges when you finish a long rest, or all charges if you are in a forest. Common Speak with Beasts. While attuned to this magic weapon, you can speak its command word, granting you the ability to comprehend and verbally communicate with Beasts for 1 minute. During this time, you also have advantage on Persuasion checks involving Beasts whose CR is less than your level. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. Uncommon While holding this weapon, you can use a bonus action to expend 1 charge to cast the command spell, targeting a Beast. The spell is considered a druid spell for you and uses your druid spell save DC.
