# Boots of the Zephyr

**description**: Boots Of The Zephyr Wondrous item, very rare These black leather boots have iron soles inscribed with runes. While both boots are worn, they allow the wearer to move normally while floating 4 inches above the ground. The effect allows the wearer to cross or stand on unstable surfaces, such as water or lava, and ignore the effects of difficult terrain. The wearer also leaves no tracks.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: very rare
