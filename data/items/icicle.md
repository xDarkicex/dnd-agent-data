# Icicle

**description**: Weapon (Dagger), Rare (Requires Attunement) A serrated shard of unmelting ice acts as the blade of this otherwise traditional dagger. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon, which deals an extra 1 cold damage on a hit. While holding this weapon, you have resistance to cold damage. Encounter Ideas He’s an Angry One. While traveling in snow-covered lands, an evil gnome assassin goes on a killing spree with icicle. Never Let ‘Em See You Sweat. It is rumored that Dridea Mennen, a famous explorer (priest), never sweats while she travels the desert. Her secret? A well-maintained icicle. Swizzle Stick. A local tavern is known to serve drinks so cold they give you a headache. Turns out, the barkeep Uzbeht (a plate-wearing orog with Intelligence 16 (+3) and Charisma 18 (+4), uses icicle to stir drinks, and she is reluctant to rid herself of an item that has bolstered her reputation.
