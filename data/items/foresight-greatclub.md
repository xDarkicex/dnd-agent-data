# Foresight Greatclub

**description**: Foresight Greatclub Melee weapon (simple, greatclub) , very rare (requires attunement) This weapon’s haft is engraved with sigils of divination. You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. In combat, the weapon imparts subtle hints of the future to you, letting you predict a foe’s defensive movements when you initially engage them. The first time you attack each creature during an encounter with this weapon, you have advantage on the attack roll. If that attack hits, maximize your weapon damage dice against the target. Additionally, when you miss with an attack using this weapon, you can use your reaction to shift the flow of causality and turn the miss into a hit. Once you use this reaction, you can’t do so again until the next dawn. Two-­Handed. This weapon requires two hands when you attack with it. Damage: 1d8 Damage Type: bludgeoning Modifiers: Melee Attacks +1, Melee Damage +1 Properties: Two-handed Weight: 10
