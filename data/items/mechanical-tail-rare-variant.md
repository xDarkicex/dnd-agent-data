# Mechanical Tail (rare variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) Component: phial of construct blood This item is another creation of the eccentric Anton Spark, tinkerer extraordinaire. This mechanical tail is powered by a magic-infused steam core. Any creature can wear it on its back and, once attuned, use it as if it was born with a tail. When you attune to the mechanical tail, it attaches to your lower back and can’t be removed against your will. You can use the tail to make grapples and have advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks made to grapple creatures of your size or smaller with it. Steam-Powered Strike. As an action, you can sweep your tail with wicked, steam-powered ferocity. Each creature within 5 feet of you must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 2d8 slashing damage and is knocked prone. On a successful one, it takes half as much damage and isn’t knocked prone. Once this property of the tail has been used, roll a d4. On a 1, the tail runs out of steam and this property can’t be used again until the next dawn. Rare variant: Increase the DC to 15 and the damage to 3d8.
