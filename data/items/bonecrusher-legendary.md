# Bonecrusher (Legendary)

**description**: Bonecrusher Weapon (Club), Legendary (Requires Attunement) You can use Bonecrusher as a holy symbol. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this adamantine magic weapon. When you hit a skeleton or zombie with it, the target takes an extra 4d4 radiant damage, and it deals an extra 2d4 radiant damage to all other Undead creatures on a hit. Corporeal Undead creatures have disadvantage on saving throws against your Channel Divinity effects. On your turn, when you score a critical hit with Bonecrusher against a skeleton or zombie, or reduce a skeleton or zombie to 0 hit points, you can immediately make one melee attack with Bonecrusher as part of the same action. You can make this attack only once per round.
