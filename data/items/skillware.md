# Skillware

**description**: Skillware Augment (head), varies (requires affinity) This implant grants simulated knowledge and training in a particular skill. It is a rare augment. With this augment, you can add your proficiency bonus to skill checks for one skill or tool of your choice. It provides no benefit for skills in which you are already proficient. Skillplex
