# Wastelander's All-Knowing Protective Device

**description**: Wastelander’s All-Knowing Protective Device Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) This skullcap has a core of steel bands sandwiched between a durable boiled leather exterior and comfortable silken interior. While you wear the skullcap, you have a +1 bonus to your Armor Class. The skullcap has 2 charges. When a creature makes a critical hit against you, you can use your reaction to expend 1 charge. When you do so, you can move 5 feet to an empty space and avoid taking damage from the attack. You can use an action to expend 2 charges to cast detect thoughts . The skullcap regains any expended charges at dawn.
