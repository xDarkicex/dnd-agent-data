# Bezoar of the Great Horned Owl

**description**: Wondrous item, rare While this bezoar sits in your stomach, you are immune to the charmed and frightened conditions. In addition, creatures must be at least two sizes larger than you to grapple you. Curse. Your scent, demeanor, personality, or attitude irritates creatures around you. When interacting socially with creatures with an Intelligence of 5 or higher, you have disadvantage on Charisma checks. Creatures with an Intelligence of 4 or lower are immediately hostile toward you, and the first such creature to attack you each day has advantage on the attack roll.
