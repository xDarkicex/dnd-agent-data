# Scroll of Quickening

**description**: Scroll, Common This parchment scroll is held in a container etched with golden symbols and has a smooth texture. Any text written on this scroll can be read at half its usual pace. When the parchment scroll is used to create a Spell Scroll with a spell that has a casting time of an action, the caster can use a bonus action instead of an action to attempt to cast the spell. The Spell Scroll otherwise functions as normal and crumbles to dust once a spell has been cast from it.
