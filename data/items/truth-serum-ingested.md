# Truth serum (Ingested)

**description**: Truth Serum Adventuring gear (poison, ingested) A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw or become poisoned for 1 hour. The poisoned creature can’t knowingly speak a lie, as if under the effect of a zone of truth spell.
