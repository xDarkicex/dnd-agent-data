# Black Powder Popper

**description**: Black Powder Popper Wondrous item, uncommon This pea-sized ball of paper filled with a pinch of black powder and gravel is often sold in pouches of 20, 50, or 100. When a creature within 30 feet of you makes an attack, you can use your reaction to throw the popper at its feet to distract it as the powder ignites with a popping sound. The creature must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or make its attack roll with disadvantage.
