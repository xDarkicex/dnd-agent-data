# Ring Mail of the Holy Crusade

**description**: Ring Mail of the Holy Crusade Armor (heavy), rare (requires attunement) While you wear this golden armor, it sheds dim light in a 5-foot radius around you. If you are within 60 feet of a fiend or undead, the armor instead sheds bright light in a 30-foot radius, and dim light for an additional 30 feet, and you gain a +2 bonus to your AC. The bright light shed by this armor is sunlight. Ring Mail. This armor is leather armor with heavy rings sewn into it. The rings help reinforce the armor against blows from swords and axes. Ring mail is inferior to chain mail, and it's usually worn only by those who can't afford better armor. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks.
