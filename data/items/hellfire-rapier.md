# Hellfire Rapier

**description**: Hellfire Rapier Melee weapon (martial, rapier), uncommon This weapon is fashioned from infernal iron and traced with veins of hellfire that shed dim light in a 5-foot-radius. Any humanoid killed by an attack made with this weapon has its soul funneled into the River Styx, where it’s reborn instantly as a lemure devil (described in the Monste r Manual ). Finesse. When making an attack with a finesse weapon, you use your choice of your Strength or Dexterity modifier for the attack and damage rolls. You must use the same modifier for both rolls. Damage: 1d8 Damage Type: piercing Properties: Finesse Weight: 2
