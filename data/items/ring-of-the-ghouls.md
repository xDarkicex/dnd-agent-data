# Ring of the Ghouls

**description**: Ring, Rare This ring has 3 charges, and it regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the ring, when you make a successful melee attack and use your reaction to expend 1 of the ring's charges, the target must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or be paralyzed until the end of its next turn.
