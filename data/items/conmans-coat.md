# Conman's Coat

**description**: Armor (Leather), Common This magic leather armor has numerous pockets. The armor's inner chest pocket functions as a Bag of Holding , except that it can hold only 5 pounds, not exceeding 10 cubic inches, and can only fit items no longer than 5 inches on any side. As a bonus action, you can tap a sequence on the pocket to turn it invisible, or make it visible again. The pocket becomes visible if you take the coat off.
