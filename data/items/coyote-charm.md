# Coyote Charm

**description**: Coyote Charm Wondrous item, rare Whenever they walk on desecrated ground, the wearer of the charm emanates bright light to 10 feet and deals an additional 7 (2d6) radiant damage when they damage undead. The effects end one hour after leaving the desecrated ground. The charm may be used to cast the detect evil and good spell; a long rest must be completed before this feature can be used again.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: rare
