# Orb of Direction

**description**: Wondrous Item, Common This orb can be used as an Arcane Focus. While holding this orb, you can take a Magic action to determine which way is magnetic north. Nothing happens if the orb is used in a location that has no magnetic north.

**properties**:

- Item Type: Arcane Spell Focus
- Item Rarity: No
