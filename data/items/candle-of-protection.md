# Candle of Protection

**description**: Candle of Protection Wondrous item, rare This item appears to be an ordinary wax candle in a brass holder. The candle’s magic is activated when the candle is lit, which requires an action. After burning for 1 hour, the candle is destroyed, or you can snuff it out early for later use. Deduct the time it burned in increments of 1 minute from the candle’s total burn time. When lit, it sheds bright light in a 10-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet. Any undead creature that starts its turn within that light takes 1d6 radiant damage.
