# Darkvision

**description**: Donor: Any creature with darkvision Slot: Head You have replaced your own eyes with those of a monster with keen nightvision. You gain darkvision, the ability to see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can’t discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.
