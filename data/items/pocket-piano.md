# Pocket Piano

**description**: Pocket Piano Wondrous Item, very rare (requires attunement) Weight: ½ lb. or 500 lbs. This finely crafted piano is three inches wide and weighs half a pound in its reduced state. When enlarged, it is 5 ft. wide, 4ft. tall, 2ft. deep and weighs 500 pounds. Enlarge / Reduce. You can use a bonus action to enlarge the piano to full size in a space within 10 feet of you. Each creature in the area where the piano appears must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 2d6 bludgeoning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. In either case, the creature is pushed to an unoccupied space next to the piano. You can use a bonus action to reduce the piano again. You can use an action to play a magic tune on the piano. Choose one of the following: Rousing Tune. You cast Bless on a number of creatures equal to your proficiency bonus. Melancholy Tune. You cast Bane on a number of creatures equal to your proficiency bonus. Heroic Tune. You cast Heroism on a number of creatures equal to your proficiency bonus. Each day you can play a number of magic tunes equal to your proficiency bonus.
