# Dragon's Fang Rapier (Common)

**description**: Dragon's Fang Weapon (Rapier), Common (Requires Attunement) This magic weapon acts as a dowsing rod for valuables, granting advantage on Intelligence (Investigation) or Wisdom (Perception) checks to find treasure. Progression The Dragon’s Fang progresses when tempered in the breath of a dragon—provided the wielder survives the damage. A wyrmling’s breath increases the rarity from common to uncommon, a young dragon’s breath from uncommon to rare, and so on.
