# Rod of Celestia

**description**: The Rod of Celestia Weapon (mace), legendary This magic mace is wrought from gold and features a sun-shaped head. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with the weapon. The weapon has 7 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 charge to cast detect magic . The mace regains all expended charges daily at dawn. Along the mace’s handle are three insets, each of which can be filled with a stone of Celestia . Placing a stone of Celestia into the rod requires an action and must be done while the rod is bathed in sunlight. While three stones of Celestia are set into the rod, the weapon deals an additional 7 (2d6) radiant damage to any target it hits. In addition, a creature holding the rod can use an action to touch one of the rod’s stones and expend the rod’s charges to cast a spell determined by the stone. A creature doesn’t need to be attuned to the rod to use it in this way. Cat’s Eye: The rod’s wielder can expend 6 charges to cast true seeing . Emerald: The rod’s wielder can expend 3 charges to cast water walk . Diamond: The rod’s wielder can expend 6 charges to cast sunbeam . Moonstone: The rod’s wielder can expend 2 charges to cast moonbeam . Purple Amethyst: The rod’s wielder can expend 2 charges to cast acid arrow . Ruby: The rod’s wielder can expend 5 charges to cast hold monster . Sapphire: The rod’s wielder can expend 7 charges to cast regenerate .
