# Exceptional Warding Armor

**description**: Exceptional Warding Armor Armor (any), varies (requires affinity) This armor protects the wearer’s body and mind from debilitating, paranormal effects. You have advantage on Wisdom saving throws made to avoid being charmed or frightened. Exceptional. As an action, you can use the armor to activate the restore pattern power, targeting yourself. Once you use this feature, you can’t use it again until you finish a short or long rest.
