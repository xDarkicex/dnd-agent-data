# Quill of Truth

**description**: Quill of Truth Wondrous item, uncommon This large quill is made from the feather of a phoenix and has a nib of gold. It is not possible to write a deliberate lie using this quill. If you attempt to do so, the ink in the nib evaporates and the words cannot be formed on the page.
