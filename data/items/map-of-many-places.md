# Map of Many Places

**description**: Wondrous Item, Common This magic map looks like a blank sheet of grungy parchment. As an action you can use the command phrase “where am I” to have the parchment magically show a topographical map of the area within a 5-mile radius, including all objects, structures, and terrain features larger than 20 cubic feet. The map remains for 1 hour or until you roll it up as an action. Once used, this property can’t be used again until the next dawn.
