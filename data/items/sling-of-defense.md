# Sling of Defense

**description**: Sling of Defense Ranged weapon (simple, sling), rare The weapon has the defensive property, seeming to move by itself to aid in its wielder’s defense. Ammunition. You can use a weapon that has the ammunition property to make a ranged attack only if you have ammunition to fire from the weapon. Each time you attack with the weapon, you expend one piece of ammunition. Drawing the ammunition from a quiver, case, or other container is part of the attack (you need a free hand to load a one-­‐‑handed weapon). At the end of the battle, you can recover half your expended ammunition by taking a minute to search the battlefield. If you use a weapon that has the ammunition property to make a melee attack, you treat the weapon as an improvised weapon. A sling must be loaded to deal any damage when used in this way. Defensive. While you wield one or more weapons with this property, you gain a +1 bonus to AC if you are proficient in the weapon and not using a shield. Damage: 1d4 Damage Type: bludgeoning Properties: Ammunition, Defensive Range: 30/120
