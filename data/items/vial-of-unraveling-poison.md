# Vial of Unraveling Poison

**description**: Potion, Rare This vial contains 3 doses of unraveling poison. As an action, you can apply one dose of this poison to a weapon or a piece of ammunition you're holding. The first time you hit a creature with the poisoned weapon or ammunition within the next minute, the creature takes 2d8 Force damage and must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be Poisoned for 1 minute. While Poisoned in this way, the creature can't cast spells and any spells or magic effects on it, as well as the magical properties of each non-Artifact magic item it is carrying, are suppressed. The creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.
