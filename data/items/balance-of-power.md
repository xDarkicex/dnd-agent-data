# Balance of Power

**description**: Balance of Power Wondrous item, very rare When a gem worth at least 500 gp is placed on the right-hand tray of this neat brass scale, the gem is destroyed and an ability score you select is changed to 20 until the next dawn. There is no effect if your score is already 20 or higher. Once used, this property of the scale can’t be used again until the next dawn.
