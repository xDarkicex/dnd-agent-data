# Hazewalker Plate

**description**: Hazewalker Plate Armour (plate), very rare This plate armour includes a helmet and is hermetically sealed when worn. You gain the following benefits when its worn and the helmet visor is closed: You are immune to diseases and the poisoned condition, and gain advantage on saving throws against any effect that causes contamination. You have resistance to necrotic and poison damage. You don’t need to breathe.
