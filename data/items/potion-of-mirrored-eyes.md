# Potion of Mirrored Eyes

**description**: Potion, uncommon For 1 minute after you drink this potion, you gain immunity to any effect which can normally be avoided by averting your gaze from it, such as a medusa’s petrifying gaze. While under the influence of this potion, you can safely look at such creatures without being subjected to their abilities.
