# Mimic Ship-in-a-Bottle

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Component: monstrosity (mimic) egg Each time this ship-in-a-bottle is observed, something is slightly different. One time, it might have three masts. Another time, the figurehead might have changed from dragon to narwhal, or the rigging has become a light shade of mauve. The only constants are the cannons, its malevolent ‘grin’, and the tentacles. This ship is, in fact, a baby mimic that hasn’t quite mastered the art of ‘mimicking’. Find Matey. As an action, you can uncork and upend the bottle, casting the find familiar spell. When you cast the spell in this way, the familiar takes the statistics of the ship mimic offspring and can attack. If the mimic is killed, a new mimic appears 8 hours after you fill the bottle with water and salt.
