# Electric Shock Trap

**description**: Mechanical trap When touched, this trap electrifies the barrier. All creatures touching the barrier take 14 (4d6) lightning damage per round, or half with a successful DC 17 Dexterity saving throw. This trap may be detected with a DC 20 Wisdom (Perception) check and disarmed with a DC 20 Dexterity check on which thieves’ tools apply. The trap electrifies the barrier for 1 minute.
