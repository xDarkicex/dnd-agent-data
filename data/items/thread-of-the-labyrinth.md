# Thread of the Labyrinth

**description**: Thread of the Labyrinth Wondrous Item, Rare (requires attunement) Woven from persistent illusions and given as a gift of love to a hero of ages past, this ball of magic thread allows you to navigate the mightiest of mazes and find your way when lost. While no creature is attuned to the thread, it is indistinguishable from an ordinary ball of thread. When a creature attunes to it, the thread’s true nature is revealed. While attuned, the thread is invisible to everyone except the creature attuned to it and creatures with truesight. Only the creature attuned to it and creatures with truesight can interact with it. Untangled Guidance. When unraveled at a specific point, the Thread of the Labyrinth allows you to retrace your way back to that point without a fault as long as you are on the same plane of existence. While attuned to the thread, you can use a bonus action to magically ravel it back into a ball. The thread can extend for a number of miles equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of one) and is impervious to tangling. Labyrinth Mastery. While attuned to the Thread of the Labyrinth, you have advantage on any ability checks related to navigating the interior of buildings, as well as any magic labyrinth created by maze or similar spells and effects.
