# Low-light Goggles

**description**: Low-light Goggles Utility, varies The standard version of this eyewear improves your vision’s photonic range, increasing clarity of detail in any form of lighting. It is an uncommon item. While wearing these goggles, you have darkvision out to a range of 60 feet. If you already have darkvision, wearing the goggles increases its range by 60 feet. Spectrum Goggles. This variant is a very rare item that grants you both darkvision and infravision. You can only use one type of vision at a time. You can switch between the two types as an action.
