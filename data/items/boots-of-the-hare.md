# Boots of the Hare

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) While attuned to these boots, whenever you use your action to Dash, you gain 90 feet of extra movement instead of the usual amount. Curse. These boots are cursed, a fact that is revealed only when an identify spell is cast on the boots or you attune to them. Attuning to the boots curses you until you are targeted by the remove curse spell or similar magic. While cursed, your speed is 15 feet and can’t be increased by any means other than taking the Dash action.
