# Fountain of the God

**description**: Kekel'Ji, uncommon While a fairly common mushroom within the jungle, the kekel'ji holds a secret ingredient used in many healing potions. The cap of the fungi is hollow, and often holds a few gallons of water, depending on the size of the mushroom. The water, while seemingly normal and clean, is infused with powerful vitamins and minerals deposited and refined by the mushroom. The water is used by the plant if there comes a time when the surrounding soil is low on those stored vitamins and minerals. Curative Enhancer. Healing potions or similar restorative liquids crafted using water from the fountain of the god doubles the normal strength of the curative.
