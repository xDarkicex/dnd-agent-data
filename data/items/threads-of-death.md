# Threads of Death

**description**: Threads of Death Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This cloak resembles a burial shroud and emits an unsettling aura, granting you a +1 bonus to AC. Whenever you are hit with a melee weapon or spell attack while wearing this cloak, you can use your reaction to teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. When you do, you take 1d6 psychic damage.
