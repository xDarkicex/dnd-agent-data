# Hydraulic Boosters

**description**: Hydraulic Boosters Augment (limb), rare (requires affinity) Installing this augment takes up two of your maximum number of limb augments (one for each leg). A hydraulic frame is implanted into each of your legs. With this augment, your jump distance is tripled. In addition, when you take the Dash action, the increase in movement is equal to double your speed, after applying any modifiers.
