# Ritual Pouch

**description**: Ritual Pouch Adventuring gear When performing an invocation, a character with a ritual pouch can the material offerings it contains to gain 1 bonus favor for that invocation's empowerments. After using a ritual pouch, the character cannot use that ritual pouch or any other until they spend 4 hours at a shrine preparing new offerings.
