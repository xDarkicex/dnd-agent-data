# Wand of Treasures

**description**: Wand, uncommon The wand has 3 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 of its charges, and if there is treasure worth at least 1,000 gold pieces within a 1-mile radius, the wand pulses and points at the one nearest to you. The wand regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn.
