# Ascendant Dragon Vessel

**description**: Ascendant Dragon Vessel Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement) This vessel can be a potion bottle, drinking horn, or other container meant to hold a liquid. As a bonus action, if the vessel is empty, you can speak the command word to fill the vessel with one of the following (your choice): ale, olive oil, a potion of healing , a potion of climbing , mead, a potion of fire breath , a potion of healing (greater), wine, a potion of flying , a potion of healing (superior), whiskey, a potion of healing (supreme), or a potion of dragon's majesty . Once this property is used, it can't be used until the next dawn. A potion you create in this way loses its magical properties if it isn't imbibed within 24 hours.
