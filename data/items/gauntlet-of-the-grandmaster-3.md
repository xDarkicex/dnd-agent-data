# Gauntlet of the Grandmaster +3

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare (+3) (requires attunement by a warmage) While wearing this gauntlet, you can use it as a spellcasting focus for your warmage spells, and you gain a bonus to spell attack and damage rolls and to the saving throw DCs of your warmage spells. This bonus is determined by the gauntlet’s rarity. This gauntlet counts as a specially prepared gauntlet for cantrips such as force buckler . In addition, you gain one of the following cantrips of your choice: force buckler , force dart , or force weapon . The cantrip doesn’t count against your number of cantrips known. Whenever you finish a long rest, you can change your selection.
