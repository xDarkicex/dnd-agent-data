# Needle of the Gods

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) A small, cold iron needle, about 6 inches long and complete with an eye at one end that pulsates with blue magical energy. The powers of these needles are mysterious, although sages have historically theorized that they are used by the deep races as rods and wands. When the wielder of a needle of the gods casts a divination spell, there is a 50% chance the spell or spell slot is not consumed. At the GM’s discretion, a needle of the gods also functions as a wand of the GM’s choice, with 3 (1d6) charges that replenish each day.
