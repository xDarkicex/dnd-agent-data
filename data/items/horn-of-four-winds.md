# Horn of Four Winds

**description**: Horn of Four Winds Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This item is made from brass and is inscribed with a swirling pattern. You can use an action to blow it and cast one of the following spells: fog cloud (3rd-level version), gust of wind , stinking cloud , or wind wall . Once used, this property of the horn can’t be used again until the next dawn.
