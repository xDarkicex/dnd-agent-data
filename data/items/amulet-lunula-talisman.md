# Amulet, Lunula Talisman

**description**: Wondrous item, neck This crescent moon-shaped golden amulet is engraved with beautiful filigree. While wearing this amulet, if your alignment is good, you have advantage on saving throws against spells and magical effects cast by aberrations and monstrosities.
