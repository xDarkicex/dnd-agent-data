# Grasshopper Staff (Legendary)

**description**: Grasshopper Staff Weapon (Quarterstaff), Legendary (Requires Attunement) You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While attuned to it, your walking speed is increased by 10 feet. Rubbing the grasshopper causes the staff to emit a soft, buzzing sound. Jump! The staff has 8 charges. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge to cast the jump spell on yourself. In addition, you can use a bonus action to expend 2 charges to cast misty step . The staff regains 1d6 + 2 expended charges when you finish a long rest. Speed Boost. At the start of each of your turns, you can convert the staff’s power into speed. You reduce the staff’s to hit and damage modifier by 1, 2, or 3, and your walking speed and jump distance are both increased by a number of feet equal to the amount reduced x 5 until the start of your next turn. You must be holding the staff to gain the bonus to speed and jump distance. Progression The Grasshopper Staff progresses each time the creature who is attuned to it defeats a powerful insectoid Beast or Monstrosity. When the attuned wielder deals damage to such a creature with an attack using the Grasshopper Staff and the creature is reduced to 0 hit points in the same encounter, the staff progresses. To progress to rare, the creature must be CR 8 or greater, CR 13 or greater for very rare, and CR 18 for legendary. The staff cannot progress more than once per week.
