# Runecarver's Whetstone

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon This magic whetstone is inscribed with arcane runes. You can spend 1 minute sharpening a melee weapon that deals Piercing or Slashing damage to give the weapon your choice of one of the following properties for 1 hour (the weapon becomes magical for the duration if it isn't already): Power. The weapon deals an extra 1d4 Force damage on a hit. Precision. Attacks made with the weapon score a critical hit on a roll of 18–20. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
