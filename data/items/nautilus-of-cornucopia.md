# Nautilus of Cornucopia

**description**: Wondrous item, rare This large nautilus shell shimmers with pearlescent colors even in the absence of light. Once per day, you can command the shell to produce a feast of delicious food for up to 8 creatures. If left uneaten, the food disappears at dawn. It takes 1 hour to consume the food and drink produced by the shell . A creature who eats from the food is completely nourished for one day and benefits from the effects of a heroes’ feast spell. In addition, the creature also gains water breathing for 24 hours.
