# Sphere of Foresight

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This amulet consists of an orb that floats seamlessly inside a metal ring set to a silver chain. The amulet has 3 charges for the following properties and regains all expended charges daily at dawn: Glimpse. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge to glimpse the future. You can roll a d4 and add the result to the next ability check, attack roll, or saving throw you make before the start of your next turn. Foresee. As a reaction when a creature you can see hits you with an attack, you can expend 1 charge to roll a d6 and add the number rolled to your AC against the attack, potentially causing the attack to miss you. Sense. When you roll Initiative, you can expend 1 charge to roll a d8 and add the number rolled to your Initiative roll.
