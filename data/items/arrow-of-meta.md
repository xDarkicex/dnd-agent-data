# Arrow of Meta

**description**: Weapon (Arrow), Rare This black arrow with a red stripe down one side is distinctly more balanced than your standard fare. Even a novice can tell this arrow has a special aura to it. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic ammunition. This ammunition also comes with a property that defies the laws of worldly knowledge. When you make an attack with a weapon using this ammunition, you can whisper a question to it. If the attack hits, the question is answered correctly in a whispered telepathic message. You are only able to ask questions about the game system itself, such as: “What is the creature’s armor class?” “How many hit points does the creature have?” “What level is the character?” “Is the creature immune to [any single type of damage]?” If used on an inanimate object, the ammunition must be able to pierce the object (such as a wooden door but not a mental one) and questions can be asked such as, “When was the last time this door was used?” or, “Is the door trapped?” However, what makes a viable question is ultimately at the DM’s discretion. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. In addition, this magic ammunition is recoverable and retains its magic. Encounter Ideas Sales Pitch. Though it has a hefty price, a shopkeeper may well convince the characters this arrow is worth its cost. Grave of the Hunter. Rumors about a legendary hunter claim that they always seemed to know exactly what needed to be done to fell their quarry. They were buried with their prized bow and quiver of arrows. Meta Begets Meta. The gods, seen and unseen, love a good source of entertainment. A visiting deity might gift an arrow for a favor.
