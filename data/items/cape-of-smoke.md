# Cape of Smoke

**description**: Cape of Smoke Wondrous item Price: 2,000 gp This cape, once owned by the incredible performing magician Marvelous Mardigast, is made of fine material. The inside of the cape is always a flashy red color. The character may pull the cape around them and a puff of smoke comes out. The character then appears at a nearby location with another puff of smoke just seconds later. As part of their Movement Action, the character activates the cape and can move up to their Movement Speed. They are both invisible and silent during this movement. They then reappear with another puff of smoke, making it appear that they have teleported to that location. The character cannot attack, cast spells, or speak during the movement without reappearing first.
