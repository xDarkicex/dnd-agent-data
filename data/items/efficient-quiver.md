# Efficient Quiver

**description**: Efficient Quiver Wondrous item, uncommon Each of the quiver's three compartments connects to an extradimensional space that allow the quiver to hold numerous items while never weighing more than 2 pounds. The shortest compartment can hold up to sixty arrows , bolts , or similar objects. The midsize compartment holds up to eighteen javelins or similar objects. The longest compartment holds up to six long objects, such as bows, quarterstaffs , or spears . You can draw any item the quiver contains as if doing so from a regular quiver or scabbard.
