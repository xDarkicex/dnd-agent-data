# Flute of the Faun

**description**: Flute of the Faun Wondrous Item, rare (requires attunement) Weight: 2 lbs. These pan flutes are frequently played by Satyrs in the fey realm. The instruments sometimes find their way to the prime material plane, where they are treasured by bards of all races. The flute has 5 charges. While playing the flute, you can expend 1 or more charges as an action to cast a spell. 1 charge: Animal Friendship, Charm Person 2 charges: Calm Emotions, Suggestion 3 charges: Compulsion, Confusion 5 charges: Irresistible Dance, Mass Suggestion The spell save is DC 13 unless your spell save DC is higher. The flute regains all expended charges daily at dawn. If you have proficiency with a pan flute, you can add your proficiency bonus to any ability checks you make to play music with the instrument. A bard can use a musical instrument as a spellcasting focus.
