# Reverberating Shield

**description**: Armor (shield), very rare (requires attunement) While holding this shield, you have a +1 bonus to AC. This bonus is in addition to the shield’s normal bonus to AC. Whenever a creature misses you with a melee weapon attack while you are holding this shield, it must succeed a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw. On a failure, the creature drops its weapon, which is thrown 15 feet away from you. If the weapon can’t be dropped, such as a dragon’s bite or claws, the attacker instead has disadvantage on all weapon attacks until the end of its turn.
