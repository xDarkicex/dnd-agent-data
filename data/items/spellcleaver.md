# Spellcleaver

**description**: Weapon (Any Axe), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This magic axe is energy made manifest. When you attack with the axe, the attack ignores any bonuses to the target's AC gained from spells or other magical effects, and its damage is Force instead of Slashing. Bond. As a bonus action, you can magically teleport the weapon to your hand as long as you are within 60 feet of it. Moreover, the weapon has the Thrown property with a range of 20/60 feet and magically returns to your hand immediately after it has been used to make a ranged attack. Eldritch. When you cast a spell of 1st level or higher, the weapon becomes charged with arcane energy. Until the end of your next turn, the weapon deals extra Force damage equal to the level of the spell on a hit.
