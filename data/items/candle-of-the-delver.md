# Candle of the Delver

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon This magic candle is decorated with ornate charms. Delver's Light. As an action, you can speak the candle's command word to make it hover in the air 5 feet in front of you for 1 hour, following you wherever you go. While the candle is hovering in this way, you can use a bonus action to cause its wick to light up with a magical light or cause the wick to extinguish, producing one of the following effects: Conceal. While the candle's wick is extinguished, the candle is invisible. Any creature within 20 feet of it has advantage on Stealth checks, and can't be targeted by divination magic or perceived by magical sensors. Illuminate. While the candle's wick is lit, it sheds bright light in a 10-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet. Any creature within its light has advantage on Investigation and Perception checks. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
