# Mariner's Leather Armor

**description**: Armor (Leather Armor), Uncommon While wearing this armor, you have a Swim Speed equal to your Speed. In addition, if you start your turn underwater with 0 Hit Points, you immediately regain 1d4 Hit Points. The armor can’t heal anyone again until the next dawn. The armor is decorated with fish and shell motifs.
