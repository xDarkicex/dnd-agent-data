# La Befana's Broom

**description**: La Befana’s broom functions as a +3 flying light mace, a staff of feast and famine, and when flying, it can carry any load. Fire cannot damage or destroy the broom, and in fact its fly speed increases by 10 feet for 10 minutes if it is placed into a fire for 1 round. La Befana’s broom allows the user to cast greater invisibility at will, as well as slow once per day. In the hands of anyone other than La Befana, it behaves as a broom of flying. Destruction La Befana's broom falls to ashes if used to deliver gifts to 300 bad children and coal to 300 good children in one night.
