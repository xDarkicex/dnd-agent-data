# Steelsilk Mantle

**description**: Steelsilk Mantle Wondrous item, rare This dull gray, hooded cloak feels unusually heavy. On rare occasions, it seems to shine with a bit of reflected light. When targeted with an attack, you can use your reaction to whip this cloak around your body, causing it to harden into a solid, metallic barrier. Until the end of your next turn, you have half–cover and resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage. Once you use this ability, you can’t use it again until the next dawn.
