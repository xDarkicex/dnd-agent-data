# Kettle of Drumming

**description**: Kettle of Drumming Wondrous item, uncommon This item consists of an 18-inch-wide copper bowl with an animal skin stretched across the open end. When struck, the kettle drum makes a terrifying din. Each creature within 30 feet of you (not including you) must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened of you for 1 minute. At the end of each of its turns, a creature can repeat the saving throw, ending the effect on itself on a success. Once used, this property of the drum can’t be used again until the next dawn.
