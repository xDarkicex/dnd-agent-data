# Infinite Scroll Case

**description**: Infinite Scroll Case Wondrous item, uncommon This cylindrical silver tube has been engraved with an elaborate pattern in high relief and has a removable cap. You can place as many scrolls as you wish within this scroll case, including magic ones. Whenever you pull a scroll from the case, it is always the one that you desired.
