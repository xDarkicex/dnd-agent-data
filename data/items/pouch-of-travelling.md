# Pouch of Travelling

**description**: Pouch of Travelling Wondrous item, uncommon This sturdy leather pouch weighs 20 pounds and is 10 inches square. Inside it are a tent, a bedroll, a mess kit, a tinderbox, a hooded lantern, 10 days of rations, 6 iron spikes, a crowbar, a waterskin, and 50 feet of hempen rope. All these items may be easily drawn out and replaced whenever required. If any additional item is placed in the pouch, it bursts and scatters the contents.
