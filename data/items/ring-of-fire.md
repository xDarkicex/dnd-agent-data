# Ring of Fire

**description**: Ring of Fire Wondrous item, legendary A band of amber with gold and red flecks intermixed into the stone. The band is always warm to the touch. The wearer of this ring may spend an action to ignite all flammable objects within 30 feet of themselves to burst into flame. A creature within 5 feet of an object that has been lit on fire must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw. If they fail, they suffer 3d6 fire damage and gain the weakened condition for 2 rounds ( Adventures in Rokugan ). On a success, they take half as much damage instead. Additionally, melee weapon attacks the wearer makes deal an additional 1d4 fire damage. If the Ring of Fire is worn during a long rest, the wearer becomes attuned. The empowerment word can only be used once per long rest. While attuned, the wearer benefits from the following effects: If the wearer speaks the empowerment word “Unveils” as they active the ring, the wearer casts the fireball spell (see the SRD-OGLv5.1). Any melee attacks the wearer makes count as magical and deal an additional 1d6 fire damage. The wearer gains resistance to fire damage. If they already had resistance, they instead reduce the damage they would receive by 10, after resistance is applied.
