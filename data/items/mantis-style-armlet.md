# Mantis Style Armlet

**description**: Mantis Style Armlet Wondrous item, rare This green brass armlet depicts a mantis with its forelegs raised. While wearing this wondrous item, you gain a +1 bonus to the attack and damage rolls of your unarmed strikes. In addition, when you are hit by a melee weapon attack and you have at least one hand free, you can use your reaction to parry. When you do, you reduce the damage by 1d6 + your Strength or Dexterity modifier + 1.
