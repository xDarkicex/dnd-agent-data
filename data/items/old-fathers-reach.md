# Old Father's Reach

**description**: Weapon (trident), very rare (requires attunement) Upon command, this +2 warfork becomes sheathed in water from the deepest oceans, cold to the touch. This water does not harm you and remains until another command removes it. On a hit, the enchanted fluid deals an extra 1d6 points of cold damage. Being a weapon made from boletann, old father’s reach can hold up to three doses of poison, each expended when delivered on a hit.
