# Harp of Thunder

**description**: Harp of Thunder Wondrous item, very rare You must be proficient with a string instrument to use this small lap harp. When you use an action to play this item, it emits a thunderous blast in a 20-foot cone. Each creature in the cone must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or become stunned until the end of your next turn. Once used, this property of the harp can’t be used again until the next dawn.
