# Deathfibrillator

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Component: construct lifespark A versatile tool, the deathfibrillator is as capable of reinvigorating a dazed creature’s motor skills as it is at sending them into a paralytic stupor. Designed by none other than Anton Spark himself, this medical apparatus sees as much use in brawling pits as it does sedating overstimulated patients on the wards of the Night Hospitaller. This item has 10 charges and regains 1d6 + 4 expended charges daily at dawn. While holding it in your hand, unarmed strikes you make that hit with that hand deal piercing damage instead of bludgeoning damage. Clear! While holding a deathfibrillator, you can expend 1 or more charges in the following ways: Zap (1 charge). You squeeze this item’s handle when you hit a target with an unarmed strike using it, dealing an extra 1d4 lightning damage. First Aid (3 charges). As an action, you touch a stunned or paralysed creature within your reach, sending a wave of electrostatic energy through it. The creature takes 2d8 lightning damage and is no longer stunned or paralysed.
