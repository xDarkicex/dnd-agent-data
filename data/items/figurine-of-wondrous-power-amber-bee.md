# Figurine of Wondrous Power, Amber Bee

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Amber Bee (Uncommon). This amber statuette is of a honeybee. It can become a giant honey bee (see Tome of Beasts 2 ) for up to 6 hours. Once it has been used, it can’t be used again until 2 days have passed.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: uncommon
