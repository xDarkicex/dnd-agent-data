# Common Scents

**description**: Weapon (Flail), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This flail is unremarkable in appearance, except for the head, which is pierced with small holes. Faint odors of flowers and herbs emanate from the holes. While attuned to this magic weapon, you can use an action to speak the command word, causing the weapon to emit white smoke. The smoke has either a pleasant scent or a foul odor, which you choose when speaking the command word. Every creature within 5 feet of the weapon (excluding you) must succeed on a DC 12 Wisdom saving throw or be calmed (by the pleasant scent) or enraged (by the foul odor) until the end of its next turn. A creature who can’t smell or doesn’t need to breathe succeeds automatically. A calmed creature is immune to the frightened condition and does not make attacks or take other offensive action unless it is attacked first. An enraged creature becomes hostile and attacks the nearest creature. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a short or long rest. Encounter Ideas Battle. The adventurers stumble onto a battle in progress. In the middle of the free-for-all is a dead goblin, this flail still clutched in one hand. Ruined Temple. The adventurers explore a forgotten temple inhabited by specters. The ghost of the last wielder of a common scents guards its own burial, and the flail within. Tavern. The adventurers are in a local tavern, ready for an evening of music, drink, and food. Taltor, a local thug decides to make trouble by inciting a riot using the foul odor of this flail.
