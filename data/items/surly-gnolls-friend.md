# Surly Gnoll's Friend

**description**: Surly Gnoll’s Friend Weapon (flail), rare (requires attunement) The spiked end of this flail is the size of a halfling’s head. While you are wielding this weapon, you add your proficiency bonus to your Charisma (Intimidation) checks. If you already have proficiency in that skill, you add double your proficiency bonus. When you hit a humanoid of your size or smaller with an attack using this weapon, you can use your bonus action to make a Charisma (Intimidation) check against it contested by its Wisdom saving throw. On a successful check, the creature becomes frightened of you until the end of your next turn. Once a creature has been frightened in this way, it is immune to further uses of this feature for 24 hours.
