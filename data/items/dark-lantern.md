# Dark Lantern

**description**: Dark Lantern Wondrous item, uncommon When you light this hooded lantern, it burns with a black flame that casts no light. While you hold it in your hand, you can see as though the lantern were giving off bright light in a 60-foot radius and dim light for an additional 30 feet. The lantern burns for 6 hours on a flask of oil.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: uncommon
