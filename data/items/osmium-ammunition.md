# Osmium Ammunition

**description**: Weapon (Ammunition), Uncommon The tip of this ammunition is made of osmium, offering unmatched momentum. Osmium Impact When you make a ranged attack using this ammunition, the attack's range is doubled and you have Advantage on the attack roll. Unless the target is wearing Heavy Armor or has Natural Armor and an AC of 15 or higher, it takes an extra die of the weapon's damage on a hit. Once it hits a target, the ammunition is destroyed.
