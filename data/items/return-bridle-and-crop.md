# Return Bridle and Crop

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon A creature with the Mounted trait that wears this bridle is linked with you telepathically while you hold the crop. The creature can communicate with you over any distance as long as the bridle and crop are on the same plane of existence. Once per day while holding the crop, you can use your action to swing it in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of you with enough room for the creature. The creature is instantly teleported into that space. You can’t transport the creature again in this way until next dawn.
