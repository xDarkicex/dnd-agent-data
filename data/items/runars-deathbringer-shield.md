# Runars Deathbringer Shield

**description**: Runar’s Deathbringer Shield Shield, legendary (requires attunement) This is a +1 magical shield. Additionally, as a reaction to being hit by an enemy, the wielder can inflict 7 (2d6) lightning damage to its attacker. If wielded alongside another deathbringer weapon, such as Runar’s deathbringer battleaxe, this item does not require attunement.
