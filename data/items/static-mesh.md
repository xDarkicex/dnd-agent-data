# Static Mesh

**description**: Weapon (Net), Uncommon This net is made from a tensile, superconductive alloy. It can't be destroyed by dealing damage to it. Shocking Net At the start of each of its turns, a creature Restrained by the net takes 1d4 Lightning damage and must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution save or it can do only one of the following on that turn: move, take an Action, or take a Bonus Action. A creature wearing metal armor takes twice as much Lightning damage and has Disadvantage on its save against the effect.
