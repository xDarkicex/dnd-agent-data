# Hellfire Cannon

**description**: Hellfire Cannon Wondrous item, very rare Damage: Variable Weight: 2,000 lbs. Properties: Large Object, Loading, (Range 600/2,400 ft) This heavy iron cannon was forged in the lower planes to battle the enemies of Hell. It shoots streams of searing hellfire or shrieking, flaming skulls. It takes one action to load and aim the cannon and one action to fire it. Hellfire Blast. The cannon discharges hellfire in a line 600 feet long and 5 feet wide. Each creature in the area must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 33 (6d10) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The hellfire ignites any flammable objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried. Skull Blast. The cannon fires a flaming, shrieking undead skull in a line up to 2,400 ft. The skull explodes upon striking a creature or object. Each creature in a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on that point must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw. A target takes 22 (4d10) piercing and 22 (4d10) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Siege Weapon. The cannon deals double damage to objects and structures. After the cannon has fired 3 times, it must cool for 1 minute before it can be fired again. If it is fired again without cooling, roll 1d6 each time it is fired. On a 1, the cannon explodes. Each creature in a 20-foot-radius must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 22 (4d10) piercing and 33 (6d10) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
