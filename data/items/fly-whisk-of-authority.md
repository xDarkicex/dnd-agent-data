# Fly Whisk of Authority

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon If you use an action to flick this fly whisk, you have advantage on Charisma (Intimidation) and Charisma (Persuasion) checks for 10 minutes. You can’t use the fly whisk this way again until the next dawn.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: uncommon

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Southlands Worldbook
