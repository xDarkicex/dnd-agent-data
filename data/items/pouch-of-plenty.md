# Pouch of Plenty

**description**: Pouch of Plenty Wondrous item, rare This small brown leather pouch is embossed with the image of a bee. When you place up to 10 coins in the pouch and leave it closed until the following dawn, the coins are converted to the next higher metal. So copper coins become silver, silver coins become electrum, and so on. The pouch has no effect on platinum coins.
