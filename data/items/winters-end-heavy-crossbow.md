# Winter's End Heavy Crossbow

**description**: Weapon (any bow), rare (requires attunement) Component: bundle of plant roots Woven from the first branches to bloom at the end of winter, this living bow is strung with a length of snowspider’s silk. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While holding this bow, you can cast the druidcraft cantrip. In addition, when an arrow is nocked on this bow, the arrow immediately lights up, emanating blue mist and shedding bright light in a 15-foot radius and dim light for an additional 15 feet. This light lasts for 1 minute after the arrow is shot from the bow. Winter Hunt. This weapon deals an extra 1d6 cold damage to each creature it hits that is in freezing temperatures or is standing on snow or ice. Ice Shower. As an action, you can choose a point you can see within the normal range of this weapon and fire a volley of up to 6 arrows. These arrows levitate in the air when they enter a 15-foot radius centred on that point. For 1 minute, or until all the arrows are used, whenever a creature other than you enters the area for the first time on its turn or starts its turn there, one arrow shoots at it. The creature must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take 1d6 piercing damage and 1d6 cold damage. Once this property of the bow has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
