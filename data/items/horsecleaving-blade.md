# Horsecleaving Blade

**description**: Horsecleaving Blade Melee Weapon (martial, sword) These enormous swords have a long, thick, katana-like blade roughly six feet in length attached to a two-handed hilt. Terrifyingly enormous and heavy, zanbatō, or horsecleaving blades, are extremely difficult to use effectively, but can hit with devastating force intended to cut down horse and rider in a single swing. These anti-cavalry weapons were adopted into the curriculum of the Matsu family of the Lion Clan after their ill-fated encounter with the Utaku cavalry at the Battle of Plum Blossom Pass. Heavy. Small creatures have disadvantage on attack rolls with heavy weapons. A heavy weapon's size and bulk make it too large for a Small creature to use effectively. Special. You cannot make opportunity attacks with this weapon. Two-Handed. This weapon requires two hands when you attack with it. Damage: 2d8 Damage Type: Slashing Properties: Heavy, Special, Two-Handed Weight: 8
