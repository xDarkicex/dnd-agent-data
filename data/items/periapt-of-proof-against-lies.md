# Periapt of Proof Against Lies

**description**: Wondrous item, rare A pendant fashioned from the claw or horn of a pact drake (see Creature Codex ) is affixed to a thin gold chain. While you wear it, you know if you hear a lie, but this doesn’t apply to evasive statements that remain within the boundaries of the truth. If you lie while wearing this pendant, you become poisoned for 10 minutes.
