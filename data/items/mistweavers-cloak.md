# Mistweaver's Cloak

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This magic cloak is woven from wool and fur. In the Mist. Whenever you take the Dash action, opportunity attacks against you are made with disadvantage until the start of your next turn. Moreover, when a creature makes a weapon attack against you with disadvantage and hits you, the attack's damage against you is halved. Conjure Fog. As an action, you can create a 20-foot-radius sphere of fog centered on you. The fog spreads around corners and moves with you, and its area is heavily obscured. It lasts for 1 minute, until a wind of moderate or greater speed (at least 10 miles per hour) disperses it, or you lose your concentration on it (as if concentrating on a spell). Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
