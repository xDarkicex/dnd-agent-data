# Two Cat Chariot

**description**: Two Cat Chariot Wondrous item, very rare This ornate chariot has carvings of frolicking cats along its surface. The chariot is large enough to hold three riders, but the two harnesses are sized for a cat-sized creature. When two cats are secured to the cat-sized harnesses, the runes and sigils on the wooden chariot’s surface ignite, glowing with an eldritch blue. Each time activated in this manner, the chariot’s rider must make a DC 15 Wisdom (Animal Handling) check. If successful, the cats transform into lions (with the Fey property and able to fly the chariot at a speed of 60) and the cats will obey directions from the riders. If either cat takes damage, they attempt to land on their next turn and ignore any further direction until the next time they are activated. Once they land, they turn back into normal cats. Once activated, the chariot cannot be activated again until after a long rest is finished.
