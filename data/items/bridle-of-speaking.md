# Bridle of Speaking

**description**: Bridle of Speaking Wondrous item, rare This bridle is made from yellow leather stamped with random letters. When placed on the head of a mount, that creature gains an Intelligence of 10 and the ability to speak common. If the bridle is removed, the mount reverts to normal.
