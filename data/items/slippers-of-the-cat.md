# Slippers of the Cat

**description**: While you wear these fine, black cloth slippers, you gain advantage on Dexterity (Acrobatics) checks to keep your balance. When you fall while wearing these slippers, you land on your feet and can make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: uncommon

**publisher**: Kobold Press
