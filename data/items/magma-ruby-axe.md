# Magma Ruby Axe

**description**: The most important part of a Magma Ruby Axe is the eponymous ruby. It was mined on the elemental plane of fire itself, and brought into the world of Ashandri through portals in the Ember Wastelands. The elemental allies of the mortals living there showed them how to fuse the rubies with weapons to grant them magical powers. This is how the first Magma Ruby Axes were created. Nowadays, the process is well known among those who specialize in crafting magic items. Merchants have spread these items all over the world, and made a small fortune in doing so. Magic schools: Evocation Cost: 5.000 gp Damage: 1d6 slashing + 2d6 fire Magic properties: The embedded fire ruby instills the Magma Ruby Axe with elemental fire. Touching the ruby and speaking a command word engulfs the whole weapon with fire. Only the wielders hands and arms are magically protected from being burned. The flames shed bright light in a 40-foot radius, and additional dim light for another 40 feet. While the handaxe is ablaze, it deals an extra 2d6 fire damage to any target it hits. The flames last until you use a bonus action to touch the ruby and speak the command word again. Dazra approached the customer with a smile. “You have a very good eye. It’s just in your price range, and this weapon can inflict devastating damage on your enemies. Well, if they aren’t as resistant to fire as we Ember Orcs are, of course. Even then, a very good weapon.” The human smiled. She had noticed the branded rune on his forehead right when he entered. He was a Branded Tribute, the poor soul. “I need it to fight the servants of an ice demon. So this is exactly what I’m looking for. When I’m done there... I might come back for anything to help me fight the demon itself.” Dazra raised an eyebrow. Killing the demon that your soul was sold off to? One way to get it back, for sure. She didn’t expect him to succeed, but she’d make him a good price to give him a fighting chance. “I’ll tell you something, if you buy the axe, I’ll throw in a few potions that protect you against any magic that relies on cold. Deal?”
