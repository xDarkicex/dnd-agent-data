# Killer Bee Bomb

**description**: Killer Bee Bomb Wondrous Item, rare Weight: 2 lbs. This small metal object has wings that stay folded when not in use. It vibrates from the buzzing of the bees inside. Guided Bomb. As an action, you can throw this item and direct it to fly to any space within 120 feet that you can see. The bomb then releases 1d4 Swarms of Killer Bees that will attack any creature within 5 feet. After 1 minute, any remaining bees return to the bomb. The bomb can be used 3 times per day and refills with killer bees at dawn.
