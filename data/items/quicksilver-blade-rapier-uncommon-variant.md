# Quicksilver Blade Rapier (uncommon variant)

**description**: Weapon (any sword), rare (requires attunement) Component: pouch of dragon (silver) scales Made of true quicksilver—an alloy of mithril, mercury, and silver—this weapon’s blade is in constant, fluid motion. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Freeflow. While holding this weapon, you can use a bonus action to cause its form to shift to that of any melee weapon with which you are proficient that lacks the two-handed and heavy properties. Throw. When you make an attack with this weapon, you can instead flick a shard of the quicksilver blade towards a target, making a ranged weapon attack (range 30/90) that uses your Strength modifier for the attack and damage rolls. On a hit, it deals the weapon’s normal damage plus an extra 2d6 poison damage, or 4d6 if the target is a shapechanger. Once this property is used three times, the quicksilver blade becomes a +2 dagger with no other properties until the next dawn, when it regrows to its original form and regains all expended uses of this property. Uncommon variant: Reduce the attack and damage bonus to +1. The Throw property can only be used once before the quicksilver blade becomes a dagger, which is a +1 dagger instead of +2.
