# Knightly Seed

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon You must crack this seed and throw it—greater skill gains greater results. Make a Strength (Athletics) or Dexterity (Acrobatics) check and consult the table below. The creature that appears serves you as a knight and steed for a week and a day. If you are already a titled knight, the creature and steed serve as a squire.
