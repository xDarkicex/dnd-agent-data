# Bone Cloud Pipe of Chittr'k'k

**description**: A pipe of cracked bone inlaid with golden glyphs. This pipe is associated with a particular gaseous magical effect: fog cloud . A creature smoking the pipe can cast the associated spell as a bonus action 1/day.
