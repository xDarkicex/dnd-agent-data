# Dagger of the Death Lord

**description**: Dagger of the Death Lord Weapon (dagger), rare Damage: 1d4+1 piercing Weight: 1 lb. Properties: Finesse, Light, Thrown (range 20/60) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Life Drain. When you hit a creature with a melee attack using this dagger, the target takes an additional 2d6 necrotic damage, and you regain hit points equal to half the amount of necrotic damage dealt. This feature can be used a number of times per day equal to your proficiency bonus and can not be used on the undead. The Order of the Black Skull, a cult devoted to Sarkonis, awarded these magic daggers to the highest members of the order. The daggers are often used to sacrifice innocent souls to the Death Lord.
