# Amulet of Freshness

**description**: Wondrous Item, Common This locket carries a faint scent of fresh herbs. As an action, you can open the locket to cause the scent to grow more intense. Until the end of your next turn, creatures within 10-foot of you have advantage on saving throws against poison. Once used, this property can’t be used again until the next dawn.
