# Half Plate of Weightlessness

**description**: Half Plate of Weightlessness Armor (Medium), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This armor has 5 charges. While you wear it, you can use a bonus action to expend 1 or more charges to cast one of the following spells from the armor, targeting yourself: Jump (1 charge) or Levitate (2 charges). This armor regains 1d4 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. Half Plate. Half plate consists of shaped metal plates that cover most of the wearer's body. It does not include leg protection beyond simple greaves that are attached with leather straps. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC : 15 + Dex modifier (max 2) Stealth : Disadvantage Weight : 40
