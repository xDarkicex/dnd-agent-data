# Ale Seed

**description**: Wondrous Item, Common When thrown, this illusion seed turns into a small cask of wholesome ale, roughly 4 gallons worth, together with 4 tankards. It is often used by shadow goblins or porters to avoid carrying heavy, full-sized casks.
