# Arrow of Explosions

**description**: Weapon (arrow), very rare When this arrow strikes a solid surface, be it a creature, object, wall, or floor, it explodes with a fiery blast. Each creature within 20 feet of the point of impact must make a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 8d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. The arrow is destroyed in the blast.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Weapon (arrow)
- Item Rarity: very rare
