# Feather Token, Held Heart

**description**: Wondrous item, Very Rare Held Heart (Very Rare). This red feather is shaped like a heart. While carrying this token, you have advantage on initiative rolls. As an action, you can press the feather against a willing, injured creature. The target regains all its missing hit points and the feather disappears.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: very rare

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Vault of Magic
