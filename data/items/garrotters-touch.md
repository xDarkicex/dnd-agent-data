# Garrotter’s Touch

**description**: Poison, rare This thick, black tar is made of the boiled spores of a lesser-known fungus, anoxymorcanthia. One touch will send a creature’s lungs into paralysis, killing them shortly after. Any creature hit by an attack made with a weapon coated in this poison must make a DC 18 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned for 1 minute. If they fail the saving throw by 5 or more, they immediately begin suffocating, and cannot breathe until it succeeds on a DC 18 Constitution saving throw at the end of its turn, the poison is removed, or for 1 minute, whichever is shorter.
