# Rod of Thorns

**description**: Rod, uncommon This rod is used by those in the wilds of Tolia to protect themselves. While holding this rod, you can use your action to make a ranged weapon attack against a creature within 30 feet of you that you can see. The rod shoots its thorns at the creature, dealing 2d4 piercing damage on a hit. The rod can also be used to end life in the area by sticking it in the ground. If the rod stays in the soil for 1 minute, the plant life in a 15-foot-radius sphere centered on the rod withers and turns to dust. As long as the rod is in place, any creature who enters the sphere for the first time is affected by the destructive aura of the rod and takes 1d4 blood damage. If a creature dies within this aura, the area expands by a 5-foot-radius to a maximum of a 60-foot-radius sphere.
