# Pocket Gem

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) You attune this small gem to an item; the item is then stored in a pocket dimension within the gem. While stored in this way, any magical properties of the item are suppressed and the item cannot be scryed upon. As part of the attunement process, the item is marked with a rune visible only to you. By tracing the rune upon one facet of the gem, the item is released from the pocket dimension, appearing in your grasp or at a location within your reach. You can destroy most items stored within the gem by tracing the rune’s reverse. If the gem cannot destroy the item, the gem is instead destroyed and the item returned to your hand, though you gain some insight into how the item could be destroyed with a successful DC 10 Intelligence (Arcana) check.
