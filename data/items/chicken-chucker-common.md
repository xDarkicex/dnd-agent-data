# Chicken Chucker (Common)

**description**: Weapon, Common (Requires Attunement) This staff-sling is a simple ranged weapon with a range of 40/160 feet, which deals 1d4 bludgeoning damage on a hit. Any creature that is proficient with the sling or quarterstaff is also proficient with this magic weapon. Common Chicken Ammunition. You can make an attack with this weapon when the projectile pocket is empty. If you do so, a white, clucking chicken manifests in the pocket. On a hit, the chicken deals 1d4 bludgeoning damage and 1 slashing damage. The chicken then dissipates into a small cloud of white feathers. This weapon has 1 charge, which it regains when you finish a long rest. When you manifest a chicken as part of an attack, you can expend 1 charge to give the chicken a permanent physical form. Once the attack has dealt damage, the chicken does not dissipate, and instead lands at the target’s feet. Chickens created this way are full-grown white chickens of any sex (your choice) and use the chicken ammunition stat block (see below). They act like ordinary chickens and are neither an ally nor an enemy to you. Chicken Ammunition Tiny Beast, Unaligned Armor Class 12 Hit Points 6 (3d4 − 1) Speed 30 ft, fly 20 ft.
