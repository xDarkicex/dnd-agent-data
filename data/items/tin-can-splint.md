# Tin Can Splint

**description**: Armor (Splint), uncommon This armor is covered in weird gizmos and cogs connected to a series of metal plates. When a ranged weapon attack misses you, you can use your reaction to activate the defensive mechanisms and bounce the projectile back to the attacker. Once activated, the armor needs to be recharged before you can use this effect again, which can be done while completing a Short Rest.
