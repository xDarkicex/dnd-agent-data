# Helm of Telepathy

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) While wearing this helm, you have telepathy with a range of 30 feet, and you can cast D etect Thoughts or Suggestion (save DC 13) from the helm. Once either spell is cast from the helm, that spell can’t be cast from it again until the next dawn.
