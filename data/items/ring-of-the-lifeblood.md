# Ring of the Lifeblood

**description**: Ring, rare (requires attunement) This ring is made of three strands of braided crimson metal which radiate a gentle heat. Each strand pulses faintly in time with the wearer’s heartbeat. The ring has three charges, and regains all expended charges at dawn. While this ring has at least one charge, spells cast on you that restore hit points restore an additional 2 hit points per level of the spell slot expended, and you gain an additional 2 hit points per hit die you spend to regain hit points. By spending one or more charges from the ring, you may cast the following spells: greater medic’s grace * (3rd level) 1 charge cure wounds (5th level) 1 charge heal (6th level) 2 charges
