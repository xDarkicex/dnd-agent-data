# Mourning Jewelry

**description**: Mourning Jewelry Ring, very rare (requires attunement) This ring has 1 charge for each of the following spells: antipathy/sympathy , commune , etherealness , and spirit guardians . Expended charges cannot be regained, and once each spell has been cast the ring disintegrates.
