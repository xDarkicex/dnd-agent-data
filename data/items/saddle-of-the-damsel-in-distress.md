# Saddle of the Damsel in Distress

**description**: Invented by the brigand Thumpa Otherside to teach the unwary that there’s no such a thing as a damsel in distress in the Kingdom. Through a convenient mechanism hidden in this saddle, it’s possible to make the horse rider fall sideways, pretending to be in apparent distress. The saddle shifts back again through the same marvelous mechanism, allowing the rider to return to sitting normally. Effect : You can use the saddle to pretend to be in difficulty. Doing so gives you advantage on a Charisma (Deception) check.
