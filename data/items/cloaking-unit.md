# Cloaking Unit

**description**: Cloaking Unit Utility, epic This device is worn as a belt. While you wear it, you can turn invisible as an action. Anything you are wearing or carrying is invisible with you. You remain invisible until the device is removed, until you attack or use a power, or until you use a bonus action to become visible again.
