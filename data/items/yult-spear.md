# Yult Spear

**description**: Weapon (ranseur), very rare The eponymous spears of Yult, are balanced, deadly weapons, with a cross-guard below the long blade, wielded with deadly force by the order. Melee weapon, simple, finesse, reach 5 ft.: You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. The spear does 1d8 piercing damage and has the ‘versatile (1d10)’ property. The spear’s cross guard allows for parrying: the wielder can use their reaction to gain a +2 to its AC against one melee attack that would hit it. To do so, the wielder must be able to see the attacker.
