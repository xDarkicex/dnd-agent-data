# Potion of Corrosive Touch

**description**: Potion, uncommon When you drink this potion, you gain the ability to dissolve material with a touch of your hand for 1 minute. On your turn, you can use your action to dissolve a cube no more than 1 foot on a side of non-living matter or you can take the Attack action to make two melee spell attacks using Strength as your spellcasting ability. On a hit, the target takes 4 (1d8) acid damage.
