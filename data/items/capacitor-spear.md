# Capacitor Spear

**description**: Capacitor Spear Melee weapon Cost : 1,000 gp Damage : 1d6 piercing Weight : 5 lb. Properties : Thrown (range 20/60), Versatile (1d8) This heavy spear is made of orichalcum-laced steel and features a housing at the base of its head to accept a vril battery. This weapon functions as a normal spear. In addition, as a reaction when you are subjected to lightning damage, you may divert half the damage suffered into the spear. For every 5 damage so diverted, rounded down, you recharge 1 charge of the attached vril battery. If overcharged, make a Dexterity saving throw with a DC equal to 10 + the number of charges overcharged. (You can add your proficiency bonus to your saving throw if you are proficient with vril weapons.) If you fail, the battery explodes, and everyone within 5 feet of the spear takes 2d6 lightning damage. The spear cannot be used until it has been repaired. Overcharged charges are lost.
