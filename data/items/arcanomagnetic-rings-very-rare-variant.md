# Arcanomagnetic Rings (very rare variant)

**description**: Ring, rare (requires attunement) Component: pouch of dragon (magnetite) scales This pair of rings forms an arcanomagnet. One, a brilliant gold that reflects light in wondrous prismatic hues, is intensely magical, while the other, a dull grey that grows darker as the surrounding light brightens, is its antimagical opposite. By creating pockets of magical and antimagical energy, you can bend the Weave, causing magic to veer off its intended trajectory. These rings can only be attuned to as a pair and count as a single magic item with regard to the number of magic items you can attune to. Spellbend. Whenever a creature or point you can see within 30 feet of you is targeted by a ranged spell attack, an attack using a magic ranged weapon or magic ammunition, or a spell with an area of effect centred on a point, you can use your reaction to redirect it. You cause a ranged attack to target a different target of your choice within 10 feet of the original (after the attack roll is made but before the outcome is determined). Alternatively, you move the area of effect’s point of origin up to 10 feet. After you use this reaction, roll a d6. On a 1-2, the rings become demagnetized and can’t be used again until the next dawn. Very rare variant: Increase the range at which you can use this property to 60 feet, the distance a different target must be from the original to 20 feet, the distance you can move the point of origin of an effect to 20 feet, and the d6 to a d10.
