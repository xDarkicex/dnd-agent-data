# Bloodwork Arms

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) You’ve enhanced your arms with bloodwork machinery. You are stronger than ever. You gain the following benefits: Increase your Strength score by 1. When you make a successful unarmed strike, you deal an extra 1d6 bludgeoning damage. When you score a critical hit with an unarmed strike, the target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be knocked prone. You have advantage on grapple checks.
