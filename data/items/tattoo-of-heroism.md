# Tattoo of Heroism

**description**: Wondrous Item (Tattoo), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) When you become frightened, or when you speak the tattoo’s command word as a bonus action, you become imbued with bravery and the tattoo disappears. You gain temporary hit points equal to your character level and you are immune to being frightened for 1 minute.
