# Armor of the Valiant Soul, Dormant

**description**: Armor of the Valiant Soul, Dormant Armor (scale), legendary (requires attunement) This antique armor forged from jade and black-dragon scales was once worn by J’mon Sa Ord of Ank’Harel. Dormant While the Armor of the Valiant Soul is in a dormant state, you gain a +1 bonus to AC while wearing it. You can also cast the command spell (save DC 14) while attuned to this armor. Once this property is used, it can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. AC : 15 + Dex modifier (max 2) Stealth : Disadvantage Weight : 45
