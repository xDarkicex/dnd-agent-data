# Thornbow

**description**: Weapon (Longbow), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This magic longbow has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. When you hit a creature with an attack roll using the bow, you can expend 1 charge to deal an additional 2d8 piercing damage to the target. If you do, you lose 1d8 hit points. Hail of Thorns. When you roll a 20 on your attack roll using this magic longbow, a rain of thorns sprays from the arrow. The target of the attack and each creature within 5 feet of it must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 2d10 piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.
