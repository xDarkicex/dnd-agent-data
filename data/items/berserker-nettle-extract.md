# Berserker Nettle Extract

**description**: Poison, rare This stinging plant normally causes intense irritation and clouded thoughts. When thickened into an extract, it can serve as a boost to warriors, though at a cost which makes it harmful to their companions. When a creature is injected with this poison, it must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and have its attacks deal an additional 1d4 damage. Additionally, that creature can no longer easily distinguish friend from foe, and must make a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw at the start of each of its turns or attack the nearest creature. If it succeeds its Wisdom saving throw, it acts normally.
