# Arrow of Glue

**description**: Weapon (arrow), uncommon An arrow of glue is one of a few arrows known as trick arrows. When you fire an arrow of glue, the arrow’s shaft becomes pliant on impact and hardens immediately after. You can fire this arrow at any point you can see within range to cover a 1-foot square area with hardened glue. The GM may rule that especially challenging shots are made with disadvantage. A creature hit by this arrow must succeed a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or be glued to a nearby surface, unable to move. It can break free with a successful DC 15 Strength (Athletics) check.
