# Glamoured

**description**: Master armor property Components: Masterwork suit of exotic armor This armor doesn’t impose disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. You can also use a bonus action to cause the armor to assume the appearance of a normal set of clothing or some other kind of armor. You decide what it looks like, including color, style, and accessories, but the armor retains its normal bulk and weight. The illusory appearance lasts until you use this ability again or remove the armor.
