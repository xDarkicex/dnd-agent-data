# Vestments of the Bleak Shinobi

**description**: Armor (padded), uncommon (requires attunement) This padded black armor is fashioned in the furtive style of shinobi shōzoku garb. You have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks while you wear this armor. Darkness. While wearing this armor, you can use an action to cast the darkness spell from it with a range of 30 feet. Once used, this property can’t be used again until the next dawn.
