# Lunar Lance

**description**: Weapon (Any Polearm), Uncommon Made by shadow goblins to keep larger foes at a distance, lunar lances are often given to goblins on battlements or other vantage points. They weigh literally nothing but are as sharp as any dagger. This weapon adds 10 feet to your reach when attacking with it.
