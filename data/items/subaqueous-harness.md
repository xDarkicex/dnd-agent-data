# Subaqueous Harness

**description**: Subaqueous Harness Utility, uncommon This collapsible harness comes with an aquatic breather, flaps, and compression jets allowing for underwater maneuvering. While wearing the harness, you can breathe underwater, and you have a swimming speed of 60 feet. Putting the harness on takes 1 minute. Removing it requires an action.
