# Flute of Courage

**description**: Flute of Courage Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) You must be proficient with wind instruments to use this flute. If you play the flute as an action, each humanoid in a 20-foot-radius sphere is immune to being frightened while it can hear you for up to 1 minute. Once used, this property of the flute can’t be used again until the next dawn.
