# Origami Swallow

**description**: Wondrous item, rare Component: pouch of plant leaves Over the course of a minute, you can fold this enchanted paper into the shape of a 6-inch-wingspan swallow, activating its magical potential. Improved Familiar. Swift and observant, the origami swallow is an espionage tool available only to the upper echelons of the Bureau’s secret agents. When you craft this origami animal, it becomes your familiar, as per the find familiar spell, with the following changes: It uses the statistics of a hawk , but is a construct with a flying speed of 120 feet and a +6 Dexterity (Stealth) modifier. There is no limit to the distance at which you can communicate with the swallow telepathically. You can’t dismiss the swallow. It can use its action to make a Talons attack. If it hits a creature with eyes with this attack, the creature must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or be blinded until the end of its next turn. The origami swallow loses its magic 1 hour after being activated, becoming a mundane, folded piece of paper.
