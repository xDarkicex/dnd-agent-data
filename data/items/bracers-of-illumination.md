# Bracers of Illumination

**description**: Wondrous Item, Common (Requires Attunement) These two magic leather bracers are adorned with symbols of a sun and an eclipsed sun. Illuminate. As a bonus action, you can cause one of the following effects in a 10-foot radius around you: Eclipse. Nonmagical dim light becomes darkness and nonmagical bright light becomes dim light. Sun. Nonmagical dim light becomes bright light and nonmagical darkness becomes dim light. The effect lasts for 1 hour or until you end it as a bonus action. Once used, this property can’t be used again until the next dawn.
