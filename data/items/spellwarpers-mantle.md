# Spellwarper's Mantle

**description**: Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement) While wearing this magic cloak, you have a +2 bonus to saving throws you make against spells and to your AC against spell attacks. The cloak has 3 charges for the following properties and regains all expended charges daily at dawn: Attract. When a creature you can see within 30 feet is the target of a spell that targets only that creature, you can expend 1 charge and make a Constitution saving throw with a DC equal to 10 + the spell's level. On a success, the spell targets you instead of that creature. Reflect. As a reaction when you succeed on a saving throw against a spell that affects only you, or a spell attack targeting only you misses, you can expend 1 charge to turn the spell back on its caster. The spell has no effect on you and instead targets the caster, using the spell slot level, spell save DC, attack bonus, and spellcasting ability of the caster. If the spell requires concentration, it lasts until the end of the caster's next turn, even if they don't maintain their concentration on it.
