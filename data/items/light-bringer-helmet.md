# Light Bringer Helmet

**description**: Wondrous Item (Helmet), Legendary (requires attunement) Provides +2 to AC while being worn. Mighty Striker: As an action, you can select a creature within 30ft that you can see. For the next hour you gain Advantage on attack rolls against the creature; when you roll the damage for these attacks you can reroll the dice and use either total. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest. Heavenly Protector: You gain Advantage on saving throws against spells and effects that deal radiant damage. Divine Intervention: When you are reduced to 0 hit points but not killed outright, you can choose to be reduced to 1 hit point instead. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest. Godly Radiance: As an action, you can invoke the blessing of the God of Light, shedding a bright light in a 60-foot radius until you keep Concentration, up to 1 minute. During this time, you have resistance to all damage, and any hostile creatures within the radius must make a Constitution saving throw (DC 17) at the start of their turn or be blinded until the end of their turn. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until the next dawn.
