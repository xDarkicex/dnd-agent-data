# Aldrnari Blade Rapier (very rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (any sword), uncommon (enhanced attunement) Component: dragon (red, gold, or brass) breath sac This exceptionally well-crafted steel blade bears four runes at the end of its length. These runes represent the wielder’s ideals and can be found burned into the skin of its true owner. In northern clans, when such a weapon is passed from parent to child, it is accompanied by a branding ritual, bonding the blade to its new owner. Entire lineages can be identified by the brands that mark their skin. This magic weapon has 4 charges and regains 1d4 expended charges after it spends 8 continuous hours in fiery embers. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge to ignite the blade for 10 minutes, or until you sheathe it. Flames erupt along the sword’s length; it sheds bright light in a 40-foot radius and dim light for an additional 40 feet, and it deals an extra 1d4 fire damage on a hit. Enhanced Attunement. If you undergo a branding ritual to become bonded to the blade, it gains the Brand property. In addition, when the weapon regains charges, it regains all expended charges. Once you have undergone this branding ritual, you can’t willingly end your attunement to the blade until another creature willingly undertakes the branding ritual, at which point it becomes attuned to the sword instead. EA: Brand. As an action, you can expend 2 charges to cast the heat metal spell (save DC 13). Rare variant: Increase the charges to 5, the recharge to 1d4 + 1, the damage to 1d6, and the DC to 15. Heat metal is cast at 3rd level. Very rare variant: Increase the charges to 6, the recharge to 1d4 + 2, the damage to 1d8, and the DC to 16. Heat metal is cast at 4th level.
