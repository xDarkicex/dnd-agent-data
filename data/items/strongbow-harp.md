# Strongbow Harp

**description**: Strongbow Harp Weapon (Heavy Crossbow), Very Rare (requires attunement) Crafted by an adventurous halfling bard after a failed assassination attempt against him, this magic harp is also a fully functional heavy crossbow. The Strongbow Harp can be used as a musical instrument and as a heavy crossbow. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with Strongbow Harp. Tipsy Bolt. The Strongbow Harp has 3 charges. While holding the harp, you can use a bonus action and expend one of its charges to cause translucent, almost invisible poison that smells faintly of alcohol to coat a crossbow bolt loaded into it. If you are playing the harp, this bonus action does not interrupt your performance. The poison remains for 1 minute or until an attack using this crossbow bolt hits a creature. That creature must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or become poisoned for 1 hour. While poisoned that way, the creature is drunk and has disadvantage on Dexterity saving throws. The Strongbow Harp regains 1d3 charges daily at dawn.
