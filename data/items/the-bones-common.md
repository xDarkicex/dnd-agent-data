# The Bones (Common)

**description**: Weapon (Longbow), Common (Requires Attunement) Common The Bones will not fire any arrows the wielder knocks, with the string simply refusing to draw. To the Bone. To make an attack with this magic weapon, you must choose to lose 1 hit point, which manifests a bone-shard arrow. This arrow deals 1d8 + 2 necrotic damage on a hit. Dead Echoes. As a bonus action, you can speak a command word, causing groans of the dead to emit from the bow for 1 minute. The sound is audible up to 60 feet away.
