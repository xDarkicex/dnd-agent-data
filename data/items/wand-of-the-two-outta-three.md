# Wand of the Two Outta Three

**description**: Wand, uncommon (requires attunement) There are various types of Wands of the Two Outta Three, each capable of a different type of magical cantrip. Whenever you hold the wand, you can use it to cast the cantrip magically infused into it. After you use the wand, you must roll 1d6: on a result of 1 or 2, the wand no longer works until you finish a short rest.
