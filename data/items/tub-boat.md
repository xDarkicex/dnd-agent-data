# Tub-Boat

**description**: Tub-Boat Vehicle Tub-boats are small, barrel-shaped, circular vessels five to six feet in diameter, designed for one operator who steers by oar. Sturdy and cheap to build, they serve as additional vessels for families in many fishing villages across Rokugan, and as single-person landing craft aboard some Mantis vessels.
