# Pitbone Arrow (very rare variant)

**description**: Ammunition, uncommon Component: pouch of fiend ( pitcrow *) feathers These crude arrows thrum with energy, ready to shatter upon impact. The flights—pitcrow feathers—are enchanted to flare when a target is missed, turning the arrow to the ground where it erupts in a cloud of bone fragments. Hit or miss, these single-use arrows are a nuisance on the battlefield. Hit. When you hit with this arrow, the target must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or have shattered bone fragments lodged in obtrusive places within its body. While a creature has these fragments in it, it takes 1 piercing damage when it moves for the first time on a turn and whenever it takes the Attack action. As an action, an affected creature can make a DC 13 Wisdom (Medicine) check, ending the effect on itself on a success. Miss. When you miss with this arrow, the arrow changes direction to hit the nearest surface to the target, where it explodes into sharp fragments. Each creature within 5 feet of the arrow must succeed on a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw or take 1d4 piercing damage. Rare variant: Increase the DCs to 15, the damage of Hit to 2, and the damage of Miss to 1d8. Very rare variant: Increase the DCs to 16, the damage of Hit to 4, and the damage of Miss to 1d12. *See Throne of the Devourer in Motes of the Divine.
