# Unguent of Recovery

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare This small, beautifully lacquered clay urn is filled with a powerful healing salve. When full, an urn holds 10 doses of the unguent, and when found usually contains 2d4+2 doses. During a long rest, a creature proficient in Medicine may apply this salve to a creature recovering from an Injury or lasting effect. When the recovering creature finishes its long rest, the time required to recover from that Injury or lasting effect is halved, down to a minimum of one rest remaining. The unguent may be applied to multiple Injuries or lasting effects, using a new dose for every new Injury or lasting effect.
