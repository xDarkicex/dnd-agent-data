# Skithship

**description**: Skithship Wondrous item, legendary The skithship is a magical longship. It has two forms and an action can be spent by whomever is holding or touching it as they speak a command word to swap between the forms. It is either Folded—a decorative ship that weighs less than a pound and is easily stowed inside a backpack. Or it is Expanded—a full-sized longship. When Folded, all cargo and a certain number of creatures, totaling no more than 120 hit points can be stowed within it. Creatures stowed this way are unconscious even if normally immune to such a state and have no recollection of their experience inside the Folded space. Folded creatures do not require air or water to breathe. Any excess creatures beyond the hit point total, are safely ejected from the ship when it is Folded to the nearest unoccupied space. It has additional properties. All allied creatures on the ship and within 120 feet of it can speak telepathically to one another as if under the influence of the telepathic bond spell. When the ship is Folded, it regains 2d100 hit points. The ship can be Folded or Unfolded as many times as required but it only regains the hit points once; a long rest must be completed before it can heal itself again. This ship is intelligent and can communicate telepathically up to 120 feet away. It has no special purpose, other than to carry the heroes, and is not especially talkative. It does alert nearby heroes to intrusions by enemies and the like however. It also refuses to transform into a ship if the heroes are mistreating it—it only wants to sail the seas.
