# Unarmed Strike

**description**: Unarmed Strike Melee weapon Instead of using a weapon to make a melee weapon attack, you can use an unarmed strike: a punch, kick, head-‐‑butt, or similar forceful blow (none of which count as weapons). On a hit, an unarmed strike deals bludgeoning damage equal to 1 + your Strength modifier. You are proficient with your unarmed strikes. Damage: 1 Damage Type: Bludgeoning
