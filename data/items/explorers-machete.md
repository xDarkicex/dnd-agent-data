# Explorers Machete

**description**: Explorer’s Machete Weapon (short sword), uncommon Damage: 1d6+1 slashing Weight: 2 lbs. Properties: Finesse, Light You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Compass. You can use a bonus action to make the machete point north. Plant Slasher. The machete easily cuts through thick vegetation. While holding this weapon, you can move through the difficult terrain of forests, swamps, and jungles at your normal speed. Proficiency with a shortsword allows you to add your proficiency bonus to the attack roll for any attack you make with the machete.
