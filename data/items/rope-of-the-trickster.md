# Rope of the Trickster

**description**: Rope of the Trickster Wondrous Item, Uncommon This 50-foot length of rope feels slightly greasy to the touch. The rope has been infused with subtle conjuration magic. Any creature that knows the rope’s nature can touch it and use a bonus action to instantly untie any knot on its length or make any section of it slippery smooth. Creatures have disadvantage on any ability checks and saving throws related to climbing or holding on to the rope while it’s slippery.
