# Tongue of the Bard

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) This shriveled bard's tongue clips to a character's coat or cloak by a brass pin, acting as a brooch or clasp (taking up space as any similar magic item would). While you wear it, you can cast the vicious mockery cantrip (save DC 15) from it at will.
