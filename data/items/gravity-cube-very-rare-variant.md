# Gravity Cube (very rare variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, rare Component: construct plating Using the same dunamantic technology as powers the vessels of the astral plane, this cube is capable of manipulating gravity within a confined area. This cube has 6 charges and regains 1d4 + 2 expended charges daily at dawn. As an action, you can program and throw this cube at a surface you can see within 50 feet of you, where it sticks to the surface, expending charges to warp a 30-foot-radius sphere centred on that point in one of the ways described below. If an effect requires you to expend charges each turn, you expend them at the start of your turn. If you don’t, the effect ends. Nullify (1 Charge/Turn). Gravity in the area is nullified, causing all objects and creatures to drift freely. When the cube activates, each creature in the area must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw. A creature that enters the area for the first time on its turn while the effect lasts must also make this saving throw. On a failure, a creature rises vertically, up to 5 feet, and remains suspended in mid-air for the duration of the effect. On a success, a creature clings to the nearest surface. Suspended creatures can only move within the area by pushing off surfaces or other creatures. Intensify (1 Charge/Turn). Gravity in the area is intensified. For the duration, a creature that moves in the area must spend 3 feet of movement for each foot that it moves, and each creature’s jump distance is divided by 3. A flying creature that starts its turn in the area, enters the area for the first time on its turn, or tries to begin flying in the area must make a DC 15 Strength check. On a failure, it is safely pulled to the ground and has a flying speed of 0 feet until the start of its next turn. Attract (2 Charges). Each creature and unsecured object within the area must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be pulled 20 feet towards the cube. Creatures and objects that collide as a result of this each take 1d6 bludgeoning damage. Repel (2 Charges). Each creature and unsecured object within the area must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be pushed 20 feet directly away from the cube. Creatures and objects that collide as a result of this each take 1d6 bludgeoning damage. Very rare variant: Increase the DCs to 16, the charges to 9, and the recharge to 1d6 + 3.
