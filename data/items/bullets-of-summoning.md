# Bullets of Summoning

**description**: Bullets of Summoning Ammunition (Sling Bullet), Rare This simple leather pouch contains a handful of sling stones. Each stone is carved in the shape of a different small animal, wrapped around itself. A pouch of bullets of summoning contains 1d4 + 2 bullets. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with each of these magic bullets. When you hit a creature with an attack using one of these bullets, the bullet transforms into a random beast (determined by rolling a d6, as shown in the table below) and lands in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the target. The beast is friendly to you and your companions. In combat, the beast shares your initiative count, but it takes its turn immediately after yours. It can move and use its reaction on its own, but the only action it takes on its turn is the Attack action, exclusively targeting the creature you hit with the bullet that summoned it, unless you take a bonus action on your turn to command it to take another action. That action can be one in its stat block or some other action. The beast transforms back into a bullet after 5 rounds. If the beast is reduced to 0 hit points, it transforms into a mundane bullet, losing its magical properties. 1d6 Beast 1d6 Beast 1 Baboon 2 Eagle 3 Cat 4 Goat 5 Deer 6 Spider
