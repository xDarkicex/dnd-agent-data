# Puzzle Box

**description**: Wondrous item, rare Though a magic item in and of itself, this cubic box often contains far more powerful and destructive items. The puzzle box can hold one cubic foot of contents, which can’t be targeted by divination spells or perceived through scrying sensors created by divination spells. Once the box is closed, it can only be opened by solving the box’s puzzle. You can attempt to solve it by spending 24 hours working on the puzzle mechanism, after which you must succeed a DC 23 Intelligence check to open the box. You can close the box as an action, automatically scrambling the puzzle.

**properties**:

- Item Rarity: rare
