# Orrery

**description**: Type Weight Price Tellurium 2 lbs. 100 gp Calculating tellurium 2 lbs. 200 gp Standard 5 lbs. 750 gp Grand 12 lbs. 1,000 gp Calculating standard 5 lbs. 1,500 gp Calculating grand 12 lbs. 2,000 gp This complex mechanism simulates the angles and relative distances between one or more bodies in a solar system in relation to the system’s star with marks or spheres on moving gears or arms. Anyone consulting an orrery gains a +2 bonus on Intelligence checks involving bodies it models and on Wisdom (Survival) checks to navigate the void of space. An orrery can be used in combination with a sextant on a clear night to determine the time on any of the stellar bodies it models. Any orrery can be used as a focus component for the augury spell in place of that spell’s normal focus. Orreries come in many shapes and sizes and with a variety of functions in mind. A tellurion models a single planet and any moons it may have. A standard orrery tracks the inner planets of a system and their moons. A grand orrery tracks all the known planets of a system including outer planets, moons, and asteroid belts.
