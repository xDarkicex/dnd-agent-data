# Breathtaker

**description**: Weapon (Blowgun), Very Rare This slim, black lacquered tube serves as both a blowgun and smoking pipe. Intricate gold filigree patterns adorn the ends, reminiscent of wisps of smoke. As an action, you can take a deep breath through this magic weapon, drawing the air out of the lungs of a creature you can see within 100 feet. If the target needs to breathe air, it must succeed on a DC 16 Constitution saving throw or immediately begin suffocating. The target can survive for a number of rounds equal to its Constitution modifier (minimum of 1 round). At the start of its next turn, it drops to 0 hit points and is dying, and it can’t regain hit points or be stabilized until it can breathe again. The creature continues to suffocate as long as you hold your breath and maintain concentration (as if concentrating on a spell). You can hold your breath for a number of minutes equal to 1 + your Constitution modifier (minimum of 30 seconds). You can use this property three times, and regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. Encounter Ideas Fatal Attraction. An alluring stranger catches an adventurer’s attention, only to literally steal their breath away before slipping away. Prize Piper. A smoke trick contest in a seedy tavern offers this weapon as its grand prize. Lost Speech. A noble is found suffocated before she can expose a traitor. Despite no sign of struggle, foul play is suspected.
