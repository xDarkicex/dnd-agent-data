# Sanctified Partisan Spear

**description**: *Roll20 Note: This weapon should be added to a character sheet with the Sanctified Partisan to populate attacks in its untransformed form. Untransformed. Using this pair of weapons together requires the spear to be held in one hand and the shield in the other. When you are targeted by a melee weapon attack, you can use your reaction to deflect with the shield, gaining a +2 bonus to AC against that attack. If the attack misses, you can immediately make a single melee weapon attack with the spear as part of the same reaction.
