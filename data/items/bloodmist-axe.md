# Bloodmist Axe

**description**: The Red Shadows are a group of elite warriors serving an organization known as the Shadow Sanctuary. While they are highly trained in battle, they have other, darker qualities: Each member of the Red Shadows is either a necromancer or a vampire. When joining this military unit, the soldiers receive a Bloodmist Axe, among other equipment. Bloodmist Axes are not exclusive to the Red Shadows, but if you meet a group of people equipped with these weapons, you can be pretty sure who they are... Magic schools: Necromancy Cost: 12.500 gp Damage: 1d8 slashing + 1d8 necrotic Magic properties: When first found, the Bloodmist Axe appears as a normal, red-colored weapon. Its necromantic powers only become apparent after it’s used in battle. Every successful hit inflicts an additional 1d8 points of necrotic damage as the axe drains the target of its life force. This energy is transformed into a mist of blood surrounding the entire blade. The Bloodmist Axe can store up to 50 hit points this way, but it can’t take in more than 10 points a day. The necromantic damage is still added to every successful attack. When used in battle, the bloodmist protects the axe’s wielder. Any damage dealt to them is first substracted from the stored hit points. Delandra finally saw an opening. She was raised as a vampire hunter, and she had killed dozens of these vile creatures, but this enemy was tougher than most. As her opponent raised his axe, she plunged forward and drove her holy blade into his chest. He didn’t even twitch. The vampire jumped backwards as if nothing had happened, his wound closing immediately. The only thing that changed was that some of the red mist surrounding her opponent’s axe vanished. For the first time in years, she wasn’t certain that she would win this fight.
