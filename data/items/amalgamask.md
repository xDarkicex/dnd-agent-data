# Amalgamask

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) Component: phial of ooze mucus In order to remain as impartial bastions of righteousness, the Knights Penitent are forbidden from emotional outbursts or impassioned self-expression. Those most committed to this form of self-sacrifice wear this many-faced mask, which they shift to convey the appropriate emotion and prevent their true thoughts from ever being known. Fickle Faces. While wearing this mask, you have advantage on ability checks using a certain skill, depending on which face is at the front. When you first attune to the mask, and each day at dawn while you remain attuned to it, you can select the mask’s expression. You can use a bonus action to shift a different expression to the front, but spiked chains drag across your skin when you do so, and you take 2d6 slashing damage.
