# Mummy Hand

**description**: Mummy Hand Spell catalyst, very rare When you cast the vampiric touch spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, for the spell’s duration, a creature you hit with the spell’s melee attack must succeed on a Constitution saving throw against your spell save DC or become cursed with mummy rot until it dies or the curse is removed with the remove curse spell or similar magic. A target cursed with mummy rot can’t regain hit points, and its hit point maximum decreases by 3d6 for every 24 hours that elapse. If the curse reduces the target’s hit point maximum to 0, the target dies, and its body turns to dust.
