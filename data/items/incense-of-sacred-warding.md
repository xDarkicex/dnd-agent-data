# Incense of Sacred Warding

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare When lit, this magical incense produces a floral scent of lilies and frankincense, and burns for 1 hour before it is consumed. A creature who inhales the incense during a short rest gains 10 temporary hit points and has advantage on saving throws against being Charmed or Frightened for 8 hours. If the creature would drop to 0 hit points as a result of taking damage, it can end the blessing early to drop to 1 hit point instead.
