# Warhorn of the Undead

**description**: Warhorn of the Undead Wondrous item, artifact (requires attunement) The warhorn has 6 charges usable for the following abilities. It regains 1d4+2 charges daily at dawn. Each ability requires an action and expends the specified number of charges. To expend a charge and use each ability you must blow an equal number of blasts of sound through the warhorn. Grasp from the Grave. Blow the warhorn once and expend 1 charge to use this ability and call upon the undead buried beneath your feet to attack your enemies. Skeletal and ghoulish arms reach out of the ground in a 30-foot-radius centered on you and grasp and slash at the legs of hostile creatures. Each enemy within the area at the start of their turn must succeed on a DC 14 Dexterity saving throw or suffer 1d12 slashing damage and become grappled. The affected area becomes difficult terrain for enemies for 2 minutes. You can dismiss this effect at any time. Call of the Haunted. Blow the warhorn twice and expend 2 charges to call forth an undead spirit. It manifests in an unoccupied space that you can see within 90 feet. This corporeal form uses the Undead Spirit stat block When you cast the spell, choose the creature’s form: Ghostly, Putrid, or Skeletal. The spirit resembles an undead creature with the chosen form, which determines certain traits in its stat block. The creature disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or after 1 hour. The creature is an ally to you and your companions. In combat, the creature shares your initiative count, but it takes its turn immediately after yours. It obeys your verbal commands (no action required by you). If you don’t issue any, it takes the Dodge action and uses its move to avoid danger. Raze the Living. Blow the warhorn three times and expend 6 charges to use this ability. A red cloud of smoke erupts from the horn to a point you choose within a range of 120 feet and then spreads with a loud hum into a cloud of undead energy. The cloud begins as a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on the cast point and grows by 10 feet each round, up to a maximum of a 60-foot-radius sphere Each creature in the sphere must make a Constitution saving throw. A target takes 6d6 + 6 necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Any creature that is killed by this cloud is raised as a zombie , ghast , or wight under your control.
