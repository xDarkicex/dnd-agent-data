# Blood of Warrior-Saints

**description**: Potion, very rare This vial of divine-enchanted blood is only provided by the higher-ranking clerics of the Blood God. When you drink this potion, you gain the following benefits for 10 minutes: Increase your Strength score by 4, up to a maximum of 23. You gain 10 temporary hit points. You are enlarged as if the enlarge/reduce spell is cast on you (no concentration required by you).
