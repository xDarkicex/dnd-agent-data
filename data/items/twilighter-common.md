# Twilighter (Common)

**description**: Twilighter Weapon (Halberd), Common (Requires Attunement by a Creature That Can Cast at Least One Spell) Life for Death. When you cast a necromancy spell of 1st-level or higher, you gain a number of temporary hit points equal to the spell’s level. Progression The Twilighter progresses after the creature attuned to it casts 30 necromancy spells. Once the weapon progresses, it can’t do so again until the attuned creature’s proficiency bonus increases.
