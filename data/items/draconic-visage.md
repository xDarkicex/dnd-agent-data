# Draconic Visage

**description**: Draconic Visage Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) This mask bears the visage of a fearsome white-scaled dragon. When you finish a long rest, you can change the color of the mask’s scales to another color on the Draconic Visage Color table. When you wear the mask, your body becomes covered in dragon scales, granting you a +1 bonus to AC and resistance to the damage type associated with the mask’s color. You can speak and understand Draconic, and you have advantage on Charisma checks made to influence dragons. Additionally, when you wear the mask and deal damage to a creature, you can deal an extra 3d10 damage of the type associated with the mask’s color. The mask can deal extra damage in this way three times and regains all expended uses daily at dawn.
