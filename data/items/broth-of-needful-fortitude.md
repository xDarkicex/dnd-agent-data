# Broth of Needful Fortitude

**description**: Potion, very rare Favored by intelligent undead, this rich stock is brewed from the flesh and bones of humanoids and typically kept sealed in an earthenware jar. When you consume this broth, you can reduce your exhaustion level by up to 2, and you have advantage on saving throws against disease, poison, and environmental effects for 8 hours. If you are undead, you also regain 3d6 hit points.
