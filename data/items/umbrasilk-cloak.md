# Umbrasilk Cloak

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) Silver thread dances across the sheer black surface of this cloak like shadowy liquid. When worn, the cowl of the cloak descends entirely over your face, cloaking you in the safety of darkness and hiding your identity, but allowing you to see normally through the sheer fabric to a range of 30 feet. You ignore disadvantage from Sunlight Sensitivity.
