# Redemption

**description**: Weapon (greatmace), very rare (requires attunement) 1d10 bludgeoning - heavy, two-handed You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls with this magic weapon. Redemption deals an additional 1d6 radiant damage on a hit and you are immune to being frightened while attuned to it. Spells. Redemption has 4 charges. While holding it, you can use an action and expend 1 or more of its charges to cast one of the following spells (save DC 16) from it: cure wounds (1 charge), death ward (4 charges), lesser restoration (2 charges), or revivify (3 charges) Redemption regains 1d4 expended charges after a long rest.
