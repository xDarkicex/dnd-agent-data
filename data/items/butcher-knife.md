# Butcher Knife

**description**: Weapon (dagger), very rare This wide, gently curved knife is actually a chef’s knife, not a cleaver, but gained its name for the horrific acts it was used for. This weapon functions as a +2 dagger, except that it deals 2d4 damage on a hit rather than 1d4. Additionally, whenever the weapon scores a critical hit while trying to inflict an Injury, the severity of the Injury is automatically increased by 1 (inflicting an Injury even if no thresholds were met). If the Injury was already at the highest severity it could be, the target gains a special Injury of Deadly severity. This Injury has no effect other than to count toward the number of Injuries required for the target to become Imperiled.
