# Deadshot Visor

**description**: Deadshot Visor Wondrous item, uncommon This ancient device looks like a pair of thick spectacles composed of a black glassy material and connected by an orichalcum wire to a small steel pack worn on the hip. When you wear this item, a red or green targeting reticle appears on the visor over your left or right eye, and you gain advantage on any ranged attack rolls you make with advanced weaponry such as firearms. The visor doesn’t work with longbows, crossbows, or other more primitive ranged weapons.
