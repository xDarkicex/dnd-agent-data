# Astral Echo Earrings

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) These magic earrings are forged from a fallen star and imbued with astral energy. Astral Veil. As an action, you can create an auditory illusion of a celestial hum in a 10-foot radius around you for 1 hour or until you end it as a bonus action. The illusion masks conversations and sounds within its area, making them inaudible to creatures outside the area. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn. Eldritch Echo. As an action, you can open a sensory connection with a creature you are familiar with. If the creature is willing, you can both see, hear, smell, taste, and feel what the other is feeling for 1 minute. You can use this connection to communicate verbally with each other, though doing so has an odd, reverberating echo as you each hear yourself talking through your own and the other's ears at the same time. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
