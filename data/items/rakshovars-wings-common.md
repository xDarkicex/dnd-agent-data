# Rakshovar's Wings (Common)

**description**: Weapon (Longbow), Common (Requires Attunement) Common While carrying this magic weapon, you can cast levitate on yourself (no concentration required). If you draw the bow to make an attack while levitating, the spell ends, and you fall. Once cast, the spell can’t be cast from the bow again until you finish a long rest.
