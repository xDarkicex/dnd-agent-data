# Power Gauntlet

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon This armored gauntlet emits a constant, soft hum and electrical sparkles from time to time; it holds a highly electrified charge in its hidden capacitors. When you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack held in the hand with the Power Gauntlet, you can use your bonus action to electrify the weapon. The target must succeed on a CON SAVE 13 or take 1d12 lightning damage and be stunned until the end of its next turn; on a save, they take half damage and are not stunned, but their speed becomes 0 until the end of their turn. Once activated, the Gauntlet needs to be recharged before you can use this effect again, which can be done while completing a Short Rest.
