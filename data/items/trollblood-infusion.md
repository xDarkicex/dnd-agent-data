# Trollblood Infusion

**description**: Potion, rare An enchanted syringe of troll’s blood, this “potion” causes the user to temporarily gain the enhanced regenerative qualities of a troll. For one minute after injection, the recipient regains 2d8 hit points at the start of each of its turns. Additionally, as an action, it may make a Constitution saving throw with a bonus of +8 (or with advantage if its own bonus is higher) to heal from an Injury it suffers from. If it succeeds, the lasting effect of the Injury lasts for half the usual time, and the effect of the infusion ends.
