# Sir Wrenchinald the Third

**description**: Weapon (mace), very rare (requires attunement) Component: fey psyche Sir Wrenchinald the Third is an eccentric wrench, given sentience after years of being used to create items imbued with magic. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While holding this weapon, you have advantage on Intelligence checks and Intelligence saving throws. The wrench is contained in a scabbard; unsheathing it reveals a blade and allows it to be used as a dagger instead of a mace. If you aren’t attuned to the wrench when you unsheathe the dagger, you take 1d4 bludgeoning damage as the wrench closes around your hand. Foul Mouth. While holding this weapon, you can use a bonus action to cast vicious mockery (save DC 16). When the spell is cast in this way, Sir Wrenchinald hurls insults at the target. Pun Master. As an action, you can hold out Sir Wrenchinald toward a creature, causing it to spurt out a series of puns as you cast hideous laughter (save DC 16) on the creature. This property can be used three times, regaining all expended uses daily at dawn. Sentience. The wrench is a sentient lawful neutral mace with an Intelligence of 18, a Wisdom of 10, and a Charisma of 14. It has hearing and darkvision out to a range of 120 feet. The wrench communicates by speaking out loud and can speak, read, and understand Binary (a language spoken in the Clockwork Arcadia), Common, and Gnomish. Personality. While Sir Wrenchinald is being wielded, it emits a constant stream of peculiar observations and insults, directed at anyone who would listen. Sir Wrenchinald values intelligence and mechanical skill above all, and may occasionally challenge its wielder with contests of wit in order to test them. Conflict arises if it feels that its wielder doesn’t match its intelligence or makes decisions it views as foolish. Although Sir Wrenchinald acts as if all challenges are beneath it, it is easily intrigued by a puzzle or strange problem.
