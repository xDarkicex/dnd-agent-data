# Threefold Arrow

**description**: Threefold Arrow Weapon (arrow), rare This arrow has a thick brown shaft with blue and green feathers. Its shaft splits into three separate arrowheads, each tipped with a black metal head. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls with this magic arrow. When launched, it splits into three separate arrows that target the same creature; you make a separate attack roll for each arrow. Once the arrow splits, it loses its enchantment.
