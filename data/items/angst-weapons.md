# Angst Weapons

**description**: Weapon (any melee weapon), rare Since angst is a material that interacts with blood, it is commonly used in many everyday items, including weapons. Angst weapons are crafted with a cavity in the middle of them, which is later filled with angst. This angst is connected to the hilt with tiny tubes, and the spiked hilt of the weapon has a space in which a blood shell can be inserted. There is a button on the back of the hilt to move the blood in the shell through the angst, which ejects the shell afterwards, making room for a new one. You can take a bonus action to push the button and activate the weapon. Once activated, your successful weapon attacks made with the angst weapon deal an extra 1d6 fire damage for 1 minute. In case you lack blood shells, you can squish the spiked hilt as a bonus action, which drains your blood. You take 3 piercing damage that can’t be reduced by any means, and the weapon is activated in the same way described above. Attacks made with angst weapons are magical owing to the nature of the angst crystals. The magical properties of this weapon keep working in areas devoid of magic, such as an area affected by the antimagic field spell, again, owing to the nature of the angst.
