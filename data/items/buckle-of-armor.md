# Buckle of Armor

**description**: Buckle of Armor Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) This round, silver-plated belt buckle has been engraved to resemble a small, round shield with a moonstone for the shield boss. When worn, so long as you aren’t wearing any other armor, your base Armor Class is 17. You may not add your Dexterity bonus to your armor class, but you can enhance your armor class with other items, such as a shield or a magic ring.
