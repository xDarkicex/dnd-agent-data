# Gnomeskin Satchel

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) A gnomeskin satchel grants the ability to change the appearance of its contents; whatever is within it can be made to look like any other object of similar size or shape or overall volume. Despite the origin of the item's material, gnomeskin satchels are frequently passed down through generations of gnome families as prized heirlooms.
