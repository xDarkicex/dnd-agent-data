# Waxing Moon Chakram

**description**: Weapon (Dart), Uncommon This magic dart is crescent-shaped and glows with a faint light. It deals Slashing damage instead of Piercing damage and flies back to your hand immediately after each ranged attack you make with it. Waxing Light. The dart has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. When you hit a creature with an attack using the dart, you can expend 1 charge to deal an extra 1d8 Radiant damage to the target and cause it to emit dim light in a 5-foot radius until the start of your next turn. While the creature emits this light, attack rolls against it have advantage and it can’t benefit from being Invisible.
