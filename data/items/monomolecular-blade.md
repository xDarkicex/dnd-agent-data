# Monomolecular Blade

**description**: Weapon (Longsword), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This thin, almost translucent longsword has an edge that shimmers with a faint light. Atomic Sharpness You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with the weapon and your attack rolls with it score a Critical Hit on a roll of 19 or 20 on the d20. Splitting Edge When you hit a target with the sword, you can choose to deal an extra 1d10 Slashing damage to the target or 2d10 Slashing damage if the target is an object. When you do, roll a d4. On a roll of 1, the sword's edge is temporarily dulled and you can't use this property again until the next dawn.
