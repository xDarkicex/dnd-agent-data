# Trimauler (Very Rare)

**description**: Trimauler Weapon (Trident), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Trimauler’s Touch . The trident has 3 charges. As an action, you can expend a charge to restore 4d4 + 4 hit points to all creatures of your choice within 20 feet of you. The trident regains all expended charges when you finish a long rest. Progression The Trimauler progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. As the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, so does the weapon’s rarity.
