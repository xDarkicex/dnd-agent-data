# Lampyridae Lantern

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon This hooded glass lantern is filled with bioluminescent flies. Entrancing Glow You can open or close the lantern's hood as an Action. While the hood is open, the lantern casts Bright Light in a 20-foot radius and Dim Light for an additional 20 feet. As a Bonus Action, you can cause one of these effects of your choice for 1 minute. Luring Light. The first time a creature starts its turn within the lantern's Bright Light or enters it on its turn, it must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom save or be Charmed. While Charmed in this way, the creature is Incapacitated and must spend its turn moving toward the light. The creature can repeat its save at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success. The effect also ends for a creature if it takes damage. Soothing Light. Creatures within the lantern's Bright Light have Advantage on saves against being Charmed or Frightened. Once the lantern has been used to create an effect, it can't create that effect again until the next dawn.
