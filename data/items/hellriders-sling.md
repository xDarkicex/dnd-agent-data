# Hellrider's Sling

**description**: Weapon (Sling), Common This sling is made from hide, hair, and sinew of a hell hound and its pouch is marked with an abyssal rune. Hellfire Strike. When you make a ranged weapon attack using this sling, you can imbue the ammunition with hellfire. On a hit, the target ignites with flames and takes 1d4 Fire damage at the start of each of its turns. A creature can extinguish the flames on itself or another creature within 5 feet of it by using its action and succeeding on a DC 10 Dexterity check. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
