# Harp of Silence

**description**: Harp of Silence Wondrous item, uncommon You must be proficient with a string instrument to use this small lap harp. When you use an action to play this item, you cast the silence spell with the area of effect centered on yourself. Once used, this property of the harp can’t be used again until the next dawn.
