# Thorn the Caustic Torment (Rare)

**description**: Thorn, the Caustic Torment Weapon (Halberd), Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. When you make an attack using this weapon, the target takes an extra 1d6 acid damage on a hit. You can cast acid splash from this magic weapon (save DC 11 + your proficiency bonus). Once cast, the spell can’t be cast this way again for 10 minutes. Corrosion. When you hit a creature wearing nonmagical armor with an attack using Thorn , the armor is partially dissolved and takes a permanent and cumulative −1 penalty to the AC it offers. The armor is destroyed if it is reduced to an AC of 10. Progression Thorn progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, so does the weapon’s rarity.
