# Fell Pauldron

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic pauldron is crafted from sturdy leather and inset with bestial spikes. While wearing the pauldron, you have resistance to Acid and Poison damage. Abyssal Transformation. As an action, you can magically transform yourself, taking on demonic features. For 1 minute, you gain the following benefits: Your Strength score increases by 4. You gain temporary hit points equal to twice the number of Hit Dice you have. When a creature within 5 feet of you hits you with a melee attack, it takes 1d8 Acid damage. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
