# Book of Bog

**description**: Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement) This weighty tome is a relic gifted by Oghma, the god of knowledge, speech, and writing, to a (mostly) pure-hearted goblin named Bog. Although its pages are blank until queried, it is said the book’s size represents the burden of responsibility that comes with nearly unlimited knowledge. While attuned to this item, you can open the book and ask it a question. If Oghma knows the answer to the question, the right page fills with the answer and the left page fills with a quest that you must complete in service to Oghma before the book can be used again. The quest provides you with any information you need to get started, including a map if necessary. You may not end your attunement with this item until you complete the quest it has given you.
