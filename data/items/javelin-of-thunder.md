# Javelin of Thunder

**description**: Javelin of Thunder Weapon (Javelin), Uncommon The shaft of this javelin is made from a dark wood decorated with silver filigree shaped like clouds. When you hold it, it gives off a low humming sound. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. In addition, when you hit a target with an attack using this weapon, you can choose for it to release a clap of thunder. Each creature within 10 feet of the target (excluding you) must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 2d8 thunder damage and is deafened until the end of their next turn. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and isn’t deafened. You can use this property twice, regaining all expended uses when you finish a long rest.
