# Crosstacean Light Crossbow (very rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (any crossbow), uncommon (requires attunement) Component: beast (giant crab) pincer The longevity of lobsters is well known. Using biomancy, this phenomenon can be reversed, resulting in a weapon that can magically age and necrose a target. This weapon deals an extra 1d4 necrotic damage to any target it hits. The Seize. While holding this weapon, you can use a bonus action to enhance one piece of loaded ammunition with debilitating, arthritic energy that causes the target’s joints to seize solid. If the next attack you make with the weapon hits, the target must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or become restrained until the end of your next turn. If the target fails by 5 or more, it is paralysed instead of restrained. After this property has been used, roll a d6. On a 3 or lower, the lobster’s colour fades to grey, and this property can’t be used again until the next dawn. Rare variant: Increase the damage to 1d6 and the DC to 15. If you roll a 3 on the d6, you can use The Seize property again. Very rare variant: Increase the damage to 1d8 and the DC to 16. If you roll a 3 or a 2 on the d6, you can use The Seize property again.
