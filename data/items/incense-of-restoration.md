# Incense of Restoration

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon This magical incense burns for 1 hour and produces a pleasant scent of lavender and aloe. Inhaling the incense during a short rest cures a creature of any temporary blindness, deafness, diseases, or reduction of their hit point maximum, and reduces their exhaustion level by one.
