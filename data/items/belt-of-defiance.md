# Belt of Defiance

**description**: Wondrous Item, uncommon This sturdy, leather belt is worn with use and scarred from countless battles. While wearing this belt, if you would be dealt damage while at zero hit points, you take no damage instead. Once this ability has triggered, it cannot be triggered again until the next dawn. Additionally, when you are reduced to zero hit points, your Setback Injury threshold is reduced by 5 less than normal, to a minimum reduction of 5.
