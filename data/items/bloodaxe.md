# Bloodaxe

**description**: Bloodaxe Weapon (greataxe), very rare (requires attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic axe. The axe deals an extra 1d6 necrotic damage to creatures that aren’t constructs or undead. If you reduce such a creature to 0 hit points with an attack using this axe, you gain 10 temporary hit points. This axe is forged from a dark, rust-colored metal and once belonged to the goliath barbarian Grog Strongjaw of Vox Machina. Damage : 1d12+2 Damage Type : Slashing Secondary Damage: 1d6 Secondary Damage Type: Necrotic Properties : Heavy, Two-Handed Weight : 7
