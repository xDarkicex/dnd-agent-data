# Skillplex

**description**: Skillware Augment (head), varies (requires affinity) This implant grants simulated knowledge and training in a particular skill. It is a rare augment. With this augment, you can add your proficiency bonus to skill checks for one skill or tool of your choice. It provides no benefit for skills in which you are already proficient. Skillplex. The very rare version of this augment holds data for up to three skills, which you choose at installation. You choose which skill is active and gain the skillware benefit for that skill. As an action, you can switch to a different skill, which then becomes your active skill. You can’t have more than one active skill at at time.
