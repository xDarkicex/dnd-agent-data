# Voidshot Bolt (rare variant)

**description**: Ammunition (arrow or bolt), very rare Component: aberration (beholder) main eye This jagged black, glass arrowhead drinks in light and seems lit as if with an unholy nimbus. Spelldrain. When you hit a creature with an attack using this ammunition, it projects a 20-foot-radius sphere of magic suppression, centred on the creature, for 1 minute. When a creature in the sphere tries to cast a spell, it must make a DC 16 ability check using its spellcasting ability. On a failure, the spell fails, and the spell slot is absorbed by the black hole. On a success, it casts the spell as normal. After this property of the ammunition is used, the ammunition is destroyed. Rare variant: Reduce the radius of the sphere to 10 feet and the DC to 15. When a creature attempts to cast a spell within the area of magic suppression and fails its saving throw, the spell fails but the spell slot isn’t lost.
