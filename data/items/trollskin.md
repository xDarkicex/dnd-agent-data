# Trollskin

**description**: Master armor property Components: Masterwork suit of exotic medium armor This suit of armor is made of or lined with troll hide. While wearing this armor, you can use a bonus action to regain hit points equal to 1d10 + your craftsman level. Once you use this property, you can’t do so again until you finish a short or long rest.
