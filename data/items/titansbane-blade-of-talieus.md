# Titansbane, Blade of Talieus

**description**: Weapon (longsword), legendary (requires attunement) Attacks made with this weapon have a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls. When a giant or celestial is hit by this weapon, the creature takes an extra 2d6 necrotic damage. Gygans and cyclopes are also affected. Titansbane is sentient and wishes to be returned to its master. It was forged by Talieus, the ancient titan who pulls Lutheria’s barge. It was wielded against Sydon by Hergeron and then lost in the War of the Titans.
