# Turtle Mace (Legendary)

**description**: Turtle Mace Weapon (Mace), Legendary (Requires Attunement) You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Push. Once per turn, when you hit a creature with this magic weapon, the target must succeed on a DC 19 Strength saving throw or be pushed back 15 feet. Steam Cloud. This weapon has 3 charges which is regained when you finish a long rest. As part of an attack you make with the Turtle Mace , you can expend 1 charge to create a concentrated cloud of steam in a 10-foot radius around you. Each creature in the area (other than you) must make a DC 19 Constitution saving throw, taking 4d12 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. Additionally, the area is heavily obscured until the end of your next turn.
