# Speak No Evil (common variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Component: giant tooth This headgear – a gag consisting of two leaden ‘hands’ connected by thick leather straps – is designed to make speech impossible. Applying the Gag. As an action, you can attempt to gag a creature you are grappling. Make a Strength (Athletics) check contested by the target’s Strength (Athletics) or Dexterity (Acrobatics) check. On a success, you get the gag in place and the leather straps magically fasten. The Gagged. The gagged creature can’t verbalise in any way, which includes speaking, casting spells that require a verbal component, or even simply humming and grunting. In addition, after the creature casts a spell, the gag heats up and the creature takes 1d4 fire damage plus an extra 1d4 fire damage per level of the spell. Removing the Gag. You and any creature you designate when you use the gag can use an action to remove it. The gagged creature or a creature within reach of it can use its action to make a DC 20 Strength or DC 15 Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) check, removing the gag on a success. The knock spell can also unfasten the leather straps, causing the gag to fall off. Common variant: Reduce the DCs to 13 and 10, respectively. The gag doesn’t deal damage to a creature that casts a spell.
