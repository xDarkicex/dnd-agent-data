# Ankh Dagger

**description**: Ankh Dagger Weapon, uncommon This silver-bladed dagger is shaped like a classic ankh. A scabbard covers the blade, so the dagger can be used as a holy symbol or a spell focus. When the scabbard is removed, the hoop can be used as the handle, and when wielded as a weapon, the ankh dagger is +1 to hit and damage.

- Item Type: Weapon (dagger)
- Properties: Finesse, Light, Range, Thrown
