# Medal of Muscle

**description**: Medal of Muscle Wondrous item, common You can squeeze this medal tightly in the palm of your hand as an action. Doing so gives you advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws for 1 hour. Once this property has been used, it can’t be used again, and the medal becomes nonmagical.
