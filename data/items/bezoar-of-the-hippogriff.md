# Bezoar of the Hippogriff

**description**: Wondrous item, rare While this bezoar sits in your stomach, you have advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on sight. In addition, you can use a bonus action to sprout eagle wings from your back, gaining a flying speed equal to your walking speed for 1 minute. If you are flying when the duration expires, you descend at a rate of 30 feet per round until you land. When you’ve used the wings for a total of 1 hour, you can’t use them again until you finish a long rest.
