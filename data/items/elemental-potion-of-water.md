# Elemental Potion of Water

**description**: Potion, Very Rare Water specked with icy crystals swirls inside this glass bottle. When you drink the contents of this potion, ice forms on your body for 1 hour, granting you the following benefits: You have immunity to Cold damage and resistance to Fire damage. You ignore difficult terrain created by ice or snow. The ground in a 10-foot radius around you is covered in ice, making it difficult terrain for other creatures. As a bonus action, you can end the effect early to create a 30-foot cone of frost extending from your outstretched hand in a direction of your choice. Each creature in the area must succeed on a DC 17 Constitution saving throw or take 4d8 Cold damage and until the end of its next turn, its speed is halved and it must choose whether it gets an action, a bonus action, or a move on its turn; it gets only one of the three.
