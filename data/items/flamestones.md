# Flamestones

**description**: Flamestones Wondrous item, uncommon Each bundle contains five small red gems. When smashed against the ground, the stones jostle and explode, as if the burning hands spell was cast. This item may only be used once. If only one gem is crushed at a time, a small fire is started instead.
