# Healer's Kit

**description**: Healer's Kit Adventuring gear This kit is a leather pouch containing bandages, salves, and splints. The kit has ten uses. As an action, you can expend one use of the kit to stabilize a creature that has 0 hit points, without needing to make a Wisdom (Medicine) check . Weight : 3
