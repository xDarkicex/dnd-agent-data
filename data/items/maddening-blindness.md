# Maddening blindness

**description**: Contact or Injury This poisonous paste is a mixture of the crushed spores of bioluminescent fungi. A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be blinded for 1d4 hours. The poison lingers in the creature’s body even after the blindness fades, causing the creature to have nightmares the next time it rests. The creature must repeat the saving throw when it finishes its next long rest. On a failed save, the creature gains only the benefits of a short rest instead.
