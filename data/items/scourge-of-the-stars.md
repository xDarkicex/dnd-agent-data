# Scourge of the Stars

**description**: Scourge of the Stars Weapon (whip), rare This whip ends in five strands of brown leather. Each strand is studded with shards of glass in a different color; red, purple, yellow, blue, and green. You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls you make with this whip. When you hit with it, you deal an extra 2d6 damage, according to the color of the shards as determined by the table below. d6 Shards’ Color 1 Red (fire damage) 2 Purple (necrotic damage) 3 Yellow (acid damage) 4 Blue (cold damage) 5 Green (poison damage) 6 Multicolored (psychic damage). In addition, the target must succeed on a DC 12 Wisdom saving throw or become stunned until the end of its next turn.
