# Seven-Flavored Tea of Forgetfulness

**description**: Potion, very rare This potent brew is made to erase the memories of the souls of the deceased in the Lower Planes. When you drink this tea, you must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or immediately and permanently forget all the events of your life. You remember your name and recognize people you knew as familiar faces, but you have no context in which to place them. The memory loss lasts until removed by the greater restoration spell or similar magic.
