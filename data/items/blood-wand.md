# Blood Wand

**description**: Wand, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This magic wand is infused with a unicorn's blood. Blood Infusion. When you cast a spell of 1st level or higher while holding this wand, you can use your blood to fuel the spell. You take 1d10 Necrotic damage and your hit point maximum is reduced by the same amount until you finish a long rest (this damage and effect can't be reduced or negated in any way). When you do, the spell is empowered in one of the following ways of your choice: Bane. One creature affected by the spell must roll a d6 and subtract the number rolled from the first saving throw it makes against the spell. Gain. The spell takes effect as if it was cast using a spell slot one level higher. Pain. All damage dealt by the spell becomes Necrotic damage and you can roll a d10 and add the result to one damage roll of the spell.
