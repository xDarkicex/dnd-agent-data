# Necklace of Invisibility

**description**: Necklace of Invisibility Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This necklace has 5 charges and regains 1d4 expended charges daily at dawn . While wearing this necklace, you can use a bonus action to expend a charge and cast the invisibility spell on yourself. You remain invisible until the necklace is removed, you attack or cast a spell, or you use a bonus action to become visible again.
