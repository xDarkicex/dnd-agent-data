# Light Refractor

**description**: Light Refractor Mod (armor), uncommon (requires affinity) When you activate this mod, Wisdom (Perception) checks made to see you have disadvantage, and you have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide, as your shield generator refracts the surrounding light to camouflage you. Activating the mod requires an action.
