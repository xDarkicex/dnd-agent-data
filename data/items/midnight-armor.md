# Midnight Armor

**description**: Armor (Light or Hide), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) Spun of pure shadow and invisible in sunlight, armor of this type is typically of shadow goblin or derro construction. While wearing this armor, you gain a +1 bonus to AC and resistance to radiant damage. In addition, you have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks.
