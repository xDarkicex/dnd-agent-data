# Thurimbar Rod

**description**: Thurimbar Rod Rod, common The Thurimbar rod is a magical instrument developed by the gnomes of Zilargo. While holding the rod, you can use an action to produce auditory illusions that unerringly replicate the sound of any instrument with which you’re proficient, with an effect similar to the minor illusion spell, with the sound emanating from the rod. To determine the quality of the performance, make a Charisma check with the musical instrument you’re replicating. If you are proficient with the Thurimbar rod itself, you can produce unearthly music unlike any natural instrument.
