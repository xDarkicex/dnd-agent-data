# Sin: Pride

**description**: Item modification, rare (optional or enhanced attunement) The Task. The fiend requests a medium task, such as refusing to accept help or healing for 3 days, refusing the next gift or donation offered to you, or insisting on fighting a creature of CR 4 or higher alone. OA or EA: Pride. While the item is on your person, you can use a bonus action to cast the bless spell on yourself (no concentration required). While you are blessed in this way, you become hard-headed; you can’t benefit from the Help action and you are always considered an unwilling target for other creatures’ spells. Once this property of the item has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
