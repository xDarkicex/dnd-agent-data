# Circlet of Holly

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) While wearing this circlet, you gain the following benefits: Language of the Fey. You can speak and understand Sylvan. Friend of the Fey. You have advantage on ability checks to interact socially with fey creatures. Poison Sense. You know if any food or drink you are holding contains poison.
