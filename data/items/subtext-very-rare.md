# Subtext (Very Rare)

**description**: Subtext Weapon (Warhammer), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon and it deals an extra 2d6 psychic damage on a hit. Tragedy . When you roll a 20 on your attack roll with this magic weapon, the target is frightened of you 1 minute. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make a Wisdom saving throw if it no longer has line of sight to you, ending the condition on a success. The DC is equal to 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Charisma modifier. In addition, when a creature becomes frightened by Tragedy, it must drop whatever it is holding at the start of its next turn and use all of its movement to move away from you by the safest available route before it can take any other actions. Progression Subtext progresses each time its attuned wielder completes an adventure worthy of being turned into a play (what satisfies this definition is at the GM’s discretion). While the weapon prefers tragedies and horrors, any compelling story suffices.
