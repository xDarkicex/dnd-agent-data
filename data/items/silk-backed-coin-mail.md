# Silk-Backed Coin Mail

**description**: Made of silver coins from conquered nations and backed by a layer of quilted red wormsilk, these hauberks are typically awarded to soldiers, mercenaries, and generals with conspicuous valor in the campaign that conquered the land from where the coins originated. While wearing this armor, you have resistance to piercing damage from nonmagical weapons. If the piercing damage is from a critical hit, the armor loses this property until you spend a short rest repairing it (no special tools or check required).

- Properties: Stealth Disadvantage
