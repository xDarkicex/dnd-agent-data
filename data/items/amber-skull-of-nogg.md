# Amber Skull of Nogg

**description**: Amber Skull of Nogg Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) Named after an ogre magi warlord who terrorized the wastes for several decades and was infamous for his collection of skulls, these objects look like normal human, elf, or orc skulls constructed out of semiprecious stone. The saving throw DC for any spell cast from a skull is DC 15. Amber Skull (Rare). While holding this yellow skull, you have resistance to psychic damage and you can cast suggestion from it once per day.
