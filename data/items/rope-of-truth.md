# Rope of Truth

**description**: Rope of Truth Wondrous Item, Uncommon This 50-foot length of rope has thin strands of gold woven along its length. The rope has been infused with subtle divination magic. The rope glows golden yellow whenever a creature that is restrained by it speaks a deliberate lie.
