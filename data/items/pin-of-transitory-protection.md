# Pin of Transitory Protection

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon This simple-looking pin or charm is made of fragile, decorated ceramic. Spiderweb cracks run through its surface. While attached to an article of clothing, it protects the wearer from harm. The first time the wearer would gain an Injury of Handicap severity or higher, the charm breaks and the Injury is avoided. A broken charm has no power, but is worth 25gp.
