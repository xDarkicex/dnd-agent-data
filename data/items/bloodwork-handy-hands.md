# Bloodwork Handy Hands

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) You’ve enhanced your hands with bloodwork tools. You now have a mechanical hand with small blood tubes within. You can use your action to alter the shape of your hands and turn them into tools, or vice versa. You can turn your hands into three artisan’s tools of your choice.
