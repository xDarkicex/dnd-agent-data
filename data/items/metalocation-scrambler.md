# Metalocation Scrambler

**description**: Metalocation Scrambler Utility, rare (requires affinity) This sorium trinket is usually found in the form of a necklace or bracelet. While wearing this trinket, you are hidden from clairsentient esper powers. You can’t be targeted by such powers or and you can’t be perceived supernatural perception. You also can’t be targeted by digital signal locators such as GPS trackers or sensor tags.
