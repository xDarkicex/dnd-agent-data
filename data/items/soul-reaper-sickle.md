# Soul Reaper Sickle

**description**: Soul Reaper Sickle Weapon (sickle), rare Damage: 1d6+1 piercing Weight: 2 lb. Properties: Light You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Soul Reaper. Once per turn, you can use this weapon to slice at your enemy’s soul. On a hit, the target must make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw. If it fails, the target takes an additional 2d6 necrotic damage, and you regain hit points equal to half the amount of necrotic damage dealt. On a success, there is no effect. This attack has no effect on undead or fiends. Zalarak’s Curse. If you are killed while holding this weapon and are not raised within 1 hour, your body rises as a ghoul . These weapons were used by the hordes of warrior ghouls raised by the necromancer Zalarak. Most were destroyed during the War of the Risen Dead, but some of the sickles survived, hidden by the sands and wielded by the ghouls that outlived their master.
