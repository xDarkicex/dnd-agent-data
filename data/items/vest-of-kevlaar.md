# Vest of Kevlaar

**description**: Vest of Kev’laar Wondrous Item, rare Weight: ¼ lb. You gain a +1 bonus to AC while you wear this vest, which requires no armor proficiency. In addition, you have resistance to piercing damage from nonmagical weapons while you wear this vest. The great spellweaver Alistair Kev’laar created these lightweight vests that can be worn under clothes or armor. Many have been passed down through generations of noble families.
