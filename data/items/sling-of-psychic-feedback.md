# Sling of Psychic Feedback

**description**: Sling of Psychic Feedback Weapon (Sling), Rare This sling is embroidered with thin, reflective thread in a nesting fractal pattern. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When an attack made using this weapon deals damage to a creature concentrating on a spell, the target must make a choice: they can choose to end their concentration willingly or make a Constitution saving throw to maintain concentration at disadvantage. On a failed save, the target takes 1d8 psychic damage per level of the spell they were concentrating on.
