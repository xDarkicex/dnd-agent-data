# Aeromancer's Fan

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) This fan is made from purple feathers attached to a handle that clasps a bright, blue crystal. Oxygen Manipulation This fan has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. As an Action, you can expend 1 charge to produce one of these effects in a 20-foot Sphere centered on the fan for 1 minute or until you end the effect (no action required). Oxygen Surge. You enrich the air, causing the following effects: Fire damage dealt to targets in the area is increased by 1d6. The radius of light created by any flames in the area is doubled. A creature that starts its turn in the area gains 5 Temporary Hit Points (creatures that don't breathe oxygen don't gain this benefit). Oxygen Depletion. You suck oxygen from the air, causing the following effects: Fire damage dealt to targets in the area is reduced by 1d6 (to a minimum of 1). The radius of light created by non-magical flames in the area is halved. A creature that starts its turn in the area must succeed on a DC 15 CON save or suffer 1 Exhaustion level (creatures that don't breathe oxygen are immune to this effect). All levels of Exhaustion gained this way are removed when the creature ends its turn outside the area.
