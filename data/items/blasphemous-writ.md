# Blasphemous Writ

**description**: Scroll, uncommon The Infernal runes inscribed upon this vellum scroll radiate a faint, crimson glow. When you use this spell scroll of command , the save DC is 15 instead of 13, and you can also affect targets that are undead or that don’t understand your language.
