# Rain of Chaos

**description**: Weapon (longbow), uncommon (requires attunement) This magic weapon imbues arrows fired from it with random energies. When you hit with an attack using this magic bow, the target takes an extra 1d6 damage. Roll a d8. The number rolled determines the damage type of the extra damage. d8 Damage Type 1 Acid 2 Cold 3 Fire 4 Lightning 5 Necrotic 6 Poison 7 Radiant 8 Thunder
