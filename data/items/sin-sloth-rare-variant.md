# Sin: Sloth (rare variant)

**description**: Item modification, uncommon (optional or enhanced attunement) The Task. The fiend requests a minor task, such as refusing to take a watch when it’s your turn, leaving dirty dishes in the sink, or not bathing for 3 weeks before an important meeting. OA or EA: Sloth. While the item is on your person, you can cast the slow spell (save DC 13). For the spell’s duration, your speed is halved and you can’t take reactions. After this property of the item has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn. Rare variant: Increase the DC to 15. The Sloth property can be used twice, regaining all expended uses daily at dawn. The fiend asks for a medium task, such as deliberately not taking an action in combat once a day for a week, or refusing to take the Help action for a week.
