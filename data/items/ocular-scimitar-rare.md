# Ocular Scimitar (Rare)

**description**: Weapon (Scimitar), Rare (Requires Attunement) Common While holding this magic weapon, you can use an action to cast minor illusion (spell save DC 10 + your proficiency bonus). You can cast the spell this way a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. Uncommon You can now cast minor illusion from the weapon at will. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. Rare Everywhere All at Once. When you hit a creature with this weapon, you can choose up to four additional creatures within 30 feet. The original target and each additional target must make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be stunned until the start of their next turn. Once used, you can’t use this property again until you finish a long rest.
