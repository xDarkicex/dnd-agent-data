# Deathlord's Guard

**description**: Armor (Shield), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This magic shield is made from gold-embossed wood and is decorated with a grotesque skull on its front. Deathlord's Curse. As a reaction to a creature missing you with a melee attack, you can force the creature to make a DC 13 Charisma saving throw. On a failure, the creature is cursed until the end of its next turn. While cursed in this way, the creature has disadvantage on attack rolls. Deathlord's Guardian. If you start your turn Unconscious and with 0 hit points, a skeletal figure springs forth from the shield in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of you. Your spirit inhabits the skeletal figure, which has the statistics of a skeleton , except it has your Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma scores, your proficiencies, and your memories, personality, and alignment. The skeleton takes its turn in combat immediately after yours. The skeleton crumbles to dust and magically returns to the shield after 1 minute, if it is reduced to 0 hit points, or if you are no longer Unconscious. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
