# Staff of the Archmagus

**description**: An ancient staff harvested from the Grenadilla tree is pitch black through and through. Imbude by the arts of an archmagus over centuries, this staff has been honed and imfused with the powers of a a master of their craft. This staff will yield its full power to one who has studied the great arts. Cantrips: Light, Mage Hand, Prestidigitation, Dancing Lights Level 1: Alarm, Expeditious Retreat, Feather Fall, Magic Missile Level 2: Knock, Misty Step, Mirror Image Level 3: Counterspell, Fly, Lightning Bolt Level 4: Dimension Door, Phantasmal Killer, Stoneskin Level 5: Conjure Elemental, Cone of Cold, Hold Monster Level 6: Globe of Invulnerability, Guards and Wards Level 7: Magnificent Mansion, Prismatic Spray Level 8: Power Word Stun Level 9: True Polymorph Expended spells will recharge at the break of dawn. There is a 3% chance per upper level spell (Level 6 or greater) used that the staff will shatter during recharge due to the great forces required to restore the spells. A wizard capable of casting a particular level decreases that chance to zero for that level.
