# Archer’s Armband

**description**: Archer’s Armband Wondrous item, uncommon From this armband dangles a small carved face, representing Skathi, a Vanir from the Lost Lands. Archers wear this talisman to improve their aim. While the armband is worn, any bow that the wearer holds in their hand becomes magical with a +1 bonus to hit and a +1 bonus to damage until the wearer is no longer holding it. If the bow was already magical, these benefits are cumulative with existing benefits.
