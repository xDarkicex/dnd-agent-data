# Arcanist's Armament

**description**: Armor (Light), Rare (Requires Attunement) This sleek armor is made of a mesh of black metal set with tiny crystals. You can use it as a spellcasting focus and can cast spells without performing somatic components while wearing it. Piezoelectric Fit You can doff or don the armor as an Action. When you don it, the armor covers your body tightly and can't be removed against your will. Dynamic Buffer As a Reaction when you take non-Psychic damage from a spell or magical effect, you can halve the damage you take. You then gain Temporary Hit Points equal to the amount of damage reduced in this way, which lasts until the end of your next turn. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
