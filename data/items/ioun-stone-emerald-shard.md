# Ioun Stone, Emerald Shard

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) Emerald Shard (Very Rare). This irregularly shaped fragment of translucent emerald turns dull grey when its magic is expended. While the emerald orbits your head, it absorbs up to 5 damage from each attack or damaging effect that hits you. Once the stone has absorbed 120 damage, it loses all its magical properties and ceases to function.
