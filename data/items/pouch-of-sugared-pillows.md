# Pouch of Sugared Pillows

**description**: Pouch of Sugared Pillows Wondrous items Price: 500 gp This tiny pouch contains one small sweetened and fluffy treat. It is sugary, and probably not very good for you. This pouch actually contains exactly 10 million marshmallows even though it only looks large enough to contain one. When someone looks into the pouch they can see one marshmallow inside, and they can remove the marshmallow from the pouch one at a time by hand. Each time the pouch is turned over, one marshmallow will fall out. An entire room could be filled with it, but it might take several years to do so. The marshmallows have no magical benefit. Nothing else can be put into the pouch because it is too full of marshmallows to fit anything else.
