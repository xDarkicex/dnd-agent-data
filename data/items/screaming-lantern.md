# Screaming Lantern

**description**: Screaming Lantern Wondrous item, uncommon This brass lantern has shutters. When the shutters are opened, no light is emitted, but an ear-splitting scream comes from the lantern and continues until the shutters are closed. Any creature that can hear and starts its turn within 20 feet of the lantern while the lantern screams takes 1 point of thunder damage.
