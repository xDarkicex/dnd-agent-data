# Blanket of Healing

**description**: Blanket of Healing Wondrous item, rare This woolen blanket is 4 feet square and has a pattern of red and white stripes. If you cover yourself with the blanket while taking a short rest, you regain the maximum number of hit points for any Hit Dice you spend.
