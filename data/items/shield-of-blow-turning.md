# Shield of Blow Turning

**description**: Armor (shield), rare (requires attunement) This shield has 6 charges. When you are the target of an attack, you can use your reaction to expend 1 charge to redirect the attack toward another creature you can see within 5 feet of you. You can use a bonus action to expend a charge to cast the sanctuary spell (spell save DC 15). The shield regains 1d4 + 2 charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the shield becomes a non-magical shield.
