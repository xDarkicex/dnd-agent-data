# Lantern of the Traveler's Respite

**description**: Lantern of the Traveler's Respite Wondrous item, rare This unassuming but well made lantern provides weary travelers with small comforts. While holding the lantern you can cause it to light or darken as an action. Additionally, while holding the lantern you can use an action to cast the alarm , make camp UAH , or unseen servant spell.
