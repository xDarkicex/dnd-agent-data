# Firebelcher

**description**: Firebelcher Ranged weapon (martial, boomstick), very rare You have a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The damage done by this weapon is fire, rather than piercing. When you attack a creature with this magic weapon and roll a 20 on the d20, the target and each creature within 5 feet of it must succeed on a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw or take 2d6 fire damage.
