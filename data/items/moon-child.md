# Moon Child

**description**: Lunarae, legendary While Lunarae mushrooms grow heartily within the Fungi Forest, they are very rare. Generally growing in small groups with a larger Moon Mother, they are considered so scarce that even the residents of the forest have only had the pleasure of glimpsing a “Moon Child” once or twice in their lives. Lunarae mushrooms are imbued with lunar power and the Moon Mother mushroom watches over the small fungi, bathing them in a soft blue glow and protecting them with its innate power to reverse gravity, as the spell. Distilled Gravity. The luminescent blue frills of a Moon Mother, when distilled over several weeks with a successful DC 12 Wisdom (Survival) check, allows a character to create an elixir that grants the imbiber darkvision to 120 feet and the ability to cast reverse gravity once per long rest for one week.
