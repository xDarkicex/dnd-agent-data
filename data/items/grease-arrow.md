# Grease Arrow

**description**: When this arrow impacts a surface, slick grease covers the ground in a 10-foot square, centered on the impact, and turns it into difficult terrain for the duration. When the grease appears, each creature standing in the radius must succeed on a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw or fall prone. A creature that enters the area or ends its turn there must also succeed on a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw or fall prone. This grease is flammable.
