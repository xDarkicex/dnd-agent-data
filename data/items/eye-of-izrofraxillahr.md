# Eye of Izrofraxillahr

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare The Eye of Izrofraxillahr is a palm-sized ruby cut in the shape of a dragon's eye, said to have been crafted by the red dragon wizard, Izrofraxillahr, after losing an eye in a fierce battle. This wondrous item has a total of 3 charges, which recharge at dawn. While holding the gem in your hand, you can use an action to expend 1 to 3 charges to gain the following benefits for 10 minutes, during which time, your eyes emit a faint crimson glow: 1 charge: You gain darkvision with a range of 120 feet. 2 charges: You gain darkvision with a range of 120 feet, you can double your proficiency bonus when making Wisdom (Perception) skill checks. 3 charges: You gain darkvision with a range of 120 feet, you can double your proficiency bonus when making Wisdom (Perception) skill checks, you gain advantage on Wisdom (Perception) skill checks, and blindsight with a range of 60 feet. Alternatively, you can use an action to command the Eye of Izrofraxillahr to implode and destroy itself to release all of its stored power. When this happens, you are transformed into an adult red dragon for 1 minute, assuming the form and abilities as described in the true polymorph spell.
