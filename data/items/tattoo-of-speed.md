# Tattoo of Speed

**description**: Wondrous Item (Tattoo), Rarity Varies (Requires Attunement) The tattoo has a number of charges, depending on its rarity, as shown on the Tattoo of Speed table below. You can expend 1 charge on your turn to gain the following benefits until the start of your next turn: Your speed is doubled. You gain a +2 bonus to AC. You have advantage on Dexterity saving throws. You gain an additional action on your turn, that can be used only to take the Attack (one weapon attack only), Dash, Disengage, Hide, or Use an Object action.
