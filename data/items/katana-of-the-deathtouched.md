# Katana of the Deathtouched

**description**: Weapon (Longsword), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic longsword. Deathtouched. When you reduce a creature to 0 hit points while holding this magic weapon, you can use your reaction to gain temporary hit points equal to your character level. Death Ward. Whenever you would drop to 0 hit points as a result of taking damage, you can choose to drop to 1 hit point instead. Once used, this property can’t be used again until the next dawn.
