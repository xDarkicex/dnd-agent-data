# Merchant's Headwear

**description**: Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic headwear is woven from silk cloth and decorated with gold studs. You have advantage on Insight and Persuasion checks while wearing it. The headwear has 3 charges for the following properties and regains all expended charges daily at dawn: Merchant's Protection. When you fail an Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma saving throw, you can expend 1 charge to succeed instead. Merchant's Sight. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge to gain Truesight out to a range of 30 feet for 1 minute.
