# Potion of Burning Blood

**description**: Potion of Burning Blood Potion, uncommon Weight: ½ lb. When you drink this potion, your blood becomes dangerous to any creature but you for 1 hour. During this time, any creature within 5 feet of you when you take piercing, slashing, or acid damage must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 2d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
