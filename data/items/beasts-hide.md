# Beast's Hide

**description**: Donor: Beast of Large size or larger Slot: External You have transplanted the thick (and possibly furry) hide from a wild Beast onto your body. When you aren't wearing armor, your base AC is 13 + your Dexterity modifier. You can use your Beast’s Hide to determine your AC if the armor you wear would leave you with a lower AC. A shield’s benefits apply as normal while you use your Beast’s Hide.
