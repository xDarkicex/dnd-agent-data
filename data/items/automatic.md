# Automatic

**description**: Apprentice weapon property Components: Masterwork ranged weapon with the Reload property This weapon gains the Automatic property and its damage decreases by one step. The weapon’s normal range decreases by 20 feet and its long range decreases accordingly. Automatic. When you make an attack with this weapon on your turn, you can choose to make two attacks instead. These attacks are always made with disadvantage, regardless of circumstance. These attacks use twice the normal amount of ammunition.
