# Chain Mail of Weightlessness

**description**: Chain Mail of Weightlessness Armor (Heavy), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This armor has 5 charges. While you wear it, you can use a bonus action to expend 1 or more charges to cast one of the following spells from the armor, targeting yourself: Jump (1 charge) or Levitate (2 charges). This armor regains 1d4 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. Chain Mail. Made of interlocking metal rings, chain mail includes a layer of quilted fabric worn underneath the mail to prevent chafing and to cushion the impact of blows. The suit includes gauntlets. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC: 16 Stealth: Disadvantage Weight: 55 Minimum Strength: 13
