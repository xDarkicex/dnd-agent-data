# Phoenix Egg

**description**: Phoenix Egg Spell catalyst, legendary A fertilized egg stolen from the legendary phoenix is a powerful catalyst to restore the dead to life. When you cast the raise dead spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, the spell’s casting time becomes 1 action. In addition, the creature returns to life with its maximum hit points and has no penalty to attack rolls, saving throws, or ability checks. When this spell catalyst is consumed, it leaves behind a diamond worth 500 gp.
