# Thunderbringer (Legendary)

**description**: Weapon (Maul), Legendary (Requires Attunement) Common While you are outdoors and holding this magic weapon, you can use a bonus action to call thick gray clouds overhead. The clouds disperse after 1 minute. Uncommon You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon, which deals an extra 1d6 force damage to creatures made of metal or wearing metal armor. Rare Bring the Thunder. When you hit a target with this weapon and are outdoors, you can choose to call thunder overhead, casting the silence spell (no concentration required). The thunder’s own sound is not affected by the spell. Once used, you can’t use this property again until you finish a long rest. Very Rare The bonus to attack and damage rolls increases to +2 and the extra force damage dealt to creatures made of metal or wearing metal armor increases to 2d6. Legendary Thunderstruck! When you score a critical hit with Thunderbringer, the target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier) or be stunned until the start of its next turn.
