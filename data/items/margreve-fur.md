# Margreve Fur

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) Many tales tell of those who gain the powers of Margreve’s animals by wearing their fur. While wearing this cloak, you have proficiency with Dexterity saving throws (if you don’t have it already). You also have proficiency in the Deception and Persuasion skills. If you already have either of these proficiencies, your proficiency bonus is doubled for those checks.

- Item Type: Adventuring Gear
- Item Rarity: rare
