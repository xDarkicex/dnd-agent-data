# Switch-Blade

**description**: Switch-Blade Weapon (Dagger), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) One side of this dagger’s blade is black, the other is white. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. In addition, when you hit a creature with an attack using this weapon, you can speak the command word to switch places with the target, both of you teleporting as you do so. Once you have used this property, you can’t use it again until you finish a short or long rest.
