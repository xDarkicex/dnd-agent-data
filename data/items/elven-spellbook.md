# Elven spellbook

**description**: This tome is bound in wood wrapped in treated leaves and holds 120 pages of papyrus suitable for recording spells. Unlike other spellbooks, this spellbook contains several reference pages, marked pages, and pages folded or shaped in unique patterns. To wizards, the seemingly nonsensical organization of the pages simply feels right. The time required to prepare wizard spells from this spellbook is halved.
