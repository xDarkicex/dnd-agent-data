# Boots of Haste

**description**: Boots of Haste Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) While you wear these boots, you can click your heels together to cast the haste spell on yourself as a bonus action. You don’t suffer from lethargy when the spell ends when cast using these boots. Once this property is used, it can’t be used again until next dawn.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: very rare
- Requires Attunement: requires attunement
