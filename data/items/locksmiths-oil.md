# Locksmith's Oil

**description**: Potion, common This shimmering oil can be applied to a lock. Applying the oil takes 1 minute. For 1 hour, any creature that makes a Dexterity check to pick the lock using thieves’ tools rolls a d4 and adds the number rolled to the check.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Potion
- Item Rarity: common
