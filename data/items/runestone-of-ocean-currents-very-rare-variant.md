# Runestone of Ocean Currents (very rare variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Component: elemental volatile mote of water An ancient, malachite runestone from the depths of the sunken city, this stone thrums with energy and leaves whichever pocket or pouch it is stored in wet with frothy saltwater. This item has 4 charges and regains 1d4 expended charges daily at dawn. Saltbolt. As a bonus action while holding the stone, you can speak its command word, expending 1 charge and causing a frothy, turquoise bolt of force to strike a target within 120 feet of you. The target takes 1d6 cold damage and 1d6 force damage. A creature under the influence of the shield spell or similar magic takes no damage from this effect. Rare variant: Increase the cold and force damage to 2d6 each. Very rare variant: Increase the cold and force damage to 3d6 each.
