# Ginger Wonders

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Ginger wonders is a company that produces cigarettes with angst dust in them. These cigarettes are also known by the same name. Burnt angst dust, when inhaled regularly for a long time, is extremely deadly. However, these cigarettes are still popular among the people of Tolia as they have a particular kick to them, which sharpens one’s senses and makes them more agile. These cigarettes come in packs of 8. Whenever you smoke a ginger wonder, you must make a DC 10 Constitution saving throw. On a successful save, you have advantage on Dexterity-related ability checks and Wisdom (Perception) checks, and you gain a +2 bonus to initiative rolls for 1 hour or until you fall unconscious. On a failed save, you feel anxious, and you have disadvantage on saving throws against being frightened for 1 hour. You cannot benefit from, or have the penalties of, multiple ginger wonders at the same time. When you smoke 8 ginger wonders, you must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, you become addicted; you must consume at least one of them while taking a short or long rest, or you cannot finish the rest. On a successful save, you are not addicted, but the DC of the save increases by 1 each time you consume eight ginger wonders from then on.
