# Crystals of Entanglement

**description**: Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement) These purple crystal shards come in pairs, each meticulously carved to appear identical. Entangled Fates When the crystals are worn by two separate creatures on the same plane of existence, both creatures gain the following effects. Experience. When either creature makes an ability check it isn't proficient with, it can add half its Proficiency Bonus (rounded down) to the result if the other creature is proficient with the check. Mind. When either creature fails an Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma save against an effect that doesn't affect the other creature, the other creature can choose to make the same save. On a success, the first creature's save is treated as a success. On a failure, both creatures are affected as if they failed the save against the effect. Life. When either creature is reduced to 0 Hit Points, the other creature must make a DC 15 Charisma save if it is conscious. On a failure, it can do only one of the following on its next turn: move, take an Action, or take a Bonus Action. On a success, the dying creature instead stabilizes at the start of its next turn.
