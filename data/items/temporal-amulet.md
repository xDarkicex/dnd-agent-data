# Temporal Amulet

**description**: Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic amulet has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing it, you can expend 1 charge as a reaction at the end of another creature’s turn. You magically appear in the spot you were at the start of that creature’s turn, regain any hit points you lost during the turn, and any conditions or effects that began on you since the start of that creature’s turn no longer affect you.
