# Meaty Mushroom

**description**: Sumulis Tangrielis, rare The meaty mushroom is edible; in fact they are both delicious and nutritious whether raw or cooked. Considered a delicacy of the Fungi Forest, all manner of creatures may be encountered around these mushrooms, much like Upperworld beasts huddled around a waterhole. Immunity Booster. A character that eats meaty mushrooms at least once a day for a week gains advantage on Constitution saving throws brought about by strenuous physical activity. The bonus remains as long as the character maintains this diet.
