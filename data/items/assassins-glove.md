# Assassin's Glove

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon This magic glove is made from dark silk and resizes to fit its wearer. While wearing the glove, you can use a bonus action to make it invisible or visible. Touch of Deception. As an action, you can whisper the glove's command word and cause a Tiny object you're holding with it to magically change appearance, such as making a dagger look like a flask of ale. The object retains its size, weight, and feel, regardless of the illusory appearance you choose for it. A creature studying the object can see through the illusion with a successful DC 15 Intelligence (Investigation) check. Touch of Death. The glove has small compartments that can hold up to five doses of poison. As a bonus action, you can cause one dose of poison held by the glove to either coat a piece of ammunition or a weapon you're holding or be mixed with a liquid contained within an object you're holding, such as a flask or cup.
