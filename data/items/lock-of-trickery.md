# Lock of Trickery

**description**: Wondrous Item, Common This lock appears to be an ordinary Lock (of the type described in chapter 6 of the Player’s Handbook ) and comes with a single key. The tumblers in this lock magically adjust to thwart burglars. Dexterity checks made to pick the lock have Disadvantage.
