# Mycelosuit

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) Funglet spellcasters have mastered the art of crafting fungal skins into protective outfits known as mycelosuits. When activated as a bonus action, a mycelosuit covers the wearer (and their equipment) from head to toe, but allows them to breathe and eat normally. While encased in a mycelosuit, the wearer has disadvantage to all hearing- and scent-based Wisdom (Perception) checks. The skin is connected to the user in a primal fashion and recedes appropriately when access to equipment or parts of the body is needed; held items or those specifically excluded are not covered by a mycelosuit. Only one mycelosuit can be worn at a time. The type of mushroom used to create the mycelosuit indicates what powers it grants. Mycelosuit Types Suit Made from... Grants...
