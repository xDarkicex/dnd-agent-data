# Amulet of Great Change

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This amulet consists of an iridescent stone with a mesmerizing glow set on a golden chain. While you wear it, you gain a +1 bonus to D20 Tests. Moreover, whenever you make an ability check using Charisma, you can treat a d20 roll of 9 or lower as a 10. Radioactive One hour after you attune to the amulet and whenever you finish a Long Rest while attuned to it, you must succeed on a DC 10 Constitution save or suffer Radiation Sickness .
