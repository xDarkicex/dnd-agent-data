# Psionblade Shortsword (very rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (any sword), uncommon (requires attunement) Component: pouch of monstrosity (phase spider) teeth As locomotion in the astral plane is powered by thought, wayfarers of this vast, waterless sea tend to be of greater than normal intelligence. Hence it follows that their armaments should be manipulated by their greatest asset—thought itself—in place of the strength of their arm. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. In addition, while wielding this weapon, you can use your mind to manipulate and empower its blade. You can use your Intelligence modifier for the attack and damage rolls of this weapon. Psionslice. While holding the weapon, you can use a bonus action to cause it to switch from an ethereal blade of nebulous energy that deals psychic damage on a hit to a hardened, crystalline structure that deals the sword’s normal damage type on a hit, or vice-versa. Rare variant: The sword has 6 charges and regains 1d4 + 2 expended charges daily at dawn. The sword has the Thoughtstrike property. Thoughtstrike. When you make an attack with this weapon, you can expend 1 charge to launch it out of your hand, controlling it with your mind to make a melee weapon attack against a target up to 30 feet away. When making an attack in this way, you must use your Intelligence modifier for the attack and damage rolls. After this attack, the sword flies back to your hand. Very rare variant: Increase the attack and damage bonus to +2. The sword has 6 charges and regains 1d4 + 2 expended charges daily at dawn. The sword has the Thoughtstrike property.
