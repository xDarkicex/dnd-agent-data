# Piwafwi of Fire Resistance

**description**: Piwafwi of Fire Resistance Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This cloak is a cloak of elvenkind. It also grants you resistance to fire damage while you wear it. It loses its magic if exposed to sunlight for 1 hour without interruption. While you wear this cloak with its hood up, Wisdom (Perception) checks made to see you have disadvantage. and you have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide, as the cloak's color shifts to camouflage you. Pulling the hood up or down requires an action.
