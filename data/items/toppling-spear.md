# Toppling Spear

**description**: Toppling Spear Weapon (Spear), Rare A rounded hook protrudes from the butt-end of this metal spear. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The spear has 3 charges. When you hit a Large or smaller creature with an attack using this weapon, you can expend 1 charge to force the target to make a DC 15 Strength saving throw. On a failed save, the target is knocked prone. The spear regains all expended charges when you finish a long rest.
