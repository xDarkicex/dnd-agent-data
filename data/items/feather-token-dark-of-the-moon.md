# Feather Token, Dark of the Moon

**description**: Wondrous item, Rare Dark of the Moon (Rare). This black feather is shaped like a crescent moon. As an action, you can brush the feather over a willing creature’s eyes to grant it the ability to see in the dark. For 1 hour, that creature has darkvision out to a range of 60 feet, including in magical darkness. Afterwards, the feather disappears.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: rare
