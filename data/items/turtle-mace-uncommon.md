# Turtle Mace (Uncommon)

**description**: Turtle Mace Weapon (Mace), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Push. Once per turn, when you hit a creature with this magic weapon, the target must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be pushed back 10 feet. Progression The Turtle Mace progresses whenever time is taken to restore the full model. By spending 8 hours and 2,000 gp of bronze, a creature proficient with smith’s tools or tinker’s tools can make a DC 15 Dexterity check to attempt to increase the weapon’s rarity. On a success, the weapon’s rarity increases by one step. On a failure, you lose half the value of the materials and must try again. The weapon’s rarity can’t be increased by more than one step in the same week.
