# Shard of Tusk

**description**: Shard of Tusk Wondrous item Price: 22,000 gp A small shard of black onyx, 2 inches in length strung to the end of a chain. It is said to have come from the tusk of the god Gormash. It is said to have extremely powerful protective properties. When it is held in your hand you feel a warmth in your body and a sense of safety and protection come over you. When the wielder is hit by an attack or spell that causes damage, they may use their Reaction to protect them, causing the attack to do 0 damage instead. Any additional effects from that attack may still impact them. This ability can be used once per Long Rest.
