# Environmental Pocket

**description**: Environmental Pocket Utility, rare (requires affinity) This device has 3 charges. It can be attached to a belt or worn on the arm. As an action, you can expend a charge to activate the device, creating a thin, atmospheric layer around your body that lasts for 1 hour. It allows you to breathe normally in any atmosphere or within a vacuum. You automatically succeed on saving throws made against exposure to extreme heat or cold and take no damage from outer space exposure. The device regains 1d3 expended charges when you finish a long rest.
