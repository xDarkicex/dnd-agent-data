# Bloodwork Blades

**description**: Weapon, rare (requires attunement) You’ve enhanced your back by attaching a bloodwork blade on it. You can take a bonus action to attack with this blade. You can attach a maximum of two bloodwork blades on your back at a time. As a bonus action, you can make a melee weapon attack with your bloodwork blades. You can make two melee weapon attacks with these blades instead of 1, if you have two of these blades. Each blade deals slashing damage equal to 1d6 + your Strength or Dexterity modifier (one of your choice). To be able to wear armor on these blades, you must modify the armor accordingly. A blacksmith modifies an armor in this way for 500 KF/CS in 1 hour.
