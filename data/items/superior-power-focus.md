# Superior Power Focus

**description**: Power Focus Focus, varies (requires affinity by a channeler or forger) While holding this focus, you gain a +1 bonus to channeling and forging attack rolls. In addition, you ignore half cover when making a channelling or forging attack. Exceptional. The bonus to attack rolls increases to +2 Superior. The bonus to attack rolls increases to +3 Optimum. While holding the focus, you can use action to regain one use of Esper Mastery. You can’t use this property again until you finish a long rest.
