# Device for the Precision Application of Violence at a Distance (Model No. 786, Hand Crossbow)

**description**: Device for the Precision Application of Violence at a Distance (Model No. 786) Weapon (Any Crossbow), Uncommon Crudely bolted on top of this otherwise ordinary crossbow is a bronze tube which has been filled with polished glass discs and soaked in divination magic. Looking through the tube allows you to see distant targets with ease and indicates the bolt’s landing spot with a glowing red dot. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon, and its normal and long ranges are doubled. As a bonus action, you can look through the tube at a creature you can see within this weapon’s long range. Until you or the target moves, attacks against the target using this weapon ignore half cover and three-quarters cover, and attacking them at long range doesn’t impose disadvantage on your attack roll.
