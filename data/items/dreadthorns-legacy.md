# Dreadthorn's Legacy

**description**: Weapon (quarterstaff), rare (requires attunement by a druid) The creation of a powerful druid who turned their back on the natural world, this dead and sunbleached oaken staff is forever overgrown by dry, thorny vines. While attuned, this item functions as a +1 quarterstaff that deals 1d8 piercing damage or 1d8 bludgeoning damage. When you hit a plant with this weapon, the weapon deals an additional 2d10 damage of the weapon’s type as it saps the water from their body. Once per long rest, you may cast desiccating sphere * without expending a spell slot. Additionally, once per short rest as an action, you may spend a spell slot to cause a lash of dead, thorny vines to sprout from the ground and grapple a creature within 30 feet of you. The creature must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw (DC equal to your spell save DC) or be grappled by the vines. A creature grappled by the vines is restrained and takes 1d8 piercing damage per level of the spell slot expended at the start of each of its turns. It can try to break the grapple by making a DC 15 Athletics check. The vines are highly flammable, and burn to ash almost immediately, dealing twice the fire damage they took to the grappled creature.
