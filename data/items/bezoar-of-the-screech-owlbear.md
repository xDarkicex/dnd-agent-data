# Bezoar of the Screech Owlbear

**description**: Wondrous item, rare While this bezoar sits in your stomach, you are immune to the blinded and deafened conditions. In addition, you can communicate with beasts. This works like the speak with animals spell, except it lasts until you expel the bezoar. Curse. The animals—and even the forest spirits that you can now see floating and cavorting all around—are chattier and more distracting than would be ideal. You have disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks.
