# Water Wagon

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon This wagon carries a suspended 10-foot cube of weightless water. Creatures, objects, and magic can freely pass through the tank, but none of the water can spill outside the bounds of the wagon. A creature submerged in the wagon's tank can pilot the wagon’s movement using its swimming speed. However, it can’t take the Dash action unless the wagon is on a decline. If there are multiple creatures submerged in the tank, only the creature that has been there for the longest consecutive amount of time gains these benefits.
