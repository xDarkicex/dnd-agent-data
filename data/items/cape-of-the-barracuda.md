# Cape of the Barracuda

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare This cape was fashioned out of the skin of a single dire barracuda. While wearing this cape in water with its hood up, you can breathe underwater, have a swimming speed of 80 ft., gain advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) skill checks, and blindsight in a 30 ft. radius. As a bonus action, you can use the hood to make a bite attack using your attack roll. The bite deals 1d8+2 piercing damage.
