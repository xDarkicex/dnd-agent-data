# Gauntlets of Restoration

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) These magic gauntlets have 7 charges and regain all expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing them, you can heal creatures and mend objects with your touch. Once per turn when you hit a non-Undead, non-Construct creature with an unarmed attack, you can expend the gauntlets’ charges to have the target regain hit points equal to your Strength or Dexterity modifier (your choice) + 1d4 (1 charge), 1d8 (2 charges), or 2d6 (3 charges), instead of taking damage from your attack. If the target is a Construct or an object, you can have it be affected as by the spell mending instead (no charges expended).
