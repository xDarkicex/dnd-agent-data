# Truestrike Crossbow

**description**: Weapon (Any Crossbow), Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic crossbow has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge and set the crossbow’s sight on a target you can see within 60 feet. For 1 minute or until you choose a new target, you have advantage on ranged attack rolls against it using this weapon. In addition, you ignore half cover and three-quarters cover when making ranged attacks against the target with this crossbow. If the target becomes invisible or heavily obscured, you can see it as if it was fully visible.
