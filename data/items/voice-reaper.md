# Voice Reaper

**description**: Voice Reaper Weapon (Sickle), Rare (Requires Attunement) Images of screaming mouths are carved into the blade of this tarnished silver sickle. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you attack a creature with this weapon and roll a 20 on the attack roll, the target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be rendered mute. Talking or casting a spell that includes a verbal component is impossible in such a state. Casting lesser restoration , greater restoration , or similar magic on the target restores its speech. While you are attuned to this weapon, you can flawlessly mimic the speech of any creature whose voice you have stolen with it.
