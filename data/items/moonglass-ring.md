# Moonglass Ring

**description**: Moonglass Ring Wondrous Item, Rare (requires attunement by a spellcaster) Crafted in a rare collaboration between a moon elf sage and a group of deep dwarf explorers, this ring has been forged with ancient deep dwarven techniques and imbued with pure moonlight. While wearing this ring, you can use a bonus action and roll 1d4 + 1. You gain a temporary spell slot of that level. This temporary spell slot lasts until it is used, until you finish a long rest, or until the next dawn. The ring can’t be used this way again until the next dawn. Optional Rule: Upgrading Moonglass Ring into Asterite Ring At the gamemaster’s discretion, player characters may find a way to upgrade a Moonglass Ring into an Asterite Ring. The process might require a complex ritual to transmute moonglass into asterite that could be used as a plot hook for future adventures. Gamemaster Tip: Godsteel Ring A legendary ring that allows spellcasters to gain up to 9th level temporary spell slots is rumored to exist. You can find item details and artwork for the Godsteel Ring in Catilus’ Tome of Curious Creations: Madam Catifa’s Caravan of Magic Wonders .
