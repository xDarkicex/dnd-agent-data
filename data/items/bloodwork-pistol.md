# Bloodwork Pistol

**description**: Pistols are metal constructs that fit on top of a wooden grip. They have an angst buried within their pistol grip, and a revolving mechanism that carries 6 ammunition in its chambers and a blood cartridge in the middle. Once inserted, the blood cartridge fills each chamber with blood, ready to be shot.
