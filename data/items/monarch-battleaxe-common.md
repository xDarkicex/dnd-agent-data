# Monarch Battleaxe (Common)

**description**: Monarch Battleaxe Weapon (Battleaxe), Common (Requires Attunement) Druids are proficient with the Monarch Battleaxe. Summon Butterflies. While holding this weapon, you can speak a command word as a bonus action to summon 12 Tiny purple butterflies which flutter around the axe head for 1 hour. Each butterfly has AC 10, 1 hit point, and automatically fails saving throws. While at least one butterfly remains, the weapon emits a purple glow, creating bright light in a 10-foot radius, and dim light for an additional 10 feet. Once you have summoned butterflies, you can’t do so again until you finish a long rest. Progression The Monarch Battleaxe progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
