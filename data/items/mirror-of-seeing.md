# Mirror of Seeing

**description**: Mirror of Seeing Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) While looking into this hand mirror, you can use your action to cast the scrying spell (save DC 15). While scrying this way, the images appear in the mirror. Once used, this property of the mirror can’t be used again until the next dawn.
