# Boots of the Vigilant

**description**: Boots of the Vigilant Wondrous item, uncommon While you wear these boots, you can sense impending danger. After you roll initiative, you can choose to roll a d8 and add it to your initiative roll. Once this property is used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
