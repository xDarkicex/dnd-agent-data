# Thermal Transmuter

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) This device has a jagged crystal at its center. Thermal Transmutation The device has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. As an Action, you can expend 1 charge to produce one of these effects in a 20-foot-radius Sphere centered on the device for 1 minute or until you end the effect (no action required). Fog. The area is Heavily Obscured by dense fog. Heat Sink. You create a heat sink, causing the following effects: Fire damage dealt to targets in the area is reduced by 1d6 (to a minimum of 1). Cold damage dealt to targets in the area is increased by 1d6. A creature that starts its turn in the area must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution save or its speed is halved and it has Disadvantage on attack rolls until the end of its next turn. Superheat. You superheat the air, causing the following effects: Cold damage dealt to targets in the area is reduced by 1d6 (to a minimum of 1). Fire damage dealt to targets in the area is increased by 1d6. A creature that starts its turn in the area wearing or touching a metal object must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution save or take 1d6 Fire damage (this damage isn't increased) and have Disadvantage on attack rolls until the end of its next turn.
