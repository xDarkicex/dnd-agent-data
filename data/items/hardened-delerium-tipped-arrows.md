# Hardened Delerium-tipped Arrows

**description**: Hardened Delerium-tipped Arrows Weapon (any arrow or bolt), rare This piece of ammunition has a refined delerium arrowtip. A creature hit by a ranged attack using one of these arrows takes an extra 3d6 necrotic damage and must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or gain one level of contamination. Once it hits a target, the delerium tip crumbles to dust and the arrow becomes nonmagical.
