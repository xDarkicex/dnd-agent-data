# Spectral Optics

**description**: Cyber Optics Augment (head), varies (requires affinity) The standard version of this augment replaces or modifies your eyes with cybernetic components. It is a rare augment. With this augment, you have darkvision out to a range of 120 feet. The augment has no effect if you already have darkvision at this range or greater. Spectral Optics. With the very rare version of this augment, you also have infravision out to a range of 60 feet. You can activate or deactivate your infravision as a bonus action. Spectral Analyzer. The epic version of this augment provides the benefits of spectral optics with additional features. The range of your infravision increases to 120 feet. If you spend an action to target and examine a specific object or area you can see, you gain the following benefits for 1 minute. You know the exact distance between you and the target. If the target is a creature, you know its creature type. If the target is a location, you know its exact dimensions up to a maximum of 200 square feet. You have advantage on Intelligence and Wisdom checks made to identify or gain additional information on the target.
