# The Art of Tea

**description**: The Art of Tea Wondrous item, rare This weathered book is written in Common and illustrated with line art. It details the proper way of preparing, serving, and enjoying tea. When you use this book to complete a one hour tea ceremony ritual (which counts as light activity), you have advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened for the next 24 hours.
