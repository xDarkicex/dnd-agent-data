# Manychop Handaxe

**description**: Manychop Axe Weapon (handaxe), rare (requires attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. In addition, while you are attuned to this weapon, if you score a critical hit on a target, you can immediately make an additional attack with the manychop axe against every target of your choice within range, with a separate attack roll for each target.
