# Treefeller Greataxe

**description**: Treefeller Greataxe Melee weapon (marital, greataxe), rare You gain a +1 bonus to the attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you deal damage to a plant creature with this weapon, you deal an additional 1d6 necrotic damage. While you are wielding this magic item, you have advantage on all Charisma (Intimidation) checks you make against plant creatures. When used to fell a mundane tree, it takes no more than three chops with this weapon, regardless of the size of the tree. Heavy. Small creatures have disadvantage on attack rolls with heavy weapons. A heavy weapon’s size and bulk make it too large for a Small creature to use effectively. Two-­Handed. This weapon requires two hands when you attack with it.
