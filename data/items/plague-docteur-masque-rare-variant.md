# Plague Docteur Masque (rare variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Component: beast (giant rat) eye As any disease control specialist can attest, identifying a disease vector is half the battle in fighting it. During the veinworm plague, the symbol of the crow became synonymous with the Night Hospitaller—a representation of their hook-nosed masks and a symbol of both fear and hope. These masks give their wearers the ability to detect disease, even in the incubation stage, allowing plague-carriers to be identified and quarantined before they become infectious. In many places, these masks are still regarded as a symbol of authoritarianism and terror. Triage. While wearing this mask, you can cast the detect poison and disease spell at will. The spell ends early if you remove the mask. Rare variant: While wearing this mask, you have darkvision out to a range of 60 feet. If you already have darkvision, wearing the mask increases its range by 60 feet.
