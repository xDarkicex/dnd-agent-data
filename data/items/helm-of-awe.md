# Helm of Awe

**description**: Halm of Awe Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement) This helm grants a +1 bonus to the wearer’s armor class and a +1 bonus to their Persuasion skill. The wearer of this helm is also able to use a bonus action to conjure a magical symbol that appears 5 feet above their head. Any creatures of the wearer’s choice within 60 feet is affected by the symbol. The wearer selects one of three symbols when they activate this ability. Symbol of the Defeated. Each target must make a Charisma saving throw against a DC of 20. On a failed save, the target is overwhelmed with despair for 1 minute. During this time, it can’t attack or target any creature with abilities, spells, or other magical effects. Symbol of the Steadfast. The target must make a Charisma saving throw against a DC of 18. If they fail, the target cannot die if their hit points are reduced to zero (they can still die from effects that kill them outright). After 1 minute the effect ends and the target suffers one level of exhaustion. Symbol of the Valiant. For every ally of the target within 30 feet, the target gains a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage, to a maximum of +3. After 1 minute the effect ends. Once activated this feature may not be used again until the wearer completes a short or long rest.
