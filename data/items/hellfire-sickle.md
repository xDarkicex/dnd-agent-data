# Hellfire Sickle

**description**: Hellfire Sickle Melee weapon (simple, sickle), uncommon This weapon is fashioned from infernal iron and traced with veins of hellfire that shed dim light in a 5-foot-radius. Any humanoid killed by an attack made with this weapon has its soul funneled into the River Styx, where it’s reborn instantly as a lemure devil (described in the Monste r Manual ). Light. A light weapon is small and easy to handle, making it ideal for use when fighting with two weapons. Damage: 1d4 Damage Type: slashing Properties: Light Weight: 2
