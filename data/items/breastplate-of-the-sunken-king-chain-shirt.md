# Breastplate of the Sunken King Chain Shirt

**description**: Armour (medium or heavy), very rare (requires attunement) Component: aberration chitin When donning this armour, you hear the faint rumble of distant thundering waves as it passes over your head. This armour has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. Lurker in the Deep. While wearing this armour, you have the following benefits: You can speak and understand Abyssal. You can breathe underwater. You can’t float in water and instead sink at a rate of 60 feet per round. Walking on the bed of a water body doesn’t cost you extra movement. Sunken King’s Favour. As an action while wearing the armour, you can expend 1 charge to cast the arcane hand spell (+8 to hit). When cast in this way, the spell is modified as follows: You create two hands instead of one. They take the form of Medium spectral tentacles that sprout from the shoulders of the Breastplate. They occupy your space, move with you, and can’t be moved away from you. You can only command one of the tentacles with your bonus action each turn. The reach of ‘Clenched Fist’, ‘Forceful Hand’, and ‘Grasping Hand’ is increased to 15 feet.
