# Webspinner Apparatus

**description**: Donor: Any creature with a Web action Slot: Internal You have installed spinnerets from a spiderlike creature into your body, allowing you to produce webs. You can cast the web spell without using spell slots or spell components (DC equals 8 + your Constitution modifier + your proficiency bonus). Once you use this ability, you can’t use it again until you finish a short or long rest.
