# Arrow of Holding

**description**: Weapon (arrow), uncommon When you hit a target with this magic arrow, the target must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw, or become paralyzed for 1 minute. At the end of each of its turns, an affected creature can repeat the saving throw, ending the effect on a success.
