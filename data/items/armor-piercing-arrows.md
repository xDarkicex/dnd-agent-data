# Armor-Piercing Arrows

**description**: Armor-Piercing Arrows Ammunition Armor-piercing arrows are worked with sharp tines that punch through protective gear. When an armor-piercing arrow hits a creature, that creature must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw. On failure, the creature suffers the distracted condition (–2 AC, removed when hit by an attack) for 1 minute.
