# Flaying Star Shotel

**description**: Flaying Star Shotel Weapon (Scimitar), Artifact (Requires Attunement) The flaying star shotel functions as a +2 scimitar. Attacks made with this weapon score a critical hit on a roll of a 19 or 20 and deal 2d6 extra slashing damage to creatures with exposed flesh or hide. In addition, when a creature attuned to this weapon scores a critical hit with a melee attack with it, an inked page is flayed from the target if it has flesh or hide. The GM determines the nature of the flayed page: A spell scroll of 4th level or higher thematically relevant to the flayed creature. A partial dungeon or treasure map. A page from a tome or other legendary text that has significance in the campaign.
