# Air Fragment

**description**: Air Fragment Wondrous item, Rare (requires attunement) While attuned to this fragment, you have resistance to lightning damage and you can hold your breath indefinitely while you’re not incapacitated. Additionally, you can speak, read, and write Auran, and you can cast the minor gust cantrip at will. Awakened Air Fragment. Attuning to the Air Fragment while also attuned to the Shattered Staff awakens the fragment and it magically merges with the staff, becoming one item that takes up a single attunement slot. After the Air Fragment has been awakened, the following spells are added to the staff’s spell list: fly (3 charges), gust of wind (2 charges), gaseous form (3 charges), wind control (5 charges), and wind wall (3 charges).
