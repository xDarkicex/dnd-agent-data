# Silver Strings

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement by a bard) These silver wires magically adjust to fit any stringed instrument, making its sound richer and more melodious. You have advantage on Charisma (Performance) checks made when playing the instrument. Magical Melody. When you use the instrument as an arcane focus to cast a spell, each target has disadvantage on its saving throw against the spell. Once used, this property can’t be used again until the next dawn.
