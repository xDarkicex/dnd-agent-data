# Voidsong Shakuhachi

**description**: Voidsong Shakuhachi Wondrous item, rare (requires a ttunement) An unusual looking flute made of bleached bamboo the color of bone. You can spend your action to play a magical melody on it, causing up to 10 creatures within 70 feet that can hear it to receive certain effects and make a saving throw (detailed below). The DC of this saving throw is increased by your proficiency bonus if you are proficient in at least one musical instrument. Once this magic melody has been played, you cannot use this item again until you complete a long rest. Effect on the living. If a listener is a living creature, it is immediately no longer frightened or charmed. Then, it must make a DC 14 Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, the creature can only use its action to perform an Attack action during its next turn. Effect on the undead. If the listener is undead, it takes 3d8 psychic damage. Then it must make DC 14 Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, it becomes frightened of the flute player. Each creature frightened this way can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns. On a successful save, it is no longer frightened.
