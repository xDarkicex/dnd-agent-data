# Bloodspeed Elixir

**description**: Potion, rare When you drink this potion, your speed triples for 1 minute, but if you take the Dash action, you take 2d6 poison damage as your body is pushed beyond its normal limits. The murky, crimson liquid smells of ozone and has a thick, sludge-like consistency.
