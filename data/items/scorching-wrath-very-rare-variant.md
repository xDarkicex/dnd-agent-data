# Scorching Wrath (very rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (dagger), uncommon (requires attunement) Component: pouch of monstrosity (remorhaz) teeth Within this sharp-edged bone dagger lies an inert lump of indigo obsidian. In the heat of battle, the obsidian erupts into a continual flow of molten stone to which you are impervious. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. This dagger has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. Eruption. As an action, you can thrust the dagger into the ground and expend 1 charge. A 5-foot-radius, 40-foot-high cylinder of lava erupts, centred on a point you can see on the ground within 30 feet of you. Each creature in that area must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Rare variant: Increase the attack and damage bonus to +2 and the DC to 15. Very rare variant: Increase the attack and damage bonus to +2, the DC to 16, and the damage of Eruption to 4d10.
