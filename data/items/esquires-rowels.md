# Esquire's Rowels

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) While riding a mount and wearing these silver-chased spurs, you have a +1 bonus to initiative rolls, and your mount’s Armor Class increases by 2. In addition, while riding a mount and wearing these spurs, you and your mount have advantage on Constitution saving throws against exhaustion caused by traveling more than 8 hours in a day.
