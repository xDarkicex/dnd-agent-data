# Renewable Blade Belt

**description**: Renewable Blade Belt Utility, rare (requires affinity) This strap is attached to the belt or worn diagonally across the torso. As an action, you can pull up to two throwing blades from the belt and immediately hurl them, making a ranged attack with each blade. A throwing blade vanishes if you don’t hurl it right away, and the blades disappear right after they land. The belt never runs out of throwing blades.
