# Agile Armor

**description**: Armor (any medium or heavy), common This magically enhanced armor is less bulky than its nonmagical version. While wearing a suit of medium agile armor, the maximum Dexterity modifier you can add to determine your Armor Class is 3, instead of 2. While wearing a suit of heavy agile armor, the maximum Dexterity modifier you can add to determine your Armor Class is 1, instead of 0.
