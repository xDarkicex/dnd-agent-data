# Weapons of Blood Feud

**description**: Weapon (dagger or shortsword), very rare (requires attunement) Sorcerers developed magic weapons for their elite warriors to use against vampires during the early conflicts between Kanrath and Cyherested. These weapons, mostly daggers, are known as weapons of blood feud. It is rumored that all weapons of blood feud are now collected by vampire nobles who fear the consequences if they fall in the wrong hands. The weapon deals an extra 1d6 radiant damage on a hit. If the target is undead, the extra damage increases to 2d6. If the target is a vampire or has vampiric powers, the extra damage increases to 3d6. When you score a critical hit with this weapon, your target’s hit point maximum decreases by the amount of radiant damage inflicted, and you regain hit points equal to half of the radiant damage dealt.
