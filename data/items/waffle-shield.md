# Waffle Shield

**description**: Armor (shield), common (requires attunement) When you wear this shield, if you take a critical hit the shield breaks into pieces, but you take no damage at all. The fragments of the destroyed shield cannot be reassembled, but they become edible cookies, equivalent to 2d4 Rations.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Armor
- Item Rarity: common
