# Witchlight Scimitar

**description**: Weapon (Scimitar), Rare (Requires Attunement) You can use a bonus action to speak this magic scimitar's command word, causing golden radiance to spring from the blade. It sheds bright light in a 30-foot-radius sphere and dim light for an additional 30 feet. While the scimitar is ablaze, it deals an extra 2d6 radiant damage to any target it hits. The witchlight glow lasts until you use a bonus action to speak the command word again or until you drop or sheathe the scimitar.
