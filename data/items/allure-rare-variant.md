# Allure (rare variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) Component: fey eye As the saying goes: “Lights on the surface serve a purpose. Lights in the deep lure then reap.” This magical headpiece, while not the most attractive, has a certain allure. While wearing this circlet, you can cast the dancing lights cantrip. Lure. While wearing this circlet, you can use your action to briefly create an alluring pattern of glowing lights around you. Each creature within 10 feet of you who sees the pattern must make a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the creature becomes charmed until the start of your next turn. While charmed in this way, the creature is incapacitated and must use all its movement to approach you. If a creature ends its movement within 5 feet of you, it reaches towards your circlet. As a reaction, you can cause the circlet to bite down on the creature, dealing 1d6 piercing damage and 1d6 poison damage to it. Once this property of the circlet has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn. Rare variant: Increase the range to 15 feet, the DC to 15, and the poison damage to 2d6. The Lure property can be used twice, regaining all expended uses daily at dawn. When you cast dancing lights, you can choose any number of the lights to shed bright light in a 10-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet.
