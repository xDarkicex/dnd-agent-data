# Insectbane candle

**description**: This heavily scented candle is repellent to insects. While the candle burns, nonmagical insects don’t come within a 5-foot radius of it. This candle burns for 1 hour and sheds bright light in a 5-foot radius and dim light for an additional 5 feet.
