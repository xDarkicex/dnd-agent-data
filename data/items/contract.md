# Contract

**description**: Scroll, rare This long magical parchment is mostly used to make business deals, diplomatic agreements, and to form alliances. Terms. The signatories of the parchment should agree on the terms, and lay them out clearly in Common and Old Common. The parchment can be as long as it needs to be. Signatures. All signatories must be willing to sign and must fully understand the terms laid out. Once these conditions are met, the parties must sign under each clause. Agreement. One of the signatories uses their action to roll up and seal the parchment in order to put the contract into effect. When the contract is in effect, the scroll creates one copy of itself for each signatory, and the original parchment disappears, bonding the creatures who signed the parchment to the terms of the agreement. When one of these creatures breaches the contract in any way or if it dies, the other creatures become aware of this case.
