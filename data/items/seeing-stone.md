# Seeing Stone

**description**: Magical stone, unique A small smooth stone with a hole through the center. While looking through it, a creature obtains Truesight. It allows them to see in normal and magical darkness, reveal invisible or illusory items or creatures, and offers a glimpse into the Ethereal Plane, all up to 120 feet. The seeing stone also has 4 charges which can be used to cast the identify spell. It regains 1d4 charges at dawn.
