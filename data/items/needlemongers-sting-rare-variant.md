# Needlemonger's Sting (rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (rapier), uncommon (requires attunement) Component: fey brain An old wives’ tale, the Needlemonger is said to catch wandering children and sew them into their own shadows so that they can become playthings of the unseelie court. Despite an innocuous appearance, this long, narrow blade specialises in immobilising targets and disabling limbs. This weapon has 6 charges and regains 1d4 + 2 expended charges daily at dawn. Darts and Crafts. While holding this sword, you can pull pins out of the cushion at its hilt and use them as magical darts. You are considered proficient with these darts. When you hit a creature with one of these darts, you can expend 1 charge to cast the pins & needles cantrip on it (save DC 13; no action required). A dart that’s been removed from the cushion disappears at the end of your turn, and a new one appears in the cushion to replace it. Shadow Stitcher. When you hit a creature in dim or bright light with this sword, you can expend 1 charge to manifest ethereal threads that attempt to stitch the creature to its own shadow. The creature must succeed on a DC 13 Charisma saving throw or have its speed reduced to 0 feet until the end of its next turn. Rare variant: Increase the DCs to 15. The pins & needles cantrip is cast as a 5th-level caster. A creature that fails its saving throw against the Shadow Stitcher property is restrained until the end of its next turn.
