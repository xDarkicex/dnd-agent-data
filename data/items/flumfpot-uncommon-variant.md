# Flumfpot (uncommon variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, rare Component: aberration tentacle Flumfpots are usually found hovering around a corpse. If you can’t see the corpse, then it’s almost definitely because it’s been so long the corpse has disintegrated into dust. This is because a flumfpot is magically enchanted to always follow the last creature whose lips it touched. A creature can use its action to pour a potion into an empty flumfpot within its reach. Mastery and Movement. After a creature touches the flumfpot with its lips, it becomes the flumfpot’s master. The flumfpot follows its master around, hovering just over their shoulder. It has a flying speed of 30 feet and a swimming speed of 60 feet. If it is within 5 feet of its master when its master teleports, it teleports with them. Otherwise, it remains in place until touched by another creature’s lips. A flumfpot that is prevented from moving will fly towards its master via the most direct route when released. Send Aid. As a bonus action, you can command the flumfpot to move up to its speed towards a willing or unconscious creature you can see and feed its contents to it. Filling Flumfpot. An empty flumfpot magically refills itself with a random potion each day at dawn. Roll a d20 and consult the following table to determine the potion: d20 Potion 1-3 Potion of healing 4 Potion of climbing 5 Philter of love 6 Potion of animal friendship 7 Potion of growth 8 Potion of hill giant strength 9 Potion of water breathing 10 Potion of acid resistance 11 Potion of cold resistance 12 Potion of fire resistance 13 Potion of force resistance 14 Potion of lightning resistance 15 Potion of necrotic resistance 16 Potion of poison resistance 17 Potion of psychic resistance 18 Potion of radiant resistance 19 Potion of thunder resistance 20 Potion of greater healing Uncommon variant: Remove the Filling Flumfpot property.
