# Siren Song Lyre

**description**: Siren Song Lyre Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) You can use an action to play this lyre and cast one of the following spells from it: animal friendship , charm person , enthrall , suggestion . If the spell requires a saving throw, the spell save DC is 13. Once the instrument has been used to cast a spell, it can't be used to cast that spell again until the next dawn.
