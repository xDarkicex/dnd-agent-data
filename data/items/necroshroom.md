# Necroshroom

**description**: Mortem Diasporus, rare Few speak of the dreaded necroshroom for fear that the very utterance might draw the netherworldly spores of the fungus to the speaker, an embrace that begins with death and ends with the birth of a new mortem diasporus. Despite the fact that the spores display no intelligence or motive when they infest a host, the morbid folktale that frightens adult and child alike remains. Necrotic Void. A product of some mad mage’s meddling, necroshrooms are steeped in dark magic, drawing into itself necromantic energy for miles around, upsetting the natural balance of magic. Within 100 feet of a necroshroom, necrotic damage is halved and radiant damage is doubled. Spell Component. Both necromancers and evil clerics alike seek out mortem diasporus to harvest its necrotically-infused stocks and caps. A character who incorporates a dried ounce of necroshroom as a material component in casting a necromancy spell, causes the spell’s targets to make saving throw rolls with disadvantage.
