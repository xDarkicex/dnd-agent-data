# Voice Box

**description**: Donor: Any creature with the Mimicry trait Slot: Head You have augmented your own vocal folds with those of a creature that can mimic sounds. You can mimic any sounds you have heard, including voices. A creature that hears the sounds can tell they are imitations with a successful Wisdom (Insight) check, contested by your Charisma (Deception) check.
