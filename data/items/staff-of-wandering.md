# Staff of Wandering

**description**: Staff of Wandering Staff (arcane or druidic focus) , very rare (requires attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. You can use Dexterity, instead of Strength, for the weapon’s attack and damage rolls. While you hold the staff, you have advantage on saving throws you make against traps, you can’t become lost except by magical means, and you remain alert to danger even when you are engaged with another activity while traveling. The weapon magically awakens you and your companions within 30 feet of it if any of you are sleeping naturally when combat begins. Versatile . This weapon can be used with one or two hands. A damage value in parentheses appears with the property—the damage when the weapon is used with two hands to make a melee attack. Damage: 1d6+2 (1d8+2) Damage Type: Bludgeoning Properties: Versatile Weight: 4
