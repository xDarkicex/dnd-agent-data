# Voidmaw Sphere

**description**: Voidmaw Sphere Wondrous item, uncommon You can use your action to throw this 1-inch-diameter black sphere up to 30 feet. When it reaches that distance, or strikes a solid surface, the sphere unfolds into a voidmaw, a 5-foot-diameter opening into the Void ringed with gnashing teeth. Each creature within 5 feet of where the voidmaw appeared must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take 3d6 piercing damage. For 1 minute, any creature that begins its turn within 5 feet of the voidmaw must repeat the saving throw or take the same damage. The voidmaw sphere can unfold in a horizontal or vertical orientation, and even form in midair. When the duration ends, the voidmaw is destroyed.
