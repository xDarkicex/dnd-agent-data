# Airskimmer Bridle

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon While a creature with the Mounted trait wears this bridle and bit, it gains the following benefits: Any swimming or burrowing speed becomes a flying speed, and the creature can hover. The creature can breathe air and water. The creature weighs half as much as it normally does.
