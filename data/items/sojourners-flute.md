# Sojourner's Flute

**description**: Wondrous Item, Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This slender flute has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. As an action, you can play a tune on the flute and expend 1 charge to cast the teleport spell from it. When you do so, you can teleport only to the exact spot where you last played that specific tune on the flute and you always arrive on target. If you play a tune you haven’t played on the flute before, you aren’t teleported, but instead designate where you return to the next time you use the flute to play that tune.
