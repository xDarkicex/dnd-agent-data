# Sharktooth Longsword

**description**: Hammerhead Knight’s Legacy Item Set. While attuned to the helmet of the hammerhead knight and sharktooth, you gain advantage on Dexterity saving throws against effects you can see or smell, and you can’t be surprised. In addition, you can use your action to grow gills and morph your legs into a tail, or to reverse this transformation. While morphed, you gain a swimming speed of 40 feet, your walking speed can’t exceed 5 feet, and you can breathe both air and water. Sharktooth Weapon (longsword or greatsword), rare (requires attunement) Component: pouch of beast (giant shark) teeth Fashioned especially for the Hammerhead Knight, this blade caused its victims to bleed, allowing the knight, with its superior sense of smell, to track a would-be assassin back to its lair. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Maim. Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with an attack roll using this weapon, you can speak its command word, causing its teeth-like protrusions to rip through the target. Roll a d4 and consult the following table to determine the effect: d4 Effect 1 No additional effect. 2 Hamstring. The creature’s speed is reduced to 0 feet until the end of its next turn. 3 Rend. The creature has disadvantage on attack rolls until the end of its next turn. 4 Sever. If the creature has blood, it begins bleeding for 1 minute. Each time it takes bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing damage while bleeding, it takes an extra 1d4 necrotic damage. The target or a creature within reach can use an action to make a DC 15 Wisdom (Medicine) check, stopping the bleeding on a success. The Maim property can’t be used again until the next dawn. Hydrodynamic. Attacking while underwater doesn’t impose disadvantage on attack rolls you make with this weapon.
