# Enspelled Staff (Level 6)

**description**: Staff, Legendary (Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster) Bound into this staff is a spell of level 6. The spell is determined when the staff is created and can be of any school of magic. The staff has 6 charges and regains 1d6 expended charges daily at dawn. While holding the staff, you can expend 1 charge to cast its spell. If you expend the staff’s last charge, roll 1d20. On a 1, the staff loses its properties and becomes a nonmagical Quarterstaff. The spell’s saving throw DC is 17 and its attack bonus +9.
