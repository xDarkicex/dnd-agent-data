# Mercurial Suit

**description**: Mercurial Suit Armor (nanoweave suit), cypheos (requires affinity) These experimental hardsuits were created during the Great Breach War at the dawn of the current age. Only three of them were created, forged from the core of the Crucible of Kul Kothar. A Mercurial Suit is a Nanoweave Suit made from state-of-the-art nanocarbon weave combined with a sorium outer shell. The armor’s material is highly flexible and the sorium frame changes its size and shape to fit any form. A creature that doesn’t gain affinity with the armor can’t put it on. The armor instead shifts off of the creature, moving to an unoccupied space within 10 feet. When an affined creature suits up into a Mercurial Suit , the armor moves and conforms onto the wearer’s body. You have a +2 bonus to AC while wearing this armor. The armor has the Environmental, Sealed, and Silenced properties (see “Armor Upgrade Properties”). You can use an action to suit up or un-suit from this armor. When you un-suit, the armor appears to meld into your body. While wearing the armor, you can change its color, style, and decor as a bonus action, but the armor retains its normal bulk and weight. Random Properties. The mercurial suit has the following random properties. 1 minor beneficial property 1 major beneficial property 1 minor detrimental property 1 major detrimental property Symbiosis. The Mercurial Suit can’t be removed from you while you have affinity with it. When you lose affinity with the armor, it detaches from you. Special Properties. The armor has 10 charges and regains all expended charges after you finish a long rest. An affined creature wearing the Mercurial Suit can harness the armor’s properties, which are as follows: Your Strength score is 19. This has no effect on you if your Strength is already 19 or higher. You have a flying speed equal to your walking speed. You can expend 1 charge as an action to increase your flying speed to 60 feet for up to 10 minutes. If you instead choose to spend 3 charges, the duration increases to 1 hour. As an action, you can expend 1 charge to use the laser blast power as a ranged channeling, forging, or weapon Attack (your choice). The power is activated at rank 9 and deals 12d6 radiant damage. As an action, you can expend 2 charges to use the flame blast power (save DC 19) with the armor. The power is actvated at rank 9 and deals (14d6) fire damage. Destroying a Suit. A Mercurial Suit is impervious to all damage and the effects of Esper Powers. It can be destroyed by casting it into the core of Kul Kothar’s Crucible. If cast into the core of any other Crucible, it reappears in a random star system in the same galaxy.
