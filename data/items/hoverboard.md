# Hoverboard

**description**: This 3-foot long metal board produces a low hum and curiously hovers above any surface. You can mount or dismount this board as a bonus action. While mounted, you ignore nonmagical difficult terrain and can move above the surface of liquids without sinking. Additionally, if you move at least 15 feet in a line downhill on your turn, your speed increases by 10 feet until the end of your turn. The board can't scale any angle greater than 45 degrees, unless it is moving in a zero-gravity environment. In zero gravity, the board moves across any surface as if it were downhill. Lastly, you can use a Dexterity (Acrobatics) check, instead of a Charisma (Performance) check, to perform tricks on the board.
