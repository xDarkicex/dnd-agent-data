# Ring of Resonance

**description**: Ring, Rare (Requires Attunement) This silver ring is set with a large multifaceted crystal. Resonating Tune The ring has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. As an Action, you can expend 1 charge to create a sound that affects specific creatures. Choose a creature type. Each creature of that type within 30 feet that can hear the sound is affected in your choice of one of the following ways for 1 minute, until you end the sound (no action required), or use this property again. Bolstering. The creature gains 5 Temporary Hit Points at the start of each of its turns. Disruptive. If the creature is concentrating at the start of its turn, it must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution save or lose its Concentration. Soothing. The creature has Advantage against being Charmed or Frightened. Staggering. The creature must make a DC 13 Constitution save at the start of each of its turns. On a failure, the creature can do only one of the following on that turn: move, take an Action, or take a Bonus Action. On a success, it is immune to the effects of the sound for the next 24 hours.
