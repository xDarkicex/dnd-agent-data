# Origami Gold-Leaf Dragon

**description**: Wondrous item, legendary Component: pouch of plant leaves Over the course of a minute, you can fold this enchanted, golden paper into a dragon, activating its magical potential in one of the following ways: Draconic Familiar. Proud and brilliant, the 6-inch-long origami dragon is usually the Bureau Director’s personal assistant. When you craft this origami animal, it becomes your familiar, as per the find familiar spell, with the following changes: It uses the statistics of a gold dragon wyrmling , but is a Tiny construct with 10 hit points, a flying speed of 80 feet, a +8 Dexterity (Stealth) modifier, and a +14 Wisdom (Perception) modifier. There is no limit to the distance at which you can communicate with the dragon telepathically or share its senses. You can’t dismiss the dragon. As an action, the dragon can magically turn invisible until it attacks or its concentration ends (as if concentrating on a spell). The dragon doesn’t have a breath weapon and can make no attacks other than the following Weaken action: Weaken. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft. Hit: 1 slashing damage and the target must succeed on a DC 17 Strength saving throw or have disadvantage on Strength-based attack rolls, Strength checks, and Strength saving throws until the end of its next turn. After 1 hour, the dragon reverts to a sheet of paper and can’t be used again until the next dawn. Get-Away Dragon. When you fold this paper, you can speak its command word, causing the origami shape to manifest into a Large dragon made of paper. This creature has the statistics of a young gold dragon . The dragon is friendly to you and obeys your verbal commands (no action required by you), and rolls its own initiative in combat. If you don’t issue any commands to the dragon, it defends itself from hostile creatures but otherwise takes no actions. After 1 hour, or if the dragon drops to 0 hit points, the paper loses its magic and shrinks back down into a mundane, folded piece of paper.
