# Greater Sun Whip

**description**: Sun Whip Weapon (Whip), Rare (requires attunement) Created by an eccentric vampire hunter of angelic ancestry to outsmart her elusive prey, this weapon produces a luminous whip made of pure radiance that can strike foes with the power of the blazing sun. When inactive, this item appears to be a whip handle. While holding it, you can use a bonus action to cause a luminous whip of pure light to spring into existence, or make the whip disappear. Made of pure radiance, the Sun Whip has the light property. Luminous Lash. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon, which deals radiant damage instead of slashing damage. When you hit an undead with it, that target takes an extra 1d8 radiant damage. Radiant Reach. The luminous whip emits bright light in a 15-foot radius and dim light for an additional 15 feet. The light is sunlight. Instead of a whip’s regular reach, the Sun Whip allows you to attack any target within the 15-foot-radius sphere of bright light it emits. Item Variant: Greater Sun Whip At the gamemaster’s discretion, a legendary version of this item can be found. The Greater Sun Whip grants a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls (instead of +2) and deals an extra 2d8 radiant damage to undead (instead of 1d8). Player characters may undertake a quest to upgrade a Sun Whip into a Greater Sun Whip, possibly by defeating or redeeming a corrupt celestial creature.
