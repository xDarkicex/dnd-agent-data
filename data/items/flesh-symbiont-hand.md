# Flesh Symbiont, Hand

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement by a spellcaster missing a hand) Component: pouch of humanoid teeth Crafted by Thumblemore the wizard, this cursed lump of dormant flesh needs a host to emerge from its slumber. It slowly attaches itself to the stump of a creature’s lost hand, shifting hue and morphing its shape to represent the lost limb. You must have a missing hand to attune to this item. Once attuned to it, it takes the place of your missing hand and functions as a normal hand. The results aren’t always perfect; roll 1d6 + 1 to determine the number of digits your new hand has. The first digit is always a thumb, and a mouth forms in the hand’s palm. Cost. The symbiont feeds off of you, syphoning your magical energy. Each time you finish a long rest, you must make a DC 10 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, you lose one spell slot of the lowest level you can cast. Mockery. You can use the hand to cast the vicious mockery and words of discouragement spells at will, using your spell save DC. If a creature succeeds on its saving throw against a words of discouragement spell cast in this way, the mouth blurts out insults and mockeries at you, causing you to become timorous for 1 minute, as per the spell. As a bonus action while timorous, you can punish your insolent hand, taking 1d4 bludgeoning damage, restoring your sense of self-worth, and ending this effect. Symbiotic Nature. The symbiont can’t be removed from you while you’re attuned to it, and you can’t voluntarily end your attunement to it. If you’re targeted by a spell that ends a curse, your attunement to the symbiont ends and it falls off, reverting to a dormant lump of flesh.
