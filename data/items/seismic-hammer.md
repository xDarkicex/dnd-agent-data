# Seismic Hammer

**description**: Seismic Hammer Weapon (Warhammer), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This warhammer is an overly large, jagged piece of rock engraved with glowing runes and bound to an iron handle. When you hit with an attack using this magic weapon, the target takes an extra 1d6 thunder damage. In addition, while you hold the weapon, you have resistance to thunder damage. The hammer has 3 charges. While holding it, you can use an action and expend 1 charge to release a 30-foot cone of violent vibrations. Each creature in the affected area must make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw. A target takes 8d6 thunder damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The hammer regains 1d3 expended charges when you finish a long rest.
