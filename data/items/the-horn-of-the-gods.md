# The Horn of the Gods

**description**: The Horn of the Gods Wondrous item, legendary The origins of this horn are unknown, though its last recorded location was in the Underworld. It can wake any sleeper, no matter how deep the sleep. Blowing the horn requires an action. It has a range of 1 mile when blown. The first time blown, anyone sleeping in range due to non-magical sleep wakens. If blown a second time, within a minute of the first, anyone sleeping in range, including because of magical sleep, wakens. If blown a third time, within a minute of the first, any creature petrified in range is no longer petrified, though they now have one level of exhaustion. Additional special events determined by the GM may occur on the third, or subsequent, uses of the horn.
