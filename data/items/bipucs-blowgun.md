# Bipuc's Blowgun

**description**: Bipuc’s Blowgun Weapon (Blowgun), Rare (Requires Attunement) This wooden blowgun is painted pitch black, with strange Celestial insults carved into it and painted blood red. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you hit a creature with an attack using this blowgun, you can attempt to charm the target. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Charisma saving throw or become charmed. While charmed, the target must spend its turn attacking the nearest creature. The target can repeat this save at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a short or long rest.
