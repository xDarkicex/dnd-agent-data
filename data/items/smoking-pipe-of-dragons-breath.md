# Smoking Pipe of Dragon's Breath

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This pipe has 2 charges and regains 1 expended charge daily at dawn. If the pipe is full of one ounce of smoking herb, you can use an action and expend 1 charge to inhale deeply from it and exhale dragon’s flame. Each creature in a 30-foot-radius cone must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take 8d6 fire damage, or half as much on a successful one.
