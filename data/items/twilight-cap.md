# Twilight Cap

**description**: Videre Spongos, common Twilight caps form the backbone of the funglet diet, and the mushroom is a commodity in those markets that reach the Upperworld. Vision Enhancer. A character who eats twilight caps at least once a day for a week gains darkvision to 120 feet as long as the character maintains this diet. Spell Component. Incorporating twilight caps as a material component into a spell that modifies vision, such as blindness, causes the spell’s targets to make saving throw rolls with disadvantage.
