# Orb of Time

**description**: Wondrous Item, Common This orb can be used as an Arcane Focus. While holding the orb, you can take a Magic action to determine whether it is morning, afternoon, evening, or nighttime. This property functions only on the Material Plane.

- Item Type: Arcane Spell Focus
