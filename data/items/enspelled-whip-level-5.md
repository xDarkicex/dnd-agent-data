# Enspelled Whip (Level 5)

**description**: Weapon (Whip), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) Bound into this weapon is a spell of level 5. The spell is determined when the weapon is created and must belong to the Conjuration, Divination, Evocation, Necromancy, or Transmutation school of magic. The weapon has 6 charges and regains 1d6 expended charges daily at dawn. While holding the weapon, you can expend 1 charge to cast its spell. The spell’s saving throw DC is 17 and its attack bonus is +9.
