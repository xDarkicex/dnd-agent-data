# Ring of Winter's Kiss

**description**: Ring, Rare (Requires Attunement) When you suffer cold damage while wearing the ring of winter's kiss , you take damage normally. However, on your next turn, you can target any creature within 30 feet, making an attack roll with a +7 bonus. On a hit, the target takes the same cold damage you took.
