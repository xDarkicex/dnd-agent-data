# Darkskull

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This skull is carved from a single block of ebony and is wholly evil. Wherever the skull is situated, the space around it is considered to be under the effect of a hallow spell, with the skull as the point of origin.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: rare
