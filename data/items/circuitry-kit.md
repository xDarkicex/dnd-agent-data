# Circuitry Kit

**description**: A circuitry kit contains a soldering iron, spare wire, and dozens of spare arcane components, such as abjurators, illusiometers, evocation shifters, and conjuration processors—everything necessary to rewire a piece of magical technology. Proficiency with this kit allows you to add your proficiency bonus to any checks you make to build, modify, and repair handheld gadgets, such as blasters, which contain an arcane battery.
