# Tactical Vest

**description**: This light vest is covered in pouches, straps, and holsters. The vest can carry a two-handed melee weapon on its back and a dagger or an antimatter dagger on its front. It has two holsters and one sling can attach to it. The tactical vest can be worn atop armor.
