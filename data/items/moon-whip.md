# Moon Whip

**description**: Moon Whip Weapon (Whip), Rare (requires attunement) Created by a relentless hunter of evil lycanthropes, polymorphist criminals, and other shapechangers, this weapon produces a luminous whip made of pure moonlight that can reveal the true form of creatures that stalk the night. When inactive, this item appears to be a whip handle. While holding it, you can use a bonus action to cause a silvery whip of pale light to spring into existence, or make the whip disappear. Made of moonlight, the Moon Whip has the light property. Lunar Lash. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon, which deals radiant damage instead of slashing damage. When you hit a shapechanger with it, that target takes an extra 1d8 radiant damage and instantly reverts to its original form. Moonlit Revelation. When active, the Moon Whip sheds moonlight, creating bright light in a 10-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet. While in its original form, a shapechanger can’t assume a different form until it leaves the whip’s light. Item Variant: Greater Moon Whip At the gamemaster’s discretion, a legendary version of this item can be found. The Greater Moon Whip grants a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls (instead of +2) and deals an extra 2d8 radiant damage to shapechangers (instead of 1d8). Player characters may undertake a quest to upgrade a Moon Whip into a Greater Moon Whip, possibly by defeating a mythical shapeshifter, or receiving the blessing of a moon deity.
