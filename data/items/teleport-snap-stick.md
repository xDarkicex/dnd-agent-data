# Teleport Snap-Stick

**description**: Wondrous item, rare When you snap it in half, this 6-inch-long rune-inscribed stick teleports you and up to two additional Medium creatures touching you to a single location attuned to the stick during its creation. Each snap-stick is linked to a single location and is expended when used. Teleport snap-sticks are often given to non-spellcasting servants to return to their masters after assignments are completed or as a means to escape danger.
