# Mithral Makhaira +1

**description**: Weapon, longsword, uncommon This mithral-forged longsword has a single curved blade with a flat edge along the top. It is heavier than most longswords, making it more effective while mounted. Attacks made with this weapon have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls. When you attack with this blade while you are mounted, it does an additional d4 slashing damage on hit.
