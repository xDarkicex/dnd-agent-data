# Bloodrazor

**description**: Weapon (longsword), very rare (requires attunement) This black iron, wave-edged blade is stained crimson with the blood of countless enemies. The Bloodrazor functions as a +1 longsword . Whenever you score a critical hit with this weapon against an NPC or monster, choose an Aspect of your target that has not been Injured. Your target suffers an Injury of Handicap severity to that Aspect. If your target does not have any unInjured Aspects, you may inflict an Injury to any Aspect of your choice. Additionally, the first time you reduce a creature to 0 hit points after finishing a short or long rest, the bloodrazor’s attack and damage bonuses increase by 1 until you finish a short or long rest, and you treat Injury thresholds as 10 lower (to a minimum of 5).
