# Ram

**description**: Large Object Armor Class: 15 Hit Points: 100 Ram (Requires Position). Melee Attack Roll: +8, reach 5 ft. Hit: 16 (3d10) Bludgeoning damage. A Ram consists of a movable gallery equipped with a heavy log suspended from two roof beams by chains. The log is shod in iron and used to batter through doors and barricades. Positioning a Ram requires three Utilize actions. Then a crew member can use the Ram action. The gallery roof gives the operators Total Cover against attacks and other effects from above.
