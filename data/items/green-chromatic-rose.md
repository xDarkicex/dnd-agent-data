# Green Chromatic Rose

**description**: Green Chromatic Rose Wondrous Item, Rare This magic rose is green. While a rose is held, it gains a harmless visual effect as indicated on the table. The green rose issues green gas. While holding the rose by its stem, you gain resistance to poison damage. If you would take more than 10 damage of poison from a single source (after applying the resistance), the rose disintegrates, and you take no damage instead. As an action, you can blow the petals from the rose to produce a 20-foot cone of poison. Each creature in the cone must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, taking 3d10 poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Using this property destroys the rose.
