# Clockwork crossbow

**description**: This weapon resembles a traditional light crossbow but with a removable, wooden and clockwork magazine. Though this crossbow is lighter and less powerful than a heavy crossbow, its magazine allows for faster volleys. The attached magazine must have at least one bolt loaded into it to make a ranged attack with this weapon.

- Item Type: Weapon
- Properties: Ammunition, magazine (6), two-handed
