# Mithral Chakram +1

**description**: Weapon, chakram, uncommon This mithral-forged, bladed circular disc is an unusual thrown weapon. It glides through the air, slicing through anything in its path before returning to the thrower. Attacks made with this weapon have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls. When you miss an attack with this weapon, you may try the same attack roll against another creature within 10 feet of the first target.
