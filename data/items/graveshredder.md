# Graveshredder

**description**: Weapon (battleaxe), rare (requires attunement) Component: pouch of undead teeth Morticai Ignaceous Fiéd dreamed of superstardom. Unfortunately, Mort I. Fiéd’s father had different plans. Namely: digging graves, filling the graves, and watching the graves to make sure they didn’t undig themselves. And so, Mort cobbled together a lute from old grave-digging shovels, channelled his teen spirit into angst-filled melodies, and surprised himself by animating the whole graveyard. Thus was formed the travelling six-piece musical act: “Avenged Sixfold”. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon, which can also act as a lute. If you are proficient with a stringed instrument, you are proficient with this battleaxe, can use it as a spellcasting focus for your bard spells, and can use the Shredder property. The battleaxe has 4 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. Shredder. While holding this axe, you can expend charges in the following ways: As an action, you can sweep-pick a sick lick, expending 2 charges to cast the speak with dead spell. Whenever you ask questions, the ‘question and answer’ takes the form of a ‘call and response’ duet between you the guitarist, and the corpse as a singer. Over the course of 1 minute, you can rip a face shredding solo, expending 2 charges to cast the animate dead spell. When the spell is cast in this way, you command creatures created by it by playing a killer riff. As an action, you can fingerpick a mournful instrumental ballad, expending 4 charges to cast the revivify spell. Casting the spell in this way requires its material components. When you expend 1 or more charges, you must make a Dexterity or Charisma (stringed instrument) check (your choice). The DC for this check equals 10 + 1 for each charge expended. On a success, the spell is cast as normal. On a failure, the spellcasting action, any material components, and the charges are wasted.
