# Tail of the Clownfish

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement by a creature missing its legs) Component: beast fin When the rakin ousted Dr. Stein from his tower, he had only one means of escape: swim. His last act in the battle was to sever his own legs, attune to the tail, flop into a well, and slink out into the sea, tail between his... well. He didn’t have legs anymore. You must be missing your legs to attune to this item. Once attuned to it, the tail grafts into place, the scales slowly petering out into your normal skin, and can’t be removed against your will. You gain the shapechanger type and can breathe both air and water. Morph. As an action, you can morph your tail into legs or vice-versa, gaining the benefits indicated below. Legs. You have a walking speed of 20 feet. You can stand on and move across any liquid surface as if it were solid ground. Tail. You have a swimming speed of 60 feet and advantage on ability checks and saving throws made to avoid being grappled or restrained while underwater. On land, you are always prone, can’t stand up, and have a walking speed of 5 feet. Dolphin: Heads or Tails. As a bonus action, you can target one creature you can see within 60 feet of you and unleash the power of the chimera. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be partially-polymorphed for 1 minute. Roll a d6 to determine the effect. Once this property of the item has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn. On an even result, the creature’s torso and head become fishified: it loses the ability to speak and can breathe only water. Furthermore, its Intelligence score is reduced to 1, and it can’t cast spells or activate magic items, though it can still recognise its friends, follow them, and even protect them. The creature gains 10 temporary hit points. When these hit points are lost, or if the creature falls unconscious, the effect ends and it returns to normal. On an odd result, the creature’s legs become fishified, as described in the Tail option of the Morph property. Rare variant, Tail of the Clownfish: Replace the Heads or Tails property with the Clowning Around property. Clownfish: Clowning Around. As an action, you can cast the hideous laughter spell (save DC 15). This property can be used three times, regaining all expended uses daily at dawn.
