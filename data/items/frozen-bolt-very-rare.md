# Frozen Bolt (Very Rare)

**description**: Frozen Bolt Weapon (Spear), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you hit with an attack using this weapon, the target takes an extra 1d6 cold damage and the target’s speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of your next turn. Iceplosion. Whether you hit or miss with this weapon, you can cause shards of ice to explode outward from it in a 15-foot radius around the target. Each creature in the area (including you) must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw, taking 2d6 piercing damage plus 2d6 cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. The affected area is covered in a slippery sheen of ice, making it difficult terrain for 1 minute. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. Freeze Stiff. When you hit a creature with an attack using this weapon, you can force the target to make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the target is cocooned in ice, becoming petrified for 1 minute. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success. A freedom of movement spell causes the ice to shatter, instantly freeing the target. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. Progression The Frozen Bolt progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. As the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, so does the weapon’s rarity. However, in addition to the required increase in proficiency bonus, to progress from very rare to legendary, the attuned creature or their allies must kill an ancient red dragon during a combat encounter in which the dragon has been wounded by the Frozen Bolt in its very rare form.
