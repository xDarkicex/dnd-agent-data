# Spider's Kiss

**description**: Weapon (Longbow), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) When you hit with an attack roll using this magic longbow, the target takes an extra 1d8 poison damage. In addition, while you are carrying the bow, you have immunity to poison damage and can’t be poisoned. Spider’s Web. The bow has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. When you hit a creature with an attack roll using this bow, you can expend 1 charge to have magical webbing envelop it. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or be restrained (escape DC 15) until the end of its next turn.
