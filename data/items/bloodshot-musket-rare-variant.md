# Bloodshot Musket (rare variant)

**description**: Weapon (any firearm), uncommon Component: phial of celestial blood Ever parched, this sinister firearm greedily drinks any lifeblood it’s offered, and an otherworldly mechanism inside it converts it into deadly shards, which it fires instead of bullets. The slightest trickle of crimson is enough for the pupils on this hellish weapon to dilate and for its eyes to appear wild and bloodshot. Feed the Beast. This weapon has 10 charges. Over the course of a short or long rest, any creature can cause bloodshot to regain expended charges by dripping their own blood down its barrel and spending Hit Dice. Roll each Hit Die spent in this way, and the weapon regains a number of charges equal to the total. After absorbing 3 Hit Dice worth of blood, the weapon can’t absorb more blood in this way until the next dawn. Alternatively, over the course of a minute, you can extract blood from the corpse of a creature that has been dead for no longer than 10 minutes. When you do so, the weapon gains a number of charges equal to the roll of one of the dead creature’s Hit Dice. After absorbing the blood from a corpse in this way, the weapon can’t do so again until 1 hour has passed. Shot Selection. This weapon doesn’t have the reload or ammunition properties. Instead, in order to make a ranged weapon attack with it, you must expend charges in one of the following ways: Bloodshard (1 charge). On a hit, the weapon deals its normal damage. Septic Shock (3 charges). On a hit, the weapon deals its normal damage and the target must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or become poisoned until the end of its next turn as its blood becomes corrupted. A creature immune to disease automatically succeeds on this saving throw. Rare variant: You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Increase the DC to 15.
