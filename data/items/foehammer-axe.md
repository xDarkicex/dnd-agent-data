# Foehammer Axe

**description**: Foehammer Axe Weapon (greataxe), rare You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Telescoping Handle. As a bonus action, you may press a button hidden in the shaft of the axe and extend its telescoping handle. While extended, the reach of the axe increases from 5 feet to 10 feet. This greataxe was forged by the famous dwarven weaponsmith Ghelryn Foehammer. The weapon is made of darksteel which is known for being strong by light. The axe can be identified by the inlay on its handle, a trademark of Ghelryn on all the weapons he creates. The weapon should be cleaned with boiled linseed oil once a week to avoid rust. Heavy. Small creatures have disadvantage on attack rolls with heavy weapons. A heavy weapon's size and bulk make it too large for a Small creature to use effectively. Two-Handed. This weapon requires two hands when you attack with it. Damage : 1d12+1 Damage Type : Slashing Properties : Heavy, Two-Handed Weight : 7
