# Silverfang

**description**: Weapon (spear), very rare (requires attunement) Silverfang is a sentient magic spear that was forged by the skilled elven wizard and master weaponsmith, Elthandryll, who sought vengeance for the loss of his beloved silver dragon companion, Tannastimaahlya. After Tannastimaahlya's tragic demise at the hands of a ruthless red dragon, Elthandryll channeled her companion’s soul into the weapon during its creation. By imbuing the spear with her essence, he hoped to create a weapon capable of delivering a deadly blow to his dragon adversary. The creation of Silverfang was a labor of love, mourning, and revenge as Elthandryll poured his grief and fury into the forging process. As time passed, Silverfang was passed down through the generations of Elthandryll's elven descendants. Each wielder added their own experiences and triumphs to the weapon's history, further fueling its power and resonance. Silverfang possesses a distinct personality, reflecting the noble and protective spirit of Tannastimaahlya. The weapon is fiercely loyal to its wielder and possesses an innate understanding of their desires, often whispering words of guidance and encouragement in their minds. It carries the memories and wisdom of Tannastimaahlya, providing valuable insights into the ways of dragons and their weaknesses. Silverfag is lawful good, has an Intelligence score of 18, a Wisdom score of 15, and a Charisma score of 23. It communicates by transmitting emotions to the creature carrying or wielding it. It can hear and has blindsight in a 60 feet and darkvision in a 120 feet radius. If Silverfang’s wielder is not of lawful good alignment, it demands to be given to someone else. When such a conflict occurs, Silverfang makes a Charisma check contested by the wielder's Charisma check. If Silverfang wins the contest, the wielder is compelled to give it to someone else. If its wielder wins the contest and refuses to comply with Silverfang’s wishes, the spear makes it impossible for its wielder to attune to it and suppress all of its properties. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you hit a red dragon with this weapon, the red dragon takes an extra 3d6 cold damage. Once per day, Silverfang can unleash a cone of freezing breath in a 90-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 24 Constitution saving throw , taking 67 (15d8) cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
