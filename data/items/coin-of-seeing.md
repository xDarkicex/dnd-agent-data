# Coin of Seeing

**description**: Thieves do not only need to be unseen - it’s just as important that they themselves don’t miss important things in their surroundings. A relatively common tactic used by thieves’ guilds is to employ wizards that create Coins of Seeing. For high-profile targets, they are used to enhance the user’s perception significantly. Magic schools: Transmutation Cost: 200 gp Magic properties: If you put this coin on your forehead, it dissolves into a golden shimmer. You gain Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks for one hour. With a quick jump, Arthi grabbed the ledge above her and pulled herself up on the roof. Stealing from a museum wasn’t exactly her type of task - but then again, the museum had purchased the statues knowing they were stolen goods. She was doing a good thing here. But also a dangerous thing. The guards were all over the place since the alarm went off. She would have to be cautious... which probably was a good moment to use one of the coins her employer had given her. Arthi grabbed into her pouch, pulling one of the crude coins out, and placed it on her forehead. It instantly dissolved, with no visible effect. But Arthi felt... different. Her senses were sharpened. And in that exact moment, she noticed the tripwire just an arm’s length in front of her.
