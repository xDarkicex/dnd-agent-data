# The Hammer of Adrix

**description**: Weapon (warhammer), very rare (requires attunement) 1d8 bludgeoning - versatile (1d10) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls with this magic weapon and you have advantage on attack rolls against frightened creatures. Spells. The Hammer of Adrix has 5 charges. While holding it, you can use an action and expend 1 charge to cast the fear spell. Curse. This hammer is cursed and possessed by the vengeful spirit of a fallen paladin, Adrix, The Hammer. Becoming attuned to it extends the curse to you until you are targeted by a remove curse spell or similar magic. Casting remove curse does not remove the spirit from the weapon. As long as you remain cursed, you are unwilling to part with the hammer, keeping it within reach at all times. You also have disadvantage on Wisdom and Intelligence saving throws. Adrix and his allies were slain by Krexillius Kornath and Adrix’s spirit remained trapped within the hammer he once wielded. He blames himself for the deaths of his allies and will attempt to influence and goad anyone who wields the hammer into slaying Krexillius Kornath by any means. The curse drives the wielder to behave recklessly in their pursuit of this goal. Successfully slaying Krexiullius Kornath will both remove the curse, and allow the spirit of Adrix to achieve redemption and move on from this plane. Alternatively, casting banishment on the hammer forces the spirit of Adrix to leave it. The hammer then becomes a +1 weapon with no other properties. © Dean Spencer
