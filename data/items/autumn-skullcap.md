# Autumn Skullcap

**description**: The autumn skullcap has brown to yellow-brown caps that fade in color when drying. They are a classic little brown mushroom that also have many edible varieties that are hard to tell apart. Autumn skullcap causes renal failure, and eating one forces a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or the eater gains the poisoned condition and 1d12 poison damage.
