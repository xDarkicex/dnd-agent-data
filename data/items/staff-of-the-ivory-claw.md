# Staff of the Ivory Claw

**description**: Staff of the Ivory Claw Staff, rare (requires attunement by a spellcaster) This gray-and-cerulean staff is topped with a small dragon claw carved from ivory. While holding the staff, you gain a +1 bonus to spell attack rolls. Whenever you score a critical hit with a spell attack, the target takes an extra 3d6 radiant damage. Damage: 1d6 (1d8) Damage Type: Bludgeoning Properties: Versatile Weight: 4
