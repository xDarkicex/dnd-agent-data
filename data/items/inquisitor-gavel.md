# Inquisitor Gavel

**description**: Melee Weapon (mace), rare (requires attunement) Strike of the Believer. You gain a +1 bonus to your attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. Your attacks with this weapon score a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20. Auctoritas. When you score a critical hit with the Inquisitor Gavel, the target is restrained until the end of its next turn. Court Law. While attuned to the Inquisitor Gavel, you gain advantage on Insight and Intimidation checks.
