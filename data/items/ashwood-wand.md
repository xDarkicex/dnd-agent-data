# Ashwood Wand

**description**: Wand, common (requires attunement by a druid) You can use this wand as a spellcasting focus. The wand has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. When you cast a spell that deals fire damage while using this wand as your spellcasting focus, the spell deals 1 extra fire damage. If you expend 1 of the wand’s charges during the casting, the spell deals 1d4 + 1 extra fire damage instead.
