# Potion of Ogre Stench

**description**: Potion of Ogre Stench Potion, uncommon Weight: ½ lb. This foul concoction was accidentally created by a clumsy alchemist while trying to create a Potion of Ogre Strength . When you drink this potion, you gain the horrible stench of concentrated ogre musk for 1 hour. Any creature other than an ogre that starts its turn within 5 feet of you must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned until the start of the creature’s next turn. On a successful saving throw, the creature is immune to the stench for 1 hour. In addition, any ogre in a 1 mile radius of you is irresistibly drawn to your location. Ogres attracted in this way will usually attack once they realize that you are not an ogre.
