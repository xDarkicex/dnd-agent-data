# Aduro Shield

**description**: Wondrous Item (shield), artifact (requires attunement by a creature of good alignment) Armor Class (AC). While holding this shield, you have a +2 bonus to AC. This bonus is in addition to the shield’s normal bonus to AC. Bright Burn. While holding this shield, your Intelligence and Wisdom scores are increased by 2 points (up to a maximum of 22) and you are immune to fire damage. Radiant. The Aduro Shield casts a bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for an additional 20 feet. Creatures of evil alignment can’t turn invisible while illuminated by this light. Sanctified Protection. The front of the shield bears holy inscriptions that emanate a calming aura. Every creature of your choice within 30 ft. of the shield has advantage on saving throws against being frightened or charmed and can’t be frightened or charmed by Fiends. The Touch of Light. As an action, you regain hit points equal to half your maximum hit points and end any condition you are suffering from. This action can be used while you are incapacitated (but not unconscious). Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a short or long rest.
