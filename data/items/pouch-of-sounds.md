# Pouch of Sounds

**description**: Pouch of Sounds Wondrous item, common This magical pouch appears as a normal belt pouch. As an action, a creature holding the pouch can speak or make a sound into the pouch, and then seal it. When the pouch is unsealed, squeezed, sat on, or otherwise opened, the sound that it was holding emerges at exactly the volume that it was originally made into the pouch. The sound can be no longer than 6 seconds. Once the sound escapes, it must be refilled.
