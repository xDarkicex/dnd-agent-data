# Wand of Whooping Explosions

**description**: Wand of Whooping Explosions Wand, Very Rare (requires attunement) Crafted by a technognome supervillain in a crisis of megalomania, this volatile wand can turn mundane objects into deadly explosives. Designed to strike from afar and without revealing the supervillain’s identity, the Wand of Whooping Explosions can be targeted through scrying sensors or the eyes of faithful minions. Bombify. This wand has 7 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more of its charges and target a Medium or smaller nonmagical object that is not being worn or carried that you can see on the same plane of existence regardless of distance and line of sight. The targeted object’s structure becomes explosively unstable for the next minute. While the object is in this unstable state, it explodes if it takes 1 point of damage. You can also use your bonus action to cause as many affected objects as you wish to explode at once. Each creature within 5 feet of an object that explodes that way must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d6 bludgeoning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature in the area of more than one explosion is affected only once.
