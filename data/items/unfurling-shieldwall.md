# Unfurling Shieldwall

**description**: Unfurling Shieldwall Shield, very rare (requires attunement) This is a +2 shield with two additional features, each activated by speaking a different command word. When a feature is used, neither feature can be used again until a short or long rest is completed.Unfurled Shield. When the first command is given, two additional shields unfurl from either side of the shield. Any ally standing behind or beside the wielder and staying no further than 5 feet away from the wielder, gains the benefits of three-quarters cover (+5 bonus to AC and Dexterity saving throws). Archer’s Wall. When the second command is given the shield must be released and set on the ground as numerous small shields unfurl and fan out in all directions. They create a U-shaped wall, 10 feet long, 10 feet high, and 5 feet wide, with arrow slits every few feet. There is room for two Medium-sized creatures to use ranged weapons behind this wall. Anyone standing behind the wall has total cover.
