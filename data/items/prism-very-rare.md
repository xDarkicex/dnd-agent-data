# Prism (Very Rare)

**description**: Prism Weapon (Warhammer), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When within 1000 feet of a planar portal, the weapon flickers with dim light in a 5-foot radius. If the nearest portal is connected to one of the Elemental Planes, this light is colored according to the Duplicate Colors table below (for example, red light for the Plane of Fire). Tetradic Refraction . While in an area of dim or bright light, you can use your bonus action to raise Prism and cast mirror image , refracting your form into four monochromatic duplicates as you do so. When cast this way, the spell has the following modifications. You split into four different colored duplicates, each with an associated plane (see the Duplicate Colors table). Your essence flits between the four duplicates for the duration. If you have three or more duplicates, you must roll a 6 or higher to change the attack’s target to a duplicate. When you attack with this weapon, you attack as a chosen duplicate, dealing an additional 3d8 damage of its associated type on a hit. When a duplicate is destroyed, the GM randomly determines which one has been destroyed. If it is destroyed by a melee attack, it deals 3d8 damage of the duplicate’s associated type to the attacker. When three duplicates have been destroyed, the spell ends as normal, and you return to your original form. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. Duplicate Colors Plane Color Damage Air Yellow Lightning Earth Green Poison Fire Red Fire Water Blue Cold Progression Prism progresses when it spends 7 consecutive days in an Elemental Plane (Air, Earth, Fire, or Water) that it hasn’t already spent 7 consecutive days in.
