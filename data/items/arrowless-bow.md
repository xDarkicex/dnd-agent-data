# Arrowless Bow

**description**: Arrowless Bow Ranged weapon Price: 1,000 gp This ornate bow was clearly a failed attempt to craft a bow of unlimited ammunition. The arrowless bow can fire any object that the wielder can hold, the size of an arrow or smaller, except for arrows. The wielder of the bow must have the object in their hand in order to use it as ammunition for the bow. Make an Attack Roll as if firing a normal bow. The damage of the attack is dependent on the object being fired, ranging from 1 damage to as much as 1d12 damage, determined by the game master. The Dexterity bonus should still be added to each Attack Roll.
