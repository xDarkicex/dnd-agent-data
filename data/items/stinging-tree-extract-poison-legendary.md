# Stinging Tree Extract Poison, legendary

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: While even a sting from this deadly tree can cause a person to suffocate (or more likely suffer permanent and debilitating pain) this extract ensures their death. In fact, the toxin is so volatile that it must be stored in a dart or other container to be delivered, as even being exposed to air near the liquid can subject a person to its effects. A creature transferring the fluid or standing within 10 feet of a coated weapon without proper respiratory protection must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw each round they are exposed or be paralyzed for 1 hour. If a creature fails this saving throw while paralyzed, the creature begins suffocating. The extract loses its potency after being exposed to air for 30 seconds. A creature hit by a weapon coated in the poison, or injected with the poison, must make a DC 18 Constitution saving throw or be paralyzed for 1 hour and gain the Lethal Injury “The End is Nigh”. On a successful saving throw, the creature is paralyzed and cannot breathe for 1 minute.
