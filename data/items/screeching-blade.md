# Screeching Blade

**description**: Weapon (Dagger), Common When a creature within 60 feet of you that you aren't aware of makes a Dexterity (Stealth) check to hide and the result of the check is less than 20, the dagger emits a screech that can be heard out to a range of 60 feet. The screech does not reveal the hiding creature's location, but prevents you and any other creature that can hear it from being surprised by that creature.
