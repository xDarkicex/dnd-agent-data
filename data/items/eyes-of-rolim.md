# Eyes of Rolim

**description**: Eyes of Rolim Wondrous Item, Legendary (requires attunement) Built by the deep dwarf demigod Rolim the Technurge, these arcane-infused spectacles allow sages to skim through entire libraries, solve magic mysteries, and discern the true nature of illusions and shapeshifting creatures. While wearing the Eyes of Rolim, you have truesight out to 10 feet. Arcana Mastery. While wearing the Eyes of Rolim, you have advantage on Intelligence (Arcana) checks when examining magic items, creations wrought by magic, spell effects, magic abilities, and supernatural phenomena. Additionally, you can use an action to cast detect magic or identify at will. Rolim’s Sagacity. While wearing the Eyes of Rolim, you can read all writing that you see through the eyes’ lens. Additionally, you can read at triple your normal speed and have advantage on Constitution checks and saving throws related to mental fatigue from prolonged cognitive activity.
