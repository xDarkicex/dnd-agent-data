# Staff of Ionization

**description**: Staff, Rare This staff is crafted from a conductive alloy made of adamantine and magnetite. It can be wielded as a magic quarterstaff. Ionize Air The staff has 3 charges for the following properties and regains 1 charge after each hour you spend rubbing it with a lodestone. Electrical Discharge. When you make a melee attack using the staff, you can expend 1 charge to fill the staff with electricity. On a hit, the target takes an extra 1d6 Lightning damage or twice as much if it is wearing metal armor or is made of metal. On a miss, you take 1d6 Lightning damage instead. Redirect Energy. As a Reaction when you are targeted by a spell or effect that deals Lightning damage, you can expend 1 charge to redirect the spell or effect to instead target another creature within 30 feet that isn't already targeted by it. Ionize. As an Action, you can expend 2 charges to amplify electrical conductivity around the staff for 1 minute. When a creature within 20 feet of the staff takes Lightning damage, it takes an extra 1d6 Lightning damage and must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution save or it can't take Reactions until the end of its next turn.
