# Pinpoint Barrier

**description**: Pinpoint Barrier Mod (armor), uncommon (requires affinity) This mod generates a 2-foot diameter invisible energy shield that hovers inches from you and intuitively shifts to locations on your body susceptible to incoming attacks. You gain a +1 bonus to AC and saving throws while you use this mod.
