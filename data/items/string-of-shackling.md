# String of Shackling

**description**: String of Shackling Wondrous item, rare This piece of woven flax is 3 feet long. If tied about the wrists of a creature that is incapacitated, that creature cannot break or untie the string except with a wish , though any other creature may do so with ease. If the string is cut or broken it loses its magic.
