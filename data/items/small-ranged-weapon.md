# Small Ranged Weapon

**description**: Small Ranged Weapon Ranged weapon Price: 15 gp A small weapon used to attack from a distance. (See rules for weapons in the Combat section) Ranged. Ranged weapons are used to attack enemies at a distance. These weapons can be either shot like a bow and arrow or crossbow, or thrown like a spear or javelin. All ranged weapons use Dexterity for both the Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus. Small. Small weapons are used one-handed and are usually small enough to be hidden away in a boot, up a sleeve, or secretly tucked into a small bag. Small and light melee weapons can use either Strength (Str) or Dexterity (Dex) for the Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus, whichever is higher. Damage: 1d4
