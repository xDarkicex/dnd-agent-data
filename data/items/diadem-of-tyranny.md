# Diadem of Tyranny

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement, cursed) This symbol of royalty takes the form of a gold circlet set with a large, softly glowing ruby. Stories of its creation vary from a tyrannical ruler who wanted to ensure their successors did not stray from the path they had laid out, to an evil advisor seeking to damage a king’s reputation in preparation for a coup. Whatever the reason, this diadem makes its bearer a tyrannical ruler, but an effective one. While attuned to this item, you gain the ability to cast thaumaturgy once per turn without using an action. Additionally, you gain a +2 bonus to Deception, Intimidation, and Insight checks, and may cast dominate person from the diadem once per day. While attuned, your alignment becomes lawful evil if it was not already, and you gain the following flaw: Tyrant. I have a need to control everyone and everything. All must bow to me and follow my orders. I am the rightful ruler of my region. You may not unattune from the diadem until a remove curse or similar effect is cast on you.
