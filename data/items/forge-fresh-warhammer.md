# Forge-Fresh Warhammer

**description**: Forge-Fresh Warhammer Weapon (Warhammer), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This massive metallic warhammer is warm to the touch and covered in minor cracks. The red, glowing light emanating from beneath the cracks suggests a core of molten lava. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While attuned to this weapon, you are resistant to cold damage. A creature (other than you) that touches this weapon while you are attuned to it takes 1d6 fire damage. If the creature continues to hold the weapon, it takes 1d6 fire damage at the start of each of its turns.
