# Pluckpatch (uncommon variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) Component: fiend skin This plain, worn, leather eyepatch is an unassuming addition to any treasure hoard. However, each day at dawn, eyeballs are plucked from ‘donor’ fiends in the Hells in order to slowly orbit around your head. Whether fiends are willing donors or victims of a crafty wizard’s devilish machinations is an unanswered moral dilemma. This eyepatch has 4 charges, represented by four floating eyeballs, and regains 1d4 expended charges daily at dawn. Peekaboo. As an action, you can expend 1 charge to telepathically link yourself to one eyeball. While linked, you see through the eyeball, which has darkvision out to 120 feet, and you are blind with regards to your own vision. As a bonus action while linked to the eyeball, you can move the eyeball up to 30 feet in any direction. There is no limit to how far away from you the eye can move, but it can’t enter another plane of existence. The eyeball has 2 hit points, an AC of 15, and a Dexterity (Stealth) modifier of +7. After 10 minutes, if the eyeball is reduced to 0 hit points, or if you sever the connection (no action required), the eyeball disappears, temporarily returning to its fiendish donor. Uncommon variant: Reduce the charges (and eyes) to 2, the recharge to 1d2, and the eyeballs’ Stealth modifier to +5.
