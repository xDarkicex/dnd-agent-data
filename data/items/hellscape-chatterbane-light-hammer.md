# Hellscape Chatterbane Light Hammer

**description**: Weapon (any melee), uncommon Component: pouch of fiend teeth When metal is in short supply, devils and demons alike turn to more plentiful resources of the Lower Planes: teeth, bones, and molten rock. The skull of a hellscape chatterbane is often the focus of the magical enchantments weaved into it, producing flame from its eye sockets and laughter from its mouth. While holding this magic weapon, you can use an action to cast the produce flame cantrip (+5 to hit). Posthumous Mirth. As an action, you can shake the weapon to cast the hideous laughter spell (save DC 13), causing the skull’s jaw to rattle up and down, and a malevolent cackling sound to fill the air. Once this property of the weapon has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
