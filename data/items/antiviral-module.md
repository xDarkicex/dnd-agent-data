# Antiviral Module

**description**: Antiviral Module Utility, uncommon (requires affinity) This 3-inch diameter device adheres to your skin and secretes a specialized antiviral solution. While you wear it, you are immune to contracting any disease. If you are already infected with a disease, the effects of the disease are suppressed while you wear the device.
