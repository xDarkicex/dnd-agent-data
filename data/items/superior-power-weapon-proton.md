# Superior Power Weapon (Proton)

**description**: Power Weapon Weapon (any), varies (requires affinity) This weapon generates energy that deals additional damage of a specific damage type. It often bears physical traits related to this energy type (surrounded by fire, frost, lightning, etc.). At the GMs discretion, they can also possess alternate damage type properties (see “Variant: Alternative Damage Properties” earlier in this chapter). You deal an extra 1d6 damage of a specific damage type to any target you hit with this weapon. The GM chooses the damage type or determines it randomly. If the weapon is a melee weapon, it sheds bright light in a radius and dim light in an additional radius defined by the designated damage type.
