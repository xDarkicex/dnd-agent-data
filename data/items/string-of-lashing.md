# String of Lashing

**description**: String of Lashing Wondrous item, uncommon This item consists of a 4-inch piece of black twine tied to a small wooden peg. While holding this item, you can speak its command word as a bonus action to cause it to transform into a +1 whip . Speaking the command word again causes it to revert to string form.
