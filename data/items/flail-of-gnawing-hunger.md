# Flail of Gnawing Hunger

**description**: Weapon (Flail), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This black iron flail has a head embossed with two rows of spikes resembling a chaotic maw of teeth. When swung, air moves erratically through the teeth, creating a distorted whistle resembling the cackle of a hyena. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you hit a creature with this weapon and roll a 19 or 20 on the attack roll, the target must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take an extra 2d6 piercing damage and have the maw of teeth detach from the head and begin chewing on the target. Once attached, the target takes 2d6 piercing damage at the end of each of your turns until it reaches 0 hit points, or until you end the effect with a bonus action. The target can attempt a DC 15 Strength saving throw at the start of each of its turns. On a success, the maw of teeth is removed, becoming inert, and this property can’t be activated again until you finish a long rest (whereupon the weapon regenerates new teeth). Encounter Ideas Relentless Famine. A gnoll clan ravages the countryside. Xin, a surviving eyewitness, describes a black flail and a soldier crumbling on the battlefield. The community begs for help. Lycan Surprise. The local magistrate asks the party to investigate rumors of a gnoll with a flail plaguing the countryside. It is a werewolf. (Twist: the werewolf is kin to the magistrate!) Assassinate Emptor. A noble who purchased a flail of gnawing hunger from a traveling merchant hires the party for protection. The merchant, a disguised green hag, named Auntie Yummy, encouraged a gnoll clan to claim the weapon.
