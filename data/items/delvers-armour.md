# Delver's Armour

**description**: Delver's Armour Armour (heavy, plate), very rare (requires attunement) You have a +1 bonus to AC while wearing this armour. While wearing the armour underwater, you can speak it’s command word as an action to create a bubble of air around your head. It allows you to breathe normally underwater. This bubble stays with you until you speak the command word again, you doff the armour, or you are no longer underwater. AC: 18 Modifiers: AC +1 Stealth: Disadvantage Weight: 65
