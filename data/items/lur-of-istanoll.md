# Lur of Istanoll

**description**: Lur of Istanoll Wondrous item, very rare This magical lur was crafted by artisans working at the University of Istanoll to be used for a variety of purposes. When used to perform, it grants a +2 bonus to Charisma (Performance) checks and it may be used as a shield, though it only grants a +1 bonus to armor class. The lur is also capable of producing several magical notes. To learn a single note requires a long rest and a successful DC 17 Charisma (Performance) check. The notes are learned in the following order. All the notes prior to a note must be learned before that note can learned. Ignite/Dim. Either all candles and torches within 120 feet are lit, or their flames are extinguished (performer’s choice). Inspire. All creatures of your choice within 30 feet gain the benefits of the bless spell for 1 minute. Open. All non-magical locks are unlocked, and all non-magical doors are opened within 500 feet. Replenish. As a reaction to an ally casting a spell, the performer may use this feature to restore the spell slot the caster just used to the caster. Regroup. The performer selects up to 3 specific individuals they know on the current plane of existence. For the next twelve hours those individuals know the location of the performer and that the performer wishes to be found by them. Once this note is learned, the user knows there are no more notes to learn. The user is unable to teach notes to other potential users—each student must discover their own knowledge, themselves, which was once a motto used by the university. Once a specific note is played, that note cannot be played again until a short or long rest is finished.
