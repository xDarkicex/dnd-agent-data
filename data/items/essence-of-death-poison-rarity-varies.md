# Essence of Death Poison, rarity varies

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Essence of Death is the distilled extract of the Destroying Angel mushroom whose cap is so toxic that consuming even one can kill an adult human. A creature that ingests or is Injured with a weapon coated in this poison must make a Constitution saving throw of the DC indicated in the table below or drop to 0 hit points. If they fail their saving throw by 5 or more, they gain the Lethal Injury “The End is Nigh”. On a successful saving throw, the creature is stunned for one round and poisoned until it finishes a short rest. When “The End is Nigh” is gained in this way, it can be removed with a spell that cures poison cast using a spell slot of at least 4th, 5th, or 6th level, depending on the poison concentration.\n POISON CONCENTRATION DC RARITY\n Weak 13 Rare\n Strong 15 Very rare\n Exceptional 18 Very rare\n
