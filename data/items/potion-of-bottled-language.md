# Potion of Bottled Language

**description**: Potion of Bottled Language Potion, Uncommon (rare for exotic or dead languages) Prized by merchants and diplomats alike, these potions have been infused with the concentrated essence of a language. Different potions for different languages exist, and potions infused with exotic or dead languages are often more expensive than potions of more common languages. When you drink this potion, you can magically speak, read, and write the language whose essence the potion includes for 1 hour. This potion’s color may vary depending on language and dialect, but all variants have tiny translucent letters floating in them. Shaking the bottle momentarily combines the letters into random words of the language it includes. If you drink one potion of the same bottled language every day for 30 days, you permanently learn to read and write that language. Linguistic Intoxication. If you drink a Potion of Bottled Language while still under the effects of another Potion of Bottled Language you must succeed on a DC 15 Intelligence saving throw or become linguistically intoxicated for 1 hour. While linguistically intoxicated, you feel light-headed and your speech is slurred; and you can only speak, read, and write only the languages of the potions that currently affect you.
