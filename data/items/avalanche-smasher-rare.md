# Avalanche Smasher (Rare)

**description**: Avalanche Smasher Weapon (Flail), Rare (Requires Attunement) Avalanche Smasher has 3 heads and 6 charges. It regains 3 charges daily at dawn. While holding this magic weapon, you can expend 2 or more of its charges to activate one of its properties. The saving throw DC for this weapon’s properties is equal to 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Constitution modifier. Infuse (2 Charges). As a bonus action, you infuse biting frost into the weapon. The next time you hit a creature with an attack using Avalanche Smasher , the attack deals 3d6 cold damage and the target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or have their speed halved until the end of their next turn. Avalanche Break (4 Charges). When you are targeted by an effect that deals cold damage in an area, you can use your reaction to create a 60-foot cone originating from the flail. The effect is nullified inside this area. Flash Freeze (4 Charges). As an action, you channel the frost from Avalanche Smasher into the flesh of a creature you can see within 30 feet. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or become frozen for 1 minute. While frozen, the creature is immune to cold damage and vulnerable to bludgeoning, force, and thunder damage. The target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of their turns, ending the effect on a success. Progression The tears of a cold-breathing dragon form new heads that can be attached to the flail.
