# Eigenblade

**description**: Weapon (Dagger), Rare (Requires Attunement) This dagger is made of a black, shimmering alloy. It emits a barely audible humming sound and its blade vibrates intensely when used. Lethal Resonance As an Action, you can tune the dagger's vibrational frequency to match a specific creature type for 1 hour. When you attack a creature of that type with the dagger, you gain the following benefits: Penetrating. You have a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls and the attack ignores any Resistances the creature has. Lethal. You score a Critical Hit on a roll of 19 or 20 on the d20. If the attack is a Critical Hit, the target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution save or have Disadvantage on all D20 Tests until the end of your next turn. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
