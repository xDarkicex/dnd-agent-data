# Soulkeeper Pendant

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (optional attunement) Component: phial of celestial blood Tools of devilish origin, these mundane-looking miniature animal skulls are designed to increase the throughput of souls to the Hells. When a humanoid dies within 5 feet of the pendant and the pendant doesn’t already have a soul trapped in it, its soul becomes trapped in a flame-filled, hellish demiplane, causing the pendant’s eyes to glow fiery red. Casting the remove curse spell or similar magic on the pendant releases a soul from its captivity. A soul can be stored indefinitely, and time spent in captivity within the pendant doesn’t count against the time limit of resurrection spells such as raise dead. A creature who is resurrected after its soul has been stored within the pendant must make a Wisdom saving throw or develop one long-term madness effect. The DC for the saving throw equals 10 + the number of days the soul was trapped in the pendant. Optional Attunement: Sellsoul. Upon attuning to this pendant, a devil, Eebezelbub, appears to you with an offer. She will trick souls you capture into following her to the Hells in exchange for granting you minor favours. If the pendant has a soul trapped in it, you can use an action to call Eebezelbub’s name and permit her entry to the pendant. Roll a d6. On a 1, the soul refuses Eeb’s offer and drifts away, causing the miniature animal’s eyes to slowly dim. On a 2-6, the soul accepts her offer and the skull flashes brightly before petering out. Eebezelbub reappears 1d20 minutes later, offering to perform one of the following tasks of your choice: She will deliver and return one message, as per the sending spell, and can send the message to other planes of existence without the possibility of failure, but she can’t enter good-aligned planes to deliver the message. She will answer three questions, as per the commune spell. She will divulge information on a topic, as per the legend lore spell. Eebezelbub needs time to process the souls that accept her offers, and after getting two souls from the pendant, she won’t take any more until the next dawn.
