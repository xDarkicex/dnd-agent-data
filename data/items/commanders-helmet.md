# Commander's Helmet

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This magic helmet has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. When another creature within 30 feet that can hear you makes an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can use your reaction and expend 1 charge to give that creature advantage on its roll.
