# Fire Whispers

**description**: Fire Whispers Wondrous item, rare This is a small droplet made of glass. If dropped into a fire, a short message can be sent to another person. When the flames touch the fire whisper, they summon an invisible valkry who listens to the message and flies to the target, whispering the message to them. This functions much like the message spell except the target can be anywhere on the plane of existence. This item is consumed when used.
