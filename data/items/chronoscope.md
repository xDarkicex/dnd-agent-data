# Chronoscope

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) This pocket watch is set with a perpetually spinning dial that indicates various probabilities. Precognition You have Advantage on Initiative rolls and on checks made to find and identify traps. Bend Luck As a Reaction when a creature within 60 feet makes a d20 roll, you can roll a d10 and add or subtract the number rolled from the result. You can do so after the creature rolls the die, but before the outcome is determined. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
