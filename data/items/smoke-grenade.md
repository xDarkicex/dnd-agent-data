# Smoke Grenade

**description**: As an action, you can throw a grenade at a point up to 60 feet away. The grenade explodes at that point, creating a particular effect in a 20-foot-radius Sphere. Smoke Grenade. The area of the Sphere is Heavily Obscured by smoke for 1 minute. A strong wind (such as the Gust of Wind spell) disperses the smoke. Weight: 2 lb. Cost: 50 GP
