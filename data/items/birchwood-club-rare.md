# Birchwood Club (Rare)

**description**: Birchwood Club Weapon (Club), Rare (Requires Attunement by a Creature Who Can Read) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Unless stated otherwise, each of the weapon’s properties can only be used once between long rests. Curl of Light. One curl forms on the club with the rune for light. You can use an action to rip the curl off and speak its command rune aloud, choosing a point that you can see within 100 feet as you do so. Bright white light fills a 30-foot-radius area emanating from that point for 1 minute. Curl of Growth. A second curl forms on the club, with the rune for growth. You can use an action to rip the curl off and speak its command rune aloud, choosing a creature you can see within 30 feet as you do so. The target grows in size, as per the enlarge/reduce spell, for 1 minute. Curl of Health. A third curl forms on the club, with the rune for health. You can use an action to rip the curl off and speak its command rune aloud, choosing a creature you can see within 30 feet as you do so. The target regains 4d4 hit points and ends one disease or one condition of your choice afflicting it. The condition can be blinded, charmed, deafened, petrified, or poisoned; or you can reduce the target’s exhaustion level by one. Progression The Birchwood Club progresses each time the attuned creature learns a new language, but it can be progressed only once between proficiency bonus increases. As the club progresses, additional curls form in the bark of the club’s wood, a rune inscribed on the underside.
