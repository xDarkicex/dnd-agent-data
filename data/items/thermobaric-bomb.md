# Thermobaric Bomb

**description**: By replacing your bomb’s explosive contents with a lower speed explosive, you can change it into a thermobaric bomb. A thermobaric bomb’s damage dice are d6s, and it explodes in a 10-foot radius.

**properties**:

- Item Type: Weapon (bomb)
