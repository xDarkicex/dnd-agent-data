# Wheel of Flying

**description**: Wheel of Flying Wondrous item, uncommon This wheel is about 1 foot in diameter and constructed from polished wood. While holding it in both hands, you can use an action to speak its command word and gain a flying speed of 60 feet for 1 hour. You must keep both hands on the wheel for the duration or the effect ends. Once used, the wheel can’t be used again until the next dawn. I think the inspiration for this device may have been taken from a peculiar world I visit from time to time. Real magic is rare there, while gnome-like gadgetry is very advanced indeed. In this place, they use wheels to control steel vehicles, some of which traverse the land, some the water, and others the air.
