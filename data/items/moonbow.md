# Moonbow

**description**: Moonbow Ranged weapon (shortbow or longbow), rare (requires attunement) You can use a bonus action to speak this magic bow’s command word, causing moonlight to glow from the length. This moonlight shed bright light in a 40-foot radius and dim light for an additional 40 feet. While the bow is ablaze, it deals an extra 2d6 fire damage to any target it hits. The flames last until you use a bonus action to speak the command word again or until you drop or sheathe the bow.
