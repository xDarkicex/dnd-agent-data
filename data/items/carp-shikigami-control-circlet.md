# carp shikigami control circlet

**description**: Carp Shikigami Control Circlet Very rare, wondrous item, (requires attunement) This circlet is one of four created by Nahoko as a defense mechanism for Closed Shell Castle. Each has been connected to a powerful shikigami perched as a statue atop the castle. To use it, a circlet must be charged through deep meditation with the circlet in hand. For every two hours of meditation, the circlet can be used for one hour, for a maximum of six hours of usage total. When a circlet is activated within a quarter mile of the castle, a carp shikigami descends from its perch and comes to the wearer of the circlet. The wearer can use their action each round to control the precise actions of the carp using its profile and powers, or, using their bonus action can command the carp to do a general action such as “protect me,” “fly me to [a location within a quarter mile of the castle],” or “attack that creature.” If left without a command, the GM determines the actions of the carp. Once the stored connection expires, the shikigami becomes sluggish and makes its way to open air and back to its perch.
