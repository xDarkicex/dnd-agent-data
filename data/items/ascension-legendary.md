# Ascension (Legendary)

**description**: Ascension Weapon (Greatsword), Legendary (Requires Attunement) You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While attuned to this magic weapon, you have advantage on Charisma checks made to advance the interests of the territory you rule, such as negotiating a treaty or securing resources for your kingdom. It is not intended for checks that only benefit you personally. Luster of Rule. As an action, you can cast the charm person spell. If the target has a CR of 1/2 or less, the duration increases to 1 year. While charmed, the target views you as an exceptional ruler and is more amenable to offering their services to your territory. Once used, this property can’t be used again for 7 days. Impeccable Statesperson. Whenever you make a Charisma check or saving throw, you can roll an additional d20. You can choose to do this after you roll the initial die, but before the outcome is determined. You choose which of the d20s is used. You can use this property a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus and regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.
