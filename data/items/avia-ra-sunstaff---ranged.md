# Avia-Ra Sunstaff - Ranged

**description**: This long staff, forked at its end near a prominent gemstone, is a blaster in its own right. When used in expert hands, the sunstaff can bludgeon, trip, and strike foes as a quarterstaff, only to be squeezed in both hands to fire a burst of hot plasma seconds later. This weapon can also be used as a simple melee weapon, which deals 1d6 bludgeoning damage and has the Versatile (1d8) property.
