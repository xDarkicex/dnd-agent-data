# Ring of the Shadowdancer

**description**: Ring of the Shadowdancer Ring, rare (requires attunement) Weight: none This dark, obsidian ring has a faint glow of magic energy. While wearing this ring, you gain the following benefits: You have advantage on Stealth checks. You can cast Disguise Self once per day without expending a spell slot. You can cast Darkness once per day without expending a spell slot. You can cast Misty Step once per day without expending a spell slot. The Shadowdancers are a secretive thieves guild based in Morgos. Each member of the guild receives a magic ring that gives them special abilities and also allows the Guildmaster to keep track of members’ locations without their knowledge.
