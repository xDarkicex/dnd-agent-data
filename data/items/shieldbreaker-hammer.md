# Shieldbreaker Hammer

**description**: Shieldbreaker Hammer Weapon (warhammer), very rare (requires attunement) Damage: 1d8+2 bludgeoning Weight: 3 lbs. Properties: Versatile (1d10+2) This massive warhammer has a heavy, blackened head. You have a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Power Slam. Once per turn, when you hit a creature with the hammer, the target must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. Shieldbreaker. Once per long rest, you can use a bonus action to speak a command word and activate the hammer's special ability. For the next minute, your attacks with this hammer deal an additional 1d6 force damage, and any shield-bearing creature you hit must make a DC 15 Strength saving throw or have their shield shattered. This saving throw is at advantage if the shield is magic. Creatures with Uncommon magic shields gain +5 on their save. Rare shields have advantage on the save. Very Rare and higher shields can’t be broken by the hammer.
