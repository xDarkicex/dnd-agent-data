# Wand of Conjure Animals

**description**: Wand, uncommon (requires attunement by a spellcaster) This wand has 9 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 of its charges to cast the conjure animals spell from it. The wand regains 1d8 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the wand’s last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the wand crumbles into ashes and is destroyed.

- Item Type: Wand
- Item Rarity: uncommon
