# Fabulous Wig

**description**: Fabulous Wig Wondrous Item, Uncommon Created by a legendary drag queen who mastered the art of transformation, this magic wig helps bards to be more fabulous and rogues to be sneakier. You can use your action to wear the wig, which magically adjusts to fit perfectly onto your head. Thereafter, the wig can’t be removed unless you detach it as an action or you die. Not even an antimagic field causes it to fall off. Feeling the Fantasy. While wearing this wig, you can use a bonus action to cause it to take on any hairstyle you wish. The wig physically changes its color, length, and texture to match the new hair style. You can also use this property to instantly clean or soil the wig. Wig Reveal. You can wear a number of Fabulous Wigs at once equal to your Constitution modifier (minimum of one). Realness. While worn, the Fabulous Wig is indistinguishable from real hair. Only a wish spell can reveal the wig’s true nature while worn. The wig envelops whoever wears it in a faint aura of transmutation magic if targeted by detect magic . Hair Pull Immunity. While wearing this wig, you are immune to any damage related to hair pulling. Optional Rule: Dreaded Wig Snatch At the gamemaster’s discretion, creatures wearing one or more Fabulous Wigs can have the ability to remove each other’s Fabulous Wigs. When a creature wearing one or more Fabulous Wigs wants to remove another creature’s Fabulous Wig, they can use the attack action to make a special melee attack, the Dreaded Wig Snatch. If the creature is able to make multiple attacks with the attack action, this attack replaces one of them. The target of the Dreaded Wig Snatch must be within the attacking creature’s range. Using at least one free hand, the creature tries to seize the targeted creature’s Fabulous Wig by making a Strength (Athletics) check contested by the targeted creature’s Strength (Athletics) or Dexterity (Acrobatics) check (the targeted creature chooses the ability to use). If the creature performing the Dreaded Wig Snatch wins the contest, they remove the targeted creature’s topmost Fabulous Wig.
