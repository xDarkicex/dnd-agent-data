# Oath of the Green

**description**: Armor (shield), uncommon (requires attunement by a druid) This wenge wood shield is marked with druidic runes that glow with a faint green light. You gain a +1 to AC while you wear this shield. The shield functions as a holy symbol for a servant of a deity associated with nature.
