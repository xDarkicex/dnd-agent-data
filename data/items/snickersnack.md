# Snickersnack

**description**: Snickersnack Weapon (Rapier), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This nearly weightless blade looks like it is made of smoke. Tiny moonstones decorate the guard and grip. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While you are attuned to this weapon, you can cast haste on yourself as a bonus action. Once you have cast the spell in this way, you can’t do so again until you finish a long rest.
