# Field Camouflage Unit

**description**: Field Camouflage Unit Utility (limited), uncommon When you use an action to activate this small, disc-shaped device, you and each creature and object within 10 feet of you become invisible for 2d4 minutes. The duration is the same for all subjects, and the device itself becomes useless once activated. If a creature affected by the device attacks or uses an esper power, the invisibility ends for that creature.
