# Coral Cutlass

**description**: Weapon (Scimitar), Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic scimitar is crafted from colorful corals. You don't suffer any penalties on melee weapon attacks while using the scimitar while underwater. Coral Guard. As a reaction when a creature you can see hits you with a weapon attack, you can cause the weapon's coral guard to expand. You gain a +2 bonus to your AC against the triggering attack, potentially causing it to miss. Wave Slash. As an action, you can create a wave of water extending from the sword in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 15 Strength saving throw. On a failure, the creature takes 3d8 Bludgeoning damage, is pushed back 10 feet, and falls Prone. On a success, the creature takes half as much damage and suffers no additional effects. The water extinguishes unprotected flames in its area and then it vanishes. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn. Ossifying Strike. Once per turn, when you hit a creature with the scimitar, you can force the target to make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw. On a failure, the creature begins ossifying. Until the end of its next turn, the creature's speed is halved and it must choose whether it gets an action, a bonus action, or a move on its turn; it gets only one of the three.
