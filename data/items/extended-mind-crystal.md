# Extended Mind Crystal

**description**: Extended Mind Crystal Wondrous Item, Uncommon These gemstones contain a crystallized bit of spellcasting magic. Different types of mind crystals exist, each with a different single-use effect. When you cast a spell that has a casting time of 1 action while holding a mind crystal , you can modify the spell in a specific way that has an effect described below. You can use only a single mind crystal to modify the spell, and you can’t use a mind crystal and a Metamagic option on the same spell. Once you use a mind crystal , it becomes a nonmagical gem worth 50 gp. Extended. If the spell has a duration of 1 minute or longer, double the spell’s duration, to a maximum duration of 24 hours.
