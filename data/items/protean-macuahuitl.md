# Protean Macuahuitl

**description**: Weapon (Club or Greatclub), Rare The edge of this wooden club has notches designed to hold shards of various materials. Mineral Strike Working for 1 hour, you can embed the club with 100 gp worth of Adamantine, Diamond, Quartz, or Tungsten, granting the club one of these properties until you replace the material. Adamantine. The club deals double damage to objects. Diamond. The club deals an extra 1d6 Piercing damage on a hit. Quartz. The club deals an extra 1d4 Lightning damage on a hit. On a Critical Hit, the target can't take Reactions until the start of your next turn. Tungsten. The club deals an extra 1d4 Bludgeoning damage on a hit and, if the target is a Large or smaller creature, it is pushed 5 feet away. Fragile Sharpness If you roll a 1 on an attack roll using the club, the shards embedded in it shatter and the club loses its magical properties until you spend 1 hour to insert new shards of material worth at least 25 gp.
