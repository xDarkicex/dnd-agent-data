# The Beast (Common)

**description**: The Beast Weapon (Maul), Common (Requires Attunement) The Beast deals piercing damage instead of the bludgeoning damage normal for a maul. The save DC for each of this magic weapon’s properties is equal to 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier. As a bonus action, you can speak the weapon’s command word, causing the maul to issue a deep-throated growl for 1 round. Progression To progress the weapon from one rarity to another, the attuned wielder must reduce a creature to 0 hit points with an attack using The Beast . This creature must have a CR equal to or greater than the wielder’s current level (or CR if it doesn’t have levels). The weapon can be progressed only once between proficiency bonus increases.
