# Leaf of Falling

**description**: Leaf of Falling Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This charm is shaped like a small silver leaf. When you fall while wearing this charm, you descend 60 feet per round and take no damage from falling. It is the rare adventurer who does not occasionally fall from a great height. This item is relatively simple to manufacture but is infinitely useful.
