# Key of Opening

**description**: Key of Opening Wondrous item, uncommon This bronze key has a head shaped like a crown. When inserted into a nonmagical lock and twisted, the key expends a charge and opens the lock. The key has 1d6 + 2 charges when found and becomes nonmagical when you use the last charge.
