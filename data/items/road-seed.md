# Road Seed

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon This seed must be used outdoors, and when thrown, it creates a well-built road through any terrain, up to 5 miles long. The road includes up to one bridge crossing for a stream or chasm up to 50 feet across, and it may likewise include supports, log causeways, or leveled sections along a single section of marsh, hillside, or even mountainous terrain. A road seed combined with a tavern seed can generate a road twice as long as a road seed alone. A road seed can be countered or removed with an earthquake or shadow walk spell.
