# Vial of Spell Conduit Poison

**description**: Potion, Rare This vial contains 3 doses of spell conduit poison. As an action, you can apply one dose of this poison to a weapon or a piece of ammunition you're holding. The first time you hit a creature with the poisoned weapon or ammunition within the next minute, the creature must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or become hypersensitive to magic. For 1 hour, the creature has disadvantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects and the first time each turn it takes damage from a spell, it takes an extra 1d8 of that damage.
