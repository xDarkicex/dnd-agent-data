# Gergulsush (Very Rare)

**description**: Gergulsush Weapon (Whip), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The whip’s handle is tacky, allowing you to grip it firmly. You can’t drop this whip unless you choose to and checks made to forcefully remove the whip from your hand have disadvantage. Extended Reach. The whip’s reach extends to 15 feet. A Mimic Says What? You know how to speak Mimic, which is a series of grunts, slurps, belches, groans, and guttural sounds. What Is It? As a bonus action, you can speak a command word to change the form of the whip into an object of similar size, or back into its whip form. While in object form, it is indistinguishable from an ordinary object. Sticky. When you hit a target with an attack using this weapon, you can choose to have the whip adhere to it. The target is grappled (escape DC 15). Mimic Ally. As a bonus action, you can drop the whip to the ground while uttering a command word. The whip instantly changes into a Medium-sized mimic in the form of a treasure chest for 1 hour. The mimic is friendly to you and your companions. In combat, it shares your your initiative count, but it takes its turn immediately after yours. You can use a bonus action to revert the mimic to its whip form. Any damage, conditions, or other effects are not removed when the mimic transforms back into a whip. The mimic regains all of its hit points when you finish a long rest. If the mimic dies, it reverts to its whip form and can’t be used again in this way until you finish a long rest. Progression Gergulsush progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, so does the weapon’s rarity.
