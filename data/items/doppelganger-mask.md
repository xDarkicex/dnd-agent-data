# Doppelganger Mask

**description**: The doppelganger mask is crafted from the facial skin of a doppelganger, which has been surgically removed and treated with magic. The mask’s wearer can use its action to cast the polymorph spell and change into a Small or Medium humanoid it has previously seen or back into its original form. The user of the doppelganger mask can change between these two forms for the next hour. The transformation ends if the mask is removed. Once used, the magical property of the doppelganger mask can’t be used again until the next dawn.
