# Scorching Fists

**description**: Wondrous item, rare Component: phial of construct oil Made of ceramic and metal plating, these gloves allow their wearer to smelt, weld, and otherwise manipulate metal without fear of burning oneself. In underground battle pits, these gloves see more unconventional uses: with the limiters removed, the glove’s knuckle-exhausts can form blades of devastating flame. While wearing these gloves, you can handle white-hot metal without fear of burning yourself. This item has 10 charges. Over the course of 1 minute, you can fill the gloves’ fuel canisters with 10 gp of high quality oil, causing it to regain 1d8 + 2 expended charges. Once this item regains charges, it can’t regain charges again until the next dawn. Scorching Fist. While wearing this item, you can expend 1 charge (no action required) to deal an additional 2d4 fire damage whenever you hit with an unarmed strike with your fist on your turn. Overheat. As an action, you can expend 3 charges to remove the gloves’ limiter, causing a flaming blade to blossom from each one. The gloves shed bright light in a 10-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet. These blades last for 1 minute or until you extinguish them (no action required). They are simple melee weapons with the finesse and light properties, with which you are proficient. Each deals 3d8 fire damage on a hit.
