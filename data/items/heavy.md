# Heavy

**description**: Apprentice weapon property Components: Masterwork martial or exotic weapon with the Two-Handed property that doesn’t have the Double property This weapon gains the Heavy property. If it is a melee weapon, its damage die increases by one step. If it is ranged, its normal range increases by 20 feet and its long range increases accordingly.
