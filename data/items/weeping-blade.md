# Weeping Blade

**description**: Weeping Blade Melee weapon (martial, whip sword), legendary (requires a ttunement) 1d6 slashing damage. Reach, Special ( Adventures in Rokugan ). Attacks made by this weapon have +1 to hit and deal +1 damage. Creatures damaged by this weapon gain the bleeding condition for 1 minute. Special: When you make an opportunity attack with this weapon, its damage profile is 2d6 slashing. Reach. This weapon adds 5 feet to your reach when you attack with it, as well as when determining your reach for opportunity attacks with it.
