# Deadwalker's Ring

**description**: Deadwalker’s Ring Ring, very rare (requires attunement) While attuned to this ring, whenever you cast a necromancy spell with a casting time of 1 minute, you can cast it as an action instead. You can also create one additional undead creature if you cast a spell that creates or summons undead creatures. In addition, the undead created or summoned by your spells gain a bonus to their attack rolls equal to half your proficiency bonus.
