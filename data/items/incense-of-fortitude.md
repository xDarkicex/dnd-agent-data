# Incense of Fortitude

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon This magical incense burns for 1 hour and produces an intoxicating scent of spices and myrrh. A creature that inhales the incense during a short rest gains temporary hit points equal to their character level and has advantage on Constitution saving throws for 8 hours.
