# Fragment of Deception

**description**: Fragment of Deception Benefit . When you take damage, you can use your reaction to turn invisible and teleport up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. You remain invisible until the start of your next turn or until you make a damage roll or cast a spell. Drawback . You have disadvantage on Wisdom checks.
