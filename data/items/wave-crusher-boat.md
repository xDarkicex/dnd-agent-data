# Wave-crusher Boat

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This 15-feet long boat is a magnificent vessel crafted from a combination of enchanted wood and reinforced metal. Its sleek and sturdy design reflects its purpose as a vessel that fearlessly navigates treacherous waters. The wave-crusher boat is virtually unsinkable and cannot capsize. Even in the face of monstrous waves, turbulent currents, or supernatural forces, the boat remains upright and steady. You can control it merely by thinking of its designated course. It has a movement speed of 50 feet. The wave-crusher boat features an enclosed waterproof and airtight compartment located in the center of the vessel. The compartment can comfortably house up to four medium-sized creatures. It is equipped with cushioned seating, storage compartments, and small viewing windows. It has AC 15 and 100 hit points, a damage threshold of 10, and immunity against poison and psychic damage.
