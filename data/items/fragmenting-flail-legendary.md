# Fragmenting Flail (Legendary)

**description**: Fragmenting Flail Weapon (Flail), Legendary (Requires Attunement) You gain a + 3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. As an action, you twist a metal ring on the handle, causing 1d10 Tiny or smaller nonmagical metal objects not being worn or carried within a 10-foot radius to be drawn to the flail’s head and attached to it. The attached objects may change the weapon’s damage type from bludgeoning to piercing or slashing (at the GM’s discretion). As an action, you can release all attached objects harmlessly. Manufactured Critical. When you hit with an object with an attack using this flail, you can choose to turn that hit into a critical hit. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a short or long rest. Magnetic Draw. When you hit a creature with an attack using this weapon, you can have all creatures wearing metal within a 30-foot-radius sphere centered on you make a DC 19 Strength saving throw. On a failure, each creature is moved 5 feet closer to you. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a short or long rest. Shrapnel. As an action, you can cause the objects attached to the flail to explode in a 30-foot-radius sphere centered on you. All creatures (except you) must make a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw, taking 10d6 magical piercing damage on a failure, and half as much on a success. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a short or long rest. Eroding Critical. On a critical hit with this weapon, the target must make a DC 19 Dexterity saving throw if it is wearing metal armor. On a failure, the target’s metal armor or shield takes a permanent and cumulative –1 penalty to the AC it offers. Armor reduced to an AC of 10 or a shield that drops to a +0 bonus is destroyed.
