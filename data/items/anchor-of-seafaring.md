# Anchor of Seafaring

**description**: Anchor of Seafaring Wondrous item, uncommon This item consists of a 2-foot-long central iron shaft with an upper crosspiece and two curved bars at the base. Attached to it is a 4-foot length of stout, coarse rope. When the anchor is fastened to a vessel and thrown overboard, you can use an action to speak the anchor’s command word. The rope magically lengthens until the anchor touches bottom or the rope reaches its maximum length of 1 mile. If you use an action to speak the command word again, the rope shortens back to its original length.
