# Refined Delerium Dust

**description**: Refined Delerium Dust Wondrous item, rare This pouch contains an ounce of purified and magically charged delerium dust. It may be ingested as an action. When you consume a full pouch, you gain advantage on spell attack rolls and saving throws against spells for one minute. You gain one level of contamination when you consume refined delerium dust .
