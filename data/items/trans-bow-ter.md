# Trans-bow-ter

**description**: Trans-bow-ter Weapon (Shortbow), Rare (Requires Attunement) Black stars of various shapes and sizes adorn this shortbow, which is constructed from a piece of white oak. You can attack twice, instead of once, with this weapon whenever you take the Attack action on your turn, if you can’t already. Shift-Shot. As an action, you can make an attack against a creature using this weapon. On a hit, the target must make a DC 16 Charisma saving throw; a creature can choose to fail this saving throw if it wishes. On a failed save, the target teleports to an unoccupied space of your choice that you can see within this weapon’s range. Once used to successfully teleport a target, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest.
