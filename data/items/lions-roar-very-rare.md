# Lion's Roar (Very Rare)

**description**: Weapon (Warhammer), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) Common As an action, you can wave this magic weapon in a circle over your head, causing it to emit a lion’s roar that can be heard up to 1 mile away. Uncommon This weapon has 3 charges, and regains 1d3 charges when you finish a long rest. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 charge to cast thunderwave or 2 charges to cast shatter (spell save DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Charisma modifier). Rare Silencing Roar. When you hit a creature with this weapon, you can attempt to curse it. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Charisma modifier) or be cursed for 1 minute. While cursed, the creature is deafened and cannot speak or make sound. Casting a spell that includes a verbal component is impossible. At the end of each of its turns, the target can make another Wisdom saving throw. On a success, the curse ends. Once used, you can’t use this property again until you finish a short or long rest. Very Rare Rallying Roar. As an action, you can rally allies within 60 feet with an inspiring roar. Rallied allies gain 10 temporary hit points and can immediately move up to their speed as a reaction without provoking opportunity attacks. In addition, your allies are immune to the charmed and frightened conditions for 1 minute. If they are currently charmed or frightened, those conditions end on them. Once used, you can’t use this property again until you finish a long rest.
