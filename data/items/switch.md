# Switch

**description**: Apprentice weapon property Components: Two masterwork exotic weapons This weapon gains the Switch property and its damage die decreases by one step. You can integrate two weapons together with the Switch property, forming a single unit in which each weapon is one of the unit’s two forms. You can choose to use two identical weapons with the Light property for one of the weapon’s forms. Switch. This weapon has two forms. You can swap between which weapon is being used at any time, even between attacks.
