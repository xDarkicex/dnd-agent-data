# Mind's Bite

**description**: Weapon (Morningstar), Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic morningstar is crafted from the flesh of a strange, otherworldly creature. The morningstar has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. When you hit a creature with the morningstar, you can expend 1 charge to deal an extra 1d8 Psychic damage to the target and cause one of the following effects: Aberrant Whisper. The target must succeed on a DC 13 Intelligence saving throw or be Frightened until the end of its next turn. Eldritch Curse. The target must succeed on a DC 13 Charisma saving throw or be affected as by the Bane spell until the end of its next turn. Mind Numbing. The target must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or its Intelligence and Charisma scores are reduced by 1d4, to a minimum of 1, until it finishes a short or long rest. If this reduces either ability score to 1, the creature can't cast spells, activate magic items, understand language, or communicate in any intelligible way, until the ability score is more than 1.
