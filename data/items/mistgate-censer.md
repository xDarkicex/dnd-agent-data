# Mistgate Censer

**description**: Mistgate Censer Weapon (Mace), Legendary (Requires Attunement by a Cleric or Paladin) This flail-like mace was created as a censer for religious ceremonies, creating crimson clouds of redolent incense that hang in the air. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. You can use this weapon as a holy symbol, and while you are attuned to it, you gain a +2 bonus to your spell attack rolls and the saving throw DCs of your spells. Sacred Smoke. While holding this weapon, you can light the incense in the censer as a bonus action, causing billowing, red smoke to issue forth from it until the end of your turn. When you light the censer, you can choose for the smoke to magically glow, shedding dim light for 10 feet. As you move, smoke lingers in the spaces you moved into, filling those spaces. Areas of smoke remain for 1 minute (or until dispersed by a wind with a speed of 20 miles an hour or more) and are heavily obscured for all creatures except you. You can teleport to any unoccupied smoke-filled space as a bonus action. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest.
