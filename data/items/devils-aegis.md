# Devil's Aegis

You have resistance to Cold, Fire, and Poison damage. Devil's Sight. You have Darkvision out to a range of 60 feet and magical darkness doesn't impede your vision. Magic Resistance. You have advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
