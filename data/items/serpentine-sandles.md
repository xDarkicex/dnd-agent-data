# Serpentine Sandles

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) While wearing these glistening, snake-skin sandals, you ignore difficult terrain created by marsh, swamps, or other shallow water, such as a rain-soaked rooftop. In addition, you have advantage on ability checks and saving throws made to escape a grapple or the restrained condition while wearing these sandals.
