# Goodberry Hat

**description**: Wondrous Item, Common A single berry hangs on the stalk on this magical hat. A creature can pluck and eat the berry as an action. Eating the berry restores 1 hit point, and the berry provides enough nourishment to sustain a creature for one day. The berry loses its potency if it hasn’t been consumed within 1 hour after being plucked. If the berry is plucked, a new berry grows on the stalk at the next dawn.
