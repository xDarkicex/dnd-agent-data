# Erathor Tea

**description**: Fael , or " Erethor tea" as it is known among the Dorns, is a valuable elixir used by the elves in place of bulky and heavy trail rations. The tea is created magically from various herbs and roots native to Erethor. The tea consists of dark, flaky grounds that, when mixed with water, provide sustenance equivalent to actual rations. The resulting broth is thick and slightly sweet. Two small cups per day serve to keep the drinker energized and healthy. The tea can be used as a sole source of nourishment for up to a month at a time. After that its efficacy quickly fades, and the drinker must spend at least two weeks eating actual food before resuming use of the tea. The tea takes getting used to, and for the first few days, drinkers suffer mild hunger pains and occasional stomach cramps. Heating the tea and drinking it warm helps ease this discomfort, but consuming any food while on the tea restarts the period of adjustment. One pound of Erethor tea powder is enough to make fifty servings. The tea is uncommon in these grim times, even in Erethor itself; it is therefore rather valuable.
