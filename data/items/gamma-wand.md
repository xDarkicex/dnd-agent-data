# Gamma Wand

**description**: Wand, Rare This wand has a radioactive core contained within a white crystal that prevents exposure to radiation. Gamma Ray This wand has 5 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. As an Action, you can expend 1 charge to release a ray of radioactive energy in a 15-foot Cone. Each creature in the area must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution save or take 2d8 Radiant damage and suffer an Acute Radiation Effect until the start of your next turn. If the save fails by 5 or more, it also suffers Radiation Sickness . When you use the wand's Gamma Ray, roll a d4. If the result is equal to or higher than the number of charges the wand has (not including the charge just expended to use it), you are also affected by the ray.
