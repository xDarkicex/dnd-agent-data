# Freewalker’s Shadowgarb

**description**: Freewalker’s Shadowgarb Wondrous Item, Evolving Artifact (requires attunement by a sorcerer) Crafted by a sorcerer of supreme prowess, this morphing outfit appears to be made of a multicolored shimmering silk and can take the form of any outfit the wearer desires. Its true power has since lain dormant, waiting for someone worthy to release its potential. Dormant. While wearing the outfit in its dormant state, you gain the following benefits: You can cast disguise self at will. If you aren’t wearing armor, your base Armor Class is 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Charisma modifier. Empowered (level 11). After you reach level 11, the artifact becomes empowered, gaining the following additional properties: You have a swimming, climbing, and flying speed equal to your walking speed while wearing this outfit. This outfit gains a number of charges equal to your Charisma modifier. You can spend 5 feet of movement and one charge to automatically escape restraints, or end an effect that reduces your speed or causes you to be grappled, paralyzed, restrained, or stunned. You can escape multiple restraints and end multiple effects, expending 5 feet of movement and one charge of this feature per effect. You regain all expended charges daily at dawn. Supreme (level 17). After you reach level 17, the outfit evolves into its supreme state, gaining the following additional properties: Your Charisma score increases by 2, as does your maximum for that score. Your walking speed increases by 10 feet. You can ignore the verbal and somatic components of spells you cast. When you use the Subtle Spell Metamagic feature, you can also ignore any material component that has no gold cost. You can use your action to gain the benefits of the etherealness spell. This effect can be active for up to an hour, all at once or in shorter intervals of a minimum of 1 minute for the duration. This time limit resets daily at dawn. Optional Rule: Story-Based Evolution The Freewalker’s Shadowgarb is an evolving artifact that is typically found in its Dormant state and increases in power as its wearer gains levels. At the gamemaster’s discretion, the shadowgarb’s evolution can be tied to significant events during the campaign or occur when the shadowgarb’s magic is used to achieve a heroic feat. As an evolving artifact, the shadowgarb is powerful in all tiers of play regardless of its state.
