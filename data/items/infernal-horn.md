# Infernal Horn

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare This brass horn is engraved with images of cavorting imps torturing small creatures, whispering in the ears of human warlords, starting fires and destroying precious artworks. You can use an action to speak the command word and blow the horn. In response, 2d4+1 imps appear in a burst of brimstone to do your bidding. They remain until the task they are given is finished, until 1 hour has elapsed, or when they drop to 0 hit points. Once you have used the horn, it can’t be used again until 7 days have passed.
