# Bag of Cannonballs

**description**: Bag of Cannonballs Wondrous item, uncommon You can store up to 100 cannonballs in this bag. The bag weighs 3 pounds, no matter how many cannonballs are stored. You can store or take out up to 3 cannonballs at a time once on each of your turns with no actions required.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: uncommon
