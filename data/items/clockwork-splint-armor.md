# Clockwork Splint Armor

**description**: Clockwork Splint Armor Armor (Heavy), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) The outside surface and internal joints of this armor whir with interlocking gears, drawing on the orderly magic of the plane of Mechanus. The armor has 4 charges. If you make a d20 roll while wearing this armor, you can expend 1 charge to change the number rolled to a 10. The armor regains 1d4 expended charges daily at dawn. Splint Armor. This armor is made of narrow vertical strips of metal riveted to a backing of leather that is worn over cloth padding. Flexible chain mail protects the joints. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC : 17 Stealth : Disadvantage Weight : 60
