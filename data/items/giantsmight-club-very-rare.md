# Giantsmight Club (Very Rare)

**description**: Giantsmight Weapon (Club), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls with this magic weapon. When you hit a creature larger than you with this weapon, the creature takes an extra 1d6 bludgeoning damage. While wielding this weapon, your Strength score becomes 20 (+5). As a bonus action, the club turns into a greatclub, or from a greatclub into a club. In addition, you can read, write, and speak Giant. Roll20 Note: Add both the Giantsmight Club and Giantsmight Greatclub for the appropriate rarity to your character sheet for attack automation for both the club and greatclub. Knock ‘Em Down! When you score a critical hit against a creature larger than you with this weapon, the creature must succeed on a DC 17 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. Progression Giantsmight progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases. However, in addition to the required increase in proficiency bonus, to progress from rare to very rare, the attuned creature or their allies must kill a creature at least one size larger than the attuned creature during a combat encounter in which the target has been wounded by Giantsmight . The weapon progresses from very rare to legendary after a creature at least two sizes larger than the wielder dies in the same manner.
