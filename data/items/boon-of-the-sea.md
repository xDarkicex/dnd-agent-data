# Boon of the Sea

**description**: Boon of the Sea Weapon (Trident), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This trident is fashioned from beautiful blue coral. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. In addition, you gain a swim speed equal to your walking speed and can breathe underwater. The trident has 5 charges and regains 1d4 + 1 expended charges when you finish a long rest. While holding the trident, you can use an action to expend some of its charges to cast one of the following spells (save DC 17): animal friendship (1 charge), create or destroy water (1 charge), dominate beast (4 charges), speak with animals (1 charge), or thunderwave (1 charge). When casting animal friendship, dominate beast, or speak with animals from this weapon, you can only target creatures with a swim speed.
