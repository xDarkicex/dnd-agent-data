# Chlorophyll Cloak

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) This cloak is woven from silk interlaced with thin plant fiber threads, giving it a moss-like texture. Chlorophyll Infusion This cloak can have up to 3 charges and regains 1 charge for each hour you wear it while in sunlight. As a Bonus Action while wearing the cloak, you can expend 1 charge to gain your choice of one of these benefits for 1 minute or until you use the cloak again. Barkskin. You gain a +2 bonus to AC. Photosynthesis. You gain 2d10 Temporary Hit Points. Solar Shield. You gain resistance to Fire and Radiant damage.
