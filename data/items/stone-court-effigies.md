# Stone Court Effigies

**description**: Stone Court Effigies Wondrous item, uncommon Knitted effigies of the Stone Court gods are important items for most raider families. Their prayers cannot be heard, except in the presence of such an effigy. Though not a magical item in its own right, they complement the use of other religious items. Armband of the Stone Court. If someone owns both an effigy of a particular god and an armband of the Stone Court, for that same god, at the end of each long rest they can make a DC 15 Wisdom check. If successful, any expended uses of the armband are restored. Any Charged Item. The effigy can be used to pray for any item reduced to zero charges, to regain a charge. If one hour is spent praying and a DC 20 Wisdom check is successful, a single charge is restored.
