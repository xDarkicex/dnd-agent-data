# Crocodile Armor

**description**: Armor (leather), rare While you wear this magic armor fashioned from crocodile skin, you gain a +1 bonus to AC. In addition, you can hold your breath for 15 minutes, and you have a swimming speed equal to your walking speed. You also have advantage on Charisma (Intimidation) checks when interacting with subek or werecrocodiles, but you have disadvantage on all other Charisma checks made during an interaction with such creatures.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Light Armor
- Item Rarity: rare
