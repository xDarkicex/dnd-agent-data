# Large Tent

**description**: Large Tent Adventuring gear Large pavilion tents are used periodically in Rokugan, such as to house command staff during battle, but the Unicorn Clan uses them at many other times as well. While traveling, the Unicorn Clan often assemble large, elaborate portable homes called chomchong. Sturdy and well protected from the elements, these tents can accommodate up to a dozen people in relative comfort. They are also used to house small noble families in as much luxury as can be afforded in the field. Chomchong take quite a bit of time to set up and tear down, and usually require their own dedicated pack animals to carry.
