# Elixir of the Destroyer

**description**: Potion, rare A thin red-brown in color, this potion tastes of vomit and iron. After consuming this potion, you must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the potion is wasted and you are sickened until the end of your next turn. On a successful save, you gain 20 temporary hit points, a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls, you treat Injury thresholds as 5 lower (to a minimum of 5), and you deal an extra die of weapon damage whenever you score a critical hit for one minute.
