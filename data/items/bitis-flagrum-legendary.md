# Bitis Flagrum (Legendary)

**description**: Bitis Flagrum Weapon (Whip), Legendary (Requires Attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you hit a creature with this whip, the target must succeed on a DC 18 Constitution saving throw or take an additional 3d6 poison damage. Snake Form. As a bonus action or an action, you can grab the Bitis Flagrum’s tail and transform it into a whip if it is a snake, or a snake if it is a whip. The whip’s snake form is a poisonous snake, giant poisonous snake, or a swarm of poisonous snakes . The snake or swarm is friendly to you and your allies. In combat, the creature shares your initiative count, but it takes its turn immediately after yours. It obeys your verbal commands (no action required by you). If you don’t issue any, it takes the Dodge action and uses its move to avoid danger. Legendary. The bonus to attack and damage rolls increases to +2. The additional poison damage increases to 3d6 and the save DC to avoid it increases to 18. In addition, you can now choose to transform the whip into a swarm of poisonous snakes when you use Snake Form.
