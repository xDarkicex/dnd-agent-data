# Written Prayer

**description**: Written Prayer Spell catalyst, common A small note containing a prayer to a deity that contains a portion of the worshipper’s faith. When you cast the bless spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, each target of the spell uses a d6 for the duration, instead of a d4.
