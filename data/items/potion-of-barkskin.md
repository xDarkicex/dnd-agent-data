# Potion of Barkskin

**description**: Potion of Barkskin Common potion This turbid brown potion hardens the skin of the consumer. A person who samples the liquid feels a tingling on the skin and slight stiffness in the joints. Drinking it causes the skin to harden into a bark-like natural armor. Property [Consumable] : As an action, you drink the potion and gain a +2 bonus to your AC for one minute.
