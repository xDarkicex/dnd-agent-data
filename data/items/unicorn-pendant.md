# Unicorn Pendant

**description**: Unicorn Pendant Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement) This pendant is made from pure gold and is shaped like the head of a unicorn. While wearing this item, you are immune to being charmed, paralyzed, or poisoned, and you have advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. The pendant has 10 charges. While wearing it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more of its charges to cast one of the following spells: teleport (7 charges), dispel evil and good (5 charges), protection from energy (3 charges), or cure wounds (1 charge). The pendant regains 1d8 + 2 charges daily at dawn. I have been called both “good” and “evil” so many times that the labels barely mean anything to me. Still, if any creature in the multiverse is pure, innocent, and noble, it is a unicorn. And even a unicorn can disrupt the Balance.
