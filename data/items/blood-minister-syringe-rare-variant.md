# Blood Minister Syringe (rare variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Component: phial of beast blood Made of silver and glass, this peculiar apparatus magically refills with blood. A small dial around the plunger depicts carved images of beasts. Legend says blood from the sacred animal spirits of each of these species was extracted in the crafting of this macabre, performance-enhancing syringe. Beastblood Serum. The dial around the plunger can be rotated to select one of the following symbols, changing the hue of the blood within: bear (iridescent green); bull (sanguine red); cat (silky purple); eagle (piercing yellow); fox (opulent orange); and owl (midnight blue). As an action, you can select a symbol and inject the blood in the syringe into a willing creature within your reach. The creature gains the benefits of the enhance ability spell that correspond to the chosen animal for 10 minutes. Once this property of the item has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn. Rare variant: You can rotate the vial to select two beast symbols simultaneously, causing the blood in the syringe to acquire both colours, which swirl around one another without blending together. When a creature is injected with the solution, it gains the effects of both of the selected options of the enhance ability spell, but takes 4d6 necrotic damage from the unnatural mix. This damage can’t be reduced or prevented in any way. Increase the duration of the serum’s effects to 1 hour.
