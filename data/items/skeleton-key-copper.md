# Skeleton Key, Copper

**description**: Wondrous item, rarity varies This arcane master key is the prized possession of many an intrepid thief. Several types of skeleton key exist, each type made from a distinct material. Copper (Common). While this key is on your person, you have advantage on ability checks made to disarm traps or open locks.
