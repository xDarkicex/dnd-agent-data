# SIM AR Adapter

**description**: This 2-inch square device is adhered to the head against the temple. Like SIM goggles, it allows you to view the screen of any SIM-linked device you are carrying in a holographic display that only you can see. The display can’t be interacted with directly, however it responds to any voice commands that would normally function on the linked device.
