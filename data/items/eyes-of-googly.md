# Eyes of Googly

**description**: Wondrous item, common Firm, and always slightly moist to the touch, this pair of eyes wanders of their own accord. When placed on a single object that can fit inside a 5-foot cube, the object becomes sentient. It can’t speak, and has statistics the GM deems appropriate.
