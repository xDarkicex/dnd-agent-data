# Reaper's Claw

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic glove is fitted with adamantine claws. While you're wearing the glove, it counts as a simple melee weapon. It has the light and finesse properties and deals 1d8 Slashing damage on a hit. You are proficient with it and attacks you make with it score a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20. Rend. When you score a critical hit against a creature with the glove, the creature must succeed on a DC 15 Charisma saving throw or be cursed. While cursed in this way, the creature must roll a d4 and subtract the result from any attack roll, ability check, or saving throw it makes using Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution. The cursed creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the curse on itself on a success.
