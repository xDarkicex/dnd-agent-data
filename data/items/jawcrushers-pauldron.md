# Jawcrusher's Pauldron

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) This pauldron is fashioned from the jawbone of an ogre affixed to a backing of metal and leather. The pauldron has 3 charges for the following properties and regains all expended charges daily at dawn: Resilience. When you fail a Strength or Constitution ability check or saving throw, you can expend 1 charge to reroll the ability check or saving throw. You must use the new roll. Strength. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge to bolster yourself with an ogre's strength. For the next minute, your Strength score increases by 4.
