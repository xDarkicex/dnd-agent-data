# Brazen Band

**description**: Ring, uncommon While you wear this polished brass ring, you have advantage on Charisma (Intimidation) checks and on saving throws against being frightened. If you fail a saving throw against being frightened, you can choose to succeed instead. If you do, the ring’s magic ceases to function until the next dawn.
