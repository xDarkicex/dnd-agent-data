# Piecemaker Musket

**description**: Weapon (any firearm*), uncommon Component: elemental core of fire Originally named the ‘Peacemaker’, this field combat device has built in protections to prevent its agents from exceeding their mandate. If the magic within the firearm detects that a target is unarmed, the agent is unable to discharge the weapon. Elemental Ammunition. Instead of normal ammunition, this weapon uses special capsules of elemental energy. The weapon can hold one capsule at a time, which can be loaded into it as an action and, while using these capsules, you ignore the loading or reload properties of the weapon if it has them. Each capsule contains 6 charges, and new capsules can be requisitioned from the quartermaster or purchased on the grey market for 5 gp each. Capsules come in five flavours: acid, cold, fire, lightning, and thunder. Each attack with the piecemaker expends 1 charge from the loaded capsule, and deals damage of the capsule’s type instead of its normal damage type. Conflict Avoidance. If the target of your attack is an unarmed creature, the weapon doesn’t fire and the attack is wasted. The piecemaker is sophisticated enough to detect whether a creature has natural weapons, such as a bear’s claws and bite. If a creature’s natural weapons are restricted—such as handcuffed claws, or a muzzled maw—the piecemaker deems the target to be unarmed. Unarmed strikes notably aren’t classified as weapons. For this reason, the Shadow Enclave’s agents often manage to escape confrontations with the FBI unscathed.
