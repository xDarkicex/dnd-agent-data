# Lichslayer Dagger

**description**: Weapon (any), legendary (requires attunement) Component: undead (lich) undying heart The faces of trapped souls can be seen floating beneath the polished surface of this deep, inky glass weapon. The secrets of fashioning soulveil, the faceted material from which these weapons are made, predate even the Spellbreakers. Before wielding such a weapon against a magic user, they must have first been tried by a Spellbreaker Council and found guilty of the most dire crimes. Death by this weapon is absolute, a final curtain. You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Soultrap. This weapon can store a maximum of 20 charges. When you use this weapon to kill a creature capable of casting spells, the creature’s soul is trapped in the weapon, which gains a number of charges equal to half the creature’s total number of Hit Dice. As these charges are expended, the creature’s soul is shredded. A creature whose soul is trapped or shredded can’t come back to life by any means short of a true resurrection spell or similar magic. Empower. When you hit a target with an attack using this weapon, you can expend up to 5 charges, dealing an extra 1d8 force damage for each charge expended.
