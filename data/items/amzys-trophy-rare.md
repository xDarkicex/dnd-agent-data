# Amzy's Trophy (Rare)

**description**: Amzy's Trophy Weapon (Dagger), Rare (Requires Attunement) Your hit point maximum increases by 5 while you are attuned to this weapon. When you score a critical hit with this weapon, the attack deals an extra 2d8 piercing damage. Additionally, on a critical hit, you can activate a Celestial Shield option. You can also activate a Celestial Shield option as a bonus action, without needing to score a critical hit. Once you have activated a Celestial Shield option this way, you can’t do so again until you finish a short or long rest. Celestial Shield (Self). You gain a +2 bonus to AC until the end of your next turn. Celestial Shield (Them). You form a protective shield around another creature you can see within 15 feet, granting them a +2 bonus to AC until the end of your next turn. Progression Amzy’s Trophy progresses any time it reduces an evil-aligned creature with a CR equal to or greater than the attuned character’s level to 0 hit points.
