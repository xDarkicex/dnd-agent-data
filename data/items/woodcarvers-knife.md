# Woodcarver's Knife

**description**: Woodcarver’s Knife Weapon (Dagger), Uncommon This dagger looks more like a well-worn woodcarving tool than a weapon, despite being perfectly serviceable in a fight. If you spend 10 minutes carving a block of wood in the likeness of a Small CR 0 Beast using this magic dagger and succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity check, your carving animates for 1 hour. For every 5 that your Dexterity check result exceeds the DC, the carving remains animated for an additional hour. If you are proficient with woodcarver’s tools, you have advantage on this check. The animated creature is friendly to you and your companions, obeys your commands, and has the statistics of the beast that you carved except that it doesn’t need to breathe and is vulnerable to fire damage. In combat, the beast shares your initiative count, but takes its turn immediately after yours. It can move and use its reaction on its own, but the only action it takes on its turn is the Dodge action, unless you take a bonus action on your turn to command it to take another action. That action can be one in its stat block or some other action. If you are incapacitated, the beast can take any action of its choice, not just Dodge. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest.
