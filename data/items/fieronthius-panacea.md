# Fieronthius Panacea

**description**: Get some alfalfa, chicory, brimstone, mallow, fennel, then boil them all together. To be taken on an empty stomach: it boils inside of you like it's rinsing a barrel, then a roaring fire comes out of the back... and you are brand new! Effect : When you take this concoction, you can end either one disease or one condition afflicting you. The condition can be blinded, deafened, paralyzed, or poisoned. Unfortunately, the effect of this tonic can strain the body of those who drink it. You must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or suffer 1 level of exhaustion.
