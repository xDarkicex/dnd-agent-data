# Inscrutable Staff

**description**: Inscrutable Staff Staff, l egendary (requires attunement by a sorcerer, warlock, or wizard) This staff can be wielded as a magic quarterstaff that grants a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with it. While holding it, you gain a +2 bonus to spell attack rolls and your spell save DC. The staff has 20 charges, and regains 2d10 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the staff triggers a random arcane anomaly . On a 20, the staff regains 1d10 charges. While holding the staff, you can use an action to expend some of its charges to cast one of the following spells from it, using your spell save DC and spellcasting ability: haste (3 charges), slow (3 charges), arcane eye (4 charges), dimension door (4 charges), arcane hand (5 charges), wall of force (5 charges), disintegrate (6 charges), reverse gravity (7 charges), or maze (8 charges) You can also use an action to cast one of the following spells from the staff without using any charges: detect magic, floating disk, mage armour, mending, message, or misty step .
