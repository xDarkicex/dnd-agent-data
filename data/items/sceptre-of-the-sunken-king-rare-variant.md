# Sceptre of the Sunken King (rare variant)

**description**: Rod, very rare (requires attunement) Component: aberration tentacle An ancient nautilus, a scion of the Deep One, wraps the driftwood shaft of this sceptre in its wet, rubbery tentacles. Though this nautilus appears dead and unmoving when dry, its tentacles slowly writhe while submerged in water. You can use the sceptre as a spellcasting focus for your spells. Power of the Depths. While holding this rod, you gain the following benefits: You have resistance to cold damage. You have a swimming speed equal to your walking speed. While at least waist-deep in water, you have advantage on Strength checks and Strength saving throws, and can cast any of the following spells at any level between its normal level and 4th-level (save DC 16): control water , create or destroy water , riptide *, water breathing . After you do so, roll a d6. If the result is equal to or less than the level the spell is cast at, the sceptre’s eye goes dim and you can’t use the rod to cast spells in this way until the next dawn. *See Appendix A Rare variant: Remove the option to cast spells from the Power of the Depths property.
