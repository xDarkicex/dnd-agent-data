# Demented Cultist's Scimitar

**description**: Weapon (scimitar), rare (requires attunement) Component: pouch of aberration teeth This obsidian blade reflects light with an opalescent sheen. The hilt is made of dead driftwood bound with ragged fabric. Ritual Branding. As an action, you can place a brand on yourself, choosing from the list below and taking 1d8 fire damage. The brand lasts until the next dawn, or until removed by a greater restoration spell or similar magic. Once you brand yourself, you can’t do so again until the next dawn. Brand of the Devout. As a bonus action while you hold this weapon, you can expend a number of Hit Dice up to your proficiency bonus (minimum 2). You regain one expended spell slot of a level up to half the number of Hit Dice expended. Brand of the Flagellated. When you hit a creature with an attack with this weapon, you can expend a number of Hit Dice up to your proficiency bonus, dealing an additional 2d6 damage of the attack’s type per Hit Die expended. Brand of the Zealot. As a bonus action, you can expend a number of Hit Dice up to your proficiency bonus. You have advantage on the next number of attack rolls equal to the number of Hit Dice expended that you make before the start of your next turn.
