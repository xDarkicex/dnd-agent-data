# Obsidian Inkstick

**description**: Obsidian Inkstick Adventuring gear An obsidian inkstick can be identified with a successful DC 20 Intelligence (Arcana) check. These seemingly ordinary inksticks contain pulverized obsidian in addition to the usual ingredients. When material from an obsidian inkstick is mixed with water and used to write or draw, it bestows the blessings of Lord Moon upon the paper. But when it is mixed with blood, the resulting ink attracts evil spirits and empowers blood magic rituals. Obsidian inksticks are a necessary component for the Bloodspeakers’ ritual.
