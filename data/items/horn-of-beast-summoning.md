# Horn of Beast Summoning

**description**: Horn of Beast Summoning Wondrous Item, rare Weight: 1 lb. You can use an action to blow this horn. In response, a creature determined by the Summoned Beast table appears within 60 feet of you. Roll a d8. d8 Summoned Beast 1 Cave Bear 2 Giant Boar 3 Giant Elk 4 Rhinoceros 5 Saber-toothed Tiger 6 Giant Scorpion 7 Elephant 8 Triceratops The creature is friendly to you and your companions. It understands your languages and obeys your spoken commands. If you issue no commands, the creature defends itself but takes no other actions. The creature disappears after 1 hour or when it drops to 0 hit points. Once three beasts have been summoned, the horn can't be used this way again until the next dawn.
