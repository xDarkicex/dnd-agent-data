# Moonbeam Chainmail

**description**: Armor (Chain Mail), Very Rare (Requires Attunement by a Druid) This armor is made of woven moonlight by the bearfolk. It weighs only 5 pounds and always glows with a gentle radiance. Its glow cannot be hidden by tunics or cloaks. While wearing this armor, you gain a +1 bonus to AC, increasing to +2 under moonlight. In addition, you gain an additional use of Wild Shape. You are considered proficient with this armor even if you lack proficiency with medium armor.
