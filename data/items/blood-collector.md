# Blood Collector

**description**: Armor (breastplate), uncommon The blood collector armor helps their wielder save a certain amount of their blood in heavy combat. You can take a bonus action to place a round vial in the middle of this armor. If you take at least 12 piercing or slashing damage while wearing this armor, the vial is filled with your own blood. The armor is magical, thus, the vial cannot be broken while it is intact. You can take a bonus action to remove the vial and then use your action to drink its contents to regain 6 hit points. You can use the blood for other measures at your and your GM’s discretion. For example, you can place three blood cartridges ( Bloodwork Weapons ) instead of the vial.
