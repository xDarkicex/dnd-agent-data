# Swiftwing Armor

**description**: Swiftwing Armor Armor (Plate), Very Rare (requires attunement) Crafted for defiant heroes who oppose cruel angels and other evil celestial creatures, this armor allows ironclad paladins to fly and hover weightlessly in pursuit of their corrupt quarries. While you wear this armor, you can use a bonus action to cause it to manifest a pair of spectral eagle wings from your back. While the wings are present, you have a flying speed equal to your walking speed and can hover. The wings last until you die, or until you dismiss them as a bonus action. Item Variant: Swiftwing Steed Armor At the gamemaster’s discretion, a version of the armor made specifically for mounts, the Swiftwing Steed Armor, may be found. The Swiftwing Steed Armor has the same features as the original armor made for humanoids, but magically adjusts to fit on any Large or smaller mount, including rare animals, summoned steeds, aquatic mounts, and flying mounts. Only willing creatures that can serve as rideable mounts can attune to it.
