# Diving

**description**: Journeyman armor property Components: Masterwork suit of exotic armor This suit of armor is equipped with webbed fins, a mask, and an air bladder containing 8 hours of breathable air. While wearing it, you gain a swimming speed equal to your walking speed. Additionally, while breathing from the air bladder, you can breathe normally underwater or in a vacuum, and you ignore the effects of inhaled poisons. The suit’s air supply can be refilled over the course of a long rest.
