# Shoes of Flight

**description**: Shoes of Flight Wondrous item Price: 50,000 gp These beautiful shoes have embroidered wings up each side. They are said to have been gifts from a god passed down to their most devoted followers. There are only a handful of shoes in existence, they are very rare and valuable. When the wearer speaks the command word as a Free Action, the embroidered wings turn into real ones, granting the wearer the power of flight. This lasts until the wearer speaks the command word again. While activated the shoes allow the wearer to fly at a speed equal to their Movement Speed.
