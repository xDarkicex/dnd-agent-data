# Torture Tincture

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare Component: monstrosity poison gland This two-inch-tall phial of oily liquid has a decidedly untrustworthy air to it. Using its bandage scarf, it can climb up any surface, before depositing a dose of poison in whatever vessel, meal, or concoction its wielder chooses. Stories say such a magical contraption was used to poison a king who’d locked himself in a high tower, and that this was the origin of the veinworm plague. Venomous Intentions. As an action while holding the torture tincture, you can speak the command phrase “Poison please”. This miniscule glass bottle creeps into life for the next minute. It is a Tiny construct with AC 13, 40 hit points, walking and climbing speeds of 30 feet, a Strength score of 1, a Dexterity score of 20, proficiency in the Stealth skill (+7 bonus), immunity to poison and psychic damage, immunity to all conditions except grappled and restrained, and it can’t attack. If it drops to 0 hit points, it loses its magic and is destroyed. It contains one dose of torpor poison and regains an expended dose daily at dawn. The DC for the poison is 16 when administered by the torture tincture. The torture tincture takes its turn immediately after yours. As part of the action to summon it, and as a bonus action on subsequent turns, you can command it to deposit a poison in a vessel, food, or drink as its action on its next turn.
