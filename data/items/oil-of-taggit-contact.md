# Oil of taggit (Contact)

**description**: Oil of Taggit Adventuring gear (poison, contact) A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or become poisoned for 24 hours. The poisoned creature is unconscious. The creature wakes up if it takes damage.
