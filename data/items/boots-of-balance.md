# Boots of Balance

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement by a rogue) These boots have 5 charges. While wearing them you can use a bonus action to gain advantage in any Dexterity (Acrobatics) check that involves movement, such as walking on a rope or on a rooftop. The boots regain 1d4 + 1 charges daily at dawn.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: Uncommon
- Requires Attunement: Yes
