# Buccaneer's Ring

**description**: Ring, Common This golden ring depicts a skull wearing a hat and holds a sliver of a deceased sailor's soul. While wearing the ring, you have advantage on all Intelligence checks you make related to sea travel, navigating on the sea, and recalling information about sea vessels. Sentience. The ring is a sentient, chaotic good object with an Intelligence of 12, Wisdom of 9, and Charisma of 14. It has hearing and Darkvision out to a range of 60 feet. The ring understands Common and can communicate telepathically with its wearer. The ring is boisterous, impatient, and foolhardy, and always tries to spur its wearer into action, typically with remarks such as "there's no such thing as a safe harbor, so just set sail and get on with it already” or "by the Sea Queen, this ain't the time to think, 'tis the time to act!”
