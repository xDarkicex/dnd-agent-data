# Sin: Envy (very rare variant)

**description**: Item modifier, uncommon (optional or enhanced attunement) The Task. The fiend requests a minor task, such as copying a friend’s dress sense or mimicking someone’s accent for a day. OA or EA: Envy. While you hold the item, when a creature within 30 feet of you that you can see makes an attack roll, saving throw, or ability check, you can use your reaction to capture the creature’s reflection in the item’s surface and store a copy of the result of that creature’s roll before any modifiers are applied to it. You can decide to use this reaction after the die is rolled, but before the GM determines if the result succeeds or fails. Once within the next minute, after a creature you can see within 30 feet of you makes an attack roll, saving throw, or ability check, you can choose to replace its roll of the d20 with this stored die roll (no action required). You must choose to do so before the roll, and you can replace a roll in this way only once per turn. After you replace a die in this way, until the end of your next turn, if the creature you mirrored takes damage, you take half as much damage as psychic damage. Once this property of the item has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn. Rare variant: Increase the range to 60 feet. The fiend asks for a medium task, such as stealing credit for someone else’s achievement or publicly disparaging a friend so you look better in comparison. Very rare variant: Increase the range to 120 feet. The fiend asks for a major task, such as murdering someone you consider more beautiful than yourself or sabotaging a happy relationship.
