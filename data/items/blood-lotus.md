# Blood Lotus

**description**: wondrous item, rare The crimson petals of a blood lotus smell of mint when bruised, but offer no other noticeable fragrance. It grows near running water, along the shore. When you consume it, you have disadvantage on Charisma ability checks and saving throws until you finish a short rest. If you are a spellcaster, you can use one application of Lotus Infused ability to gain one of the following: Increase the DC of an evocation spell you cast by 2 Cast an evocation or blood magic spell as if you used a spell slot one level higher than the one you expended. Cast the spell as if you had applied one of the following metamagic benefits to it, whether or not you know the particular metamagic: Careful Spell or Distant Spell (when stacking uses, each metamagic benefit may be chosen only once). You can use one to three uses of the blood lotus as part of the action of casting a spell. When using the same ability boost more than once, the effects stack. If you aren’t a spellcaster, you can expend a use as a reaction when you fail a saving throw against an evocation or blood magic spell. Reroll the saving throw and use the new result.
