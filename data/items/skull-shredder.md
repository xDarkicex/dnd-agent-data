# Skull Shredder

**description**: Weapon (Maul), Rare A brutal two-handed weapon topped with four horizontally stacked bronze gears, three inches thick and one foot in diameter, attached to a central steel shaft which also serves as the handle. This magic weapon has 3 charges, and regains all expended charges when you finish a long rest. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge to cause the four gears on top to spin in alternating directions for 1 minute. While activated this way, the weapon deals an extra 2d10 slashing damage on a hit as the grinding gears gnash flesh and bone. Encounters Ideas Cult of Order. Isolated for centuries, the dark dwarves created monumental machines which they worshipped as gods. This ceremonial weapon was wielded by the executioner who punished heretics. Forever Loyal. Although his master died decades ago, a sentient shield guardian still protects the evil artificer’s workshop, carrying this gruesome weapon. Wasteland Survival. The grandest airship ever built vanished in the deep desert. Those who survived the crash forged these mauls from the wreckage to protect themselves from hostile tribes. In the end, they turned on one another.
