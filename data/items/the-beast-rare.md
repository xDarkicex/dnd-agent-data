# The Beast (Rare)

**description**: The Beast Weapon (Maul), Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The Beast deals piercing damage instead of the bludgeoning damage normal for a maul. The save DC for each of this magic weapon’s properties is equal to 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier. As a bonus action, you can speak the weapon’s command word, causing the maul to issue a deep-throated growl for 1 round. Bleed. When you hit a creature other than an Undead or Construct with an attack using The Beast , the target bleeds, losing 1d4 hit points at the start of each of its turns. It can then make a Constitution saving throw, ending the effect of all such wounds on itself on a success. Alternatively, the wounded creature, or a creature within 5 feet of it, can use an action to make a Wisdom (Medicine) check, ending the effect of such wounds on it on a success. Roar. The weapon gains the ability to emit a debilitating roar as a bonus action. Each creature you choose within 30 feet of you must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or have its weapon attacks that use Strength deal half damage for the next minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. Once this feature has been used, it can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. Progression To progress the weapon from one rarity to another, the attuned wielder must reduce a creature to 0 hit points with an attack using The Beast . This creature must have a CR equal to or greater than the wielder’s current level (or CR if it doesn’t have levels). The weapon can be progressed only once between proficiency bonus increases.
