# Amulet of Death's Defiance

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) This amulet’s pendant is of a tiny human skull protruding from a polished black stone. While attuned to the amulet, the wearer cannot have disadvantage on death saving throws, and if they would be subjected to a spell or effect that would kill them without dealing damage, the effect is delayed by 1 round. If the effect normally has an instantaneous duration, it gains a duration of 1 round, taking effect at the beginning of the current initiative count on the following round. If it is a spell, it may be dispelled (but not countered) during that round. Once per day, as an action, the wearer may activate the amulet to remove any effect delayed by the amulet. Once it has been activated, the amulet’s delay function ceases until the next day at dawn. Legends tell of one such amulet which does not require attunement and which continues to function after activation.
