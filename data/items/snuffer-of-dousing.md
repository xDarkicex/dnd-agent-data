# Snuffer of Dousing

**description**: Snuffer of Dousing Wondrous item, uncommon This thin silver-plated rod is 6 inches long and has a small cup attached to one end. When you use it to douse a candle, all non-magical fires within a 20-foot radius are also extinguished. Once used, this property of the snuffer can’t be used again until the next dawn.
