# Solar's Crest

**description**: Armor (Any Medium or Heavy), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic armor is crafted from hardened steel and embossed with a golden sun symbol. You have resistance to Radiant damage while wearing it. Cleansing Light. As a bonus action, you can cause cleansing light to course through you. You regain 2d10 hit points and are affected as by the Greater Restoration spell. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn. Warding Flare. When a creature within 10 feet of you misses an attack roll against you, you can use your reaction to force the creature to make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw as the armor flares with light. On a failure, the target is Blinded until the start of its next turn.
