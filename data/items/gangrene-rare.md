# Gangrene (Rare)

**description**: Gangrene Weapon (Mace), Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. As an action, you can touch this weapon to a portion of food no more than 1 cubic foot in volume, instantaneously rotting it. Once used, you can’t use this property again until you finish a long rest. Rot. When you hit a creature with an attack using this weapon, its runes pulse a sickly green and the target must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or take an extra 1d8 necrotic damage. Progression Gangrene progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, so does the weapon’s rarity. However, in addition to the required increase in proficiency bonus, to progress from very rare to legendary, three creatures must be reduced to 0 hit points by an attack using Gangrene in a single combat.
