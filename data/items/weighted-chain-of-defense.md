# Weighted Chain of Defense

**description**: Weighted Chain of Defense Melee weapon (martial, weighted chain), rare The weapon has the defensive property, seeming to move by itself to aid in its wielder’s defense. Light. A light weapon is small and easy to handle, making it ideal for use when fighting with two weapons. Reach. This weapon adds 5 feet to your reach when you attack with it, as well as when determining your reach for opportunity attacks with it. Defensive. While you wield one or more weapons with this property, you gain a +1 bonus to AC if you are proficient in the weapon and not using a shield. Damage: 1d4 Damage Type: Bludgeoning Properties: Light, Reach, Defensive Weight: 8
