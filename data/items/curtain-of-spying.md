# Curtain of Spying

**description**: Curtain of Spying Wondrous item, uncommon This appears to be a regular brocade curtain from one side and is 9 feet tall and 6 feet wide. When viewed from the other side, it is entirely transparent, making it an excellent one-way observation tool. If the curtain is placed on the ground with its transparent side up, a successful DC 15 Wisdom (Perception) check is required to find it.
