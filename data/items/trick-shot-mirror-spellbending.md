# Trick Shot Mirror, Spellbending

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) A trick shot mirror is a round, steel-framed hand mirror with no handle, but its 5-inch diameter makes it easy to hold. A trick shot mirror comes in different styles, but each allows you to adjust the trajectory of an attack or spell. Each mirror has 3 charges, and it regains all expended charges daily at dawn. Spellbending Trick Shot Mirror (Rare). While holding the mirror and casting a spell that forms a line, you can expend 1 or more of the mirror’s charges to focus part of the spell into the mirror and change the angle of the line. Choose one space along the line. The line bends at a 90-degree angle in the space in the direction of your choice. This bend doesn’t extend the length of the line, but it could redirect the line in such a way as to hit a creature previously not within the line’s area of effect. For each charge you expend, you can bend the line in an additional space.
