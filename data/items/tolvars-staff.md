# Tolvar's Staff

**description**: Melee Weapon (Quarterstaff, Simple, Melee), Legendary (Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster) 1d6 bludgeoning (versatile 1d8) While wielding this weapon, you gain a +1 bonus to AC While attuned you gain a +1 bonus to your spell attack rolls and your spell save DC. Arcane Foundation: When you cast a spell that requires a saving throw, you can choose to channel the staff's power. If you do, the target has disadvantage on its saving throw against the spell. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of 1), and you regain expended uses at dawn. Cantamen: You can inscribe up to three spells from your spellbook into the staff. These spells are considered prepared and do not count against the number of spells you can prepare each day. You can change the inscribed spells during a short or long rest. Tolvars Fury (3/day): When you deal damage to a single creature with a spell, you can choose to imbue it with energy from the staff. The target takes an extra 7 (2d6) force damage. Aura of the Ether: As an action, you can use the staff to create an aura that envelops you and your allies within 30 feet. The aura lasts for 1 minute or until you are incapacitated or stop wielding the staff. While the aura is active, every creature of your choice within it has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
