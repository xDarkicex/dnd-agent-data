# Elemental Potion of Air

**description**: Potion, Very Rare Wind whirls inside this glass bottle. When you drink the contents of this potion, winds surround you for 1 hour, granting you the following benefits: You gain a Fly speed of 60 feet. You have resistance to Lightning and Thunder damage. Ranged weapon attacks made against you are made with disadvantage. As a bonus action, you can end the effect early to create a raging wind in a 60-foot cone extending from your outstretched hand in a direction you choose. Each creature in the area must succeed on a DC 17 Strength saving throw or take 3d10 Bludgeoning damage and be pushed up to 20 feet away from you.
