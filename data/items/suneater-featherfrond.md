# Suneater Featherfrond

**description**: Wondrous item, common (socketable, requires attunement) When this featherfrond is socketed on a holdable or wearable item, the item becomes augmented and attuneable. The item gains the Sun Torch property for as long as a creature is attuned to it. Sun Torch. As a bonus action, you can light or snuff out the featherfrond . While lit, it sheds bright light in a 20-foot radius , and dim light for an additional 20 feet .
