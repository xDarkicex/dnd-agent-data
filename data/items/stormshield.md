# Stormshield

**description**: Armor (shield), very rare (requires attunement) This large iron-banded wooden shield is etched with runes. The shield functions as a holy symbol for a servant of a deity associated with storms, lightning, or thunder. You gain a +1 to AC and resistance to lightning and thunder damage while you wear this shield.
