# Peace Bonding of Diplomacy

**description**: Peace Bonding of Diplomacy Wondrous item, uncommon This bit of silken rope can be used to peace bond any melee or ranged weapon. When you have a weapon tied up with the peace bond, it takes an action to untie the rope and draw the weapon. If the only weapon you have is peace-bonded with this item, you can use an action to cast the calm emotions spell (DC 14). Once you use this ability, a minute must pass before you can use it again.
