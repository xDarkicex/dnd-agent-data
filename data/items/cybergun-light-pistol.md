# Cybergun (Light Pistol)

**description**: Cybergun Augment (limb), varies (requires affinity) A firearm is mounted directly into one of your arms. Its type and rarity is determined by using the table below. It does not need to be drawn nor held to be used in combat. When you attack with this weapon during your turn, you can’t use that hand for other purposes. The weapon uses the same features and properties as a normal weapon of its type. Proficiency with the weapon allows you to add your proficiency bonus to the attack roll for any attack you make with it.
