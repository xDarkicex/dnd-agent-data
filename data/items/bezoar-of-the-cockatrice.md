# Bezoar of the Cockatrice

**description**: Wondrous item, common While this bezoar sits in your stomach, you are immune to the petrified condition, and you have darkvision out to a range of 60 feet. If you already have darkvision, your vision is unaffected by this bezoar.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
