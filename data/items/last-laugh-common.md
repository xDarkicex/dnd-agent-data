# Last Laugh (Common)

**description**: Last Laugh Weapon (Mace), Common (Requires Attunement) You can utter a command word to cause the weapon to emit soft giggling sounds like that of a small child, which lasts until the end of your next turn. Progression The Last Laugh progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
