# Cobra Mace (Rare)

**description**: Weapon (Mace) Rare (Requires Attunement) Common As an action, you can sense the presence and location of creatures within 30 feet of you that are diseased, poisoned, or in pain. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a short or long rest. Uncommon You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. In addition, you have advantage on saving throws against poison, and you have resistance against poison damage. Rare Attacks with this weapon deal an extra 1d6 poison damage on a hit. Toxic Critical. When you attack a creature with this weapon and roll a 20 on the attack roll, the weapon also bites the target, injecting them with poison. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned for 1 minute. At the end of each of its turns, the target can repeat the saving throw, ending the condition on a success.
