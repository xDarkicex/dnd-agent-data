# Mincer

**description**: Weapon (any melee), common To kill a creature fast and steal its blood without getting caught by law enforcement, the people of Tolia have developed new slightly-magical techniques to sharpen their weapons. When you score a critical hit with the mincer, you deal an extra 1d4 slashing damage. These weapons cost 200 KF/CS more than their regular counterparts.
