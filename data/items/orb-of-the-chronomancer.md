# Orb of the Chronomancer

**description**: Orb of the Chronomancer Wondrous Item, Legendary (requires attunement by a spellcaster) Crafted by an ancient wizard who mastered the secrets of time itself, the Orb of the Chronomancer allows spellcasters to transcend the limitations of the time stop spell. Chronomancy. While attuned to the orb, you can use an action to cast time stop from it. Once time stop has been cast through the orb, it can’t be cast again until the next dawn. Mastery of Time. When casting time stop , you can choose how many turns to take in a row (maximum of 5) instead of rolling 1d4 + 1 turns. Temporal Anchor. While attuned to this orb, you are unaffected by time stop cast by other creatures and can act normally during their extra turns.
