# Chalice of Detection

**description**: Chalice of Detection Wondrous item, uncommon This chalice is made from beautiful crystal and has a circular base, an orb-shaped stem and a goblet-style vessel. If it is filled with water and brought within 5 feet of poison, the water turns a sickly green hue. Poisonous monsters, gasses, weapons, and traps all trigger this effect. The water remains green, even if the chalice is moved more than 5 feet away from the poison. Once used, this property of the chalice can’t be used again until the next dawn. The chalice must also be emptied and refilled with water before it is reused.
