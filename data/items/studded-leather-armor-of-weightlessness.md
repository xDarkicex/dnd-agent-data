# Studded Leather Armor of Weightlessness

**description**: Studded Leather Armor of Weightlessness Armor (Light), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This armor has 5 charges. While you wear it, you can use a bonus action to expend 1 or more charges to cast one of the following spells from the armor, targeting yourself: Jump (1 charge) or Levitate (2 charges). This armor regains 1d4 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. Studded Leather Armor. Made from tough but flexible leather, studded leather is reinforced with close-set rivets or spikes. AC: 12 + Dex modifier Weight: 13
