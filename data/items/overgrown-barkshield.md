# Overgrown Barkshield

**description**: Armour (shield), uncommon (requires attunement) Component: plant bark This crude shield made of bark and wood teems with life as if it were still part of a tree. The smell of sap and various fragrant flowers perfumes the air around it. Bark Armour. As an action, you can place the shield on your torso and speak its command word, causing the wood and vines to wrap around your figure. For 1 hour, or until you use an action to end this effect, you are unable to hold a shield, and you gain the following benefits: You gain 1d8 temporary hit points. You gain the benefits of the barkskin spell (no concentration required). You have advantage on saving throws against being poisoned. When this effect ends, the overgrown barkshield falls from its place on your torso, fading to a lifeless shade of grey. This property can’t be used again until the next dawn, when the shield blooms with life once more. Vines and Thorns. While touching this shield, you can use an action to animate one of the shield’s thorny vines and make a melee weapon attack (+5 to hit) against one target within 30 feet of you. On a hit, the target takes 2d8 piercing damage, and, if the target is a Large or smaller creature or an object that weighs less than 500 pounds, you can pull it up to 10 feet closer to you.
