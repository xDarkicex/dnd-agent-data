# Starcaller

**description**: Weapon (Quarterstaff), Uncommon A smooth staff of ebony heartwood decorated with liquid silver patterns depicting all the known constellations. While attuned to this magic weapon, you are resistant to radiant damage. In addition, you can cast the dancing lights , light , and sacred flame cantrips (spell save DC 12). In each case, the effect appears as silver starlight. Encounter Ideas Fire in the Sky. For the past three nights, a brilliant shooting star has raced across the sky before vanishing over the horizon. If the characters follow its trail, they find a starcaller in a moonlit glen. Night Fey. A coven of dryads has been corrupted by aberrant sorcery. Upon defeat, one of the dryads transforms into a starcaller. Stars Align. A priest of a dark celestial who wielded a starcaller was executed a century ago. Before his death, he prophesied he would return on the night of the stellar conjunction. That is tomorrow night.
