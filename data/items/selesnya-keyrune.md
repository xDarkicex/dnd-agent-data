# Selesnya Keyrune

**description**: Wondrous item, rare Carved from white and green marble in the shape of a wolf’s head, this keyrune transforms into a dire wolf . The wolf persists for 8 hours. Its Intelligence is 6, and it understands Elvish and Sylvan but can’t speak those languages. While it is within 1 mile of you, you can communicate with each other telepathically.
