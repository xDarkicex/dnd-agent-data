# Song's Sticks of Gracious Parting

**description**: Song's Sticks of Gracious Parting Wondrous item Price: 1,000 gp These two unassuming small sticks, each engraved with a small heart, look like they might also function as an eating utensil. When these two small bamboo sticks, each about six inches in length, are clicked together, the surrounding lights become slightly dimmer and the wielder has Advantage on a Charisma check to end a conversation. No matter how the conversation was going, the participants all leave feeling friendly towards each other. This item must be used every day or the magic fades.
