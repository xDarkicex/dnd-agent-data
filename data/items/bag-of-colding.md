# Bag of Colding

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon In the dank, humid conditions of the average dungeon, ingredients spoil at an alarming rate. To deal with this loss of product, a clever wizard modified a bag of holding to have the perfect internal temperature for cold storage. This item is identical to a bag of holding with one additional feature: any food items stashed in it have their shelf life quadrupled.
