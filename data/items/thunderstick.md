# Thunderstick

**description**: Weapon (Club), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This wooden club, crafted from a tree struck by lightning, has jagged black markings along its length. When you make a successful attack roll with this magic weapon, you can cast thunderwave (spell save DC 12) from the weapon. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a short or long rest. Encounter Ideas Orcs. A group of young orcs have moved into the area, claiming territory. Their leader wields a thunderstick. Kobolds. A kobold tribe has “acquired” a thunderstick. They have incorporated the weapon into the falling-ceiling trap that guards their treasure room. Cleric. The adventurers are asked to stop a high elf priest named Geraldine who has moved into a sea cave and is making trouble for the local communities. She wields a thunderstick.
