# Kanrath Armor

**description**: Armor (any armor), rare (requires attunement) When a new Kanrath vampire is created by another one, the older one gives the new one a weapon or an armor exclusive to the vampires of Kanrath. It is a tradition for a Kanrath vampire to carry these weapons or armor as a sign of power and pride. Only Kanrath vampires can attune to these items. Otherwise, they display no magical properties. Once attuned, these items have the following magical properties: Kanrath Armor. You are considered proficient with this armor, even if you lack the proficiency. Also, if you attune to two pieces of Kanrath armor (one of them must be a shield), spell attacks against you are made with disadvantage while wearing the armor or carrying the shield.
