# Bow of Elements

**description**: Weapon (bow), rare (requires attunement) This bow is a magic weapon for the purposes of overcoming damage resistance. When a target takes damage from this magic bow, the target takes an additional 3 (1d6) damage. The DM chooses the damage type or determines it randomly, using the following table. d6 Result 1-2 Fire 3-4 Cold 5 Lightning 6 Thunder
