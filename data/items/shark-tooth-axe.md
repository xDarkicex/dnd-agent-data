# Shark Tooth Axe

**description**: Shark Tooth Axe Weapon (battleaxe), very rare (requires attunement) Damage: 1d8+2 slashing Weight: 4 lbs. Properties: Versatile (1d10+2) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic axe. Shredding Teeth. The axe edge is lined with serrated shark teeth. On a hit, the target must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw. If it fails, the creature begins to bleed uncontrollably. The creature has disadvantage on Constitution checks and Constitution saving throws until it regains hit points. Any shark teeth that are broken off grow back the next day. Shark Frenzy. For 1 minute, you are frenzied by the smell of blood. You have advantage on melee attack rolls against any creature with blood that doesn't have all its hit points. This feature can be used once per day.
