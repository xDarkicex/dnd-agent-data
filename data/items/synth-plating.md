# Synth Plating

**description**: Synth Skin Augment (body), varies (requires affinity) A thin web of synthetic mesh is grafted over your skin to provide additional protection. It is a rare augment. While you are not wearing any armor, your base AC equals 12 + your Dexterity modifier. If you are wearing armor, your AC can’t be less than 12, regardless of what kind of armor you’re wearing. Synth Plating. The very rare version of this augment is made from layers of interlocking polymer plating. While you are not wearing any armor, your base AC equals 14 + your Dexterity modifier. If you are wearing armor, your AC can’t be less than 14, regardless of what kind of armor you’re wearing.
