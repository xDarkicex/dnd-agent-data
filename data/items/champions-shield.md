# Champion's Shield

**description**: Armor (Shield), Rare (Requires Attunement) When a creature you can see attacks a target within 30 feet of you while you are carrying this magic shield, you can use your reaction to magically swap places with the target of the attack, if it is willing. The attack is made against you instead.
