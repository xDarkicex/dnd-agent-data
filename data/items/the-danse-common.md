# The Danse (Common)

**description**: The Danse Weapon (Rapier), Common (Requires Attunement) Each slice of The Danse is accompanied by a violin note. When you slay a creature with this rapier, you can make a Charisma (Performance) check. The slain creature must then make a Charisma saving throw, the DC of which is equal to the result of your Charisma (Performance) check. On a failed save, their soul is immediately delivered to The Singing Sister and cannot be revived until she releases it (which she can do willingly at any time). When the night hag dies, all her ensnared souls are released. Progression The Danse progresses when it ends the life of a sufficiently famous artist, determined by how widely known they are, as per the Progression Requirements table.
