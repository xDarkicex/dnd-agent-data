# Mace of Domination

**description**: Mace of Domination Weapon (mace), very rare (requires attunement) Damage: 1d8+2 bludgeoning Weight: 5 lbs. Properties: Simple You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Dominate. While attuned to and holding this weapon, you can use an action to cast Dominate Beast at 7th level (concentration, 8 hour duration), Dominate Person at 7th level (concentration, 1 hour duration), or Dominate Monster at 8th level (1 hour duration). The target’s Wisdom saving throw has a DC 15 or your spell DC, whichever is higher. This property can be used once per day.
