# Feywrought Hide Armor

**description**: Feywrought Hide Armor Armor (Medium), Rare (Requires Attunement) This colorful, flowery armor was forged in the Feywild and is infused with that plane’s captivating magic. While wearing this armor, you have advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the charmed condition on yourself. This armor has 3 charges. You can use an action to expend a charge to cast the Compulsion spell (save DC 15) from this armor. The armor regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. Hide Armor. This crude armor consists of thick furs and pelts. It is commonly worn by barbarian tribes, evil humanoids, and other folk who lack access to the tools and materials needed to create better armor.
