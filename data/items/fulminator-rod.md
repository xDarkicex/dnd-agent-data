# Fulminator Rod

**description**: Fulminator Rod Melee weapon Cost : 1,000 gp Damage : 1d6 lightning, 1d6 thunder Weight : 2 lb. Properties : Light This short bar of carved orichalcum houses a vril battery and is tipped with two reinforced copper tines. On a successful melee attack, which consumes 1 charge from the attached vril battery, the target takes 1d6 lightning and 1d6 thunder damage. The explosion of electrical energy can be heard up to 300 feet away.
