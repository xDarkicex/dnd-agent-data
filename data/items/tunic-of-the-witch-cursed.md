# Tunic of the Witch (Cursed)

**description**: Tunic of the Witch (Cursed) Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This magical item, which can be worn under armor, grants a +1 AC bonus. However, while worn, any ironwood witch within 100 feet can see and hear everything happening to the wearer. In addition, a witch may use their Control Thrall ability on the wearer. Removing this tunic requires a remove curse spell.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: rare
