# Bronze Claw of Rulership

**description**: Bronze Claw of Rulership Wondrous item, uncommon Grants a +2 bonus to any Charisma based skill check while worn. A bonus action may also be used to grant advantage on a Charisma skill check or saving throw. Once this feature is used it may not be used again until dawn of the next day.
