# Abyssal Heart

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare (Requires Attunement) Golden needles protrude from this faintly pulsing, grotesque organ. Abyssal Implant. During a 10-minute ritual, you can place the heart into the empty chest cavity of a Medium or larger creature that has been dead for no longer than 24 hours. The heart becomes a functioning heart for the creature; the creature regains all its hit points and is brought back to life as an Undead creature. While Undead, the creature doesn't need to eat, drink, or sleep, and it can't regain hit points or gain the benefits of a short or long rest. As an action, you can force the creature to make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw. On a failure, the creature is controlled by you as if affected by the Dominate Monster spell while in its Undead form (no concentration required). The creature can repeat the saving throw each time it takes damage, ending the domination on itself on a success. If the heart is removed or the creature is reduced to 0 hit points, the creature dies, and the heart can't be used again in this way until seven days have passed.
