# Poison, Basic

**description**: Poison, Basic Adventuring gear Price: 100 gp Quantity: vial You can use the poison in this vial to coat one bladed weapon or up to three pieces of ammunition. Applying the poison requires a Main Action. A creature hit by the poisoned weapon or ammunition must make a DC 10 Constitution check or take 1d4 poison damage. Once applied, the poison retains potency for 1 minute before drying.
