# Hush

**description**: Weapon (Net), Rare (Requires Attunement) This silvery net’s mesh consists of intricately woven mithral threads. As a bonus action, you can speak this magic weapon’s command word, causing it to retract into a ball the size of an apple. When thrown, it expands outward, issuing sounds of tinkling bells and whistling strings. The peaceful noise comes to an abrupt stop when it hits its mark, creating a deafening silence. You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls made with this weapon, which has a range of 15/45 feet. On a hit, a Large or smaller creature is restrained and under the effects of a silence spell until freed. A creature can use its action to make a DC 14 Strength check to free itself or another creature from the net. Dealing 20 slashing damage to the net (AC 14) also frees the creature without harm. The net isn’t destroyed; rather, it retracts into a ball and flies back to the attuned creature, repairing itself when you finish a long rest. Encounter Ideas The Right Tool for the Job. A menacing mage is causing trouble, and the adventurers require this weapon to take them down. The heroes must deal with a famous, but grumpy, wyvern hunter (gladiator) to acquire it, either by barter or force. Wanted: Alive. The party has made powerful enemies and well-equipped bounty hunters (four bandits and one bandit captain) use the net on them (they don’t care about fighting fairly). Fish Tales. People in town are talking about a fisher who tricked a siren, catching her in a shiny net. The net once belonged to the fisher’s great-grandfather, who apparently was a seasoned adventurer.
