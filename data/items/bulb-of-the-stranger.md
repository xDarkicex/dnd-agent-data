# Bulb of the Stranger

**description**: Bulb of the Stranger Wondrous item, very rare This bulb comes from an alien plant and bulges with purple veins. As an action, you can plant the bulb in a corpse within 5 feet of you. Doing so consumes the bulb. After 10 minutes, the corpse is returned to a semblance of life as a Bizarre Stranger. A Bizarre Stranger uses the statistics of the original creature with the following changes: It has confused memories of the original creature’s life. Its type is construct. It has immunity to psychic damage. It has 100 hit points. After 8 hours, it is reduced to 0 hit points and dies. The Bizarre Stranger acts on its own accord, though is friendly to you and your allies.
