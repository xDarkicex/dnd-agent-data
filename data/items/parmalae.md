# Parmalae

**description**: Parmalae Uncommon magic weapon (rapier) This thin, delicate blade is gilded in pure silver. When swung, glowing blue runes appear on the flat of the blade. The basket hilt is fashioned from sturdy electrum in the fanciful design of ivy leaves. Property : You gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this weapon. When attuned to a creature with the fey type (or a creature touched by fey in some way), once per day on a successful hit, the target is imbued with a glittery fey lining. This glowing aura grants advantage on ranged attacks and lasts for one minute.
