# Armband of Strength

**description**: Armband of Strength Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This thick, bronze armband is engraved with the image of two bulls butting horns. Your Strength score is 20 while you wear this item. It has no effect on you if your Strength is 20 or higher without it. Modifiers: Strength: 20
