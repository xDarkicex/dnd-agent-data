# Gravitational Lens

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon This black, walnut-sized orb distorts the air around it. Gravitational Distortion As a Bonus Action, you can use the orb to create an area of distortion in a 15-foot radius Sphere centered on a point you can see within 60 feet. The area becomes Heavily Obscured for creatures outside the area for 1 minute (creatures inside the area can see normally). A creature can use an Action to make a DC 20 Intelligence (Investigation) check, ending the effect for itself on a success. Once used, this property can't be used again until the orb has been stored in complete darkness for 8 uninterrupted hours.
