# Amulet of a Thousand Voices

**description**: Amulet of a Thousand Voices Wondrous Item, Rare Created by a legendary drag queen bard who sought to sing in every voice, this amulet allows anyone to perfectly mimic the speech of countless different persons. A Thousand Voices. While wearing this amulet and you hear another person speaking, you can use your action to copy that person’s voice in the amulet. You can use a bonus action to cause the amulet to change your voice to perfectly mimic any copied voice as long as you know it has been copied by the amulet. You can use another bonus action to use another copied voice or end this effect. The amulet is rumored to be able to hold a thousand different copied voices. Many sages believe this figure to be exaggerated, while some believe the amulet could potentially hold every voice in existence, but no person has been able to verify this yet. Realness. A copied voice by this amulet is indistinguishable from the original in terms of articulation, texture, timbre, and tone. The amulet envelops whoever wears it in a faint aura of transmutation magic if targeted by detect magic . Hoarseness Immunity. While wearing this amulet, your voice cannot become hoarse due to disease, overuse, or magic. Item Variant: Legendary Disguise Kit The Amulet of a Thousand Voices is one of five magic items in this book that allow player characters to transform their appearance. At the gamemaster’s discretion, player characters may seek to collect all five items to complete a Legendary Disguise Kit, or they may be able to obtain a Legendary Disguise Kit that includes all five items together. The Legendary Disguise Kit is a legendary item that does not require attunement and includes the following individual items: Amulet of a Thousand Voices Bag of Prodigious Props Fabulous Wig Kaleidoscopic lenses Masterful Makeup Kit
