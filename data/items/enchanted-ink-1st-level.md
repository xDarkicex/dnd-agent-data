# Enchanted Ink, 1st Level

**description**: Enchanted Ink, 1st Level Potion, common (1st) This set of inks can be used to make a single tattoo, which consumes the inks. When a creature is tattooed with enchanted inks, choose one spell. The spell’s level cannot exceed the level determined by the rarity of the enchanted inks. The tattooed creature can cast this spell, using the tattoo as a spellcasting focus. Constitution is the spellcasting ability for this spell. After casting the spell with the tattoo, the creature cannot cast the spell in this way again until it finishes a long rest.
