# Wafer of Warmth

**description**: Potion, uncommon This small biscuit can be eaten in just one or two bites. It always feels warm, as if it just came out of the oven. For 1 hour after eating the wafer, you have resistance to cold damage, and you can tolerate temperatures as low as -50 degrees Fahrenheit without any additional protection. If you wear heavy clothes, you can tolerate temperatures as low as -100 degrees Fahrenheit.
