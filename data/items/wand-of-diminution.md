# Wand of Diminution

**description**: Wand Of Diminution Wand, uncommon (requires attunement by a spellcaster) This wand has 7 charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more of its charges to cast the “reduce” effect of the enlarge/reduce spell (DC 15) from it. The wand regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the wand’s last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the wand crumbles into ashes and is destroyed.
