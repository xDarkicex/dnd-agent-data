# Adder's Embrace (Legendary)

**description**: Adder's Embrace Weapon (Pike), Legendary (Requires Attunement) You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While attuned to the pike, your Dexterity score increases by 2, as does your maximum for that score. The pike has 11 charges. The pike regains 1d10 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. Snakebite. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge to make a melee attack with the pike’s serpent head. On a hit, the pike deals 1d4 piercing damage plus 2 poison damage. Lunging Coils. As an action, you can expend 2 charges to cause the serpent to lock around a Large or smaller creature within the weapon’s reach, grappling the target (escape DC 17). Blinding Venom. As an action, you can expend 3 charges to spit venom from the serpent’s mouth in a 15-foot cone. Each creature in the cone must succeed on a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw or take 5 poison damage and be blinded until the end of their next turn. Petrify. As an action, you can expend 6 charges to cast flesh to stone (save DC 17) from the weapon.
