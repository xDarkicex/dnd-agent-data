# Philter of Mercy

**description**: Potion, Rare As you imbibe this potion, you remember a time of mercy, atonement, and forgiveness—the specific memories depend on the philter's creator. You are immune to being frightened for 1 hour and have advantage on Wisdom ability checks during that time. The effects of this philter cancel the effects of a philter of righteous anger .
