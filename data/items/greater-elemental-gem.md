# Greater Elemental Gem

**description**: Greater Elemental Gem Wondrous Item, Rare This gem contains a mote of multiple combined elemental energies. When you use an action to break the gem, an elemental is summoned as if you had cast the conjure elemental spell, and the gem's magic is lost. The type of gem determines the elemental summoned by the spell. Gem Summoned Elemental Spectrolite Ice Elemental Olivine Magma Elemental Moonstone Mist Elemental Pietersite Storm Elemental
