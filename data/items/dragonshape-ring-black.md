# Dragonshape ring, black

**description**: Dragonshape ring, black Ring, very rare (requires attunement) The wearer gains resistance to acid damage but this ring also has three charges. The wearer can use a bonus action to expend a charge and transform into an adult black dragon. One expended charge is restored each dawn.

- Item Type: Ring
- Item Rarity: very rare
