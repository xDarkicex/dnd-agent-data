# Chaos Pearl (Rare)

**description**: Chaos Pearl Wondrous Item, Rare This strange and beautiful pearl carries a portion of the power that birthed the 3 central realms of the world: sky, earth and the pit. Upon defeating a chaos spawn, the strange beings on occasion release this pearl from their swiftly disappearing bodies. You can use an action to crush the pearl in your hand and activate one of the following effects: You recover a 4th level spell slot. If you don’t have a spell slot of the highest level possible, the next closest will be replenished. You recover 3d12+10 hit points. You can refresh an ability or feature, aside from spellcasting, that has a recharge time equal to a short rest. Warlocks can refresh their Warlock spell slots. After the effect has taken place, a chaos surge manifests, chosen from the Chaos Surge table native to the chaos spawn that created the pearl. After the surge is manifested the pearl is destroyed.
