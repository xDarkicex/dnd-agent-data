# Amulet of Sustaining Health

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) While wearing this amulet, you need to eat and drink only once every 7 days. In addition, you have advantage on saving throws against effects that would cause you to suffer a level of exhaustion.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: uncommon
- Requires Attunement: requires attunement

**publisher**: Kobold Press
