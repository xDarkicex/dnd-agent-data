# Ironwing's Perch

**description**: Ironwing’s Perch Weapon (Halberd), Uncommon An engraved iron hawk sits atop this knotted wooden polearm. Its wings are stretched back, forming the axe-like blade of the weapon. Druids are proficient with Ironwing’s Perch and can use it as a focus for their druid spells. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. As an action, you can speak the weapon’s command word and make its blade come alive, transforming it into a blood hawk for 1 hour, or until you speak the command word again, at which point the hawk returns to the weapon and reforms the blade. The hawk is friendly to you and your companions and obeys your commands. In combat, it shares your initiative count, but takes its turn immediately after yours. If the hawk drops to 0 hit points, it shatters, and a new blade begins reforming on the polearm, ready for use after 1d4 days. While the hawk is not on the polearm, or is reforming, the weapon is treated as a quarterstaff. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a short or long rest.
