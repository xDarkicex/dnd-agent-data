# Whip of Lightning

**description**: Whip of Lightning Weapon (whip), rare (requires attunement) Damage: 1d8+1 lightning Weight: 2 lbs. Properties: Finesse, Reach When activated, a long whip of electricity extends from the metal handle. This whip deals 1d8 lightning damage instead of the usual 1d4 slashing damage. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic whip. Additionally, when making an attack with this whip, you get a +2 bonus to the attack roll if the target is wearing metal armor. Thunderclap. On a hit, you may use your bonus action to crack the whip and release a wave of thunderous energy. Each medium or smaller creature in a 5 foot radius of the target must make a DC 10 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 2d6 thunder damage and is knocked prone. On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage and isn't knocked prone.
