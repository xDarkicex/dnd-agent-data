# Living Stake

**description**: Wondrous item, rare Fashioned from mandrake root, the living stake longs to taste the heart’s blood of vampires. A successful melee attack attaches it to a vampire’s chest. On the following round, roots force their way into the creature’s heart, negating fast healing and preventing gaseous form. If the target is reduced to 0 hit points, it is immobilized as if it had been staked. The stake can be pulled out with a successful DC 17 Strength (Athletics) check made as an action; doing this destroys the magic item. The stake has no eﬀect on targets other than vampires.
