# Dark Candle (very rare variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Component: undead rancid fat In shadowy meeting rooms beneath respectable establishments, in crypts and catacombs around the tombs of the dead, and lining hallways filled with the bodies of adventurers, these tentacle-like purple-black candles burn with a dim malice. Eldritch Light. When found, this candle has enough wax to burn for 1 hour. As an action, you can light this candle, causing it to emit dim light in a 15-foot radius. Whenever a creature other than you moves within 15 feet of the candle for the first time on its turn or starts its turn there, the creature must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw as shadowy tentacles lash out at it. The creature takes 1d6 necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature that fails the saving throw by 5 or more has its speed reduced to 0 feet by the tentacles until the start of its next turn. No amount of wind can extinguish the candle. The candle can be extinguished by you as an action, by a creature within 5 feet of you that uses its action to make a successful DC 15 Dexterity (Acrobatics) check, or by the use of a dispel magic spell or similar magic. Each time the candle is lit and extinguished, a minimum of 10 minutes’ worth of wax is consumed. Very rare variant: Increase the save DC to 16, the DC of the ability check to extinguish the candle to 20, and the damage to 2d6. If a creature fails the saving throw by 5 or more, it’s also incapacitated until the start of its next turn.
