# Garlycanthrope

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Component: plant poison gland Originally commissioned by an infamous vampire huntress, Dr. Francis N. Stein’s garlycanthropes have proved themselves the ‘must-have commodity of the season’ for monster hunters everywhere. Their ability to detect undead and shapechangers has saved many potential victims from the jaws of a mimic, or from walking into a vampire’s lair. Animate Bulb. As a bonus action while holding this item, you can speak its command word. In response, this inanimate bulb of garlic becomes a creature that uses the garlycanthrope stat block, sprouting little legs, eyes, and a panting muzzle. It is extremely friendly to you and your allies, but is skittish if any of them are undead or shapechangers. In combat, the garlycanthrope shares your initiative count, but it takes its turn immediately after yours. It can move and use its reaction on its own, but the only action it takes on its turn is the Dodge action, unless you take a bonus action on your turn to command it to take one of the actions in its stat block or the Dash, Disengage, Help, Hide, or Search action. After 1 hour, or if the garlycanthrope is reduced to 0 hit points, it becomes an inanimate bulb of garlic once more. Once this property of the item has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn. Be careful not to eat it in the meantime!
