# Shell Relic of A’rvanshy

**description**: Wondrous item, legendary This enchanted oyster shell is composed of two separate halves decorated with pearls, some carved to resemble skulls. The inside of the shell bears the rune of A’rvanshy spread out on its two halves and a sentence written on it in Deep-Sea Elvish language that reads: “The orb of my ascension is… / …under the tower of the Caelhaa'y.” This wondrous item has 6 charges and it regains 1d6 expended charges daily at dawn. As an action, you can use the top or lower half of the shell relic to expend 1 of its charges to adapt your body to an aquatic environment, sprouting gills and growing webbing between your fingers. You can breathe underwater and gain a swimming speed equal to your walking speed for 24 hours.
