# Lamp of Darkness

**description**: Lamp of Darkness Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This lamp is made from black iron and has shutters. It does not consume any oil. When you unshutter the lamp, magical darkness spreads out from it to fill a 15-foot-radius sphere for 10 minutes. The darkness spreads around corners. A creature with darkvision can’t see through this darkness, and nonmagical light can’t illuminate it. Once used, this property of the lamp can’t be used again until the next dawn.
