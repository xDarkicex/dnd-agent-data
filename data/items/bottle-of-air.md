# Bottle of Air

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon This item appears to be a normal glass bottle with a cork. When taken to any airless environment (such as underwater or in a vacuum), it retains air within it at all times, continually renewing its contents. You can use an action to remove the cork and breathe out of the bottle. You can use a bonus action to pass the bottle to a nearby ally.
