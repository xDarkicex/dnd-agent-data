# Armor of Gleaming

**description**: Armor of Gleaming Armor Price: 4,000 gp This armor always looks polished and shiny enough to see yourself in it. Simple dents, scratches, and dirt do not show up on the armor, and it can never collect dust. The Armor of Gleaming is so shiny that it can conduct light in the darkest of places. Speak a command word as a Free Action to cause the armor to light up and shine with bright light out to 30 feet. Once per Long Rest you can cause the armor to emit a flash of light that can blind anyone who is looking at it. Make a Spell Attack with a +5 bonus against each target in 30 feet that can see you. On hit, the target is blinded for 1 minute. They have Disadvantage or automatically fail (game master’s choice) any checks or attacks that require sight.
