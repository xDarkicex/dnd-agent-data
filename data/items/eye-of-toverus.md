# Eye of Toverus

**description**: Eye of Toverus Wondrous item Price: 2,500 gp A highly polished silver torus, roughly the same size and shape as a donut. Once per Short Rest, the bearer can focus on the reflection in the torus and choose any point they can see within 60 feet. For up to 1 minute, or until they end the effect as a Free Action, the bearer can see as if they were standing in that location. Useful for seeing around corners or over walls.
