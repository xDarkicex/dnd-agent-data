# Garganoff's Leftovers Jar

**description**: Garganoff's Leftovers Jar Wondrous item Price: 17,000 gp This glass jar can contain the effects of one spell, which can then be released and directed later on by the caster or by anyone else. When full, the jar seems to contain a brightly glowing sphere which glows different colors depending on the spell that it contains. Any spellcaster may cast a spell to be “placed” in the jar. Placing the spell into the jar still requires a spell point, but once it is placed the spell exists until the jar is opened. Anyone may open the jar to direct the spell at the target of their choice. Opening the jar requires a Main Action. If the spell is an attack, the holder of the jar may use the Attack Bonus and Damage of the original caster.
