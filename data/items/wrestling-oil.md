# Wrestling Oil

**description**: Wrestling Oil Wondrous item, uncommon This brass canister has a swirling pattern engraved upon it and contains 1d4 + 1 doses of a thick, sticky, translucent mixture. As an action, one dose of the oil can be applied to your skin. You then have advantage on any ability check you make to escape a grapple for 1 hour.
