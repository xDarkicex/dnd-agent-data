# Alchemical bolts

**description**: As an action, you can fill this bulb-headed crossbow bolt with any alchemical liquid, such as alchemist’s fire or holy water. When you hit a creature with this bolt, it deals damage as normal then shatters, releasing the alchemical liquid inside it onto the target. The target is then subjected to the substance.

**properties**:

- Item Type: Ammunition
