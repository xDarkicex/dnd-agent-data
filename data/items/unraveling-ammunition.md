# Unraveling Ammunition

**description**: Weapon (Ammunition), Rare This magic ammunition hums with arcane energy. When you make a ranged weapon attack using the ammunition, the attack ignores any bonuses to the target's AC gained from spells or other magical effects. When you hit a creature with a ranged weapon attack using the ammunition, any spell of 5th level or lower on the target ends and it takes 1d8 Force damage for each spell ended this way. In addition, the first time the target attempts to cast a spell of 1st level or higher before the end of its next turn, it must make a Constitution saving throw with a DC of 10 + the spell's level. On a failure, the casting fails and the action is wasted, though any spell slot used to cast the spell isn't expended. Once the piece of ammunition hits a target, it is no longer magical.
