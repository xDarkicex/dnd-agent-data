# Ring of Birdseye Maple

**description**: Ring, uncommon (optional attunement) Component: bundle of plant (awakened tree) roots Pale green, eye-like tourmalines have been encapsulated in maple roots sung by seelie fey into pleasing knots and braids. This ring has 4 charges and regains 1d4 expended charges daily at dawn. Watchful Woods. While wearing the ring, whenever you touch a plant or wooden surface, you can expend 1 charge to cause an eye to grow anywhere on that plant or surface within 60 feet of you. The eye lasts for 1 hour, until you cease touching the plant or surface, or until you create a new eye. You can see out of the eye, which has darkvision out to 60 feet, for as long as you remain touching the plant or surface. While you look out of the eye, you are blinded with regards to your own senses. Optional Attunement. If you choose to attune to this item, the eye doesn’t disappear if you cease touching the plant or surface, and you can use the Eyesoar property while you remain attuned to it. OA: Eyesoar. So long as the eye is within 300 feet of you, you can use an action to look through the eye. While looking through the eye, you can use your action to move the eye up to 30 feet along the plant or surface. Very rare variant: The eye can see invisible creatures and objects as if they were visible and can see into the Ethereal Plane. In addition, you have advantage on Intelligence (Investigation) checks made to peer through illusions while looking through the eye.
