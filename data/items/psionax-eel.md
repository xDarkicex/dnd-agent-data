# Psionax Eel

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This tiny eel has two tentacles and sharp teeth. Symbiotic Attunement To attune to this symbiont, you must hold it up to your ear canal, whereupon it burrows into your head and wraps around your cerebral cortex. You gain the following effects while attuned to the eel. Wisdom. You have Advantage on Wisdom saves. Telepathy. You can communicate simple ideas, emotions, and images telepathically with any creature you can see within 30 feet that can understand a language. Frail Mind. You have Vulnerability to Psychic damage. If you have Resistance to Psychic damage from another source, you instead lose that Resistance. The eel can't be removed while you're attuned to it, and you can't voluntarily end your attunement to it. If you're targeted by magic that ends a curse, your attunement to the eel ends, and it exits your body.
