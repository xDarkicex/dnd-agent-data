# Blister Sap

**description**: Poison, rare Containing sap taken from a toxic tree, this vial is designed to be thrown at its victim, shattering on impact and dowsing them in blistering fluid. As an Action, you can throw the vial up to 30 feet to a point you can see. Each creature within a 5-foot radius of the impact must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw or be covered in the toxic fluid. On a failed saving throw, a creature takes 2d6 points of acid damage and gains a special Injury of Dangerous severity, “Blistered”. While Injured in this way, a creature has disadvantage on attack rolls and saving throws until the end of its next turn, after which it takes a -1 penalty instead. The creature also gains advantage on Charisma (Intimidation) checks and disadvantage on Charisma (Persuasion) checks. This Injury is removed automatically at the end of a long rest, or if the creature receives magical healing from a spell of 5th level or higher in conjunction with a DC 15 Wisdom (Medicine) check made over a period of at least 10 minutes.
