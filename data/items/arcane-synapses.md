# Arcane Synapses

**description**: Donor: Any creature with the Spellcasting or Innate Spellcasting trait Slot: Head You have stolen a bit of arcane potential from a creature by inserting some of its nerves into your brain. You learn one cantrip of your choice from the wizard spell list. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for this cantrip. By harvesting a second creature, you can improve this graft and learn an additional wizard cantrip of your choice.
