# Scroll of Convenience

**description**: Scroll, Uncommon This parchment scroll has a rough but durable texture. When the parchment is used to create a Spell Scroll , the time required to do so is halved. Moreover, a spellcaster can attempt to cast the spell even if the spell isn't on the caster's spell list. When the scroll is used to cast a spell, the blue runes on it glow brightly and then fade, leaving the scroll blank and ready for another spell to be inscribed upon it. The scroll can hold only one spell at a time.
