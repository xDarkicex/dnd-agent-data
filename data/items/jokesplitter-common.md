# Jokesplitter (Common)

**description**: Jokesplitter Weapon (Handaxe), Common (Requires Attunement) Jokesplitter normally takes the form of a harmless wooden hatchet. As a bonus action, you can speak a command word, causing a metal blade to emerge for 10 minutes, transforming it into a magical handaxe. Progression Jokesplitter progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s Strength modifier increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
