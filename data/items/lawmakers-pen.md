# Lawmaker's Pen

**description**: Wondrous Item, Common This magic quill is made from the feather of a celestial bird. The quill produces its own ink, which appears as a vibrant silvery script. Any script written with the quill has the following magical properties: The script is untouched by time and elements. It never fades or smears, although the medium on which it is written retains its normal properties and may be physically destroyed. The script is immune to magical tampering and can't be altered, erased, or obscured by any magical means, including illusion magic.
