# Lamp of Honesty

**description**: Lamp of Honesty Wondrous item, rare This item appears to be an ordinary lamp except it burns for just 10 minutes on a flask of oil. Once lit, any creature within 15 feet of it cannot speak a deliberate lie. A creature may choose not to speak. Once you have lit the lamp, it cannot be re-lit until the next dawn. It may surprise you to learn that this item was first devised by Rary, who was a master of mental magic. Tenser considered him a mediocre wizard and gave him the nickname “Medium Rary.” Rary had his revenge, and much strife followed.
