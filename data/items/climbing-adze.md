# Climbing adze

**description**: This tool is made of a sharp, wide blade perpendicular to a short handle with a leather thong to loop over the user’s wrist. Climbing adzes are used primarily to peel bark from felled trees or remove buried stones from the soil, but if you use a pair of climbing adzes together, you have advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks made to climb.
