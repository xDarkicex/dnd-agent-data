# Tear of the Hamadryad

**description**: Tear of the Hamadryad Uncommon wondrous item This teardrop-shaped piece of smooth amber is warm to the touch and grants its bearer a feeling of calmness and serenity. Property : Once per day, while clutching the tear of the hamadryad , you gain a 1d4 bonus to any saving throw or ability check.
