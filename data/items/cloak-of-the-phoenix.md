# Cloak of the Phoenix

**description**: Cloak of the Phoenix Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) This floor-length cloak is composed of golden phoenix feathers and is fastened around the neck by a chain of fine platinum. While wearing this cloak, you have a flying speed equal to your walking speed.
