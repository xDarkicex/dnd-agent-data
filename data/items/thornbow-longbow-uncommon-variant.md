# Thornbow Longbow (uncommon variant)

**description**: Weapon (any bow), rare (requires attunement) Component: plant bark Unseelie fey—those with a love for the cold and ugly facets of the world—cultivate the malevolent syklthorn, a green-brown creeper with bright red thorns. Syklthorn is a carnivorous plant that snatches creatures who venture too close before eviscerating them with its bladelike thorns. Weapons made from this plant are just as bloodthirsty. This weapon has 7 charges and regains 1d4 + 3 expended charges daily at dawn. Sykl Arrow. As a bonus action, you can expend 1 charge to cause a thorny arrow to grow from the bow, choosing whether it is a Bramble or Splinter arrow. The next attack you make with the bow has additional effects on a hit, as described below: Bramble. The arrow transforms into grasping vines. The target of the attack must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or become restrained by the vines until the end of its next turn. A Large or larger creature has advantage on this saving throw. Splinter. The arrow erupts in a storm of splinters and thorns. The target of the attack and each creature within 5 feet of it must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 1d10 piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Overgrown. As an action, you can expend 3 charges to cause three thick-limbed, thorny arrows to grow from the bow and fire them at a point within 120 feet of you. Where the arrows land, a mass of syklthorn brambles grow, creating an effect identical to the wall of thorns spell (save DC 15), except it deals 4d8 damage instead of the spell’s normal damage. Uncommon variant: Reduce the charges to 5, the recharge to 1d4 + 1, and the DCs to 13. The weapon doesn’t have the Overgrown property.
