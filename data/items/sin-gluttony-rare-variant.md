# Sin: Gluttony (rare variant)

**description**: Item modification, uncommon (optional or enhanced attunement) The Task. The fiend requests a minor task, such as eating a friend’s food before they’re finished, not sharing your birthday cake, or taking candy from a baby. OA or EA: Gluttony. While the item is on your person, whenever you roll Hit Dice to recover hit points at the end of a short rest, you regain 1 additional hit points for each die rolled. You can regain up to 5 additional hit points from this property per day; the additional hit points you can regain resets each day at dawn. You require twice as much food to sustain yourself each day. Rare variant: Increase the hit points regained to 2 per Hit Die rolled, and the maximum you can regain before the next dawn to 10. The fiend asks for a medium task, such as stealing a party member’s rations or demanding that a restaurant increase its portion size.
