# St. Skaneetles's Deossifying Rod

**description**: Wondrous item, legendary (requires attunement) This rod is said to be carved from the leg bone of St. Skaneetles, a once-legendary cleric of undeath. Holy words and phrases of the god of undeath are carved along its length, which is bound with several gold bands. The writing glows with an eerie green light. This rod functions as a +1 mace . While attuned, whenever you hit a vertebrate creature with an attack made using this weapon that creature must make a DC 17 Constitution saving throw or gain a special Injury of Deadly severity, “Deossified”, as part of its skeleton is absorbed by the rod. While Injured in this way, the creature takes a penalty randomly determined from the table below. This Injury can be gained multiple times, but no more than once per turn and it cannot gain the same version of the Injury more than once (if it would gain the same version of the Injury, reroll). If a creature would gain the Injury for a fourth time its entire skeleton is dissolved, killing it. Undead skeletons have disadvantage on this saving throw. D6 Deossified Injury 1-2 Deossified Arms. The creature has disadvantage on attack rolls 3-4 Deossified Ribs. The creature has disadvantage on Strength, Constitution, and Dexterity saving throws 5-6 Deossified Legs. The creature's movement speed is reduced to 0. Additionally, each time the rod inflicts the Deossified Injury it gains 2 charges, and each time the rod kills a creature as a result of a Death Blow or the Deossified Injury it gains 8 additional charges, to a maximum of 10. When the rod reaches 10 charges, it transforms into a +3 quarterstaff with the ability to sling bullets of magical bone as though it were a +3 sling , spending 1 charge per bullet thrown. These bullets and staff also apply the Deossified Injury as above, but the staff cannot gain charges. When the staff is reduced to 0 charges, it transforms back into its original form.
