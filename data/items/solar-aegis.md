# Solar Aegis

**description**: Solar Aegis Armor (Shield), Legendary (requires attunement) Forged for a defiant paladin general who waged war on corrupt angels in ages past, this shield allows you to withstand the fury of the sun itself. While holding this shield, you have a +3 bonus to AC. This bonus is in addition to the shield’s normal bonus to AC. Additionally, you have resistance to fire and radiant damage, and you can’t be blinded by bright light. Radiant Reign. The shield has 5 charges. When you take fire or radiant damage, you can use your reaction and spend one charge to gain immunity to both fire and radiant damage, including against the triggering damage, until the end of your next turn. The shield regains 1d4+1 charges daily at dawn.
