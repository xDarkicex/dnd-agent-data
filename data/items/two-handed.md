# Two-Handed

**description**: Apprentice weapon property Components: Masterwork weapon that doesn’t have the Finesse, Foregrip, Light, Thrown, or Versatile properties This weapon gains the Two-Handed property and its damage die increases by one step. Additionally, if it is a ranged weapon, its normal range increases by 50 feet and its long range increases accordingly.
