# Gauntlets of Fjalar

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) While wearing these gauntlets, you can speak their command word as a bonus action to gain magical strength for 1 minute. For the duration, you can make two unarmed strikes when you take the Attack action (or more if your class features would allow you to make more attacks) on your turn. On a successful hit, you deal 2d10 plus your Strength modifier magical bludgeoning damage. Once used, this property of the gauntlets can't be used again until the next dawn. Fjalar Fjalar is a dwarf from Norse mythology. In your campaign, you can replace him with another appropriate character.
