# Diabolical Swinging Mace

**description**: Weapon (mace), legendary (requires attunement) 2d8 bludgeoning damage Oversized Weapon: when wielded by a Medium or smaller creature, this weapon gains the Two-Handed and Heavy tags. Infernal Fire: On a successful hit, this mace deals an additional 2d6 fire damage. The attacks made with this weapon count as magical. Embrace the Abyss: While attuned to this mace, you gain resistance to fire and necrotic damage. Infernal Swing: As a bonus action, you can command the mace to unleash a violent whirlwind of infernal flames in a 15-foot cone originating from you. Creatures in the area must make a DEX SAVE with a DC equal to (16 + your proficiency bonus), taking 4d6 fire damage on a failed save or half as much on a successful one. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a short long rest.
