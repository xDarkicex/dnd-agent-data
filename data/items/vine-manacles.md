# Vine Manacles

**description**: Vine Manacles Wondrous Item, Rare Used by intrepid paladins of Nature who hunt down agents of destruction, these thorny restraints can bind any target of large size or smaller. Escaping the Vine Manacles requires a successful DC 25 Dexterity check and breaking them requires a successful DC 25 Strength check. Vine Manacles can’t be unlocked, but they can be removed by the creature who placed them on the target. The manacles have 50 hit points. If they are damaged, they regain 1 hit point per minute. If the Vine Manacles are reduced to 0 hit points, they wither, leaving behind a spore. If the spore is planted on fertile soil, it will sprout a new set of Vine Manacles at the next dawn.
