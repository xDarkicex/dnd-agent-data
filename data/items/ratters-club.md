# Ratter's Club

**description**: Weapon (Mace), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This magic mace is decorated with four steel rats. Rat's Bite. When you roll a 20 on your attack roll against a creature with the mace, the creature must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or be Poisoned for 1 minute. Rat Form. As an action, you can transform into a rat or a giant rat . The transformation functions as the Polymorph spell, except you retain your Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma scores. The transformation lasts for 1 hour or until you end it as a bonus action. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn. Rat Whisperer. While holding the mace, you can speak with rats as with the Speak With Animals spell. Moreover, you automatically sense the location of any rats or giant rats within 30 feet of you, and ordinary rats and giant rats are indifferent toward you and will not attack you unless you threaten or harm them.
