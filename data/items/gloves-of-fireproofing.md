# Gloves of Fireproofing

**description**: Gloves of Fireproofing Wondrous items Price: 15,000 gp These thick leather gloves were made for a blacksmith who was tired of burning himself, but he may have gone overboard with the magical effect. These gloves make the wearer completely immune to damage from fire or heat. This effect applies to their entire body, and any weapons or equipment that they may be carrying.
