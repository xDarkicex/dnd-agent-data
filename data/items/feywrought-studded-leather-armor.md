# Feywrought Studded Leather Armor

**description**: Feywrought Studded Leather Armor Armor (Light), Rare (Requires Attunement) This colorful, flowery armor was forged in the Feywild and is infused with that plane’s captivating magic. While wearing this armor, you have advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the charmed condition on yourself. This armor has 3 charges. You can use an action to expend a charge to cast the Compulsion spell (save DC 15) from this armor. The armor regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. Studded Leather Armor. Made from tough but flexible leather, studded leather is reinforced with close-set rivets or spikes. AC: 12 + Dex modifier Weight: 13
