# Matlan's Crossbow

**description**: Weapon (Martial, Ranged), Rare Weapon Damage: 1d12 piercing Ammunition (80/320), Two-Handed, Heavy, Loading, Special Spiked: You can use the crossbow to bash enemies with its spiked design. When you use it this way, the Crossbow is treated as a Melee Simple weapon with the Finesse tag. It has a reach of 5 ft. and it deals 1d6 piercing damage. Designed to Kill: your weapon attacks with Matlan’s Crossbow score a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20. Explosive Bolt (3/day): When you make a ranged weapon attack with Matlan’s Crossbow you can infuse a bolt with magical force. The target and every other creature within 10 ft. of them must make a CON SAVE 14 or take 7 (2d6) force damage.
