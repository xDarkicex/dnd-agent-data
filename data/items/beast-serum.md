# Beast Serum

**description**: Potion, rare Component: phial of humanoid (shapechanger) blood Doctor Jake Kill advertises his proprietary blend of bull’s pituitary and caffeine as a “sure fire way to lose friends and alienate people, especially if those people are a swarm of marauding goblins.” Oddly enough, he seems more than a little keen to get rid of this particular concoction… Inner Monster. Tied up in a protective claw is a philter of bubbling red liquid. You can use an action to drink this potion, filling you with a sense of rage and selfishness as your muscles inflate and your veins bulge. You turn into a brute, the physical and mental manifestation of your most evil and selfish thoughts, for 10 minutes, and undergo the following changes: Your size increases by one category and your weight is multiplied by eight. You have advantage on Strength-based attack rolls, Strength checks, and Strength saving throws. Your alignment changes to chaotic evil; you think of your own survival first and any trace of compassion or mercy is shredded. When the effect ends, you return to normal and make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, you gain 1 level of exhaustion.
