# Trickster's Eye Poison, uncommon

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Made of a toxic but occasionally recreational plant this poison enhances focus and perceptual acuity to a breaking point, overwhelming the mind with sensory information. A creature hit by a weapon coated in this poison must make a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or be affected by the poison for 1 minute. While under the influence of this poison, a creature gains a +5 bonus to Wisdom (Perception) checks, but has disadvantage on attack rolls, saving throws against pain-inducing effects (such as the agonizing eternity* or vile incantation* spells) or effects that deal psychic damage, and ability checks other than Wisdom (Perception) checks.

**publisher**: Lone Colossus Games

**book**: Injuries and Vile Deeds
