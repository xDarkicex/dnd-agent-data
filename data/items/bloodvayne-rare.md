# Bloodvayne (Rare)

**description**: Bloodvayne Weapon (Longsword), Rare (Requires Attunement) Blood Sacrifice. When you attune to Bloodvayne , you sacrifice one of your Hit Dice to the sword. This sacrificed Hit Die cannot be regained and is lost permanently. You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. The sword deals additional damage equal to the Hit Die you sacrificed during attunement. You can utter a command word to cause Bloodvayne to emit the metallic scent of freshly spilled blood for 1 minute. This scent is noticeable to all creatures within 30 feet of you who can smell. Sanguine Smite. As a bonus action, you can empower the sword with your lifeforce by expending up to three Hit Dice. The next time you hit a creature with an attack using Bloodvayne , the attack deals additional slashing damage to the target equal to the roll of the Hit Dice. This damage is in addition to the bonus damage the sword gained during attunement. You don’t regain hit points from expending these Hit Dice. If you sacrifice your last remaining Hit Dice to increase Bloodvayne’s damage, roll a d6. On a 1, you drop to 0 hit points. You can use this property three times, and regain all expended Hit Dice and uses when you finish a long rest. Progression Bloodvayne progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
