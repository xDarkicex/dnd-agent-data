# Monarch Battleaxe (Rare)

**description**: Monarch Battleaxe Weapon (Battleaxe), Rarity Varies (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Druids are proficient with the Monarch Battleaxe. Summon Butterflies. While holding this weapon, you can speak a command word as a bonus action to summon 18 Tiny purple butterflies which flutter around the axe head for 1 hour. Each butterfly has AC 10, 1 hit point, and automatically fails saving throws. While at least one butterfly remains, the weapon emits a purple glow, creating bright light in a 10-foot radius, and dim light for an additional 10 feet. Once you have summoned butterflies, you can’t do so again until you finish a long rest. Butterfly Burst. When you hit with an attack using this weapon, you can expend up to 6 of your summoned butterflies to deal additional radiant damage to the target equal to the number of butterflies expended. Expended butterflies are destroyed. Monarch Wings. You can use a bonus action to expend up to 6 butterflies to cause spectral, purple butterfly wings to sprout from your back, giving you a fly speed equal to 5 times the number of butterflies expended. The wings remain for 1 hour, or until you use a bonus action to end the effect. Progression The Monarch Battleaxe progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
