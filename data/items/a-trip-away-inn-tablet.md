# A Trip Away Inn Tablet

**description**: Wonderous Item, Uncommon These small writing tablets contain 30 pieces of parchment bound together with stitching that allows a sheet to be easily pulled out and taken. The tablets look unassuming, however anything written in it will appear in Flip's Codex exactly as it is written or drawn.
