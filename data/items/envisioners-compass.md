# Envisioner's Compass

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare While holding this compass, you can use your action to set a compass course to a location, object, or creature that is on the same plane of existence as you. The compass needle points in the direction of the target for 24 hours or until this property is used again. If the creature or object can’t be targeted by divination magic or be perceived through magical scrying sensors, the attempt to set a course to it automatically fails. Once used, this property can’t be used again until the next dawn.
