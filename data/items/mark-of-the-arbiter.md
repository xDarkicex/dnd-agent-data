# Mark of the Arbiter

**description**: Poison, Rare This crystal clear, odorless liquid is one of two vials worn around the neck of all sworn Arbiters. Designed to kill efficiently and quietly, it causes the eyes of the dead or living victim to become bloodshot and a dark purple bruising forms below the eye that fades after 12 hours. The guild’s symbol is derived from this poison, the key ingredient of which is the Lily of the Valley. Duration: 2 minutes. Anyone ingesting a single dose of the Mark of the Arbiter must succeed on a DC 18 Constitution saving throw or suffer 7 (1d10 + 2) poison damage at the start of each of their turns.
