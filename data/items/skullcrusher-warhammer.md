# Skullcrusher Warhammer

**description**: Melee Weapon (Maul, Martial, Melee), Very Rare, requires Attunement 2d6 bludgeoning Two-Handed, Heavy Blood Seeker: Once attuned to this weapon, your eyes glow red. You gain resistance to necrotic damage but gain vulnerability to radiant damage. Additionally, your attacks with the Skullcrusher Warhammer inflict an additional 3 (1d6) necrotic damage. Life Reaper (3/day): Whenever you deal necrotic damage to a creature, you can choose to regain hit points equal to half the necrotic damage dealt. Bone Crusher: When you hit a creature with a melee attack with the Skullcrusher Warhammer, you can magically enhance the force behind the strike. The target must succeed a STR SAVE with a DC equal to (12 + your proficiency bonus) or be knocked Prone and receive an additional 3 (1d6) bludgeoning damage. You can use this feature a number of times each day equal to your Strength modifier +1, and you regain expended uses at dawn. War Menace: You have advantage on Intimidation checks when wielding this weapon. On a Critical Hit, the target becomes Frightened of you until the start of your next turn. If a player takes the Warhammer, the gamemaster reads: As you take the artifact, pools of fire begin to bubble, and from within them, a colossal monster emerges, bathed in fire. It rises to the chamber’s ceiling, and its loud howl knocks you backward. Each player must make a DEX SAVE DC 16 to avoid taking 2d12 Fire damage from the monster. The players will have to find their way out of the chasm as pools of lava and fire begin to enter the corridors. ATHLETICS SAVE DC 18: Is needed for the player to exit the chasm successfully. If any fails, add 1d12 Fire Damage to the players until they find the exit.
