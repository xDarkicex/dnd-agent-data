# Ankh of Ra

**description**: This golden holy symbol, blessed by the Sun Above, shields you from death. When you drop to 0 hit points as a result of taking damage while you are attuned to the ankh, you instead regain 4d10 hit points. The ankh then loses its luster and becomes nonmagical.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: wondrous item
- Item Rarity: uncommon
- Requires Attunement: requires attunement

**publisher**: Mage Hand Press

**book**: Dark Matter Starter Kit
