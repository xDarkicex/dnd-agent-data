# Wings of Prey (uncommon variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) Component: pouch of beast (giant eagle) feathers Bestowed upon defenders of city walls and mountain rangers alike, these wings provide great mobility, as well as packing a secret weapon. The appearance of the wings varies from one individual to another, shifting in colour to mirror their soul. When you attune to the wings, they attach to your back and can’t be removed against your will. As a bonus action, you can speak the wings’ command word to cause them to disappear or reappear. Additionally, the wings have 6 charges and regain 1d4 + 2 expended charges daily at dawn. While the wings are present, you have a flying speed equal to 5 times the number of remaining charges in feet. Razor Sharp. As an action while the wings are present, you can expend 2 charges to hurl a flurry of razor-sharp feathers in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in the area must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d8 slashing damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one. Uncommon variant: Reduce the charges to 3, the recharge to 1d3, the DC to 13, and the damage to 4d6.
