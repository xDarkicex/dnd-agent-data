# Handkerchief of Length

**description**: Handkerchief of Length Wondrous item, uncommon This item appears to be a normal silk handkerchief. However, when you place it in your pocket and speak the command word, you can then pull from your pocket a 50-foot-long line of multi-colored handkerchiefs tied to each other. This functions exactly like a regular piece of rope. When you stuff the handkerchiefs back in your pocket, they revert to the original handkerchief.
