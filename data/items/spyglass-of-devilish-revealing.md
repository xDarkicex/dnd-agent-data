# Spyglass of Devilish Revealing

**description**: This magic spyglass has the functioning eye of a bone devil bound to it. Objects viewed through the spyglass are magnified to four times their size. While viewing through the spyglass, the item confers darkvision that is not impeded by magical darkness. In addition, once per day as an action, the user of the spyglass can activate it to gain Truesight for 10 minutes.
