# Monocle of Secrets

**description**: Monocle of Secrets Wondrous item, uncommon While you wear this gold-rimmed monocle, you have advantage on ability checks made to find secret doors and traps. Additionally, you can use an action while holding a mundane object to learn that object’s exact value, the materials used to create it, and whether it is genuine or counterfeit.
