# Arc of Ignis

**description**: Weapon (Handaxe), Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic handaxe is fashioned from hardened amber and seems to glow from within. The handaxe deals an extra 1d6 Fire damage on a hit, and when you make a ranged attack with it, it flies back to your hand immediately after the attack. Searing Arc. As an action, you can cause the axe to circle around you to a distance of 10 feet until the start of your next turn, whereupon it returns to your hand. Each other creature that starts its turn within 10 feet of you or comes within 10 feet of you for the first time on its turn takes 4d6 Fire damage and must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be Blinded until the start of its next turn. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
