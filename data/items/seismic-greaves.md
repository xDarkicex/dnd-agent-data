# Seismic Greaves

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) These leather boots are outfitted with soles made from natural rubber interwoven with spider silk. Seismic Steps The greaves can hold up to 3 charges and gain 1 charge after each hour you spend moving while wearing them. As a Bonus Action, you can expend 1 charge to cause one of these effects. Muted Steps. For 10 minutes, your steps make no sound, regardless of the surface you are moving across, and you have Advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks that rely on moving silently. Silence. You mute all sound within 10 feet of you until the start of your next turn. No sound can be created within or pass through the area. Any creature or object entirely inside the area is immune to Thunder damage, and creatures are Deafened and can't cast spells that include a verbal component while entirely inside the area.
