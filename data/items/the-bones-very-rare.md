# The Bones (Very Rare)

**description**: Weapon (Longbow), Very Rare (Requires Attunement) Common The Bones will not fire any arrows the wielder knocks, with the string simply refusing to draw. To the Bone. To make an attack with this magic weapon, you must choose to lose 1 hit point, which manifests a bone-shard arrow. This arrow deals 1d8 + 2 necrotic damage on a hit. Dead Echoes. As a bonus action, you can speak a command word, causing groans of the dead to emit from the bow for 1 minute. The sound is audible up to 60 feet away. Uncommon Blood Sacrifice. Prior to making an attack roll, you can choose to lose additional hit points when firing The Bones to increase the extra necrotic damage dealt on a hit. The damage increases by 2 for each additional hit point lost (up to a maximum of 2 hit points per attack). Rare When sacrificing hit points to activate Blood Sacrifice, the maximum number of hit points you can lose per attack increases to 3. Blood Mark. As a bonus action, you can choose to lose up to 2 hit points. When you do, you mark a target that you can see within the bow’s range. Each attack made using this weapon against a marked target gains a bonus to hit equal to the hit points lost this way for 1 minute. Very Rare When sacrificing hit points to activate Blood Sacrifice, the maximum number of hit points you can lose per attack increases to 4. Summon the Horde. As a bonus action, immediately after you hit with an attack using this weapon, you can choose to lose 10 hit points to summon 1d4 + 1 skeletons which appear in the nearest unoccupied spaces around your target. Each skeleton disappears after 1 minute or when it is reduced to 0 hit points and acts as if summoned per the animate dead spell. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest.
