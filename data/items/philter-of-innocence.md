# Philter of Innocence

**description**: Potion, Rare As you imbibe this potion, you return to a childlike state, remembering your life as it was in your formative years—the specific memories depend on the philter's creator. You have resistance to psychic damage for 1 hour and have advantage on Constitution ability checks during that time.
