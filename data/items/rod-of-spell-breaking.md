# Rod of Spell Breaking

**description**: Rod, very rare (requires attunement) This simple-looking iron rod has a flat hoop etched with magical runes of abjuration at its tip. You can use it to target a creature, object, or magical effect within 120 feet. Any spell of 9th level or lower on the target ends. The rod has 50 charges and expends one charge for each level of the spells dispelled this way. A newly found rod has 3d10 charges left. A rod that has no charges left becomes nonmagical.
