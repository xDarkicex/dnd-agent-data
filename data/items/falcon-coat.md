# Falcon Coat

**description**: Falcon Coat Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) This is an elegant coat of falcon feathers and refers to itself as Finpela. It grants a flying speed equal to the wearer's walking speed. The coat is made from the feathers of a slain valkry and still maintains traces of that valkry's personality. It has the following ability scores: Intelligence 9, Wisdom 13, and a Charisma of 17. The coat can speak, read, and understand Common. In addition, it communicates telepathically with whomever is attuned to it. It has hearing and vision to 60 feet and an alignment of Lawful Neutral. The coat is protective of valkry and Thonir. It expects its wearer to adhere to the ideals of Valholl. If the wearer attempts to perform an evil action, the coat reacts and tries to take control of the wearer (the wearer must succeed on a Charisma saving throw against a DC of 15 or be charmed by the coat for 1 minute). If it fails to take control, Finpela revokes attunement and will not allow this wearer to attune to it again.
