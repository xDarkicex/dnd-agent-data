# Starrunner's Twinblade

**description**: Weapon (twinblade), uncommon (optional attunement) Component: aberration brain An astral raider deprived of their dragon needs to travel light. The starrunner’s twinblade is the ideal, multifunctional tool; a devastating blade, bow, and shield rolled into one. With such a diverse weapon, one can hunt food, clear a watering hole, and defend oneself, all while retaining mobility. Psionic Deflection. While spinning the twinblade as part of its Special: Whirl property, whenever a creature misses you with a ranged weapon attack, you can use your reaction to divert the projectile back to its origin. Make an attack roll against the creature using your Intelligence modifier. On a hit, the creature takes the damage from its own attack. Optional Attunement. If you attune to this item, it gains the Psionic Shot property. OA: Psionic Shot. When you make an attack with this weapon as part of the Attack action, you can mime pulling back on it as if it were a bow, causing an arrow of shimmering psionic energy to materialise, and make a ranged weapon attack, using the twinblade as a shortbow with which you are proficient. You can use either your Dexterity or Intelligence modifier for attack and damage rolls made with the twinblade in this way, and it deals psychic damage instead of piercing damage.
