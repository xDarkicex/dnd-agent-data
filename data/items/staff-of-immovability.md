# Staff of Immovability

**description**: Staff, legendary (requires attunement) You can wield this staff as a magic shield that grants a +2 bonus to Armor Class. Additionally, opportunity attacks against you are made with disadvantage. You gain advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks to resist being pushed while you hold this staff. A new staff has 20 charges which you can spend to use its interposition and spells properties. The staff regains 2d8 + 4 expended charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the staff retains its defensive magic, loses its charge abilities, and gains the functionality of an immovable rod. On a 20, the staff immediately regains 1d8 + 2 charges. Interposition. When you are attacked but do not move, you can expend 1 charge to produce a wall of shimmering, translucent force between you and the attacker, providing you with half cover against the attacker. Spells. While holding the staff, you can use an action to expend 4 or more of its charges to cast one of the following spells from it, using your spell save DC and spell attack bonus: flesh to stone (6 charges), hold monster (5 charges), imprisonment (9 charges), indigo spray (7 charges; eight indigo rays as prismatic spray), and resilient sphere (4 charges).
