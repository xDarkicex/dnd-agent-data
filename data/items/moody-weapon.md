# Moody Weapon

**description**: Weapon (any), common (requires attunement) “What d'you mean it's not a moony sword?! Paisàn, I said moody!... a moody sword! ...No refunds!” When you make an attack with this magical weapon and roll a 1 on the d20 for the attack roll, the weapon switches and becomes a random different weapon (the Condottiero can choose the new weapon).

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Weapon
- Item Rarity: common
- Requires Attunement: requires attunement
