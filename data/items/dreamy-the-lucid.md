# Dreamy the Lucid

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon These adorable stuffed toys can be found in bedrooms following the intrusion of a dreamholder into a settlement’s collective dreamscape. Each toy’s magic gives the villagers autonomy over their dreams, and acts as a preventative measure should another aberration try to take over the villagers’ dreams again. While this toy is on your person, magic can’t put you to sleep. If you fall asleep while holding this stuffy, its stimulating enchantment magic activates your psyche, causing you to become a lucid dreamer. While lucid dreaming in this way, you are aware if a creature uses the dream spell to influence you. In addition, if you are asleep when initiative is rolled, you instantly awaken and aren’t surprised.
