# Spell Stabilizer

**description**: Weapon (Light Crossbow), Very Rare (Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster) This light crossbow has a row of arcane runes carved into its body. Rather than a drawstring, a cord of pure blue light connects its arms. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls using this magic weapon. While holding this weapon, you can use it as an arcane focus, adding its attack bonus to your spell attack rolls. The weapon has 5 charges, and regains 1d4 + 1 expended charges when you finish a long rest. When you use your action to cast a spell, you can expend 1 charge to make a ranged attack using the weapon as a bonus action. On a hit, the attack deals extra force damage equal to the spell’s level. Encounter Ideas Planar Bounty. Xi, a plane-hopping bounty hunter (githyanki warrior) wielding a spell stabilizer, opens a portal near the characters and attacks, claiming an extraplanar lord placed a tidy sum on their heads. Spell Forge. While exploring an ancient ruin, the characters discover an inventor’s workshop guarded by an iron golem. The inventor’s final creation, the spell stabilizer, is miraculously intact. Escort Mission. The characters are hired to protect an important noble from a spellcasting assassin with an enchanted crossbow.
