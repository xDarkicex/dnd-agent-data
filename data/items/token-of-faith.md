# Token of Faith

**description**: Token of Faith Spell catalyst, uncommon A trinket given to you by someone you’ve protected or helped, symbolizing their gratitude to you and faith in you. When you cast the shield of faith spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, the spell lasts its full duration without requiring your concentration and grants the target a +3 bonus to its AC, instead of +2.

**properties**:

- Item Type: Spell catalyst
- Item Rarity: uncommon
