# Bloodwork Sniper Rifle

**description**: Sniper rifles are very long and heavy rifles with a scope that is almost as long as the barrel itself. They contain a single ammunition in their metal chamber at a time, and a whole blood cartridge is required to shoot each bullet. Although less efficient, this lets the rifle shoot much heavier bullets at tremendous speed, proving their impact to be deadly indeed.
