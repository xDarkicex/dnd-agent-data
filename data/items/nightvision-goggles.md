# Nightvision Goggles

**description**: Nightvision Goggles Miscellaneous gear These provide you with the ability to see in dim light and darkness within 30 feet of you as if it were bright light, and within 60 feet of you as if it were in dim light. You cannot discern color, only shades of gray. While wearing these goggles, you have disadvantage on all Wisdom (Perception) checks and Intelligence (Investigation) checks having to do with sight. If you are wearing these goggles in bright light during your turn, you must succeed on a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw to remove them or become blinded until the end of your next turn. Weight : 1
