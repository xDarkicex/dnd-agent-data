# Gauntlets of Heat

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) These gauntlets are capable of generating heat and fire on command. They have 7 charges. While attuned to them, you can use your action to cast: produce flame (1 charge) burning hands (2 charges) heat metal (2 charges) flame arrows (3 charges) The gauntlets regain 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn.
