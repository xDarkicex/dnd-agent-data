# Killer's Crossbow

**description**: Weapon (light crossbow), rare (requires attunement) This weapon functions as a +2 light crossbow. On a command word, even if you are not holding the bow, an effect similar to a mage hand aims and fires the crossbow. The crossbow grants you advantage on attack rolls and deals critical hit damage against a target that hasn’t yet taken an action in combat. Curse. If your alignment is not lawful or evil, the crossbow bestows upon you a curse. When roll you 1 on your attack roll, you take the attack’s damage intended for your target. When the curse takes effect, the bolt curves around to strike you in your back.
