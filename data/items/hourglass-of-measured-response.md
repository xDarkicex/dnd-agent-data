# Hourglass of Measured Response

**description**: Hourglass of Measured Response Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) This locket-sized hourglass hangs from a fine silver chain. When it is flipped over, the cerulean sand moves from the top to the bottom of the glass with exceptional quickness. If you have used your reaction in the current round, you can flip the hourglass over (no action required) to use a second reaction in the same round. After the second and any subsequent time you use the hourglass in a day, you must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, gaining one level of exhaustion on each failed save.
