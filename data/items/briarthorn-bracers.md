# Briarthorn Bracers

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon These leather bracers are inscribed with Elvish runes. While wearing these bracers, you gain a +1 bonus to AC if you are using no shield. In addition, while in a forest, nonmagical difficult terrain costs you no extra movement.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: uncommon

**publisher**: Kobold Press

**book**: Vault of Magic
