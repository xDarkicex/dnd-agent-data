# Crossbow of Healing

**description**: Crossbow of Healing Ranged weapon Price: 25,000 gp This light crossbow has a clean and sterile design. You can pull the handle of the crossbow back to cause it to load a magical and semi-transparent blue bolt. This crossbow functions as a normal ranged weapon, except that it can fire a magical bolt that heals instead of doing damage. Once per day you may fire a healing bolt instead of a regular one. Make an attack as normal, this attack has Advantage against a friendly target. On hit, the crossbow provides 2d6+2 healing to the target. Ranged. Ranged weapons are used to attack enemies at a distance. These weapons can be either shot like a bow and arrow or crossbow, or thrown like a spear or javelin. All ranged weapons use Dexterity for both the Attack Bonus and Damage Bonus. Damage: 1d6
