# Feywrought Scale Mail

**description**: Feywrought Scale Mail Armor (Medium), Rare (Requires Attunement) This colorful, flowery armor was forged in the Feywild and is infused with that plane’s captivating magic. While wearing this armor, you have advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the charmed condition on yourself. This armor has 3 charges. You can use an action to expend a charge to cast the Compulsion spell (save DC 15) from this armor. The armor regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. Scale Mail. This armor consists of a coat and leggings (and perhaps a separate skirt) of leather covered with overlapping pieces of metal, much like the scales of a fish. The suit includes gauntlets. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC : 14 + Dex modifier (max 2) Stealth : Disadvantage Weight : 45
