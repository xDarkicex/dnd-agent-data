# Celestial Compass

**description**: Wondrous Item, Covenant Item (requires attunement) Once a powerful, but mundane, navigational tool, this covenant item gives the bearer with an unerring sense of direction and an uncanny knack for finding what might otherwise have been lost. This elaborate device was crafted by a Sarcosan explorer whose name was lost to time long before the dominion of the Shadow. The solar compass was passed down through generations of intrepid navigators and far-­roaming wanderers, ever guiding them to new horizons or safe havens, wherever their hearts desired. Table B-­5: The Celestial Compass Character Level Covenant Item Properties 1 You always know which way is north and can perfectly recall any path you have traveled. 2-4 You can cast the locate object spell, using the Celestial Compass as a spellcasting focus. Once you cast that spell with the Celestial Compass, you can’t do so again until you finish a long or short rest. 5-10 You can cast the locate creature spell, using the Celestial Compass as a spellcasting focus. Once you cast that spell with the Celestial Compass, you can’t do so again until you finish a long or short rest. 11-16 You can cast the find the path spell, requiring no material component. Once you cast that spell with the Celestial Compass, you can’t do so again until you finish a long rest. 17+ When you cast the find the path spell with the Celestial Compass, the spell does not fail if the destination you choose moves or is unfamiliar to you. The destination must still be a specific place on the same plane of existence as you, but it can be a destination that you have only heard of second hand.
