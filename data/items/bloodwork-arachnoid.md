# Bloodwork Arachnoid

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) The bloodwork arachnoid is one of the latest examples of magic and blood technology and is a very useful automaton to its user. This Tiny automaton resembles a spider with four legs, and has a hexagonal blood capsule on its back. You can use your action to activate this automaton. Upon activation, the automaton waits for your telepathic command (no action required by you). It has a base walking speed of 20 feet, as well as a climbing speed of 20 feet. It has AC 15 and 10 hit points, and immunity to psychic damage. It has a +10 bonus to Wisdom (Perception) checks, and you can hear everything an activated arachnoid can hear as long as it is within 100 feet of you. Also while it’s activated, you can use your action to see through the bloodwork arachnoid’s eyes until the start of your next turn. During this time, you are blind with regard to your own senses. Once activated, the bloodwork arachnoid stays active for 10 minutes and shuts down at the end of this duration. Once activated, the bloodwork arachnoid can’t be activated again until the next dawn.
