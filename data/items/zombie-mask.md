# Zombie Mask

**description**: A zombie mask is crafted from the skin that has been removed from the face of a zombie. The decayed flesh smells putrid, and wearing the mask can easily make you nauseated. While attuned to the mask and wearing it, you gain darkvision 60 ft., and you become invisible to zombies as if you were under the effects of an invisibility spell. When you don the mask and while wearing it, you must make a DC 10 Constitution saving throw against poison every hour or become poisoned for one hour.
