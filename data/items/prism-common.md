# Prism (Common)

**description**: Prism Weapon (Warhammer), Common (Requires Attunement) When within 1000 feet of a planar portal, the weapon flickers with dim light in a 5-foot radius. If the nearest portal is connected to one of the Elemental Planes, this light is colored according to the Duplicate Colors table below (for example, red light for the Plane of Fire). Duplicate Colors Plane Color Damage Air Yellow Lightning Earth Green Poison Fire Red Fire Water Blue Cold Progression Prism progresses when it spends 7 consecutive days in an Elemental Plane (Air, Earth, Fire, or Water) that it hasn’t already spent 7 consecutive days in.
