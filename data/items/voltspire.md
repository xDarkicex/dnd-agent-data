# Voltspire

**description**: Staff, Rare (Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster) This magic staff is crowned with an intricate orb woven with pure silver. Conductive. When a target takes Lightning damage from a spell you cast while you're holding the staff, it takes an extra 1d8 Lightning damage. Supercharge. When you cast a spell of 1st-level or higher that deals Lightning damage, the staff gains 1 charge. The staff can hold up to 3 charges for the following properties and loses unspent charges 1 hour after gaining them: Jolt. At the start of your turn, you can expend 1 charge to gain a jolt of energy. Until the start of your next turn, your speed is doubled, you gain a +2 bonus to AC, you have advantage on Dexterity saving throws, and you can take the Dash, Disengage, Hide, or Use an Object action as a bonus action. Recharge. When you are reduced to 0 hit points but not killed outright, you can expend 1 charge to drop to 1 hit point instead.
