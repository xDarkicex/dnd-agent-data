# Dragon's Teeth

**description**: Dragon's Teeth Wondrous Item, Very Rare Teeth that have been taken from an old and powerful dragon, such as an adult or ancient one. These items are imbued with a mysterious power that resonates with the earth plowed by creations of Hephaestus. As an action you can plant a dragon tooth onto the plowed soil. When you do so 1d4 Dragon Tooth Warriors spawn from the ground. These warriors have a 50% chance of being loyal to you or completely indifferent, but never outright hostile unless threatened. If they are not loyal to you, the DM assumes control of the warriors. The Dragon Tooth Warriors only die after they’ve been destroyed in combat and they cannot recover hit points they have lost by any means.

**properties**:

- Item Rarity: very rare
