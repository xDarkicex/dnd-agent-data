# Privateer Coat

**description**: Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) Component: beast (lion) hair Worn to shield airship crewmates against cold and violent winds, this jacket is equipped with pockets and pouches fit for repair tools and weapons. Some coats are adorned with decorative shoulder pads and pins to distinguish rank and aerial prowess. Built for the Skies. While wearing this coat, you have advantage on ability checks and saving throws made to overcome the effects of cold weather or violent winds. You also have advantage on saving throws against effects that deal cold damage. Finally, you have advantage on Charisma checks against any creature that perceives itself to be below the rank indicated by the coat. When you find this coat, roll a d4 to determine its rank: d4 Rank 1 Petty officer 2 Lieutenant 3 Captain 4 Admiral Repair Adept. While wearing this coat, you can cast the mending cantrip at will. Aerial Messaging. While wearing this coat, you can write a message of up to 25 words in the sky, creating one letter out of clouds every 30 seconds. The letters last for 1 hour on a calm day, 10 minutes in a moderate wind (at least 10 miles per hour), or 1 minute in a strong wind (at least 20 miles per hour). Once this property of the coat has been used, you can’t use it again until the next dawn, unless you are operating aircraft.
