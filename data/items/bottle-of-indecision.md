# Bottle of Indecision

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon The glass surface of this magical bottle forms a face that can move and change expressions, though it typically looks nervous or tentative. Sentience. The bottle is a sentient, lawful neutral object with an Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma of 8. It has hearing and sight out to a range of 30 feet. The bottle communicates by speaking in a high-pitched voice; it can speak and understand Common. Indecisive Contents. As an action, you can fill the empty bottle with water or another non-magical liquid. While holding the filled bottle, you can ask it to transform its contents into one of the potions from the table below. The GM rolls a d20; on a 11 or higher, the bottle transforms its contents into the potion you chose, otherwise it becomes another potion depending on the roll. In either case, neither you nor the bottle itself knows which magical properties the liquid it holds within has before it is consumed.
