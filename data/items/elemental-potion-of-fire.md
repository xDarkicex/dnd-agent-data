# Elemental Potion of Fire

**description**: Potion, Very Rare Flames rage inside this glass bottle. When you drink the contents of this potion, wispy flames wreathe your body for 1 hour, granting you the following benefits: You shed bright light in a 10-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet. You have immunity to Fire damage and resistance to Cold damage. Whenever a creature within 5 feet of you hits you with a melee attack, it takes 1d8 Fire damage. As a bonus action, you can end the effect early to exhale fire in a 60-foot cone. Each creature in the area must make a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw, taking 6d6 Fire damage on a failure, or half as much damage on a success.
