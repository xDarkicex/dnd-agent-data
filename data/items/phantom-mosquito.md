# Phantom Mosquito

**description**: Phantom Mosquito Wondrous item Price: 2,000 gp A tiny wooden box with a hinged lid. It is plain with dark wood, and there is an etching of a mosquito followed by swirling dotted lines. This box doesn’t seem to contain anything. However, when the person who opens the box points to a target, that person then starts hearing the sound of a mosquito flying around their head, though no mosquito actually exists. The target has Disadvantage on any checks to notice someone who is sneaking or moving stealthily, and Disadvantage on any Charisma checks while talking to someone. Only the target can hear the mosquito. This effect lasts 10 minutes or until the target realizes that the effect is an illusion. It can be used once per Long Rest.
