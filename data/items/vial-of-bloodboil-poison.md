# Vial of Bloodboil Poison

**description**: Potion, Very Rare This vial contains 3 doses of bloodboil poison. As an action, you can apply one dose of the poison to a weapon or a piece of ammunition you're holding. The first time you hit a creature with the poisoned weapon or ammunition within the next minute, the creature must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or be affected as by the Dominate Monster spell for 1 hour or until you lose your concentration, as if concentrating on a spell. The target can repeat the saving throw whenever it takes damage, ending the effect on a success.
