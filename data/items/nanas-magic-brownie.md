# Nanas Magic Brownie

**description**: Nana’s Magic Brownie Potion (food), uncommon Weight: none Nana Kindleaf's special brownies are chock full of gnomish granny goodness. Consuming one of Nana's brownies as an action puts you in a mellow, restful state for 2 hours. During this time, you have disadvantage on Perception checks. At the end of the 2 hours, you gain all the benefits of a Long Rest.
