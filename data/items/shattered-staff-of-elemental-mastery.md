# Shattered Staff of Elemental Mastery

**description**: Shattered Staff of Elemental Mastery Staff, Shattered Artifact (5 pieces) Crafted by an ancient planestrider mage who forged alliances with primordial elemental lords, this staff was once among the most powerful artifacts in the world. Yet mortal greed and divine envy saw the staff used against the very elemental lords who worked together to create it. Offended, the lords shattered the staff, fragmenting its power and scattering the pieces across space and time. Now, the elemental lords have long since gone and mortalkind has forgotten the true power of this shattered artifact. Yet, if its fragments were to be brought together once more, its true power might once again awaken… Fragmented Power. The Staff of Elemental Mastery has been shattered into five parts: four elemental fragments and the Shattered Staff that used to hold them. Individually, each item holds some of the artifact’s original power. Each elemental fragment that is reunited with the staff becomes awakened, enhancing the staff’s power and unlocking new abilities. When all four fragments have been recovered, the Staff of Elemental Mastery becomes whole again.
