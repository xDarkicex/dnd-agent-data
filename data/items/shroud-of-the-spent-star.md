# Shroud of The Spent Star

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) This ancient cloth veil can be worn over the eyes, exchanging one’s vision for a sharper mind. The following features are granted by the Shroud of the Spent Star: Sidereal Intellect. While you wear the Shroud of the Spent Star, your Intelligence score becomes 19. If it is already 19 or higher, it is unaffected. Sightless Sight, Speechless Speech. While wearing and attuned to the blindfold, you have blindsight within a range of 60 feet, but are blind beyond this point. You can communicate telepathically with creatures within 60 feet, as long as they can understand at least one language, which you do not need to share. Borne on Thought Alone. You have a fly speed equal to your walking speed, propelled through the air by sheer force of will alone. Ancient tomes state the vor’tai this veil once belonged to perished in combat with their astral master. Though the warrior was victorious, the vast energies released during the astral’s death were enough to rend both master and servant asunder, leaving nought but this cloth in its wake.
