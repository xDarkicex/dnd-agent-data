# Leaden Manacles (very rare variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Component: giant nail The stink of burnt flesh and vapours of boiling sweat waft from the wrists of the shackled spellcaster. As you watch, the creature mutters an incantation and the lead begins to glow once more. The creature lets out a pained wail, its concentration broken, its wrists seared. Arcane Forbiddance. You can use an action to place these manacles on an incapacitated creature. The shackles adjust to fit a creature of Small to Large size. Leaden manacles behave as normal manacles with the following addition: A creature that attempts to cast a spell while wearing the manacles must make a DC 13 ability check using its spellcasting ability. On a failure, the spell’s magic is cancelled; the action to cast the spell is wasted but the creature doesn’t expend a spell slot or a use of the spell. The creature also takes 1d4 fire damage plus an extra 1d4 fire damage per level of the spell on a failed check, or half as much damage on a success. Very rare variant: Increase the DC to 16 and the extra damage to 2d4 per level of the spell. If the creature fails the ability check, it’s also stunned until the end of its next turn and expends the spell slot or a use of the spell.
