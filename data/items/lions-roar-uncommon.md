# Lion's Roar (Uncommon)

**description**: Weapon (Warhammer), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) Common As an action, you can wave this magic weapon in a circle over your head, causing it to emit a lion’s roar that can be heard up to 1 mile away. Uncommon This weapon has 3 charges, and regains 1d3 charges when you finish a long rest. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 charge to cast thunderwave or 2 charges to cast shatter (spell save DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Charisma modifier).
