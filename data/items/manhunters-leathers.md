# Manhunter's Leathers

**description**: Armor (any leather or hide), rare This armor is made of patchwork layers of humanoid leather. While worn, a creature with this armor has advantage on Strength or Charisma (Intimidation) checks, and disadvantage on Charisma (Persuasion) and Charisma (Deception) checks made to convince people of their innocence. Additionally, once per short or long rest, the creature may increase the saving throw DC of a Death Blow that they deliver to a humanoid creature by 2.
