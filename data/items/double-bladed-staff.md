# Double-bladed Staff

**description**: When wielding this weapon, you can use the Attack action with both ends of the staff as if holding two light melee weapons in each hand. Each bladed end deals 1d6 slashing damage (see Two-Weapon Fighting in chapter 9 of the Core Manual ).
