# Feywrought Splint Armor

**description**: Feywrought Splint Armor Armor (Heavy), Rare (Requires Attunement) This colorful, flowery armor was forged in the Feywild and is infused with that plane’s captivating magic. While wearing this armor, you have advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the charmed condition on yourself. This armor has 3 charges. You can use an action to expend a charge to cast the Compulsion spell (save DC 15) from this armor. The armor regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. Splint Armor. This armor is made of narrow vertical strips of metal riveted to a backing of leather that is worn over cloth padding. Flexible chain mail protects the joints. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC : 17 Stealth : Disadvantage Weight : 60
