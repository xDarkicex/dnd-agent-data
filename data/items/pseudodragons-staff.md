# Pseudodragon's Staff

**description**: Staff, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This magic scepter is topped with a dragon figurine, its wings clasped around a blue orb. While holding the staff, you can telepathically communicate simple ideas, emotions, and images with any creature you can see within 100 feet that can understand a language. Summon Pseudodragon. As an action, you can transform the staff's figurine into a pseudodragon that appears in an unoccupied space within 5 feet. The dragon is under your control and acts on your turn in combat. Moreover, you and creatures friendly to you have advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects while within 10 feet of the dragon. The dragon remains for 1 hour, until it is reduced to 0 hit points, or until you dismiss it as a bonus action, upon which it reappears as a figurine at the staff's top. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
