# Lance of the Ostrich Knight

**description**: Lance of the Ostrich Knight Weapon (Lance), Rare This yellow-painted lance is made from simple materials, crafted in a basic, blocky style. Its only adornment is the word “joust” carved into its side. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. As a bonus action, you can speak the command word, “joust,” which summons an ostrich in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of you. The ostrich uses the statistics of a warhorse , except that it has a flying speed of 10 feet. While riding the ostrich, you have advantage on melee weapon attacks against creatures below you and creatures above you have advantage on melee weapon attacks against you. The ostrich disappears after 1 hour or when it is reduced to 0 hit points. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a short or long rest.
