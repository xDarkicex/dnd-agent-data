# Monkshood resin

**description**: Injury This poison is a thick syrup extracted from the petals of monkshood flowers. A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or take 14 (4d6) poison damage. If the saving throw fails by 5 or more, the creature also loses the use of a single limb for 1 minute. The GM determines which limb and the effect of its temporary loss. The creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, regaining the use of its limb on a success.
