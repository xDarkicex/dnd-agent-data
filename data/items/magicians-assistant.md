# Magician’s Assistant

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon Magician’s Assistant has 3 charges. It regains all expended charges after a long rest. While holding this magical orb, you can use your reaction to expend one charge and cast the shield spell. You can use the orb as a spellcasting focus for your spells.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: uncommon
- Requires Attunement: requires attunement by a spellcaster

**publisher**: Brave New Worlds

**book**: TOMB
