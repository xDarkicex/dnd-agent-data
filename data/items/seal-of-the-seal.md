# Seal of the Seal

**description**: Seal of the Seal Wondrous Item, Uncommon (requires attunement) Bestowed to paladins of Nature who undertake quests at sea, a Seal of the Seal gives its wielder an edge against aquatic enemies. While attuned to the Seal of the Seal, you can choose to float on or sink in water and other liquids at will, regardless of weight. You can use your action to attach the seal to a piece of armor or clothing by pressing the seal against it. Thereafter, the seal can’t be removed unless you detach it as another action, your attunement to the seal ends, or the piece of armor or clothing is destroyed. Not even an antimagic field causes it to fall off. Navy Seal. While the Seal of the Seal is attached to a piece of armor or clothing you wear, you have a swimming speed equal to double your walking speed and advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks to swim. You can also hold your breath for a number of minutes equal to 30 x your Constitution modifier (minimum of 30 minutes). Seal the Deal. While attuned to the Seal of the Seal, you can communicate with seals, and they are always friendly to you unless you harm them first.
