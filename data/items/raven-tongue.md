# Raven Tongue

**description**: Raven Tongue Spell catalyst, common Not all magical catalysts are especially difficult to come across — this is simply the tongue cut from the throat of a living raven pickled in alchemical reagents. When you cast the speak with dead spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, you and the corpse can understand each others’ speech even if you don’t share a language.
