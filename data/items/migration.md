# Migration

**description**: Weapon (Dart), Common (Requires Attunement) This dart is carved from polished ebony wood into the shape of a sleek duck. Its tail is decorated with duck-feather fletching, and its beak is tipped with an electrum barb. At the start of your turn, if this magic weapon is not in your possession, it re-materializes in your hand with the soft quack of a duck. Encounter Ideas Clever Trick. Boasting of its magical power, a young street urchin (commoner) named Chadwick sells the dart to an adventurer for 1d10 gp. Shortly after, the dart suddenly disappears from the buyer’s possession. A loud quack! issues forth from the child as they laugh, turn, and run away with both the dart and the gold. Help, It’s Cursed! A young thief (scout) named Evely asks the party to rid her of a “cursed” weapon she stole from a local hero, who is now looking for her. No matter how hard she tries to get rid of it, the dart always finds its way back into Evely’s possession, waiting to incriminate her. Dead Duck. A bird watcher falls out of a tree to his death. The adventurers find the dart in the bird watcher’s pack (as well as a spyglass).
