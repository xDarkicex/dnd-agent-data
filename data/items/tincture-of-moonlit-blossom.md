# Tincture of Moonlit Blossom

**description**: Potion, very rare This potion is steeped using a blossom that grows only in the moonlight. When you drink this potion, your shadow corruption (see Midgard Worldbook ) is reduced by three levels. If you aren’t using the Midgard setting, you gain the effect of the greater restoration spell instead.

- Item Type: Potion
