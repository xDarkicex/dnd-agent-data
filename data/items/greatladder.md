# Greatladder

**description**: Greatladder Weapon (Greatclub), Common This wooden greatclub has ten evenly spaced notches carved into opposite sides of its body. As an action, you can speak the command word to transform this magic weapon into a 15-foot-long wooden ladder. It remains a ladder until you speak the command word again to return it to its greatclub form.
