# Shadowweave Boots

**description**: Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement) The fabric of these magic boots seems to be intertwined with strands of shadow. While you're wearing the boots in dim light or darkness, you gain the following benefits: You have advantage on Dexterity (Acrobatics) checks. Opportunity attacks against you are made with disadvantage. As a bonus action, you can magically teleport to an unoccupied space you can see within 30 feet that is also in dim light or darkness. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
