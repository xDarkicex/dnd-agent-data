# Ring of the Unicorn

**description**: Ring, very rare (requires attunement) This ring is crafted from the horn of a unicorn, willingly given on its death to pass its grace to the intended wearer. Rainbows swirl through the pearlescent material, shining with the light of celestial grace. This ring has three charges, and regains all expended charges at dawn. As an action, you can spend one or more charges and touch a creature to use one of the following abilities: Restore 4d8+8 hit points (1 charge) The target immediately makes a saving throw against a negative effect currently affecting it that a saving throw can end. The creature has advantage on this saving throw. (1 charge) End any condition, curse, or disease afflicting the creature, excluding an Injury or lasting effect (2 charges) Additionally, once per day as an action, you may have the ring make a Wisdom (Medicine) check with a +10 bonus to remove an Injury. When you do so, you may expend a charge to restore hit points as above and gain an additional +4 bonus to the Wisdom (Medicine) check as though healing with a 4th-level spell.
