# Scepter of Light

**description**: Scepter of Light Wondrous item, uncommon This short, gold-plated rod is capped by a clear crystal orb. As an action, you can speak the scepter’s command word and, for the next 4 hours, a 60-foot-radius sphere of bright light spreads out from the orb. Dim light is shed for an additional 60 feet. If any of this light area overlaps with an area of darkness created by a spell of 3rd level or lower, the spell that created the darkness is dispelled. Once used, this property of the scepter can’t be used again until the next dawn.
