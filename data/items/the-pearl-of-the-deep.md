# The Pearl of the Deep

**description**: The Pearl of the Deep Wondrous item Price: 15,000 gp This necklace has a large pearl on the end of a silver chain. While wearing the necklace, the wearer can breathe underwater as if it was air. Additionally, the wearer gains a swim speed equal to their normal Movement Speed, and has Advantage on any checks related to swimming.
