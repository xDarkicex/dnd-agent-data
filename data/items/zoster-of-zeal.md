# Zoster of Zeal

**description**: Zoster of Zeal Wondrous item, very rare This is a wide leather belt covered in bronze plates. You can use an action to don this item, and you gain a +2 bonus to all attack and damage rolls for 1 minute. Once used, the zoster can’t be used again until the next dawn.
