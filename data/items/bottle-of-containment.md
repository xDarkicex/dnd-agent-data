# Bottle of Containment

**description**: Bottle of Containment Wondrous item, uncommon This 6-inch-tall square glass bottle has a funnel-shaped neck and a rubber stopper. It can hold up to 20 units of any liquid, each unit being either a magic potion or up to 1 gallon of ordinary liquid. While in the bottle, each liquid is contained separately from the others. A liquid may be recalled from the bottle by speaking its name and tipping the bottle upside down. If the bottle is smashed, all the contained liquids are expelled.
