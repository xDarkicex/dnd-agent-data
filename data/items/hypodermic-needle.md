# Hypodermic Needle

**description**: This syringe can directly inject a substance into someone’s bloodstream. You can load a liquid, such as a Potion of Healing , into a hypodermic needle as a Bonus Action , then administer it to another creature as an action on Your Turn . If the creature is unwilling, you must make a melee weapon Attack , treating the needle as an Improvised Weapon , to do so.
