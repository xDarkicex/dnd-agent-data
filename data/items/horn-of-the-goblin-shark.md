# Horn of the Goblin Shark

**description**: Horn Of The Goblin Shark Wondrous item, uncommon This magic instrument is fashioned out of the conical, cartilaginous skull of a goblin shark, and usually adorned with a thick leather cord as a strap. Once per day you can use an action to blow the horn underwater to summon two goblin sharks, as per conjure animals . The goblin sharks appear within 30 feet of the horn blower, and can be commanded to attack. The goblin sharks last for 1 hour, or until reduced to 0 hit points.
