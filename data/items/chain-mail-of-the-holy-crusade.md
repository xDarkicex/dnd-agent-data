# Chain Mail of the Holy Crusade

**description**: Chain Mail of the Holy Crusade Armor (medium) , rare (requires attunement) While you wear this golden armor, it sheds dim light in a 5-foot radius around you. If you are within 60 feet of a fiend or undead, the armor instead sheds bright light in a 30-foot radius, and dim light for an additional 30 feet, and you gain a +2 bonus to your AC. The bright light shed by this armor is sunlight. Chain Mail. Made of interlocking metal rings, chain mail includes a layer of quilted fabric worn underneath the mail to prevent chafing and to cushion the impact of blows. The suit includes gauntlets. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC: 16 Stealth: Disadvantage Weight: 55 Minimum Strength: 13
