# Dire Bacon

**description**: Potion, uncommon Cut from a giant boar, this bacon still bears the beast’s ferocity. When you consume this food, you have advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks for 8 hours. In addition, if you take 10 damage or less that would reduce you to 0 hit points, you are reduced to 1 hit point instead. This effect can happen only once during the food’s duration.
