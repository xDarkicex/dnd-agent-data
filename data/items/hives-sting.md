# Hive's Sting

**description**: Weapon (Any Crossbow), Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic crossbow is crafted from gold-plated mahogany. Hive Strike. When you roll a 20 on your attack roll with the crossbow, the attack doesn't deal extra damage; instead, you can immediately make two ranged attacks against the target using the crossbow. Sting. As a bonus action, you can cause a missile loaded in the crossbow to become coated in poison until the end of your next turn. If the missile is used to make a ranged weapon attack and hits a creature, the target must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or until the end of its next turn, it must choose whether it gets an action, a bonus action, or a move on its turn; it gets only one of the three.
