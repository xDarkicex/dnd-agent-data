# Ring of the Seraphim

**description**: Ring, legendary This ring glows with divine light, crafted from a willingly-given seraph’s feather shaft. Once attuned, the ring grants its bearer all of the following benefits: Once per month, when the bearer would die, it is instead healed of all Injuries and restored to its maximum hit points. Once per long rest, when the bearer would become Injured, they may choose to prevent that Injury. Each time the wearer becomes Injured by another creature, that creature takes 2d8 radiant damage per severity of the Injury (i.e. 2d8 for a Setback up to 12d8 for Death)
