# Hellfire Greatsword

**description**: Hellfire Greatsword Melee weapon (martial, greatsword), uncommon This weapon is fashioned from infernal iron and traced with veins of hellfire that shed dim light in a 5-foot-radius. Any humanoid killed by an attack made with this weapon has its soul funneled into the River Styx, where it’s reborn instantly as a lemure devil (described in the Monste r Manual ). Heavy. Small creatures have disadvantage on attack rolls with heavy weapons. A heavy weapon’s size and bulk make it too large for a Small creature to use effectively. Two-­Handed. This weapon requires two hands when you attack with it. Damage: 2d6 Damage Type: slashing Properties: Heavy, Two-Handed Weight: 6
