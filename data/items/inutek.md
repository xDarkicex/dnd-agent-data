# Inutek

**description**: This weapon consists of a rope that is knotted at one end, with two shorter ropes that form a sort of open lasso. Heavy stones, or sometimes metal weights, are tied to the ends of the shorter ropes, giving them weight and stability in the air. This weapon was created by the gnomes of the Eren River Valley. Gnome guardians especially love to use these weapons to bring their enemies “down to size.” Inuteks use the weapon profile of a net.
