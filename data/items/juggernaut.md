# Juggernaut

**description**: Journeyman armor property Components: Masterwork suit of exotic heavy armor This suit of armor is fitted with massive plates and a heavy, reinforced helmet. While wearing this armor, you count as an obstacle providing three-quarters cover, instead of half cover. You must have a Strength score of 16 or higher to wear this armor.
