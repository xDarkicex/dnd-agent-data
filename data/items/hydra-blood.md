# Hydra Blood

**description**: Hydra Blood Wondrous Item, Very Rare Blood taken from the Lernean Hydra, known for its horrifying poisonous effect which was used as an assasination tool against the demigod Heracles, bringing him to near death right before he was reborn as a god who rose to Olympus. As an action you can coat a weapon or 10 pieces of ammunition that deal slashing or piercing damage with this poison for 10 minutes. The poison vial will be empty after this application. The next attack made with that weapon or piece of ammunition will subject the target to the effect of the Hydra’s Blood. A creature subjected to this poison must make a DC 18 Constitution saving throw, taking 45 (10d8) poison damage and becoming poisoned for 1 hour on a failed save, or half as much damage and preventing the poison’s effect on a successful one. While poisoned in this way the target continuously takes 9 (2d8) poison damage at the start of each of their turns. The target can repeat the Constitution saving throw at the end of each of their turns.
