# Trimauler (Legendary)

**description**: Trimauler Weapon (Trident), Legendary (Requires Attunement) You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Trimauler’s Touch . The trident has 3 charges. As an action, you can expend a charge to restore 5d4 + 5 hit points to all creatures of your choice within 30 feet of you. The trident regains all expended charges when you finish a long rest.
