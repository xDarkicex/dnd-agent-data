# Gnawing Net

**description**: Gnawing Net Weapon (Net), Legendary (Requires Attunement) This net incorporates preserved mimic sinew and roper tendril into its strands. The fibers extrude teeth when they meet flesh. You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls made with this magic weapon. Its normal range is 10 feet, and its long range is 25 feet. The net can affect formless creatures and creatures that are Huge or smaller. Additionally, being within 5 feet of a hostile creature doesn’t impose disadvantage on your ranged attack rolls with this net against an adjacent creature. The gnawing net deals 2d4 + 4 piercing damage to a creature on a hit. While restraining a creature, the net deals 1d4 + 2 piercing damage at the start of each of the creature’s turns. A creature can use its action to make a DC 15 Strength check, freeing itself or another creature within reach on a success. Dealing 20 slashing damage to the net (AC 15) also frees the creature. The net magically repairs itself when you finish a long rest.
