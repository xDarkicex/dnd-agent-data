# Consecrated Crescent

**description**: Weapon (Greataxe), Rare (Requires Attunement) This magic greataxe is fashioned from steel and mithral and is wrapped with a strip of parchment containing a sacred prayer. You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with the axe. Anoint. As a bonus action, you can speak the sacred prayer wrapped around the axe and anoint yourself for 1 minute. While anointed, you gain the following benefits: When you hit a target with an attack roll using the axe, the target takes an extra 1d8 Radiant damage. You can teleport up to 10 feet to an unoccupied space you can see immediately before or after each attack you make with the axe. Once used, you can’t use this feature again until you finish a long rest, unless you are also attuned to the consecrated aegis , in which case you can use it again once you finish a short or long rest.
