# Moraks Staff

**description**: MORAK´S STAFF Staff, rare (requires attunement by a spellcaster) Morak´s Gaze. When a creature within 15ft of you attacks you, they must make a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or they become frightened by you. Once they succeed in this save, they are immune to this effect for the next 24 hours. Morak´s Rage. While holding the staff, you have resistance to fire damage. Morak´s Fury. As an action, you can cast a wave of magic in a 60ft semi sphere around you. Every creature in the area must make a DC WISDOM 14 save or receive 13 (2d12) necrotic damage. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a short or long rest. Morak´s Curse. The staff is cursed and must consume the life of a living creature each day or risk being destroyed. At the start of each day, the creature attuned to this staff can feed its life force to the staff - they suffer a random effect from this list: d6 Curses 1 You suffer 19 (3d12) necrotic damage, or half of your maximum hp in necrotic damage - whichever is highest 2 You suffer 11 (2d10) necrotic damage 3 You suffer 9 (2d8) necrotic damage 4 You suffer 2 (1d4) necrotic damage 5 Nothing occurs 6 Nothing occurs The creature attuned to this staff can press the staff against a willing or unconscious living creature with at least 4 maximum hp and have them suffer the effects instead. If the attuned creature chooses not to feed the staff or if the chosen creature is immune to necrotic damage, the staff is destroyed, disintegrates and turns to dust.
