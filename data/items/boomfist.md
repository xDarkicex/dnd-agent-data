# Boomfist

**description**: Boomfist Melee weapon Cost : 1,000 gp Damage : Unarmed + 1d12 lightning Weight : 5 lb. Properties : Light This heavy, articulated gauntlet of orichalcum and lead is laced with wires that terminate in copper knuckle plates. Just behind the hand of the gauntlet is a housing for a vril battery. You cannot wield a weapon in the same hand, and you automatically fail any Dexterity checks that require the use of your hand. Making a successful unarmed attack with a boomfist allows you to spend 1 charge from the installed vril battery to add 1d12 lightning damage to your normal unarmed damage. You must then wait for 1 round to do this again so that the boomfist can recharge.
