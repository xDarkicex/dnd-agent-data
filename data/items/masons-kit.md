# Mason's Kit

**description**: Mason's Kit Adventuring gear (artisan's tools) A mason’s kit is usually an assortment of chisels (not suitable as weapons), chalk, hammers, and measuring implements. Most Rokugani masonry is “dry stone,” meaning that there is no mortar binding the stones together, and the stones rely on interlocking friction to remain intact. Even so, some kits may include ingredients for mortar, and those that do also include trowels.
