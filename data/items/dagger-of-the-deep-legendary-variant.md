# Dagger of the Deep (legendary variant)

**description**: Weapon (dagger), uncommon Component: monstrosity bone A green jewel sits like an eye in the handle of this dagger, giving life to the carved bone cuttlefish that forms its grip. Ichorous Blight. While wielding this dagger, you can use a bonus action to imbue its next strike with an inky-black ichor that gets injected into its victim. The next time you hit a creature with the dagger before the end of your next turn, it must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw. On a failure, the creature takes an additional 2d6 necrotic damage and is blinded for 1 minute. On a success, it takes half as much damage and isn’t blinded. A creature can repeat this saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. Once this property has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn. Rare variant: You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Increase the DC to 15 and the damage to 4d6. Very rare variant: You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Increase the DC to 16 and the damage to 6d6. Legendary variant: You gain a +3 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Increase the DC to 17 and the damage to 8d6.
