# Bloodbone Kris

**description**: Bloodbone Kris Weapon (Dagger), Common (Requires Attunement) This magic dagger is crafted from bone and engraved with blood-smeared glyphs. Whisper of Blood. When you draw blood from a creature using the dagger, such as by hitting it with an attack, you can glean a small piece of information about the target. You can ask the GM one question, such as "does the creature have more than half its maximum hit points left,” "is the creature resistant to Fire damage,” or "is the creature a Humanoid.” If the answer can be found by looking at the creature's statistics, the GM answers it with a yes or no. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
