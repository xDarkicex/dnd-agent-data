# Bag of Prodigious Props

**description**: Bag of Prodigious Props Wondrous Item, Very Rare Created by a legendary drag queen bard, this outrageously diverse and exotic collection has virtually every prop you will ever need to radically transform your appearance. You can use your action to reach into the bag and pull out any prop you are looking for that can alter your appearance, including claws, ears, fangs, feathers, fins, fur, horns, hooves, scales, tails, tentacles, wings, and other distinguishing characteristics at the gamemaster’s discretion. You can spend 1 minute to apply a prop directly on your body. Thereafter, the prop can’t be removed unless you detach it as an action or you die. Not even an antimagic field causes it to fall off. Prolific Props. You can wear a number of prodigious props at once equal to your Constitution modifier (minimum of one). Realness. Unless you purposefully wear it so its nature is obvious, a prop from the Bag of Prodigious Props looks perfectly real but does not grant you additional abilities. You can’t use prop wings to fly, for example. The props also can’t make you appear as a creature of a different size than you, and you cannot use them to increase the number of functional arms, legs, or heads you have. Only a wish spell can reveal the true nature of the prop while worn. The prop envelops whoever wears it in a faint aura of transmutation magic if targeted by detect magic .
