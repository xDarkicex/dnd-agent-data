# Jawbone Machete

**description**: Weapon (scimitar), uncommon Component: pouch of beast (dinosaur) teeth A tool for travel and warfare, this machete possesses a homemade quality, reminiscent of the deepest parts of the jungle. Rough-cut bone, teeth, and feathers are bonded with tree resin and magic into a versatile instrument, rarely far from the side of its creator. Jungle Dweller. You ignore difficult terrain caused by thick vegetation. Landscaper. You have perfected the art of navigating through dense foliage and vines, and know exactly where to strike with your machete to make quick work of any plant. You have advantage on attack rolls made with this weapon against plants and on Tracking checks made to navigate in jungle terrain.
