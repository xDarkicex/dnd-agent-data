# Monster and Spawn Reunion +2

**description**: Monster and Spawn Reunion +2 Spell catalyst, rare (+2) By replacing the normal material components, mundane eggshell and snakeskin, with the eggshell and snakeskin of a cockatrice, the bigby’s hand spell is greatly enhanced. When you cast the bigby’s hand spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, the summoned hand’s strength increases by an amount determined by the rarity of the spell catalyst.
