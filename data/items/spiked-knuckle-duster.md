# Spiked knuckle duster

**description**: Knuckle Dusters A knuckle duster is a simple melee weapon, usually made of metal or another hard material, worn around the knuckles of one hand. They deal 1d4 bludgeoning damage on a hit and have the Attached, Light, and Pugilist properties. Variant: Spiked Knuckle Dusters. This variant on the knuckleduster has the Adaptable (piercing) property.
