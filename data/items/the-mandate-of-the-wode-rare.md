# The Mandate of the Wode (Rare)

**description**: Weapon (Mace), Rare (Requires Attunement by a Druid) This magic weapon has 5 charges. It regains 1d4 expended charges when you finish a long rest, or all charges if you are in a forest. Common Speak with Beasts. While attuned to this magic weapon, you can speak its command word, granting you the ability to comprehend and verbally communicate with Beasts for 1 minute. During this time, you also have advantage on Persuasion checks involving Beasts whose CR is less than your level. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. Uncommon While holding this weapon, you can use a bonus action to expend 1 charge to cast the command spell, targeting a Beast. The spell is considered a druid spell for you and uses your druid spell save DC. Rare While holding this weapon, you can use an action to expend 1 or more charges to summon Beasts to aid you. You can summon a number of Beasts up to your proficiency bonus, and the total CR of the Beasts you summon must be less than or equal to the number of charges expended. Each Beast appears in an unoccupied space you can see within 30 feet. The Beasts are allies to you and your companions. In combat, they share your initiative count, but take their turns immediately after yours. They obey your verbal commands (no action required by you). If you don’t issue any, they take the Dodge action and use their move to avoid danger. Alternatively, when you use your Wild Shape, you can use a reaction to expend charges to activate this ability. If you summon any of the same type of Beast that you wild shaped into, hostile creatures must succeed on a Wisdom (Perception) check against your spell save DC to tell which Beast you are.
