# Ring of Water

**description**: Ring of Water Wondrous item, legendary A band of blue coral that constantly emits the soft sound of waves. The band feels wet to the touch. When worn, the wearer may spend an action to cause a torrent of rainfall to immediately form in a 10-foot radius around the user, causing approximately 30 gallons of water to fall over the course of the next 30 seconds. If the Ring of Water is worn during a long rest, the wearer becomes attuned. The empowerment word can only be used once per long rest. While attuned, the wearer benefits from the following effects: If the wearer speaks the empowerment word “Cycles” as they active the ring, any creature within 10 feet of the wearer regains 2d4 hit points at the start of each of their turns as long as they are in contact with the rain. Undead creatures who start their turn within 10 feet of the wearer suffer 2d4 radiant damage instead. The wearer can breathe underwater. When an effect from any source besides this ring would cause the wearer to gain hit points, they gain an additional 3 hit points.
