# Invertebreaker

**description**: Weapon (mace), rare 1d10 bludgeoning - two-handed, reach You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made using this magic weapon, and you score a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20. Whenever you successfully land a critical hit with Invertebreaker, the AC of the target is permanently reduced by 1 up to a maximum of -3.
