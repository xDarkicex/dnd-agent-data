# Ghostfinger Extract

**description**: Posion (Ingested) A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or become poisoned for 1 hour. While poisoned in this way, the target can't cast spells with somatic components, and any weapons or items they wield are treated as if they had the two-handed property.
