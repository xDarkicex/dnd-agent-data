# Black Heart (Legendary)

**description**: Black Heart Weapon (Longsword), Legendary (Requires Attunement) You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While attuned to this weapon, your Constitution score increases by 2 and your maximum for that score increases to 25. You gain darkvision out to 120 feet. As an action, you can grant another creature this darkvision for a number of hours equal to your proficiency bonus, losing it yourself when you do so. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a short or long rest. Amethyst Wings. As a bonus action, you can speak a command word, causing a pair of purple dragon wings to sprout from your back. You gain a flying speed of 30 feet for 1 minute, or until you speak the command word again to dismiss the wings (no action required). Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. Violet Outburst. As an action, you can exhale a 30-foot cone of purple energy. Each creature in the area must make a DC 19 Strength saving throw, taking 6d8 force damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Once used, this property can’t be used again until you finish a long rest. Spells. This sword has 5 charges. While holding the sword, you can cast vicious mockery or use an action to expend some of its charges to cast one of the following spells (save DC 19): charm person (1 charge), command (1 charge), compulsion (4 charges), dominate person (5 charges), hold monster (5 charges), hold person (2 charges), or phantasmal killer (4 charges). The sword regains 1d4 + 1 expended charges when you finish a long rest. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20. On a 1, the crystal in the center cracks, the sword loses all magical properties, and Teindrian ( ancient black dragon ) is released into the world.
