# Staff of Ancestry

**description**: Staff, rare While holding this staff, you can use your action to dip it into a pinch of blood to have it cast the [Blood Taste] spell by consuming the blood as the material component of the spell. You can’t use this property of the staff again until dawn. The staff has a white transparent crystal on top, which changes color if two creatures hold it at the same time. This change occurs as follows: Crimson. If the creatures are first-degree blood relatives, siblings, parents, and children, the crystal turns crimson. Vampire parents and their children, and vampire siblings are considered immediate family as well. Scarlet. If the creatures are second-degree blood relatives; grandparent, grandchild, aunt, nephew, etc., the crystal turns scarlet. Vampire parents and their vampire grandchildren, vampire children and their aunts, uncles etc. are also considered second-degree blood relatives. Black. If the creatures share a bloodline, but are not first or second degree relatives, the crystal turns black. No Change. If the creatures are not related by blood, the crystal does not change color. This staff can be wielded as a magic quarterstaff that grants a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with it.
