# String of Warding

**description**: String of Warding Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) This piece of strong, silvery twine is roughly 30 feet long and has the ends tied together. When placed on the ground in a circular shape, you can use an action to speak the command word and a 20-foot-tall cylinder of magical energy rises from the string. Choose one of the following types of creatures: celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, or undead. Creatures of the affected type can’t willingly enter or leave the cylinder by nonmagical means. If the creature tries to use teleportation or interplanar travel to do so, it must first succeed on a DC 15 Charisma saving throw. The magical cylinder lasts for 1 hour or until you speak the command word again. Once used, the string can’t be used again until the next dawn.
