# Flutterby Rod

**description**: Rod, uncommon This rod has 7 charges and regains 1d6 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. While holding the rod, you can use it to cast the light cantrip targeting the rod. As an action, you can expend a charge to shoot a shimmering, green, butterfly-shaped flutterbolt at a target within 60 feet. Make a ranged spell attack with a +5 attack bonus. On a hit, the target takes 1d4 + 1 force damage and is outlined in sparkling green butterflies. The next attack roll made against this target before the end of your next turn has advantage, and the target can’t benefit from being invisible until the end of your next turn.
