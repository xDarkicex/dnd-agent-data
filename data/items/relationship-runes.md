# Relationship Runes

**description**: Relationship Runes Wondrous item, rare This is a set of two blank rune stones, paired to one another. Whatever happens to one stone happens to the other, as long as they are on the same plane of existence. They are used by raiding parties to alert their home ports of how they fare. Many a clan speak of the “breaking of the stone”, to refer to a lost expedition.
