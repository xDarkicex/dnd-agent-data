# Rod of the Sovereigns' Servant

**description**: Rod of the Sovereigns’ Servant Rod, very rare (requires attunement by a spellcaster) While holding this rod of blackened bone, you can cast the speak with dead spell without expending a spell slot. Additionally, while you hold the rod, you can cast the following spells using your spell save DC: animate dead as a 5th-level spell, bestow curse , geas , and raise dead . Once the rod has been used to cast one of these four spells, it can’t be used to cast that spell again until the next dusk.
