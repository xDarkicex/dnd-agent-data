# Celestial Blinders

**description**: Armor of the Defiant Dawn Armor (medium or heavy, but not hide), Very Rare (requires attunement) First worn by an adventurer who dared defy Nelchior, the warring god of night and dark secrets, this armor has been infused with motes of the Eternal Light that allow its wearer to see the unseen and bring light to the darkest of places. While you wear this armor, you can use an action to cause it to shed bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for an additional 30 feet. Invisible creatures and objects are visible as long as they are in the armor’s bright light. You can use a bonus action to end this effect. Luminous Retribution. This armor has 3 charges, and it regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. When you are hit with an attack while wearing this armor, you can use your reaction and spend 1 charge to cause the armor to flash with blinding light in a 15-foot radius. Any creature in this light excluding you must make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 4d8 radiant damage and is blinded until the end of your next turn. On a successful save, it takes half as much damage and isn’t blinded by this effect. Undead creatures have disadvantage on this saving throw. Optional Rule: Celestial Blinders At the gamemaster’s discretion, player characters who obtain an Armor of the Defiant Dawn or an Armor of the Midnight Sun may also be able to obtain matching Celestial Blinders for their mounts. Celestial Blinders is an uncommon magic item magically paired to a specific Armor of the Defiant Dawn or Armor of the Midnight Sun and makes a mount that wears it immune to the Luminous Retribution and Dawn’s Fury effects of the armor they are paired with while the wearer of the armor rides the mount.
