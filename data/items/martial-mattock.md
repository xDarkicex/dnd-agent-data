# Martial Mattock

**description**: Melee weapon (martial) The mattock is a glorious peasant tool, widespread and skillfully used by peasants from all over the Kingdom and beyond. Together with the Sword, it constitutes one of the symbols of the Kingdom. The "Martial" version of it is more expensive, and expressly created to alternate the work in the fields with the defense of the granaries against bandits, wolves, and marauders.
