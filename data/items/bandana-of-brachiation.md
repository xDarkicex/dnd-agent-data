# Bandana of Brachiation

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) While wearing this bright yellow bandana, you have a climbing speed of 30 feet, and you gain a +5 bonus to Strength (Athletics) and Dexterity (Acrobatics) checks to jump over obstacles, to land on your feet, and to land safely on a breakable or unstable surface, such as a tree branch or rotting wooden rafters.

**properties**:
- Category: Items
- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: uncommon
- Requires Attunement: requires attunement
