# Armor of Blending

**description**: Armor (light, medium, or heavy), rare (requires attunement) While wearing this armor, you gain a +1 bonus to AC. You can use a bonus action to speak the armor's command word and cause the armor to assume the shape and form of any set of clothes. The armor maintains its armor class while in this form. You can return the armor to its original shape as a bonus action. A detect magic spell can't reveal its true nature but truesight can.
