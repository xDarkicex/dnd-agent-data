# Plate of the Magehunter Sergeant Breastplate

**description**: Armour (breastplate, half plate, or plate), very rare (requires attunement) Component: monstrosity (sphinx) pelt Made of a dark grey, lead-rich metal, this armour seems especially dull around magical sources of light. Absorption. While wearing this armour, when you are subjected to a magical effect that allows you to make a saving throw to take only half damage, you instead take no damage on a success and only half as much damage on a failure. Once this property has triggered three times, it can’t trigger again until the next dawn. Spellbreak. While wearing this armour, you have resistance to force damage. Acid, cold, fire, lightning, and thunder damage you take from magical sources, as well as magical healing you receive, is reduced by 3. In addition, whenever you are hit by a magical melee weapon, the attacker must roll a d20. On a 10 or lower, the weapon’s magic is suppressed for 1 hour, causing it to temporarily lose its magical effects immediately after the attack. Uncommon variant, Plate of the Magehunter Sergeant: Once the Absorption property has triggered, it can’t trigger again until the next dawn. Reduce the magical damage and healing reduction to 2. Remove the resistance to force damage. The Spellbreak property can’t be used to suppress a magical weapon.
