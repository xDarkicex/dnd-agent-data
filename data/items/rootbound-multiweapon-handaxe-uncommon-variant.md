# Rootbound Multiweapon Handaxe (uncommon variant)

**description**: Weapon (any melee), common (requires attunement) Component: bundle of plant roots The Plane of Fey is an innately magical domain teeming with flora that would enthuse even the most elitist horticulturist. In the aftermath of a battle, once all the useful knick knacks have been salvaged, the plants move in to feast on the rich, blood-soaked earth, sometimes waiting only a matter of days. Seeking roots can bind up broken weapons into clumps that, when properly harvested, can shift into any configuration. Reroot. If you find a rootbound multiweapon, roll a d3 and consult the table below to determine which type of weapon it is.
