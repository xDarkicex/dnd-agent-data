# Casper's Compact

**description**: Casper's Compact Wondrous item Price: 2,000 gp This small makeup compact has a brush and tiny mirror inside. Everything you need to apply your makeup on the go! Casper’s Compact allows the user to apply invisibility makeup to themself as as a Main Action. It may be used once per Long Rest. This invisibility lasts up to 10 minutes. During this time the target and all of their clothing and equipment become completely invisible. The invisibility ends when the spell expires or as soon as the user takes damage, makes an attack, or casts a spell. After the user becomes visible again, glowing lines appear on the wearer’s face, fading to one of the following makeup effects which cannot be removed or covered up, even by magic, for 8 hours. Roll a 1d6 to determine effect: Gothic style dark eyes, eyeliner, and black lips. Clown makeup with white skin, red outlined lips, a large red nose and teardrop marks on cheeks Garish bright blue sparkly eyeshadow, pink lips, and red cheeks.
