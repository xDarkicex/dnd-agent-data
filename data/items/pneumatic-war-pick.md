# Pneumatic war pick

**description**: This clockwork war pick features a spiked head on a pneumatic coil. Originally designed for demolition, the pneumatic war pick found great use in dwarven tunnels and narrow urban streets alike. When you hit an object or structure with this weapon, double the damage dice on the attack.
