# Charged Crossbow

**description**: Charged Crossbow Weapon (heavy crossbow), uncommon Instead of physical projectiles, a charged crossbow fires bolts of energy contained in the bulbs fastened to its frame. This crossbow does not have the loading property, but you must replace the spent energy bulbs after firing the crossbow twenty times, which requires 10 minutes and a set of tinker’s tools to accomplish. When you make a successful attack with this crossbow, roll on the table below to determine the type of damage type the attack deals. Charged Crossbow Damage d12 Damage Type 1–2 Poison 3–4 Fire 5–6 Cold 7–8 Acid 9–10 Lightning 11 Force 12 Psychic
