# Cape of the Wind Rider

**description**: Cape of the Wind Rider Wondrous Item, rare (requires attunement) Weight: 2 lbs. While wearing this cape, you can use an action to speak its command word. You gain a flying speed of 60 feet for 1 hour or until you repeat the command word as an action. You can use this feature again in 1d12 hours. You can also use one hand to raise the cape as a shield, increasing your Armor Class by 2. A human paladin named Kalial Kendis discovered this magic cape in the wreckage of a crashed airship. He used it to patrol Gnompentopolis, fighting air pirates until the day he collided with a wyvern.
