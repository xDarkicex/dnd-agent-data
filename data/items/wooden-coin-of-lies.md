# Wooden Coin of Lies

**description**: Wondrous item, very rare (requires attunement) While attuned to this coin, you are constantly under the effect of the glibness spell. Curse. This coin is cursed; attuning to it extends its curse to you. You can only break attunement to the coin with the remove curse spell or similar magic. While attuned to the coin, you can’t deliberately speak the truth. You can be evasive in your wording, lie by omission, or speak half-truths, as long as your words remain within the boundaries of deception.
