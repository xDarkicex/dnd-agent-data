# Wolf Whistle

**description**: Wolf Whistle Wondrous Item, uncommon Weight: none When you blow this silver whistle, you summon a fey spirit that takes the form of a Dire Wolf . The summoned dire wolf is friendly to you and your companions. It understands your languages and obeys your spoken commands, taking its action after your turn. If you issue no commands, the creature defends itself but takes no other actions. The wolf disappears after three hours or when it drops to 0 hit points. Once the whistle has been used, it can't be used again until the next dawn.
