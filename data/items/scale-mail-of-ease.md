# Scale Mail of Ease

**description**: The illithid around here tell me they prefer their meals with a crunchy coating. — Xanathar Scale Mail of Ease Armor (medium), uncommon While the magic of this armor doesn't provide any additional protection, scale mail of ease can be slept in with no detrimental effects. Scale Mail. This armor consists of a coat and leggings (and perhaps a separate skirt) of leather covered with overlapping pieces of metal, much like the scales of a fish. The suit includes gauntlets. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC : 14 + Dex modifier (max 2) Stealth : Disadvantage Weight : 45
