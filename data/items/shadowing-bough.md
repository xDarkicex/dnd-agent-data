# Shadowing Bough

**description**: Shadowing Bough Spell catalyst, rare A leafy branch from a tree that has provided shade for at least 100 years. When you cast the pass without trace spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, the spell lasts for its full duration without requiring you to maintain concentration.

- Item Type: Spell catalyst
