# Earth Fragment

**description**: Earth Fragment Wondrous item, Rare (requires attunement) While attuned to this fragment, you have resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage; and you can move across difficult terrain made of earth or stone without expending extra movement. Additionally, you can speak, read, and write Terran, and you can cast the earth mold cantrip at will. Awakened Earth Fragment. Attuning to the Earth Fragment while also attuned to the Shattered Staff awakens the fragment and it magically merges with the staff, becoming one item that takes up a single attunement slot. After the Earth Fragment has been awakened, the following spells are added to the staff’s spell list: catapult object (1 charge), move earth (6 charges), rock transmutation (5 charges), stone shape (4 charges), and wall of stone (5 charges).
