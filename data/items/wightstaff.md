# Wightstaff

**description**: Weapon (quarterstaff), rare (requires attunement) This staff is carved in the Vikmordere style, eliciting spirits from the wood’s natural patterns. Images of death—skulls, bones, and ravens—adorn the length of a wightstaff. The staff functions as a holy symbol for a servant of a deity associated with death or necromancy. You gain a +1 to attack and damage rolls made with this weapon. Death Spirit. If the staff is on your person when you cast a spell that deals necrotic damage (such as inflict wounds), the staff gains a charge. The staff holds up to 3 charges. While holding the staff, you can expend 3 charges to cast animate dead, 2 charges to cast blindness/deafness, and 1 charge to cast false life.
