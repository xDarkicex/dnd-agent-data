# Wings of the Hellspawn (very rare variant)

**description**: Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement) Component: fiend skin The membranes of these wings are almost transparent in bright daylight, the bones appearing like fleshless claws. Come night or thick shadow, the membranes coalesce into blood-red curtains of opaque, ragged flesh. As you attune to this item, bands of sinew wrap around your form, binding you to these grotesque bone-and-membrane wings. Until your attunement to the wings ends, you can’t be parted from them, willingly or otherwise. Fiendish Flight. When you fall and aren’t incapacitated, you can subtract up to 100 feet from the fall when calculating falling damage, and you can move up to 2 feet horizontally for every 1 foot you descend. Devil’s Cocoon. As an action, you can cause these wings to wrap around you, forming a fleshy carapace. You gain 1d4 + 4 temporary hit points that last for 1 hour. If you use the Fiendish Flight property, you lose any temporary hit points you have from this property. Once this property of the wings has been used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn. Rare variant: Increase the number of temporary hit points gained to 3d4 + 4. The Fiendish Flight property has the following additional benefits: When you jump, you can choose to propel yourself with your wings (no action required); you no longer require a running start to jump up to your maximum distance. Your jump distance is doubled. Very rare variant: Increase the number of temporary hit points gained to 5d4 + 4. The Fiendish Flight property grants you a flying speed equal to your walking speed, and has the following additional benefits: When you jump, you can choose to propel yourself with your wings (no action required); you don’t require a running start to jump up to your maximum distance. Your jump distance is doubled.
