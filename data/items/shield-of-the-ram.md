# Shield of the Ram

**description**: Shield of the Ram Shield Price: 7,000 gp This metallic shield has a symbol of a ram’s head etched into the front of it. Every once in a while the ram seems like it is moving out of the corner of your eye. Once per Short Rest the wielder of this shield can utter a word and use the shield as a battering ram. Against a door or other stationary object this gives the wielder a +5 bonus to any Strength check to bash the door in, or otherwise push the object. It may be used against a creature in place as a weapon attack. Make an Attack Roll against the creature with a +5 Attack Bonus. On a successful hit the creature takes 1d6 damage and is pushed 10 feet backwards. Melee. Melee Weapons are used to attack enemies up close in hand-to-hand combat.
