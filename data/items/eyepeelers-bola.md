# Eyepeeler's Bola

**description**: Weapon (Bola), Uncommon This magical bola consists of rope and lidless eyeballs encased in weighted bronze spheres. The bola is a simple, ranged weapon that has the thrown property and a range of 30/120 feet. On a hit, the bola deals 1d4 Bludgeoning damage and, if the target is a creature and has moved 10 or more feet since the start of its last turn, the creature must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or fall Prone. When you hit a creature with the bola, it wraps itself around the creature, causing one of the following effects of your choice: Capture. The creature is Grappled. Reveal. You have advantage on attack rolls against the creature and it gains no benefits from being Invisible. Watch. The creature has disadvantage on attack rolls against you and you have advantage on Dexterity saving throws you make against its spells and abilities. A creature can free itself or another creature within 5 feet of it from the bola by using an action and succeed on a DC 13 Strength (Athletics) check. Otherwise, the effect ends after 1 minute and the bola falls free from the target.
