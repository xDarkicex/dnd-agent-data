# Hellish Grappler (Common)

**description**: Hellish Grappler Weapon (Mace), Common (Requires Attunement) If you tie a rope to the end of this weapon, it functions as a grappling hook. You have advantage on checks made to use it as such. Progression The Hellish Grappler progresses as the creature who is attuned to it progresses. Whenever the creature’s proficiency bonus increases, the weapon’s rarity also increases.
