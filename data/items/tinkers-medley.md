# Tinker's Medley

**description**: Tinker’s Medley Weapon (Light Hammer), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) A small, well-worn hammer whose oak handle is covered in Gnomish words—mostly half-formed ideas for inventions, melting points for metal, and other artisan’s notes. One is a command word. You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Gnomes gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls. While attuned to this weapon, you have proficiency with smith’s tools. If you are already proficient in smith’s tools, you gain expertise instead, which means your proficiency bonus is doubled for any check you make using the tools. Mending. When you strike a broken object with this weapon and speak the command word written on the shaft, you cast mending on it. You can use this property three times, and regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest.
