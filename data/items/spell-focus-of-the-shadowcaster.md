# Spell Focus of the Shadowcaster

**description**: This item that bears the raw energies of the Shadowrealm can be utilized to heighten the effects of illusion spells. The spell focus can function as a material component of up to 100 gold pieces in value that doesn’t need to be expended. The spell focus of the shadowcaster has 3 charges. While attuned to it, you can expend 1 charge to make an illusion spell you cast harder to disbelieve, granting disadvantage on Intelligence (Investigation) skill checks to disbelieve the illusion. Additionally, you can expend 1 charge to add +1 DC to an illusion spell you cast. You can expend multiple charges to use both effects on your spell. The spell focus of the shadowcaster regains all expended charges at dawn.
