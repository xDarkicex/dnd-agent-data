# Vanarion the Harp

**description**: Vanarion the Harp Wondrous Item, Artifact (requires attunement by a non-evil bard) This exquisitely crafted harp houses the soul of legendary dark elf bard Vanarion who sought to spread love and beauty to the Overworld. Persecuted by evil forces, Vanarion had to channel his essence into his unbreakable harp to avoid certain death. A creature that attempts to play Vanarion without his consent must succeed on a DC 20 Wisdom saving throw or take 3d8 psychic damage. Magic Repertoire. You can use an action to play Vanarion and cast one of the spells from Vanarion’s Spell List below. Once Vanarion has been used to cast a spell, he can’t be used to cast that spell again until the next dawn. The spells use your spellcasting ability and spell save DC. Vanarion maintains concentration on any spells cast from his spell list that require concentration so that you don’t have to. Vanarion’s Spell List. Cure wounds (5th level), darkness , faerie fire , fly , invisibility , irresistible dance , levitate , protection from evil and good , telekinesis . Sentience. Vanarion is a sentient chaotic good harp with an Intelligence of 14, a Wisdom of 8, and a Charisma of 20. He has hearing and darkvision out to a range of 120 feet. He can communicate telepathically with any creature he can see within 60 feet of him. His telepathic messages ring melodic and pleasant. Vanarion knows Common, Draconic, Dwarvish, and Elvish. Personality. Vanarion honestly believes he was the most talented bard that has ever existed. In his new life as an unbreakable harp, he seeks to aid and guide bards who would fight to make the world a better place. Optional Rule: Signature Songs, Signature Spells At the gamemaster’s discretion, Vanarion’s Magical Repertoire may expand to include more spells as the attuned character earns Vanarion’s trust and performs heroic deeds with him. These extra spells may include the attuned character’s most frequently used spells or any other story-appropriate spell. At the height of his power, Vanarion’s Magical Repertoire may include true polymorph and wish . Adventure Hook: Vanarion’s Second Tour At the gamemaster’s discretion, player characters may find a way to restore Vanarion to life as a dark elf once more. Doing so turns the harp into a conventional magical instrument with the unbreakable minor property but creates a powerful and loyal bard ally. Restoring Vanarion to life as a dark elf includes persuading him that he will be safe from the evil forces that pursued him before and he won’t suffer a fate worse than being a sentient item, and might involve the use of true resurrection , wish , or divine intervention.
