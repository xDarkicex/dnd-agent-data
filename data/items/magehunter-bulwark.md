# Magehunter Bulwark

**description**: Armour (shield), rare (requires attunement) Component: monstrosity (sphinx) bone Like a leaden wall, Spellbreakers would encircle evil magic users, sending spells ricocheting up into the night sky. Eventually, when the enemy’s magic was spent, these red and black shields would thrust forward, hammering their foe to the ground where they could be shackled. Though few are now found, these magehunter bulwarks can be a thorn in the side of even the mightiest lich. While holding this shield, you have a +1 bonus to AC. This bonus is in addition to the shield’s normal bonus to AC. Deflect Spell. When you are hit by a spell attack or subjected to a spell or magical effect that allows you to make a Dexterity saving throw to reduce or avoid the damage taken while holding this shield, you can use your reaction to deflect some of the spell’s energy. When you do so, the damage you take from the attack or effect is reduced by 2d10. If you reduce the damage to 0, the spell has no effect on you, and you can attempt to reflect the spell’s raw energy at its origin. Make a ranged attack roll (+7 to hit) against the target, dealing force damage equal to the result of your 2d10 roll on a hit. Once this property of the shield has been used, roll a d4. On a 1, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
