# Stonesteel

**description**: Made from an alloy of steel and crystalline stone, this unique form of plate armor represents the height of dwarven metallurgy. It consists of overlapping, interlocking plates that cover the entire body, and it includes gauntlets, heavy leather boots, and a helmet. Stonesteel is heavy and somewhat unwieldy but offers superior protection to standard plate. Donning and doffing stonesteel armor takes twice as long as normal plate armor.
