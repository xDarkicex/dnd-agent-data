# Shadowblood Living Armor (Recently Grown)

**description**: Grown in the Night Forges of the Shadowblood Elves, then twisted and forced into the shape of an armor, these items feel like putting on a second skin. The material feels like something between tentacles and thick vines, and its inside is always covered in a thin layer of transparent mucus. To attune to a Shadowblood Living Armor, you have to wear it during the entire attunement time. The tentacles move and shift until they fit your body perfectly. The recently grown's armor's base AC is 13. When attuned, its inherent magic increases its AC to 14. When you've been successfully hit with a weapon by an enemy no more than one size category larger than you, you can command the Living Armor's teeth and tentacles to grasp your enemy's weapon. Once per long rest, as a reaction, you try to disarm your opponent. Your enemy has to succeed on a Strength or Dexterity (their choice) Saving Throw against DC 14, or drop their weapon. The Shadowblood Living Armor also has self-healing properties. If it is damaged in any way, but not entirely destroyed, it fully repairs itself within 24 hours.
