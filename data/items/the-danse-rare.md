# The Danse (Rare)

**description**: The Danse Weapon (Rapier), Rare (Requires Attunement) You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. Each slice of The Danse is accompanied by a violin note. When you slay a creature with this rapier, you can make a Charisma (Performance) check. The slain creature must then make a Charisma saving throw, the DC of which is equal to the result of your Charisma (Performance) check. On a failed save, their soul is immediately delivered to The Singing Sister and cannot be revived until she releases it (which she can do willingly at any time). When the night hag dies, all her ensnared souls are released. Deadly Danse. As part of an attack with this rapier, you can cause it to ring with an eerie vibrato, animating all Small or Medium corpses within 90 feet as skeletons for 1 minute. The Deadly Danse ends early if your turn ends and you haven’t attacked a hostile creature with The Danse since your last turn. If you are proficient in the Performance skill, the skeletons gain a bonus to their attack and damage rolls equal to your proficiency bonus. In combat, the skeletons share your initiative count, but take their turns immediately after yours. They can move and use their reaction on their own, but the only action they take on their turn is the Attack action, targeting the nearest creature (other than you), unless you take a bonus action on your turn to command them to take another action. That action can be one in their stat block or some other action. You can only command four skeletons at a time with a single bonus action. If you are incapacitated, the skeletons can take any action of their choice, not just Dodge. Once a skeleton is destroyed or the Deadly Danse ends, it disintegrates. You can use this property a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus and regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. Progression The Danse progresses when it ends the life of a sufficiently famous artist, determined by how widely known they are, as per the Progression Requirements table.
