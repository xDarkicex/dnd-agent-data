# Gloomwrought Studded Leather Armor

**description**: Gloomwrought Studded Leather Armor Armor (Light), Rare (Requires Attunement) This intricate grayscale armor was forged in the Shadowfell and is infused with that plane’s gloom. While you’re wearing this armor, you have advantage on saving throws you make to avoid or end the frightened condition on yourself. This armor has 3 charges. You can expend a charge to cast the Calm Emotions spell (save DC 15)from the armor. This armor regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. Studded Leather Armor. Made from tough but flexible leather, studded leather is reinforced with close-set rivets or spikes. AC: 12 + Dex modifier Weight: 13
