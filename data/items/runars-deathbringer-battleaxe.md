# Runars Deathbringer Battleaxe

**description**: Runar’s Deathbringer Battleaxe Weapon (battleaxe), legendary (requires attunement) This magical battleaxe was created by Runar, a legendary maker of weapons and armor. Attacks made with this weapon have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls. If wielded while also wielding a deathbringer shield, this battleaxe delivers its versatile damage (1d10) instead of its standard damage, even when only used in one hand. A bonus action may be used to strike this item against a deathbringer shield. This generates a lightning bolt with a range of 100 feet and a width of 5 feet, originating from the wielder in a direction of their choosing. Each creature in the line must make a Dexterity saving throw against a DC of 15. A creature takes 28 (8d6) lightning damage on a failed save, half as much on a success. Once this feature is used it may not be used again until the wielder completes a short or long rest.
