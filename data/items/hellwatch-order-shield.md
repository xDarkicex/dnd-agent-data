# Hellwatch Order Shield

**description**: Armor (shield), very rare (requires attunement) Properties Armor Class (AC): While holding this shield, you have a +2 bonus to AC. This bonus is in addition to the shield’s normal bonus to AC. Heavy Duty: This shield can only be attuned to a creature with a Strength score of 16 or higher. Defender from Evil: While holding this shield you gain an advantage on saves against spells and other magical effects cast by evil creatures. Banish: As an action, you can target a Medium or smaller creature within 5ft. That creature must make a DC CON SAVE 16 or be pushed back up to 30ft and be knocked Prone. They also receive 4 (1d8) force damage. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest. Defender of the Order: As a Bonus Action you can provide every ally within 5ft of you a +1 bonus to AC until the start of your next turn. Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a short or long rest.
