# Ring Mail of Weightlessness

**description**: Ring Mail of Weightlessness Armor (Heavy), Uncommon (Requires Attunement) This armor has 5 charges. While you wear it, you can use a bonus action to expend 1 or more charges to cast one of the following spells from the armor, targeting yourself: Jump (1 charge) or Levitate (2 charges). This armor regains 1d4 + 1 expended charges daily at dawn. Ring Mail. This armor is leather armor with heavy rings sewn into it. The rings help reinforce the armor against blows from swords and axes. Ring mail is inferior to chain mail, and it's usually worn only by those who can't afford better armor. Stealth. The wearer has disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks. AC : 14 Stealth : Disadvantage Weight : 40
