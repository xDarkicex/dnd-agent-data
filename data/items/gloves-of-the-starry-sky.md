# Gloves of the Starry Sky

**description**: Gloves of the Starry Sky Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement) These supple gloves are made from black velvet and have tiny diamonds sewn into them. While wearing them, you can cast the light spell at will. The gloves have 3 charges. You can use an action and expend 1 charge to cast magic missile as a 5th-level spell. The missiles created look like a shower of falling stars. The gloves regain 3 charges daily at dawn.
