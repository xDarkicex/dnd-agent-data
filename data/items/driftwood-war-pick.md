# Driftwood War Pick

**description**: Driftwood Weapon Weapon (any), rare (requires attunement) You have a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you roll to attack with this weapon, you score a critical hit when you roll either a 19 or 20 on a d20, but you fail to hit on either a 1 or 2.
