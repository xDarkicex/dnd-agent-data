# Figurine of Wondrous Power, Tin Dog

**description**: Wondrous item, common Tin Dog (Common). This simple, tin statuette can become a dog for up to 8 hours, loyally following your commands to the best of its abilities. The dog uses the statistics of a jackal, except the dog has a Strength of 10. Once it has been used, the figurine can’t be used again until 2 days have passed.

- Item Type: Wondrous item
- Item Rarity: common
