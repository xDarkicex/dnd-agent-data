# Dancing Caltrops

**description**: Wondrous item, common (requires attunement) After you pour these magic caltrops out of the bag into an area, you can use a bonus action to animate them and command them to move up to 10 feet to occupy a different square area that is 5 feet on a side. Tripping Hazard. You can use a reaction when a creature enters the area containing the caltrops to make the caltrops skitter into the creature’s path. If the creature isn’t moving at half speed, it has disadvantage on the saving throw. If the creature is moving at half speed, it must make the saving throw, but it doesn’t have disadvantage. Once used, this feature of the caltrops can’t be used again until the next dawn.
