# Miliese Prism

**description**: Miliese Prism Spell catalyst, common This transparent triangular, column-shaped crystal fractures light into dozens of colors when held to the light. When you cast the magic missile spell, you can choose to allow the spell to consume this spell catalyst. When you do, the darts created by this spell deal a damage type of your choice instead of force damage.
