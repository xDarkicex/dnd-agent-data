# Flask of the Fallen

**description**: Wondrous Item, Rare This tiny crystal flask is adorned with the figure of a weeping person and is attached to a silver chain. During a process that takes 1 minute, you can fill the flask with a few drops of tears or blood from a creature that has a CR of 1/4 or higher and isn't conjured or summoned by magic. Consume Essence. As a bonus action, you can drink the flask's content to gain one of the following benefits determined by the type of creature for 1 hour: Beast. You have advantage on Perception checks and your speed increases by 10 feet. Celestial. You are affected as by the Bless spell. Dragon. You have a Fly speed equal to your walking speed. Fey. You have advantage on ability checks and saving throws you make to avoid or end the Charmed and Frightened conditions on yourself. Fiend. You have advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. Giant. You gain the Enlarge effect of the Enlarge/Reduce spell. Humanoid. You have advantage on ability checks and saving throws you make using one ability of your choice. Monstrosity. You gain temporary hit points equal to twice the number of Hit Dice you have. Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
